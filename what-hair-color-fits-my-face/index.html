What Hair Color Fits My Face A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Oliver Oct 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and want to switch it up, but not sure which shade will complement your face best? It can be overwhelming trying to figure out what hair color fits your face, but we’re here to help. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to finding your perfect shade based on your skin tone, eye color, and face shape.

Description

Choosing the right hair color can be a game-changer for your overall look. Hair color can enhance your features, make your skin glow, and boost your confidence. The key is to find a shade that complements your natural features and highlights your best qualities.

How to Find Your Perfect Hair Color

Before you start browsing through hair color swatches, there are a few things you need to consider. The first step is to determine your skin tone, eye color, and face shape. These factors will help you narrow down which hair colors will suit you best.

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors in finding the right hair color. There are two main categories of skin tones: warm and cool. If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow or golden hue. If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blue hue.

Here’s a quick way to determine your skin tone:

  • If you look best in gold jewelry, you likely have warm undertones.
  • If you look best in silver jewelry, you likely have cool undertones.
  • If you’re still not sure, try holding up a piece of white fabric to your face. If your skin looks yellowish or golden, you likely have warm undertones. If your skin looks pink or blue, you likely have cool undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in finding the right hair color. Certain shades can make your eyes pop and enhance their natural beauty.

Here are a few hair color suggestions based on eye color:

  • If you have blue eyes, consider shades of blonde or light brown.
  • If you have green eyes, consider shades of red or copper.
  • If you have brown eyes, consider shades of dark brown or black.

Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape

Your face shape can also influence which hair color will suit you best. Certain shades can help balance out your features and create a more harmonious look.

Here are a few hair color suggestions based on face shape:

  • If you have a round face, consider shades of caramel or honey blonde.
  • If you have a square face, consider shades of chestnut or chocolate brown.
  • If you have an oval face, consider shades of golden or strawberry blonde.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Now that you’ve determined your skin tone, eye color, and face shape, it’s time to start browsing through hair color options. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Stick with shades that are within two shades of your natural hair color for a more natural look.
  • Consider the maintenance required for different shades. Lighter shades may require more upkeep than darker shades.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new! Hair color can always be changed if you don’t love the result.

FAQ

Can I dye my hair a drastically different color than my natural hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair a drastically different color than your natural hair. However, keep in mind that it may require more maintenance and upkeep to keep the color looking fresh.

Will certain hair colors wash me out?

Yes, certain hair colors can wash out your complexion. If you have fair skin, try to avoid very dark or black hair colors. If you have darker skin, try to avoid very light or icy blonde hair colors.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you’ve found your perfect hair color, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are a few hair care tips for colored hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and fade your color.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Now that you have a better idea of what hair color fits your face, it’s time to book that salon appointment and get ready to rock your new look!
