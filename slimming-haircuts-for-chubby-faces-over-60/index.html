Slimming Haircuts For Chubby Faces Over 60 A Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 60 and have a chubby face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. As we age, our faces tend to lose definition and can become fuller. But don't worry, there are plenty of slimming haircuts that can help you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best hairstyles for chubby faces over 60, including how to achieve them and some tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Slimming Haircuts
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for a chubby face over 60, the goal is to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. This can be achieved by adding layers or volume to the top or sides of the hair while keeping the length at or below the chin. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that are shorter on the sides and longer on top.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as this can make the face look rounder.
  • Consider getting a side-swept bang to draw attention away from the chin and towards the eyes.
  • Try adding layers to create movement and texture.
  • Consider getting a bob or lob, but make sure it's layered and not blunt.

Step-by-Step Guide to Slimming Haircuts

Now that you know what to look for in a slimming haircut, here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed to add volume and texture.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  4. If you have bangs, blow dry them to the side to create a side-swept look.
  5. If you are getting a layered cut, section your hair and use a razor to create soft, textured layers.
  6. If you are getting a bob or lob, make sure the ends are layered and not blunt.
  7. Once your haircut is complete, use a texturizing spray to add movement and volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have the perfect slimming haircut, it's important to maintain it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo between washes to add texture and lift.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural texture.

FAQs

Q: Can I still have long hair with a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair with a chubby face. The key is to add layers and volume to create movement and texture.

Q: Will a pixie cut look good on a chubby face over 60?

A: While a pixie cut can look great on some people, it may not be the best choice for a chubby face over 60. Shorter haircuts tend to emphasize the face, so it's best to go for a style that adds length and volume.

Hair Care Tips for Chubby Faces Over 60

In addition to choosing the right haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals like hair dyes and relaxers, as they can damage your hair and scalp.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect slimming haircut for your chubby face over 60 and keep it looking great for years to come. Remember to consult with your stylist to find the best look for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
