How To Get Dreads Fast With Short Hair A Complete Guide

Have you always wanted to rock the dreadlocks but thought it isn't possible with short hair? Well, think again! In this article, we will show you how to get dreads fast with short hair. We will cover all the steps, tips, and hair care needed to maintain your dreadlocks. So, let's get started!

How to Get Dreads Fast with Short Hair

The first step to getting dreads fast with short hair is to understand that it will take time and patience. However, there are some ways to speed up the process:

  • Use a dreadlock shampoo to dry out your hair and make it more malleable.
  • Backcomb your hair to create knots and tangles that will eventually form into dreadlocks.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten the knots and tangles.
  • Use a locking gel to keep the knots and tangles in place.
  • Leave your hair alone and let the dreadlocks form naturally.

These methods will help you get dreads fast with short hair. However, it is important to note that everyone's hair is different and may take longer or shorter to form dreadlocks.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, let's go through the step-by-step process of getting dreads fast with short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a dreadlock shampoo to dry it out and make it more malleable.
  2. Section your hair into small, even sections.
  3. Backcomb each section of hair to create knots and tangles.
  4. Use a crochet hook to tighten the knots and tangles. Be gentle and take your time to avoid damaging your hair.
  5. Apply a locking gel to keep the knots and tangles in place.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 for each section of hair.
  7. Leave your hair alone and let the dreadlocks form naturally. Avoid brushing or combing your hair.
  8. Sleep with a durag or headscarf to protect your hair while you sleep.
  9. After a few weeks, your hair should start to form into dreadlocks. You can use the crochet hook and locking gel to maintain them.
  10. Be patient! It can take several months for your hair to fully form into dreadlocks.

Tips for Getting Dreads Fast with Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you get dreads fast with short hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a dreadlock shampoo to dry out your hair and make it more malleable.
  • Section your hair into small, even sections.
  • Backcomb each section of hair to create knots and tangles.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten the knots and tangles.
  • Apply a locking gel to keep the knots and tangles in place.
  • Be gentle and patient. Avoid damaging your hair.
  • Let your hair form into dreadlocks naturally.
  • Sleep with a durag or headscarf to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Maintain your dreadlocks with the crochet hook and locking gel.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting dreads fast with short hair:

Can I get dreads with short hair?

Yes, you can get dreads with short hair. It may take longer than with longer hair, but it is possible.

How long does it take to get dreads with short hair?

It can take several months to a year to get dreads with short hair. It depends on your hair type and how well you maintain your dreadlocks.

What is the best way to maintain dreadlocks?

Use a crochet hook and locking gel to maintain your dreadlocks. Be gentle and patient, and avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Now that you have dreadlocks, it is important to take care of them. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners or oils that can build up in your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Congratulations, you now know how to get dreads fast with short hair! Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and to take care of your dreadlocks with the proper hair care techniques.
