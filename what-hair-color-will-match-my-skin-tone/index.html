What Hair Color Will Match My Skin Tone

Written by Eveline Aug 29, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking to change up your hair color but aren't sure what will look best with your skin tone? Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can make all the difference in how you look and feel. In this article, we'll explore what hair color will match your skin tone and give you some tips on how to find the perfect shade.

Description

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair color. The right hair color can complement your skin tone and make you look radiant, while the wrong color can make you look washed out or even sickly. Your skin tone is determined by the amount of melanin in your skin, which affects how light or dark your skin appears. There are three main types of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral.

How to Find Your Skin Tone

The easiest way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear greenish, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell if they're blue or green, you likely have a neutral skin tone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the undertones in your skin. If your skin has pink, red, or blue undertones, you have a cool skin tone. If your skin has yellow, peach, or golden undertones, you have a warm skin tone. Neutral skin tones will have a mix of both cool and warm undertones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Now that you know your skin tone, it's time to find the perfect hair color to match. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Use the methods above to determine if you have a cool, warm, or neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Choose a Hair Color

Once you know your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that will complement it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors, like ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, or black.
  • Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors, like golden blonde, honey blonde, warm brown, or auburn.
  • Neutral skin tones can pull off a variety of hair colors, but look best with neutral shades like caramel or beige.

Step 3: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colors will complement them. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colors will bring out the warmth in your eyes.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you find the perfect shade to match your skin tone and give you advice on how to maintain your new color.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've found the perfect hair color, it's important to take care of it to make it last as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, which can also fade the color.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

FAQs

Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors, like ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, or black.

What hair colors are best for warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors, like golden blonde, honey blonde, warm brown, or auburn.

What hair colors are best for neutral skin tones?

Neutral skin tones can pull off a variety of hair colors, but look best with neutral shades like caramel or beige.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Color

Now that you have your new hair color, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, which can also fade the color.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can seem daunting, but with these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect shade to match your unique complexion. Remember to take care of your new color with proper hair care and maintenance to keep it looking its best.
