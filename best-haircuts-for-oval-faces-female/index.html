Best Haircuts For Oval Faces Female A Complete Guide

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky enough to have one of the most versatile face shapes when it comes to haircuts. An oval face shape is characterized by a gently rounded hairline and a slightly wider forehead than the chin. This face shape is considered to be the ideal face shape as it can pull off a variety of haircuts. In this article, we will guide you through the best haircuts for oval faces female in 2023.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for oval faces female, let's first determine if you have an oval face shape. Here are the steps you need to follow:

  1. Take a full-face photo of yourself with your hair pulled back.
  2. Print out the photo and draw a line from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin.
  3. Draw a line across the widest part of your face (usually the cheekbones).
  4. Measure the length of your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin and compare it to the width of your face.

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and a slightly wider forehead than the chin, then you have an oval face shape.

Best Haircuts for Oval Faces Female

As we mentioned earlier, oval face shapes can pull off a variety of haircuts. Here are some of the best haircuts for oval faces female:

1. Long Layers

Long layers work great for oval face shapes as they add volume and movement to the hair. They also help to frame the face and accentuate your features.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on oval face shapes. It can be done in many different lengths and styles, from a short bob to a shoulder-length bob.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped haircut that works well for oval face shapes. It can be styled in many different ways, from messy and textured to sleek and polished.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs work well for oval face shapes as they help to balance out the forehead and add dimension to the hair. They can be worn long or short and can be styled in many different ways.

5. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob (long bob) is a great haircut for oval face shapes as it adds texture and movement to the hair. It can be worn with or without bangs and can be styled in many different ways.

Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know the best haircuts for oval faces female, it's time to get your hair cut. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting hair for oval face shapes.
  2. Bring photos of the hairstyle you want to your appointment.
  3. Communicate with your hairstylist about what you like and don't like about your hair.
  4. Trust your hairstylist's recommendations on what will look best on you.
  5. After your haircut, ask your hairstylist for styling tips and product recommendations.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

1. Can oval face shapes pull off short haircuts?

Yes, oval face shapes can pull off short haircuts such as a pixie cut or a bob haircut.

2. Do bangs work well for oval face shapes?

Yes, bangs work well for oval face shapes. Side-swept bangs are a great option for oval face shapes as they help to balance out the forehead.

3. What is the best way to style a bob haircut?

The best way to style a bob haircut is to use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume and movement in the hair. You can also use a curling iron or straightener to create different styles.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape

Aside from getting the right haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for oval face shapes:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as it can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.

Now that you know the best haircuts for oval faces female and how to take care of your hair, it's time to book your next salon appointment. Remember to communicate with your hairstylist about what you want and trust their recommendations. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can have the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape.
