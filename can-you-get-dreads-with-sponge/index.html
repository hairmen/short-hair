Can You Get Dreads With Sponge

Written by Daniel Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for an easy way to get dreadlocks, you might have heard about using a sponge to create them. While this technique is not new, it has gained popularity in recent years as a quick and affordable way to achieve the look you want. But can you really get dreads with a sponge?

Description

In this blog post, we will explore whether you can get dreads with a sponge, how to do it, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care. We will also discuss the advantages and disadvantages of using a sponge for dreads and help you decide if it is the right option for you.

How to Get Dreads with Sponge?

Getting dreads with a sponge is a straightforward process that requires a few tools and some patience. Here are the steps you need to follow to get started:

Step 1: Get a sponge

The first thing you need to do is to get a sponge designed for dreadlocks. These sponges are usually made of foam or rubber and have small holes that help to create the dreadlock pattern. You can find them online or at your local beauty supply store.

Step 2: Wet your hair

Once you have your sponge, wet your hair thoroughly. This will help to make it easier to work with and create the dreadlock pattern.

Step 3: Apply product

Apply some dreadlock product, such as locking gel or wax, to your hair. This will help to hold the dreads in place and give them a more defined look.

Step 4: Sponge your hair

Take your sponge and rub it in a circular motion over your hair. The sponge's holes will help to create the dreadlock pattern by twisting and knotting your hair. Continue to sponge your hair until you achieve the desired look.

Step 5: Let your hair dry

Once you have created your dreads, let your hair dry completely. This can take several hours or even overnight, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Tips for Getting Dreads with Sponge

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using a sponge to create dreadlocks:

  • Use a good quality dreadlock product to help hold your dreads in place.
  • Wet your hair thoroughly before starting to sponge it.
  • Be patient and take your time. Creating dreads with a sponge can take several hours.
  • Try different sponge sizes and shapes to create different dreadlock patterns.

FAQ about Getting Dreads with Sponge

Can you get dreads with a sponge on any hair type?

Yes, you can get dreads with a sponge on any hair type, but it might be easier on hair that is naturally curly or kinky.

Do you need to use a dreadlock product?

While you can create dreads without using a dreadlock product, using one will help to hold your dreads in place and give them a more defined look.

How often should you sponge your hair to maintain your dreads?

You should sponge your hair every few weeks to maintain your dreads and keep them looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for Dreads with Sponge

Once you have created your dreads with a sponge, you need to take proper care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your dreads:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a dreadlock shampoo to keep them clean and free of buildup.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your dreads and make them brittle.
  • Protect your dreads from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Get your dreads retwisted every few months to maintain their shape and prevent them from unraveling.

Conclusion

Getting dreads with a sponge is a quick and affordable way to achieve the look you want. While it might take some time and patience to create your dreads, the results are well worth it. By following the tips and taking proper care of your dreads, you can enjoy your new hairstyle for years to come.
