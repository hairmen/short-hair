How To Cut Front Of Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the hair salon just to get a simple trim? With a little practice and some patience, you can learn how to cut the front of your hair with scissors at home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step so you can achieve a fresh, polished look without leaving your house.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's completely doable. In this guide, we'll focus on cutting the front of your hair with scissors. This is a great way to maintain your bangs or add some layers to the front of your hair without having to go to the salon.

How to Cut Front of Hair with Scissors: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to help section your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. This will help remove any buildup or dirt that may interfere with your cutting. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting, as wet hair can be more difficult to work with.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, section off the front of your hair. You can do this by parting your hair down the middle or to one side, depending on the look you want to achieve. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way so you can focus on the front.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use the scissors to make small, upward snips at a slight angle to create texture and movement. Be sure to work slowly and carefully, checking your progress in the mirror as you go.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat this process on each section of hair, working from one side to the other. Keep in mind that you can always take off more hair, but you can't put it back on, so start with small snips and work your way up.

Step 6: Blend and Check

Once you've finished cutting the front of your hair, use the scissors to blend any harsh lines or uneven sections. Check your work in the mirror to make sure everything looks even and polished.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Scissors

Cutting your own hair can be tricky, but with these tips, you can achieve a salon-worthy look at home:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors specifically designed for cutting hair.
  • Work slowly and carefully, checking your progress in the mirror as you go.
  • Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid taking off too much hair at once.
  • Use the comb to section off your hair and clip the rest out of the way to make it easier to work with.
  • When in doubt, leave it to the professionals. If you're not comfortable cutting your hair, don't hesitate to make an appointment with a stylist.

FAQ

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

While you can technically use regular scissors to cut your hair, it's not recommended. Regular scissors can be dull or uneven, which can lead to uneven cuts and damaged hair. Invest in a pair of scissors specifically designed for cutting hair for the best results.

How often should I cut the front of my hair?

It's recommended to trim the front of your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy. However, the frequency of your cuts may depend on your hair type and personal preference.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

Don't panic! If you make a mistake, take a deep breath and try to correct it as best you can. If the mistake is minor, you may be able to blend it in with the rest of your hair. If it's more severe, consider making an appointment with a stylist to fix it.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, be sure to take care of it properly to keep it looking healthy and fresh. This may include using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can learn how to cut the front of your hair with scissors and achieve a salon-worthy look at home. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to maintain your hair and keep it looking its best.
