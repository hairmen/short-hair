How To Straight Hair With Blow Dry Tips Steps And Hair Care

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of having unruly, curly hair? Do you want to achieve that sleek and straight look? Worry no more! In this article, we will teach you how to straight hair with blow dry. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to ensure that you achieve a salon-worthy look.

  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Straightening Hair with Blow Dry

How to Straight Hair with Blow Dry

Blow drying your hair is one of the easiest and quickest ways to straighten your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product build-up that might be on your hair. It will also make your hair more manageable and easier to straighten.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair. Make sure that your hair is not too wet before you start blow-drying it. If your hair is too wet, it will take longer to dry and straighten.

Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before you start blow-drying your hair, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from the heat of the blow dryer and prevent damage.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or ties. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to dry and straighten each section of your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a nozzle attachment on your blow dryer to direct the air flow and make sure that the heat is distributed evenly.

Step 6: Finish with a Cold Shot

Once you have blow-dried your hair, use the cool setting on your blow dryer to give your hair a blast of cool air. This will help to set the style and make your hair shiny and smooth.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to straighten hair with blow dry:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly to make sure that there is no shampoo or conditioner left in your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair. Make sure that your hair is not dripping wet before you start blow drying it.

Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before you start blow-drying your hair, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from the heat of the blow dryer and prevent damage.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or ties. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 5: Start Blow-Drying

Start by blow-drying the bottom section of your hair. Use a round brush to guide your hair while blow-drying. Hold the brush and the blow dryer in one hand and use the other hand to hold the section of hair taut.

Move the brush and the blow dryer down the length of your hair while pulling your hair taut. This will help to straighten your hair and remove any kinks or curls.

Step 6: Repeat on Other Sections

Once you have finished blow-drying the bottom section of your hair, release the next section and repeat the process. Work your way up until you have blow-dried all of your hair.

Step 7: Finish with a Cold Shot

Once you have blow-dried your hair, use the cool setting on your blow dryer to give your hair a blast of cool air. This will help to set the style and make your hair shiny and smooth.

Tips for Straightening Hair with Blow Dry

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when straightening your hair with a blow dryer:

  • Choose a good quality blow dryer with a nozzle attachment. This will help to direct the air flow and ensure that the heat is distributed evenly.
  • Use a round brush to guide your hair while blow-drying. This will help to straighten your hair and give it a smooth finish.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before blow-drying. This will help to protect your hair from the heat and prevent damage.
  • Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or ties. This will make it easier to blow-dry your hair and ensure that you don't miss any areas.
  • Use the cool setting on your blow dryer to give your hair a blast of cool air. This will help to set the style and make your hair shiny and smooth.

FAQs about Straightening Hair with Blow Dry

Q: How long does it take to straighten hair with a blow dryer?

A: It depends on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it takes around 30 minutes to straighten hair with a blow dryer.

Q: Can I use a blow dryer to straighten curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use a blow dryer to straighten curly hair. However, it may take longer to straighten curly hair than straight hair.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair with a blow dryer?

A: It is best to avoid straightening your hair with a blow dryer too often as it can cause damage to your hair. Try to limit it to once a week or less.

Hair Care Tips for Straightened Hair

Straightening your hair with a blow dryer can cause damage to your hair. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, straightened hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for straightened hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.

With these tips, you can achieve beautiful, straight hair with a blow dryer. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
