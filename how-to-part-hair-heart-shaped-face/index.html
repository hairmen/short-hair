How To Part Hair For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Frank Sep 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your heart-shaped face? One of the most important things to consider is how you part your hair. A well-placed part can balance out the proportions of your face and accentuate your best features. In this blog post, we'll teach you how to part your hair for a heart-shaped face.

Table of Contents

  • How to Part Hair for Heart Shaped Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Parting Hair
  • FAQs

How to Part Hair for Heart Shaped Face

When it comes to parting your hair for a heart-shaped face, the goal is to create balance between your forehead and chin. Here are a few tips:

  • A center part can elongate your face and draw attention away from a wider forehead.
  • A side part can soften the angles of your face and create the illusion of a more balanced jawline.
  • Consider a deep side part to accentuate your cheekbones and draw attention to your eyes.

Ultimately, the best way to part your hair will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face. Experiment with different parts to find the one that works best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to part your hair for a heart-shaped face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to create your desired part. Remember to keep the part in line with the arch of your eyebrow for the most flattering look.
  3. Use a styling product, such as hairspray or pomade, to keep your part in place.

That's it! With these simple steps, you can create a part that flatters your heart-shaped face.

Tips for Parting Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when parting your hair:

  • Use a comb with fine teeth for a precise part.
  • If you have a cowlick, try parting your hair on the opposite side to help tame it.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a part. A center part can be difficult to maintain with curly or thick hair, while a side part may be too subtle for fine hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I part my hair in the middle if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, a center part can be flattering for a heart-shaped face. Just make sure to keep the part in line with the arch of your eyebrow.

Q: How deep should a side part be for a heart-shaped face?

A: A deep side part can be very flattering for a heart-shaped face. Aim for a part that starts at the outer corner of your eyebrow and extends diagonally towards the crown of your head.

Hair Care Tips

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove buildup and keep your scalp healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and complementing your heart-shaped face.

Conclusion

Parting your hair for a heart-shaped face is all about creating balance and accentuating your best features. Experiment with different parts to find the one that works best for you, and remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
