How To Tame Wavy Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a guy with wavy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep it under control. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about how to tame wavy hair for guys. We’ll cover what causes wavy hair, how to style it, and essential hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

What Causes Wavy Hair?

Wavy hair is caused by genetics. If your parents have wavy or curly hair, it’s likely that you will too. Your hair’s texture is determined by the shape of your hair follicles, which can range from straight to curly.

Wavy hair can also be caused by environmental factors such as humidity, heat, and exposure to chemicals. If you live in a humid climate, your hair is more likely to become frizzy and wavy.

How to Style Wavy Hair

Styling wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, it’s possible to achieve a variety of looks. Here are some of our top tips for styling wavy hair:

1. Know Your Hair Type

Before you start styling your hair, it’s important to understand your hair type. If you have thick, coarse hair, you’ll need different products and techniques than someone with fine, thin hair.

2. Invest in Good Products

Investing in quality hair products is essential for taming wavy hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for wavy or curly hair.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid tangles and breakage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage wavy hair and cause frizz. Instead of using a blow dryer or straightener, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling wavy hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair.

2. Towel-Dry Your Hair

Gently towel-dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your damp hair. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

4. Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your natural waves.

5. Air-Dry Your Hair

Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Tips for Taming Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for taming wavy hair:

1. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause frizz and dryness. Try washing your hair every other day instead of every day.

2. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and tangles.

3. Avoid Touching Your Hair Too Much

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz and disrupt your natural waves. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day.

FAQ

1. Can I straighten my wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten wavy hair using a straightener or a blow dryer. However, heat styling can damage your hair over time, so it’s important to use a heat protectant and not to straighten your hair too often.

2. Can I cut my own wavy hair?

We recommend going to a professional stylist to get your hair cut, especially if you have wavy or curly hair. A stylist will be able to give you a cut that works with your hair’s texture and shape.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for wavy hair:

1. Use a Detangling Brush

Use a detangling brush to gently brush your hair and avoid tangles.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep it hydrated and healthy.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can tame your wavy hair and keep it looking its best. Remember, taking care of your hair is an important part of your overall grooming routine.
