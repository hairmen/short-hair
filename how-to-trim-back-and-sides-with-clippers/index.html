How To Trim Back And Sides With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Trimming your hair at home can seem quite daunting, especially if you are used to going to a professional hairdresser. However, with the right tools and a bit of practice, it can be an easy and cost-effective way to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to trim your back and sides with clippers, as well as some tips and tricks to help you get the best results possible.

How to Trim Back and Sides with Clippers

Trimming the back and sides of your hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you don't have much experience. However, with a bit of practice and some patience, you can achieve a great result. Here are the basic steps:

1. Choose the Right Clippers

The first step is to choose the right clippers. Look for clippers that come with different attachments, so you can choose the right length for your hair. If you have thick hair, choose clippers with a more powerful motor. Also, make sure the clippers are well-maintained and oiled, so they run smoothly.

2. Wash Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and then towel-dry it. This will make it easier to cut and will help you achieve a more even result.

3. Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair in the direction you want it to go. This will help you see any uneven patches and will make it easier to cut.

4. Start with the Sides

Attach the right length clipper to your clippers and start trimming the sides. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion. Make sure you don't go too high up, as this can create a harsh line. Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure both sides are even.

5. Move to the Back

Once you have trimmed the sides, move on to the back. Use the same technique as for the sides, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Again, use a mirror to check your progress and make sure the back is even.

6. Blend the Sides and Back

Once you have trimmed the sides and back, blend them together using a clipper-over-comb technique. This involves using a comb to lift the hair and then using the clippers to trim the hair that sticks out of the comb. This will create a smooth transition between the sides and back.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide to help you trim your back and sides with clippers:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start, make sure you have all the tools you need. This includes clippers, clipper attachments, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and then towel-dry it. This will make it easier to cut and will help you achieve a more even result.

Step 3: Choose the Right Attachment

Choose the right attachment for your clippers, depending on the length you want. Start with a longer attachment and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Step 4: Start with the Sides

Start trimming the sides, using an upward motion. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure both sides are even.

Step 5: Move to the Back

Once you have trimmed the sides, move on to the back. Use the same technique as for the sides, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Again, use a mirror to check your progress and make sure the back is even.

Step 6: Blend the Sides and Back

Blend the sides and back together using a clipper-over-comb technique. Use a comb to lift the hair and then use the clippers to trim the hair that sticks out of the comb. This will create a smooth transition between the sides and back.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the best results:

  • Practice makes perfect - don't be discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Don't rush - take your time and make sure you cut evenly.
  • Start with a longer attachment and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a clipper-over-comb technique to blend the sides and back.

FAQs

Q: Can I use clippers to trim my hair if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can use clippers to trim curly hair. However, make sure you use a longer attachment and be gentle to avoid cutting off too much hair.

Q: Can I use clippers to trim my hair if it's thin?

A: Yes, you can use clippers to trim thin hair. However, be gentle and avoid using a clipper-over-comb technique, as this can create a harsh line.

Hair Care

After trimming your hair with clippers, make sure you wash it again to remove any loose hairs. Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny, and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to trim your back and sides with clippers like a pro. Remember to take your time and be patient, and soon you'll be able to achieve the perfect haircut at home.
