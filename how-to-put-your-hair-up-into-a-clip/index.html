How To Put Your Hair Up Into A Clip

Written by Aprilia Nov 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to style your hair, putting it up into a clip is a great option. Not only is it a simple style, but it's also a great way to keep your hair out of your face. Whether you're running errands, working out, or just need to get your hair out of the way, a clip can be a lifesaver. In this article, we'll cover how to put your hair up into a clip, step-by-step, and provide some tips and hair care advice along the way.

Description

A hair clip is a simple hair accessory that can be used to hold your hair in place. Clips come in a variety of sizes, shapes, and styles, making them a versatile option for any hair type. They can be used to create a half-up, half-down style, secure a ponytail, or keep your hair out of your face. Clips are also great for adding a little bit of flair to your hairdo.

How to

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before you start putting your hair up into a clip, it's important to brush it out. This will help to remove any tangles and make it easier to work with. Use a brush or comb to gently detangle your hair from root to tip.

Step 2: Decide on a Style

Next, decide on the style you want to create. Do you want a simple clip to hold your hair out of your face, or do you want to create a more intricate updo? Once you have an idea of the style you want, you can start to gather your hair.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Using your fingers, gather the hair you want to clip up. If you're creating a half-up, half-down style, gather the top section of your hair. If you're creating a ponytail, gather all of your hair at the nape of your neck. Use a brush or comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 4: Secure the Clip

Now it's time to secure the clip. Open the clip and position it where you want it on your head. Then, use your other hand to hold your hair in place as you snap the clip shut. Make sure the clip is secure and that your hair is held firmly in place.

Tips

  • Experiment with different clip styles and sizes to find what works best for your hair type.
  • If your hair is thick or heavy, use two clips to secure it in place.
  • If you're using a metal clip, be careful not to pinch your scalp or pull your hair when securing it.
  • Add some hairspray or texturizing spray to your hair before putting it up to help keep it in place.

FAQ

What type of clip should I use?

The type of clip you use will depend on your hair type and the style you want to create. For example, a small claw clip can be used to create a half-up, half-down style, while a larger jaw clip can be used to create an updo. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

How do I keep my clip from slipping?

If your clip is slipping, try adding some hairspray or texturizing spray to your hair before putting it up. You can also use bobby pins or hair elastics to help hold your hair in place.

Hair Care

When putting your hair up into a clip, it's important to be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair. This can cause breakage and damage over time. Additionally, make sure to give your hair a break from clips and other hair accessories to prevent damage.

Regularly washing and conditioning your hair can also help to keep it healthy and strong. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. With these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling its best, whether you're wearing it up or down.
