Good Short Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thick curly hair that just won't behave? Well, you're not alone! Many people with thick curly hair struggle to find a suitable haircut that works with their hair type. But fear not, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss some of the best short haircuts for thick curly hair that will have you looking great and feeling confident in no time.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's full of volume and texture, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. The key to finding a good short haircut for thick curly hair is to choose a style that works with your natural curls and doesn't require too much maintenance.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for thick curly hair, it's important to consider the shape of your face and the texture of your hair. A good stylist will be able to recommend a style that works with your hair type and face shape. It's also important to communicate your expectations and preferences with your stylist so that they can create a style that you'll be happy with.

Step by step

Here are some of the best short haircuts for thick curly hair:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well with thick curly hair. This style is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It's a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great short haircut for thick curly hair. This style works best when the hair is cut to chin length or just above the shoulders. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or curly.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered haircut that works well with thick curly hair. This style is short on the sides and back, with longer layers throughout. It's a great style for adding volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

When styling your short haircut for thick curly hair, it's important to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl enhancing creams and gels. It's also important to avoid over-styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for thick curly hair?

The best short haircut for thick curly hair depends on your face shape and hair texture. Some of the most popular short haircuts for thick curly hair include the pixie cut, bob cut, and shag cut.

How do I style my short haircut for thick curly hair?

To style your short haircut for thick curly hair, use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl enhancing creams and gels. Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut for thick curly hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

With the right short haircut and hair care routine, you can embrace your thick curly hair and feel confident every day!
