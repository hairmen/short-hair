How To Flatten Puffy Asian Side Hair

Written by Robby Jul 15, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have puffy Asian side hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style it. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to flatten your hair and achieve the look you want. In this article, we’ll provide you with tips and tricks on how to flatten puffy Asian side hair.

Description

Puffy Asian side hair is a common hair type among Asians. It’s characterized by hair that sticks out on the sides, creating a puffy appearance. This can be caused by genetics, humidity, or improper hair care. It can be difficult to style and often requires a lot of effort to achieve a sleek look.

How to Flatten Puffy Asian Side Hair

Step 1: Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the most important steps in flattening puffy Asian side hair is to use a good shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed to add moisture to your hair without weighing it down. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals, as these can dry out your hair and make it more difficult to style.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a blow dryer to dry it. This will help to flatten your hair and make it easier to style. Use a round brush to smooth out your hair as you blow dry it, focusing on the sides to help flatten them.

Step 3: Use a Flat Iron

If your hair is still puffy after blow drying, use a flat iron to straighten it. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Start at the roots and slowly work your way down to the ends, using the flat iron to smooth out any bumps or kinks.

Step 4: Use Hair Products

Once your hair is straightened, use hair products to keep it in place. Look for products that are designed to add shine and control frizz, as these will help to keep your hair looking sleek and smooth throughout the day.

Tips

  • Don’t wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more difficult to style.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look even more puffy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair after washing it, as this will help to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is designed to help flatten puffy Asian side hair, such as a layered cut or a bob.

FAQ

What causes puffy Asian side hair?

Puffy Asian side hair can be caused by genetics, humidity, or improper hair care. It’s important to use products that are designed to add moisture to your hair without weighing it down.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day, as washing it every day can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more difficult to style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. This includes using a good shampoo and conditioner, avoiding harsh chemicals, and using heat protectant spray when using styling tools.

By following these tips and tricks, you can flatten puffy Asian side hair and achieve the look you want. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
