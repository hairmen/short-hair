How To Do Butterfly Locs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new and unique hairstyle to try? Look no further than butterfly locs! This trendy hairstyle involves creating long, flowing twists adorned with delicate butterfly-shaped parts. Not only are butterfly locs visually stunning, but they're also low-maintenance and protective for your natural hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve this look, as well as tips for maintenance and care.

How to

Before you begin, gather your materials. You'll need:

  • Pre-stretched braiding hair
  • Rat tail comb
  • Butterfly clips
  • Braid spray or oil
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair ties

Now, follow these steps to create your own butterfly locs:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into small, square sections. Use your rat tail comb to create a straight part down the center of your head, then make additional parts on each side of the center part. Use butterfly clips to secure each section of hair out of the way.

Step 2: Braid Your Hair

Starting with one section, use your rat tail comb to create a small, triangular part at the base of the section. Begin braiding your hair, adding in pre-stretched braiding hair as you go. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your natural hair, then secure the braid with a hair tie. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 3: Create Butterfly Parts

Once all of your hair is braided, use your rat tail comb to create a small, triangular part at the base of each braid. Use bobby pins to secure the braids out of the way.

Next, create butterfly-shaped parts on top of each braid. To do this, use your rat tail comb to create a small loop at the base of the braid, then create two small triangles on either side of the loop. Use your bobby pins to secure the loop and triangles in place.

Step 4: Finish the Look

Once all of your butterfly parts are in place, use braid spray or oil to smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your hair. You can also accessorize your butterfly locs with beads or jewelry for an extra special touch.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating and maintaining your butterfly locs:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and detangled before you begin braiding.
  • Use pre-stretched braiding hair to help reduce tension on your natural hair.
  • Don't braid your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase to protect your butterfly locs while you sleep.
  • Keep your scalp hydrated and healthy by massaging in a lightweight oil or scalp serum every few days.

FAQ

Here are answers to a few common questions about butterfly locs:

How long do butterfly locs last?

With proper care, butterfly locs can last up to two months.

Can you wash butterfly locs?

Yes, you can wash your butterfly locs. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and be sure to thoroughly rinse out all product.

Can you swim with butterfly locs?

While you can swim with butterfly locs, it's best to wear a swim cap to protect your hair from chlorine and saltwater.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for keeping your butterfly locs looking their best. Here are a few hair care tips to follow:

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension and breakage.

With this guide, you're ready to create your own stunning butterfly locs! Remember to take your time and be gentle with your hair, and your new style is sure to turn heads.
