Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins 2019

Do you have a round face or a double chin and are looking for a new hairstyle to flatter your features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the top short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2019. Whether you prefer a pixie cut or a bob, we have got you covered. So, let's dive in!

Description

Having a round face or a double chin can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right haircut, you can emphasize your best features and draw attention away from areas you're not too fond of. Short haircuts are a great option for those with round faces and double chins, as they can help elongate the face and create a slimming effect.

How To

The first step to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are roughly the same width, you have a round face. If your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones, you have a square face. If your forehead is wider than your jawline and cheekbones, you have a heart-shaped face.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut. The key is to choose a style that adds height to your face and creates angles. Pixie cuts and bobs are great options, as they can help elongate the face and draw attention upward. Avoid styles that are too blunt or too heavy on the bottom, as they can make the face appear even rounder.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that adds height and angles
Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to ensure the cut is right for you
Step 4: Style your hair using volumizing products to add height and texture
Step 5: Embrace your new look and rock it with confidence!

Tips

- Avoid styles that are too blunt or heavy on the bottom
- Opt for layers and texture to add dimension to your hair
- Use volumizing products to add height and texture
- Consult with a hairstylist to ensure the cut is right for you
- Embrace your new look and rock it with confidence!

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a bob if I have a round face?
A: Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a bob with layers and texture to add dimension to your hair.

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look even rounder?
A: No, if done correctly, a pixie cut can actually help elongate the face and draw attention upward.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny. And don't forget to get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know the top short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2019, it's time to book that appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. Remember, confidence is key, so wear your new hairstyle with pride and rock it!
