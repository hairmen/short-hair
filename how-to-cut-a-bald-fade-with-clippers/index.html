How To Cut A Bald Fade With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and stylish haircut, a bald fade may be the perfect choice for you. This haircut features a gradual taper from short to shorter, eventually blending into the skin for a seamless finish. The good news is that you can achieve a bald fade with clippers right at home. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps, provide some tips and answer frequently asked questions to help you achieve a perfect bald fade.

How to Cut a Bald Fade with Clippers

To achieve a perfect bald fade, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner, then dry it completely with a towel or blow dryer. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles, making it easier to cut.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Fade

Decide on the length of your fade. A low fade starts above the ears, while a high fade starts above the temples. The higher the fade, the more dramatic the contrast will be.

Step 3: Start with the Clippers

Attach the appropriate guard to your clippers and start at the bottom of your hairline. Work your way up to the desired length, using a higher guard to blend the hair as you move up. Make sure to use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 4: Start the Fade

Once you've reached the desired length, remove the guard and start to fade the hair. Start with the longest guard and gradually switch to shorter guards as you move up the hairline. Blend the hair smoothly to create a seamless transition from long to short.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

Use a trimmer to clean up the edges around the ears and neck. Make sure to take your time and create clean lines.

Step 6: Clean Up the Top

Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length. Keep in mind that the top should be longer than the sides to create a balanced look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Apply some hair product and style your hair as desired. You can create a slicked-back look, a messy style or a textured finish.

Tips for Cutting a Bald Fade with Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a bald fade with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.
  • Take your time and make small, gradual adjustments to create a seamless blend.
  • Use a trimmer to clean up the edges and create clean lines.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member.

FAQs About Cutting a Bald Fade with Clippers

What is a bald fade?

A bald fade is a haircut that features a gradual taper from short to shorter, eventually blending into the skin for a seamless finish.

Can I cut a bald fade with clippers at home?

Yes, you can achieve a bald fade with clippers right at home. Follow the steps outlined in this article for a perfect finish.

What guards should I use for a bald fade?

Start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually to achieve a perfect blend. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Hair Care for a Bald Fade with Clippers

To maintain your bald fade, make sure to trim the hair regularly and use hair product to style it. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. Protect your hair from the sun and wear a hat to prevent fading or damage.

Follow these steps, tips and hair care advice to achieve a perfect bald fade with clippers. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look right at home.
