What Kind Of Curly Hair Is Best

Written by Christine Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair comes in many different shapes and sizes, and each type requires special care and attention. If you have relaxed curly hair, you may be wondering what kind of curls work best for your hair type. In this article, we'll explain the different types of curly hair and help you determine which one is best for you.

Types of Curly Hair

Before we get into which type of curly hair is best for relaxed hair, let's first discuss the different types of curly hair. There are three categories of curly hair: wavy, curly, and coily.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair is typically categorized as 2A, 2B, or 2C. This type of hair has loose, flowing waves that are easy to manage. Wavy hair is the least curly of the three types of curly hair.

Curly Hair

Curly hair is typically categorized as 3A, 3B, or 3C. This type of hair has tighter curls that range from loose ringlets to corkscrew curls. Curly hair is more prone to frizz and dryness than wavy hair.

Coily Hair

Coily hair is typically categorized as 4A, 4B, or 4C. This type of hair has tight coils that range from springy to kinky. Coily hair is the most prone to dryness and requires the most moisture.

What Kind of Curly Hair is Best for Relaxed Hair?

If you have relaxed curly hair, it's best to choose a type of curl that is similar to your natural curl pattern. This will help your hair blend in seamlessly and reduce the risk of damage from over-processing.

For example, if you have wavy hair, you may want to choose a relaxed curl pattern that is 2A or 2B. If you have curly hair, you may want to choose a relaxed curl pattern that is 3A or 3B. If you have coily hair, you may want to choose a relaxed curl pattern that is 4A or 4B.

It's important to note that everyone's hair is different, and what works for one person may not work for another. It's best to experiment with different curl patterns and find the one that works best for your hair type.

How to Style Relaxed Curly Hair

Now that you know which type of curl pattern is best for your hair type, let's talk about how to style your relaxed curly hair. Here are some tips:

1. Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it moisturized and reduce frizz.

3. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product that is designed for your hair type to help define your curls and hold their shape.

4. Use a diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help dry your hair without disrupting your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your relaxed curly hair:

1. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair moisturized and reduce breakage. Use a deep conditioner once a week or as needed.

3. Protect your hair while sleeping

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce friction and prevent breakage while you sleep. You can also wear a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect your hair.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I relax my hair if it's already curly?

A: Yes, you can relax your hair if it's already curly. However, it's important to choose a curl pattern that is similar to your natural curl pattern to avoid damage from over-processing.

Q: How often should I wash my relaxed curly hair?

A: You should wash your relaxed curly hair once or twice a week, depending on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces.

Q: How do I prevent my relaxed curly hair from becoming dry?

A: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, deep condition regularly, and avoid heat styling as much as possible.

Conclusion

Choosing the right kind of curly hair for your relaxed hair type can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Remember to take good care of your hair by using the right products, avoiding heat styling, and deep conditioning regularly. With the right care, your relaxed curly hair can look healthy and beautiful.
