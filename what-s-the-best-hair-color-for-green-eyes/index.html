What s The Best Hair Color For Green Eyes

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Green eyes are one of the rarest eye colors in the world, with only 2% of the world's population having them. If you're one of the lucky few with green eyes, you might be wondering what hair color will really make your eyes pop. Look no further, because we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hair colors for green eyes, as well as some tips for maintaining your new hair color.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color for green eyes can be a bit tricky, but it's all about finding the right balance. You want to choose a hair color that enhances your green eyes without overpowering them. Generally speaking, warm hair colors such as copper, golden blonde, and caramel tend to work best with green eyes. However, there are a few other factors to consider when choosing the perfect hair color for you.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hair color for green eyes is to determine your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, which means you have yellow or golden undertones, then warm hair colors will work best for you. If you have a cool skin tone, which means you have pink or blue undertones, then cool hair colors such as ash blonde or platinum will work better with your green eyes.

Step by step

Once you've determined your skin tone, it's time to start thinking about the specific hair colors you want to try. Here are a few options to consider:

  • Copper: Copper hair color is a great choice for those with green eyes and warm skin tones. It's a warm, rich color that really brings out the green in your eyes.
  • Golden blonde: Golden blonde hair color is another great option for those with green eyes and warm skin tones. It's a bright, sunny color that looks great on just about everyone.
  • Caramel: Caramel hair color is a warm, rich brown that looks great on those with green eyes and warm skin tones. It's a great choice if you're looking for a more natural look.
  • Ash blonde: Ash blonde hair color is a cool, ashy color that works well with green eyes and cool skin tones. It's a great choice if you're looking for something a bit more edgy.
  • Platinum: Platinum hair color is a cool, silvery color that looks great on those with green eyes and cool skin tones. It's a bold choice that will really make your eyes stand out.

Tips

When choosing a hair color for green eyes, it's important to keep a few things in mind:

  • Don't go too dark: Dark hair colors can sometimes overpower green eyes, so it's best to stick with lighter, warmer colors.
  • Consider highlights: Highlights can really enhance your green eyes, especially if you choose a color that's a few shades lighter than your natural hair color.
  • Think about your lifestyle: If you're someone who spends a lot of time outdoors, you might want to choose a hair color that won't fade easily in the sun.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about choosing the best hair color for green eyes:

  • Q: Can I dye my hair a cool color if I have warm skin tones?
  • A: While warm hair colors tend to work best with green eyes and warm skin tones, you can still experiment with cool colors if you want to. Just keep in mind that it might not be the most flattering color for you.
  • Q: What if I don't want to dye my whole head of hair?
  • A: You can always try highlights or ombre hair color to enhance your green eyes without committing to a full head of hair color.
  • Q: What if I have hazel eyes?
  • A: If you have hazel eyes, you can still follow the same guidelines for choosing a hair color as someone with green eyes. Just keep in mind that you might want to choose a color that works well with both the green and brown tones in your eyes.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect hair color for your green eyes, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

Now that you know what the best hair color for green eyes is, it's time to go out and try it for yourself! Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
