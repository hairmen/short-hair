This style has been popular for several years now and shows no signs of going out of fashion anytime soon. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The mens haircut styles long top short sides is a popular hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit your individual preferences. You can choose to keep the top of your hair very long or shorten it slightly for a more conservative look. The sides and back can be buzzed short or faded to create a more gradual transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Styles Long Top Short Sides

The first step in getting the mens haircut styles long top short sides is to find a skilled barber or stylist who has experience with this type of haircut. Once you've found someone you trust, you'll want to communicate your vision for the cut clearly. Bring in a photo or two of the style you're hoping to achieve, and be prepared to answer questions about the length and style of the top, as well as the length and shape of the sides and back.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Mens Haircut Styles Long Top Short Sides

To cut the mens haircut styles long top short sides, your stylist will typically follow these steps:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair and clip it out of the way.
  3. Begin cutting the sides and back, either buzzing them short or fading them gradually.
  4. Blend the sides and back into the longer hair on top.
  5. Cut the top of the hair to your desired length and style it as desired.

Tips for Styling Mens Haircut Styles Long Top Short Sides

Once you've gotten your mens haircut styles long top short sides, there are several ways you can style it to create different looks. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a hair product like pomade or wax to add texture and hold to the hair on top.
  • Try slicking the hair on top back for a more classic look, or combing it to the side for a more modern look.
  • Experiment with different lengths on top to find the one that looks best on you.

FAQs about Mens Haircut Styles Long Top Short Sides

Here are a few frequently asked questions about mens haircut styles long top short sides:

How long should the hair on top be?

The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your personal preference and the shape of your face. However, most men opt for hair that is between 2 and 4 inches long on top.

How often do I need to get my hair cut?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you want to maintain the shape of the cut. Generally, it's a good idea to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Can I style this haircut differently for different occasions?

Yes! The mens haircut styles long top short sides is very versatile and can be styled in many different ways to suit different occasions. For a more formal look, you might slick the hair back with a bit of product. For a more casual look, you might let the hair on top fall naturally.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Styles Long Top Short Sides

To keep your mens haircut styles long top short sides looking its best, you'll want to follow a few basic hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair product like pomade or wax to add texture and hold to the hair on top.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers or flat irons too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep the shape of the cut looking sharp.

And there you have it - everything you need to know about mens haircut styles long top short sides! Whether you're looking for a classic or modern look, this style is sure to impress.
