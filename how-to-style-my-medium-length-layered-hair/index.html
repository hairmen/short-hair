Are you tired of wearing the same old ponytail or bun every day? If you have medium length layered hair, there are plenty of easy and stylish ways to switch up your look. In this article, we'll go over some simple and chic hairstyles that you can try at home.

How to Style Medium Length Layered Hair

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, let's first discuss the basics of styling medium length layered hair. The key to achieving a great hairstyle is to start with clean and dry hair. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, consider using a styling product to enhance your natural texture. For straight hair, use a straightening iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

When it comes to styling, you have a lot of options. You can pull your hair back into a chic ponytail or bun, create loose waves or curls, or wear your hair straight and sleek. The key is to find a look that complements your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some easy and stylish hairstyles:

1. Half-Up Half-Down:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take the top section of hair on one side and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Optional: tease the crown of your hair for some added volume.

2. Loose Waves:

  1. Section your hair into small sections.
  2. Take a curling iron and wrap each section around the barrel, holding for a few seconds.
  3. Release the curl and repeat on the rest of your hair.
  4. Optional: use a texturizing spray for some added volume and hold.

3. Low Bun:

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail and secure with a hair tie.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the base and secure with bobby pins.
  3. Optional: pull out some pieces around your face for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Layered Hair

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length layered hair:

  • Invest in some high-quality styling tools, like a straightening iron or curling wand.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands, clips, and scrunchies.
  • Don't be afraid to try a new hairstyle â it's just hair and it will grow back!
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a top knot with medium length layered hair?

A: Absolutely! Just gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around into a bun. Secure with bobby pins and you're good to go.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people find that washing their hair every two to three days is sufficient.

Q: How can I add volume to my medium length layered hair?

A: Try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo for some added lift.

Hair Care for Medium Length Layered Hair

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
