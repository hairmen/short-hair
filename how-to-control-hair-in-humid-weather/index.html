How To Control Hair In Humid Weather Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Humidity is the enemy of good hair, especially for those of us with curly or wavy locks. It can cause frizz, fly-aways, and just general chaos. But fear not, there are ways to control your hair in humid weather. In this article, we’ll cover some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great no matter the weather.

How to

The first step to controlling your hair in humid weather is to understand what causes the frizz and fly-aways. When the air is humid, the moisture in the air penetrates the hair, causing it to swell and become frizzy. This can be exacerbated by heat, so hot and humid days are the worst for your hair.

To combat this, you’ll want to focus on keeping your hair moisturized, but not too much. Too much moisture can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Here are some steps you can take to control your hair in humid weather:

Step 1: Start with a good haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to controlling your hair in humid weather. Speak to your stylist about a cut that will work with your hair texture and face shape, and that won’t require a lot of styling.

Step 2: Use the right products

The right products can help keep your hair moisturized and under control. Look for products that contain anti-humidity ingredients, such as silicone or keratin. These ingredients will help to seal the hair cuticle and keep the moisture out.

Step 3: Don’t over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to frizz. Try to wash your hair every other day, or even every three days if you can. When you do wash your hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and follow up with a leave-in conditioner or oil to help lock in moisture.

Step 4: Style your hair wisely

When it comes to styling your hair in humid weather, less is more. Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Opt for lightweight mousses or gels instead.

Step 5: Consider protective styles

Protective styles, such as braids or buns, can be a lifesaver on humid days. Not only do they keep your hair out of your face, but they also help to protect it from the humidity.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help control your hair in humid weather:

  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Carry a small bottle of anti-frizz serum with you for touch-ups throughout the day
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz

FAQ

Q: Can I use hairspray to control my hair in humid weather?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to control your hair in humid weather, but be careful not to use too much. Hairspray can be heavy and make your hair look greasy, so opt for a lightweight spray and use it sparingly.

Q: Will wearing a hat help control my hair in humid weather?

A: Yes, wearing a hat can help protect your hair from the humidity, but be careful not to wear it too tight or too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Q: Can I use a hairdryer to dry my hair in humid weather?

A: Yes, you can use a hairdryer to dry your hair in humid weather, but be sure to use a diffuser attachment and set it to a low heat setting.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it under control in humid weather. Be sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and follow up with a leave-in conditioner or oil to help lock in moisture. Avoid using too many styling products, and opt for lightweight mousses and gels instead. And remember, less is more when it comes to styling your hair in humid weather.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking great no matter the weather. Don’t let humidity ruin your hair day – take control and enjoy your beautiful locks!
