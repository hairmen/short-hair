Do Side Bangs Look Good On Round Faces

Description

Side bangs have been a popular trend for a while now and can be seen on many different face shapes. However, for those with round faces, the question remains: do side bangs look good on round faces?

The answer is yes! Side bangs can actually be very flattering for round faces. The key is to make sure the bangs are styled in a way that complements the roundness of the face rather than accentuating it.

In this article, we will go over how to get side bangs for round faces, a step-by-step guide for styling them, tips for maintaining them, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get Side Bangs for Round Faces

Before diving into the steps for getting side bangs, it's important to note that consulting with a professional hairstylist is always recommended. They can help determine the best length, thickness, and angle for your face shape.

When getting side bangs, it's important to consider the following:

  • Length: The length of your side bangs will depend on the length of the rest of your hair. For those with shorter hair, shorter side bangs may be more flattering. For those with longer hair, longer side bangs may be more suitable.
  • Thickness: The thickness of your side bangs will depend on the thickness of your hair. Thicker hair can handle thicker side bangs while thinner hair may require thinner side bangs to avoid looking too sparse.
  • Angle: The angle of your side bangs should complement the roundness of your face. Side bangs that are angled towards the cheekbones can help elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps for styling your side bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your side bangs by parting your hair diagonally from the crown of your head to the top of your ear.
  3. Clip the rest of your hair away to avoid cutting it by accident.
  4. Trim your side bangs to the desired length, starting longer and gradually cutting shorter until you reach the desired length.
  5. Use thinning shears to blend any harsh lines or edges.
  6. Style your side bangs using a round brush and blow dryer, gently curling them towards your face.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Side Bangs on Round Faces

Here are some tips for keeping your side bangs looking their best:

  • Use a volumizing spray to give your side bangs some lift and body.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your side bangs to prevent damage and split ends.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and extend the life of your side bangs between washes.
  • If your side bangs are too long, try styling them to the side rather than straight down to create a more flattering angle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about side bangs for round faces:

What face shape is best suited for side bangs?

Side bangs can work on many different face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces. For round faces, it's important to make sure the angle and length of the side bangs are flattering.

How often do I need to trim my side bangs?

How often you trim your side bangs will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it starts to look unruly. On average, trimming every 4-6 weeks will help keep your side bangs looking fresh and neat.

Can I still wear my hair up with side bangs?

Absolutely! Side bangs can be styled in many different ways, including being pinned back or tucked behind the ear. Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

Here are some tips for keeping your side bangs looking healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools on your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, side bangs can be a great addition to any hairstyle, regardless of face shape. By following these tips and consulting with a professional hairstylist, you can achieve a look that is both flattering and trendy.
