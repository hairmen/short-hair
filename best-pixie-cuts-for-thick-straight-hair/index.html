Best Pixie Cuts For Thick Straight Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thick straight hair, you might be wondering what the best pixie cut is for you. Pixie cuts are a great way to show off your hair's natural texture and thickness, and they can be easy to maintain and style. In this article, we'll go over some of the best pixie cuts for thick straight hair, along with some tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Description

Pixie cuts are short haircuts that usually have longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. They can be a great option for people with thick straight hair, as they can help to show off the thickness and texture of the hair. Some popular pixie cuts for thick straight hair include:

1. The Classic Pixie

The classic pixie is a short haircut that is cropped close to the head on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. This is a great option for people with thick straight hair, as the short sides and back can help to keep the hair from getting too bulky, while the longer top can show off the hair's natural texture.

2. The Layered Pixie

The layered pixie is a variation on the classic pixie that features layers throughout the haircut. This can help to add texture and movement to the hair, while also keeping it from looking too bulky. This is a great option for people with thick straight hair who want a more textured look.

3. The Asymmetrical Pixie

The asymmetrical pixie is a short haircut that is longer on one side than the other. This can help to add interest and asymmetry to the haircut, while also showing off the hair's natural thickness and texture. This is a great option for people with thick straight hair who want a more edgy look.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Cut for Your Thick Straight Hair

When choosing a pixie cut for your thick straight hair, there are a few things to consider:

1. Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in determining which pixie cut will look best on you. If you have a round face, for example, you might want to opt for a pixie cut with a longer top and shorter sides to help elongate your face. If you have a square face, on the other hand, you might want to opt for a pixie cut with softer, more rounded edges to help soften your features.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair's natural texture can also play a role in determining which pixie cut will look best on you. If you have thick straight hair, for example, you might want to opt for a shorter pixie cut that can show off the hair's natural thickness and texture. If you have fine hair, on the other hand, you might want to opt for a longer pixie cut with layers to help add volume and texture.

3. Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in determining which pixie cut will work best for you. If you lead an active lifestyle, for example, you might want to opt for a shorter pixie cut that is easy to style and maintain. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, on the other hand, you might want to opt for a longer pixie cut with more layers and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Pixie Cut

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie cut:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, while also keeping it hydrated and healthy.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair, while also helping to set the style.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your fingers or a styling tool, such as a flat iron or curling wand. This will help to add texture and movement to your hair, while also giving it a more polished look.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish by applying a hairspray to your hair to help set the style and keep it in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your pixie cut looking fresh and healthy. Aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it in shape.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat can damage your hair over time, so it's important to protect it from heat styling tools. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and try to limit your use of these tools as much as possible.

4. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks with your pixie cut. Try adding in some braids or twists, or play around with different hair accessories to add some interest to your hair.

FAQs

1. Will a pixie cut work for thick straight hair?

Yes! Pixie cuts can be a great option for people with thick straight hair, as they can help to show off the hair's natural texture and thickness.

2. How often should I get my pixie cut trimmed?

It's recommended to get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

3. How do I style my pixie cut?

You can style your pixie cut using your fingers or a styling tool, such as a flat iron or curling wand. Apply a styling product to your hair first to add texture and hold.

Hair Care for Thick Straight Hair

To keep your thick straight hair looking its best, here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type and texture. This will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to help hydrate and protect your hair throughout the day. This can also help to add shine and softness to your hair.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray and limiting your use of these tools as much as possible.

There you have it - some of the best pixie cuts for thick straight hair, along with some tips for styling and caring for your hair. With the right cut and care, your thick straight hair can look its best and be easy to maintain!
