How To Keep Thick Curly Hair Straight A Comprehensive Guide

If you have thick curly hair, you know the struggle of trying to keep it straight. It seems like no matter what you do, your hair always ends up frizzy and out of control. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to keep your thick curly hair straight and sleek all day long.

How to Keep Thick Curly Hair Straight

There are several ways to keep your thick curly hair straight:

  • Using a straightening iron
  • Using a blow dryer and round brush
  • Using a straightening brush
  • Chemical treatments

Each of these methods has its pros and cons, so it's important to choose the one that works best for your hair type and lifestyle.

Using a Straightening Iron

Using a straightening iron is a popular way to straighten thick curly hair. To do this, you'll need a high-quality straightening iron and a heat protectant spray. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Starting at the roots, clamp the straightening iron onto a small section of hair.
  5. Gently glide the straightening iron down the length of your hair, using a comb to help guide the hair.
  6. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  7. Finish with a shine serum or hairspray to keep your hair looking sleek and shiny.

Using a Blow Dryer and Round Brush

Another way to straighten thick curly hair is by using a blow dryer and round brush. This method is great for those who want to avoid using heat tools on their hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Starting at the roots, use a round brush to pull the hair taut.
  5. Blow dry the hair, using the round brush to guide the hair as you go.
  6. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  7. Finish with a shine serum or hairspray to keep your hair looking sleek and shiny.

Using a Straightening Brush

A straightening brush is a great tool for those who want to straighten their hair quickly and easily. To use a straightening brush, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Starting at the roots, brush the hair with the straightening brush.
  5. Gently glide the brush down the length of your hair, using a comb to help guide the hair.
  6. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  7. Finish with a shine serum or hairspray to keep your hair looking sleek and shiny.

Chemical Treatments

If you want a more permanent solution to straightening your hair, you can try chemical treatments like a keratin treatment or a relaxer. These treatments can be expensive and time-consuming, but they can give you straight hair for several months. It's important to consult with a professional stylist before trying any chemical treatments on your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

To keep your thick curly hair straight, follow these steps:

  1. Choose the straightening method that works best for your hair type and lifestyle.
  2. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into small sections.
  5. Use your chosen straightening tool to straighten each section of hair.
  6. Finish with a shine serum or hairspray to keep your hair looking sleek and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Once you've straightened your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking sleek and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining straight hair:

  • Avoid getting your hair wet or exposing it to humidity.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQs about Straightening Thick Curly Hair

Q: Can I straighten my hair every day?

A: It's not recommended to straighten your hair every day, as this can be damaging to your hair. Try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

Q: How can I prevent heat damage when straightening my hair?

A: Always use a heat protectant spray on your hair before straightening it. Additionally, try to use the lowest heat setting possible on your straightening tool.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

A: Use a shine serum or hairspray to keep your hair looking sleek and shiny. Additionally, avoid getting your hair wet or exposing it to humidity.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Thick curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for thick curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.

By following these tips and using the right straightening techniques, you can keep your thick curly hair looking sleek and straight all day long.
