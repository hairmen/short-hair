How To Cut And Shape Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be tricky to cut and shape, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a beautiful, bouncy style. In this blog post, we'll walk you through the process of cutting and shaping curly hair, including tips for maintaining your style and caring for your curls.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut and Shape Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting and Shaping Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Cut and Shape Curly Hair

Cutting and shaping curly hair requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. Curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries, so it's important to cut it when it's dry and in its natural state. This allows you to see how the hair will lay and how much it will shrink up.

Before you start cutting, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a good pair of hair cutting scissors, a wide-tooth comb, and clips to section off the hair.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that are designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and make them more manageable.

Step 2: Section Off Hair

Next, use clips to section off your hair. Start by dividing your hair down the middle, then clip each section up and out of the way. This will make it easier to cut each section evenly.

Step 3: Cut Hair in Sections

Starting at the back of your head, take down one section of hair at a time. Use your wide-tooth comb to comb through the hair, then use your scissors to trim the ends. Be sure to cut straight across and avoid cutting into the hair too much, as this can cause split ends.

Continue cutting each section of hair until you've cut all of your hair. Be sure to check the length and shape as you go, to ensure that everything is even.

Step 4: Shape Hair

Once you've cut your hair, you can shape it using your scissors. This is where you can add layers or shape the hair around your face. Use your scissors to cut into the hair, creating texture and movement.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting and shaping curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Section off your hair
  3. Cut hair in sections
  4. Shape hair with scissors

Tips for Cutting and Shaping Curly Hair

Here are some tips for cutting and shaping curly hair:

  • Always cut curly hair when it's dry and in its natural state
  • Use products that are designed for curly hair to make your curls more manageable
  • Be sure to cut straight across and avoid cutting into the hair too much
  • Check the length and shape as you go, to ensure that everything is even
  • Shape the hair around your face to create a flattering style

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting and shaping curly hair:

Can you cut curly hair with regular scissors?

No, it's important to use hair cutting scissors when cutting curly hair. Regular scissors can cause split ends and damage to the hair.

How often should you cut curly hair?

It's recommended that you cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can you cut curly hair when it's wet?

No, it's best to cut curly hair when it's dry and in its natural state. This allows you to see how the hair will lay and how much it will shrink up.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair is just as important as cutting and shaping it. Here are some tips for keeping your curls healthy and beautiful:

  • Use products that are designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Air dry your hair whenever possible
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb through your hair, rather than a brush
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep

By following these tips, you can maintain beautiful, healthy curls that are easy to cut and shape.
