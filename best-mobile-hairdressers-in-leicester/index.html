Best Mobile Hairdressers In Leicester Your Guide For Perfect Hair

Written by Eveline Nov 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are looking for the best mobile hairdressers in Leicester, you have come to the right place. In this article, you will find a list of the top hairdressers who can take care of all your hair needs in the comfort of your own home. Whether you want a simple trim, a new hair color or a full hair styling, these mobile hairdressers have got you covered. So, sit back, relax and let's get started.

Why Choose a Mobile Hairdresser?

Mobile hairdressers offer a convenient solution for those who don't have the time to visit a salon or prefer to have their hair done in the comfort of their own home. With a mobile hairdresser, you can get the same professional service you would expect from a salon but without the hassle of travelling or waiting in line. Plus, you can choose a time and date that suits you, making it easier to fit into your busy schedule.

How to Choose the Right Mobile Hairdresser

Choosing the right mobile hairdresser is important to ensure that you get the best service and results. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairdresser:

  • Ask for recommendations from friends and family who have used mobile hairdressers in Leicester
  • Check the hairdresser's website or social media pages for reviews and feedback from previous clients
  • Look for a hairdresser who specializes in the type of service you need, such as hair coloring or styling
  • Check the hairdresser's availability and pricing to ensure that it fits your schedule and budget

Step-by-Step Guide to Booking a Mobile Hairdresser

Booking a mobile hairdresser is easy and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Research and choose a hairdresser who meets your needs and preferences
  2. Contact the hairdresser through their website, social media or phone number to enquire about their availability and pricing
  3. Agree on a date and time for the hairdresser to come to your home
  4. Discuss your hair requirements with the hairdresser to ensure that they understand your needs and preferences
  5. Sit back, relax and let the hairdresser work their magic
  6. Pay the hairdresser according to their agreed pricing and leave a review or feedback to help other potential clients

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Between Appointments

To keep your hair looking great between appointments, here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind and other environmental factors

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mobile hairdressers in Leicester:

Q: How much does a mobile hairdresser in Leicester charge?

A: The cost of a mobile hairdresser in Leicester varies depending on the type of service you require and the hairdresser's pricing. It's best to enquire with the hairdresser directly to get an accurate quote.

Q: How long does a mobile hairdressing appointment take?

A: The length of a mobile hairdressing appointment depends on the type of service you require. A simple trim may take 30 minutes, while a full hair color or styling may take a few hours. It's best to discuss the timing with the hairdresser before booking.

Q: Do I need to provide any equipment for the hairdresser?

A: No, mobile hairdressers in Leicester usually bring all the necessary equipment with them, including scissors, combs, hairdryers, and styling products. However, it's best to confirm with the hairdresser before the appointment.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, here are some tips:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to provide your hair with the necessary nutrients
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Limit your use of chemical treatments, such as hair dyes and relaxers
  • Protect your hair from damage by wearing a hat or scarf in the sun or wind

So, there you have it, our guide to the best mobile hairdressers in Leicester. Now you can sit back, relax and let the professionals take care of your hair.
