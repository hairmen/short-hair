How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for You

Before you commit to a short haircut, it's important to think about what will work best for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some things to consider:

  • Face shape: Different short hairstyles will flatter different face shapes. For example, a pixie cut or bob can look great on oval or heart-shaped faces, while a chin-length cut or shaggy bob may be better for square or round faces.
  • Hair texture: Some short hairstyles require more maintenance than others, depending on your hair texture. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt bob may work well. If you have curly hair, a layered cut may help you avoid the dreaded triangle shape.
  • Personal style: Your personality and lifestyle should also play a role in your hairstyle choice. Are you low-maintenance and sporty? A messy pixie cut may be perfect for you. Do you like to experiment with different looks? A bold, asymmetrical bob could be just the thing.

Ultimately, the best way to choose a short hairstyle is to talk to your stylist. They can help you find a cut that works with your unique features and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Once you've chosen your short hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair. This will help give your hair texture and hold.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture to your hair. You can curl small sections of hair away from your face for a flirty look, or create beachy waves for a more laid-back vibe.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to help your style hold throughout the day.

Remember, the key to styling short hair is to experiment and have fun. Don't be afraid to try new things and find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

Short hair may be low-maintenance, but that doesn't mean it doesn't require any upkeep. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your shape.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • When you're not styling your hair, use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

By following these tips, you can ensure your short hair stays looking fresh and fabulous.

FAQs About Short Hair

Still have questions about short hair? Here are some common FAQs:

Is short hair easy to style?

Yes! Short hair is generally easier to style than longer hair, as it requires less time and product. Plus, there are plenty of fun and easy short hairstyles to try.

Can I still wear my hair up with short hair?

Absolutely! While you may not be able to pull your hair into a traditional ponytail, there are plenty of cute and creative ways to style short hair up, such as with a half-up, half-down style or a cute hair accessory.

Will short hair make my face look rounder?

Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually help complement your face shape and draw attention to your best features.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind when you have short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your shape and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap when you're outside or in the pool.

By following these hair care tips, you can help keep your short hair looking healthy and gorgeous.

Final Thoughts

Whether you're looking to freshen up your current style or make a bold statement, short hair can be a great choice. With so many hairstyles for short length hair in 2020 to choose from, there's sure to be a look that works for you. By following our tips for styling and hair care, you can keep your short hair looking its best all year round.
