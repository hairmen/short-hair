How to Get Rid of Brassy Orange Hair Color - Tips and Tricks

If you have ever tried to dye your hair blonde or light brown, you may have experienced brassy orange hair color. This unwanted color can make you feel self-conscious and embarrassed. Fortunately, there are ways to fix it. In this article, we will discuss how to get rid of brassy orange hair color and restore your hair to its natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of Brassy Orange Hair Color
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Get Rid of Brassy Orange Hair Color

Brassy orange hair color is caused by the oxidation of hair dye. This can happen if the dye is left on for too long or if it is not the right shade for your hair. Here are a few ways to get rid of brassy orange hair color:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a great way to neutralize brassy orange hair color. The purple color cancels out the orange tones in your hair, leaving it looking more natural. Use the shampoo once or twice a week to see results.

2. Try a Toning Treatment

Toning treatments are designed to correct hair color. They can be found at most beauty supply stores and come in a variety of shades. Choose a shade that is opposite to the color of your hair. For example, if your hair is brassy orange, choose a toning treatment with a blue or green tint.

3. Use a Color Corrector

If your hair is extremely brassy, a color corrector may be the best option. Color correctors work by neutralizing unwanted tones in your hair. They can be found at most beauty supply stores and come in a variety of shades.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to get rid of brassy orange hair color:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any buildup and prepare your hair for the treatment.

Step 2: Apply the Treatment

Apply the toning treatment or color corrector to your hair, making sure to cover all areas. Leave it on for the recommended time.

Step 3: Rinse and Condition

Rinse the treatment out of your hair and condition it as usual. This will help to restore moisture to your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you get rid of brassy orange hair color:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner

Deep conditioning treatments can help to restore moisture to your hair and prevent breakage. Use one once a week to keep your hair healthy.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair air dry whenever possible.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my hair after using a toning treatment?

Yes, you can dye your hair after using a toning treatment. Just make sure to wait a few days to give your hair time to recover.

2. How often should I use a toning treatment?

You should use a toning treatment once or twice a week to see results.

3. Can I use a toning treatment on natural hair?

Yes, you can use a toning treatment on natural hair. Just make sure to choose a shade that is appropriate for your hair color.

Hair Care

Here are a few tips to help you take care of your hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for a formula that is sulfate-free and contains natural ingredients.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair at Night

Use a satin or silk pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get rid of brassy orange hair color and restore your hair to its natural beauty. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
