Hairstyles For Medium Length Straight Hair With Bangs

Written by Luffy Jul 31, 2023 · 3 min read

Medium length straight hair with bangs is a popular hairstyle that provides versatility and ease of styling. It can be worn for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. In this article, we will go over some of the best hairstyles for medium length straight hair with bangs, as well as tips and tricks for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style
  • Step by Step Tutorial
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Style

There are a variety of ways to style medium length straight hair with bangs. Here are some of the most popular:

1. Straight and Sleek

The straight and sleek look is perfect for a formal event or a night out. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and finish with a shine serum for a glossy finish.

2. Loose Waves

Add some texture to your hair with loose waves. Use a curling wand to create loose waves throughout your hair, and finish with a texturizing spray for added volume.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie, leaving the bottom half of your hair down.

Step by Step Tutorial

Here is a step-by-step tutorial for creating the loose waves hairstyle:

  1. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Using a curling wand, curl each section away from your face.
  4. Once you have curled all sections, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for styling and caring for medium length straight hair with bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling wand.
  • Regularly trim your bangs to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I still style my hair if I have grown-out bangs?

Absolutely! You can still style your hair by parting it to the side and sweeping your bangs to the side.

2. What is the best way to keep my hair healthy?

The best way to keep your hair healthy is by regularly washing and conditioning it, using a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and avoiding too much product.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length straight hair with bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

With these hairstyles and hair care tips, you can keep your medium length straight hair with bangs looking beautiful and healthy. Whether you're going for a sleek and straight look or loose waves, your hair will look amazing with the right styling and care.
