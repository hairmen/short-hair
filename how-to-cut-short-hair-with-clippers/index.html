How To Cut Short Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts? Or maybe you just prefer the convenience of cutting your own hair at home? Whatever your reason may be, learning how to cut short hair with clippers is a valuable skill that can save you time and money in the long run. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process of cutting short hair with clippers, step-by-step.

How to Cut Short Hair with Clippers

Cutting short hair with clippers can be intimidating at first, but with a little practice and patience, anyone can do it. Here are the steps you’ll need to follow:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You will need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Comb
  • Scissors (optional)
  • Mirror
  • Cape or towel to protect your clothes
  • Clip or hair tie to section off your hair

Once you have all your supplies, find a well-lit area to work in.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Use a clip or hair tie to section off the hair on the top of your head. This will make it easier to cut the hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Choose the Right Guard

Choose the right guard for the length you want to cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the sides and back of your head. Use the clippers to go against the grain of your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Be sure to use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 6: Blend the Top

Once you’ve finished cutting the sides and back, remove the clip or hair tie and blend the top with scissors or clippers. Use the comb to guide the clippers or scissors and blend the top with the sides and back.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments or touch-ups.

Step 8: Clean Up

Once you’re happy with your haircut, clean up any stray hairs with a brush or vacuum.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair with Clippers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting short hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Blend the top with the sides and back to create a seamless look.
  • Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  • Practice makes perfect – don’t be discouraged if your first few haircuts don’t turn out perfectly.

FAQs About Cutting Short Hair with Clippers

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, anyone can learn how to cut their own hair with clippers. It just takes practice and patience.

What size guard should I use to cut short hair?

The size guard you use will depend on the length you want to cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Do I need to use scissors to cut short hair?

You can use either clippers or scissors to cut short hair. Clippers are faster and easier to use, but scissors can give you more control and precision.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Hair with Clippers

After cutting your hair with clippers, be sure to wash and condition your hair as you normally would. If you notice any uneven spots or mistakes, don’t be afraid to touch them up with scissors or clippers. With a little practice and patience, you’ll be able to cut your own hair with confidence and ease.
