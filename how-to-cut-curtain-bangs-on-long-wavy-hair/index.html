How To Cut Curtain Bangs On Long Wavy Hair

Are you tired of your long wavy hair always getting in your face? Curtain bangs might be the perfect solution for you! Not only do they frame your face beautifully, but they also add a touch of elegance to any look. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of cutting curtain bangs on long wavy hair.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs on Long Wavy Hair

Curtain bangs are a great way to add some style to your long wavy hair. Here's how you can cut them:

1. Section your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair in the middle. Use a comb to create a straight line from the middle of your forehead to the back of your head.

2. Cut the Bangs

Take a small section of hair from your fringe area and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, then cut the hair in a straight line. Repeat this process on the other side.

3. Check the Length

Once you've cut the bangs, check if they are the same length. If not, trim the longer side until they are even.

4. Style your Bangs

Now that you've cut your bangs, you can style them as you like. You can part them in the middle for a classic look or sweep them to the side for a more modern look.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut curtain bangs on long wavy hair:

Step 1: Section your Hair

Section your hair in the middle using a comb. Create a straight line from the middle of your forehead to the back of your head.

Step 2: Wet your Hair

Wet your bangs with a spray bottle or by dipping them in water. This will make it easier to cut them.

Step 3: Comb your Hair

Comb your bangs straight down with a fine-toothed comb.

Step 4: Cut the Bangs

Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, then cut the hair in a straight line.

Step 5: Check the Length

Check if your bangs are the same length. If not, trim the longer side until they are even.

Step 6: Style your Bangs

Style your bangs as you like. You can part them in the middle or sweep them to the side.

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs on Long Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you cut curtain bangs on long wavy hair:

1. Start Small

It's better to cut a little bit at a time, so start with a small section of your bangs and work your way out.

2. Use Professional Scissors

Use professional scissors to cut your bangs. This will ensure a clean cut and prevent split ends.

3. Cut your Bangs Dry

If your hair is naturally wavy, it's better to cut your bangs dry. This will give you a better idea of how they will look once they are styled.

4. Practice Patience

Take your time when cutting your bangs. Rushing can lead to mistakes and uneven cuts.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

A: Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

Q: How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Q: What face shape is best for curtain bangs?

A: Curtain bangs look great on all face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs on Long Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curtain bangs looking great:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you style your bangs with heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

2. Avoid Over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

3. Invest in Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are specifically designed for wavy hair. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent frizz.

4. Trim your Bangs Regularly

Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut curtain bangs on long wavy hair, go ahead and give it a try! With a little patience and practice, you'll have a beautiful new look in no time.
