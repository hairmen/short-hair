Washing your hair regularly can help keep your afro clean and prevent buildup. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep your afro moisturized and prevent breakage. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it out.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your afro moisturized throughout the day. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair and style as desired.
