How To Cut A Messy Bob Hairstyle Step By Step Guide

Welcome to our blog post on how to cut a messy bob hairstyle! This style has been popular for years and continues to be a favorite among women of all ages. If you're looking to switch up your look and want to try a messy bob, then you've come to the right place!

Description

A messy bob hairstyle is a bob cut that is purposely styled to have a messy, undone look. It's a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and trendy. This style can be achieved with any hair type or texture, and it's perfect for those who want to add volume and dimension to their hair.

How to Cut a Messy Bob Hairstyle

Prep Your Hair

The first step in cutting a messy bob is to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, and then apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

Sectioning Your Hair

Next, section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section. Start with one of the back sections and comb out any tangles or knots. Use a razor comb or scissors to cut your hair at an angle. Repeat this process on the other back section.

Cutting Your Hair

Move to the front sections of your hair. Use your razor comb or scissors to cut your hair at a diagonal angle towards your chin. Cut both front sections of your hair to the same length. This will create a frame for your face and add texture to your hair.

Texturizing Your Hair

After you've cut your hair, it's time to add some texture. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair, creating a messy, undone look.

Tips for Cutting a Messy Bob Hairstyle

  • Use a razor comb or scissors to cut your hair.
  • Section your hair into four parts to make it easier to cut.
  • Start with the back sections of your hair and work your way to the front.
  • Cut your hair at an angle to create texture and dimension.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

Can I cut a messy bob hairstyle on my own?

While it's possible to cut your own hair, we recommend going to a professional stylist to get the best results. A stylist will have the experience and expertise needed to create a great messy bob hairstyle.

What hair type is best for a messy bob?

A messy bob can work for any hair type or texture. It's a great option for those with fine hair who want to add volume and dimension to their hair.

Hair Care After Cutting a Messy Bob Hairstyle

After cutting your hair into a messy bob, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to maintain volume and texture. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair as needed. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Congratulations! You now know how to cut a messy bob hairstyle. We hope this guide was helpful and that you're excited to try this stylish and trendy hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking great!
