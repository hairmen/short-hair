Best Hair Styles For Over 60 With Thick Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have thick hair and are over 60? If so, you may be wondering what hair styles are best for you. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to decide on a style that complements your hair type and age. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hair styles for over 60 with thick hair, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Hair Style
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Choose a Hair Style

When it comes to choosing a hair style, there are several factors to consider. One of the most important is your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you'll want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head. Instead, opt for a style that adds height to the top of your head.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. If you have thick hair, you'll want to choose a style that works with your hair's natural texture. For example, a layered cut can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

Finally, you'll want to consider your lifestyle and personal style. If you're someone who likes to keep things simple, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different looks, you may want to choose a more elaborate style that allows you to play around with different textures and shapes.

Step by Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen a hair style, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step by step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots to add volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs

Q: What are the best hair styles for over 60 with thick hair?

A: Some of the best hair styles for over 60 with thick hair include a layered bob, a shaggy cut, and a pixie cut.

Q: How do I know if a hair style will work for my face shape?

A: The best way to determine if a hair style will work for your face shape is to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a style that complements your features.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or clips, as these can damage your hair and cause breakage.

With these tips and ideas for hair styles, you can find the perfect look for you over 60 with thick hair. Remember to take care of your hair and keep it healthy, and you'll have a beautiful head of hair for years to come!
