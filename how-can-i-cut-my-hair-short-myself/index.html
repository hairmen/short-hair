How Can I Cut My Hair Short Myself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune at the hair salon every time you need a trim? Or maybe you just want to switch up your look and try a short hairstyle? Whatever your reason may be, cutting your own hair can seem like a daunting task. But fear not, with a little bit of patience and practice, you can become a DIY hair cutting pro. In this article, we will walk you through the steps of how to cut your hair short yourself, along with some helpful tips and tricks to make the process as easy as possible.

How to Cut Your Hair Short Yourself

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, there are a few things you'll need to get started:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Clippers (if you want a buzz cut or shorter sides)
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Towel or cape to catch hair

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it. This will give you a better idea of how your hair falls naturally and will make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Secure each section with a hair tie or clip.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin with one of the back sections. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and snip off the ends using your scissors. Repeat this process with small sections of hair until you've cut all the way around that section. Then, move on to the other back section and repeat.

For the front sections, it's best to start by cutting longer than you want and then gradually trimming more. This will give you more control over the final length.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

If you want shorter sides or a buzz cut, use your clippers to trim the sides. Start with the longest guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length. Blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top by using a comb and scissors to gradually trim the hair where the two sections meet.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, use your mirror to check your hair from all angles. Make any necessary touch-ups or adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short Yourself

  • Start with small snips and trim gradually. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with a small trim and work your way up to a bigger cut.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and be patient.
  • Watch tutorials or ask a friend with experience for help.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair short myself if I have curly hair?

Cutting curly hair can be tricky, so it's best to consult with a professional stylist who has experience cutting curly hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always go to a salon to have it fixed or try again at home.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair Short Yourself

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and style.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent further damage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use hair masks or oils to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know how to cut your hair short yourself, you can save money and feel confident in your DIY hair cutting skills. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
