How To Apply Mousse On Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. One product that can help you achieve the look you want is mousse. In this post, we’ll show you how to apply mousse on curly hair, step by step.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Apply Mousse on Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Applying Mousse on Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Apply Mousse on Curly Hair

Mousse is a lightweight, foam-like product that can help define curls, reduce frizz, and add volume to your hair. Here’s how to apply mousse on curly hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, then gently towel-dry your hair until it’s damp.

Step 2: Dispense a small amount of mousse into your hand. The amount of mousse you need will depend on the length and thickness of your hair. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Step 3: Rub your palms together to distribute the mousse evenly.

Step 4: Flip your head upside down and scrunch the mousse into your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Make sure you distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Set your dryer on low or medium heat and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser until it’s dry.

Step 6: Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently scrunch your curls to break up any crunchiness from the mousse.

Tips for Applying Mousse on Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when applying mousse on curly hair:

  • Start with a small amount of mousse and add more as needed. Too much mousse can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Apply the mousse to your hair in sections to make sure you’re distributing it evenly.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help reduce frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

FAQs

Q: Can I apply mousse to dry hair?

A: It’s best to apply mousse to damp hair. If you apply it to dry hair, it may not distribute evenly and can leave your hair looking greasy.

Q: Can I use mousse on straight hair?

A: Yes, you can use mousse on straight hair to add volume and texture.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your curls. If you must use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray and set your tools on a low heat setting.

Now that you know how to apply mousse on curly hair, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls with ease. Remember to start with a small amount of mousse and add more as needed, and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. With the right products and techniques, you can have healthy, gorgeous curls every day.
