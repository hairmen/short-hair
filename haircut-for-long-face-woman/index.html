Haircut For Long Face Woman Flatter Your Features With The Best Hairstyles

Written by Petter Nov 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your long face? The right haircut can do wonders to flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we’ll guide you through the best haircuts for long face women, from how to choose the right style to tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, elongated features, and a narrow chin. The right haircut can help balance out your face shape and draw attention to your best features. Whether you have straight or curly hair, there are plenty of styles to choose from that will flatter your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut for a long face is all about creating balance. You want to avoid styles that make your face look even longer, such as super-long hair or blunt bangs. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face and draw attention away from your forehead.

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in the mirror. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Step 2: Consider your hair type

Your hair type will also play a role in choosing the right haircut. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for shorter styles that add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, longer styles with layers can help lighten up your look.

Step 3: Look for styles that add volume to the sides

When choosing a haircut for a long face, you want to look for styles that add volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved with layers, waves, or curls. Avoid blunt cuts or super-long hair, as these can make your face look even longer.

Best Haircuts for Long Face Women

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for long face women. They add volume to the sides of your face and draw attention away from your forehead. This style works well with straight or wavy hair and can be dressed up or down with accessories.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to balance out a long face. They draw attention to your eyes and add volume to the sides of your face. This style works well with both short and long hair and can be styled with a side part or center part.

3. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers is a great option for long face women with fine hair. The layers add volume and texture to your hair, while the bob length helps balance out your face shape. This style works well with both straight and curly hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Once you’ve chosen the perfect haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are a few tips:

1. Use the right products

Using the right products for your hair type can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best. If you have fine hair, look for volumizing products that add texture and lift. If you have thick hair, look for products that help control frizz and add shine.

2. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Using heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a long face?

The best haircut for a long face is one that adds volume to the sides of your face and draws attention away from your forehead. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and bobs with layers are all great options.

Can long hair work for a long face?

Long hair can work for a long face, but it’s important to add layers and volume to the sides of your face to balance out your features. Avoid blunt cuts and super-long hair, as these can make your face look even longer.

What products should I use to maintain my new haircut?

Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. If you have fine hair, look for volumizing products. If you have thick hair, look for products that help control frizz and add shine.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is an important part of maintaining your new look. Be sure to use high-quality products that are designed for your hair type and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can flatter your features and feel confident and beautiful every day.
