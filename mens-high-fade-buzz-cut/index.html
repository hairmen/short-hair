Mens High Fade Buzz Cut A Timeless And Trendy Look

Written by Luffy Nov 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and on-trend? Then the mens high fade buzz cut might be the perfect choice for you. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about this classic haircut, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of your hair after the cut. So, let’s get started!

Description

The mens high fade buzz cut is a popular haircut that features a high fade on the sides and back, with the top of the hair cut short with clippers. This gives a clean and polished look that is perfect for guys who want a low-maintenance style. It’s also a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Get a Mens High Fade Buzz Cut

To get a mens high fade buzz cut, you’ll need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced with this type of haircut. They will use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair, gradually tapering the length up towards the top of your head. The top of your hair will be cut short with clippers or scissors, depending on your desired style. It’s important to communicate your preferences clearly with your barber, so they can give you the exact look you want.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Mens High Fade Buzz Cut

If you want to try cutting your own mens high fade buzz cut at home, here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Clippers

Choose a pair of clippers with different guard lengths, so you can taper the sides and back of your hair gradually. A number 1 or 2 guard is a good starting point for the sides and back, while a number 3 or 4 guard can be used to blend the top of your hair with the sides and back.

Step 2: Start with the Sides and Back

Using the clippers, start at the bottom of the sides and back of your hair, using the longest guard length you’ve chosen. Gradually move up towards the top of your head, changing to shorter guard lengths as you go. Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure the length is consistent on both sides.

Step 3: Taper the Sides and Back

Once you’ve reached the top of your head, switch to a shorter guard length and start tapering the length of the hair towards the top. This will create a fade effect. Use a comb to blend the hair between different guard lengths, making sure there are no harsh lines.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using scissors or clippers, cut the top of your hair to the desired length. You can leave it short for a classic buzz cut look, or leave it slightly longer for a more textured style. Use your fingers to create texture and movement in the hair.

Tips for Styling Mens High Fade Buzz Cut

Here are some tips for styling your mens high fade buzz cut:

  • Use a pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Slick your hair back for a polished look, or create texture by tousling it with your fingers.
  • If you have a receding hairline, consider leaving the hair on top slightly longer to create the illusion of more hair.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQs

Is the mens high fade buzz cut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens high fade buzz cut can be adapted to suit different face shapes. If you have a round face, consider leaving the hair on top slightly longer to create more height. If you have a square face, keep the top of the hair shorter to balance out the face shape.

How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain my mens high fade buzz cut?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your mens high fade buzz cut and keep it looking sharp.

Hair Care Tips for Mens High Fade Buzz Cut

Here are some tips for taking care of your mens high fade buzz cut:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • If you have dry or flaky scalp, use a moisturizing scalp treatment once a week.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals or excessive heat, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the mens high fade buzz cut with confidence and style. Whether you’re going for a classic, polished look or a more textured and messy style, this haircut is versatile and easy to maintain. So why not try it out for yourself?
