How To Make Your Hair Curly For The Next Day

Written by Frank Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools every day to get those perfect curls? Do you want to know how to make your hair curly for the next day? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss different ways to get curly hair without using heat and how to maintain those curls for the next day.

Description

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be challenging to maintain. If you have naturally straight hair or have recently straightened your hair, getting those perfect curls can seem impossible. Fortunately, there are several ways to get curly hair without using heat. Not only are these methods less damaging to your hair, but they also last longer.

How to

There are several ways to get curly hair without using heat. Here are a few methods:

Method 1: Braids

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry. Then, separate your hair into sections and braid each section tightly. Leave the braids in overnight, and in the morning, take them out and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls. You can also use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Method 2: Twist Out

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry. Then, separate your hair into sections and twist each section tightly. Leave the twists in overnight, and in the morning, take them out and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls. You can also use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Method 3: Foam Rollers

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry. Then, section your hair and wrap each section around a foam roller. Leave the rollers in overnight, and in the morning, take them out and run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls. You can also use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before taking out the braids/twists/foam rollers.
  • Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.
  • Don't brush your hair after taking out the braids/twists/foam rollers, use your fingers instead.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Try different methods to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

How long will the curls last?

The curls should last up to 2-3 days if you take care of them properly.

Can I use these methods on wet hair?

No, it's best to use these methods on dry or slightly damp hair.

Can I use these methods on all hair types?

Yes, these methods can work on all hair types, but the results may vary.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain those beautiful curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair curly for the next day without using heat, try out these methods and see which one works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain those curls for as long as possible. Good luck!
