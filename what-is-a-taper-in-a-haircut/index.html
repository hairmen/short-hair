What Is A Taper In A Haircut

Written by Frank Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A taper is a popular haircut style that has been around for decades. It is a style that involves cutting hair shorter in length as it moves down the head, giving it a tapered appearance. In this article, we will discuss what a taper is, how to achieve it, and some tips to maintain it.

Description

A taper haircut is a style where the hair is gradually tapered from the top of the head to the neckline. This creates a clean and polished look that is suitable for both formal and casual occasions. The hair is cut shorter at the back and sides of the head, while the top of the hair remains longer. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

Here are the steps to achieve a taper haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, towel dry it and use a blow dryer to dry it completely.

Step 2: Create a Guide

Use a comb to create a guide for the taper. This involves creating a line around the head where the hair will be cut shorter. The guide should be about an inch above the neckline and should extend from one ear to the other.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair at the back of the head, working your way up towards the guide. Use clippers to create a gradual taper, making sure to blend the hair as you go along. Repeat this process on the sides of the head, taking care to create a seamless transition from the top of the head to the shorter hair at the sides and back.

Step 4: Finish the Top

Once you have finished the sides and back of the head, move on to the top of the head. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, leaving it longer than the hair at the sides and back. Blend the hair at the top of the head into the shorter hair at the sides and back using scissors or clippers.

Step 5: Clean Up

Finally, use a comb and scissors to clean up any stray hairs, and style the hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your taper haircut:

  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your taper haircut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Apply a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about taper haircuts:

What is the difference between a taper and a fade?

A taper involves cutting the hair shorter as it moves down the head, while a fade involves cutting the hair shorter as it moves towards the neckline. A taper is a more gradual transition, while a fade is more abrupt.

Is a taper suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a taper can be customized to suit different hair types and face shapes. Your barber can advise you on the best taper style for your hair.

How often should I get a taper haircut?

You should get a taper haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks and feels healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With proper care and maintenance, your taper haircut will look great and last for weeks. So go ahead and try out this classic and versatile hairstyle!
