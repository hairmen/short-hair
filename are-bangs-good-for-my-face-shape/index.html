Are Bangs Good For My Face Shape

Written by Oliver Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to changing up your hairstyle, one of the most common questions is whether or not bangs are a good option for your face shape. The answer isn't always straightforward, as it depends on your unique features and preferences. In this article, we'll explore the different face shapes and offer some tips on whether or not bangs are a good choice for you.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shapes
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Bangs and Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing Bangs

Face Shapes

Before we dive into whether or not bangs are a good choice for your face shape, it's important to identify your face shape. There are typically six different face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Diamond
  • Heart
  • Rectangle

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics, and certain hairstyles can help accentuate or balance these features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're unsure of your face shape, there are a few steps you can take to figure it out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've drawn.

Your face should fall into one of the six categories mentioned earlier. If you're still unsure, there are online quizzes and tools that can help you determine your face shape.

Bangs and Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to consider whether or not bangs are a good choice for you.

Round Face

If you have a round face, bangs can help elongate your features and make your face appear slimmer. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs that graze your cheekbones.

Oval Face

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of bang styles. Consider trying blunt bangs, wispy bangs, or a side-swept look.

Square Face

If you have a square face, bangs can help soften your features. Go for a softer, side-swept look that falls just above your eyebrows.

Diamond Face

Diamond faces tend to have a narrow forehead and chin, with wider cheekbones. Consider trying wispy, textured bangs that hit just above your eyebrows.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for longer, side-swept bangs that hit at or below your chin. Avoid blunt bangs or anything too short.

Rectangle Face

For rectangle faces, try wispy, textured bangs that hit above your eyebrows. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs that can make your face appear longer.

Tips for Choosing Bangs

When it comes to choosing bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape and the features you want to accentuate or balance.
  • Think about your hair texture and how easy it will be to style your bangs.
  • Take into account your personal style and the level of maintenance you're willing to commit to.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion and advice.

FAQs

Can bangs be good for any face shape?

Yes, bangs can be a good choice for any face shape. It's all about finding the right style that works for you.

How should I style my bangs?

Styling your bangs will depend on the type of bangs you have and your hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist for the best styling tips and products.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Once you've chosen your bangs, it's important to take proper care of them to keep them looking their best:

  • Wash your bangs regularly, as they tend to get greasy faster than the rest of your hair.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to help shape them and add volume.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Avoid touching or brushing your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy or frizzy.

Overall, whether or not bangs are a good choice for your face shape depends on a variety of factors. Take your time to consider your options and consult with a hairstylist before making the cut.
