How To Thin Short Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Aprilia Jun 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short hair and want to achieve a more textured and layered look, thinning shears can be a great tool to use. Thinning shears are scissors with notched blades that can remove some of the hair while leaving the rest intact. In this article, we'll discuss how to thin short hair with thinning shears.

Description

Thinning shears are a great tool for anyone who wants to add texture and volume to their hair. They can be especially helpful for those with fine hair or hair that tends to be flat. Thinning shears work by removing some of the hair without cutting it all the way through. This creates a more layered and textured look without sacrificing length.

How to Thin Short Hair with Thinning Shears

Here are the steps to thin short hair with thinning shears:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It's important to work with clean, dry hair so you can see the natural texture and shape of your hair.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Start thinning

Take your thinning shears and start working on one section at a time. Hold the shears horizontally and start cutting into the hair, taking small sections at a time. Work your way around the head, taking care to keep the length even.

Step 4: Blend the layers

Once you've cut all the sections, use a regular comb to blend the layers together. This will ensure that the layers look natural and not choppy.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using thinning shears:

  • Use sharp shears to ensure a clean cut
  • Take small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much hair
  • Keep the length even throughout the head
  • Use a regular comb to blend the layers together

FAQ

How often should I thin my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to thin your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I use thinning shears on curly hair?

Yes, you can use thinning shears on curly hair, but it's important to be careful not to thin too much or it can create a frizzy look.

Can I use thinning shears on my own hair?

It's possible to use thinning shears on your own hair, but it can be tricky. It's best to have a professional do it for you to ensure that it's done correctly.

Hair Care

After thinning your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant if you do use heat styling tools. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

Thinning shears can be a great tool for anyone who wants to add texture and volume to their short hair. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a more layered and textured look that will help you feel confident and stylish.
