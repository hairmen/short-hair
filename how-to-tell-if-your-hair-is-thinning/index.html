One of the first signs of hair thinning is a noticeable decrease in hair density. You may start to notice that your hair looks thinner or that your scalp is more visible than before. Other symptoms of hair thinning include:

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Thinning

There are many things you can do to prevent hair thinning and promote healthy hair growth, including:

  • Eating a healthy, balanced diet
