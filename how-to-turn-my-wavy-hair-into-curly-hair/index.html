How To Turn Your Wavy Hair Into Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy hair and have always envied those with curly locks, you're in luck! With a few simple steps, you can turn your wavy hair into bouncy, curly hair that will turn heads. In this guide, we'll go through the steps, tips, and hair care you need to know to get the curly hair of your dreams.

Table of Contents

  • How to Turn Your Wavy Hair into Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Curls
  • FAQs

How to Turn Your Wavy Hair into Curly Hair

There are several ways to turn wavy hair into curly hair, but the most popular methods include using a curling iron, perm, or natural methods. No matter which method you choose, the key to getting the perfect curls is to start with clean, dry hair.

Using a Curling Iron

If you're looking for a quick fix and don't want to commit to a perm, using a curling iron is a great option. Start by washing and drying your hair, then section it off into small pieces. Take each section and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Continue this process until all sections are curled, then finish with a light hairspray.

Getting a Perm

If you're looking for a longer-lasting solution, getting a perm is the way to go. A perm involves using chemicals to break down the protein bonds in your hair, then reforming them into a curly shape. While this method is more permanent, it does require more maintenance and can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly.

Using Natural Methods

If you're looking for a natural way to get curly hair, there are several methods you can try. One popular method is using a salt spray, which involves mixing sea salt with water and spritzing it onto your hair. Another method is using braids or twists to create curls, which involves braiding or twisting your hair and leaving it overnight. In the morning, you'll have natural, bouncy curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

No matter which method you choose, these steps will help you get the perfect curls.

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into small pieces.
  3. Use a curling iron, perm, or natural method to create curls.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Perfect Curls

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't brush your curls after they've been set. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate them.
  • Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and maintain your curls.

FAQs

How long do curls last?

The longevity of your curls depends on the method you used to create them. If you used a curling iron, your curls will last until you wash your hair. If you got a perm, your curls will last for several months. If you used a natural method, your curls will last until you wash your hair.

Can I get a perm if I have colored hair?

Yes, you can get a perm if you have colored hair, but it's important to wait at least two weeks after coloring your hair before getting a perm.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Once you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its shape and prevent damage. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips, you'll be able to turn your wavy hair into curly hair and maintain its shape for days to come. Whether you choose to use a curling iron, get a perm, or use natural methods, remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and bouncy.
