Long Crop Top Men s Haircut The Trending Hairstyle For Men In 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle to try in 2023, the long crop top men's haircut might be the perfect fit for you. This trendy haircut has been gaining popularity among men all over the world, thanks to its versatility and modern look. In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about the long crop top men's haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The long crop top men's haircut is a modern take on the classic crop top haircut. It's characterized by short sides and back, with longer hair on top that's styled in a messy, textured manner. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to Get the Long Crop Top Men's Haircut

The first step in getting the long crop top men's haircut is to find a skilled barber who can help you achieve the look you want. Make sure to bring a photo of the hairstyle you want, so your barber can have a clear idea of what you're looking for.

During the haircut, your barber will trim the sides and back of your hair to a short length, while leaving the hair on top longer. The length of the hair on top will depend on your personal preference, but generally, it should be at least 2 inches long.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Long Crop Top Men's Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the long crop top men's haircut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  2. Show your barber a photo of the hairstyle you want.
  3. Ask your barber to trim the sides and back of your hair to a short length, leaving the hair on top longer.
  4. Ask your barber to add texture to the hair on top using scissors or a razor.
  5. Style your hair using a matte hair product, such as a clay or paste.

Tips for Styling the Long Crop Top Men's Haircut

Here are some tips for styling the long crop top men's haircut:

  • Use a matte hair product to add texture to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, such as a messy, tousled look or a slicked-back style.
  • Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.

FAQs

Is the long crop top men's haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the long crop top men's haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, the styling techniques may vary depending on your hair type.

How often should I get a haircut to maintain the long crop top men's haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the long crop top men's haircut.

Hair Care Advice for the Long Crop Top Men's Haircut

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining the long crop top men's haircut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

The long crop top men's haircut is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that can work for any occasion. Whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a slicked-back style, this haircut is sure to turn heads. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can maintain this look for years to come.
