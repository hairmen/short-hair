Short Cuts For Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle? Short cuts can be a great option, as they are easy to maintain and can help you look younger. In this post, we will discuss some tips and tricks for getting the best short cut for your age. Read on to learn more!

How to Choose the Right Short Cut

Before you go to the salon, it's important to have an idea of what type of short cut you want. Look for inspiration in magazines or online, and consider the shape of your face and the texture of your hair. For example, a pixie cut may be perfect for someone with fine hair, while a bob can be flattering for a round face.

It's also important to talk to your stylist about your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend on your hair each day. A low-maintenance cut may be best if you don't want to spend a lot of time styling your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Cut

Once you've decided on a short cut, it's time to visit the salon. Follow these steps to get the best results:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Make sure to wash your hair before you go to the salon. This will give your stylist a clean slate to work with.

Step 2: Bring Inspiration Photos

Bring photos of the short cut you want to your appointment. This will give your stylist a clear idea of what you're looking for.

Step 3: Discuss Your Options

Talk to your stylist about your hair type and lifestyle. They may have suggestions for a cut that will work best for you.

Step 4: Trust Your Stylist

Your stylist is a professional and knows what will look best on you. Trust their judgement and be open to their suggestions.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Cut

Once you've gotten your short cut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Choose products that are designed for your hair type and the style you want. For example, use a volumizing mousse if you have fine hair and want to add volume.

Tip 2: Get Regular Trims

Short cuts need regular trims to maintain their shape. Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to keep your cut looking fresh.

Tip 3: Experiment with Styling

Short cuts are versatile and can be styled in many ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a style that works for you.

FAQ

Q: What is the best short cut for over 60?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. The best short cut for you will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Talk to your stylist about your options.

Q: Can I color my short cut?

Yes, you can color your short cut. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

Q: How do I know if a short cut is right for me?

A short cut can be a great option if you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that can help you look younger. However, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture before making a decision. Talk to your stylist about your options.

Hair Care for Over 60

As we age, our hair may become thinner and more brittle. To keep your hair looking healthy, try these tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for products that are designed for mature hair.

Tip 2: Use a Moisturizing Conditioner

Conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage. Look for a conditioner that is designed for your hair type.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, so it's important to use them sparingly. When you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short cut for your age and maintain healthy, beautiful hair. So why not try a new short cut today?
