Long Oval Face Short Hair The Perfect Match

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that will accentuate your long oval face? Look no further than short hair! Whether you prefer a pixie cut or a bob, short hair can make your face look more balanced and proportionate. In this article, we'll go over some tips on how to rock short hair with a long oval face.

Description

A long oval face is characterized by a forehead and jawline that are similar in width, with the face being longer than it is wide. This face shape is often considered the most versatile, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Short hair is a great option for long oval faces because it can help balance out the length of the face.

How To

When getting a short haircut for a long oval face, it's important to consider the placement of the layers. Layers that start at the cheekbones or chin can help add width to the face and create a more balanced look. Avoid layers that start at the top of the head, as they can make the face look even longer.

Another thing to consider when getting a short haircut is the shape of the cut. A bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front, also known as an A-line bob, can help create the illusion of a more round face. A pixie cut with long bangs can also help balance out the length of the face.

Step By Step

The first step in getting a short haircut for a long oval face is to find a hairstyle that you like. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and bring pictures to your hairstylist to show them what you're looking for.

Once you've found a hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist about the placement of the layers and the shape of the cut. They can help you decide on the best style for your face shape.

After your hair is cut, style it in a way that complements your face shape. Use a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head, and use a flat iron to add texture to the ends of your hair.

Tips

  • Choose a hairstyle with layers that start at the cheekbones or chin.
  • Avoid layers that start at the top of the head.
  • Consider an A-line bob or pixie cut with long bangs.
  • Style your hair to add volume to the crown of your head and texture to the ends.

FAQ

Can I still wear a short hairstyle if I have a long oval face?

Absolutely! Short hair can actually help balance out the length of a long oval face.

What's the best way to style short hair for a long oval face?

Use a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head, and use a flat iron to add texture to the ends of your hair.

What should I avoid when getting a short haircut for a long oval face?

Avoid layers that start at the top of the head, as they can make the face look even longer.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for a long oval face with short hair, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser to dry your hair on low heat.

Regular trims are also important to maintain the shape of your short haircut. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock short hair with a long oval face and feel confident and stylish. Remember to choose a hairstyle that you love, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you!
