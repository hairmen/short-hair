How To Box Braid Your Own Hair Guys Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Box braids are a protective hairstyle that is popular among men. They are easy to maintain, and they give you a stylish look. If you're looking to learn how to box braid your own hair guys, then you're in the right place. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve perfect box braids.

Description

Box braids are a type of protective hairstyle that consists of square-shaped sections of hair. They are braided from the roots to the tips, and they can be styled in different ways. Box braids are perfect for men who want to protect their hair from damage and promote healthy hair growth.

How to Box Braid Your Own Hair Guys: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start braiding your hair, you need to gather your supplies. You'll need a comb, hair elastics, hair clips, and braiding hair. Make sure that you have enough braiding hair to complete the style.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Prep your hair by washing it and allowing it to air dry. Once it's dry, detangle it using a wide-tooth comb. Divide your hair into small sections using a comb and secure each section with a hair elastic.

Step 3: Add Braiding Hair

Take a small section of braiding hair and fold it in half. Place the folded end underneath your natural hair, and use your fingers to create a loop. Take the ends of the braiding hair and pull them through the loop. Pull tight to secure the braiding hair to your natural hair.

Step 4: Start Braiding

Divide the section of hair into three equal parts. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the new middle section. Continue braiding in this manner until you reach the end of the section.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat steps 3 and 4 until all of your hair is braided. Make sure to take breaks if your arms get tired.

Tips

  • Use hair oil or moisturizer to keep your hair hydrated
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep
  • Don't braid your hair too tightly as this can cause damage to your hairline

FAQ

How long do box braids last?

Box braids can last up to 8 weeks with proper care and maintenance.

Can I swim with box braids?

Yes, you can swim with box braids. However, it's important to rinse your hair with clean water after swimming to prevent chlorine damage.

How do I take care of my box braids?

To take care of your box braids, you should wash your hair once a week and apply hair oil or moisturizer to keep your hair hydrated.

Hair Care

Box braids are a great way to protect your hair from damage and promote healthy hair growth. However, it's important to take care of your hair while wearing this style. Make sure to wash your hair regularly and apply hair oil or moisturizer to keep your hair hydrated. Also, avoid pulling your hair too tightly as this can cause damage to your hairline. With proper care and maintenance, you can enjoy your box braids for up to 8 weeks.
