How To Style Dense Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you have dense hair that can be difficult to manage and style? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with thick, dense hair struggle to find the right styling techniques that work for them. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style dense hair, from blow-drying to updos.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Dense Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Dense Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Dense Hair

How to Style Dense Hair

The first step in styling dense hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage, and then use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to smooth out your hair and add volume.

Next, use a styling product such as mousse or gel to help hold your style. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

If you want to add curls, use a curling iron or hot rollers. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, taking small sections of hair at a time. Once you have curled your entire head, use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume.

If you prefer a straighter look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Take small sections of hair at a time and run the flat iron through each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

3. Use a blow dryer and round brush to dry your hair.

4. Apply a styling product such as mousse or gel.

5. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls, or a flat iron to straighten your hair.

6. Use your fingers to separate curls and add volume.

7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Dense Hair

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.

2. Don't overuse styling products, as this can weigh down your hair.

3. Use a high-quality blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair.

4. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

5. Consider getting regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and manageable.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a hair mask on dense hair?

A: Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and hydrate your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my dense hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and styling routine, but most people with dense hair can wash their hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Dense Hair

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

4. Consider using a leave-in conditioner to help reduce frizz and add shine.

5. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your dense hair with ease and confidence. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you, and always prioritize hair care to keep your hair healthy and manageable.
