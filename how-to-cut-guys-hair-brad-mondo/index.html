How To Cut Guys Hair Brad Mondo A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to cut your guy's hair at home, you may have stumbled upon Brad Mondo's Youtube channel. Brad Mondo is a well-known hairstylist who shares tips and tricks on how to cut and style hair. In this post, we'll be sharing a step-by-step guide on how to cut guys hair Brad Mondo style!

Description

In this guide, we'll be covering the basics of cutting guys hair. We'll be following Brad Mondo's techniques to ensure that you get the best possible results. We'll be covering everything from the tools you'll need to the actual cutting process. So, grab your scissors and let's get started!

How To

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting guys hair Brad Mondo style is to gather your tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape
  • Haircutting spray bottle
  • Thinners

Once you have all your tools, you're ready to start cutting!

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use the spray bottle to dampen the hair if needed.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Next, section the hair into smaller sections. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any spots. Use the comb to section the hair and clip it back.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now, it's time to start cutting! Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Start from the back and work your way to the front. Use the clippers to trim the sides and back of the head.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair, it's time to blend it. Use the thinners to blend the hair and ensure that there are no harsh lines. This will give the hair a more natural look.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Finally, it's time to style the hair. Use a styling product of your choice to style the hair. You can use gel, wax, or pomade to achieve the desired look.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut the hair shorter. It's easier to cut more hair off than to add more.
  • Use the comb to hold the hair in place while cutting.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. Take small sections and cut slowly.
  • Be patient. Cutting hair takes time and practice.

FAQ

Can I cut my guy's hair if I have no experience?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to follow the steps carefully and take your time.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't worry. Hair grows back! You can always go to a professional to fix the mistake.

How often should I cut my guy's hair?

This depends on the desired length and style. Generally, guys should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your guy's hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much heat on the hair, as this can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.

That's it! Now you know how to cut guys hair Brad Mondo style. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
