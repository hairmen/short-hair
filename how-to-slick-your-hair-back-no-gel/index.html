How To Slick Your Hair Back No Gel

Written by Christine Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read
Are you looking for a sleek, polished look for your hair without using gel? Slicking your hair back without gel can seem like a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the look you want. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to slick your hair back with no gel, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Your Hair Back No Gel
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Hair Back No Gel
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to slick your hair back without gel:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue from previous hair products. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.
  2. Apply a lightweight styling cream or pomade to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair and a quarter-sized amount for longer hair.
  3. Comb your hair back with a fine-tooth comb, starting from the hairline and working your way back. Smooth out any bumps or knots along the way.
  4. Once your hair is slicked back, use a hair dryer on low heat to set the look. Use a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.
  5. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Voila! You have slicked back your hair without gel.

Tips for Slicking Hair Back No Gel

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a lightweight styling cream or pomade to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or heavy.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to create a smooth, polished look.
  • Blow dry your hair on low heat to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair partings to find the most flattering style for your face shape.

FAQs

Can I slick my hair back without any product?

While it is possible to slick your hair back without any product, using a styling cream or pomade can help hold the style in place and create a polished look.

What type of hair works best for this style?

This style works best for those with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten it first before slicking it back.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

Avoid using too much product and use a lightweight styling cream or pomade. Also, don't forget to wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup or residue from previous hair products.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your hair when styling it in a slicked back style:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue from previous hair products.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy, polished look.

Now you know how to slick your hair back without gel. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a sleek, polished look for any occasion. Experiment with different hair partings and styles to find the one that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using a deep conditioning treatment to keep it healthy and moisturized. Happy styling!
