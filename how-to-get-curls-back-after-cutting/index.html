How To Get Curls Back After Cutting Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to get your curls back after cutting? You’re not alone. Many people make the mistake of cutting their curly hair too short, and then struggle to get their curls back. Fortunately, there are a few tips and tricks that you can use to help bring your curls back to life. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best ways to get your curls back after cutting.

Description: Why Cutting Curly Hair Can Be Tricky

Curly hair can be tricky to cut, especially if you’re not familiar with how it behaves. When you cut curly hair too short, it can lose its curl pattern and become straight. This happens because curly hair is more fragile than straight hair, and cutting it can cause damage to the hair shaft. The good news is that there are ways to get your curls back after cutting, so don’t despair if you’ve made this mistake.

How to Get Your Curls Back After Cutting: Step by Step

Step 1: Don’t Cut Your Hair Too Short

The first step to getting your curls back after cutting is to avoid cutting your hair too short in the first place. If you have curly hair, you should always go to a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. They will know how to work with your curls and ensure that they don’t lose their shape when cut.

Step 2: Use a Diffuser

If you’ve already cut your hair too short and want to encourage your curls to come back, one of the best things you can do is to use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair. A diffuser is a special attachment that you can add to your hair dryer that helps to distribute the heat more evenly, reducing the risk of damage to your hair. Using a diffuser can help to enhance your natural curl pattern, so be sure to use one when blow-drying your hair.

Step 3: Use the Right Products

Another important step in getting your curls back after cutting is to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos and conditioners, curl creams, and gels. These products will help to define your curls and keep them looking their best.

Step 4: Sleep on Silk

Believe it or not, the type of pillowcase you use can have an impact on your curls. Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and preserve your curls overnight. Silk is a smooth material that won’t rub against your hair, so it’s a great choice for anyone with curly hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Now that you know how to get your curls back after cutting, it’s important to take steps to maintain them. Here are a few tips to help you keep your curls looking their best:

Tip 1: Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to limit washing your hair to every other day, and use a gentle shampoo that won’t dry out your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause it to lose its curl pattern. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible, and opt for heat-free styling methods instead.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Make sure to go to a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair, and ask for a trim every 2-3 months.

FAQ

Can I Get My Curls Back After Cutting?

Yes, you can get your curls back after cutting. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can help to encourage your curls to come back and maintain them looking their best.

What Products Should I Use on My Curly Hair?

You should look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing shampoos and conditioners, curl creams, and gels. These products will help to define your curls and keep them looking their best.

How Often Should I Wash My Curly Hair?

You should try to limit washing your hair to every other day, and use a gentle shampoo that won’t dry out your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you care for your curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles and breakage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

Tip 2: Don’t Rub Your Hair Dry

Instead of rubbing your hair dry with a towel, gently squeeze out the excess water. Rubbing your hair can cause damage and lead to frizz.

Tip 3: Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help to keep your hair looking healthy and moisturized. Try to do a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls looking their best.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get your curls back after cutting and keep them looking their best. Remember to be patient, as it may take some time for your curls to come back. With a little bit of extra care, you can have beautiful, bouncy curls that you’ll love.
