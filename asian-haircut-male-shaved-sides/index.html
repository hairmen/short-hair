Asian Haircut Male Shaved Sides A Trendy Look For 2023

Written by William Sep 09, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re looking for a new hairstyle that’s trendy and easy to maintain, consider the Asian haircut with shaved sides. This look has been popular for several years now and continues to be a favorite among men. In this article, we’ll provide a step-by-step guide on how to get this haircut, tips for styling it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get the Asian Haircut Male Shaved Sides

Before you go to your barber, it’s important to know exactly what you want. The Asian haircut with shaved sides typically involves cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving it longer on top. The length on top can vary, but it’s usually around 2-3 inches. To get this look, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Decide how short you want your sides to be. You can go for a full buzz cut, or leave a little bit of length. It’s up to you.

Step 2: Show Your Barber

Take a picture of the style you want, or describe it to your barber. Make sure they understand exactly what you’re looking for.

Step 3: Let Them Work Their Magic

Sit back and relax while your barber works their magic. They’ll use clippers to cut the sides of your hair, and scissors to trim the top.

Tips for Styling Your Asian Haircut Male Shaved Sides

Once you’ve got your new haircut, it’s time to style it. Here are some tips to help you get the perfect look:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair in place. Pomade or wax works well for this style.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles. You can slick your hair back, comb it to the side, or leave it messy.

Tip 3: Keep Your Hair Clean

Regularly wash your hair to keep it clean and healthy. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the Asian haircut male shaved sides:

What Face Shapes Work Best with This Haircut?

This haircut works well with most face shapes, but it’s particularly flattering for men with oval or square faces.

How Often Should I Get This Haircut?

You should get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Can I Style This Haircut with a Beard?

Yes, this haircut looks great with a beard. Just make sure your beard is well-groomed and matches the style of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your Asian haircut male shaved sides looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often. If you do use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

In conclusion, the Asian haircut male shaved sides is a trendy and low-maintenance look that’s perfect for men who want to look stylish without spending too much time on their hair. Follow our tips and advice to get the perfect look for you.
