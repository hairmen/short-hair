Does Buccal Fat Removal Make You Age Faster

Written by Robby Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In recent years, buccal fat removal has become an increasingly popular procedure for those looking to achieve a more chiseled and contoured facial appearance. However, many people are concerned that this procedure may lead to premature aging. In this blog post, we will discuss whether or not buccal fat removal makes you age faster and provide you with some tips and insights on the topic.

Description

Buccal fat removal is a cosmetic procedure that involves the removal of fat pads from the cheeks to create a more defined and sculpted appearance. The procedure is typically performed under local anesthesia and involves making small incisions inside the mouth to access the fat pads. Once the fat pads are located, they are carefully removed with a surgical instrument. The procedure usually takes around 30 minutes to an hour and is relatively straightforward.

Many people opt for buccal fat removal because they want to achieve a more streamlined and defined facial appearance. However, some individuals are concerned that this procedure may lead to premature aging. The concern is that by removing the fat pads from the cheeks, the face may appear sunken and gaunt, which can make a person look older than their actual age.

How to

If you are considering buccal fat removal, it is important to do your research and find a board-certified plastic surgeon who has experience with the procedure. During your consultation, your surgeon will evaluate your facial structure and determine if you are a good candidate for the procedure. They will also discuss the risks and benefits of the procedure and answer any questions you may have.

If you decide to move forward with the procedure, your surgeon will provide you with pre-operative instructions to follow. It is important to follow these instructions carefully to ensure a smooth and successful surgery. After the surgery, you will also receive post-operative instructions to follow, which will include tips on how to care for your incisions and manage any discomfort or swelling.

Step by step

The buccal fat removal procedure typically involves the following steps:

  • You will receive local anesthesia to numb the area.
  • Your surgeon will make small incisions inside your mouth to access the fat pads.
  • The fat pads will be carefully located and then removed with a surgical instrument.
  • Your surgeon will close the incisions with dissolvable sutures.
  • You will be provided with post-operative instructions to follow.

Tips

If you are considering buccal fat removal, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Find a board-certified plastic surgeon with experience performing buccal fat removal.
  • Follow all pre-operative and post-operative instructions provided by your surgeon.
  • Be patient with your recovery and allow yourself time to heal.
  • Discuss any concerns or questions you may have with your surgeon.

FAQ

Is buccal fat removal safe?

When performed by a board-certified plastic surgeon with experience, buccal fat removal is generally safe. However, like any surgical procedure, there are risks and potential complications to be aware of, such as infection, bleeding, and nerve damage.

Will buccal fat removal make me look older?

While there is a concern that buccal fat removal may lead to premature aging by causing the face to appear sunken and gaunt, this is not always the case. The key is to find a skilled plastic surgeon who can create a natural-looking result that complements your facial structure.

Hair Care

After buccal fat removal, it is important to avoid using any hair products that may come into contact with your incisions. This includes hair sprays, gels, and oils. It is also important to avoid using any hair tools that generate heat, such as hair dryers and curling irons, as this can cause irritation to your incisions.

In conclusion, buccal fat removal is a safe and effective procedure for those looking to achieve a more defined and sculpted facial appearance. While there is a concern that the procedure may lead to premature aging, this is not always the case. By finding a skilled plastic surgeon and following all pre-operative and post-operative instructions, you can achieve a natural-looking result that complements your facial structure.
