What Hairstyle Suits Me Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by Christine Nov 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but still cannot find the perfect one that suits your round face? Do not worry; you are not alone. Many people with round faces struggle with finding the right hairstyle that will complement their features. In this article, we will provide you with tips and ideas on what hairstyle suits me round face.

Description

A round face has soft features and lacks sharp angles. The width and length of the face are almost equal, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. The chin is rounded, and the forehead is usually broad. When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create an illusion of length and height. The hairstyle should also highlight the best features of the face.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it is essential to determine your face shape. To do this, tie your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Use a lipstick or a washable marker to trace the outline of your face on the mirror. Once you have done this, step back and analyze the shape. If the width and length of the face are almost equal, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face, then you have a round face.

Step by Step Guide on Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hairstyle for a round face:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

As mentioned earlier, the first step is to determine your face shape. This will help you narrow down the hairstyles that will suit your face shape.

Step 2: Consider your hair texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or voluminous. If you have thick hair, go for hairstyles that are layered and textured.

Step 3: Look for hairstyles that create height

When choosing a hairstyle, look for hairstyles that create height at the crown. This will help elongate your face and create an illusion of length. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat, as they will make your face look rounder.

Step 4: Consider the length of your hair

The length of your hair also plays a crucial role in choosing a hairstyle. If you have short hair, go for hairstyles that add volume and texture. If you have long hair, avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they will make your face look rounder.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Go for hairstyles that create height at the crown
  • Layered and textured hairstyles work best for round faces
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too voluminous
  • Side-swept bangs can help elongate the face

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too voluminous.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. However, avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this will make your face look rounder.

Do side-swept bangs work for round faces?

Yes, side-swept bangs can help elongate the face and create an illusion of length.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Regardless of your face shape, it is essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape

In conclusion, finding the right hairstyle for a round face can be challenging, but it is not impossible. By following the tips and ideas provided in this article, you can find a hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident.
