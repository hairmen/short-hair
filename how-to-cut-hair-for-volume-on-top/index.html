How To Cut Hair For Volume On Top

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to add volume to your hair? One of the best ways to achieve this is by cutting your hair in the right way. In this blog post, we will be discussing how to cut hair for volume on top. We will cover the steps, tips, and hair care that you need to know to achieve the look you desire. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Hair for Volume on Top
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Cut Hair for Volume on Top

Cutting hair for volume on top involves a few specific techniques that are different from a typical haircut. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need to prepare your tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. Make sure your scissors are sharp to ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by dividing it into two parts – the top and the bottom. Use hair clips to hold the bottom section in place while you work on the top section.

Step 3: Cut the Top Section

Starting at the front of your head, cut the hair on the top section in small sections, holding the hair straight up and cutting the ends at an angle. This will add layers to your hair, which will create volume.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Bottom Sections

Once you have finished cutting the top section, remove the hair clips and let down the bottom section. Use your scissors to blend the top and bottom sections together by cutting at an angle.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to cutting hair for volume on top:

  1. Prepare your tools.
  2. Section your hair.
  3. Cut the top section.
  4. Blend the top and bottom sections.
  5. Check for any uneven sections and trim as needed.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair for volume on top:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and cut in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Use a comb to ensure that the hair is straight and even before cutting.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once – it's better to take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure that the cut is even.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair for volume on top if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still cut your hair for volume on top if you have curly hair. However, you may need to adjust the cutting technique to ensure that the curls are not cut too short.

Q: How often should I cut my hair for volume on top?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the look. Generally, cutting your hair every 6-8 weeks is recommended to maintain the volume on top.

Q: Will cutting my hair for volume on top make it look thinner?

A: No, cutting your hair for volume on top will not make it look thinner. In fact, it can actually make your hair look thicker and fuller.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair for volume on top, it's important to take care of it to maintain the look. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance the volume of your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

By following these tips, you can achieve the perfect cut for volume on top and keep your hair looking healthy and full.
