Best Hair Color For 65 Year Old Woman A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a 65 year old woman who's looking to switch up your hair color, you're in the right place. Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially given the wide range of options available in the market. In this article, we'll guide you through the best hair color options for 65 year old women, how to choose the right color, step by step process, tips, FAQ and hair care.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color?

Before we dive into the best hair color for 65 year old women, let's first talk about how to choose the right hair color. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your skin tone: Your hair color should complement your skin tone.
  • Look for low maintenance hair colors: As you age, you may prefer hair colors that require less maintenance.
  • Consider your lifestyle: Your hair color should match your lifestyle and personality.
  • Go for natural shades: Natural shades of hair color work great for older women.
  • Choose a color that flatters your features: Your hair color should bring out the best in your features.

Best Hair Color Options for 65 Year Old Women

Now that you know how to choose the right hair color, let's look at some of the best hair color options for 65 year old women:

1. Blonde

Blonde is a great option for older women, especially those with fair skin. It brightens up your complexion and gives you a youthful look. You can opt for highlights or balayage if you don't want to go for a full head of blonde.

2. Red

Red hair color is perfect for older women who want to make a statement. It's a bold and vibrant color that looks great on all skin tones. If you have fair skin, go for a lighter shade of red. If you have darker skin, opt for a deeper shade of red.

3. Brown

For a more natural look, brown hair color is a great option. It's low maintenance and looks great on all skin tones. You can opt for highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

4. Gray

Gray hair color is a trendy option for older women. It gives you a sophisticated and chic look. You can opt for a full head of gray or go for highlights if you don't want to go all in.

Step by Step Process

Here's a step by step process on how to color your hair:

  1. Choose the right hair color
  2. Do a patch test
  3. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to avoid staining
  4. Mix the hair color according to the instructions on the package
  5. Apply the hair color to your hair
  6. Leave it on for the recommended time
  7. Rinse off the hair color with warm water
  8. Apply conditioner to your hair

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when coloring your hair:

  • Wash your hair a day before coloring
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair for two days after coloring
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair after coloring
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

1. Can I color my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it's best to do a patch test before coloring your hair. Apply a small amount of the hair color on your skin and wait for 24 hours to see if you have any allergic reactions.

2. How often should I color my hair?

You can color your hair every 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

3. Can I color my hair at home?

Yes, you can color your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions on the package carefully and do a patch test before coloring your hair.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

Choosing the best hair color for 65 year old women can be a fun and exciting experience. With the right color and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best. Follow the tips and advice in this article to achieve the perfect hair color for you.
