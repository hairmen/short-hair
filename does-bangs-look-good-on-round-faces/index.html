Does Bangs Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Luffy Aug 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Adding bangs to your hairstyle is a great way to switch up your look. However, if you have a round face, you may be wondering if bangs would look good on you. In this article, we’ll answer the question, “does bangs look good on round faces?” and provide you with tips and tricks for styling your bangs to enhance your facial features.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by equal length and width, with a soft and circular jawline. If you have a round face, you may want to create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face shape. Bangs can help achieve this by drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones while creating angles that break up the roundness of your face.

How to

When it comes to choosing the right type of bangs for your round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, opt for a longer, side-swept bang that falls at an angle. This will elongate your face and draw attention to your cheekbones. Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead, as they will make your face appear even rounder.

Second, consider the thickness of your bangs. Thicker bangs can add volume to your forehead and make your face appear wider. Opt for thinner, wispy bangs instead to create a more delicate and feminine look.

Step by step

Here’s how to style side-swept bangs for a round face:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs in the opposite direction of where you want them to fall.
  3. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to create a subtle curve at the ends.
  4. Brush your bangs to the side and secure them with hairspray.

Tips

  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and dimension.
  • Avoid heavy, thick bangs that can weigh down your face.
  • Experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect style for your face shape.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

What are the best bangs for a round face?

Side-swept bangs that fall at an angle are the best option for a round face. Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead.

Can bangs make a round face look slimmer?

Yes, side-swept bangs can create angles that break up the roundness of your face and draw attention to your cheekbones, making your face appear slimmer.

Hair Care

When styling your bangs, it’s important to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage. Consider using a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration. Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, bangs can look great on round faces if styled correctly. Opt for side-swept bangs that fall at an angle and avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead. Experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect style for your face shape, and remember to keep your hair healthy and moisturized for a polished and put-together look.
