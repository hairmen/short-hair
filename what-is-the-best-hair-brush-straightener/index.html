What is a Hair Brush Straightener?

A hair brush straightener is a styling tool that combines the function of a hairbrush and a straightener. How to Use a Hair Brush Straightener

Using a hair brush straightener is easy. Simply plug it in and let it heat up to the desired temperature. Then, brush through sections of your hair, making sure to get all the way down to the roots.
