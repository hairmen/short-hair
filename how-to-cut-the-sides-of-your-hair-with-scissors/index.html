How To Cut The Sides Of Your Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save money on haircuts or simply want to try cutting your own hair, learning how to cut the sides of your hair with scissors can be a great place to start. While it may seem intimidating at first, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting the sides of your hair with scissors, including tips, tricks, and common questions.

How to Cut the Sides of Your Hair with Scissors

Before you begin, it's important to gather the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also helpful to have a spray bottle of water to dampen your hair, as well as hair clips to section your hair.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by parting your hair down the middle, dividing it into two equal sections. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 2: Dampen Your Hair

Using a spray bottle of water, dampen one section of your hair. This will make it easier to cut and help prevent split ends.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Using a comb, comb your hair straight down from the roots to the tips. This will help ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Begin Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting your hair at the desired length. It's best to start with a little less than you think you need, as you can always go back and cut more if necessary.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After cutting a small section of hair, use your comb to lift it up and check the length. If it's the desired length, continue cutting in small sections until you reach the end of your hair. If it's too long, go back and trim a little more.

Step 6: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you've finished one side, repeat the process on the other side of your head.

Tips for Cutting the Sides of Your Hair with Scissors

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind as you cut the sides of your hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Start with a little less than you think you need, as you can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes and uneven cutting.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. If you're struggling to cut the back of your hair, ask a friend or family member for assistance.

FAQ

Can I Cut My Own Hair?

Yes! With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience.

Can I Use Any Scissors to Cut My Hair?

No. It's important to use sharp scissors designed specifically for cutting hair. Using dull scissors or kitchen scissors can damage your hair and lead to split ends.

How Often Should I Cut My Hair?

The frequency of haircuts will vary depending on your hair type and desired length. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and techniques, you can successfully cut the sides of your hair with scissors and achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
