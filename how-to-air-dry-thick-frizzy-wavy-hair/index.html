How to Air Dry Thick Frizzy Wavy Hair

Air drying your hair is a great way to achieve a natural, effortless look. Hereâs how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy.
  2. Apply a lightweight, nourishing conditioner to your hair. Focus on the ends, as they tend to be the driest part of your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and minimize frizz.
  4. Gently squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.
  5. Apply a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to your hair. This will help to smooth your hair and prevent frizz.
  6. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your natural waves.
  7. Let your hair air dry completely. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps for perfectly air-dried, thick, frizzy, wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots. Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a lightweight, nourishing conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb to ensure even distribution. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse with lukewarm water.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle to avoid causing breakage or frizz.

Step 4: Squeeze Out Excess Water

Use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

Step 5: Apply Leave-In Conditioner or Anti-Frizz Serum

Apply a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to your hair. Focus on the ends, as they tend to be the driest part of your hair. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your natural waves.

Step 6: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry completely. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz. Once your hair is dry, you can fluff it up with your fingers for added volume.

Hair Care for Thick Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for keeping your thick, frizzy, wavy hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
