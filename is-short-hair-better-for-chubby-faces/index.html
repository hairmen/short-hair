Is Short Hair Better For Chubby Faces

Written by Aprilia Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a chubby face, you may be wondering whether short hair is better for you. While there's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, there are some general guidelines you can follow to determine whether short hair would be a good choice for your face shape.

Description

Chubby faces are characterized by a round shape with full cheeks and a soft jawline. People with chubby faces often feel self-conscious about their appearance and may seek ways to create a more balanced and flattering look. One option is to consider a short hairstyle.

How to

To determine whether short hair is better for your chubby face, you'll need to consider a few factors:

Face shape:

The shape of your face plays a significant role in determining whether short hair will be flattering. If you have a round or oval face shape, short hair can help create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. However, if you have a square or heart-shaped face, short hair may emphasize your strong jawline or wide forehead, making your face appear wider.

Hair texture:

The texture of your hair is another important factor to consider. If you have thick or curly hair, short hair can help lighten the weight and create a more balanced look. However, if you have fine or thin hair, short hair may make your face appear even rounder.

Personal style:

Your personal style and preferences should also come into play when deciding whether short hair is right for you. Short hair can be versatile and easy to maintain, but it may not be suitable for everyone's lifestyle or fashion sense.

Step by Step

If you've decided that short hair is the right choice for your chubby face, here are some steps to follow:

1. Consult with a stylist:

A professional stylist can help you determine the best short hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Choose a length:

Short hair can range from a pixie cut to a bob, so consider which length will be most flattering for your face shape.

3. Consider layers:

Layers can add dimension and movement to your hair, making it appear softer and more balanced.

4. Think about color:

Adding highlights or lowlights can create depth and dimension, helping to flatter your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a chubby face:

1. Avoid blunt cuts:

Straight lines can emphasize the roundness of your face, so consider a style with softer edges.

2. Don't go too short:

A very short haircut can make your face appear wider, so consider a length that falls just below your chin.

3. Play with angles:

Angled cuts can create the illusion of length and slimness, so consider a style with a side-swept fringe or asymmetrical layers.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look even rounder?

A: It depends on the length and style of your haircut. Very short haircuts can make your face appear wider, but a longer bob or layered cut can create a more balanced look.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up?

A: Yes! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and half-up styles.

Q: Do I need to use special products on my short hair?

A: Short hair can benefit from styling products like mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular trims and maintenance to keep it looking its best. Be sure to schedule appointments with your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and length. You may also need to adjust your hair care routine, using products that are specifically formulated for short hair and washing your hair more frequently to avoid oil buildup.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great choice for people with chubby faces, but it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before making the leap. With the right cut and styling techniques, short hair can help create a balanced and flattering look that highlights your best features.
