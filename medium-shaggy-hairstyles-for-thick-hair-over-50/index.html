Medium Shaggy Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 50

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide to some of the best medium shaggy hairstyles for women over 50 with thick hair. As we age, our hair changes in texture and thickness, and sometimes we need to adjust our hairstyles to suit our new hair. Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse, with its beautiful volume and body, but also being difficult to manage. A medium shaggy hairstyle is a perfect way to balance out the thickness and create a flattering and youthful look. Read on to discover some of our favorite styles, tips, and haircare advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium shaggy hairstyles are perfect for women over 50 with thick hair. They are low maintenance, versatile, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. These hairstyles work well for women who want to keep their hair at a medium length, as they can add volume and texture to the hair while also framing the face and accentuating the cheekbones. There are many different styles to choose from, including layered bobs, choppy pixies, and messy waves. The key is to find a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.

How to

Before deciding on a medium shaggy hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face, a layered bob or a choppy pixie can help to elongate the face and add definition. If you have a square face, try a shaggy lob or messy waves to soften the angles. If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can rock almost any medium shaggy hairstyle.

When it comes to hair texture, thick hair is perfect for medium shaggy hairstyles. The volume and body of the hair will help to create a beautiful and textured look. If you have thin hair, you can still achieve a shaggy look by using texturizing products and getting regular trims.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving a medium shaggy hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to the hair.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or curls.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to create a messy and tousled look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium shaggy hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep the ends of your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause breakage.
  • Experiment with different texturizing products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium shaggy hairstyles for women over 50:

What face shapes are best suited for medium shaggy hairstyles?

Medium shaggy hairstyles work well for most face shapes, but they are especially flattering for round and square faces.

What hair types are best suited for medium shaggy hairstyles?

Thick hair is perfect for medium shaggy hairstyles, as it can add volume and texture to the hair. However, even if you have thin hair, you can still achieve a shaggy look with the right products and techniques.

How often should I get a trim?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some haircare tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet, as nutrition plays a big role in the health of your hair.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful and youthful medium shaggy hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. Whether you prefer a layered bob, choppy pixie, or messy waves, there is a style out there for everyone. Remember to take care of your hair by getting regular trims and using nourishing haircare products. Happy styling!
