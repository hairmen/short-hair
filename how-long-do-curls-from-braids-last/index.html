How Long Do Curls From Braids Last

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning to get your hair braided but wondering how long the curls will last? Braids are a popular hairstyle that can offer a range of benefits, including protecting your hair from damage, reducing hair breakage, and promoting hair growth. However, one of the most common questions that people have when getting braids is how long the curls will last.

Description

Braids can create beautiful curls that can last for several days or even weeks. The length of time the curls will last depends on several factors, such as the size of the braids, the type of braids, the texture of your hair, and how well you maintain your hair.

How To

The process of getting curls from braids involves braiding your hair while it's wet and then letting it dry. Once the braids are dry, you can take them out and reveal the beautiful curls. Here's how you can do it:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that may be on your hair, leaving it clean and ready for braiding.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small parts, depending on the size of the curls you want. The smaller the sections, the tighter the curls will be.

Step 3: Braid Your Hair

Take each section of hair and braid it while it's still wet. You can use any type of braid that you prefer, such as a two-strand twist, three-strand braid, or cornrow braid. Once you've braided all the sections of your hair, let it dry completely.

Step 4: Take Out The Braids

Once your hair is completely dry, gently take out the braids. You should be left with beautiful curls that will last for several days.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your braids and curls:

  • Use a moisturizing product to keep your hair moisturized while it's in braids.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent frizz and preserve the curls.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as it can damage the curls and cause them to lose their shape.
  • Don't leave your braids in for too long as it can cause hair breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush to avoid damaging the curls.

FAQ

How long do curls from braids last?

The length of time curls from braids last depends on several factors such as the size of the braids, the type of braids used, and the texture of your hair. Generally, curls from braids can last for several days or even weeks if you maintain them properly.

Can I use heat on my hair after braiding it?

Avoid using heat on your hair after braiding it as it can damage the curls and cause them to lose their shape. Instead, use a moisturizing product to keep your hair moisturized and preserve the curls.

How often should I wash my hair when it's in braids?

You should wash your hair at least once a week when it's in braids to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and follow up with a moisturizing product to keep your hair hydrated.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy hair and beautiful curls. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension and damage to your hair.

With proper care, your curls from braids can last for several days or even weeks, giving you a beautiful and low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
