How To Cut Small Girl Hair At Home In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Small Girl Hair at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Small Girl Hair

Description

Cutting your small girl's hair at home can be a daunting task, especially if you don't have any experience. However, it is possible to achieve a great result with some knowledge and practice. In this blog post, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut small girl hair at home in 2023. We will also share some tips and answer common questions about cutting hair. After reading this post, you will feel confident enough to give your small girl a beautiful haircut at home.

How to Cut Small Girl Hair at Home

Cutting small girl hair at home requires some preparation and the right tools. Here is what you will need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Trimming shears (optional)

Once you have gathered all the necessary tools, follow these steps to cut small girl hair at home:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your small girl's hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Section the hair by parting it down the middle and clipping each side.
  3. Take one section and comb it straight down. Make sure it is smooth and free of tangles.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers and determine the length you want to cut. Remember to cut less rather than more, as you can always go back and cut more if needed.
  5. Cut the hair in a straight line, using the scissors. It's important to cut horizontally, rather than vertically, to avoid unevenness.
  6. Repeat steps 3 to 5 for the other section of hair.
  7. Release the clipped sections and comb the hair straight down.
  8. Trim the hair around the ears and the nape of the neck, using trimming shears or scissors.
  9. Check for any unevenness and trim as needed.
  10. Style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Small Girl Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great result when cutting small girl hair at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's better to cut less than to cut too much.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Make sure the hair is free of tangles before cutting.
  • Use a spray bottle to mist the hair with water if it's difficult to comb.
  • Trim the hair around the ears and the nape of the neck carefully, as these areas can be tricky.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting small girl hair at home:

Can you cut small girl hair with regular scissors?

Yes, you can cut small girl hair with regular scissors. However, it's better to use scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair, as they are sharper and more precise.

How often should you cut small girl hair?

It depends on how fast the hair grows and the style you want to achieve. Generally, it's recommended to cut small girl hair every 3 to 4 months to maintain the length and shape.

How do you cut small girl hair in layers?

To cut small girl hair in layers, follow the same steps as for a straight cut, but cut small sections of hair at an angle instead of straight across. This will create layers and add texture to the hair.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting small girl hair at home, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

With these tips and instructions, you can successfully cut small girl hair at home in 2023. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you will achieve a great result.
