How To Style Your Short Pixie Cut A Complete Guide

Are you tired of your long locks and want to try something new? A short pixie cut might just be the answer. Not only is it low maintenance, but it also adds a touch of edginess to your look. In this article, we'll guide you on how to style your short pixie cut with ease.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Your Short Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

How to Style Your Short Pixie Cut

Styling a short pixie cut is simple and easy. Below are some tips that will help you achieve the perfect look.

1. Use the Right Products

The right styling products are essential for any haircut, and a short pixie cut is no exception. Use a lightweight styling cream or gel to add texture and hold to your hair. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is the best way to add volume and texture to a short pixie cut. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots while you blow dry. This will create a fuller, more voluminous look.

3. Add Some Texture

Short haircuts can sometimes look flat and lifeless. To add some texture, use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray on your hair. This will give it a tousled, beachy look.

4. Accessorize Your Hair

If you want to add some flair to your pixie cut, try accessorizing with headbands, clips, or scarves. This will add a touch of personality to your look and make your haircut stand out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to style your short pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair while lifting it at the roots. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Add Texture

Spray a texturizing or sea salt spray on your hair to add some texture and create a tousled look.

Step 5: Accessorize

If you want to add some personality to your look, accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or scarves.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short pixie cut:

1. Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to maintain their shape and style. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Use the Right Products

Use lightweight, non-greasy styling products that won't weigh down your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot styling tools.

4. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I still style my short pixie cut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Curly hair can add volume and texture to a short pixie cut, making it even more stylish.

2. How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

Use a lightweight, non-greasy styling product and avoid using too much product.

3. Will a short pixie cut work on all face shapes?

Yes, a short pixie cut can work on all face shapes. However, you may want to consult with your stylist to determine the best style for your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining the health of your short pixie cut:

1. Moisturize

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Protect Your Scalp

Protect your scalp from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a scalp sunscreen.

3. Avoid Overwashing

Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness.

4. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and strong.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short pixie cut with ease. Remember to use the right products, blow dry your hair, add some texture, and accessorize to create a unique and stylish look.
