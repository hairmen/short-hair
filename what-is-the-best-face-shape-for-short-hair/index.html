What Is The Best Face Shape For Short Hair

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair is a bold and confident look that can accentuate your facial features. However, not all short haircuts are created equal. The right haircut can make your face look more symmetrical, highlight your best features, and even make you look younger. So, what is the best face shape for short hair? In this article, we’ll explore the different face shapes and the best short haircuts for each one.

Different Face Shapes

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for each face shape, let’s identify the different face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangular

Now, let’s look at the best short haircuts for each face shape.

Best Short Haircut for Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you’re lucky because almost any short haircut will look good on you. However, the best short haircut for an oval face is a pixie cut. This cut will accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

Best Short Haircut for Round Face

If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length. The best short haircut for a round face is a layered bob or a lob (long bob). These cuts will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Best Short Haircut for Square Face

If you have a square face, you want to soften your jawline. The best short haircut for a square face is a textured bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs. These cuts will soften your jawline and make your face appear more feminine.

Best Short Haircut for Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance your forehead and jawline. The best short haircut for a heart-shaped face is a bob or a lob with side-swept bangs. These cuts will balance your forehead and jawline and make your face appear more symmetrical.

Best Short Haircut for Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you want to create the illusion of width at your cheekbones. The best short haircut for a diamond-shaped face is a pixie cut or a bob with volume at the crown. These cuts will accentuate your cheekbones and make your face appear wider at the middle.

Best Short Haircut for Triangular Face

If you have a triangular face, you want to balance your jawline. The best short haircut for a triangular face is a pixie cut or a bob with side-swept bangs. These cuts will soften your jawline and make your face appear more symmetrical.

FAQ

1. Can I get a short haircut if I have a long face?

Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have a long face. The best short haircut for a long face is a pixie cut or a bob with volume at the crown. These cuts will create the illusion of width at your cheekbones and balance your face.

2. Can I get a short haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have curly hair. However, you want to make sure that the cut works with your curls. The best short haircut for curly hair is a layered bob or a lob.

3. Can I get a short haircut if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have thin hair. The best short haircut for thin hair is a pixie cut or a bob with layers. These cuts will add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape. You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. You also want to use products that are designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays and pomades. These products will add volume and texture to your hair. Finally, make sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

In conclusion, the best short haircut for your face shape depends on your unique features. By identifying your face shape and choosing the right short haircut, you can accentuate your best features and feel confident in your new look.
