Can You Have Layers With Curly Hair

Written by William Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous and voluminous, it can be challenging to maintain and style, especially when it comes to getting a haircut. One of the most common questions that curly-haired individuals ask is, "Can you have layers with curly hair?" The answer is a resounding yes! In this blog post, we'll go over how to get layers in your curly hair, step-by-step, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to keep your curls looking their best.

  • How to Get Layers in Curly Hair
  • Tips for Layered Curly Hair

How to Get Layers in Curly Hair

Getting layers in your curly hair requires a bit of extra care and attention. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Find a Hair Stylist Who Is Experienced with Curly Hair

Not all stylists are familiar with working with curly hair, so it's essential to find someone who has experience in cutting and styling it. Ask friends or family members with curly hair for recommendations or search for stylists who specialize in curly hair online.

Step 2: Discuss the Type of Layers You Want

There are different types of layers that work well with curly hair, including long layers, short layers, and face-framing layers. Discuss the type of layers you want with your stylist, and they'll recommend the best option based on your hair type, face shape, and style preferences.

Step 3: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Before your appointment, wash and detangle your hair thoroughly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to make it easier to comb through your curls.

Step 4: Let Your Stylist Work Their Magic

Once you're in the stylist's chair, let them work their magic. They'll cut your hair in layers, starting from the bottom and working their way up. They may use a combination of scissors and a razor to create the desired effect.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After your haircut, style your hair as usual. Use a diffuser to dry your curls gently, and apply a curl-enhancing product to define and hold your curls in place.

Tips for Layered Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep your layered curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your layers and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting layers in curly hair:

Can you get layers in short curly hair?

Yes, you can get layers in short curly hair. Short layers can add volume and texture to your curls, while long layers can create a more relaxed, beachy look.

How often should you get a haircut if you have layered curly hair?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 8-12 weeks to maintain the shape of your layers and prevent split ends.

What's the best way to style layered curly hair?

The best way to style layered curly hair is to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer and apply a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. You can also try scrunching your hair with your hands to encourage the curls to form.

Hair Care for Layered Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for layered curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to your ends to keep them moisturized and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can damage your curls and cause breakage.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and preserve your curls while you sleep.

Getting layers in curly hair is a great way to add volume, texture, and dimension to your curls. By following the steps and tips outlined in this blog post, you can achieve a gorgeous, layered look that complements your natural curls. Remember to take good care of your hair by using gentle products, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to keep your curls looking their best.
