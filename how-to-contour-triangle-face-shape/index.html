How To Contour Triangle Face Shape A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you enhance your best features and create a more defined look. However, when it comes to contouring a triangle face shape, it can be a bit tricky. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to contour triangle face shape, step-by-step, and provide some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To Contour Triangle Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

A triangle face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and wider cheekbones and jawline. The goal when contouring a triangle face shape is to create balance and enhance the features of the face. Contouring can help to soften the angles of the jawline and forehead while highlighting the cheekbones.

How To Contour Triangle Face Shape

Before you begin contouring your triangle face shape, you will need to gather the necessary tools. You will need a contouring powder or cream, a highlighter, and a blending brush. It is also important to have good lighting and a mirror that allows you to see your entire face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to contour your triangle face shape:

  1. Start by applying your foundation and concealer as usual.
  2. Use your contouring powder or cream to create shadows on the areas of your face that you want to recede. This includes the temples, the area below the cheekbones, and the jawline. Use a small brush to apply the contouring product in a diagonal line from your ear to the corner of your mouth.
  3. Blend the contouring product using a blending brush. Make sure to blend well to avoid harsh lines.
  4. Apply highlighter to the areas of your face that you want to bring forward. This includes the tops of the cheekbones, the bridge of the nose, and the cupid's bow. Use a small brush to apply the highlighter in a diagonal line from the outer corner of your eye to the top of your cheekbone.
  5. Blend the highlighter using a blending brush.
  6. Set your contouring and highlighting products with a translucent powder.
  7. Finish your makeup look as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect contour for your triangle face shape:

  • Choose a contouring product that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone.
  • Choose a highlighter that is one or two shades lighter than your natural skin tone.
  • Blend well to avoid harsh lines.
  • Use a light hand when applying products to avoid a heavy, cakey look.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your face shape.

FAQs

What other makeup techniques can I use to enhance my triangle face shape?

In addition to contouring, you can use blush to enhance your cheekbones and create a more youthful appearance. Choose a blush shade that complements your skin tone and apply it to the apples of your cheeks.

What hairstyles work best for a triangle face shape?

Hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, such as a pixie cut or a layered bob, can help to balance out a triangle face shape. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate the narrowness of the forehead.

Hair Care

In addition to makeup techniques, taking care of your hair can also help to enhance your triangle face shape. Keep your hair healthy and well-groomed with regular trims and conditioning treatments. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently contour your triangle face shape and enhance your best features. Remember to have fun and experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you!
