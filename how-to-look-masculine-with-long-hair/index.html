How To Look Masculine With Long Hair A Guide For Men

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair is often associated with femininity, but that doesn't mean men can't rock long locks and still look masculine. In fact, long hair can make men look rugged and masculine if styled properly. In this article, we'll show you how to look masculine with long hair and provide tips on how to care for it.

How to Look Masculine with Long Hair

When it comes to looking masculine with long hair, it's all about finding the right style that suits your face shape and personal style. Here are some tips to help you achieve a masculine look with long hair:

1. Keep it neat and tidy

Long hair can easily become unruly and messy, which can detract from a masculine look. Keep your hair neat and tidy by regularly trimming the ends and keeping it clean.

2. Embrace your natural texture

Long hair can look masculine even if it's curly or wavy. Embrace your natural texture and work with it rather than against it. Use products that enhance your curls or waves and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Try a half-up, half-down style

A half-up, half-down style can be a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your length. This style can also be easily dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

4. Go for a slicked-back look

A slicked-back style can be a great way to look masculine with long hair. Use a styling product such as gel, pomade or wax to slick your hair back and keep it in place.

5. Experiment with braids or a man bun

Braids or a man bun can be a great way to keep your hair out of your face while also looking masculine. These styles can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a masculine look with long hair:

Step 1: Find the Right Style

The first step in achieving a masculine look with long hair is to find the right style that suits your face shape and personal style. Experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

Step 2: Keep Your Hair Clean and Healthy

Long hair can easily become dirty and greasy, which can detract from a masculine look. Keep your hair clean and healthy by regularly washing it and using a conditioner to keep it soft and shiny.

Step 3: Invest in the Right Products

Invest in the right products to help you achieve the style you want. This may include styling products such as gel, pomade or wax, as well as products to keep your hair healthy such as a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.

Step 4: Regularly Trim Your Hair

Long hair needs to be regularly trimmed to keep it looking neat and tidy. Visit a hairdresser or barber every 6-8 weeks to have the ends trimmed.

Step 5: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. Try out different braids, knots and buns to find a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Masculine Long Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining masculine long hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Invest in the right products to help you achieve the style you want and keep your hair healthy.

2. Regularly Trim Your Hair

Long hair needs to be regularly trimmed to keep it looking neat and tidy. Visit a hairdresser or barber every 6-8 weeks to have the ends trimmed.

3. Keep Your Hair Clean and Healthy

Long hair can easily become dirty and greasy, which can detract from a masculine look. Keep your hair clean and healthy by regularly washing it and using a conditioner to keep it soft and shiny.

4. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Long hair can look masculine even if it's curly or wavy. Embrace your natural texture and work with it rather than against it.

FAQs

Can I still look masculine with long hair?

Absolutely! Long hair can make men look rugged and masculine if styled properly.

What are some good styles for masculine long hair?

Some good styles for masculine long hair include a slicked-back look, a half-up, half-down style, braids, and a man bun.

How often should I trim my long hair?

Long hair should be trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips for Masculine Long Hair

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a Conditioner

Use a conditioner every time you wash your hair to keep it soft and shiny.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as hair dryers, straighteners and curling irons as much as possible as they can damage your hair.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays.

5. Brush Your Hair Regularly

Brush your hair regularly to distribute natural oils and prevent tangles.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a masculine look with long hair. Embrace your natural texture, find the right style, and keep your hair clean and healthy for a rugged and masculine look.
