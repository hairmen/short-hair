Description

Hair crimping was a popular trend in the 80s and 90s, but is it still in style today? The answer is yes! Hair crimping has made a comeback in recent years and is set to continue as a popular hair trend in 2023. Crimped hair adds texture and volume to any hairstyle, making it perfect for achieving that effortless chic look.

There are various types of hair crimpers available on the market today, from traditional crimpers to flat irons with crimping plates. This means that there is a hair crimper to suit every hair type and style. Hair crimping can be done on both short and long hair, making it a versatile trend that anyone can try.

How to Crimp Your Hair

Before crimping your hair, it's important to wash and dry it thoroughly. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the crimper. Divide your hair into sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Take a small section of hair and clamp the crimper down on the section, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process on each section of hair until your entire head is crimped. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Crimping

1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

3. Divide your hair into sections.

4. Take a small section of hair and clamp the crimper down on the section.

5. Hold the crimper for a few seconds before releasing.

6. Repeat this process on each section of hair.

7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips to Make Your Crimped Hair Look Perfect

1. Use a high-quality hair crimper for best results.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray before crimping your hair to prevent damage.

3. Divide your hair into small sections for a more defined crimp.

4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

5. Experiment with different hairstyles, such as half-up, half-down or a messy bun, to show off your crimped hair.

FAQs About Hair Crimping

Q: How long does crimped hair last?

A: Crimped hair can last up to three days with the right hair care and styling products.

Q: Can I crimp my hair if it's naturally curly?

A: Yes, you can crimp your hair even if it's naturally curly. However, it's important to use a high-quality hair crimper and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Q: Can I crimp my hair if it's short?

A: Yes, hair crimping can be done on both short and long hair.

Hair Care Tips for Crimped Hair

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

4. Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.

5. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

In conclusion, hair crimping is definitely in style in 2023. With the right hair care and styling products, you can achieve the perfect crimped hair look that will last for days. So why not give it a try and add some texture and volume to your hair with this fun and trendy hair trend?
