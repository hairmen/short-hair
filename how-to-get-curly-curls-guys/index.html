How to Get Curly Curls Guys - A Step by Step Guide

Do you want to achieve curly curls as a guy? It can be tough to know where to start, but we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to get those curly locks you've been dreaming of. Here's what we'll cover:

How to Get Curly Curls Guys

Before we get started, it's important to note that not everyone can achieve curly curls. The texture and thickness of your hair play a big role in how curly it can get. That being said, here are some steps you can take to help enhance your natural curls:

1. Use a Curl Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help enhance your curls and keep them looking healthy. Look for products that contain ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil.

2. Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

After washing your hair, apply a curl enhancing product like a mousse or gel. This will help define your curls and keep them looking bouncy. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to scrunch it in as you go.

3. Use a Diffuser When Blow Drying

If you need to blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment. This will help distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz. Keep the dryer on a low heat setting and use a low speed.

4. Don't Touch Your Hair While It's Drying

While your hair is drying, avoid touching it as much as possible. This can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. Once your hair is completely dry, you can gently scrunch it to help define your curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to achieving curly curls:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl enhancing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

2. Apply Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product like a mousse or gel to your hair. Make sure to apply it evenly, scrunching it in as you go.

3. Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Keep the dryer on a low heat setting and use a low speed. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible while it's drying.

4. Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is completely dry, you can gently scrunch it to help define your curls. Use your fingers to separate any clumps and give your curls more volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly curls:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons as much as possible.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Can I get curly curls if I have straight hair?

It's unlikely that you'll be able to achieve curly curls if you have straight hair. However, you can try using a curling iron or getting a perm to add some texture to your hair.

What's the best way to prevent frizz?

Use a curl enhancing product and a diffuser when blow drying your hair. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible while it's drying and sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

By following these steps and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve curly curls as a guy. Remember, everyone's hair is different, so it may take some trial and error to find the products and techniques that work best for you. Good luck!
