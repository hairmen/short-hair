Short Hairstyles for Oblong Faces over 50

If you have an oblong face shape and are over 50, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But, don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll go through some of the best short hairstyles for oblong faces over 50 that are both stylish and easy to manage.

Description

Oblong faces are longer than they are wide, with a narrow forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. As we age, our skin loses elasticity, and our hair thins out, making it even more important to find a hairstyle that flatters our face shape and works with our hair texture.

How to

Short hairstyles are a great option for oblong faces over 50 because they help to balance out your features and make your face appear wider. Here are some tips for finding the right short hairstyle for your face shape:

  • Look for hairstyles with volume at the sides to widen your face
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or long, as they will only elongate your face further
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or layers to add texture and movement to your hair
  • Choose a style that works with your natural hair texture to make styling easier

Step-by-Step

Here are some short hairstyles to try if you have an oblong face over 50:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for oblong faces over 50. It's easy to maintain and can be worn with or without bangs. To style, apply a small amount of styling cream to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush for volume.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great short hairstyle for oblong faces over 50. Choose a chin-length bob with layers to add volume and movement to your hair. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush and finish with a texturizing spray.

3. Shag Cut

A shag cut is a layered haircut that works well for oblong faces over 50. It adds volume and texture to your hair and can be worn with or without bangs. To style, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow-dry with a diffuser.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for oblong faces over 50:

What is the best short hairstyle for oblong faces over 50?

The best short hairstyle for oblong faces over 50 is one that adds volume and width to your face. Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and shag cuts are all great options to consider.

Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle if I have an oblong face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a short hairstyle if you have an oblong face. Consider a side-swept fringe or wispy bangs to add softness to your face.

How often should I get a trim with a short hairstyle?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and hairstyles, you'll be able to find the perfect short hairstyle for your oblong face over 50. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny!
