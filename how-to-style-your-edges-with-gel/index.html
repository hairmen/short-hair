How To Style Your Edges With Gel A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your edges not staying in place? Do you want to achieve that sleek, polished look? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on how to style your edges with gel, the easy way.

  • How to Style Your Edges with Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Your Edges with Gel

Styling your edges with gel is a quick and easy way to achieve a sleek, polished look. To get started, you will need a few materials:

  • Edge control gel
  • Toothbrush or edge brush
  • Water spray bottle
  • Hair tie or headband

The first step is to ensure that your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet, the gel may not hold as well, and you may not achieve the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by tying your hair or using a headband to keep it out of your face. Then, use a water spray bottle to lightly mist your edges.

Step 2: Apply the Gel

Squeeze a small amount of edge control gel onto your toothbrush or edge brush. Begin to apply the gel to your edges, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Brush Your Edges

Use your toothbrush or edge brush to brush your edges in the desired direction. Be sure to brush gently to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Edges

Use your fingers to smooth out any flyaways or frizz. If necessary, use bobby pins to secure any loose hairs in place.

Tips to Achieve the Best Results

  • Use a toothbrush or edge brush to apply the gel for better precision.
  • Avoid using too much gel, as this can cause buildup and weigh down your hair.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, use a lighter gel or pomade to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Experiment with different brushes and combs to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use any type of gel?

It is recommended to use a gel specifically designed for styling your edges, as these are formulated to provide a stronger hold and are less likely to cause buildup.

How often should I style my edges with gel?

It is best to limit the use of gel on your edges to avoid causing damage to your hair. Try to limit styling to once or twice a week, and be sure to wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup.

Edges Hair Care Tips

  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your edges, such as tight braids or ponytails.
  • Use a satin or silk bonnet or pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz while you sleep.
  • Apply a small amount of oil or leave-in conditioner to your edges to keep them moisturized and healthy.
  • Be gentle when styling your hair, and avoid pulling or twisting your edges excessively.

With these tips, you can achieve the perfect, polished look for your edges. Remember to be gentle with your hair, and don't use too much product to avoid buildup and damage. Happy styling!
