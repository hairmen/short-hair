How To Wear A Scarf In Your Short Hair

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Scarves are versatile accessories that can add color and personality to any outfit, and they're not just for long hair. Short hair can also be accessorized with scarves, and there are many ways to wear them that will look great. In this post, we'll show you how to wear a scarf in your short hair with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

How to Wear a Scarf in Your Short Hair

Wearing a scarf in short hair is easy and can be done in many ways. Here are some of the most popular ways:

Bandana Style

The bandana style is a classic way to wear a scarf in short hair. Simply fold the scarf into a triangle and tie it at the back of your head, leaving the point of the triangle facing downwards. You can adjust the knot to be tighter or looser depending on your preference.

Headband Style

The headband style is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while adding some color to your outfit. Fold the scarf into a long strip and tie it at the top of your head, leaving the ends hanging down. You can also tie the scarf at the back of your head for a different look.

Ponytail Style

If you have a short ponytail, you can add a scarf to it for a pop of color. Tie the scarf around the base of the ponytail and let the ends hang down. You can also tie the scarf in a bow or knot for a different look.

Braid Style

If you have short braids, you can add a scarf to them for a bohemian look. Simply tie the scarf around the base of the braid and let the ends hang down. You can also wrap the scarf around the entire braid for a different look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to wear a scarf in your short hair:

  1. Choose a scarf that matches your outfit or adds a pop of color.
  2. Decide on the style you want to wear (bandana, headband, ponytail, or braid).
  3. Fold the scarf into the desired shape (triangle for bandana, long strip for headband, etc.).
  4. Tie the scarf in place using a knot or bow.
  5. Adjust the scarf to your liking (tighter or looser, higher or lower).

Tips for Wearing a Scarf in Short Hair

Here are some tips to make sure your scarf looks great in your short hair:

  • Use a scarf that matches your outfit or adds a pop of color.
  • Choose a scarf that is the right size for your hair (not too big or too small).
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that looks best on you.
  • Try different knots or bows to add variety to your look.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to keep the scarf in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a scarf in short hair:

Can I wear a scarf in my pixie cut?

Yes, you can wear a scarf in a pixie cut. Try tying the scarf around the back of your head or using it as a headband.

What type of scarf should I use?

You can use any type of scarf that you like, but lightweight scarves are best for short hair.

Can I wear a scarf with curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a scarf with curly hair. Try tying the scarf around the base of a ponytail or using it as a headband.

Hair Care for Short Hair with Scarves

Here are some tips for caring for your short hair when wearing a scarf:

  • Don't tie the scarf too tightly to avoid pulling on your hair.
  • Use bobby pins or hair clips to keep the scarf in place without damaging your hair.
  • Avoid wearing a scarf in wet or damp hair to prevent breakage.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup from the scarf.

Wearing a scarf in short hair is a fun and easy way to add some personality to your look. Try out different styles and knots to find the one that looks best on you, and don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and patterns. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a scarf in your short hair in no time!
