How To Make Man Bun With Short Hair Step By Step Guide In 2023

Written by Robby Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair but still want to sport the trendy man bun, you're in luck. Contrary to popular belief, it is absolutely possible to make a man bun with short hair. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of making a man bun in easy-to-follow steps. You'll also find tips, FAQs and hair care advice to help you rock your man bun with confidence. So, let's get started!

How to Make Man Bun with Short Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to understand that making a man bun with short hair requires some length. Ideally, your hair should be at least 4-5 inches long on the top. If your hair is shorter than that, you may want to consider growing it out a bit before attempting a man bun.

Now, let's get to the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair back and away from your face. Use a comb or brush to create a smooth, even part down the center of your head.

Step 2: Gather your hair at the back of your head, just above the nape of your neck. Use a hair elastic to tie your hair in a ponytail. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 3: Twist the ponytail into a tight coil, wrapping it around the base of the elastic. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Step 4: Use a hair tie or rubber band to secure any loose strands or flyaways. You can also use hairspray or hair wax to keep your bun in place.

That's it! You've successfully made a man bun with short hair. Now, let's take a look at some tips to make the process easier.

Tips for Making Man Bun with Short Hair

Tip 1: Use a hair tie or rubber band that matches your hair color. This will help to camouflage the tie and make your bun look more natural.

Tip 2: If you have very short hair, you may want to use bobby pins to secure your bun instead of a hair tie. Pins can be easier to hide and can create a more compact bun.

Tip 3: Experiment with different bun styles. You can create a top knot, a low bun, or even a half-up half-down style.

FAQs

Q: Can I make a man bun with very short hair?

A: While it is possible to make a man bun with short hair, it does require some length. If your hair is very short, you may want to consider growing it out a bit first.

Q: Do I need to use hair wax or hairspray to keep my bun in place?

A: It's not necessary to use products, but they can help to keep your bun in place and prevent flyaways.

Q: Can I wear a man bun to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear a man bun to a formal event. Just make sure your bun is neat, tidy, and well-groomed.

Hair Care Tips for Man Buns

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.

Tip 2: Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair before attempting to make a bun. This will make the process easier and prevent hair breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid using heat tools like hair dryers or straighteners too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

With these tips and steps, you're ready to rock a man bun with short hair. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be discouraged if your first attempt isn't perfect. Keep trying and experimenting until you find the perfect man bun style for you!
