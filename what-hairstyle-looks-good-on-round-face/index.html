What Hairstyle Looks Good On Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't suit your round face? Well, worry no more because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on what hairstyle looks good on round face. We'll also answer some of the frequently asked questions about round face hairstyles and provide you with some hair care tips. So, let's get started.

Description

A round face has soft curves and full cheeks, and it's often proportional in length and width. Choosing the right hairstyle is essential to balance out the roundness of your face and create an illusion of length. The ideal hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height at the crown and elongates the face.

How To Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

There are several factors to consider when choosing the right hairstyle for a round face:

1. Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to determine your face shape. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the width of your cheekbones is roughly equal to the width of your forehead and jawline, then you have a round face shape.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a significant role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have thin hair, go for a layered cut to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, go for a blunt cut to tame the volume.

3. Hair Length

The length of your hair can also affect the way your face looks. Short haircuts can make your face look wider, while long hair can elongate your face.

Step-by-Step Guide on What Hairstyle Looks Good on Round Face

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller. It also elongates your face by creating an illusion of length.

2. Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can balance out the roundness of your face by creating an angle. It also adds height at the crown, making your face look longer.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can add height at the crown, making your face look longer. It also highlights your cheekbones and jawline.

4. Bob Cut

A bob cut can elongate your face by creating an angle. It also adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller.

Tips for Round Face Hairstyles

1. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin as it can make your face look wider.

2. Opt for hairstyles with layers and angles to create an illusion of length.

3. Haircuts with volume and texture can add dimension to your hair and make it look fuller.

4. Avoid center parts as it can make your face look rounder.

FAQs

1. Can I wear a middle part with a round face?

Avoid center parts as it can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a side part.

2. Can I wear a short haircut with a round face?

Yes, you can wear a short haircut with a round face. Just make sure to go for a haircut that adds height at the crown and texture to your hair.

3. What are some hair care tips for round face hairstyles?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Hairstyles

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh your hair down.

3. Use a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair while blow-drying.

4. Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as it can damage your hair.

5. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and hair length when choosing a hairstyle. Don't forget to follow our hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
