How To Cut My Own Short Hair At The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Dec 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time at the hair salon for a simple trim? Learning how to cut your own short hair at the back can save you both. In this article, we’ll go through a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a perfect cut, tips, and hair care to keep your hair looking great.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. But with the right tools and technique, it's possible to achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we’ll focus on how to cut the back of your short hair.

How to Cut My Own Short Hair at the Back

The first step in cutting your own hair at the back is to gather the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps for a perfect cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Comb your hair straight down the middle of the back of your head.
  3. Using the handheld mirror, check the back of your head to make sure your hair is even.
  4. Using your scissors, cut your hair straight across the middle of your head.
  5. Next, comb your hair to one side and trim any uneven pieces.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Check the back of your head again with the mirror to make sure everything looks even.
  8. Trim any pieces that are too long or uneven.
  9. Finish by lightly trimming the hair around your ears and neckline.

Congratulations, you've just cut your own hair at the back!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great cut:

  • Go slow and steady. It's better to take off a little at a time than to cut off too much.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a clean cut.
  • Keep your hair damp while cutting. This will help the hair lay flat and make it easier to see any uneven pieces.
  • Don’t forget to clean up. After you’re done cutting, sweep up any hair on the floor and wipe down your scissors.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

  • Can I cut my hair at home without any experience?
  • Yes, you can! Cutting your hair at home can be a great way to save money and time, but it’s important to start with small trims and work your way up.
  • What if I make a mistake?
  • If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always go to a salon and have a professional fix it.
  • How often should I cut my hair?
  • It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After you’ve cut your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

In conclusion, cutting your own short hair at the back is possible with the right tools and technique. Remember to take it slow, use sharp scissors, and follow our step-by-step guide. With a little practice and patience, you’ll be able to achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home.
