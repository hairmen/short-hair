How Low Is A 1 Haircut

Description

A 1 haircut is a very short haircut that is typically achieved with a clipper guard that is set to 1/8th of an inch or 3mm. This haircut is also known as a "number 1" or "one guard" and is one of the shortest haircuts available. It is often used by men who want a low-maintenance haircut or who prefer a more masculine look.

How to

To get a 1 haircut, you will need to use clippers with a 1 guard. This guard will only leave 1/8th of an inch or 3mm of hair on your head, so it is important to be very careful when cutting your hair with this guard. Here are the steps to follow:

Step by step

  1. Start with clean, dry hair that is free of tangles.
  2. Attach the 1 guard to your clippers.
  3. Turn on the clippers and start at the base of your neck, moving the clippers upward.
  4. Continue moving the clippers upward, against the direction of hair growth.
  5. Be careful around the ears and the hairline, as these areas are more sensitive.
  6. Make sure to go over your entire head, checking for any stray hairs.
  7. Once you are finished, use a comb to check for any uneven spots or missed hairs.
  8. Trim any stray hairs with scissors if necessary.
  9. Clean your clippers and comb.
  10. Apply some hair oil or moisturizer to your scalp and hair to keep it healthy.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a 1 haircut:

  • Make sure to use a sharp pair of clippers to avoid tugging on your hair.
  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a 1 guard if you are not sure how short you want to go.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with the back of your head if you cannot see it well.
  • Be careful not to cut yourself with the clippers or scissors.
  • Consider using a mirror to check for any missed spots.

FAQ

Q: Will a 1 haircut look good on me?

A: This depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. It is best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if a 1 haircut is right for you.

Q: How often should I get a 1 haircut?

A: This depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Some people get a 1 haircut every 2-3 weeks, while others can go up to a month between haircuts.

Q: Can I style my hair after getting a 1 haircut?

A: Yes, you can use hair gel or pomade to style your hair after getting a 1 haircut. However, keep in mind that a 1 haircut is very short, so there may not be much hair to style.

Hair Care

After getting a 1 haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons as much as possible.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Overall, a 1 haircut is a very short haircut that is easy to maintain and gives a masculine look. It is important to be careful when cutting your hair with a 1 guard and to take care of your hair after getting the cut. With the right tools, technique, and care, you can rock a 1 haircut with confidence.
