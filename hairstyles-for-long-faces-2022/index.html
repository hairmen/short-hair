Hairstyles For Long Faces 2022 Create The Perfect Look

Written by Oliver Jul 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle to complement your long face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the top hairstyles for long faces in 2022. From sleek and straight to bouncy curls, we have got you covered. Read on to create the perfect look for your long face shape.

Description

Long faces are characterized by a more extended forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The perfect hairstyle for long faces should add width and volume to the sides, creating the illusion of a more oval face shape. The right hairstyle can balance your features and bring out your best features. The key is to find a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for Long Faces

Choosing the right hairstyle is crucial for long faces. Here are some tips to consider when selecting the perfect hairstyle for your face shape:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle will work best for you. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or a layered pixie cut can add volume and dimension to your hair. If you have thick hair, consider a long layered hairstyle to soften your features.

2. Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, determine your face shape. Long faces often have high foreheads, narrow cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The right hairstyle can balance these features and create a more oval face shape.

3. Look for Hairstyles that Add Width to the Sides

Hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face can create the illusion of a more oval face shape. Consider hairstyles with volume on the sides, like wavy or curly hair, to add fullness to your face.

4. Avoid Hairstyles that Lengthen the Face

Avoid hairstyles that add length to your face, like long straight hair or a sleek ponytail. These hairstyles can make your face appear even longer.

Step-by-Step Guide for Hairstyles for Long Faces 2022

Now that you have a better understanding of what to look for in a hairstyle for long faces let's dive into the top hairstyles for long faces in 2022.

1. Long Bob

The long bob or "lob" is a versatile hairstyle that works well for long faces. The lob adds volume to the sides of your face while framing your face shape. To create a lob, cut your hair to shoulder length and add layers to create movement and dimension.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for long faces. The bangs add width to your forehead and balance your features. To create side-swept bangs, part your hair to the side and sweep your bangs across your forehead.

3. Soft Waves

Soft waves are a classic hairstyle that works well for long faces. The waves add volume to the sides of your face, creating the illusion of a more oval face shape. To create soft waves, use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create loose waves in your hair.

4. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can add volume and dimension to your hair, making it an excellent choice for long faces. The layers add fullness to the sides of your face, balancing your features. To create a layered pixie cut, ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair, focusing on the sides of your face.

Tips for Styling Hairstyles for Long Faces 2022

Now that you have chosen the perfect hairstyle for your long face, here are some tips for styling and maintaining your hair:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a significant difference in the look and feel of your hair. Consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness to your hair. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can add volume and dimension to your hair. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume. Blow-dry your hair in sections, focusing on the sides to add width to your face.

3. Use Heat Protectant

Using heat protectant is essential to maintaining healthy hair. Heat protectant can prevent damage from heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners. Apply heat protectant to your hair before using any heat styling tools.

FAQ: Hairstyles for Long Faces 2022

1. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that add length to your face, like long straight hair or a sleek ponytail. These hairstyles can make your face appear even longer. Instead, choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

2. Can I wear a bob if I have a long face?

A bob can work well for long faces if it is cut to shoulder length and has layers to add volume and movement to your hair.

3. How often should I cut my hair?

The frequency of haircuts depends on your hair type and the style you are trying to achieve. It is recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care for Long Faces

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieving the perfect hairstyle for your long face. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help prevent buildup and keep your hair healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Comb your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up to prevent tangles.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF. The sun can damage your hair and lead to dryness and breakage.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect hairstyle for long faces can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can create the perfect look for your face shape. Consider your hair texture, face shape, and the style that flatters your features. Use the right hair products and styling tools to maintain healthy hair. Now go out and rock your new hairstyle!
