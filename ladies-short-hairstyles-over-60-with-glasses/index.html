Ladies Short Hairstyles Over 60 With Glasses A Complete Guide

If you are a woman over 60 with glasses, you might think that your hairstyle options are limited. But that's not true! There are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and compliment your glasses. In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for ladies over 60 with glasses, along with some tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to choose the perfect short hairstyle
  • Step-by-step guide to styling your hair
  • Tips for maintaining your short hairstyle
  • FAQs

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for older women as they are low maintenance and can make you look younger. If you wear glasses, you need a hairstyle that will not interfere with your frames. A short hairstyle that frames your face and highlights your best features can help you look and feel confident.

How to choose the perfect short hairstyle

The first thing to consider when choosing a short hairstyle is your face shape. There are different short hairstyles that can flatter different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob with side-swept bangs can make your face look slimmer. If you have a square face, a layered pixie cut can soften your features.

You should also consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have thin hair, a short layered cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob can help you manage your hair better.

Another factor to consider is your glasses. If you have bold, statement glasses, you might want to choose a more subtle hairstyle. If you have rimless glasses, you can experiment with bolder hairstyles.

Step-by-step guide to styling your hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a hair serum or oil to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a straightener or curling iron to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips for maintaining your short hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair fresh.

FAQs

Q. Can I dye my hair if I wear glasses?

A. Yes, you can dye your hair as long as you choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and glasses.

Q. Can I wear bangs if I wear glasses?

A. Yes, you can wear bangs with glasses. Side-swept bangs can help to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. You should wash your hair every 2-3 days or as needed depending on your hair type.

Hair care for ladies over 60

As you age, your hair can become thinner and drier. Here are some hair care tips for ladies over 60:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using hair supplements to boost hair growth and thickness.

With the right short hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel confident at any age. We hope this guide has helped you choose the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape and glasses. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
