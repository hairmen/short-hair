Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Long Faces

Written by Aprilia Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly searching for the perfect hairstyle that flatters your long face shape? Look no further than shoulder length hairstyles! Not only do they add volume and texture to your locks, but they also balance out the length of your face. In this article, we'll go over some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for long faces, as well as provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Hairstyle for Your Long Face

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a long face, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to your face, such as:

  • Layered haircuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves
  • Bouncy curls
  • Choppy bob

These hairstyles will help balance out the length of your face and create a more flattering look.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair for Long Faces

Styling shoulder length hair can be quick and easy with the right tools and products. Here's a step by step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to protect against heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair.
  5. Finish off with a texturizing spray or hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember to always protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair for Long Faces

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is key to maintaining a flattering hairstyle. Here are some tips for maintaining shoulder length hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to protect and enhance your hair.

With these tips, you can keep your shoulder length hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Frequently Asked Questions About Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Long Faces

Q: Will a layered haircut work for my long face?

A: Yes, a layered haircut is a great option for long faces. It adds volume and texture to your hair while balancing out the length of your face.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long face. Just be sure to add volume and texture to your hair to balance out the length of your face.

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care Products for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some hair care products that are perfect for shoulder length hair:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron or straightener
  • Texturizing spray or hairspray

Investing in high-quality hair care products can make all the difference in the health and appearance of your hair.

Conclusion

Shoulder length hairstyles are a great option for those with long faces. They add volume and texture to your hair while balancing out the length of your face. With the right tools and products, styling and maintaining your hair can be quick and easy. Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and to invest in high-quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
