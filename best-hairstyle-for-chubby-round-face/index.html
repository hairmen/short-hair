Best Hairstyles For Chubby Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face shape with chubby cheeks, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this ultimate guide, we'll be sharing the best hairstyles for a chubby round face. From short to long hair, we've got something for everyone.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a round chin and full cheeks. It's a cute and youthful face shape, but finding the right hairstyle can be tricky. The goal is to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Chubby Round Face

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about how to choose the best one for you. First, consider your hair texture and thickness. Thick hair can handle more volume, while thin hair may require a more layered cut to add movement. Second, think about your face shape and features. The goal is to create balance and symmetry. Lastly, consider your personal style and lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that you feel confident and comfortable in.

Best Hairstyles for Chubby Round Face

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are great for chubby round faces because they create volume on top and elongate the neck. Here are some of the best short hairstyles:

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Asymmetrical Cut

Medium Haircuts

Medium haircuts are also great for round faces. They add length to the face and create a slimming effect. Here are some of the best medium hairstyles:

  • Shoulder Length Cut
  • Layered Cut
  • Side-Swept Bangs

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can also work for round faces, but it's important to add layers and texture to avoid a boxy look. Here are some of the best long hairstyles:

  • Layered Cut
  • Beach Waves
  • Side Part

Step by Step: How to Style Your Hair for a Chubby Round Face

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas, let's talk about how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots for added lift.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create movement.
  4. If you have a short haircut, use a texturizing spray to add definition and texture.
  5. If you have a medium or long haircut, curl your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a chubby round face?

The best haircut for a chubby round face is one that adds height and volume on top and creates length on the sides. Short haircuts like the pixie, bob, and asymmetrical cuts work well, as do medium and long haircuts with layers and texture.

Can I have bangs with a chubby round face?

Yes, you can have bangs with a chubby round face. Side-swept bangs are a great option because they create a diagonal line that elongates the face. Avoid straight bangs, as they can make the face look wider.

Should I avoid certain hairstyles with a chubby round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make the face look wider. Also, avoid hairstyles with too much volume on the sides, as they can make the face look rounder.

Hair Care for Chubby Round Face

When it comes to hair care for a chubby round face, it's important to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas and tips for maintaining your hair, go out there and rock your chubby round face with confidence!
