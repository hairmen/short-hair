How To Style Your Short Hair For A Wedding Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Sep 25, 2023 · 5 min read

When it comes to attending a wedding, figuring out how to style your hair can be just as important as picking out the perfect outfit. If you have short hair, you might be wondering what your options are for creating a chic and elegant look. Luckily, there are plenty of ways to style short hair for a wedding that will have you looking and feeling your best. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for styling short hair for a wedding.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair for a Wedding
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Style Short Hair for a Wedding

There are many different ways you can style short hair for a wedding, depending on the length and texture of your hair, as well as your personal style. Here are a few ideas to get you started:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great choice for a wedding, especially if you want to show off your face and neck. You can add some volume and texture to your hair by using a styling product like mousse or texturizing spray, and you can even add some accessories like a headband or hair clip to dress it up.

2. Bob Cut

If you have a bob cut, you can create a sleek and sophisticated look by straightening your hair with a flat iron and adding a deep side part. You can also add some curls or waves to your hair for a more romantic look. A hair accessory like a flower or jeweled hairpin can add a touch of elegance.

3. Braided Styles

Even if your hair is short, you can still create a braided style that looks beautiful and elegant. A braided crown or half-up style can be a great choice for a wedding, and you can even add in some hair extensions or a hairpiece to create a more elaborate look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for a wedding:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture. Use a hair dryer with a concentrator nozzle to direct the airflow and create a smooth finish.

3. Style Your Hair

Choose a styling product that works well for your hair type, such as mousse or texturizing spray. Apply the product to your hair and style it using a flat iron, curling iron, or other styling tool. Use a hairpin or headband to add some extra style.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Once you’re happy with your hairstyle, finish it off with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place. Choose a hairspray that provides a flexible hold so that your hair still looks natural and soft.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling short hair for a wedding:

1. Experiment with Accessories

Adding a hair accessory like a headband, flower, or jeweled hairpin can make a big difference in how your hairstyle looks. Try out a few different accessories to see what works best with your hair and dress.

2. Consider Hair Extensions

If you want to create a more elaborate hairstyle, you can use hair extensions or a hairpiece to add length and volume to your hair. Make sure to choose a quality product that matches your hair color and texture.

3. Get a Trim Before the Wedding

To ensure that your hair looks its best on the day of the wedding, schedule a trim with your hairstylist a few weeks beforehand. This will help to get rid of any split ends and ensure that your hair is healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl my short hair for a wedding?

A: Absolutely! You can use a curling iron or hot rollers to add some curls or waves to your hair for a more romantic look. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and to not overdo it with the heat.

Q: How can I make my short hair look more formal?

A: Adding a hair accessory like a headband or hairpin can make your short hair look more formal and elegant. You can also create a sleek and sophisticated look by straightening your hair with a flat iron and adding a deep side part.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days with a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using hot water and harsh products that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a Heat Protectant Spray

If you use hot tools like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your short hair looking its best, get a trim every six to eight weeks to get rid of any split ends and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks for styling short hair for a wedding, you’ll be able to create a chic and elegant look that will have you feeling confident and beautiful on the big day.
