Can I Get My Hair Permanently Wavy

Written by Bardi Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog where we will be discussing the topic, "Can I Get My Hair Permanently Wavy?" If you're someone who desires wavy hair but doesn't want to use heat tools every day, then this post is for you. We'll be covering everything from how to get permanent waves to hair care tips for maintaining your new look. So, let's dive in!

Description

Permanent waves, also known as perms, are a type of chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair to create waves or curls. The process involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the protein bonds, allowing the hair to be reshaped. The hair is then wrapped around rods of various sizes to create the desired wave pattern. The solution is then neutralized, and the rods are removed, leaving you with beautiful, wavy hair.

How to Get Permanent Waves

Getting permanent waves is a process that should only be done by a licensed professional. It's important to find a stylist who is experienced in perms and can assess your hair's health and texture to determine the best course of action. Here are the steps involved in getting permanent waves:

Step 1: Consultation

Your stylist will start by consulting with you to determine the type of wave you want and if your hair is healthy enough for the process. They'll also evaluate your hair's texture and current condition to ensure it can handle the chemicals used in the perm.

Step 2: Preparation

Your hair will be washed, and a protective cream will be applied to your scalp to prevent any irritation. Your hair will be sectioned and wrapped around perm rods of varying sizes, depending on the type of wave you want to achieve.

Step 3: The Perm Solution

Your stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair, which will break down the protein bonds. The solution will be left on your hair for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair's texture and condition.

Step 4: Neutralizing

Once the perm solution has been applied, your stylist will rinse it out and apply a neutralizer. The neutralizer helps to re-form the protein bonds, locking in the new wave pattern.

Step 5: Conditioning

After the neutralizer has been rinsed out, your stylist will apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair. This will help to restore any moisture lost during the perm process.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Now that you have your beautiful new waves, it's important to take care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your new waves.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hair products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make your waves frizzy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting permanent waves:

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It's recommended that you wait at least 2 weeks before coloring your hair after getting a perm to avoid damaging your hair.

Can I wash my hair after getting a perm?

You should wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after getting a perm to allow the new wave pattern to set.

Hair Care

Getting a perm is a great way to achieve beautiful, wavy hair without having to use heat tools every day. However, it's important to find a licensed professional and take care of your new waves to ensure they last as long as possible. By following our tips and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy your new look for months to come.
