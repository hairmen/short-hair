Best Haircut For Someone With A Round Face

Written by Eveline Aug 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for someone with a round face, along with tips on how to style and care for your new look.

Description

When it comes to finding the best haircut for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and angles. A round face typically has a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. To balance out these features, it's important to choose a hairstyle that elongates the face and adds definition to the cheekbones and jawline.

How to

The best haircut for someone with a round face is a layered cut with long, face-framing layers. This style adds movement and texture to the hair while slimming down the face. The layers should start at the chin or below and be cut in a way that creates angles around the cheekbones and jawline.

Another great option for a round face is a long bob, or "lob," with side-swept bangs. This style adds length to the face while the bangs create a diagonal line that slims down the forehead. The lob should be cut to just above or below the collarbone and the bangs should be cut at an angle to follow the line of the cheekbones.

Step by Step

1. Start by consulting with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.

2. Choose a layered cut with long, face-framing layers or a long bob with side-swept bangs.

3. During the haircut, make sure the layers are cut in a way that creates angles around the cheekbones and jawline.

4. Style your new haircut by blow-drying with a round brush to create volume and movement.

5. Finish with a texturizing spray or serum for added definition.

Tips

- Avoid blunt cuts or heavy, straight-across bangs as they can make the face appear rounder.

- Opt for side-swept bangs or a diagonal fringe that follows the line of the cheekbones.

- Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add lift and definition to the hair.

FAQ

What other hairstyles are good for a round face?

In addition to the layered cut and long bob with side-swept bangs, other hairstyles that are good for a round face include a pixie cut with volume at the crown, a shoulder-length cut with layers that start at the jawline, and a high ponytail with volume at the crown.

How can I maintain my new haircut?

To maintain your new haircut, schedule regular trims with your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks. Use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools and avoid over-washing the hair to prevent dryness and damage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and applying a leave-in conditioner or oil to the ends of the hair to prevent split ends.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance your natural features and feel confident in your new look. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
