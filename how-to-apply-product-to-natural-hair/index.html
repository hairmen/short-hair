How To Apply Product To Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have natural hair, you know that finding the right products and applying them correctly can be a challenge. With so many different products on the market, it can be overwhelming to figure out what works best for your hair type and texture. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to apply product to natural hair, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you achieve healthy, beautiful hair.

  • Step 1: Start with clean hair
  • Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner
  • Step 3: Use a styling product
  • Step 4: Define your curls
  • Step 5: Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser
  • Tips for Applying Product to Natural Hair
  • FAQs

Step 1: Start with clean hair

The first step in applying product to natural hair is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo, and then condition it with a moisturizing conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly to make sure there is no product residue left behind.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Once your hair is clean, apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize and detangle your hair. Apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends where your hair is the driest. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

Step 3: Use a styling product

After applying the leave-in conditioner, it's time to use a styling product. There are many different types of styling products to choose from, including creams, gels, and mousses. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and texture. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to coat each strand.

Step 4: Define your curls

If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to define your curls using a curl defining product. Apply the curl defining product to your hair, making sure to scrunch your hair gently to encourage your curls to form.

Step 5: Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser

Once you've applied your products, it's time to let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair. If you're air drying your hair, make sure to avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz. If you're using a diffuser, use it on a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Tips for Applying Product to Natural Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when applying product to your natural hair:

  • Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture
  • Apply products evenly throughout your hair to ensure that each strand is coated
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently
  • Don't touch your hair too much when it's drying to avoid frizz
  • Use a diffuser on a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair

FAQs

Q: How often should I apply product to my natural hair?

A: It's best to apply product to your hair as needed. If your hair feels dry, apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize it. If you're styling your hair, use a styling product to help hold your style in place.

Q: What type of products should I use on my natural hair?

A: It's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. Look for products that are moisturizing and free of harsh chemicals that can dry out your hair.

Q: Can I apply product to my hair when it's wet?

A: Yes, it's best to apply product to your hair when it's wet. This will help the product to distribute evenly throughout your hair.

Hair Care for Natural Hair

In addition to applying product to your natural hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve healthy, beautiful natural hair.
