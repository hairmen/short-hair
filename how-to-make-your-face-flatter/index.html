How To Make Your Face Flatter Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may want to make it appear flatter in shape. There are a few things you can do to achieve this look, and we have put together a guide to help you. In this post, we will discuss the best ways to make your face flatter, including tips, tricks, and hair care.

Description

A round face is characterized by softer, fuller cheeks and a wider forehead. While round faces can be cute and youthful, some people may want to achieve a flatter appearance. This can be done through various makeup techniques, hairstyles, and even facial exercises. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve a flatter, more defined look.

How to Make Your Face Flatter: Step by Step

1. Use Contouring Makeup

One of the easiest ways to make your face appear flatter is by using contouring makeup. This technique involves using dark and light shades of makeup to create the illusion of depth and shadows on your face. Use a contouring powder or cream that is a few shades darker than your skin tone to create a shadow under your cheekbones, along your jawline, and on the sides of your forehead. Use a highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bones, and the tip of your nose. Blend well to create a seamless, natural look.

2. Try a Layered Haircut

The right haircut can also help make your face appear flatter. A layered haircut can add volume and movement to your hair, which can balance out the roundness of your face. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your jawline and angle downwards. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs that can make your face look even rounder.

3. Use Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and create a more defined look. Try exercises such as puckering your lips and holding for 10 seconds, then releasing. Repeat 10 times. You can also try smiling as wide as you can and holding for 10 seconds, then relaxing. Repeat 10 times. These exercises can help target the muscles around your mouth and cheeks, which can help create a flatter appearance.

Tips for Making Your Face Flatter

Here are a few more tips to help you achieve a flatter face:

  • Wear your hair off your face to make it appear less full
  • Avoid wearing hats or headbands that add volume to the top of your head
  • Use a matte finish foundation to avoid adding shine and dimension to your face
  • Avoid overly round or circular glasses frames
  • Use a darker shade of lipstick to create a more defined lip shape

FAQ

Q: Can I change the shape of my face permanently?

A: No, you cannot change the shape of your face permanently without surgery. However, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and exercises to create the illusion of a flatter face.

Q: Can I use bronzer instead of contouring powder?

A: Yes, you can use bronzer to contour your face. Just make sure to choose a shade that is a few shades darker than your skin tone and has a matte finish.

Q: How often should I do facial exercises?

A: You can do facial exercises as often as you like, but aim for at least three times a week to see results.

Hair Care for a Flatter Face

Your hair can also play a role in making your face appear flatter. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using heavy conditioners that can weigh down your hair and make it look fuller
  • Use a volumizing shampoo to add body and movement to your hair
  • Avoid using too much product, which can make your hair appear greasy and limp
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots

Conclusion

If you want to make your face appear flatter, there are a few things you can do. Use contouring makeup, try a layered haircut, and do facial exercises to tone your muscles. Remember to wear your hair off your face and avoid adding volume to the top of your head. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a flatter, more defined look.
