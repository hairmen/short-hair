Description

A round face is characterized by its fullness and lack of angular features. Glasses can also add width to the face, making it appear even rounder. The right hairstyle can help balance out the roundness and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

When choosing a hairstyle for round faces and glasses, it's important to consider the shape of your face and the type of glasses you wear. The best hairstyles will complement your features and frame your face in a flattering way.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Round Face and Glasses

When choosing a hairstyle for round faces and glasses, there are a few key factors to consider:

Face Shape

Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown, creating the illusion of a more elongated face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs or blunt cuts that end at the jawline.

Glasses Style

The style of your glasses can also influence your hairstyle. If you wear large, bold frames, you may want to opt for a simpler hairstyle that doesn't compete with your glasses. On the other hand, if you wear small, delicate frames, you can afford to be more adventurous with your hairstyle.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also affect the way your hairstyle looks. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or voluminous, as they can weigh your hair down. If you have thick hair, on the other hand, you may want to opt for layered hairstyles that add movement and texture.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your round face and glasses, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair before styling it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply styling products.

If you have fine hair, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots to add height and volume. If you have thick hair, apply a smoothing serum or cream to tame any frizz or flyaways.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair.

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the crown to add height. If you have bangs, blow-dry them to the side or forward, rather than straight down.

Step 4: Style your hair.

Depending on your hairstyle, you may need to use a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tool to create the desired look. Follow the instructions that come with your tool, and be careful not to overheat your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray.

Apply a light mist of hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for round faces and glasses?

The best hairstyles for round faces and glasses are those that add height and volume at the crown, creating the illusion of a more elongated face shape. Examples include layered cuts, long bobs, and pixie cuts with volume at the crown.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for round faces and glasses?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs or blunt cuts that end at the jawline. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or voluminous if you have fine hair.

Can I wear bangs with glasses?

Yes, you can wear bangs with glasses. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and glasses. Side-swept bangs or long, layered bangs are usually the most flattering.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces and Glasses

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and styling it properly, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for round faces and glasses:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces and Glasses

Short hairstyles can be flattering for round faces and glasses, as long as they add height and volume at the crown. Here are some examples:

  • Pixie cut with volume at the crown
  • Layered bob
  • Side-swept bangs with a short bob

Medium Hairstyles for Round Faces and Glasses

Medium hairstyles are also a great option for round faces and glasses. Here are some examples:

  • Long bob with layers
  • Shoulder-length cut with side-swept bangs
  • Wavy lob

Long Hairstyles for Round Faces and Glasses

Long hairstyles can be tricky for round faces and glasses, but it's still possible to find a flattering look. Here are some examples:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Half-up, half-down style with volume at the crown
  • Loose waves with a center part

Conclusion

Choosing the best hairstyle for round faces and glasses can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find a look that flatters your features and frames your face in a flattering way. Remember to consider your face shape, glasses style, and hair texture when choosing a hairstyle, and follow our tips for styling and hair care to keep your hair looking its best.
