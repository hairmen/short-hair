What Length Is A No 3 Haircut

Written by Aprilia Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are thinking of getting a haircut and want to know what length is a No 3 haircut, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will discuss what a No 3 haircut is and how you can achieve this style. We will also provide tips on how to maintain your hair after the haircut.

Description

A No 3 haircut refers to the length of the hair that is left on the scalp after the haircut. It is a buzz cut that leaves about 3/8 inch or 9.5mm of hair on the head. This length is perfect for those who want a short and low-maintenance hairstyle. It is also a popular choice for men who want to show off their facial hair, as it provides a clean and polished look.

How to Achieve a No 3 Haircut

If you want to achieve a No 3 haircut, you can either do it yourself or go to a professional barber. To do it yourself, you will need a clipper with a No 3 guard or attachment. Start at the bottom of the head and work your way up, making sure to go over each section of hair several times to ensure an even cut. For best results, use a mirror to check the back of your head.

If you prefer to go to a barber, simply ask for a No 3 haircut. They will use clippers with a No 3 guard to achieve the desired length. Be sure to communicate clearly with your barber about the style you want and any specific instructions you may have.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to achieve a No 3 haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Dry your hair thoroughly with a towel.
  3. Attach the No 3 guard to your clipper.
  4. Start at the bottom of your head and work your way up, making sure to go over each section of hair several times to ensure an even cut.
  5. Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  6. Trim the hair around your ears and at the back of your neck with scissors or a trimmer.
  7. Wash your hair again to remove any loose hairs.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best result with your No 3 haircut:

  • Use a quality clipper with sharp blades to ensure a smooth cut.
  • Trim your hair in a well-lit area to avoid missing any spots.
  • Take your time and go over each section of hair several times to ensure an even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Consider using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy after the cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about No 3 haircuts:

Is a No 3 haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a No 3 haircut is suitable for all hair types.

How often should I get a No 3 haircut?

If you want to maintain the length, you should get a No 3 haircut every 2-4 weeks.

Can I style my hair after a No 3 haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair after a No 3 haircut. However, keep in mind that the shorter length may limit your styling options.

Hair Care after No 3 Haircut

After getting a No 3 haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

Now that you know what a No 3 haircut is and how to achieve it, you can confidently go to the barber or do it yourself at home. Remember to follow the tips and take care of your hair after the cut to maintain its health and appearance.
