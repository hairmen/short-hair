How To Style Bangs With Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Are you tired of struggling to style your bangs with wavy hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Wavy hair can be tricky to style, especially when it comes to bangs. But with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect look. In this article, we'll show you how to style bangs with wavy hair, step by step. We'll also share some tips and tricks to help you maintain your style and keep your hair healthy. Let's get started!

How to Style Bangs with Wavy Hair

The first step to styling bangs with wavy hair is to determine the type of bangs you want. Do you want blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, or something in between? Once you've decided on your style, it's time to start styling.

The key to styling bangs with wavy hair is to embrace your natural texture. Use products that enhance your waves and add volume to your hair. Avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy.

Products You'll Need

  • Texturizing spray
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron or wand
  • Hair pins
  • Hairspray

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your bangs with wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair, focusing on the roots. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create volume.
  3. Use a curling iron or wand to curl your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the barrel of the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  4. Take a section of your bangs and twist it away from your face. Pin it back with a hair pin. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Using your fingers, loosen the curls and create a natural, tousled look.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your bangs with wavy hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools. Too much heat can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Invest in a good quality curling iron or wand. Look for one with adjustable heat settings and a ceramic or tourmaline barrel.
  • Use hair pins to pin back your bangs when they start to get too long or become unruly.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling bangs with wavy hair:

Q: Can I straighten my bangs if I have wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your bangs if you have wavy hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. If you have wavy hair, you may be able to go longer between washes as your natural oils can help keep your hair moisturized. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. You can also use hair masks and treatments to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your bangs with wavy hair like a pro. Remember to embrace your natural texture and use products that enhance your waves. With a little practice, you'll have the perfect look in no time.
