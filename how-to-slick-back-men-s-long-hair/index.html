How To Slick Back Men s Long Hair The Ultimate Guide

Long hair has become more and more popular for men in recent years, and with that comes the desire to try out new hairstyles. One of the most classic and stylish ways to wear long hair is to slick it back. In this guide, we'll show you how to slick back men's long hair in a few easy steps.

  • How to Slick Back Men's Long Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Make it Look Great
  • FAQ

How to Slick Back Men's Long Hair

Slicking back long hair is a relatively easy hairstyle to achieve, but it does require some preparation and product. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair spray (optional)

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to slick back your long hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it on a low heat setting until it's completely dry.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your fingertips and rub them together to warm up the product.
  3. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  4. Using a comb, slick your hair straight back from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  5. If you want a more polished look, use a hair spray to set the style in place.

Tips to Make it Look Great

To achieve the perfect slick back hairstyle, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade that won't flake or leave residue in your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have a receding hairline or a widow's peak, consider leaving some hair at the front to cover it up.
  • If you have a cowlick or unruly hair at the crown of your head, use a bit more product to tame it down.
  • If you have a square or round face, consider leaving some hair at the sides to soften your features.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back men's long hair:

Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

Yes, but you may need to use more product and a stronger hold hairspray to keep it in place.

How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to prevent product buildup and keep your hair and scalp healthy.

Can I use wax instead of gel or pomade?

Yes, but wax can be harder to distribute evenly and may not provide as much hold as a gel or pomade.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

While slicking back your hair can look great, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and hair loss over time.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to slick back your long hair like a pro. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
