Does Curly Hair Suit Oval Faces

Written by Daniel Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful and unique hair type that can add a lot of personality and style to your overall look. However, if you have an oval face shape, you may be wondering if curly hair is a good choice for you. In this article, we will explore whether or not curly hair suits oval faces, as well as provide some tips and tricks for styling and caring for your curly locks.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair for Oval Faces

Description

Oval face shapes are considered to be one of the most versatile face shapes, as they can pull off a variety of different hairstyles with ease. With a slightly longer face shape and a gentle curve at the jawline, oval faces can benefit from hairstyles that add width and volume to the sides of the face.

Curly hair, on the other hand, can be quite voluminous and tends to add width to the hair overall. This can be a great thing for oval faces, as it can help balance out the face shape and create a more harmonious and flattering look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few simple steps you can take to find out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape you've drawn.

Generally speaking, if your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently curved jawline, you likely have an oval face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face shape and curly hair, here are some tips for styling your hair to flatter your face:

  1. Opt for haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of your face, such as layers.
  2. Avoid haircuts that are too long, as this can elongate the face and make it appear even longer.
  3. Use curl-enhancing products to define your curls and add volume.
  4. Avoid heavy oils and creams that can weigh down your curls and make them look limp.
  5. Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your curls without creating frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care and attention to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful curls:

  1. Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness and frizz.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help moisturize and protect your curls.
  4. Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  5. Consider sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Can oval faces pull off short curly hair?

Yes, oval faces can look great with short curly haircuts. Just be sure to choose a haircut that adds volume and width to the sides of your face.

Do bangs suit oval faces with curly hair?

Bangs can look great on oval faces with curly hair, as long as they are cut to flatter your face shape. Consider opting for side-swept bangs or wispy, textured bangs.

Does coloring curly hair suit oval faces?

Yes, coloring curly hair can be a great way to add dimension and texture to your locks. Just be sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and overall style.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care and attention to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some additional tips to help you care for your curly locks:

  1. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  2. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  3. Apply a weekly deep conditioning treatment to help keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  4. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  5. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel to dry it, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel or t-shirt.

In conclusion, if you have an oval face shape, curly hair can be a great choice for you. Just be sure to choose a haircut and styling routine that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. With a bit of extra care and attention, you can keep your curly locks looking healthy, bouncy, and beautiful for years to come.
