How To Slick Back Hair With Volume

Written by Eveline Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to slick back hair with volume. Slicking back your hair can be a great way to look polished and put together, but it can also leave your hair looking flat and lifeless. Adding volume to your slicked back hairstyle is a great way to give it some personality and make it more interesting. Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve a sleek and voluminous look.

Description

When it comes to slicking back your hair, the goal is to create a smooth and shiny finish. However, if you don't add any volume, your hair may end up looking flat and lifeless. Adding volume to your slicked back hair can give it some personality and make it more interesting. The key to creating volume is to use the right products and techniques.

How to Slick Back Hair with Volume

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help plump up your hair and create a good base for your slicked back style.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or foam. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots.

3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to lift the roots and create volume. Direct the nozzle of the blow dryer downwards to smooth out the hair.

4. Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your fingertips and work it through your hair. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working towards the back of your head.

5. If you want even more volume, you can tease your hair at the crown. Use a teasing comb to backcomb your hair, working in sections. Once you have achieved the desired volume, smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section.

Step by Step Guide to Slicking Back Hair with Volume

Step 1: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your roots.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to lift the roots and create volume.

Step 4: Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your fingertips and work it through your hair.

Step 5: Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working towards the back of your head.

Step 6: Tease your hair at the crown if you want even more volume.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair with Volume

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create a good base for your style.

- Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your roots before blow drying.

- Use a round brush to lift the roots and create volume while blow drying.

- Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your fingertips and work it through your hair for a sleek finish.

- Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working towards the back of your head.

FAQ

Q: Can I slick back my hair without using any product?

A: While it is possible to slick back your hair without using any product, it may be difficult to achieve a sleek and polished finish. Using a small amount of pomade or gel can help to smooth out any flyaways and give your hair a more polished look.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer instead of a blow dryer?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer instead of a blow dryer. However, a blow dryer is generally more powerful and can help to create more volume.

Hair Care Tips

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause it to become dry and brittle.

- Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools on your hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a sleek and voluminous slicked back hairstyle. Remember to use the right products and techniques to create volume and give your hair some personality. With a little practice, you'll be able to master this stylish look and rock it with confidence.
