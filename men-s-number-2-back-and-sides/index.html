Men s Number 2 Back And Sides A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Oliver Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut, then the men's number 2 back and sides is the perfect choice for you. This haircut has been popular for decades and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve the perfect men's number 2 back and sides haircut step by step.

Description

The men's number 2 back and sides haircut is a short, buzzed cut on the back and sides of the head, with longer hair on top. The number 2 refers to the length of the hair, which is approximately 1/4 inch or 6mm. This haircut is perfect for men who want to look sharp and clean-cut without spending too much time on styling.

How to Achieve the Men's Number 2 Back and Sides Haircut

Step 1: Choose a Good Barber

The first step to achieving the perfect men's number 2 back and sides haircut is to choose a good barber. Look for a barber who has experience with this haircut and can give you the look you want. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or you can do some research online to find a reputable barber in your area.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before going to the barber, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly. This will help remove any dirt or oil from your hair, making it easier for the barber to work with. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Barber

When you get to the barber, make sure to communicate clearly what you want. Show them a picture of the men's number 2 back and sides haircut you want, and explain any specific details you want. This will ensure that you get the exact look you're going for.

Step 4: Cut the Back and Sides

The barber will start by cutting the back and sides of your hair using clippers. They will use a number 2 guard to achieve the desired length. Make sure to sit still and relax while the barber is cutting your hair.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once the back and sides are cut, the barber will blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths. The barber may use scissors or clippers to achieve the desired effect.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair as desired. You can use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold to your hair. Alternatively, you can leave it natural for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Men's Number 2 Back and Sides Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's number 2 back and sides haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.

FAQ

What face shapes suit men's number 2 back and sides?

Men's number 2 back and sides haircut suits most face shapes. However, it looks best on men with oval, square or heart-shaped faces.

Can men with curly hair get the men's number 2 back and sides haircut?

Yes, men with curly hair can get the men's number 2 back and sides haircut. However, the barber may need to use scissors instead of clippers to achieve the desired length.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your men's number 2 back and sides haircut looking its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your men's number 2 back and sides haircut looking sharp and stylish for weeks to come.
