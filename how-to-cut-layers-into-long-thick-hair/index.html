How To Cut Layers Into Long Thick Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long, thick hair can be beautiful, but it can also be heavy and difficult to manage. One way to add movement and texture to long, thick hair is by adding layers. In this article, we will show you how to cut layers into long, thick hair in a relaxed language.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Layering is a popular technique used to add volume and shape to long, thick hair. By cutting different lengths of hair, layers can create movement and texture throughout the hair. Additionally, layers can help to frame the face and accentuate your best features.

How To

Before you start cutting layers into long, thick hair, it is essential to have the right tools. You will need sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and clips to section off the hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair.

2. Section off the hair into manageable sections using clips.

3. Decide on the length of the layers you want to create.

4. Take a small section of hair from the first section you have clipped off.

5. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across.

6. Repeat this process, working your way through the hair. Remember to cut each section to the same length to ensure even layers.

7. Use your comb to check that the layers are even and blend together nicely.

8. Once you have finished cutting all the layers, style your hair as desired.

Tips

1. Start by cutting a small amount of hair. You can always cut more if you need to.

2. Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

3. Take your time and work through the hair methodically.

4. Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my hair when I have layers?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of the layers.

Q: Can I cut layers into my hair if it is curly?

A: Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to have your hair cut when it is dry so that you can see the shape of the curls.

Hair Care

After cutting layers into your long, thick hair, it is essential to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and apply a leave-in conditioner or serum to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Additionally, try to avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.

Cutting layers into long, thick hair can be a great way to add movement and texture to your hair. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and stylish look that suits your personality and lifestyle.
