How To Get Loose Curls Without Heat For Short Hair

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that getting loose curls is impossible without using heat. However, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to achieve those beautiful curls without damaging your hair. In this article, we will share with you how to get loose curls without heat for short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Loose Curls Without Heat for Short Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Get Loose Curls Without Heat for Short Hair

Before we dive into the steps, it’s important to note that this technique works best on clean, damp hair. Here’s how to get loose curls without heat for short hair:

1. Apply Mousse or Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product or mousse to your hair. This will help your hair hold the curls better. Apply it evenly throughout your hair, from roots to tips.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl. Use hair clips to secure each section.

3. Twist Each Section

Take each section and twist it tightly. The tighter you twist it, the tighter the curl. Keep twisting until you reach the end of the section.

4. Pin the Twisted Section

Take a bobby pin and pin the twisted section to your head. Make sure the pin is secure and won’t come loose. Repeat this process for each section.

5. Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry. This can take several hours, so be patient. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the process.

6. Remove the Bobby Pins

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the bobby pins. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get loose curls without heat for short hair:

Step 1:

Apply a curl enhancing product or mousse to your hair.

Step 2:

Section your hair into small sections.

Step 3:

Take each section and twist it tightly.

Step 4:

Pin the twisted section to your head.

Step 5:

Let your hair air dry.

Step 6:

Remove the bobby pins and gently separate the curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before you start.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner before you apply the curl enhancing product to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to avoid them being visible.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, you can skip the curl enhancing product and just twist and pin your hair.

FAQ

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Q: Can I use this technique on long hair?

A: Yes, you can use this technique on long hair. However, it might take longer to dry and you might need more bobby pins.

Hair Care

Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get loose curls without heat for short hair, you can rock those beautiful curls anytime you want! Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
