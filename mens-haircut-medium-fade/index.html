Mens Haircut Medium Fade A Guide For Relaxed Style In 2023

Written by Bardi Oct 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a medium fade might be the perfect choice for you. With its versatility and easy upkeep, the mens haircut medium fade has become increasingly popular in recent years. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the medium fade, including how to get it, style it, and care for it.

Description of Mens Haircut Medium Fade

The medium fade is a type of haircut that involves gradually tapering the hair from the top to the sides and back of the head. Unlike a high or low fade, the medium fade offers a more relaxed and natural look. The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from a messy, textured look to a slicked-back pompadour. The medium fade can also be combined with other haircuts, such as a crew cut or side part, to create a unique and personalized style.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Medium Fade

If you're interested in getting a medium fade, it's important to find a skilled barber who can create the look you want. When you go to the barber, be sure to bring a picture of the style you're interested in. This will give your barber a clear idea of what you're looking for and ensure that you're both on the same page.

During the haircut, your barber will use clippers to gradually taper the hair on the sides and back of your head. The length of the fade can vary, depending on your preference. For a more natural look, you can opt for a longer fade, while a shorter fade will create a more dramatic contrast between the top and sides.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Mens Haircut Medium Fade

Once you've got your medium fade haircut, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or oil buildup and leave your hair clean and healthy.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Depending on your hair type and preference, you can use a pomade, wax, gel, or cream. Work the product evenly through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way outwards.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Using a comb, brush, or your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume. For a slicked-back style, use a comb to smooth your hair back and create a polished finish.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure that your style lasts all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to lock your hair in place without making it feel stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Maintaining a Mens Haircut Medium Fade

To keep your medium fade looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips to follow:

  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups
  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat from styling tools, as this can damage the hair

Frequently Asked Questions about Mens Haircut Medium Fade

Q: How long does a medium fade last?

A: The length of a medium fade can vary depending on your preference, but it typically lasts for 2-4 weeks before needing a touch-up.

Q: Can a medium fade work for any hair type?

A: Yes, a medium fade can work for any hair type, from straight to curly. However, the styling process may differ depending on your hair texture.

Q: Is a medium fade easy to maintain?

A: Yes, a medium fade is easy to maintain with regular visits to your barber and simple styling techniques.

Hair Care Products for Mens Haircut Medium Fade

To keep your medium fade looking its best, here are some hair care products to consider:

  • Shampoo and conditioner: Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Styling product: Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type and desired style.
  • Hairspray: Use a light mist of hairspray to finish your style and keep it in place all day.
  • Wide-tooth comb: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

With this guide, you now have all the information you need to rock a stylish and low-maintenance mens haircut medium fade. Whether you're looking for a relaxed and natural style or a polished and professional look, the medium fade is a versatile and timeless choice. Take care of your hair with the right products and styling techniques, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
