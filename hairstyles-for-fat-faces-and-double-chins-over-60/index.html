Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 07, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are over 60 with a round face and a double chin, finding the perfect hairstyle can be daunting. However, there are plenty of flattering hairstyles that can enhance your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we’ll provide tips, step-by-step instructions, and FAQs on choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and double chin.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Mature Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

The key to finding the right hairstyle for a round face and double chin is to look for styles that elongate the face and balance the features. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle with layers that fall below your chin to elongate your face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin as they will accentuate your double chin.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe to draw the eye towards your eyes and cheekbones.
  • Try a bob with layers that start at your chin and angle downwards to create a slimming effect.

Best Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins Over 60

Here are some of the best hairstyles for fat faces and double chins over 60:

  • The Long Bob: Also known as the lob, this hairstyle is perfect for round faces and double chins as it elongates the face and neck. Add layers to give the style more volume and texture.
  • The Shag: This versatile style is perfect for older women with a round face and double chin as it adds volume and texture to the hair. Ask your stylist to add layers and choppy ends to create a modern look.
  • The Pixie Cut: This short and chic hairstyle is perfect for women over 60 with a round face and double chin. Ask your stylist to add layers and texture to create a soft and feminine look.
  • The Shoulder-Length Cut: This flattering hairstyle works well for round faces and double chins as it elongates the face and neck. Ask your stylist to add layers and texture to create a modern look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating the perfect hairstyle to flatter your round face and double chin:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for mature hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots to add volume and texture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, pulling the hair upwards and outwards to create volume.
  4. If you have a long bob or shoulder-length cut, use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling your hair to flatter your round face and double chin:

  • Use a straightening iron to create sleek and straight hair that elongates the face.
  • Avoid wearing your hair in a high ponytail or top knot as it will accentuate your double chin.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing spray to add texture and fullness.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for fat faces and double chins over 60:

Q: Can I wear my hair short if I have a round face and double chin?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair short if you have a round face and double chin. However, it’s important to choose a style that balances your features and elongates your face. The pixie cut and the long bob are great options.

Q: Is it better to have curly or straight hair if I have a round face and double chin?

A: It’s a matter of personal preference, but both curly and straight hair can look great on a round face and double chin. However, it’s important to choose a style that elongates the face and balances the features.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a round face and double chin?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every other day if you have a round face and double chin. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Mature Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your mature hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for mature hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair mask to hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle to flatter your round face and double chin over 60. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
