How To Cut Layers In Short Hair Yourself

Written by Petter Jun 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to change up your short hair, adding layers can give it a whole new look. While getting your hair cut by a professional is always a good idea, you can also cut layers in short hair yourself. Here's how:

Description

Layered hair is a popular hairstyle that can add volume and texture to short hair. It involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create layers that fall in a natural-looking way. Cutting layers in short hair yourself can be a cost-effective and convenient way to achieve this style at home.

How to Cut Layers in Short Hair Yourself

To cut layers in short hair yourself, you'll need the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips

Follow these steps:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. This will help you see where the natural layers fall.
  2. Section your hair. Use clips to separate your hair into several sections.
  3. Start with the bottom layer. Take a small section of hair from the bottom layer and comb it straight. Cut the hair at a slight angle, making sure to cut less than you think you need to.
  4. Move to the next layer. Take a section of hair from the next layer and use the cut hair as a guide for the length. Cut the hair at the same angle as before, again cutting less than you think you need to.
  5. Continue cutting layers. Repeat the process for each layer until you've cut all the layers you want.
  6. Blend the layers. Once you've cut all the layers, blend them together by combing through your hair and trimming any sections that look uneven.

Remember, it's better to cut less than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much.
  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors.
  • Don't cut straight across - angle the scissors for a more natural look.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring!

FAQ

Can I cut layers in my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut layers in curly hair. It's important to cut curly hair when it's dry so you can see how the curls fall naturally.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional to fix it, or you can try to even out the layers yourself by trimming the longer sections.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. The average person should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and cutting layers in short hair yourself, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look at home.
