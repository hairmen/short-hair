Is Short Or Long Hair Better For A Fat Face

Written by Aprilia Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you may have wondered whether short or long hair is better for your facial features. The truth is, both can work well, depending on the style and cut. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of short and long hair for fat faces and give you some tips on how to choose the best one for you.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Best Hair Length for a Fat Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Description

The shape of your face can greatly influence the way your hair looks. If you have a round or chubby face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features and make your face look slimmer. Short hair can make your face look wider, while long hair can elongate your face. However, there are exceptions to these rules, and it ultimately depends on the style and cut that suits your face shape and personal preferences.

How to Choose the Best Hair Length for a Fat Face

Before choosing a hairstyle, it is important to determine your face shape. If you have a round or chubby face, you can try the following tips:

  • Long hair with layers: Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, making your face look slimmer.
  • Long bob: A long bob or lob can also elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.
  • Side-swept bangs: Bangs can help to frame your face and create an illusion of a slimmer face. Side-swept bangs are great for fat faces as they create an asymmetrical look that can make your face look longer and narrower.
  • Short pixie cut: A short pixie cut can also work well for fat faces, especially if you have a small forehead. It can draw attention away from your cheeks and create a more balanced look.
  • Shoulder-length hair: Shoulder-length hair can also work well for fat faces, especially if you have a long neck. It can create a slimming effect and balance out your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when choosing the best hair length for a fat face:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture, thickness, and color.
  3. Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist who can recommend a style that suits your face shape and hair type.
  5. Try different styles and cuts to see what works best for you.

Tips for Styling Short and Long Hair for Fat Faces

Once you have chosen a hairstyle, here are some tips for styling short and long hair for fat faces:

  • Blow-dry your hair: Blow-drying your hair can create volume and texture, making your face look slimmer.
  • Use a round brush: A round brush can help to create a more polished and sleek look.
  • Use hair products: Use hair products like mousse, hairspray, or gel to add volume and hold.
  • Avoid heavy bangs: Heavy bangs can make your face look wider, so it is best to avoid them if you have a round face.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles like ponytails or buns can make your face look wider, so it is best to avoid them if you have a round face.

FAQs

Q: Is short or long hair better for a fat face?

A: Both can work well, depending on the style and cut. Long hair can elongate your face, while short hair can create a more balanced look.

Q: Can bangs work for fat faces?

A: Yes, bangs can work for fat faces, but it is best to choose side-swept bangs that create an asymmetrical look and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Hair Care for Fat Faces

Regardless of your hair length and style, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent oil buildup and scalp irritation.
  • Use a conditioner: Use a conditioner to hydrate your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair, so it is best to avoid it as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet: Eating a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals can promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, whether you have short or long hair, there are plenty of ways to style it to flatter your facial features. By following these tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your fat face and feel confident in your appearance.
