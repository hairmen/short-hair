How To Grow An Undercut Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to grow an undercut male! If you're looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle, the undercut might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been popular among men for years, and it's not going away anytime soon. But growing an undercut male can be tricky, especially if you're not sure where to start. That's why we've put together this step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect undercut. Let's get started!

Description of an Undercut Male

An undercut male is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of the head is shaved or cut very short, while the hair on top is left longer. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back. The undercut can be styled in many different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit your personal style.

How to Grow an Undercut Male

Growing an undercut male requires patience and commitment. If you're starting with longer hair, you'll need to gradually cut the sides and back shorter over time. If you're starting with shorter hair, you may need to wait a few months for the top to grow out. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber

When it comes to getting an undercut male, it's important to choose a barber who is experienced with this style. They will know how to create the right shape and texture for your hair. Look for barbers who specialize in men's haircuts or who have experience with undercuts.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting your hair, decide on the length you want for the top and the sides. Do you want a short or long undercut? Do you want a fade or a sharp contrast between the top and sides? These are all things to consider before you start cutting.

Step 3: Grow Out the Top

If you're starting with shorter hair, you'll need to wait a few months for the top to grow out. Be patient and avoid cutting the top until it's the length you want. You can use hair products to style the top in the meantime.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Once the top is the right length, it's time to start cutting the sides and back. Your barber will use clippers or scissors to create the desired length and shape. They may also use a razor to create a clean line around the edges.

Step 5: Style Your Undercut

Once your haircut is complete, it's time to style your undercut. You can use hair products like pomade, wax, or gel to create the desired look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Growing an Undercut Male

Here are some tips to keep in mind when growing an undercut male:

  • Be patient and don't rush the process
  • Choose a barber who is experienced with undercuts
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best
  • Use hair products to create texture and hold
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims and touch-ups

FAQ About Undercut Males

Q: How long does it take to grow an undercut male?

A: It can take several months to grow out the top of an undercut male, depending on how long you want it to be. The sides and back can be cut shorter in the meantime.

Q: How often should I get my undercut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your undercut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can I still get an undercut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, an undercut can work with curly hair. Your barber can adjust the length and shape to suit your hair texture.

Hair Care for Undercut Males

To keep your undercut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often
  • Use hair products that are designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to grow an undercut male that looks stylish and trendy. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. Good luck!
