Is Curly Hair Good For Dreads

Written by Daniel Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to dreadlocks, many people assume that only straight hair can be used to create them. However, this is far from the truth. In fact, curly hair can be perfect for dreads, and they can turn out just as amazing as dreads made from straight hair.

Description

Dreadlocks have been around for centuries, and they are a popular hairstyle among people of all ages and cultures. They are created by allowing hair to mat and tangle together, forming natural locks. Dreads can be made from all types of hair, including curly hair.

How to Create Dreads with Curly Hair

If you have curly hair and want to create dreadlocks, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before starting the dreadlocking process, make sure your hair is clean and free from any products. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and make your hair easier to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small, equal-sized sections. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair and the size of the dreads you want to create.

Step 3: Backcomb Your Hair

Take each section of hair and backcomb it using a comb. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. This will create knots and tangles that will eventually form into dreads.

Step 4: Roll Your Hair

Once your hair is backcombed, use your hands to roll each section of hair back and forth. This will help the knots and tangles to lock together and form dreads.

Step 5: Maintain Your Dreads

After your dreads are formed, it is important to maintain them. This includes washing them regularly and using a residue-free shampoo. You can also use a crochet hook to tighten any loose strands and keep your dreads looking neat and tidy.

Tips for Creating Dreads with Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating dreads with curly hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup before starting the dreadlocking process.
  • Section your hair evenly to create dreads that are similar in size.
  • Backcomb your hair gently to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Roll your hair gently to avoid breaking or damaging your curls.
  • Use a residue-free shampoo to avoid buildup in your dreads.
  • Regularly maintain your dreads to keep them looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Can Curly Hair Be Used to Create Dreads?

Yes, curly hair can be used to create dreads. In fact, curly hair can create some of the most unique and beautiful dreads.

Will Creating Dreads Damage My Curly Hair?

Creating dreads can be a bit harsh on curly hair, but if done correctly, it should not cause any permanent damage. It is important to be gentle when backcombing and rolling your hair to avoid damaging your curls.

How Do I Maintain My Dreads?

To maintain your dreads, you should wash them regularly with a residue-free shampoo. You can also use a crochet hook to tighten any loose strands and keep your dreads looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Dreads

When it comes to caring for your curly hair dreads, it is important to use products that are gentle and formulated for dreadlocks. Use a residue-free shampoo to avoid buildup in your dreads, and use a light oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.

Conclusion

Curly hair can be perfect for creating dreadlocks. With the right technique and maintenance, your curly hair dreads can turn out just as amazing as dreads made from straight hair. Follow these tips and steps, and enjoy your beautiful new hairstyle!
