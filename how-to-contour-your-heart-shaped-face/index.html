How To Contour Your Heart Shaped Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know that it can be a bit of a challenge to contour. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create a perfectly sculpted look that enhances your natural features. In this article, we'll show you how to contour your heart shaped face like a pro, step-by-step. Let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This face shape can be tricky to contour because you don't want to make your forehead look wider or your chin look even narrower. The key is to create balance by sculpting your cheekbones and jawline while keeping the focus on your eyes and lips.

How to Contour Your Heart Shaped Face

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the products you'll need to contour your heart shaped face. You'll need a contour powder or cream that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone, a highlighter that's one or two shades lighter than your skin tone, and a blush that complements your skin tone.

It's also important to choose the right brushes for the job. You'll need a small, angled brush for contouring, a fluffy brush for blending, and a small tapered brush for highlighting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep your skin
Start by prepping your skin with moisturizer and primer. This will help your makeup go on smoothly and last longer.

Step 2: Apply foundation
Apply your foundation as usual, making sure to blend it well into your skin.

Step 3: Contour your cheekbones
Using your small, angled brush, apply your contour powder or cream just below your cheekbones. Start at your ear and blend towards the corner of your mouth. Be sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Contour your jawline
Apply your contour powder or cream along your jawline, starting at your ear and blending towards your chin. Again, be sure to blend well.

Step 5: Highlight your cheekbones
Using your small tapered brush, apply your highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones. This will create a subtle glow and enhance your cheekbones.

Step 6: Highlight your nose
Apply your highlighter down the center of your nose to create the illusion of a slimmer nose.

Step 7: Apply blush
Using your fluffy brush, apply your blush to the apples of your cheeks. Be sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 8: Finish with setting spray
Finish your look with a setting spray to keep your makeup in place all day.

Tips for Flawless Contouring

  • Choose a contour shade that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone to avoid looking too dark or orange.
  • Blend, blend, blend! Blending is key to creating a natural, sculpted look.
  • Use a light hand when applying product to avoid looking too heavy.
  • Apply your blush after your contour and highlight to avoid muddying the colors.

FAQs

Q: Can contouring make my forehead look wider?
A: Yes, if you apply your contour too high on your forehead. Be sure to keep your contour below your temples to avoid widening your forehead.

Q: Can I use a bronzer instead of a contour powder?
A: It's best to use a product specifically designed for contouring, as bronzers can be too warm and orange for this purpose.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

If you have a heart shaped face, you'll want to balance your wider forehead with fuller hair at the sides. Try adding layers or volume to the sides of your hair to create the illusion of a wider face. Avoid adding volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead look even wider.

With these contouring tips and hair care suggestions, you'll be able to enhance your heart shaped face and create a stunning look that's uniquely you!
