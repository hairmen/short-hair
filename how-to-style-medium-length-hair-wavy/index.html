How To Style Medium Length Hair Wavy

Written by Bardi Oct 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Medium length hair is the perfect length for those who want to experiment with different hairstyles. Wavy hair is a popular choice because it adds volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to style medium length hair wavy.

Description

Styling medium length hair wavy is a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. All you need is a curling iron or a flat iron, some hair products, and a little bit of patience.

How to Style Medium Length Hair Wavy

Follow these steps to style your medium length hair wavy:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help create volume and texture in your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Before using any hot tools on your hair, it's important to apply a heat protectant. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged from the heat.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to curl or straighten your hair.

Step 4: Curl or straighten your hair

Using a curling iron or a flat iron, curl or straighten your hair in small sections. Make sure to curl or straighten your hair away from your face for a more flattering look.

Step 5: Apply a texturizing spray

Once you have curled or straightened your hair, apply a texturizing spray to add volume and texture. This will also help hold the waves in place.

Step 6: Scrunch your hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance the waves and add more texture.

Step 7: Set your hair with hairspray

Finish off your look by setting your hair with a light hairspray. This will help hold the waves in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium length hair wavy:

  • Use a curling iron or a flat iron with a ceramic coating to prevent hair damage
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays to find the one that works best for your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length hair wavy:

Can I style my hair wavy without using heat tools?

Yes, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create natural waves in your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too dry.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth

Styling medium length hair wavy is easy and fun. With the right tools and products, you can create beautiful waves that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
