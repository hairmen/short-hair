Get Curly Hair Without A Perm Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to achieve curly hair without going through the damaging process of a perm? Well, you’re in luck! There are many ways to get curly hair without a perm, and we’ve got all the tips and tricks you need to know. In this article, we’ll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to get curly hair without a perm. Let’s dive in!

How To Get Curly Hair Without a Perm?

Before we get started, it’s important to understand that there are different methods to achieve curly hair without a perm. Some methods work better for certain hair types, so it’s important to experiment and find what works best for you. Here are some methods to try:

Method 1: Twist Out

A twist out is a popular method for getting curls without a perm. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  5. Once your hair is dry, carefully unravel each twist.
  6. Fluff your hair to create volume.

This method works well for natural hair types and can last for several days.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another popular method for achieving curly hair without a perm. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Take a small section of hair and twist it from the root to the end.
  5. Wrap the twisted section around itself to create a knot.
  6. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are in knots.
  7. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  8. Once your hair is dry, carefully unravel each knot.
  9. Fluff your hair to create volume.

This method works well for all hair types and can last for several days.

Method 3: Roller Set

A roller set is another great method for achieving curly hair without a perm. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a roller.
  5. Secure the roller with a clip.
  6. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are in rollers.
  7. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  8. Once your hair is dry, carefully remove each roller.
  9. Fluff your hair to create volume.

This method works well for all hair types and can last for several days.

Tips For Getting Curly Hair Without a Perm

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to get curly hair without a perm:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.
  • Be gentle when unraveling your twists, knots, or rollers to avoid causing frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a satin pillowcase or wearing a satin bonnet.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs About Getting Curly Hair Without a Perm

Can you get curly hair without a perm?

Yes, there are many ways to get curly hair without a perm, such as using the twist out, bantu knots, or roller set methods.

What products can I use to enhance my curls?

You can use curl-enhancing products such as mousse, gel, or curl cream to help define your curls.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls can last for several days, depending on the method you use and how well you maintain them.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people prefer to wash their hair once a week, while others may need to wash their hair more frequently.

Hair Care For Curly Hair

It’s important to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve curly hair without a perm and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Experiment with different methods and find what works best for you. Happy styling!
