Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins

Written by Luffy Jul 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face or a double chin, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. Long hairstyles can be a great option for those with fat faces and double chins, as they can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced appearance. In this article, we'll explore some of the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, as well as tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

Having a round face or a double chin can be a source of insecurity for many people. However, with the right hairstyle, you can highlight your best features and create a more balanced appearance. Long hairstyles are a great option for those with fat faces and double chins, as they can help to create an elongated effect and draw attention away from the chin area.

How to

So, what are some of the best long hairstyles for fat faces and double chins? Here are a few options to consider:

Layers

Adding layers to your hair can help to create movement and volume, which can be particularly flattering for those with round faces. Layers can also help to draw attention away from the chin area and create a more balanced appearance.

Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great option for those with round faces, as they can help to create a more angular appearance. They can also help to draw attention away from the chin area and create a more balanced look.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can be a great option for those with round faces, as they can help to create a more relaxed, carefree look. They can also help to draw attention away from the chin area and create a more balanced appearance.

Step by Step

To create beachy waves, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Section your hair into small sections.
  3. Using a curling iron, wrap each section around the barrel and hold for a few seconds.
  4. Release the curl and repeat on the remaining sections.
  5. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more relaxed look.
  6. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a long hairstyle for a fat face or double chin:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as blunt bangs or straight hair.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and movement in the hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying to create volume at the roots.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as they can emphasize the chin area.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for a round face?

Some of the best hairstyles for a round face include layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long beachy waves.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a double chin?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as blunt bangs or straight hair. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as they can emphasize the chin area.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving a flattering hairstyle. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and styles in mind, you can find the perfect long hairstyle for your fat face or double chin. Remember to embrace your unique features and rock your new look with confidence!
