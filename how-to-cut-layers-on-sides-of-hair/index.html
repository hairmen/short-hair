How To Cut Layers On Sides Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 15, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking to add some texture and movement to your hair, cutting layers on the sides is a great way to do it. Whether you have long or short hair, layers can give your locks a fresh and modern look.

How to Cut Layers on Sides of Hair

Cutting layers on the sides of your hair can be done at home, but it's important to have the right tools and technique. Here are the basic steps:

1. Gather the Necessary Tools

You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to hold your hair in place. It's also helpful to have a mirror and good lighting.

2. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair so you can see the natural texture and shape of your hair.

3. Section Your Hair

Use the comb to part your hair down the middle, then clip each side out of the way.

4. Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair from the front of one side and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, starting with small snips and working your way down. Repeat this process, working your way back towards the crown of your head.

Repeat on the other side, making sure to cut the same amount of hair and angle.

5. Blend the Layers

Once you've cut both sides, use the comb to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair so you can see the natural texture and shape of your hair.

2. Section Your Hair

Use the comb to part your hair down the middle, then clip each side out of the way.

3. Take the First Section

Take a small section of hair from the front of one side and hold it between your fingers.

4. Cut at an Angle

Use the scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, starting with small snips and working your way down.

5. Repeat on the Same Side

Repeat this process, working your way back towards the crown of your head. Make sure to cut the same amount of hair and angle.

6. Repeat on the Other Side

Repeat the same process on the other side, making sure to cut the same amount of hair and angle.

7. Blend the Layers

Once you've cut both sides, use the comb to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless look and prevent any harsh lines.

Tips for Cutting Layers on Sides of Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start with small snips and work your way down to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Work in small sections to ensure a more even cut.
  • Don't try to cut too much hair at once. It's better to take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Always cut at an angle to create the desired layered effect.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQs

Can I cut layers on the sides of my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still cut layers on the sides of short hair. Just make sure to work in small sections and use sharp scissors to avoid any mistakes.

How often should I cut layers on the sides of my hair?

It depends on the rate of your hair growth, but generally, every 6-8 weeks is a good time to trim your hair and maintain the layered look.

Can I add layers on the sides of my hair if it's already layered?

Yes, you can add layers even if your hair is already layered. Just make sure to blend the new layers with the existing ones to avoid any harsh lines or uneven cuts.

Hair Care After Cutting Layers on Sides of Hair

After cutting layers on the sides of your hair, it's important to keep your locks healthy and moisturized. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the layered look.
  • Avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Cutting layers on the sides of your hair can give you a fresh and modern look. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve the perfect layered look that suits your hair type and face shape. Whether you have long or short hair, layers can add texture and movement to your locks, making it easier to style and maintain.
