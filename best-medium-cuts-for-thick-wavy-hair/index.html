Best Medium Cuts For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unmanageable, frizzy hair? Do you have thick wavy hair that never seems to cooperate? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best medium cuts for thick wavy hair. Whether you want a low-maintenance hairstyle or a trendy look, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Medium cuts are the perfect length for thick wavy hair. They offer versatility and can be styled in various ways, making them suitable for any occasion. A medium cut falls between the chin and shoulders, providing enough length to showcase your natural waves without weighing down your hair.

How to Choose the Best Medium Cut for Thick Wavy Hair

When choosing a medium cut for thick wavy hair, it's essential to consider your face shape and hair texture. A layered cut is ideal for those with round faces as it adds dimension and movement to the hair. If you have an angular face, a blunt cut with side-swept bangs can soften your features.

For those with thick wavy hair, a long bob or shoulder-length cut is perfect. It allows you to show off your waves while keeping your hair manageable. Adding layers can also help control the volume and add texture to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Medium Cut

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best medium cut for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Shampoo and condition your hair before your appointment.

3. Sit back and relax as your hairstylist works their magic.

4. Style your hair at home using a texturizing spray or mousse to enhance your natural waves.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Cut

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush to prevent frizz.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

3. Use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your waves and minimize frizz.

4. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your medium cut.

FAQs

Q: Will a medium cut work for all hair types?

A: While a medium cut is suitable for most hair types, it may not work for those with extremely curly or fine hair.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight with a medium cut?

A: Yes! A medium cut is versatile and can be styled straight or curly.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your hair.

2. Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.

4. Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while sleeping.

In conclusion, a medium cut is the perfect length for thick wavy hair. It offers versatility, manageability, and can be styled in various ways. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair texture. Follow our tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair, and you'll be rocking your medium cut in no time!
