How To Make Your Hair Curly Without Using Any Products

Written by Bardi Aug 10, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re looking for a way to add some bouncy curls to your hair without using any products, you’re in luck! In this article, we’ll show you how to make your hair curly using only a few simple techniques. Whether you have straight or wavy hair, these methods will work for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Curly Without Using Any Products
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Hair Curly
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Make Your Hair Curly Without Using Any Products

There are several methods you can use to make your hair curly without using any products. These methods include using heat, braiding, twisting, and more. Below, we’ll walk you through each method step by step.

Method 1: Heat

The first method to make your hair curly without using any products is by using heat. Here’s how:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry completely. This is important because wet or damp hair won’t hold curls well.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. The more sections you create, the tighter your curls will be.
  3. Take a section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or straightener. Hold it in place for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this step until all sections of your hair have been curled.
  4. Once you’ve curled your entire head, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

Method 2: Twist

The second method to make your hair curly without using any products is by twisting your hair. Here’s how:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry completely.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. The more sections you create, the tighter your curls will be.
  3. Take a section of hair and twist it tightly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Secure the twist with a bobby pin or hair tie, then repeat this step until all sections of your hair have been twisted.
  5. Leave the twists in your hair for several hours or overnight. The longer you leave them in, the tighter your curls will be.
  6. Once you’ve removed the twists, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

Method 3: Braids

The third method to make your hair curly without using any products is by braiding your hair. Here’s how:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry completely.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. The more sections you create, the tighter your curls will be.
  3. Take a section of hair and braid it tightly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Secure the braid with a bobby pin or hair tie, then repeat this step until all sections of your hair have been braided.
  5. Leave the braids in your hair for several hours or overnight. The longer you leave them in, the tighter your curls will be.
  6. Once you’ve removed the braids, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

Step by Step Guide

To summarize, here’s a step by step guide on how to make your hair curly without using any products:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry completely.
  2. Choose a method (heat, twist, or braid) and divide your hair into sections.
  3. Follow the steps for your chosen method.
  4. Leave your hair in the curls for several hours or overnight.
  5. Use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

Tips for Making Your Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you make your hair curly without using any products:

  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before starting.
  • Don’t use too much tension when twisting or braiding your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • For tighter curls, leave your hair in the curls for a longer period of time.
  • For looser curls, use larger sections of hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair curly without using any heat?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without using any heat. Simply use the twist or braid method as described above.

How long will the curls last?

The length of time the curls last will depend on your hair type and the method you used. Generally, the curls will last for several hours to a few days.

Can I use these methods on any hair type?

Yes, these methods can be used on any hair type, although the results may vary depending on your hair’s natural texture.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you have naturally curly hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to make your hair curly without using any products, give it a try and see how it works for you. With a little bit of practice, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day long!
