Best Hairstyles For Thick Coarse Frizzy Hair In 2023

Thick, coarse, and frizzy hair can be a challenge to manage, but there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you tame your mane. Whether you prefer short or long hair, there are many options that can work for you. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for thick coarse frizzy hair in 2023.

How to Style Thick Coarse Frizzy Hair

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for thick coarse frizzy hair, it's essential to know how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and make it frizzier.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to damp hair to help tame frizz and add shine.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and more frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

Best Hairstyles for Thick Coarse Frizzy Hair

Now that you know how to style thick coarse frizzy hair let's discuss the best hairstyles for this hair type:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to break up the thickness of your hair and make it more manageable. They also add movement and texture to your hair.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well with thick coarse frizzy hair. A chin-length bob with layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that can work well with thick coarse frizzy hair. It's low-maintenance and can help to show off your natural texture.

4. Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face and under control. You can try a simple braid, a French braid, or even a fishtail braid.

5. Bun

A bun is a simple and elegant hairstyle that can work well with thick coarse frizzy hair. You can try a high bun, a low bun, or a messy bun.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Coarse Frizzy Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your thick coarse frizzy hair:

1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any residue.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to damp hair.

Distribute it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

Start from the ends and work your way up to avoid any breakage.

4. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.

This will help to enhance your natural texture and reduce frizz.

5. Style your hair using one of the hairstyles mentioned above.

Use bobby pins, hair ties, or hair spray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Thick Coarse Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick coarse frizzy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too often, as this can transfer oils from your hands to your hair and cause more frizz.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it frizzier.
  • Try not to brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and more frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I wash my thick coarse frizzy hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

2. What hair products should I use for thick coarse frizzy hair?

You should use moisturizing shampoos and conditioners, leave-in conditioners, hair oils, and hair masks.

3. Can I use heat styling tools on my thick coarse frizzy hair?

You can use heat styling tools, but it's best to use them sparingly and with heat protectant products.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Coarse Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thick coarse frizzy hair:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry it out and make it frizzier.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts, as they can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hairbrushes with plastic bristles, as they can cause static and more frizz.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of natural oils.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for thick coarse frizzy hair, you can try them out and see which one works best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair by using moisturizing products and avoiding excessive heat styling. With the right care and styling, you can have beautiful, manageable hair that you'll love.
