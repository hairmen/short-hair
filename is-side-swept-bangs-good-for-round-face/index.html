Is Side Swept Bangs Good For Round Face Tips And Faqs

Written by Robby Oct 23, 2023 · 4 min read
Is Side Swept Bangs Good for Round Face: Tips and FAQs | Hair Care in 2023

If you have a round face, you may have wondered if side swept bangs would be a good look for you. While there is no one-size-fits-all answer, in most cases, side swept bangs can be a great choice for those with round faces. In this article, we’ll explore the ins and outs of this hairstyle, including how to style it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Side Swept Bangs for Round Face
  • Tips to Maintain Side Swept Bangs for Round Face
  • FAQ about Side Swept Bangs for Round Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

How to Style Side Swept Bangs for Round Face

When it comes to styling side swept bangs for a round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

1. Get the Right Length

The length of your bangs can make a big difference in how they look on your face. If you have a round face, consider going for longer bangs that hit just below your eyebrows. This will help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

2. Angle Your Bangs

When you’re styling your side swept bangs, be sure to angle them slightly to the side. This will help create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face, which is generally considered to be the most flattering face shape.

3. Use a Round Brush

When blow-drying your bangs, use a round brush to create a gentle curve in the hair. This will help give your bangs a soft, feminine look that works well with a round face.

4. Add Some Volume

To create the appearance of a slimmer face, add some volume to your bangs. This can be achieved by using a volumizing mousse or spray, or by teasing the hair slightly at the roots.

Tips to Maintain Side Swept Bangs for Round Face

Once you’ve got your side swept bangs looking just right, you’ll want to keep them looking that way. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

1. Trim Regularly

To keep your bangs looking neat and tidy, be sure to trim them every 4-6 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and strong, which is essential for maintaining any hairstyle.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. To keep your hair healthy, try to avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands as much as possible.

FAQ about Side Swept Bangs for Round Face

Q: Will side swept bangs make my face look even rounder?

A: No, in fact, side swept bangs can help create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face, which is generally considered to be the most flattering face shape.

Q: Can I wear side swept bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear side swept bangs with curly hair. However, you’ll need to style them carefully to avoid creating a frizzy or unruly look.

Q: How do I know if side swept bangs are right for me?

A: If you have a round face, side swept bangs are generally a good choice. However, it’s always best to consult with a stylist to get their professional opinion.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

To keep your side swept bangs looking their best, be sure to follow these hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it looking clean and healthy. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

2. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any buildup from styling products or environmental pollutants.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a protective spray to shield your hair from the sun’s harmful rays.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock side swept bangs on your round face with confidence. Remember to consult with a stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape, and don’t forget to maintain your hair with regular trims and good quality hair care products.
