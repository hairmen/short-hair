How To Wear Long Hair Up Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 50 and wondering how to wear your long hair up? Look no further! In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to style your long hair in a way that's both chic and age-appropriate.

How to Wear Long Hair Up Over 50: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first for a sleeker look.

Step 2: Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 3: Decide on the type of updo you want. Do you want a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo?

Step 4: If you're doing a messy bun or ponytail, tease your hair at the crown to add volume.

Step 5: Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 6: If you're doing a braided updo, start by creating a French braid at the crown of your head. Continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck, then secure the braid with a hair tie. Gather the remaining hair and twist it into a bun, securing it with bobby pins.

Tips for Wearing Long Hair Up Over 50

Tip 1: Keep it simple. As we age, our features become more prominent, so it's best to avoid complicated hairstyles that distract from our natural beauty.

Tip 2: Use hair accessories. A simple barrette or hair clip can add a touch of elegance to your updo.

Tip 3: Don't be afraid of volume. Adding volume to your hair can make your updo look more youthful and playful.

Tip 4: Consider your outfit. When choosing an updo, think about what you'll be wearing. A sleek ponytail may look great with a formal gown, while a messy bun is perfect for a casual outfit.

FAQ: How to Wear Long Hair Up Over 50

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a high ponytail?

A: Yes! A high ponytail can be a chic and easy way to wear your long hair up.

Q: What if I have thinning hair?

A: If you have thinning hair, consider using volumizing products or extensions to add fullness to your updo.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid?

A: It's best to avoid hairstyles that are too complicated or trendy, as they can make you look older. Stick to classic, timeless styles.

Hair Care for Long Hair Over 50

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your long hair healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Limit heat styling to prevent damage.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear your long hair up over 50 in a way that's both stylish and age-appropriate. Remember to keep it simple, add volume, and choose hairstyles that flatter your features.
