How To Style Short Pixie Hair Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short pixie hair has been a popular hairstyle for women for decades, and it's easy to see why. It's low-maintenance, versatile, and can be styled in many different ways. However, styling short pixie hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you're new to the style. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips, tricks, and FAQs to help you style your short pixie hair with ease.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Short Pixie Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Style Short Pixie Hair

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of styling short pixie hair, it's important to understand that the style requires some maintenance. Regular trims are necessary to keep the style looking fresh, and you'll need to invest in some quality hair products to help you achieve your desired look.

When it comes to styling short pixie hair, there are a few basic techniques you can use:

  • Blow-dry with a round brush for volume
  • Flat iron for a sleek, polished look
  • Add texture with a curling iron or texturizing spray

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short pixie hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten any kinks or frizz
  5. Add texture with a curling iron or texturizing spray
  6. Finish with a hair spray or light-hold gel

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style short pixie hair:

  • Invest in a good quality blow dryer and flat iron
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Try different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type
  • Play around with different styling techniques to find the ones that flatter your face shape
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different accessories, like headbands or hair clips

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my short pixie hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Q: Can I still style my short pixie hair if it's curly?

A: Absolutely! Curly hair can be styled in many different ways, including with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer or with a curling iron.

Q: What products should I use to style my short pixie hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the look you're going for. Some popular products for short hair include texturizing spray, pomade, and light-hold gel.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your short pixie hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that are tailored to your hair type
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF

With these tips, tricks, and FAQs, you'll be able to style your short pixie hair with confidence and ease. Remember to experiment with different techniques and products to find the ones that work best for you, and don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
