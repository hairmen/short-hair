How To Cut Men s Hair Shaved Sides Long Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new and stylish haircut? The shaved sides long top hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years. This edgy and trendy haircut is perfect for men who want to look stylish and modern. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting men's hair shaved sides long top.

Description

The shaved sides long top hairstyle is characterized by short or shaved hair on the sides and back of the head and longer hair on the top. This hairstyle can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences. You can choose to have a fade or undercut on the sides, and the length of the hair on top can vary from short to long. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice among men of all ages.

How to

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, comb, and hair product of your choice.

Step 2: Decide on the length

The length of the hair on top will depend on your personal preference. If you want a shorter hairstyle, you can choose to cut the hair to a length of 1-2 inches. For a longer hairstyle, you can leave the hair on top at a length of 3-5 inches.

Step 3: Cut the sides

Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. You can choose to have a fade or undercut, depending on the style you want. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve a gradual fade.

Step 4: Cut the top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length. Start by cutting small sections of hair and work your way around the head. Blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides to create a seamless transition.

Step 5: Style your hair

Apply your favorite hair product to style your hair. You can choose to slick it back, part it to the side, or create a messy textured look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard when cutting the sides and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve a gradual fade.
- Use scissors to cut the hair on top to achieve a more natural and textured look.
- Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair to achieve your desired style.
- If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, consider going to a professional barber for a consultation or haircut.

FAQ

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. On average, most men get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I style my hair differently every day?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles and products to achieve different looks. The shaved sides long top hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

How do I maintain my haircut?

Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy. Use hair product to style your hair and keep it in place throughout the day.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is essential for any hairstyle. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and blow dryers too often, as they can damage your hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking neat and tidy. Use hair product to style your hair and protect it from environmental damage.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve the perfect shaved sides long top hairstyle. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find the one that suits you best. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and modern look that will turn heads.
