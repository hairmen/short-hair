What To Say To Your Barber For The Best Possible Haircut

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of leaving the barbershop with a haircut that doesn't meet your expectations? It's time to change that. Knowing what to say to your barber can make all the difference in getting the best possible haircut. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on what to say to your barber for the best possible haircut.

Description

Getting a great haircut starts with effective communication with your barber. A good barber will always ask you what you want and give you their professional opinion on what will suit you best. However, it's essential to know what to say to your barber to get the best possible haircut.

How to

Before you sit down in the barber's chair, take some time to think about what you want. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. This will help you communicate effectively with your barber.

Step-by-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on what to say to your barber for the best possible haircut:

  1. Start by greeting your barber and introducing yourself if it's your first time.
  2. Explain what you want in detail. Be specific about the length, style, and any other details. Use visual aids like pictures or videos to help your barber understand your vision.
  3. Ask for your barber's professional opinion. They may have suggestions that would suit you better or make your hairstyle more manageable.
  4. Discuss maintenance. Ask your barber how often you should come back for a trim and what products you should use to maintain your new haircut.
  5. Ask questions. If there's anything you're unsure about, don't hesitate to ask your barber. They are there to help you get the best possible haircut.
  6. Thank your barber and leave a tip if you're satisfied with their service.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for communicating with your barber:

  • Be honest about your hair history. Let your barber know if you have any specific concerns or if you've had any issues with previous haircuts.
  • Be open to suggestions. Your barber is a professional and may have ideas that would suit you better than your initial vision.
  • Don't be afraid to speak up. If your barber is doing something you don't like or if you change your mind about something, let them know.
  • Establish a good relationship with your barber. Building a good rapport with your barber can lead to better haircuts in the future.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what to say to your barber:

What if I don't know what I want?

It's okay if you don't know exactly what you want. Bring in pictures or describe the general look you're going for, and your barber can help you refine your vision.

Should I tip my barber?

Yes, it's customary to tip your barber. The standard tip is 15-20% of the haircut price.

What if I don't like my haircut?

If you're not satisfied with your haircut, let your barber know. They may be able to fix it or offer suggestions for how to style it differently.

Hair Care

After getting a great haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Use quality hair care products like shampoo, conditioner, and styling products recommended by your barber. Follow their advice on how often to come back for touch-ups and trims.

Remember, communication is key to getting the best possible haircut. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to articulate your vision to your barber effectively. Happy styling!
