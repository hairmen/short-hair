How To Do A Low Messy Bun With Fine Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Low messy buns are a perfect hairstyle for those who want to look chic and effortless. But if you have fine hair, it can be challenging to create a voluminous and messy bun without it looking flat and lifeless. In this guide, we'll show you how to do a low messy bun with fine hair and share some tips to make it last all day.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we start, here are some things you'll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastic
  • Hairspray
  • Texture spray or dry shampoo

Now let's get started!

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by spraying some texture spray or dry shampoo on your roots to add some volume and grip to your hair. This will help your bun stay in place all day.

Step 2: Gather your hair

Gently gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it into a low ponytail using a hair elastic.

Step 3: Create your bun

Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base of the elastic to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide the ends of your hair.

Step 4: Mess it up

Now it's time to make your bun look messy and effortless. Gently pull out some strands of hair from the sides and the top of your bun to create a relaxed look. Use your fingers to tease the bun and create more volume.

Step 5: Secure it in place

Once you're happy with the shape and messiness of your bun, use bobby pins to secure any loose strands in place. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

  • If you have fine hair, try using a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair. This will give your hair more body and make it easier to create a messy bun.
  • Don't worry if your bun isn't perfectly round or symmetrical. Messy buns are meant to look effortless and imperfect.
  • If you have shorter layers in the front, leave them out of your bun to frame your face.
  • If you want to add some extra texture to your bun, try teasing the ends of your hair before wrapping them around the base of the elastic.

FAQ

Can I do a low messy bun with shoulder-length hair?

Yes, you can still create a low messy bun with shoulder-length hair. Just make sure to leave some layers out to frame your face, and use bobby pins to secure any loose strands in place.

How do I make my bun look fuller?

Try teasing your hair before tying it into a ponytail to add some extra volume. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair.

How do I make my bun last all day?

Use a strong-hold hairspray to keep your bun in place, and don't be afraid to use bobby pins to secure any loose strands throughout the day.

Hair Care

To keep your fine hair healthy and voluminous, make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and try to let your hair air dry as often as possible. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now you know how to do a low messy bun with fine hair! With these tips and tricks, you can rock this effortless hairstyle all day long.
