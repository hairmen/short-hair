How To Cut Your Own Short Side Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of constantly visiting the salon just to maintain your side bangs? With the right tools and technique, you can easily cut your own short side bangs at home. In this article, we’ll walk you through the process from start to finish, including tips and tricks to ensure a successful outcome. So, grab your scissors and let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Short Side Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Outcome
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Bangs

How to Cut Your Own Short Side Bangs

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to gather the necessary tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Once you have these tools, follow these simple steps to cut your own short side bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with dry, clean hair. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Use hair clips to secure the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Spray the sectioned hair with water to make it easier to cut.

Step 4: Hold the scissors vertically and start cutting your bangs. Start by cutting less hair than you want as you can always trim more later. Cut slowly and carefully, making small snips.

Step 5: As you cut, keep checking your progress in the mirror. Make sure your bangs are even and the length is what you desire.

Step 6: Once you’re happy with the length, use the scissors to cut into the ends of your bangs. This will create a textured look and prevent your bangs from looking too blunt.

Step 7: Comb your bangs to remove any loose hairs. Style as desired.

Tips for a Successful Outcome

Follow these tips to ensure a successful outcome when cutting your own short side bangs:

  • Always cut less hair than you think you need to. You can always trim more later.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and fraying.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry to ensure the length is accurate.
  • Start with small snips and cut slowly to prevent mistakes.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my own bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own short side bangs if you have curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve an even cut as curly hair tends to bounce up when dry. Consider straightening your hair before cutting to ensure a more accurate cut.

Q: How often should I cut my own bangs?

A: It’s recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Bangs

After cutting your own short side bangs, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain the shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot styling tools.
  • Condition your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs as it can weigh them down.

Now that you know how to cut your own short side bangs, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Just remember to follow the steps carefully and use the right tools to ensure a successful outcome.
