Mens Short Fade Haircut Tutorial A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 14, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, a short fade might be just what you need. This popular haircut features a gradual taper on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. In this tutorial, we'll guide you through the steps to achieve a perfect short fade haircut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Short Fade Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Short Fade
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Fades

How to Get a Short Fade Haircut

A short fade haircut is a versatile and trendy look that can be achieved with a variety of hair types and styles. It's important to find a skilled barber who can help you achieve the desired look.

Before getting a short fade, decide how short you want the sides to be. A high fade will have more contrast between the top and sides, while a low fade will have a more gradual transition. You should also consider how much hair you want to keep on top, as this will determine the overall length of the haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving a perfect short fade haircut:

1. Wash your hair

Before getting a haircut, it's important to start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or oils that might interfere with the haircut.

2. Decide on the length

Work with your barber to decide on the length of the fade. This will depend on how short you want the sides to be and how much hair you want to keep on top.

3. Start with the sides

The barber will start by cutting the sides of your hair with clippers. They will use a higher guard to create a gradual taper, or a lower guard for a more defined line.

4. Blend the sides

The barber will use a comb and scissors to blend the hair on the sides with the longer hair on top. This creates a seamless transition between the two lengths.

5. Trim the top

The barber will use scissors or clippers to trim the hair on top to the desired length. They will also shape the hair to create texture and volume.

6. Check for balance

The barber will check to make sure the haircut is balanced and even on both sides. They will make any necessary adjustments to create a symmetrical look.

7. Style your hair

Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair with your favorite products. A short fade looks great with a textured, messy style or a slicked-back look.

Tips for a Perfect Short Fade

Here are some tips for getting the perfect short fade haircut:

  • Bring a photo of the desired haircut to show your barber
  • Communicate clearly with your barber about the length and style you want
  • Go to a skilled and experienced barber for the best results
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short fade haircuts:

What is a short fade?

A short fade is a haircut that features a gradual taper on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. It can be customized to suit different hair types and styles.

How do I maintain a short fade?

To maintain a short fade, you should get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy, and style it with your favorite products.

Can I get a short fade with curly hair?

Yes, a short fade can look great with curly hair. Your barber can customize the haircut to suit your hair type and style.

Hair Care for Short Fades

To keep your short fade looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished. You can also use styling products like pomade or wax to add texture and volume to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a perfect short fade haircut. Whether you're going for a classic or trendy look, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.
