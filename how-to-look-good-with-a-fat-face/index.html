How To Look Good With A Fat Face In 2023

Written by Oliver Nov 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to feel confident with a fuller face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people find it challenging to find the right style and makeup for their face shape. But fear not, because we have put together some tips and tricks on how to look good with a fat face. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Look Good with a Fat Face
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for a Fuller Face

How to Look Good with a Fat Face

First and foremost, it's important to embrace your facial features and not be too harsh on yourself. However, if you're looking to enhance your features and feel more confident, here are some tips:

1. Contouring

Contouring can work wonders for a fuller face. Use a bronzer or contouring powder two shades darker than your skin tone and apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline. This will create the illusion of sharper cheekbones and a slimmer face.

2. Highlighting

Highlighting can also help to bring out your best features. Use a highlighter or illuminator on the high points of your face such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the tip of your nose. This will add dimension and make your face appear more defined.

3. Bold Lips

Wearing a bold lip color can help to draw attention away from your face shape. Opt for bright shades such as red or pink to make a statement and add a pop of color to your look.

4. Eyebrows

Your eyebrows frame your face, so it's important to pay attention to them. For a fuller face, go for a more defined, arched brow to create a lifted effect. Avoid over-plucking as this can make your face appear rounder.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to apply contouring and highlighting:

Step 1: Apply Foundation

Start with a clean, moisturized face and apply your favorite foundation as you normally would.

Step 2: Contouring

Using a contouring brush, apply a bronzer or contouring powder two shades darker than your skin tone to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline. Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Highlighting

Using a highlighting brush, apply a highlighter or illuminator to the high points of your face such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the tip of your nose. Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Finish with Powder

Finish off with a light dusting of translucent powder to set your makeup in place.

Tips and Tricks

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a middle part to elongate your face.

2. Go for Voluminous Hair

Voluminous hair can help to balance out a fuller face. Try adding some volume to your hair with a blowout or volumizing spray.

3. Choose the Right Glasses

If you wear glasses, opt for frames that are wider than your face to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

FAQs

1. Can I still wear a smokey eye?

Absolutely! A smokey eye can work on any face shape. Just make sure to balance it out with some contouring and highlighting.

2. Should I avoid wearing blush?

No, you can still wear blush. Just make sure to blend it well and apply it to the apples of your cheeks to create a natural flush.

3. Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to add some volume to your hair to balance out your face.

Hair Care for a Fuller Face

Here are some hair care tips for a fuller face:

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.

2. Use Volumizing Products

Use volumizing products such as mousse or volumizing spray to add some lift to your hair.

3. Try a Side Part

A side part can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

In conclusion, there are many ways to look good with a fat face. Embrace your features and try out some of these tips and tricks to enhance your natural beauty.
