How To Make Your Straight Short Hair Fluffy A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have straight short hair, you may find it difficult to style it in a way that looks voluminous and bouncy. However, with a few tips and tricks, you can easily transform your hair into a fluffy and full hairstyle. In this article, we will be discussing how to make your straight short hair fluffy.

Description

Fluffy hair is a hairstyle that looks voluminous, bouncy, and full of life. It is a perfect hairstyle for those who have straight short hair and want to add some texture and volume to it. Fluffy hair can be achieved by using the right products, tools, and techniques. In this article, we will be discussing how to make your straight short hair fluffy.

How to Make Your Straight Short Hair Fluffy

Here are some tips and tricks to make your straight short hair fluffy:

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner that will help add volume to your hair. These products work by lifting the hair from the roots, making it look fuller and bouncier.

Blow-dry your hair upside down

After washing your hair, flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair. This will help lift the roots and create volume. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair for an even more voluminous effect.

Add some texture

To add texture to your hair, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. These products will add grip to your hair, making it easier to style and hold its shape.

Tease your hair

Teasing your hair is a great way to add volume and create a fluffy look. To tease your hair, take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots using a teasing brush. Do this all over your hair for maximum volume.

Use a volumizing powder

If you want to add even more volume to your hair, use a volumizing powder. This product works by adding texture and grip to your hair, making it easier to style and hold its shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your straight short hair fluffy:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair upside down

Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair using a round brush.

Step 3: Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo

Spray a texturizing spray or dry shampoo onto your hair to add texture and grip.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots using a teasing brush.

Step 5: Use a volumizing powder

For even more volume, use a volumizing powder on your hair.

Tips for Fluffy Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving fluffy hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse before blow-drying your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add volume
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute the natural oils in your hair

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. However, if you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I achieve fluffy hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can achieve fluffy hair without using heat by using a volumizing mousse and letting your hair air dry.

Hair Care for Fluffy Hair

To maintain your fluffy hair, make sure to use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from products. Also, avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Finally, trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily make your straight short hair fluffy and voluminous. Try out different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and style.
