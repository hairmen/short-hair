How To Do Shoulder Length Hairstyles A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for some inspiration for your shoulder length hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to do shoulder length hairstyles that are easy to follow and perfect for any occasion. From braids to buns, we’ve got you covered.

How to Do Shoulder Length Hairstyles

Shoulder length hair is incredibly versatile, allowing you to experiment with various styles and looks. From loose beachy waves to polished updos, there are countless options to choose from. Here are some popular shoulder length hairstyles that you can try:

  • Beachy Waves
  • Braids
  • Half Up Half Down
  • Buns
  • Sleek Straight

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are perfect for a casual day out or a night on the town. Here’s how to achieve this look:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  3. Section your hair and curl it using a curling iron. Hold the iron vertically and wrap the hair around the barrel away from your face.
  4. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and create a tousled look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Braids

Braids are not only stylish but also practical, keeping your hair out of your face on a hot summer day. Here’s how to create a simple braid:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three sections.
  3. Take the right section and cross it over the middle section.
  4. Then, take the left section and cross it over the middle section.
  5. Continue braiding in this manner until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.

Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is a classic look that is perfect for any occasion. Here’s how to create this look:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail.
  3. Tease the ponytail to add volume.
  4. Wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Buns

Buns are a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways. Here’s how to create a simple bun:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail and tie it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Sleek Straight

The sleek straight hairstyle is a classic look that is perfect for any occasion. Here’s how to create this look:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a straightening balm or serum to your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  3. Section your hair and straighten it using a flat iron.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Styling shoulder length hair can be tricky, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve amazing results. Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder length hair:

  • Use the right products: Choose products that are suitable for your hair type and will help you achieve the desired style.
  • Invest in quality tools: Invest in tools such as a good quality hairbrush, curling iron, and flat iron to achieve the desired results.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don’t be afraid to try out different styles and experiment to find what works best for you.
  • Take care of your hair: Proper hair care is essential for healthy hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-styling your hair.
