How To Cut The Back Of Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 17, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of shelling out cash at the barber every time you need a haircut? Learning how to cut your own hair can save you time and money in the long run. However, the back of the head can be tricky to reach and cut properly. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut the back of men's hair with clippers.

How to Cut the Back of Men's Hair with Clippers

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. A good pair of clippers is essential for cutting men's hair. Look for clippers with adjustable blades, as this will give you more control over the length of the cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Hair

Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly before you start cutting. Wet hair can make it difficult to cut evenly, and it can also clog up your clippers.

Step 3: Set Up Your Clippers

Attach the appropriate guard to your clippers. The guard will determine the length of the cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you need to.

Step 4: Start at the Nape of the Neck

Begin at the nape of the neck, where the hair is the longest. Use your clippers to create a straight line across the back of the head. Work your way up the head, cutting in a straight line.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you've cut the back of the head, it's time to blend the sides and back. Use a shorter guard to trim the sides, and blend them into the back using an upward motion.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, use a mirror to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that the cut is even all around.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Men's Hair with Clippers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting the back of men's hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you need to
  • Use an upward motion to blend the sides and back
  • Check for evenness using a mirror
  • Take your time and be patient

FAQ

Can I Cut the Back of My Own Hair?

Yes, you can cut the back of your own hair with clippers. However, it can be difficult to reach and cut properly. Consider enlisting the help of a friend or family member to make the process easier.

What Length Should I Cut My Hair?

The length of the cut will depend on your personal preference. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you need to.

How Often Should I Cut My Hair?

The frequency of haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and your personal preference. Most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

Now that you know how to cut the back of men's hair with clippers, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and be patient. With a little practice, you'll be able to give yourself a professional-looking haircut in no time.
