Best Way To Train Your Hair To Slick Back

Written by Christine Aug 27, 2023 · 4 min read

In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on the best way to train your hair to slick back. Slicked-back hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades, and it’s not going anywhere anytime soon. It’s a timeless and classic look that suits any occasion, from casual to formal. If you want to achieve this look, keep reading!

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

How to Slick Back Your Hair

Slicking back your hair requires a little bit of patience and practice. The first step is to choose the right product for your hair type. Pomades, gels, and waxes are great options for slicked-back hair. Make sure to choose a product that provides a strong hold and a shiny finish.

Once you have your product, you need to prepare your hair. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of your chosen product onto your fingertips. Rub your fingertips together to warm up the product and evenly distribute it throughout your hair.

After applying the product, use a comb to slick back your hair. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back. Use a comb with fine teeth to ensure a sleek and polished finish. Keep combing until you achieve the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to slick back your hair:

  1. Choose the right product for your hair type.
  2. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  3. Towel dry your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of your chosen product onto your fingertips and rub them together.
  5. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  6. Use a comb with fine teeth to slick back your hair.
  7. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back.
  8. Keep combing until you achieve the desired look.
  9. Use hairspray to set your hair in place.
  10. Enjoy your slicked-back hair!

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when slicking back your hair:

  • Use a small amount of product to avoid a greasy look.
  • Start with a lower hold product and work your way up if needed.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, blow-dry your hair straight before applying product.
  • Don't over-comb your hair, as this can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicked-back hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

What product should I use for my hair type?

Pomades work best for thick hair, gels work best for fine hair, and waxes work best for curly hair.

Can I slick back my hair if I have a receding hairline?

Yes, you can still slick back your hair if you have a receding hairline. However, make sure to use a light hold product to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product to avoid buildup.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.

Now that you know the best way to train your hair to slick back, you can achieve this timeless and classic look with ease. Remember to choose the right product for your hair type, prepare your hair properly, and follow our step-by-step guide. With a little bit of practice, you’ll have slicked-back hair that looks polished and sophisticated.
