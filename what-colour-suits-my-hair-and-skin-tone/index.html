What Colour Suits My Hair And Skin Tone

Written by Daniel Nov 25, 2023 · 3 min read

Choosing the right hair colour that complements your skin tone can be a daunting task. With so many shades available in the market, it’s easy to get confused. But fret not! In this blog post, we’ll help you figure out what colour suits your hair and skin tone.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

First things first, let’s understand what skin tone and hair colour are. Skin tone refers to the natural colour of your skin, while hair colour refers to the shade of your hair. When it comes to picking the right hair colour, it’s essential to choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Your skin tone can be categorised into warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, while neutral skin tones have a mix of both. Similarly, hair colours can be categorised into warm or cool tones. Warm hair colours include shades of red, brown, and blonde with golden or honey undertones, while cool hair colours include shades of ash brown, platinum blonde, and black with blue or grey undertones.

How To

The first step to figuring out what colour suits your hair and skin tone is to determine your skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear greenish, you have warm undertones. If they appear bluish, you have cool undertones. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral undertone.

Next, consider your eye colour. If you have warm undertones and brown, green, or hazel eyes, warm hair colours like golden blonde, caramel, or copper will suit you best. If you have cool undertones and blue or grey eyes, cool hair colours like ash blonde, platinum, or black will look great on you.

Step By Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hair colour for your skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Consider your eye colour
  3. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour
  4. Consult a professional stylist for advice

Tips

  • Consider your lifestyle before choosing a hair colour. If you have a busy schedule, opt for a low-maintenance shade.
  • If you’re unsure about which hair colour to choose, start with a subtle change like highlights or lowlights.
  • Don’t forget to use hair care products that are specifically formulated for coloured hair to maintain its vibrancy and shine.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what colour suits hair and skin tone:

Q: Can I dye my hair a colour that doesn’t complement my skin tone?

A: You can, but it may not look as flattering as a hair colour that complements your skin tone.

Q: How often should I touch up my coloured hair?

A: It depends on the type of colour and your hair growth rate. Consult your stylist for advice.

Hair Care

Coloured hair requires extra care to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips for coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Now that you know what colour suits your hair and skin tone, go ahead and experiment with different shades to find the perfect one for you!
