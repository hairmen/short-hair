How To Get Bouncy Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some bounce and volume to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to get bouncy hair for guys. From hair care tips to styling techniques, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Get Bouncy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Bouncy Hair
  • FAQs

Hair Care

Before we dive into the styling, it’s important to take care of your hair. Healthy hair is the key to achieving bouncy locks. Follow these tips to maintain healthy hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair 2-3 times a week to prevent excessive oil buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair.

2. Use a hair mask

Once a week, use a hair mask to nourish your hair. Apply the mask to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before washing it off. This will help to moisturize your hair and make it more manageable.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools such as hair dryers and straighteners can damage your hair. Try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before styling.

How to Get Bouncy Hair

Now that your hair is healthy, it’s time to add some bounce to it. Follow these steps to achieve bouncy hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with damp hair

After washing your hair, towel dry it until it’s damp. Don’t use a hair dryer as it can make your hair flat.

2. Apply mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your hair. Distribute it evenly from root to tip.

3. Blow-dry your hair

With a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, blow-dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. This will help to create volume and bounce.

4. Finish with hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish it off with a light hairspray. This will help to maintain the volume throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Bouncy Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your bouncy hair:

1. Avoid touching your hair too much

The more you touch your hair, the more it will lose its volume. Avoid playing with it throughout the day.

2. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentle on your hair and prevent it from becoming tangled overnight. This will help to maintain the volume in your hair.

3. Use dry shampoo

If your hair starts to become oily, use dry shampoo instead of washing it. This will help to maintain the volume in your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I use gel instead of mousse?

A: While gel can help to create volume, it can also make your hair sticky and hard. Mousse is a better option for achieving bouncy hair.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment?

A: Using a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment can make your hair flat. It’s best to invest in a diffuser attachment if you want to achieve bouncy hair.

Q: How often should I apply mousse?

A: You only need to apply mousse once a day. Applying too much can make your hair greasy and weigh it down.

Conclusion

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve bouncy hair that will turn heads. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. With a little bit of effort, you can have healthy, voluminous locks that will boost your confidence.
