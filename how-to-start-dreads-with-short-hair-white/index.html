How To Start Dreads With Short Hair White A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a white person with short hair who's always wanted to try out dreadlocks? Well, you're in luck because starting dreads with short hair white is possible and can be achieved with a bit of patience and dedication. In this article, we'll go through the steps to help you get started with your new look.

Description

Dreadlocks are a hairstyle that has been around for centuries, but it has gained more popularity in recent years. It involves allowing hair to mat and form into long, rope-like strands. While traditionally associated with African, Caribbean, and Rastafarian culture, people of all races and backgrounds can rock this hairstyle.

How to Start Dreads with Short Hair White

Starting dreads with short hair white can be a bit challenging, but it's not impossible. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Do Your Research

Before you start, it's essential to do your research and learn everything you can about dreadlocks. This includes the different methods of starting them, the maintenance required, and common mistakes to avoid.

Step 2: Choose Your Method

There are several methods of starting dreads, but the most common ones are the twist and rip method, the backcombing method, and the crochet method. Choose the one that you feel most comfortable with.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oils. It's essential to start with clean hair to avoid any complications during the process.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Using a rat-tail comb, section your hair into small, even sections. The size of the sections will depend on the method you choose and the size of the dreads you want.

Step 5: Start the Process

Start the process by using your chosen method on each section. If you're using the twist and rip method, twist the section of hair and then rip it apart. Repeat this until the entire section is twisted and ripped.

Step 6: Maintain Your Dreads

Once you've started your dreads, it's essential to maintain them properly. This includes regular washing, separating the dreads, and retwisting them when necessary.

Tips

- Be patient. It can take several months for your dreads to fully form.

- Avoid using wax or other products that can cause buildup in your dreads.

- Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your dreads from frizzing.

FAQ

Can You Start Dreads with Short Hair White?

Yes, you can start dreads with short hair white. It may take a bit more time and patience, but it's possible.

How Long Does It Take for Dreads to Fully Form?

It can take anywhere from six months to a year for dreads to fully form.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when it comes to maintaining healthy dreads. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.

- Avoid using heavy products or oils that can cause buildup in your dreads.

- Use a good leave-in conditioner to keep your dreads moisturized.

- Protect your dreads when swimming by wearing a swim cap or tying them up.

Starting dreads with short hair white can be a fun and exciting journey. With the right tools and knowledge, you can achieve the look you want and rock your dreads with confidence.
