How To Know If You Look Better With Light Or Dark Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color is crucial in enhancing your natural beauty. However, with so many hues to choose from, it can be challenging to determine whether light or dark hair suits you better. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to know if you look better with light or dark hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which shade complements your skin tone, eye color, and facial features. In general, people with fair skin and light eyes tend to look better with lighter hair shades, while those with darker skin and eyes suit darker hair colors. However, this is not always the case, and the best way to determine which hue suits you best is by considering your natural coloring and personal style.

How to

Here are some factors to consider when determining whether you look better with light or dark hair:

1. Skin tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining which hair color suits you best. If you have fair skin with cool undertones, you may look better with a lighter hair color, such as platinum blonde or ash brown. On the other hand, if you have warm undertones, you may look better with a darker shade, such as chocolate brown or auburn.

2. Eye color

Your eye color can also help you determine which hair color suits you best. If you have light eyes, such as blue or green, you may look better with a lighter hair color, while those with darker eyes, such as brown or hazel, suit darker hues.

3. Facial features

Your facial features also play a role in determining which hair color is best for you. If you have delicate features, such as a small nose and chin, you may look better with lighter hair, while those with more prominent features, such as a strong jawline or high cheekbones, suit darker hair colors.

4. Personal style

Ultimately, the best hair color for you is the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Consider your personal style when choosing a hair color, and opt for a shade that complements your wardrobe and overall aesthetic.

Step by step

Here are the steps to follow when determining which hair color suits you best:

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones, while green veins indicate warm undertones. If you can't determine whether your veins are blue or green, you may have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider your eye color

Look at your eyes in natural light and determine whether they are light or dark in color.

Step 3: Analyze your facial features

Consider the shape of your face and the size of your nose, chin, and cheekbones.

Step 4: Choose a hair color

Based on your skin tone, eye color, and facial features, choose a hair color that complements your natural coloring and personal style.

Tips

  • Consider trying on wigs or using hair color apps to visualize different shades before committing to a new hair color.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine which hair color suits you best.
  • Remember to consider your lifestyle and maintenance preferences when choosing a hair color.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair a different color if I don't like how it looks?

A: Yes, you can always change your hair color if you're not happy with the result. However, keep in mind that frequent hair dyeing can damage your hair, so it's important to take proper hair care measures to maintain healthy hair.

Q: Can I pull off both light and dark hair colors?

A: Yes, some people can pull off both light and dark hair colors. Consider experimenting with different shades to determine which one suits you best.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's essential to take proper hair care measures to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of natural oils and cause color fading.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair and cause color fading.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and taking into consideration your natural coloring and personal style, you can determine whether light or dark hair suits you best. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist if you're unsure which hair color to choose.
