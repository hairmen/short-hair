How To Do Pin Up Hairstyles For Long Hair

Written by Robby Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for an elegant and classic hairstyle for your long hair? Look no further than pin-up hairstyles! Whether you're attending a formal event or just want to add some vintage flair to your everyday look, pin-up hairstyles are a great choice. Keep reading to learn how to create pin-up hairstyles for long hair.

Description

Pin-up hairstyles are a classic and elegant hairstyle that has been popular since the 1940s. This style is known for its voluminous curls and classic vintage look. Pin-up hairstyles can be created in a variety of ways, from sleek and polished to messy and tousled. With a little bit of practice and some styling tools, you can create a pin-up hairstyle that is perfect for you.

How to

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before you start styling your hair, you need to prepare it first. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. Then, apply a styling mousse or volumizing spray to your hair to give it extra hold and volume.

Step 2: Curl your hair

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to start curling. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create large, loose curls. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, curling small sections of hair at a time. Once all of your hair is curled, let it cool for a few minutes.

Step 3: Brush out your curls

Once your curls have cooled, use a soft-bristle brush to gently brush out your curls. This will create a soft, voluminous look that is perfect for pin-up hairstyles.

Step 4: Create a Victory Roll

A Victory Roll is a classic pin-up hairstyle that is perfect for long hair. To create a Victory Roll, take a section of hair from the front of your head and roll it towards the back of your head. Pin it in place with bobby pins or hair clips. Repeat on the other side of your head.

Step 5: Add finishing touches

Once your Victory Rolls are in place, it's time to add some finishing touches. Use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place, or add a hair accessory like a flower or headband for a touch of vintage glamour.

Tips

  • Practice makes perfect! Pin-up hairstyles can be tricky, so don't be discouraged if it takes a few tries to get it right.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage when using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the pin-up hairstyle that is perfect for you.

FAQ

What length of hair is best for pin-up hairstyles?

Pin-up hairstyles can be created on any length of hair, but long hair is ideal. The longer your hair, the more voluminous and dramatic your pin-up hairstyle will be.

Can I create pin-up hairstyles without heat styling?

While heat styling tools like curling irons and hot rollers are the most effective way to create pin-up hairstyles, you can also create this look using braids or twist-outs. Simply braid or twist your hair while it's damp, let it dry completely, and then remove the braids or twists for a soft, voluminous look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Additionally, make sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to create pin-up hairstyles for long hair, you're ready to rock this classic and elegant look. With a little bit of practice and some styling tools, you can create a pin-up hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion.
