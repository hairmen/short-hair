Easy Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair School - Tips and Tricks in 2023

If you're looking for some easy hairstyles for medium length hair to wear to school, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll be sharing some quick and simple hairstyles that you can do on your own without breaking a sweat. Whether you're running late or just want to switch up your look, these hairstyles will have you looking your best in no time.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium length hair is a popular choice for many young girls because it's versatile and easy to manage. However, finding the perfect hairstyle for school can be a challenge. That's why we've put together a list of easy hairstyles that you can do in minutes. These hairstyles are perfect for school because they're simple, stylish, and won't get in the way of your studies.

How to

Learning how to do easy hairstyles for medium length hair is a must for any student. To get started, you'll need some basic hair tools like a brush, hair ties, and bobby pins. You'll also need some hair products like hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place. Once you have your tools and products ready, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some easy hairstyles for medium length hair:

1. Half-up Half-down

- Brush your hair and gather the top half of your hair.

- Secure with a hair tie.

- Optional: tease the crown of your hair for added volume.

- Release the bottom half of your hair.

- Optional: curl the ends of your hair for added texture.

2. Low Ponytail

- Brush your hair and gather it into a low ponytail.

- Secure with a hair tie.

- Optional: wrap a section of hair around the hair tie to hide it.

3. Messy Bun

- Brush your hair and gather it into a high ponytail.

- Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to form a bun.

- Secure with bobby pins.

- Pull out some pieces of hair around your face for a messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing easy hairstyles for medium length hair:

  • Use hair products sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands or clips to add some flair to your hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and have fun with your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for medium length hair:

1. Can I do these hairstyles on my own?

Yes, these hairstyles are designed to be easy to do on your own.

2. How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

Most of these hairstyles can be done in under 5 minutes.

3. Can I wear these hairstyles to formal events?

While these hairstyles are perfect for school, they may not be suitable for formal events. You may want to opt for more sophisticated hairstyles for formal events.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons or straighteners too often.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

With these easy hairstyles for medium length hair, you'll be able to switch up your look and feel confident every day. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and have fun with your hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
