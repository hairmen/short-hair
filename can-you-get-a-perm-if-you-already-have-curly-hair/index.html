Can You Get A Perm If You Already Have Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you may be wondering if you can get a perm. After all, a perm is designed to add curl to straight hair, so it may seem counterintuitive to get a perm if you already have curly hair. The good news is that it is possible to get a perm if you have curly hair, but there are some things you should consider before making the decision to do so.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that is used to add curl or wave to straight hair. The process involves applying a solution to the hair that breaks down the bonds that give hair its shape. The hair is then wrapped around rods and a neutralizer is applied to set the new curl pattern. The result is curly or wavy hair that can last several months.

How To

If you have curly hair and are considering getting a perm, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

1. Determine Your Hair Type

Before getting a perm, it's important to determine your hair type. If you have thick, coarse hair, a perm may not be the best option for you. On the other hand, if you have fine or thin hair, a perm may be a good way to add volume and texture.

2. Consult with a Professional

It's important to consult with a professional hairstylist before getting a perm. They can help you determine if a perm is right for your hair type and recommend the best type of perm for your hair.

3. Consider the Maintenance

Permed hair requires regular maintenance to keep the curls looking their best. This includes regular trims to prevent split ends and using a deep conditioner to keep the hair hydrated.

Step by Step

The process of getting a perm involves several steps:

1. Consultation

Your hairstylist will examine your hair and determine if a perm is appropriate for your hair type. They will also recommend the best type of perm for your hair.

2. Preparation

Your hairstylist will wash your hair and apply a protective solution to your scalp and hairline to prevent irritation from the perm solution.

3. Perm Solution

The perm solution will be applied to your hair, and your hair will be wrapped around rods to set the new curl pattern. The solution will be left on your hair for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and the desired results.

4. Neutralizer

The neutralizer is applied to your hair to stop the chemical process and set the new curl pattern. It will be left on your hair for a specific amount of time before being rinsed out.

5. Styling

Your hairstylist will remove the rods from your hair and style your hair as desired. They may also recommend products to help maintain your new curls.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind if you decide to get a perm:

- Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

- Avoid using heat styling tools on your permed hair.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can you get a perm if you already have curly hair?

Yes, it's possible to get a perm if you have curly hair. However, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist before making the decision to do so.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last several months, but the exact length of time will depend on your hair type, the type of perm you get, and how well you take care of your hair.

Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least a week after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical process of the perm.

Hair Care

If you decide to get a perm, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep the curls looking their best. This includes using a deep conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, getting regular trims to prevent split ends, and avoiding heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

Overall, getting a perm if you have curly hair is possible, but it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist before making the decision to do so. With the right care and maintenance, permed hair can look beautiful and add volume and texture to your look.
