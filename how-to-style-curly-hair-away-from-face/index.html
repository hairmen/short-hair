How To Style Curly Hair Away From Face A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful, it can also be difficult to style. One of the biggest challenges that curly hair presents is keeping it away from your face. However, with the right techniques and products, you can easily style your curly hair away from your face. In this guide, we will show you how to style curly hair away from face in relaxed English language, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Style Curly Hair Away from Face

There are several ways to style curly hair away from your face. Here are some of the most popular methods:

  • Pull your hair back into a ponytail
  • Create a half-up, half-down hairstyle
  • Braid your hair
  • Twist your hair into a bun
  • Pin your hair back with bobby pins

Each of these methods can be customized to suit your personal style, and they all work well for keeping curly hair away from your face.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for each of the methods listed above:

Ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head, leaving some hair loose around your face.
  3. Secure your hair with an elastic band.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  5. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-up, Half-down:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and pull it back, leaving some hair loose around your face.
  3. Secure the section of hair with an elastic band.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  5. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Braid:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three sections.
  3. Begin braiding your hair, starting at the top of your head.
  4. Braid until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Secure the braid with an elastic band.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  7. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Twist:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun at the back of your head.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  5. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Bobby Pins:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Part your hair on one side.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and pin it back with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  6. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair Away from Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling curly hair away from your face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to help hold your style in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize with headbands, hair clips, or scarves to add some extra flair to your style.
  • Be gentle when handling your hair to avoid causing damage or frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling curly hair away from face:

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair using a flat iron or a hair straightening treatment. However, keep in mind that frequent heat styling can damage your hair over time.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash their hair once or twice a week.

Q: What is the best way to dry curly hair?

A: The best way to dry curly hair is to air dry it, or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires some special attention. Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style your curly hair away from your face and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
