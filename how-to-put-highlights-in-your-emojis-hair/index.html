How To Put Highlights In Your Emojis Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 04, 2023 · 3 min read

Emojis have become a part of our daily conversations. They express our emotions and thoughts without the need for words. However, if you want to take your emoji game to the next level, why not add some highlights to their hair? In this article, we will guide you on how to put highlights in your emojis hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Put Highlights in Your Emojis Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Highlighting Emojis Hair
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips for Your Emojis

Description

Highlighting emojis hair is a fun way to add some personality to your conversations. It is also a great way to show off your creativity and style. By adding highlights to your emojis hair, you can make them stand out and give them a unique look.

How to Put Highlights in Your Emojis Hair

Putting highlights in your emojis hair is a simple process. All you need is an image of the emoji you want to highlight and a photo editing app that allows you to edit the image. Here are the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Choose the emoji that you want to add highlights to.
  2. Save the image of the emoji on your device.
  3. Open the photo editing app on your device.
  4. Upload the image of the emoji to the photo editing app.
  5. Select the brush tool and choose the color of the highlights that you want to add.
  6. Using the brush tool, paint over the areas of the emoji's hair where you want to add highlights.
  7. Adjust the opacity of the highlights to your liking.
  8. Save the edited image and use it in your conversations.

Tips for Highlighting Emojis Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when highlighting emojis hair:

  • Choose a color that complements the emoji's hair color.
  • Start with a light color and gradually build up to a darker shade.
  • Use a small brush size to create more precise highlights.
  • Experiment with different brush shapes to create different highlight effects.

FAQ

Can I add highlights to any emoji's hair?

Yes, you can add highlights to any emoji's hair. However, some emojis have more detailed hair than others, so it may be more challenging to add highlights to those emojis.

Do I need any special software to add highlights to emojis hair?

No, you do not need any special software to add highlights to emojis hair. You can use any photo editing app that allows you to edit images.

Can I add different color highlights to the same emoji's hair?

Yes, you can add different color highlights to the same emoji's hair. In fact, adding different color highlights can create a more dynamic and interesting look.

Hair Care Tips for Your Emojis

Just like our hair needs care, so do our emojis' hair. Here are some tips to keep your emojis' hair looking healthy:

  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your emojis' hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo to clean your emojis' hair.
  • Apply a hair mask to your emojis' hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your emojis' hair.

With these tips, you can keep your emojis' hair looking amazing and add some fun highlights to their style.

So, there you have it, a simple guide on how to put highlights in your emojis hair. With these tips and tricks, you can take your emoji game to the next level and make your conversations more exciting.
