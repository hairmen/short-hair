Mid Length Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by William Nov 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 60 and wear glasses? Do you struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your age and glasses? Look no further! Here are some mid length hairstyles for over 60 with glasses that will make you look and feel fantastic.

Description

Mid length hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 with glasses. They provide enough length to style and add volume, while still being easy to maintain. These hairstyles can also help to frame your face and highlight your features, especially if you have a pair of stylish glasses that you want to show off.

How to

Before you start styling your mid length hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a comb, a hair dryer, a round brush, and some styling products like hairspray or mousse.

Start by washing your hair and towel drying it. Use a comb to detangle your hair and then apply a small amount of mousse to add volume. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape. If you have curly hair, you can use a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.

Once your hair is dry, you can add some finishing touches with a curling iron or straightener. Use hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.

2. Apply a small amount of mousse to add volume.

3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.

4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add finishing touches.

5. Use hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

- Opt for layers to add volume and dimension to your hair.

- Consider bangs to frame your face and draw attention to your glasses.

- Choose a hairstyle that complements your glasses and face shape.

- Use styling products to add volume and hold your style in place.

FAQ

Q: What are some good mid length hairstyles for women over 60 with glasses?

A: Some good mid length hairstyles include layered cuts, shaggy hairstyles, and hairstyles with side-swept bangs.

Q: How can I style my mid length hair with glasses?

A: You can style your mid length hair with glasses by adding volume, using a curling iron or straightener, and choosing a hairstyle that complements your glasses and face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your mid length hair looking healthy and shiny, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray before using styling tools. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your style.

With these mid length hairstyles for over 60 with glasses, you can feel confident and stylish no matter your age or glasses. Try out different styles and find the one that suits you best.
