What Is A Regular Fade Haircut

Written by Petter Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you curious about the latest hair trend for men? If yes, then you might have heard about the regular fade haircut. In this article, we will discuss what a regular fade haircut is, how to get one, and some hair care tips to maintain it.

Description

A fade haircut is a popular hairstyle for men that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter from the top to the sides and back. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on how short you want your hair to be. A regular fade haircut, also known as a low fade, is a type of fade that starts from the hairline and gradually tapers down to the ear. It creates a clean and sharp look that is perfect for any occasion.

How to Get a Regular Fade Haircut

If you want to get a regular fade haircut, you can either do it yourself or go to a professional barber. However, it is recommended to go to a barber who has experience in doing fades to achieve the best results. Here are the steps to get a regular fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Length

Decide on the length of your hair on top and how short you want it to be on the sides and back. You can show a picture to your barber as a reference.

Step 2: Start Cutting

The barber will start cutting your hair with a clipper, starting from the nape of your neck and gradually moving upwards. They will use different guard sizes to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

The barber will blend the hair on the sides and back using a comb and scissors. They will create a smooth transition from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Finally, the barber will style your hair using a hair gel or wax. You can ask them for recommendations on what product to use based on your hair type.

Tips to Maintain a Regular Fade Haircut

Now that you have a regular fade haircut, you need to maintain it to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a touch-up.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair product to keep your hair in place.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you go swimming.

FAQ

What is the difference between a regular fade and a high fade?

A regular fade starts from the hairline and tapers down to the ear, while a high fade starts from the temple and tapers down to the back of the neck.

Is a regular fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a regular fade can work for all hair types, but the length of the hair on top and the type of product used may vary.

Can I do a regular fade haircut at home?

Yes, you can do a regular fade haircut at home, but it may require some practice and the right tools.

Hair Care

A regular fade haircut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and maintain. By following the tips mentioned above, you can keep your hair looking sharp and stylish. Remember to visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and to try out different hair products to find the one that works best for you.

Don't be afraid to experiment with your hair and try out different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. A regular fade haircut is a great option for men who want a clean and polished look that is both timeless and trendy.
