How Do You Do A Messy Bun Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance yet chic hairstyle, a messy bun is the perfect solution. It's effortless, versatile, and can be worn for any occasion. In this blog post, we'll show you how to create a messy bun hairstyle in just a few easy steps. Let's get started!

How To

Before we start, make sure you have the following tools: a comb, a hair tie, bobby pins, and hairspray. Now, let's begin:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. If you have greasy hair, you can apply dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add texture. This will help make the messy bun look more voluminous.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair

Gather all your hair in a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Make sure to position the ponytail where you want your bun to be - the higher the ponytail, the higher the bun.

Step 3: Create The Bun

Twist the ponytail around the hair tie until you reach the end of the hair. Then, wrap the twisted hair around the base of the ponytail to form a bun. You can make it as loose or as tight as you want, depending on the look you're going for.

Step 4: Secure The Bun

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Pin the hair in different directions to create a messy effect. Don't worry if some pieces fall out - it's all part of the messy bun charm!

Step 5: Finish With Hairspray

Spray some hairspray over your hair to keep the bun in place and prevent flyaways. You can also use your fingers to pull out some strands of hair to create a more undone look.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your messy bun look even better:

  • For a more textured look, use a sea salt spray before tying your hair.
  • For a more polished look, use a smoothing serum to tame any frizz.
  • If you have short hair, you can still do a messy bun by using a hair donut or hair extensions to create more volume.
  • Experiment with different bun shapes and sizes to find what works best with your face shape and hair length.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy buns:

Q: Can I do a messy bun with curly hair?

A: Absolutely! Messy buns work well with all hair types and textures. You can embrace your curls and create a more natural-looking bun.

Q: Is a messy bun appropriate for formal occasions?

A: While messy buns are commonly associated with casual looks, you can still make them work for formal events. Opt for a sleeker bun with less flyaways and accessorize with statement earrings or a headband.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

With these steps and tips, you can easily create a messy bun hairstyle that looks effortless and chic. Whether you're running errands or attending a wedding, this hairstyle is the perfect go-to. Give it a try and see how it transforms your look!
