The first step in choosing the right haircut for wavy thick hair is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes require different haircuts to achieve the most flattering look. Once youâve determined your face shape, consider the texture and thickness of your hair. Hair Care Tips for Wavy Thick Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your wavy thick hair looking its best:
