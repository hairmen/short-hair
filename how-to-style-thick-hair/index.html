How To Style Thick Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's luxurious, voluminous, and full of life. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage, style, and maintain. If you have thick hair, you know the struggle of finding the right hairstyle that won't make you look like a poodle. In this article, we'll go over some tips, tricks, and hair care for styling thick hair.

How to Style Thick Hair

When it comes to styling thick hair, the key is to find a cut that complements your hair's natural texture and thickness. Whether you have long locks or a short bob, there are plenty of styles that can work for you. Here are a few ideas:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to add movement and shape to your thick hair. This style works well for those who want to keep their locks long but still want to add some dimension.

2. Bob with Bangs

If you're looking for a shorter style, a bob with bangs can be a great choice for thick hair. This style can help to frame your face and add some texture to your locks.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring choice for those with thick hair. This style can help to showcase your natural texture and thickness while also being easy to manage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some ideas for your hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide to styling your thick hair:

1. Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for thick hair. This will help to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

2. Towel Dry

Once you're out of the shower, gently towel dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

3. Apply Products

Apply a styling product that is designed for thick hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

4. Blow Dry

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using a round brush to add volume and shape. Make sure to dry your hair in sections to ensure that you get all of your locks dry.

5. Style

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tool to achieve your desired look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for styling your thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Don't be afraid to use heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if it's thick?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if it's thick. Just make sure to use a straightener that is designed for thick hair, and use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage.

Q: How often should I wash my thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but as a general rule, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

To keep your thick hair healthy and strong, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use products that are designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

With these tips, tricks, and hair care, you'll be able to style your thick locks with ease. Embrace your natural texture and thickness, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
