How To Cut Curly Hair Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

If you have curly hair and want to try out the trendy curtain bangs hairstyle, you might be wondering how to go about it. Cutting curly hair can be tricky, but it's not impossible. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut your own curly hair into curtain bangs.

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before cutting your hair, you'll want to make sure it's clean and dry. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would and let it air dry or blow dry it on a low heat setting.

You'll also want to make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to help section your hair.

How to Section Your Hair

Sectioning your hair is important when cutting bangs to ensure you get an even cut. Start by parting your hair down the middle, from your hairline to the nape of your neck. Then, using your comb, section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.

Clip the rest of your hair out of the way so it doesn't get in the way while you're cutting.

How to Cut Your Bangs

Now it's time to start cutting. Hold the section of hair you want to cut straight out from your head, and use your scissors to trim the ends. Start by cutting a little bit at a time, and remember that you can always cut more later.

When cutting curly hair, it's important to cut in small sections to avoid cutting too much at once.

Once you've cut your bangs to the desired length, you can use your scissors to add some texture by cutting into the ends at an angle. This will help blend the bangs into the rest of your hair.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair can be tricky, so here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Cut your hair dry, not wet, so you can see how it will look when styled
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Cut in small sections to avoid taking off too much at once
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure about cutting your own hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my curly hair into curtain bangs if I have a round face?

Yes! Curtain bangs can look great on any face shape. Just make sure your bangs are long enough to frame your face.

How do I know if curtain bangs will suit me?

You can use an online virtual hairstyle simulator to see how different hairstyles will look on you. You can also ask your hairstylist for advice.

Can I cut my bangs while they're wet?

It's best to cut your hair dry so you can see how it will look when styled. Wet hair can look longer than it actually is, so it's best to avoid cutting it while wet.

How to Style Your Curtain Bangs

To style your curtain bangs, you can use a small amount of hair gel or mousse to define your curls. You can also blow dry them straight if you prefer.

How Often to Trim Your Curtain Bangs

How often you need to trim your curtain bangs will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look too long. On average, you'll need to trim them every 4-6 weeks.

How to Maintain Your Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair looking its best, you'll want to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated.

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Conclusion

Cutting your own curly hair into curtain bangs can be a fun way to change up your look. Just remember to take it slow, cut in small sections, and use the right tools. With a little practice, you'll be able to master the art of cutting curly hair.
