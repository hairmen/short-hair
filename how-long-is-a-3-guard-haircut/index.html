How Long Is A 3 Guard Haircut

Written by William Nov 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're wondering how long a 3 guard haircut is, you're not alone. Many people are confused about the different guard sizes and how they correspond to hair length. In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about a 3 guard haircut, including how long it is and how to achieve the look.

Description

A 3 guard haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, buzzed hairstyle. It's also commonly used in barbershops as a base for longer hairstyles. The 3 guard is the third shortest guard size, measuring 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm in length. It's shorter than a 2 guard but longer than a 4 guard. The 3 guard is ideal for creating a clean, uniform look, and it's suitable for all hair types.

How to Achieve a 3 Guard Haircut

If you want to achieve a 3 guard haircut, you can do it yourself at home or visit a barber. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Before you start, you'll need a pair of clippers with a 3 guard attachment. Make sure the clippers are fully charged and oiled to ensure a smooth cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and towel dry it before starting. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can clog the clippers.

Step 3: Attach the 3 Guard

Attach the 3 guard to your clippers and turn them on. Start at the back of your head and work your way up to the top. Use short, even strokes to ensure a uniform cut.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

Once you've trimmed the top, move on to the sides and back. Use the same technique, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use a lower guard size, such as a 2 guard, to blend the hair between the sides and the top. Use short, upward strokes to create a seamless transition.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use a trimmer or razor to clean up the neckline and around the ears for a polished finish.

Tips for Maintaining a 3 Guard Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining a 3 guard haircut:

  • Trim your hair every 2-3 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

How long is a 3 guard haircut?

A 3 guard haircut measures 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm in length.

Is a 3 guard haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a 3 guard haircut is suitable for all hair types.

Can I achieve a 3 guard haircut at home?

Yes, you can achieve a 3 guard haircut at home with the right tools and technique.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding harsh styling products. If you're experiencing hair loss or other hair problems, consult a dermatologist or hair specialist for advice and treatment.

In conclusion, a 3 guard haircut is a versatile and stylish option for men who want a short, buzzed hairstyle. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve this look at home or in the barbershop. Follow our tips for maintaining your haircut and keeping your hair healthy and strong.
