Are Perms In Style 2020

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Perms have been in and out of style for decades, but the question remains: are perms in style in 2020? In this blog post, we will explore the latest trends in perms and help you decide whether a perm is right for you.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that adds curls or waves to straight hair. The process involves wrapping hair around rods and applying a solution to break down the hair's natural structure, allowing it to be reshaped. Perms can give your hair a new texture and add volume, but they can also damage your hair if not done correctly.

How to get a perm

If you're considering getting a perm, here are the steps you can expect:

  1. Consult with a stylist to determine if your hair is healthy enough for a perm.
  2. Choose the size of the rods you want to use to create your curls or waves.
  3. Your stylist will apply a solution to your hair to break down the natural structure.
  4. Your hair will be wrapped around the rods and left to set for a period of time.
  5. The rods will be removed, and your hair will be rinsed and neutralized.
  6. Your stylist will style your new curls or waves.

It's important to choose a reputable stylist and salon for your perm to ensure that the process is done correctly and safely.

Tips for getting a perm

If you decide to get a perm, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a size of rods that will give you the desired curl or wave pattern.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair for the first 48 hours after getting a perm to allow the curls to set.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

Are perms damaging to hair?

Perms can be damaging to hair if not done correctly. It's important to choose a reputable stylist and salon and to follow the proper aftercare instructions.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on your hair type and the size of the rods used.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least 2 weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair to avoid damaging it further.

Hair care after getting a perm

After getting a perm, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and maintain your new curls or waves. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for the first 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for permed hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.

In conclusion, perms can be a fun and stylish way to change up your hair, but it's important to consider the potential damage and to choose a reputable stylist and salon. With proper care, you can enjoy your new curls or waves for months to come.
