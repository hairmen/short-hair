Women's Short Hair 2021: A Guide to Rocking the Trend

Short hair has always been a popular trend among women, and it's no different in 2021. If you're thinking of chopping off your locks, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through everything you need to know about women's short hair 2021. From how to style it to hair care tips, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

How to Cut Women's Short Hair 2021

Before you cut your hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Short hair doesn't work for everyone, but if you have the right face shape and hair texture, it can be a great look. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for you. Some popular cuts for women's short hair 2021 include the pixie cut, bob, and lob (long bob).

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that's been around for decades. It's a short, layered cut that's perfect for women with oval or heart-shaped faces. This cut is low-maintenance and looks great with a bit of texture or waves.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a versatile haircut that can be customized to fit any face shape or hair texture. It's a short to medium length cut that's perfect for women who want to show off their neck and shoulders. The bob cut can be styled straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your preference.

Lob (Long Bob)

The lob is a longer version of the bob cut, typically reaching just below the shoulders. It's a great option for women who want to keep some length but still have a short, stylish haircut. The lob can be styled straight or with waves for a more relaxed look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Women's Short Hair 2021

Styling short hair can be easy and fun. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling women's short hair 2021:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Use a towel to gently pat your hair dry.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product to your hair to add texture and hold. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, style your hair to your desired look. Use a round brush to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a flat iron or curling iron. Add waves or curls for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Maintaining Women's Short Hair 2021

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Tip 2: Use Heat Protectant

Before using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Experiment with Products

Try different products to find what works best for your hair type and styling needs. Don't be afraid to mix and match products to achieve your desired look.

Frequently Asked Questions about Women's Short Hair 2021

Q: Will short hair look good on me?

A: Short hair can look good on anyone, but it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture before choosing a cut.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: Short hair can be washed every other day or as needed. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Use bobby pins and hair ties to create a variety of styles.

Hair Care Tips for Women's Short Hair 2021

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and stylish:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that's free of harsh chemicals to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Styling

Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage and breakage. Let your hair air dry as often as possible.

With these tips and tricks, you're ready to rock the women's short hair 2021 trend. Whether you choose a pixie cut, bob, or lob, make sure to consult with your hairstylist to find the best cut for you. Happy styling!
