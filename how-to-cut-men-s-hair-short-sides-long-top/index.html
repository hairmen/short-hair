How To Cut Men s Hair Short Sides Long Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 08, 2023 · 3 min read
If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle, the short sides long top look is a great option. This style can be customized to suit different hair types and face shapes, making it a popular choice for men of all ages. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's hair short sides long top, step by step.

Description

The short sides long top hairstyle is characterized by shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top of the head. This style can be achieved with different haircuts, such as an undercut, fade, or taper. The length of the hair on the top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference. This hairstyle is low maintenance and can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, textured, or messy.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, clippers, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide how long you want the hair on the top to be. Use the clippers to start cutting the sides, starting with a longer guard and gradually decreasing the length.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Top

Once you've cut the sides, use the scissors to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top. Start by combing the hair on the top forward and cutting it to the desired length.

Step 4: Texturize the Top

To add texture to the hair on the top, use the scissors to point cut the ends. This will create a choppy and layered look.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once you've cut the hair, style it according to your preference. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to achieve the desired look.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard on the clippers and gradually decrease the length to avoid cutting too much hair at once. - Use a comb to ensure the hair is even and straight while cutting. - Take breaks to step back and assess the haircut to ensure it's balanced.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair short sides long top?

Yes, you can cut your own hair short sides long top. However, it's recommended to have a friend or family member help you to ensure an even cut.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. On average, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the short sides long top look.

Hair Care

To maintain your short sides long top haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much product. Additionally, protect your hair from heat by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. In conclusion, the short sides long top haircut is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that's easy to maintain. By following our step-by-step guide, you can achieve this look at home or with the help of a professional. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain the style with regular haircuts.
