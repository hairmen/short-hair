How to Choose the Right Hair Style for Your Round Face

The first step in finding the perfect hair style for your round face is to determine your face shape. Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a wider forehead. The goal of your hair style is to create a balance and elongate your face shape.

When choosing a hair style, consider styles that add height to the crown of your head and have length around the face. Avoid styles that add volume at the sides of your face or have a straight fringe that cuts across your forehead.

Layered haircuts, bobs, and hairstyles with volume at the crown and length around the face are the most flattering for round face women. Let's explore some of the best hair styles for round face women in 2023.

The Classic Bob Haircut

The classic bob haircut is a timeless style that is perfect for round face women. This style is short, with the hair cut to just below the chin. The bob can be styled with or without bangs and can be worn straight or with a slight wave.

If you have a round face, opt for a longer bob that falls just below your chin. This will add length to your face and create a more elongated shape. Avoid short bobs that end at your chin or above, as they will accentuate the roundness of your face.

Layered Haircut with Bangs

A layered haircut with bangs is another great option for round face women. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, while the bangs draw attention to the eyes and away from the roundness of the face.

If you have a round face, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other. This will create an angled look that elongates your face shape. Avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead, as they will make your face appear shorter and rounder.

The Pixie Haircut

The pixie haircut is a bold and daring style that is perfect for round face women who want to make a statement. This style is short and cropped close to the head, with layers and texture throughout.

If you have a round face, opt for a pixie cut with longer layers on top that can be styled to add height and elongate your face shape. Avoid short, blunt pixie cuts that accentuate the roundness of your face.

Updo Hairstyles for Round Face Women

Updo hairstyles are a great option for special occasions or when you want to create a more polished look. There are many updo styles that are flattering for round face women, including chignons, braids, and messy buns.

If you have a round face, opt for updo styles that add height to the crown of your head and have length around the face. Avoid styles that are too tight or sleek, as they will accentuate the roundness of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Women

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hair style to look its best. Here are some hair care tips for round face women:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

FAQs About Hair Styles for Round Face Women

Q: Can round face women wear short hair?

A: Yes, round face women can wear short hair. However, it is important to choose a style that adds height to the crown of the head and has length around the face to create an elongated shape.

Q: What hair color is best for round face women?

A: There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as the best hair color for round face women depends on skin tone and personal preference. However, highlights or lowlights can add dimension to the hair and create a more elongated shape.

Q: Should round face women avoid bangs?

A: No, round face women can wear bangs. However, it is important to choose a style that is longer on one side than the other to create an angled look that elongates the face shape.

With these tips and styles, you're sure to find the perfect hair style for your round face in 2023. Remember to choose styles that add height to the crown of your head and have length around the face to create an elongated shape. Happy styling!
