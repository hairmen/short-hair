How To Cut Male Hair With Scissors Only A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save a few bucks or just prefer to maintain your own hair, learning how to cut male hair with scissors only can be a valuable skill. With a bit of practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting male hair with scissors only.

How to Cut Male Hair with Scissors Only

Before we dive into the steps, there are a few things you'll need to get started:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape to protect clothing

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a great haircut:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a more even cut.
  2. Comb the hair in the direction it naturally falls, and create a part if desired.
  3. Begin by trimming the hair on the sides and back. Use the comb to lift a section of hair, and cut the ends with the scissors. Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length. Repeat on the other side.
  4. If you prefer a fade or tapered look, switch to clippers to blend the hair from the shortest length to the longest length. Use different guard sizes for a more gradual fade.
  5. Move to the top of the head and trim the ends to the desired length. Use the comb to lift sections of hair and cut straight across with the scissors. Be sure to cut evenly and avoid taking off too much at once.
  6. To add texture or layers, take small sections of hair and point cut with the scissors. This will create a choppy, textured look.
  7. Trim the hairline around the ears and neck. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut the excess with the scissors. Be sure to cut in small sections and avoid cutting too close to the skin.
  8. Check for any uneven spots or long strands, and trim as necessary.
  9. Style the hair as desired using products such as gel or pomade.
  10. Clean up any hair clippings and wash the scissors to keep them sharp and ready for next time.

Tips for Cutting Male Hair with Scissors Only

Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length to avoid taking off too much at once.
  • Use the comb to lift sections of hair and cut straight across with the scissors. This will ensure an even cut.
  • Take breaks if needed to avoid getting frustrated or tired.
  • If you're unsure about cutting a certain area, leave it for a professional barber to handle.
  • Invest in a good pair of scissors that are sharp and easy to handle.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting male hair with scissors only:

Can I achieve a professional-looking haircut at home?

Yes, with practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Try to fix it with additional snips or leave it for a professional barber to handle.

Do I need special scissors to cut hair?

Yes, it's important to invest in a good pair of scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. These scissors are sharp and easy to handle, making the cutting process much easier.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and attractive look. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition regularly to keep hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling, as this can damage the hair.
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain shape.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep hair healthy and strong.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cut male hair with scissors only and achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Remember to take your time, start with small snips, and invest in a good pair of scissors. Happy cutting!
