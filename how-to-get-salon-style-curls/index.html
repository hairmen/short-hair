How To Get Salon Style Curls At Home

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Salon style curls are a classic look that never goes out of style. Whether you have an important event or just want to switch up your everyday look, knowing how to achieve salon style curls can be a game changer. In this article, we’ll go over step by step instructions on how to get salon style curls at home.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Salon Style Curls
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips for Achieving Perfect Curls
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Healthy Curls

How to Get Salon Style Curls

Before we dive into the step by step instructions, it’s important to note that there are a few things you’ll need. First, you’ll need a curling iron or wand. Second, you’ll need a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Third, you’ll need a styling product to help hold your curls in place. Now let’s get started!

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

It’s best to start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it’s more prone to damage from the heat of the curling iron. Make sure to blow dry your hair before you start curling.

2. Apply a heat protectant spray.

Before you start curling your hair, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray. This will help prevent damage to your hair from the heat of the curling iron. Simply spray the product onto your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that are prone to damage.

3. Section your hair.

Section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to curl each section and ensure that all of your hair is evenly curled.

4. Curl your hair.

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, take a small section and wrap it around the barrel of your curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

5. Apply a styling product.

Once you’ve finished curling your hair, apply a styling product to help hold your curls in place. You can use a hairspray, texturizing spray, or any other styling product that works for your hair type.

Step by Step

Here’s a step by step breakdown of how to get salon style curls:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Section your hair.
  4. Curl your hair.
  5. Apply a styling product.

Tips for Achieving Perfect Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect salon style curls:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a barrel size that works for your hair length and thickness.
  • Don’t curl your hair too tightly. This can make your curls look unnatural.
  • Use a lower heat setting on your curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don’t brush your curls out too much. This can make your curls lose their shape.
  • Use a styling product to help hold your curls in place.

FAQs

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This depends on your hair type and the styling product you use. Generally, your curls should last for several hours.

Q: Can I curl my hair if it’s wet?

A: It’s not recommended to curl your hair if it’s wet. This can cause damage to your hair from the heat of the curling iron.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It’s best to avoid curling your hair every day. This can cause damage to your hair over time. Try to limit curling to once or twice a week.

Hair Care for Healthy Curls

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Don’t brush your curls out too much.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

With these tips and step by step instructions, you’ll be able to achieve salon style curls at home. Remember to take care of your hair to keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful.
