What Is A Number 3 Haircut In Inches

Written by Luffy Oct 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering what is a number 3 haircut in inches? If you're new to haircuts, it can be confusing to understand the different clipper guard sizes. A number 3 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, but not too short, hairstyle. This guide will explain everything you need to know about getting a number 3 haircut in inches.

Description

A number 3 haircut is achieved using a hair clipper with a guard size of 3/8 inch or 9.5mm. This means that the hair will be cut to a length of 3/8 inch or 9.5mm all over the head. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways and suits different face shapes and hair types.

How to

If you're planning to get a number 3 haircut, you can either do it yourself or go to a barber. Here are the steps to do it yourself:

  1. Choose a good quality hair clipper with a number 3 guard attachment.
  2. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  3. Attach the number 3 guard to the clipper and turn it on.
  4. Start at the back of your head and move the clipper up towards the crown of your head. Repeat until you've covered the entire back and sides.
  5. Move to the top of your head and use the clipper to cut the hair to the same length as the sides.
  6. Trim any longer hairs around the hairline with scissors or a trimmer.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

If you're not confident to do it yourself, it's best to go to a barber who can give you a professional cut.

Step by step

Here's a step by step guide to getting a number 3 haircut at a barber:

  1. Make an appointment with a barber who's experienced in men's haircuts.
  2. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and explain that you want a number 3 haircut.
  4. The barber will use a hair clipper with a number 3 guard to cut your hair to the desired length. They may use scissors to trim any longer hairs around the hairline.
  5. Once the haircut is finished, the barber will style your hair as desired.
  6. Pay for the haircut and tip the barber if you're satisfied with the service.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a number 3 haircut:

  • Choose a barber who's experienced in men's haircuts to ensure a good result.
  • Communicate clearly with the barber about the length and style you want.
  • Consider the shape of your face and the texture of your hair when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair and keep it in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 3 haircuts:

What is the difference between a number 3 and a number 4 haircut?

A number 3 haircut is 3/8 inch or 9.5mm long, while a number 4 haircut is 1/2 inch or 12.7mm long. The difference in length may not be noticeable to some people, but it can make a difference in the overall look of the haircut.

Can I use a number 3 guard on any hair clipper?

No, not all hair clippers are compatible with all guard sizes. Check the compatibility of the guard size with your hair clipper before buying or using it.

What face shapes suit a number 3 haircut?

A number 3 haircut can suit different face shapes, but it looks particularly good on square or oval faces. It can also help to balance out a long or narrow face.

Hair Care

After getting a number 3 haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow drying or straightening.
  • Use hair products, such as leave-in conditioner or hair oil, to nourish your hair and prevent dryness.
  • Get regular haircuts every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Now that you know what is a number 3 haircut in inches, you can decide if it's the right haircut for you. It's a classic and versatile style that can suit different ages and lifestyles.
