How To Cut Men s Side Swept Haircut

Written by Aprilia Sep 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that is not too difficult to maintain? Look no further than the men's side swept haircut. This versatile hairstyle can be worn in a variety of ways and is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will go through a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's side swept haircut.

Description

The men's side swept haircut is a popular style that involves the hair being swept to one side of the head. This style can be worn with short or long hair and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair texture. The side swept hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How to

Step 1: Preparation

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. This includes a pair of scissors, clippers, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Sectioning

Start by sectioning the hair. Use the comb to separate the hair at the crown of the head and clip it up. This will help you to focus on the sides of the head.

Step 3: Cutting

Using the clippers, start cutting the hair on the sides of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to guide you. Make sure to keep the hair longer on top and shorter on the sides.

Once you have finished cutting the sides, release the hair at the crown of the head and start cutting the top. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.

Step 4: Styling

Once you have finished cutting, it's time to style the hair. Use the comb to sweep the hair to one side of the head. You can use a hair product, such as gel or pomade, to help keep the hair in place.

Tips

- Start with clean and dry hair.

- Use a mirror to check your progress as you cut.

- Take your time and don't rush the process.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

How long does a men's side swept haircut last?

A men's side swept haircut can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style.

Can I wear a men's side swept haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a men's side swept haircut with curly hair. However, you may need to use some extra styling products to keep your hair in place.

Will a men's side swept haircut suit my face shape?

The men's side swept haircut can be customized to suit any face shape. Talk to your hairstylist about the best way to adapt the style to your unique features.

Hair Care

To maintain your men's side swept haircut, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a comb or brush to keep your hair in place and use a hair product, such as gel or pomade, to help keep the hair in place throughout the day.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a men's side swept haircut that is stylish and easy to maintain.
