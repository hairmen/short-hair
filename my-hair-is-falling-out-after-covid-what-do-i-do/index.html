My Hair Is Falling Out After Covid What Do I Do

Written by Aprilia Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you facing hair loss after recovering from Covid? You are not alone. Many people have reported hair loss as a long-term side effect of Covid. In this article, we will discuss what causes hair loss after Covid and what you can do to prevent it.

Description

Hair loss after Covid is a common side effect that can last for months. It is caused by the virus attacking the hair follicles, which results in hair loss. The severity of hair loss can vary from person to person, and it can be temporary or permanent.

How To Prevent Hair Loss After Covid

Here are some tips to prevent hair loss after Covid:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Include foods rich in protein, iron, vitamin C, and biotin in your diet. Consult a nutritionist if required.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair loss. Practice stress-reducing activities like meditation, yoga, or breathing exercises.

3. Use Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo to avoid further damage to your hair. Avoid harsh chemicals and hot styling tools.

4. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to keep your body hydrated. Dehydration can cause hair loss.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to prevent hair loss after Covid:

Step 1: Consult a Doctor

If you notice excessive hair loss after Covid, consult a doctor. They can prescribe medication or suggest other treatments to prevent hair loss.

Step 2: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a diet rich in vitamins and minerals essential for hair growth. Include biotin, iron, vitamin C, and protein-rich foods in your diet.

Step 3: Manage Stress

Practice stress-reducing activities like meditation, yoga, or breathing exercises to manage stress levels.

Step 4: Use Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo to avoid further damage to your hair. Avoid harsh chemicals and hot styling tools.

Step 5: Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to keep your body hydrated. Dehydration can cause hair loss.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair loss after Covid:

1. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair, like braids, ponytails, or buns.

2. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can increase blood flow to your hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Use gentle circular motions with your fingertips.

3. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is essential for overall health and hair growth. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep every night.

FAQ

Q: Is hair loss after Covid permanent?

A: Hair loss after Covid can be temporary or permanent. It depends on the severity of the damage to your hair follicles.

Q: Can I take supplements to prevent hair loss after Covid?

A: You can take supplements like biotin, iron, and vitamin C to promote hair growth. Consult a doctor before taking any supplements.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss after Covid?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss after Covid. Practice stress-reducing activities like meditation, yoga, or breathing exercises.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to prevent hair loss after Covid. Follow these tips:

1. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo to avoid further damage to your hair. Avoid harsh chemicals and hot styling tools.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid harsh chemical treatments like coloring, perming, or straightening that can damage your hair.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, hair loss after Covid can be prevented with proper hair care, a balanced diet, stress management, and hydration. Consult a doctor if you notice excessive hair loss. Follow these tips and steps to promote healthy hair growth.
