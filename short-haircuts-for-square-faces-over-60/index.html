What is a Square Face Shape?

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. Women with square faces may also have a slightly wider face shape than other face shapes. The key to finding the right short haircut for a square face shape is to soften the angles and create a balance between the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Faces Over 60

When it comes to short haircuts for square faces over 60, there are several options that can work well. Here are some of the best short haircuts for square faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can work well for women with square faces. The short layers and choppy texture can help soften the angles of the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Plus, the pixie cut is easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another versatile short haircut that can work well for square faces over 60. A chin-length bob with side-swept bangs can help balance the forehead and jawline, while also adding volume to the hair. A layered bob can also work well for women with square faces, as it can add movement and texture to the hair.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a trendy short haircut that can work well for women with square faces over 60. The choppy layers and textured ends can help soften the angles of the face, while also adding volume and movement to the hair. A shag cut with side-swept bangs can also help balance the forehead and jawline.

How to Style Short Hair for Square Faces Over 60

Once you've chosen the right short haircut for your square face, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling short hair for square faces over 60:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to the hair.
  • Blow dry the hair with a round brush to add volume and movement.
  • Add texture to the hair with a texturizing spray or pomade.
  • Use a flat iron to add texture and movement to the hair.
  • Try a side-swept bang to balance the forehead and jawline.

Tips for Hair Care for Square Faces Over 60

To keep your new short haircut looking fresh, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care for square faces over 60:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage to the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can women over 60 wear short haircuts?

Yes, absolutely! Short haircuts can be stylish and flattering for women over 60. Just be sure to choose a haircut that works well with your face shape and hair type.

2. What are some other face shapes besides square?

Other common face shapes include round, oval, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has its own unique features and characteristics, which can influence the best haircut and styling options.

3. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's generally recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep the hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of haircuts may vary depending on your hair type and length.

That's all for our guide to short haircuts for square faces over 60! We hope this article has been helpful in finding the right haircut for you. Remember to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained to keep your new haircut looking fresh. Happy styling!
