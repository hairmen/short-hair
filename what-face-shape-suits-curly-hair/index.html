What Face Shape Suits Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, but not all hairstyles look good on every face shape. If you have curly hair, it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. In this article, we'll discuss what face shape suits curly hair and give you tips on how to style your curls to look your best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into what face shape suits curly hair, let's first discuss the different face shapes. There are six main face shapes, including:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Long

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and requires different hairstyles to flatter it. When it comes to curly hair, the goal is to choose a hairstyle that balances out your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to

So, what face shape suits curly hair? Here's a breakdown of which curly hairstyles work best for each face shape:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. For curly hair, try a long layered cut that frames your face and highlights your curls.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a curly hairstyle that adds height to your face. A long layered cut with volume at the crown is a great option. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a curly hairstyle that has lots of layers. A shoulder-length cut with long layers that frame your face is a great option. Avoid blunt cuts that accentuate your jawline.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to balance out your features with a curly hairstyle that adds volume to the bottom half of your face. A long layered cut with curls that start at chin-length is a great option. Avoid short hairstyles that accentuate your forehead.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to choose a curly hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin. A shoulder-length cut with volume at the crown and curls that start at chin-length is a great option. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your cheeks.

Long Face Shape

If you have a long face shape, you'll want to choose a curly hairstyle that adds width to your face. A shoulder-length cut with layers and curls that start at chin-length is a great option. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Step by Step

Now that you know what face shape suits curly hair, here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your curls:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls.
  4. Use a curling iron to touch up any curls that need a little extra definition.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling curly hair:

  • Invest in a good quality diffuser attachment for your hair dryer.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as it can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz.
  • Try a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Can I still straighten my curly hair if I have a certain face shape?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have curly hair and a certain face shape. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, whether it's curly or straight.

What type of curling iron should I use on my curly hair?

For curly hair, it's best to use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel, as these materials help to distribute heat evenly and prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your curly hair is important to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what face shape suits curly hair, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your unique features. Remember to take care of your curly hair to keep it looking its best!
