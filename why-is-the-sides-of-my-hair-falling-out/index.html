Why Is The Sides Of My Hair Falling Out

Written by Daniel Oct 25, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you noticing hair loss on the sides of your head? This can be a concerning and frustrating issue. In this article, we’ll explore the possible causes of hair loss on the sides of your head, as well as tips for preventing further hair loss and promoting healthy hair growth.

Description

Hair loss on the sides of the head can manifest in a few different ways. You may notice thinning hair or bald patches on the temples, behind the ears, or along the hairline. This type of hair loss is often referred to as “traction alopecia,” which is caused by consistent tension or pulling on the hair.

How to

If you’re experiencing hair loss on the sides of your head, there are a few steps you can take to prevent further damage:

1. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles can cause tension on the hair follicles and lead to hair loss. Avoid styles like tight braids, cornrows, and high ponytails.

2. Be Gentle

Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle, and avoid pulling or tugging on the hair.

3. Moisturize

Dry hair is more prone to breakage and hair loss. Keep your hair moisturized with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.

4. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from damage by wearing a silk scarf or bonnet at night. This can prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.

Step by Step

If you’re experiencing hair loss on the sides of your head, follow these steps for healthy hair growth:

1. Assess Your Hair Routine

Take a look at your current hair routine and identify any habits that may be causing hair loss. Adjust your routine accordingly.

2. Consult with a Professional

A professional stylist or dermatologist can help diagnose the cause of your hair loss and suggest appropriate treatments.

3. Eat a Balanced Diet

A healthy diet can promote healthy hair growth. Make sure you’re eating a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients.

4. Take Supplements

Supplements like biotin and vitamin D can promote healthy hair growth. Consult with your doctor before taking any new supplements.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for preventing hair loss on the sides of your head:

- Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

- Limit your use of hair extensions and weaves

- Massage your scalp to promote blood flow and healthy hair growth

FAQ

Q: What causes hair loss on the sides of the head?

A: Hair loss on the sides of the head is often caused by consistent tension or pulling on the hair, known as traction alopecia.

Q: How can I prevent hair loss on the sides of my head?

A: Avoid tight hairstyles, be gentle when brushing and styling your hair, moisturize your hair, and protect your hair from damage.

Q: Can supplements help with hair loss?

A: Yes, supplements like biotin and vitamin D can promote healthy hair growth. Consult with your doctor before taking any new supplements.

Hair Care

When it comes to preventing hair loss on the sides of your head, it’s important to take a holistic approach to hair care. This includes maintaining a healthy diet, avoiding damaging hair practices, and using gentle products that promote healthy hair growth.

By following the tips outlined in this article, you can prevent further hair loss and promote healthy, beautiful hair growth.
