Cutting Men s Hair Longer On Top A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Christine Jun 22, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, consider cutting your hair longer on top. This style has been popular for several years now and shows no signs of slowing down. With a longer top, you can style your hair in various ways, from slicked back to messy, and everything in between. In this guide, we'll provide you with tips and tricks to achieve the perfect longer top hairstyle.

Description

Longer top haircuts are often referred to as "men's haircuts with volume" where the hair is left longer on top and trimmed shorter on the sides and back. This style works for all hair types, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair. It's versatile and can be customized to suit your preferences.

How to Cut Men's Hair Longer on Top

Here's how to cut men's hair longer on top:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into three parts: the crown, the top, and the sides. This will make it easier to cut each section and ensure that the length is even.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. The length will depend on your preference, but typically, the sides and back should be shorter than the top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Start by cutting the crown section, then move on to the top section. Use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Remember to cut a little bit at a time and check the length frequently to avoid cutting too much.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair using scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the top and sides. This will make the haircut look more natural and polished.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Longer on Top

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect longer top haircut:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need.
  • Use professional hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and cut the hair in small sections.
  • Check the length frequently to avoid cutting too much.
  • Use a comb to keep the hair in place while cutting.

FAQ

What face shape does this haircut work best for?

This haircut works well for most face shapes. However, it's particularly flattering for men with oval or round faces.

How often do I need to get this haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Yes! That's the beauty of a longer top haircut. You can style it in various ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your longer top haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy.

Cutting men's hair longer on top is a great way to switch up your hairstyle and try something new. By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect look and maintain it for weeks to come.
