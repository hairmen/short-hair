What Is The Haircut Called With Shaved Sides And Back

Written by Aprilia Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that will make you stand out in the crowd? If yes, then the haircut called with shaved sides and back is the perfect choice for you. This haircut has become increasingly popular among men and women of all ages, and it is easy to see why. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including what it is, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The haircut called with shaved sides and back is a modern and edgy hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, while the top is left longer. The shaved sides and back create a dramatic contrast with the longer hair on top, which can be styled in various ways. This haircut can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures, making it a versatile and trendy choice for anyone.

How to Get the Haircut Called with Shaved Sides and Back

If you are interested in getting the haircut called with shaved sides and back, the first step is to find a reputable hair salon or barber shop that specializes in this type of haircut. Once you have found a stylist that you trust, you can discuss your desired look and they will help you choose the right length and style for your hair. The stylist will then use clippers to shave the sides and back of your head, leaving the top longer. They may also use scissors to trim and shape the top layer of hair to create the desired style.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to get the haircut called with shaved sides and back:

  1. Research and find a reputable salon or barber shop that specializes in this type of haircut.
  2. Discuss your desired look with the stylist and choose the right length and style for your hair.
  3. Sit back and relax while the stylist uses clippers to shave the sides and back of your head, leaving the top longer.
  4. The stylist may also use scissors to trim and shape the top layer of hair to create the desired style.
  5. Once the haircut is complete, the stylist will show you how to style and maintain your new look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your haircut called with shaved sides and back:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Use a hair styling product, such as wax or pomade, to create different looks and hold your hair in place.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the length and style of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the haircut called with shaved sides and back:

  1. Is the haircut called with shaved sides and back suitable for all hair types?

    2. Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit different hair types and textures.

  2. How often should I visit my stylist to maintain my haircut?

    3. You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style of your haircut.

  3. Can I style my hair in different ways with this haircut?

    4. Yes, you can experiment with different hairstyles and hair products to create different looks.

Hair Care

The key to maintaining a healthy and stylish haircut called with shaved sides and back is to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the haircut called with shaved sides and back is a trendy and stylish choice for anyone looking for an edgy and modern hairstyle. With the right care and maintenance, this haircut can keep you looking sharp and fashionable for years to come.
