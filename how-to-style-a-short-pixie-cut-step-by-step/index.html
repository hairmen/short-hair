How To Style A Short Pixie Cut Step By Step

Written by Bardi Jul 05, 2023 Â· 2 min read

If you have just gotten a short pixie cut and are wondering how to style it, you have come to the right place. A short pixie cut is a trendy and modern look, but it can be tricky to style. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style a short pixie cut, tips for maintaining your style, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

How to Style a Short Pixie Cut

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2: Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.

Step 3: Use a comb or your fingers to create your desired style.

Step 4: Use a blow dryer to set your style.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Pixie Cut

Tip 1: Get regular trims to keep your style looking fresh.

Tip 2: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Tip 3: Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short pixie cut?

A: It is best to wash your hair every other day to keep it looking fresh and clean.

Q: Can I still style my short pixie cut if I have fine hair?

A: Yes, you can still style a short pixie cut if you have fine hair. Just be sure to use lighter styling products and avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair, especially when you have a short pixie cut. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

- Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Styling a short pixie cut can be fun and easy with the right tools and techniques. Follow the steps outlined in this article and experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
