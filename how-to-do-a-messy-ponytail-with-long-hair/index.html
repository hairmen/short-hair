How To Do A Messy Ponytail With Long Hair

Description

A messy ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. It's perfect for those days when you don't have time to style your hair or just want to achieve a casual, effortless look. This hairstyle is especially great for those with long hair as it allows you to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish.

How to

To achieve a messy ponytail with long hair, you will need a hairbrush, hair ties, bobby pins, and hairspray. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to create a smooth ponytail later on.

Step 2: Gather your hair

Using your hands, gather your hair at the crown of your head. Make sure to leave a few strands of hair loose around your face for a more effortless look.

Step 3: Secure with a hair tie

Once you have gathered your hair, secure it with a hair tie. Make sure to wrap the hair tie around your hair several times to ensure it is secure.

Step 4: Create volume

To create a messy look, gently pull on the hair at the crown of your head to create volume. You can also use a comb to tease the hair for more volume.

Step 5: Loosen the ponytail

Using your fingers, gently loosen the hair in your ponytail. This will create a messy, effortless look.

Step 6: Secure with bobby pins

If you have any loose strands of hair, use bobby pins to secure them in place. You can also pull out a few strands of hair from the ponytail to create a more relaxed look.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

To keep your messy ponytail in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also help to tame any flyaways.

Tips

  • For a more textured look, use a sea salt spray before creating your ponytail.
  • If you have thick hair, use two hair ties to ensure your ponytail stays secure.
  • For an even messier look, pull out a few strands of hair from the ponytail and curl them with a curling iron.
  • If you have bangs, leave them out of the ponytail for a more flattering look.

FAQ

Can I do a messy ponytail with short hair?

A messy ponytail is best suited for those with longer hair. However, if you have shorter hair, you can still achieve a similar look by using hair extensions or a hairpiece.

How do I prevent my messy ponytail from falling out?

To prevent your messy ponytail from falling out, make sure to use a hair tie that is strong and secure. You can also use bobby pins to keep any loose strands of hair in place.

Hair Care

When doing a messy ponytail, it's important to take care of your hair. Make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools. Additionally, try to avoid using hair ties that are too tight as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

By following these tips, you can achieve a effortless, messy ponytail with long hair that is perfect for any occasion.
