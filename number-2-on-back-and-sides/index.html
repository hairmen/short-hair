Number 2 On Back And Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on getting a number 2 on the back and sides of your head. This classic hairstyle has been popular for decades and is still a go-to for many men today. In this article, we will be covering everything you need to know about the number 2 on the back and sides, from how to achieve it to tips for maintaining it. So, let's get started!

Description

A number 2 on the back and sides is a simple yet stylish haircut that involves using a clipper to trim the hair on the back and sides of the head to a length of 1/4 inch. This creates a neat and tidy appearance that is easy to maintain and style. It is a versatile haircut that can be dressed up or down, making it a popular choice for both casual and formal occasions.

How to

Achieving a number 2 on the back and sides is relatively easy if you have the right tools and know-how. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Gather your tools

To achieve a number 2 on the back and sides, you'll need a few essential tools, such as:

  • Clippers with a number 2 guard
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair product (optional)

Step 2: Prepare your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's essential to prepare it properly. This involves washing and drying your hair thoroughly and combing it out to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Start cutting

Using the clippers with the number 2 guard, start cutting the hair on the back and sides of your head. Begin at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown, moving the clippers in an upward motion. Repeat this on both sides of your head, making sure to keep the length consistent. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs around the edges.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once you've achieved your desired length, you can style your hair using your preferred product. Apply a small amount of product to your hair and use your fingers or a comb to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect number 2 on the back and sides:

  • Invest in high-quality clippers and scissors to ensure a clean and even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head as you cut.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a number 2 if you're unsure about the length.
  • If you're not confident in cutting your hair yourself, consider going to a professional barber.
  • Use a styling product that suits your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

Is a number 2 on the back and sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a number 2 on the back and sides is suitable for all hair types, but it may look different depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

How often should I trim my hair to maintain a number 2 on the back and sides?

You should trim your hair every 2-4 weeks to maintain a number 2 on the back and sides.

Can I achieve a number 2 on the back and sides with longer hair?

It's possible to achieve a number 2 on the back and sides with longer hair, but you may need to trim the top of your hair to create a neat and tidy appearance.

Hair Care

To maintain a number 2 on the back and sides, it's essential to take good care of your hair. This involves washing and conditioning your hair regularly and using a high-quality styling product that suits your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Conclusion

We hope you found our ultimate guide on the number 2 on the back and sides helpful. With the right tools and techniques, achieving this classic haircut is easy and straightforward. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain your new look and always consult a professional if you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself.
