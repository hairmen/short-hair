Does Lighter Hair Colour Make You Look Younger The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Nov 29, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

Are you tired of looking older than you feel? If so, you may be wondering whether lighter hair colour can help you turn back the clock. In this guide, we'll explore the question of whether lighter hair colour makes you look younger, and we'll offer tips and advice on how to achieve a more youthful look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve a More Youthful Look with Lighter Hair Colour

Description

Many people believe that as we age, we should stick to more natural hair colours to avoid drawing attention to our wrinkles and fine lines. However, lighter hair colour can actually make you look younger by brightening up your complexion and softening harsh facial features.

Lighter hair colour can also help to disguise grey hairs, which are a tell-tale sign of ageing. By choosing a shade that complements your skin tone and eye colour, you can create a more youthful, radiant look that will help you to feel more confident and energized.

How to Achieve a More Youthful Look with Lighter Hair Colour

If you're ready to experiment with lighter hair colour, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and eye colour.
  • Consider getting highlights or balayage instead of a full colour change.
  • Be prepared for regular touch-ups to maintain your new look.
  • Invest in hair care products that will keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Choosing the Right Shade

The first step in achieving a more youthful look with lighter hair colour is to choose the right shade. If you have cool undertones in your skin, you'll want to stick to cooler shades like platinum blonde, ash blonde, or cool beige. If your skin has warm undertones, try warmer shades like golden blonde, honey blonde, or caramel. Your hair stylist can help you choose the best shade for your skin tone and eye colour.

Highlights and Balayage

If you're not ready to commit to a full colour change, consider getting highlights or balayage instead. Highlights involve adding lighter strands throughout your hair, while balayage creates a more natural, sun-kissed look. Both techniques can help to brighten up your complexion and create a more youthful appearance.

Regular Touch-Ups

Once you've achieved your desired lighter hair colour, be prepared for regular touch-ups to maintain your new look. Lighter hair can fade or become brassy over time, so you'll need to visit your hair stylist every 6-8 weeks for a touch-up.

Hair Care Products

Investing in high-quality hair care products is essential for maintaining healthy, shiny hair. Look for products that are specifically formulated for colour-treated hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and fade your colour more quickly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Lightening Your Hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and lighten your hair, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your hair stylist to choose the best shade for your skin tone and eye colour.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  3. Apply the lightening product to your hair, following the instructions carefully.
  4. Leave the product on for the recommended time, checking your hair regularly to avoid over-processing.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  6. Apply a toner to your hair to neutralize any brassy tones.
  7. Condition your hair with a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture and prevent damage.

Tips for Maintaining Your Lighter Hair Colour

After you've achieved your desired lighter hair colour, follow these tips to maintain your new look:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade more quickly.

FAQs

Does lighter hair colour make you look younger?

Yes, lighter hair colour can help to brighten up your complexion and create a more youthful appearance.

What are the best shades of blonde for a more youthful look?

Platinum blonde, ash blonde, cool beige, golden blonde, honey blonde, and caramel are all great options for creating a more youthful look.

How often do I need to touch up my lighter hair colour?

You'll need to visit your hair stylist every 6-8 weeks for a touch-up to maintain your new look.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Youthful-Looking Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and youthful:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
