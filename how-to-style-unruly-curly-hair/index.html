How To Style Unruly Curly Hair Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look gorgeous with the right styling, it can also be a challenge to manage and tame. If you have unruly curly hair, don't worry, you're not alone. In this post, we'll share tips, tricks, and FAQs on how to style unruly curly hair.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Unruly Curly Hair

Unruly curly hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and defined look. Here are the basic steps for styling unruly curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Towel dry your hair gently, avoiding rubbing or pulling, to prevent frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your hair gently as you go to enhance your curls.
  • Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or iron to touch up any curls that need extra definition.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to style unruly curly hair:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to help seal your hair cuticles and reduce frizz.
  2. Gently towel dry your hair, avoiding rubbing or pulling, to prevent frizz. Do not use a brush or comb, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Work the product through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your hair, scrunching your hair gently as you go to enhance your curls. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or iron to touch up any curls that need extra definition. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the barrel of the curling wand or iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place. Hold the can of hairspray about 8-10 inches away from your head and spray lightly, focusing on the ends of your hair.

Tips for Styling Unruly Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling unruly curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or loose bun on top of your head to preserve your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting on low.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling unruly curly hair:

  • How often should I wash my curly hair? It depends on your hair type and texture, but most curly hair types only need to be washed once or twice a week. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz.
  • What's the best way to dry curly hair? The best way to dry curly hair is by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This helps enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair.
  • What's the best haircut for curly hair? The best haircut for curly hair is one that enhances your natural curl pattern and works with your face shape. A layered haircut can help add volume and shape to your curls, while a blunt cut can help weigh down thicker curls. Consult with your stylist to find the best haircut for your curly hair.

Hair Care for Unruly Curly Hair

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care for unruly curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to nourish and hydrate your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting on low.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

By following these tips and techniques, you can learn how to style unruly curly hair and achieve a beautiful and defined look. Remember to be patient with your hair, as it may take some trial and error to find the right products and techniques that work for you. With the right care and styling, your unruly curly hair can be your best asset.
