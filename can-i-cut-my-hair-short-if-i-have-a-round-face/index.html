Can I Cut My Hair Short If I Have A Round Face

Written by Robby Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may wonder if you can pull off a short haircut. The answer is yes! You can absolutely cut your hair short, regardless of your face shape. However, there are certain styles that can flatter your features and complement your face shape.

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, circular features. Your cheekbones and jawline are typically similar in width, and your forehead may be slightly wider than your chin. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How to Choose a Short Haircut for a Round Face

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, consider the following tips:

1. Look for Length

A short haircut with some length can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Consider a bob or a lob (long bob) that falls just below your chin.

2. Consider Layers

Layers can also help elongate your face and add texture to your hair. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your cheekbones and angle downward.

3. Avoid Blunt Cuts

A blunt cut can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for a cut with layers and texture.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break up the circular shape of your face and draw attention to your eyes. Ask your stylist for bangs that start at your temples and angle downward.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to get a short haircut for a round face:

1. Research

Research different short haircuts and styles that flatter round faces. Look for inspiration on social media, magazines, and blogs.

2. Consultation

Schedule a consultation with your stylist to discuss your desired haircut and any concerns you may have about your face shape. They can recommend styles that will complement your features.

3. Cut and Style

Once you've decided on a style, your stylist will cut your hair and style it to perfection. They may add layers, texture, and side-swept bangs to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

1. Schedule Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh, schedule regular trims with your stylist. They can remove any split ends and keep your layers looking defined.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays and pomades. These products can help create volume and texture in your hair.

3. Experiment with Styling

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening your hair. This can help you find your favorite look and keep your haircut versatile.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair short if I have a round face?

Yes! You can absolutely cut your hair short regardless of your face shape. Just be sure to choose a style that flatters your features and elongates your face.

What short haircuts are best for round faces?

Short haircuts with some length, layers, and side-swept bangs can flatter round faces. Consider a bob or a lob that falls just below your chin.

What products should I use for short hair?

Texturizing sprays and pomades are great products for short hair. They can help create volume and texture in your hair.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require some special care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every few days to keep it clean and fresh. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools. Avoid using hot tools on wet hair.

3. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage in your hair.

In conclusion, you can absolutely cut your hair short if you have a round face. Just be sure to choose a style that flatters your features and elongates your face. With the right cut and care, you can rock a short haircut and feel confident and stylish.
