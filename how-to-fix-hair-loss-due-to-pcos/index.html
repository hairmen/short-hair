How To Fix Hair Loss Due To Pcos A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a common hormonal disorder that affects women worldwide. One of the most common symptoms of PCOS is hair loss, which can be distressing and affect a woman's self-esteem. If you're experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, don't worry; there are several ways to fix it. In this article, we'll discuss how to fix hair loss due to PCOS, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Fix Hair Loss due to PCOS

There are several ways to fix hair loss due to PCOS, including medication, surgery, and lifestyle changes. The most effective way to treat hair loss due to PCOS is to address the underlying hormonal imbalance. Here are some of the most common treatment options:

Medication

Medication is one of the most common ways to treat hair loss due to PCOS. Your doctor may prescribe medications such as:

  • Birth control pills
  • Anti-androgen drugs
  • Topical minoxidil

These medications can help regulate your hormones and promote hair growth.

Surgery

If you have advanced hair loss due to PCOS, your doctor may recommend hair transplant surgery. During this procedure, hair follicles are taken from a donor site and transplanted to the balding area.

Lifestyle Changes

While medication and surgery can be effective, making lifestyle changes can also help treat hair loss due to PCOS. Here are some tips:

  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Manage stress

These lifestyle changes can help regulate your hormones and promote hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your doctor to determine the best treatment plan for you.
  2. If prescribed medication, take it as directed.
  3. If recommended surgery, schedule an appointment with a hair transplant specialist.
  4. Make lifestyle changes to promote hormone regulation and hair growth.
  5. Follow a regular hair care routine to promote healthy hair.

Tips for Treating Hair Loss due to PCOS

Here are some additional tips for treating hair loss due to PCOS:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Limit chemical treatments
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Consider using hair growth supplements

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair loss due to PCOS:

Can hair loss due to PCOS be reversed?

Yes, with the right treatment, hair loss due to PCOS can be reversed.

How long does it take to see results?

The time it takes to see results varies from person to person. It can take several months to a year to see significant hair growth.

Can lifestyle changes alone help treat hair loss due to PCOS?

If your hair loss is mild, making lifestyle changes alone may help. However, if your hair loss is severe, medication or surgery may be necessary.

Hair Care Tips for PCOS Patients

Here are some additional hair care tips for PCOS patients:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Don't brush your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using tight hair accessories
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and treatment options, you can successfully treat hair loss due to PCOS and promote healthy hair growth.
