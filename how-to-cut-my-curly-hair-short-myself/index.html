How To Cut My Curly Hair Short Myself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have curly hair. But with the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a fabulous new haircut without spending a fortune at the salon. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your curly hair short yourself, step by step. Whether you're looking to refresh your look or simply trim your ends, this guide has got you covered.

Description

Curly hair can be challenging to cut, but it's not impossible. With the right approach, you can achieve a gorgeous, low-maintenance style that complements your natural texture. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting your curly hair short yourself, from selecting the right tools and products to executing the perfect cut.

How to Cut My Curly Hair Short Myself: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

TIP: Make sure your scissors are sharp and designed for cutting hair. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make your cut look uneven.

Step 2: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Before you start cutting, you'll want to make sure your hair is clean and detangled. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then comb it out with a wide-tooth comb. This will make it easier to see where you're cutting and ensure a smooth, even finish.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, separate your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle, then dividing it into two sections: one in the front and one in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting with the front section, take a small section of hair between your fingers and hold it at an angle away from your head. Use your scissors to trim the ends, cutting at an angle to create layers. Work your way around your head, taking small sections and cutting at an angle to create layers and texture.

TIP: Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones. This will help you avoid cutting too much off at once.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've finished cutting, check your progress in a mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your cut is even and balanced.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you're happy with your cut, it's time to style your hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and reduce frizz. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your natural curl pattern.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Short Yourself

  • Start with clean, detangled hair
  • Use sharp scissors designed for cutting hair
  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones
  • Cut at an angle to create layers and texture
  • Check your progress in a mirror as you go
  • Style your hair with a curl-enhancing product to define your curls

FAQ

Can I cut my curly hair short myself?

Yes, you can! With the right tools and techniques, cutting curly hair short can be a fun and rewarding experience.

What should I use to cut my curly hair?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors designed for cutting hair, a wide-tooth comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle filled with water.

How do I know how much to cut off?

Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones. This will help you avoid cutting too much off at once.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always trim a little more off to even things out. If you're really unhappy with your cut, you can always visit a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a little extra effort, but it's worth it for the beautiful, bouncy curls you'll achieve. Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and reduce frizz
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your curly hair short yourself and achieve a fabulous new look. Happy cutting!
