How To Style Men s Hair With Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Styling men's hair with gel is a classic and versatile look that can be tailored to fit any occasion or personal style. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or a messy, textured style, using gel is a quick and easy way to achieve a put-together look. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to style men's hair with gel, and provide tips and tricks for achieving the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Men's Hair with Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Men's Hair with Gel
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Men's Hair with Gel

Before you start styling, you'll need to choose the right gel for your hair type and desired look. There are a variety of gels available, from light hold to strong hold, and some designed specifically for curly or thick hair. It's important to choose a gel that will work with your hair type, and provide the hold and texture you're looking for.

Once you've chosen your gel, it's time to start styling. Here's a basic guide to styling men's hair with gel:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a nickel-sized amount of gel to your palms and rub them together.
  2. Starting at the roots, work the gel through your hair, making sure to evenly distribute it.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape. For a sleek look, comb your hair straight back or to the side. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or finishing spray to lock in your style.

And that's it! With a few quick steps, you can achieve a polished and put-together look.

Tips for Styling Men's Hair with Gel

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect style:

  • Less is more. Start with a small amount of gel and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb or brush to distribute the gel evenly through your hair.
  • If you have thick or curly hair, try a stronger hold gel to keep your style in place.
  • For a more natural look, use your fingers to style your hair, rather than a comb or brush.
  • Always finish with a hairspray or finishing spray to lock in your style and prevent frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's hair with gel:

  • Q: Can I use gel on dry hair?
    A: Yes, but it's best to start with damp hair to ensure even distribution of the gel.
  • Q: How much gel should I use?
    A: Start with a small amount and add more as needed. It's easier to add more than to remove excess gel.
  • Q: Will gel make my hair look greasy?
    A: Only if you use too much. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Hair Care Tips

Styling your hair with gel can be a quick and easy way to achieve a polished look, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy locks. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove product buildup and keep your scalp healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like blow dryers or straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

And there you have it - a step-by-step guide on how to style men's hair with gel. With a little practice and the right products, you can achieve a polished and put-together look in no time.
