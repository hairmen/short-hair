What Hair Type Do Guys Like The Ultimate Guide For Women

Written by William Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wondering what hair type do guys like? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this ultimate guide, we will be discussing everything you need to know about the hair type that men prefer. From how to style your hair to the best products to use, we have got all the information you need. So, let's get started.

Description

The type of hair that men prefer varies from person to person. Some men might prefer straight hair, while others might like curly hair. However, there are certain hair types that are universally popular among men. These include:

  • Straight hair
  • Wavy hair
  • Curly hair
  • Thick hair
  • Voluminous hair

While some men might have a specific preference, most men are attracted to healthy, well-maintained hair. So, no matter what your hair type is, taking care of it and keeping it healthy is the key to making it attractive to men.

How to Determine Your Hair Type

The first step in figuring out what hair type do guys like is to determine your hair type. Here are the different hair types:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Coily

To determine your hair type, wash your hair and let it air dry without using any products. Then, examine your hair and determine which of the above categories it falls under. Once you determine your hair type, you can start figuring out the best way to style it.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know your hair type, here is how you can style it to attract the attention of men:

Straight Hair

Straight hair is the easiest to style. You can wear it down, in a ponytail, or in a bun. However, to make it more interesting, you can add some volume by using a volumizing mousse or spray. You can also add some texture by using a texturizing spray.

Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, you can enhance your waves by using a curl-enhancing product. You can also wear it in a messy bun or braid for a more casual look.

Curly Hair

Curly hair requires more maintenance than other hair types. To keep your curls looking defined, use a curl-enhancing product and avoid brushing your hair. You can also wear your hair in a messy bun or braid for a casual look.

Coily Hair

If you have coily hair, you can wear it in a natural afro or in a protective style such as braids or twists. To keep your hair healthy, use a moisturizing product and avoid using heat styling tools.

Tips for Attracting Men with Your Hair

In addition to styling your hair, here are some tips for making it more attractive to men:

  • Keep your hair healthy by using a good shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, and other accessories

FAQ

What hair color do guys prefer?

There is no one hair color that guys prefer. Some might like blondes, while others might prefer brunettes or redheads. However, as long as your hair is healthy and well-maintained, it will be attractive to men regardless of the color.

What hairstyle do guys find most attractive?

Again, there is no one hairstyle that all guys find attractive. However, a hairstyle that is healthy, well-maintained, and suits your face shape is always attractive to men.

Hair Care Essentials

To keep your hair healthy and attractive, here are some hair care essentials you should have:

  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Hair oil
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Volumizing mousse or spray
  • Curl-enhancing product
  • Texturizing spray
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hairbrush
  • Hair ties and clips
  • Headbands and other accessories

By using these products and following the tips mentioned above, you can make your hair attractive to men and feel confident about your hair.

Conclusion

Now that you know what hair type do guys like, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you. Remember to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained to make it attractive to men. With these tips and tricks, you can have the beautiful, luscious hair that men can't resist.
