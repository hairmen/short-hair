Medium Length Hair With Bangs And Face Framing Layers

Description

Medium length hair with bangs and face framing layers is a versatile hairstyle that suits different face shapes and hair types. This hairstyle features hair that falls just above the shoulders or slightly below with bangs and layers that frame the face. The bangs can be blunt, side-swept, or wispy, depending on the person's preference.

Face framing layers can be added to enhance the look of the face and add dimension to the hair. This hairstyle is suitable for both casual and formal events, making it a popular choice among women who want a low-maintenance yet stylish hairstyle.

How to Style

To style medium length hair with bangs and face framing layers, you will need a blow dryer, round brush, and a styling product such as a mousse or hairspray. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product such as a mousse or hairspray to your hair to add texture and hold.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair while pulling it away from your face to create volume and body. Focus on the roots for more lift.

Step 4: Style the Bangs

Style your bangs according to your preference. You can either blow-dry them straight or use a round brush to create a side-swept effect. If you have wispy bangs, you can use your fingers to style them.

Step 5: Add Face Framing Layers

If you want to add face framing layers, use a pair of scissors to snip off a few strands of hair around your face. Make sure to do this carefully to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve medium length hair with bangs and face framing layers:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

Before getting a haircut, it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best style and length for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, left, right, and back. Clip each section to keep them separated.

Step 4: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section and cut your hair to the desired length. Make sure to cut straight across to avoid unevenness.

Step 5: Cut the Side Sections

Move on to the side sections and cut them to match the length of the back section. Make sure to cut at an angle to create face framing layers.

Step 6: Cut the Top Section

Cut the top section to the desired length, making sure to blend it with the rest of the hair. Cut at an angle to create more layers.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush and style your bangs according to your preference. Add face framing layers if necessary.

Tips for Maintenance

Here are some tips to maintain your medium length hair with bangs and face framing layers:

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much styling products as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, medium length hair with bangs and face framing layers can suit different face shapes. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best style for your face shape.

Q: Can I still style my hair differently with this hairstyle?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be styled differently depending on your preference. You can curl or straighten your hair or tie it up in a ponytail or bun.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair with bangs and face framing layers healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as bleach or hair dye. If you must use them, make sure to use a hair mask or leave-in conditioner to prevent damage.

By following these tips, you can maintain your medium length hair with bangs and face framing layers and keep it looking healthy and stylish.
