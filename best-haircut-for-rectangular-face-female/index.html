Best Haircut For Rectangular Face Female

Written by Frank Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a rectangular face shape, you may find it challenging to find the perfect haircut that will flatter your features. Fortunately, there are many haircuts that can complement your face shape and make you look stunning. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for rectangular face female, with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A rectangular face is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a long and narrow chin. The best haircut for rectangular face female should soften the angles and add volume to the sides of the face. This can be achieved with layered haircuts, long bobs, side-swept bangs, and wavy or curly hairstyles. Avoid straight and blunt haircuts that emphasize the length of your face and make it appear longer.

How to

When selecting the best haircut for rectangular face female, consider the following factors:

  • Your hair type and texture
  • Your lifestyle and personal style
  • Your face shape and features

You should also consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut that will suit your face shape and enhance your natural beauty. Bring photos of hairstyles that you like and discuss your preferences and concerns with your hairstylist. They can recommend the best haircut for your face shape and provide styling tips to maintain your hair's health and beauty.

Step by step

Here are the steps to achieve the best haircut for rectangular face female:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a haircut that will soften the angles of your face and add volume to the sides.
  3. Consider adding layers or bangs to your haircut to create movement and texture.
  4. Style your hair with a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
  5. Use volumizing products such as mousse or root lift spray to add volume to your hair.
  6. Avoid straight and blunt haircuts that emphasize the length of your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best haircut for rectangular face female:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your personal style and lifestyle.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and depth.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and create a soft, natural look.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear longer.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for rectangular face female:

What is the best haircut for a rectangular face with a high forehead?

The best haircut for a rectangular face with a high forehead is a long bob or lob. This haircut will soften the angles of your face and create a balance between your forehead and chin. Consider adding side-swept bangs to your haircut to create a flattering frame for your face.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a rectangular face?

Avoid wearing your hair straight if you have a rectangular face, as it can emphasize the length of your face and make it appear longer. Instead, opt for wavy or curly hairstyles that add volume and softness to your features.

Should I choose a short or long haircut if I have a rectangular face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short if you have a rectangular face, as they can make your face appear longer. Opt for medium to long haircuts that add volume and texture to the sides of your face.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with products such as hairspray and gel, as they can make your hair look dull and lifeless.

In conclusion, the best haircut for rectangular face female should soften the angles of your face and add volume to the sides. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type, and follow these tips and hair care advice to maintain your hair's health and beauty.
