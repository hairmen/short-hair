Short Bob Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 60

Written by Luffy Oct 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a chic and trendy hairstyle that will suit your thick hair? Look no further than the short bob! This versatile haircut can be tailored to suit any face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks to help you make the most of your short bob hairstyle.

How to Choose the Right Short Bob Hairstyle for You

Before you head to the salon, take some time to think about what kind of short bob hairstyle you want. Do you want a classic bob that falls just above the shoulders? Or do you prefer a more modern, angled bob with layers? Here are some things to consider when choosing your perfect bob:

Face Shape

Your face shape will play a big role in determining the best short bob hairstyle for you. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a longer bob that falls just below the chin to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a shorter, more angular bob can help soften your features.

Hair Texture

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse when it comes to short bobs. On the one hand, you have plenty of volume to work with. On the other hand, if your hair is too thick, a short bob can end up looking bulky and unflattering. If you have thick hair, consider getting some layers cut into your bob to help lighten it up.

Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a short bob hairstyle. Do you prefer a classic, sophisticated look? Or are you more of a free spirit who likes to experiment with bold colors and edgy cuts? Whatever your style, there's a short bob hairstyle out there that will suit you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Bob

Once you've decided on the perfect short bob hairstyle for you, it's time to head to the salon! Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short bob:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you head to the salon, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your stylist get a better idea of your hair's natural texture and volume.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist about the type of short bob you want. Bring pictures or examples of hairstyles you like to give your stylist a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Once you've agreed on a style, your stylist will begin cutting your hair. They may use scissors or a razor to create the perfect shape and length for your bob.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After your hair is cut, your stylist will blow dry and style it to perfection. They may use styling products like mousse or gel to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Bob Hairstyle

Now that you have your new short bob hairstyle, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short bob:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically formulated for short hair, like volumizing mousses and texturizing sprays. Avoid heavy products like oils and serums, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your short bob looking fresh and healthy, it's important to get regular trims. Aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep split ends at bay.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks with your short bob. You can add texture with a curling iron or straighten it for a sleek, sophisticated look. There are so many ways to style a short bob, so have fun and try new things!

FAQs

Q: Will a short bob hairstyle suit my thick hair?

A: Absolutely! A short bob can look great on thick hair, but it's important to get the right cut to suit your hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I wash my short bob?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If your hair is dry or damaged, you may want to wash it less frequently to avoid stripping away natural oils.

Q: Can I color my short bob?

A: Of course! Short bobs can be a great canvas for experimenting with different hair colors. Just make sure to use high-quality color products and follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Short Bob Hairstyles

Short bobs require a little extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for keeping your short bob healthy and shiny:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using hot styling tools like curling irons or straighteners, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip away natural oils and leave your hair looking dry and dull. Aim to wash your short bob no more than every other day.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for keeping your short bob looking its best. Aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it in shape.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a fabulous short bob hairstyle that flatters your thick hair and complements your personal style. So go ahead and make that appointment with your stylist - your new look is waiting for you!
