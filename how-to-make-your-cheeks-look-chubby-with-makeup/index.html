How To Make Your Cheeks Look Chubby With Makeup A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 20, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume to your cheeks, makeup can be a great way to achieve this look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to make your cheeks look chubby with makeup, including tips and tricks to get the most out of your products. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Prep Your Skin
  • Step 2: Choose the Right Products
  • Step 3: Apply Blush
  • Step 4: Blend and Contour
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

The first step to making your cheeks look chubby with makeup is to properly prep your skin. This means cleansing, toning, and moisturizing your face to create a smooth canvas for your makeup. You'll want to make sure your skin is hydrated and free of any dry patches or flakiness.

Once your skin is prepped, apply a primer to help your makeup last longer and go on more smoothly. This will also help to prevent your makeup from settling into any fine lines or wrinkles.

Step 2: Choose the Right Products

When it comes to making your cheeks look chubby with makeup, the right products can make all the difference. You'll want to choose a blush that complements your skin tone and has a natural-looking finish. Cream blushes can be a great option for creating a more subtle, dewy look, while powder blushes can be better for a more dramatic effect.

You'll also want to choose a contouring powder or cream that is a few shades darker than your skin tone. This will help to create the illusion of shadows and depth, giving your cheeks a more defined, sculpted look.

Step 3: Apply Blush

Now it's time to apply your blush. Start by smiling in the mirror to locate the apples of your cheeks. Using a fluffy blush brush, apply the blush in circular motions to the apples of your cheeks, blending upwards and outwards towards your temples. Be sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines or patches.

If you're using a cream blush, you can apply it with your fingers or a damp beauty sponge for a more natural-looking finish.

Step 4: Blend and Contour

After applying your blush, it's time to blend and contour. Using a contour brush or your fingers, apply the contouring powder or cream to the hollows of your cheeks, blending upwards towards your ears. This will create the illusion of shadows and depth, giving your cheeks a more defined, sculpted look.

Be sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines or patches. You can also use a clean, fluffy brush to blend out any excess product or soften any harsh edges.

Tips and Tricks

  • Choose a blush color that complements your skin tone and the rest of your makeup look.
  • Use a light hand when applying products to avoid looking overdone.
  • Blend, blend, blend! This is key to achieving a natural-looking finish.
  • Consider using a highlighter on the tops of your cheekbones to further enhance the chubby cheek effect.

FAQ

What if I have naturally thin cheeks?

While makeup can help create the illusion of chubby cheeks, it's important to remember that everyone's face shape is different. If you have naturally thin cheeks, embrace them! There's no need to try and change them with makeup.

What if I don't have contouring products?

If you don't have contouring products, you can still achieve a chubby cheek look by focusing on blush application. Apply your blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend upwards towards your temples to create a natural-looking flush.

Hair Care

When it comes to making your cheeks look chubby with makeup, your hairstyle can also play a role. Consider trying a hairstyle with volume at the crown of your head, such as a messy bun or a half-up style. This will help to balance out the volume in your cheeks and create a more cohesive look.

Remember, makeup should be fun and used to enhance your natural beauty, not to try and change your features completely. Have fun experimenting with different products and techniques, and don't be afraid to embrace your unique features!
