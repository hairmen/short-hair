What Sort Of Hairstyle Would Suit Me

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering what sort of hairstyle would suit you? Choosing the right hairstyle can enhance your features and make you feel more confident. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this blog post, we will explore different hairstyles that could suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Read on to discover your perfect match!

Description

Before we dive into different hairstyles, let's first understand how to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in what hairstyle would suit you best. The four main face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. You can determine your face shape by looking at your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

How to

Once you have identified your face shape, it's time to look for hairstyles that complement it. Here are some suggestions:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, the goal is to elongate it and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and angled bobs can all achieve this look.

Oval Face Shape

Oval face shapes are considered the most versatile, as they can pull off almost any hairstyle. From pixie cuts to long layers, the options are endless.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your features with hairstyles that have soft layers or wispy bangs. Shoulder-length hair or longer with loose waves can also complement your face shape.

Heart-Shaped Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces can benefit from hairstyles that add volume around the jawline, such as long layers or bob haircuts. Side-swept bangs can also create a more balanced look.

Step by Step

Once you have selected a hairstyle, it's essential to know how to style it properly. Here are some steps to achieve some popular hairstyles:

Beachy Waves

  1. Start with damp hair, and apply a heat protectant.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and curl using a curling wand.
  3. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  4. Apply texturizing spray for a beachy finish.

Sleek Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair back and secure with a hair tie.
  2. Take a small section of hair from underneath the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it.
  3. Use a hairspray to tame any flyaways.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when selecting a hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when selecting a hairstyle.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairdresser to help them understand your vision.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about selecting a hairstyle:

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair growth and how fast your hair becomes damaged. Generally, getting a haircut every 6-8 weeks can help maintain healthy hair.

Can I still style my hair if it's damaged?

Yes, you can still style your hair if it's damaged, but it's essential to be gentle and use products that help repair and protect your hair.

Do I have to follow the rules for my face shape?

No, you don't have to follow the rules for your face shape, but it can be a helpful starting point in finding a flattering hairstyle.


Hair Care

Lastly, maintaining healthy hair is crucial for any hairstyle to look its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

We hope this blog post has helped you determine what sort of hairstyle would suit you. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
