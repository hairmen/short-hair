How To Style Short Wavy Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Oct 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read
If you're a guy with short wavy hair, you might be struggling to find the right style that works for you. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to figure out what will look best. That's why we've put together this comprehensive guide on how to style short wavy hair guys! From the basics to more advanced techniques, we've got you covered.

Description

In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about styling short wavy hair for guys. We'll start with the basics, including how to choose the right products and tools for your hair type. Then, we'll move on to step-by-step instructions for some of the most popular short wavy hairstyles. We'll also share some tips and tricks for maintaining your style and keeping your hair healthy.

How to Style Short Wavy Hair Guys

When it comes to styling short wavy hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you'll need to choose the right products for your hair type. This might include a lightweight styling cream or mousse, as well as a high-quality hair dryer and a diffuser attachment. You'll also want to consider the length and texture of your hair, as well as your personal style preferences.

Once you have your products and tools ready, it's time to start styling! Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few popular short wavy hairstyles:

The Messy Crop

The messy crop is a popular style for guys with short wavy hair. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet. Apply a small amount of lightweight styling cream to your hair, focusing on the roots and tips. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

The Classic Side Part

The classic side part is a timeless look that works well for guys with short wavy hair. To style this look, start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, then apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair. Use a comb to create a deep side part, then blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

The Textured Fringe

The textured fringe is a modern style that's perfect for guys with short wavy hair. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, then apply a small amount of texturizing spray to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style and maintain your short wavy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different products and tools to find what works best for your hair type and style.
  • Try not to over-style your hair, as this can lead to product buildup and greasiness.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

Q: Can I style short wavy hair without using any products?

A: While it's possible to style short wavy hair without using any products, it's generally easier and more effective to use at least a lightweight styling cream or mousse. These products can help add texture and volume to your hair, while also providing hold and control.

Q: How often should I wash my short wavy hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Some guys with short wavy hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once or twice a week. If you have dry or damaged hair, you may want to avoid washing it too frequently, as this can strip away natural oils and cause further damage.

Q: Can I use a straightening iron on my short wavy hair?

A: While it's possible to use a straightening iron on short wavy hair, it's generally not recommended. Straightening irons can cause damage and breakage, especially if used too often or at high temperatures. If you want to straighten your hair, it's best to visit a professional stylist who can use the appropriate tools and techniques.

Hair Care

To keep your short wavy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using high-quality styling products, and avoiding excessive heat and styling. It's also important to visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance, as this can help prevent split ends and breakage.

With the right products and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish looks for your short wavy hair. Whether you prefer a messy, textured look or a classic side part, the key is to experiment and find what works best for you. So go ahead and try out some of these styles, and don't be afraid to get creative!
