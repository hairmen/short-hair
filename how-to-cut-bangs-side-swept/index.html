How To Cut Side Swept Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read
Are you thinking of giving yourself a new look by cutting your own side swept bangs? It may seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a chic and stylish look without breaking the bank. In this article, we’ll guide you through the steps on how to cut side swept bangs and give you some useful tips to help you achieve the best results.

Description

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of glamour and elegance to any look. They are versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. Side swept bangs are characterized by their angled shape, which falls diagonally across the forehead, framing the face and accentuating the eyes.

How to Cut Side Swept Bangs

Before you start, you’ll need to gather the following tools: - A pair of sharp scissors - A comb - Hair clips - A spray bottle filled with water Now, follow these steps to cut your side swept bangs: 1. Start by sectioning off the bangs. Use a comb to create a triangular section at the front of your head, with the point of the triangle at the center of your forehead. 2. Clip the rest of your hair back to keep it out of the way. Make sure the sectioned hair is damp by spraying it with water from the spray bottle. 3. Comb the hair straight down over your forehead. 4. Decide on the length you want your bangs to be. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. It’s important to make small cuts instead of one big cut to avoid mistakes. 5. Now, it’s time to create the angle. Hold the scissors diagonally and cut from the outside of the bangs towards the center. This will create a longer piece of hair on the side of your face. 6. Repeat this process on the other side, making sure the angle matches the first side. 7. Check the length and angle of your bangs in the mirror. If you need to make any adjustments, do so by making small cuts. 8. Once you’re satisfied with the length and angle, use a comb to blend the bangs into the rest of your hair.

Tips

- Always start with small cuts and make adjustments gradually. - Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends. - Make sure your hair is damp before cutting to prevent it from bouncing back. - If you have thick hair, consider thinning out your bangs with thinning scissors to avoid a bulky look. - If you’re uncertain about cutting your own bangs, seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

FAQ

Q: How often should I trim my side swept bangs? A: It’s recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length. Q: Can I cut side swept bangs with curly hair? A: Yes, you can. However, it’s important to cut your bangs when your hair is dry and curly to ensure an accurate length and angle. Q: How do I style side swept bangs? A: Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs. Blow dry them in the direction you want them to fall, and use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.

Hair Care

To keep your side swept bangs looking their best, it’s important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage, and avoid excessive heat styling. Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and prevent split ends. And lastly, don’t forget to embrace your new look and have fun with it! Cutting your own side swept bangs may seem intimidating, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a professional-looking cut. Follow these steps and tips to give yourself a fresh new look that’s sure to turn heads.
