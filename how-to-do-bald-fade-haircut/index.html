How To Do Bald Fade Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then a bald fade is definitely worth considering. This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years and is known for its clean and sharp look. In this article, we will guide you on how to do a bald fade haircut in a relaxed English language to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step-by-Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A bald fade haircut is a type of haircut that starts with long hair on top and gradually fades to a shorter length on the sides and back, eventually leading to a clean-shaven or bald look. This type of haircut is perfect for men who want to maintain a stylish, masculine look with minimal hair maintenance.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step

  1. Start by cutting the hair on the sides and back with a clipper, using a number 1 or 2 guard. Make sure to blend the hair towards the top, leaving it longer.
  2. Move to the top of the hair and use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Make sure to leave enough length to create a contrast with the sides and back.
  3. Next, use the clipper to create a bald fade by slowly decreasing the guard size as you move towards the bottom. Start with a number 3 guard and gradually decrease to a number 2, then a number 1, and finally, a 0 guard.
  4. Use a comb to blend the hair between the top and the sides, making sure there are no harsh lines.
  5. Check the haircut in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  6. Finish the haircut by trimming any stray hairs and cleaning up the neckline.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect bald fade haircut:

  • Make sure to use a sharp clipper and scissors for the cleanest cut.
  • Start with a longer guard size to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from all angles.
  • Try to blend the hair gradually to avoid any harsh lines.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bald fade haircuts:

  • How often should I get a bald fade haircut?

    • You can get a bald fade haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.

  • Can I do a bald fade haircut at home?

    • Yes, you can do a bald fade haircut at home if you have the right tools and follow the instructions carefully.

  • Is a bald fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

    • Yes, a bald fade haircut can work for all hair types, but it's best suited for thicker and coarser hair.

Hair Care

To maintain the bald fade haircut, you will need to keep it trimmed regularly. You can use a clipper with a number 1 or 2 guard to maintain the sides and back, and scissors to trim the top. Make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it soft and healthy.

Now that you know how to do a bald fade haircut, you can confidently walk into any salon or barbershop and ask for the style you want. With a little practice, you can even do it yourself at home. Remember to use the right tools, be patient, and follow the instructions carefully for the best results.
