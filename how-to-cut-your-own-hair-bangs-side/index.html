How To Cut Your Own Hair Bangs Side Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you in need of a change but want to save money on salon visits? Cutting your own hair bangs side is easier than you think! With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish and effortless look. In this post, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair bangs side, along with some tips and hair care advice. Let’s get started!

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair Bangs Side
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Your Own Hair Bangs Side

Cutting your own hair bangs side requires some preparation beforehand. Here are the tools you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Once you have these tools ready, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. Comb your hair and separate your bangs from the rest of your hair.
  2. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face using hair clips.
  3. Decide on the length of your bangs. It's best to start with longer hair and cut gradually.
  4. Hold your bangs between your fingers and cut them straight across. Use the tip of the scissors to make small cuts instead of cutting straight across.
  5. Check the length of your bangs by letting them fall naturally on your forehead. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  6. Trim any uneven edges or strays.
  7. Release the rest of your hair and style as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Bangs Side

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start with longer hair and cut gradually to avoid cutting too much.
  • Use the tip of the scissors to make small cuts instead of cutting straight across.
  • Wet your hair before cutting for a more precise cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Hair tends to shrink when it dries, and you may end up with shorter bangs than you intended.
  • Trim a little at a time and check the length often. It's better to take your time and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair bangs side:

How often should I cut my bangs?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want your bangs to be. On average, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame to trim your bangs.

Should I cut my bangs when they're wet or dry?

It's best to cut your bangs when they're dry. Wet hair tends to shrink when it dries, and you may end up with shorter bangs than you intended.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my bangs?

No, it's best to use sharp hair-cutting scissors. Regular scissors can damage the hair and make it look uneven.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've cut your own hair bangs side, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut your own hair bangs side and enjoy a new and stylish look. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
