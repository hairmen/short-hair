How To Do A High Top Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 29, 2023 · 4 min read
If you’re looking to change up your hairstyle and want to try something new, a high top fade might be the perfect choice for you. This classic hairstyle has been around for years and is still popular today. In this article, we’ll be going over how to do a high top fade in relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a High Top Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect High Top Fade
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Do a High Top Fade

A high top fade is a hairstyle where the hair on the top of your head is much longer than the hair on the sides and back. The longer hair is styled into a flat top, while the sides and back are faded down to the skin. Here’s what you’ll need to do a high top fade: - Clippers - Trimmer - Comb - Haircutting scissors - Hair gel or pomade

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and will ensure that your hair is clean. 2. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head down to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to achieve a gradual fade. 3. Use a trimmer to clean up the hairline around your ears and neck. 4. Cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length using scissors. This can be anywhere from 2-4 inches depending on your preference. 5. Use a comb to create a flat top on the hair on the top of your head. Make sure it is even and symmetrical. 6. Use clippers to fade the hair from the sides up to the flat top. Use a shorter guard as you get closer to the top to create a gradual fade. 7. Use a trimmer to clean up any stray hairs around the edges of the flat top. 8. Apply hair gel or pomade to the hair on the top of your head to keep it in place. 9. Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head to ensure that the fade is even.

Tips for a Perfect High Top Fade

- When using clippers, start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create a gradual fade. - Use a comb to make sure the flat top is even and symmetrical. - Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head to ensure that the fade is even. - Apply hair gel or pomade to the hair on the top of your head to keep it in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I do a high top fade on my own?
A: Yes, you can do a high top fade on your own, but it’s recommended that you have someone to help you with the back and sides. Q: How often should I get a high top fade?
A: It’s recommended that you get a high top fade every 2-4 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care Tips

- Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage. - Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. - Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. In conclusion, a high top fade is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily do a high top fade on your own. Just remember to take your time and follow the step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect look. Don’t forget to take care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly and using the right products.
