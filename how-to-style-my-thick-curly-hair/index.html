How To Style My Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it can be gorgeous and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you're struggling with how to style your thick curly hair, don't worry - we've got you covered. In this post, we'll walk you through a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair to perfection.

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny, and manageable thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Detangle your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions with a hat or scarf.

How to Style My Thick Curly Hair

Now that you know how to take care of your hair, let's move on to styling it. Here are some ideas for how to style your thick curly hair:

  • Wear your hair down and let your curls flow freely.
  • Create loose waves using a curling wand or flat iron.
  • Pull your hair back into a messy bun or ponytail.
  • Twist your hair into a braided updo.
  • Add a hair accessory such as a headband, scarf, or bow.

Step-by-Step Guide

To achieve the perfect style for your thick curly hair, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair by gently squeezing out excess water.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair to help define your curls.
  4. Gently comb through your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle any knots.
  5. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section loosely.
  6. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a diffuser on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low heat.
  7. Once your hair is dry, gently separate the twists to create defined curls.
  8. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.
  9. Style your hair as desired using any of the ideas listed above.
  10. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your thick curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and have fun with your hair!

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick curly hair:

What's the best haircut for thick curly hair?
A layered haircut is great for thick curly hair, as it helps to reduce bulk and add movement to your curls.
How often should I wash my thick curly hair?
It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying out your curls.
How can I prevent frizz in my thick curly hair?
Apply a smoothing serum or hair oil to your hair before styling, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.
Can I straighten my thick curly hair?
Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron, but be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid straightening your hair too often to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your thick curly hair with ease. Remember to take care of your hair properly and have fun experimenting with different styles!
