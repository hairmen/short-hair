White Boy Low Fade Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy yet classic hairstyle, the white boy low fade haircut might be just what you need. This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It's easy to maintain, versatile, and looks great on virtually any guy.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Get White Boy Low Fade Haircut
  3. Step by Step Guide

Description

The white boy low fade is a popular haircut that features a gradual taper from the hairline down to the neck. The hair on top is left longer, while the sides and back are shaved or cut very short. This creates a contrasting look that is both stylish and easy to maintain. This haircut is perfect for guys who want a clean and polished look that is still on-trend.

How To Get White Boy Low Fade Haircut

The first step in getting a white boy low fade haircut is finding the right barber. Look for a barber who has experience with this style and can give you the look you want. Once you've found a barber, it's time to decide on the length and style of your hair. Do you want a longer top or a shorter one? How short do you want the sides and back?

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a white boy low fade haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the length and style you want.
  3. Ask your barber to start with a longer guard on the clippers and gradually work their way down to a shorter guard as they get closer to the neck.
  4. Have your barber use scissors to blend the top and sides for a natural look.
  5. Ask your barber to define the hairline with a straight razor or trimmer.
  6. Finish with styling products, if desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your White Boy Low Fade Haircut

To keep your white boy low fade looking fresh, here are a few tips to follow:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Consider using a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair to avoid damaging it or disrupting the shape of your haircut.

FAQs

How long does a white boy low fade last?

A white boy low fade can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the shape of the haircut.

Is a white boy low fade easy to maintain?

Yes, a white boy low fade is relatively easy to maintain. Regular trims and a good hair care routine can help keep your hair looking fresh and stylish.

What face shapes work best with a white boy low fade?

A white boy low fade can work well with a variety of face shapes, but it generally looks best on guys with oval or square-shaped faces.

Hair Care for Your White Boy Low Fade Haircut

To keep your white boy low fade looking great, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your hair to avoid damaging it or disrupting the shape of your haircut.

With the right barber, regular trims, and a good hair care routine, your white boy low fade can look great for weeks to come.
