How To Cut Long Layers In Short Hair At Home

Written by Frank Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a way to switch up your short hair and give it some volume and texture, long layers are the perfect solution. But going to a salon every few weeks to get your hair trimmed can get expensive. The good news is that you can easily learn how to cut long layers in short hair at home. In this article, we’ll guide you through the steps to achieve this look.

Table of Contents

  • Step by Step Guide

How to Cut Long Layers in Short Hair

Firstly, it's important to gather the necessary tools for this haircut. You will need a pair of hair cutting shears, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. Once you have all the tools ready, wash and dry your hair. It's important to start with clean hair to make sure that the layers come out even.

The next step is to section off your hair. Use the comb to create a middle part and divide your hair into two halves. Then, clip each section to keep them separate from each other. After that, take one section of your hair and divide it into another two parts, one at the top and one at the bottom.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow the steps below to cut long layers in short hair:

  1. Take the top section of hair and comb it straight up. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and slide your fingers down to the desired length of the layer. Make sure that you hold the hair at an angle to create a diagonal line.
  2. Use the hair cutting shears to cut the hair along the diagonal line. It's important to cut the hair in a straight line to avoid any uneven layers.
  3. Repeat the same process for the other side of your hair.
  4. After you’ve cut both sides, let down the other section of hair and repeat the process.
  5. Once you’re done with both sections of your hair, take a look at your hair in the mirror to see if the layers are even. If there are any uneven parts, trim them until you achieve the desired look.

Tips for Cutting Long Layers in Short Hair

Here are some tips that will make the process of cutting long layers in short hair easier:

  • Make sure that you have good lighting when cutting your hair. Natural light is always the best option.
  • Always cut your hair when it's dry. Cutting wet hair can cause it to appear shorter than it actually is.
  • Don't cut too much of your hair at once. It's better to cut small sections at a time to make sure that you don't accidentally cut too much.
  • If you're not confident in cutting your hair, start with a small section and work your way up.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long layers in short hair:

Q: How often should I cut my hair?
A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your layers. Q: Can I cut long layers in curly hair?
A: Yes, you can cut long layers in curly hair. However, it's important to be extra careful when cutting curly hair as it can be difficult to see the length of the layers. Q: How do I maintain my hair after cutting long layers?
A: Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Layers in Short Hair

After you've cut your long layers, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the shape and health of your layers. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from getting dry and brittle. Apply a hair serum to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

Now that you know how to cut long layers in short hair, you can achieve a new look from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time and be patient. With practice, you'll be able to cut long layers like a pro!
