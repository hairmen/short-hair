Why Does My Hair Get Curly As I Get Older

Written by William Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our bodies go through many changes, and our hair is no exception. One of the most common changes we experience is the development of curly hair. In this blog post, we will discuss why this happens and what you can do to care for your curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Have you noticed that your hair is getting curlier as you age? You're not alone. Many people experience a change in their hair texture as they get older. This is because of changes in the hair follicles, which can cause the hair to become curlier or wavier.

The reason for this change is due to the decrease in sebum production in the scalp. Sebum is a natural oil that helps to moisturize and protect the hair. As we age, our bodies produce less sebum, which can cause the hair to become dry and brittle. When this happens, the hair follicles become more porous, and they absorb more moisture from the air, causing the hair to become curly or wavy.

How to

Step by step

If you're new to caring for curly hair, it can be overwhelming. Here are some steps to help you care for your curly hair:

  1. Start by choosing the right products for your hair type. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo, as sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  3. Condition your hair with a moisturizing conditioner, and leave it in for a few minutes to allow it to penetrate the hair shaft.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  5. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  6. Air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use a light oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair and cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about why hair gets curly as we age:

Q: Is it normal for hair to get curlier as you get older?

A: Yes, it's normal for hair to become curlier or wavier as we age.

Q: Can you reverse curly hair?

A: No, you can't reverse curly hair, but you can manage it with the right products and techniques.

Q: What causes curly hair?

A: Curly hair is caused by the shape of the hair follicles. The more oval-shaped the follicle, the curlier the hair will be.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to choose products that are specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid using heat styling tools that can damage the hair. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy, and protect it from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray. With these tips, your curly hair will look its best at any age.
