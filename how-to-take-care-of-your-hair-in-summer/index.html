How To Take Care Of Your Hair In Summer Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Dec 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is a time for fun in the sun, but it can also wreak havoc on your hair. From humidity to chlorine, the summer months can leave your locks feeling dry, frizzy, and damaged. But fear not! With a few simple steps, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful all summer long. In this article, we'll show you how to take care of your hair in summer with our top tips and tricks.

How to Take Care of Your Hair in Summer

Before we dive into the specifics, let's start with some general tips for taking care of your hair in summer:

  • Wash your hair less frequently to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons
  • Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay

Now, let's take a closer look at how to take care of your hair in summer.

1. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's rays can be just as damaging to your hair as they are to your skin. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a scarf when you're outside for extended periods of time. You can also use a hair product that contains SPF to provide an extra layer of protection.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Summer heat and humidity can leave your hair feeling dry and frizzy. To combat this, use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and smooth. Look for a product that contains ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, or argan oil.

3. Rinse Your Hair After Swimming

Chlorine and saltwater can be incredibly damaging to your hair. After swimming, rinse your hair thoroughly with fresh water to remove any chemicals or salt. You can also use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup.

4. Air-Dry Your Hair

During the summer months, avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally. If you need to style your hair, try using a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray for a beachy, tousled look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to take care of your hair in summer:

  1. Wash your hair less frequently to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  2. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair
  4. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons
  5. Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf
  6. Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming
  7. Air-dry your hair naturally
  8. Use a texturizing or sea salt spray for a beachy look
  9. Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay
  10. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy

Tips for Healthy Summer Hair

Here are a few additional tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful during the summer months:

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your hair while you sleep
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free

FAQs

Q: Can I still dye my hair in the summer?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair in the summer, but you should take extra care to protect it from the sun and other environmental factors. Consider using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair to prevent color fading.

Q: How often should I trim my hair in the summer?

A: It's a good idea to get a trim every 8-12 weeks to keep split ends at bay. If you're spending a lot of time in the sun or swimming in chlorinated pools, you may want to get more frequent trims to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful all summer long. Whether you're spending your days at the beach or by the pool, don't forget to take care of your hair!
