How To Get Rid Of Red Hair Dye Without Bleach A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your red hair dye and want to change it up without using bleach? Well, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of red hair dye without bleach.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Dye Without Bleach

Before we get started, it's important to note that getting rid of red hair dye without bleach may take a few tries. Additionally, your hair may not lighten as much as you'd like. However, these methods are much gentler on your hair than using bleach.

Method 1: Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Clarifying shampoos are designed to remove buildup from your hair. They can also help to fade hair color over time. To use a clarifying shampoo to get rid of red hair dye, follow these steps:

  1. Wet your hair
  2. Apply the clarifying shampoo to your hair
  3. Massage the shampoo into your hair and scalp
  4. Leave the shampoo on your hair for 5-10 minutes
  5. Rinse the shampoo out of your hair

Repeat this process every time you wash your hair until you achieve your desired results.

Method 2: Use Vitamin C

Vitamin C is a natural hair lightener and can help to fade red hair dye. To use vitamin C to get rid of red hair dye, follow these steps:

  1. Crush up 10-15 vitamin C tablets
  2. Mix the crushed tablets with shampoo to create a paste
  3. Apply the paste to your hair
  4. Let the paste sit on your hair for 30-60 minutes
  5. Rinse the paste out of your hair

Repeat this process every few days until you achieve your desired results.

Method 3: Use Baking Soda

Baking soda can help to remove buildup from your hair and can also help to fade hair color. To use baking soda to get rid of red hair dye, follow these steps:

  1. Mix baking soda and water to create a paste
  2. Apply the paste to your hair
  3. Let the paste sit on your hair for 15-20 minutes
  4. Rinse the paste out of your hair

Repeat this process every few days until you achieve your desired results.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get rid of red hair dye without bleach:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to help preserve your hair color
  • Deep condition your hair after using any of these methods
  • Be patient - it may take a few tries to achieve your desired results
  • If you have sensitive skin, test these methods on a small patch of skin before applying them to your entire head

FAQ

Can I use these methods on colored hair?

Yes, you can use these methods on colored hair. However, keep in mind that they may also fade your natural hair color.

How long will it take to get rid of my red hair dye?

It may take a few tries before you achieve your desired results. Additionally, the amount of time it takes to get rid of your red hair dye will depend on the method you choose and how long you leave the product on your hair.

Will these methods damage my hair?

These methods are much gentler on your hair than using bleach. However, they may still cause some damage. Be sure to deep condition your hair after using any of these methods.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, be sure to:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray
  • Limit your use of hair tools that use heat

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
