How To Grow Out Wavy Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of short, boring haircuts and ready to embrace your natural waves? Growing out wavy hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the long, luscious locks you’ve always dreamed of. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps on how to grow out wavy hair for guys and provide tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair along the way.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Grow Out Wavy Hair for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Growing Out Wavy Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

How to Grow Out Wavy Hair for Guys

The first step in growing out wavy hair is to stop getting haircuts. Yes, you heard that right. You’ll need to let your hair grow out for a few months before you start shaping it into the style you want. During this time, it’s important to keep your hair healthy by using the right products and avoiding heat styling.

Next, you’ll want to find a good stylist who knows how to work with wavy hair. They can help you choose a style that complements your natural waves and will provide tips on how to style your hair as it grows out.

Another important step is to invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for wavy hair. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that will help keep your hair moisturized and frizz-free. You may also want to try a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help define your waves and keep them looking their best.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to grow out wavy hair for guys:

  1. Stop getting haircuts and let your hair grow out.
  2. Find a stylist who knows how to work with wavy hair.
  3. Invest in high-quality hair products designed for wavy hair.
  4. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  5. Use a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to define your waves.
  6. Avoid heat styling as much as possible.
  7. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  8. Be patient and embrace the awkward stages of hair growth.
  9. Experiment with different styles and products as your hair grows out.
  10. Enjoy your new, luscious locks!

Tips for Growing Out Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for growing out wavy hair:

  • Be patient – growing out hair takes time and there will be awkward stages.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by avoiding hot tools and blow-drying on a low setting.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth from the inside out.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out wavy hair:

How long does it take to grow out wavy hair?

It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year or more to grow out wavy hair, depending on how long you want it to be and how fast your hair grows.

Should I use hair products while growing out my hair?

Yes, it’s important to use hair products designed for wavy hair to keep your hair healthy and defined as it grows out.

Can I still get haircuts while growing out my hair?

Yes, you should still get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and promote healthy growth. Just be clear with your stylist that you’re growing out your hair and don’t want to take off too much length.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips specifically for wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and frizz-free.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to define your waves and keep them looking their best.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible to protect your hair from damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be well on your way to growing out beautiful, healthy wavy hair. Remember to be patient, experiment with different styles and products, and enjoy the journey!
