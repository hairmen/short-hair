How To Style Short Curly Pixie Cut Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a short curly pixie cut, you might be wondering how to style it to make it look its best. Don't worry, you're not alone! In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to style your short curly pixie cut and keep it looking fabulous.

Description

A short curly pixie cut is a popular hairstyle that is both trendy and low maintenance. It's perfect for those who want to show off their natural curls without having to spend a lot of time on styling. This hairstyle is also great for those who want to experiment with different hair colors and highlights.

How to Style a Short Curly Pixie Cut

Styling a short curly pixie cut is easier than you might think. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in styling your short curly pixie cut is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for curly hair to keep your curls looking their best.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

After washing your hair, apply a styling product that will help define your curls. You can use a gel, mousse, or cream, depending on your preference. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Diffuse Your Hair

Next, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and keep them from becoming frizzy. Use a low heat setting and move the diffuser around your head, focusing on the roots and ends of your hair.

Step 4: Define Your Curls

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to define your curls. You can also use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition. Be careful not to overdo it, as too much heat can damage your hair.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Spray

Finally, finish off your look with a hair spray that will help hold your curls in place. Choose a lightweight hairspray that won't weigh down your hair or make it feel crunchy.

Tips for Styling a Short Curly Pixie Cut

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short curly pixie cut:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing it.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Try different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair colors and highlights.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling a short curly pixie cut:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Q: How do I prevent frizz?

A: To prevent frizz, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, avoid brushing your hair, and use a lightweight hairspray.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting on your flat iron.

Hair Care for Short Curly Pixie Cut

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your short curly pixie cut looking its best:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short curly pixie cut with confidence and keep it looking great. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
