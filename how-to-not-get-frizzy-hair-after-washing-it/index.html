How To Not Get Frizzy Hair After Washing It

Written by Bardi Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly or wavy hair, you are probably familiar with frizz. Frizz is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair, which can occur after washing, especially if you don't take the right steps to prevent it. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to not get frizzy hair after washing it.

  • How to Not Get Frizzy Hair After Washing It
  • Step by Step

How to Not Get Frizzy Hair After Washing It

The key to preventing frizz after washing your hair is to keep it moisturized. Here are some steps you can take to avoid frizz:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

When washing your hair, use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to frizz. Look for shampoos that are specifically designed for curly or wavy hair.

2. Condition, condition, condition

After shampooing, be sure to condition your hair. Look for a moisturizing conditioner that will help hydrate your hair and prevent frizz. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Don't towel dry your hair

When you get out of the shower, don't rub your hair with a towel. This can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.

4. Use a leave-in conditioner

After towel drying, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help lock in moisture and prevent frizz. Be sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

5. Don't brush your hair when it's wet

Brushing your hair when it's wet can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

6. Use a diffuser when blow-drying

If you need to blow-dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment. This will help distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz. Be sure to use a low heat setting and hold the dryer at least six inches away from your hair.

7. Use a serum or oil

After blow-drying, apply a serum or oil to your hair. This will help smooth down any frizz and add shine. Be sure to apply the serum or oil evenly throughout your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to prevent frizzy hair after washing it:

  1. Use a gentle shampoo
  2. Condition your hair
  3. Don't towel dry your hair
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner
  5. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb
  6. Use a diffuser when blow-drying
  7. Apply a serum or oil

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help prevent frizz:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons
  • Wear a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to bed
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, which can dry out your hair

FAQ

Q: Why does my hair get frizzy after washing it?

A: Frizz is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair. When you wash your hair, you strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz.

Q: What kind of shampoo should I use to prevent frizz?

A: Look for a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo that is specifically designed for curly or wavy hair.

Q: Can I brush my hair when it's wet?

A: It's best to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet. Brushing can cause breakage and frizz.

Hair Care

By following these tips and tricks, you can prevent frizz and keep your hair looking healthy and hydrated. Remember to use gentle products and avoid using hot tools too often. Taking good care of your hair will help prevent damage and keep it looking great.
