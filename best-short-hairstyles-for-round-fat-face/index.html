Best Short Hairstyles For Round Fat Face

Written by Aprilia Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't flatter your face? If you have a round and fat face, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that suits you. But don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you a list of the best short hairstyles for round fat faces. From pixie cuts to bobs, we've got it all.

Description

Round and fat faces have a unique characteristic, and it's essential to choose a hairstyle that can balance the shape of your face. Short hairstyles are perfect for round and fat faces as they create an illusion of length and add volume to your hair.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, but it's crucial to consider the shape of your face. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is by pulling your hair back and looking into a mirror. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also affect the way your hairstyle looks. If you have thin hair, you may want to consider a layered hairstyle to add volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut might work better for you.

Best Short Hairstyles for Round Fat Face

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle that looks fantastic on round faces. It's a short hairstyle that can create a lot of volume and add height to your hair. This hairstyle is perfect for women who want to show off their facial features.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on round faces. It's a short hairstyle that can help elongate your face and add volume to your hair. You can choose a straight or angled bob cut depending on your preference.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add dimension to your hairstyle. They can help balance out the roundness of your face and create a flattering look. You can pair side-swept bangs with a pixie cut or a bob cut for a chic and stylish look.

4. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is perfect for women with round faces as it can add volume and texture to your hair. You can choose a short or medium-length layered haircut depending on your hair texture and preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. - Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair to create volume. - Use a hair straightener or curling iron to add texture to your hair. - Use hair styling products like mousse or hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

1. Can I dye my hair if I have a round fat face?

Yes, you can dye your hair regardless of your face shape.

2. How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Hair Care

It's crucial to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. - Use a heat protectant spray before using a hair straightener or curling iron. - Avoid using too many styling products on your hair as they can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
