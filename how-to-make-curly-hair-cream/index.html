How To Make Curly Hair Cream Diy Guide

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair is unique and requires special care. It can be challenging to find the right hair products that work for you. One way to ensure that you have a product that works for your curls is to make your own curly hair cream. In this article, we will guide you through how to make curly hair cream that is perfect for your hair type.

Description

Curly hair cream is a product that helps define and enhance the natural curl pattern of your hair. It is a styling product that helps tame frizz and add moisture to your hair. Commercial curly hair creams can be expensive, and they may not work for everyone. Making your own curly hair cream is a cost-effective and customizable option.

How to Make Curly Hair Cream

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup shea butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup aloe vera gel
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vitamin E oil
  • 10 drops essential oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Melt the shea butter and coconut oil in a double boiler.
  2. Add the aloe vera gel, honey, and vitamin E oil.
  3. Stir until well combined.
  4. Add essential oils if desired and stir again.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a clean jar or container.
  6. Let it cool and solidify at room temperature.

Step by Step

1. Start by melting the shea butter and coconut oil in a double boiler. You can also use a microwave to melt them, but make sure to do so in short intervals to avoid burning the mixture.

2. Once the shea butter and coconut oil are melted, add the aloe vera gel, honey, and vitamin E oil. Stir until all the ingredients are well combined.

3. If you want to add essential oils for fragrance, you can do so now. Stir again to ensure the oils are evenly distributed.

4. Transfer the mixture to a clean jar or container. Make sure the container has a tight-fitting lid to prevent air from getting in and spoiling the cream.

5. Allow the mixture to cool and solidify at room temperature. This may take a few hours, depending on the temperature in your home.

Tips

- You can customize this recipe by using different essential oils or adding more honey for extra moisture.

- Be careful not to add too much oil, as this can make your hair greasy.

- If you have fine hair, use this cream sparingly to avoid weighing down your curls.

FAQ

What are the benefits of using curly hair cream?

Curly hair cream helps define and enhance your natural curls, reduces frizz, and adds moisture to your hair. It can also make your hair more manageable and easier to style.

Can I use this cream on straight hair?

This cream is designed for curly hair and may be too heavy for straight hair. However, you can try using a small amount and see how your hair reacts.

How long will this cream last?

This cream should last for several months if stored in a cool, dry place. However, if you notice any changes in the texture or smell of the cream, it may be time to discard it.

Hair Care

Using the right products and techniques is essential for curly hair care. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid disrupting your curl pattern.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

Making your own curly hair cream is a great way to ensure that you have a product that works for your hair type. With this DIY guide, you can make a cost-effective and customizable cream that will help define and enhance your natural curls. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and techniques, and your curls will thank you.
