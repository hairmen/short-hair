How To Tell If You Look Better With Blonde Or Brown Hair

Written by Daniel Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a hair color change? Choosing between blonde and brown can be a difficult decision. In this article, we'll guide you through how to tell if you look better with blonde or brown hair. We'll cover everything from skin tone to hair texture, so you can make an informed decision.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in figuring out if you look better with blonde or brown hair is to determine your skin tone. There are two main categories of skin tone: warm and cool.

If you have warm undertones, your skin has a yellow or golden hue, and you may tan easily. If you have cool undertones, your skin has pink or blue undertones, and you may burn easily.

One way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you likely have cool undertones.

Tip:

If you're still unsure about your skin tone, try holding up a piece of gold jewelry and a piece of silver jewelry to your face. If the gold makes your skin look brighter, you likely have warm undertones. If the silver makes your skin look brighter, you likely have cool undertones.

How to Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Another factor to consider when deciding between blonde and brown hair is your natural hair color. If you have naturally blonde hair, it may be easier to maintain a blonde shade. If you have naturally dark hair, it may be more difficult to achieve a lighter shade of blonde.

Additionally, if you're looking to make a drastic change, keep in mind that it's typically easier to go from light to dark rather than dark to light.

How to Determine Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in determining whether blonde or brown hair is a better choice for you. If you have fine hair, blonde hair may make it appear thicker. If you have thick hair, brown hair may be more flattering.

Additionally, if you have curly or wavy hair, keep in mind that blonde hair may require more maintenance to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

FAQs About Blonde vs. Brown Hair

Q: Will blonde hair make me look younger?

A: Blonde hair can give a more youthful appearance, but it ultimately depends on your individual features and skin tone.

Q: Will brown hair make me look more mature?

A: Brown hair can give a more sophisticated appearance, but again, it ultimately depends on your individual features and skin tone.

Q: Can I dye my hair blonde or brown at home?

A: It's possible to dye your hair at home, but it's recommended to seek professional help to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde and Brown Hair

Whether you choose blonde or brown hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair.

- Limit heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Conclusion

Choosing between blonde and brown hair can be a difficult decision, but by considering your skin tone, natural hair color, and hair texture, you can make an informed choice. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant, no matter what color you choose.
