How To Wear A Fedora Hat With Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to add some style and flair to your wardrobe with a fedora hat, but unsure how to wear it with your long locks? Fear not, for we have put together a complete guide on how to wear a fedora hat with long hair. From choosing the right hat to hair care tips, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Fedora Hat
  • Hair Care Tips
  • How to Wear a Fedora Hat with Long Hair
  • Tips for Wearing a Fedora Hat with Long Hair
  • FAQs

Choosing the Right Fedora Hat

When choosing a fedora hat to wear with long hair, there are a few things to consider. First, make sure the hat fits properly. A hat that is too small will not sit comfortably on your head, while a hat that is too large will slip and feel unstable. Measure your head and try on hats to find the right fit.

Next, consider the color and style of the hat. A darker colored hat can be more versatile and easier to match with different outfits. A classic fedora with a medium brim and a simple band is a great place to start.

Hair Care Tips

Before wearing a fedora hat with long hair, it's important to make sure your hair looks its best. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.
  • Style your hair in a way that complements the hat. A loose wave or a low ponytail can look great under a fedora.

How to Wear a Fedora Hat with Long Hair

Now that you have chosen the right hat and taken care of your hair, it's time to learn how to wear a fedora hat with long hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Style your hair as desired.
  2. Hold the fedora by the brim and place it on your head, tilting it slightly forward.
  3. Adjust the hat so it sits comfortably on your head. Make sure it is not too tight or too loose.
  4. Pull your hair back and tuck it behind your ears. If you have longer hair, you can also twist it into a low bun or braid it.
  5. Check your reflection in the mirror to make sure the hat looks balanced and stylish with your hair.

Tips for Wearing a Fedora Hat with Long Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you rock your fedora hat with long hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the look that works best with your hat.
  • Accessorize your fedora with a scarf or a statement necklace to add some extra flair to your outfit.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles and colors to create a unique look.

FAQs

Can I wear a fedora hat with curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can look great with a fedora hat. Just make sure to style your hair in a way that complements the hat, such as a loose wave or a low bun.

What type of hat should I wear with long hair?

A fedora hat is a great choice for those with long hair. It is versatile, stylish, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Can I wear a fedora hat with short hair?

Yes, you can definitely wear a fedora hat with short hair. Just make sure to choose a hat that fits your head properly and complements your style.

Conclusion

Wearing a fedora hat with long hair can be a stylish and fun way to add some personality to your wardrobe. By choosing the right hat, taking care of your hair, and following our tips and guidelines, you can rock this look with confidence and ease.
