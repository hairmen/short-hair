Best Pomade To Slick Back Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, thick hair that just won't stay put? Look no further than a high-quality pomade to slick back your locks into a stylish and manageable 'do. In this guide, we'll explore the best pomades for thick hair, as well as tips for application and hair care to keep your style looking fresh and polished.

Description

Pomade is a hair styling product that has been used for decades to create slick, polished hairstyles. Unlike gel or hairspray, pomade offers a more natural and flexible hold, making it a great option for thick hair that needs some taming. The best pomades for slicking back thick hair are those that offer a strong hold without leaving your hair feeling greasy or weighed down.

How to Apply Pomade to Slick Back Thick Hair

Before you start applying pomade to your hair, it's important to make sure it's clean and dry. Wet hair will not hold pomade well, and can result in a messy, greasy look. Once your hair is dry, follow these steps to achieve a slick and stylish look:

Step 1: Scoop a small amount of pomade onto your fingers.

Start with a small amount of pomade, about the size of a dime. You can always add more if needed, but it's better to start with less and build up as you go.

Step 2: Rub the pomade between your hands.

Rub your hands together to warm up the pomade and distribute it evenly on your fingers.

Step 3: Apply the pomade to your hair.

Starting at the crown of your head, work the pomade through your hair, using your fingers to slick it back. Make sure to evenly distribute the pomade throughout your hair, paying extra attention to the roots and ends.

Step 4: Comb your hair into place.

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair back into your desired style, smoothing out any bumps or flyaways. For a more polished look, you can also use a boar bristle brush to help distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair.

Tips for Slicking Back Thick Hair

Slicking back thick hair can be a bit trickier than other hair types, but with these tips, you'll be able to achieve a sleek and stylish look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure the pomade holds well.
  • Use a strong-hold pomade to keep thick hair in place.
  • Apply the pomade evenly throughout your hair, paying extra attention to the roots and ends.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

FAQs

What is the best pomade for slicking back thick hair?

The best pomades for slicking back thick hair are those that offer a strong hold without leaving your hair feeling greasy or weighed down. Look for matte pomades for a more natural look, or high-shine pomades for a polished finish.

Can you slick back thick hair without pomade?

While it's possible to slick back thick hair without pomade, it may be more difficult to achieve a sleek and polished look. Pomade offers a flexible hold that can help tame even the most unruly hair.

How often should I wash my hair if I use pomade?

It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days if you use pomade regularly. This will help prevent build-up and keep your hair looking and feeling healthy.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

To keep your slicked-back hairstyle looking fresh and polished, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent build-up.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the follicles and lead to breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With the right pomade and quality hair care, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look that will turn heads wherever you go. So go ahead and give it a try!
