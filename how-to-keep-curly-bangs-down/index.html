How To Keep Curly Bangs Down Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your curly bangs always bouncing back up no matter how much you try to keep them down? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many individuals with curly hair struggle with keeping their bangs down. But fear not, we've got you covered with some tips and tricks to help you keep your curly bangs down all day long.

Description

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful and unique, it can also be difficult to manage. Curly bangs, in particular, can be quite challenging to style and keep in place. Fortunately, there are some simple techniques you can use to keep your curly bangs down without ruining your hairstyle.

How to Keep Curly Bangs Down

Here are some tips on how to keep your curly bangs down:

1. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser

Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer can help set your curls in place and prevent your bangs from bouncing back up. Simply apply some styling cream or mousse to your wet hair and use the diffuser on low heat to dry your hair.

2. Use a styling product

Using a styling product such as hairspray or gel can help keep your curly bangs down. Apply the product to your bangs and use a brush or your fingers to smooth them down.

3. Pin your bangs down

Using bobby pins or hair clips to pin your bangs down is a simple and effective way to keep them in place. Simply twist your bangs and pin them down with a bobby pin or hair clip. Make sure to hide the pins by placing them underneath your hair.

4. Use a headband

Wearing a headband can help keep your curly bangs down and prevent them from bouncing back up. Choose a headband that matches your hair color and style it over your bangs.

5. Trim your bangs

If your bangs are too long, they may be more difficult to keep down. Consider trimming your bangs to a shorter length to make them easier to manage.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to keep your curly bangs down:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to keep it hydrated.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product such as mousse, gel, or hairspray to your wet hair. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, paying special attention to your bangs.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Set the dryer to low heat and gently scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 4: Pin your bangs down

Twist your bangs and use bobby pins or hair clips to pin them down. Make sure to hide the pins by placing them underneath your hair.

Step 5: Use a headband

If your bangs are still bouncing back up, try wearing a headband over your bangs to keep them in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you keep your curly bangs down:

1. Use a silk or satin pillowcase

Using a silk or satin pillowcase can help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact while you sleep.

2. Avoid touching your hair

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz and make your curls lose their shape. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help detangle your curls without causing breakage. Use the comb to gently brush through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

FAQ

Q. How can I prevent my bangs from frizzing?

A. To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much and use a silk or satin pillowcase.

Q. How often should I trim my curly bangs?

A. You should trim your bangs every six to eight weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Keeping your curly bangs down requires proper hair care. Make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and avoid using hot tools on your hair too often. Also, be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
