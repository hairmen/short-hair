How To Make Hair Waves Without Heat

How to Make Hair Waves Without Heat

Table of Contents:

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

If you're tired of using heat to create waves in your hair, fear not! There are several ways to make hair waves without heat. Not only is this method healthier for your hair, but it's also more sustainable and saves you money on styling tools.

Tools You Will Need

Before we get started, you will need a few tools:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair elastics
  • Hair clips
  • Hair mousse or sea salt spray
  • Satin scarf or bonnet

Step by Step

Method 1: Braids

Follow these steps to create waves with braids:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply hair mousse or sea salt spray to your hair.
  3. Part your hair in the middle, or create a side part.
  4. Divide your hair into three sections: left, right, and back.
  5. Braid each section of hair tightly all the way down to the ends.
  6. Secure each braid with a hair elastic.
  7. Leave the braids in for at least 4-6 hours, or overnight.
  8. Unravel the braids and gently comb through your hair with your fingers.
  9. Use hair clips to separate your hair and create volume.
  10. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Method 2: Twists

Follow these steps to create waves with twists:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply hair mousse or sea salt spray to your hair.
  3. Part your hair in the middle, or create a side part.
  4. Divide your hair into two sections: left and right.
  5. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it tightly all the way down to the ends.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Secure each twist with a hair elastic.
  8. Leave the twists in for at least 4-6 hours, or overnight.
  9. Unravel the twists and gently comb through your hair with your fingers.
  10. Use hair clips to separate your hair and create volume.
  11. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make the most out of this heatless hair wave method:

  • Apply hair mousse or sea salt spray to damp hair to help hold the waves.
  • For tighter waves, create smaller braids or twists.
  • For looser waves, create larger braids or twists.
  • Leave the braids or twists in for a longer period of time for more defined waves.
  • Use a satin scarf or bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep.

FAQ

Can I use this method on all hair types?

Yes, this method can be used on all hair types.

What products should I use?

You can use either hair mousse or sea salt spray to help hold the waves.

How long should I leave the braids or twists in?

You should leave the braids or twists in for at least 4-6 hours, or overnight for best results.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and minimizing heat styling. This heatless hair wave method is a great way to give your hair a break from heat damage.
