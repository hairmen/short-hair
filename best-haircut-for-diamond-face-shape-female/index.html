Best Haircut For Diamond Face Shape Female

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a female with a diamond face shape and wondering which haircut would best suit your features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for diamond face shape female and provide you with tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones. The goal of a good haircut for this face shape is to balance out the width of the cheekbones and create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircut for diamond face shape female, it's crucial to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do so is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or washable marker. Once you've done that, compare the outline to the following face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Best Haircut for Diamond Face Shape Female

The best haircut for diamond face shape female is one that adds volume to the top of the head and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. Here are some of the best options:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and chic haircut that is perfect for diamond face shape females. It adds volume to the top of the head and creates a more balanced look. Opt for a longer pixie cut with soft layers to frame the face and add texture.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic and timeless haircut that works well with all face shapes, including diamond. Choose a chin-length bob with soft layers to add volume to the top of the head and create a more defined jawline.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

If you're not ready for a drastic haircut, consider adding side-swept bangs to your current hairstyle. This will help balance out the width of your cheekbones and create a more symmetrical look.

Step by Step Guide

If you've decided to go for a pixie or bob cut, here is a step-by-step guide for achieving the perfect haircut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back
  3. Starting from the back, use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length
  4. Move to the sides and cut at a slight angle to add volume to the top of the head
  5. Finally, cut the top section of your hair to add texture and volume

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

FAQ

1. Can diamond face shape females have long hair?

Yes, diamond face shape females can have long hair. However, it's essential to add volume to the top of the head and create a more defined jawline to balance out the width of the cheekbones.

2. Can I get a fringe with a diamond face shape?

Avoid getting a full fringe as it can make your face look shorter. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs to balance out the width of your cheekbones.

3. Can I still style my hair in different ways?

Yes, you can still style your hair in different ways. However, it's essential to keep in mind the shape of your face and add volume to the top of the head to balance out the width of your cheekbones.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving the perfect haircut. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant before using heat-styling tools
  • Avoid brushing wet hair to prevent breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair

Now that you know the best haircut for diamond face shape female, it's time to book your next hair appointment and rock your new look!
