How To Do Hairstyles For Long Hair At Home

Written by William Jun 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long hair, you are probably always looking for new and interesting ways to style it. Going to a salon can be expensive, but with a little bit of practice, you can learn how to do hairstyles for long hair at home. In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to create a variety of hairstyles, as well as tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before we get into the specific hairstyles, here are some general tips on how to do hairstyles for long hair at home:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb
  • Use heat protectant spray before using any hot tools
  • Practice on a mannequin head or a friend before trying a new style on yourself
  • Be patient and don't rush the process
  • Have fun and experiment with different styles!

Step by Step

1. Braids

Braids are a versatile hairstyle that can be worn on any occasion. Here is how to create a basic three-strand braid:

  1. Start by dividing your hair into three equal sections
  2. Take the right section and cross it over the middle section
  3. Take the left section and cross it over the middle section
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you reach the end of your hair
  5. Tie off the braid with a hair tie

You can also experiment with different types of braids, such as a fishtail braid or a Dutch braid.

2. Ponytail

A simple ponytail can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. Here is how to create a basic ponytail:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail or a low ponytail
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie
  3. You can also add a hair accessory, such as a scrunchie or a bow, to dress up the ponytail

3. Bun

A bun is a classic hairstyle that can be worn to formal events or to the gym. Here is how to create a basic bun:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  4. You can also add a hair accessory, such as a hairpin or a flower, to dress up the bun

Tips

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and hairpins
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques
  • Take care of your hair by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week

FAQ

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled?

A: Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it is wet.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people can get away with washing their hair every other day or every three days.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it is important to take care of it on a daily basis. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and practicing different hairstyles, you can learn how to do hairstyles for long hair at home with confidence. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles!
