How To Stop Short Hair Sticking Up At The Back Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair, you know how frustrating it can be when it sticks up at the back. No matter how much you try to style it, it seems to have a mind of its own. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to stop short hair from sticking up at the back.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Stop Short Hair Sticking Up at the Back

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it's important to understand why your short hair is sticking up at the back. There are a few reasons why this might be happening:

  • Your hair is too short
  • Your hair is too thick
  • Your hair is too dry
  • Your hair is damaged

Now that you know the possible reasons, let's get into the tips and tricks to stop short hair from sticking up at the back.

1. Use a Strong Hold Product

One of the easiest ways to combat short hair sticking up at the back is to use a strong hold product like gel or pomade. These products will help keep your hair in place and prevent it from sticking up. Just make sure not to use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair in the Right Direction

Another helpful tip is to blow dry your hair in the right direction. Use a round brush to guide your hair in the direction you want it to go. This will help set your hair in place and prevent it from sticking up.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

If your hair is too short, it might be time for a trim. Regular trims can help keep your hair looking neat and prevent it from sticking up at the back. Make sure to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair in top shape.

4. Use a Flat Iron

A flat iron can be a useful tool to tame short hair that sticks up. Use it to smooth out any flyaways or stubborn pieces of hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand to prevent damage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to stop short hair from sticking up at the back:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  2. Apply a strong hold product like gel or pomade to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair in the right direction using a round brush.
  4. Finish with a flat iron to smooth out any flyaways.
  5. Use hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help prevent short hair from sticking up at the back:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron to prevent damage.
  • Try a different haircut if your current style isn't working for you.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage.

FAQs

Q: Why is my short hair sticking up at the back?

A: There could be a few reasons why your short hair is sticking up at the back, including your hair being too short, too thick, too dry, or damaged.

Q: What products can I use to stop short hair from sticking up at the back?

A: Strong hold products like gel or pomade can be helpful in keeping short hair in place. Hairspray can also be used to set your style in place.

Q: How often should I trim my short hair?

A: It's recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hair looking neat and prevent it from sticking up at the back.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Proper hair care is essential for preventing short hair from sticking up at the back. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can say goodbye to short hair sticking up at the back and hello to a sleek and stylish look!
