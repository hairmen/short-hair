How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for School

When it comes to styling shoulder length hair for school, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to make sure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles. Second, you want to choose a hairstyle that is easy to do and doesn't take too much time. And finally, you want to make sure that the hairstyle you choose complements your facial features and overall style.

Choose the Right Hairstyle

There are many different hairstyles that are perfect for shoulder length hair. Some of the most popular options include:

  • Braids
  • Ponytails
  • Buns
  • Half-Up, Half-Down Styles
  • Beachy Waves
  • Straight and Sleek

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your personal style and what will work best for your hair type. If you have thick hair, a sleek and straight style may be best, while those with finer hair may prefer a wavy or curly style.

Step-by-Step Guide for Easy Hairstyles

Now that you know what types of hairstyles are best for shoulder length hair, let's take a look at some step-by-step guides for easy hairstyles.

Braids

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a small section of hair from one side and begin braiding it.
  4. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section.
  5. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. If desired, pull the braids apart slightly to create a fuller look.

Ponytails

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with a hair tie.
  3. If desired, wrap a small piece of hair around the hair tie to hide it.

Buns

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with a hair tie.
  3. Twist the ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins or another hair tie.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

Now that you have some easy hairstyles for school, let's talk about how to maintain your shoulder length hair.

  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair type is best for shoulder length hair?

Shoulder length hair works well for all hair types, but those with fine hair may find that shorter hair works better.

How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. However, most people find that washing their hair every 2-3 days works well.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

In addition to the tips mentioned earlier, there are a few more things you can do to keep your shoulder length hair healthy and looking great.

  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

With these easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair for school and hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking great all year round. Whether you prefer braids, buns, or ponytails, these hairstyles are sure to make you look and feel confident.
