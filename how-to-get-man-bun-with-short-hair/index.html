How To Get Man Bun With Short Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair and want to rock a man bun, don’t worry! It’s possible to achieve the look you want with just a few simple steps. In this blog post, we’ll show you how to get a man bun with short hair in easy-to-follow steps. We’ll also give you some tips on how to maintain your man bun and answer some frequently asked questions about the style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Man Bun with Short Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to get a man bun with short hair:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair Out

If your hair is currently too short for a man bun, you’ll need to give it some time to grow. Depending on how fast your hair grows, this could take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months.

Step 2: Start with a Low Ponytail

Once your hair is long enough, start by tying it back into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Use an elastic hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Next, twist your hair around the base of the ponytail until it forms a tight spiral. Keep twisting until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 4: Wrap the End Around the Base

Once you’ve twisted your hair, wrap the end around the base of the ponytail. Use another hair elastic to secure it in place.

Step 5: Tuck in any Loose Ends

If you have any loose ends sticking out, tuck them in with some bobby pins. You can also use a bit of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect man bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color for a more natural look.
  • Try using a hair wax or pomade to help keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a half-up bun.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about man buns:

Can I get a man bun with really short hair?

If your hair is too short for a ponytail, you won’t be able to achieve a man bun. However, if your hair is at least a few inches long, you can follow the steps above to create a man bun.

How long does my hair need to be to get a man bun?

It’s best to have at least 6-8 inches of hair to create a man bun. However, you may be able to achieve the look with shorter hair if your hair is thick or curly.

How do I maintain my man bun?

To maintain your man bun, make sure to keep your hair clean and well-groomed. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as from a blow dryer or flat iron.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and steps, you can easily achieve a man bun with short hair. Remember to be patient while your hair grows out and experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you. With a little practice and care, you can rock a man bun like a pro!
