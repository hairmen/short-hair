How To Cut Curtain Bangs Professionally Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to try this year? Curtain bangs are a popular trend that can add a touch of sophistication and style to your look. However, if you're not careful, cutting your own bangs can be a disaster. In this article, we'll teach you how to cut curtain bangs professionally so you can achieve the perfect look.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted in the center and styled to frame your face. They're called "curtain" bangs because they resemble the drapes on a window. This style is perfect for those who want to add some flair to their hair without committing to a drastic cut.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs Professionally

Follow these simple steps to cut your own curtain bangs like a pro:

Step 1: Gather your materials

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Section off your hair

Use the comb to create a center part in your hair. Then, section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Determine your bangs' length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. It's always best to start longer than you think you want because you can always cut more later. Use your scissors to trim your bangs to the desired length.

Step 4: Cut your bangs at an angle

To achieve the curtain effect, you'll need to cut your bangs at an angle. Start at the outer corner of your eyebrow and cut upward at a 45-degree angle. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Blend your bangs

Use your scissors to blend the ends of your bangs so they're not too blunt. This will create a more natural look.

Step 6: Style your bangs

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to style your bangs. Blow dry them in the direction you want them to fall. You can also use a flat iron to smooth them out.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curtain bangs:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Don't cut too much at once
  • Take breaks to step back and assess your progress
  • Be patient and take your time

FAQ

Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs. However, it's important to be careful and take your time to avoid mistakes.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

What face shape looks best with curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs look great on most face shapes, but they're especially flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid oil buildup
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your bangs to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, which can make your bangs look stiff

With these tips, you can cut your own curtain bangs like a pro and enjoy a stylish new look.
