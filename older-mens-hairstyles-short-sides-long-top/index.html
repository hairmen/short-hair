Description

Older mens hairstyles short sides long top have become increasingly popular in recent years. This style is characterized by shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. It is a versatile look that can be worn in many different ways, depending on your personal style and preference. Whether you want a classic, polished look, or something more edgy and modern, this hairstyle is a great choice for older men.

How To

Getting the perfect older mens hairstyles short sides long top requires some skill and technique. Here are the basic steps to achieve this look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup, and make your hair easier to work with.
  2. Next, use a comb to create a part on one side of your head. This will help to give your hair some structure and direction.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, trim the hair on the sides of your head. The length will depend on your personal preference, but a good rule of thumb is to keep it shorter than the hair on top.
  4. Once you have trimmed the sides, move on to the top of your head. Use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length, leaving it longer than the hair on the sides.
  5. Style your hair using a styling product, such as gel or pomade. This will help to keep it in place and give it some shape.

Step By Step

Here is a more detailed breakdown of the steps involved in creating older mens hairstyles short sides long top:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, and towel dry it until it is damp.
  2. Use a comb to create a part on one side of your head.
  3. Starting at the bottom of your head, use clippers to trim the hair on the sides. Use a longer guard if you want to leave more length, or a shorter guard if you want a more closely cropped look.
  4. Move on to the top of your head, and use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. If you want a more textured look, you can use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk.
  5. Style your hair using a product of your choice. Use a small amount to start, and add more as needed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect older mens hairstyles short sides long top:

  • Choose a product that works well with your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to use a lightweight product that won't weigh it down.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. You can try slicking your hair back, creating a quiff, or leaving it messy and textured.
  • Visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting this style. They can help you to achieve the perfect look, and give you advice on how to maintain it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about older mens hairstyles short sides long top:

  • Is this style suitable for all hair types? Yes, this style can work well with most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.
  • How often should I get my hair cut? It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how neat you want to keep the style. A good rule of thumb is to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks.
  • What products should I use to style my hair? This will depend on your personal preference and hair type. Some good options include gel, pomade, wax, and clay.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle, including older mens hairstyles short sides long top. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can damage your hair over time.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, which can help to promote healthy hair growth.
