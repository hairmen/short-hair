How To Control Frizzy Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy, wavy hair? Do you struggle to find products that actually work to tame your locks? If so, you're not alone. Many people with wavy hair struggle with frizz, especially in humid or rainy weather. But don't worry, there are some simple steps you can take to control frizzy wavy hair and achieve the smooth, sleek look you desire.

Description

Frizzy wavy hair can be frustrating to deal with, especially when you're trying to achieve a sleek and polished look. Frizz can be caused by a number of factors, including humidity, dryness, and damage from heat styling tools. Wavy hair is particularly prone to frizz, as the natural texture of the hair makes it more susceptible to moisture and humidity. However, with the right techniques and products, you can control frizz and achieve a smooth, defined wavy look.

How to Control Frizzy Wavy Hair

Step 1: Use the Right Products

The first step in controlling frizzy wavy hair is to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as curl defining creams or mousses. These products will help to define your waves and keep them in place, while also controlling frizz. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and make frizz worse. Instead, look for products that contain natural oils or moisturizing ingredients.

Step 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair and can make frizz worse. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings. Instead, use a lower heat setting and take your time when styling your hair.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair Gently

When drying your hair, avoid rubbing it vigorously with a towel. This can cause frizz and damage to your hair. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water with a towel and allow your hair to air dry as much as possible. If you need to blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment and dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Step 4: Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and prevent damage to your hair. Silk is a smooth and gentle fabric that won't cause friction or tangles, which can lead to frizz. It also helps to retain moisture in your hair, which can prevent dryness and frizz.

Tips for Controlling Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for controlling frizzy wavy hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment or other smoothing treatment to help control frizz
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize and protect your hair from frizz
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and make frizz worse

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair curly if I want to control frizz?

A: Yes! You can still wear your hair curly even if you want to control frizz. In fact, many people with wavy hair prefer to wear their hair curly. The key is to use the right products and techniques to define your curls and control frizz.

Q: Will cutting my hair help control frizz?

A: It depends on the length and texture of your hair. If your hair is long and prone to frizz, cutting it shorter may help to control frizz. However, if your hair is naturally wavy or curly, cutting it too short may actually make frizz worse. Talk to your stylist about the best haircut for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for Frizzy Wavy Hair

Caring for your hair is an important part of controlling frizz. Here are some hair care tips for frizzy wavy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for wavy hair
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and make frizz worse
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help moisturize and nourish your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or scarf, or using a heat protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to frizz

By following these tips and techniques, you can control frizzy wavy hair and achieve the sleek, defined waves you desire. Remember, everyone's hair is unique, so it may take some trial and error to find the right products and techniques that work for you.
