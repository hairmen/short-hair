Best Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 50

As we age, our hair starts to lose its shine and volume. However, with the right hairstyle, we can still look fabulous and confident. If you have an oval face and you're over 50, you're in luck! This face shape is one of the most versatile, and there are many hairstyles that can flatter your features. In this post, we'll share the best hairstyles for oval faces over 50, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval faces over 50, let's define what an oval face is. An oval face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline and high cheekbones. This face shape is considered the most balanced and versatile, as it can pull off almost any hairstyle.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Your Oval Face

When choosing a hairstyle for your oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some hairstyles work better with thin hair, while others are better suited for thick hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle. Do you have time to style your hair every day, or do you prefer low-maintenance styles?
  • Consider your personal style. Are you more classic, edgy, or bohemian?
  • Choose a hairstyle that balances your features. For example, if you have a long face, you'll want to add width with layers or bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the best hairstyle for your oval face, it's time to style your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from hot tools.
  3. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or straighten your hair, depending on your chosen hairstyle.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to hold your style in place.
  5. Finish with a shine spray or serum to add gloss and smooth any flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for oval faces over 50?

The best hairstyles for oval faces over 50 include short bobs, long layers, and medium-length cuts with soft waves or curls.

What hair color looks best on oval faces over 50?

The best hair color for oval faces over 50 depends on your skin tone and personal style. However, some popular options include warm browns, cool blondes, and natural-looking highlights.

How can I add volume to my hair?

To add volume to your hair, try backcombing the roots, using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, or using a dry shampoo to add texture.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

Short Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Short hairstyles can be chic and sophisticated, and they're perfect for oval faces over 50. Here are some options to consider:

  • A classic bob with side-swept bangs
  • A pixie cut with textured layers
  • A short shag with feathered layers

Medium Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Medium-length hairstyles are versatile and easy to style, making them a great choice for oval faces over 50. Here are some options to consider:

  • A shoulder-length cut with long layers
  • A lob (long bob) with beachy waves
  • A layered cut with face-framing highlights

Long Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 50

Long hairstyles can be elegant and youthful, and they're perfect for oval faces over 50. Here are some options to consider:

  • A long, layered cut with side-swept bangs
  • A half-up, half-down style with loose waves
  • A sleek, straight style with a center part

Conclusion

Choosing the best hairstyle for your oval face over 50 can be a fun and rewarding process. Remember to consider your hair texture, personal style, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny!
