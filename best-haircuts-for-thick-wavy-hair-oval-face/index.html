Best Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair Oval Face

Written by Eveline Jun 25, 2023 · 5 min read
Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair and oval face? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best haircuts for this specific hair type and face shape. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the best haircut, step-by-step instructions for achieving the look, tips for maintenance, and frequently asked questions about haircare.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be overwhelming to manage, especially if you have an oval face shape. However, with the right haircut, you can accentuate your natural hair texture and highlight your facial features. The perfect haircut for thick wavy hair oval face should balance out the volume of your hair and enhance your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Best Haircut

When choosing the best haircut for your thick wavy hair and oval face, consider the following factors:

Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile, as it can accommodate a variety of haircuts. However, make sure to avoid haircuts that elongate your face, such as long and straight haircuts. Instead, opt for haircuts that add volume and texture to your hair, such as layered and tousled haircuts.

Hair Texture

Thick wavy hair requires haircuts that enhance its natural texture. Avoid haircuts that require excessive styling or straightening, as they can damage your hair over time. Instead, choose haircuts that embrace your natural waves and curls, such as messy bobs, shags, and long layers.

Lifestyle

Consider your daily routine and lifestyle when choosing the best haircut for your thick wavy hair and oval face. If you have a busy schedule and don't have much time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to spare, experiment with haircuts that require more maintenance and styling.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Once you have chosen the best haircut for your thick wavy hair and oval face, follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve the look:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before getting a haircut, consult with your hairstylist to discuss your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Your hairstylist can recommend the best haircut for your specific needs and provide professional advice on how to maintain your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help retain moisture in your hair and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Towel-Dry Your Hair

Towel-dry your hair gently to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as it can cause breakage and frizz.

Step 4: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that is suitable for your hair type, such as a curl-defining cream or mousse. This will enhance your natural waves and curls and provide hold and definition.

Step 5: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat. This will help enhance your natural waves and curls and provide volume and texture.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or wand to add more definition to your waves and curls. You can also use a flat iron to straighten out any frizz or flyaways.

Tips for Maintenance

To maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy, follow these tips:

Tip 1: Use Sulfate-Free Products

Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out and becoming frizzy.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 4: Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural waves and curls and avoid excessive styling or straightening, as it can damage your hair over time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircare for thick wavy hair and oval face:

Q: What haircut is best for thick wavy hair and oval face?

A: The best haircut for thick wavy hair and oval face is a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair, such as layered and tousled haircuts.

Q: How do I maintain my thick wavy hair?

A: To maintain your thick wavy hair, use sulfate-free products, trim your hair regularly, protect your hair from heat, and embrace your natural texture.

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your thick wavy hair using a flat iron. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your scalp from becoming dry and itchy.

Tip 2: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment every week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

Tip 3: Avoid Overstyling Your Hair

Avoid overstyling your hair with excessive heat and chemicals, as it can damage your hair over time.

Tip 4: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. In conclusion, the best haircut for thick wavy hair oval face should enhance your natural texture and balance out the volume of your hair. Choose a haircut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, and follow the step-by-step instructions and tips for maintenance to achieve the perfect look. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock your thick wavy hair and oval face with confidence.
