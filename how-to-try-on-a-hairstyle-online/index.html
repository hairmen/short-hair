How To Try On A Hairstyle Online Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Eveline Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Trying on a new hairstyle can be a daunting task. What if it doesn't suit you? What if it takes too long to style? With the rise of technology, trying on a hairstyle online has become a popular way to experiment with different looks. In this article, we'll discuss how to try on a hairstyle online, step by step, as well as share some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Try on a Hairstyle Online
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Try on a Hairstyle Online

Trying on a hairstyle online can be done through virtual makeover tools, apps, and websites. These tools use your uploaded photo or live camera to show you how different hairstyles would look on you. To get started, here are the steps:

Step 1: Find a Virtual Makeover Tool or App

There are many virtual makeover tools and apps available online. Some popular examples include:

  • Hair Color Booth
  • Try On Hairstyles
  • ModiFace
  • Virtual Makeover

Select the tool or app that suits your needs and preferences.

Step 2: Upload Your Photo or Use Your Live Camera

Most virtual makeover tools and apps will require you to upload a photo of yourself or use your live camera to capture your current look. Make sure the lighting is good and your face is visible.

Step 3: Choose a Hairstyle

Once your photo is uploaded or your live camera is activated, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles. Browse through the available options and select the one you want to try on.

Step 4: Adjust the Hairstyle to Fit Your Face

Most virtual makeover tools and apps allow you to adjust the hairstyle to fit your face shape and size. Use the available tools to resize, rotate, and move the hairstyle until it looks natural on you.

Step 5: Save or Share Your New Look

Once you're happy with your new hairstyle, you can save the photo or share it with your friends and family on social media. Some virtual makeover tools and apps also allow you to purchase the hairstyle or book an appointment with a hairstylist.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step by step guide on how to try on a hairstyle online:

  1. Find a virtual makeover tool or app that suits your needs and preferences.
  2. Upload a clear photo of yourself or use your live camera to capture your current look.
  3. Browse through the available hairstyles and select the one you want to try on.
  4. Adjust the hairstyle to fit your face shape and size using the available tools.
  5. Experiment with different hair colors and accessories (optional).
  6. Save your new look or share it with your friends and family on social media.

Tips for Trying on a Hairstyle Online

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying on a hairstyle online:

  • Choose a virtual makeover tool or app that offers a wide variety of hairstyles to choose from.
  • Make sure the lighting in your photo is good and your face is visible.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, but also consider your face shape and personal style.
  • Adjust the hairstyle to fit your face shape and size, but also keep it looking natural.
  • Consider trying on different hair colors and accessories to complete your look.
  • Save your new look and show it to your hairstylist for inspiration.

FAQs

What is a virtual makeover tool?

A virtual makeover tool is an online tool that allows you to try on different hairstyles, makeup, and accessories using your uploaded photo or live camera.

Do virtual makeover tools and apps cost money?

Most virtual makeover tools and apps offer free trials or limited versions. However, some may require a subscription or purchase to access all features.

Can virtual makeover tools and apps accurately show me how a hairstyle would look on me?

Virtual makeover tools and apps can give you an idea of how a hairstyle would look on you, but keep in mind that they are not always 100% accurate. Factors such as lighting, hair texture, and face shape can affect the final result.

Hair Care Tips

Trying on a new hairstyle can be exciting, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and health.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions with a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to try on a hairstyle online, go ahead and experiment with different looks! Remember to also take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Happy hairstyling!
