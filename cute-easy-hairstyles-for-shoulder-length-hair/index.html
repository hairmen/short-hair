Cute Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Robby Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you have shoulder length hair and want to switch up your look? Look no further! We have compiled a list of cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair that will have you looking fabulous in no time.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can take on many different styles. From braids to updos, there are endless options to choose from. Our list includes hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion, whether it be a casual day out or a formal event.

How To

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it is important to know some basic tips for styling shoulder length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools
  • Invest in good quality hair products
  • Don't over wash your hair, 2-3 times a week is enough
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair

Step by Step

1. The Half Up Half Down

This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out. Simply take the top half of your hair and tie it into a ponytail, leaving the rest of your hair down. You can also add some loose waves to give your hair some texture.

2. The Messy Bun

A messy bun is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it into a bun. Pull out some pieces of hair to create a messy, effortless look.

3. The Side Braid

A side braid is a cute and easy hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Simply gather your hair to one side and braid it. You can also add some texture by pulling out some pieces of hair.

4. The Top Knot

A top knot is a great hairstyle for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a knot. Secure it with bobby pins and you're done!

5. The Low Bun

A low bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for formal events. Gather your hair into a low ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins and add some hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your shoulder length hair:

  • Invest in good quality hair accessories, such as hair ties and bobby pins
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Q: Can I still style my hair if it's thin?

A: Yes, absolutely! There are many different hairstyles that work well for thin hair, such as the half up half down and the messy bun.

Q: Can I use hot tools on my hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant before using any hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Are these hairstyles suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, these hairstyles are versatile and can be adapted to suit any face shape.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your hair, it is important to take care of it as well. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

With these cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you'll never have a bad hair day again!
