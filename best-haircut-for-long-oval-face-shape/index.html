Best Haircut For Long Oval Face Shape Tips And Hairstyle Ideas

Written by Christine Sep 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a long oval face shape, you're lucky because you can pull off many different hairstyles. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape. In this blog post, we'll share the best haircuts for long oval face shape and provide you with tips and hairstyle ideas to help you achieve your desired look.

Description

A long oval face shape is characterized by a lengthened face with a curved jawline and a high forehead. The length of the face is longer than the width, and the cheekbones are not very prominent. The goal of a haircut for this face shape is to create the illusion of width and balance the face's length.

How to

Before you head to the salon, it's essential to know what kind of haircut you want. It's best to bring a photo to show your hairstylist to ensure you both have the same understanding of your desired look. Here are some tips to help you choose the best haircut for your long oval face shape:

1. Choose Haircuts that Add Width

Since long oval faces are elongated, you'll want to add width to your hair to balance your face's length. To achieve this, you can opt for haircuts with layers, waves, or curls. These textures add volume and body to your hair, making your face appear wider.

2. Avoid Haircuts with Length

While long hair is beautiful, it's not ideal for long oval face shapes. Long hair can make your face appear even longer, which can throw off your facial proportions. Instead, opt for haircuts that fall above or below your collarbone. These lengths will balance your face's length and create a more harmonious look.

3. Consider Bangs

Bangs are a great way to soften your face's features and add balance to your face shape. If you have a high forehead, choose bangs that fall at or above your eyebrows to conceal it. If you have a small forehead, opt for wispy bangs that fall diagonally across your forehead.

Step by Step Guide

If you're not sure what kind of haircut to get, here are some hairstyle ideas to consider:

1. Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a timeless haircut that suits long oval face shapes. The bob's length should fall just below your chin to create the illusion of width. You can add texture by incorporating subtle layers or opting for an asymmetrical cut.

2. Shoulder-Length Hair with Layers

Shoulder-length hair with layers is another great option for long oval face shapes. The layers add volume and dimension to your hair, making your face appear wider. You can style your hair in loose waves or curls to add texture.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut is an excellent hairstyle for long oval faces. The short haircut adds volume to your hair, making your face appear wider. You can add texture by incorporating layers or opting for a choppy cut.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for your long oval face shape:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can impact the haircut you choose. If you have fine hair, opt for haircuts with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, avoid haircuts that add too much volume, as they can make your face appear wider.

2. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is a professional who can provide you with personalized recommendations based on your face shape and hair type. Be open to their suggestions and ask for their opinion when choosing a haircut.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. You can try different lengths, textures, and cuts until you find the perfect look that flatters your face shape.

FAQ

What kind of bangs should I get for a long oval face shape?

If you have a high forehead, choose bangs that fall at or above your eyebrows to conceal it. If you have a small forehead, opt for wispy bangs that fall diagonally across your forehead.

Can I wear long hair with a long oval face shape?

While long hair is beautiful, it's not ideal for long oval face shapes. Long hair can make your face appear even longer, which can throw off your facial proportions. Instead, opt for haircuts that fall above or below your collarbone.

What should I tell my hairstylist when getting a haircut?

Be clear about the length, texture, and style you want. Bring a photo to show your hairstylist to ensure you both have the same understanding of your desired look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant, it's essential to maintain a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair at least two to three times a week to keep it clean and healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage. Apply the product to your hair before using any heat styling tools.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to remove split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Regular trims also promote hair growth and prevent breakage.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can choose the best haircut for your long oval face shape and look your best. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. With proper hair care, you can keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
