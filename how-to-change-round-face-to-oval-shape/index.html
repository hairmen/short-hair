How To Change Round Face To Oval Shape A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having a round face and want to change it to an oval shape? Well, you're not alone! Many people want to change their face shape to look more attractive and confident. In this article, we will discuss how to change a round face to an oval shape using various techniques and tips. So, let's get started!

Description

Having a round face can make you look younger and cuter, but it can also make you look chubby and less attractive. On the other hand, an oval face shape is considered the most balanced and attractive face shape. It gives a more sophisticated and mature look to your face.

There are different ways to change your face shape, including surgery, makeup, and haircuts. However, in this article, we will focus on natural ways to transform a round face to an oval shape.

How to Change Round Face to Oval Shape

Step 1: Identify your face shape

The first step to changing your face shape is to identify your current face shape. You can do this by standing in front of a mirror and analyzing your face shape. A round face shape has the same width and length, while an oval face shape is longer than it is wide.

Step 2: Lose weight

Losing weight can help you achieve an oval face shape by reducing the fat in your cheeks and chin. You can do this by following a healthy diet and exercise routine. Focus on eating a balanced diet that includes fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains. Avoid sugary and processed foods that can cause inflammation and weight gain.

Step 3: Do facial exercises

Facial exercises can help tone your facial muscles and reduce the puffiness in your face. Some of the best exercises for an oval face shape include cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and neck stretches. You can find many tutorials online that show you how to do these exercises.

Step 4: Use makeup

You can also use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of an oval shape. Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to contour your cheeks and jawline. Apply a lighter shade of foundation or concealer to the center of your face to highlight your forehead and chin.

Step 5: Get a new haircut

A new haircut can also help you achieve an oval face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks and instead opt for a layered haircut that frames your face. A side-swept bang can also help create the illusion of an oval shape.

Tips

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can cause premature aging and sagging skin.
  • Get enough sleep to prevent dark circles and puffiness in your face.
  • Use sunscreen to protect your skin from harmful UV rays.

FAQ

What is the most attractive face shape?

The oval face shape is considered the most attractive and balanced face shape.

Can I change my face shape naturally?

Yes, you can change your face shape naturally by following a healthy lifestyle, doing facial exercises, and using makeup and hairstyles that flatter your face shape.

Is surgery an option to change my face shape?

Yes, surgery is an option to change your face shape, but it is a more invasive and expensive option. It is important to consult with a qualified plastic surgeon and weigh the risks and benefits before undergoing any surgery.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and attractive appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair. Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your haircut and avoid split ends.

Conclusion

Changing your face shape from round to oval is possible with natural techniques such as exercise, weight loss, makeup, and haircuts. By following these tips and making lifestyle changes, you can achieve a more attractive and balanced face shape. Remember to take care of your hair and skin to maintain a healthy and youthful appearance.
