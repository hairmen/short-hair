How To Style Side Swept Bangs With Flat Iron

Written by Oliver Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have side swept bangs, you know that they can be a bit tricky to style. However, with a flat iron, you can easily achieve the perfect look. In this article, we'll show you how to style side swept bangs with a flat iron in just a few easy steps. We'll also give you some tips on how to maintain your bangs and keep them looking great.

How to Style Side Swept Bangs with Flat Iron

Before we get started, make sure you have a good quality flat iron. You don't want to use a cheap one that could damage your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your bangs to prevent damage. Comb your hair and make sure your bangs are separated from the rest of your hair.

Step 2: Heat Up Your Flat Iron

Turn on your flat iron and wait for it to heat up. It's important to use the right temperature for your hair type. Fine hair requires a lower temperature, while thicker hair needs a higher temperature. Check the instructions that came with your flat iron for guidance.

Step 3: Section Your Bangs

Section your bangs and clip the rest of your hair away. This will make it easier to style your bangs without getting in the way.

Step 4: Start Straightening

Take a small section of your bangs and place the flat iron at the root. Slowly glide the flat iron down your hair, following the direction of your bangs. Make sure to keep the flat iron moving and don't hold it in one spot for too long. Repeat this process until all your bangs are straightened.

Step 5: Create the Side Sweep

Once your bangs are straightened, it's time to create the side sweep. Take a small section of your bangs and twist it away from your face. Using the flat iron, curl the twisted section towards your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until all your bangs are curled towards your face.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your side swept bangs in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Don't use too much or your hair will look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Styling Side Swept Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you style your side swept bangs with a flat iron:

  • Don't use too much heat. High heat can damage your hair, so use the lowest temperature possible to achieve the desired result.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Don't straighten your bangs too often. Overuse of a flat iron can lead to split ends and breakage.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Can I style my side swept bangs with a curling iron instead of a flat iron?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to style your side swept bangs. Follow the same steps as with a flat iron, but instead of gliding the iron down your hair, wrap the hair around the iron and hold for a few seconds.

How often should I straighten my bangs?

Try not to straighten your bangs more than once or twice a week. Overuse of a flat iron can lead to damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your side swept bangs looking great:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and using a flat iron to style your side swept bangs, you can achieve a beautiful and polished look that will turn heads.
