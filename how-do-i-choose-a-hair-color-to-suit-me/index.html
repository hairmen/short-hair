How Do I Choose A Hair Color To Suit Me

Written by Frank Jun 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a change in your hair color, choosing the right one can be a daunting task. With so many different shades and tones available, it's important to choose one that suits your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. In this blog post, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to choose a hair color that suits you.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color is not just about picking a shade that you like. It's important to consider your skin tone, eye color, and personal style when choosing a hair color. Here are some tips on how to choose the right hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue-green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Another factor to consider when choosing a hair color is your eye color. Certain hair colors can bring out the color of your eyes and make them pop. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warm brown hair color can make your eyes appear bluer.

Step 3: Decide on a Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone and eye color, it's time to decide on a shade. If you have a cool skin tone, consider shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or black. If you have a warm skin tone, consider shades like golden blonde, caramel, or auburn. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can choose from a wide range of shades.

Step 4: Choose a Tone

After deciding on a shade, it's important to choose a tone. Hair colors can have warm, cool, or neutral tones. If you have a cool skin tone, choose a hair color with a cool tone. If you have a warm skin tone, choose a hair color with a warm tone.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle – If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hair color that doesn't require frequent touch-ups.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment – If you're unsure about a hair color, try a temporary color or highlights to see how it looks.
  • Consult a professional – If you're still unsure about which hair color to choose, consult a professional stylist who can provide you with personalized advice.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair color:

What is the best hair color for pale skin?

For pale skin, consider shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, or light auburn. These shades will complement your skin tone and make you look radiant.

What hair color looks best on olive skin?

For olive skin, consider shades like warm brown, honey blonde, or auburn. These shades will bring out the warmth in your skin tone and make you look stunning.

Hair Care Tips

After choosing the right hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its color and health. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid hot tools like curling irons and straighteners that can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and tricks, you can choose a hair color that suits you and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
