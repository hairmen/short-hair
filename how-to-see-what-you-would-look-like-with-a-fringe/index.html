Tips for Getting and Maintaining a Fringe

If you decide to get a fringe, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a fringe that complements your face shape and hair type.
  • Get your fringe trimmed regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your fringe.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your fringe to prevent damage.
  • Consider using dry shampoo to keep your fringe looking fresh between washes.

FAQs

What face shape is best for a fringe?

A fringe can work for most face shapes, but some fringes may be more flattering than others. If you have a round face, a side-swept fringe can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a wispy fringe can soften your features.

How often should I get my fringe trimmed?

You should get your fringe trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

Can I style my fringe with a straightener?

Yes, you can use a straightener to style your fringe. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Your Fringe

Here are a few tips for caring for your fringe:

  • Wash your fringe separately from the rest of your hair.
  • Blow dry your fringe with a round brush to style it.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your fringe to prevent damage.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your fringe looking fresh between washes.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your fringe soft and healthy.
