How To Make Cornrows For Guys Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 17, 2023 · 3 min read

Description

Cornrows have been a popular hairstyle for both men and women for centuries. This hairstyle is not only stylish and trendy but also practical, making it perfect for guys who want a low-maintenance hairdo. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to make cornrows for guys in the most relaxed way possible.

How to Make Cornrows for Guys

Step 1: Gather Your Tools and Materials

Before you start making your cornrows, you need to gather all the necessary tools and materials. You will need a comb, hair clips, hair gel, and a pair of scissors. If you have longer hair, you may also need a hair tie to keep it out of the way.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

The next step is to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make it more manageable. Then, use your comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots or tangles.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is detangled, you can begin sectioning it. Use your comb to create a straight line down the middle of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, use hair clips to hold the two sections of hair in place.

Step 4: Make Your First Cornrow

Start by taking a small section of hair from the front of one of the sections. Divide the section into three equal parts and begin braiding the hair, starting at the root. As you braid, add more hair from the same section to each strand of the braid until you reach the end of the section. Secure the braid with a hair tie or clip.

Step 5: Repeat the Process

Continue making cornrows across your head, working from front to back. Be sure to make each cornrow the same size and shape as the previous one. To get a neater finish, use hair gel to smooth down any flyaway hairs.

Step 6: Finish Your Cornrows

Once you have made all the cornrows, you can add some finishing touches. Use your scissors to trim any excess hair from the ends of your cornrows. Apply more hair gel to your scalp and to the cornrows to keep them neat and tidy.

Tips for Making Cornrows for Guys

- Practice makes perfect when it comes to making cornrows. Don't be discouraged if your first attempt is not perfect. - Use a mirror to check your progress as you make your cornrows. - Don't pull your hair too tight when making cornrows, as this can cause discomfort and damage to your hair. - Take breaks if you start to feel tired or uncomfortable.

FAQ

Q. How long does it take to make cornrows for guys?

A. The time it takes to make cornrows depends on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it can take anywhere from 1-3 hours.

Q. How long do cornrows last?

A. Cornrows can last for up to two weeks, but this can vary depending on your hair type and how well you care for your hair.

Hair Care for Cornrows

- Avoid washing your hair for at least two days after making cornrows. - Use a moisturizing hair oil to keep your scalp and hair hydrated. - Cover your hair with a satin or silk scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage. Now that you know how to make cornrows for guys, you can experiment with different styles and designs. Don't be afraid to get creative and have fun with your hair!
