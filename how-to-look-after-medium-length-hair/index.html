How To Look After Medium Length Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Medium length hair can be versatile and easy to manage, but it still requires proper care and attention to look its best. In this article, we’ll go over some essential tips and tricks to help you maintain your medium length hair and keep it healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Look After Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products

How to Look After Medium Length Hair

Looking after medium length hair is all about finding the right balance between washing and conditioning, styling, and protecting your hair from damage. Here are some basic steps to follow:

1. Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash your hair using a gentle shampoo and conditioner at least twice a week. This will help to remove dirt and oil from your hair and scalp, keeping your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using hot water, as it can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.

2. Use Heat Protection

If you use heat styling tools such as a curling iron or straightener, make sure to use a heat protection spray or serum to protect your hair from damage. Apply the product to damp hair before styling.

3. Get a Regular Trim

Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy. How often you need to trim your hair will depend on how quickly it grows and the style you’re going for.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun’s UV rays can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair, wear a hat or use a UV protection spray when you’re outside for extended periods.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to looking after your medium length hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair using a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Massage the shampoo into your scalp, then rinse thoroughly with cool water. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too hard, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protection

Before using heat styling tools, apply a heat protection spray or serum to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from damage and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using a brush or comb. You can use a hair dryer to speed up the drying process, but make sure to use the lowest heat setting and hold the dryer at least six inches away from your hair.

Step 5: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

If you’re going to be outside for extended periods, protect your hair from the sun’s UV rays by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you look after your medium length hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid tugging and pulling on your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

If your hair tends to be dry, use a leave-in conditioner to help keep it hydrated and healthy.

3. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Medium length hair can be versatile, so don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and looks.

4. Get a Professional Haircut

Visit a professional hairstylist to get a haircut that’s tailored to your face shape and hair type. They can also offer advice on how to look after your hair and maintain your style.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It’s generally recommended to wash your hair at least twice a week, but this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle. If your hair tends to be oily, you may need to wash it more often.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on my colored hair?

A: If you have colored hair, it’s best to use a shampoo that’s specifically formulated for colored hair. Regular shampoo can strip the color from your hair and cause it to fade more quickly.

Q: Can I use a hair mask on medium length hair?

A: Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.
