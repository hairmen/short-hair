How to Cut Short Hair at Home Yourself | Step by Step Guide

Cutting hair at home has become a trend nowadays due to the convenience it offers. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your hair at home, regardless of the length. In this post, we will guide you through the steps to cut short hair at home yourself.

How to

Before starting the process, make sure to have the following tools on hand:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

The first step is to wash and dry your hair. Make sure to remove all the tangles and knots using a comb. Dry your hair completely using a towel or a hair dryer.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair from the crown of your head using hair clips. This will help you to work on each section easily.

Step 3: Start cutting

Take one section of your hair and comb it straight. Use sharp scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Make sure to trim only a small amount of hair at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 4: Repeat the process

Repeat the same process for all the sections of your hair. Make sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Check the length

After cutting all the sections, check the length of your hair. If you find any unevenness, trim the hair accordingly.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you are done with the cutting process, style your hair as you like. You can use a hair straightener or a curling iron to create different styles.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting short hair at home:

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid any damage to your hair.
  • Don't cut your hair too short in the beginning. You can always trim it later.
  • Take breaks in between the cutting process to avoid any strain on your hands.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

It is not recommended to cut your hair when it's wet as it can cause the hair to shrink after drying. Make sure to dry your hair completely before cutting.

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on the growth rate of your hair. Generally, it is recommended to cut short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and shape.

What if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. You can always trim the hair to correct the mistake or visit a professional hair stylist to fix it.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair at home can be a cost-effective way to maintain your hair. However, it is important to take care of your hair after cutting. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

Cutting short hair at home yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional look without spending a lot of money. Follow these steps and tips to get the perfect cut every time!
