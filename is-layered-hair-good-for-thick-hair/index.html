Is Layered Hair Good For Thick Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Layered hair is a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair into different lengths. This technique creates layers that add volume and texture to the hair, making it more manageable and versatile. But is layered hair good for thick hair? In this post, we will explore this question and provide you with some tips on how to achieve the perfect layered hairstyle for your thick hair.

Description: What is Layered Hair?

Layered hair is a haircut that involves cutting the hair into different lengths to create layers. This technique allows for more volume and texture in the hair, making it look more voluminous and bouncy. Layered hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

How to Achieve a Layered Haircut for Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, you may be wondering if layered hair is the right choice for you. The answer is yes! Layered hair is perfect for thick hair because it helps to reduce bulk and add movement to the hair. Here are some tips on how to achieve the perfect layered haircut for your thick hair:

1. Consult with Your Hairstylist

The first step in achieving the perfect layered haircut is to consult with your hairstylist. Your hairstylist will be able to assess your hair type, texture, and thickness, and recommend the best layered haircut for your hair. They may suggest a long layered haircut, a short layered haircut, or something in between.

2. Choose the Right Length

The length of your layered haircut will depend on your personal style and hair type. If you have thick hair, your hairstylist may recommend a longer layered haircut to help reduce bulk and add movement to your hair. Shorter layered haircuts are also an option, but they may require more maintenance and styling.

3. Consider the Shape of Your Face

The shape of your face is an important factor to consider when choosing a layered haircut. If you have a round face, for example, a long layered haircut can help to elongate your face and create a more angular look. If you have a square face, on the other hand, a short layered haircut can help to soften your features and create a more feminine look.

4. Add Some Texture

Adding some texture to your layered haircut can help to create more movement and volume in your hair. Your hairstylist may recommend adding some layers around your face or using a texturizing spray to create a more tousled look.

Step-by-Step: How to Style Layered Hair for Thick Hair

Now that you have your layered haircut, it's time to style it! Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style layered hair for thick hair:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will help your hair hold its shape and style better. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.

2. Apply a Heat Protectant

If you plan on using heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

3. Use a Round Brush to Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. This will help to create more volume and texture in your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to create lift and movement in your hair.

4. Add Some Waves or Curls

If you want to add some waves or curls to your hair, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls. Be sure to wrap the hair around the barrel of the curling iron or wand, starting at the mid-lengths and working your way down to the ends.

5. Finish with a Hairspray

To keep your hair in place and looking shiny, finish with a light hairspray. This will help to hold your style in place and prevent frizz and flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Layered Hair for Thick Hair

Maintaining your layered haircut is important to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining your layered hair for thick hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Thick hair can sometimes be prone to dryness and damage, so it's important to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your layered haircut looking fresh and healthy, it's important to trim your hair regularly. Your hairstylist can recommend how often you should get your hair trimmed based on your hair type and length.

3. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can help to add volume and texture to your hair between washes. Use a texturizing spray on your roots and mid-lengths to create a more tousled, undone look.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair, so it's important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray and avoiding excessive heat styling.

FAQs

Is layered hair good for thick hair?

Yes, layered hair is good for thick hair because it helps to reduce bulk and add movement to the hair.

What length should I choose for my layered haircut?

The length of your layered haircut will depend on your personal style and hair type. If you have thick hair, your hairstylist may recommend a longer layered haircut to help reduce bulk and add movement to your hair.

How do I style my layered hair?

To style your layered hair, start with clean, dry hair and apply a heat protectant spray. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, and add some waves or curls with a curling iron or wand. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

Layered hair is a great choice for those with thick hair because it helps to reduce bulk and add movement to the hair. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect layered haircut for your thick hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny.
