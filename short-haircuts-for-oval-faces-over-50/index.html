Short Haircuts For Oval Faces Over 50

Written by Frank Sep 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are over 50 and have an oval face, short haircuts can enhance your facial features and personality. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect haircut. In this article, we will guide you on how to select the best short haircut for your oval face shape.

Description

An oval face is considered the ideal face shape as it has balanced proportions and symmetry. However, as we age, our face shape changes, and the oval shape may become less defined. Short haircuts can help to frame your face and accentuate your features while giving you a youthful and modern look.

How to

The first step in selecting the perfect short haircut for your oval face is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and observe the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are almost equal, you have an oval face shape.

Next, consider the texture and thickness of your hair. Short haircuts work best for fine to medium hair as they can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may opt for layered haircuts to reduce the bulk.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your oval face shape.

2. Choose a haircut that complements your hair texture and thickness.

3. Consider adding layers to thin out thick hair.

4. Opt for haircuts that frame your face and accentuate your features.

5. Use styling products such as mousse or hairspray to add volume and texture.

Tips

1. Avoid haircuts that are too short as they can make your face appear wider.

2. Consider adding bangs to soften your facial features.

3. Use a round brush when blow-drying to add volume to your hair.

4. Experiment with different styling products to achieve your desired look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have an oval face?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have an oval face. However, shorter haircuts can help to frame your face and accentuate your features.

Q: What are the best short haircuts for oval faces over 50?

A: The best short haircuts for oval faces over 50 include layered bobs, pixie cuts, and asymmetrical cuts.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and healthy appearance.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it is essential to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage. Apply a hair mask once a week to restore moisture and shine. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot styling tools.

Conclusion

Short haircuts can enhance your facial features and give you a modern and youthful look. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and thickness, you can select the perfect short haircut for your oval face over 50. Remember to maintain healthy hair care practices to keep your hair looking its best.
