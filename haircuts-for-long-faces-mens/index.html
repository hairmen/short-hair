Best Haircuts for Long Faces Mens in 2023

Long faces can be a bit tricky when it comes to haircuts. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for long faces mens in 2023. From how to choose the right haircut to how to care for your hair, we've got all the information you need.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in getting the perfect haircut for your long face is to determine your face shape. Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long nose, and a narrow chin. The best haircuts for long faces mens are those that add width to the sides of the face, while avoiding styles that are too long or too short.

Some good haircut options for long faces include:

  • Short haircuts with texture on the top and sides
  • Medium length haircuts with a side part
  • Long haircuts with layers and volume

Short Haircuts

If you have a long face, a short haircut can be a great option. A textured cut with layers on the top and sides can add volume and width to the face. Avoid styles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Medium Length Haircuts

A medium-length haircut with a side part can be a great option for long faces. This style adds volume to the sides of the face, making it appear wider. Avoid middle parts or styles that are too long or too short.

Long Haircuts

If you prefer longer hair, a layered haircut with volume can be a good option for long faces. This style adds width to the sides of the face, making it appear less narrow. Avoid styles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Haircut

Now that you've chosen the perfect haircut for your long face, it's time to head to the salon. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut:

  1. Research salons in your area and read reviews to find a reputable stylist.
  2. Bring a photo of the haircut you want to show your stylist.
  3. Communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want.
  4. Relax and let your stylist work their magic!

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've got your new haircut, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles!

FAQs About Haircuts for Long Faces Mens

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long, too short or have a middle part. These styles can make your face appear even longer.

How often should I get my haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Should I use hair products?

Yes, using hair products can help you achieve your desired style and keep your hair healthy. Use a product that suits your hair type and style.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces Mens

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated for healthy hair from the inside out.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

With these tips and the right haircut, you'll be able to rock your long face with confidence. Happy styling!
