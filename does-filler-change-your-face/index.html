Does Filler Change Your Face Everything You Need To Know

Are you considering getting dermal fillers but unsure if they will change your face? In this article, we will answer your questions and provide you with all the information you need to know about how fillers can affect your appearance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Dermal fillers are injectable substances that add volume to the skin, reducing the appearance of wrinkles, fine lines, and other signs of aging. These fillers are made of hyaluronic acid, collagen, or other materials and are injected into the skin to enhance facial features or restore lost volume. While fillers can enhance your appearance, many people wonder if they will change their face significantly.

How to

The process of getting dermal fillers is relatively simple. First, you will have a consultation with a licensed healthcare professional who will assess your needs and discuss your goals. They will then recommend the appropriate filler and injection sites. During the procedure, the filler will be injected into the skin using a small needle. The entire process typically takes less than an hour, and there is minimal downtime.

Step by step

1. Consultation: You will meet with a healthcare professional who will assess your needs and recommend the appropriate filler.

2. Injection sites: The healthcare professional will identify the injection sites and mark them on your face.

3. Injection: The filler will be injected into the skin using a small needle.

4. Aftercare: You will receive aftercare instructions, such as avoiding strenuous exercise and sun exposure, to ensure optimal results.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you are considering getting dermal fillers:

  • Choose a licensed healthcare professional who has experience administering dermal fillers.
  • Be clear about your goals and expectations during the consultation.
  • Follow all aftercare instructions to ensure optimal results.
  • Be patient, as it may take a few days to see the full results of the procedure.

FAQ

Will fillers change my face shape?

Fillers can enhance your facial features and restore lost volume, but they should not significantly change the shape of your face.

Will fillers make me look unnatural?

If administered by a licensed healthcare professional, fillers should enhance your appearance in a natural-looking way. It is important to choose a provider who has experience administering fillers and has an eye for aesthetics.

How long do fillers last?

The duration of the results depends on the type of filler used and other factors such as age and lifestyle. Generally, fillers last between six months to two years.

Hair Care

After getting dermal fillers, it is important to take care of your skin to ensure optimal results. Here are some hair care tips to consider:

  • Avoid exposing your skin to direct sunlight for extended periods of time.
  • Use a gentle cleanser and moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh skincare products or exfoliants for the first few days after the procedure.
  • Avoid strenuous exercise or activities that may cause sweating for the first 24 hours after the procedure.

Overall, dermal fillers can enhance your appearance by adding volume to your skin and reducing the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines. While they should not significantly change the shape of your face, it is important to choose a licensed healthcare professional who has experience administering fillers and has an eye for aesthetics.
