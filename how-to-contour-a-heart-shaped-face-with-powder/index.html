How To Contour A Heart Shaped Face With Powder

Written by Robby Nov 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help enhance your facial features, and one of the most popular face shapes to contour is a heart-shaped face. If you have this face shape, you may have a wider forehead, a narrow chin, and prominent cheekbones. In this post, we will discuss how to contour a heart shaped face with powder to help you achieve a more defined and chiseled look.

Description

Contouring involves applying makeup to specific areas of the face to create the illusion of shadows and highlights. This can help define and enhance your facial features, such as your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead. When contouring a heart-shaped face, the goal is to balance out the proportions of the face and create a more defined jawline.

How to Contour a Heart Shaped Face with Powder

To contour a heart-shaped face with powder, you will need a contour shade, a highlight shade, and a blending brush. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Before applying any makeup, make sure your skin is clean and moisturized. Apply a primer to help your makeup last longer.

Step 2: Apply Contour Shade

Using a contour brush, apply the contour shade to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline. Make sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Apply Highlight Shade

Using a highlight brush, apply the highlight shade to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Blend

Using a blending brush, blend the contour and highlight shades together to create a seamless finish.

Step 5: Set with Powder

Using a translucent powder, set your makeup to help it last longer and prevent any creasing or smudging.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when contouring a heart-shaped face with powder:

  • Use a matte contour shade to create a more natural look.
  • Choose a highlight shade that complements your skin tone.
  • Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Set your makeup with powder to help it last longer.

FAQ

What if I don't have a contour shade?

If you don't have a contour shade, you can use a matte bronzer instead. Just make sure to choose a shade that is a few shades darker than your skin tone.

Can I contour my forehead?

If you have a wider forehead, you can contour it to create a more balanced look. Just apply the contour shade to the sides of your forehead and blend well.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't worry! You can always blend it out or use a makeup wipe to remove the excess makeup.

Hair Care

When contouring a heart-shaped face, it's also important to consider your hairstyle. A side-swept bang or long layers can help balance out the proportions of your face and create a more flattering look.

Contouring a heart-shaped face with powder can help create a more defined and chiseled look. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a natural and flawless finish.
