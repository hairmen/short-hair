How To Work Out If Short Hair Suits You

Written by Bardi Jun 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're considering getting a short haircut, it's important to figure out whether it will suit you or not. While short hair can look great on some people, it may not be the best choice for others. In this article, we'll take you through some steps to help you determine whether short hair is right for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Work Out If Short Hair Suits You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Step by Step Guide

1. Consider your face shape: Short hair can look great on people with oval, heart-shaped or square faces. People with round faces may want to think twice about short hair, as it can make their face appear wider.

2. Look at your hair texture: Short hair can work with any hair texture, but you'll want to consider how your hair will look when it's cut short. If you have curly hair, for example, you may want to choose a cut that works with your natural curl pattern.

3. Think about your lifestyle: Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it's not for everyone. If you're used to spending a lot of time styling your hair, you may want to think twice about going short. On the other hand, if you're an active person who doesn't want to spend a lot of time on hair care, short hair may be the perfect choice for you.

4. Consult with a stylist: A professional stylist can help you determine whether short hair will suit you. They'll be able to take your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle into account to recommend the best style for you.

Tips for Choosing Short Hair

1. Consider your skin tone: Short hair can be a great way to show off your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, you may want to choose a warm-toned hair color to complement your skin. If you have cool undertones, a cool-toned hair color may be a better choice.

2. Choose a style that flatters your face: Short hair can be styled in many different ways, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. If you have a long face, for example, you may want to choose a style with volume at the sides to balance out your features.

3. Consider your hairline: If you have a high hairline, you may want to choose a style that covers your forehead. If you have a low hairline, a pixie cut may be a good choice for you.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make my face look wider?
A: Short hair can make round faces appear wider, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape.

Q: Can I still style short hair?
A: Yes, short hair can be styled in many different ways. Talk to your stylist about different styling options.

Q: Will short hair be low-maintenance?
A: Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it depends on your hair texture and the style you choose. Talk to your stylist about the best low-maintenance options for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape, so be prepared to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks. You'll also want to shampoo and condition your hair regularly, and use styling products to keep your hair looking its best.

When styling short hair, start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. Too much product can make short hair look greasy or weighed down.

Now that you know how to work out if short hair suits you, you can make an informed decision about whether to take the plunge. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
