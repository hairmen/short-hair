How To Make A Wig Come Back To Life

Written by Oliver Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a wig wearer, you know that wigs can lose their luster over time. But don't worry, there are ways to revive your wig and make it look like new again. In this article, we will explore some tips and tricks on how to make a wig come back to life.

Description

Wigs can be a great way to change up your look or hide hair loss. However, over time, wigs can lose their shine and bounce. This can be due to a variety of reasons, including exposure to heat, humidity, and chemicals. But with the right care, you can bring your wig back to life.

How to

Here are the steps you can take to make your wig come back to life:

Step 1: Detangle

The first step to reviving your wig is to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to gently remove any knots or tangles. Start at the ends and work your way up, being careful not to pull too hard or damage the hair fibers.

Step 2: Wash

Next, wash your wig with a gentle shampoo. Use cool water and avoid rubbing or scrubbing too hard. Rinse thoroughly and then apply a conditioner to help soften the hair fibers. Rinse again and gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step 3: Dry

To dry your wig, place it on a wig stand or mannequin head. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can damage the fibers. Instead, let your wig air dry. You can also use a towel to gently blot away any excess water.

Step 4: Style

Once your wig is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a wig brush or comb to smooth out any kinks or frizz. You can also use a heat tool, such as a flat iron or curling iron, to add waves or curls. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for reviving your wig:

- Use a wig conditioner to help restore moisture and shine. - Avoid exposing your wig to heat or direct sunlight for long periods of time. - Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape. - Use a wig cap to help protect your natural hair and keep your wig in place. - Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down the hair fibers and cause buildup.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: It depends on how often you wear your wig and how much product you use. As a general rule, you should wash your wig every 6-8 wears.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on my wig?

A: It's best to use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wigs. Regular shampoo can be too harsh and damage the fibers.

Q: Can I dye my wig?

A: It's not recommended to dye a synthetic wig, as the fibers may not react well to the chemicals. However, human hair wigs can be dyed with professional hair dye.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is key to keeping your wig looking its best. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for wigs. - Don't brush your wig when it's wet, as this can cause damage. - Avoid using hot tools on synthetic wigs, as they can melt the fibers. - Store your wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. - Cover your wig with a scarf or hat when exposed to wind or rain.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your wig come back to life and enjoy a fresh, new look.
