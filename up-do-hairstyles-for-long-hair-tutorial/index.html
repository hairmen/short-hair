Up Do Hairstyles For Long Hair Tutorial

Written by Daniel Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing your long hair down every day? Do you want to try something new and elegant? Up do hairstyles for long hair are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a wedding, prom, or just a night out. In this tutorial, we will show you step by step how to create three beautiful up do hairstyles for long hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the tutorials, let's first discuss what you will need to create these up do hairstyles for long hair. You will need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastics
  • Hairspray
  • Teasing comb
  • Hair clips
  • Curling iron or straightener (depending on the hairstyle)

Step by Step

1. Romantic Up Do

To create this romantic up do, follow these steps:

  1. Curl your hair using a curling iron or straightener.
  2. Take a small section of hair at the crown of your head and tease it with a teasing comb to create volume.
  3. Smooth the teased section back and pin it in place with bobby pins.
  4. Take the rest of your hair and twist it into a loose bun at the nape of your neck.
  5. Pin the bun in place with bobby pins.
  6. Finish with hairspray.

2. Braided Up Do

To create this braided up do, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair in the middle and braid two small sections of hair on each side of your head.
  2. Pull the braids back and secure them with bobby pins at the nape of your neck.
  3. Take the rest of your hair and twist it into a bun.
  4. Pin the bun in place with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with hairspray.

3. Half-Up Half-Down Up Do

To create this half-up half-down up do, follow these steps:

  1. Curl your hair using a curling iron or straightener.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it with a teasing comb.
  3. Smooth the teased section back and pin it in place with bobby pins.
  4. Take two small sections of hair on each side of your head and twist them towards the back of your head.
  5. Pin the twisted sections in place at the nape of your neck.
  6. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating up do hairstyles for long hair:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start styling.
  • Use hairspray to keep your up do in place.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories like flowers or hairpins to your up do to make it more elegant.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't worry if your up do doesn't turn out perfectly the first time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about up do hairstyles for long hair:

1. How long does it take to create an up do hairstyle for long hair?

It can take anywhere from 10 minutes to an hour to create an up do hairstyle for long hair, depending on the complexity of the style.

2. Can I create an up do hairstyle for long hair on my own?

Yes, you can create an up do hairstyle for long hair on your own, but it may be easier to have a friend or professional stylist help you.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair before and after creating up do hairstyles for long hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Also, make sure to use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools like curling irons or straighteners to prevent damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to create three beautiful up do hairstyles for long hair, it's time to try them out for yourself. Whether it's a wedding, prom, or just a night out, these up do hairstyles will make you look elegant and chic.
