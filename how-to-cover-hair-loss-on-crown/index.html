How To Cover Hair Loss On Crown Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're struggling with hair loss on your crown, you're not alone. Many people experience thinning or balding in this area, which can be frustrating and impact your confidence. Fortunately, there are several ways to cover up hair loss on your crown and achieve a full, voluminous look. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you feel confident and comfortable with your hair.

How to Cover Hair Loss on Crown

1. Change Your Hairstyle

One of the easiest ways to cover up hair loss on your crown is to change your hairstyle. Opt for shorter haircuts that add volume and texture, or try a side part to draw attention away from the thinning area. Avoid slicked-back styles or high ponytails, which can emphasize hair loss.

2. Use Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are a great way to instantly cover up hair loss on your crown. These microfibers are made from keratin, the same protein as your hair, and can be sprinkled onto your scalp to create the appearance of fuller hair. Choose a color that matches your hair and apply the fibers with a special applicator for best results.

3. Try a Hairpiece

If you're looking for a more long-term solution, consider trying a hairpiece. There are many different types of hairpieces available, from toupees to wigs, that can cover up hair loss on your crown. Look for a hairpiece that matches your natural hair color and texture for the most seamless look.

4. Use Hair Extensions

Hair extensions are another great option for covering up hair loss on your crown. These extensions can be clipped or sewn onto your existing hair to add volume and length. Choose extensions that are made from real hair for the most natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cover Hair Loss on Crown

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you cover up hair loss on your crown:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash and condition your hair before attempting to cover up hair loss on your crown. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply Hair Fibers

If you're using hair fibers, apply them to your scalp with the special applicator. Shake the fibers onto your scalp in a thin, even layer, focusing on the areas where you have hair loss.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired, using a blow dryer or styling tools to add volume and texture. Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can emphasize hair loss on your crown.

Step 4: Use Hair Extensions or a Hairpiece

If you're using hair extensions or a hairpiece, follow the manufacturer's instructions for application. Be sure to blend the extensions or hairpiece with your natural hair for a seamless look.

Tips for Covering Hair Loss on Crown

Here are a few additional tips to help you cover up hair loss on your crown:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help to add body and thickness to your hair, making it easier to cover up hair loss on your crown.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

As mentioned earlier, avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can emphasize hair loss on your crown. Instead, opt for loose, textured styles.

3. Consider Hair Growth Products

There are many different hair growth products available, such as minoxidil or finasteride, that can help to stimulate hair growth and reduce hair loss. Talk to your doctor or a hair loss specialist to determine if these products are right for you.

FAQ About Covering Hair Loss on Crown

Q: Can hair fibers damage my hair?

A: No, hair fibers are safe to use and will not damage your hair.

Q: How long do hair fibers last?

A: Hair fibers typically last until your next shampoo, but can be easily removed with a gentle cleanser.

Q: Can I swim or exercise with a hairpiece or hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can swim and exercise with a hairpiece or hair extensions, but be sure to follow the manufacturer's instructions for care and maintenance.

Hair Care Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

While there's no guaranteed way to prevent hair loss, there are several things you can do to promote healthy hair growth and reduce your risk of hair loss:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

A diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth. Be sure to include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein in your diet.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and lead to hair loss over time. Try to limit your use of blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and lead to hair loss. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair when you're outside.

4. Manage Stress

Stress can contribute to hair loss. Try to manage stress through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Conclusion

If you're struggling with hair loss on your crown, there are several ways to cover it up and achieve a full, voluminous look. Try changing your hairstyle, using hair fibers or a hairpiece, or using hair extensions. Be sure to follow these tips and tricks to help you feel confident and comfortable with your hair. And remember, promoting healthy hair growth through a healthy diet, avoiding heat styling, protecting your hair from the sun, and managing stress can help to reduce your risk of hair loss in the future.
