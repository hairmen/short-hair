What Are The Best Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Daniel Jun 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Having a round face can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it gives you a youthful, soft look. On the other hand, it can make you appear heavier than you actually are. The good news is that with the right hairstyle, you can balance out your face shape and highlight your best features. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for round faces and provide you with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces, let's first determine if you have a round face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Stand back and examine the shape of your face.

If your face is widest at the cheeks and has soft, round edges, then you have a round face shape. Other common face shapes include oval, square, heart, and diamond.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. Here are some of the best hairstyles to achieve this look:

1. Long Layers

Long, layered hair can elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Opt for layers that start at your chin or below and avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear wider.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to break up the roundness of your face and create a slimming effect. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that can make your face appear wider.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for round faces as it can create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. Opt for a layered pixie cut with volume at the crown to balance out your face shape.

4. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume can help to elongate your face and create a slimming effect. Opt for a chin-length bob with layers and volume at the crown.

5. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention upward. Avoid low ponytails that can make your face appear wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know which hairstyles are best for a round face, here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls.
  5. If you have bangs, use a round brush to blow-dry them to the side for a side-swept look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with products as this can weigh it down and make it look flat.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt, straight-across bangs, and chin-length bobs with no volume at the crown. Also, avoid center parts as they can make your face appear wider.

What face shapes do bangs look best on?

Bangs can look great on a variety of face shapes, but they work particularly well on oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, opt for side-swept bangs to create a slimming effect.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips for those with round faces:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Be gentle when brushing your hair to prevent breakage.

Conclusion

With the right hairstyle, you can balance out your face shape and highlight your best features. Remember to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and creates a slimming effect, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, a pixie cut, a bob with volume, or a high ponytail. Use our step-by-step guide to style your hair, and follow our tips for maintaining your hairstyle and caring for your hair. With these tips, you can look and feel your best every day.
