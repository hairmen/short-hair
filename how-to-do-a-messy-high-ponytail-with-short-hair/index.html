How To Do A Messy High Ponytail With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? If you have short hair, you might think that a messy high ponytail is out of reach. But fear not! With the right technique and a few simple steps, you can achieve a chic and effortless look that is perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we will show you how to do a messy high ponytail with short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A messy high ponytail is a popular hairstyle that is both stylish and practical. It is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face and off your neck. The messy look adds a touch of effortless chic to any outfit, and it works well with both casual and formal attire. The best part is that it's easy to do, even with short hair.

How to

To achieve a messy high ponytail with short hair, you will need a few things:

  • Hair ties
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray (optional)

Once you have these items, you can follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and hold it firmly with one hand.
  3. With your other hand, use a hair tie to secure your hair in a high ponytail. Make sure to leave some hair loose at the front to create a messy look.
  4. If you want to add some texture to your hair, you can use a texturizing spray at this point.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie. Secure it with a bobby pin.
  6. Gently pull on the hair at the top of your head to create some volume and a messy look.
  7. If you want to add more volume, you can tease the hair at the crown of your head with a comb.
  8. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair and to create a more messy look.
  9. Finish off with some hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

  • If you have thin hair, you can use a volumizing spray before starting to create more volume.
  • If you want to add more texture to your hair, you can use a curling iron or a straightener to create waves or curls.
  • If you have bangs, you can leave them loose or pin them back with bobby pins.
  • If you want a more polished look, you can use a hair serum or a shine spray to add some shine to your hair.

FAQ

Can I do a messy high ponytail with very short hair?

Yes, you can! The key is to use a hair tie to secure your hair and to leave some hair loose at the front to create a messy look. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

How long does it take to do a messy high ponytail with short hair?

It should only take a few minutes to do a messy high ponytail with short hair. Once you get the hang of it, you can do it in under a minute!

Can I wear a messy high ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, you can! A messy high ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that works well with both casual and formal attire. You can dress it up with some hair accessories or leave it as is for a more casual look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a hair mask or treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling whenever possible. You should also trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to do a messy high ponytail with short hair, you can try it out for yourself and see how it looks. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a chic and effortless look that is perfect for any occasion. So go ahead and give it a try!
