How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Boy With A Hat

Written by Daniel Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a new way to switch things up? Why not try making your hair look like a boy with a hat? This style is trendy, edgy, and perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd. In this article, we'll teach you how to achieve this look step by step, provide tips and frequently asked questions, and share some hair care advice to keep your locks in top shape. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Look Like a Boy with a Hat
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfecting the Look
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

Before we get into the nitty-gritty, it's essential to note that hair texture and length will affect how this style looks. However, that doesn't mean you can't achieve this look if you have shorter hair or hair with more curls.

Here are the steps to make your hair look like a boy with a hat:

Step 1: Clean Your Hair

The first step is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with your preferred shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. We recommend using a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray to give your hair some body and hold.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to create lift and texture. If you have shorter hair, use a comb to create a side part.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Take a small amount of pomade or wax and apply it to your fingertips. Then, run your fingers through your hair, starting at the back and moving forward. This will give your hair some hold while creating a messy, textured look.

Step 5: Add the Hat

The final step is to add the hat. Choose a hat that complements your outfit and style. We recommend a beanie or a baseball cap. Place the hat on your head, making sure it sits comfortably and doesn't flatten any of your hair.

Tips for Perfecting the Look

Now that you know the steps to make your hair look like a boy with a hat let's go over some tips to perfect the look.

  • Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair in different directions to create texture and volume.
  • Experiment with different hats to find the one that suits your style the best.
  • Make sure your hair is clean before styling to avoid any product buildup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair look like a boy with a hat:

Can I achieve this look with shorter hair?

Yes, you can still achieve this look with shorter hair. Use a comb to create a side part, and add the hat to finish off the style.

What type of hat works best?

A beanie or a baseball cap works well with this style. However, you can experiment with different hat styles to find the one that best suits your look.

How often should I wash my hair when styling it like this?

It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to avoid over-washing and drying out your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Styling your hair like a boy with a hat can be a lot of fun, but it's essential to take care of your locks to avoid damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when blow drying your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  • Avoid using too much product to avoid product buildup and weighing down your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

And that's it! Now you know how to make your hair look like a boy with a hat, some tips to perfect the look, and some hair care advice to keep your locks in top shape. So, go ahead and try this trendy, edgy style and take your hair game to the next level.
