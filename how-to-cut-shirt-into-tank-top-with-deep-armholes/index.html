How To Cut Shirt Into Tank Top With Deep Armholes

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to transform your old t-shirts into something new and stylish, then cutting them into a tank top with deep armholes is a great idea. Not only will it give you a new piece of clothing to wear, but it will also help you beat the summer heat. In this article, we'll show you how to cut shirt into tank top with deep armholes in a few easy steps.

Description

A tank top with deep armholes is a great way to show off your summer bod and stay cool in the heat. This style of tank top is perfect for the beach, pool, or any casual outing. The deep armholes allow for more ventilation, making it a great option for those hot and humid days.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right T-Shirt

The first step to cutting a shirt into a tank top with deep armholes is to choose the right t-shirt. Look for a shirt that is loose-fitting and has a crew neck. Make sure the shirt is long enough to cover your torso so that you can cut it to your desired length.

Step 2: Lay the Shirt Flat

Lay the shirt flat on a table or other flat surface. Smooth out any wrinkles or folds in the fabric so that you have a flat surface to work with.

Step 3: Cut off the Sleeves

Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut off the sleeves of the shirt, starting at the shoulder seam and cutting down to the bottom hem. Make sure to cut in a straight line so that both sleeves are the same length.

Step 4: Cut the Armholes

To create the deep armholes, mark a spot on each side of the shirt where you want the armhole to start. This should be about halfway down the side of the shirt. Using your scissors, cut a straight line from the bottom hem of the shirt to the spot you marked.

Next, cut a curved line from the bottom of the armhole up to the shoulder seam. Repeat this step on the other side of the shirt to create the second armhole. Make sure to cut both armholes the same size.

Step 5: Cut the Neckline

If you want to create a scoop neck or a V-neck, you can cut the neckline of the shirt. Start at the center of the collar and cut down to your desired depth. You can also cut off the collar completely if you prefer a raw edge.

Step 6: Finish the Edges

To finish the edges of your new tank top, you can use a sewing machine, hand sew, or leave them raw. If you decide to sew, turn the shirt inside out and sew a zigzag stitch along the edges to prevent fraying. If you prefer a raw edge, you can leave the edges as they are.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Measure and mark the armholes before you cut to ensure they are the same size.
  • Wash and dry the shirt before cutting to prevent shrinking.
  • Experiment with different necklines and armhole sizes to find the style that works best for you.

FAQ

Can I use any type of shirt?

Yes, you can use any type of shirt as long as it is loose-fitting and has a crew neck.

Do I need to sew the edges?

No, you don't have to sew the edges. You can leave them raw for a more casual look.

How deep should the armholes be?

The depth of the armholes is up to you. Just make sure they are big enough to allow for ventilation and movement.

Hair Care

When wearing a tank top with deep armholes, your underarm area will be exposed. This means that you should pay extra attention to your underarm hair. Make sure to keep it trimmed or shaved to avoid any embarrassing situations. You can also use a deodorant that contains antiperspirant to help reduce sweat and odor.

Now that you know how to cut shirt into tank top with deep armholes, you can transform your old t-shirts into something new and stylish. This is a great way to save money and create a unique piece of clothing that reflects your personal style. So grab your scissors and start cutting!
