Layered Hairstyles For Over 60 Round Face And Glasses

Written by William Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural volume and thickness, leading to the thinning of hair. For women over 60 with a round face and glasses, finding the perfect hairstyle that complements their features can be challenging. However, layered hairstyles can provide a perfect solution. Here are some of the best-layered hairstyles for over 60 round face and glasses.

Description

Layered hairstyles are popular among women of all ages, but they are especially suited for women over 60 with a round face and glasses. Layered hairstyles add volume and texture to thinning hair, making it look fuller and more youthful. These hairstyles also complement the round face shape, creating a balanced and proportionate look.

How to

To achieve a layered hairstyle, you need to have the right cut and styling technique. Here is how to achieve a layered hairstyle for women over 60 with a round face and glasses.

Step 1: Consult a Hairdresser

Before you choose a layered hairstyle, you need to consult a hairdresser who specializes in hairstyles for women over 60. The hairdresser will examine your hair type, face shape, and glasses and recommend the best-layered hairstyle that complements your features.

Step 2: Choose the Right Layered Hairstyle

There are several layered hairstyles to choose from, including long layers, short layers, choppy layers, and feathered layers. Choose a layered hairstyle that suits your hair type, face shape, and glasses.

Step 3: Style Your Layered Hair

Once you have your layered hairstyle, you need to style it correctly to achieve the desired look. You can use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create a bouncy and voluminous look. You can also use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling layered hairstyles for women over 60 with a round face and glasses.

  • Choose a layered hairstyle that complements your face shape and glasses
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create a bouncy and voluminous look
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves
  • Use a hairspray to set your hairstyle in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions and answers about layered hairstyles for women over 60 with a round face and glasses.

What are the best-layered hairstyles for women over 60 with a round face and glasses?

The best-layered hairstyles for women over 60 with a round face and glasses are long layers, short layers, choppy layers, and feathered layers.

How do I style my layered hair?

You can style your layered hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and using a curling iron or hot rollers.

How often should I cut my layered hair?

You should cut your layered hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your layered hairstyle looking its best, you need to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent hair damage
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your layered hairstyle

In conclusion, layered hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 with a round face and glasses. They add volume and texture to thinning hair, creating a fuller and more youthful look. By following the tips and hair care advice outlined in this article, you can achieve a layered hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty.
