Description

If you have long hair and want to save some money, cutting your hair at home may be a good option. Brad Mondo is a popular hair stylist and YouTuber who has shared his tips and tricks on how to cut long hair at home. In this article, we will go over his advice and give you some additional tips on how to cut long hair at home like a pro.

How to Cut Long Hair at Home Brad Mondo

The first step to cutting long hair at home is to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. It's also a good idea to have a mirror in front of you and another one behind you to see the back of your head.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair.

2. Comb your hair and divide it into sections. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

3. Take one section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to snip the ends. Start with a small amount and continue until you reach the desired length.

4. Move on to the next section and repeat the process.

5. Once you have cut all the sections, check the length of your hair by combing it straight down. If you notice any unevenness, make small adjustments with the scissors.

Tips

- Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid cutting too much off.

- Use a comb to help guide the scissors and keep the cut straight.

- Don't cut wet hair as it may result in uneven lengths.

- Take breaks to stretch your arms and neck to avoid getting tired.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair's texture and how fast it grows. On average, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends.

Q: Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

A: It's not recommended as regular scissors may not be sharp enough and may damage your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain healthy ends. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and avoid using heat styling tools too often. It's also a good idea to trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
