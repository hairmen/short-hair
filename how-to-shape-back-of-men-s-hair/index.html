How To Shape Back Of Men s Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of having a messy and unorganized hairstyle at the back of your head? Do you want to learn how to shape your hair like a pro? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on how to shape the back of men's hair. From the basic steps to the tips and tricks, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

  1. How to Shape Back of Men's Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Shape Back of Men's Hair

Shaping the back of men's hair is not as difficult as it seems. In fact, it's a simple process that can be done at home. The first step is to determine the length of your hair. Once you know the length, you can decide on the shape you want. There are different shapes you can choose from, such as a rounded shape, a squared shape, or an angled shape. Once you have decided on the shape, you can start trimming your hair.

Tools Needed

Before you start trimming your hair, you will need the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to shape the back of your hair:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Use the clippers to trim the hair at the nape of your neck. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use the longest guard and gradually switch to a shorter guard until you reach the desired length.
  4. Use the scissors to trim the hair around your ears. Comb the hair forward and use the scissors to trim any excess hair.
  5. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the sides of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use the longest guard and gradually switch to a shorter guard until you reach the desired length.
  6. Use the scissors to trim the hair at the back of your head. Comb the hair down and trim any excess hair.
  7. Shape the hair at the back of your head using the scissors. For a rounded shape, cut the hair in a circular motion. For a squared shape, cut the hair in a straight line. For an angled shape, cut the hair at an angle.
  8. Apply hair gel or pomade to style your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you shape the back of your hair:

  • Start with a longer guard when using the clippers and gradually switch to a shorter guard until you reach the desired length.
  • Use a comb to ensure that the hair is straight before trimming.
  • Trim small sections of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use hair gel or pomade to style your hair after trimming.

FAQs

How often should I shape the back of my hair?

You should shape the back of your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Should I use scissors or clippers?

You should use both scissors and clippers to shape the back of your hair. Use scissors to trim the hair and clippers to trim the length.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the shape and length of the back of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid tangles.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Trim the back of your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

There you have it! A comprehensive guide on how to shape the back of men's hair. Follow these steps, tips, and tricks to achieve the perfect hairstyle. Happy grooming!
