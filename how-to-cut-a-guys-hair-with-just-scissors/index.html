How To Cut A Guy s Hair With Just Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the salon or barbershop? With just a pair of scissors and a little bit of practice, you can easily cut a guy's hair at home. In this guide, we'll take you through the process step-by-step and provide some helpful tips and advice along the way.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Cut a Guy's Hair with Just Scissors

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the basic tools you'll need to get started:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle with water
  • A mirror
  • A towel or cape to catch the hair

Once you have these tools ready, you're ready to start cutting!

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut a guy's hair with just scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing the hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry the hair until it is damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section the hair into manageable sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up, clipping each section with a hair clip as you go.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting at the back of the head, use the comb to lift a section of hair and use the scissors to trim the ends. Work your way around the head, cutting each section of hair to the desired length.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the sections of hair, use the scissors to blend the hair together. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines from forming.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

Use the scissors to clean up any stray hairs around the edges of the hairline and ears. This will give the haircut a more polished look.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, style the hair as desired using a hairbrush, comb, or your fingers.

Tips for Cutting Hair at Home

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when cutting hair at home:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a comb to guide the scissors and create even cuts.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or your hands start to cramp.
  • Be patient and take your time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can definitely cut your own hair. However, it can be more difficult to cut the back of your own head, so you may want to ask a friend or family member for help.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on your hair type and desired length. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry if you make a mistake – hair grows back! If you're unhappy with the results, you can always go to a professional stylist to have it fixed.

Hair Care and Maintenance

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for hair care and maintenance:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut a guy's hair with just scissors like a pro. Whether you're on a tight budget or just prefer to cut your hair at home, this step-by-step guide will help you achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut.
