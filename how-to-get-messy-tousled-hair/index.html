How To Get Messy Tousled Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to get messy tousled hair! Whether you have naturally straight, wavy, or curly hair, achieving a messy tousled hairstyle is easier than you think. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect tousled look. Let's get started!

How to Get Messy Tousled Hair

Messy tousled hair is a popular hairstyle that looks effortless, yet chic. It is perfect for any occasion, whether you are heading to work or going out for a night out with friends. Here are the basic steps to achieve this look:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Texturizing Product

Apply a texturizing product to your hair to create volume and texture. You can use a sea salt spray or a dry texturizing spray. Spray the product onto your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create waves and texture.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you blow-dry. This will help create volume and texture. Once your hair is dry, flip your head upside down and shake your hair to add more volume.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or a wand, curl random sections of your hair. Don't worry about being too precise. The goal is to create a messy, tousled look. Use a smaller barrel for tighter curls or a larger barrel for looser waves. Once you have curled your hair, run your fingers through it to loosen the curls and create a messy look.

Step 5: Finish with a Texturizing Spray

To set your tousled hairstyle, finish with a texturizing spray. This will give your hair a matte finish and hold the style in place. Spray the product onto your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create more texture.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know the basic steps to achieve a tousled hairstyle, let's break it down further with step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add body and texture to your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Texturizing Product

Apply a texturizing product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the mid-lengths. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you apply the product. This will help create waves and texture.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it on low heat. Flip your head upside down and use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you blow dry. This will help create volume and enhance the texture.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or a wand, curl random sections of your hair. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Use a smaller barrel for tighter curls or a larger barrel for looser waves. Once you have curled your hair, run your fingers through it to loosen the curls and create a messy look.

Step 5: Finish with a Texturizing Spray

Spray a texturizing spray onto your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create more texture. This will help set your hairstyle and give it a matte finish.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Tousled Look

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect tousled hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as twisting your hair instead of wrapping it around the barrel.
  • Use bobby pins to pin up any sections of your hair that are too long or don't blend well with the rest of your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace imperfection. The beauty of a tousled hairstyle is that it doesn't need to be perfect.

FAQs on Tousled Hair

Q: Can I achieve a tousled hairstyle if I have naturally straight hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve a tousled hairstyle even if you have naturally straight hair. Use a texturizing product and a curling iron or wand to create waves and texture.

Q: How can I make my tousled hairstyle last longer?

A: To make your tousled hairstyle last longer, use a texturizing spray to set the style. You can also sleep with your hair in braids or a bun to help maintain the waves and texture.

Q: Can I achieve a tousled hairstyle without using hot styling tools?

A: Yes, you can achieve a tousled hairstyle without using hot styling tools. Try using braids or twists to create waves and texture. You can also use a sea salt spray to enhance your natural texture.

Hair Care Tips for Tousled Hair

To keep your tousled hairstyle looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect messy tousled hair look. Remember to have fun with your hair and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
