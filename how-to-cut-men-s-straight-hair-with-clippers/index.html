How To Cut Men s Straight Hair With Clippers

Written by Bardi Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to cut men's straight hair with clippers? This article is for you! In this guide, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a clean and professional-looking haircut at home. We will also include tips on how to care for your hair after the cut. Let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Before starting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle of water. Follow these steps for a perfect cut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner, and towel-dry it. Make sure the hair is completely dry before starting the cut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Choose the right clipper guard depending on the length of the hair you want to cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way to a shorter one if needed.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Section the hair by parting it down the middle, from the forehead to the nape of the neck. Use clips to hold the hair in place on either side of the part.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting from the back of the head, at the nape of the neck. Use the chosen clipper guard and make sure to go against the direction of the hair growth. Move the clippers in an upward motion, towards the crown of the head.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

Blend the sides and back of the head by using a shorter clipper guard. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Cut the top of the head with scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair straight up and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to blend the sides and the top together.

Step 7: Define the Hairline

Use the clippers without a guard to define the hairline around the ears and the neck. Make sure to go slow and be careful not to nick the skin.

Step 8: Final Touches

Check for any uneven spots or stray hairs and trim them with scissors. Use the clippers to create a clean line around the edges of the hairline.

Tips for Cutting Men's Straight Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way to a shorter one
  • Use a spray bottle of water to dampen the hair if it becomes too dry
  • Use scissors for the top of the head and clippers for the sides and back
  • Make sure to blend the sides and the top together for a seamless look
  • Use the clippers without a guard to define the hairline around the ears and neck

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the length of your hair and your personal preference. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain a clean and healthy look.

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. Just make sure to follow the steps carefully and use the right tools.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always trim the hair a little shorter to even it out. If you're not confident in fixing the mistake yourself, you can always go to a professional stylist for help.

Hair Care after the Cut

After the cut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and health

Cutting men's straight hair with clippers is easy and convenient with the right tools and techniques. Follow these steps and tips for a professional-looking haircut at home.
