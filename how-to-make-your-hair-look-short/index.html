How To Make Your Hair Look Short A Guide To Short Hair In 2023

Written by Frank Aug 11, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle, but don't want to commit to a short cut, you're in luck! There are plenty of ways to make your hair look shorter without actually chopping it all off. In this guide, we'll go over some tips and tricks for creating the illusion of short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Look Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Short Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Make Your Hair Look Short

There are several ways to make your hair look shorter:

  • Use hair accessories
  • Create volume at the crown
  • Try a faux bob
  • Pin your hair up
  • Slick back your hair

Each of these methods can help create the illusion of shorter hair, even if your locks are long and flowing.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for creating a faux bob:

  1. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  2. Next, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Take the ends of your hair and tuck them under, securing with more bobby pins.
  4. Adjust the length of your "bob" as desired.
  5. Finish off with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember, the key to making your hair look short is to create the illusion of shorter hair. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

Tips for Short Hair

If you're considering a short haircut, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a cut
  • Invest in regular trims to maintain your style
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

Remember, short hair can be just as versatile as long hair. You can still experiment with different styles and looks, even with a shorter cut.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair look shorter without cutting it?

Yes! There are several ways to create the illusion of shorter hair, including using hair accessories, creating volume at the crown, and trying a faux bob.

What should I consider when choosing a short haircut?

When choosing a short haircut, consider your face shape and hair texture. A good stylist can help you choose a cut that flatters your features and works well with your hair type.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

To maintain a short haircut, it's important to get regular trims. Aim to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than long hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short locks healthy and shiny:

  • Use a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Invest in a good quality styling product to keep your style looking fresh
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to keep your hair looking and smelling fresh

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair look short without actually cutting it. Whether you're looking for a new style or just want to switch things up, these techniques can help you achieve the look you want.
