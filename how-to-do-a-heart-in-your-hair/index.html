How To Do A Heart In Your Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a cute and easy hairstyle to try, why not do a heart in your hair? This playful look is perfect for Valentine's Day, but it's also a fun way to add some flair to your everyday style. In this blog post, we'll show you how to create a heart in your hair in just a few simple steps.

Description

A heart in your hair is a hairstyle where you create a heart shape using your hair. It's a fun and playful look that's perfect for any occasion. This hairstyle is easy to do and can be done on any hair type and length.

How to

To create a heart in your hair, you'll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray
  • A comb

Follow these steps to create a heart in your hair:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head on one side of the parting and twist it towards the back of your head.
  3. Take a section of hair from the top of your head on the other side of the parting and twist it towards the back of your head.
  4. Bring the two twisted sections of hair together to create a heart shape.
  5. Secure the heart shape with bobby pins.
  6. Use hairspray to hold the heart shape in place.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a heart in your hair:

Step 1: Part Your Hair

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Use a comb to create a clean and straight parting.

Step 2: Twist Your Hair

Take a section of hair from the top of your head on one side of the parting and twist it towards the back of your head. Repeat this step on the other side of the parting.

Step 3: Bring the Twisted Sections Together

Bring the two twisted sections of hair together to create a heart shape. Adjust the size and shape of the heart as needed.

Step 4: Secure with Bobby Pins

Once you're happy with the shape of the heart, secure it with bobby pins. Make sure to hide the bobby pins under the twisted sections of hair.

Step 5: Use Hairspray

Use hairspray to hold the heart shape in place. Spray the hairspray all over your hair to make sure the heart stays put.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect heart in your hair:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start.
  • If you have shorter hair, you can still create a heart in your hair. Just use smaller sections of hair and make a smaller heart shape.
  • Experiment with different heart shapes. You can create a pointy heart, a round heart, or a heart with a dip in the middle.
  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place. This will help the heart stay put all day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating a heart in your hair:

Can I create a heart in my hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can create a heart in your hair no matter what your hair type is. Just make sure to twist your hair tightly and use enough hairspray to hold the style in place.

How long does it take to create a heart in your hair?

It should only take a few minutes to create a heart in your hair. Once you get the hang of the technique, you'll be able to do it quickly and easily.

Is it easy to create a heart in your hair?

Yes, creating a heart in your hair is very easy. It only requires a few simple steps, and you don't need any special tools or skills.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair when creating a heart in your hair. Use a good quality hairspray that won't damage your hair, and avoid pulling your hair too tightly when twisting it. If you have any concerns about creating a heart in your hair, talk to your hairstylist for advice.

Now that you know how to create a heart in your hair, it's time to give it a try. Whether you're going out for a special occasion or just want to add some fun to your everyday style, a heart in your hair is a great choice. Have fun experimenting with different heart shapes and sizes, and don't forget to use hairspray to hold the style in place!
