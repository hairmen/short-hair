Do Beards Make You Look Tougher The Answer May Surprise You

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a fan of facial hair, you've probably wondered at some point if beards make you look tougher. After all, there's something undeniably rugged and masculine about a well-groomed beard. But is there any truth to the idea that beards make you look tougher, or is it just a myth? In this article, we'll explore the science behind beards and masculinity, as well as some tips for growing and maintaining your own beard.

  2. How to Grow a Beard
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Maintaining Your Beard

The Science Behind Beards and Masculinity

Believe it or not, there is some science to back up the idea that beards make you look tougher. According to a study published in the journal Personality and Individual Differences, women rated men with full beards as more aggressive and socially dominant than men with lighter or no facial hair. This may be because beards accentuate masculine features such as a strong jawline and a prominent brow ridge.

Another study published in the journal Evolution and Human Behavior found that men with beards were perceived as more attractive and dominant by both sexes. This may be because beards are a sign of sexual maturity and the ability to grow a full, healthy beard is seen as a sign of good genes.

So, it seems that there is some truth to the idea that beards make you look tougher. But of course, there are many other factors that contribute to how masculine or tough someone appears, including body language, clothing, and grooming habits.

How to Grow a Beard

If you're interested in growing a beard to enhance your masculine appearance, here are some tips to get started:

  • Stop shaving and let your facial hair grow out for at least a few weeks.
  • Invest in a good beard trimmer to keep your beard neat and tidy as it grows.
  • Consider using beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated.
  • Be patient! It can take several months for a full beard to grow in.

Step-by-Step Guide to Maintaining Your Beard

Once you have a full beard, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here's a step-by-step guide to beard maintenance:

  1. Trim your beard regularly to keep it neat and tidy.
  2. Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated.
  3. Wash your beard regularly with a gentle shampoo designed for facial hair.
  4. Use a comb or brush to help distribute beard oil evenly throughout your beard.
  5. Avoid over-trimming or shaping your beard too much, as this can make it look unnatural.

Tips for Keeping Your Beard Looking Its Best

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your beard looking its best:

  • Experiment with different beard styles to find one that suits your face shape and personal style.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your beard, as this can dry it out and damage the hair.
  • If you have sensitive skin, consider using a beard oil or balm that is formulated for sensitive skin.
  • Don't be afraid to seek out professional help if you're struggling to maintain your beard on your own.

FAQs About Beards and Masculinity

Q: Do all women find beards attractive?

A: No, not all women find beards attractive. Like any physical trait, attractiveness is subjective and varies from person to person.

Q: Can I still look tough without a beard?

A: Yes, of course! While beards can enhance a masculine appearance, there are many factors that contribute to how tough or masculine someone appears, including body language, clothing, and grooming habits.

Q: Will my beard grow in evenly?

A: It's possible that your beard may grow in unevenly or with patchy areas. If this happens, don't worry – it's completely normal. You can use a beard trimmer to carefully shape your beard and disguise any patchy areas.

Hair Care for Your Beard

Just like the hair on your head, your beard needs regular care and maintenance to stay healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for keeping your beard hair in top condition:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a gentle shampoo designed for facial hair.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated.
  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your beard, as this can dry it out and damage the hair.

So, do beards make you look tougher? The answer is yes – but it's not just the beard itself that determines how masculine or tough you appear. By taking care of your beard and paying attention to other factors like body language and grooming habits, you can enhance your overall appearance and project the confident, masculine image you desire.
