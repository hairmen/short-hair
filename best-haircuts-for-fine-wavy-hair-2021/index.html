Best Haircuts For Fine Wavy Hair 2021

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine wavy hair, you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want something that will enhance your natural texture without weighing it down or making it look too thin. Luckily, there are plenty of options for 2021 that will flatter your hair type and give you a fresh new look. In this article, we'll go over the best haircuts for fine wavy hair in 2021, including how to style them and care for your hair.

Description

Fine wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it has a natural bounce and movement that can be very attractive. On the other hand, it can easily become limp and lifeless, especially if you don't use the right products or styling techniques. That's why choosing the right haircut is so important. A good haircut can add volume, texture, and shape to your hair, making it look fuller and more vibrant.

How to

When choosing a haircut for fine wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid anything too heavy or blunt, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, opt for layers and soft, textured cuts that will enhance your natural waves. Second, consider your face shape and personal style. You want a haircut that will flatter your features and reflect your personality. Finally, make sure you communicate your needs and preferences to your stylist. They can help you choose a haircut that will work best for you.

Step by Step

The best haircuts for fine wavy hair in 2021 include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a classic choice for wavy hair. They add movement and texture without sacrificing length. To style, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Air dry or use a diffuser to enhance your waves.

2. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a chic and modern option for fine wavy hair. Keep the length just above your shoulders and add some soft layers for extra texture. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush and use a texturizing spray to enhance your waves.

3. Shaggy Layers

A shaggy haircut with lots of layers is perfect for fine wavy hair. This style adds volume and texture while also giving you a cool, edgy look. To style, apply a texturizing spray to dry hair and tousle with your fingers.

4. Lob with Highlights

A lob (long bob) with highlights is a great way to add dimension and depth to your fine wavy hair. Keep the length just above your shoulders and add some subtle highlights throughout. To style, use a curling wand to create loose waves and finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

To keep your fine wavy hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves
  • Try air drying your hair whenever possible to avoid heat damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut

FAQ

Q: Can I still get a pixie cut if I have fine wavy hair?

A: Yes, but you'll want to choose a cut that is tailored to your hair type. A pixie cut with lots of layers and texture can look great on fine wavy hair.

Q: How often should I wash my fine wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with fine hair prefer to wash every day to keep their hair looking fresh, while others prefer to wash every other day or less to avoid stripping their hair of natural oils.

Hair Care

To keep your fine wavy hair healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools (like curling irons or straighteners) too often, as this can cause heat damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to nourish and hydrate your strands
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and choosing a flattering haircut, you can enhance your fine wavy hair and achieve a look that is both stylish and natural. Whether you prefer long layers, a bob with bangs, or a shaggy cut, there is a perfect haircut for you in 2021.
