The Best Mens Haircuts For Long Face In 2023

Written by Christine Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have a long face and are struggling to find the perfect haircut that suits your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best mens haircuts for long face in 2023. We will cover everything from how to determine your face shape to tips on how to care for your hair. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right haircut for a long face is to determine your face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Take a picture of your face directly in front of you.
  2. Print out the picture or view it on your computer.
  3. Draw an imaginary line across your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Measure the length and width of your face.
  5. Compare your measurements to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and oblong.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have a long face shape. Now that you know your face shape, let's discuss the best mens haircuts for long face in 2023.

The Best Mens Haircuts for Long Face in 2023

The Side Part

The side part is a classic mens haircut that is perfect for long face shapes. It adds volume to the sides of the head, which balances out the length of the face. To achieve this look, ask your barber to leave more length on the top and to create a side part.

The Undercut

The undercut is a modern haircut that is great for long face shapes. It involves shaving the sides and back of the head while leaving the top longer. This creates a contrast that draws attention away from the length of the face. To achieve this look, ask your barber to shave the sides and back of your head with a clipper and to leave more length on the top.

The Quiff

The quiff is a versatile mens haircut that works well with long face shapes. It involves styling the hair on top of the head into a voluminous wave. This creates an illusion of width, which balances out the length of the face. To achieve this look, ask your barber to leave more length on the top and to use a blow dryer and a round brush to create volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have found the perfect mens haircut for long face in 2023, it's important to know how to care for your hair to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type and scalp.
  • Condition your hair to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a hair dryer and a styling product to achieve the desired look.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

What hairstyles should a man with a long face avoid?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, such as a pompadour or a spiky hairstyle. These hairstyles will make your face look even longer.

Can a man with a long face have short hair?

Yes, a man with a long face can have short hair. However, it's important to choose a short haircut that adds width to the face, such as a buzz cut or a crew cut.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

Conclusion

Choosing the right mens haircut for long face in 2023 can be a challenge, but with the right information, it's possible to find the perfect style. Remember to determine your face shape, choose a haircut that adds width to your face, and take care of your hair to maintain the look. With these tips, you'll have a stylish and confident look that suits your face shape.
