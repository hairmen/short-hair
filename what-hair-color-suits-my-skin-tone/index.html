What Hair Color Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Daniel Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be difficult to determine what will look best on you. Fortunately, there are some simple tips and tricks that can help you find the perfect hair color to complement your skin tone. In this article, we will discuss how to determine your skin tone and what hair colors work best for each tone.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool tones have a pink or blue undertone, warm tones have a yellow or gold undertone, and neutral tones have a mix of both.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the jewelry that looks best on you. If you look better in silver jewelry, you likely have a cool undertone. If you look better in gold jewelry, you likely have a warm undertone. If you look good in both, you likely have a neutral undertone.

What Hair Color Suits Cool Skin Tones?

Cool skin tones look best with hair colors that have cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, and black. These colors complement the pink or blue undertones in the skin.

If you want to add some warmth to your hair, consider adding some highlights in a cool tone. For example, if you have cool brown hair, you could add some ash blonde highlights.

What Hair Color Suits Warm Skin Tones?

Warm skin tones look best with hair colors that have warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey blonde, warm brown, and chestnut. These colors complement the yellow or gold undertones in the skin.

If you want to add some coolness to your hair, consider adding some highlights in a cool tone. For example, if you have warm brown hair, you could add some ash brown highlights.

What Hair Color Suits Neutral Skin Tones?

Neutral skin tones look great with a wide range of hair colors, from cool to warm. You can experiment with different shades and tones to find what looks best on you.

If you want to add some depth to your hair, consider adding some lowlights in a different shade. For example, if you have neutral brown hair, you could add some caramel lowlights.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your natural hair color. It's usually best to stay within a few shades of your natural color for a natural look.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hair color.
  • Consider your skin tone. As we discussed earlier, your skin tone plays a big role in what hair color will look best on you.
  • Consider your eye color. Your eye color can also influence what hair color will look best on you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair color:

Can I dye my hair a completely different color from my natural color?

Yes, you can dye your hair a completely different color from your natural color. However, keep in mind that it may require more maintenance and upkeep to keep it looking good.

What if I have multiple skin tones?

If you have multiple skin tones, you can choose a hair color that complements one or both of them.

What should I do if I'm still not sure what hair color to choose?

If you're still not sure what hair color to choose, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine what will look best on you.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can also damage the color.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and tricks, you can find a hair color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
