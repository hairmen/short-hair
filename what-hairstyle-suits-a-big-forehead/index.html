What Hairstyle Suits a Big Forehead: Tips and Examples

Are you someone with a big forehead and wondering what hairstyle would suit you best? You're not alone! A lot of people with big foreheads find themselves struggling to find a hairstyle that flatters their face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing some tips and examples of hairstyles that would look great on someone with a big forehead. So, let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles that would look great on someone with a big forehead, it's important to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back so you can see your entire face. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face. Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that would suit you best.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle for a Big Forehead

If you have a big forehead, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that either minimizes or balances out your forehead. Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle:

  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can help balance out a big forehead and draw attention away from it.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, which can help minimize the appearance of a big forehead.
  • Long hair: If you have long hair, you can create a side part and sweep your hair over to one side to balance out your forehead.
  • Updos: Updos can also help to balance out a big forehead by drawing attention to other parts of your face, like your eyes and cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you've determined your face shape and have some tips for choosing a hairstyle, let's talk about how to style your hair.

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent heat damage.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or curls in your hair.
  4. If you're adding bangs, use a pair of scissors to cut them to the desired length.
  5. Apply a small amount of styling product, like hairspray or mousse, to hold your hairstyle in place.

Examples of Hairstyles for a Big Forehead

Now that you know some tips and how to style your hair, let's take a look at some examples of hairstyles that would look great on someone with a big forehead:

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for someone with a big forehead. They can help balance out your face and draw attention away from your forehead. You can also add some layers to your hair to create texture and volume.

Long Hair with a Side Part

If you have long hair, you can create a side part and sweep your hair over to one side. This can help balance out your forehead and create a romantic, feminine look.

Updo with Side-Swept Bangs

If you're looking for a more formal hairstyle, an updo with side-swept bangs can be a great option. This hairstyle can help balance out your forehead and create an elegant, sophisticated look.

FAQs About Hairstyles for a Big Forehead

What is the best hairstyle for a big forehead?

The best hairstyle for a big forehead is one that either minimizes or balances out your forehead. This can include side-swept bangs, layers, long hair with a side part, or an updo with side-swept bangs.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a big forehead?

You should avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, like a slicked-back ponytail or bun. These hairstyles can emphasize your forehead and make it look even bigger.

Hair Care Tips for a Big Forehead

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, there are some hair care tips that can help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and examples, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your big forehead and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
