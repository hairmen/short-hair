How To Get Natural Curly Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a man with straight hair, you might be wondering how you can get those luscious curls that many women envy. The good news is that getting natural curly hair as a male is possible, and it doesn’t require a lot of effort. In this article, we’ll show you how to get natural curly hair male with our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Natural Curly Hair Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting Natural Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Get Natural Curly Hair Male

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to understand that everyone’s hair is different. Some people have naturally curly hair, while others have straight hair. However, regardless of your hair type, there are several ways to get natural curly hair as a male.

The first step is to determine your hair type. If you have straight hair, you’ll need to use heat styling tools to create curls. If you have wavy hair, you might be able to create curls using only hair products. And if you have curly hair, you’ll need to focus on enhancing your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to get natural curly hair male:

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step is to wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type. If you have curly hair, look for products that are designed to enhance curls. If you have straight hair, look for volumizing products that can add body to your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, use a towel to dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair aggressively, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, gently pat your hair until it’s damp.

Step 3: Apply Products

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz. If you don’t have a diffuser attachment, you can use a regular hairdryer on a low heat setting.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. You can use a hairbrush or your fingers to create a natural look. If you want more defined curls, use a curling iron or wand to create tighter curls.

Tips for Getting Natural Curly Hair

Here are some tips for getting natural curly hair as a male:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush on your curls, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Can men get natural curly hair?

Yes, men can get natural curly hair by using hair products and heat styling tools.

What products can I use to enhance my curls?

You can use curl-enhancing mousses, gels, and creams to enhance your curls.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and damage.

Hair Care for Natural Curly Hair Male

Here are some tips for taking care of your natural curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

By following these tips and using the right hair products, you can get natural curly hair as a male. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage and breakage. With a little patience and effort, you can achieve the curly hair you’ve always wanted.
