How To Turn Your Curly Hair Into Wavy Hair

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair and want to try something new? Wavy hair is a great option for those who want a change without sacrificing their hair's natural texture. In this article, we will discuss how to turn your curly hair into wavy hair with step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Step-by-Step Instructions

The first step to turning your curly hair into wavy hair is to wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

Next, towel dry your hair and apply a curl-defining cream or gel. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Separate your hair into sections and twist each section into a loose bun. Secure the bun with a hair tie or clip and allow your hair to air dry overnight.

The next morning, remove the hair ties or clips and gently finger comb your hair to separate the waves. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips for Creating Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you create beautiful wavy hair:

  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can break up the waves and create frizz.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent tangles and keep your hair smooth.

FAQs

Q: Can I turn my curly hair into wavy hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use the twist and bun method described in this article to create waves without using heat.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is, but generally, washing your hair every 2-3 days is sufficient to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

To keep your wavy hair looking its best, it's important to follow a healthy hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily turn your curly hair into beautiful wavy locks. Experiment with different products and methods to find what works best for you and enjoy your new hairstyle!
