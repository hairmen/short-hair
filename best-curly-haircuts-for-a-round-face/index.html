Best Curly Haircuts For A Round Face Flattering Styles For Every Curl Type

Written by Eveline Jul 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the best curly haircuts for a round face? As a curly hair expert, I understand that finding the right hairstyle can be difficult, especially if you have a round face shape. In this article, I'll share with you the best haircuts for curly hair that will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural curls. From short bobs to long layers, there's a perfect hairstyle for every curly girl.

Description

When it comes to choosing the best curly haircut for a round face, the goal is to create balance and definition. A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with soft edges, so the right haircut should elongate your face and create the illusion of more angular features. In other words, you want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face and opt for haircuts that add height and length instead.

How to Choose the Best Curly Haircut for a Round Face

Before we dive into the best curly haircuts for a round face, let's talk about how to choose the right one for you. The key is to look for haircuts that create vertical and diagonal lines, which will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose haircuts with layers that start at your jawline or below to add length to your face
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, which can make your face look wider
  • Consider hairstyles with side-swept bangs or a deep side part to create diagonal lines
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the crown to add height to your face

Step by Step: Best Curly Haircuts for a Round Face

1. Long Layers

If you have long curly hair, adding layers can help create movement and dimension. Long layers that start at your jawline or below will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. To style, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and diffuse with a hair dryer. Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your natural curls.

2. Short Bob

A short bob is a great option for curly hair because it's easy to style and can help create a more defined shape. For a round face, opt for a bob that's chin-length or longer and has layers that start at your jawline. To style, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and diffuse with a hair dryer. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls as you dry to enhance your natural texture.

3. Curly Shag

A curly shag is a great option for curly hair because it adds volume and texture without adding width to your face. For a round face, opt for a shag with layers that start at your chin or below. To style, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and diffuse with a hair dryer. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls as you dry to enhance your natural texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Haircut

Once you've found the perfect curly haircut for your round face, it's important to maintain it to keep your curls looking their best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, which can cause frizz and breakage
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to enhance your natural curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, which can damage your curls
  • Get regular trims to keep your curls looking healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs

What are the best haircuts for curly hair?

The best haircuts for curly hair are those that enhance your natural texture and flatter your face shape. Some popular haircuts for curly hair include long layers, a short bob, and a curly shag.

How do you style curly hair?

To style curly hair, start by applying a curl-enhancing product to damp hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls with your hands as you go. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, which can cause frizz and breakage.

How do you maintain curly hair?

To maintain curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair. Limit your use of heat styling tools and get regular trims to keep your curls looking healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing the best curly haircut for a round face can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural curls. Whether you opt for long layers or a curly shag, the key is to create vertical and diagonal lines that elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a gorgeous curly hairstyle in no time!
