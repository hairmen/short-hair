How To Curl Hair With Bendy Foam Rollers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a heat-free and easy way to curl your hair, bendy foam rollers are a great option. They come in different sizes and can give you anything from tight ringlets to loose waves. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of using bendy foam rollers to achieve beautiful curls.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Use Bendy Foam Rollers
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Using Bendy Foam Rollers

How to Use Bendy Foam Rollers

Bendy foam rollers are lightweight and easy to use. They work best on damp hair, so start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's about 80% dry. Then follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections you create depends on how thick your hair is. If you have thin hair, you can create more sections. If you have thick hair, you can create fewer sections. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Apply a heat-protectant spray or a leave-in conditioner to each section of hair. This will help protect your hair from damage and keep it moisturized.

Step 3: Roll Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a bendy foam roller. Start at the ends of your hair and roll the roller up towards your scalp. Once you reach your scalp, bend the foam roller to secure it in place. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry

Leave the rollers in your hair until it's completely dry. You can either air dry your hair or use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process.

Step 5: Take Out the Rollers

Once your hair is dry, carefully remove the rollers one by one. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create your desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to using bendy foam rollers:

  1. Section your hair into small sections.
  2. Apply a heat-protectant spray or leave-in conditioner to each section of hair.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a bendy foam roller.
  4. Roll the roller up towards your scalp, bending it to secure it in place.
  5. Repeat this process for each section of hair.
  6. Let your hair dry completely.
  7. Remove the rollers one by one.
  8. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create your desired look.

Tips for Using Bendy Foam Rollers

Here are some tips for using bendy foam rollers:

  • Use smaller rollers for tighter curls and larger rollers for looser waves.
  • For best results, use the rollers on damp hair.
  • Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair before rolling it for added hold.
  • Leave the rollers in your hair until it's completely dry to ensure long-lasting curls.
  • For extra volume, use the rollers at the crown of your head.

FAQs

Q: How long should I leave the rollers in my hair?

A: Leave the rollers in your hair until it's completely dry. This can take anywhere from a few hours to overnight.

Q: Can I use bendy foam rollers on dry hair?

A: While it's possible to use bendy foam rollers on dry hair, they work best on damp hair. If you're using them on dry hair, be sure to apply some water or a styling product to each section of hair before rolling it.

Q: How do I store my bendy foam rollers?

A: Store your bendy foam rollers in a cool, dry place. Avoid exposing them to direct sunlight or heat, as this can cause them to lose their shape.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and using bendy foam rollers to curl your hair, you can achieve beautiful, healthy-looking curls without any heat damage.
