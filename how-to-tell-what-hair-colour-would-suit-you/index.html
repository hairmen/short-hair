How To Tell What Hair Colour Would Suit You

Written by Oliver Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking to switch it up? Or maybe you've never dyed your hair before and don't know where to start. Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and tricks, you can easily determine what hair colour would suit you best. In this article, we'll go over how to tell what hair colour would suit you and provide some helpful hair care tips along the way.

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining what hair colour would suit you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have blue or pink undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of cool and warm undertones.

To figure out your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear bluish, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear greenish, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you probably have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining what hair colour would suit you. Generally, warm hair colours like red and gold complement brown or hazel eyes, while cool hair colours like ash blonde or black complement blue or green eyes.

Step 3: Think About Your Natural Hair Colour

If you're not looking for a drastic change, consider your natural hair colour. Going a few shades lighter or darker can still make a big difference without being too extreme. If you're looking for a more dramatic change, consider going for a completely different colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Step 4: Look at Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in choosing your hair colour. If you have a busy schedule and don't want to spend a lot of time maintaining your hair, consider a lower maintenance colour. On the other hand, if you have a more flexible schedule and enjoy experimenting with your hair, a higher maintenance colour may be right for you.

Tips for Choosing Your Hair Colour

  • Consider your wardrobe and make sure your hair colour complements it.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hair colour apps to get an idea of what the colour would look like on you.
  • Consult with a hair stylist for professional advice.

FAQs About Hair Colour

How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on the colour you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, root touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Will dyeing my hair damage it?

Dyeing your hair can cause damage, but using the right products and taking proper care of your hair can minimize the damage.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and use the right products for your hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you can achieve a beautiful and vibrant new look.
