What Hairstyle Suits Oval Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we all know, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task. But it's even more challenging when you have an oval face shape. With so many options available, it's hard to know which one will suit your face shape. But worry not, we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle suits oval faces and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Oval face shapes are considered to be the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. This face shape is characterized by a balanced proportion of features, including a slightly curved jawline, high cheekbones, and a forehead that is wider than the chin. This means that oval faces can pull off a wide range of hairstyles, from short to long, curly to straight, and everything in between.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a close look at your face and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly curved jawline and high cheekbones, you have an oval face shape.

What Hairstyle Suits Oval Faces

Now that you know your face shape, let's dive into the hairstyles that will suit your oval face.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a great option for oval faces. The right cut can highlight your cheekbones and jawline, while also adding volume to your hair. Here are some options:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob cut
  • Layered short cut

Medium Haircuts

If you're not a fan of short haircuts, medium haircuts can also work well for oval faces. Here are some options:

  • Shoulder-length cut
  • Long bob
  • Layered medium cut

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can also work well for oval faces. However, it's important to avoid cuts that add too much volume to the top of your head. Here are some options:

  • Straight and sleek
  • Beachy waves
  • Side-swept bangs

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know what hairstyles suit oval faces, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Avoid adding too much volume to the top of your head.
  • Consider adding layers to add movement to your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to add volume to the ends of your hair.
  • Use a straightening iron to create sleek and straight hair.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose waves.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for oval faces?

The best haircut for oval faces is a short, medium, or long cut that highlights your cheekbones and jawline. Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and shoulder-length cuts are all great options.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes, oval faces can pull off bangs. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs, such as side-swept or wispy bangs, that complement your face shape.

Can oval faces wear their hair straight?

Yes, oval faces can wear their hair straight. However, it's important to avoid adding too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there's a style out there that will suit you perfectly.
