Short Hairstyles For Round Faces 2022 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new short hairstyle but have a round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for round faces in 2022. From bobs to pixies, we have a variety of styles that will flatter your face shape and have you feeling confident and stylish. So, let's dive in!

Description

Round faces are characterized by their equal width and length, with a soft, curved jawline. The goal when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face is to elongate the face shape and create more angles. This can be achieved through layers, angles, and texture. The right hairstyle can not only flatter your features but also highlight your best assets.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Round Face

Choosing the right short hairstyle for your round face can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect style:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair plays an important role in choosing the right short hairstyle. If you have thin hair, a pixie cut or layered bob can add volume and dimension to your locks. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or asymmetrical cut can help to thin out your hair and create a more balanced look.

Think About Your Face Shape

While round faces share similar characteristics, not all round faces are the same. Take a closer look at your face shape and determine if you have a round face with a high forehead, a round face with a square jawline, or a round face with a narrow chin. This will help you choose a style that will complement your features.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle plays an important role in choosing the right short hairstyle. If you're a busy mom or have a demanding job, you may want a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you're more adventurous and like to switch up your look, a short, layered style may be perfect for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your round face, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair for round faces:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you achieve the best results and ensure your style lasts all day.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or texturizing spray, to your hair. This will help to add volume and texture to your locks.

Step 3: Use a Blowdryer

Using a blowdryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to create volume and texture. If you have a round brush, you can also use this to create more volume and shape.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool, such as a flat iron or curling iron, to create your desired look. If you have a pixie cut, you may not need to use any styling tools, as the cut itself is already textured and edgy.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to keep your style in place. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more dimension and texture to your locks.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Round Faces

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair for round faces:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh and polished, it's important to trim your hair regularly. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Short hair is more prone to damage, so it's important to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks healthy and moisturized.

Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

While heat styling tools can be great for creating different hairstyles, using them too often can damage your hair. To prevent damage, limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant before styling.

FAQs

What are the Best Short Hairstyles for Round Faces?

The best short hairstyles for round faces include layered bobs, asymmetrical cuts, and pixie cuts. These styles help to elongate the face shape and create more angles.

Can I Wear Bangs with a Short Hairstyle for a Round Face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a round face. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or choppy bangs are great options for round faces.

Can I Dye My Short Hair?

Yes, you can dye your short hair. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and features. If you're unsure about which color to choose, consult with a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Round Faces

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for short hair for round faces:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Short hair can get oily and greasy more quickly than longer hair, so it's important to wash your hair regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks healthy.

Avoid Using Too Much Product

While styling products can help to create different hairstyles, using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use only the amount of product you need to achieve your desired look.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause split ends. To prevent damage, use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

Conclusion

Short hairstyles for round faces in 2022 are all about creating angles and texture. From layered bobs to pixie cuts, there are a variety of styles that will flatter your face shape and have you feeling confident and stylish. Remember to consider your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle when choosing the perfect style, and don't forget to maintain your locks with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments. With the right short hairstyle, you can highlight your best features and feel like a million bucks!
