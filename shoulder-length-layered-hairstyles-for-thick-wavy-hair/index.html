Shoulder Length Layered Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will suit your thick wavy hair? Look no further than shoulder length layered hairstyles. Not only do they add volume and texture to your hair, but they are also low maintenance and easy to style. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve the perfect shoulder length layered hairstyle for your thick wavy hair, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining it.

Description

Shoulder length layered hairstyles are a great choice for those with thick, wavy hair. The layers add movement and texture, while the length provides versatility in styling. These hairstyles can range from subtle, face-framing layers to bold, choppy layers. Whatever your preference, a shoulder length layered hairstyle is sure to add depth and dimension to your hair.

How To

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for thick, wavy hair. This will help to tame frizz and add moisture to your locks.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to enhance your waves.

3. Use a large round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves throughout your hair.

5. Use a texturizing spray to add definition to your layers.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Section off your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

3. Use a razor or scissors to create layers throughout your hair. Start with longer layers near the bottom and gradually work your way up to shorter layers near the top.

4. Once your layers are complete, use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves throughout your hair.

5. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold to your layers.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair while detangling.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

- Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your layers.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have thick, wavy hair, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight with layers?

A: Yes! Shoulder length layered hairstyles can be worn straight or wavy. Use a straightening iron to achieve a sleek, straight look.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length layered hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use products specifically designed for thick, wavy hair to keep it moisturized and healthy. Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair. And don't forget to get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your layers.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect shoulder length layered hairstyle for your thick wavy hair. Whether you prefer subtle layers or bold choppy layers, a shoulder length layered hairstyle is sure to add depth and dimension to your locks.
