Spiky Hairstyle 2020 A Guide To Effortlessly Rocking This Trendy Look

Written by Eveline Nov 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that's edgy, stylish, and easy to maintain? Look no further than the spiky hairstyle! This versatile cut has been around for years, and it's still going strong in 2020. Whether you're a man or a woman, you can rock the spiky look with confidence. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about spiky hair, from how to style it to how to care for it. So, let's get started!

  2. How to Style Spiky Hair

Description

Spiky hair is a short, textured haircut that's been styled to stand up in spikes. It's a popular look for men and women of all ages, and it's easy to see why. Spiky hair is edgy, modern, and low-maintenance, making it a perfect choice for anyone who wants to look effortlessly cool.

There are many different ways to style spiky hair, from subtle spikes to bold, dramatic ones. Whether you want a messy, bed-head look or a sleek, polished style, spiky hair can be customized to suit your individual taste and personality.

How to Style Spiky Hair

Styling spiky hair is easy, and it doesn't take long to achieve the perfect look. Here's what you need to do:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Wax, pomade, or gel can all work well, depending on your hair type and the look you're going for.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes in your hair. Start at the roots and work your way up, using a twisting motion to create texture and volume.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold your spikes in place.

That's it! Depending on your hair type, you may need to experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect spiky look for you. But with a little practice, you'll be rocking the spiky hairstyle like a pro.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to spiky hair or you want to try a different style, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp but not completely dry.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a wax, pomade, or gel that's designed for spiky hair.
  4. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes in your hair. Start at the roots and work your way up, using a twisting motion to create texture and volume.
  5. If you want a messy, bed-head look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a more natural look.
  6. If you want a sleek, polished look, use a comb to smooth your hair and create a more refined look.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your spikes in place.

Remember, the key to a great spiky hairstyle is to experiment and find what works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a look that's uniquely yours.

Tips for Maintaining Spiky Hair

Spiky hair is easy to maintain, but there are a few tips you should keep in mind to keep your hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Use a styling product that's appropriate for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • Don't apply too much product to your hair. A little goes a long way, and too much can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

FAQs About Spiky Hair

Q: Who can wear a spiky hairstyle?

A: Anyone can wear a spiky hairstyle! It's a versatile look that can be customized to suit your individual style and personality.

Q: Is spiky hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes, spiky hair is easy to maintain. It's a low-maintenance style that requires minimal effort to keep looking great.

Q: Can spiky hair be worn at any age?

A: Absolutely! Spiky hair can be worn by men and women of all ages. It's a timeless style that never goes out of fashion.

Hair Care Tips for Spiky Hair

Spiky hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips to help you care for your spiky hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Use a styling product that's appropriate for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid excessive exposure to the sun, as this can dry out your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • If you have color-treated hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color looking vibrant.

By following these tips, you can keep your spiky hair looking great for years to come. So, what are you waiting for? Get out there and rock that spiky hairstyle with confidence!
