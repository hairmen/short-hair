Best Haircut For Square Face Over 50 A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are over 50 and have a square face shape, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know what style will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural features. In this guide, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to choose the best haircut for square face over 50.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide: How to Get the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. As we age, our skin loses elasticity and our facial features become more defined, making it even more important to choose a haircut that flatters our face shape. A good haircut can help soften the edges of a square face, balance out the proportions of the face, and enhance our natural features.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for square face over 50, it is important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the crown of your head to balance out the width of your jawline.
  • Avoid straight blunt cuts as they can accentuate the angular features of a square face. Instead, opt for soft layers and textured cuts.
  • If you have thinning hair, consider a shorter haircut that will add volume and thickness to your hair.
  • If you have thick hair, consider a longer haircut that will help weigh down your hair and prevent it from looking too bulky.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair. Choose a haircut that is easy to maintain and style.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Get the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect haircut:

  1. Start by finding inspiration for your haircut. Look for pictures of haircuts that you like and that will flatter your face shape.
  2. Book an appointment with a hairstylist who specializes in haircuts for square faces over 50.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist and show them the pictures of haircuts that you like. They will be able to recommend a haircut that will flatter your face shape and hair texture.
  4. During the haircut, communicate with your hairstylist and let them know if you want any changes made. It is important to be clear about what you want to avoid any miscommunications.
  5. Once the haircut is complete, ask your hairstylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as they can damage your hair and cause split ends. If you do use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using hair masks or deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for square faces over 50:

  • What is the best haircut for a square face over 50? A layered haircut with soft, textured layers is the best haircut for a square face over 50.
  • What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face? Avoid blunt, straight cuts and hairstyles that are too short as they can accentuate the angular features of a square face.
  • How often should I get my haircut? It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

Hair Care: Best Products for Square Face Over 50

Here are some of the best hair care products for square faces over 50:

  • Shampoo and Conditioner: Look for a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type and texture. If you have thinning hair, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thick hair, consider using a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Hair Mask: Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Look for a hair mask that is formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Heat Protectant Spray: If you use hot tools on your hair, it is important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you are now ready to choose the best haircut for square face over 50. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose a haircut that will flatter your face shape and hair texture. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best at any age.
