How To Wear A Scarf On Your Head With Short Hair

Written by Christine Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Scarves are not just for the neck, they can also be worn on the head in different styles. However, if you have short hair, you may find it challenging to wear a scarf on your head. In this article, we will show you how to wear a scarf on your head with short hair by providing you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Wear a Scarf on Your Head with Short Hair

Wearing a scarf on your head with short hair can be very stylish and trendy. Here are some of the ways that you can wear a scarf on your head:

  • As a headband
  • As a turban
  • As a bandana
  • As a hair wrap

Each of these styles has different variations that you can experiment with to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear a scarf on your head with short hair:

  1. Choose a scarf that is long enough to wrap around your head at least once.
  2. Fold the scarf in half to create a triangle shape.
  3. Place the scarf at the back of your head with the point of the triangle facing down.
  4. Take the two ends of the scarf and tie them at the top of your head.
  5. Tuck any loose ends of the scarf under the knot.

This style is perfect for creating a headband look with a scarf. You can adjust the knot to make it smaller or larger depending on your preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when wearing a scarf on your head with short hair:

  • Choose a scarf that matches your outfit or complements your hair color.
  • Experiment with different scarf styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the scarf in place if needed.
  • Make sure the scarf is not too tight or too loose on your head.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a scarf on your head with short hair:

Can I wear a scarf on my head with curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a scarf on your head with curly hair. However, you may need to use bobby pins to secure the scarf in place and prevent it from sliding off.

What kind of scarves are best for wearing on the head with short hair?

Silk or satin scarves are ideal for wearing on the head with short hair. They are gentle on the hair and do not cause breakage or damage.

Hair Care Advice

When wearing a scarf on your head with short hair, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to consider:

  • Avoid wearing scarves that are too tight or too rough on your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.

By following these hair care tips, you can protect your hair from damage and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Wearing a scarf on your head with short hair can be a great way to add some style and flair to your look. By following the step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice provided in this article, you can wear a scarf on your head with confidence and ease.
