How To Do 40 S Style Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to channel your inner vintage glam? One of the easiest ways to do so is by styling your hair in a 40's-inspired look. This era was defined by voluminous, sculpted hairstyles that exuded elegance and sophistication. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve the perfect 40's style hair, complete with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

How to

Before we dive into the step-by-step instructions, here are some things you'll need to ensure you have on hand:

  • Hot rollers or curling iron
  • Teasing comb
  • Hairspray
  • Bobby pins
  • Headband or scarf (optional)

Now that you have your tools ready, let's start with the steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a small amount of mousse to add some texture and hold. This will make it easier to style your hair.

Step 2: Roll Your Hair

Using hot rollers or a curling iron, curl your hair in small sections. Roll the hair in the direction away from your face to create the iconic 40's wave. Leave the rollers in for at least 10-15 minutes to ensure the curls set properly.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Using a teasing comb, gently backcomb your hair at the crown to create volume. Smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section and secure with bobby pins.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Gently brush out the curls to create soft waves. Use bobby pins to pin the sides of your hair back, leaving the crown section loose. You can also add a headband or scarf for an extra touch of vintage charm.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your hair in place, spritz some hairspray over the finished style. This will also add some shine and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair in a 40's-inspired look:

  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair - this is what will give you the volume and shape you need for this style.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to secure your hair in place. This will give a more polished finish.
  • If you're using a headband or scarf, choose one that complements your outfit and creates a cohesive look.
  • Experiment with different curl sizes to create a more varied and textured look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling your hair in a 40's-inspired look:

How long does it take to style your hair in a 40's look?

Depending on your hair length and texture, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour to style your hair in a 40's-inspired look.

Can I do this style on short hair?

Yes! While this style is typically associated with longer hair, you can still achieve a 40's-inspired look with shorter hair. Use smaller rollers or a curling iron to create tighter curls and tease your hair for volume.

How can I make my style last longer?

Use a strong-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place and avoid touching or brushing your hair throughout the day. You can also use dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and steps, you're ready to rock a vintage-inspired look that's both elegant and timeless. Have fun experimenting with different variations and finding the style that works best for you!
