How To Decide What Hairstyle Suits You Tips And Tricks

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be hard to decide which one will suit you best. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect hairstyle for you. From face shape to hair texture, we will cover all the important factors that you need to consider.

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining what hairstyle will suit you best. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. It's important to identify your face shape before choosing a hairstyle, as certain styles will flatter your features more than others.

If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides.

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Almost any hairstyle will suit you.

If you have a square face, try to soften your angles with waves or curls. Avoid straight, blunt cuts.

If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides and narrow your chin.

If you have a diamond-shaped face, try to balance your narrow chin and forehead with layers or bangs.

If you have an oblong face, try to add width to your face with curls or waves. Avoid straight, long hairstyles.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are four main hair textures: straight, wavy, curly, and kinky. Different hairstyles will suit different textures.

Straight hair looks great with blunt cuts and sleek styles.

Wavy hair looks great with layered cuts and beachy waves.

Curly hair looks great with voluminous styles and defined curls.

Kinky hair looks great with protective styles like braids and twists.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have an active lifestyle, opt for low-maintenance styles that can withstand sweat and movement. If you have a more formal lifestyle, opt for polished, sophisticated hairstyles.

Personality

Your personality is also an important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you're a free spirit, opt for bohemian, relaxed styles. If you're more conservative, opt for classic, polished styles.

Hair Length

Your hair length will also play a role in determining what hairstyle will suit you best. Short hair looks great with edgy, textured styles. Medium hair looks great with layered cuts and beachy waves. Long hair looks great with voluminous styles and sleek, straight styles.

Hair Color

Your hair color can also affect what hairstyle will suit you best. Lighter hair colors look great with beachy waves and textured styles. Darker hair colors look great with sleek, polished styles.

Budget

Your budget is another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Some styles require more upkeep and maintenance than others. Consider how often you're willing to visit the salon and how much you're willing to spend on hair products.

Hair Care

Your hair care routine is also an important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have damaged hair, opt for styles that will minimize further damage. If you have healthy hair, you have more options to choose from.

Tips and Tricks

  • Bring pictures to your hairstylist to show them exactly what you're looking for.
  • Consider trying out a virtual hairstyle app to see how different styles will look on you.
  • If you're unsure about a style, start with a small change like adding bangs or layers.
  • Remember that hairstyles can always grow out or be changed, so don't be afraid to experiment.

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a certain hairstyle even if it doesn't suit my face shape?

A: While certain styles may be more flattering for certain face shapes, ultimately it's up to you to decide what you feel most confident and comfortable in.

Q: Can I change my hairstyle frequently?

A: Yes! Hair grows back, and hairstyles can always be changed. Experimenting with different styles can be fun and exciting.

Q: How often should I visit the salon to maintain my hairstyle?

A: It depends on the style and your hair type. Some styles require more upkeep than others. Consult with your hairstylist about how often you should visit the salon.

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can seem overwhelming, but by considering all the factors above, you can find a style that truly suits you. Remember to have fun and experiment, and don't be afraid to try something new.
