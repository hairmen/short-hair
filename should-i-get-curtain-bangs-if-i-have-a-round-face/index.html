Should I Get Curtain Bangs If I Have A Round Face

Written by Luffy Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're considering getting curtain bangs and you have a round face, you may be wondering if they will suit you. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add some flair to your look, but they may not be the best option for everyone. In this article, we'll explore whether or not you should get curtain bangs if you have a round face and provide some tips for caring for your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. This style has become popular in recent years and can be seen on many celebrities and influencers. However, if you have a round face, you may be concerned that curtain bangs will make your face appear even rounder.

How to

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the shape of your face. Round faces tend to have soft, curved lines, so blunt bangs may not be the best option. However, curtain bangs can help to create more angles and definition in your face, which can be flattering.

Next, think about the length of your bangs. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid bangs that hit at the widest part of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for bangs that are slightly longer and frame your face on either side.

Step by Step

If you've decided to go for it and get curtain bangs, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Find a stylist who has experience cutting curtain bangs.
  2. Show them pictures of the style you want and discuss how the bangs will be cut to flatter your face shape.
  3. Consider the length and thickness of your hair, as this can impact how the bangs will look.
  4. Be prepared for some maintenance, as curtain bangs will need to be trimmed regularly to maintain their shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when caring for your new curtain bangs:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Blow-dry your bangs straight to create a smooth, sleek look.
  • Use a round brush to add some volume and shape to your bangs.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray to add some texture and movement to your bangs.
  • Be prepared to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking their best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Will curtain bangs suit my face shape?

Curtain bangs can be flattering on many different face shapes, including round faces. However, it's important to work with a stylist who can help you choose the right length and shape of bangs to complement your face.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs will need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from becoming too long or uneven.

Can I style my curtain bangs in different ways?

Yes, curtain bangs can be styled in many different ways. You can wear them straight and sleek, or add some volume and texture with a texturizing spray.

Hair Care

When caring for your hair with curtain bangs, it's important to use high-quality products and keep your hair healthy. This can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking shiny and smooth. Be sure to trim your bangs regularly and style them with a round brush and blow dryer for best results.

So, should you get curtain bangs if you have a round face? The answer is yes, as long as you work with a stylist who can help you choose the right length and shape of bangs to flatter your face. With the right care and maintenance, your new curtain bangs can be a stylish and flattering addition to your look.
