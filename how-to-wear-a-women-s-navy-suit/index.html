How To Wear A Women s Navy Suit A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Navy suits are a timeless classic that never go out of style. They are perfect for any formal occasion, and they can also be dressed down for a more casual look. In this guide, we'll give you all the tips and tricks you need to wear a women's navy suit with confidence.

How to Choose the Right Navy Suit

When it comes to choosing a navy suit, there are a few things to consider. First, you'll want to think about the occasion you'll be wearing it for. If it's a formal event, you'll want a more structured suit with clean lines. If you're wearing it to work, you may want a more relaxed fit.

Another factor to consider is the material. Wool is a classic choice for suits, but it can be heavy and uncomfortable in warmer weather. If you're looking for a more breathable option, consider a cotton or linen suit.

How to Wear a Navy Suit

Now that you've chosen the right navy suit for your needs, it's time to figure out how to wear it. Here are some tips to help you put together the perfect outfit:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shirt

The shirt you wear with your navy suit is just as important as the suit itself. For a classic look, opt for a white dress shirt. If you want to add a pop of color, a light pink or blue shirt can look great.

Step 2: Select the Perfect Accessories

Accessories can make or break an outfit, so choose wisely. A simple tie in a complementary color can add a touch of sophistication to your look. For a more casual look, skip the tie and opt for a statement necklace or earrings instead.

Step 3: Choose the Right Shoes

Shoes are another important factor to consider when wearing a navy suit. For a formal event, opt for classic black pumps. If you're dressing down your suit, a pair of loafers or sneakers can look great.

Tips for Wearing a Women's Navy Suit

Here are some additional tips to help you wear your women's navy suit with confidence:

  • Make sure your suit fits properly. A poorly fitting suit can ruin your entire look.
  • Don't be afraid to play with patterns. A navy suit looks great with a striped or polka-dot shirt.
  • Keep your accessories simple. A statement necklace or earrings can add a pop of color, but don't overdo it.

FAQ

Can I wear a navy suit to a wedding?

Yes, a navy suit is a great choice for a wedding. Just make sure to dress it up with a dress shirt and tie.

What color shoes should I wear with a navy suit?

For a formal event, black pumps or heels are a classic choice. For a more casual look, brown loafers or sneakers can look great.

Hair Care

When wearing a navy suit, it's important to make sure your hair is styled appropriately. A simple updo or sleek ponytail can look great with a formal navy suit. For a more casual look, loose waves or a messy bun can be a great choice.

With these tips, you're ready to wear a women's navy suit with confidence. Whether you're attending a formal event or dressing down for a casual day at the office, a navy suit is a versatile and timeless choice.
