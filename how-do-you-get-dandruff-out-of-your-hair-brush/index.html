How to Get Dandruff Out of Your Hair Brush

  • A comb or brush cleaner

Step by Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to get dandruff out of your hair brush:

  1. Use a comb or brush cleaner to remove any loose hair from the brush bristles.
  2. Fill a bowl with warm water and add a few drops of shampoo.
  3. Dip the brush into the soapy water and swish it around for a few minutes to loosen any dirt or dandruff.
  4. Remove the brush from the water and use a comb or brush cleaner to remove any remaining debris from the bristles.
  5. Rinse the brush under warm water to remove any remaining soap.
  6. Shake the brush to remove excess water.
  7. Lay the brush on a towel or paper towel to air dry.

Tips for a Clean Hair Brush

Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair brush clean and free of dandruff:

  • Remove any loose hair from the brush every time you use it.
  • Clean your hair brush at least once a week to prevent the buildup of dirt and dandruff.
  • Use a comb or brush cleaner to remove any debris from the bristles before washing the brush.
  • Use a mild shampoo or dish soap to clean your brush.
  • Let your brush air dry completely before using it again.
