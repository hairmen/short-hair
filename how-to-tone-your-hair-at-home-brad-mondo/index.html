How To Tone Your Hair At Home Brad Mondo s Guide

Written by Frank Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to tone your hair at home, you’re not alone. Many people want to change the color of their hair without going to a salon, and Brad Mondo has become a go-to source for hair advice. In this article, we’ll walk you through his process for toning your hair at home.

Description

Toning your hair at home involves using a hair toner to neutralize any unwanted brassy or yellow tones. This process helps to enhance the overall color and shine of your hair. Brad Mondo is a popular hairstylist and YouTuber who has become known for his hair tutorials and advice. In this guide, we’ll be using his tips to help you tone your hair at home.

How to Tone Your Hair at Home: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Toner

The first step in toning your hair at home is to choose the right toner. Brad Mondo recommends using a toner with a blue or purple base to counteract any orange or yellow tones in your hair. Look for toners with names like “cool” or “ash” to ensure that you’re getting the right shade.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying the toner, you need to prepare your hair. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Then, towel dry your hair until it’s slightly damp.

Step 3: Mix the Toner

Follow the instructions on the toner packaging to mix the toner with the developer. Brad Mondo recommends using a 1:2 ratio of toner to developer for best results. Mix the toner and developer in a plastic bowl using a plastic spoon until it’s well combined.

Step 4: Apply the Toner

Using a hair dye brush, apply the toner mixture evenly to your hair. Make sure to saturate each strand completely to ensure even coverage. Leave the toner on your hair for the amount of time recommended on the packaging. This is usually around 20-30 minutes.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

After the recommended time, rinse the toner out of your hair with cool water until the water runs clear. Then, apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage.

Tips for Toning Your Hair at Home

Now that you know the steps to tone your hair at home, here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Always wear gloves when applying the toner to avoid staining your skin.
  • Test the toner on a small section of hair before applying it to your entire head to ensure that you like the color.
  • If your hair is severely damaged, it’s best to avoid toning it at home and seek professional help.
  • Use a toner with a lower volume developer if you have fine or damaged hair to prevent further damage.

FAQs

Can I tone my hair at home without using toner?

No, toner is necessary to neutralize any unwanted brassy or yellow tones in your hair. Without toner, your hair may turn out a different color than you intended.

Can I use a toner on colored hair?

Yes, you can use a toner on colored hair. However, it’s best to wait at least two weeks after coloring your hair to tone it to avoid damage.

How often should I tone my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the color you’re trying to achieve. Generally, it’s best to tone your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color.

Hair Care After Toning

After toning your hair at home, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently to prevent color fading.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair from damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent further damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to restore moisture and keep your hair healthy.

Conclusion

Toning your hair at home can be an easy and affordable way to change your hair color. By following Brad Mondo’s tips, you can achieve the perfect tone for your hair without leaving your house. Just remember to choose the right toner, prepare your hair, apply the toner evenly, and take care of your hair after toning.
