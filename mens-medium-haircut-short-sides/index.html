Mens Medium Haircut Short Sides Keep It Fresh And Stylish

Written by Robby Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In 2023, mens hairstyles have evolved to be more versatile and trendy. One of the most popular haircuts for men is the medium haircut with short sides. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair length manageable, yet maintain a fresh and stylish look. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this haircut, the steps involved, some tips, frequently asked questions, and how to take care of your hair after the cut.

Description

The mens medium haircut short sides is a style that features a medium length of hair on top with shorter sides and back. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways. The length on top can be styled in a messy or slicked-back look, while the sides and back can be faded or tapered. This haircut is perfect for men who want to keep their hair length manageable and versatile.

How to Achieve Mens Medium Haircut Short Sides

To achieve the mens medium haircut short sides, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with Your Barber or Stylist

Before getting any haircut, it's essential to consult with your barber or stylist. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and the style you want to achieve. Your barber or stylist will recommend the best haircut that suits your needs.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oils from your hair, making it easier to cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts. Two on the sides, one on top, and one on the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use clippers with a guard to achieve the desired length. The length of the sides and back can be faded or tapered, depending on the style you want.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Once you've finished cutting the sides and back, move on to the top. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. The length can be left longer or shorter, depending on the style you want to achieve.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it to your desired look. You can use hair products such as pomade or wax to achieve a slicked-back or messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens medium haircut short sides:

- Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type. - Use good quality hair products to maintain your hairstyle. - Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut. - Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you the most.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens medium haircut short sides:

Q. How often should I get my haircut?

A. It's recommended to get your haircut every four to six weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

Q. Can I style my hair differently after getting a mens medium haircut short sides?

A. Yes, you can style your hair in various ways after getting this haircut. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you the most.

Q. What hair products should I use for this haircut?

A. Use hair products such as pomade or wax to achieve a slicked-back or messy look.

Hair Care

After getting a mens medium haircut short sides, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons. - Use hair products that don't contain harsh chemicals. - Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

In conclusion, the mens medium haircut short sides is a versatile and trendy hairstyle that is perfect for men who want to keep their hair length manageable. Follow the steps mentioned above, and you'll be able to achieve this stylish haircut. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you the most, and take care of your hair regularly to maintain your hairstyle.
