How To Get Wavy Curls Naturally

Written by Luffy Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to get those perfect waves? Look no further! In this article, we'll show you how to get wavy curls naturally. From how to prep your hair to the best products to use, we've got you covered. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Wavy Curls Naturally
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • The Technique
  • Products to Use
  • Tips for Perfect Waves
  • FAQs

Prepping Your Hair

The key to getting natural waves is to start with clean, damp hair. Begin by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel as this can cause frizz.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to your hair. This will help define your curls and keep them from frizzing. Use a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Finally, scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage the curls to form. This will also help remove excess water from your hair.

The Technique

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to start the curling process. Begin by dividing your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. If you have thin hair, you may only need to divide your hair into two sections. If you have thick hair, you may need to divide your hair into four or more sections.

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, twist the hair away from your face. Then, wrap the twisted hair around your finger to form a small curl. Use a hair clip or bobby pin to secure the curl in place. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Once all of your hair is clipped, let it air dry. This may take several hours, so be patient! You can also use a diffuser on your hair dryer to speed up the process.

Products to Use

Using the right products can make all the difference when it comes to getting natural waves. Here are some products to try:

  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl-enhancing cream
  • Sea salt spray
  • Curl-defining gel

Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

Tips for Perfect Waves

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you try to get those perfect waves:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it dries. This can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain your waves overnight.

FAQs

Q: Can I get wavy curls naturally if my hair is straight?

A: Yes! The twisting and curling technique can work on straight hair as well. You may need to use more product to help define your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain my waves?

A: It's best to avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days and use dry shampoo in between washes if necessary.

Q: Can I use heat tools after getting natural waves?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Wavy Curls

Here are some tips to keep your wavy curls looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to get wavy curls naturally in no time. Say goodbye to heat tools and hello to beautiful, natural waves!
