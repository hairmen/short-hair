Do Lobs Suit Long Faces

Written by William Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering if a lob hairstyle is the right choice for your long face? If you are, then you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll discuss whether lobs are a suitable hairstyle for long faces, and we’ll also provide some tips on how to achieve the perfect lob look.

Description

A lob (short for long bob) is a trendy haircut that falls just above the shoulders or slightly below. It’s a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways, including straight, wavy, or curly. Lobs are a popular hairstyle choice because they’re easy to maintain and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

If you’re looking to get a lob haircut, the first step is to find a salon and a stylist that you trust. Once you’ve found a stylist, you can discuss your desired length and style, and they can advise you on whether a lob would suit your face shape.

When it comes to styling a lob, there are several options. You can wear it straight and sleek, add some loose waves for a beachy vibe, or curl it for a glamorous look. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

Step by Step

If you’ve already decided that a lob is the right hairstyle for you, here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect look:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Section your hair and use a curling iron to add loose waves or curls.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when considering a lob for a long face:

  • A lob that falls just below the chin can help balance out a long face.
  • Adding layers can add volume and movement to a lob, making it a great choice for long faces.
  • Opt for a side part rather than a middle part to help create the illusion of width.
  • Consider adding bangs to soften the look and draw attention to your eyes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about lobs for long faces:

Will a lob make my face look longer?

Not necessarily. A lob that falls just below the chin can actually help balance out a long face and make it appear shorter.

What’s the best way to style a lob?

The best way to style a lob depends on your personal preference and face shape. Some options include straight and sleek, loose waves, or curls.

Can lobs be customized for different face shapes?

Yes, lobs can be customized to suit different face shapes. A skilled stylist can advise you on the best length and style for your face shape.

Hair Care

Whether you have a lob or any other hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, lobs can be a great hairstyle choice for long faces. With the right length, style, and care, a lob can help balance out a long face and highlight your best features. If you’re considering a lob, be sure to consult with a skilled stylist who can advise you on the best style for your face shape.
