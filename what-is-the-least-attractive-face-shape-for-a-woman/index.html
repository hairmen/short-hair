What Is The Least Attractive Face Shape For A Woman

Written by Christine Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a woman, you may have wondered what the most attractive face shape is. However, it's also important to know what the least attractive face shape is so you can avoid it. In this blog post, we'll discuss what the least attractive face shape for a woman is, why it's considered unattractive, and what you can do if you have this face shape.

Description

The least attractive face shape for a woman is the rectangular shape. This type of face shape is characterized by a high forehead, strong jawline, and a long, straight chin. It's also common for rectangular face shapes to have angular features, such as sharp cheekbones and a pointed nose. Women with rectangular face shapes tend to have a more masculine appearance, which is why it's considered unattractive.

How to Identify Rectangular Face Shape

If you're not sure whether you have a rectangular face shape, there are a few key features to look out for. Firstly, your face will be longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead and a strong jawline. Your cheekbones will be high and angular, and your chin will be long and pointed. You may also have a prominent nose and a narrow jawline.

Step by Step Guide

If you're still unsure whether you have a rectangular face shape, here's a step-by-step guide to help you identify it:

  1. Measure the length and width of your face using a tape measure.
  2. If your face is longer than it is wide, you may have a rectangular face shape.
  3. Look at your forehead – if it's high and prominent, this is another sign of a rectangular face shape.
  4. Check your jawline – if it's strong and angular, this is another feature of a rectangular face shape.
  5. Finally, look at your chin – if it's long and pointed, this is another indicator of a rectangular face shape.

Tips for Rectangular Face Shape

If you have a rectangular face shape, there are a few things you can do to make it look more attractive. Here are some tips:

  • Choose hairstyles that soften your features, such as loose waves or curls.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer, such as slicked-back ponytails or buns.
  • Wear makeup that emphasizes your eyes or lips, rather than your cheekbones.
  • Avoid contouring your cheekbones, as this can make your face look even more angular.
  • Wear glasses with rounded frames to soften your features.

FAQ

Q: Is a rectangular face shape the same as a square face shape?

A: No, a rectangular face shape is longer than it is wide, whereas a square face shape is equal in length and width.

Q: Can I still be attractive if I have a rectangular face shape?

A: Absolutely! Attractiveness is subjective, and there are many ways to enhance your features and highlight your natural beauty.

Q: How can I make my rectangular face shape look more feminine?

A: You can soften your features with hairstyles, makeup, and accessories. Experiment with different looks to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Rectangular Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle is crucial for women with rectangular face shapes. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered bob or pixie cut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Experiment with bangs to soften your features and add dimension to your face.
  • Use volumizing products to add body to your hair, and avoid heavy products that can weigh it down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Overall, if you have a rectangular face shape, don't worry – there are plenty of ways to enhance your features and look your best. By following these tips, you can embrace your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
