How Long Do Crochet Braids Last

Written by Aprilia Aug 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're considering getting crochet braids, you may be wondering how long they last. Crochet braids are a protective hairstyle that involves braiding your natural hair and then attaching extensions to the braids with a crochet hook. This technique creates a natural-looking, low-maintenance hairstyle that can last for weeks or even months. In this article, we'll discuss how long crochet braids last and provide some tips for making them last as long as possible.

How To Install Crochet Braids

Before we get into how long crochet braids last, let's first talk about how to install them. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any product buildup and prepare your hair for braiding.

Step 2: Braid Your Hair

Next, braid your hair into cornrows. Make sure the braids are tight and close to your scalp to ensure a secure base for the extensions.

Step 3: Attach the Extensions

Using a crochet hook, attach the extensions to the braids. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown of your head. Make sure to knot the extensions securely to prevent them from slipping or falling out.

Step 4: Trim and Style

Once all the extensions are attached, trim them to your desired length and style as desired. You can curl or straighten the extensions to create different looks.

Tips for Making Crochet Braids Last Longer

Now that you know how to install crochet braids, let's talk about how to make them last as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin bonnet or scarf.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down the extensions.
  • Try not to scratch your scalp, as this can cause the braids to loosen or fall out.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp clean and healthy.
  • Don't leave the braids in for too long. While crochet braids can last for weeks or even months, it's important to take them out and give your hair a break every so often.

FAQ

How long do crochet braids last?

Crochet braids can last for several weeks to several months, depending on how well you take care of them.

Can you wash crochet braids?

Yes, you can wash crochet braids. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp clean and healthy.

Can you swim with crochet braids?

Yes, you can swim with crochet braids, but it's important to protect them by wearing a swim cap or tying them up in a bun.

Hair Care for Crochet Braids

While crochet braids are a low-maintenance hairstyle, it's still important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Keep your scalp clean and healthy by washing it regularly.
  • Moisturize your hair and scalp with a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin bonnet or scarf.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down the extensions.
  • Don't leave the braids in for too long. While crochet braids can last for weeks or even months, it's important to take them out and give your hair a break every so often.

With proper care and maintenance, crochet braids can last for weeks or even months. Follow these tips and you'll be rocking your crochet braids for as long as possible!
