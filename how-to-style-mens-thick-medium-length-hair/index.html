How To Style Men s Thick Medium Length Hair

Written by Bardi Sep 10, 2023 · 3 min read

Thick medium length hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. On one hand, it’s versatile and easy to style, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. In this article, we’ll show you how to style men’s thick medium length hair so that you can look your best every day.

Description

Thick medium length hair is hair that falls between the jaw and the shoulders and is dense and voluminous. This hair type can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques and products, it can look sleek and polished.

How to Style Men’s Thick Medium Length Hair

To style men’s thick medium length hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to styling men’s thick medium length hair is to wash and condition it regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and make sure to rinse thoroughly. This will help your hair stay healthy and manageable.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it until it’s damp. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. For thick medium length hair, a pomade or wax is a good choice. Rub a small amount of product between your hands and apply evenly to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to style it. Use a low heat setting and avoid directing the heat directly onto your hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your style with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your hair stay in place and prevent frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style men’s thick medium length hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to try new styles and techniques.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick medium length hair?

The best haircut for thick medium length hair is one that adds texture and movement to your hair. A layered haircut or a fade can help to thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

How often should I wash my thick medium length hair?

You should wash your thick medium length hair every two to three days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage.

Hair Care

To keep your men’s thick medium length hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily style men’s thick medium length hair and look your best every day.
