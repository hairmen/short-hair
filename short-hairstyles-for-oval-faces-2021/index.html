Short Hairstyles For Oval Faces 2021

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Oval faces are considered to be the most versatile among all face shapes. They have balanced features, with a wider forehead and a slightly curved jawline. Short hairstyles for oval faces 2021 are perfect for those who want to enhance their facial features and show off their beautiful cheekbones. In this article, we will show you some of the best short hairstyles for oval faces, along with tips and tricks to maintain them. Let's get started!

Description

If you have an oval face, you can experiment with a range of short hairstyles, from pixie cuts to bobs. The key is to choose a style that complements your facial features and highlights your best attributes. Short hairstyles for oval faces can be playful, edgy, or sophisticated, depending on your personal style. They are also easy to maintain and can save you a lot of time and money on hair care.

How to

Before choosing a short hairstyle for your oval face, it's important to consider your hair type, texture, and density. Short hairstyles work best on straight or wavy hair, but can also be adapted for curly hair. Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for your oval face:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are equal and your face has an oval shape, then you have an oval face.

Step 2: Research short hairstyles

Look for short hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair type. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by consulting with a hairstylist.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you choose the best short hairstyle for your face shape and provide advice on how to maintain it. They can also recommend hair products and styling tools to use at home.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and maintaining short hairstyles for oval faces:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features and highlights your best attributes.
  • Consider your hair type, texture, and density when choosing a short hairstyle.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling wand.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for oval faces:

Q: What are the best short hairstyles for oval faces?

A: Some of the best short hairstyles for oval faces include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered cuts.

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle for an oval face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a short hairstyle for an oval face. Side-swept bangs or blunt bangs can add dimension to your face and complement your facial features.

Q: Do I need to use hair products to maintain a short hairstyle for an oval face?

A: Yes, you may need to use hair products to maintain a short hairstyle for an oval face. Depending on your hair type and texture, you may need to use a volumizing mousse, a texturizing spray, or a smoothing serum to achieve your desired look.

Hair Care

Short hairstyles for oval faces require minimal hair care, but it's still important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face and keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
