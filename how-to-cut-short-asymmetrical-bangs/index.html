How To Cut Short Asymmetrical Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short asymmetrical bangs are a trendy and chic hairstyle that can transform your look. These bangs are shorter on one side and longer on the other, creating an edgy and modern feel. If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, cutting short asymmetrical bangs could be the perfect choice for you. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut short asymmetrical bangs at home.

How to Cut Short Asymmetrical Bangs?

To cut short asymmetrical bangs, you'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before cutting your hair, it's essential to wash and dry it. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut. Once your hair is clean and dry, comb it through to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Bangs

Decide on the length of your bangs by using the comb to section off the hair you want to cut. Typically, asymmetrical bangs are shorter on one side, so decide which side you want to be shorter. Use the comb to create a horizontal parting across your forehead, where you want your bangs to fall.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Take the hair in front of the parting and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and slide your fingers down to the length you want to cut. Use the hair cutting scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Repeat this process on the other side, making sure to cut the hair to the same length.

Step 4: Check the Length

Use the mirror to check the length of your bangs. If they're too long, you can always trim them again, but it's better to start with a longer length and work your way down.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Once you're happy with the length of your bangs, style them using a blow dryer or flat iron. For a messy look, use your fingers to tousle the hair, or for a sleek look, use a comb to create a defined parting.

Tips for Cutting Short Asymmetrical Bangs

Here are some tips to help you cut short asymmetrical bangs like a pro:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with a longer length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Try to keep the length of your bangs even on both sides.

FAQs

Can I cut my bangs myself?

Yes, you can cut your bangs yourself, but it's best to take your time and follow the steps carefully. If you're not confident cutting your hair, it's always best to go to a professional stylist.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?

If you make a mistake while cutting your bangs, don't panic. You can always trim them again or go to a professional stylist to fix them.

Hair Care for Short Asymmetrical Bangs

Once you've cut your short asymmetrical bangs, it's essential to take care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Cutting short asymmetrical bangs is a great way to switch up your hairstyle and create a trendy look. By following these steps and tips, you can cut your bangs at home with ease. Remember to take care of your hair and keep it healthy to maintain your new look. Happy cutting!
