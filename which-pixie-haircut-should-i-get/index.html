Which Pixie Haircut Should I Get A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering getting a pixie haircut, you're not alone. Pixie haircuts have been a popular choice for women for years, and they continue to be a trendy and stylish option. However, with so many different types of pixie haircuts available, it can be tough to decide which one is right for you. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the most popular pixie haircuts and help you determine which one is best for your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short, cropped hairstyle that typically features short sides and a longer top. Pixie haircuts can be tailored to suit a variety of face shapes and hair types, making them a versatile option for women of all ages. Pixie haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured, making them a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that can still be customized to suit their unique style.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Haircut

When choosing a pixie haircut, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. Certain pixie haircuts may be more flattering on certain face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that complements your features. Second, consider your hair type. Pixie haircuts can work well on a variety of hair types, but some styles may be better suited for certain textures or thicknesses. Finally, think about your personal style. Pixie haircuts can be customized to suit a variety of styles, from edgy and bold to classic and sophisticated.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Pixie Haircut

Step 1: Determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and identify your face shape. Common face shapes include round, square, heart-shaped, and oval.

Step 2: Consider your hair type. Determine whether your hair is thick or thin, curly or straight, and whether it has any unique features like cowlicks or waves.

Step 3: Research different pixie haircuts. Look online or in magazines for inspiration, and take note of which styles catch your eye.

Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine which pixie haircut will work best for your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut

Once you've chosen a pixie haircut, it's important to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are a few tips for maintaining your new 'do:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking sharp.
  • Invest in quality hair products designed for short hair, like texturizing sprays and pomades.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as tousled and messy or sleek and polished.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your haircut.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a pixie haircut work for my face shape?

A: Pixie haircuts can be customized to suit a variety of face shapes, so it's likely that there's a pixie haircut that will work for you. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which style will be most flattering for your features.

Q: Will a pixie haircut work for my hair type?

A: Pixie haircuts can work well on a variety of hair types, from thick and curly to fine and straight. However, some styles may be better suited for certain textures or thicknesses. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which style will work best for your hair type.

Q: Will a pixie haircut require a lot of maintenance?

A: Pixie haircuts can be low-maintenance, but they do require regular trims to keep them looking sharp. You may also need to invest in quality hair products designed for short hair to keep your haircut looking its best.

Hair Care for Pixie Haircuts

When it comes to caring for your pixie haircut, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, be gentle when washing and styling your hair to avoid damaging it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners too often. Second, protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside in harsh weather. Finally, get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing the right pixie haircut can be a challenge, but with the right guidance, you can find a style that works for you. Consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a pixie haircut, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look. With the right care and maintenance, your pixie haircut will be a stylish and low-maintenance option for years to come.
