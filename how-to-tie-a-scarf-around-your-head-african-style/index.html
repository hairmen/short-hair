How To Tie A Scarf Around Your Head African Style

Written by Christine Jun 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a way to switch things up? Look no further than the African style of tying a scarf around your head. Not only is it a stylish and versatile look, but it can also protect your hair from sun damage and breakage. In this article, we will show you how to tie a scarf around your head African style, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step to tying a scarf around your head African style is to choose the right scarf. Look for a scarf that is long and wide enough to cover your head comfortably. You can use any material, but cotton or silk scarves are the most comfortable and breathable.

Next, decide on the style you want to achieve. There are several ways to tie a scarf around your head African style, including the turban, the knot, and the wrap. We will show you how to do the turban style in this article.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to tie a scarf around your head African style:

  1. Start by folding the scarf in half diagonally to create a triangle shape.
  2. Place the folded edge of the scarf at the nape of your neck.
  3. Take the two ends of the scarf and tie them at the top of your head, making sure that the knot is centered.
  4. Take the two corners of the scarf and cross them at the nape of your neck.
  5. Bring the corners of the scarf up to the top of your head and tie them into a knot.
  6. Tuck any loose ends of the scarf into the knot to secure it.

And there you have it - a stylish and comfortable African-style scarf headwrap!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when tying a scarf around your head African style:

  • Experiment with different scarf sizes, colors, and patterns to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't tie the scarf too tightly - you want to be comfortable and avoid headaches.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose ends of the scarf.
  • Pair your scarf headwrap with statement earrings or a bold lip color for an extra pop of style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about tying a scarf around your head African style:

Is it cultural appropriation to wear a scarf around your head African style?

No, it is not cultural appropriation to wear a scarf around your head African style. Scarf headwraps have been worn by women of many cultures and countries throughout history.

Can I wear a scarf headwrap with any outfit?

Yes, a scarf headwrap can be worn with any outfit. It is a versatile accessory that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How do I care for my hair when wearing a scarf headwrap?

Hair Care

When wearing a scarf headwrap, it is important to protect your hair from breakage and sun damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your hair before tying the scarf to prevent dryness.
  • Use a satin or silk scarf to avoid friction and breakage.
  • Avoid tying the scarf too tightly to prevent hair loss.
  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.

Now that you know how to tie a scarf around your head African style, try it out for yourself and embrace this stylish and versatile look!
