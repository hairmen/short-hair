Does Bleached Hair Fall Out More

Written by Petter Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this day and age, we are all about coloring our hair in the wildest colors and experimenting with new hairstyles. One of the most popular hair trends of the last few years has been bleaching your hair. However, many people are worried that their hair will start to fall out more frequently after they bleach it. In this article, we'll explore this topic in detail and discuss whether bleached hair falls out more.

How to Prevent Hair Fall After Bleaching

If you're worried about hair fall after bleaching, there are a few things you can do to prevent it. Firstly, you should avoid over-bleaching your hair. Over-bleaching can damage your hair and cause it to fall out. Secondly, you should make sure to use a good quality hair conditioner after you bleach your hair. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from falling out. Lastly, you should avoid using hot styling tools on your hair for a few weeks after you bleach it. Hot styling tools can further damage your hair and cause it to fall out.

Step-by-Step Bleaching Process

The bleaching process is a multi-step process that involves the use of chemicals to lighten your hair. The first step is to mix the bleach powder and developer together. You should then apply the mixture to your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time. Once your hair has lightened to the desired shade, you should rinse the bleach out of your hair and apply a good quality hair conditioner.

Tips to Keep Your Hair Healthy After Bleaching

If you want to keep your hair healthy after bleaching, there are a few things you can do. Firstly, you should avoid washing your hair too often. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Secondly, you should avoid using hot styling tools on your hair too often. Hot styling tools can damage your hair and cause it to break. Lastly, you should make sure to use a good quality hair conditioner every time you wash your hair. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from falling out.

FAQ

Q: Does bleached hair fall out more?

A: Bleached hair can fall out more if it is not taken care of properly. Bleaching your hair can damage it, making it more prone to breakage and hair fall.

Q: Can I bleach my hair if I have thin hair?

A: If you have thin hair, you should be cautious when bleaching it. Bleaching your hair can cause it to become weaker and more prone to breakage, which can lead to hair fall. It's best to consult with a professional stylist before bleaching your hair.

Q: How often should I bleach my hair?

A: You should avoid bleaching your hair too often. Over-bleaching can damage your hair and cause it to fall out. It's best to wait a few weeks between bleaching sessions to allow your hair to recover.

Hair Care

If you want to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from falling out, there are a few things you can do. Firstly, you should avoid using hot styling tools on your hair too often. Hot styling tools can damage your hair and cause it to break. Secondly, you should avoid washing your hair too often. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Lastly, you should make sure to use a good quality hair conditioner every time you wash your hair. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from falling out.

In conclusion, bleached hair can fall out more if it is not taken care of properly. However, if you take the necessary precautions and take good care of your hair, you can prevent it from falling out and keep it healthy and beautiful.
