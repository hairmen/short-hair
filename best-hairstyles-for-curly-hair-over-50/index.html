Best Hairstyles For Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Eveline Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 50 and have curly hair, you might be struggling to find a hairstyle that suits you. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for curly hair over 50. From short to long, we have something for everyone. So, let's get started.

Description

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's beautiful and unique, it can also be difficult to manage. As we age, our hair changes, and it can become even more challenging to style. However, there are plenty of hairstyles for curly hair over 50 that are easy to maintain and stylish.

How to

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for curly hair over 50 is to work with your natural texture. Embrace your curls and choose a cut that complements them. Shorter haircuts are often easier to manage and can give you a more youthful look. However, if you prefer longer hair, there are plenty of styles that work well with curly hair.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are a popular choice for women over 50 with curly hair. They are low maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways. A layered bob is a great option for those who want a chic and modern look. This style works well with curly hair and can be styled straight or with natural curls.

If you prefer a shorter cut, a pixie haircut is a great option. This style is youthful and edgy, and it works well with curly hair. A curly pixie cut can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy and tousled to sleek and polished.

Long Haircuts

If you prefer longer hair, there are plenty of styles that work well with curly hair. A long layered haircut is a great option for those who want a more romantic and feminine look. This style works well with curly hair because it adds movement and dimension.

If you want a more bohemian look, consider a long curly shag. This style is perfect for those who want a carefree and relaxed look. It's also easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your curly hair:

  • Start with clean, damp hair
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product
  • Divide your hair into sections
  • Twist each section of hair, wrapping it around your finger
  • Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry it
  • Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate the curls
  • Finish with a hairspray or other styling product

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet

FAQ

What is the best haircut for curly hair over 50?

The best haircut for curly hair over 50 depends on your personal style and preferences. Short haircuts, such as a layered bob or pixie cut, are often easier to manage and can give you a more youthful look. However, if you prefer longer hair, a long layered haircut or curly shag can be a great option.

How do you style curly hair?

To style curly hair, start with clean, damp hair and apply a curl-enhancing product. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section around your finger. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry it. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and finish with a hairspray or other styling product.

What are some tips for maintaining curly hair?

Some tips for maintaining curly hair include using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, applying a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized, avoiding brushing your hair when it's dry, and using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for curly hair over 50, it's important to use products that are designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are formulated for curly hair. These products will help to keep your curls moisturized and defined.

In addition to using the right products, it's important to develop a hair care routine that works for you. This might include washing your hair less frequently, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can rock beautiful and stylish curly hair over 50.
