How To Style Thick Wavy Hair Men Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know the struggle of trying to style it. It can be difficult to find the right products and techniques to tame your locks. But fear not, we have some tips and tricks to help you style your hair with ease.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it has a natural texture and volume that many people envy. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. However, with the right products and techniques, you can make the most of your hair type and look great every day.

How to

Step 1: Find the Right Products

The first step in styling your thick wavy hair is to find the right products. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as thickening or volumizing sprays, mousse, or styling creams. These will help to enhance your natural texture and provide hold without weighing your hair down.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Properly

Proper washing and conditioning is crucial for maintaining healthy hair and making it easier to style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid buildup.

Step 3: Towel Dry Gently

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently to avoid damaging your locks. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, gently blot your hair with a towel and let it air dry as much as possible before styling.

Step 4: Use the Right Tools

When styling your hair, choose the right tools for the job. For thick wavy hair, a wide-tooth comb is ideal for detangling and distributing product evenly. A diffuser attachment for your hair dryer can also help to enhance your natural texture and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Experiment with Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Some popular styles for thick wavy hair include the messy look, the slicked-back look, and the textured crop. Play around with different products and techniques to find the style that suits you best.

Tips

  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style in the long run.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Embrace your natural texture and use it to your advantage when styling your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you may need to wash it more often. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my thick wavy hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid using heat tools too often and use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. Use a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to keep your hair smooth and hydrated, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care

Taking care of your thick wavy hair is important for maintaining its health and making it easier to style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid buildup. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair, and trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your thick wavy hair with ease and look great every day.
