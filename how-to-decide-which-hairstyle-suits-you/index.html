How To Decide Which Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Christine Aug 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle, it can often be overwhelming with the sheer number of options available. With so many shapes, lengths, and colors to choose from, it can be challenging to decide which one will best suit your face shape, lifestyle, and personality. In this guide, we'll go through how to decide which hairstyle suits you, step by step, to ensure that you find the perfect look for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in deciding which hairstyle suits you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Then, use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to consider your lifestyle. Are you someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, or do you prefer a low-maintenance look? Do you work in a professional setting that requires a more conservative hairstyle, or do you have more freedom to experiment?

Next, think about your personality. Are you someone who likes to stand out from the crowd, or do you prefer a more classic look? Do you enjoy experimenting with new trends, or do you prefer to stick with what you know works for you?

Step by step

1. Determine your face shape
2. Consider your lifestyle
3. Think about your personality
4. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape, lifestyle, and personality
5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion
6. Choose a hairstyle that you love and that makes you feel confident and beautiful

Tips

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you
- Take your hair texture into consideration when choosing a hairstyle
- Look for inspiration in magazines, online, and on social media
- Remember that hair grows back, so don't be afraid to try something new
- Take care of your hair by using quality products and getting regular trims

FAQ

Q: What if I don't know my face shape?
A: There are many online resources that can help you determine your face shape, or you can consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will suit my lifestyle?
A: Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair each day, as well as any restrictions your job or other commitments may have on your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use quality hair products that are suited to your hair type, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends. Additionally, be sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to choose a hairstyle that suits you perfectly and leaves you feeling confident and beautiful.
