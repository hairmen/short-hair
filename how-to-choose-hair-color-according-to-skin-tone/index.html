How To Choose Hair Color According To Skin Tone A Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Jun 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure which shade will complement your skin tone. With so many colors to choose from, it’s easy to get overwhelmed. But fear not, this guide will help you determine the best hair color according to your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose Hair Color According to Skin Tone
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs

Description

The right hair color can enhance your natural features and make you feel confident. But, choosing the right hair color can be tricky, especially if you’re not sure what shade will complement your skin tone. Your skin tone is an important factor to consider when choosing a hair color. It can make all the difference in how your hair looks against your skin.

How to Choose Hair Color According to Skin Tone

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone is all about balance. You want to find a shade that complements your skin tone, enhances your natural features, and brings out your best self. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color based on your skin tone:

Fair Skin

If you have fair skin with pink undertones, you’ll want to choose a hair color that doesn’t wash you out. Avoid colors that are too dark or too warm. Instead, opt for cool-toned shades like platinum blonde, ash blonde, or light brown.

Medium Skin

For those with medium skin tones, you have more options when it comes to hair color. You can go for warm, cool, or neutral shades. Some great options include caramel highlights, honey blonde, or a rich chestnut brown.

Dark Skin

If you have dark skin, you’ll want to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone without being too harsh. Avoid colors that are too light or too cool. Instead, opt for warm, rich shades like dark brown, burgundy, or auburn.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know which hair colors work best for your skin tone, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right shade:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.
  2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone based on the tips above.
  3. Consider your natural hair color and how much maintenance you want to do. If you have naturally dark hair, going platinum blonde will require more upkeep.
  4. Consult with a professional stylist to ensure you choose the best hair color for your skin tone and hair type.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Use hair color swatches to help you visualize how different shades will look against your skin tone.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for advice from friends or family members.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you’re willing to do.
  • Remember that hair color can fade over time, so be prepared for touch-ups.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a different color than what’s recommended for my skin tone?

A: Of course! These tips are simply guidelines to help you choose a hair color that will complement your skin tone. Ultimately, the decision is up to you.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. Typically, you’ll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I color my hair at home?

A: Yes, but it’s important to follow the instructions carefully and choose the right shade for your skin tone. For best results, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist.

Hair Care After Coloring

After coloring your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid hot tools as much as possible to prevent further damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can choose the right hair color for your skin tone and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Happy coloring!
