How To Get Volume With Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your short hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you struggle to get the volume and texture you crave? Look no further! In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about how to get volume with short hair. From styling tips to product recommendations, we’ve got you covered. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Volume with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Volume
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Get Volume with Short Hair

If you’re wondering how to get volume with short hair, the answer is simple: use the right products and styling techniques. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect voluminous look:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

The right shampoo and conditioner can make all the difference when it comes to adding volume to short hair. Look for products that specifically target volume and texture, and avoid heavy formulas that can weigh your hair down. Some great options include:

  • OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Batiste Dry Shampoo
  • Living Proof Full Shampoo and Conditioner

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down

One of the easiest ways to add volume to short hair is to blow-dry your hair upside down. This technique helps to lift your roots and create a fuller, more voluminous look. Simply flip your head over and blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of your natural part.

3. Use a volumizing mousse or spray

Another great way to add volume to short hair is to use a volumizing mousse or spray. These products work by adding texture and lift to your hair, without weighing it down. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers, then style as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to add some volume to your short hair? Follow these simple steps:

1. Wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add texture and lift to your hair, setting the stage for voluminous styling.

2. Towel-dry your hair

Gently towel-dry your hair, making sure to remove excess water without rubbing too harshly. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.

3. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers. This will help to add texture and lift to your hair, creating the perfect base for voluminous styling.

4. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Flip your head over and blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of your natural part. This will help to lift your roots and create a fuller, more voluminous look.

5. Style as desired

Once your hair is dry, style it as desired. You can use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls, or simply use your fingers to tousle your hair for a messy, textured look.

Tips for Maintaining Volume

Now that you’ve got the perfect voluminous look, you’ll want to make sure it lasts all day. Here are some tips for maintaining volume in short hair:

  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair throughout the day
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can flatten your style
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down every time you wash it

FAQs

Q: Can I add volume to short hair without heat styling?

Yes! You can add volume to short hair without heat styling by using a volumizing mousse or spray, and blow-drying your hair upside down.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair to maintain volume?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people may need to wash their hair every day to maintain volume, while others can go a few days between washes. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Maintaining healthy, voluminous hair starts with a good hair care routine. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to prevent breakage
  • Use a heat protectant when heat styling your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect voluminous look for your short hair. Whether you’re heading to work or out on the town, you’ll feel confident and stylish with your new and improved hair. Happy styling!
