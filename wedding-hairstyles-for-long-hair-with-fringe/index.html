Wedding Hairstyles For Long Hair With Fringe

Written by Robby Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair with fringe can be a beautiful and elegant choice for a wedding hairstyle. Whether you're the bride, bridesmaid, or a guest, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll explore some of the best wedding hairstyles for long hair with fringe, as well as tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.

Description

Long hair with fringe has been a popular hairstyle for several years now, and it's no surprise that it's a popular choice for weddings. This hairstyle can be worn in a variety of ways, from loose waves to sleek updos. The fringe adds an extra touch of elegance, framing the face and adding dimension to the overall look.

How to

There are several ways to style long hair with fringe for a wedding. Here are some options:

Loose Waves

For a relaxed and romantic look, try loose waves. Start by applying a heat protectant to your hair, then use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Sleek Updo

If you're looking for a more formal look, try a sleek updo. Start by straightening your hair, then pull it back into a low bun or chignon. Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure the style, and finish with a strong hold hairspray.

Half Up, Half Down

If you can't decide between an updo and wearing your hair down, try a half up, half down style. Pull the top half of your hair back into a ponytail or bun, leaving the rest of your hair down. This is a great option for those with long, thick hair.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating a wedding hairstyle for long hair with fringe:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Choose your desired style, whether it's loose waves, a sleek updo, or a half up, half down style. Use the appropriate styling tools and products to create your chosen look.

Step 3: Add Accessories

To add some extra elegance to your wedding hairstyle, consider adding accessories such as a tiara, veil, or hairpins. These can add a touch of sparkle and glamour to your overall look.

Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your long hair with fringe looking its best:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain its overall health.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wedding hairstyles for long hair with fringe:

What wedding hairstyles are popular for long hair with fringe?

Some popular wedding hairstyles for long hair with fringe include loose waves, sleek updos, and half up, half down styles.

How do I keep my hair healthy for my wedding day?

To keep your hair healthy for your wedding day, use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, apply a heat protectant before styling, and regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week as well.

Should I add accessories to my wedding hairstyle?

Adding accessories such as a tiara, veil, or hairpins can add a touch of elegance and glamour to your wedding hairstyle. Consider what would complement your overall look and wedding theme.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining healthy long hair with fringe, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, avoid using too much heat on your hair, and regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking its best. By taking good care of your hair, you'll be able to create beautiful and elegant wedding hairstyles for years to come.
