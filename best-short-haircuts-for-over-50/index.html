Best Short Haircuts For Over 50 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Luffy Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, our hair begins to change, and we may find ourselves looking for a new haircut that flatters our face shape and embraces our natural texture. If you're over 50 and considering a short haircut, you're in luck! There are plenty of stylish and low-maintenance options to choose from. In this guide, we'll explore the best short haircuts for over 50, including how to style them, tips for maintenance, and more.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless and versatile short haircut that can be styled in many different ways. For women over 50, a layered pixie cut can help to add volume and texture to thinning hair. This style is also low-maintenance and easy to style with just a bit of product and a blow dryer. To add some edge to your pixie cut, consider adding a side-swept fringe or choppy layers around the crown.

The Bob

A classic bob is another great short haircut option for women over 50. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For example, a chin-length bob with layers can help to add volume to fine hair, while a blunt bob can create a sleek and sophisticated look. To add some interest to your bob, consider adding some subtle highlights or lowlights.

The Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that can add volume and texture to any hair type. For women over 50, a shag with choppy layers around the crown can help to add volume to thinning hair. This style can also be customized with bangs or face-framing layers to flatter your face shape. To style a shag, simply add some texturizing product and blow dry with your fingers to create a messy and lived-in look.

The Fauxhawk

If you're looking for a more edgy and daring short haircut, the fauxhawk may be the perfect option. This style features longer layers on top that can be styled into a fauxhawk or messy spiky look. For women over 50, a shorter fauxhawk with choppy layers can help to add volume and texture to fine hair. To style a fauxhawk, simply add some texturizing product and use your fingers to create the desired shape.

Maintenance Tips

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. To keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish, consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks. This can help to prevent split ends and keep your layers looking sharp. Additionally, be sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily! A well-chosen short haircut can actually make you look more youthful and vibrant. By embracing your natural texture and choosing a style that flatters your face shape, you can create a look that is both stylish and age-appropriate.

Q: What if I don't like my short haircut?

A: If you're not happy with your short haircut, don't panic! Talk to your stylist about what changes you would like to make, and they can work with you to create a new look that you love. Additionally, remember that hair grows back, so even if you're not happy with your new haircut, it won't be permanent.

Hair Care Tips

Short haircuts require a bit of extra care to keep them looking their best. To care for your short haircut, be sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. Additionally, avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. Finally, be sure to use a texturizing product to add volume and texture to your hair, and consider getting regular trims to keep your layers looking sharp.

Overall, there are many stylish and low-maintenance short haircuts for women over 50 to choose from. By choosing a style that flatters your face shape and embracing your natural texture, you can create a look that is both chic and age-appropriate. Remember to take good care of your hair, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you.
