Best Haircut For Rectangle Face Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are a male with a rectangular face shape, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. A rectangular face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and forehead, with the length of the face being longer than the width. In order to balance out the features of a rectangular face shape, it is important to choose a haircut that complements your unique features. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for rectangle face males and provide tips and tricks for maintaining your new look.

Description

When it comes to choosing a haircut for a rectangle face male, it is important to consider the shape of the face and the individual's personal style. A good haircut will enhance the facial features and create balance, while also fitting with the individual's lifestyle and preferences.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for a Rectangle Face Male

When choosing the best haircut for a rectangle face male, there are several factors to consider:

Face Shape

The first thing to consider when choosing a haircut is the shape of the face. A rectangular face shape requires a haircut that will add width to the face and reduce the appearance of length. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they will only accentuate the length of the face.

Hair Texture

The texture of the hair is also an important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you have thick, coarse hair, a shorter haircut may be more suitable. If you have fine hair, a longer haircut may be better to add volume and texture.

Personal Style

Finally, it is important to choose a haircut that fits with your personal style and lifestyle. Consider whether you want a low maintenance haircut or a more styled look, and whether you prefer a more classic or trendy style.

Best Haircuts for Rectangle Face Males

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, here are some of the best haircuts for rectangle face males:

1. Short and Textured

A short, textured haircut can add width to the face and reduce the appearance of length. This type of haircut is low maintenance and can be styled with a small amount of product for a messy, textured look.

2. Side Part

A side part can create the illusion of a wider face and can be styled with a variety of lengths and textures. This type of haircut is versatile and can be worn in a more classic or trendy style.

3. Buzz Cut

A buzz cut can be a great option for rectangle face males with thick, coarse hair. This type of haircut is low maintenance and can create a more masculine look.

4. Medium Length

A medium length haircut can add volume and texture to fine hair, while also creating a more balanced look for rectangle face males. This type of haircut can be styled in a variety of ways, from a messy, textured look to a more polished, classic style.

Step by Step Guide to Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you have chosen the best haircut for your rectangle face, it is important to maintain the look with proper hair care. Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

1. Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type and texture.

2. Use Product Sparingly

When styling your hair, use product sparingly to avoid weighing down the hair or creating a greasy look. Use a small amount of product and work it through the hair evenly.

3. Visit the Barber Regularly

Visit the barber regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh. Depending on the length and style of your hair, you may need to visit the barber every 4-6 weeks.

Tips for Choosing the Right Barber

Choosing the right barber is important for maintaining your new haircut. Here are some tips for choosing the right barber:

1. Look for Reviews and Recommendations

Read reviews and ask for recommendations from friends or family members to find a barber with a good reputation.

2. Consider Location and Convenience

Choose a barber that is conveniently located and easy to access, whether it is near your home or workplace.

3. Check for Certifications and Training

Look for a barber with certifications and training in the latest techniques and styles to ensure that you are receiving the best possible haircut.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a rectangle face male?

The best haircut for a rectangle face male is one that adds width to the face and creates balance. Short, textured haircuts and side parts are great options for rectangle face males.

How often should I visit the barber?

Depending on the length and style of your hair, you should visit the barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.

What should I look for in a barber?

When choosing a barber, look for reviews and recommendations, consider location and convenience, and check for certifications and training.

Hair Care Tips for Rectangle Face Males

Here are some general hair care tips for rectangle face males:

1. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons, to prevent damage and breakage.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss.



Overall, choosing the best haircut for a rectangle face male requires careful consideration of the individual's face shape, hair texture, and personal style. By following these tips and tricks, you can find a haircut that complements your unique features and creates balance. Remember to maintain your new haircut with proper hair care and regular visits to the barber, and you will have a stylish and confident look that is sure to turn heads.
