What Hair Parting Suits A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Aprilia Aug 31, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hair parting that suits your heart-shaped face? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on what hair parting suits a heart-shaped face, and how to rock it with confidence.

Table of Contents

In this article, we will cover the following:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of choosing the right hair parting is to create balance and harmony in your facial features. A good hair parting can help soften your sharp angles and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

How to

The best hair parting for a heart-shaped face is a side parting. This parting helps balance the width of your forehead and the narrowness of your chin, creating a more balanced and proportional appearance. A side parting also helps soften the angles of your face, making it appear more feminine and delicate.

Step by Step

Here’s how to create a side parting:

  1. Start by wetting your hair and combing it straight back.
  2. Use the end of a comb to create a straight line from the arch of your eyebrow to the crown of your head.
  3. Part your hair along the line using the end of the comb.
  4. Using your fingers or a comb, gently brush the hair on either side of the parting, creating a smooth and natural look.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the parting in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you rock your side parting:

  • If you have long hair, try creating loose waves or curls to add texture and movement to your hair.
  • If you have short hair, try a messy or textured style to add volume and dimension.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add interest and variety to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair parting for a heart-shaped face:

What other hair partings can I try?

You can also try a middle parting or a diagonal parting. However, be careful with a middle parting as it can make your forehead appear even wider and your chin even narrower.

Can I part my hair on the opposite side?

Yes, you can part your hair on the opposite side if you prefer. However, keep in mind that it may make your face appear imbalanced and draw attention to your forehead.

Do I need to change my hair parting if I change my hairstyle?

Not necessarily. The side parting is a versatile and timeless hairstyle that can work with various hairstyles, including long, short, curly, and straight hair.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain a healthy length.

Now that you know what hair parting suits a heart-shaped face, you can confidently rock your new look. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to find what works best for you.
