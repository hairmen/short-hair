What Are Marine Haircuts Called

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Marine haircuts are a popular style among men, especially those who have served in the military. But what are marine haircuts called in relaxed English language? In this article, we will explain what marine haircuts are, how to get them, and share tips for maintaining the style.

What are Marine Haircuts?

Marine haircuts are also known as buzz cuts or military haircuts. They are a short, low-maintenance style that is popular among men in the military because they are easy to maintain and keep hair out of the way during combat. The style is characterized by short hair on the sides and top of the head, with a slightly longer length on the crown.

How to Get a Marine Haircut

If you are interested in getting a marine haircut, the good news is that it is a simple style that can be achieved with clippers and a steady hand. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of your haircut. Marine haircuts are typically very short, with a length of between 1/8 inch and 1/2 inch. Choose the length that you prefer and adjust the clippers accordingly.

Step 2: Start on the Sides

Begin by using the clippers to cut the hair on the sides of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, moving the clippers against the direction of hair growth.

Step 3: Move to the Top

Once the sides are done, move to the top of your head. Use the clippers to cut the hair from front to back, taking care to keep the length even.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

After the sides and top are cut, use the clippers to blend the two sections together. This will create a seamless transition between the short hair on the sides and the slightly longer hair on the top.

Step 5: Clean Up

Finally, use scissors to trim any stray hairs and clean up the edges around your ears and neck.

Tips for Maintaining a Marine Haircut

Marine haircuts are easy to maintain, but there are a few tips that can help you keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of product buildup.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

What is the difference between a marine haircut and a buzz cut?

A marine haircut is a specific type of buzz cut that is characterized by short hair on the sides and top of the head, with a slightly longer length on the crown. Buzz cuts can refer to any style of haircut that involves using clippers to cut the hair very short.

Can anyone get a marine haircut?

Yes, anyone can get a marine haircut. It is a versatile style that can work well for men of all ages and hair types.

Do I need to use any special products to maintain my marine haircut?

No, you do not need to use any special products to maintain a marine haircut. However, using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care

While marine haircuts are low-maintenance, it is still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle wet hair, as using a brush can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know what marine haircuts are called and how to get them, you can decide if this style is right for you. Whether you are a military veteran or simply looking for a low-maintenance haircut, a marine haircut could be the perfect choice.
