How To Do Bun Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old ponytail or letting your medium length hair hang loose? Well, a bun hairstyle can be the perfect solution! Buns are not only stylish, but they are also easy to do and can be worn for both casual and formal events. In this article, we will guide you through how to do bun hairstyles for medium length hair.

Description

Bun hairstyles are a classic look that can be worn by women of all ages. This hairstyle involves pulling your hair back into a tight or loose knot, depending on the occasion. Buns can be worn high or low on the head, and they can be styled in a variety of ways, including messy, sleek, or braided.

How to

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do bun hairstyles for medium length hair:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Before starting, ensure your hair is brushed and has no tangles. This will prevent any bumps or knots from appearing in your bun. You can also use a detangling spray or a leave-in conditioner to make brushing easier.

Step 2: Decide on the placement of your bun

Decide where you want to place your bun. It can be high, low, or to the side. You can also choose to have a center or side part.

Step 3: Secure your hair in a ponytail

Gather your hair into a ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Make sure the ponytail is positioned where you want your bun to be.

Step 4: Twist and wrap your hair

Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of your ponytail. Keep wrapping until you reach the end of your hair. Tuck the ends under the bun and secure them with bobby pins.

Step 5: Secure the bun with bobby pins

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Insert the pins into the bun, making sure they are hidden and secure.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish your bun hairstyle with a light hairspray to keep it in place all day.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your bun hairstyle even better:

  • For a sleek look, spray your hair with a shine spray before brushing it back into a ponytail.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse before blow-drying to add some body to your hair.
  • For a messy bun, pull out some strands of hair around your face to create a relaxed look.
  • If you have trouble with your bun staying in place, use a hair donut to create a more secure base.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bun hairstyles for medium length hair:

Can I wear a bun hairstyle to a formal event?

Yes! A bun hairstyle can be styled in a sleek and polished way, making it perfect for formal events.

How can I make my bun look fuller?

You can use a hair donut or add some volume to your hair with a volumizing mousse or dry shampoo.

Do I need to use bobby pins to secure my bun?

Yes! Bobby pins are essential for keeping your bun in place. Make sure to use enough pins to ensure your bun stays secure all day.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair before and after styling it. Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using heat tools, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to do bun hairstyles for medium length hair, you can experiment with different styles and make them your own. Whether you're going to work or attending a special event, a bun hairstyle is a chic and easy way to style your hair.
