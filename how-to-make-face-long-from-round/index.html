How To Make Your Face Look Longer

Do you have a round face and have always wanted to make it look longer? Well, you're in luck because we have some tips and tricks that can help you achieve that look! In this article, we'll go over how to make face long from round, step by step. We'll also provide some hair care tips and answer some common questions you may have about this topic.

  • How to Make Face Long from Round

How to Make Face Long from Round

There are a few things you can do to make your face look longer. One of the most important things is to create angles on your face. This can be done through the use of makeup, hairstyles, and clothing.

Makeup

When it comes to makeup, focus on contouring your face. Use a contouring kit to shade the sides of your forehead, temples, and cheeks. This will create the illusion of a more angular face. Also, avoid using blush on the apples of your cheeks, as this will make your face appear rounder. Instead, use a bronzer to contour your cheeks and give your face definition.

Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also make a big difference in how long your face appears. Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and keep hair close to your face. This will help elongate your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear rounder. Instead, try a lob or a shoulder-length cut.

Clothing

The clothing you wear can also help create the illusion of a longer face. Choose clothing that has vertical lines or patterns, as this will make your body appear taller. Avoid clothing with horizontal stripes, as this will make your body appear wider and your face appear rounder.

Step by Step

Here are the steps you can take to make your face look longer:

  1. Contour your face with makeup
  2. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and keeps hair close to your face
  3. Wear clothing with vertical lines or patterns

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your face look longer:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Use a bronzer to contour your cheeks
  • Avoid clothing with horizontal stripes
  • Try a lob or a shoulder-length cut

FAQ

Q: Can losing weight make my face look longer?

A: Yes, losing weight can make your face look longer. When you lose weight, you may lose fat from your cheeks, which can make your face appear less round.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to make my face look longer?

A: Yes, hair extensions can add volume to the top of your head and help make your face look longer. Just be sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to use products that are gentle and nourishing. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. Instead, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and let your hair air dry whenever possible. Also, be sure to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your face look longer and achieve the look you've always wanted!
