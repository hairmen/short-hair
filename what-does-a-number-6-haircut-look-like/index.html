What Does A Number 6 Haircut Look Like Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting a number 6 haircut but not sure what it looks like? Look no further because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide on what a number 6 haircut looks like, how to get it, tips, and hair care advice. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Number 6 Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips Before Getting a Number 6 Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for a Number 6 Haircut

Description

A number 6 haircut is a medium-length haircut that is approximately ¾ inch long. It is often referred to as a “medium fade” or “taper” because the hair gradually tapers down from the top to the sides and back. The number 6 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a stylish yet low-maintenance look.

How to Get a Number 6 Haircut

The best way to get a number 6 haircut is to visit a professional barber or stylist. They will have the expertise and tools to create the perfect look for you. However, if you prefer to do it yourself, you will need a good quality clipper with a number 6 guard attachment. You will also need a mirror, comb, and scissors.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to get a number 6 haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and drying it completely.
  2. Use a comb to separate the top section of your hair from the sides and back.
  3. Attach the number 6 guard to your clipper and turn it on.
  4. Starting from the bottom, gently run the clipper up the back of your head, stopping at the point where the top section of your hair begins.
  5. Repeat this process on the sides of your head.
  6. Use scissors to trim any longer strands of hair on the top section of your head.
  7. Blend the top section of your hair with the sides and back using the clipper without a guard attachment.
  8. Check for any uneven spots and make adjustments as needed.
  9. Use a comb to style your hair as desired.

Tips Before Getting a Number 6 Haircut

Before getting a number 6 haircut, consider the following tips:

  • Bring a photo of the style you want to show your barber or stylist.
  • Be clear about the length you want and how much fade you prefer.
  • Ask for advice on how to style and maintain your haircut.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a number 6 haircut.
  • Do not attempt to cut your own hair if you are not experienced with clippers and scissors.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a number 6 haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. On average, you should get a number 6 haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I get a number 6 haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, a number 6 haircut can work well with curly hair. However, you may need to adjust the length and fade to suit your hair texture and style.

Q: Can I style my hair differently with a number 6 haircut?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in various ways with a number 6 haircut. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy and textured.

Hair Care for a Number 6 Haircut

Here are some tips on how to care for your number 6 haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape.
  • Visit your barber or stylist for touch-ups and adjustments as needed.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Now that you know what a number 6 haircut looks like, how to get it, and how to care for it, you can confidently rock this stylish and versatile haircut. Whether you prefer a classic or modern style, a number 6 haircut can suit your needs and personality. So, go ahead and book your appointment with your barber or stylist today!
