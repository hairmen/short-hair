How To Make Your Curly Hair Look Thicker

Written by Daniel Jun 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Having curly hair is a blessing, but sometimes it can feel like your hair lacks volume and density. If you're looking for ways to make your curly hair look thicker, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks that will help you achieve the fuller, more voluminous hair you desire.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Your Curly Hair Look Thicker
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Your Curly Hair Look Thicker

There are many ways to make your curly hair look thicker, but not all methods work for everyone. Here are some general tips that can help:

  • Get a haircut: A good haircut can make your hair look thicker and fuller. Ask your stylist for layers to add volume.
  • Use volumizing products: Shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are designed to add volume can help make your hair look fuller.
  • Blow-dry your hair: Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add volume and definition to your curls.
  • Use a root-lifting spray: Spritz a root-lifting spray at the roots of your hair to add volume and lift.
  • Try a hair topper: If you're looking for a quick fix, a hair topper can add instant volume and thickness to your hair.
  • Consider hair extensions: Hair extensions can add length and volume to your hair, but make sure to choose extensions that match your curl pattern.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to make your curly hair look thicker:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and body to your hair.
  2. Apply a root-lifting spray to the roots of your hair. This will help add volume and lift.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. Focus on the roots of your hair and use your fingers to scrunch your curls as you dry them.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your curls. Hold the iron vertically and wrap small sections of hair around it, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to hold your curls in place and add extra volume.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help make your curly hair look thicker:

  • Try a scalp massage: Massaging your scalp can help stimulate hair growth and make your hair look thicker.
  • Avoid heat styling: Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to look thin and brittle. Try air-drying your hair instead.
  • Eat a healthy diet: A balanced diet that's rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can help promote hair growth and make your hair look thicker.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles: Braids, updos, and half-up styles can help add volume and thickness to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to make your curly hair look thicker:

  • What's the best haircut for making curly hair look thicker? A layered haircut can help add volume and body to curly hair.
  • Do hair extensions damage curly hair? Hair extensions can damage curly hair if they're not installed or maintained properly.
  • Can I use a volumizing mousse on curly hair? Yes, a volumizing mousse can help add volume and definition to curly hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to stay healthy and look its best. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your curly hair look thicker and more voluminous. Remember to take good care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
