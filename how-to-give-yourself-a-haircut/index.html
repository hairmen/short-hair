How To Give Yourself A Haircut At Home

Written by Petter Aug 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to our step-by-step guide on how to give yourself a haircut at home. With the pandemic still affecting our daily lives, it's important to know how to maintain your hair without having to go to the salon. Plus, cutting your own hair can save you money in the long run. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about giving yourself a haircut. Let's get started!

How to

Before we get into the actual cutting process, there are a few things you need to prepare beforehand. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, so make sure your hair is clean and dry before starting.

Step by Step

1. Start by parting your hair in the middle and clipping each side away from the other. This will make it easier to work with one section at a time.
2. Take one section of hair and comb it straight down towards your toes.
3. Hold the hair between your fingers where you want to cut it. Start by cutting a small section at a time, gradually cutting more if needed.
4. Repeat the process on the other side, making sure to cut evenly.
5. Trim any split ends or uneven areas.
6. Use the comb to blend the layers if needed.
7. Check the back of your head with a mirror to make sure everything is even.
8. If you want to add layers, take a small section of hair and cut upwards at a slight angle. Repeat on both sides.
9. Style your hair as desired.
10. Clean up any stray hairs and put away your tools.

Tips

- Start with small cuts and work your way up.
- Take breaks if you need to.
- Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if needed.
- Don't cut too much at once, as you can't put hair back once it's cut.
- Ask a friend or family member to assist you if you're unsure.
- Have a backup plan in case something goes wrong. You can always go to a professional if needed.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?
A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to cut it when it's dry to prevent any shrinkage.
Q: How often should I cut my hair?
A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks.
Q: What if I mess up?
A: Don't panic! Take a break and assess the situation. If it's fixable, try to correct it. If not, wait a few days and try again or seek professional help.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after cutting it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you're unsure about what products to use, ask your stylist for recommendations.

That's it! You now know how to give yourself a haircut at home. Remember to take your time and be patient. With practice, you'll become a pro in no time. Happy cutting!
