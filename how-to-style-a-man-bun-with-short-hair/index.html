How To Style A Man Bun With Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair and are looking to style a man bun, you may be wondering if it's even possible. The good news is that it is! With a little bit of patience and some styling products, you can achieve a sleek and stylish man bun that will turn heads. In this post, we'll walk you through the steps of how to style a man bun with short hair.

Description

The man bun is a popular hairstyle for men that has gained popularity in recent years. It involves pulling the hair back into a bun at the crown of the head. While it was traditionally reserved for men with long hair, it is now possible to style a man bun with short hair.

How to Style a Man Bun with Short Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Grow Out Your Hair

The first step in styling a man bun with short hair is to grow out your hair. Your hair should be at least 3-4 inches long on top to achieve a man bun. This can take several months, so be patient.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it's important to wash and condition it. This will help to remove any product buildup and make your hair easier to style. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. A pomade or wax will work well for short hair. Apply a small amount to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the ends. This will help to give your hair some hold and texture.

Step 4: Pull Your Hair Back

Using a comb or your fingers, pull your hair back into a ponytail at the crown of your head. If your hair is too short to create a full ponytail, you can use a small hair tie or elastic to secure it.

Step 5: Twist and Wrap Your Hair

Take the ponytail and twist it into a tight coil. Then, wrap the coil around the base of the ponytail to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your man bun with a spritz of hairspray to help keep it in place. Choose a hairspray that is suited for your hair type and offers a strong hold.

Tips for Styling a Man Bun with Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect man bun with short hair:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that is suited for your hair type.
  • Be patient when growing out your hair.
  • Experiment with different bun styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your bun in place.
  • Don't be afraid to ask a hairstylist for help or advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I style a man bun with very short hair?

If your hair is very short, it may be difficult to style a full man bun. However, you can create a half bun by pulling the top half of your hair back into a small bun at the crown of your head.

Do I need to use hairspray to keep my man bun in place?

While hairspray can be helpful in keeping your man bun in place, it's not always necessary. If you have thick or coarse hair, you may not need to use hairspray at all.

Can I wear a man bun to work or formal events?

It depends on the dress code of your workplace or the event. In general, a man bun is a casual hairstyle that may not be appropriate for more formal settings.

Hair Care for Men with Short Hair

When styling a man bun with short hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style a man bun with short hair like a pro. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. Happy styling!
