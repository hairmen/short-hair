How To Hide Dark Armpits With Makeup

Written by Aprilia Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about dark armpits? Luckily, there is a solution that does not involve harsh chemicals or expensive treatments. By using makeup, you can easily hide your dark armpits and feel confident in your own skin. In this article, we will discuss how to hide dark armpits with makeup, step by step.

Description

Dark armpits are a common problem for many people, and it can be caused by a variety of factors such as shaving, deodorant, and genetics. While there are many treatments available, they can be expensive and may not work for everyone. Makeup is an affordable and easy solution that can help you achieve the look you want without breaking the bank.

How to Hide Dark Armpits with Makeup

Here are the steps you can follow to hide your dark armpits with makeup:

Step 1: Exfoliate

Before applying makeup, it is important to exfoliate your armpits to remove any dead skin cells or buildup. You can use a gentle scrub or a loofah to do this. Exfoliating will help the makeup go on smoothly and evenly.

Step 2: Moisturize

After exfoliating, it is important to moisturize your armpits to prevent dryness and flakiness. Use a light, non-greasy moisturizer to hydrate the skin.

Step 3: Apply Concealer

Choose a concealer that matches your skin tone and apply it to your armpits using a makeup brush or your finger. Make sure to blend it in well to avoid any harsh lines or unevenness.

Step 4: Set with Powder

After applying the concealer, set it with a translucent powder to prevent it from smudging or rubbing off. Use a large powder brush to apply the powder evenly.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when hiding dark armpits with makeup:

  • Choose a concealer that matches your skin tone as closely as possible.
  • Use a light hand when applying makeup to avoid a cakey or heavy look.
  • If you will be wearing sleeveless clothing, make sure to apply makeup to your shoulders and chest to achieve a seamless look.
  • Try using a color-correcting concealer if your dark armpits have a bluish or purplish hue.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding dark armpits with makeup:

Is it safe to use makeup on my armpits?

Yes, it is safe to use makeup on your armpits as long as you use products that are non-irritating and non-toxic.

What kind of makeup should I use to hide dark armpits?

You can use any type of makeup that you feel comfortable with, such as concealer, foundation, or powder. Just make sure to choose products that match your skin tone and are non-irritating.

How long will the makeup last?

The makeup should last for several hours, but it may need to be reapplied if you sweat or engage in physical activity.

Hair Care

While makeup can help hide dark armpits, it is important to address the root cause of the problem. Here are some tips to help you take care of your underarm hair:

  • Avoid shaving too frequently or using harsh razors, as this can cause irritation and discoloration.
  • Consider using a natural deodorant that does not contain harsh chemicals or irritants.
  • Try waxing or using hair removal creams instead of shaving.
  • Exfoliate regularly to remove dead skin cells and prevent buildup.

By following these tips and using makeup to hide your dark armpits, you can feel confident and beautiful in your own skin. Remember to take care of your underarm hair to prevent further discoloration and irritation.
