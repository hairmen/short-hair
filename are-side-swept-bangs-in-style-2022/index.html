Are Side Swept Bangs In Style 2022 Find Out Here

Written by Petter Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Side swept bangs have been a popular hairstyle for years, but are they still in style for 2022? In this article, we'll take a look at this timeless hairstyle and give you some tips on how to rock it in the coming year.

Description: What are Side Swept Bangs?

Side swept bangs are a hairstyle where the front of the hair is cut shorter than the rest and swept to one side. They can be cut to various lengths and can be styled in different ways to suit your face shape and personal style.

How to Style Side Swept Bangs

If you're thinking of getting side swept bangs, there are a few different ways you can style them. Here are some tips:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin styling your side swept bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Bangs

Use a round brush and a hair dryer to blow dry your bangs. Start by blowing them straight down and then sweep them to one side. This will help to create the side swept look.

Step 3: Use a Flat Iron

If you want to make your bangs even straighter, you can use a flat iron. Use it on a low heat setting and run it through your bangs a few times until they are straight and sleek.

Step 4: Add Some Volume

If you want to add some volume to your side swept bangs, you can use a volumizing mousse or spray. Apply it to your roots and then blow dry your hair upside down for extra volume.

Tips for Rocking Side Swept Bangs in 2022

If you're thinking of getting side swept bangs in 2022, here are some tips to help you rock this classic hairstyle:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Length

The length of your side swept bangs will depend on your face shape and personal style. If you have a longer face, you may want to go for longer bangs. If you have a rounder face, shorter bangs may be more flattering.

Tip 2: Keep Your Bangs Trimmed

To keep your side swept bangs looking fresh and stylish, you'll need to get them trimmed regularly. Aim to get them trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Side swept bangs can be styled in many different ways, so don't be afraid to experiment. You can wear them straight and sleek or add some waves or curls for a more relaxed look.

FAQ: Common Questions About Side Swept Bangs

Q: Will side swept bangs suit my face shape?

A: Side swept bangs can suit many different face shapes, but it's important to choose the right length and style for your face. Consult with your hairstylist to find the right look for you.

Q: Can I wear side swept bangs with glasses?

A: Yes, side swept bangs can be worn with glasses. Just make sure to choose glasses that complement your face shape and don't clash with your bangs.

Q: How do I keep my side swept bangs looking fresh?

A: To keep your side swept bangs looking fresh, use a dry shampoo between washes to absorb any excess oil. You can also use a hairspray to keep them in place throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs require some extra care and attention to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain this classic hairstyle:

Tip 1: Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and strong, which is essential for maintaining your side swept bangs.

Tip 2: Avoid Overstyling

While it can be tempting to style your side swept bangs every day, it's important to give them a break from time to time. Overstyling can damage your hair and make it harder to maintain your bangs in the long run.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, even if you style it regularly.

Conclusion

So, are side swept bangs in style for 2022? The answer is yes! This classic hairstyle is still going strong and can be adapted to suit any face shape and personal style. By following these tips and hair care advice, you can rock side swept bangs with confidence in the coming year and beyond.
