Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Straight Hair With Bangs

Written by William Oct 06, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have shoulder length straight hair with bangs, you're in luck! There are countless hairstyles that you can try to change up your look. In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best hairstyles for shoulder length straight hair with bangs. Whether you're looking for something casual or formal, we've got you covered.

Description

Shoulder length straight hair with bangs is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a versatile look that can be worn in many different ways. Shoulder length hair is long enough to style, but not too long that it becomes a hassle to manage. Bangs add a touch of personality and can be styled to frame the face.

How to

When styling shoulder length straight hair with bangs, it's important to start with clean and dry hair. You can use a straightening iron to achieve a sleek and straight look. If you want to add some volume, you can use a round brush to blow dry your hair. Once your hair is straight, you can start styling it.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean and dry hair
2. Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair
3. Use a round brush to add volume
4. Style your bangs to frame your face
5. Add accessories like clips or headbands to complete the look

Tips

When styling shoulder length straight hair with bangs, here are some tips to keep in mind:

- Use heat protectant when using a straightening iron
- Use a round brush to add volume
- Don't overuse hair products as they can weigh down your hair
- Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?
A: Yes, you can! You can try a half-up, half-down hairstyle or a low bun to keep your hair out of your face.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and how often you use hair products. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length straight hair with bangs healthy and shiny, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
- Use a heat protectant when using hot tools
- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
- Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends

Overall, shoulder length straight hair with bangs is a versatile and timeless hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. Whether you're looking for something casual or formal, there's a hairstyle out there for you. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.
