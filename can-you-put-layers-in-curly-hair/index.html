Can You Put Layers In Curly Hair

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you may be wondering if you can add layers to your locks. The good news is that yes, you can put layers in curly hair! Layers can help enhance your curls and give your hair a more dynamic and voluminous look. In this blog post, we will be discussing how to add layers to your curly hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. So, let's get started!

How to Add Layers to Curly Hair

Adding layers to curly hair can be done at a salon or by yourself at home. If you choose to go to a salon, it's important to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair. Here are some steps to add layers to curly hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and a conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Gently towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into 4-6 sections, depending on the thickness of your hair. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut your hair

Starting at the bottom section, use a pair of hair cutting scissors to cut your hair at an angle. Make sure to cut each section slightly longer than the previous one. This will create a layered effect. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, you can style it as desired. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair and use a diffuser to dry your hair, or let it air dry.

Tips for Adding Layers to Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when adding layers to curly hair:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven layers.
  • Start with small layers and gradually add more if you want a more dramatic effect.
  • Avoid cutting your hair when it's wet, as this can cause shrinkage and lead to uneven layers.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself.

FAQs

Q: Will layers make my hair look shorter?

A: Yes, layers can make your hair look shorter, especially if you have tight curls. However, the volume and shape that layers add to your hair can make it look fuller and more dynamic.

Q: Can I add layers to my hair if I have fine curls?

A: Yes, you can add layers to fine curly hair. However, it's important to be cautious when cutting fine hair, as it can easily become damaged or frizzy.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair if I have layers?

A: It's recommended to trim your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your layers and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Curly Hair with Layers

Once you have added layers to your curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair with layers:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Adding layers to curly hair can be a great way to enhance your natural curls and add volume and dimension to your hair. By following these tips and taking care of your hair properly, you can keep your curly locks looking healthy and beautiful.
