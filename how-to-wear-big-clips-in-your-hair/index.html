How To Wear Big Clips In Your Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 09, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Big clips are a great way to add some style and personality to your hair. They come in a variety of colors, shapes, and sizes, making them a versatile accessory that can be worn in many different ways. In this article, we will show you how to wear big clips in your hair, step by step. We will also provide some tips and tricks for making the most out of your big clips. Let's get started!

How to Wear Big Clips in Your Hair

Here are the steps to follow when wearing big clips in your hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clip

Before you start, it's important to choose the right clip for your hair type and style. If you have thick hair, you will need a stronger and more durable clip. If you have fine hair, a smaller clip might be better suited. Choose a clip that complements your hair color and matches your outfit.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Brush and detangle your hair before you start. If you have curly hair, you might want to apply a styling product to help define your curls. If you have straight hair, you can add some texture by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

Step 3: Decide Where to Place the Clip

You can wear a big clip in many different places on your head. You can clip it on the side, on top, or at the back. Decide where you want to place the clip and section off the hair accordingly.

Step 4: Insert the Clip

Open the clip and position it where you want it. Hold the hair in place with one hand and use the other hand to snap the clip shut. Make sure the clip is secure and that it's not pulling or tugging on your hair.

Step 5: Adjust as Needed

If the clip feels uncomfortable or if it's not sitting right, you can adjust it by loosening or tightening the clip. You can also move it slightly to the left or right until you find the perfect spot.

Tips for Wearing Big Clips

Here are some tips for making the most out of your big clips:

Tip 1: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try different styles and placements. You can wear a big clip with a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or loose waves. The possibilities are endless.

Tip 2: Use Multiple Clips

You can create a unique and stylish look by using multiple clips. You can clip them all on one side or spread them out throughout your hair.

Tip 3: Coordinate with Your Outfit

Choose a clip that complements your outfit. You can match it to your shoes, bag, or jewelry.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a big clip with short hair?

A: Yes, you can. You might need a smaller clip, but big clips can also be used to hold back shorter layers of hair.

Q: Can I wear a big clip to work?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure the clip is not too flashy or distracting. Stick to neutral colors and simple designs for a professional look.

Q: How do I care for my hair when wearing a big clip?

A: Be gentle when removing the clip to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Hair Care

Wearing a big clip can be a fun and easy way to change up your look. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Make sure you are using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to wear big clips in your hair, it's time to experiment and have fun. Remember to choose a clip that matches your style and personality, and don't be afraid to try new things. Happy styling!
