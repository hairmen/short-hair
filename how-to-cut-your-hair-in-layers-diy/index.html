How To Cut Your Hair In Layers Diy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and add some texture and movement, cutting your hair in layers is a great option. However, going to a salon can be expensive, and you might not be able to get an appointment right away. That's where DIY haircuts come in handy. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your hair in layers at home, step-by-step.

How to Cut Your Hair in Layers DIY

Cutting your hair in layers at home can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it's easy to achieve. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your hair in layers:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting.
  2. Section your hair. Use clips to section your hair into manageable sections. Start by sectioning the top half of your hair from the bottom half.
  3. Start cutting. Take a small section of hair from the top layer and comb it straight up. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the length you want the shortest layer to be. Repeat this process for each section of hair.
  4. Blend the layers. Once you've cut all the sections of your hair, remove the clips and blend the layers together by combing through your hair with your fingers. This will ensure a seamless transition between the layers.
  5. Check for symmetry. Stand in front of the mirror and check that both sides of your hair are even. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect layered haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair. It's easier to cut more hair than to put it back after you've cut too much.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Be patient. Cutting your hair in layers can take some time, especially if you're doing it for the first time. Take breaks if you need to.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your hair in layers:

  • Can I cut my hair in layers if it's curly?
  • Yes, you can cut your curly hair in layers. However, it's best to cut curly hair when it's dry, so you can see how the curls fall.
  • How often should I cut my hair in layers?
  • It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.
  • Can I cut my hair in layers if it's short?
  • Yes, you can still create layers in short hair. However, it's best to consult a professional stylist if you're unsure about how to cut short hair.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair in layers, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and texture. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut your hair in layers DIY, you can experiment with different styles and textures from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and be patient. Happy cutting!
