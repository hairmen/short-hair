How To Put Short Name In Facebook

Written by Oliver Jul 02, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of having a long and complicated Facebook name? Do you want to simplify it and make it easier for your friends to find you? Well, you're in luck because Facebook allows you to create a short name for your profile. In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to put a short name in Facebook.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Short Name in Facebook
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing a Short Name
  • FAQs

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create a short name for your Facebook profile:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Click on the down arrow icon at the top right corner of your screen.
  3. Select "Settings & Privacy" from the drop-down menu.
  4. Click on "Settings".
  5. Scroll down to the "Username" section and click on "Edit".
  6. Type in your desired short name. It should be between 5-50 characters and can include letters, numbers, and periods.
  7. Click on "Create Username".
  8. Facebook will check the availability of the username. If it's available, click on "Confirm" to save your new short name.
  9. Once you've confirmed your short name, it will be visible on your profile and can be used as a URL to access your profile.

That's it! You've successfully created a short name for your Facebook profile.

Tips for Choosing a Short Name

Here are some tips to help you choose the best short name for your Facebook profile:

  • Keep it simple and easy to remember.
  • Avoid using special characters or symbols as they may be difficult to type or remember.
  • Make it unique to stand out from other profiles.
  • Consider using your real name or a nickname.
  • Check the availability of the short name before finalizing it.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about creating a short name in Facebook:

Q: Can I change my short name?

A: Yes, you can change your short name on Facebook. Simply follow the same steps as above and type in a new short name.

Q: Can someone else use my short name?

A: No, once you've created a short name, it's unique to your profile and can't be used by anyone else.

Q: Can I use spaces in my short name?

A: No, spaces aren't allowed in short names. You can use periods instead.

Q: Is there a limit to how many times I can change my short name?

A: Yes, Facebook has a limit on how many times you can change your short name. You can only change it once every 60 days.

Hair Care Tips

While you're updating your Facebook profile, don't forget to take care of your hair too! Here are some tips for healthy and beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these hair care tips and creating a short name for your Facebook profile, you'll be looking and feeling your best both online and offline.
