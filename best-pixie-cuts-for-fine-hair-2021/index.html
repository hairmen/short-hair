Best Pixie Cuts For Fine Hair 2021 A Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have fine hair, it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that suits your hair type. Pixie cuts are a great option for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair. In this guide, we will walk you through the best pixie cuts for fine hair in 2021.

Description

Before we dive into the best pixie cuts for fine hair, let's talk about what pixie cuts are. Pixie cuts are short haircuts that are typically short on the sides and back and longer on top. These haircuts have been popular for decades and continue to be a go-to option for women with fine hair.

How To

Getting a pixie cut is a big decision, so it's important to know what to expect. Here's how to get a pixie cut:

  1. Research the different types of pixie cuts and find one that you like.
  2. Find a hairstylist who has experience cutting pixie cuts.
  3. Bring a photo of the haircut you want to your hairstylist.
  4. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with your hairstylist.
  5. Get your hair cut!

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when styling your pixie cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your pixie cut.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts look great on oval, heart-shaped, and square faces.

Will a pixie cut work for thick hair?

Pixie cuts work well for thick hair, but you may need to get them thinned out to avoid a bulky look.

Can you color your hair after getting a pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your hair after getting a pixie cut. Just make sure to consult with your hairstylist first.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

Conclusion

Pixie cuts are a great option for those with fine hair who want to add volume and texture to their hair. There are many different types of pixie cuts to choose from, so it's important to find one that suits your hair type and face shape. Follow the tips in this guide to maintain your pixie cut and keep your hair healthy.
