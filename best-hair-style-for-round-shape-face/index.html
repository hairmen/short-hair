Best Hair Style For Round Shape Face

If you have a round face shape, you may be wondering what the best hair style is for you. It can be tricky to find the perfect style that flatters your features while also being easy to maintain. In this article, we'll explore the best hair styles for round shape faces, as well as some tips for caring for your hair.

Description of Round Shape Face

A round face shape is characterized by fullness in the cheeks, as well as a rounded jawline and forehead. This face shape tends to be symmetrical, with a width that is roughly equal to its height. The key to finding the best hair styles for round shape faces is to create balance and definition, while also highlighting your best features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few simple steps you can take to find out. First, pull your hair back and take a look at your face in the mirror. Then, use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Finally, compare these measurements to determine your face shape.

Round Face Shape Measurements

If your face is round, your cheekbones and face length will be roughly the same size. Your jawline and forehead will also be rounded, with no sharp angles.

Best Hair Style for Round Shape Face

When it comes to finding the best hair style for round shape faces, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you'll want to create the illusion of length in your face, which can be achieved with long layers or a side-swept fringe. You'll also want to add volume and texture to your hair, which can be achieved with beachy waves or a volumizing mousse.

Some of the best hair styles for round shape faces include:

  • Long Layers
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Beachy Waves
  • Pixie Cut
  • Asymmetrical Bob

These styles all create the illusion of length in the face, while also adding volume and texture to the hair. They also highlight the best features of a round face shape, such as the cheeks and jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hair styles for round shape faces, it's time to learn how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving some of the most popular styles:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair, then comb through to distribute evenly.
  3. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the crown of your head.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a comb, part your hair on the side.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your hairline and comb it forward.
  4. Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut the hair at an angle, starting just above your eyebrows and ending at the outer corner of your eye.
  5. Comb the bangs to the side, then use a curling iron to add loose waves to the rest of your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Once you've found the best hair style for your round shape face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair from the inside out.

FAQs

What if I don't want to cut my hair?

If you don't want to cut your hair, you can still achieve a flattering style for your round shape face. Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture, or try a deep side part to create the illusion of length in your face.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still achieve a flattering style for your round shape face. Consider a shoulder-length cut with layers to add volume and definition to your curls. You can also try a side-swept fringe to create the illusion of length in your face.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protecting spray.

By following these tips and choosing the best hair style for your round shape face, you can achieve a beautiful, flattering look that highlights your best features and boosts your confidence.
