Is Grey Hair In Style 2021

Written by Aprilia Aug 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Grey hair has been a topic of conversation for many years, with some believing it to be a sign of old age and others seeing it as a sign of maturity and wisdom. However, in recent years, grey hair has become a trend in the fashion industry, causing many people to wonder if it is still in style in 2021. In this article, we will explore the current state of grey hair in the fashion world, how to achieve it, and some tips on how to care for grey hair.

Description

Grey hair has been a trend for several years now, with many celebrities and influencers rocking the look. It has been seen on runways, in magazines, and on social media. Grey hair is a unique and daring look that can make a statement and add character to your overall appearance.

How to Achieve Grey Hair

There are several ways to achieve grey hair, but the most popular method is to dye your hair. If you have naturally grey hair, then you are already ahead of the game. However, if you have naturally dark hair, you will need to bleach your hair first before applying a grey dye. It’s important to note that bleaching your hair can be damaging, so it’s best to seek professional help.

If you don’t want to dye your hair, you can also achieve the grey look by using hair extensions or wigs. This is a great option for those who don’t want to commit to the look permanently or want to try it out before taking the plunge.

Step by Step Guide

1. Decide on the shade of grey you want to achieve. There are several shades of grey, from light silver to dark charcoal.

2. Determine if you need to bleach your hair. If you have dark hair, you will need to bleach your hair first before applying the grey dye.

3. Choose a high-quality grey hair dye. Look for a product that is specifically designed for grey hair and is gentle on your hair.

4. Apply the dye according to the instructions on the packaging. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully to avoid any mishaps.

5. Rinse out the dye and apply a deep conditioner to help protect and nourish your hair.

Tips for Grey Hair Care

Grey hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips on how to care for grey hair:

- Use a purple shampoo to tone down any yellow or brassy tones in your hair.

- Invest in a good quality conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

- Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

FAQ

Q: Is grey hair still in style in 2021?

A: Yes, grey hair is still in style in 2021. It has been a popular trend for several years now and shows no signs of slowing down.

Q: Can I achieve grey hair without bleaching my hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the grey look without bleaching your hair by using hair extensions or wigs.

Conclusion

Grey hair is still in style in 2021 and is a great way to make a statement and add character to your appearance. Whether you choose to dye your hair or use hair extensions, grey hair is a unique and daring look that is sure to turn heads. However, it’s important to care for your grey hair properly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Follow the tips in this article to keep your grey hair looking its best.
