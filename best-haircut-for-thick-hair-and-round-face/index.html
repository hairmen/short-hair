Best Haircut For Thick Hair And Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your thick hair and round face? Look no further, because we’ve got you covered with this complete guide on the best haircut for your hair type and face shape. From how to choose the right style to tips on how to care for your hair, we’ll cover everything you need to know to achieve the perfect look.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it may be enviable to have a full head of hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. When combined with a round face shape, finding the right haircut can feel like an impossible task. However, with the right style, you can accentuate your features and create a look that is both flattering and easy to maintain.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for your hair type and face shape, there are a few key factors to consider:

Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. A round face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, rounded cheekbones, and a rounded jawline. The goal is to create balance by adding volume and height to the crown of the head and elongating the face.

Hair Type

Thick hair requires a style that can handle the weight and volume. Look for styles that incorporate layering and texture to add movement and dimension.

Lifestyle

Finally, consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you have a busy schedule and don’t have a lot of time to style your hair, look for a low-maintenance style that can be easily air-dried or styled quickly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, let’s take a look at some of the best styles for thick hair and round faces.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for thick hair and round faces. The layers add movement and volume, while the length helps to elongate the face. To style, simply blow-dry your hair with a round brush and add some texturizing spray for extra volume.

2. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is another great option for thick hair and round faces. The bob adds structure and shape, while the bangs help to frame the face. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush and use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks.

3. Pixie Cut

If you’re looking for a low-maintenance style, a pixie cut is a great option. The short length adds height to the crown of the head, while the layers add texture and movement. To style, simply use a texturizing spray and tousle with your fingers.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy and stylish:

Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for keeping your haircut looking fresh and healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Look for products that are formulated for your hair type and use them consistently to keep your hair looking its best.

Avoid Heat Damage

Heat damage can cause your hair to become dry and brittle, leading to breakage and split ends. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray before using heat on your hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face with thick hair?

The best haircut for a round face with thick hair is one that adds volume and height to the crown of the head and elongates the face. Look for styles that incorporate layering and texture, such as long layers, a bob with bangs, or a pixie cut.

Can I still have bangs with a round face?

Yes, you can still have bangs with a round face. Bangs can help to frame the face and add structure to your haircut. Just make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape.

How do I know if a haircut will work for my face shape?

The best way to determine if a haircut will work for your face shape is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a haircut that complements your features and flatters your face shape.

Hair Care

In addition to choosing the right haircut, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips for caring for your hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly is essential for keeping it clean and healthy. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair helps to keep it soft and nourished. Use a conditioner that is formulated for your hair type and apply it from mid-length to the ends of your hair.

Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your hair can cause damage and breakage. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and try to air-dry your hair whenever possible. If you do use heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant spray.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and easy to maintain. Use this guide to find the best haircut for your thick hair and round face, and enjoy healthy, vibrant hair all year long.
