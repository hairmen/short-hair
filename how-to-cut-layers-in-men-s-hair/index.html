How To Cut Layers In Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 21, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to add texture and volume to your hair, then cutting layers might be just what you need. However, cutting layers can be a bit tricky, especially if you've never done it before. That's why we've put together this step-by-step guide on how to cut layers in men's hair.

How to Cut Layers in Men's Hair

Cutting layers in men's hair is a great way to add depth, texture, and volume to your hair. However, it's important to keep in mind that cutting layers is a skill that requires practice and patience. Here's how you can do it:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to help you section off your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's best to start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see the natural movement and texture of your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair horizontally from your temple to your temple. Clip the top part of your hair up so that it doesn't get in the way.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting with the bottom section, hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut it at a slight angle. This will create a soft, layered effect. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've finished cutting all the layers, it's time to blend them together. Use your scissors to gently blend the layers together, creating a seamless transition from one layer to the next.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've blended the layers, it's time to style your hair. Use a styling product such as gel or wax to add texture and volume to your hair.

Tips for Cutting Layers in Men's Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting layers in men's hair:

  • Start with a smaller section of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut a little at a time than to cut too much and have to start over.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and ensure that your cuts are straight.

FAQ

Can you cut layers in short hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in short hair. However, it's important to keep in mind that the length of the layers will be shorter in short hair.

How often should you cut your hair when you have layers?

It's recommended that you get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks when you have layers. This will help keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Cutting layers in your hair can make it look great, but it's important to take care of it as well. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut layers in men's hair like a pro. Just remember to take your time, practice, and have patience. Good luck!
