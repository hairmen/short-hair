Do You Keep Your Own Eyebrows With Microblading

Written by Eveline Jun 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Microblading has become a popular beauty trend in recent years, especially for those who want to achieve fuller and more defined eyebrows. However, many people are still hesitant to try it because they are not sure if they will be able to keep their own eyebrows with microblading. In this blog post, we will answer this question and provide you with some tips on how to take care of your microbladed eyebrows.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Microblading is a semi-permanent eyebrow tattooing technique that uses a small handheld tool to create hair-like strokes on the skin. This creates a more natural-looking eyebrow that lasts for up to two years. The procedure involves numbing the area with a topical anesthetic, and then the technician will use the tool to create the strokes. The process takes about two hours to complete.

How to

To keep your own eyebrows with microblading, you should choose a reputable and experienced technician who will work with you to create the look you want. Before the procedure, you should discuss your desired shape and color with your technician. They will then draw on the shape of your eyebrows with a pencil to ensure that you are happy with the result before they start microblading.

Step by Step

The following are the steps involved in microblading:

  1. Clean the eyebrow area
  2. Numb the area with a topical anesthetic
  3. Create hair-like strokes with a handheld tool
  4. Apply pigment to the strokes
  5. Wipe away excess pigment
  6. Apply ointment to the area to promote healing
  7. Repeat the process until the desired look is achieved

Tips

Here are some tips on how to take care of your microbladed eyebrows:

  • Avoid getting the area wet for at least a week after the procedure
  • Avoid using makeup on the area for at least a week after the procedure
  • Avoid picking at the area as it heals
  • Avoid tanning or excessive sun exposure for at least a week after the procedure
  • Apply ointment to the area as directed by your technician
  • Use a clean pillowcase to avoid infection

FAQ

How long does microblading last?

Microblading lasts for up to two years, depending on your skin type and how well you take care of your eyebrows.

Does microblading hurt?

The procedure involves numbing the area with a topical anesthetic, so you should not feel any pain. However, some people may experience slight discomfort during the procedure.

How much does microblading cost?

The cost of microblading can vary depending on the technician and location, but it typically ranges from $300 to $800.

Hair Care

After microblading, it is important to take care of your eyebrows to ensure that they last as long as possible. You should avoid using any harsh chemicals or treatments on your eyebrows, and avoid rubbing or scratching the area. You can also use a brow serum to promote hair growth and keep your eyebrows looking full and healthy.

In conclusion, you can keep your own eyebrows with microblading by choosing a reputable technician and following the proper aftercare instructions. With the right care, your microbladed eyebrows can last for up to two years and give you the perfect brow shape you have always wanted.
