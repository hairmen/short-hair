Hairstyles To Suit Round Fat Faces And Double Chin

Written by Christine Oct 22, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a round fat face and a double chin, you may feel limited when it comes to hairstyles. But fear not, there are many styles that can complement your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for round fat faces and double chin, as well as tips for hair care and styling.

  • Hairstyles to Suit Round Fat Faces and Double Chin

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features. To determine your face shape, use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Use these measurements to determine your face shape:

  • Round: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a rounded chin and full cheeks.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded chin and a forehead that is slightly wider than your jawline.
  • Square: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a strong, angular jawline and a square hairline.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and has a pointed chin.

Hairstyles to Suit Round Fat Faces and Double Chin

If you have a round fat face and a double chin, the best hairstyles are those that add height and volume to the top of your head, while slimming down the lower part of your face. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option for round fat faces and double chin. The layers add volume and texture to the top of your head, while the length of the bob elongates your face. Ask your stylist to add layers around your face to slim down your cheeks and jawline.

Long Layers

If you have long hair, long layers are a great option. The layers add volume and movement to your hair, while slimming down your face. Ask your stylist to add layers around your face to slim down your cheeks and jawline.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round fat faces and double chin. The bangs draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, while the length of the hair elongates your face. Ask your stylist to cut the bangs so that they are longer on one side, and sweep them across your forehead.

High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a great option for round fat faces and double chin. The height of the ponytail adds volume to the top of your head, while the length of the ponytail elongates your face. Use a hair tie to secure your hair at the crown of your head, and pull the ponytail tight to create height.

Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great option for round fat faces and double chin. The height of the hair on top of your head adds volume, while the length of the hair elongates your face. Use a hair tie to secure half of your hair at the crown of your head, and leave the rest of your hair down.

Tips for Hair Care and Styling

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles, here are some tips for hair care and styling:

Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

A volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

Avoid Heavy Products

Heavy products like oils and serums can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, opt for lightweight products like mousse or texturizing spray.

Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down can help add volume to the roots, making your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Use a Round Brush

Using a round brush while blow drying your hair can help add volume and texture. Wrap small sections of hair around the brush and pull it away from your head to create volume.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails can pull on your hairline and make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for looser styles that are more relaxed.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round fat face and double chin?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as they can make your face look wider. Also avoid hairstyles that are too tight, as they can pull on your hairline and make your face look rounder.

Do I have to cut my hair short if I have a round fat face and double chin?

No, you don't have to cut your hair short. Long hair can be just as flattering, as long as you choose the right style and cut.

Should I part my hair in the middle or on the side?

It depends on your face shape and personal preference. If you have a round fat face and double chin, a side part can help slim down your face. Experiment with different parts to see what works best for you.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less often. Experiment with different washing schedules to see what works best for you.

What can I do to keep my hair healthy?

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling as much as possible, and get regular trims to prevent split ends. Also, make sure to eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles and tips for hair care and styling, you can feel confident and beautiful no matter what your face shape or hair type. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
