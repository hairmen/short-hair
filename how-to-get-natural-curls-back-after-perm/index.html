How To Get Natural Curls Back After Perm A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the damage caused by perms and longing for your natural curls to come back? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get your natural curls back after a perm. We will also provide you with some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the results you desire.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Cut off the Permed Hair
  • Step 2: Deep Condition Your Hair
  • Step 3: Use a Protein Treatment
  • Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling Tools
  • Step 5: Use a Curl Enhancing Product
  • Tips for Getting Your Natural Curls Back
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Natural Curls

Step 1: Cut off the Permed Hair

The first step in getting your natural curls back after a perm is to cut off the permed hair. You can do this yourself or go to a salon to have it done. Make sure to cut off as much of the permed hair as possible to avoid any future damage to your natural hair.

Step 2: Deep Condition Your Hair

Once you have cut off the permed hair, the next step is to deep condition your hair. Use a deep conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Leave the conditioner on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it off.

Step 3: Use a Protein Treatment

Protein treatments help to strengthen your hair and promote healthy hair growth. Use a protein treatment once a week to help restore your natural curls. Make sure to follow the instructions on the product carefully.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. Heat can cause damage to your hair and make it harder to restore your natural curls. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair.

Step 5: Use a Curl Enhancing Product

Use a curl enhancing product to help define your natural curls. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Apply the product to your hair when it is damp and scrunch your hair to define your curls.

Tips for Getting Your Natural Curls Back

  • Be patient. Restoring your natural curls takes time and effort.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

FAQs

Can I restore my natural curls after a perm?

Yes, you can restore your natural curls after a perm by following the steps outlined in this article.

How long does it take to restore natural curls after a perm?

It can take several months to a year to restore your natural curls after a perm. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Can I color my hair while I am restoring my natural curls?

Avoid coloring your hair while you are restoring your natural curls. Coloring your hair can cause damage and make it harder to restore your natural curls.

Hair Care for Natural Curls

Now that you have restored your natural curls, it is important to take care of them. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Avoid using products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair. Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to help define your curls. Get regular trims to help promote healthy hair growth.

By following the steps outlined in this article and taking care of your natural curls, you can achieve healthy, beautiful curls that you will love.
