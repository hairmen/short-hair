Men s Hair Long On Top Short Sides And Back A Versatile And Stylish Look

Written by Aprilia Aug 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Men's hair long on top short sides and back has become increasingly popular in recent years. This hairstyle offers a versatile and stylish look that can be adapted to suit different hair types, face shapes, and personal styles. In this article, we will explore how to achieve this look, provide step-by-step instructions, offer tips for maintaining and styling your hair, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

The men's hair long on top short sides and back style is characterized by a longer length of hair on the top of the head, which is often styled in a variety of ways, such as a quiff, pompadour, or messy fringe. The sides and back of the head are typically cut short, often with a fade or undercut, which creates a contrast with the longer hair on top. This style can be adapted to suit different hair types, including straight, curly, wavy, and thick hair, and can be styled in different ways to suit different occasions.

How to Achieve This Look

If you want to achieve the men's hair long on top short sides and back look, you will need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in creating this style. The first step is to decide on the length of hair you want on top and the style you want to achieve. You may want to bring a picture of the style you like to show your stylist, so they can get an idea of what you're looking for.

The next step is to decide on the length of hair you want on the sides and back. This will depend on your personal preference and the style you want to achieve. A fade or undercut is a popular choice for this hairstyle, as it creates a sharp contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Once your hair has been cut to the desired length, your stylist will likely use a hair clipper to create a fade or undercut on the sides and back. They may also use scissors or a razor to trim and shape the hair on top. Once the haircut is complete, you can style your hair using a variety of products, such as wax, pomade, or gel.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving the men's hair long on top short sides and back look:

  1. Visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in creating this style.
  2. Decide on the length of hair you want on top and the style you want to achieve.
  3. Decide on the length of hair you want on the sides and back.
  4. Ask your stylist to use a hair clipper to create a fade or undercut on the sides and back.
  5. Ask your stylist to trim and shape the hair on top using scissors or a razor.
  6. Style your hair using a product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling your men's hair long on top short sides and back:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the length and shape of your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a styling product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider using a hairdryer to style your hair, as this can help to create volume and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hair long on top short sides and back:

Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can be adapted to suit different hair types, including straight, curly, wavy, and thick hair.

How often should I visit the barber or hairstylist?

You should visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your hair.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You should use a styling product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Wax, pomade, and gel are popular choices.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, this style can be adapted to suit different occasions. A more formal occasion may require a neater, more polished look, while a casual occasion may allow for a more relaxed, messy style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, strong, and shiny hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your men's hair long on top short sides and back:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy a stylish and versatile men's hair long on top short sides and back look that suits your personal style and enhances your overall appearance.
