How To Use Claw Clips On Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

If you have thick hair, you know that finding hair accessories that can hold all of your hair can be a challenge. Claw clips are a great option for those with thick hair, but they can be intimidating to use if you're not familiar with them. In this guide, we'll show you how to use claw clips on thick hair, as well as provide some tips and tricks for using them effectively.

How to Use Claw Clips

Claw clips are designed to hold large amounts of hair in place, making them a great option for those with thick hair. To use a claw clip, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Before using a claw clip, make sure to brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that the clip holds your hair in place.

Step 2: Gather your hair

Next, gather your hair into a ponytail or bun. Use your hands or a brush to smooth your hair and make sure that there are no bumps or lumps.

Step 3: Open the claw clip

Hold the claw clip in one hand and use your other hand to press down on the lever to open the clip. The teeth of the clip should be facing down.

Step 4: Position the clip

Place the clip at the base of your ponytail or bun, with the teeth facing up. Make sure that the clip is centered and that all of your hair is inside the clip.

Step 5: Close the clip

Use your fingers to close the clip, pressing down on the lever until it clicks into place. The clip should feel secure and hold your hair in place.

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to using claw clips on thick hair:

Step 1: Choose the right clip

When choosing a claw clip, look for one that is large enough to hold all of your hair. You may need to experiment with different sizes and styles to find the one that works best for you.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for using claw clips on thick hair:

  • Use multiple clips for extra hold. If you have very thick hair, you may need to use more than one clip to hold your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles. Claw clips can be used to create a variety of styles, from a classic bun to a messy updo.
  • Choose the right size clip. If your clip is too small, it won't be able to hold all of your hair. If it's too large, it may not feel secure.
  • Don't pull your hair too tight. If you pull your hair too tight when using a claw clip, it can cause breakage and damage.

FAQs

Can claw clips be used on all hair types?

Yes, claw clips can be used on all hair types. However, those with very fine or thin hair may find that the clip doesn't hold their hair well.

How do I clean my claw clip?

To clean your claw clip, simply wipe it down with a damp cloth or rinse it under running water. Make sure to dry it thoroughly before using it again.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

By following these tips and using claw clips correctly, you can create beautiful hairstyles that will hold all day long.
