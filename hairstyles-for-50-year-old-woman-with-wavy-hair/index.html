Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Wavy Hair

Written by Robby Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair undergoes changes in texture and condition. If you have wavy hair, you may have noticed that it has become more unruly and difficult to manage over the years. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can enhance your waves and make you look your best as a 50-year-old woman. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for 50 year old woman with wavy hair, along with tips on how to style and care for your locks.

Description

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it has natural body and texture that can be styled in many different ways. On the other hand, it can be prone to frizz and can become unruly in humid weather. As we age, our hair also becomes thinner and more delicate, which can make styling even more challenging. However, with the right haircut and styling products, you can enhance your waves and create a look that is both flattering and age-appropriate.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your wavy hair is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best cut and style for your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Some of the most popular hairstyles for 50 year old woman with wavy hair include:

1. Shoulder-length layers

Layers can help add volume and movement to your hair, creating a soft and feminine look. Shoulder-length layers are especially flattering for women over 50, as they can help frame the face and draw attention to your best features. To style, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow dry using a round brush. Use a curling iron to enhance your waves and add definition.

2. Bob with side-swept bangs

A bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. For women with wavy hair, a bob with side-swept bangs can be a chic and modern look. This hairstyle is easy to maintain and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To style, apply a smoothing serum to damp hair and blow dry with a paddle brush. Use a straightener to smooth out any frizz and create a sleek look.

3. Long layers with curtain bangs

If you have longer hair, long layers with curtain bangs can be a great option. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn straight or wavy, depending on your mood. Curtain bangs are also a great choice for women over 50, as they can help soften the face and create a youthful look. To style, apply a texturizing spray to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Allow your hair to air dry for a natural, beachy look.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or serum.

3. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to smooth out your hair and add volume.

4. Use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired look.

5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.


Tips

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.

4. Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

5. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

A: Yes, ponytails and buns can be a great option for women with wavy hair. Use a scrunchie or a hair tie that is gentle on your hair to avoid damage.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have wavy hair, you may be able to go longer between washes than someone with straight hair. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed to keep it clean and fresh.

Q: Can I color my hair if I have wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can color your hair if you have wavy hair. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and is appropriate for your age. Talk to your stylist about the best options for you.

Hair Care

The key to maintaining healthy, beautiful hair as a 50-year-old woman with wavy hair is to take good care of it. This means using the right products, avoiding damaging styling techniques, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. Some tips for caring for your hair include:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

- Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends

- Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type

By following these tips and choosing a flattering hairstyle, you can enhance your natural waves and look your best as a 50-year-old woman with wavy hair. Whether you prefer a short bob or long layers, there are plenty of options to choose from that will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural beauty.
