How To Know What Hair Color Will Suit You

Written by Frank Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair color but unsure of which shade to choose? With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to pick the right one. But fear not, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of determining which hair color will suit you best.

Description

Choosing the right hair color is important as it can enhance your features and make you feel confident. However, what looks good on your friend may not necessarily suit you. There are various factors to consider, such as your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

One of the key things to consider when choosing a hair color is your skin tone. There are three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can’t tell, you may have a neutral undertone.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Identify Your Skin Tone

As mentioned earlier, determining your skin tone is crucial in selecting the right hair color. Once you’ve identified your undertone, you can narrow down your choices.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

It’s important to consider your natural hair color when choosing a new shade. If you have dark hair, going for a lighter color may require more maintenance, while going for a darker shade may be easier to maintain. If you have light hair, going for a drastic change may damage your hair. It’s best to stick to shades that are within a few shades of your natural color.

Step 3: Look at Your Eye Color

Another factor to consider is your eye color. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop, while others may clash. For example, if you have blue eyes, going for a warm hair color such as honey blonde or copper can bring out the blue in your eyes.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair color, it’s important to consider your lifestyle. If you lead an active lifestyle, going for a low maintenance color may be best. On the other hand, if you have more time and resources to spend on your hair, you can go for a high maintenance color.

Tips

  • Consult with a professional stylist for advice on what hair color will suit you best.
  • Consider your personal style and preferences when choosing a hair color.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see what different shades would look like on you.

FAQ

What hair color suits warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with shades such as golden blonde, caramel, and warm brown.

What hair color suits cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with shades such as platinum blonde, ash brown, and black.

Can I dye my hair if it’s damaged?

It’s best to avoid dyeing damaged hair as it can cause further damage. It’s important to repair your hair before dyeing it.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain the color and prevent damage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for colored hair. Avoid washing your hair too often and use heat protectant products when styling with hot tools.

By following these tips, you can determine what hair color will suit you best and achieve your desired look. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and color!
