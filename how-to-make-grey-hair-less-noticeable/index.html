How To Make Grey Hair Less Noticeable Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Aug 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair naturally turns grey or white. While some people embrace this change, others prefer to keep their natural hair color. If you're someone who wants to make grey hair less noticeable, you're not alone. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to achieve that.

How to

There are several ways to make grey hair less noticeable. Here are some of them:

1. Hair Dye

The most obvious solution is to dye your hair. You can either go to a salon or do it yourself at home. There are many hair dyes available in the market, and you can choose one that matches your natural hair color. If you're not sure which color to pick, consult a hairstylist. They can help you choose the best shade for your skin tone and hair type.

2. Highlights or Lowlights

If you don't want to dye your hair completely, you can opt for highlights or lowlights. This involves adding subtle streaks of color to your hair, which can camouflage the grey strands. Highlights are lighter than your natural hair color, while lowlights are darker. You can also try a combination of both.

3. Hair Extensions

If you don't want to change your natural hair color, you can try hair extensions. They can add volume and length to your hair, and also cover the grey strands. There are many types of hair extensions available, such as clip-ins, tape-ins, and sew-ins. Choose one that suits your budget and lifestyle.

Step by Step

Here's how you can make grey hair less noticeable step by step:

Step 1: Choose a Hair Dye

If you decide to dye your hair, choose a hair dye that matches your natural hair color. You can either go to a salon or buy a hair dye kit from a drugstore. Follow the instructions carefully, and do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

Step 2: Apply the Hair Dye

If you're doing it yourself, apply the hair dye evenly to your hair. Start from the roots and work your way down to the ends. Leave it on for the recommended time, and then rinse it off thoroughly.

Step 3: Try Highlights or Lowlights

If you don't want to dye your hair completely, try highlights or lowlights. You can either do it yourself or go to a salon. Choose a shade that complements your natural hair color, and apply it to the desired areas.

Step 4: Consider Hair Extensions

If you want to cover the grey strands without changing your natural hair color, consider hair extensions. Choose a type that suits your lifestyle and budget, and get it installed by a professional.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make grey hair less noticeable:

1. Use a Color-Depositing Shampoo

If you don't want to dye your hair, use a color-depositing shampoo. It can add subtle color to your hair and also nourish it. Choose a shade that matches your natural hair color, and use it once a week.

2. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can keep your hair healthy and also remove the grey strands. Visit a hairstylist every six to eight weeks, and get a trim. They can also give you a new hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.

3. Use Hair Accessories

You can also use hair accessories to cover the grey strands. Try a headband, scarf, or hat. They can add style and also hide the grey strands.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin. Just make sure to do a patch test first, and use a hair dye kit that's suitable for sensitive skin.

Q: Can I use hair extensions if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can use hair extensions if you have thin hair. Just choose a type that's lightweight and doesn't damage your natural hair. Consult a hairstylist before getting them installed.

Q: How do I maintain my hair after dyeing it?

You can maintain your hair after dyeing it by using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your hair. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you dye your hair or use hair extensions, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair at least twice a week to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

2. Use a heat protectant

If you use hot tools such as flat irons and curling wands, use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

3. Eat a balanced diet

Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals. This can promote healthy hair growth and also prevent grey hair.

4. Get enough sleep

Get enough sleep to reduce stress and promote healthy hair growth. Lack of sleep can also lead to premature greying of hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can make grey hair less noticeable and maintain healthy hair. Choose a method that suits your lifestyle and budget, and consult a hairstylist if you're not sure. Remember to take care of your hair and embrace your natural beauty.
