Mens Skin Fade Hairstyles 2020 A Guide To The Latest Hair Trends

Written by Petter Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that’s both stylish and low-maintenance, the mens skin fade hairstyle might be perfect for you. This trendy haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it’s not hard to see why. With a fade that seamlessly blends the hair on the sides and back of the head into the longer hair on top, the skin fade creates a clean and modern look that’s perfect for any occasion.

How to Get a Mens Skin Fade Hairstyle

If you’re interested in getting a mens skin fade hairstyle, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience with fades and can give you the look you want. When you’re at the salon, be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you’re looking for. It’s also important to keep in mind that skin fades require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to getting a mens skin fade hairstyle:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Decide on the length you want for the hair on top
  • Ask your barber to use clippers to create a fade on the sides and back of your head, gradually blending the hair into the longer hair on top
  • Once the fade is complete, use scissors or clippers to trim the hair on top to the desired length and style
  • Finish with a styling product to keep your hair in place

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Skin Fade Hairstyle

Once you have your mens skin fade hairstyle, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips for maintaining your skin fade:

  • Get regular trims to keep the fade looking clean
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Apply a styling product to keep your hair in place
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf

FAQ About Mens Skin Fade Hairstyles

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens skin fade hairstyles:

Q: What’s the difference between a skin fade and a regular fade?

A: The main difference is that a skin fade is cut very close to the scalp, creating a “barely there” look, while a regular fade leaves more hair on the sides and back of the head.

Q: Can anyone get a skin fade?

A: Yes, anyone can get a skin fade, but it might not be the best choice for everyone. If you have a very round or square face, a skin fade might accentuate those features.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade starts to grow out. Typically, you’ll need to get a trim every 2-4 weeks to keep the fade looking sharp.

Hair Care for Mens Skin Fade Hairstyles

To keep your mens skin fade hairstyle looking its best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a styling product that’s appropriate for your hair type
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to maintain your mens skin fade hairstyle and keep it looking sharp for weeks to come!
