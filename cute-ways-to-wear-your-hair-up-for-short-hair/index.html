Cute Ways To Wear Your Hair Up For Short Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 10, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair, you might think your styling options are limited. However, there are plenty of cute ways to wear your hair up! Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there's an updo that will suit your needs. Read on for some tips and tricks on how to style your short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair doesn't have to be boring! By getting creative with your styling, you can create a variety of cute and unique looks. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, there are many ways to wear your hair up. From braids to buns to twists, you can experiment with different techniques to find the perfect look for you.

How to

If you're new to styling short hair, it can be intimidating to try new looks. However, with a little practice and patience, you can master a variety of updos. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Invest in some good hair accessories, such as bobby pins, hair elastics, and hair clips.
  • Watch tutorials online to learn new techniques and get inspiration.
  • Experiment with different products, such as hairspray and texturizing spray, to achieve the desired look.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new! Even if it doesn't turn out perfectly the first time, you can always try again.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few cute updos:

Twist and Pin

  1. Start by parting your hair to one side.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front and twist it away from your face.
  3. Secure the twist with a bobby pin.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Take the remaining hair and twist it into a messy bun at the nape of your neck.
  6. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and braid it all the way down to the end.
  3. Wrap the braid around the back of your head and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Take the remaining hair and twist it into a bun at the nape of your neck.
  6. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short hair:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to create different looks.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to your updos.
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hair looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair:

Q: Can I still curl my short hair?

A: Yes! You can use a curling wand or flat iron to create curls or waves in your short hair.

Q: How do I prevent my updo from falling out?

A: Use plenty of bobby pins and hairspray to hold your updo in place. You can also try using hair elastics or hair clips to secure your hair.

Hair Care

Keeping your short hair healthy and shiny is important for achieving a cute updo. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too greasy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and manageable.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a variety of cute updos for your short hair. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there's a style that will suit your needs. So why not experiment with some new looks and see what works best for you?
