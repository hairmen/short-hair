How To Get A Choppy Pixie Cut

Written by Frank Aug 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you ready for a bold new look? A choppy pixie cut can be a great way to shake things up and give yourself a fresh, modern style. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know to get the perfect choppy pixie cut, from how to prepare for the cut to how to style it once you're done.

Table of Contents

  • Preparing for the Cut
  • The Cut
  • Styling Your Pixie Cut
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

Preparing for the Cut

Before you head to the salon, there are a few things you should do to prepare for your choppy pixie cut. Here are some tips:

1. Decide on the length

Pixie cuts come in many different lengths, from super short to slightly longer. Think about what length you want and bring pictures to show your stylist.

2. Consider your face shape

Some pixie cuts look better on certain face shapes than others. Ask your stylist for advice on what will flatter your face shape the most.

3. Wash your hair

Make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products before you head to the salon. This will make it easier for your stylist to work with.

The Cut

Now that you're prepared, it's time for the cut. Here's what you can expect:

1. Your stylist will section off your hair

Your stylist will section off your hair and start cutting in small sections. They'll use scissors and/or clippers to create the choppy, textured look you're going for.

2. They'll shape your hair to your face

Your stylist will shape your hair to flatter your face shape and accentuate your best features. They may also add layers for extra texture.

3. The finishing touches

Your stylist will blow dry and style your hair to perfection. They may also add products to help hold the style in place.

Styling Your Pixie Cut

Now that you have your choppy pixie cut, it's time to style it. Here are some tips:

1. Use products

Use products like texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hair.

2. Experiment with different styles

Try different styles, like slicking your hair back or adding a side part, to find what works best for you.

3. Embrace the messiness

A choppy pixie cut looks best when it's a little bit messy. Don't be afraid to embrace the texture and let your hair do its thing.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your choppy pixie cut looking great, follow these tips:

1. Get regular trims

Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your cut looking fresh.

2. Use heat protectant

If you use heat styling tools, like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Deep condition regularly

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

What face shapes look best with a choppy pixie cut?

A choppy pixie cut can look great on many different face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on heart-shaped faces or faces with strong bone structure.

Can I still style my choppy pixie cut if I have fine hair?

Absolutely! A choppy pixie cut can actually be a great way to add volume and texture to fine hair.

Do I need to use a lot of product to style my choppy pixie cut?

Not necessarily. It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Experiment with different products and amounts to find what works best for you.
