How To Make Your Own Curly Hair Products

Written by William Sep 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on curly hair products that just don't work for your hair type? Or maybe you want to use more natural ingredients in your hair care routine? Whatever your reason may be, making your own curly hair products is easier than you might think. In this article, we will walk you through how to make your own curly hair products using simple ingredients that you may already have in your pantry.

Description

Curly hair is notoriously hard to manage, and finding the right products can be a challenge. Many curly hair products on the market can be expensive, and they may contain ingredients that can damage your hair over time. Making your own curly hair products allows you to control the ingredients and tailor your hair care routine to your specific hair type.

How to Make Your Own Curly Hair Products

The first step in making your own curly hair products is to determine what type of product you want to make. Some popular options include:

  • Shampoo
  • Conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl defining gel
  • Deep conditioning treatment

Once you have decided on the type of product you want to make, you can gather the necessary ingredients. We will provide specific recipes for each type of product in the step-by-step instructions section below.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Shampoo

To make your own curly hair shampoo, you will need:

  • 1/4 cup liquid castile soap
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 10 drops of essential oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix all of the ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Pour the mixture into a bottle with a pump top.
  3. Shake well before each use.

Conditioner

To make your own curly hair conditioner, you will need:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Mash the avocado in a bowl until it is smooth.
  2. Add the coconut milk, honey, and olive oil to the bowl and mix well.
  3. Apply the mixture to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Leave the conditioner on your hair for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Rinse the conditioner out with lukewarm water.

Leave-in Conditioner

To make your own curly hair leave-in conditioner, you will need:

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp aloe vera gel
  • 1 tbsp vegetable glycerin
  • 1 tsp jojoba oil
  • 10 drops of essential oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix all of the ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Pour the mixture into a spray bottle.
  3. Shake well before each use.

Curl Defining Gel

To make your own curly hair defining gel, you will need:

  • 1/2 cup flaxseed
  • 2 cups water
  • 10 drops of essential oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add the flaxseed and water to a pot and bring the mixture to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth.
  4. Allow the gel to cool and then add the essential oil (if using).
  5. Pour the gel into a jar or container with a lid.
  6. Store the gel in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

Deep Conditioning Treatment

To make your own curly hair deep conditioning treatment, you will need:

  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl until it is smooth.
  2. Add the honey and olive oil to the bowl and mix well.
  3. Apply the mixture to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Cover your hair with a shower cap or towel and leave the treatment on for 30 minutes.
  5. Rinse the treatment out with lukewarm water.

Tips for Making Your Own Curly Hair Products

  • Use high-quality ingredients to ensure the best results for your hair.
  • Experiment with different recipes to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Store your homemade products in a cool, dry place to prolong their shelf life.
  • Label your products with the date they were made so you know when to use them by.
  • Don't be afraid to add or subtract ingredients to suit your specific needs.

FAQs

Is it safe to make my own curly hair products?

Yes, making your own curly hair products is safe as long as you use high-quality ingredients and follow the recipes carefully.

Will making my own curly hair products save me money?

Yes, making your own curly hair products can be more cost-effective than buying commercial products.

Are homemade curly hair products as effective as store-bought products?

Yes, homemade curly hair products can be just as effective as store-bought products, and they may even be better for your hair since you can control the ingredients.

Curly Hair Care Tips

In addition to making your own curly hair products, there are a few other tips you can follow to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and making your own curly hair products, you can achieve healthy, beautiful curls that you will love.
