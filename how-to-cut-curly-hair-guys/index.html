How To Cut Curly Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to cut, especially for guys who want to keep their curls looking their best. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great curly haircut that will make you look and feel your best. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly hair guys, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining your new cut.

How to Cut Curly Hair Guys: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting curly hair is to gather the right tools. You'll need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to invest in a hair clip or two to help section off your hair as you cut.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. This will help you get a more accurate cut and make it easier to work with. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. Starting at the ends, gently work your way up to the roots. This will help prevent breakage and make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Section Off Your Hair

Use a hair clip to section off your hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, clipping each section out of the way as you go.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Starting at the back of your head, use your comb to guide your scissors as you cut. Cut small sections at a time, working your way forward until you've cut all the sections you've clipped out of the way. Remember to cut your hair dry, as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

After you've cut your hair, take a look in the mirror to check for evenness. If you notice any uneven sections, go back and trim them until your hair looks even all over.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style it. Use a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls and give them some bounce. You may also want to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help dry your hair without disturbing your curls.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Guys

Now that you know the basics of cutting curly hair guys, here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Cut your hair dry, as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.
  • Cut small sections at a time, working your way forward until you've cut all the sections you've clipped out of the way.
  • Check for evenness as you go, and trim any uneven sections until your hair looks even all over.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls and give them some bounce.
  • Consider getting a layered cut to help reduce bulk and give your curls some definition.

FAQ About Cutting Curly Hair Guys

Q: Can I cut my own curly hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own curly hair, but it can be challenging. We recommend seeking the help of a professional stylist if you're not confident in your own abilities.

Q: How often should I cut my curly hair?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it. We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your curls looking their best.

Q: Should I use a razor to cut my curly hair?

A: No, we don't recommend using a razor to cut curly hair. Razors can cause damage and breakage, which can lead to frizz and uneven curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Guys

Now that you've learned how to cut curly hair guys, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Get regular trims to help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your curly hair looking its best. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, you can achieve a great curly haircut that will make you look and feel your best.
