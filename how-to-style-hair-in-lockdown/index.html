How To Style Hair In Lockdown A Complete Guide

If you're stuck at home during a lockdown and struggling to find ways to style your hair, don't worry! With a little bit of creativity and some helpful tips, you can achieve a variety of stylish looks without ever leaving your house. In this article, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to style hair in lockdown.

How to Style Hair in Lockdown

When it comes to styling your hair during lockdown, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to start with clean, dry hair. This will help your hair hold its style better and prevent any unwanted frizz or flyaways. Second, make sure you have all the necessary tools and products on hand, such as a brush or comb, hair ties or bobby pins, and any styling products you prefer.

Once you have your tools and products ready, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles. Some popular options include:

  • Braids: Braids are a great option for lockdown because they're easy to do and can last for several days. Try a simple three-strand braid, a fishtail braid, or a Dutch braid.
  • Buns: Buns are another easy and stylish option. You can try a classic top knot, a messy bun, or a low bun at the nape of your neck.
  • Ponytails: Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Try a high ponytail for a sleek look, or a low ponytail for a more casual vibe.
  • Curls: If you want to add some texture and volume to your hair, try curling it with a curling iron or wand. You can opt for loose, beachy waves or tight, bouncy curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for how to style your hair in lockdown:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the hairstyle you want to create.
  3. Brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  4. Apply any styling products you prefer, such as mousse, hairspray, or texturizing spray.
  5. If you're doing a braid or ponytail, gather your hair into a high or low ponytail first.
  6. Divide your hair into sections if necessary.
  7. Start braiding, twisting, or curling your hair, working one section at a time.
  8. Secure your hairstyle with hair ties, bobby pins, or clips.
  9. Add any finishing touches, such as hairspray or hair oil.
  10. Enjoy your new lockdown hairstyle!

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some helpful tips for styling your hair during lockdown:

  • Invest in some high-quality hair accessories, such as hair ties that won't leave dents or damage your hair, or bobby pins that won't slip out.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to try new things, such as adding extensions or using different styling products.
  • If you're struggling with frizz or flyaways, try using a smoothing serum or hair oil.
  • Take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding excessive heat styling.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair in lockdown:

  • Q: Can I still color or cut my hair during lockdown?
  • A: It depends on the restrictions in your area. Some salons may be open for limited services, while others may be closed completely. Check with your local salon or hairstylist for more information.
  • Q: How often should I wash my hair during lockdown?
  • A: It's up to you and your hair type. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days without washing. Experiment to find what works best for you.
  • Q: What if I don't have any styling products?
  • A: You can still achieve a variety of hairstyles without styling products. Try braiding your hair while it's damp and letting it air dry for natural waves, or using bobby pins to create a messy bun.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using excessive heat styling, such as flat irons and curling wands, as this can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your hair at home during lockdown and achieve a variety of stylish looks. Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using the right products for your hair type. Happy styling!
