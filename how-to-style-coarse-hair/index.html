How To Style Coarse Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Coarse hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right styling techniques, you can make it work for you. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks to help you style your coarse hair and keep it looking its best.

  • How to Style Coarse Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Coarse Hair

Coarse hair can be difficult to style because it tends to be thick, rough, and prone to frizz. But with the right techniques, you can create a stylish and manageable look that suits your personality and lifestyle. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it’s clean and free of any product build-up. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for coarse hair to keep it soft and manageable.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Coarse hair can be dry and brittle, so it’s important to apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep it moisturized. This will also help to reduce frizz and make it easier to style.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you’re using heat tools like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Apply it evenly throughout your hair before using any heat tools.

4. Choose the Right Styling Products

When it comes to styling coarse hair, it’s important to choose the right products that will help you achieve the look you want. Look for products that are designed for coarse hair and provide hold and definition.

5. Experiment with Different Styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Whether you’re going for a sleek and straight look or a curly and voluminous one, there are plenty of styles to choose from.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you style your coarse hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for coarse hair. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it moisturized and reduce frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair. Work in small sections, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward. Use the round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.

4. Use a Flat Iron or Curling Iron

If you want to straighten or curl your hair, use a flat iron or curling iron. Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using any heat tools. Work in small sections and use the flat iron or curling iron to create the desired style.

5. Finish with Styling Products

Once you’ve achieved the desired style, finish with styling products that provide hold and definition. Use a small amount of product and work it through your hair evenly.

Tips for Styling Coarse Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your coarse hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Look for products that are designed for coarse hair and provide hold and definition. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it more difficult to manage.

2. Don’t Over-Wash Your Hair

Coarse hair can be dry and brittle, so it’s important not to over-wash it. Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week and use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for coarse hair.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and damaging your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

4. Don’t Over-Process Your Hair

Avoid over-processing your hair with chemicals like hair dye or relaxers. These can weaken your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

5. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you’re using heat tools like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can also lead to damage.

FAQs

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my coarse hair?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized and reduce frizz. You can also try using a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my coarse hair?

A: Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for coarse hair.

Q: Can I use hair dye on coarse hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair dye on coarse hair. However, it’s important not to over-process your hair with chemicals, as this can weaken it and make it more prone to breakage.

Hair Care for Coarse Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your coarse hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for coarse hair to keep it soft and manageable.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it moisturized and reduce frizz.

3. Avoid Over-Processing Your Hair

Avoid over-processing your hair with chemicals like hair dye or relaxers. These can weaken your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you’re using heat tools like a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can also lead to damage.

5. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 8-12 weeks.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your coarse hair and keep it looking its best. By using the right products and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look that suits your personality and lifestyle.
