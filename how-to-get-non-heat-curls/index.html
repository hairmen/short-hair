How To Get Non Heat Curls A Step By Step Guide

Introduction

Non heat curls are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair without the damage that comes with using heat tools. There are many different methods for achieving non heat curls, including braiding, twisting, and using foam rollers. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to get non heat curls and tips for maintaining them.

How to Get Non Heat Curls

There are many different methods for achieving non heat curls, but some of the most popular include:

  • Braiding
  • Twisting
  • Using foam rollers

Each method will produce a slightly different type of curl, so it's important to experiment and find the method that works best for your hair type and desired outcome.

Step-by-Step Guide

Braiding

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

2. Divide hair into sections.

3. Braid each section tightly, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends.

4. Secure the end of each braid with a hair tie or clip.

5. Leave braids in overnight or until hair is completely dry.

6. Undo braids and gently separate curls with your fingers.

Twisting

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

2. Divide hair into sections.

3. Twist each section tightly, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends.

4. Secure the end of each twist with a hair tie or clip.

5. Leave twists in overnight or until hair is completely dry.

6. Undo twists and gently separate curls with your fingers.

Using Foam Rollers

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

2. Divide hair into sections.

3. Roll each section onto a foam roller, starting at the end and rolling up towards the root.

4. Secure the foam roller with a clip or hair tie.

5. Leave rollers in overnight or until hair is completely dry.

6. Undo rollers and gently separate curls with your fingers.

Tips for Maintaining Non Heat Curls

  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep curls in place.
  • Avoid brushing or combing curls, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat tools on non heat curls, as this can cause damage and undo the curl pattern.

FAQs

How long will non heat curls last?

The longevity of non heat curls will depend on your hair type, the method used to achieve the curls, and how well you maintain them. Generally, non heat curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Can I use non heat curls on short hair?

Absolutely! Non heat curls can be achieved on hair of any length. Just adjust the size of the sections you are braiding, twisting, or rolling to fit your hair length.

Can I use non heat curls on color-treated hair?

Yes, non heat curls can be used on color-treated hair. However, it's important to use products that are safe for color-treated hair and to avoid excessive manipulation that can cause breakage.

Hair Care for Non Heat Curls

To keep your non heat curls looking their best, it's important to follow a hair care routine that includes:

  • Regular washing and conditioning with products that are safe for your hair type and any color treatments.
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling or chemical treatments that can damage hair.
  • Trimming hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve beautiful non heat curls that last for days. Experiment with different methods and find the one that works best for your hair type and desired outcome.
