How To Choose The Right Haircut For Your Face

Written by Christine Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right haircut for your face is crucial to achieving your desired look. You want to make sure that the haircut complements your facial features and enhances your overall appearance. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right haircut for your face, step by step, with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Research Hairstyles
  • Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Pay attention to the width and length of your face, as well as the shape of your jawline.

If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that add height and lengthen your face. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any haircut. If you have a square face, choose hairstyles with soft, rounded edges to balance out your angular features. If you have a heart-shaped face, choose hairstyles that draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. If you have a diamond-shaped face, choose hairstyles that add width at the cheekbones. If you have an oblong face, choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The second step in choosing the right haircut for your face is to consider your hair type. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a shorter haircut to avoid looking weighed down. If you have fine, straight hair, you may want to opt for a longer haircut to add volume and dimension.

Other factors to consider when choosing a haircut for your hair type include the texture, density, and color of your hair. Your hairstylist can help you determine which hairstyles will work best with your hair type.

Step 3: Research Hairstyles

The third step in choosing the right haircut for your face is to research hairstyles that complement your face shape and hair type. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles. Create a Pinterest board or save photos to your phone to show your hairstylist.

When researching hairstyles, be realistic about the amount of time and effort you are willing to put into styling your hair. Choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

The fourth and final step in choosing the right haircut for your face is to consult with your hairstylist. Your hairstylist is trained to analyze your facial features and hair type to recommend the best haircut for you. They can also provide advice on styling and maintenance.

During your consultation, be honest about your hair history, lifestyle, and styling habits. This will help your hairstylist determine which hairstyles will work best for you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

  • Choose a haircut that complements your facial features and enhances your overall appearance.
  • Consider your hair type, texture, density, and color when choosing a haircut.
  • Research hairstyles that fit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  • Be open to trying new hairstyles and experimenting with different looks.

FAQs

How often should I get a haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health and shape of your hair.

Can I pull off a short haircut?

Short haircuts can work for almost any face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which short hairstyles will work best for you.

What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you are unhappy with your new haircut, talk to your hairstylist about your concerns. They may be able to adjust the haircut to better suit your preferences.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and steps, you can choose the right haircut for your face and maintain its health and shape. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and be open to trying new hairstyles to achieve your desired look.
