Where To Get A Good Haircut In Singapore The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you on the hunt for a great haircut in Singapore? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll be sharing our top picks for places to get a good haircut in Singapore. From high-end salons to budget-friendly options, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Singapore is known for its trendy fashion and beauty scene, and that includes its hair salons. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide where to go for your next haircut. That’s why we’ve done the research for you and compiled a list of the best places to get a good haircut in Singapore.

How to Find the Right Hair Salon

Before we dive into our top picks for hair salons in Singapore, let’s talk about how to find the right salon for you. Here are some things to consider:

1. Your Budget

The first thing to consider is your budget. Haircuts in Singapore can range from budget-friendly to high-end. Decide how much you’re willing to spend before you start your search.

2. Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. Different salons specialize in different hair types, so be sure to look for a salon that caters to your hair type.

3. Location

The location of the salon is also important. Look for a salon that is convenient for you to get to, whether that’s close to your home or office.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to look for in a hair salon, let’s dive into our top picks for places to get a good haircut in Singapore.

1. Toni & Guy

Toni & Guy is a well-known salon chain that has locations all over the world, including Singapore. They offer a wide range of services, including haircuts, coloring, and styling. Their stylists are highly trained and experienced, so you can trust that you’ll get a great haircut.

2. The Big Blow

The Big Blow is a popular salon in Singapore that specializes in blowouts and updos. They also offer haircuts and coloring services. Their stylists are known for their attention to detail and ability to create a variety of looks.

3. Artistry Hair

Artistry Hair is a boutique salon in Singapore that offers a wide range of services, including haircuts, coloring, and styling. They specialize in creating personalized looks for each client, so you can be sure that you’ll get a haircut that is tailored to your individual style.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Now that you know where to go for a great haircut in Singapore, here are some tips to ensure that you get the best possible haircut:

1. Bring Inspiration

Bring pictures or inspiration to your appointment so that your stylist can get a clear idea of the look you’re going for.

2. Be Honest

Be honest with your stylist about your hair type, lifestyle, and styling habits. This will help them create a haircut that works for you.

3. Communicate During the Cut

Don’t be afraid to speak up during the haircut if you’re not happy with how it’s turning out. Your stylist wants you to be happy with the result, so they will appreciate your feedback.

FAQ

1. How much does a haircut cost in Singapore?

Haircuts in Singapore can range from as low as $10 to as high as $300, depending on the salon and the stylist.

2. How often should I get a haircut?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

1. Use Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair every other day, or less frequently if possible.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With this guide, you’re now equipped with all the information you need to find the perfect place to get a good haircut in Singapore. Whether you’re looking for a high-end salon or a budget-friendly option, there’s something for everyone in this vibrant city.
