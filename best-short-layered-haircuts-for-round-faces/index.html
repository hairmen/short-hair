Best Short Layered Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Frank Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin, which can make it difficult to find a flattering haircut. Short layered haircuts are a great option for round faces, as they add volume and movement to the hair, which can help to balance out the face. In this article, we will be discussing the best short layered haircuts for round faces, and providing tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short layered haircuts are a popular choice for women with round faces, as they can add volume and movement to the hair, which can help to balance out the face. There are a variety of different short layered haircuts to choose from, including pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and shag haircuts. The key is to find a haircut that complements your face shape and brings out your best features.

How to

The first step in choosing a short layered haircut for a round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you likely have a round face. Once you have determined your face shape, you can begin looking for short layered haircuts that will flatter your features.

Step by Step

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that is perfect for round faces. To create this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving the top longer. This will help to create the illusion of length and height, which can help to balance out your face. You can style your pixie cut with a side part or slicked back for a more edgy look.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is another great option for women with round faces. To create this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair to chin length, with layers that frame your face. This will help to create the illusion of length and add volume to your hair. You can style your bob with a middle part or side part, and add waves or curls for a more romantic look.

3. Shag Haircut

A shag haircut is a layered haircut that is perfect for adding volume and movement to your hair. To create this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair into layers, with the shortest layers at the crown of your head. You can style your shag haircut with a messy, tousled look, or sleek and straight for a more polished look.

Tips

  • Choose a short layered haircut that complements your face shape and brings out your best features.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and styling tools to achieve the perfect look.

FAQ

What is the best short layered haircut for a round face?

The best short layered haircut for a round face will depend on your individual features and personal style. Pixie cuts, bob haircuts, and shag haircuts are all great options for round faces.

How do I style my short layered haircut?

You can style your short layered haircut with a variety of different techniques, including blow drying, curling, and straightening. Experiment with different styling tools and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered haircut looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Consider investing in a quality hair mask or treatment to keep your hair healthy and strong.

In conclusion, short layered haircuts are a great option for women with round faces. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a look that is both flattering and stylish. Follow our tips and advice to find the best short layered haircut for your face shape, and keep your hair looking its best with proper care and maintenance.
