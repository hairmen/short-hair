How To Wear A Wig With A Shaved Head

Written by Robby Jun 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a shaved head and want to wear a wig, you may be wondering how to go about it. Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people wear wigs for a variety of reasons, including hair loss or just for fun. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a wig with a shaved head, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before you start, you need to make sure you have the right wig for your head. Look for a wig that is designed for a shaved head or has a cap that will fit snugly. You may also want to consider a wig with a lace front, which will give you a more natural-looking hairline.

Once you have your wig, you can follow these steps:

Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare Your Head

Make sure your head is clean and free of any oils or lotions. You can use a wig cap to help keep your wig in place, but it's not necessary.

Step 2: Put on the Wig

Hold the wig at the sides and slide it over your head from front to back. Adjust the wig until it sits comfortably on your head. You may need to use the ear tabs to adjust the placement of the wig.

Step 3: Secure the Wig

Use the adjustable straps at the back of the wig to tighten or loosen the fit. You can also use hairpins to secure the wig to your head.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you wear your wig comfortably and confidently:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your wig in place and protect your scalp from irritation.
  • Experiment with different styles and colors to find the one that suits you best.
  • Take care of your wig by washing it regularly and storing it properly.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a wig with a shaved head:

Can I wear a wig if I have a shaved head?

Yes, you can wear a wig with a shaved head. Just make sure you have the right type of wig and follow the steps above to put it on.

Do I need to use a wig cap?

No, you don't need to use a wig cap, but it can help keep your wig in place and protect your scalp from irritation.

How do I care for my wig?

Follow the care instructions that come with your wig. Generally, you should wash your wig with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry on a wig stand.

Hair Care

If you have a shaved head, it's important to take care of your scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp with a gentle lotion or oil.
  • Protect your scalp from the sun by wearing a hat or using sunscreen.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.

By following these tips, you can wear a wig with a shaved head and feel confident and comfortable.
