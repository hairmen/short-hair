Will Cutting My Hair Short Make It Look Thicker

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine or thin hair, you may be wondering if cutting your hair short can make it look thicker. The answer is yes, but there are some things you should know before making the chop. In this article, we'll explore how cutting your hair short can create the illusion of thicker hair, as well as provide tips for maintaining and caring for your new style.

Description

When it comes to hair, thickness is often associated with volume. The more hair you have, the more volume you can create. However, if you have fine or thin hair, it can be challenging to achieve that full, voluminous look. That's where cutting your hair short comes in. By removing length, you can create the illusion of thicker hair because there is less hair weighing down your roots.

How To

If you're considering cutting your hair short to make it look thicker, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short style for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose a style that adds volume and texture, such as a layered bob or pixie cut.
  3. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.
  4. Invest in quality hair products that are designed to add volume and texture.
  5. Maintain regular trims to keep your style looking fresh and healthy.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your hair short to make it look thicker:

  1. Wash and dry your hair, then brush it out to remove any tangles.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Starting with the back section, use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Move on to the side sections, cutting them to match the length of the back.
  5. Finish with the top section, cutting it slightly shorter than the sides and back to create volume.
  6. Use thinning shears to remove bulk and add texture if needed.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your short hair look thicker:

  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying to add texture and hold.
  • Experiment with different partings to create volume and texture.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and serums.
  • Consider investing in a good quality hair topper or extensions if you want even more volume.

FAQ

Will cutting my hair short make it look thicker?

Yes, cutting your hair short can create the illusion of thicker hair because there is less hair weighing down your roots.

What is the best short hairstyle for fine or thin hair?

A layered bob or pixie cut with added texture and volume is a great option for fine or thin hair.

How can I maintain my short hair to keep it looking thick?

Regular trims, quality hair products, and blow-drying upside down can help maintain the volume and texture of your short hair.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short hair, it's important to use products that are designed to add volume and texture. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that contain ingredients such as keratin, biotin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look flat and lifeless. Instead, try washing every other day or using a dry shampoo in between washes. Finally, don't forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using styling tools.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create the illusion of thicker hair by cutting it short. With the right style, products, and care, you can rock a short, voluminous look that is sure to turn heads.
