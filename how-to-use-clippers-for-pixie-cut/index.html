How To Use Clippers For Pixie Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering a pixie cut, you're in the right place! Pixie cuts are a popular hairstyle that can give you a fresh and trendy look. However, getting a pixie cut can be intimidating, especially if you're using clippers for the first time. In this article, we'll guide you step-by-step on how to use clippers for a pixie cut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Clippers for Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Pixie Cut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After a Pixie Cut

How to Use Clippers for Pixie Cut

Before we start, it's important to have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, hair scissors, and hair clips. Make sure your clippers have different sizes of guards, as this will determine the length of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when using clippers for a pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will make it easier to cut and style your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: front, back, and two sides. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard

Choose the right guard size for the length you want. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter size if needed.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the back of your head, moving towards the front. Use the clippers to trim the hair in a straight line. Make sure to hold them at a 45-degree angle to get a smooth cut.

Step 5: Move to the Sides

Move to the sides of your head and use the clippers to cut the hair in a straight line. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Cut the top of your hair with hair scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to your desired length.

Step 7: Trim the Edges

Use the clippers to trim the edges around your ears and the back of your neck.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Once you're done cutting, style your hair as desired. You can use a hair product like wax or pomade to add texture and hold.

Tips for a Perfect Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using clippers for a pixie cut:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter size if needed.
  • Use hair scissors to cut the top of your hair for a more precise cut.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  • Use a hair product like wax or pomade to add texture and hold.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using clippers for a pixie cut:

  • Can I use clippers on wet hair? No, it's best to use clippers on dry hair as wet hair can cause the clippers to slip.
  • Can I cut my hair myself? Yes, you can cut your hair yourself if you're comfortable using clippers. However, it's best to have someone else help you, especially for hard-to-reach areas.
  • How often should I trim my hair? It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care After a Pixie Cut

After getting a pixie cut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons as much as possible.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the style and keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to use clippers for a perfect pixie cut. Remember to take care of your hair after the cut to keep it healthy and shiny.
