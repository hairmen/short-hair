Wavy Short Sides Long Top Hairstyles The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Aug 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle, the wavy short sides long top hairstyle might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and it's not hard to see why. With its classic and versatile look, it's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about wavy short sides long top hairstyles, including how to achieve the look, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style
  • FAQs

How to Achieve the Look

The wavy short sides long top hairstyle is all about contrast. It's a classic look that involves short sides and a longer top that's styled in waves. To achieve this look, you'll need to have some length on top of your head. The length can vary depending on how long you want the hair to be. The sides and the back should be short, usually a number two or three on the clippers.

When it comes to styling the hair, you'll need to use a hair product that provides hold and texture. Pomade or wax works well for this hairstyle. Apply the product to damp hair and use your fingers to create waves on the top of your head. You can also use a comb to create more defined waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

To achieve the wavy short sides long top hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply your preferred hair product to your hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly.
  3. Use your fingers to create waves on the top of your head. You can also use a comb to create more defined waves.
  4. Style the sides and back of your hair with clippers. Use a number two or three to achieve the short sides and back.
  5. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining the wavy short sides long top hairstyle:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back of your hair to maintain the short length.
  • Use a hair product that provides hold and texture to keep the waves in place.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from getting too greasy.
  • Avoid using too much product as this can weigh down the hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the wavy short sides long top hairstyle:

  • Can this hairstyle be worn by both men and women?
  • What length should the hair be on top?
  • What type of hair product should I use?
  • How often should I wash my hair?

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too frequently as this can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

With these tips and the right hair product, you can easily achieve the wavy short sides long top hairstyle. It's a classic look that's perfect for any occasion and can be worn by both men and women. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance, and you'll have a stylish and trendy hairstyle that you can be proud of.
