How To Style A Thick Headband

Written by Aprilia Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've been searching for a way to add some extra style to your hair, look no further than the thick headband. This versatile accessory can be worn with a variety of hairstyles, making it a must-have in any fashionista's collection. In this post, we'll show you how to style a thick headband in a few simple steps, along with some helpful tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Thick Headband
  • Step-by-Step

How to Style a Thick Headband

One of the best things about a thick headband is how easy it is to style. Here are a few popular looks you can try:

1. The Classic Headband

The classic headband look is timeless and works with virtually any hairstyle. Simply place the headband over your hairline, making sure it's sitting comfortably on your head. You can wear it with your hair down or up, depending on your preference.

2. The Half-Up, Half-Down Look

If you want to add a little extra style to your classic headband look, try pulling half of your hair up and securing it with a clip or elastic. This creates a chic, effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.

3. The Top Knot

For a more polished look, try pulling your hair up into a top knot and securing it with a hair tie. Then, place the headband over your hairline and adjust it so it's sitting just above your ears. This look is perfect for a day at the office or a night out on the town.

Step-by-Step

Now that you know a few popular headband styles, here's how to achieve them:

1. The Classic Headband

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Place the headband over your hairline, making sure it's sitting comfortably on your head.
  3. Adjust the headband so it's sitting just above your ears.
  4. You're done! Wear your hair up or down, depending on your preference.

2. The Half-Up, Half-Down Look

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and pull it back, securing it with a clip or elastic.
  3. Place the headband over your hairline, making sure it's sitting comfortably on your head.
  4. Adjust the headband so it's sitting just above your ears.
  5. You're done! Enjoy your effortlessly chic look.

3. The Top Knot

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Pull your hair up into a top knot and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Place the headband over your hairline, making sure it's sitting comfortably on your head.
  4. Adjust the headband so it's sitting just above your ears.
  5. You're done! Enjoy your polished, put-together look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few helpful tips to keep in mind when styling your thick headband:

  • Choose a headband that complements your hair color and skin tone.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to add some extra accessories, like earrings or a necklace, to complete your look.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a thick headband with curly hair?

A: Yes, absolutely! A thick headband looks great with all hair types, including curly hair.

Q: Can I wear a headband with short hair?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a headband that's not too wide, so it doesn't overpower your hair.

Q: How do I keep my headband from slipping?

A: If your headband is slipping, try spraying a little hairspray on the inside of the band before putting it on. This will help it grip your hair better.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Now that you know how to style a thick headband, it's time to experiment with different looks and find the one that works best for you. Whether you're going for a classic, effortless look or a polished, put-together style, a thick headband is the perfect accessory to add some extra flair to your hair.
