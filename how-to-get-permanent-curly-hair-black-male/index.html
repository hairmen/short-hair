How To Get Permanent Curly Hair For Black Men

Written by Daniel Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch it up with some permanent curls? If so, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll discuss how to get permanent curly hair for black men. From the basics to the pro tips, we've got you covered.

Description

Many black men have straight hair, but they want to have permanent curls. Curly hair can add volume and texture to your hair, and it can be a great way to switch up your look. There are many ways to get permanent curls, but it's important to find the right method for your hair type and texture.

How to Get Permanent Curly Hair for Black Men

Step 1: Choose the Right Method

There are many methods to get permanent curls, but the two most popular methods are the perm and the texturizer. A perm uses chemicals to break down the bonds in your hair and then reform them into a curly shape. A texturizer is a milder form of a perm that loosens the curl pattern of your hair.

Step 2: Find a Professional

It's important to find a professional who knows how to work with black hair. Not all hair stylists are trained to work with black hair, so make sure you find someone who is experienced in working with your hair type.

Step 3: Consultation

Before you get the perm or texturizer, it's important to have a consultation with your stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and texture and recommend the best method for you. They will also be able to tell you what to expect during the process and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Step 4: Pre-treatment

Before the perm or texturizer, your hair will need to be pre-treated. This involves washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up and oils that may interfere with the chemicals. Your hair will also be conditioned to protect it from damage.

Step 5: The Perm or Texturizer Process

The perm or texturizer process will involve applying the chemicals to your hair and then wrapping it around rods to create the curls. The chemicals will then be rinsed out, and a neutralizing solution will be applied to set the curls.

Step 6: Aftercare

After getting a perm or texturizer, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the curls. This includes using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Tips

  • Don't wash your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm or texturizer.
  • Avoid swimming or sweating excessively for the first few days after getting a perm or texturizer.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after getting a perm or texturizer.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

FAQ

How long does a perm or texturizer last?

A perm or texturizer can last for several months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I still straighten my hair after getting a perm or texturizer?

Yes, but you should wait at least two weeks before using any heat styling tools on your hair. Using heat styling tools too soon after getting a perm or texturizer can damage your hair.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm or texturizer?

You should wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair after getting a perm or texturizer. Coloring your hair too soon after the perm or texturizer can damage your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain your permanent curls, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. You should also deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

Getting permanent curls can be a great way to switch up your look, but it's important to find the right method for your hair type and texture. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve permanent curls that will last for months.
