Hairstyles For Fine Medium Length Hair 2020

Written by Oliver Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine medium length hair, you may find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type. Fortunately, there are several hairstyles that can give your hair volume and texture while accentuating your facial features. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for fine medium length hair in 2020.

Description

Fine medium length hair is hair that is not too thick or too thin and falls between the chin and the shoulders. This type of hair can be easily styled with the right haircut and can look great with different hairstyles. However, it can also look flat and lifeless if not styled properly.

How to

The key to styling fine medium length hair is to add volume and texture to it. Here are some hairstyles you can try:

1. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that can look great on fine medium length hair. It can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A layered bob can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

2. Shag Haircut

A shag haircut is a layered haircut that can give your hair more texture and movement. It can be styled with layers that start from the crown of your head and go down to the ends of your hair. This type of haircut can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

3. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and texture to your fine medium length hair. It can be styled with layers that start from the chin and go down to the ends of your hair. This type of haircut can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to style your fine medium length hair:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add more volume to your hair.

2. Curl Your Hair

Curl your hair with a curling iron to add more texture and movement to your hair. Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

3. Use Hair Accessories

Use hair accessories such as hair clips or headbands to add more style to your hair. You can also use a hairband to create a messy bun or a ponytail.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your fine medium length hair:

1. Use Quality Hair Products

Use quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. This can help prevent damage to your hair and keep it healthy.

2. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This can help your hair look more voluminous and textured.

3. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat-styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often. This can damage your hair and make it look flat and lifeless.

FAQ

Q. What is the best haircut for fine medium length hair?

A. The best haircut for fine medium length hair is a layered bob or a shag haircut. These haircuts can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

Q. How can I add volume to my fine medium length hair?

A. You can add volume to your fine medium length hair by blow drying your hair with a round brush, using a volumizing mousse or spray, and curling your hair with a curling iron.

Q. How often should I get a haircut for my fine medium length hair?

A. You should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your fine medium length hair healthy:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for your hair type. This can help prevent damage to your hair and keep it healthy.

2. Use a Conditioner

Use a conditioner to moisturize your hair and prevent dryness. This can help your hair look more healthy and shiny.

3. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Avoid overwashing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. This can make your hair look dry and lifeless.

In conclusion, styling fine medium length hair can be challenging but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a voluminous and textured look. Try out these hairstyles and tips to give your hair a new look in 2020.
