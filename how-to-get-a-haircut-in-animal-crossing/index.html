How To Get A Haircut In Animal Crossing A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 · 3 min read

Animal Crossing is a popular game where you build your own world, collect resources, and interact with other animals. One of the fun things you can do in the game is get a haircut. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to get a haircut in Animal Crossing, including tips and FAQs.

How to Get a Haircut in Animal Crossing

Getting a haircut in Animal Crossing is easy. All you need to do is visit the salon in your town, which is run by Harriet the poodle. Once you’re inside the salon, you can choose from a variety of hairstyles and colors.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get a haircut in Animal Crossing:

  1. Visit the salon in your town
  2. Talk to Harriet the poodle
  3. Select the “Makeover” option
  4. Choose a hairstyle and color
  5. Confirm your new look
  6. Pay for your new haircut

That’s it! You now have a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing.

Tips for Getting a Haircut in Animal Crossing

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut in Animal Crossing:

  • Make sure you have enough bells to pay for your new haircut
  • Try on different hairstyles before you commit to one
  • Consider getting a haircut to match the season or a special event
  • Don’t forget to save your game after your new haircut

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a haircut in Animal Crossing:

  • How much does a haircut cost in Animal Crossing?

    • A haircut in Animal Crossing costs 3,000 bells.

  • Can I change my hairstyle again?

    • Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want.

  • Do I need to provide any materials for my new hairstyle?

    • No, you don’t need to provide any materials for your new hairstyle. Harriet will take care of everything.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Once you have a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Wear a hat or umbrella to protect your hair from the sun and rain
  • Use a hairbrush to keep your hair looking neat
  • Visit the salon for a trim every few weeks

With these tips, you can keep your hair looking great in Animal Crossing.

Getting a haircut in Animal Crossing is a fun way to change up your look. With this guide, you can easily get a new hairstyle and start showing off your new ‘do to your animal friends.
