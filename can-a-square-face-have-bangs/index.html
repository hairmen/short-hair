Can A Square Face Have Bangs

Description

Having a square face shape can be tricky when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. Bangs, in particular, can be a daunting decision for those with a square face. But the good news is, yes, a square face can definitely have bangs! The key is to choose the right type of bangs that will complement and soften the angles of the face.

How to

When deciding on bangs for a square face, it's important to consider the width of the forehead and the jawline. Ideally, the bangs should be longer on the sides to create a softer, more rounded look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider the length of the bangs

Longer bangs that hit just below the eyebrows or even longer can be a great choice for a square face. This style will help to elongate the face and soften the strong jawline.

2. Avoid blunt bangs

Blunt bangs that are cut straight across can make the face look even more angular. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or choppy, textured bangs that will create movement and soften the edges of the face.

3. Choose the right thickness

Thick, heavy bangs can overwhelm a square face. Instead, choose a more wispy or feathered style that will create a softer, more feminine look.

Step by step

Here's how to get bangs that will flatter a square face:

Step 1: Consult with a stylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and give you tips on how to style them at home.

Step 2: Decide on the length and style

Based on your consultation with your stylist, decide on the length and style of bangs that will suit your face shape best.

Step 3: Wash and blow-dry your hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair as you normally would. Make sure your bangs are dry before cutting them.

Step 4: Cut the bangs

Using sharp hair cutting scissors, carefully cut the bangs to the desired length and style. It's always better to start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter, rather than cutting them too short right away.

Step 5: Style the bangs

Using a round brush, blow-dry the bangs to create volume and movement. You can also use a straightening iron to create a sleeker look. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling bangs on a square face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to the hair.
  • Blow-dry the bangs in the direction that you want them to fall.
  • Use a small amount of hair serum or oil to tame any frizz or flyaways.
  • Experiment with different styles of bangs to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can a square face have short bangs?

A: Short bangs can be tricky for a square face, as they can make the face look even more angular. However, if you're set on having short bangs, opt for a choppy, textured style that will soften the edges of the face.

Q: Can a square face have blunt bangs?

A: Blunt bangs can be difficult to pull off with a square face, as they can make the face look even more boxy. However, if you really want blunt bangs, opt for a longer length that hits just above the eyebrows.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is key to maintaining a great hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish the hair.

With the right type of bangs and some careful styling, a square face can definitely rock this trendy hairstyle. So go ahead and give it a try!
