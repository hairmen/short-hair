What Hair Colour Makes Me Look Younger A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to turn back the clock on your appearance, changing your hair colour is a great place to start. But with so many options out there, it can be hard to know where to begin. In this article, we'll explore what hair colour makes you look younger and provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve it.

Description

As we age, our skin tone changes, and so does our hair. To look more youthful, you'll want to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and brightens your complexion. The right hair colour can also help to disguise grey hairs and add volume and shine to your locks.

So, what hair colour makes you look younger? In general, warm, natural-looking shades tend to be the most flattering. Think honey blonde, caramel brown, or auburn. These shades all have a warm undertone that can help to brighten your complexion and give you a youthful glow.

How to Choose a Hair Colour That Makes You Look Younger

The first step in choosing a hair colour that makes you look younger is to determine your skin's undertone. There are three main undertones: cool, warm, and neutral. To figure out your undertone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Once you've determined your undertone, choose a hair colour that complements it. If you have a cool undertone, look for hair colours with ash or cool tones, such as platinum blonde or ash brown. If you have a warm undertone, opt for hair colours with warm tones, such as golden blonde or chestnut brown. If you have a neutral undertone, you can pull off both cool and warm tones.

Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

Another factor to consider when choosing a hair colour is your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, going too light can make you look washed out and older. Instead, opt for a shade that's only a few shades lighter than your natural colour. If you have light hair, going too dark can have the same effect. Stick with a shade that's only a few shades darker than your natural colour.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving a Youthful Hair Colour

Ready to achieve a more youthful hair colour? Follow these steps:

  1. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin's undertone.
  2. Consider your natural hair colour and choose a shade that's only a few shades lighter or darker.
  3. If you're dyeing your hair at home, follow the instructions carefully and conduct a patch test first.
  4. If you're going to a salon, bring photos of the hair colour you want and discuss your options with your stylist.
  5. After dyeing your hair, use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your colour.

Tips for Maintaining a Youthful Hair Colour

To keep your hair looking youthful and vibrant, follow these tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip away colour and natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but be sure to follow the instructions carefully and conduct a patch test first to avoid an allergic reaction. If you're unsure, it's best to go to a salon.

How often should I wash my hair?

Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip away colour and natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days instead.

What should I do if I don't like my new hair colour?

If you don't like your new hair colour, don't panic. Most hair colours fade over time, so you can wait it out or try a colour-correcting product. If you're really unhappy, you can always go back to your natural colour or try a different shade.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Youthful-Looking Hair

To keep your hair looking healthy and youthful, follow these tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients that promote hair health, such as biotin, vitamin E, and omega-3 fatty acids.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair colour can make a big difference in your appearance. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve a youthful, vibrant hair colour that complements your skin tone and brightens your complexion. Good luck!
