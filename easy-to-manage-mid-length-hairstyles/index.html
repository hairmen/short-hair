Easy To Manage Mid Length Hairstyles

Written by Oliver Jul 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with long hair? Or maybe you're ready to switch things up and try a new look? Mid length hairstyles are a great option for those looking for something that's easy to manage and versatile. Whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair, there's a mid length style out there for you. In this article, we'll go over some easy to manage mid length hairstyles, how to achieve them, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Mid length hairstyles typically fall between the shoulders and collarbone. This length is easy to maintain and style, making it a great option for those with busy schedules or those who don't want to spend hours on their hair every day. There are a variety of mid length hairstyles to choose from, including bobs, lobs, shags, and layered cuts.

How to

The key to achieving easy to manage mid length hairstyles is finding a cut that works for your hair type and face shape. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which style is best for you. Once you have the right cut, maintaining the style is simple.

Tools needed:

  • Comb
  • Hairbrush
  • Hair dryer
  • Straightener or curling iron (optional)
  • Hair ties or clips

Step by step

Here's how to achieve some easy to manage mid length hairstyles:

1. Bob

For a classic bob, blow dry your hair straight using a round brush. Use a straightener to touch up any areas that need it. Finish with a light hairspray.

2. Lob

A lob is a longer version of the bob. Blow dry your hair straight and use a straightener to create loose waves. Finish with a texturizing spray.

3. Shag

A shag cut is layered and textured. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance natural waves. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining easy to manage mid length hairstyles:

  • Invest in quality hair products
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the cut
  • Experiment with different styling tools and products to find what works for you
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather

FAQ

What face shapes work well with mid length hairstyles?

Mid length hairstyles work well with most face shapes, but those with oval, heart, and square faces tend to look best with this length.

Can I still wear my hair up with a mid length cut?

Yes! Mid length hairstyles can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and half updos.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, washing every other day or every three days is sufficient.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and easy to manage mid length hairstyles. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to restore moisture
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb

With these easy to manage mid length hairstyles and tips, you'll be able to achieve a stylish look without spending hours on your hair every day. Consult with a hairstylist to find the right cut for you and experiment with different styling tools and products to find what works best for your hair type.
