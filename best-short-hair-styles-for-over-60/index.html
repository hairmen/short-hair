Best Short Hair Styles For Over 60 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Robby Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a fresh new look, consider a short haircut. Short hair styles are easy to maintain and can be incredibly chic. In this article, we'll share some of the best short hair styles for over 60 to help you find the perfect style for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQ: Everything You Need to Know About Short Hair Styles for Over 60
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style

When choosing a short hair style, consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some short hair styles that work well for women over 60:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hair style that's perfect for women of all ages. This cut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. The pixie cut can be styled in many different ways, from edgy to elegant.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another timeless short hair style. This cut is usually chin-length or shorter and can be worn straight or with waves. The bob cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for women with thin or fine hair. This cut adds volume and texture to hair, making it look fuller and more youthful. The layers can be cut at different lengths to create a customized look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair is easy and quick. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and waves, if desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair is low maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep it looking its best:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ: Everything You Need to Know About Short Hair Styles for Over 60

Q: Are short hair styles suitable for women over 60?

A: Yes, short hair styles can be incredibly flattering for women over 60. They can help to create a more youthful and stylish look.

Q: What is the best short hair style for a round face?

A: A layered bob is a great option for women with round faces. The layers help to create a more angular look, while the bob length helps to elongate the face.

Q: Can I wear my short hair style straight and curly?

A: Yes, short hair styles can be worn straight or curly. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can damage it.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and short hair style ideas, you'll be well on your way to looking fabulous over 60. Embrace your new look with confidence and style!
