What Does A Number 8 Clipper Cut Look Like

Written by Christine Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking of getting a haircut, you may have heard of the term "clipper cut". A clipper cut is a type of haircut that is done using hair clippers instead of scissors. It is a popular choice for men's haircuts, especially for those who want a short and low-maintenance style. One of the most common clipper guide sizes used by barbers is the number 8 clipper guide. In this article, we'll discuss what a number 8 clipper cut looks like and how to achieve it.

Description

A number 8 clipper cut is a relatively long haircut that leaves the hair around 1 inch (25mm) long. It is longer than a number 6 or 7 clipper cut but shorter than a number 9 or 10. The hair is cut evenly all around the head, leaving a uniform length. This type of haircut is perfect for those who want a short but not too short haircut that can be styled in different ways.

How to Achieve a Number 8 Clipper Cut

If you want to achieve a number 8 clipper cut, you can either do it yourself or go to a barber. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Prepare Your Clippers

Make sure that your clippers are clean and oiled. Choose a number 8 clipper guide and attach it to your clippers. Make sure that the clipper guide is securely attached and won't come off while you're cutting.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Make sure that your hair is clean and free of any styling products. Towel-dry your hair until it is slightly damp.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair using the clippers. Begin at the back of your head and work your way up to the top. Use the clipper guide to ensure that the length is even all around. Make sure to cut against the direction of hair growth for a smoother cut. Keep checking in the mirror to make sure that the length is consistent.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and the Top

Once you've finished cutting the back and the top, move on to the sides. Use a shorter clipper guide to blend the sides with the top. Make sure that there are no visible lines or uneven spots.

Step 5: Clean Up the Neckline

Use a trimmer to clean up the neckline and around the ears. Make sure that the hairline is even and straight.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 8 Clipper Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining a number 8 clipper cut:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.
  • Use a hairbrush or a comb to style your hair.
  • Use a hair product like wax or pomade to add texture and hold.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about a number 8 clipper cut:

1. Is a number 8 clipper cut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a number 8 clipper cut can work for all hair types, but it may look different depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

2. How long does a number 8 clipper cut last?

A number 8 clipper cut can last around 4-6 weeks before it needs to be trimmed again.

3. Can I style a number 8 clipper cut in different ways?

Yes, a number 8 clipper cut can be styled in different ways depending on your preference. You can add texture and volume using hair products or comb it to the side for a classic look.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get a haircut regularly to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

In conclusion, a number 8 clipper cut is a popular choice for men who want a short but not too short haircut. It's easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways. If you're thinking of getting a number 8 clipper cut, follow the steps above or go to a barber for a professional cut. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good!
