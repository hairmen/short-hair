Best Hair Color Brand For Indian Girl A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an Indian girl who loves to experiment with her hair, you must have thought about coloring your hair at least once in your life. Hair color can add a new dimension to your look, making you feel confident and glamorous. But with so many hair color brands available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your hair. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the best hair color brands for Indian girls and provide you with tips on how to color your hair at home.

Description: What to Look for in a Hair Color Brand?

Before we dive into the best hair color brands for Indian girls, let's discuss what to look for in a hair color brand. The first thing to consider is the ingredients. Hair color brands that use natural ingredients like henna, amla, and indigo are best for Indian hair. These ingredients not only color your hair but also nourish and strengthen them. The second thing to consider is the color range. Look for a brand that offers a wide range of colors to choose from, so you can find the perfect shade for your skin tone. Last but not least, consider the price. Choose a brand that fits your budget without compromising on quality.

How to: Coloring Your Hair at Home

Coloring your hair at home may seem intimidating, but it's not as difficult as it seems. Follow these steps to color your hair at home:

Step 1: Choose the Right Color

Choosing the right color is crucial to achieve the desired result. Consider your skin tone and natural hair color while choosing the shade. If you're unsure, consult a hair color specialist.

Step 2: Prepare the Color

Follow the instructions on the hair color box to prepare the color. Wear gloves to protect your hands from staining.

Step 3: Apply the Color

Apply the color to your hair using a brush. Start from the roots and work your way to the ends. Make sure you cover all the strands evenly.

Step 4: Wait

Wait for the recommended time as mentioned on the box. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.

Step 5: Condition

Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair color box to your hair. Leave it on for 2-3 minutes and rinse it off.

Tips: How to Care for Your Colored Hair

Caring for your colored hair is crucial to maintain the color and prevent damage. Follow these tips to care for your colored hair:

- Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
- Avoid hot water and use lukewarm water instead
- Don't wash your hair too often
- Avoid heat styling tools
- Protect your hair from the sun
- Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I color my hair?

A: It's best to wait for at least 4-6 weeks between coloring sessions to prevent damage.

Q: Can I color my hair while it's wet?

A: No, it's best to color your hair when it's dry.

Q: Can I bleach my hair before coloring it?

A: Bleaching your hair before coloring it can damage your hair. It's best to consult a hair color specialist before attempting to bleach your hair.

Hair Care: Best Hair Color Brands for Indian Girls

1. L'Oreal Paris

L'Oreal Paris is a popular hair color brand that offers a wide range of shades to choose from. Their hair color products are enriched with natural ingredients like henna and amla, making them perfect for Indian hair. The brand also offers ammonia-free hair color products, which are gentle on the hair.

2. Garnier

Garnier is another popular hair color brand that offers a range of shades to choose from. Their hair color products are enriched with natural ingredients like olive oil and avocado, which nourish and strengthen the hair. The brand also offers ammonia-free hair color products.

3. Indus Valley

Indus Valley is a natural hair color brand that uses only natural ingredients like henna and indigo. Their hair color products are free from chemicals like ammonia and PPD, making them gentle on the hair. The brand offers a wide range of shades to choose from, suitable for all skin tones.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair color brand for Indian girls can be overwhelming, but with this comprehensive guide, we hope we've made it easier for you. Remember to choose a brand that uses natural ingredients, offers a wide range of shades, and fits your budget. Also, follow the tips to care for your colored hair and consult a hair color specialist if you're unsure. Happy coloring!
