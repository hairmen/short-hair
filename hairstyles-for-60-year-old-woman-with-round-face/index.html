Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman With Round Face

Written by Bardi Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a 60 year old woman with a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that flatters your face shape and complements your age, but you also want something that is easy to maintain and style. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for 60 year old women with round faces, along with tips for hair care and styling.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Hairstyle Guides
  • Tips for Maintaining your Hairstyle
  • FAQs about Hairstyles for 60 Year Old Women with Round Faces

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, you want to create the illusion of length and height. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones.
  • Opt for hairstyles that have layers and volume at the crown to create height.
  • Consider side-swept bangs to elongate your face.
  • Avoid center parts that can make your face appear wider.
  • Soft waves or curls can also help create a more elongated look.

Best Hairstyles for 60 Year Old Women with Round Faces

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here are some of the best options for 60 year old women with round faces:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Shoulder-length bob with layers and volume at the crown
  • Pixie cut with long bangs swept to the side
  • Soft, loose waves with a center part
  • Messy updo with face-framing layers

Step by Step Hairstyle Guides

If you're not sure how to style your new hairstyle, here are some step by step guides to help you:

Long Layers with Side-Swept Bangs

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.

3. Part your hair on the side.

4. Sweep your bangs to the side and pin them in place.

5. Run your fingers through your hair to create volume and texture.

6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Shoulder-Length Bob with Layers and Volume at the Crown

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the crown to create volume.

3. Use a straightener to add subtle waves to your hair.

4. Part your hair in the middle.

5. Tuck one side behind your ear.

6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Pixie Cut with Long Bangs Swept to the Side

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

3. Part your hair on the side.

4. Sweep your bangs to the side and pin them in place.

5. Use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Visit your stylist regularly to keep your hair trimmed and healthy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for mature hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach and hair dye that can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs about Hairstyles for 60 Year Old Women with Round Faces

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones, center parts that can make your face appear wider, and hairstyles that add width to your face.

What are the best hairstyles for 60 year old women with round faces?

The best hairstyles for 60 year old women with round faces include long layers with side-swept bangs, shoulder-length bobs with layers and volume at the crown, pixie cuts with long bangs swept to the side, soft waves with a center part, and messy updos with face-framing layers.

How often should I visit my stylist?

You should visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair trimmed and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Mature Hair

As you age, your hair may become drier and more brittle. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for mature hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age. Keep these tips in mind when choosing a hairstyle and maintaining your hair, and you'll be sure to look and feel your best.
