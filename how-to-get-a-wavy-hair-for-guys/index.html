How To Get Wavy Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy wanting to add some texture to your hair, wavy hair is a great option. It's effortless and stylish, and it works well for any occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to get wavy hair for guys in a few simple steps.

Description

Wavy hair is a popular hairstyle for guys because it's versatile and easy to achieve. It's a great way to add some texture to your hair without having to spend hours in front of the mirror. With just a few simple steps, you can get the perfect wavy hair for any occasion.

How to Get Wavy Hair for Guys

Step 1: Start with clean hair

The first step in getting wavy hair is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry your hair. Avoid using a hairdryer as it can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your damp hair. You can use a mousse, curl enhancer, or sea salt spray to add texture to your hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the ends.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

After applying the styling product, use a blow dryer on low heat to dry your hair. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair while blow-drying to create waves. Make sure to dry your hair completely, as wet hair can cause frizz.

Step 4: Use a curling iron

If you want more defined waves, you can use a curling iron. Wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Continue this process until you've curled all of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To keep your waves in place, finish with a hairspray. Spray your hair evenly, holding the can about 12 inches away from your head. Make sure to use a light hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips for Getting Wavy Hair for Guys

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the styling product evenly throughout your hair
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying to prevent frizz
  • Use a heat protectant spray if using a curling iron
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type

FAQ

Can guys with short hair get wavy hair?

Yes, guys with short hair can get wavy hair. Use a sea salt spray or curl enhancer to add texture to your hair, and blow-dry your hair while scrunching with your fingers to create waves.

How long will my wavy hair last?

Your wavy hair will last until your next shower or until you wash your hair. You can refresh your waves by applying a styling product and scrunching your hair with your fingers.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

To keep your wavy hair looking its best, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for wavy hair. Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Getting wavy hair for guys is easy with these simple steps. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the one that works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to create the perfect wavy hair for any occasion.
