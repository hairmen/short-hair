Description

Round faces and thick hair can be a winning combination when it comes to hairstyles. However, finding the right style can be a challenge. You want a hairstyle that will flatter your features and complement your natural hair texture.

In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for round faces and thick hair. From short and sassy to long and luscious, we will cover a range of styles to suit your needs. We will also provide tips on how to maintain your style and care for your hair.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle

When choosing the best hairstyle for your round face and thick hair, there are a few things to consider:

  • Length: Shorter hairstyles tend to work well with round faces, while longer styles can make your face appear wider. However, if you have thick hair, longer styles can help to balance out your features.
  • Layers: Layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair. They can also help to frame your face and draw attention to your best features.
  • Bangs: Bangs can be a great way to add dimension to your hairstyle. They can also help to balance out the width of your face.
  • Texture: Your natural hair texture can play a big role in the type of hairstyle that will work best for you. Curly or wavy hair can be styled in a way that flatters your face shape, while straight hair may require more layers or texture to achieve your desired look.

Step by Step Guide to Styling

Here are some step by step instructions for styling some of the best hairstyles for round faces and thick hair:

Short Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to your hair.
  3. Create a deep side part.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  5. Tuck one side behind your ear.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a center part.
  3. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair.
  4. Add layers to the front of your hair to frame your face.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Side Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a side part.
  3. Add long, side-swept bangs to one side of your face.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use the right products: Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid heat damage: Use heat protectant products and avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Experiment: Don't be afraid to try different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face and thick hair?

The best hairstyle for a round face and thick hair depends on your personal style and preferences. Short bobs, long layers, and side-swept bangs are all great options to consider.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face and thick hair?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face and thick hair. However, be sure to add some volume to the crown of your head to balance out the width of your face.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a round brush and blow dryer, adding layers, or using volumizing products.

Hair Care for Round Faces and Thick Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid over-brushing: Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging your hair or causing frizz.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can damage your hair.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can find the perfect style for your round face and thick hair. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair to find what works best for you.
