Should I Cut My Hair Short Again Quiz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering cutting your hair short again but unsure if it's the right decision for you? Take our "Should I Cut My Hair Short Again Quiz" to help you make an informed decision. This quiz will help you determine if a shorter hairstyle is the right fit for your face shape, lifestyle, and hair type. Read on to learn more.

How to Take the "Should I Cut My Hair Short Again Quiz"

To take the quiz, answer the following questions honestly and keep track of your answers. At the end of the quiz, tally up your answers and refer to the scoring system to determine if you should cut your hair short again or not.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before taking the quiz, it's important to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help determine which hairstyle will flatter your features the most. Use a mirror to identify your face shape by examining the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The four main face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped.

Step 2: Take the Quiz

Answer the following questions honestly:

  1. What is your face shape?
  2. How much time are you willing to spend styling your hair each day?
  3. Do you have thick or thin hair?
  4. Are you comfortable with a dramatic change in your appearance?
  5. Do you have any upcoming events or occasions that may require a specific hairstyle?
  6. Do you prefer low-maintenance hairstyles or are you willing to put in the effort for a more intricate style?
  7. How often do you typically get your hair cut?
  8. What is your preferred hair texture (e.g. straight, wavy, curly)?
  9. Are you willing to experiment with different styling products to achieve your desired look?
  10. How confident do you feel with short hair?

Step 3: Tally Up Your Answers

Refer to the following scoring system to tally up your answers:

  • For questions 1 and 10, assign yourself 10 points for each "yes" answer and 0 points for each "no" answer.
  • For questions 2, 6, 7, and 9, assign yourself 5 points for each "yes" answer and 0 points for each "no" answer.
  • For questions 3, 5, and 8, assign yourself 3 points for each "yes" answer and 0 points for each "no" answer.
  • For question 4, assign yourself 1 point for each "yes" answer and 0 points for each "no" answer.

Step 4: Interpret Your Results

Refer to the following scoring system to interpret your results:

  • 0-10 points: A short hairstyle may not be the best fit for you at this time.
  • 11-25 points: A short hairstyle may be a good fit for you, but consider the time and effort required for maintenance.
  • 26-35 points: A short hairstyle may be the perfect fit for you. Go for it!

Tips for Maintaining a Short Hairstyle

If you've decided to cut your hair short again, here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Consider using styling products such as gel, mousse, or hairspray to achieve your desired look.
  • Visit the salon regularly to maintain the shape and length of your hairstyle.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent hair breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will a short hairstyle suit my face shape?

A: A short hairstyle can flatter any face shape if the right style is chosen. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which style will suit your face shape the best.

Q: Will a short hairstyle be easy to maintain?

A: A short hairstyle can be low maintenance, but it depends on the style you choose. Talk to your hairstylist to determine which style will require the least amount of maintenance.

Q: Can I still style my hair in different ways with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair in different ways with a short hairstyle. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to achieve your desired look.

The Importance of Hair Care

Whether you decide to cut your hair short again or not, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and trimming to prevent split ends and breakage. Invest in good quality hair care products that are suitable for your hair type and avoid using heat styling tools frequently. A healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can also promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

Taking the "Should I Cut My Hair Short Again Quiz" can help you determine if a short hairstyle is the right fit for you. Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before making a decision. If you do decide to cut your hair short again, follow the tips for maintaining your new hairstyle and prioritize hair care to promote healthy hair growth.
