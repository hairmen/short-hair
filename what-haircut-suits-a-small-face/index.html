What Haircut Suits A Small Face

Written by Eveline Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a small face, choosing the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your features and doesn't overwhelm your face. But with so many options to choose from, it can be tough to know where to start. In this article, we'll explore what haircut suits a small face and offer tips on how to find the perfect style for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Having a small face can be a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, you can get away with wearing bold makeup and statement accessories without looking overdone. On the other hand, you need to be careful with your hair. If you choose the wrong style, it can make your face look even smaller and more delicate than it already is.

The key to finding the right haircut for a small face is to look for styles that add volume and dimension to your hair without overwhelming your features. You want a style that balances your face and draws attention to your best features, whether that's your eyes, cheekbones, or lips.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for a small face is to determine your face shape. Small faces can come in a variety of shapes, from round to heart-shaped to oval. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for styles that complement your features.

For round faces, try a long bob or a layered cut with volume at the crown. This will help elongate your face and create the illusion of more height. For heart-shaped faces, go for a side-swept fringe or a chin-length bob. This will balance out your features and draw attention to your jawline. For oval faces, experiment with different lengths and textures. You can pull off just about any style, so have fun and try something new!

Step by Step

If you're not sure where to start, here's a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect haircut for your small face:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research different styles that complement your features
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to see what they recommend
  4. Try on wigs or use online virtual try-on tools to see how different styles look on you
  5. Choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a small face:

  • Avoid styles that are too blunt or severe, as these can make your face look smaller
  • Use layers to add volume and movement to your hair
  • Experiment with different parting positions to see what flatters your face shape
  • Use styling products to add texture and definition to your hair
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for small faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a pixie cut if I have a small face?

A: Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a small face! However, make sure to choose a style that adds volume at the crown and doesn't cling to your head.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a small face?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that are too severe or blunt, as well as styles that add too much volume to the sides of your face.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have a small face?

A: It depends on the style you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

Hair Care

No matter what haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

Remember, your hair is an extension of your personality and style. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things until you find the perfect haircut for your small face. With the right style, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and beautiful every day.
