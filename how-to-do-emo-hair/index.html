How To Do Emo Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a fan of emo music and culture? Do you want to rock the signature emo hairstyle? Look no further, as this guide will teach you how to do emo hair in just a few easy steps.

Description

Emo hair is characterized by its choppy layers, side-swept bangs, and bold colors. It's a popular hairstyle among those who identify with the emo subculture, which is known for its emotional and expressive music. Whether you're going to a concert or just want to switch up your look, emo hair is a fun and edgy choice.

How to Do Emo Hair

Step 1: Choose Your Cut

The first step to achieving emo hair is to choose the right cut. Most emo hairstyles have choppy layers that frame the face, with longer pieces in the back. If you're not sure what cut to get, bring some inspiration photos to your stylist. They can help you decide on a cut that works for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Color Your Hair

Emo hair often features bold, bright colors like pink, blue, or purple. If you want to try a new color, visit a professional stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. You can also use temporary hair color products if you want to experiment without committing to a permanent change.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

To style your emo hair, start by blow-drying it with a round brush to create volume. Then use a straightening iron to add waves or curls, focusing on the ends of your hair. Use a texturizing spray to create a messy, piece-y look, and finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

- Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

- Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands or clips to add some extra flair to your emo hairstyle.

- Consider getting a fringe or side-swept bangs to add some edge to your look.

FAQ

Q: How long does emo hair usually last?

A: Emo hair can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your style.

Q: Can I do emo hair at home?

A: While it's best to visit a professional stylist for a major haircut or color change, you can definitely style your emo hair at home with the right tools and products.

Hair Care

To keep your emo hair looking its best, be sure to wash and condition it regularly with products that are designed for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep split ends at bay, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
