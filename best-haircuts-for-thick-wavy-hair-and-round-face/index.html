Best Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair And Round Face

Are you someone who has thick wavy hair and a round face? It can be quite challenging to find the perfect haircut that complements both your hair and face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step

Description

Thick wavy hair can be difficult to manage, especially if you have a round face. The wrong haircut can make your face look even rounder, while the right haircut can enhance your features and give you a more flattering look. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for thick wavy hair and round face that will make you look and feel confident.

How to

The first step in finding the best haircut for your hair and face shape is to identify your face shape. Round faces have soft curves and no sharp angles, with the width and length being almost the same. Once you have identified your face shape, you can choose a haircut that adds more angles and structure to your face.

Step by step

1. Long Layered Cut: A long layered cut is perfect for thick wavy hair and a round face. It adds volume and texture to your hair while elongating your face. Make sure to ask your hairstylist to cut long layers that start at your chin or below.

2. Bob Cut: A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on round faces. It adds structure and definition to your face while making your hair more manageable. A chin-length bob is perfect for thick wavy hair and a round face.

3. Pixie Cut: A pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that looks great on round faces. It adds height and volume to your hair while making your face look slimmer. Make sure to ask your hairstylist to add layers and texture to your hair.

4. Side Swept Bangs: Side swept bangs are perfect for round faces as they add angles and structure to your face. They also complement thick wavy hair by adding texture and movement to your hair.

Tips

1. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline as they can make your face look wider.

2. Opt for hairstyles that add angles and structure to your face.

3. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your waves and add texture to your hair.

4. Avoid heavy fringes that cover your forehead as they can make your face look rounder.

FAQ

Q. Can I get a short haircut if I have a round face?

A. Yes, you can get a short haircut if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a haircut that adds angles and structure to your face.

Q. Will a bob cut make my face look rounder?

A. No, a bob cut will not make your face look rounder. In fact, a chin-length bob is perfect for round faces as it adds structure and definition to your face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Here are some tips to keep your thick wavy hair in good condition:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

3. Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can rock your thick wavy hair and round face with confidence.
