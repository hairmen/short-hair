Does Straight Hair Suit Round Face

Description

Round faces are characterized by a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you might be wondering if straight hair is the right choice for you. Straight hair can certainly suit a round face, but there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure that it flatters your features.

How to

The key to making straight hair work for a round face is to create some angles and volume. This can be achieved through layering and texturizing your hair. Layers can help create movement and dimension, while texturizing can add volume and lift.

Step by Step

Here's how to achieve straight hair that suits a round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and lift.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair straight using a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten your hair from root to tip.
  5. Finish off with a texturizing spray to add volume and lift to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making straight hair work for a round face:

  • A side part can help create the illusion of length and slim down your face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs as they can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Opt for longer layers and face-framing pieces to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid volume at the sides of your head as this can make your face appear wider.

FAQ

Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can definitely wear your hair straight if you have a round face. The key is to add some angles and volume to your hair through layering and texturizing.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs as they can make your face appear even rounder. You should also avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of your head as this can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

To keep your straight hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from styling products.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip it of its natural oils.

In conclusion, straight hair can certainly suit a round face, but it's important to create some angles and volume to ensure that it flatters your features. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can achieve straight hair that complements your round face shape.
