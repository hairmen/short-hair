How To Cut Curly Guy Hair At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting curly guy hair at home can be daunting, but with the right tools and guidance, it can be done easily. Not only is it cost-effective, but it also gives you the freedom to experiment with different styles. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting curly guy hair at home.

How to Cut Curly Guy Hair at Home

Step 1: Gather the Necessary Tools

The first step in cutting curly guy hair at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror. Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid any jagged cuts.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It is important to start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Dry your hair with a towel or a hair dryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable parts. Use clips to hold the sections in place. This will make it easier for you to cut your hair evenly and systematically.

Step 5: Start Cutting Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way towards the front.

Step 6: Check Your Progress

Take regular breaks to check your progress in the mirror. Make sure your hair is cut evenly and to the desired length. If you notice any unevenness, make adjustments as necessary.

Step 7: Trim the Sides and Back

Once you have cut the top of your hair, it's time to trim the sides and back. Use a mirror to see the back of your head. Take small sections of hair and cut them to the desired length. Work your way towards the sides, making sure to cut your hair evenly.

Step 8: Clean Up the Edges

Use the scissors to clean up the edges around your ears and neck. This will give your haircut a polished look.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use your fingers, a comb, or a brush to style your hair to your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Curly Guy Hair at Home

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Take breaks to check your progress and make adjustments as necessary.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid jagged cuts.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.

FAQ About Cutting Curly Guy Hair at Home

Can I cut my curly guy hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your curly guy hair at home with the right tools and guidance.

What tools do I need to cut my curly guy hair at home?

You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror.

How often should I cut my curly guy hair?

It is recommended to cut your curly guy hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and length.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Guys

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as it can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling product to define your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can cause frizz.

Cutting curly guy hair at home can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and guidance, it can be done easily. Follow these steps to get a professional-looking haircut at home. Remember to take breaks, check your progress, and make adjustments as necessary. With practice, you'll be able to perfect your skills and experiment with different styles.
