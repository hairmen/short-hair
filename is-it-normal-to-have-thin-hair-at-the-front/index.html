The first step in addressing thinning hair at the front is to determine the cause. Tips for Preventing Thinning Hair at the Front

  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles like braids, buns, or ponytails.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
