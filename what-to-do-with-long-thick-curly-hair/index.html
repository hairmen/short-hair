What To Do With Long Thick Curly Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Long thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks stunning and voluminous, it can also be hard to manage and style. In this blog post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on what to do with long thick curly hair, as well as some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

How to Style Long Thick Curly Hair

Styling long thick curly hair can be overwhelming, but there are several ways to tame your curls and make them look fabulous. Here are some popular styling options:

1. Loose Curls

Loose curls are perfect for a romantic or bohemian look. To achieve this style, use a curling iron or wand with a large barrel and wrap sections of hair around it, leaving the ends out. Hold for a few seconds and release. Use your fingers to separate the curls and add some hairspray for hold.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

For a chic and easy-to-do hairstyle, try a half-up half-down look. Gather the top section of hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the rest of your hair down or add some loose curls for extra volume.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your curls under control and add some texture to your hair. Try a fishtail braid, a French braid, or a classic three-strand braid. You can also braid small sections of hair and pin them back for a unique look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Thick Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your long thick curly hair:

1. Start with clean, moisturized hair.

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to add moisture and prevent frizz.

2. Detangle your hair.

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

3. Apply a styling product.

Use a styling product that works for your hair type and desired style. For example, if you want to enhance your curls, use a curl enhancing cream or gel. If you want to add volume, use a mousse or volumizing spray.

4. Blow-dry your hair.

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls. Flip your hair over and dry the roots for extra volume.

5. Style your hair.

Choose a styling option that works for you and follow the steps outlined above.

6. Finish with hairspray.

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling and caring for your long thick curly hair:

1. Don’t over-wash your hair.

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week.

2. Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair.

Regular towels can rough up your hair cuticles and cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water from your hair.

3. Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase.

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and breakage, especially if you toss and turn at night. Sleeping on a satin or silk pillowcase can help reduce friction and keep your curls intact.

FAQs about Long Thick Curly Hair

1. How often should I trim my hair?

It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

2. How can I prevent frizz?

Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to add moisture to your hair and prevent frizz. Avoid touching your hair too much, and use a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce friction.

3. Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it’s important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat. Straightening your hair too often can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for keeping your long thick curly hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

A deep conditioning treatment can help nourish and hydrate your hair, especially if it’s dry or damaged. Look for a treatment that’s specifically designed for curly hair.

2. Avoid using too much heat.

Excessive heat can damage your hair and cause breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

3. Protect your hair from the sun.

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your locks.

With these tips and tricks, you can make the most of your long thick curly hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Experiment with different styles and products, and don’t be afraid to embrace your natural curls!
