How To Make Face Shape Oval Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to create the illusion of an oval face shape? Whether you have a round face, heart-shaped face, or square face, there are ways to contour and style your hair to give the appearance of an oval face. In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions, tips, and answers to FAQs for achieving an oval face shape.

Description

Face shape is determined by the bone structure of the face, and there are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. While you cannot change your bone structure, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and other techniques to create the illusion of a different face shape. An oval face shape is often considered the ideal face shape because it is balanced and symmetrical.

How to

There are several ways to make your face shape oval:

1. Contouring

Contouring is the process of using makeup to create shadows and highlights on the face. To create the illusion of an oval face shape, use a contouring powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply the contouring product to the sides of your forehead, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend well with a makeup brush or sponge.

2. Hairstyles

Hairstyles can also help create the illusion of an oval face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail, top knot, or voluminous curls. You can also try a side part and tuck one side of your hair behind your ear to create the illusion of a longer face.

3. Bangs

Bangs can also help create the illusion of an oval face shape. Avoid blunt bangs, as these will make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will create a diagonal line that slims the face.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to creating an oval face shape:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you can create the illusion of an oval face shape, you need to determine your current face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.

Step 2: Apply contouring product

Using a contouring powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone, apply the product to the sides of your forehead, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend well with a makeup brush or sponge.

Step 3: Style your hair

Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail, top knot, or voluminous curls. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Step 4: Consider bangs

If you want to try bangs, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for creating an oval face shape:

- Use a light-reflecting highlighter on the tops of your cheekbones to further emphasize the oval shape. - Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as this can make your face look wider. - Consider getting a haircut with long layers to add movement and dimension to your hair. - Experiment with different makeup techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I change my face shape permanently?

A: No, you cannot change your bone structure permanently. However, there are surgical procedures that can alter the appearance of your face shape.

Q: How long does contouring last?

A: Contouring can last all day if you use a long-wearing product and set it with a powder.

Q: Can I make my face shape oval without makeup?

A: Yes, you can create the illusion of an oval face shape with hairstyles and bangs.

Hair Care

Using heat styling tools and chemical treatments can damage your hair. To keep your hair healthy, use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or flat iron. Also, try to limit your use of chemical treatments, such as hair dye or relaxer, and use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create the illusion of an oval face shape. Remember, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, so embrace your unique features and love yourself just the way you are.
