Would I Look Good With Blonde Hair Quiz Everything You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering dyeing your hair blonde but not sure if it will suit you? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll take you through everything you need to know about the "would I look good with blonde hair quiz," including how to take it, tips to help you decide, and how to take care of your blonde hair once you've made the change. So, let's get started.

How to Take the Quiz

Before taking the quiz, it's essential to understand that it's not a surefire way to know whether you'll look good with blonde hair or not. However, it can give you a rough idea of whether blonde hair will suit you or not.

There are several online quizzes available that you can take to determine whether you'll look good with blonde hair or not. Most of these quizzes will require you to answer a few questions about your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Based on your answers, the quiz will provide you with a result that indicates whether you'll look good with blonde hair or not.

One popular quiz is the "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair Quiz" available on Buzzfeed. The quiz is straightforward and easy to take. All you need to do is answer ten simple questions, and the quiz will provide you with a result at the end.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you take the "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair Quiz" on Buzzfeed:

  1. Visit the Buzzfeed website and search for "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair Quiz."
  2. Click on the quiz link and start the quiz.
  3. Answer the ten simple questions about your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.
  4. Once you've answered all the questions, the quiz will provide you with a result that indicates whether you'll look good with blonde hair or not.

Tips to Help You Decide

While online quizzes can be helpful, they're not always accurate. Here are a few tips to help you decide whether blonde hair will suit you or not:

  • Consider your skin tone: If you have a warm skin tone, blonde hair with warm tones will suit you. On the other hand, if you have a cool skin tone, blonde hair with cool tones will suit you better.
  • Consider your eye color: If you have blue or green eyes, blonde hair will look great on you. However, if you have brown eyes, warm blonde shades will suit you better.
  • Consider your natural hair color: If you have dark hair, you may want to start with highlights before going completely blonde. This will help you get used to the change gradually.
  • Try on a wig: If you're not sure whether blonde hair will suit you or not, try on a blonde wig to see how it looks on you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair Quiz":

Is the quiz accurate?

While the quiz can give you a rough idea of whether blonde hair will suit you or not, it's not always accurate. It's essential to consider other factors such as your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color before making a decision.

Can I take the quiz more than once?

Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you want. However, keep in mind that the questions and results may vary.

Hair Care

If you've decided to dye your hair blonde, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips to help you take care of your blonde hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

So, there you have it – everything you need to know about the "Would I Look Good with Blonde Hair Quiz." Remember, hair color is a personal choice, and what matters most is how you feel about it. Whether you decide to go blonde or not, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.
