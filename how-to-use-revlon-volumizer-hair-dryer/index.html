How To Use Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a hair dryer that can give you salon-quality blowouts at home, the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer might just be the tool for you. In this article, we will guide you on how to use the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer and provide tips for hair care. By the end of this guide, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking blowout in just a few easy steps.

Description

The Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer is a 2-in-1 styling tool that combines a round brush and a hair dryer in one. It has a ceramic coating that helps distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz, while the ionic technology helps to prevent damage to your hair. The oval brush design adds volume and smoothness to your hair, and the multiple heat and speed settings make it suitable for all hair types.

How to Use Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before using the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer, it's important to prep your hair by washing and conditioning it. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet. You can also apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use hair clips to secure the sections you're not working on.

Step 3: Turn on the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer

Plug in the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer and select your desired heat and speed settings. Wait for it to heat up before using it on your hair.

Step 4: Start Styling

Take a section of your hair and place the brush underneath it, close to the roots. Use the brush to lift your hair up and away from your head, while simultaneously gliding it down the length of your hair. Repeat this motion until your hair is dry and styled.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the most out of your Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer:

  • Use the low heat and speed setting if you have thin or damaged hair.
  • Use the high heat and speed setting if you have thick or coarse hair.
  • Twist the brush as you glide it down your hair for added volume and curl.
  • Don't get too close to your scalp to avoid burning it.
  • Use a cool shot button to set your hairstyle in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I use the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer on wet hair?

A: It's recommended to towel-dry your hair until it's damp before using the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer to avoid damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I clean the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer?

A: It's recommended to clean the brush attachment after each use and to wipe down the body of the hair dryer with a damp cloth once a week.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

Using the Revlon Volumizer Hair Dryer can be a game-changer in your hair styling routine. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a salon-quality blowout at home. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
