How To Shape Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair game, shaping your hair can make a big difference. It can help you achieve a more polished and put-together look, whether you're going for a professional or casual style. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to shape hair male.

How to Shape Hair Male: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in shaping your hair is to choose the right haircut. Talk to your barber or hairstylist and explain the look you're going for. They can recommend a haircut that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start shaping your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. This will help you achieve a smooth and manageable base for your styling products.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, depending on the look you're going for. If you want a sleek and polished look, use a pomade or wax. If you want a more natural and textured look, use a clay or paste.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer can help you shape your hair and add volume. Use a round brush to lift and style your hair as you blow dry it. This will help you achieve a more polished and professional look.

Step 5: Use a Comb or Brush

Once your hair is dry, use a comb or brush to shape your hair. This will help you achieve a more polished and put-together look. Use a fine-toothed comb for more precise shaping, or a wide-toothed comb for a more natural and textured look.

Step 6: Add Finishing Touches

Once you've shaped your hair, add any finishing touches you need. This may include adding a hair spray or finishing product to hold your hair in place. You can also add accessories like a headband or hair tie to keep your hair out of your face.

Tips for Shaping Hair Male

Here are some additional tips to help you shape your hair like a pro:

  • Use the right products for your hair type and desired look. Experiment with different products until you find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or hairstylist for advice. They can recommend products and techniques that will help you achieve your desired look.
  • Invest in a good quality blow dryer and styling tools. This will help you achieve a more polished and professional look.
  • Be patient and take your time when shaping your hair. It may take a few tries to get it right, but with practice, you'll be able to achieve the look you want.

FAQs about Shaping Hair Male

1. How often should I shape my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your desired style loses its shape. On average, most men get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their desired shape.

2. Can I shape my hair at home?

Yes, you can shape your hair at home with the right tools and techniques. However, if you're not confident in your skills, it's best to go to a professional barber or hairstylist.

3. How do I know which styling product to use?

It depends on your hair type and desired look. If you have thick or curly hair, you may need a stronger hold product like a pomade or wax. If you have fine or thin hair, you may want to use a lighter product like a cream or paste.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Shaping your hair is just one part of hair care. Here are some additional tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair in good shape.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

Shaping your hair can be a great way to change up your look and feel more confident. Whether you're going for a professional or casual style, follow these tips and techniques to shape your hair like a pro.
