How To Cut Men s Hair Clippers Only A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts every month? Do you want to learn how to cut men's hair with clippers only? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair using clippers only. From the tools you need to the techniques you should use, we have got you covered. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You Will Need
  • Preparation
  • Cutting Techniques
  • Cleaning Up
  • Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Tools You Will Need

Before you start cutting men's hair with clippers, you need to make sure you have the right tools. Here's what you will need:

  • Clippers (with guards)
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Brush
  • Clip

Make sure your clippers are sharp and oiled before you start cutting.

Preparation

Before you start cutting, you need to prepare the hair. Here's how:

  1. Wash the hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Comb the hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Section the hair using clips.
  4. Put the cape or towel around the person's shoulders to protect their clothing.

Cutting Techniques

Now it's time to start cutting. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to the shortest guard.
  2. Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up.
  3. Use the clippers to cut the hair to the desired length.
  4. Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers missed.
  5. Blend the sides and back into the top using the clippers and scissors.
  6. Use the comb to check for any uneven spots.
  7. Blend any uneven spots using the clippers and scissors.

Cleaning Up

After you have finished cutting the hair, it's time to clean up. Here's how:

  1. Remove the cape or towel and shake it outside to remove any hair.
  2. Use the brush to remove any loose hair from the person's neck and shoulders.
  3. Clean the clippers and scissors with a brush and oil.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair in place as you cut.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Don't cut too much hair off at once.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. However, it may be more difficult to cut the back of your head.

What is the best guard to use for men's hair?

The best guard to use for men's hair depends on the length you want. Generally, a #2 or #3 guard is a good starting point for most men.

How often should I oil my clippers?

You should oil your clippers before and after each use to keep them running smoothly.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (e.g. from a blow dryer or straightener).

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with clippers only, you can save money and cut hair like a pro. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully. Good luck!
