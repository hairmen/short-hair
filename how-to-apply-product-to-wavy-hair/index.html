How To Apply Product To Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. It can look effortlessly chic and stylish, but it can also be difficult to manage and style. One way to make the most of your wavy locks is to use the right hair products. In this article, we'll show you how to apply product to wavy hair to enhance your natural waves and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Apply Product to Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Applying Product to Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

How to Apply Product to Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the different types of hair products that are suitable for wavy hair:

  • Lightweight mousse
  • Sea salt spray
  • Curl cream
  • Leave-in conditioner

When choosing a product, look for something that is lightweight and won't weigh down your waves. Avoid heavy creams and oils that can make your hair look greasy and flat.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to apply product to your wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, then gently towel dry your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of product to your palms. The amount you need will depend on the length and thickness of your hair.
  3. Distribute the product evenly through your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your natural waves. If you have long hair, you can also twist your hair into sections to create more defined waves.
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on low heat. Avoid using a regular hair dryer or high heat, as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair.
  6. Once your hair is dry, you can use your fingers to gently separate your waves and create more volume.

Tips for Applying Product to Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your wavy hair:

  • Apply product to your hair when it is still damp. This will help to distribute the product evenly and prevent it from clumping or weighing down your waves.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before applying product. This will help to prevent breakage and make it easier to distribute the product evenly.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much once you have applied product. This can cause frizz and disrupt your natural waves.

FAQs

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on wavy hair?

Yes, you can use regular shampoo and conditioner on wavy hair. However, if you have particularly dry or damaged hair, you may want to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help nourish your hair.

How often should I apply product to my wavy hair?

This will depend on your hair type and the product you are using. Some people find that they only need to apply product once a week, while others apply product every day. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

In addition to using the right products, there are a few other things you can do to keep your wavy hair looking its best:

  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your natural waves. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can enhance your natural waves and keep your wavy hair looking beautiful, healthy, and stylish.
