How To Know Your Face Shape Female

Written by Daniel Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, knowing your face shape is key to finding the perfect cut. But how do you figure out your face shape? Follow these easy steps to determine whether you have a round, oval, square, heart, or diamond-shaped face.

Table of Contents

  2. Step 2: Analyze Your Measurements
  3. Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape
  4. Tips for Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Grab a tape measure and measure the length and width of your face. Start at the top of your forehead and measure down to the bottom of your chin for the length. Then measure across the widest part of your face, usually just below your cheekbones, for the width.

Step 2: Analyze Your Measurements

Once you have your measurements, compare the length and width of your face to each other. If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oval or oblong face shape. If your face is about as wide as it is long, you may have a round or square face shape. And if your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you may have a heart or diamond-shaped face.

Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape

Now that you have your measurements and a general idea of your face shape, it's time to determine your exact face shape. Look at your face in the mirror and compare it to the descriptions below:

  • Round: Soft features with a circular shape
  • Oval: Longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded forehead and jawline
  • Square: Angular jawline and forehead, with width and length about equal
  • Heart: Tapered jawline, wider at the forehead and temples
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and jawline, with wider cheekbones

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start searching for the perfect hairstyle to flatter your features.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, here are some tips for finding the perfect hairstyle:

  • Oval: Lucky you! Almost any hairstyle will flatter your face shape.
  • Round: Look for hairstyles that add height to your crown and length to your face, like long layers or an angled bob.
  • Square: Soften your angular features with a layered cut that falls below your jawline or a side-swept bang.
  • Heart: Balance out your features with a chin-length bob or a side-parted pixie cut.
  • Diamond: Create the illusion of width with a chin-length bob or full fringe.

FAQs

Q: Can my face shape change over time?

A: Your bone structure won't change, but weight gain or loss can alter the appearance of your face shape.

Q: Can I have a combination of face shapes?

A: Yes! It's possible to have a mix of two face shapes, like a square jawline with a heart-shaped forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, here are some hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best:

  • Oval: Keep your hair healthy and shiny with regular trims and a moisturizing hair mask.
  • Round: Use volumizing products to add height to your crown and avoid styles that add fullness to your cheeks.
  • Square: Fight frizz with a smoothing serum and avoid styles that end at your jawline.
  • Heart: Use a lightweight styling product to add volume to the bottom half of your hair and avoid heavy bangs.
  • Diamond: Keep your hair soft and shiny with a nourishing hair oil and avoid styles that add height to your crown.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape and find the perfect hairstyle, you're ready to rock a new look with confidence!
