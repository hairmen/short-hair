How To Do A Messy Bun With Thin Hair Without Donut

Written by Eveline Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using a donut to create a voluminous messy bun? Do you have thin hair and struggle to make your bun look full? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will teach you how to create a messy bun with thin hair without using a donut.

Description

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that can be done in minutes. It's perfect for those days when you don't have time to style your hair but still want to look put together. However, for those with thin hair, creating a full and voluminous messy bun can be a challenge. That's where this tutorial comes in. We will show you how to create a messy bun that looks full and voluminous, without the use of a donut.

How to

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Begin by washing your hair and letting it air dry. It's important to start with clean hair so that your bun looks neat and tidy.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray

Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to give it some grip and hold. This will help your bun stay in place and prevent it from slipping throughout the day.

Step 3: Pull Your Hair Back

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Make sure to pull your hair back tightly to create a smooth base for your bun.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Tease your ponytail with a fine-tooth comb to create volume. Start at the roots and work your way down, gently backcombing your hair to create fullness.

Step 5: Twist and Pin

Twist your hair into a bun shape and secure it with bobby pins. Start at the base of the ponytail and work your way up, pinning as you go. Make sure to tuck in any loose ends to create a seamless look.

Step 6: Pull Out Some Strands

Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, effortless look. This will also help to frame your face and soften the hairstyle.

Tips

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair to create volume.
  • If you have trouble getting your bun to stay in place, try using a hair tie with hooks on the ends. This will help to secure your bun and prevent it from slipping.

FAQ

Can I use a donut if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can use a donut if you have thin hair. However, the purpose of this tutorial is to show you how to create a messy bun without using a donut.

How do I make my bun look more messy?

To make your bun look more messy, pull out a few strands of hair around your face and loosen the bun with your fingers. You can also use a texturizing spray to create a more tousled look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and try to let it air dry whenever possible. Regular trims can also help to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a full and voluminous messy bun without using a donut. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether you're running errands or going out for a night on the town. Give it a try and see how easy it is to create a messy bun with thin hair!
