What Face Shape Does Curtain Bangs Look Good On?

If you're thinking about getting a new hairstyle, you might want to consider curtain bangs. This type of fringe has been popular for a while now, and it's not hard to see why. Curtain bangs are versatile, stylish, and easy to maintain. But what face shape do they look best on? In this article, we'll take a closer look at curtain bangs and which face shapes they suit best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. They're called "curtain" because they frame the face like a pair of curtains. Curtain bangs can be worn straight or styled to curve around the face.

How to

If you're interested in getting curtain bangs, the first step is to find a salon and a stylist you trust. Once you've found someone you're happy with, you can discuss the look you're going for and get their advice on whether curtain bangs are a good fit for your face shape.

Step by Step

  1. Find a salon and stylist you trust
  2. Discuss the look you're going for
  3. Get advice on whether curtain bangs are a good fit for your face shape
  4. Decide on the length and shape of your curtain bangs
  5. Get your new fringe cut and styled

Tips

When it comes to curtain bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Curtain bangs look best on longer face shapes
  • They're also ideal for oval and heart-shaped faces
  • If you have a round face, go for longer curtain bangs
  • Consider your hair texture â curtain bangs work best on straight or wavy hair

FAQ

What if I have a square face?

Curtain bangs can still work for square faces, but it's best to go for longer, softer bangs that will help to balance out the angles of the face.

Can I still wear curtain bangs if I have a short forehead?

Curtain bangs can work for shorter foreheads, as long as they're cut longer on the sides and don't cover the entire forehead.

Hair Care

Once you've got your new curtain bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy

Now that you know what face shape curtain bangs look good on, it's time to book your salon appointment and give them a try. With the right cut and styling, curtain bangs can be a flattering and fashionable addition to any hairstyle.
