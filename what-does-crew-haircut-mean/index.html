What Does Crew Haircut Mean A Complete Guide

Are you wondering what the term "crew haircut" means? Do you want to know how to get this popular men's hairstyle? In this blog post, we will explain what a crew haircut is, how to get it, and share some tips and FAQs about this classic hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a Crew Haircut
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining a Crew Haircut
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Crew Haircut

Description

A crew haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that is short on the sides and back and slightly longer on top. It is often referred to as a "short back and sides" haircut. This hairstyle is named after the rowing teams, or "crews," who traditionally wore their hair short for practical reasons. The crew haircut is a classic, timeless style that is suitable for all face shapes and hair types.

How to Get a Crew Haircut

The crew haircut is a low-maintenance, easy-to-style haircut that can be achieved at home or in a barbershop. Here are the steps to getting a crew haircut:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Choose the length of your crew haircut. The sides and back should be clipped short, while the top should be left slightly longer.
  2. Wash and towel dry your hair.
  3. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down for a more gradual fade.
  4. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length. You can also ask your barber to add texture to the top for a more modern look.
  5. Blend the sides and top together with scissors or clippers.
  6. Use a comb and hair product to style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining a Crew Haircut

A crew haircut is a low-maintenance hairstyle, but it still requires some upkeep to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining a crew haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back short and tidy.
  • Use a hair product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to the top of your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it healthy and soft.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

FAQs

What face shapes does the crew haircut suit?

The crew haircut is a versatile style that suits all face shapes, including round, square, and oval faces.

Can I style my crew haircut in different ways?

Yes, the crew haircut can be styled in different ways depending on your preferences. You can add texture and volume to the top for a modern look, or keep it short and neat for a classic style.

How often do I need to trim my crew haircut?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides and back short and tidy.

Hair Care for Crew Haircut

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a crew haircut. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

Now that you know what a crew haircut is and how to get one, it's time to book your next haircut appointment or break out the clippers and try it out at home. With a little maintenance and some styling product, you can rock this classic, timeless hairstyle.
