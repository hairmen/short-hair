Mens Haircut Fade Sides Curly Top A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Frank Nov 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, the mens haircut fade sides curly top is a great option. This haircut is characterized by short sides that gradually fade into longer hair on the top, which is left curly. It's a versatile look that can be customized to suit your personal style, and it's also easy to maintain. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of your curly hair.

Description

The mens haircut fade sides curly top is a popular hairstyle that has been trending for a few years now. It's a great option for guys who want a stylish and modern look that's also easy to maintain. The haircut features short sides that gradually fade into longer hair on the top, which is left curly. The length and volume of the top hair can vary depending on personal style and preference. The fade on the sides can also be customized to suit the individual's face shape and style.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in trying out the mens haircut fade sides curly top, here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by finding a skilled barber who is experienced in creating this type of haircut.
  2. Decide on the length and volume of the top hair, and communicate this clearly to your barber.
  3. Choose the type of fade you want on the sides, whether it's a high fade, mid fade, or low fade.
  4. During the cut, make sure to keep the top hair longer and curly, while gradually cutting the sides shorter until they blend into the longer hair on top.
  5. Once the cut is complete, style the hair using a curl-enhancing product to bring out the natural texture and curl of the hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the mens haircut fade sides curly top:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat, scrunching the hair as you go to encourage the natural curl.
  4. Use a comb to create a part on one side of the head.
  5. Trim the sides of the head, gradually fading the hair from short to long as you move up towards the top.
  6. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair on the top of the head, leaving it longer and curly.
  7. Finish by styling the hair using a hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling the mens haircut fade sides curly top:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to bring out the natural texture and curl of the hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to encourage the curl and add volume.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits your face shape and personal style.
  • Use a hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold to the hair.

FAQs

What face shapes suit the mens haircut fade sides curly top?

The mens haircut fade sides curly top can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. It's important to work with your barber to customize the haircut to your face shape and personal style.

How often do I need to get the mens haircut fade sides curly top?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the mens haircut fade sides curly top.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the hair.
  • Avoid brushing curly hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle curly hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to add moisture and reduce frizz.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

Conclusion

The mens haircut fade sides curly top is a stylish and modern hairstyle that's easy to maintain. By finding a skilled barber and following the steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve the perfect look for your personal style. Remember to experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you, and take care of your curly hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
