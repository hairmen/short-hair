Best Drugstore Volumizer For Fine Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how difficult it can be to achieve volume and body. Fortunately, there are many drugstore volumizing products that can help you achieve your desired look. In this guide, we will discuss the best drugstore volumizers for fine hair, how to use them, and some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Description

The best drugstore volumizers for fine hair are those that add volume without weighing down your hair or leaving it feeling greasy. Look for products that contain ingredients like hydrolyzed wheat protein, which can help strengthen and thicken hair strands, and panthenol, which can add shine and moisturize the hair.

Some of the best drugstore volumizers for fine hair include:

  • John Frieda Luxurious Volume Root Booster
  • L'Oreal Paris Advanced Hairstyle Boost It Volume Inject Mousse
  • TRESemme Beauty-Full Volume Touchable Bounce Mousse
  • Dove Style+Care Amplifier Mousse
  • Herbal Essences Body Envy Volumizing Mousse

How to Use

The best way to use a volumizing product is to start with damp hair. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly. If you are using a mousse, apply it in small amounts and avoid using too much, as this can weigh down your hair. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add even more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve maximum volume:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply your chosen volumizing product to your roots and work it through your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on lifting your roots.
  4. Flip your head upside down and blow dry the underside of your hair.
  5. Finish with a blast of cool air to set your style and add even more volume.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to using a volumizing product, there are some other tips you can follow to maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair health from the inside out.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can volumizing products be used on all hair types?

A: While volumizing products are designed to add body and volume to fine hair, they can be used on all hair types. However, if you have thick or coarse hair, you may need to use a larger amount of product to achieve the desired effect.

Q: How often should I use a volumizing product?

A: It is safe to use a volumizing product every day, but be sure to wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup.

Q: Can volumizing products cause damage to my hair?

A: Volumizing products are generally safe to use and do not cause damage to your hair. However, using too much product or blow drying your hair on high heat can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and using the right volumizing product for your hair type, you can achieve the volume and body you have always wanted. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain a healthy lifestyle to keep your locks looking their best.
