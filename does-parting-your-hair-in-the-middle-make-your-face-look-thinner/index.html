Does Parting Your Hair In The Middle Make Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to make your face look thinner, you might want to consider parting your hair in the middle. This simple hairstyle has been around for decades, and it's one that many people swear by when it comes to creating a more flattering look. But does it really work? Let's take a look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Parting your hair in the middle is a simple way to create the illusion of a slimmer face. By dividing your hair down the center, you create symmetry that draws attention away from any asymmetrical features on your face. This can help to balance out your features and make your face look more proportional.

Additionally, parting your hair in the middle can help to elongate your face. This is especially true if you have a round or square face shape. By creating a vertical line down the center of your face, you create the illusion of length, which can make your face look slimmer overall.

How To

Parting your hair in the middle is incredibly easy to do. Here's how:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

It's best to part your hair when it's clean and dry. This will help to create a more defined part and prevent any flyaways or frizz.

Step 2: Brush your hair straight back.

Use a brush to smooth your hair back away from your face. This will help to create a clean part and prevent any bumps or lumps.

Step 3: Divide your hair down the center.

Use the end of a comb or your finger to create a straight line down the center of your head. Make sure the line is as straight as possible.

Step 4: Style your hair as desired.

Once you've parted your hair in the middle, you can style it however you like. You can leave it down, pull it back into a ponytail, or create a bun. The choice is yours!

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to parting your hair in the middle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Brush your hair straight back.
  3. Divide your hair down the center.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when parting your hair in the middle:

  • If you have a cowlick or other natural part, you may need to work with your hair to create a more defined middle part.
  • If you have bangs, you can still part your hair in the middle. Simply tuck your bangs behind your ears or pin them back to create a more streamlined look.
  • If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten your hair before parting it in the middle. This will help to create a more defined part.

FAQ

Q: Will parting my hair in the middle work for all face shapes?

A: Parting your hair in the middle can work for most face shapes, but it may be more effective for those with round or square faces.

Q: Can I still part my hair in the middle if I have a widow's peak?

A: Yes! Simply work with your natural hairline to create a more defined part.

Q: How can I make my part look more defined?

A: You can use a comb, hair spray, or hair gel to create a more defined middle part.

Hair Care

Parting your hair in the middle can be a great way to create a more flattering look, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great, whether you're parting it in the middle or trying out a new style.

In conclusion, parting your hair in the middle can be a simple and effective way to make your face look thinner. By creating symmetry and elongating your face, this hairstyle can help to balance out your features and create a more proportional look. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
