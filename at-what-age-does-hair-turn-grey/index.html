At What Age Does Hair Turn Grey

Written by Robby Aug 28, 2023 · 3 min read

As we age, our hair naturally begins to lose pigmentation, resulting in grey or white hair. However, the age at which this occurs can vary greatly from person to person. In this article, we will explore the factors that influence when hair turns grey, and provide tips for caring for grey hair.

Table of Contents

  • Factors That Influence Grey Hair
  • At What Age Does Hair Turn Grey?
  • Genetics and Grey Hair
  • Tips for Caring for Grey Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Factors That Influence Grey Hair

Several factors can influence when hair turns grey, including:

  • Genetics
  • Age
  • Stress
  • Nutrition
  • Medical conditions
  • Environmental factors

At What Age Does Hair Turn Grey?

Grey hair can start to appear at any age, but most people begin to see some grey hair by their mid-30s to early 40s. However, some people may not notice grey hair until they are in their 50s or older. The age at which hair turns grey is largely determined by genetics, but other factors such as stress and nutrition can also play a role.


Genetics and Grey Hair

Genetics play a significant role in when hair starts to turn grey. If your parents or grandparents started going grey early, there is a good chance that you will too. Ethnicity can also play a role, as people with darker skin and hair tend to grey later than those with lighter skin and hair.


Tips for Caring for Grey Hair

Grey hair can be more fragile and prone to dryness than pigmented hair. To keep your grey hair looking healthy and vibrant, try these tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to prevent breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil

Frequently Asked Questions

Can stress cause hair to turn grey?

While stress can contribute to hair loss, there is no evidence to suggest that it can cause hair to turn grey.

Can you prevent grey hair?

There is no way to prevent grey hair, as it is a natural part of the aging process. However, taking good care of your hair can help keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

Can you dye grey hair?

Yes, you can dye grey hair. However, it can be more difficult to achieve a natural-looking color, and grey hair can be more prone to damage from color treatments.

Is it true that plucking a grey hair will cause more grey hairs to grow?

No, this is a myth. Plucking a grey hair will not cause more grey hairs to grow, but it can damage the hair follicle and lead to hair loss.

Can supplements or vitamins prevent grey hair?

There is no scientific evidence to support the use of supplements or vitamins to prevent grey hair. However, a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy.

Is it normal to have grey hair at a young age?

While it is less common, it is possible to have grey hair at a young age. If you are concerned about premature greying, it is a good idea to talk to your doctor.
