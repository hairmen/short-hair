Best Braided Hairstyles For Big Foreheads

Written by Christine Sep 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a big forehead, you might feel self-conscious about it. But don't worry! You can easily create a flattering hairstyle by braiding your hair. In this article, we will go over some of the best braided hairstyles for big foreheads.

Description

Braids are a great option for big foreheads because they add volume and texture to your hair, which can help balance out your facial features. Plus, there are so many different braided hairstyles to choose from! Whether you prefer a simple braid or a more intricate style, there is sure to be a braided hairstyle that will suit your taste.

How-To

To create a braided hairstyle for a big forehead, you will need to start by sectioning off your hair. You can either do this by parting your hair down the middle or by creating a deep side part. Once you have your hair sectioned off, you can begin braiding it.

Simple Braid

The simplest braided hairstyle is the classic braid. To create this style, start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, divide your hair into three equal sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Continue crossing the right and left sections over the middle section until you reach the end of your hair. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

French Braid

The French braid is a more intricate style that looks great on people with big foreheads. To create this style, start by dividing your hair into three sections at the crown of your head. Begin a regular braid by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section. As you continue braiding, add in small sections of hair from the sides of your head to the right and left sections before crossing them over the middle section. Continue adding in hair until you reach the nape of your neck, then finish with a regular braid.

Dutch Braid

The Dutch braid is similar to the French braid, but instead of crossing the sections over the middle, you cross them under. This creates a braid that sits on top of your hair instead of underneath it. To create a Dutch braid, start by dividing your hair into three sections at the crown of your head. Cross the right section under the middle section, then cross the left section under the middle section. As you continue braiding, add in small sections of hair from the sides of your head to the right and left sections before crossing them under the middle section. Continue adding in hair until you reach the nape of your neck, then finish with a regular braid.

Step-by-Step

Here are the steps to create a simple braid:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three equal sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Continue crossing the right and left sections over the middle section until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating braided hairstyles for big foreheads:

  • Experiment with different braided styles to find the one that looks best on you.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add some extra flair to your braids.
  • Don't be afraid to add some volume to your hair by teasing it at the crown. This can help balance out a big forehead.
  • Keep your hair healthy by using a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about braided hairstyles for big foreheads:

What braided hairstyles are best for big foreheads?

French braids, Dutch braids, and simple braids are all great options for big foreheads.

Can I wear my hair down if I have a big forehead?

Of course! Braids are just one option for creating a flattering hairstyle. You can also wear your hair down, or try a different hairstyle like a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.

Will braids damage my hair?

Braids can be damaging if they are done too tightly or if they are left in for too long. Be sure to take breaks between wearing braids, and don't pull your hair too tightly when braiding it.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and try to let it air dry whenever possible. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Additionally, be sure to eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth. Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can all have a negative impact on your hair.

Conclusion

Braids are a great option for people with big foreheads. Whether you prefer a simple braid or a more intricate style like a French braid or a Dutch braid, there is sure to be a braided hairstyle that will flatter your facial features. Be sure to experiment with different styles and use the tips in this article to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
