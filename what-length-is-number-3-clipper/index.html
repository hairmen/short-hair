What Length Is Number 3 Clipper

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to give yourself a new haircut or trim your beard? Do you want to know what length is number 3 clipper? Look no further! In this article, we will explain what a number 3 clipper is, how to use it, and some tips on hair care.

Description

A number 3 clipper guard is a hair clipper attachment that cuts hair to 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm. It is one of the most popular clipper sizes used by hairdressers and barbers. It is perfect for creating a medium-length haircut or trimming a beard. The number 3 guard can be used with any hair clipper that supports detachable guards.

How to

To use a number 3 clipper guard, you first need to attach it to your hair clipper. Make sure the clipper is clean and oiled before you start. Then, turn on the clipper and start cutting from the bottom of your hairline, moving the clipper upwards against the direction of hair growth. Repeat this process until you have cut all the hair to the desired length. Be careful around the ears and neckline, as these areas require more precision.

For trimming a beard, follow the same process but with more attention to detail. Use the clipper in the direction of the hair growth, starting from the top of the beard and moving downwards. Use a smaller clipper guard around the edges of the beard for a cleaner finish.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to use a number 3 clipper guard:

  1. Clean and oil your hair clipper before use.
  2. Attach the number 3 clipper guard to the clipper.
  3. Turn on the clipper and start cutting from the bottom of your hairline, moving the clipper upwards against the direction of hair growth.
  4. Repeat until you have cut all hair to the desired length.
  5. Be careful around the ears and neckline, as these areas require more precision.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a number 3 clipper guard:

  • Use a comb to separate and guide the hair for more precise cutting.
  • Make sure the clipper is not too hot, as it can burn the skin.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to the desired length.
  • Use a smaller clipper guard around the edges of the hair or beard for a cleaner finish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 3 clipper guards:

Can I use a number 3 clipper guard for any hairstyle?

A number 3 clipper guard is perfect for a medium-length haircut or trimming a beard. However, it may not work for all hairstyles. If you are not sure which guard to use, consult a professional hairdresser or barber.

How often should I oil my clipper?

You should oil your clipper every time you use it to keep it running smoothly and prevent rust. Use a few drops of clipper oil on the blades and turn on the clipper for a few seconds to distribute the oil evenly.

Can I use a number 3 clipper guard on wet hair?

It is not recommended to use a clipper on wet hair as it can damage the clipper blades and cause uneven cutting. Make sure your hair is dry before using a clipper.

Hair Care

After using a clipper, it is important to clean it and store it properly to prevent rust and bacteria growth. Remove the guard and clean the blades with a brush or cloth. Store the clipper in a dry and cool place, away from moisture and sunlight.

In conclusion, a number 3 clipper guard is a versatile tool for cutting hair and trimming beards to a medium length. Use it with caution and follow the tips and steps mentioned above for best results. Happy trimming!
