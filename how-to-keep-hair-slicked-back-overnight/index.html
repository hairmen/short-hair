How To Keep Hair Slicked Back Overnight Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Sep 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have ever tried to slick back your hair for a special occasion, you know how frustrating it can be when your hair starts to lose its shape and style within a few hours. However, with the right techniques, you can keep your hair slicked back overnight and wake up with a flawless hairstyle the next day. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to keep hair slicked back overnight.

How To Keep Hair Slicked Back Overnight: Step By Step

Step 1: Start With Clean Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it is essential to wash and condition it thoroughly. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that can weigh down your hair and prevent it from holding its shape. Once you have washed and conditioned your hair, towel-dry it gently and comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Apply Your Styling Products

After your hair is clean and dry, it is time to apply your styling products. Start by applying a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, use your hands to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to work it into the roots and tips.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Once you have applied your styling product, use a fine-toothed comb to slick your hair back. Start at the front of your hairline and comb your hair straight back, making sure to create a smooth and even surface. Use a mirror to check that the sides of your hair are straight and symmetrical.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair

To keep your hair slicked back overnight, you need to secure it in place. One way to do this is to use a hair tie or elastic band to create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Make sure to twist the ponytail tightly and secure it with bobby pins or hair clips to keep it from loosening during the night.

Tips For Keeping Your Hair Slicked Back Overnight

Now that you know the basic steps for keeping your hair slicked back overnight, here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

Tip 1: Use A Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help prevent your hair from getting tangled or frizzy while you sleep. Silk is smoother and gentler on hair than cotton, so it can help preserve your slicked-back style and prevent any unwanted kinks or creases.

Tip 2: Avoid Touching Your Hair

While it may be tempting to touch or adjust your hair during the night, this can cause your style to loosen or fall out of place. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible and resist the urge to check your style in the mirror.

Tip 3: Refresh Your Style In The Morning

When you wake up in the morning, your hair may have shifted or lost some of its shape. To refresh your style, use a small amount of water or hair gel to smooth down any flyaways or frizz. You can also use a fine-toothed comb to reshape the sides and back of your hair.

FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What kind of hair products should I use to keep my hair slicked back overnight?

A: You can use a variety of hair products to keep your hair slicked back overnight, including hair gel, pomade, hair wax, or hair spray. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and texture and apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Q: Can I use a hairnet or scarf to protect my hair while I sleep?

A: Yes, you can use a hairnet or scarf to protect your hair while you sleep. This can help prevent any frizz or tangling and keep your style in place. Make sure to tie the hairnet or scarf securely to prevent it from slipping off during the night.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I want to keep it slicked back overnight?

A: It is best to wash your hair every 2-3 days if you want to keep it slicked back overnight. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care Tips For Slicked-Back Hair

Keeping your hair slicked back overnight requires some extra care and attention to ensure that your hair stays healthy and strong. Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain your slicked-back style:

Tip 1: Use A Deep-Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep-conditioning treatment once a week can help nourish and moisturize your hair, making it more resilient and easier to style. Look for a treatment that contains natural oils and vitamins to help strengthen your hair from the inside out.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Try to avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do style your hair with heat.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends from forming. Schedule a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.

Conclusion

Keeping your hair slicked back overnight may seem like a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a flawless style that lasts all day and night. Remember to start with clean hair, apply your styling products evenly, and secure your hair in place with a low ponytail. Use a silk pillowcase to prevent tangling and avoid touching your hair as much as possible during the night. With these tips and tricks, you can wake up with a perfect slicked-back hairstyle every morning.
