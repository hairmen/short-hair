How To Cut Curly Hair On Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting curly hair can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not familiar with the texture and style. But with the right tools and techniques, cutting curly hair on guys can be a breeze. In this guide, we’ll walk you through everything you need to know, including how to prepare, how to cut, and how to care for curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Preparing to Cut Curly Hair on Guys
  • How to Cut Curly Hair on Guys
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair on Guys
  • FAQs About Cutting Curly Hair on Guys
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair on Guys

Preparing to Cut Curly Hair on Guys

Before you start cutting, it’s important to prepare both your client and your tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clipper with attachments (optional)
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Towel

Once you have your tools ready, it’s time to prepare your client. Start by asking them what they want and expect from the haircut. This will help you determine the length, style, and overall look they’re going for.

Next, you’ll want to assess their hair type and texture. Curly hair comes in different patterns and densities, so it’s important to know what you’re working with. Take a close look at their curls and determine if they’re loose, medium, or tight. This will help you decide how much length to take off and where to create layers.

How to Cut Curly Hair on Guys

Now that you’re prepared, it’s time to start cutting. Follow these steps:

  1. Start by wetting the hair with the spray bottle. This will help you see the curls more clearly and make them easier to cut.
  2. Use the wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair and remove any knots or snarls.
  3. Section the hair into four or five parts, depending on the thickness and length. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.
  4. Start cutting one section at a time, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use your scissors to trim off the desired length, but be careful not to cut too much at once.
  5. When you’ve finished one section, move on to the next one. Make sure to keep the length and style consistent throughout the haircut.
  6. If your client wants shorter hair, you can use clippers with attachments to create a fade or undercut. Just make sure to blend the shorter hair into the longer hair seamlessly.
  7. When you’ve finished cutting, use the wide-tooth comb to define the curls and check for any unevenness or gaps.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair on Guys

Cutting curly hair on guys can be challenging, but these tips will help you get the best results:

  • Always start with a smaller amount of hair, and cut gradually. This will help you avoid cutting too much and creating unevenness.
  • Use your fingers to feel the curls and determine the length. This will help you create a more natural and textured look.
  • Don’t cut the hair when it’s dry, as this can cause the curls to shrink and create unevenness.
  • Trim the ends of each curl individually, rather than cutting straight across. This will help create a more layered and textured look.

FAQs About Cutting Curly Hair on Guys

How often should I cut curly hair on guys?

It depends on the individual and their hair type. For most guys with curly hair, cutting every 6-8 weeks is recommended to keep the curls looking fresh and healthy.

Can I cut curly hair on guys when it’s dry?

It’s not recommended to cut curly hair when it’s dry, as this can cause unevenness and shrinkage. Always wet the hair first to make it easier to cut and style.

What’s the best way to create layers in curly hair?

The best way to create layers in curly hair is to cut each curl individually, rather than cutting straight across. This will help create a more natural and textured look.

Hair Care for Curly Hair on Guys

Once you’ve finished cutting, it’s important to educate your client on how to care for their curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the hair.
  • Avoid brushing or combing the hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance the curls and reduce frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define and moisturize the curls.
  • Protect the hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to cut curly hair on guys with confidence and ease. Remember to communicate with your client, assess their hair type, and take your time to create the best results.
