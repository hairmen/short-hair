How To Cut Short Hair In Steps At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done successfully. This guide will take you through the steps to cut short hair at home in a relaxed English language. Whether you are looking to save money or just want to try something new, follow these steps for a professional-looking cut.

How to Cut Short Hair in Steps at Home

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It’s also a good idea to have a mirror that you can position in front of you and behind you to see all angles of your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is, which can lead to uneven cuts.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then dividing it into four equal sections – two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section of your hair. Comb it out and hold it straight up from your head. Use your scissors to cut the hair straight across, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Make sure you cut small sections at a time and check your work frequently.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Move on to the side sections of your hair. Comb out each section and hold it straight up from your head. Use the back section as a guide and cut the side sections to the same length. Again, cut small sections at a time and check your work frequently.

Step 5: Cut the Bangs

If you have bangs, comb them out and hold them straight up from your head. Use your scissors to cut them to the desired length. For a softer look, cut into the ends of the bangs instead of straight across.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, remove the hair clips and shake your hair out to see how it falls. Use your mirror to check your work from all angles. Make any necessary adjustments to even out any uneven sections.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair at Home

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when cutting short hair at home:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Cut small sections at a time to avoid making big mistakes.
  • Start with longer lengths and gradually cut more off until you reach your desired length.
  • When in doubt, cut less. You can always go back and cut more later.
  • Don’t rush. Take your time and make sure you’re happy with each section before moving on.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about cutting short hair at home:

Yes, you can cut your own hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve professional-looking results.

You will need sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It’s also a good idea to have a mirror that you can position in front of you and behind you to see all angles of your hair.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Hair at Home

After cutting your hair at home, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Keep your hair clean and well-conditioned.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your new style.
  • Use hair products that are appropriate for your hair type.

Cutting short hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve professional-looking results. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to keep your hair looking great.
