My Hair Is Falling Too Much What To Do

Written by Aprilia Nov 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you experiencing excessive hair fall lately? Losing hair can be a stressful and frustrating experience, especially if you don't know what's causing it. But don't worry, you're not alone. Hair fall is a common issue that affects both men and women. In this article, we'll discuss the possible causes of hair fall and provide tips on what you can do to prevent it.

Table of Contents

  • Causes of Hair Fall
  • How to Prevent Hair Fall
  • Step-by-Step Hair Care Routine
  • Tips for Healthy Hair

Causes of Hair Fall

Hair fall can be caused by a variety of factors, including:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Poor nutrition
  • Stress
  • Medications
  • Chemical treatments

If you're unsure about the cause of your hair fall, it's best to consult a dermatologist or hair specialist. They can perform tests to determine the underlying cause of your hair fall and provide a personalized treatment plan.

How to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some tips on how to prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools or use them sparingly
  • Avoid chemical treatments
  • Reduce stress through exercise, meditation, or other techniques
  • Get enough sleep
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week

By following these tips, you can help prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.

Step-by-Step Hair Care Routine

Here's a simple hair care routine that you can follow to prevent hair fall:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Gently towel-dry your hair.
  3. Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to prevent breakage.
  4. Avoid using heat styling tools or use them sparingly.
  5. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.
  6. Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  7. Reduce stress through exercise, meditation, or other techniques.
  8. Get enough sleep.
  9. Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week.
  10. Consult a dermatologist or hair specialist if you're experiencing excessive hair fall.

By following this simple routine, you can maintain healthy hair and prevent hair fall.

Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Brush your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel.
  • Avoid using too many hair products.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy, shiny, and beautiful hair.

FAQs

Q: Is hair fall normal?

A: Yes, it's normal to lose 50-100 strands of hair per day. However, if you're losing more hair than usual or noticing bald patches, it's best to consult a dermatologist or hair specialist.

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you're stressed, your body produces cortisol, a hormone that can affect your hair growth cycle.

Q: Can nutritional deficiencies cause hair fall?

A: Yes, nutritional deficiencies can cause hair fall. Make sure to eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to keep your hair healthy.

Remember, hair fall is a common issue that can be prevented with proper care and treatment. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can maintain healthy and beautiful hair.
