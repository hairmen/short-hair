How To Hide Hair Under Bald Cap A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to create a bald look for a costume or performance? One of the key elements to achieving a convincing bald look is hiding your hair under a bald cap. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps for how to hide hair under a bald cap, including tips and FAQs to help you get the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Hair Under Bald Cap
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Hiding Hair Under Bald Cap
  • FAQs About Hiding Hair Under Bald Cap
  • Hair Care Tips for After Removing Bald Cap

How to Hide Hair Under Bald Cap

The key to hiding hair under a bald cap is to make sure that your hair is as flat and close to your head as possible. This means that you’ll need to use some products and techniques to help flatten your hair and hold it in place under the bald cap.

Products You’ll Need

Before you get started, make sure that you have the following products on hand:

  • Bald cap
  • Hairspray
  • Double-sided tape
  • Bobby pins
  • Wig cap

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to hide your hair under a bald cap:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make it as clean and smooth as possible. Then, towel dry your hair and comb it out to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Apply Hairspray

Apply a generous amount of hairspray to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to hold your hair in place and create a flatter surface for the bald cap.

Step 3: Put on a Wig Cap

Put on a wig cap to help keep your hair in place and create a smooth surface for the bald cap to sit on.

Step 4: Apply Double-Sided Tape

Cut a small piece of double-sided tape and apply it to the front, back, and sides of your head. This will help to hold the bald cap in place and prevent it from slipping or sliding around.

Step 5: Put on the Bald Cap

Place the bald cap over your head, making sure that it is centered and covers all of your hair. Use your fingers to smooth out any wrinkles or bumps in the cap.

Step 6: Secure the Bald Cap

Use bobby pins to secure the bald cap to your wig cap and hair. You can also use more double-sided tape if needed to ensure that the bald cap stays in place.

Step 7: Style the Bald Cap

If needed, you can trim the edges of the bald cap to create a more natural look. You can also use makeup or paint to create a more convincing bald look.

Tips for Hiding Hair Under Bald Cap

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when hiding your hair under a bald cap:

  • Use a high-quality bald cap that matches your skin tone as closely as possible.
  • Make sure to apply enough hairspray to hold your hair in place, but not so much that it becomes stiff or crunchy.
  • Don’t be afraid to use bobby pins to secure the bald cap – the more secure it is, the better it will look.
  • Consider wearing a wig cap under the bald cap to create a smoother surface and prevent any hair from poking through.
  • Practice applying the bald cap and securing it in place before your performance or event.

FAQs About Hiding Hair Under Bald Cap

Q: Can I wear a bald cap if I have long hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a bald cap even if you have long hair. However, you’ll need to make sure that your hair is as flat and close to your head as possible to create a smooth surface for the bald cap to sit on.

Q: How do I remove a bald cap?

A: To remove a bald cap, gently peel it off from the back to the front. Be careful not to pull on any hair or skin.

Q: How long can I wear a bald cap?

A: It’s not recommended to wear a bald cap for more than a few hours at a time, as it can become uncomfortable and cause sweating.

Hair Care Tips for After Removing Bald Cap

After removing your bald cap, make sure to wash and condition your hair to remove any hairspray or other products. Avoid using any heat styling tools for a few days to give your hair a break.

Following these tips and steps will help you achieve a convincing bald look for any performance or costume. With a little practice and patience, you’ll be able to hide your hair under a bald cap like a pro.
