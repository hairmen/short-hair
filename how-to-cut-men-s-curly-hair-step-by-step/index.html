How To Cut Men s Curly Hair Step By Step

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting men's curly hair can be tricky, but with the right technique, tools, and a little patience, it can be done easily. In this blog post, we'll walk you through the step-by-step process of cutting men's curly hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step-by-Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care

Description

Men's curly hair can be challenging to cut because of its unique texture and shape. It requires more attention and care than straight hair. Curly hair tends to shrink as it dries, so it's essential to cut it when it's dry to get the desired length.

How to

Before you start cutting curly hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wet hair can elongate, and you may end up cutting it too short.

Step 1: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using hair clips. Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Use the hair clips to keep the hair in place as you work.

Step 2: Cut the Hair

Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut the hair. Start at the ends and work your way up. Cut small sections at a time, and make sure you're cutting straight across.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

After you've cut all the sections, blend the hair by using scissors to cut into the ends. This technique helps to avoid any harsh lines and creates a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Cut the hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises
  • Start with small sections and work your way up
  • Use the point-cutting technique to create texture

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's curly hair:

Q: Can you cut curly hair with clippers?

A: It's possible to cut curly hair with clippers, but it's not recommended. Clippers can create a choppy look, and it's difficult to blend the hair.

Q: How often should you cut curly hair?

A: It depends on the individual's hair growth and style. Generally, it's recommended to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting men's curly hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the hair
  • Apply leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair

With these tips and techniques, cutting men's curly hair can be a simple and enjoyable experience. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always use sharp scissors.
