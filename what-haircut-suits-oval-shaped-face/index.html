What Haircut Suits Oval Shaped Face A Guide In 2023

Written by Aprilia Oct 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face, you are lucky as it’s a versatile face shape that can carry off various hairstyles. However, it can be difficult to decide which haircut will suit your oval face shape the best. The good news is that we’ve got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on what haircut suits oval-shaped face and give you some tips on how to maintain your hair post-haircut.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oval face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a slightly narrow chin. People with oval-shaped faces have a well-balanced face and can carry off various hairstyles. However, it’s important to choose a haircut that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.

How to

The first step in choosing a haircut that suits your oval face shape is to determine the length of your hair. Oval-shaped faces can carry off both long and short hairstyles. However, it’s important to choose a style that complements your face shape and highlights your features.

Short Haircuts

For oval-shaped faces, a short haircut that adds volume to the crown of the head works best. This includes pixie cuts, bobs, and layered hairstyles. A side-swept fringe is also a great option as it adds dimension to the face.

Long Haircuts

For long haircuts, soft layers that frame the face work best for oval-shaped faces. Avoid heavy bangs as they can make your face look shorter. Soft waves and curls also complement oval-shaped faces, but avoid tight curls as they can make your face look wider.

Step by step

Here are some easy steps to follow when choosing a haircut for an oval-shaped face:

  1. Determine the length of hair you wish to keep.
  2. Consider the texture of your hair and choose a haircut that complements it.
  3. Find a style that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist and take their advice on the best haircut for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for an oval-shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face as they can make your face look wider.
  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.
  • Consider the texture of your hair when choosing a haircut as it can affect the final look of your hairstyle.
  • Consult with your hairstylist and take their advice on the best haircut for you.

FAQ

Q. Can oval-shaped faces carry off short hairstyles?

A. Yes, oval-shaped faces can carry off short hairstyles. Pixie cuts, bobs, and layered hairstyles work best for oval-shaped faces.

Q. Can oval-shaped faces carry off long hairstyles?

A. Yes, oval-shaped faces can carry off long hairstyles. Soft layers that frame the face work best for oval-shaped faces.

Q. Which hairstyle should oval-shaped faces avoid?

A. Oval-shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face as they can make your face look wider.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen a haircut that suits your oval-shaped face, it’s important to maintain your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair post-haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as they can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

With these tips and guidelines, you can choose a haircut that flatters your oval-shaped face and keeps your hair looking healthy and stylish. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and take their advice on the best haircut for you.
