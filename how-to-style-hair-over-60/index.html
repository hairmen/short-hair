How To Style Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

As we age, our hair tends to lose its shine and volume. However, with the right styling techniques and products, you can still have gorgeous hair even in your 60s. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style hair over 60.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Hair Over 60
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling, it's important to take care of your hair first. As we age, our hair becomes more fragile and prone to breakage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair

By following these hair care tips, you'll have a good foundation for styling your hair.

How to Style Hair Over 60

When it comes to styling hair over 60, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and features. Second, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and style on a daily basis.

Here are some hairstyles that work well for women over 60:

  • Short pixie cut
  • Bob haircut
  • Layered haircut
  • Shoulder-length haircut

These hairstyles are easy to style and maintain, which is key for busy women over 60.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to style a short pixie cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define your hair and add shine.

For a bob haircut, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add shine.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair serum to tame any frizz and add shine.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your hair over 60:

  • Use a volumizing spray to add volume to thinning hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.

FAQs

Q: Is it possible to have long hair over 60?

A: Yes, it's possible to have long hair over 60. However, keep in mind that long hair can be more difficult to maintain and style on a daily basis.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people prefer to wash their hair every day, while others wash it every few days.

Q: Can I still use hot tools on my hair?

A: Yes, you can still use hot tools on your hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Conclusion

Styling hair over 60 doesn't have to be difficult. By following these tips and tricks, you can have gorgeous hair that's easy to maintain and style on a daily basis.
