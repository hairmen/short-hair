How To Put Your Hair Up With A Comb Clip

Written by Robby Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle, but don't want to spend hours in front of the mirror? A comb clip might just be the solution you need. This simple accessory can help you create a variety of hairstyles that are both stylish and easy to achieve. In this article, we'll show you how to put your hair up with a comb clip in just a few simple steps.

Description

A comb clip is a hair accessory that consists of a comb and a clip. The comb is used to create the desired style, while the clip holds the hair in place. Comb clips come in a variety of sizes, shapes, and materials, so you can choose one that suits your hair type and the style you want to create.

How to Put Your Hair Up with a Comb Clip

Putting your hair up with a comb clip is easy. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to create the style you want.

Step 2: Choose Your Comb Clip

Choose the comb clip you want to use. If you have thick hair, choose a larger comb clip. If you have thin hair, choose a smaller clip. You can also choose a clip that matches your hair color or one that adds a pop of color to your hairstyle.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into the style you want. You can create a half-up hairstyle or a full updo. Use your fingers or a comb to create the sections.

Step 4: Insert the Comb

Take the comb part of the clip and insert it into the section of hair you want to secure. Make sure the teeth of the comb are facing up and the clip is facing down.

Step 5: Secure the Clip

Clip the hair in place by sliding the clip up and over the comb. Make sure the clip is secure and the hair is held tightly in place.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat these steps for each section of hair until you have achieved the desired style. You can add more clips for extra hold or to create a more complex style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your comb clip:

  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place.
  • Try adding accessories like flowers or jewels to your hairstyle.
  • Choose a clip that matches your outfit to create a coordinated look.

FAQ

What kind of hair works best with a comb clip?

A comb clip works best with medium to long hair that is straight or has a slight wave. It may not work as well with very curly or thick hair.

Can I use a comb clip on wet hair?

It is not recommended to use a comb clip on wet hair, as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

How do I clean my comb clip?

You can clean your comb clip by gently wiping it with a damp cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or soaking it in water.

Hair Care

Using a comb clip can be a great way to change up your hairstyle, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to put your hair up with a comb clip like a pro. Give it a try and see how easy it can be to create a stylish new look.
