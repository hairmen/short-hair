Description

Humidity can make even the straightest hair frizz up, which can be frustrating and embarrassing. That's why it's important to use the right hairspray to fight humidity and keep your hair looking its best. The best hairspray for this purpose is one that is specifically designed to combat humidity and provide a strong hold. These hairsprays usually contain ingredients that create a barrier around the hair, preventing moisture from entering and causing frizz. Some of the best hairsprays to fight humidity include:

How to Use Hairspray to Fight Humidity

Using hairspray to fight humidity is easy, and it can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that are specifically designed to combat frizz. This will help prepare your hair for styling and make it easier to manage.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to smooth out any frizz or flyaways. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment to help define your curls and prevent them from frizzing up.

Step 3: Apply Hairspray

Spray a small amount of hairspray onto your hair, focusing on the areas that are prone to frizz. Be sure to hold the can about 6-8 inches away from your head to avoid applying too much product.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair as desired. If you have curly hair, scrunch your curls with your fingers to help define them and prevent them from frizzing up.

Tips for Using Hairspray to Fight Humidity

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using hairspray to fight humidity:

  • Apply hairspray to dry hair for best results.
  • Use a light mist of hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Choose a hairspray with a humidity-resistant formula for maximum frizz control.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much after applying hairspray, as this can cause frizz.
  • Don't apply too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

FAQs

Q: Can I use hairspray on wet hair?

A: It's best to use hairspray on dry hair for best results. Applying hairspray to wet hair can make it look greasy and weighed down.

Q: How often should I apply hairspray?

A: It depends on your hair type and the level of humidity in the air. If you have very curly or frizzy hair, you may need to apply hairspray more often to keep it under control.

Q: Will hairspray make my hair look stiff?

A: It depends on how much hairspray you apply. If you apply too much, your hair may look stiff and unnatural. To avoid this, use a light mist of hairspray and hold the can about 6-8 inches away from your head.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Frizz

In addition to using the right hairspray, there are some other hair care tips you can follow to prevent frizz:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to dry out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing it, as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently blot your hair to remove excess water.

Now you know the best hairspray to fight humidity and how to use it! Follow these tips and you'll be able to keep your hair looking sleek and smooth no matter the weather.
