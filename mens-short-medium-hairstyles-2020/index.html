Mens Short Medium Hairstyles 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on men's short medium hairstyles for the year 2020. Whether you're looking for a new style to rock or just want to freshen up your current look, we have got you covered. In this post, we will cover everything from the latest trends to how to get the perfect cut for your hair type. So, let's dive in!

Description

Men's short medium hairstyles have been a popular choice for years now, and the trend shows no signs of slowing down. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still looks put-together. It's also versatile enough to work for any occasion, from the office to a night out with friends.

How to Get the Perfect Cut

The key to getting the perfect short medium haircut is to find a style that works well with your hair type and face shape. If you have thick hair, a choppy, layered cut can help reduce bulk and give your hair some texture. For those with fine or thinning hair, a shorter length with a side part can help create the illusion of fullness.

When it comes to face shape, a good rule of thumb is to balance out your features. For example, if you have a round face, a cut with more height on top can help elongate your face. If you have a square jawline, a cut with softer edges can help soften the overall look of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect short medium haircut:

  1. Start by consulting with your hairstylist to find a style that works well with your hair type and face shape.
  2. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it's damp.
  3. Using a comb, part your hair where you want it to go.
  4. Using a pair of scissors or clippers, trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  5. Blend the sides and back into the top of your hair using scissors or clippers.
  6. Trim the top of your hair to your desired length and style as desired.
  7. Finish with a styling product like pomade or wax to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short medium haircut:

  • Bring in a photo of the style you want to help your hairstylist understand your vision.
  • Be open to your hairstylist's suggestions - they may have a better idea for your hair than you do!
  • Invest in a good quality styling product to keep your hair looking great.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's short medium hairstyles:

Q: What is a short medium hairstyle?

A: A short medium hairstyle is a haircut that falls somewhere between short and medium length. It's typically around two to four inches in length.

Q: What face shape is best suited for a short medium haircut?

A: Short medium haircuts work well for a variety of face shapes, but they are particularly flattering for those with oval or square-shaped faces.

Q: How often should I get a trim with a short medium haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every four to six weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your short medium hairstyle looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish short medium haircut all year long!
