How To Style My Thin Medium Length Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have thin medium length hair, you might find it challenging to style your locks. However, with the right techniques, you can achieve the desired look. In this article, we will show you how to style your thin medium length hair with ease. Keep on reading to discover our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Thin medium length hair refers to hair that has a moderate thickness and falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. This hair type can be challenging to style as it lacks volume and texture. However, with the right styling techniques, you can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

How to Style My Thin Medium Length Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your thin medium length hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it is clean and free from any product buildup. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair to add volume and texture. Distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair while lifting the roots to create volume. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold.

Step 4: Create Loose Waves

Using a curling wand or a flat iron, create loose waves in your hair. This will add movement and texture to your locks.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your thin medium length hair:

  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Consider getting layers to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thin medium length hair:

Can I straighten my thin medium length hair?

Yes, you can straighten your thin medium length hair. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

What hairstyles are suitable for thin medium length hair?

Hairstyles that add volume and texture to your hair are suitable for thin medium length hair. Some examples include loose waves, braids, and messy buns.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and voluminous hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes foods rich in vitamins and minerals.

Styling thin medium length hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve your desired look. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and voluminous.
