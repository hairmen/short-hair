How To Keep Hair Back For Guys Without Gel A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're a guy who wants to keep his hair back without using gel, you're in the right place. While gel can be an effective way to keep your hair styled, it can also be messy and difficult to work with. But don't worry, there are plenty of other options available to you. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for keeping your hair back without gel.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Hair Back for Guys Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Keep Hair Back for Guys Without Gel

Before we dive into the specifics, let's talk about why you might want to avoid using gel in the first place. For starters, gel can be sticky and difficult to wash out. It can also leave your hair looking greasy and flat. If you're looking for a more natural look, or if you have sensitive skin that reacts poorly to gel, there are plenty of other options to consider.

So, how can you keep your hair back without gel? Here are a few ideas:

Bobby Pins

Bobby pins are a great option if you have shorter hair. Simply gather your hair back and secure it in place with a few bobby pins. This is a quick and easy solution that can be done in just a few minutes.

Headbands

If you have longer hair, a headband can be a great way to keep it out of your face. Look for a headband that's made of a soft, stretchy material that won't irritate your skin. You can wear it with your hair pulled back or simply pushed out of your face.

Pomade

Pomade is a hair product that's similar to gel, but it's typically less sticky and easier to work with. It can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, including slicked-back looks. Simply apply a small amount of pomade to your hair and use a comb to style it as desired.

Hair Ties

Hair ties are another great option for guys with longer hair. Simply gather your hair back and secure it in place with a hair tie. You can wear it in a ponytail or a bun, depending on your preference.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the options available to you, let's go over a step-by-step guide for keeping your hair back without gel:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet or oily, it will be more difficult to style.
  2. Decide on the style you want to create. Do you want a slicked-back look, a ponytail, or something else?
  3. Choose the appropriate product or accessory. Depending on the style you want, you may need bobby pins, a headband, pomade, or a hair tie.
  4. Apply the product (if necessary). If you're using pomade, apply a small amount to your hair and use a comb to style it as desired.
  5. Secure your hair in place using the accessory of your choice. If you're using bobby pins, insert them into your hair at an angle so they stay in place.
  6. Check your hair in a mirror to make sure it looks the way you want it to.
  7. If necessary, adjust your hair or the accessory to get the look you want.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your hair without gel:

  • Experiment with different products and accessories to find what works best for your hair type and style.
  • Don't be afraid to ask a stylist for advice on how to style your hair without gel.
  • Consider getting a haircut that's more conducive to the style you want to create.
  • If you're using bobby pins, try criss-crossing them for added security.
  • If you're using a headband, make sure it's snug but not too tight. You don't want it to give you a headache!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping hair back for guys without gel:

Is it bad to use gel in your hair every day?

While gel is generally safe to use on hair, using it every day can lead to build-up and damage over time. If you're concerned about the long-term effects of using gel, consider trying some of the alternative styling methods mentioned above.

Can I use hairspray instead of gel?

Yes, hairspray can be used to keep your hair in place. However, it may not be as effective as gel for creating certain styles.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (such as from blow dryers or straighteners).
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to keep your hair back without gel. Whether you prefer a sleek, polished look or something more casual, there are plenty of options available to you. So go ahead and experiment, and see what works best for your hair type and style!
