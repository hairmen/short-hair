What Short Hairstyles Suit Fat Faces A Guide For 2023

If you have a round or chubby face, you may be wondering what short hairstyles suit your features. The good news is that there are plenty of options to choose from that will highlight your best features and complement your face shape.

Description

A fat face can be a challenge when it comes to choosing a hairstyle. However, with the right cut and style, you can accentuate your best features and draw attention away from any areas you may feel self-conscious about.

When choosing a short hairstyle for a fat face, it's important to consider your face shape and features. A layered cut can help to soften your features and add dimension to your hair, while a side-swept fringe can draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for a Fat Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for a fat face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: A round face will benefit from layers and volume, while an oval face can pull off a sleek bob.
  • Think about your hair texture: If you have thick, coarse hair, a layered cut can help to thin it out and make it more manageable.
  • Look for styles that complement your features: A side-swept fringe can draw attention to your eyes, while a deep side part can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for a Fat Face

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle that suits your face, it's important to know how to style it to achieve the best results:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant product to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. If you have a fringe, use a small round brush to blow dry it to the side.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and dimension.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for a Fat Face

Short hair can require more maintenance than longer styles, so it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for fat faces:

  • Can I still have bangs with a fat face? Yes, a side-swept fringe can look great with a fat face and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Will a short bob make my face look rounder? Not necessarily. A sleek bob can elongate your face and create a more balanced look.
  • Do I need to use styling products with short hair? It's a good idea to use a heat protectant and a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for a Fat Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Choose products that are appropriate for your hair type, such as a volumizing shampoo for fine hair or a smoothing serum for thick hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips in mind, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your fat face and feel confident and stylish.
