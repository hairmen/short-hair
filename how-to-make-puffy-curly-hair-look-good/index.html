How To Make Puffy Curly Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have puffy curly hair, you know the struggle of trying to tame it and make it look presentable. But fear not, with a few tips and tricks, you can make your hair look fabulous and embrace your natural curls. In this article, we’ll show you how to make puffy curly hair look good without spending hours on styling.

Description

Puffy curly hair can be a challenge to style, but it doesn't have to be. With the right hair care routine and the right styling tools, you can make your curls look bouncy and beautiful. In this guide, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make puffy curly hair look good.

How to Make Puffy Curly Hair Look Good: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with the Right Haircut

The first step to making your puffy curly hair look good is to start with the right haircut. A layered haircut can help to define your curls and reduce the bulkiness of your hair. It's best to consult with a hair stylist who has experience with curly hair to find the right cut for you.

Step 2: Hydrate Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to keep it hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and avoid products that contain sulfates and alcohol, as they can dry out your hair even more. Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair extra hydration.

Step 3: Use the Right Styling Products

The right styling products can make a big difference in how your puffy curly hair looks. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing creams and gels. Apply the products to damp hair and scrunch your hair to encourage your curls to form.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

A diffuser can help to dry your hair without disrupting your curls. Use a low heat setting and gently diffuse your hair, scrunching it as you go. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce the puffiness.

Tips for Making Puffy Curly Hair Look Good

Here are some additional tips to help you make your puffy curly hair look good:

  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, as it can cause friction and make your hair look frizzy.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I wash my puffy curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Q: Can I straighten my puffy curly hair?

A: While you can straighten your curly hair, it's not recommended as it can cause damage and make your hair look frizzy. Instead, embrace your natural curls and learn how to style them in a way that works for you.

Hair Care for Puffy Curly Hair

To keep your puffy curly hair looking good, it's important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Final Thoughts

With these tips and tricks, you can make your puffy curly hair look good and embrace your natural curls. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type. With a little effort, you can have beautiful, bouncy curls that you'll love showing off.
