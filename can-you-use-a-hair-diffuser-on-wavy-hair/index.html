What is a Hair Diffuser?

A hair diffuser is an attachment that you can use on your blow dryer to help spread out the airflow and reduce the intensity of the heat. This allows you to dry your hair without causing damage or frizz. Hair diffusers come in many different shapes, sizes, and materials, but they all work by breaking up the airflow and releasing it gently onto your hair.
