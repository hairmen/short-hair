How Can I Trim My Short Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short hair, you know how quickly it can grow out of shape. Going to the salon every few weeks can be expensive and time-consuming. But, don't worry, you can easily trim your short hair at home. In this guide, we will show you how to trim your short hair step by step.

How to Trim Your Short Hair at Home

Before we start, you should have the right tools: a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Now, let's get started:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean and dry hair. This will help you to see the shape of your hair and make it easier to trim.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to detangle your hair and make sure it is smooth. This will help you to see the shape of your hair better.

Step 3: Cut the Back of Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections and start with the back. Use a mirror to see the back of your head. Hold the hair between your fingers and trim it little by little. Don't cut too much at once, or you may end up with an uneven haircut.

Step 4: Trim the Sides

Next, move to the sides of your hair. Comb your hair down and start trimming the sides. Cut little by little to avoid cutting too much.

Step 5: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, comb them forward and trim them little by little. Make sure they are even on both sides.

Step 6: Check Your Hair in the Mirror

After trimming all sections, check your hair in the mirror. Make sure it is even and shaped the way you want it to be.

Step 7: Clean Up

After you have finished trimming your hair, clean up any loose hair on your clothes and the floor.

Tips for Trimming Your Short Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you trim your short hair at home:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and cut little by little.
  • Don't cut too much at once or you may end up with an uneven haircut.
  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head.
  • Trim your hair when it is dry to avoid cutting too much.

FAQs

1. How often should I trim my short hair?

You should trim your short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

2. Can I trim my short hair with kitchen scissors?

No, you should use sharp hairdressing scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Can I trim my short hair myself?

Yes, you can easily trim your short hair at home by following the steps above.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short hair healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.

Now that you know how to trim your short hair at home, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Remember to take your time and cut little by little to avoid cutting too much. Follow our tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
