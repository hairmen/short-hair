How To Tell If A Hairstyle Will Suit You

Written by Luffy Nov 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that doesn't suit you? Well, you're not alone. Many people struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that complements their face shape, hair type, and personality. In this article, we'll show you how to tell if a hairstyle will suit you, step-by-step.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has certain hairstyles that complement it.

If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides. Instead, go for styles that add height, like a high ponytail or top knot. Square faces look great with hairstyles that soften the jawline, like long layers or side-swept bangs. Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, but avoid styles that cover your face too much.

Heart-shaped faces look great with chin-length hairstyles or side-swept bangs. Diamond faces should opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, like a pixie cut or a bob with layers. Lastly, oblong faces look great with hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin, like a side-swept fringe or a chin-length bob.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thin hair, avoid too many layers as they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, go for blunt cuts or a lob. If you have thick hair, you can get away with more layers, but avoid adding too much volume to the sides.

If you have curly hair, embrace it and go for a style that shows off your curls. Straight hair looks great with blunt cuts or a sleek bob. If you have wavy hair, try a shoulder-length cut with layers to add movement.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, go for a low-maintenance style like a messy bun or a pixie cut. If you have a more formal job, go for a sleek bob or a classic updo.

If you're an athlete or someone who likes to work out, go for a style that can easily be tied up, like a ponytail or a braid. If you're a busy mom, go for a style that's easy to maintain, like a shoulder-length cut with layers.

Step 4: Look at Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have warm undertones, go for warm hair colors like golden blonde or auburn. If you have cool undertones, go for cool hair colors like platinum blonde or ash brown.

Step 5: Try Virtual Hairstyle Apps

If you're still not sure which hairstyle will suit you, try virtual hairstyle apps. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles before committing to a cut. Some popular virtual hairstyle apps include Hairstyle Mirror, Virtual Makeover, and YouCam Makeup.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Bring a photo of the hairstyle you want to your hairstylist.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one.

FAQs About Choosing the Right Hairstyle

What should I do if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic. Hair grows back, and you can always try a different style. Talk to your hairstylist about what you don't like and work together to find a solution.

How often should I get a haircut?

How often you should get a haircut depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, most people get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Can a hairstyle change my face shape?

A hairstyle can enhance your face shape, but it can't change it. If you want to change your face shape, you may want to consider cosmetic procedures like fillers or surgery.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips for your new hairstyle:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Consider using hair masks or oils to keep your hair healthy.

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but by following these steps and tips, you'll be sure to find the perfect style for you. Remember, your hairstyle should complement your face shape, hair type, and personality. And don't forget to take care of your hair once you've found the perfect style!
