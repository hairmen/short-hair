Shoulder Length Hair With Long Layers A Relaxed Style Guide

Written by William Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking flat and lifeless? Long layers could be the answer to your hair woes. With layers, your hair will have more movement, volume, and texture. And if you have shoulder length hair, this style is perfect for you. In this post, we'll show you how to achieve and maintain shoulder length hair with long layers. Read on for our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Long Layers

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to determine if this style will suit your face shape and hair type. Long layers work well on oval, round, and square face shapes. If you have fine or thin hair, long layers can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, long layers can help reduce weight and make it easier to manage.

Once you've determined that this style is right for you, it's time to head to the salon or grab your scissors. If you're not confident in cutting your hair, we recommend seeing a professional stylist. They can help you achieve the perfect length and layering for your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving shoulder length hair with long layers:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the back and comb it straight down.
  4. Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle of the section and working your way down. The angle will create a softer, more blended look.
  5. Repeat this step on all sections of your hair, making sure to cut at an angle and not straight across.
  6. Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Long Layers

Now that you have long layers, it's important to maintain them to keep your hair looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your layers.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add more volume and movement to your layers.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair with long layers:

What face shape is best for long layers?

Long layers work well on oval, round, and square face shapes.

Can I achieve this style at home?

If you're confident in cutting your own hair, you can achieve this style at home. However, we recommend seeing a professional stylist to ensure the best results.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your layers and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Advice

Aside from regular trims and avoiding heavy products, there are other things you can do to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.

With these tips and advice, you'll be able to achieve and maintain beautiful shoulder length hair with long layers. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this style is versatile and perfect for any occasion. So go ahead and give it a try!
