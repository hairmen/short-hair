How To Cut Medium Length Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Cutting your bangs is a simple way to switch up your look without making a drastic change. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut medium length bangs. Follow these easy steps to achieve a fresh and modern look.

How to Cut Medium Length Bangs

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before starting, make sure to gather all the tools you need for a successful haircut. You will need sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water for dampening your hair.

Step 2: Decide on Your Bangs Length

The first step in cutting your bangs is deciding on the length you want. Medium length bangs typically fall right above the eyebrows or just below. Use your fingers to measure the length you want and mark it with a comb.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

To make sure you only cut your bangs and not the rest of your hair, section off the front portion of your hair. Use hair clips to hold the remaining hair back.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Start by cutting the center of your bangs. Use the mark you made in step 2 as a guide and cut straight across. Then, gradually work your way to the sides, cutting at an angle to create a soft, gradual look. Remember to cut small amounts at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you finish cutting, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your bangs are even and at the desired length.

Tips for Cutting Medium Length Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your medium length bangs:

  • Take your time and cut small amounts at a time.
  • Make sure you're cutting straight across, not at an angle.
  • Use a comb to guide you and keep your cut even.
  • Don't cut too short, as your hair will dry and appear shorter once you're finished.
  • If you're unsure, it's always best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting medium length bangs:

  • Can I cut my bangs if my hair is curly?
  • Yes, you can cut your bangs if your hair is curly. Just make sure to cut them while they're dry and follow the same steps as outlined above.
  • How often should I trim my bangs?
  • It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.
  • Can I style my bangs differently?
  • Yes, you can style your bangs differently depending on your mood and occasion. Use a straightener for a sleek look or curl them for a more playful vibe.

Hair Care Tips

Caring for your bangs is just as important as cutting them. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup and grease.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as it can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a small amount of hair oil or serum to keep your bangs looking shiny and healthy.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and neat.

Now that you know how to cut medium length bangs, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to take your time and follow these steps carefully. With a little practice, you'll soon have the perfect set of bangs that suit your style and personality.
