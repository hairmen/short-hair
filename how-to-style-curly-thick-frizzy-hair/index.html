How To Style Curly Thick Frizzy Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly, thick, and frizzy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style it. Finding the right products and techniques can make all the difference. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to style your curly, thick, and frizzy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How To Style Curly Thick Frizzy Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we get into how to style your hair, it’s important to start with good hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.

How to Style Curly Thick Frizzy Hair

Now that you’ve got the basics of hair care down, let’s talk about how to style your curly thick frizzy hair.

1. Start with wet hair

It’s best to start styling your hair when it’s damp, so after you shower, gently squeeze out any excess water with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.

2. Apply product

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Use a diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on low heat to dry your hair. The diffuser will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.

4. Scrunch your hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. This will help to define your curls and add volume.

5. Finish with hairspray

Spray a light hold hairspray over your hair to set your curls in place and reduce frizz.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style your curly thick frizzy hair:

  1. Start with wet hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream.
  3. Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and dry your hair on low heat.
  4. Scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your curly thick frizzy hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using regular towels to dry your hair, as they can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.

FAQ

What’s the best way to detangle curly thick frizzy hair?

The best way to detangle curly thick frizzy hair is to use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers when your hair is wet and has conditioner in it.

How often should I deep condition my hair?

You should deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

What’s the best way to reduce frizz?

The best way to reduce frizz is to use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream, dry your hair with a diffuser, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Can I use a regular towel to dry my hair?

No, it’s best to avoid using regular towels to dry your hair as they can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls without the frizz. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair, and remember to start with good hair care to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
