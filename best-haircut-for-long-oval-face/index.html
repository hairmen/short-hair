Best Haircut For Long Oval Face Tips And Ideas

If you have a long oval face, you may find it challenging to choose the right haircut that flatters your facial features. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a unique look that suits your personality and lifestyle.

Description

A long oval face is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a tapered chin. The length of the face is longer than its width, making it an ideal face shape for a variety of hairstyles. However, the key is to create balance and proportion by choosing a haircut that adds width to the face and softens the angles.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Oval Face

When choosing a haircut for a long oval face, you want to avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer or narrower. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of your face to create balance and proportion. Here are some of the best haircuts for long oval face:

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a versatile haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair while creating a soft, feminine look. The layers can be cut to frame your face and add width to the sides, while the length can be adjusted to suit your personal style.

Long Layers

Long layers are another great option for long oval face as it adds volume and movement to the hair. Layers can be cut around the face to create softness and can be styled in loose waves or curls for a romantic look.

Bangs

Bangs are an excellent way to add width to the forehead and balance out a long oval face. Soft, side-swept bangs are the most flattering for this face shape as it creates a diagonal line across the face, making it appear wider.

Medium Length Haircut

A medium length haircut is another great option for a long oval face as it adds volume and width to the sides of the face. You can style it with soft waves or curls for a romantic look or keep it straight for a sleek, modern look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here's how to style your hair according to your face shape:

Layered Bob

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry it gently and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to the hair.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Long Layers

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then towel dry it gently.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots and blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  3. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls throughout your hair.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Bangs

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel dry it gently.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the bangs.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten the bangs and create a side-swept look.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Medium Length Haircut

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then towel dry it gently.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots and blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  3. Straighten your hair using a flat iron or create soft waves using a curling iron.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.

FAQs about Haircuts for Long Oval Face

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer or narrower, such as long straight hair, blunt bangs, or a center part.

Can I wear my hair in a pixie cut if I have a long oval face?

A pixie cut can be a great option for a long oval face as it adds volume to the sides of your face. However, make sure to choose a style with textured layers and side-swept bangs to create balance and proportion.

What hair color looks best on a long oval face?

Warm hair colors like honey blonde, caramel, and chestnut brown can complement a long oval face and add warmth to your complexion. However, it's essential to choose a shade that suits your skin tone and personal style.

Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a unique look that suits your personality and lifestyle. Whether you choose a layered bob, long layers, or medium length haircut, make sure to work with your stylist to find the best haircut for your long oval face.
