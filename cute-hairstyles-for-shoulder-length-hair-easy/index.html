Cute Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair Easy

Are you tired of the same old ponytail or messy bun every day? Do you want to switch up your hairstyle but don't have the time or energy for a complicated updo? Look no further! In this article, we will go over some cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair that will add some variety to your daily routine.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Shoulder length hair is versatile and perfect for trying out different hairstyles. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are many cute and easy styles you can do. From braids to twists to buns, you can change up your look every day without spending hours in front of the mirror.

How To

To get started, you'll need a few basic hair tools. These include a comb, hair ties, bobby pins, and maybe some hairspray or styling products. Once you have your tools ready, you can experiment with different hairstyles depending on your mood and outfit.

Braids

Braids are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. They're also easy to do and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For shoulder length hair, try a half-up braided crown or a Dutch braid ponytail. You can also do a simple French braid or fishtail braid for a more casual look.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a go-to hairstyle for many people, but they don't have to be boring. To make your ponytail more interesting, try a twisted ponytail or a low side ponytail. You can also add some volume by teasing the crown of your hair before securing it into a ponytail.

Buns

Buns are another easy and versatile hairstyle for shoulder length hair. Try a messy bun or a braided bun for a more bohemian look. You can also do a sleek low bun or a top knot for a more polished look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

Half-up Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle and take a small section of hair from one side.
  2. Braid the section of hair and secure it with a small elastic band.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Take the two braids and cross them over the top of your head, securing them with bobby pins.
  5. Adjust the braids and tuck the ends under the rest of your hair.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

Messy Bun

  1. Pull your hair back into a ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the elastic band, tucking the ends underneath.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair even better:

  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Invest in some cute hair accessories like headbands, clips, or scarves.
  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look.
  • Don't be afraid to add some color or highlights to your hair for a fresh new look.

FAQ

Can I do these hairstyles on curly hair?

Yes! These hairstyles can be done on any hair type, including curly hair. Just adjust the steps to fit your hair texture.

Do I need any special tools to do these hairstyles?

No, you just need some basic hair tools like a comb, hair ties, and bobby pins. You can also use styling products like hairspray or gel if you want.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to wash and condition it regularly. Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools like a curling iron or straightener. Trim your hair every few months to get rid of split ends and keep it looking fresh. And don't forget to protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these cute and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you'll never have a bad hair day again. Whether you're running errands or going out for a night on the town, these hairstyles will keep you looking stylish and put together.
