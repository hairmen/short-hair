Haircuts For Curly Thin Hair And Round Face Flattering Styles In 2023

Written by Christine Dec 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Having curly thin hair and a round face can be a challenging combination when it comes to finding the perfect haircut. But don't worry, there are plenty of flattering styles that can enhance your natural curls and complement your facial features. In this article, we'll share how to choose the right haircut, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

Choosing the Right Haircut

The key to choosing the right haircut for curly thin hair and round face is to create the illusion of volume and elongation. Avoid styles that add bulk to the sides or make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for layered cuts that add movement and texture to your hair while elongating your face shape.

Here are some flattering haircuts for curly thin hair and round face:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for curly thin hair as they add volume and movement without sacrificing length. Ask your stylist to cut long layers that start below your chin and blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair.

Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is a chic and modern style that flatters round faces. The bangs draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, while the bob elongates your face shape. Keep the length of the bob above your shoulders to avoid weighing down your curls.

Short Shag

If you prefer a shorter style, a shag is a great option for curly thin hair. The choppy layers add texture and volume, while the length at the top elongates your face shape. Keep the layers around your cheekbones and jawline to create a flattering frame for your face.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you've chosen the perfect haircut for your curly thin hair and round face, it's time to learn how to style it. Follow these step-by-step instructions for a flawless look:

Long Layers

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse from roots to ends.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add loose waves.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a smoothing serum from mid-lengths to ends.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create a smooth finish.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks.
  5. Create a deep side part and sweep your bangs to the side.
  6. Finish with a shine serum to add gloss and tame flyaways.

Short Shag

  1. Wash your hair with a texturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a texturizing spray from roots to ends.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using your fingers to create volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add loose waves.
  5. Finish with a matte pomade to create definition and hold.

Tips for Styling Curly Thin Hair

Styling curly thin hair can be tricky, but these tips will help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying to enhance your natural curls.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality curling iron or wand to create defined curls.
  • Apply a volumizing spray to your roots before blow-drying to add lift.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet to avoid breakage.

FAQ

Can I wear my curly thin hair straight?

Yes, you can wear your curly thin hair straight, but it's important to use heat protectant and avoid using high heat to prevent damage. Use a straightening iron on a low to medium setting and apply a smoothing serum beforehand to create a sleek finish.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Can I color my curly thin hair?

Yes, you can color your curly thin hair, but it's important to use a gentle formula that won't damage your hair. Consider opting for highlights or balayage instead of a full color to add dimension and texture to your hair.

Hair Care Advice

Curly thin hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for curly thin hair and round face can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look. Follow our tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and looking its best in 2023.
