How To Get Your Hair Curly Naturally A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair has always been a popular trend among women. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally curly hair. If you are looking for ways to get your hair curly naturally, you are in the right place. In this guide, we will walk you through the steps on how to achieve the curly hair look without damaging your hair.

Description

Getting curly hair naturally is a process that involves a lot of patience and care. You cannot expect to wake up with curly hair overnight. You need to follow a proper hair care routine and use the right products to achieve the desired results. In this guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to get curly hair naturally.

How to Get Your Hair Curly Naturally

Step 1: Start with a Clean Hair

The first step to getting curly hair naturally is to start with a clean hair. Shampoo your hair using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid any damage. Rinse your hair thoroughly and let it air dry. Avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Once your hair is dry, apply a heat protectant to avoid any damage from the heat styling tools. Heat protectants create a barrier between your hair and the heat, reducing the risk of damage. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Curling Wand

Using a curling wand is the easiest way to get curly hair naturally. Section your hair and wrap it around the curling wand. Hold the hair for a few seconds and release. Do this for all the sections of your hair. Once you have curled your hair, let it cool down before touching it. Do not brush your hair immediately after curling as it can cause frizz.

Step 4: Use Rollers

If you do not want to use a curling wand, you can use rollers to get curly hair naturally. Section your hair and wrap it around the rollers. Secure the rollers with a clip and leave them on for a few hours. Once you remove the rollers, you will have beautiful curls.

Step 5: Use a Diffuser

If you have curly hair but want to enhance your curls, use a diffuser. A diffuser helps to distribute the heat evenly and enhance your curls. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and dry your hair upside down. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair while drying.

Tips

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it can cause breakage.

FAQ

1. Is it possible to get curly hair naturally?

Yes, it is possible to get curly hair naturally. You can use various methods such as curling wands, rollers, and diffusers to get curly hair.

2. Can I get curly hair naturally if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can get curly hair naturally even if you have straight hair. You can use the methods mentioned in this guide to get curly hair.

3. Will using heat styling tools damage my hair?

Using heat styling tools too often can damage your hair. It is important to use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools to avoid any damage.

Hair Care

Getting curly hair naturally requires proper hair care. Make sure you follow a hair care routine that includes shampooing, conditioning, and deep conditioning. Avoid using products that contain alcohol, and use a satin pillowcase to avoid frizz. With proper care and patience, you can get the curly hair look you desire.
