Hairstyles For Small Faces Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and have a small face? Finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best hairstyles for small faces over 60. From short pixie cuts to long bobs, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to know your face shape. A small face can come in different shapes, such as oval, round, heart, or square. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. You can also trace your face on a piece of paper and match it with the shapes online. Knowing your face shape will help you choose the best hairstyle that flatters your features.

Short Hairstyles for Small Faces Over 60

Short hair is perfect for small faces as it creates an illusion of height and makes your face look longer. Here are some short hairstyles that you can try:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and chic hairstyle that is perfect for small faces. It adds volume to your hair and frames your face beautifully. You can add layers or choppy ends to create texture and movement. This hairstyle is low maintenance and easy to style.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape. A chin-length bob is perfect for small faces as it accentuates your jawline and creates a balanced look. You can add layers or blunt ends to add volume and texture.

Long Hairstyles for Small Faces Over 60

If you prefer long hair, there are plenty of hairstyles that you can try. Here are some of our favorites:

Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is perfect for small faces as it adds volume and movement to your hair. You can add layers to the front to frame your face or to the back to create volume. This hairstyle is low maintenance and easy to style.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are perfect for small faces as they create a soft and feminine look. You can add layers or choppy ends to create texture and movement. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair long but still want to change their look.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for your small face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous as they can overpower your small face
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for you

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for a small face over 60?

The best hairstyle for a small face over 60 is a short pixie cut or a chin-length bob. These hairstyles create an illusion of height and make your face look longer.

Can I still wear long hair with a small face over 60?

Yes, you can still wear long hair with a small face over 60. Just make sure to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and avoids too much volume.

Hair Care Tips for Small Faces Over 60

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf

There you have it! These are some of the best hairstyles for small faces over 60. Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style. With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you'll look and feel your best!
