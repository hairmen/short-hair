Mens Fade Haircut With Long Hair On Top A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by William Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you one of those guys who want to have a stylish and trendy haircut that is easy to maintain? If yes, then you might want to consider getting a mens fade haircut with long hair on top. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity among men in recent years, and for good reason. It is a versatile haircut that can suit any hair type and face shape. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting a mens fade haircut with long hair on top, including tips and tricks for maintaining it.

Description

A mens fade haircut with long hair on top is a hairstyle that features a gradual fade on the sides and back, while the hair on top is left long. The fade is done in a way that the hair gradually gets shorter as it approaches the bottom, creating a clean and sharp look. The long hair on top can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to have a stylish and modern look that is easy to maintain.

How to

Getting a mens fade haircut with long hair on top requires a skilled barber or hairdresser who can create the perfect look for you. Here are the steps involved in getting this haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to determine how long you want your hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal preference, face shape, and hair type. You can discuss this with your barber or hairdresser to find the right length for you.

Step 2: Decide on the Type of Fade

There are different types of fades, such as low fade, mid fade, and high fade. You can choose the type of fade that you prefer, or you can ask your barber or hairdresser to recommend the best option for you.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

The sides and back are trimmed using clippers, starting with a higher guard and gradually decreasing the length as you approach the bottom. This creates a smooth and seamless fade.

Step 4: Style the Hair on Top

The hair on top is left long and can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. You can use pomade, wax, or gel to achieve the desired style.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining a mens fade haircut with long hair on top:

  • Get regular trims to keep the fade looking sharp.
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep the hair on top healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from dirt and oil.

FAQ

1. Is a mens fade haircut with long hair on top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle can suit any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every 2-4 weeks to maintain the fade and keep the hair on top looking neat.

3. Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as slicked back, messy, or textured, depending on your personal preference.

Hair Care

Maintaining a mens fade haircut with long hair on top requires proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

Overall, a mens fade haircut with long hair on top is a stylish and modern hairstyle that can suit any man. By following the tips and tricks in this article, you can achieve the perfect look and maintain it for a long time.
