How To Do Curls Away From Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curls are a great way to add volume and movement to your hair. If you're looking for a way to do curls away from your face, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of creating beautiful curls that frame your face perfectly. Let's get started!

Description

Curls away from the face are a popular hairstyle choice for many women. This style is perfect if you want to create a more open and airy look, or if you want to show off your facial features. Curls away from the face can be achieved using a variety of styling tools, including a curling iron, hot rollers, or a flat iron. With a little practice and patience, you can create beautiful, bouncy curls that will last all day long.

How to

If you want to create curls away from your face, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the styling tools. If your hair is naturally curly, you may want to use a straightener to create a more uniform texture.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. The smaller the sections, the tighter the curls will be. Start with the bottom layers of your hair and work your way up.

Step 3: Curl Away from Your Face

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or hot roller. Make sure you curl the hair away from your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds, and then release it. Continue curling each section of hair away from your face, working your way up to the top of your head.

Step 4: Set Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, let it cool for a few minutes. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently separate the curls and create a more natural look. Finish with a hairspray to set your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls away from your face:

  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers with a smaller barrel size to create tighter curls.
  • Start with a lower heat setting on your styling tools and gradually increase the temperature if needed.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't over-curl your hair. Too many curls can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing curls away from the face:

Q: Can I do curls away from my face with a flat iron?

A: Yes, you can create curls away from your face using a flat iron. Simply twist the hair away from your face and run the flat iron down the length of the hair.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the products you use. Using a hairspray or other styling product can help to keep your curls in place for longer.

Q: Can I do curls away from my face on short hair?

A: Yes, you can create curls away from your face on short hair. Use a curling iron or hot rollers with a smaller barrel size to create tighter curls.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after styling. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to do curls away from your face, you can create a beautiful and effortless hairstyle that will turn heads. With a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve stunning curls that will last all day long. Have fun experimenting with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you!
