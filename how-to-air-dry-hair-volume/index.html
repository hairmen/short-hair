How To Air Dry Hair Volume Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of using heat styling tools to create voluminous hair? Want to achieve natural-looking volume without damaging your locks? Look no further, as we share some tips and tricks on how to air dry hair volume that will leave you with bouncy and healthy-looking tresses. Read on for our step-by-step guide, frequently asked questions, and essential hair care tips.

How to Air Dry Hair Volume

Air drying your hair is a simple and effective way to add volume to your locks. Here are some ways to air dry hair volume:

  • Flip your head upside down and scrunch your hair while it dries to create natural-looking volume.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to add volume and create shape in your hair.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse, foam, or spray to your hair before air drying to give it extra lift and body.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it dries, as this can cause frizz and flatten your hair.
  • Use hair clips to create lift at the roots while your hair dries.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to air dry hair volume:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and condition.
  2. Gently squeeze out excess water from your hair using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Do not rub your hair with a towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  3. If using a volumizing product, apply it to your hair while it is still damp.
  4. Flip your head upside down and scrunch your hair with your hands to create natural-looking volume.
  5. If using a hair dryer, attach a diffuser and use it on a low heat setting. Hold the dryer at a distance from your hair to avoid heat damage.
  6. If you prefer not to use a hair dryer, leave your hair to air dry naturally. Use hair clips to create lift at the roots while your hair dries.
  7. Once your hair is dry, gently tousle it with your fingers to create more volume and shape.

Tips for Air Drying Hair Volume

Here are some tips to ensure you get the best results when air drying your hair:

  • Choose the right products for your hair type. If you have fine hair, opt for lightweight products that won't weigh your hair down. If you have thick hair, use a more substantial product that will add moisture and definition to your curls.
  • Don't use too much product. Applying too much product can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down.
  • Be patient. Air drying your hair takes time, so don't rush the process. Plan ahead and give yourself enough time to let your hair dry naturally.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage. If using a hair dryer, always use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Get regular trims. Split ends can make your hair look flat and lifeless, so make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and voluminous.

FAQs

Can I air dry my hair overnight?

Yes, you can air dry your hair overnight. Simply wash your hair before bed and let it dry naturally while you sleep. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and tangles.

How long does it take for hair to air dry?

The time it takes for hair to air dry depends on several factors, including hair type, humidity, and temperature. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours for hair to air dry completely.

Can I use a hair dryer to speed up the air drying process?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer to speed up the air drying process. However, make sure to use a low heat setting and a diffuser attachment to avoid heat damage and maintain volume.

Essential Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and voluminous, follow these essential hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve natural-looking volume and bounce in your hair without using heat styling tools. Give your hair a break from the heat and embrace your natural texture for healthy and voluminous locks.
