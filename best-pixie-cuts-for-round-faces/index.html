Best Pixie Cuts For Round Faces

Written by William Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of sporting the same old hairstyle that doesn't flatter your round face shape? Then it's time to switch to a pixie cut! Pixie cuts are versatile, low-maintenance, and can accentuate your natural beauty. In this blog post, we'll be discussing the best pixie cuts for round faces, along with tips on how to style and maintain your new 'do.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Cut for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing the best pixie cut for round faces, it's important to keep in mind the overall goal of the cut - to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. Here are some factors to consider:

Length

Opt for a pixie cut that has a bit of length on the top as it helps to elongate the face. A short crop can make a round face look even rounder, so avoid going too short.

Texture

Adding texture to your pixie cut can give your hair more volume and create the illusion of a longer face. Consider adding layers or opting for a choppy cut.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can also help to elongate a round face. They draw attention away from the width of the face and create a more vertical line. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make a round face appear even rounder.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Pixie Cuts for Round Faces

Styling a pixie cut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here's a quick step-by-step guide to help you style your new 'do:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or styling cream to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Style your hair using your fingers or a styling wax to create a messy, textured look.
  5. Finish off with a texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

Tips on Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Maintaining a pixie cut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking clean and voluminous.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

FAQs About Pixie Cuts for Round Faces

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

A: No, not if you choose the right style. Opt for a longer pixie cut that adds height and texture to your hair.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can! Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place, or opt for a cute headband or scarf to keep your hair out of your face.

Q: Will I need to use a lot of product to maintain my pixie cut?

A: No, not necessarily. A volumizing mousse or styling cream and a texturizing spray should be enough to keep your hair looking its best.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

In addition to regular trims and conditioning treatments, here are some additional hair care tips for pixie cuts:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to avoid tangles and breakage.

Now that you know the best pixie cuts for round faces, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new, chic look. With the right style and a little bit of care, you'll be rocking your pixie cut in no time!
