What Type Of Hair Compliments My Face Shape

Written by Christine Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're wondering what type of hair compliments your face shape, you're in the right place. Your hair is an important part of your appearance and can completely change your look depending on how it's styled. In this article, we'll take a look at different face shapes and the hairstyles that complement them best.

Table of Contents:

  • Oval Face
  • Round Face
  • Square Face
  • Heart Face
  • Diamond Face
  • Oblong Face

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. From short pixie cuts to long beachy waves, an oval face can handle it all. However, it's important to keep in mind your hair texture and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.

Tips for an Oval Face:

  • Try a middle part to show off your symmetrical face
  • Experiment with different lengths and textures
  • Consider face-framing layers to add dimension

Round Face

If you have a round face, the goal is to elongate the face and create the illusion of angles. Hairstyles that add height at the top of the head and length to the face work best. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.

Tips for a Round Face:

  • Opt for a side part to create angles
  • Try a layered haircut to add volume and height
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin

Square Face

If you have a square face, your goal is to soften the angles and create a more rounded look. Soft curls and waves work well, as do hairstyles with volume at the crown. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can make the face appear boxy.

Tips for a Square Face:

  • Try a side-swept bang to soften the angles
  • Opt for soft curls or waves to add volume
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, the goal is to balance out the top and bottom of the face. A chin-length bob or lob works well, as do hairstyles with volume at the bottom. Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top of the head, as they can make the face appear top-heavy.

Tips for a Heart Face:

  • Try a chin-length bob or lob
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the bottom
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top of the head

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, the goal is to balance out the narrow forehead and chin. Hairstyles that add volume at the top of the head and narrow at the chin work well. Avoid styles that add width at the cheeks, as they can make the face appear wider.

Tips for a Diamond Face:

  • Try a pixie cut or short bob
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume at the top of the head
  • Avoid styles that add width at the cheeks

Oblong Face

If you have an oblong face, the goal is to create the illusion of width. Hairstyles with volume at the sides work best, as do shoulder-length haircuts. Avoid styles that add height at the top of the head, as they can make the face appear even longer.

Tips for an Oblong Face:

  • Try a shoulder-length haircut
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the sides
  • Avoid styles that add height at the top of the head

FAQs:

What hairstyles are best for an oval face?

Almost any hairstyle works well for an oval face, but consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.

What hairstyles are best for a round face?

Hairstyles that elongate the face and create the illusion of angles work best for a round face. Consider a layered haircut or a side part.

What hairstyles are best for a square face?

Hairstyles that soften the angles and create a more rounded look work best for a square face. Consider soft curls or waves, and avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline.

Hair Care:

No matter your face shape or hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type, and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Now that you know what type of hair compliments your face shape, it's time to try out some new hairstyles. Whether you have an oval, round, square, heart, diamond, or oblong face, there's a style out there that will make you look and feel your best.
