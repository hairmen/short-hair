What Wig Fits My Face A Comprehensive Guide In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Description

Finding the perfect wig can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what style or length will suit your face shape. In this article, we'll provide a comprehensive guide to help you determine what wig fits your face shape and enhance your natural beauty. From how to measure your face shape to the best wig styles for each shape, we've got you covered.

How to Measure Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right wig for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. To do this, you'll need to measure your face in three key areas: your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. 1. Forehead: Measure from the peak of one eyebrow arch to the peak of the opposite eyebrow arch. 2. Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye. 3. Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone begins to angle upward. Once you have these measurements, you can determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

Best Wig Styles for Each Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the wig styles that will complement it best. Here are some tips: - Oval: Lucky you! Almost any wig style will suit your face shape, so have fun experimenting with different lengths and textures. - Round: Look for wigs that add height at the crown or have layers that fall below the chin. Avoid short, curly wigs that add width to your face. - Square: Soften your angular jawline with wigs that have layers, waves, or curls. Long, straight styles will only emphasize your squareness. - Heart: Balance your wider forehead with wigs that have volume at the jawline, such as chin-length bobs or shoulder-length styles with layers. - Diamond: Add width to your narrow forehead and chin with wigs that have fullness at the temples and jawline, such as layered bobs or long, wavy styles. - Oblong: Short wigs with volume at the crown or long, wavy styles with layers can help balance your face shape and add width.

Tips for Choosing the Right Wig

- Consider your lifestyle: If you're active or have a busy schedule, a low-maintenance wig may be best. If you love to experiment with different styles, a versatile wig that can be styled in different ways may be the way to go. - Think about your natural hair color: If you want a natural look, choose a wig that matches your hair color or is close to it. - Try before you buy: If possible, try on wigs in person or purchase from a retailer with a good return policy. This will help you ensure a proper fit and avoid any surprises. - Take care of your wig: Proper care will help your wig last longer and look its best. This includes washing and conditioning it regularly, storing it properly, and avoiding heat styling tools.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear any wig style I want, regardless of my face shape? A: While you can certainly wear any wig style you like, choosing a style that complements your face shape will enhance your natural beauty and give you the most flattering look. Q: How do I know if a wig will fit me? A: Most wigs come in standard sizes, but it's important to measure your head before purchasing to ensure a proper fit. Some wig retailers may also offer custom sizing options.

Hair Care

To keep your wig looking its best, it's important to follow proper hair care practices. Here are some tips: - Wash your wig with a mild shampoo and conditioner, using cool water. - Gently comb out any tangles with a wide-tooth comb or wig brush. - Allow your wig to air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head. - Store your wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. - Avoid using heat styling tools on synthetic wigs, as they can melt the fibers.

Conclusion

Finding the right wig for your face shape can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these tips and taking proper care of your wig, you can enjoy a beautiful, natural-looking style that enhances your unique features. Remember to consider your lifestyle and natural hair color when choosing a wig, and always try before you buy if possible. With a little patience and experimentation, you'll find the perfect wig to suit your face shape and personal style.
