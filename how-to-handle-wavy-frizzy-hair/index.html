How To Handle Wavy Frizzy Hair

Written by William Sep 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Wavy frizzy hair can be a nightmare to deal with, but with the right techniques and products, it can be tamed and styled to perfection. In this article, we’ll go over how to handle wavy frizzy hair, step by step.

How to

The first step to handling wavy frizzy hair is to understand your hair type. Wavy hair is typically categorized as type 2 on the hair type scale, while frizzy hair can be caused by a number of factors, including humidity, damage, and genetics. Once you’ve identified your hair type, you can begin to develop a hair care routine that works for you.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils, leading to dry, frizzy hair. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like argan oil or shea butter, which can help to nourish and hydrate the hair.

Step 2: Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently work out any knots or tangles in your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, taking care not to pull or tug on the hair.

Step 3: Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair, focusing on the areas that are the most dry or damaged. This will help to seal in moisture and prevent further damage.

Step 4: Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz. Hold the dryer about 6 inches away from your head and use a low heat setting.

Tips

Tip 1: Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons on your hair, as this can cause further damage and lead to more frizz.

Tip 2: Try using a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials can help to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

Tip 3: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair mask on wavy frizzy hair?

A: Yes, a hair mask can be a great way to nourish and hydrate wavy frizzy hair. Look for a mask that contains natural ingredients like coconut oil or avocado oil.

Q: How often should I wash wavy frizzy hair?

A: It’s best to wash wavy frizzy hair every 2-3 days, as washing too often can strip the hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Lastly, it’s important to take care of your hair from the inside out. Eating a balanced diet and staying hydrated can help to promote healthy hair growth and prevent breakage. Additionally, avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools can help to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to handle wavy frizzy hair like a pro. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.
