How To Wear Hair Up Over 50

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and become more fragile. However, that doesn't mean we can't still rock a stylish updo! In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for wearing your hair up over 50.

Table of Contents

  • How to wear hair up over 50
  • Step-by-step guide
  • Tips for success
  • FAQ
  • Hair care for older women

How to Wear Hair Up Over 50

The first step in wearing your hair up over 50 is to choose the right style. Some classic updos that work well for older women include:

  • Chignon
  • Bun
  • French twist
  • Ponytail
  • Braids

When choosing a style, consider your hair texture, face shape, and the occasion. A sleek chignon might be perfect for a formal event, while a messy bun is great for a casual day out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to try an updo? Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is fine or thin, consider using a volumizing product before styling.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.
  4. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base, forming a bun. Use hair pins to secure the bun in place.
  5. Use a finishing spray to set your style.

For a French twist or braided updo, follow similar steps but with different techniques. You can find plenty of tutorials online to guide you.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect updo:

  • Start with healthy hair. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and consider a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Invest in good quality hair accessories, such as hair pins and elastics. These can make a big difference in the longevity of your updo.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. If a certain updo doesn't work for you, try a different one.
  • Consider getting a professional updo for special occasions. A stylist can help you achieve a more polished look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing hair up over 50:

Q: Will wearing my hair up damage it?

A: If done correctly, wearing your hair up should not cause damage. However, if you pull your hair too tightly or use harsh hair accessories, you could experience breakage.

Q: Can I wear an updo if I have short hair?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of updos that work well with short hair, such as a half-up style or a braided crown.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some women prefer to wash their hair every day, while others do it once or twice a week. If you have thin or fine hair, you might need to wash it more often to avoid oil buildup.

Hair Care for Older Women

As we age, our hair needs change. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron and biotin, which are important for hair health.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement, such as biotin or collagen, to support hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish updo at any age. Remember, confidence is key!
