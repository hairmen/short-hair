How To Cut Men s Short Back And Sides With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to cut men's short back and sides with scissors? If so, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve that perfect haircut at home. We'll also provide you with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you maintain your new look.

How to Cut Men's Short Back and Sides with Scissors

Cutting men's hair can seem intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. However, with a little practice and some guidance, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Here are the basic steps for cutting men's short back and sides with scissors:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see the hair's natural texture and make it easier to work with.

  2. Section off the top of the hair using hair clips. This will help you focus on the back and sides without getting in the way of the longer hair on top.

  3. Choose your desired length and start cutting the sides. Use your fingers to hold the hair and cut in a straight line with the scissors. Repeat on the other side.

  4. Blend the sides and back together. Use the scissors to cut at an angle, starting at the bottom and working your way up. This will create a seamless transition between the sides and back.

  5. Move onto the back of the head. Use your fingers to hold the hair and cut in a straight line with the scissors.

  6. Blend the back and sides together. Use the same technique as before, cutting at an angle to create a smooth transition.

  7. Check for any uneven spots and trim as needed. It's better to take off a little at a time than to cut too much at once.

  8. Remove the hair clips and style as desired. You can use a comb, hair dryer, or other styling tools to create your desired look.

  9. Clean up any stray hairs around the neck and ears using clippers or scissors.

  10. Admire your work! You've just given yourself or someone else a professional-looking haircut.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. They'll make the job easier and give you better results than regular household scissors.

  • Watch some tutorials or take a class to learn more advanced cutting techniques.

  • Work with small sections of hair at a time to ensure even cutting.

  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure about anything.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's short back and sides with scissors:

  • Can I use clippers instead of scissors?
  • Yes, clippers can be used to cut men's hair, but scissors are better for achieving a more precise and professional-looking cut.

  • How often should I cut my hair?
  • It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, men's hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

  • What if I make a mistake?
  • Don't panic! Mistakes can be corrected by trimming the affected area or taking off a little more hair to even it out.

Hair Care Advice

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the follicles and cause split ends.

  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it soft and smooth.

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and guidance, you can confidently cut men's short back and sides with scissors. It may take some practice, but with time and patience, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
