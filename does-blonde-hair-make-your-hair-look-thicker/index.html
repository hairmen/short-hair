Does Blonde Hair Make Your Hair Look Thicker

Written by Eveline Jun 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Blonde hair has always been a popular choice, and many people wonder if it can make their hair look thicker. The answer is not as simple as a yes or no. Several factors come into play when it comes to how your hair looks, and the color of your hair is one of them. This article will explore whether blonde hair makes your hair look thicker, how to achieve the look, and tips on taking care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Your Hair Look Thicker
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Blonde hair does not actually make your hair look thicker. However, it can create an illusion of thicker hair. When natural light hits blonde hair, it reflects off the strands, creating an extra layer of dimension. This illusion can make hair appear fuller and thicker than it is. Additionally, blonde hair tends to be lighter in color, making it easier to see individual strands, which can make hair appear thicker.

How to Make Your Hair Look Thicker

Several steps can be taken to make your hair look fuller and thicker regardless of whether it is blonde or not. These steps include:

  • Using volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Blowdrying your hair upside down
  • Using a root-lifting spray
  • Teasing your hair
  • Using a thickening hair spray

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to making your hair look thicker:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, then flip your head upside down and blowdry your hair while scrunching your hair with your hands.
  3. Use a root-lifting spray at the roots and blowdry again to create extra volume.
  4. Tease the crown of your hair gently to create extra lift.
  5. Finish with a thickening hair spray to add texture and volume.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when trying to make your hair look thicker:

  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Consider getting layers to create the illusion of volume and movement.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as it can damage your strands and create frizz.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to avoid hair breakage and frizz.

FAQ

Does blonde hair make hair look thicker?

No, blonde hair does not actually make your hair look thicker. However, it can create an illusion of thicker hair by reflecting light off the strands and creating an extra layer of dimension.

What color hair looks the thickest?

Dark hair tends to look the thickest because it absorbs more light and creates less contrast between the hair and scalp. However, any hair color can look thick with the right styling and hair care routine.

Hair Care

Regardless of the color of your hair, it is essential to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in nutrients that promote hair growth, such as protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, blonde hair does not make your hair look thicker, but it can create an illusion of thickness. By using the right hair care products and styling techniques, you can make any hair color look fuller and more voluminous. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and looking its best.
