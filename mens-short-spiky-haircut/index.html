Mens Short Spiky Haircut A Guide For The Modern Man

Written by Robby Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man looking for a fresh and modern hairstyle that's easy to maintain, then a short spiky haircut might be just what you need. This trendy hairstyle has been popular for years and shows no sign of going out of style any time soon. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about mens short spiky haircuts, including how to get one, tips for styling, and hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best.

Description

A mens short spiky haircut is a style where the hair is cut short on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and styled into spikes. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. It's a great option for men who want a low-maintenance and stylish look.

How to Get a Mens Short Spiky Haircut

Getting a mens short spiky haircut is relatively easy. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Before you head to the barbershop, decide on the type of spiky hairstyle you want. There are various options, including messy spikes, textured spikes, and neat spikes. Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a style.

Step 2: Visit a Skilled Barber

Once you've decided on your style, it's time to find a skilled barber who can execute it. Look for a barber with experience in creating mens short spiky haircuts. Bring a picture of the style you want to ensure that the barber knows exactly what you're looking for.

Step 3: Cleanse Your Hair

Before the haircut, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup. Dry your hair completely before heading to the barbershop.

Step 4: Get Your Haircut

Once you're at the barbershop, the barber will begin by cutting your hair short on the sides and back. They'll then leave the top longer and style it into spikes using scissors and/or clippers. The process usually takes around 30 minutes.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After the haircut, the barber will style your hair into spikes using a styling product. You can then style your hair at home using a hair gel or wax. Apply the product to damp hair and use your fingers to create the spikes. Use a blow dryer to set the style.

Tips for Styling Mens Short Spiky Haircuts

Here are some tips for styling your mens short spiky haircut:

- Use a hair gel or wax to create a strong hold - Apply the product to damp hair for best results - Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best - Use a blow dryer to set the style and add volume - Don't overdo it with the product, as this can make your hair look greasy

FAQ

Is a mens short spiky haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a mens short spiky haircut can be customized to suit different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get a mens short spiky haircut?

You should get a mens short spiky haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep your hair looking neat.

Can I style my mens short spiky haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. Try messy spikes, neat spikes, or textured spikes for a different look.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Short Spiky Haircuts

Mens short spiky haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent product buildup - Avoid using too much product, as this can damage your hair - Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with UV protection - Get regular haircuts to maintain the style and keep your hair healthy

Conclusion

A mens short spiky haircut is a stylish and low-maintenance option for the modern man. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can get a spiky haircut that complements your face shape and hair type. Remember to experiment with different styles and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
