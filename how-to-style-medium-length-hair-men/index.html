How To Style Medium Length Hair Men A Relaxed Guide

Medium length hair can be versatile and stylish for men, but it can also be tricky to style. Whether you're growing out your hair or just looking for some new style ideas, this guide will help you achieve the perfect look for your hair type and personality. Read on for tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to keep your medium length hair looking its best.

Understanding Your Hair Type

Before you can start styling your medium length hair, it's important to understand your hair type. Different hair types require different approaches to styling, so take a moment to assess your hair's texture, thickness, and natural shape.

Some common hair types for medium length hair include:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Thick
  • Thin

Once you know your hair type, you can start exploring different style options that will work best for you.

Popular Medium Length Hair Styles for Men

There are plenty of stylish and trendy hair styles for men with medium length hair. Here are a few popular options:

1. The Side Part

This classic style is a great option for medium length hair. Simply part your hair to one side and use a bit of pomade or wax to keep it in place. This style works well for straight, wavy, and curly hair types.

2. The Messy Look

If you prefer a more relaxed, casual look, try the messy style. This involves using a bit of product to give your hair some texture and then tousling it with your fingers. This look works well for wavy and curly hair types.

3. The Undercut

The undercut has been a popular style for men for a few years now, and it works well for medium length hair. This style involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving your hair on top longer. You can then style the longer hair in a variety of ways, such as slicking it back or combing it to one side.

4. The Man Bun

For men with longer medium length hair, the man bun is a popular style choice. Simply gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head and then twist it into a bun shape. This style works well for all hair types.

How to Style Medium Length Hair Men: Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know which style you want to try, it's time to learn how to achieve it. Here's a basic guide for styling medium length hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair healthy and manageable.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently pat your hair dry. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Product

Choose a styling product that works well for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, if you want a sleek, polished look, try using a pomade or wax. If you want a more relaxed, messy look, try using a texturizing spray or cream. Apply the product to your hair evenly, using your fingers to work it through from root to tip.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. For a more structured style, use a comb to create a part or slick your hair back. For a more relaxed style, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

If you want to keep your style in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair

Here are a few tips to help you keep your medium length hair looking its best:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

FAQs About Styling Medium Length Hair Men

1. How often should I wash my medium length hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Can I use the same styling products for all hair types?

No, different hair types require different products. For example, thick hair may require a stronger hold product than thin hair.

3. Is it okay to blow dry my hair every day?

Blow drying your hair every day can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of hot tools and use a heat protectant spray if you do use them.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

In addition to the styling tips above, here are a few more hair care tips to keep your medium length hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, as it is less likely to cause breakage.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your medium length hair like a pro. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice. With a little effort and some TLC, your medium length hair will look great every day.
