How To Make Your Long Hair Look Wet For Guys

Written by Daniel Oct 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long hair has been a popular trend for guys in recent years. One way to switch up your look is by making your long hair look wet. This can give you a sleek and polished appearance. In this blog post, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to make your long hair look wet for guys. We will also give you tips on how to maintain this style and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to

Before we get started, it is important to note that you will need some products to achieve this look. Here are the products you will need:

  • Hair gel
  • Hair spray
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair dryer

Now, let's get to the steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step to making your hair look wet is to wash it thoroughly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to ensure that your hair is clean and healthy.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it. Be careful not to rub your hair vigorously as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply hair gel

Take a small amount of hair gel and apply it evenly to your hair. Be sure to distribute the gel from the roots to the tips of your hair.

Step 4: Comb your hair

Using a wide-tooth comb, comb your hair to ensure that the gel is evenly distributed. This will also help to prevent any clumping or unevenness.

Step 5: Blow dry your hair

Using a hair dryer, blow dry your hair on a low heat setting. Be sure to keep the dryer at a safe distance from your hair to prevent damage.

Step 6: Apply hair spray

Once your hair is dry, apply some hair spray to hold the style in place. Be sure to use a light mist to avoid a stiff, crunchy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your wet look:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much gel as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to prevent damage.
  • Use a light mist of hair spray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your long hair look wet for guys:

Q: How long will this style last?

A: This style can last for a day or two, depending on how well you maintain it.

Q: Can I use a different type of gel?

A: Yes, you can use a different type of gel, but be sure to choose a gel that is meant for wet looks.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to achieve this look?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener, but be sure to use it on a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Making your hair look wet can be a fun and stylish way to switch up your look. However, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. With proper care, you can maintain a healthy and stylish look.
