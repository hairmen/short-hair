Pixie Long Top Short Sides A Trendy And Edgy Hairstyle

Written by Oliver Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Pixie long top short sides hairstyle has been in trend for quite some time now. This edgy hairstyle is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd and make a bold statement. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, some tips to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

Description

Pixie long top short sides is a hairstyle where the top of the hair is kept long and voluminous while the sides and back are shaved or cut short. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some edge and dimension to their look. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways to suit your personality and mood.

How To

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the pixie long top short sides hairstyle:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Before you decide to get this hairstyle, it is important to consult with your hairstylist. They will be able to give you advice on whether this hairstyle will suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Decide on the length

Decide on the length of the top of your hair. The length of the top hair should be long enough to style but short enough to maintain. The sides and the back can be shaved or cut short, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Get the cut

Once you have decided on the length, it's time to get the cut. Make sure to go to a professional hairstylist who has experience in cutting pixie hairstyles. A good hairstylist will be able to give you the perfect cut that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Style the hair

Once the cut is done, it's time to style the hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the top of the hair. Use a texturizing spray to add texture to the hair. Style the hair using your fingers or a comb to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your pixie long top short sides hairstyle:

- Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape of the hairstyle. - Use a texturizing spray to add texture to the hair. - Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the top of the hair. - Avoid using too much heat on the hair as it can damage the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the pixie long top short sides hairstyle:

Is the pixie long top short sides hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

It depends on the individual's face shape and hair texture. It is best to consult with a hairstylist before getting this hairstyle.

How often do I need to get a trim?

It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the hairstyle.

Can I style my hair differently?

Yes, you can style your hair differently. The pixie long top short sides hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in different ways to suit your personality and mood.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair moisturized. - Avoid using too much heat on the hair as it can damage the hair. - Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools on the hair.

In conclusion, the pixie long top short sides hairstyle is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that can add some dimension and edge to your look. With the right cut and styling, this hairstyle can suit any personality and mood. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and follow the tips mentioned in this article to maintain your hairstyle.
