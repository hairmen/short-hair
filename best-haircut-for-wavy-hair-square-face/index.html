Best Haircut For Wavy Hair Square Face A Complete Guide

Description

Wavy hair square face is a unique combination that requires a specific haircut to flatter your features. A square face has a broad forehead, wide cheekbones, and a strong jawline. The best haircut for this face shape is one that softens your facial features and adds height to the crown area to elongate the face. Wavy hair, on the other hand, adds texture and volume to the hair, making it more challenging to find the right haircut.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Wavy Hair Square Face

The first step in choosing the best haircut for wavy hair square face is to determine your face shape. If you have a square-shaped face, go for haircuts that add softness and height to your face. Avoid haircuts that accentuate your jawline or make your face appear wider. You can go for hairstyles with layers, long bangs, or side-swept bangs that frame your face and soften your features. Additionally, you can add volume to the crown area to elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Best Haircuts for Wavy Hair Square Face

Here are some of the best haircuts for wavy hair square face:

  • Long Layered Haircut
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Wavy Bob
  • Shoulder-Length Haircut
  • Short Pixie Cut

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Best Haircut for Wavy Hair Square Face

Follow these simple steps to get the best haircut for wavy hair square face:

  1. Choose the right haircut based on your face shape and hair type.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the haircut you want.
  3. Discuss the length, layers, and texture of your hair with the hairstylist.
  4. Ask the hairstylist to add layers to the crown area to elongate your face.
  5. Ask the hairstylist to add soft layers around your face to soften your features.
  6. Ask the hairstylist to add long side-swept bangs to frame your face.
  7. Use a curling iron or a hair straightener to style your hair according to your preference.

Tips to Maintain Your Haircut

Here are some tips to maintain your haircut:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently to prevent hair damage.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair.

FAQs

Q. Can I get a short haircut with wavy hair square face?

A. Yes, you can get a short haircut with wavy hair square face. However, make sure to choose a haircut that adds height to the crown area and softness around your face to balance your features.

Q. Can I get bangs with wavy hair square face?

A. Yes, you can get bangs with wavy hair square face. Long side-swept bangs are an excellent option as they frame your face and add softness to your features.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair Square Face

Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair square face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent hair damage.

Now that you know the best haircut options for wavy hair square face, it's time to experiment and find the perfect one for you. Remember, choosing the right haircut can make a significant difference in how you look and feel. So, go ahead and book an appointment with your hairstylist today!
