How To Use Thickening Shampoo For Fuller Thicker Hair

Written by Daniel Nov 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to add volume and thickness to your hair, thickening shampoo may be the solution you need. This type of shampoo is designed to make your hair look and feel fuller, giving you the appearance of thicker hair. In this blog post, we'll walk you through how to use thickening shampoo and provide some tips for getting the best results.

How to Use Thickening Shampoo

Thickening shampoo is used just like regular shampoo, but it contains special ingredients that help to plump up the hair shaft and make it look fuller. Here's how to use thickening shampoo:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair thoroughly with warm water. This will help to open up the hair cuticle and allow the shampoo to penetrate more deeply.

Step 2: Apply the Shampoo

Next, apply a generous amount of thickening shampoo to your hair. Be sure to focus on your roots and work the shampoo into your scalp. Massage your scalp gently with your fingertips to help stimulate blood flow and encourage hair growth.

Step 3: Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water, making sure to remove all of the shampoo. If you have very fine hair, you may want to skip conditioner, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look less voluminous.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to use thickening shampoo:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shampoo

Not all thickening shampoos are created equal, so it's important to choose one that's right for your hair type. Look for a shampoo that's designed specifically for fine or thinning hair, and check the ingredients to make sure it contains volumizing agents like keratin, biotin, or collagen.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Before you get started, make sure your hair is completely wet. This will help the shampoo to lather up and spread evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply the Shampoo

Pour a quarter-sized amount of shampoo into your hand, and then work it into your hair from root to tip. Be sure to focus on your roots, as this is where your hair tends to be the most flat and lifeless.

Step 4: Massage Your Scalp

Use your fingertips to massage your scalp gently as you work the shampoo through your hair. This will help to stimulate blood flow and encourage hair growth.

Step 5: Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water, making sure to remove all of the shampoo. If you have very fine hair, you may want to skip conditioner, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look less voluminous.

Tips for Using Thickening Shampoo

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from your thickening shampoo:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup and keep your hair looking its best.
  • Avoid using too much conditioner, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look less voluminous.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Q: Can thickening shampoo make my hair grow faster?

A: While thickening shampoo can help to make your hair look fuller and thicker, it won't actually make your hair grow faster. To promote healthy hair growth, eat a healthy diet, exercise regularly, and take care of your scalp.

Q: Can I use thickening shampoo every day?

A: Yes, you can use thickening shampoo every day if you like. However, if you have very fine hair, you may want to skip conditioner or use it sparingly, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look less voluminous.

Q: How long does it take to see results from using thickening shampoo?

A: You may start to see results from using thickening shampoo after just a few washes, but it can take several weeks or months to see a significant improvement in the thickness and volume of your hair.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

Once you've started using thickening shampoo, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for thicker hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and minimize breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look less voluminous.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like hair dye and bleach, as they can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and reduce frizz.

By following these tips and using thickening shampoo regularly, you can achieve fuller, thicker hair that looks and feels healthy.
