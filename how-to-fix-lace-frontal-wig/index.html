How To Fix Lace Frontal Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to fix your lace frontal wig? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to fix your lace frontal wig, as well as tips and tricks to keep it looking flawless.

Description

A lace frontal wig is a popular choice among women who want to achieve a natural-looking hairstyle. This type of wig is designed with a lace frontal that covers the hairline, creating a seamless and natural-looking hairline. However, fixing a lace frontal wig can be a little tricky, especially if it's your first time.

How to Fix a Lace Frontal Wig

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Wig

Before you start fixing your wig, make sure your hair is clean and free of any products. You also want to make sure your wig is clean and free of any tangles. Use a wig brush or comb to gently detangle your wig, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Step 2: Apply Wig Adhesive

Apply a small amount of wig adhesive around the perimeter of your hairline. Make sure to apply the adhesive in thin layers to avoid any clumping.

Step 3: Place the Wig

Once you have applied the adhesive, place the wig onto your head, starting from the front and working your way back. Make sure the wig is aligned with your natural hairline and adjust the wig until it feels comfortable.

Step 4: Cut the Lace

Using a pair of scissors, carefully cut the lace along the hairline. Make sure to cut as close to the hairline as possible, without cutting any of your natural hair.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Once you have fixed your wig, you can style it any way you like. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create the desired look.

Tips for Fixing a Lace Frontal Wig

Here are some tips to help you fix your lace frontal wig:

  • Always use a wig cap to protect your natural hair.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle your wig gently.
  • Apply wig adhesive in thin layers to avoid any clumping.
  • Make sure the wig is aligned with your natural hairline.
  • Cut the lace as close to the hairline as possible.
  • Style your wig with heat tools on a low to medium temperature.

FAQ

How long will a lace frontal wig last?

A lace frontal wig can last up to six months to a year with proper care and maintenance.

How often should I wash my lace frontal wig?

You should wash your lace frontal wig every two weeks or as needed. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner made specifically for wigs.

Can I swim with a lace frontal wig?

We recommend avoiding swimming with a lace frontal wig, as the chlorine and saltwater can damage the wig. If you must swim with your wig, wear a swim cap to protect it.

Hair Care for Lace Frontal Wigs

To keep your lace frontal wig looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle your wig gently.
  • Avoid using heat tools on high temperatures, as this can damage the wig.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid sleeping with your wig on, as this can cause tangling and matting.
  • Wash your wig every two weeks or as needed with a gentle shampoo and conditioner made specifically for wigs.

By following these tips, you can keep your lace frontal wig looking beautiful and natural for months to come.
