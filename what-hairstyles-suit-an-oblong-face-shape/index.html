What Hairstyles Suit an Oblong Face Shape?

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry! With the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your features and look amazing. In this article, we'll share some tips and ideas for hairstyles that suit an oblong face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with high cheekbones and a prominent forehead. If you have this face shape, you'll want to choose hairstyles that add width and volume to your face. This can be achieved with the right haircut and styling techniques.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your oblong face shape is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length of your face from your hairline to your chin and the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face shape.

Step by Step

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit you. Here are some ideas:

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles can work well for oblong face shapes, especially if they add volume and width to the sides of your face. Some good options include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Layered cut

Medium Hairstyles

Medium hairstyles can also work well for oblong face shapes. Look for styles that add volume and width to the sides of your face, such as:

  • Shoulder-length cut
  • Layered cut
  • Wavy or curly styles

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles can be tricky for oblong face shapes, as they can make your face look even longer. However, if you want to wear your hair long, try adding layers and volume to the sides of your face. Some good options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Loose waves or curls

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your oblong face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too straight and flat, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Try to add volume and width to the sides of your face.
  • Consider getting bangs, as they can help shorten the appearance of your face.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and cuts to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a center part with an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a center part with an oblong face shape. Just make sure to add volume and width to the sides of your face.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail with an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a high ponytail with an oblong face shape. Just make sure to add volume and width to the sides of your face.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to take care of it properly. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using heat protectant when styling, and getting regular trims to keep split ends at bay. With the right care and styling, you can rock any hairstyle with your oblong face shape.
