Easy Way To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors

Written by Frank Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting men's hair with scissors is a great way to save money and time. It is also a great way to get the exact style you want without having to go to a barber. In this article, we will discuss the easy way to cut men's hair with scissors. We will cover everything from how to prepare to how to cut, and we will also provide tips and advice on how to care for your hair after the cut.

  • How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors

Cutting men's hair with scissors requires some preparation, including having the right tools and setting up a workspace. Before you start cutting, make sure you have:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A towel or cape to cover the person's clothes
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

You should also set up a workspace with good lighting and a comfortable chair for the person getting the haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut men's hair with scissors:

  1. Start by wetting the person's hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut and reduce the amount of hair that flies around.
  2. Use the comb to section off the hair you want to cut. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it with the scissors. Cut only a small amount at a time to avoid making mistakes.
  3. Move to the next section of hair and repeat the process. Comb the hair to make sure it is even and cut the ends.
  4. Continue cutting until you have cut all the hair you want to cut.
  5. Use the scissors to trim any flyaway hairs or uneven spots.
  6. Use the comb to create any desired styles, such as a part or bangs.
  7. Check the haircut in the mirror to make sure it looks even and balanced.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to help make cutting men's hair with scissors easier and more successful:

  • Start by cutting less than you think you need to. You can always cut more later.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair in place while you cut. This will help you make cleaner cuts.
  • Take breaks if you need them. Cutting hair can be tiring and stressful.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and make sure you are cutting evenly and accurately.
  • Watch tutorials online to learn new techniques and styles.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with scissors:

Can you cut men's hair with regular scissors?

It is not recommended to cut men's hair with regular household scissors. You should use scissors specifically designed for cutting hair.

How often should men get their hair cut?

It depends on the person's hair type and the style they want. Generally, men should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

What is the difference between cutting men's hair with scissors and clippers?

Cutting men's hair with scissors allows for more precision and control over the length and shape of the hair. Clippers are better for creating short, uniform haircuts.

Hair Care After the Cut

After cutting men's hair with scissors, it is important to take care of the hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair soft and shiny.
  • Trim the ends every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, cutting men's hair with scissors can be easy, fun, and rewarding. Whether you are cutting your own hair or someone else's, take your time, be patient, and enjoy the process.
