How To Cut Comb Over Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, cutting comb over bangs is a great way to do it. Comb over bangs are a trendy and versatile look that can work for any hair type or length. In this guide, we'll be sharing everything you need to know about how to cut comb over bangs. From the tools you'll need to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your comb over bangs, you'll need to gather a few tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Section Your Hair

The first step is to section off the hair you want to cut. Using your comb, part your hair down the middle and clip the two sides away from your face. Then, take a small section of hair at the front and clip it away from the rest of your hair. This will be the section you'll cut for your bangs.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Next, using your water spray bottle, dampen the section of hair you'll be cutting. This will help you get a cleaner cut and make it easier to work with.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Using your sharp scissors, start cutting your bangs. Start at the center of your hairline and work your way outwards. Use small, precise cuts and make sure to keep your scissors at a slight angle for a more natural look. Keep checking your progress in the mirror to make sure you're happy with the length and shape of your bangs.

Step 4: Blend Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs, it's time to blend them with the rest of your hair. Take your comb and blend your bangs into the rest of your hair. This will create a more seamless look and help your bangs blend in with the rest of your hairstyle.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Finally, it's time to style your new comb over bangs. You can style them straight down for a classic look, or sweep them to the side for a more modern look. Use a bit of hairspray or styling gel to keep your bangs in place throughout the day.

Tips for Cutting Comb Over Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your comb over bangs:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use a comb to keep your bangs straight and even while you're cutting them.
  • Take breaks and step back from the mirror to check your progress. This will help you avoid overcutting or making any mistakes.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider going to a professional hairstylist.

FAQs

Can comb over bangs work for all hair types?

Yes, comb over bangs can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I trim my comb over bangs?

You should trim your comb over bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and to maintain their shape.

Can I still wear my hair up with comb over bangs?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with comb over bangs. Simply clip your bangs back or sweep them to the side for a different look.

Hair Care for Comb Over Bangs

To keep your comb over bangs looking their best, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. You can also use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Finally, avoid using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

Cutting comb over bangs can seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and a bit of practice, anyone can do it. So, grab your scissors and give it a try!
