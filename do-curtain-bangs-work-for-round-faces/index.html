Description

Curtain bangs have been a popular hairstyle for several years now. They are versatile and suit various face shapes. However, if you have a round face, you may be wondering if curtain bangs will work for you.

The answer is yes! Curtain bangs can work for round faces, but you need to get the right cut and style. In this guide, we will discuss how to get curtain bangs for round faces, step-by-step instructions, tips for styling, FAQs, and hair care.

How to Get Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

If you have a round face, getting the right cut for curtain bangs is essential. You want to make sure that the bangs frame your face and highlight your features, rather than add width to your face.

The best way to get curtain bangs for round faces is to go to a hairstylist who has experience cutting this style. They will be able to create a cut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Before your appointment, bring pictures of curtain bangs that you like and discuss them with your stylist. They can recommend the best cut and style for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get curtain bangs for round faces:

  1. Book an appointment with a hairstylist who has experience cutting curtain bangs.
  2. Bring pictures of curtain bangs that you like and discuss them with your stylist.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs to just above your eyebrows and angle them slightly longer on the sides.
  4. Have your stylist cut the rest of your hair to frame your face and create layers for texture.
  5. Style your curtain bangs by parting them in the middle and using a round brush to blow-dry them.
  6. Finish the look with a texturizing spray or hair wax to add volume and texture.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

Styling curtain bangs can be easy with these tips:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and a soft wave.
  • Use a texturizing spray or hair wax to add texture and definition to your bangs.
  • Part your bangs in the middle and sweep them to the side for a classic curtain bang look.
  • Clip your bangs to the side while they are drying to create a more dramatic side-swept look.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek look.

FAQs

Can curtain bangs make my round face look wider?

No, if you get the right cut and style for your face shape, curtain bangs can actually make your face look slimmer and more defined.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs. Just make sure to leave your bangs out or sweep them to the side.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for taking care of your curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your bangs from getting greasy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your bangs and make them frizzy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, curtain bangs can work for round faces if you get the right cut and style. Follow these tips and tricks for styling and hair care for your curtain bangs to keep them looking great.
