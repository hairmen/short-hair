Long Shaggy Hairstyles For Round Faces A Perfect Look For 2023

Written by Robby Nov 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, you might find it challenging to find a suitable hairstyle that will flatter your features. However, long shaggy hairstyles for round faces can be an excellent option to consider. In this article, we will explore how to achieve this hairstyle, tips to maintain it, and answer some of the frequently asked questions.

Description

Long shaggy hairstyles for round faces are a layered haircut that features a lot of texture and volume. The layers create an illusion of length, which helps elongate the face, making it look less round. The haircut usually involves long layers around the face and shorter layers towards the back. The texture and volume are achieved through razor cutting, which creates a choppy, messy look.

How to Achieve Long Shaggy Hairstyles for Round Faces

To achieve long shaggy hairstyles for round faces, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair a fuller look.

Step 2: Towel-dry your hair

After washing your hair, gently towel-dry it. Do not rub your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product such as a mousse or a volumizing spray to your hair. This will help add texture and volume to your hair.

Step 4: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair while using a round brush to add volume and texture. Use the brush to lift the hair at the roots and create a tousled, messy look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Using a styling tool such as a curling iron or a straightener, style your hair as desired. Use the tool to create loose waves or curls, or straighten your hair for a sleeker look.

Tips to Maintain Long Shaggy Hairstyles for Round Faces

To maintain long shaggy hairstyles for round faces, follow these tips:

  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and maintain the volume of your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ – Long Shaggy Hairstyles for Round Faces

Q: Will long shaggy hairstyles for round faces suit me?

A: Long shaggy hairstyles for round faces can suit anyone, regardless of their hair type, age, or face shape. However, it is essential to consult your hairstylist to determine the best length and style that will flatter your features.

Q: How long does it take to achieve a long shaggy hairstyle for round faces?

A: The time it takes to achieve a long shaggy hairstyle for round faces varies depending on the thickness of your hair and the desired length. However, it usually takes between 45 minutes to an hour to achieve the desired look.

Q: Can I achieve a long shaggy hairstyle for round faces at home?

A: While it is possible to achieve a long shaggy hairstyle for round faces at home, it is recommended to consult a hairstylist, especially if you are not familiar with razor cutting. A hairstylist can help you achieve the desired look while ensuring that your hair remains healthy and undamaged.

Hair Care – Long Shaggy Hairstyles for Round Faces

Long shaggy hairstyles for round faces require regular maintenance to keep them looking healthy and voluminous. Follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair a fuller look.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

In conclusion, long shaggy hairstyles for round faces can be an excellent option to consider if you are looking for a haircut that flatters your features. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to achieve the desired look and maintain healthy, voluminous hair.
