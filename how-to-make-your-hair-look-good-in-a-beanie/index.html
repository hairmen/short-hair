How To Make Your Hair Look Good In A Beanie

Written by Luffy Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Beanies are a popular accessory during the colder months, but they can be a bit of a hassle when it comes to styling your hair. You don't want to sacrifice warmth for style, but you also don't want to walk around with hat hair all day. In this article, we'll go over how to make your hair look good in a beanie, step by step. Let's get started!

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Beanie

The first step to making your hair look good in a beanie is choosing the right beanie. Look for beanies that are not too tight, but also not too loose. You want a beanie that will stay on your head without sliding off, but also won't be so tight that it squishes your hair down.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you put on your beanie, you want to prep your hair. If you have longer hair, pull it back into a low ponytail or braid. This will prevent your hair from getting tangled and messy under the beanie. If you have shorter hair, you can style it as usual, but avoid using too much product as it can make your hair look greasy and flat under the beanie.

Step 3: Put on the Beanie

Now it's time to put on the beanie. Place the beanie on your head, making sure it covers your ears. If you have a slouchy beanie, you can pull it back slightly to create a little bit of volume at the crown of your head.

Step 4: Adjust Your Hair

Once you have the beanie on, you can adjust your hair to make it look good. If you have long hair, you can pull it out from under the beanie and let it hang loose. This will create a cute, messy look that is perfect for casual outings. If you have short hair, you can use your fingers to tousle your hair a bit and create some volume.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you make your hair look good in a beanie:

  • Choose beanies that are made of soft, breathable fabrics like cotton or wool.
  • Avoid wearing a beanie too tightly as it can cause headaches and damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different beanie styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What should I do if my hair looks flat under the beanie?

If your hair looks flat under the beanie, you can try using a dry shampoo to add some volume. Simply spray a little bit of dry shampoo at the roots of your hair, then use your fingers to tousle it a bit. This will create some volume and make your hair look fuller.

Can I wear a beanie with bangs?

Yes, you can definitely wear a beanie with bangs. The key is to position the beanie so that it sits slightly back on your head, rather than right at the hairline. This will allow your bangs to peek out from under the beanie and create a cute, effortless look.

Hair Care

Remember, wearing a beanie regularly can cause damage to your hair. To prevent damage, make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know how to make your hair look good in a beanie, you can rock this accessory all winter long. Just remember to choose the right beanie, prep your hair beforehand, and adjust your hair once you have the beanie on. Happy styling!
