Boy Cut Short Sides Long Top A Trendy Hairstyle For Boys In 2023

Written by Aprilia Oct 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a cool and trendy hairstyle for your little boy? If yes, then boy cut short sides long top can be the perfect hairstyle for him. This hairstyle has been in trend for quite some time now and is here to stay. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about boy cut short sides long top, including how to style it, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

Boy cut short sides long top is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head and long hair on the top. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways, such as a slicked-back look, a messy look, or a textured look. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling and can be worn by boys of all ages.

How to Style Boy Cut Short Sides Long Top

The best thing about boy cut short sides long top is that it can be styled in various ways, depending on your preference and hair type. Here are some popular ways to style this hairstyle:

Slicked-Back Look

To achieve a slicked-back look, apply some hair gel or pomade to your boy's hair and comb it back with a comb. Make sure to apply enough product to hold the hair in place, but not too much that it looks greasy. This look is perfect for formal occasions and can make your boy look sharp and sophisticated.

Messy Look

The messy look is perfect for boys who want a more laid-back and casual hairstyle. To achieve this look, apply some hair wax or clay to your boy's hair and scrunch it up with your fingers. This will create a messy and textured look that is perfect for everyday wear.

Textured Look

The textured look is perfect for boys with thick and wavy hair. To achieve this look, use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray on your boy's hair and scrunch it up with your fingers. This will enhance the natural texture of the hair and create a tousled look that is perfect for a beachy vibe.

Step by Step Guide to Style Boy Cut Short Sides Long Top

Here is a step-by-step guide to style boy cut short sides long top:

  1. Wash your boy's hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry the hair and apply a hair styling product of your choice.
  3. Using a comb, comb the hair on top of the head forward and cut it to the desired length.
  4. Using clippers, cut the hair on the sides and back of the head short.
  5. Blend the hair on the top of the head with the hair on the sides and back using a clipper over comb technique.
  6. Style the hair according to your preference using your fingers or a comb.

Tips to Maintain Boy Cut Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips to maintain boy cut short sides long top:

  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of the hairstyle.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hair styling product as it can make the hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and nourishment to the hair.
  • Avoid exposing the hair to too much heat as it can damage the hair and make it dry and brittle.

FAQs

Can boy cut short sides long top be styled differently?

Yes, boy cut short sides long top can be styled in various ways, such as a slicked-back look, a messy look, or a textured look.

What hair type is best suited for boy cut short sides long top?

Boy cut short sides long top is best suited for boys with straight or wavy hair. Boys with curly hair may find it difficult to maintain this hairstyle.

How often should the hair be trimmed?

The hair should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the hairstyle.

Hair Care for Boy Cut Short Sides Long Top

Here are some hair care tips for boy cut short sides long top:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and nourishment to the hair.
  • Avoid exposing the hair to too much heat as it can damage the hair and make it dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using too much hair styling product as it can make the hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of the hairstyle.

In conclusion, boy cut short sides long top is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways. It is perfect for boys of all ages and hair types and requires minimal styling. By following the tips and hair care routine mentioned in this article, you can maintain this hairstyle and keep your boy looking stylish and fashionable.
