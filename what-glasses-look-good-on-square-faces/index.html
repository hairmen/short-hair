What Glasses Look Good On Square Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you one of the many people out there with a square face? If so, you may have struggled to find glasses that complement your features. But fear not, because we've put together this comprehensive guide on what glasses look good on square faces. Read on to learn more!

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. The goal when choosing glasses for a square face is to soften those angles and add some balance to your features.

How to Choose Glasses for a Square Face

When choosing glasses for a square face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, look for frames that are round or oval in shape. These softer shapes will help balance out the angles of your face. Second, consider frames that are wider than your face. This will help create the illusion of a more proportional face shape.

Step by Step Guide

1. Determine your face shape. If you're not sure, take a look in the mirror and note the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

2. Look for frames that are round or oval in shape.

3. Consider frames that are wider than your face.

4. Try on a variety of styles to see what looks best on you.

5. Don't be afraid to ask for opinions from friends or family members.

Tips for Choosing the Right Glasses

Here are a few additional tips to help you choose the right glasses for your square face:

  • Choose frames that are wider than your face
  • Avoid frames that are too angular or square
  • Look for frames that have curves or softer angles
  • Consider frames that have a pop of color or unique design element to draw attention away from your angular features

FAQs

Can square faces wear cat-eye glasses?

Yes, square faces can definitely wear cat-eye glasses! Just be sure to look for ones that have softer angles and a rounder shape to balance out your features.

What about oversized glasses?

Oversized glasses can look great on square faces, but be sure to choose ones that have a round or oval shape to balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces

When it comes to hair care for square faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, avoid hairstyles that are too angular or severe, as they can accentuate your strong jawline. Instead, opt for styles that have softer, more rounded edges. Second, consider adding some layers to your hair to help create movement and add some balance to your face shape.

Choosing the right glasses for a square face can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect pair in no time. Remember to keep your face shape in mind, look for softer shapes and wider frames, and don't be afraid to ask for opinions. Happy shopping!
