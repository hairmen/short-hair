How To Add Volume To Flat Hair Without Product

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair constantly falling flat and lacking volume? Do you want to add some bounce and body to your locks without using any products? You're in luck! In this article, we will show you how to add volume to flat hair without product using simple and easy techniques.

Table of Contents

  • How to Add Volume to Flat Hair Without Product
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Flat Hair

How to Add Volume to Flat Hair Without Product

Adding volume to flat hair without product may seem impossible, but it's not! There are a few simple techniques you can use to add body and bounce to your hair.

The first thing you need to do is change your hair part. If you always part your hair in the same spot, your hair will become flat and lifeless. Try switching your part to the other side or parting it down the middle. This will instantly add volume to your hair.

Another way to add volume to flat hair is by blow drying your hair upside down. This will help lift your roots and add volume to your hair. Use a low heat setting and dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. Then, flip your hair back over and finish drying it with the cool setting.

You can also add volume to your hair by teasing it with a comb. Take small sections of your hair and tease them at the roots. This will create volume and body. Just be careful not to over-tease your hair or you'll end up with a tangled mess.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to add volume to flat hair without product:

  1. Change your hair part to the other side or down the middle.
  2. Blow dry your hair upside down on a low heat setting until it's 80% dry.
  3. Flip your hair back over and finish drying it with the cool setting.
  4. Tease your hair at the roots with a comb, starting at the crown of your head.
  5. Smooth out the top layer of hair with a brush.

Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you add volume to your flat hair:

  • Avoid using heavy conditioners or hair masks that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Try using a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add volume to my hair without using any products?

Yes, you can! Follow the techniques outlined in this article to add volume to your hair without using any products.

Will teasing my hair damage it?

Teasing your hair can cause some damage, but as long as you do it gently and don't overdo it, your hair should be fine. Just be sure to use a wide-tooth comb and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair too hard.

How often should I wash my hair to add volume?

Washing your hair every day can actually strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to add volume. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days to keep it healthy and voluminous.

Hair Care Tips for Flat Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you keep your flat hair healthy and voluminous:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair and make it flat.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair that can damage it.

By following these simple tips and techniques, you can add volume to your flat hair without using any products. Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find what works best for you. With a little bit of patience and practice, you can achieve the voluminous hair of your dreams!
