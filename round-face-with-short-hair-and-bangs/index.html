Round Face With Short Hair And Bangs A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Christine Oct 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old long hair that seems to drag down your round face? Do you want to switch things up with a short hairstyle but don't know where to start? Look no further than the trendy and versatile combination of a round face with short hair and bangs! In this guide, we'll show you how to rock this style, step by step.

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with a width and length that are almost equal. The best short hairstyles for round faces are those that add height to the crown, elongate the neck, and create the illusion of angles and sharpness. Bangs can also help to balance out the face and draw attention away from the cheeks.

How to

Step 1: Consult with a stylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's important to talk to a professional stylist who can recommend the best short hairstyles for your face shape and hair type. They can also help you determine the right length for your hair and the most flattering type of bangs for your features.

Step 2: Choose your cut

There are many different types of short hairstyles that can work well with a round face, from pixie cuts to bob haircuts. The key is to choose a style that adds height to the crown and elongates the neck. Layered cuts and asymmetrical styles can also help to create the illusion of angles and sharpness.

Step 3: Add bangs

Bangs are a great way to balance out the face and draw attention away from the cheeks. Side-swept bangs are particularly flattering for round faces, as they create a diagonal line that breaks up the circular shape. Baby bangs and blunt bangs can also work well, depending on your personal style and hair texture.

Tips

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can emphasize the roundness of your face
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height to the crown of your head
  • Experiment with different types of bangs to find the most flattering style for your face shape

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for round faces?

The best short hairstyles for round faces are those that add height to the crown, elongate the neck, and create the illusion of angles and sharpness. Layered cuts, pixie cuts, and bob haircuts are all great options.

What type of bangs are most flattering for round faces?

Side-swept bangs are particularly flattering for round faces, as they create a diagonal line that breaks up the circular shape. Baby bangs and blunt bangs can also work well, depending on your personal style and hair texture.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and keep split ends at bay. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a flattering and stylish short hairstyle in no time. Embrace your round face and show off your unique features with confidence!
