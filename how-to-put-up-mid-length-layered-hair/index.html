How To Put Up Mid Length Layered Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have mid-length layered hair, you might be wondering how to put it up in a way that looks stylish and keeps your hair out of your face. There are many different styles you can try, from messy buns to braids to sleek ponytails. In this article, we'll go over step-by-step instructions for some of our favorite mid-length layered hair updos, as well as tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great.

How to Put Up Mid Length Layered Hair

Before we get into the step-by-step instructions, it's important to have the right tools on hand. Here are some items you may want to gather:

  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hair ties or bobby pins
  • Hairspray or other styling products
  • Optional: hair accessories like clips, headbands, or scarves

Once you have your tools ready, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles. Here are some of our favorite mid-length layered hair updos:

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic look that's perfect for casual occasions. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  4. Tuck the loose ends of your hair into the bun and secure them with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, relaxed look.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold everything in place.

Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is a trendy and elegant style that's perfect for more formal occasions. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a low ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Divide the ponytail into two equal sections.
  4. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the inside of the right section.
  5. Repeat on the right side, taking a small piece of hair from the outside and crossing it over to the inside of the left section.
  6. Continue alternating sides until you reach the end of your hair.
  7. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  8. Gently pull on the sides of the braid to loosen it and create a more relaxed look.
  9. Finish with hairspray to hold everything in place.

Sleek Ponytail

The sleek ponytail is a simple and sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Take a small piece of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it.
  4. Secure the end of the wrapped hair with a bobby pin.
  5. Smooth out any flyaways or bumps with a comb or brush.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your mid-length layered hair:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories like clips, headbands, or scarves to add some extra interest to your updos.
  • Try using different types of hair ties to achieve different looks. For example, a scrunchie can create a more relaxed, bohemian vibe.
  • Don't be afraid to play with texture. You can use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or sleekness to your hair before putting it up.
  • Try incorporating braids or twists into your updos for added interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my hair is too short for these styles?

If your hair is too short to put up in a bun or ponytail, you can try adding some volume and texture to your hair with a styling product like mousse or texturizing spray. You can also experiment with half-up styles or braids.

How can I keep my hair healthy while putting it up in updos?

It's important to be gentle with your hair when putting it up in updos. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, and use bobby pins or hair ties that won't cause damage or breakage. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Layered Hair
