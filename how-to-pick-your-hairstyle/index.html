How To Pick Your Hairstyle Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Sep 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many different styles and trends to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to pick your hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  • Identify Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Find Your Personal Style

Identify Your Face Shape

The first step in picking the right hairstyle is to identify your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle will flatter you the most. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped.

If you're not sure what your face shape is, here's how to figure it out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror with a washable marker.
  3. Step back and compare the shape to the five common face shapes.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, and almost any hairstyle will look good on it.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape has a circular shape with a rounded hairline and chin. The best hairstyles for this face shape are those that add height to the top of the head, such as a layered bob or a long, layered cut.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and a square hairline. The best hairstyles for this face shape are those that soften the angular features, such as a side-swept fringe or a chin-length bob.

Heart-Shaped Face Shape

A heart-shaped face shape has a wider forehead and a narrower chin. The best hairstyles for this face shape are those that add volume to the chin area, such as a textured bob or a side-swept fringe.

Diamond-Shaped Face Shape

A diamond-shaped face shape has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. The best hairstyles for this face shape are those that add width to the forehead and chin, such as a textured pixie cut or a side-swept fringe.

Consider Your Hair Texture

In addition to your face shape, your hair texture is also an important factor to consider when picking a hairstyle. Your hair texture can be fine, medium, or thick, and it can be straight, wavy, curly, or kinky.

If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that adds volume and body. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a longer cut that thins out the hair and reduces bulk. If you have curly hair, you may want to choose a style that enhances your natural curls, while if you have straight hair, you may want to go for a style that adds texture and movement.

Find Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when picking a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and lifestyle. If you're a busy mom who doesn't have much time for styling, you may want to choose a low-maintenance style that can be easily styled on the go. If you're a fashion-forward trendsetter, you may want to choose a bold and edgy hairstyle that makes a statement.

When picking a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your personal style, whether it's classic, trendy, edgy, or bohemian.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've picked your hairstyle, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products that are suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage and damage.

FAQs

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I change my hairstyle if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! There are plenty of hairstyles that look great on curly hair, from short bobs to long layers. Just make sure to choose a style that enhances your natural curls and works with your face shape.

Is it better to air dry or blow dry my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have curly hair, air-drying may be the best option to prevent frizz. If you have straight hair, blow-drying can help add volume and texture. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

The best way to know if a hairstyle will suit you is to try it out! You can use virtual try-on tools or consult with a hairstylist to get an idea of how a particular style will look on you.

Picking the right hairstyle can seem like a daunting task, but by considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can find a style that flatters you and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best, and don't be afraid to try something new!
