Can I Cut The Top Of My Hair With Clippers

Written by Eveline Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to our blog post about cutting the top of your hair with clippers. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this topic. From how to do it, step by step, to tips and tricks, frequently asked questions, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Description

If you're looking for a way to keep your hair trimmed without going to the salon, using clippers is a great option. Clippers are easy to use, affordable, and can be used to cut hair of various lengths. But can you cut the top of your hair with clippers? The answer is yes!

How to

Before you start cutting your hair with clippers, you need to make sure you have the right equipment. You will need a pair of clippers, hair scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Use the comb to part your hair in the middle.
  3. Clip the sides of your hair using the clippers. Use a guard to achieve your desired length.
  4. Move to the top of your hair and comb it in the direction you want it to lie.
  5. Using the clippers, start at the back of your head and work your way towards the front.
  6. Use the scissors to trim any long hairs that the clippers missed.
  7. Brush your hair to remove any loose hair.
  8. Check for any uneven spots and fix them using the clippers or scissors.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Invest in a good pair of clippers. Cheap clippers may not perform well and can damage your hair.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve your desired length.
  • Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs that the clippers missed.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Ask someone to help you if you're not comfortable doing it yourself.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with clippers if it's curly?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to use a longer guard to avoid cutting your hair too short. You can also use the scissors to trim any stray hairs.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on your hair growth rate and how quickly it becomes unruly. On average, you should aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Can I use clippers to cut my hair if it's thinning?

Yes, you can. However, it's important to be gentle and use a longer guard to avoid cutting too much hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners and curling irons.
  • Trim any split ends regularly to prevent them from traveling up the hair shaft.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

That's it for our guide on cutting the top of your hair with clippers. We hope you found this article helpful. Remember to take your time, be careful, and don't hesitate to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
