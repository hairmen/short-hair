Hairstyles For Thin Curly Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 25, 2023 · 5 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose its natural volume. This can be especially true for those with curly hair. But fear not, there are plenty of hairstyles for thin curly hair over 60 that can make you look and feel your best. In this article, we’ll discuss how to choose the right hairstyle for your thin curly hair, as well as provide some tips and tricks for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Thin Curly Hair Over 60

Choosing the right hairstyle for thin curly hair over 60 can be a daunting task. You want to find a style that complements your face shape, highlights your best features, and works with your hair’s natural texture. Here are some tips for choosing the perfect hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, it’s important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a style that adds height to the crown of your head, such as a short, layered cut. If you have a square face, a longer, layered cut can soften your features and add movement to your hair.

Highlight Your Best Features

If you have beautiful eyes or cheekbones, consider a shorter hairstyle that draws attention to your face. If you have a long neck, consider a longer hairstyle that showcases your elegant neckline.

Work with Your Hair’s Natural Texture

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s full of natural volume and bounce, it can also be difficult to manage. When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair’s natural texture. If your curls are tight and springy, a shorter, layered cut can help to define your curls and add volume. If your curls are looser, consider a longer, layered cut that will showcase your curls and add movement to your hair.

Hairstyles for Thin Curly Hair Over 60

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle for your thin curly hair over 60, let’s take a look at some popular styles:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a popular style for those with thin curly hair. This short, layered cut adds height to the crown of your head and defines your curls. It’s also low-maintenance and easy to style.

The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for those with thin curly hair. This cut can be customized to fit your face shape and hair texture. A shorter bob can add volume and bounce to your hair, while a longer bob can showcase your curls and add movement.

The Shag

The shag is a layered cut that works well for those with thin curly hair. This cut adds volume and movement to your hair, while still maintaining a natural, effortless look. The shag can be customized to fit your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step: How to Style Thin Curly Hair Over 60

Now that you have chosen the perfect hairstyle for your thin curly hair over 60, let’s take a look at how to style your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo. Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to breakage.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a moisturizing conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave the conditioner in your hair for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cool water.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl-defining cream or mousse. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to define your curls and add volume. Hold the diffuser close to your head and use a low heat setting.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Thin Curly Hair Over 60

Now that you know how to style your thin curly hair over 60, let’s take a look at some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Condition Your Hair Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Tip 4: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful rays.

FAQ: Hairstyles for Thin Curly Hair Over 60

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have thin curly hair over 60?

A: Absolutely! A longer hairstyle can be a great way to showcase your curls and add movement to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use a regular shampoo on my thin curly hair?

A: It’s best to use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Conclusion

There are plenty of hairstyles for thin curly hair over 60 that can make you look and feel your best. By choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture, as well as following some simple tips for maintaining healthy hair, you can rock a beautiful, vibrant look at any age. So go ahead and embrace your natural curls with confidence!
