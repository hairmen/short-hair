How To Cut Short Hair For Women A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and ready for a bold new look? Cutting your hair short can be a refreshing change and a great way to express your personal style. However, it can be intimidating to take the plunge and cut your own hair. But with the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a stylish short haircut at home.

How to Cut Short Hair for Women

If you're ready to try cutting your own hair, follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you'll need to gather the right tools for the job. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair to ensure an even cut. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and let it air dry or use a blow dryer to dry completely.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into manageable sections. Clip the top layers of your hair out of the way so you can focus on the bottom layers first.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting with the bottom layers, use your scissors to make small, vertical cuts into the hair. This technique is called point cutting and will give your hair a more natural, textured look.

Step 5: Work Your Way Up

Once you've finished the bottom layers, unclip the top layers of your hair and repeat the same cutting process. Be sure to blend the layers together for a seamless look.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, use your comb and scissors to make any necessary adjustments. Check for any uneven or longer pieces and trim them as needed.

Step 7: Style Your New Look

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style your new look. Use a styling product like mousse or gel to add texture and hold to your hair. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut your short hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to longer cuts to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use your fingers to measure the length of your hair as you cut to ensure an even length.
  • When in doubt, cut less rather than more. You can always go back and trim more later.

FAQs About Cutting Short Hair for Women

Can I cut my own short hair?

Yes! Cutting your own short hair can be a little intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's definitely doable.

What kind of scissors should I use to cut my hair?

It's important to use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut. Don't use regular household scissors, as they can damage your hair.

How often should I cut my short hair?

Short haircuts typically require more frequent trims to maintain their shape. Plan to get a trim every 4-6 weeks, or as needed to keep your hair looking its best.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips for keeping your short hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own short hair and enjoy a fresh new look. Happy cutting!
