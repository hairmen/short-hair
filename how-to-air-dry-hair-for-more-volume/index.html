How To Air Dry Hair For More Volume Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to add more volume to your hair without using heat styling tools, air drying is a great option. Not only is it healthier for your hair, but it's also easy and convenient. In this article, we'll show you how to air dry your hair for more volume and share some tips and tricks to make the process even easier.

How to Air Dry Hair for More Volume

To air dry your hair for more volume, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing product

Apply a volumizing product to your hair, such as a mousse or foam. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Flip your hair upside down

Flip your hair upside down and gently scrunch your hair with your hands. This will help to create volume and texture. Continue scrunching your hair until it's about 80% dry.

Step 4: Finish air drying

Flip your hair back over and let it air dry completely. Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the natural texture.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you air dry your hair for more volume:

Tip 1: Use a microfiber towel

Instead of using a regular towel to dry your hair, use a microfiber towel. This will help to absorb excess water without causing frizz or damage.

Tip 2: Use a diffuser

If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your natural texture and create more volume.

Tip 3: Don't touch your hair too much

Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the natural texture. Let your hair air dry as much as possible before touching it.

Tip 4: Sleep with your hair in a bun

If you want to add even more volume to your hair, sleep with your hair in a bun. This will help to create volume and texture while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about air drying hair for more volume:

Q: How long does it take to air dry hair for more volume?

A: It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours to air dry your hair, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I still use heat styling tools after air drying my hair?

A: Yes, you can still use heat styling tools after air drying your hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Q: What if my hair looks too frizzy after air drying?

A: If your hair looks too frizzy after air drying, try using a smoothing product or serum to tame the frizz.

Hair Care

Air drying your hair for more volume is a great way to add texture and body to your hair without using heat styling tools. Just remember to use a volumizing product, avoid touching your hair too much, and let your hair air dry as much as possible. With these tips and tricks, you'll have voluminous hair in no time!
