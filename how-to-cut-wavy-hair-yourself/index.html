How To Cut Wavy Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have wavy hair, you might find it hard to get a haircut that compliments your natural texture. Going to a salon every time can be expensive and time-consuming. However, with a little bit of practice, you can learn how to cut wavy hair yourself at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting wavy hair step by step.

  • How to Cut Wavy Hair Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Wavy Hair Yourself

Before we begin, gather all the necessary tools such as hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you start cutting.

Next, decide on the length and style you want. It is always better to start small and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but once it's gone, it's gone.

Now, separate your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with the Bottom Section

Take the bottom section of your hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and slide your fingers down to the desired length. Use the hair cutting scissors to cut the hair in a straight line.

2. Move to the Middle Section

Release the middle section of your hair and comb it straight. Use the bottom section as a guide and cut the hair in a straight line.

3. Finish with the Top Section

Release the top section of your hair and comb it straight. Use the middle section as a guide and cut the hair in a straight line.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Hair

1. Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends.

2. Cut your hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises when it dries.

3. Start with small cuts and work your way up.

4. Use a mirror to check the back of your hair.

FAQ

Q. How often should I cut my wavy hair?

A. It depends on the length and style you want. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your hair.

Q. Can I cut my wavy hair when it's wet?

A. It is not recommended to cut wavy hair when it's wet as it can look different when it dries.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair tends to be dry, so it is important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools such as a straightener or curling iron as they can damage your hair. Instead, embrace your natural texture and use products that enhance your waves such as mousse or sea salt spray.

Cutting wavy hair yourself can be intimidating at first, but with practice, it can save you time and money. Remember to start small and work your way up, and always use sharp hair cutting scissors. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll have a great haircut in no time.
