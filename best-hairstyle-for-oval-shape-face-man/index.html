The Best Hairstyles For Oval Shape Face Men In 2023

Written by Aprilia Nov 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are a man with an oval face shape, then you are in luck! Oval face shapes are versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for oval shape face men in 2023, and provide you with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width. Men with oval face shapes have the advantage of being able to pull off a variety of hairstyles, from short and cropped to long and flowing. However, it is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your features.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for your oval face shape, consider your hair type and texture, as well as your personal style. It is also important to consider the length and volume of your hair, as this can affect the overall look of your hairstyle. Some of the best hairstyles for oval face shape men include:

1. Short and Textured

A short and textured hairstyle, such as a messy crop or a textured quiff, can add volume and dimension to your hair while complementing your face shape. This style works well for men with straight or wavy hair.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can add softness to your face shape while enhancing your features. This style works well for men with longer hair and can be worn with a variety of hair textures.

3. Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that works well for oval face shape men. This style is characterized by short sides and a longer top that is styled upwards. It works well for men with thick hair and can be styled with a side part or a slicked-back look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for oval face shape men:

1. Short and Textured

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of texturizing product to your hair, such as a sea salt spray or a matte paste.
  3. Using your fingers, tousle your hair to create texture and volume.
  4. Style your hair into a messy crop or a textured quiff, using a comb or your fingers to create your desired look.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a comb, create a deep side part on one side of your head.
  3. Brush your hair to the opposite side of your part, creating a side swept look.
  4. Use a small amount of styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to hold the style in place.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Classic Pompadour

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Using a comb, create a side part on one side of your head.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair.
  4. Using a comb or your fingers, style the hair on top of your head upwards and towards the back of your head.
  5. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place, and finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for your oval face shape:

  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style and enhances your features.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for an oval face shape man?

Some of the best haircuts for oval face shape men include short and textured hairstyles, side swept bangs, and classic pompadours.

What hairstyles should oval face shape men avoid?

Oval face shape men should avoid hairstyles that add too much height or volume to the top of the head, as this can elongate the face shape. They should also avoid hairstyles that are too long and cover their face.

Can oval face shape men pull off a buzz cut?

Yes, oval face shape men can pull off a buzz cut, as this style accentuates their facial features and adds a masculine edge to their look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to practice good hair care habits. This includes washing your hair regularly, conditioning it, and using styling products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture. You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause it to become dry and brittle.

In conclusion, choosing the best hairstyle for your oval face shape can be a fun and rewarding experience. By considering your hair type and texture, personal style, and face shape, you can find the perfect hairstyle that enhances your features and makes you feel confident and stylish.
