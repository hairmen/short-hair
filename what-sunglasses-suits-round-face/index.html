What Sunglasses Suits Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect sunglasses that suit your round face? Well, you are not alone. Round faces are one of the most common face shapes, and it can be a bit challenging to find the right sunglasses that flatter the face shape. In this article, we will guide you through the different types of sunglasses that suit round faces and help you choose the perfect pair that matches your style and personality.

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and full cheeks. The width and length of the face are equal, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. The chin is usually rounded and less pronounced. When choosing sunglasses for round faces, the goal is to add definition and make the face appear longer and slimmer.

How to Choose the Right Sunglasses for Round Face

The key to finding the right sunglasses for round faces is to look for frames that contrast the face shape. Angular frames with sharp lines and corners will add definition to the softness of the round face. It's also essential to consider the size of the frames. Oversized frames can overwhelm the face and make it appear even rounder.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Sunglasses for Round Face

1. Determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and assess the shape of your face. Round faces are characterized by soft curves and full cheeks.

2. Look for angular frames. Choose sunglasses with sharp lines and corners that contrast the softness of the round face.

3. Consider the size of the frames. Avoid oversized frames that can overwhelm the face.

4. Choose frames with a wide bridge. A wide bridge will make the eyes appear farther apart and balance the roundness of the face.

5. Look for frames that are wider than the face. Frames that extend beyond the width of the face will add length to the face and make it appear slimmer.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Sunglasses for Round Face

- Opt for square, rectangular, or cat-eye frames that add definition to the softness of the round face.

- Avoid round or circular frames that will only accentuate the roundness of the face.

- Choose frames with a high bridge that will lift the sunglasses off the face and make the face appear longer.

- Look for frames with thick temples that provide balance to the face.

- Opt for frames with bold colors or patterns that draw attention away from the roundness of the face.

FAQs

Q. Can round faces wear aviator sunglasses?

A. Yes, aviator sunglasses can look great on round faces. Look for frames with a wider bridge and square or rectangular shapes to balance the softness of the face.

Q. What color sunglasses should I choose for round face?

A. Bold colors and patterns can draw attention away from the roundness of the face. Black, tortoiseshell, and dark brown frames are classic choices that suit most face shapes.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Choosing the right sunglasses is only one part of the equation. Your hairstyle can also make a significant difference in how your face looks. Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

- Opt for hairstyles with volume and height at the crown to elongate the face.

- Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

- Choose hairstyles that frame the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect sunglasses that suit your round face and enhance your natural beauty. Remember to choose frames that contrast the softness of the face and add definition to the curves. Happy shopping!
