How To Stop Hair Fall Immediately At Home For Male

Written by Robby Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are experiencing hair fall, you are not alone. Hair fall is a common problem faced by men of all ages. It can be caused by various reasons such as stress, pollution, genetics, and lack of proper hair care. However, the good news is that there are many home remedies that can help you stop hair fall immediately. In this article, we will discuss some effective ways to stop hair fall at home for male.

Step by Step Guide

1. Massage your scalp: Massaging your scalp with warm oil can help improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth. You can use coconut oil, olive oil, or almond oil for the massage. Heat the oil and apply it to your scalp. Massage gently for 10-15 minutes and leave it on for an hour before washing.

2. Use onion juice: Onion juice contains sulfur which can help strengthen hair follicles and reduce hair fall. Extract onion juice and apply it to your scalp. Massage gently for 10 minutes and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing.

3. Apply aloe vera: Aloe vera contains enzymes that can help promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. Apply aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for an hour before washing.

4. Use fenugreek seeds: Fenugreek seeds contain hormone antecedents that can help promote hair growth and strengthen hair follicles. Soak fenugreek seeds overnight and grind them to make a paste. Apply the paste to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing.

5. Use green tea: Green tea contains antioxidants that can help promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. Brew green tea and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for an hour before washing.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

1. Eat a healthy diet: A healthy diet can help promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Include foods rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet.

2. Avoid stress: Stress can cause hair fall. Practice stress-relieving activities such as yoga, meditation, or exercise.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, or buns can cause hair fall. Avoid such hairstyles and opt for loose hairstyles instead.

FAQs

1. How much hair fall is normal? It is normal to lose 50-100 hairs per day. However, if you are losing more than that, it may indicate a problem.

2. Can hair fall be reversed? Yes, hair fall can be reversed with proper hair care and treatment.

3. How long does it take to see results? It may take a few weeks to a few months to see results. Consistency is key.

Hair Care Routine

1. Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair every 2-3 days using a mild shampoo.

2. Condition your hair: Condition your hair after shampooing to keep it moisturized.

3. Avoid hot water: Hot water can damage your hair. Use lukewarm or cold water instead.

4. Avoid harsh chemicals: Harsh chemicals such as hair dyes or hair straighteners can damage your hair. Avoid using them or use them sparingly.

5. Protect your hair: Protect your hair from sun, wind, and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, hair fall can be a frustrating problem to deal with, but it can be treated with the help of home remedies and proper hair care. Follow the steps mentioned above and be consistent to see results. If the problem persists, consult a dermatologist.
