How To Make Bun Hairstyle For Long Hair

Written by Luffy Oct 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing your long hair down all the time? Do you want a quick and easy hairstyle that can make you look elegant and put together? The solution is simple - make a bun! In this post, we will guide you through the steps to make a bun hairstyle for long hair.

Description

A bun hairstyle is a classic and timeless look that can be worn for any occasion. Whether you are going to work or attending a fancy event, a bun can make you look chic and sophisticated. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to

Follow these simple steps to make a bun hairstyle for long hair:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Make sure your hair is smooth and straight.

Step 2: Tie your hair into a ponytail

Gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it into a high or low ponytail using an elastic band. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 3: Twist your hair

Take the ponytail and twist it around the elastic band. Keep twisting until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 4: Wrap your hair

Wrap the twisted hair around the elastic band to form a bun. Tuck the end of your hair under the bun to secure it in place.

Step 5: Secure the bun

Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Place the pins around the bun, making sure they are hidden from view. Spray some hairspray to hold the bun in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your bun hairstyle look even better:

  • Use a hair donut to create a fuller bun.
  • Add some accessories like flowers or jeweled pins to make the bun more elegant.
  • Create a messy bun by pulling out some strands of hair to frame your face.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less visible.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a bun hairstyle for long hair:

Can I make a bun with wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to make a bun with wet hair as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Make sure your hair is dry before making a bun.

How long does it take to make a bun?

Making a bun hairstyle for long hair can take anywhere from 5 to 10 minutes depending on your hair length and thickness.

What type of hair is best for a bun?

A bun can be made on any hair type - straight, curly, or wavy. However, it is easier to make a bun on hair that is not too slippery or too thick.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Making a bun hairstyle for long hair is easy and quick. Follow these steps to create a chic and elegant look that can be worn for any occasion. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
