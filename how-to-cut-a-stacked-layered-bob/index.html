How To Cut A Stacked Layered Bob

Written by Oliver Nov 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, a stacked layered bob might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut features short layers at the back of the head that gradually get longer towards the front, creating a stacked effect. In this blog post, we'll show you how to cut a stacked layered bob step by step, including tips and hair care advice.

How to Cut a Stacked Layered Bob

To cut a stacked layered bob, you'll need some basic haircutting tools, including a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also helpful to have a mirror and a spray bottle filled with water. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Clip away the front sections and focus on the back ones for now.

Step 2: Cut the Bottom Layer

Take a small section of hair at the nape of the neck and comb it straight down. Cut the hair to the desired length, making sure it's even all around. This will be the bottom layer of your stacked bob.

Step 3: Layer the Back

Take another section of hair just above the first one and comb it down. Use the first section as a guide and cut this one slightly shorter, creating a stacked effect. Repeat this process for the rest of the back sections until you reach the crown of the head.

Step 4: Work on the Front Layers

Release the front sections and comb them down. Cut them to the desired length, making sure they match the back layers. You can also add some shorter layers around the face for a more textured look.

Step 5: Finish the Cut

Blend the layers together by combing through the hair and cutting any uneven bits. Check the length and shape in the mirror and make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting a Stacked Layered Bob

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a stacked layered bob:

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure accuracy.
  • Use the first section as a guide for the rest of the layers.
  • Add some shorter layers around the face for a more textured look.
  • Blend the layers together by combing through the hair and cutting any uneven bits.
  • Check the length and shape in the mirror and make any final adjustments as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will a stacked layered bob suit me?

A stacked layered bob can suit many face shapes and hair types. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine if this haircut is right for you.

2. Can I cut a stacked layered bob at home?

Yes, you can cut a stacked layered bob at home. However, it's best to have some experience with haircutting or to follow a step by step guide like the one in this article.

3. How often should I get a stacked layered bob cut?

It's recommended to get a stacked layered bob cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care for Stacked Layered Bob

Here are some hair care tips to keep your stacked layered bob looking fresh and healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the layers.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling on the layers.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your stacked layered bob.

Cutting a stacked layered bob can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern haircut at home. Follow our step by step guide and hair care tips to keep your stacked layered bob looking fresh and healthy.
