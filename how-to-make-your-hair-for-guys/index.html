How To Make Your Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of having the same boring hairstyle day in and day out? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Look no further! In this guide, we will teach you how to make your hair for guys in a variety of styles. From slick back to messy, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Better Results

How to Make Your Hair for Guys

Before we dive into the specifics of each hairstyle, there are a few things you should keep in mind regardless of the style you choose.

First, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You’ll need a comb, hair gel, and possibly a hair dryer depending on the style you choose.

Second, it’s important to know your hair type. Different hair types require different care and styling techniques, so make sure you know what type of hair you have before you start.

Lastly, be patient. Styling your hair can take some time and practice to get it just right, so don’t get discouraged if it doesn’t look perfect the first time.

Slick Back

The slick back hairstyle is a classic look that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, start by combing your hair back and away from your face. Apply hair gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair. Finish by using a hair dryer on low heat to set the style in place.

Quiff

The quiff is a popular hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair. To achieve this look, start by blow drying your hair while using a comb to lift and style the front of your hair upwards. Apply hair gel to the front of your hair, working your way from the roots to the tips. Use your fingers to shape the front of your hair into a quiff shape.

Side Part

The side part is a classic and timeless hairstyle that is easy to achieve. Start by combing your hair to one side and parting it where you want it to be. Apply hair gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair. Finish by using a hair dryer on low heat to set the style in place.

Messy

The messy hairstyle is perfect for those who want a more relaxed and casual look. To achieve this look, simply apply hair gel to your hair and use your fingers to style it in a messy, tousled look. Use a hair dryer on low heat to set the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that we’ve gone over the different types of hairstyles, let’s break down the steps you should take to achieve your desired look.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any buildup and make your hair easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Use a towel to dry your hair, but don’t rub too hard as this can damage your hair. Instead, gently pat your hair dry.

Step 3: Apply Hair Gel

Apply hair gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Use a comb to evenly distribute the gel throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into your desired look. Use a hair dryer on low heat to set the style in place.

Tips for Better Results

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results when styling your hair:

  • Use high-quality hair products for better hold and a more natural look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don’t overuse hair gel as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Be gentle when combing or styling your hair to avoid damaging it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, it’s recommended to wash your hair 2-3 times per week.

Can I use hair gel on wet hair?

It’s best to apply hair gel to slightly damp hair for better hold and easier styling.

How do I prevent my hair from getting greasy?

Wash your hair regularly, avoid touching your hair throughout the day, and use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to help prevent excess oil buildup.

Hair Care Tips

Styling your hair is just one part of hair care. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Now that you know how to make your hair for guys, it’s time to experiment and find the style that works best for you. Remember to be patient and practice, practice, practice!
