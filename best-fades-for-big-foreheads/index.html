Best Fades For Big Foreheads A Complete Guide

If you have a big forehead, you might find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best fades for big foreheads that will make you look stylish and confident. We will cover everything from what a fade is to how to choose the right fade for your face shape. So, let's get started!

What is a Fade?

A fade is a hairstyle that features hair that gradually gets shorter from the top of the head to the sides and back. There are different types of fades, including low fade, mid fade, and high fade. Fades are popular among men because they are versatile and can be tailored to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Choose the Right Fade for Your Face Shape

Choosing the right fade for your face shape is crucial to achieving a flattering hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, go for a low fade that is shorter on the sides and longer on top.
  • If you have a square face, a mid fade that is tapered on the sides and back will complement your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off any type of fade, but a high fade with a short top will accentuate your features.
  • If you have a long face, a low or mid fade with a longer top will balance your facial proportions.

Best Fades for Big Foreheads

If you have a big forehead, there are several fade hairstyles that will suit you. Here are some of the best:

Low Fade with Textured Top

A low fade with a textured top is an excellent choice for men with big foreheads because it draws attention away from the forehead and towards the hair on top. The textured top adds volume and movement to the hair, creating a dynamic and stylish look.

High Fade with Quiff

A high fade with a quiff is another great option for big foreheads because the height of the quiff balances out the forehead. The high fade also creates a clean and sharp look that is perfect for the modern man.

Buzz Cut with Fade

If you prefer a shorter hairstyle, a buzz cut with a fade is a good choice for big foreheads. The buzz cut is low-maintenance and easy to style, while the fade adds a touch of style and sophistication.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fade

Now that you have chosen the right fade for your big forehead, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your fade looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still get a fade if I have thinning hair?

Yes, you can still get a fade if you have thinning hair. However, you should opt for a low fade that is shorter on the sides and longer on top. This will create the illusion of fuller hair on top.

Do I need to use a specific type of shampoo for my fade?

It's best to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type. Avoid using cheap products that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

How often should I visit my barber for a touch-up?

You should visit your barber every 2-3 weeks to keep your fade looking fresh. However, this may vary depending on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your fade grows out.

Conclusion

Choosing the right fade for your big forehead can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish and confident look. Remember to choose a fade that complements your face shape, maintain it properly, and visit your barber regularly for touch-ups. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go!
