What Is A 2 Back And Sides Haircut

Written by William Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then a 2 back and sides might be the perfect choice for you. This popular haircut features short hair on the back and sides of the head, with longer hair on top. In this blog post, we'll explain everything you need to know about the 2 back and sides haircut, including how to get it, the best styling tips, and how to take care of your hair.

Description of a 2 Back and Sides Haircut

A 2 back and sides is a short haircut that features hair that is cut to a #2 clipper guard on the back and sides of the head. The hair on top of the head is left longer, making it the perfect style for those who want a trendy, yet low-maintenance look. This haircut is also known as a "short back and sides" or simply a "2 on the sides."

How to Get a 2 Back and Sides Haircut

If you're interested in getting a 2 back and sides haircut, then the first step is to find a skilled barber or hair stylist who has experience with this cut. Once you've found someone who you trust, you can ask for a 2 on the sides and explain that you want the hair on top to be left longer. You can also show them a picture of the style you're looking for to help them understand exactly what you want.

During the haircut, the barber or stylist will use clippers to cut the hair on the back and sides of your head to a #2 guard length. They will then use scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. The end result should be a clean, stylish haircut that is easy to maintain.

Styling Tips for a 2 Back and Sides Haircut

One of the best things about a 2 back and sides haircut is that it is versatile and can be styled in different ways depending on your personal preferences. Here are some tips for styling your hair after getting a 2 back and sides:

  • Use a matte or low-shine hair product to keep your hair looking natural and textured.
  • For a casual look, you can simply use your fingers to tousle your hair into place.
  • If you want a more polished look, you can use a comb to create a side part or a slicked-back style.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs About 2 Back and Sides Haircuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about 2 back and sides haircuts:

Q: Will a 2 back and sides haircut work for all hair types?

A: Yes, a 2 back and sides can work for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair. Your barber or stylist can help you determine the best length and style for your hair type.

Q: How often do I need to get a 2 back and sides haircut?

A: The frequency of your haircuts will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. Generally, you should plan on getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your 2 back and sides style.

Q: Can I style my hair differently after getting a 2 back and sides?

A: Yes, one of the benefits of a 2 back and sides haircut is that it is versatile and can be styled in different ways. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

How to Take Care of Your 2 Back and Sides Haircut

After getting a 2 back and sides haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your 2 back and sides haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a hair product that works for your hair type to keep your hair looking styled and textured.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Overall, a 2 back and sides haircut is a stylish and trendy choice that works for all hair types. With the right styling products and techniques, you can create a look that is uniquely yours and easy to maintain.
