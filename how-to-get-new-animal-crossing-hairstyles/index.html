How To Get New Animal Crossing Hairstyles

Written by Oliver Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an avid Animal Crossing player, you know that the game allows you to customize your character's hairstyle. However, after a while, the options can start to feel limited. Luckily, there are ways to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing. In this article, we'll walk you through how to get new Animal Crossing hairstyles step by step.

How to Get New Animal Crossing Hairstyles

There are a few different ways to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

  • Purchasing from the Able Sisters shop
  • Using Nook Miles at the Nook Stop
  • Scanning amiibo

Purchasing from the Able Sisters Shop

The Able Sisters shop is a clothing store that sells a variety of items, including hairstyles. To access the Able Sisters shop, you'll need to have upgraded Resident Services to a building and have Mabel visit your island at least once. Once the Able Sisters shop is open, you can purchase new hairstyles by checking out the mirror in the back of the store.

Using Nook Miles at the Nook Stop

The Nook Stop is a machine located in Resident Services that allows you to redeem Nook Miles for various items, including hairstyles. To access the Nook Stop, simply walk up to the machine and interact with it. From there, you can browse the available hairstyles and purchase them using your Nook Miles.

Scanning Amiibo

If you have amiibo cards or figures, you can use them to invite new villagers to your island. Some amiibo cards also allow you to invite characters with unique hairstyles. Simply scan the amiibo using the Animal Crossing app on your Nintendo Switch, and follow the prompts to invite the character to your island. Once they're on your island, you can visit their house and use their mirror to try out their hairstyle.

Step by Step: How to Get New Animal Crossing Hairstyles

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting new hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

  1. Upgrade Resident Services to a building
  2. Have Mabel visit your island at least once
  3. Wait for the Able Sisters shop to open
  4. Visit the Able Sisters shop and check out the mirror in the back
  5. Purchase any new hairstyles that you like
  6. Redeem Nook Miles at the Nook Stop in Resident Services
  7. Browse the available hairstyles and purchase any that you like using your Nook Miles
  8. Scan an amiibo card or figure using the Animal Crossing app on your Nintendo Switch
  9. Invite the character to your island
  10. Visit their house and use their mirror to try out their hairstyle

Tips for Getting New Animal Crossing Hairstyles

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

  • Check the Able Sisters shop regularly for new hairstyles
  • Save up your Nook Miles so you can purchase multiple hairstyles at once
  • Scan amiibo cards to invite characters with unique hairstyles
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find one that you like

FAQ: Getting New Animal Crossing Hairstyles

Can you get new hairstyles for free?

No, all new hairstyles must be purchased using Bells or Nook Miles.

Can you get new hairstyles without having upgraded Resident Services?

No, the Able Sisters shop and Nook Stop are both located in Resident Services, so you'll need to have upgraded the building to access them.

Can you get new hairstyles by visiting other players' islands?

No, hairstyles are tied to your character and cannot be obtained by visiting other players' islands.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Once you have a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing, you'll want to take care of it to keep it looking fresh. Here are a few tips for hair care in Animal Crossing:

  • Visit Harriet at Shampoodle to get your hair styled or colored
  • Use a mirror to change your hairstyle whenever you want
  • Avoid getting stung by bees, as this will mess up your hairstyle

Now that you know how to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing, you can switch up your look whenever you want. Happy gaming!
