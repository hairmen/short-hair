Does COVID Stay on Hair? | Hair Care Tips - 2023

The world has been grappling with the COVID-19 pandemic for over two years now, and there is still much to learn about the virus. One of the questions that people have been asking is whether COVID-19 can stay on hair. In this blog post, we will explore this question and provide some helpful hair care tips in the process.

Table of Contents

  • How to Reduce the Risk of COVID-19 on Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Washing Your Hair During COVID-19
  • Hair Care Tips During COVID-19
  • FAQs About COVID-19 and Hair

How to Reduce the Risk of COVID-19 on Hair

Recent studies have shown that COVID-19 can remain on surfaces for hours to days, depending on the type of surface and environmental conditions. The virus can also be transmitted through direct contact with an infected person or by touching contaminated surfaces and then touching your face, including your hair.

To reduce the risk of COVID-19 on hair, it is important to practice good hygiene and follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and warm water.
  • Avoid touching your hair or face with unwashed hands.
  • Wear a mask when in public or around others who are not part of your household.
  • Avoid sharing combs, brushes, or other hair accessories.

Step-by-Step Guide to Washing Your Hair During COVID-19

Washing your hair regularly is one of the most effective ways to reduce the risk of COVID-19 on hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you wash your hair properly:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  2. Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage gently for at least 20 seconds.
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  4. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave on for a few minutes.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  6. Pat your hair dry with a clean towel.

Hair Care Tips During COVID-19

In addition to washing your hair regularly, there are other hair care tips you can follow during COVID-19:

  • Avoid using hair dryers in public restrooms or other shared spaces.
  • Use a hair tie or headband to keep your hair away from your face and mask.
  • Avoid using hair styling products that require frequent touching of your hair.
  • Consider wearing a hat or scarf to cover your hair when in public.

FAQs About COVID-19 and Hair

Can COVID-19 be transmitted through hair?

While there is no definitive answer to this question, it is possible for COVID-19 to be transmitted through hair if the hair comes into contact with the virus and then touches the face.

How long can COVID-19 stay on hair?

The length of time that COVID-19 can stay on hair is still unknown. However, recent studies suggest that the virus can remain on surfaces for up to several days.

Do I need to wash my hair every day during COVID-19?

While it is important to wash your hair regularly during COVID-19, you do not need to wash your hair every day. Washing your hair every other day or every few days should be sufficient.

Can I use hair styling products during COVID-19?

Yes, you can use hair styling products during COVID-19, but it is important to avoid products that require frequent touching of your hair.

Can I share hair accessories with others during COVID-19?

No, it is not recommended to share hair accessories with others during COVID-19 to reduce the risk of transmission.


By following these hair care tips and practicing good hygiene, you can reduce the risk of COVID-19 on your hair and stay healthy during these challenging times.
