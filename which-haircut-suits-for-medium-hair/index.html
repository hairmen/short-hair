Which Haircut Suits For Medium Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have medium length hair, you may be wondering what haircut would suit you best. Medium length hair can be versatile and adaptable, making it easy to switch up your look with a new cut. In this article, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for medium length hair, as well as some tips for taking care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for medium length hair, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some general guidelines:

Face Shape

The shape of your face can help determine which haircut will be most flattering. Generally speaking, the goal is to create balance and symmetry. Here are some suggestions:

  • Round face: A layered cut with side-swept bangs can help elongate the face.
  • Square face: Soft, layered cuts can help soften the jawline.
  • Heart-shaped face: Shoulder-length cuts with layers can help balance out a wider forehead.
  • Oval face: Lucky you! Almost any haircut will suit an oval face.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also help determine which haircut will work best. Here are some ideas:

  • Straight hair: A blunt cut or a long bob can look sleek and sophisticated.
  • Wavy hair: A layered cut can help enhance the texture of your hair.
  • Curly hair: Consider a layered cut with long layers to help control frizz.

Personal Style

Of course, your personal style is also an important factor in choosing a haircut. Consider whether you prefer a low-maintenance style or are willing to put in more effort to achieve a certain look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut

Once you've decided on a haircut, it's time to head to the salon. Here's what to expect:

  1. Consultation: Your stylist will discuss your desired haircut and make suggestions based on your hair type and face shape.
  2. Washing: Your hair will be washed and conditioned.
  3. Cutting: Your stylist will cut your hair according to the desired style.
  4. Drying and styling: Your hair will be blow-dried and styled to finish the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have a new haircut, here are some tips for keeping it looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in quality hair products to help maintain your style.
  • Consider using heat protectant products when using hot tools.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to switch up your look.

FAQ

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows, but generally every 6-8 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Can I still style my hair if I have a low-maintenance cut?

Yes! Even low-maintenance cuts can be styled in different ways. Talk to your stylist about different styling options.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Finally, here are some general tips for taking care of medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than every other day to prevent stripping natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle wet hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and ideas, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect haircut for your medium length hair.
