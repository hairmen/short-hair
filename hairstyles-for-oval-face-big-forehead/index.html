Hairstyles For Oval Face Big Forehead

Written by Robby Sep 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we all know, having an oval face with a big forehead can be a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. You want to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and minimizes the appearance of your forehead. In this blog post, we will be discussing different hairstyles for oval face big forehead and providing tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

An oval face with a big forehead is characterized by having a forehead that is wider than the cheekbones and jawline. This face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile face shapes, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, it can be a challenge to find a hairstyle that balances the proportions of the face.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face with a big forehead, it is important to consider the following:

  • Find a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face to balance out your forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight to the scalp, as this can emphasize the size of your forehead.
  • Consider bangs as a way to cover up your forehead and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Opt for hairstyles that have layers or texture, as this can add dimension to your face and distract from your forehead.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some popular hairstyles for oval face big forehead:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left, right, and back.
  3. Starting with the back section, use a 1-inch curling iron to curl your hair away from your face.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the left and right sections of your hair.
  5. Take the top section of your hair and tease the crown to add volume.
  6. Smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased section and spray with hairspray to hold.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into three parts: top, left, and right.
  3. Take the top section of your hair and tease the crown to add volume.
  4. Smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased section and spray with hairspray to hold.
  5. Blow-dry the left and right sections of your hair with a round brush, curling the ends away from your face.
  6. Using a flat iron, straighten your bangs and sweep them to the side.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for your hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oval face big forehead:

What are some hairstyles to avoid?

Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight to the scalp, as this can emphasize the size of your forehead. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as this can make your face appear longer.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part?

A middle part can work for an oval face with a big forehead, but make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out your forehead.

What are some hairstyles that work well with bangs?

Side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and wispy bangs are all great options for an oval face with a big forehead.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful locks. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly is important to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. However, over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dry, brittle hair. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Use a heat protectant spray

Using hot styling tools can damage your hair and lead to split ends and breakage. To protect your hair from heat damage, use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

Get regular trims

Getting regular trims is important to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as needed.

Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Look for a treatment that is specifically formulated for your hair type and apply it to your hair for 5-10 minutes before rinsing it out.

Avoid using too much product

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use only the amount of product you need to achieve your desired style.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for an oval face with a big forehead can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and minimizes the appearance of your forehead. Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using a heat protectant spray, getting regular trims, using a deep conditioning treatment, and avoiding using too much product.
