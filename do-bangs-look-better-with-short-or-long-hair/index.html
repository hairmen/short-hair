Do Bangs Look Better With Short Or Long Hair

Written by Daniel Nov 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but not sure if they will look better with short or long hair? This is a common question that many people have when considering bangs. In this article, we will explore the different factors that can impact whether bangs look better with short or long hair, as well as provide tips for taking care of your hair once you have bangs.

Factors that Impact Whether Bangs Look Better with Short or Long Hair

There are several factors that can impact whether bangs look better with short or long hair, including:

Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in determining whether bangs will look good on you. For example, if you have a round face, blunt bangs may not be the best option as they can make your face look even rounder. Instead, side-swept bangs may be a better choice as they can help to elongate your face.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also impact whether bangs look better with short or long hair. If you have thick, curly hair, blunt bangs may be difficult to style and may end up looking too heavy. In this case, longer, wispy bangs may be a better option. If you have fine, straight hair, blunt bangs may be a good choice as they can add volume and texture to your hair.

Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when deciding whether bangs look better with short or long hair. If you prefer a more classic, polished look, blunt bangs may be a good choice. If you prefer a more relaxed, bohemian style, longer, wispy bangs may be a better option.

How to Decide if Bangs are Right for You

Before you decide whether bangs look better with short or long hair, there are a few things you should consider:

  • Face Shape: Consider your face shape and whether bangs will complement it.
  • Hair Texture: Think about the texture of your hair and whether it will work well with bangs.
  • Personal Style: Consider your personal style and whether bangs will fit in with your overall look.
  • Maintenance: Keep in mind that bangs require regular trimming and styling, so make sure you are willing to commit to the upkeep.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Bangs

If you have decided that bangs are right for you, here is a step-by-step guide to getting them:

  1. Choose the type of bangs you want (blunt, side-swept, wispy, etc.)
  2. Decide on the length of your bangs (short, long, etc.)
  3. Find a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting bangs
  4. Bring pictures of the type of bangs you want to show your hairstylist
  5. Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the type and length of bangs you want
  6. Trust your hairstylist's professional opinion if they suggest adjustments to the length or style of your bangs
  7. Follow your hairstylist's instructions for styling and maintaining your new bangs

Tips for Taking Care of Bangs

Once you have bangs, there are a few things you can do to keep them looking their best:

  • Trim Regularly: Bangs require regular trimming to maintain their shape and length. Plan to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks.
  • Blow Dry: Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay using a round brush. This will help to smooth them out and give them shape.
  • Use Dry Shampoo: If your bangs tend to get oily, use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep them looking fresh.

FAQs about Bangs and Hair Length

Can you have bangs with long hair?

Yes, you can definitely have bangs with long hair. In fact, longer hair can make bangs look even more dramatic and eye-catching.

Do bangs look better with short or long hair?

As we discussed earlier, there are several factors that can impact whether bangs look better with short or long hair. Ultimately, the decision comes down to your personal style and preferences.

What type of bangs are best for a round face?

Side-swept bangs are typically the best option for a round face as they can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash regularly: Keep your bangs clean by washing them every other day.
  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner: Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style your bangs. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair: Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools, and wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

In conclusion, whether bangs look better with short or long hair depends on several factors, including face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Ultimately, the decision comes down to your own preferences and what you feel most comfortable with. If you do decide to get bangs, make sure to communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the type and length of bangs you want, and follow our tips for taking care of your hair and bangs.
