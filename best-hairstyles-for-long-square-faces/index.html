Best Hairstyles For Long Square Faces

Written by Bardi Jun 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long square face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. Luckily, there are many great hairstyles that work well with long square faces. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for long square faces, as well as provide tips and tricks for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long square face is characterized by a strong jawline and a straight forehead. The length of the face is longer than the width, which can make it difficult to find the right hairstyle. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a long square face is to soften the angles of the face and create a more rounded appearance. This can be achieved by choosing hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of the face.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a long square face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a style that adds volume to the sides of the face. This can be achieved with layers, curls, or waves. Second, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too straight, as these can make your face look longer and more angular. Third, you want to consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a style.

Step by step

1. Start by determining your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face, and compare the two measurements. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long square face.

2. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved with layers, curls, or waves.

3. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too straight, as these can make your face look longer and more angular.

4. Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a style. If you have thin hair, you may want to choose a style that adds volume. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a style that is more streamlined.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing the best hairstyle for your long square face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can accentuate the squareness of your face.
  • Choose a style that frames your face, such as a layered bob or a side-swept fringe.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long square face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long square face. However, you may want to add some volume to the sides of your face with layers or waves.

Q: What length should my hair be if I have a long square face?

A: The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference and your hair texture. However, it is generally recommended to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.

Hair Care

When caring for your hair, it is important to use products that are suited to your hair type and texture. If you have thin hair, use volumizing products to add body and lift. If you have thick hair, use smoothing products to tame frizz and flyaways.

It is also important to use heat protectant products when using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners. This will help prevent damage to your hair.

In conclusion, there are many great hairstyles for long square faces. By choosing a style that adds volume to the sides of your face and frames your features, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a more rounded appearance. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair with the right products and techniques.
