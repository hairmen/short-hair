How To Cut Your Own Hair With No Guards

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your own hair, learning how to cut your own hair with no guards can be a game-changer. With a little bit of practice and some patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll go through the steps, tips, and hair care advice to help you get started.

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair with No Guards
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • FAQs

How to Cut Your Own Hair with No Guards

Before we start, it's important to note that cutting your own hair with no guards can be challenging, especially if you've never done it before. We recommend starting with small adjustments and gradually increasing the length as you get more comfortable. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools

Make sure you have the right tools to cut your hair, such as sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. You'll also need a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp during the process.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Decide on the style you want to achieve and visualize the finished look. It's important to have a clear idea of what you want before you start cutting your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, section your hair into manageable parts. Start with the bottom layer of your hair and work your way up. This will ensure that you cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Hold your hair between your fingers and cut it in a straight line. Make sure you don't cut too much hair at once, as it can result in uneven cuts.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure you're cutting your hair evenly. Take your time and don't rush the process.

Step 6: Finishing Touches

Once you've cut your hair, use the comb to blend the layers and make sure everything looks even. If you need to make any adjustments, repeat the previous steps until you're satisfied with the results.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps to follow when cutting your own hair with no guards:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's clean and free from any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Step 3: Dampen Your Hair

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Step 6: Finishing Touches

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Cutting your own hair with no guards can be challenging, but with these tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut:

  • Start with small adjustments and gradually increase the length as you get more comfortable.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can result in uneven cuts.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Visualize the finished look before you start cutting your hair.
  • Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure you're cutting your hair evenly.

FAQs

Hair Care After Your Haircut

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the results. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

Cutting your own hair with no guards can be challenging, but with practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to get started.
