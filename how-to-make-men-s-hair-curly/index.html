How To Make Men s Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Curly hair is not just for women, and men with straight hair may want to switch things up with a curly hairstyle. Fortunately, there are ways to make men's hair curly without having to use heat or chemicals. In this article, we will discuss how to make men's hair curly naturally.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make Men's Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Men's Hair Curly:

Before we get started, it is important to note that not all hair types can be made curly. If you have naturally curly hair or coarse hair, these methods may not work for you. However, if you have straight or wavy hair, you can try the following methods to achieve curly hair:

  • Roller Set
  • Braid Out
  • Twist Out

Step-by-Step Guide:

Roller Set:

A roller set is a great way to achieve natural-looking curls without the use of heat or chemicals. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Apply a styling product of your choice to each section.
  4. Roll each section onto a roller, starting from the ends and rolling towards the scalp.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, remove the rollers and fluff your curls with your fingers.

Braid Out:

A braid out is a simple technique that can create loose, bouncy curls. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Apply a styling product of your choice to each section.
  4. Braid each section, starting from the roots and braiding all the way to the ends.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the braids and fluff your curls with your fingers.

Twist Out:

A twist out is similar to a braid out, but involves twisting sections of hair instead of braiding. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Apply a styling product of your choice to each section.
  4. Twist each section, starting from the roots and twisting all the way to the ends.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, unravel the twists and fluff your curls with your fingers.

Tips for Curly Hair:

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you want to maintain curly hair:

  • Avoid using shampoo too often, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQs:

Can men's hair be naturally curly?

Yes, some men have naturally curly hair. However, if your hair is straight or wavy, you can try the methods mentioned above to achieve curly hair.

What products can I use to maintain curly hair?

You can use a styling product such as a curl defining cream or gel to maintain curly hair. Additionally, you can use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should avoid washing curly hair too often, as it can dry out your hair. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Curly Hair:

Curly hair requires a bit more maintenance than straight hair. Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the water.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal parts, as they can damage your hair. Use a scrunchie or a hair tie made of fabric instead.

Now that you know how to make men's hair curly, you can switch up your hairstyle and try something new. Remember to take care of your curly hair by following the tips mentioned above.
