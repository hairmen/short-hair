How To Iron Hair With Volume Achieving Sleek And Bouncy Locks

Written by Daniel Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve sleek, bouncy locks with volume, then you're in the right place. In this article, we will discuss how to iron hair with volume using simple steps and tips. By the end of this article, you'll have the knowledge and confidence to achieve the perfect hairdo that will turn heads.

How to Iron Hair with Volume

Flat irons are popular styling tools that can help straighten and smoothen your hair. While they are commonly used to create sleek and straight hairstyles, they can also be used to create voluminous hairstyles. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure to wash and condition it properly. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and bounce to your locks. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before you start ironing your hair, it's essential to apply a heat protectant to prevent damage. Heat protectants create a barrier between your hair and the heat, reducing the risk of breakage and split ends. Apply a heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will help you work on one section at a time, ensuring that you don't miss any spots. Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up to the top.

Step 4: Iron Your Hair

Take a small section of your hair and clamp it between the flat iron plates. Slowly glide the flat iron down your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Repeat this process for each section of your hair, making sure to keep the flat iron moving smoothly and steadily.

Step 5: Add Volume

To add volume to your hair, use a round brush to lift your hair up at the roots while blow-drying. This will create a bouncy effect and add volume to your hair. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to add extra volume and hold.

Tips for Ironing Hair with Volume

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect voluminous hairstyle:

  • Use a flat iron with adjustable heat settings to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of your natural part to add volume to your roots.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Don't forget to brush your hair gently to avoid breakage and split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you flat iron hair with volume?

Yes, you can flat iron hair with volume. By using a flat iron and a round brush, you can create a bouncy and voluminous hairstyle.

How do you add volume to flat ironed hair?

You can add volume to flat ironed hair by using a round brush to lift your hair up at the roots while blow-drying. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to add extra volume and hold.

What is the best heat protectant for flat ironing hair?

The best heat protectant for flat ironing hair is one that is lightweight and easy to apply. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter, which can help protect your hair from heat damage.

Hair Care for Voluminous Hair

To keep your hair healthy and voluminous, it's essential to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and bounce to your locks.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect voluminous hairstyle that will turn heads everywhere you go.
