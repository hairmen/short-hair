How To Find The Best Haircuts For Oval Faces In 2023

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect haircut that flatters your oval face shape? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll be discussing the best haircuts for oval faces, how to choose the right one, and how to care for your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents

  • How to Find the Best Haircuts for Oval Faces
  • The Best Haircuts for Oval Faces
  • Tips for Styling Oval Face Haircuts
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Oval Faces

How to Find the Best Haircuts for Oval Faces

When it comes to finding the best haircut for your oval face shape, it’s important to consider your hair texture and personal style.

A great place to start is by looking at celebrities with similar face shapes to yours and seeing what haircuts they have. This can give you inspiration for what you may like and what may look best on you.

Another option is to consult with a hairstylist who can provide recommendations based on your face shape and hair type. They can also suggest ways to modify certain haircuts to better suit your individual features.

The Best Haircuts for Oval Faces

Now that you know how to find the best haircut, let’s take a look at some of the most flattering haircuts for oval faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are great for oval faces because they add volume and movement without disrupting the natural balance of your features. They can also help to elongate the face for a more flattering look.

2. Blunt Bob

A blunt bob can be a great choice for oval faces because it draws attention to your cheekbones and jawline. This haircut is also versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

3. Pixie Cut

For those with short hair, a pixie cut can be a great option for oval faces. This haircut highlights your facial features and adds a touch of edginess to your look.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to soften the forehead and create a more balanced look for oval faces. They can also highlight your eyes and cheekbones.

Tips for Styling Oval Face Haircuts

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut, it’s important to know how to style it to flatter your face shape:

  • Use a round brush to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Try different parting styles to see what works best for your face shape.
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to create different looks.

FAQ

What if I don’t like the recommended haircuts for oval faces?

Ultimately, the best haircut is one that you feel confident and comfortable with. If you don’t like the recommended haircuts, try to modify them or look for other options that work for you.

Can oval faces pull off short haircuts?

Absolutely! Short haircuts like a pixie cut can be a great choice for oval faces. However, it’s important to consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a haircut.

Hair Care for Oval Faces

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy.

With these tips and recommendations, you can find the perfect haircut for your oval face shape and style it to perfection. Happy hairstyling!
