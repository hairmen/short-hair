How to Flatten Hair on Top of Head: Tips and Tricks

Are you tired of always having hair that sticks up on top of your head? Do you want to know how to get that sleek, flattened look? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks on how to flatten hair on top of your head.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Flatten Hair on Top of Head
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Flattening Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Flattened Hair

How to Flatten Hair on Top of Head

Before we dive into the steps, it’s important to note that everyone’s hair is different. What works for one person may not work for another. However, these steps should give you a general idea of what to do.

The first step is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You don’t want to try flattening dirty or wet hair, as it won’t work as well. If you need to wash your hair, make sure to use a conditioner that will help smooth out your hair.

Next, you’ll want to use a hair straightener. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the straightener, as the heat can damage your hair. You’ll want to straighten your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Once you’ve straightened your hair, you can use a hair serum or oil to help keep your hair flat. Just make sure to only use a small amount, as too much can weigh your hair down.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to flattening hair on top of your head:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, making sure to use a conditioner that will help smooth out your hair.
  2. Dry your hair completely.
  3. Use a heat protectant spray.
  4. Straighten your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  5. Use a hair serum or oil to help keep your hair flat.

Tips for Flattening Hair

Here are some additional tips for flattening hair on top of your head:

  • Use a hair tie to pull your hair back while you’re straightening the bottom sections.
  • Use a hair clip to section off your hair while you’re straightening.
  • Don’t use too much hair serum or oil, as it can weigh your hair down.
  • If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may need to use a stronger hold product to keep your hair flat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about flattening hair on top of your head:

Can I use a hair dryer instead of a straightener?

While a hair dryer can help flatten your hair, it won’t work as well as a straightener. A straightener will give you a sleek, flat look.

How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go a few days without washing their hair, while others need to wash their hair every day.

What type of hair serum or oil should I use?

You’ll want to use a serum or oil that is specifically designed for smoothing and flattening hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil.

Hair Care for Flattened Hair

Once you’ve flattened your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking sleek and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as it can weigh it down.
  • Try using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Now you know how to flatten hair on top of your head! With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve a sleek, flat look that lasts all day.
