Best Pixie Haircut For Square Face A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Pixie haircut is a perfect hairstyle for women who want to keep their hair short and stylish. However, finding the right pixie haircut for your face shape can be challenging. In this article, we'll be discussing the best pixie haircut for square face, how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to Get the Best Pixie Haircut for Square Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircut

Description:

If you have a square face, you should consider getting a pixie haircut that will soften your strong jawline and highlight your cheekbones. The best pixie haircut for square face should be slightly longer on the top and shorter on the sides to create a balanced look. This hairstyle will also add volume to the crown of your head, making your face appear longer and slimmer.

How to Get the Best Pixie Haircut for Square Face:

The first step to getting the best pixie haircut for square face is to find a professional hair stylist who has experience in cutting short hair. Discuss the type of pixie haircut you want and show them a picture of the style you have in mind. Your hairstylist will then assess your face shape and suggest the best style that will suit your facial features.

Step by Step Guide:

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the best pixie haircut for square face:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Separate the top of your hair from the sides and back using clips.
  3. Start cutting the sides of your hair with a clipper, leaving about an inch of hair on top.
  4. Gradually blend the sides with the top using scissors.
  5. Cut the top of your hair to your desired length, leaving it slightly longer than the sides.
  6. Use a razor to add texture to your hair, giving it a softer and more natural look.
  7. Finish the look by styling your hair with a texturizing spray or wax.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut:

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your hair looking clean and fresh between washes.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best pixie haircut for square face:

What is the best length for a pixie haircut for square face?

The best length for a pixie haircut for square face is slightly longer on the top and shorter on the sides.

Will a pixie haircut make my face look too round?

No, a pixie haircut can actually make your face look longer and slimmer by adding volume to the crown of your head.

Is a pixie haircut high maintenance?

A pixie haircut requires regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape. However, it is low maintenance when it comes to styling as you can simply use a texturizing spray or wax to create a tousled look.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircut:

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your pixie haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can cause heat damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or gel as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Cover your hair with a scarf or hat when going out in the sun to protect it from UV rays.

In conclusion, the best pixie haircut for square face is a great hairstyle for women who want to keep their hair short and stylish. It's important to find a professional hair stylist who has experience in cutting short hair and discuss the type of pixie haircut you want. Remember to maintain your hairstyle by visiting your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim and using the right hair care products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
