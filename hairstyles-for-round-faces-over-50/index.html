Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face and you’re over 50, you may be wondering what hairstyles will suit you best. In this guide, we’ll be discussing the best hairstyles for round faces over 50, how to style them, and how to care for your hair. So whether you’re looking for a short or long style, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

FAQ | How To | Step By Step | Tips | Hair Care

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for round faces over 50?

The best hairstyles for round faces over 50 are those that create the illusion of length and height. This includes short pixie cuts, long bobs, and layered hairstyles.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for my round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs and straight bangs. These styles will only make your face look rounder.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to add layers and volume to create the illusion of length and height.

How To

Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is an excellent choice for those with round faces over 50. This style creates the illusion of length and height, making your face appear slimmer. To style, use a small amount of hair wax or gel to create a messy, textured look.

Long Bob

A long bob, or “lob,” is a versatile style that works well for round faces over 50. To style, use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to the roots. You can also add layers to the ends to create movement and texture.

Layered Hairstyle

A layered hairstyle is another excellent choice for those with round faces over 50. Layers create the illusion of length and height, making your face appear slimmer. To style, use a curling iron to create loose waves, or use a straightening iron for a sleek look.

Step By Step

Short Pixie Cut

1. Start with clean, dry hair.
2. Apply a small amount of hair wax or gel to your fingers.
3. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.
4. Use your fingers to create a messy, textured look.

Long Bob

1. Start with clean, dry hair.
2. Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to the roots.
3. Use a straightening iron to smooth the ends and create a sleek look.
4. Add layers to the ends to create movement and texture.

Layered Hairstyle

1. Start with clean, dry hair.
2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
3. Apply a small amount of hair wax or gel to your fingers.
4. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.
5. Use your fingers to create a messy, textured look.

Tips

- Add layers to create the illusion of length and height.
- Avoid blunt bobs and straight bangs.
- Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to the roots.
- Use hair wax or gel to create a messy, textured look.
- Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
- Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
- Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling or straightening iron.
- Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
- Invest in a good quality hair mask to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for round faces over 50, how to style them, and how to care for your hair, you can confidently choose a style that works for you. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair, and always keep it healthy and well-maintained.
