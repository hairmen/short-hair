Are you tired of your current hairstyle in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp? Do you want to change it up and try something new? Look no further! In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to change your hair in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp.

Description

Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is a popular mobile game where players create and customize their own character, including their hairstyle. Changing your hairstyle can be a fun way to refresh your character's look and express your personal style.

How to

1. Open Animal Crossing Pocket Camp on your mobile device.

2. Tap on the "More" button located in the bottom right corner of the screen.

3. Select the "Settings" option from the menu.

4. Scroll down until you see the "Edit Character" button and tap it.

5. Tap on the "Hair" option.

6. Select the hairstyle you want from the available options.

7. Tap the "Save" button to apply your new hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I change my character's hair multiple times?

A: Yes, you can change your character's hair as many times as you like.

Q: Do I have to pay for new hairstyles?

A: No, all hairstyles are included in the game and can be changed for free.
