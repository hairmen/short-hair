Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle A Timeless Look

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic hairstyle that exudes sophistication and style, then the mens slicked back hairstyle is the perfect choice for you. This timeless look has been around for decades and is still as popular today as it was in the past. In this blog post, we'll share with you everything you need to know about this stylish hairdo, including how to achieve it, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle

Description

The mens slicked back hairstyle is characterized by hair that is combed back and held in place with either hair gel or pomade. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a clean and polished look that can be easily achieved with minimal effort. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of settings, from the office to a night out on the town.

How to Achieve Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle

The first step to achieving the mens slicked back hairstyle is to make sure you have the right hair length. Ideally, your hair should be at least 3 inches long on top and shorter on the sides and back. Once you have the right hair length, you'll need to invest in a good quality hair gel or pomade. These products will help you achieve the slicked-back look and keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the mens slicked back hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not completely dry.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up towards the top of your head.
  4. Using a comb, comb your hair back from your forehead towards the crown of your head.
  5. Continue combing your hair back until it's all slicked back and in place.
  6. Finish off the look by using a hair dryer on a low setting to dry your hair and set it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle

To maintain the mens slicked back hairstyle, there are a few tips you should keep in mind:

  • Invest in a good quality hair gel or pomade that will hold your hair in place all day.
  • Use a comb to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause the style to come undone.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and polished.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I achieve the mens slicked back hairstyle with short hair?

A: While it's easier to achieve the mens slicked back hairstyle with longer hair, it's still possible to achieve this look with shorter hair. You'll just need to use more hair gel or pomade to hold your hair in place.

Q: Can I achieve the mens slicked back hairstyle without using hair gel or pomade?

A: While it's possible to achieve the mens slicked back hairstyle without using hair gel or pomade, it's much harder to maintain the style without these products. Hair gel or pomade will help keep your hair in place all day and give it a polished look.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Slicked Back Hairstyle

Maintaining healthy hair is key to achieving the mens slicked back hairstyle. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the mens slicked back hairstyle with ease. Whether you're headed to the office or out for a night on the town, this timeless hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
