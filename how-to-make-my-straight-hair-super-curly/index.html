How To Make Your Straight Hair Super Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some curls to your look? You've come to the right place! In this article, we will guide you through the process of making your straight hair super curly. We'll cover everything from how to prep your hair to the best products and techniques for achieving bouncy, voluminous curls.

Table of Contents

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Tools You'll Need
  • Curling Techniques
  • Products to Use
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's essential to prep it properly. This will ensure that your curls last longer and look more defined. Here are some steps you should follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to add moisture and definition to your curls. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage and ensure that your curls stay healthy. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Once you've applied the heat protectant, it's time to dry your hair. You can either air dry your hair or use a blow dryer. If you're using a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.

Tools You'll Need

To achieve super curly hair, you'll need the following tools:

  • Curling iron
  • Curling wand
  • Hot rollers
  • Bobby pins
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray

Curling Techniques

Now that you have your tools ready let's get into the curling techniques that will give you super curly hair:

Technique 1: Curling Iron

Start by sectioning your hair and clipping it up. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Technique 2: Curling Wand

Similar to the curling iron, take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand. Hold the wand in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Technique 3: Hot Rollers

Start by sectioning your hair and rolling each section with a hot roller. Leave the rollers in place for 10-15 minutes, then remove them. Once all of your hair is curled, run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.

Products to Use

Using the right products can make a significant difference in achieving super curly hair. Here are some products you should consider:

  • Curl Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Heat Protectant Spray
  • Curl Enhancing Cream
  • Hairspray

Hair Care Tips

Maintaining your curls is just as important as creating them. Here are some hair care tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

FAQs

Q: How long will my super curly hair last?

It depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, super curly hair can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Q: Can I use a straightener to curl my hair?

Yes, you can use a straightener to create curls, but it's not the most efficient method. Curling irons and wands are specifically designed to create curls, so they're more effective.

Q: Do I need to use hairspray after curling my hair?

Using hairspray after curling your hair can help your curls last longer and look more defined. However, it's not necessary if you prefer a more natural look.

Q: How often should I wash my super curly hair?

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, so it's best to wash it every 2-3 days. However, if your hair feels oily or dirty, you can wash it more frequently.

With these tips and techniques, you can now make your straight hair super curly. Remember to prep your hair properly, use the right tools and products, and take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Happy curling!
