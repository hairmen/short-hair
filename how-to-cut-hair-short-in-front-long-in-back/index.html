How To Cut Hair Short In Front Long In Back

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and try something new? One popular trend is cutting your hair short in the front and long in the back. This style is also known as the “mullet” and can be seen on many celebrities and fashion icons.

Description

The short in front, long in back hairstyle is a versatile and edgy look that can be customized to suit your individual style. The front layers are typically cut to chin or shoulder length, while the back layers can range from just below the shoulders to the mid-back.

How to Cut Hair Short in Front Long in Back

To achieve this hairstyle, you will need a few essential tools: scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It is also recommended to have a friend or family member assist you with the cutting process.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the Front Section

Begin by cutting the front section of your hair to your desired length. Use the comb to create a straight line and then cut the hair with the scissors. It is important to cut the hair slightly longer than your desired length, as you can always go back and trim later.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Next, move on to the back section of your hair. Start by combing the hair straight down and then cut the hair to the desired length. It is important to keep the layers long in the back to achieve the mullet style.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once the front and back sections are cut, it’s time to blend the layers. Use the scissors to create angled cuts, blending the front and back sections together. Make sure to blend the layers evenly on both sides.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and gradually increase the length to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair and make sure the layers are even.
  • Consider adding some texture to the hair with a razor or thinning shears.
  • If you’re unsure about cutting your hair yourself, consider visiting a professional stylist.

FAQ

Is the short in front, long in back hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I trim my hair to maintain the style?

It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

Can I color my hair with this style?

Yes, you can add color to your hair with this style. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to accentuate the layers.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant, it’s important to follow a proper hair care routine. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot styling tools too frequently. Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know how to cut hair short in front long in back, it’s time to try out this edgy and versatile hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a bold and unique look that expresses your individual style.
