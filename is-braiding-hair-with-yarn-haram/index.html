Is Braiding Hair With Yarn Haram

Written by Christine Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to experiment with different hairstyles, you might have come across the trend of braiding hair with yarn. This technique involves using yarn instead of traditional braiding hair to create unique, colorful braids. However, as a Muslim, you may be wondering if this practice is considered haram or not. In this article, we'll explore this topic in detail.

Description

Braiding hair with yarn has become increasingly popular in recent years, especially among those who love to experiment with different hairstyles. This technique involves using yarn, which is wrapped around small sections of hair, and then braided together. The result is a colorful, unique hairstyle that can be customized to suit your preferences.

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to braid hair with yarn:

Step 1: Choose the Right Yarn

The first step is to choose the right type of yarn. Make sure it's soft and not too thick, as it can be difficult to braid with. You can also choose different colors to create a unique look.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into small parts and tie them with a rubber band. This will make it easier to wrap the yarn around each section.

Step 3: Wrap the Yarn

Take a piece of yarn and wrap it around the section of hair, making sure it's tight enough to hold the hair in place. You can wrap the yarn around the section a few times to make it more secure.

Step 4: Braid the Hair

Once you've wrapped the yarn around each section of hair, you can start braiding them together. Make sure you braid tightly to ensure that the yarn stays in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when braiding hair with yarn:

  • Use soft, thin yarn to make it easier to braid.
  • Wrap the yarn tightly around each section of hair to ensure it stays in place.
  • Braid tightly to prevent the yarn from unraveling.
  • Experiment with different colors to create a unique look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about braiding hair with yarn:

Is braiding hair with yarn haram?

There is no clear answer to this question, as it depends on the interpretation of Islamic teachings. Some Muslims believe that any modification to the hair that involves a foreign object is haram, while others believe that it is permissible as long as it is not intended to imitate non-Muslims.

Can braiding hair with yarn damage your hair?

If done improperly, braiding hair with yarn can cause damage to your hair. Make sure to wrap the yarn tightly around each section of hair to prevent it from pulling on your scalp.

How long does braiding hair with yarn last?

Braiding hair with yarn can last for several weeks, depending on how well you take care of it. Make sure to moisturize your scalp and hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when braiding it with yarn:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a satin or silk scarf to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Don't leave the braids in for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

Overall, braiding hair with yarn can be a fun and unique way to experiment with your hairstyle. While there is no clear answer on whether it is considered haram or not, it is important to take care of your hair and scalp to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair.
