Short Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair And Round Face A Perfect Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face and thick wavy hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task. But, don't worry! We have got you covered with some amazing short hairstyles that will suit your face shape and hair texture.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face has soft features, and the width and length are almost the same. Thick wavy hair can be a blessing, but it can also be challenging to manage. Short hairstyles work great for round faces as they add length to the face and make it appear slimmer. The right short hairstyle can balance out the roundness of the face and enhance your features.

How to

To find the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and thick wavy hair, consider the following:

  • Face shape
  • Hair texture
  • Hair length
  • Personal style

Step by step

Here are some short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces and thick wavy hair:

1. Bob

A bob is a classic short hairstyle that works great for round faces. It adds length to the face and frames the jawline. If you have thick wavy hair, a layered bob will add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Pixie cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy short hairstyle that can work well for round faces. It brings attention to the eyes and cheekbones, making the face appear longer. If you have thick wavy hair, a textured pixie cut will look great on you.

3. Shaggy lob

A shaggy lob is a longer version of a bob that falls just above the shoulders. It has layers and texture, making it perfect for thick wavy hair. A shaggy lob can balance out the roundness of the face and add dimension to your hair.

4. Asymmetrical bob

An asymmetrical bob is a modern and trendy short hairstyle that can work well for round faces. It has a longer length on one side, which adds length to the face. If you have thick wavy hair, an asymmetrical bob can add texture and volume to your hair.

Tips

  • Consult a hairstylist who has experience with short hairstyles and understands your face shape and hair texture.
  • Invest in good quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.
  • Avoid heavy bangs as they can make your face appear rounder.

FAQ

1. Can I get a short hairstyle if I have a round face?

Yes, you can. Short hairstyles can work great for round faces as long as they add length to the face and balance out the roundness.

2. Will a short hairstyle work for my thick wavy hair?

Yes, it will. Short hairstyles can add texture and volume to thick wavy hair, making it look more manageable and stylish.

Hair Care

To maintain your short hairstyle for thick wavy hair and round face, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you have some amazing short hairstyles to choose from, go ahead and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one that suits your face shape and hair texture. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
