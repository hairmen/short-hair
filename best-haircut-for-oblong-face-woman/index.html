Best Haircut For Oblong Face Woman The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Welcome to our ultimate guide for the best haircut for oblong face woman in 2023. In this article, we will discuss the perfect haircut that will flatter your oblong face shape. We will also provide you with some tips on how to style and maintain your hair. So, if you’re ready to transform your look, keep reading!

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead and a long jawline. If you have an oblong face shape, you may find it challenging to find the perfect haircut that will flatter your face shape. The goal is to create an illusion of width and fullness to balance out the length of your face.

How to Determine an Oblong Face Shape

You can determine if you have an oblong face shape by measuring your face. Measure your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and the length of your face. If the length of your face is more than twice the width of your forehead, then you have an oblong face shape.

Best Haircut for Oblong Face Woman

The best haircut for an oblong face woman is a shoulder-length cut with layers. This will add width and volume to your hair, which will balance out the length of your face. A side-swept fringe will also complement your face shape by creating an illusion of width.

Avoid haircuts that are too long or too short, as they will only elongate your face. Also, avoid straight and blunt cuts, as they will make your face appear even longer.

Step by Step Guide on How to Get the Best Haircut for Oblong Face Woman

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get the best haircut for oblong face woman:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of the haircut you want.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair to create volume and width.
  3. Ask for a shoulder-length cut, as this will balance out the length of your face.
  4. Request for a side-swept fringe to create an illusion of width.
  5. Style your hair with volumizing products to add more volume and texture to your hair.

Tips on How to Style Your Hair

Here are some tips on how to style your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create more volume and texture.
  • Use a curling iron or a straightener to add more texture to your hair.
  • Finish off your look with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for oblong face woman:

What other haircuts are suitable for an oblong face shape?

Other haircuts that are suitable for an oblong face shape are a long bob with layers, a wavy lob, and a side-swept pixie cut.

What hair color is best for an oblong face shape?

Warm and rich hair colors such as caramel, honey, and chestnut are best for an oblong face shape. These colors will add depth and dimension to your hair, which will complement your face shape.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Woman

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair if you have an oblong face shape:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as a curling iron or a straightener too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

In conclusion, the best haircut for oblong face woman is a shoulder-length cut with layers and a side-swept fringe. This will add width and volume to your hair, which will balance out the length of your face. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and use the right hair care products to maintain your hair’s health and beauty. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and confidence.
