Hairstyles For Long Thick Hair With Bangs

Written by William Sep 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long thick hair with bangs can be both a blessing and a curse. While it's great to have a lot of hair to work with, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Fortunately, there are many hairstyles for long thick hair with bangs that are both stylish and easy to maintain. Here are some of the best hairstyles for long thick hair with bangs.

How to Style Long Thick Hair with Bangs

The first step to styling long thick hair with bangs is to choose the right haircut. Layered haircuts work well with long thick hair, as they help to add texture and movement. Bangs should be cut to suit your face shape, whether that be blunt, side-swept, or wispy.

Once you have the right haircut, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles. Here are some ideas:

Braids

Braids are a great way to keep long thick hair under control. French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids are all great options. You can braid your hair all the way down, or just braid a section and leave the rest of your hair loose.

Ponytails

A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well with long thick hair. You can also try a low ponytail, a side ponytail, or a braided ponytail.

Buns

A bun is a chic and sophisticated hairstyle that's perfect for special occasions. You can try a messy bun, a sleek bun, or a braided bun.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hairstyles for Long Thick Hair with Bangs

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating some of the most popular hairstyles for long thick hair with bangs:

High Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. If you have bangs, you can leave them loose or pin them back.
  4. Smooth any flyaways with hairspray.

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Twist your hair around the elastic band to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.
  5. If you have bangs, you can leave them loose or pin them back.
  6. Finish with hairspray.

French Braid

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and divide it into three sections.
  4. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Repeat steps 4 and 5, adding more hair to each section as you go.
  6. Continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck, then finish with a regular braid.
  7. If you have bangs, you can leave them loose or pin them back.
  8. Finish with hairspray.

Tips for Styling Long Thick Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to help you style your long thick hair with bangs:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to smooth your hair and add curl or wave.
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ about Hairstyles for Long Thick Hair with Bangs

What kind of bangs are best for long thick hair?

The best bangs for long thick hair will depend on your face shape. Blunt bangs work well with round faces, while side-swept bangs are great for heart-shaped faces. Wispy bangs are a good option for oval faces.

How do I keep my hair from getting tangled?

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet, and avoid brushing your hair too much when it's dry. You can also use a detangling spray to make it easier to comb through your hair.

How often should I wash my long thick hair?

Long thick hair can be washed every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type. If your hair is very oily, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care for Long Thick Hair with Bangs

Long thick hair with bangs requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Take a break from coloring or bleaching your hair to give it a chance to recover.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

Long thick hair with bangs can be a challenge to style, but with the right haircut and some practice, you can create a variety of beautiful hairstyles. Whether you prefer braids, buns, or ponytails, there's a hairstyle out there that will work for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
