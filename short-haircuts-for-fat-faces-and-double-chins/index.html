Short Haircuts For Fat Faces And Double Chins

Written by Luffy Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect short haircut to flatter your round face and double chin? Look no further! In this article, we’ll discuss some of the best short haircuts for fat faces and double chins, as well as tips for maintaining and styling your new ‘do.

Description

Women with round faces and double chins often struggle to find a haircut that flatters their features. Short haircuts can be a great solution, as they can help to elongate the neck and create the illusion of a slimmer face. However, not all short haircuts are created equal – some can actually make a round face look even rounder! In this article, we’ll explore some of the best short haircuts for fat faces and double chins.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a round face and double chin, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Length: Short haircuts that fall above the chin are generally the most flattering, as they can help to elongate the neck and create a more balanced look.
  • Layers: Layered haircuts can also be a great option, as they can help to add volume and movement to the hair.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from the chin and towards the eyes.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to choosing the perfect short haircut for your round face and double chin:

  1. Start by browsing through pictures of short haircuts that you like. Look for styles that are similar to your hair texture and thickness.
  2. Consider your face shape and features. If you have a round face and double chin, look for haircuts that are shorter in length and feature layers and/or side-swept bangs.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist. They can help you choose a haircut that flatters your features and works with your hair texture and thickness.
  4. Once you’ve chosen your new haircut, be sure to ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain it.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can be low maintenance, but they still require some TLC to look their best. Here are a few tips for maintaining and styling your new ‘do:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for your hair type.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and round brush. Blow dry your hair in sections, using the round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Consider using a straightening iron or curling wand to add texture and shape to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch ups.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for fat faces and double chins:

What is the best short haircut for a round face and double chin?

The best short haircut for a round face and double chin is one that falls above the chin and features layers and/or side-swept bangs.

Can I pull off a pixie cut with a round face and double chin?

Pixie cuts can be tricky to pull off with a round face and double chin, as they can make the face look even rounder. However, if you’re determined to try a pixie cut, consider opting for a longer, shaggier style with side-swept bangs.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

Short haircuts require more frequent trims than longer styles. Aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, vibrant-looking mane. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair in tip-top shape:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hair mask or deep conditioner to use once a week.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to the hair follicles.

With the right short haircut and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best – no matter your face shape or features!
