How To Choose A Hairstyle That Suits You Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Nov 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing a hairstyle that suits you can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. A good hairstyle can boost your confidence and make you feel amazing. However, a bad hairstyle can ruin your entire day. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to choose a hairstyle that suits you.

How to Choose a Hairstyle that Suits You

When choosing a hairstyle that suits you, there are several factors that you need to consider. These include your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style.

Face Shape

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has a hairstyle that suits it best.

For example, if you have a round face, a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head can make your face appear longer. If you have an oval face, you can try a hairstyle with soft layers that frame your face.

Hair Texture

Another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture. Hair texture refers to the thickness and coarseness of your hair. There are several hair textures, including straight, wavy, curly, and kinky.

If you have straight hair, you can try a sleek and polished hairstyle. If you have wavy hair, you can try a layered and tousled hairstyle. If you have curly hair, you can try a voluminous and defined hairstyle.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have much time to style your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle.

For example, if you have short hair, you can try a messy pixie cut that requires little styling. If you have long hair, you can try a simple ponytail or a messy bun.

Personal Style

Finally, your personal style is an important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style.

For example, if you have a bohemian style, you can try a messy, beachy hairstyle. If you have a classic style, you can try a sleek and polished hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide: How to Choose a Hairstyle that Suits You

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose a hairstyle that suits you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hairstyle that suits you is to determine your face shape. Take a look at your face in the mirror and identify its shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The second step is to consider your hair texture. Determine whether you have straight, wavy, curly, or kinky hair.

Step 3: Determine Your Lifestyle

The third step is to determine your lifestyle. Consider how much time you have to style your hair and choose a low-maintenance hairstyle if necessary.

Step 4: Consider Your Personal Style

The fourth step is to consider your personal style. Choose a hairstyle that reflects your personality and personal style.

Step 5: Consult with a Hairstylist

If you are still unsure about which hairstyle suits you, it is best to consult with a hairstylist. A hairstylist can provide you with expert advice and help you choose a hairstyle that suits you best.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle that Suits You

Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle that suits you:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair texture.
  • Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that reflects your personal style.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for expert advice.

FAQ

Q: How do I know which face shape I have?

A: You can determine your face shape by looking at the proportions of your face. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and compare them to the length of your face.

Q: What if I have thin hair?

A: If you have thin hair, you can try a layered haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. You can also try using volumizing products to add thickness to your hair.

Q: What if I have a busy lifestyle?

A: If you have a busy lifestyle, you can choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires little styling. Short hairstyles, like pixie cuts, are a great option for busy women.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen a hairstyle that suits you, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after washing to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

Choosing a hairstyle that suits you can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style, you can choose a hairstyle that makes you look and feel amazing. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
