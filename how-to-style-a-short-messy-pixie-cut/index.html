How To Style A Short Messy Pixie Cut

If you've recently chopped off your locks into a short, messy pixie cut, you may be wondering how to style it. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a fun and edgy look that's perfect for any occasion. In this blog post, we'll walk you through step-by-step instructions on how to style a short messy pixie cut. We'll also provide you with helpful tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great. Let's get started!

How to Style a Short Messy Pixie Cut

The key to styling a short messy pixie cut is to create texture and volume. You can achieve this by using the right styling products and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Texturizing spray
  • Hair wax or pomade
  • Hairspray
  • Blow dryer
  • Round brush

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps to style your short messy pixie cut:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Apply texturizing spray to damp hair and blow dry with a round brush. This will help create volume and texture.
  2. Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and scrunch your hair from the back to the front. This will create a messy, textured look.
  3. Use your fingers to create piecey sections and define the layers of your pixie cut. You can also use a comb to create a more polished look.
  4. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your short messy pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • If you have fine hair, try using a volumizing mousse before blow drying.
  • If you have thick hair, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  • Don't overdo it with the products. A little goes a long way.
  • If you're in a rush, try using a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about styling short messy pixie cuts:

Can I style my short messy pixie cut without heat?

Absolutely! Instead of blow drying your hair with a round brush, you can let it air dry and use your fingers to create a messy, textured look.

How often should I wash my short messy pixie cut?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Some people can go a few days between washes, while others need to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Can I use a curling iron on my short messy pixie cut?

Absolutely! You can use a small curling iron to add more texture and volume to your hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Hair Care for Short Messy Pixie Cuts

Short messy pixie cuts require a little extra care to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape of your cut.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Let it air dry whenever possible.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short messy pixie cut with ease. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Have fun and embrace your edgy new look!
