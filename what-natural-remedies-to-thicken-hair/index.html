What Natural Remedies To Thicken Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling with thinning hair and looking for a natural remedy to thicken it? You’re not alone—many people experience hair loss or thinning at some point in their lives. Fortunately, there are several natural remedies that can help you achieve thicker, healthier hair. In this article, we’ll explore the best ways to thicken hair naturally, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Thicken Hair Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Thicker Hair
  • FAQs

How to Thicken Hair Naturally

There are several natural remedies that can help you achieve thicker hair. Some of the most effective options include:

Eggs

Eggs are rich in protein and can help strengthen hair follicles, leading to thicker, healthier hair. To use eggs as a natural remedy, simply beat two eggs and apply the mixture to your hair. Leave the egg mixture on for 30 minutes, then rinse with cool water.

Castor Oil

Castor oil is rich in fatty acids that can help nourish the scalp and promote hair growth. To use castor oil as a natural remedy, massage a few drops into your scalp and hair, then leave it on for at least an hour before washing it out.

Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is known for its soothing properties, but it can also help thicken hair. To use aloe vera as a natural remedy, apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and hair, then leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off.

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is another natural remedy that can help thicken hair. Its fatty acids can penetrate the hair shaft and strengthen strands. To use coconut oil as a natural remedy, warm up a few tablespoons of coconut oil and massage it into your scalp and hair. Leave it on for at least an hour before washing it out.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you’re ready to try a natural remedy to thicken your hair, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Choose a natural remedy that works for you, such as eggs, castor oil, aloe vera, or coconut oil.
  2. Prepare the remedy according to the instructions (for example, beat the eggs or warm up the coconut oil).
  3. Apply the remedy to your scalp and hair, making sure to massage it in thoroughly.
  4. Leave the remedy on for at least 30 minutes (or longer, depending on the remedy).
  5. Rinse the remedy out with cool water.
  6. Repeat the process once or twice a week, depending on your hair’s needs.

Tips for Thicker Hair

In addition to using natural remedies, there are several other things you can do to promote thicker, healthier hair:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support hair growth, such as vitamin C, iron, and biotin.
  • Avoid heat styling tools and harsh chemicals that can damage hair.
  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on hair and cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about natural remedies for thickening hair:

Q: How long does it take to see results from natural remedies?

A: It varies depending on the remedy and your hair type, but you may start to see results within a few weeks to a few months.

Q: Can natural remedies really thicken hair?

A: While there is no guarantee that natural remedies will work for everyone, many people have had success using them to thicken their hair.

Q: Are there any side effects of using natural remedies for hair?

A: Natural remedies are generally safe, but some people may be allergic to certain ingredients. Always do a patch test before using a new remedy, and stop using it if you experience any adverse reactions.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind as you work to thicken your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often (2-3 times per week is usually sufficient).
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more prone to damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair gently.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel—instead, gently blot it dry.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Thick, healthy hair is achievable with the right natural remedies and hair care routine. Try out some of the tips and remedies we’ve outlined in this article, and see what works best for you!
