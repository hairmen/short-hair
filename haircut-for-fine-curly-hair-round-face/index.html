Haircut For Fine Curly Hair Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Nov 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have fine curly hair and a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your facial features and enhances your natural curls, without sacrificing volume or texture. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks for choosing the perfect haircut for your fine curly hair and round face.

What to consider when choosing a haircut for fine curly hair round face

Before you head to the salon, there are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for your fine curly hair and round face.

Face shape

Round faces tend to have soft, curved lines and full cheeks. You'll want a haircut that creates the illusion of length and angles to balance out your facial features.

Hair texture

Fine curly hair can be tricky to work with, as it tends to be fragile and prone to frizz. You'll want a haircut that enhances your natural curls without weighing them down or causing damage.

Styling routine

Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair on a daily basis. Some haircuts require more maintenance than others, so be sure to choose a style that fits your lifestyle.

How to choose the right haircut for fine curly hair round face

Now that you know what to consider, let's dive into some tips for choosing the perfect haircut for your fine curly hair and round face.

Opt for layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and texture to fine curly hair, while also creating the illusion of length. Ask your stylist for long layers that frame your face and enhance your natural curls.

Consider a long bob

A long bob, or "lob," is a versatile haircut that works well for round faces and fine curly hair. The length of the cut elongates the face, while the layers add volume and movement.

Try a side-swept fringe

If you're looking to change up your look without committing to a drastic cut, try adding a side-swept fringe to your hairstyle. This will draw attention away from your round face and create a more angular look.

Step-by-step guide to styling your fine curly hair round face

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your fine curly hair and round face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a beautiful, voluminous look:

Step 1: Wash and condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for fine curly hair. Be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and a nourishing conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a curl-enhancing cream or gel, to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 3: Diffuse your curls

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural curls and add volume.

Step 4: Finish with a texturizing spray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Tips for maintaining your fine curly hair round face

To keep your fine curly hair and round face looking their best, here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage and frizz.

Avoid over-washing

Fine curly hair tends to be dry, so avoid over-washing your hair, which can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, opt for a gentle cleansing conditioner or co-wash.

Protect your hair at night

Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage, and consider wearing a silk or satin hair bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep.

FAQ about Haircut for Fine Curly Hair Round Face

What haircut is best for fine curly hair round face?

A long bob with layers is a great option for fine curly hair and round faces. This haircut adds length and movement to the hair, while also enhancing natural curls.

How do I style my fine curly hair round face without causing damage?

To style your fine curly hair without causing damage, be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and a nourishing conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often.

How often should I get a haircut for fine curly hair round face?

It's recommended to get a haircut for fine curly hair and round face every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of the hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for fine curly hair and round face can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a style that flatters your facial features and enhances your natural curls. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and styling routine when choosing a haircut, and follow our step-by-step guide to styling and maintaining your hair for a beautiful, voluminous look.
