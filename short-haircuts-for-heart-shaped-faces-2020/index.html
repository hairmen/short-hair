Short Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces 2020

Written by Frank Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you know how difficult it can be to find the right haircut. You want a style that flatters your face and accentuates your best features. That's why we've put together a guide to the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces in 2020. Keep reading to find out which styles will work best for you.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of a good haircut for this face shape is to balance out these features and create a more symmetrical look. Short haircuts are a great option for heart-shaped faces because they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the width of the forehead.

How to

The first step in finding the right short haircut for your heart-shaped face is to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or pixie cut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or shaggy pixie can help to thin out your hair and create a more manageable style.

Next, consider the length of your hair. A short haircut can be anywhere from a daring pixie to a chin-length bob. The key is to find a length that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. A layered bob is a great option for heart-shaped faces because it adds volume to the crown while minimizing the width of the forehead.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
3. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls, depending on your desired look.
4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or hair wax, to help hold your style in place.
5. Finish with a hairspray to add shine and hold.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for your heart-shaped face, it's important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair each day. A pixie cut may be low-maintenance, but it may not be the best choice if you have a busy schedule and need a style that can be worn in different settings.

Another tip is to choose a haircut that complements your personal style. If you prefer a more edgy look, a choppy pixie or asymmetrical bob may be a great choice. If you prefer a more classic look, a sleek bob or blunt cut may be more your style.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short haircut?
A: Yes, you can definitely wear bangs with a short haircut. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. For a heart-shaped face, side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs are a great choice because they help to balance out the width of the forehead.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look too round?
A: No, a short haircut can actually help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. The key is to choose a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your short haircut, regular trims are key. Depending on the length of your hair, you may need to visit the salon every 4-8 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

It's also important to use high-quality hair care products that are designed for your hair type. This can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage from heat styling and environmental factors.

In conclusion, there are many great short haircuts for heart-shaped faces in 2020. By considering your hair texture, length, and personal style, you can find a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. With regular trims and high-quality hair care products, you can maintain your short haircut and keep it looking great all year long.
