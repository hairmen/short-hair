How Can I Fix My Brassy Hair Color

Written by Robby Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you feeling frustrated with your brassy hair color? Do you want to know how to fix it? This article will provide you with tips, tricks, and steps to help you fix your brassy hair color. We will also answer some frequently asked questions and provide you with some hair care tips to prevent further brassy hair color.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Brassy Hair Color
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Fixing Brassy Hair Color
  • FAQs About Brassy Hair Color

How to Fix Brassy Hair Color

Before we dive into how to fix your brassy hair color, let's discuss what causes it. Brassy hair color occurs when the hair is not lightened enough, and the underlying pigment is exposed. This typically happens when you bleach your hair or use hair dye to lighten your hair color.

To fix your brassy hair color, you will need to add a cool-toned color to neutralize the warm tones. This can be done by using a toner or a hair dye with cool tones. You can also use a purple shampoo or a blue shampoo to neutralize the warm tones.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to fix your brassy hair color:

  1. Choose a cool-toned hair dye or toner that matches your hair color. If you're not sure which one to choose, consult with a hair stylist.
  2. Apply the hair dye or toner to your hair, following the instructions on the box.
  3. Leave the hair dye or toner in your hair for the recommended time.
  4. Rinse the hair dye or toner out with lukewarm water.
  5. Apply a purple shampoo or blue shampoo to your hair and let it sit for a few minutes.
  6. Rinse the shampoo out with lukewarm water.
  7. Condition your hair with a deep conditioner to prevent damage.

Repeat the process as needed until you achieve your desired hair color.

Tips for Fixing Brassy Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you fix your brassy hair color:

  • Choose a hair dye or toner that matches your hair color to avoid further damage.
  • Follow the instructions on the box carefully to avoid over-processing your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip away the hair color.
  • Use a deep conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Wear a hat or a scarf when you're outside to protect your hair from the sun's rays.

FAQs About Brassy Hair Color

Here are some frequently asked questions about brassy hair color:

Q: Can I fix my brassy hair color without using hair dye or toner?

A: Yes, you can use a purple shampoo or a blue shampoo to neutralize the warm tones in your hair.

Q: How often should I use a purple or blue shampoo?

A: You can use a purple or blue shampoo once a week or as needed.

Q: Can I use a toner on my own, or do I need to go to a hair stylist?

A: You can use a toner on your own, but if you're not sure which one to choose, it's best to consult with a hair stylist.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Brassy Hair Color

Here are some hair care tips to prevent brassy hair color:

  • Avoid over-processing your hair with bleach or hair dye.
  • Use a purple or blue shampoo once a week to maintain your hair color.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun's rays by wearing a hat or a scarf when you're outside.

By following these tips and steps, you can fix your brassy hair color and maintain healthy, vibrant hair. Remember to consult with a hair stylist if you're unsure which products to use or which steps to take.
