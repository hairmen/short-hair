How To Style Long Thick Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are blessed with long, thick, and wavy hair, you know how challenging it can be to tame your mane. But with the right techniques and products, you can create beautiful hairstyles that showcase your natural texture and add volume and movement to your hair. In this article, we will share our favorite tips and tricks on how to style long thick wavy hair, from beachy waves to updos and braids.

How to

The key to styling long thick wavy hair is to work with your natural texture instead of trying to fight it. Here's a step-by-step guide to creating effortless waves:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your locks. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, then apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step 2: Create waves

If you have natural waves, you can enhance them by scrunching your hair with a curl-enhancing cream or mousse. If you want more defined waves, you can use a curling wand or a flat iron to create loose curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel or plates, hold for a few seconds, then release.

Step 3: Finish with hairspray

To keep your waves in place, spritz a light-hold hairspray all over your hair. You can also use a texture spray or dry shampoo to add volume and grit to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can create frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. If you must use a styling tool, use a low or medium heat setting and apply a heat protectant beforehand.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as half-up half-down, messy buns, or braids, to keep your look fresh and versatile.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long thick wavy hair:

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my wavy hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid rubbing your hair with a towel after washing it, as this can rough up the hair cuticles. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water and let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. You can also apply a smoothing serum or oil to your hair to tame flyaways and add shine.

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair with a flat iron or a straightening brush. However, keep in mind that excessive heat styling can damage your hair over time, so it's important to use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat settings too often.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful long thick wavy hair. Here are some tips to keep your locks in top condition:

  • Wash your hair no more than 2-3 times a week to prevent stripping your natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth, such as biotin, iron, and omega-3 fatty acids.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your long thick wavy hair with confidence and ease. Whether you prefer beachy waves or elegant updos, embrace your natural texture and make it work for you!
