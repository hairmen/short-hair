Medium Length Men s Haircut Taper Fade The Perfect Combination

Written by Eveline Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, the medium length men's haircut with a taper fade might be the perfect combination for you. This popular hairstyle has been trending for a while now, and it's easy to see why. With the perfect blend of length and fade, this haircut is suitable for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look, step-by-step, along with some helpful tips and FAQs.

Description

The medium length men's haircut with a taper fade is a stylish and timeless hairstyle that has been popular for years. This haircut features longer hair on top that is usually styled in a messy or slicked back look, while the sides and back are tapered or faded, creating a smooth and seamless transition from longer to shorter hair. The taper fade is a classic technique that involves gradually decreasing the length of the hair towards the bottom, making it look clean and sharp.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in getting this haircut, here's how you can achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to determine the length of your hair. For a medium length men's haircut, the hair on top should be around 3-4 inches long, while the sides and back should be shorter, around 1-2 inches. Keep in mind that the length can vary depending on your personal preference and face shape.

Step 2: Decide on the Fade

Next, decide on the type of fade you want. There are various types of fades, including low, mid, and high fades. For a medium length men's haircut, a mid-fade or high-fade would work best. The fade should be blended smoothly into the hair on top.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Using clippers, cut the hair on the sides and back, gradually decreasing the length towards the bottom. Make sure to blend the fade smoothly into the hair on top. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length and style it accordingly.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length men's haircut with a taper fade:

  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use hair products, such as wax or pomade, to style the hair on top.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Consider getting a touch-up on the fade every few weeks to keep it looking sharp.

FAQ

How long does the medium length men's haircut with a taper fade last?

The length of time the haircut lasts depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. On average, it can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks.

Is the medium length men's haircut with a taper fade suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut is suitable for all face shapes. The length and style can be adjusted to suit your face shape and personal preferences.

Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, the hair on top can be styled in various ways, from a messy look to a slicked-back look. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using hair products in moderation, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Additionally, make sure to eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, the medium length men's haircut with a taper fade is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can work for anyone. With the right length, fade, and styling, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and practical. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect medium length men's haircut with a taper fade.
