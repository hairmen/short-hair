How To Make Long Hair Look Like Guys Hair

Written by Luffy Oct 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a woman with long hair who wants to achieve a more masculine look? Maybe you're a man with long hair who wants to try out a more androgynous style. Whatever your reason, it's possible to make long hair look like guys hair with a few simple tips and tricks.

Description

Long hair can be a versatile and beautiful accessory, but sometimes you may want to switch up your look. Whether you want to look more masculine or simply try something new, making long hair look like guys hair is a great option. This style can be achieved with a few key techniques, such as cutting your hair in a more masculine style, using hair products to add texture and volume, and styling your hair in a way that emphasizes your masculine features.

How To

If you want to make your long hair look like guys hair, there are a few steps you can follow:

Step 1: Cut Your Hair

The first step to making your long hair look like guys hair is to cut it in a more masculine style. This can mean getting a short haircut, or simply cutting your hair in a way that emphasizes your masculine features. For example, you could ask your hairdresser to add layers or texture to your hair, or to give you a more angular haircut that emphasizes your jawline.

Step 2: Use Hair Products

Once you've cut your hair, you can use hair products to add texture and volume. Try using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to give your hair a more masculine look. You can also use a pomade or wax to add definition to your hair and create a more structured style.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Finally, you can style your hair in a way that emphasizes your masculine features. For example, you could try slicking your hair back or to the side, or wearing it in a low ponytail. Experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you make your long hair look like guys hair:

  • Experiment with hair accessories like hats, headbands, or bandanas to add a masculine touch to your look.
  • Try parting your hair on the side instead of in the middle, which can make your hair look more masculine.
  • Consider using hair dye to add some color to your hair and create a more edgy, masculine look.

FAQs

Q: Will cutting my hair short make it look more masculine?

A: Not necessarily. While short hair is often associated with masculinity, there are many other factors that can contribute to a more masculine look, such as the cut and texture of your hair.

Q: Can I make my long hair look like guys hair without cutting it?

A: Yes! You can achieve a more masculine look by using hair products to add texture and volume, and by styling your hair in a way that emphasizes your masculine features.

Q: What kind of hair products should I use?

A: It depends on your hair type and the look you're going for. Consider using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume to your hair, or a pomade or wax to add definition and structure.

Hair Care

Remember that cutting and styling your hair can be damaging, so it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy. Be sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your long hair look like guys hair and achieve a more masculine or androgynous look. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
