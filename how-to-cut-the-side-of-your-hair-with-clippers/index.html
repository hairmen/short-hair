How To Cut The Side Of Your Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Nov 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're tired of paying for haircuts or simply want to take matters into your own hands, cutting your own hair with clippers can be a great solution. However, figuring out how to cut the side of your hair with clippers can be a bit tricky. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step and provide some helpful tips to make sure you get the best results possible.

How to Cut the Side of Your Hair with Clippers

Cutting the side of your hair with clippers is all about getting the right angle and using the right technique. Here's what you'll need to do:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by selecting the right clipper guards for your desired length. If you're not sure what length to choose, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the length you like.

Step 2: Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be harder to work with and may result in uneven cutting.

Step 3: Begin by cutting the hair at the bottom of your sideburns. Use the clipper to create a straight line, working your way up to your temple.

Step 4: Once you've created the initial line, use the clipper to blend the hair above the line. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a flicking motion to create a gradual fade.

Step 5: Repeat this process on the other side of your head, making sure to use the same guard and angle for consistency.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

While cutting your hair with clippers can be relatively straightforward, there are a few tips you can follow to make sure you get the best results:

  • Invest in a high-quality pair of clippers. Cheaper models may not cut as evenly or may not last as long.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go. This will help you make sure both sides are even.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not sure what you're doing. A friend or family member with experience cutting hair can provide valuable guidance.
  • Practice makes perfect. The more you cut your own hair, the more comfortable you'll become with the process.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair with regular scissors instead of clippers?

A: While it's technically possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, clippers are generally a better option. Clippers are designed specifically for cutting hair and can make the process much easier and more efficient.

Q: Is it possible to cut my hair too short with clippers?

A: Yes, it's definitely possible to accidentally cut your hair too short with clippers. This is why it's important to start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually until you find the right length.

Hair Care After Cutting

Once you've finished cutting the side of your hair with clippers, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain the look and keep it healthy. This means using high-quality hair products, avoiding excessive heat styling, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

With a bit of practice and some patience, cutting the side of your hair with clippers can be a great way to save money and take control of your own style. Follow these tips and you'll be well on your way to a great-looking haircut!
