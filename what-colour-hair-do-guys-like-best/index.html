What Colour Hair Do Guys Like Best

Are you looking to switch up your hair colour and wondering what guys prefer? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the most popular hair colours that guys like and why they find them attractive.

  • Blonde
  • Brunette

Blonde

Blonde hair is a popular choice among guys. It's often associated with youthfulness and fun, and it's no secret that guys find this attractive. Not only does blonde hair look great, but it also gives off a carefree vibe that many guys love.

However, it's important to note that not all guys prefer blonde hair. Some find it a bit too "girly" or unapproachable. Additionally, blonde hair requires a lot of upkeep to prevent it from looking dull or damaged.

Brunette

Brunette hair is another popular choice among guys. It's often associated with intelligence and sophistication, which many guys find attractive. Brunette hair also tends to be more low maintenance than blonde hair, which can be a plus for those who don't want to spend hours in the salon.

However, like with blonde hair, not all guys prefer brunettes. Some find it too "serious" or boring. Additionally, if you have naturally dark hair, it can be difficult to dye it a lighter shade of brown without damaging it.

Redhead

Red hair is a unique and eye-catching hair colour that many guys find attractive. It's often associated with creativity and passion, which can be a huge turn-on for some guys. Additionally, red hair tends to be rare, which can make it even more desirable.

However, red hair can be difficult to maintain. It tends to fade quickly and can be prone to dryness and breakage. Additionally, not all guys find it attractive, and some may even find it too bold or attention-grabbing.

Black

Black hair is a classic and timeless option that many guys find attractive. It's often associated with mystery and sophistication, which can be a huge turn-on for some guys. Additionally, black hair tends to be low maintenance and easy to style.

However, black hair can be a bit too severe or intimidating for some guys. Additionally, if you have naturally light hair, it can be difficult to dye it black without damaging it.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

No matter what hair colour you choose, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair colour:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much sun, as it can cause fading.

FAQs

Do guys prefer natural hair colours?

It depends on the guy. Some guys prefer natural hair colours, while others are drawn to more unique or eye-catching colours.

What hair colour do guys find the most attractive?

Again, it depends on the guy. However, blonde, brunette, and black tend to be the most popular hair colours among guys.

Is it okay to dye your hair a bold colour?

Of course! It's your hair, and you should feel free to express yourself however you like. Just keep in mind that some people may not find it attractive, and you may need to take extra care to maintain the colour.


No matter what hair colour you choose, remember that the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin. Whether you prefer blonde, brunette, red, or black hair, there's someone out there who will find you attractive just the way you are.
