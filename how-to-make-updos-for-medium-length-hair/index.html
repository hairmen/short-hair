How To Make Updos For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium length hair is versatile and perfect for creating beautiful updos. Whether you're looking for a casual, everyday hairstyle or an elegant updo for a special occasion, you can easily achieve the look with a few simple steps. In this article, we'll explore how to make updos for medium length hair and provide tips and tricks to help you create the perfect style.

Description

Updos are a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some sophistication to your look. They can be worn for a variety of occasions, from a day at the office to a fancy wedding or prom. Medium length hair is a perfect length for updos, as it's long enough to create a variety of styles, but not so long that it's difficult to manage.

How to Make Updos for Medium Length Hair

To create an updo for medium length hair, you'll need a few tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair ties
  • Hairspray
  • Comb
  • Teasing brush

Once you have your tools and products ready, follow these steps to create your updo:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have oily hair, you can use dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts, with the top section being slightly smaller than the bottom section. Use a hair tie to secure the bottom section in a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Step 3: Tease your hair

Using a teasing brush, tease the top section of your hair to create volume. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the ends. Once you've teased your hair, smooth the top layer with a comb.

Step 4: Create a bun

Gather the top section of your hair and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to tuck in any loose ends. Use hairspray to hold the bun in place.

Step 5: Add accessories

To add some extra flair to your updo, you can add accessories like a headband, hairpins, or a flower crown. Just make sure not to overdo it, as too many accessories can make your hairstyle look cluttered.

Tips for Making Updos for Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect updo:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing product to add some extra lift.
  • Practice your updo a few times before the big day to make sure you're comfortable with the style.

FAQs

Can you create an updo with medium length hair?

Yes, medium length hair is perfect for creating updos. You can experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

What tools do you need to make an updo for medium length hair?

You'll need bobby pins, hair ties, hairspray, a comb, and a teasing brush to create an updo for medium length hair.

How do you make an updo last all day?

Use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is key to creating beautiful updos. Here are some tips for caring for your medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove excess oil and dirt.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily create beautiful updos for your medium length hair. Whether you're going for a casual or elegant look, there's an updo style that's perfect for you.
