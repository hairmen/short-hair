How To Manage Extremely Thick Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have extremely thick curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to manage. The curls can be tight and difficult to detangle, and the thickness can make it hard to style your hair the way you want. However, with the right techniques and products, you can manage your thick curly hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to manage extremely thick curly hair.

How to Manage Extremely Thick Curly Hair

The first step in managing your thick curly hair is to find a good stylist who understands your hair type. A stylist who is familiar with curly hair can help you find the right cut and style that will work with your hair texture. Once you have a good cut, you can start using the right products to keep your hair healthy and manageable.

When it comes to washing your hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Sulfates can dry out your hair and cause frizz, so it's best to avoid them. After washing, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to manage extremely thick curly hair:

  1. Find a stylist who understands curly hair and can give you a good cut.
  2. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  3. Detangle your hair with a wide-toothed comb after washing.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated.
  5. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to minimize frizz.
  6. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage.
  7. Use a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.
  8. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  9. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  10. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair.

Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing your thick curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Be patient with your hair and don't try to force it into a style that doesn't work for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing thick curly hair:

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using a flat iron on wet hair.

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but most people with curly hair can go 2-3 days between washes.

Q: What is the best way to style thick curly hair?

A: Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair. Some popular styles for curly hair include a messy bun, a half-up/half-down style, and a curly ponytail.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for managing thick curly hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, or use them sparingly.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Be gentle with your hair when styling, and avoid pulling or tugging on it.

With these tips and tricks, you can manage your extremely thick curly hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Remember, it may take some trial and error to find the right products and styling techniques that work for you, but with patience and persistence, you can achieve the look you want.
