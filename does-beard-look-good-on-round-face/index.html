Does Beard Look Good On Round Face

Written by Luffy Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Beards have been a symbol of masculinity since time immemorial. However, the question of whether or not a beard looks good on a round face is a tricky one. In this blog post, we will address this question and provide tips on how to style a beard for a round face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style a Beard for a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling a Beard on a Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Beards

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of styling a beard on a round face is to create an oval or triangular shape to make the face appear longer and slimmer.

Men with round faces can grow a beard, but the style and length of the beard have to be chosen carefully to complement the face shape.

How to Style a Beard for a Round Face

There are a few beard styles that look good on round faces, including:

1. The Square Beard

The square beard is a style where the beard is trimmed to form a square shape around the jawline. This style helps to create a more angular look to the face and make it appear less round.

2. The Extended Goatee

The extended goatee is a style where the beard is grown out slightly longer than the goatee and forms an inverted T-shape. This style helps to elongate the face and create a more oval shape.

3. The Anchor Beard

The anchor beard is a style where the beard is grown out to form a point at the chin and is connected to a mustache. This style helps to create a more angular look to the face and make it appear less round.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when styling a beard for a round face:

  1. Choose a beard style that complements your face shape.
  2. Grow out your beard to the desired length.
  3. Trim the sides of the beard to create a more angular shape.
  4. Trim the bottom of the beard to create a square or triangular shape.
  5. Trim the mustache to create a clean and defined look.
  6. Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized and healthy.

Tips for Styling a Beard on a Round Face

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling a beard for a round face:

  • Avoid growing a full beard that covers the entire face. This can make the face appear rounder.
  • Choose a beard style that complements your facial features.
  • Use a beard trimmer to trim your beard regularly to maintain the desired length and shape.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized and healthy.
  • Comb your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

1. Can a round face pull off a beard?

Yes, a round face can pull off a beard, but the style and length of the beard have to be chosen carefully to complement the face shape.

2. What beard styles look good on a round face?

Beard styles that look good on a round face include the square beard, extended goatee, and anchor beard.

3. How often should I trim my beard?

You should trim your beard once a week to maintain the desired length and shape.

Hair Care for Beards

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and good-looking beard. Here are a few tips on how to take care of your beard:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a mild shampoo to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized and healthy.
  • Brush or comb your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain the desired length and shape.

In conclusion, a beard can look good on a round face if styled and maintained properly. Choose a beard style that complements your face shape, trim your beard regularly, and use beard oil or balm to keep it moisturized and healthy.
