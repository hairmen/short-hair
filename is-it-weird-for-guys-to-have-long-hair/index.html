Is It Weird For Guys To Have Long Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair for men has been a topic of debate for a long time. Some people think it looks great, while others believe it looks odd or even unprofessional. However, the question remains: is it weird for guys to have long hair? Let's explore this topic in detail.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long hair has been a part of human culture for centuries. However, it was mostly associated with women until the 1960s when men started growing their hair long as a symbol of rebellion against societal norms. Since then, long hair for men has become more common, but it still raises a few eyebrows. Some people see it as a sign of a free spirit, while others think it's unprofessional or even dirty.

How to

If you're a guy considering growing your hair long, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

1. Start Slowly

Don't go from a buzz cut to waist-length hair overnight. Start with a shorter style and gradually let it grow out. This way, you'll be able to see if you like the look and feel of long hair and adjust accordingly.

2. Find a Good Stylist

Long hair requires more maintenance than short hair, so find a stylist who knows how to cut and style long hair. They'll be able to give you tips on how to care for your hair and keep it looking healthy.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Long hair doesn't have to be one style fits all. Experiment with different cuts, colors, and styles until you find what works for you. Don't be afraid to try something new.

Step by step

If you're ready to take the plunge and grow your hair long, here are some steps you can follow:

1. Start with a Shorter Style

As mentioned earlier, don't go from a buzz cut to waist-length hair overnight. Start with a shorter style, such as a bob or shag, and let it grow out gradually.

2. Get Regular Trims

Even if you're growing your hair out, you still need to get regular trims to keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends. Your stylist can recommend a trimming schedule based on your hair type and growth rate.

3. Use Quality Hair Products

Long hair requires more maintenance than short hair, so invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. This includes shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for guys with long hair:

1. Keep it Clean

Long hair can get dirty and greasy quickly, so wash it regularly to keep it looking and smelling fresh.

2. Protect it from Damage

Long hair is more prone to damage from heat styling, chemical treatments, and environmental factors. Use heat protectant sprays and avoid harsh chemicals and excessive sun exposure.

3. Embrace the Look

Long hair for men isn't for everyone, but if you like the look and feel of it, embrace it. Don't let other people's opinions affect your decision to grow your hair long.

FAQ

Q: Is it unprofessional for guys to have long hair?

A: It depends on the industry and company culture. In some industries, such as fashion or entertainment, long hair is more accepted. However, in more conservative industries, such as finance or law, it may be seen as unprofessional.

Q: How long does it take for men's hair to grow long?

A: It varies from person to person, but on average, men's hair grows about half an inch per month. It can take anywhere from 6 months to 2 years to grow hair to shoulder length.

Q: Can men with thinning hair grow their hair long?

A: It's not recommended. Long hair can accentuate thinning areas and make the hair look even thinner.

Hair Care

Long hair requires more maintenance than short hair, so here are some additional tips for keeping it looking healthy:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Long hair is more prone to tangles, so use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair after washing.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as ponytails or buns, can cause breakage and damage to the hair. Avoid them as much as possible or use a looser hair tie.

3. Don't Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

In conclusion, is it weird for guys to have long hair? The answer is no, it's not weird. It's a personal choice and a matter of preference. If you like the look and feel of long hair, go for it. Just remember to take care of it properly and find a style that works for you.
