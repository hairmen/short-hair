How To Make Hair Curly Again A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you know that maintaining those perfect curls can be a challenge. Whether it's due to age, heat damage or chemical treatments, sometimes our curls just don't bounce back the way they used to. But don't worry, you can bring your curls back to life with a few simple tips and tricks. In this article, we'll show you how to make hair curly again!

Description

As we age, our hair can become thinner, drier, and lose its elasticity. This can cause our once curly hair to become limp and lifeless. Additionally, heat damage from styling tools and chemical treatments like relaxers can permanently alter the structure of our hair, making it difficult to maintain curls. But with the right techniques and products, you can revive your curls and bring them back to their former glory.

How to Make Hair Curly Again: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with a Clean Slate

The first step to bringing your curls back to life is to start with a clean slate. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue that may be weighing your hair down. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to ensure all of the product is removed from your hair.

Step 2: Deep Condition

Once your hair is clean, apply a deep conditioning treatment to help restore moisture and elasticity. Look for products specifically designed for curly hair, as they will contain ingredients that help define and enhance curls. Leave the conditioner on for at least 10 minutes, or as directed on the packaging.

Step 3: Scrunch and Define

After rinsing out the conditioner, gently squeeze excess water out of your hair with a towel. Apply a curl-defining product, like a mousse or gel, to your damp hair. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair, using a low heat setting.

Step 4: Sleep with a Pineapple

To protect your curls while you sleep, try the pineapple method. Gather your hair at the top of your head, loosely securing with a scrunchie or hair tie. This will help preserve your curls overnight, so you wake up with bouncy, defined curls in the morning.

Tips for Making Hair Curly Again

Here are a few additional tips to help you revive your curls:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools whenever possible, as they can cause heat damage and permanently alter your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can disrupt your curls.
  • Try a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of natural oils and cause dryness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I make my hair curly again after a relaxer?

A: Yes, it is possible to make your hair curly again after a relaxer, but it will take time and patience. You will need to grow out the relaxed hair and gradually trim it away, while using the techniques outlined in this article to encourage your curls to form.

Q: Can I make my hair curly again without using heat?

A: Yes, you can make your hair curly again without using heat. Try using a curl-defining product and letting your hair air dry, or using rollers or flexi rods to set your curls. Be sure to protect your curls while they dry by using a silk or satin scarf or bonnet.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat styling tools whenever possible
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf

With these tips and tricks, you can bring your curls back to life and enjoy bouncy, defined curls once again!
