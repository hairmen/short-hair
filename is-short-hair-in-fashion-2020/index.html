Is Short Hair In Fashion 2020

Written by Oliver Nov 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are thinking of getting a new haircut, you might be wondering if short hair is still in fashion in 2020. The answer is a resounding yes! Short hair has been in style for several years now, and it shows no signs of going out of fashion any time soon. In this article, we will explore the reasons why short hair is still fashionable in 2020 and provide tips on how to style and care for short hair.

Why is Short Hair in Fashion 2020?

Short hair has been in fashion for several years now, and there are several reasons why it is still popular in 2020. For one, short hair is low-maintenance and easy to style. It also looks great on all face shapes and hair types. Short hair is also versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked-back to messy and tousled.

How to Style Short Hair

Styling short hair is easy and requires minimal effort. You can either air-dry your hair or blow-dry it for a sleeker look. To add volume and texture, you can use a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse. You can also use a flat iron or curling wand to create loose waves or curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair

If you are thinking of cutting your hair short, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Section your hair into 4 parts using clips.
  3. Starting from the back, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Move to the side sections and cut them to the same length as the back.
  5. Finally, trim the front section to frame your face.

Remember to cut your hair slowly and carefully, and to use sharp scissors.

Tips for Caring for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it still needs some care to look its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

Q: Will short hair suit my face shape?

A: Yes, short hair looks great on all face shapes.

Q: Can I still style short hair if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can use volumizing products to add texture and volume to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: You should wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Conclusion

In conclusion, short hair is still in fashion in 2020 and is a great choice if you are looking for a low-maintenance, versatile hairstyle. Whether you have thick or thin hair, short hair looks great on all face shapes and can be styled in a variety of ways. Remember to care for your short hair by washing it regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling.
