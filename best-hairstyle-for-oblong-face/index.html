Best Hairstyle For Oblong Face Flattering Cuts For Your Long Face Shape

Written by Christine Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you're in luck! Your face shape is one of the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. However, it can be tricky to find the perfect cut that flatters your long face shape. In this article, we'll go over the best hairstyles for oblong face shapes, including how to style them, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a straight jawline and forehead. The goal of a hairstyle for an oblong face shape is to create width and balance. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of the face and creating softness around the jawline.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oblong face shapes, it's important to determine if you actually have an oblong face shape. Here's how:

  1. Pull your hair back away from your face.
  2. Take a good look at your face in the mirror.
  3. Is your face longer than it is wide?
  4. Do you have a straight jawline and forehead?

If you answered yes to these questions, then you likely have an oblong face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Best Hairstyle for Oblong Face Shapes

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle. Here are some tips:

1. Consider Bangs

One of the easiest ways to add width to your face is by adding bangs. Side-swept or curtain bangs are a great option for oblong face shapes. They help break up the length of the face and create softness around the jawline.

2. Embrace Layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. They can also help create movement, which is perfect for oblong face shapes. Consider getting layers that start at your cheekbones or chin to add width to your face.

3. Try a Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on oblong face shapes. It helps create width and balance by adding volume to the sides of the face. Consider a chin-length or shoulder-length bob for the best results.

4. Avoid Length

While longer hairstyles can work for oblong face shapes, they can also accentuate the length of your face. If you do decide to go long, make sure to add layers and volume to create balance.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Make sure to use products that are appropriate for your hair type and hairstyle. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture.

2. Style Regularly

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh, make sure to style it regularly. This can be as simple as blow-drying your hair with a round brush or using a curling iron to add some waves.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for maintaining any hairstyle. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

FAQ

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, but it can also look amazing on oblong face shapes. Consider getting layers that start at your cheekbones or chin to add width to your face. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your natural curls.

Can I wear my hair straight?

Absolutely! Straight hair can look great on oblong face shapes. Just make sure to add layers and volume to create balance.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Shapes

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any face shape. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Heat can damage your hair, so make sure to use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

3. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair healthy and shiny. Aim to use a deep conditioner once a week.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oblong face shapes, it's time to find the perfect cut for you. Whether you opt for bangs, layers, or a bob, remember to maintain your hairstyle with the right products and regular trims. With these tips, you'll have a flattering and stylish hairstyle that complements your oblong face shape.
