The Best Short Haircut For Thick Wavy Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, thick wavy hair that seems impossible to tame? Look no further than the perfect short haircut for your hair type. In this guide, we’ll introduce you to the best short haircut for thick wavy hair and provide you with tips for maintaining your new style. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The best short haircut for thick wavy hair is a layered bob. This style is perfect for those with thick hair because it provides movement and texture. The layers in the bob help to reduce the weight of thick hair, making it easier to manage. Additionally, the bob’s length allows for natural waves to shine through without weighing down the hair.

Another benefit of a layered bob is its versatility. The style can be customized to fit your face shape and hair texture. For example, those with round faces may opt for a longer bob, while those with square faces may choose a shorter style. Additionally, those with tighter waves may prefer a shorter bob, while those with looser waves may prefer a longer length.

How to

The first step in getting the perfect layered bob is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in working with thick wavy hair. Once you’ve found a stylist, have a consultation to discuss your desired length and style. Bring pictures of layered bobs that you like to help your stylist understand your vision.

During the haircut, your stylist will likely use thinning shears to remove bulk from your hair. This will help to create movement and texture. Additionally, they may use a razor to shape the ends of your hair, creating a softer look.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a layered bob:

  1. Find a hairstylist who is experienced in working with thick wavy hair.
  2. Have a consultation to discuss your desired length and style.
  3. Bring pictures of layered bobs that you like to help your stylist understand your vision.
  4. During the haircut, your stylist will likely use thinning shears to remove bulk from your hair.
  5. They may use a razor to shape the ends of your hair, creating a softer look.
  6. Leave the salon with a stylish, manageable new haircut!

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new layered bob:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your hair’s natural texture.
  • Apply a lightweight mousse or hair gel to damp hair to enhance your waves.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your bob.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short haircut for thick wavy hair:

Q: Will a layered bob work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, but the length and shape of the bob may need to be customized to fit your face shape.

Q: Is a layered bob easy to style?

A: Yes, a layered bob is relatively easy to style, especially if you have natural waves. Simply apply a lightweight styling product to damp hair and let it air dry for a tousled, textured look.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair with a layered bob?

A: Yes, a layered bob can be straightened, but keep in mind that the layers may create more volume than a straighter style.

Hair Care

Here are some general hair care tips for those with thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

With these tips, you’ll be well on your way to rocking the best short haircut for thick wavy hair. Say goodbye to unruly, unmanageable hair and hello to a stylish, low-maintenance new ‘do!
