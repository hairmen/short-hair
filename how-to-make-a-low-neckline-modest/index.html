How To Make A Low Neckline Modest

Written by Eveline Jun 06, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of feeling uncomfortable in a low neckline dress or top? Do you want to make it more modest without sacrificing style? In this article, we'll show you how to make a low neckline modest in a few easy steps.

Description

A low neckline can be a beautiful and fashionable choice, but it can also feel revealing and uncomfortable. Whether you're attending a formal event or just want to feel more covered up in your day-to-day life, there are a few simple tricks you can use to make a low neckline more modest.

How to Make a Low Neckline Modest

Step 1: Add a Camisole or Tank Top

One of the easiest ways to make a low neckline more modest is to add a camisole or tank top underneath. Look for a top that matches the color of your dress or top for a seamless look. You can also choose a contrasting color for a fun pop of color.

Step 2: Layer with a Blazer or Cardigan

If you want a more professional look, consider layering your low neckline top or dress with a blazer or cardigan. This will not only provide more coverage but also add a touch of sophistication to your outfit.

Step 3: Use a Scarf or Shawl

Another option for making a low neckline more modest is to use a scarf or shawl. Drape it around your neck and shoulders for added coverage and style. You can choose a lightweight scarf for a summer look or a thicker shawl for colder weather.

Step 4: Sew on a Modesty Panel

If you're handy with a needle and thread, you can sew on a modesty panel to your low neckline top or dress. This is a piece of fabric that is attached to the inside of the garment to cover up any exposed skin. You can use a matching fabric for a seamless look or choose a contrasting color for a fun detail.

Tips

  • Choose a camisole or tank top with adjustable straps for the perfect fit.
  • Experiment with different scarves and shawls to find the right style for your outfit.
  • If you're not confident in your sewing skills, consider taking your top or dress to a tailor for a professional-looking modesty panel.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a low neckline to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a style that is appropriate for the occasion and use one of the methods above to make it more modest.

Q: Can I still look fashionable while making a low neckline more modest?

A: Absolutely! Experiment with different layering options and accessories to find a look that works for you.

Q: Can I wear a necklace with a low neckline?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a necklace that complements the neckline of your top or dress.

Hair Care

When wearing a low neckline, it's important to consider your hairstyle as well. If you're wearing your hair down, make sure it's not covering up your neckline. If you're wearing your hair up, consider a sleek ponytail or bun to show off your neckline. And don't forget to use heat protectant and other hair care products to keep your locks looking healthy and shiny!
