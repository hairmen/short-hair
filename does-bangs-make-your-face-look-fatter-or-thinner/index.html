Does Bangs Make Your Face Look Fatter Or Thinner

Written by Petter Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to haircuts, one of the most popular questions asked is "does bangs make your face look fatter or thinner?" Bangs have been a trendy haircut for years, but figuring out whether they suit your face shape can be a challenge. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of bangs and how they can affect your face shape.

Description

Bangs are a type of haircut where the hair is cut short and styled to fall over the forehead. There are many different types of bangs, including straight, side-swept, and blunt. In general, bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some dimension to your hair. However, not all bangs work for every face shape.

How to

If you're considering getting bangs, there are a few things you should consider. First, think about your face shape. Certain types of bangs work better for certain face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. On the other hand, blunt bangs can make a round face appear even rounder.

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. If you have curly hair, bangs may require more maintenance and styling than if you have straight hair. You may also want to think about how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day. If you're someone who likes to keep things low-maintenance, bangs may not be the best option for you.

Step by Step

If you've decided to take the plunge and get bangs, here are some steps to follow:

  • Step 1: Choose the type of bangs you want. Consider your face shape and hair texture when making your decision.
  • Step 2: Find a hair stylist who is experienced in cutting bangs. It's important to find someone you trust to give you a great cut.
  • Step 3: Communicate with your stylist about the type of bangs you want. Show them pictures if you have a specific look in mind.
  • Step 4: After your bangs are cut, style them according to your preference.

Tips

If you're still unsure about whether bangs are right for you, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Tip 1: Consider getting clip-in bangs to try out the look before committing to a haircut.
  • Tip 2: Ask your stylist for their opinion on what type of bangs would suit your face shape and hair texture.
  • Tip 3: Keep in mind that bangs require more maintenance than other haircuts. You may need to trim them more frequently to keep them looking neat.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs:

Q: Will bangs make my face look fatter?

A: It depends on the type of bangs and your face shape. Blunt bangs can make a round face appear even rounder, while side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows, but most people need to trim their bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I style my bangs in different ways?

A: Yes, there are many different ways to style bangs. You can wear them straight, side-swept, or even pin them back if you want to change up your look.

Hair Care

If you're planning on getting bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy bangs:

  • Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil.
  • Tip 2: Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Tip 3: Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it appear dry and brittle.
  • Tip 4: Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Overall, whether bangs make your face look fatter or thinner depends on a variety of factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and the type of bangs you choose. If you're considering getting bangs, do some research and take the time to find a style that suits you. And remember, taking care of your hair is key to keeping it looking great!
