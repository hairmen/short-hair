How To Cut Women s Short Hair With Clippers At Home

Written by Bardi Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to cut your own hair at home, using clippers can be a great option for short haircuts. Not only is it more affordable than going to a salon, but it's also a great way to experiment with different styles. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut women's short hair with clippers at home, step by step.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's definitely doable. Using clippers can make the process much easier and faster, especially for short haircuts. It's important to have a clear idea of the style you want to achieve and to take your time to avoid any mistakes.

How To

Before you start, gather all the necessary tools: clippers, comb, hair clips, scissors, and a mirror. Start by washing and drying your hair, and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 1: Choose Your Guard Size

Clippers come with different guard sizes, which determine the length of the hair. Choose the guard size that matches the length you want to achieve. If you're unsure, start with a longer guard size and work your way down.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using hair clips, section your hair into manageable parts. Start with the back of your head and work your way up to the sides and top.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using your clippers, start cutting your hair in a downward motion, following the direction of the hair growth. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use the comb to lift the hair and get a more even cut.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Once you've finished the back, move to the sides. Use a shorter guard size to blend the sides with the back, and use the comb to lift the hair and get a more natural look.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move to the top of your head. Use a longer guard size and cut the hair in a downward motion, following the direction of the hair growth. Blend the top with the sides using the comb and a shorter guard size.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use the mirror to check your work and make sure everything looks even.
  • Experiment with different guard sizes to achieve the length you want.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can be difficult to fix mistakes.
  • Use scissors to trim any longer hairs or to add texture to your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting women's short hair with clippers:

  • Can I use clippers on wet hair?

    • It's best to use clippers on dry hair, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.

  • Can I use clippers to cut bangs?

    • Clippers are not recommended for cutting bangs, as they can be difficult to control. Use scissors instead.

  • Do I need to oil my clippers?

    • Yes, it's important to oil your clippers regularly to keep them running smoothly and prevent damage.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and style. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking fresh, and consider using hair masks or treatments to keep it nourished.

Cutting women's short hair with clippers at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut that suits your style and personality.
