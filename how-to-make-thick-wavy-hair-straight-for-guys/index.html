How To Make Thick Wavy Hair Straight For Guys

Written by Oliver Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick, wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse for guys. On one hand, it can give you a rugged, masculine look. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you're looking to switch things up and make your hair straight, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to make thick wavy hair straight for guys.

Description

Thick, wavy hair is characterized by its volume and texture. While it can look great on some guys, others may prefer a more sleek and polished look. Straightening your hair can help you achieve this look. However, it's important to note that straightening your hair can be damaging if not done properly.

How to

There are several methods you can use to make your thick wavy hair straight. Here are a few:

Method 1: Blow-drying

Blow-drying your hair is a quick and easy way to make it straight. Here's how:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any remaining waves or curls.
  5. Apply a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Method 2: Flat iron

A flat iron is a more direct way of straightening your hair. Here's how to use it:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  4. Starting at the roots, clamp the flat iron onto a section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends.
  5. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  6. Apply a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Step by Step

If you're new to straightening your hair, it can be helpful to follow a step-by-step guide. Here's one to follow:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  5. Starting at the roots, clamp the flat iron onto a section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends.
  6. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  7. Apply a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Straightening your hair can be damaging if not done properly. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Always use a heat protectant spray before using heat on your hair.
  • Don't straighten your hair every day. Give your hair a break to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair. Towel-dry your hair first.

FAQ

Is it safe to straighten my hair?

Straightening your hair can be damaging if not done properly. Always use a heat protectant spray before using heat on your hair. Don't straighten your hair every day, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

What if I have very thick hair?

If you have very thick hair, it may take longer to straighten. You may need to use a higher heat setting on your flat iron or blow-dryer. However, be careful not to damage your hair with too much heat.

Can I straighten my hair without heat?

Yes, there are ways to straighten your hair without heat. You can use a straightening brush or a hair straightening cream. However, these methods may not work as well on very thick or curly hair.

Hair Care

Straightening your hair can be damaging if not done properly. To keep your hair healthy, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair.
  • Don't straighten your hair every day.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and methods, you can make your thick, wavy hair straight and sleek. Just remember to take good care of your hair to prevent damage.
