How To Stop Hair Loss On Keto Diet Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you one of the many people experiencing hair loss while on a keto diet? Don't worry, you're not alone. In fact, hair loss is a common side effect of the keto diet due to the changes in your body's nutrient intake. However, there are ways to prevent and stop hair loss while still enjoying the benefits of the keto diet. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to stop hair loss on keto diet.

How Does Keto Diet Cause Hair Loss?

The keto diet is a high-fat and low-carb diet that forces your body to enter a state of ketosis, where it burns fat for energy instead of glucose. While this diet can lead to weight loss and improved health, it can also cause hair loss due to the following reasons:

  • Lack of Nutrients: The keto diet restricts carbohydrates, which are a primary source of essential nutrients like vitamins and minerals. These nutrients are essential for hair growth and maintenance, so a lack of them can lead to hair loss.
  • Increased Stress: The keto diet can cause stress on your body due to the sudden change in your diet. This can lead to increased cortisol levels, which can cause hair loss.
  • Dehydration: The keto diet can cause dehydration, which can lead to dry and brittle hair that is more prone to breakage.

How to Stop Hair Loss on Keto Diet

If you're experiencing hair loss while on a keto diet, here are some tips and tricks to help prevent and stop it:

1. Eat Nutrient-Dense Foods

While on a keto diet, it's important to eat nutrient-dense foods that are high in vitamins and minerals. Some examples include leafy greens, nuts, seeds, and avocados. Incorporating these foods into your diet can help prevent nutrient deficiencies that can lead to hair loss.

2. Take Supplements

In addition to eating nutrient-dense foods, taking supplements can also help prevent hair loss. Some supplements that can help include biotin, vitamin D, and zinc.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water is essential for preventing dehydration, which can lead to dry and brittle hair. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water a day while on a keto diet.

4. Reduce Stress

Reducing stress levels is important for preventing hair loss. Some ways to reduce stress include practicing yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises.

5. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can put stress on your hair, such as tight ponytails, braids, or buns. These hairstyles can lead to hair breakage and loss.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hair Loss Permanent on Keto Diet?

No, hair loss on a keto diet is not permanent. Once you address the underlying cause of your hair loss, your hair should start to grow back.

How Long Does Hair Loss Last on Keto Diet?

The duration of hair loss on a keto diet varies from person to person. Some people may experience hair loss for a few weeks, while others may experience it for several months. However, with the right treatment, hair loss should stop and hair growth should resume.

Can I Still Do Keto Diet if I'm Losing Hair?

Yes, you can still do a keto diet if you're losing hair. However, it's important to address the underlying cause of your hair loss and take steps to prevent further hair loss.

Hair Care Tips for Keto Diet

In addition to the tips mentioned above, here are some hair care tips to help prevent hair loss while on a keto diet:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as wet hair is more prone to breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb your hair when it's wet.

By following these tips and tricks, you can prevent and stop hair loss while on a keto diet. Remember, hair loss is a common side effect of the keto diet, but it doesn't have to be permanent. With the right treatment and hair care, your hair can grow back healthy and strong.
