What Hair Colour Suits Medium Brown Skin

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have medium brown skin and are looking to change up your hair colour, it can be difficult to know what shades will suit you best. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to figure out which colours will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural beauty. In this blog post, we’ll explore which hair colours are best for medium brown skin and provide some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium brown skin is a beautiful and versatile skin tone that can complement a variety of hair colours. When choosing a hair colour, it's important to consider your skin's undertones. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to opt for warmer hair colours, like golden blonde or auburn. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose cooler hair colours, like ash blonde or espresso brown.

How To

When deciding on a hair colour, consider your personal style and the look you want to achieve. If you're looking for a subtle change, try adding highlights or lowlights in a shade that complements your skin tone. If you're looking for a dramatic change, consider going for a bold colour like red or blue.

It's important to keep in mind that the colour of your hair will affect the overall appearance of your skin tone. If you have medium brown skin, avoid hair colours that are too light or too dark, as they can make your skin look washed out or muddy. Instead, opt for shades that are close to your natural hair colour, or choose a shade that is only a few shades lighter or darker than your natural colour.

Step By Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a hair colour for medium brown skin:

  1. Consider your skin's undertones
  2. Think about your personal style and the look you want to achieve
  3. Avoid colours that are too light or too dark
  4. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone
  5. Consult with a professional stylist if unsure

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a hair colour for medium brown skin:

  • Consider your eye colour when choosing a hair colour
  • Choose a shade that complements your wardrobe
  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your new colour
  • Avoid over-processing your hair, which can lead to damage
  • Consult with a professional stylist for advice on what shades will suit you best

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour for medium brown skin:

What hair colours should I avoid if I have medium brown skin?

Avoid hair colours that are too light or too dark, as they can make your skin look washed out or muddy. It's also best to avoid hair colours with cool undertones if you have warm undertones, and vice versa.

What hair colours will complement my medium brown skin?

If you have warm undertones, opt for warmer hair colours like golden blonde, auburn, or caramel. If you have cool undertones, choose cooler hair colours like ash blonde, espresso brown, or burgundy.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's important to maintain it with proper hair care. Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your new colour and prevent fading. Avoid over-processing your hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know what hair colour suits medium brown skin, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out your new look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
