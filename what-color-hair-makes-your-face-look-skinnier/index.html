What Color Hair Makes Your Face Look Skinnier

Written by Aprilia Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to slim down your face without any drastic measures, changing your hair color can be a great way to do it. Not all hair colors are created equal when it comes to making your face look slimmer, though. In this article, we'll explore which hair colors can help you achieve a more svelte look, as well as some tips for maintaining your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you're looking to make your face look slimmer, choosing the right hair color can make a big difference. Generally, lighter hair colors tend to make your face look thinner than darker colors, as they reflect more light and create the illusion of depth. However, there are some nuances to consider, such as your skin tone and the shape of your face, that can affect which hair color is most flattering for you.

How To

The first step in choosing a hair color that will make your face look slimmer is to determine your skin tone. Generally, people with cool undertones (pink or blue) in their skin look best with cooler hair colors, like platinum blonde or ash brown. People with warm undertones (yellow or gold) in their skin look best with warmer hair colors, like golden blonde or chestnut brown. If you're unsure what your skin tone is, try holding up different colored fabrics to your face in natural light to see which colors look best.

Step By Step

Once you've determined your skin tone, it's time to choose a hair color that will complement it. If you have a round face, a lighter hair color with highlights can help create the illusion of angles and make your face look slimmer. If you have a square face, a darker hair color can help soften your features and create a more balanced look. If you have a heart-shaped face, a hair color that's darker at the roots and lighter at the ends can help balance out your features.

When you're ready to dye your hair, it's important to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair or creating an uneven color. Consider going to a professional stylist, especially if you're making a drastic change, to ensure that the color is applied evenly and looks natural.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a hair color that will make your face look slimmer:

  • Consider your eye color as well as your skin tone when choosing a hair color.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your natural skin tone, rather than one that completely contrasts it.
  • Opt for highlights or lowlights rather than a single, solid color to create more depth and dimension.
  • Consider getting a haircut to complement your new hair color and create a more balanced look.

FAQ

What hair colors make your face look slimmer?

Generally, lighter hair colors tend to make your face look thinner than darker colors, as they reflect more light and create the illusion of depth. However, the most flattering hair color for you depends on your skin tone and the shape of your face.

Can hair color change the shape of your face?

Hair color can create the illusion of a slimmer or more balanced face shape, but it can't actually change the underlying bone structure of your face.

How often should I dye my hair to maintain my new color?

The frequency with which you need to dye your hair depends on the type of color you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain a consistent color.

Hair Care

After you dye your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading, and avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can cause damage to your hair. Consider getting regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain a healthy length.

With the right hair color and proper care, you can achieve a slimmer, more balanced look without any drastic measures. Experiment with different shades to find the one that works best for you, and enjoy your new, confident look!
