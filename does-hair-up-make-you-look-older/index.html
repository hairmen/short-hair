Does Hair Up Make You Look Older

Written by Oliver Sep 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many women wonder whether wearing their hair up can make them look older. The answer is not that simple, as it depends on various factors such as the hairstyle, the face shape, and the age of the person wearing it. Some updos can make you look more mature, while others can make you look younger and fresher.

How to

The first step to determining whether an updo will make you look older is to consider your face shape. People with round faces may find that wearing their hair up makes them look more mature, while those with angular or heart-shaped faces may look younger with an updo. Additionally, the type of updo you choose can also impact how old you appear. A sleek, tight bun can make you look more mature, while a messy, textured updo can make you look younger.

Step by Step

To create a youthful-looking updo, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create loose waves with a curling iron or wand.
  3. Sweep your hair up and to the side, leaving some loose pieces around your face.
  4. Secure your hair with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy, textured look.

Tips

When choosing an updo, consider your outfit and the occasion. A sleek, polished bun may be appropriate for a formal event, while a messy bun may be better suited for a casual outing. Additionally, consider adding accessories such as a headband or hair clips to add some interest to your updo.

FAQ

Q: Does wearing your hair up always make you look older?

A: No, it depends on the style of the updo and your face shape.

Q: What are some updos that can make you look younger?

A: Messy buns, braids, and soft curls can all create a youthful look.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and using heat protectant products can all help keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Additionally, be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.

In conclusion, wearing your hair up does not always make you look older. It depends on the style of the updo and your face shape. By choosing a youthful-looking updo and taking care of your hair, you can create a fresh, youthful appearance no matter what your age.
