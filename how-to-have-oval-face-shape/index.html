How To Have An Oval Face Shape Tips Tricks And Hair Care

In this article, we will be discussing how to have an oval face shape. Having an oval face shape is considered to be the most desirable as it is the most versatile and suits almost all hairstyles and makeup looks. However, not everyone is naturally blessed with an oval face shape. But fear not, with the right tips, tricks, and hair care, you can achieve an oval face shape that will make you look and feel confident.

Table of Contents

  • How To Achieve an Oval Face Shape
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining an Oval Face Shape
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

How To Achieve an Oval Face Shape

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it is essential to understand what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape is characterized by being longer than it is wide, with a rounded hairline and jawline. To achieve an oval face shape, you can use the following techniques:

  • Contouring
  • Hairstyling
  • Makeup
  • Skin Care

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve an oval face shape:

  1. Start by contouring your face using a contour kit. Apply the darker shade along your hairline, temples, and the sides of your forehead. Use the lighter shade on your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your jawline. Blend it well to create a natural-looking shadow.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face, such as a layered haircut or a side-swept fringe. Avoid hairstyles that add volume around your cheeks, as this can make your face appear wider.
  3. Use makeup to enhance your features. For example, apply eyeliner and mascara to make your eyes look larger or use lipstick to draw attention to your lips.
  4. Take care of your skin by cleansing, toning, and moisturizing regularly. Use products that are suitable for your skin type and avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your skin.

Tips for Maintaining an Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips to help you maintain an oval face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume around your cheeks.
  • Use makeup to enhance your features.
  • Take care of your skin by cleansing, toning, and moisturizing regularly.
  • Avoid eating too much salt, which can cause water retention and make your face appear bloated.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about achieving an oval face shape:

  • Is it possible to change the shape of my face? While you cannot change the bone structure of your face, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and skincare to enhance your features and create the illusion of an oval face shape.
  • Can I achieve an oval face shape without contouring? While contouring is an effective way to create the illusion of an oval face shape, you can also achieve this look by choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and skincare products.
  • How often should I cleanse, tone, and moisturize my skin? You should cleanse, tone, and moisturize your skin twice a day, once in the morning and once before bed.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for oval face shapes:

  • Choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face, such as a layered haircut or a side-swept fringe.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume around your cheeks, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach, which can cause breakage and damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, tricks, and hair care, you can achieve an oval face shape that will make you look and feel confident. Remember, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, and what matters most is how you feel about yourself.
