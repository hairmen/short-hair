How To Give Straight Hair Volume Overnight

Written by Oliver Aug 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair falling flat and lifeless? Do you want to add volume and bounce to your hair without using heat or chemicals? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to give straight hair volume overnight.

Table of Contents

  • How to Give Straight Hair Volume Overnight
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Volume

How to Give Straight Hair Volume Overnight

There are several ways to give straight hair volume overnight. Here are some simple and effective methods:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner in the shower. These products are designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

2. Apply a volumizing mousse or foam

After towel-drying your hair, apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your roots. This will help lift your hair and add volume. Use your fingers to massage the product into your roots.

3. Use a root-lifting spray

If you have fine or thin hair, use a root-lifting spray to add volume to your roots. Spray the product directly onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers.

4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer

If you must use a blow dryer, attach a diffuser to it. This will help distribute the heat evenly and prevent your hair from getting frizzy. Use a low heat setting and dry your hair until it is about 80% dry.

5. Sleep with your hair in a bun

Before going to bed, put your hair up in a high bun. Make sure to secure it with bobby pins or a scrunchie. This will help create volume and texture in your hair overnight.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to give straight hair volume overnight:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your roots
  3. Spray a root-lifting spray onto your roots
  4. Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use a low heat setting to dry your hair until it is 80% dry
  5. Put your hair up in a high bun before going to bed

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you give your straight hair volume overnight:

  • Avoid using heat-styling tools like flat irons and curling irons
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can flatten it
  • If you must brush your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or a boar bristle brush
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add volume and movement to your hair

FAQs

Can I use dry shampoo to add volume to my hair?

Yes, you can use dry shampoo to add volume to your hair. Spray the dry shampoo onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers. This will help absorb any excess oil and add texture to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise regularly or live in a humid environment, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Hair Care for Volume

To maintain volume in your hair, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any product buildup
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down

By following these tips and tricks, you can give your straight hair volume overnight and enjoy bouncy, voluminous locks all day long.
