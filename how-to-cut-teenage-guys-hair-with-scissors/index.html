How To Cut Teenage Guys Hair With Scissors

Written by Bardi Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money and cut your teenage son's hair at home, using scissors may be the perfect solution. With a little practice, you can achieve a great haircut without spending a lot of money at the salon. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to cut teenage guys hair with scissors.

Description

Cutting your teenage son's hair at home can be a great way to save money and bond with your child. With a little bit of patience and practice, you can learn how to cut his hair with scissors and achieve a great-looking haircut.

How to

Before you start cutting your son's hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here's how to cut teenage guys hair with scissors:

Step 1: Wash The Hair

Start by washing your son's hair and towel drying it. Wet hair is easier to cut and style, so make sure it's damp but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Comb The Hair

Use a comb to detangle your son's hair and make sure it's smooth and easy to work with.

Step 3: Section The Hair

Take a section of hair from the top of your son's head and clip the rest of the hair out of the way. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly.

Step 4: Cut The Hair

Using your scissors, cut the hair to your desired length. Start at the back and work your way up to the top of the head. Make sure to cut small sections of hair at a time, and use the comb to guide the scissors.

Step 5: Trim The Sides

Once you've cut the top of your son's hair, it's time to trim the sides. Use your scissors to cut the hair evenly, starting at the top of the ear and working your way down to the neckline.

Step 6: Blend The Hair

Use your scissors to blend the hair around the sides and back of your son's head. This will create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 7: Style The Hair

Once you've finished cutting your son's hair, use a little bit of hair product to style it. This will help keep the hair in place and create a polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to make the cutting process easier.
  • Cut the hair in small sections to ensure evenness.
  • Take breaks if you or your son become frustrated or tired.
  • Watch online tutorials to get a better understanding of the process.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting teenage guys hair with scissors:

Can I cut my son's hair if he has curly hair?

Yes, you can cut curly hair with scissors. However, it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry, as wet hair can shrink and change shape as it dries.

How often should I cut my son's hair?

It depends on how quickly his hair grows and how short he likes to keep it. Generally, a haircut every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

How do I know what length to cut my son's hair?

It's important to communicate with your son about the length he wants. Use the clipper guards or scissors to achieve the desired length.

Hair Care

After cutting your son's hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition the hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Use a hair product that suits your son's hair type to keep it in place and looking good.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

By following these tips and steps, you can learn how to cut teenage guys hair with scissors and achieve a great-looking haircut at home.
