Simple Curly Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have medium length curly hair, you are in for a treat with this article! We have compiled a list of simple and easy hairstyles that will help you switch up your look without a lot of effort. Whether you are heading to work or an event, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion. Here are our top picks:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

When it comes to curly hair, it can be tough to find hairstyles that work for your texture and length. However, with medium length hair, you have a lot of options to choose from. Whether you like to wear your hair up or down, there are plenty of styles that you can try out.

How To

The great thing about these hairstyles is that they are all fairly easy to achieve. You don't need a lot of fancy tools or products to style your hair. All you need are some hair ties, bobby pins, and maybe some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of our favorite hairstyles:

The Half-Up, Half-Down

  1. Start by taking the top half of your hair and gently pulling it back.
  2. Secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  3. Fluff up the curls on the top of your head to create volume.
  4. Finish with hairspray to keep everything in place.

The Braided Crown

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and braid it.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Bring the braids to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep everything in place.

The Messy Bun

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some pieces of hair to create a messy look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help combat frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as it can cause them to become frizzy.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your curls.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles!

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight sometimes?

A: Of course! It's your hair, so you can wear it however you like. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid straightening it too often, as this can cause damage.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It really depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Some people can go a week without washing their hair, while others need to wash it every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

When it comes to curly hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like argan oil or shea butter.

Additionally, try to avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can cause damage to your hair. If you do use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Finally, be gentle with your hair when you're styling it. Don't pull or tug on it too hard, as this can cause breakage. Instead, work with your curls and embrace their natural texture.
