How To Get Cool Hairstyles In Animal Crossing

If you're a fan of Animal Crossing, you know how important it is to have a great hairstyle. It's a way to express yourself and stand out from the other villagers. But how do you get cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing? In this article, we'll show you exactly how to do it.

How to Get Cool Hairstyles

There are a few ways to get cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

  • Visit Harriet, the hair stylist at Shampoodle.
  • Use a mirror or vanity to change your hairstyle at home.
  • Get a makeover from a visiting NPC, like Gracie or Wendell.

Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages, so let's take a closer look at each one.

Visit Harriet at Shampoodle

Harriet is the hair stylist at Shampoodle, a salon located in the city. To unlock Shampoodle, you'll need to spend at least 10,000 bells at Able Sisters and have at least one haircut. Once you meet these requirements, Harriet will appear in the city and offer to change your hairstyle.

At Shampoodle, you can choose from a variety of hairstyles, hair colors, and eye colors. You can also get a Mii mask, which allows you to use your Mii's face as your character's face. Keep in mind that each haircut costs 3,000 bells, so make sure you have enough money before you go.

Use a Mirror or Vanity

If you don't want to go all the way to the city to change your hairstyle, you can use a mirror or vanity in your home. These items allow you to change your hairstyle, hair color, and eye color for free. However, the selection of hairstyles is limited compared to Shampoodle.

To use a mirror or vanity, simply stand in front of it and press A. You'll then be prompted to choose a new hairstyle.

Get a Makeover from a Visiting NPC

From time to time, visiting NPCs like Gracie or Wendell will offer to give you a makeover. Gracie will appear in the city and offer to give you a fashion makeover, while Wendell will appear on your island and offer to give you a pattern makeover. Both of these makeovers can change your hairstyle as well.

Keep in mind that these makeovers are rare and random, so you can't rely on them to get a new hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a cool hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  1. Unlock Shampoodle by spending at least 10,000 bells at Able Sisters and having at least one haircut.
  2. Visit Harriet at Shampoodle.
  3. Select "Change hairstyle."
  4. Choose a new hairstyle, hair color, and eye color.
  5. Purchase the new hairstyle for 3,000 bells.
  6. If you don't want to go to the city, use a mirror or vanity in your home to change your hairstyle for free.
  7. Stand in front of the mirror or vanity and press A.
  8. Choose a new hairstyle, hair color, and eye color.
  9. If a visiting NPC offers you a makeover, accept it to potentially get a new hairstyle.
  10. Enjoy your new hairstyle!

Tips for Getting the Best Hairstyles

Getting a cool hairstyle in Animal Crossing is all about personal preference, but here are some tips to help you get the best one:

  • Try on different hairstyles before you buy one. You can preview each hairstyle before you purchase it at Shampoodle.
  • Consider your character's personality and style when choosing a new hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair colors and eye colors.
  • Get inspiration from other players' hairstyles or from real-life hairstyles.

FAQ

How many hairstyles are in Animal Crossing?

There are over 80 different hairstyles in Animal Crossing, including short hairstyles, long hairstyles, curly hairstyles, and more.

Can I change my hairstyle more than once?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want, but each haircut at Shampoodle will cost 3,000 bells.

Can I change my character's gender?

No, you cannot change your character's gender in Animal Crossing.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

Once you have a cool new hairstyle, you'll want to take care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Visit Harriet at Shampoodle every once in a while to try out new hairstyles.
  • Use a mirror or vanity to change your hairstyle at home if you don't want to spend money at Shampoodle.
  • Don't forget to wash your hair regularly by using a shower or bathtub.
  • Accessorize your hair with hats or hairpins to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

Now that you know how to get cool hairstyles in Animal Crossing, it's time to go out there and get the perfect one for your character. Whether you go to Shampoodle, use a mirror or vanity, or get a makeover from a visiting NPC, there are plenty of options to choose from. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and expressing your unique style!
