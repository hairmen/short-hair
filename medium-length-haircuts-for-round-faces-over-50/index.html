Medium Length Haircuts For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Petter Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a woman over 50 with a round face looking for a new haircut? Medium length haircuts are perfect for those who want a versatile style that's easy to maintain. In this article, we'll show you some of the best medium length haircuts for round faces over 50, including how to style them and tips for keeping your hair healthy.

Description

Medium length haircuts are typically around shoulder length, making them perfect for those who want a style that's not too short or too long. For women over 50 with round faces, medium length haircuts can help to elongate the face and create a more youthful appearance. These haircuts are also easy to style and maintain, making them a popular choice among busy women.

How to

If you're considering a medium length haircut for your round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure that your stylist is experienced in working with round faces. They should be able to recommend a haircut that will flatter your face shape and bring out your best features.

Once you've chosen your haircut, it's important to keep it healthy and styled. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny, and consider using a styling product to add volume or texture. Regular trims will also help to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best medium length haircuts for round faces over 50:

1. Shoulder Length Bob

A shoulder length bob is a classic haircut that looks great on round faces. This haircut is easy to style and can be worn straight or with loose waves. To style, simply blow dry your hair with a round brush and apply a styling product for added volume.

2. Layered Lob

A layered lob is another great haircut for round faces over 50. This style features long layers that create movement and texture. To style, use a curling iron to add loose waves and finish with a texturizing spray for a beachy look.

3. Shaggy Layers

A shaggy layered haircut is perfect for those who want a low maintenance style that's still chic and modern. This haircut features choppy layers that add volume and texture to the hair. To style, simply apply a texturizing spray and scrunch your hair with your hands.

Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your medium length haircut looking its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it looking fresh and healthy
  • Use a styling product to add volume or texture to your hair
  • Try different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length haircuts for round faces over 50:

What is the best haircut for a round face over 50?

Medium length haircuts like shoulder length bobs, layered lobs, and shaggy layers are all great options for round faces over 50.

How do I style a medium length haircut?

To style a medium length haircut, use a styling product to add volume or texture, and use a curling iron or straightener to create your desired look.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny. It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

By following these tips and choosing the right medium length haircut for your round face, you can achieve a chic and stylish look that's perfect for women over 50.
