How To Style Male Hair Messy

Written by Daniel Nov 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle? Do you want to add some edge to your look? Messy hair might be the perfect solution for you! In this article, we'll show you how to style male hair messy in a relaxed and effortless way. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Male Hair Messy
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Messy hair is a popular trend that has been around for a while now. It's a style that is easy to achieve and looks effortless. Messy hair gives off a relaxed and carefree vibe that many men find appealing. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still looks stylish.

How to Style Male Hair Messy

Before you start styling your hair, make sure you have the right products. You'll need a good quality texturizing spray or pomade to achieve the messy look. Here's how to style male hair messy:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or oil that may be weighing down your hair.

2. Towel dry your hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it's damp. Don't dry it completely as it will be easier to style when it's slightly damp.

3. Apply texturizing spray or pomade

Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way up to the tips. Make sure you distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

4. Use your fingers to style your hair

Use your fingers to style your hair in the direction you want it to go. Don't worry about making it perfect, the messy look is all about imperfection. Scrunch your hair up to add more texture and volume.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hairspray to help hold your hair in place. You don't want to use too much as it will make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style male hair messy:

  1. Wash your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply texturizing spray or pomade
  4. Use your fingers to style your hair
  5. Finish with hairspray

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

  • Don't use too much product as it will make your hair look greasy and heavy.
  • Use a hairdryer to add more volume to your hair.
  • If you have long hair, try tying it back into a messy bun or ponytail.
  • Experiment with different products and hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to style male hair messy:

What products should I use to achieve the messy look?

You can use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and volume to your hair.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

Use a small amount of product and don't apply it to your roots. This will help to prevent your hair from looking greasy.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as they can cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to style male hair messy, why not give it a try? It's the perfect way to add some edge to your look and show off your laid-back style.
