How To Cut Men s Hair Shorter On The Sides

Written by Robby Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting men's hair shorter on the sides is a classic and stylish haircut that has been popular for decades. It is a simple but effective way to give a cleaner and more polished look to any man's hairstyle. In this article, we will be discussing how to cut men's hair shorter on the sides, step by step, along with tips, frequently asked questions and hair care advice.

How to Cut Men's Hair Shorter on the Sides

Before starting the haircut, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a clipper with different guard sizes. Follow these steps to achieve a shorter haircut on the sides:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry the hair with a towel or a hairdryer. Make sure the hair is completely dry before starting the haircut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Choose the right guard size for the clipper. A smaller guard size will result in a shorter haircut. If you are not sure which guard size to use, start with a larger size and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start from the sideburns and work your way up to the temple. Use the clipper to cut the hair in an upward motion against the direction of the hair growth. Repeat this step on the other side.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair between the clipped area and the top of the head. Comb the hair down and cut the hair that sticks out with scissors. Do this on both sides until you achieve a smooth blend.

Step 5: Check the Length

Check the length of the hair on the sides. If it is too long, use the clipper or scissors to trim it down until you achieve the desired length.

Tips

  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair between the clipped area and the top of the head for a smoother transition.
  • Trim the hair in small sections to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head for any unevenness.
  • Consult a professional hairdresser if you are not confident in cutting your own hair.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the length of your hair and your desired style. For shorter hairstyles, it is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Can I cut my hair when it is wet?

It is not recommended to cut hair when it is wet as it can result in an uneven cut. It is best to cut hair when it is dry as it is easier to see the shape and length.

Can I use any clipper guard size?

No, it is important to choose the right guard size for your desired length. Using the wrong guard size can result in a shorter or longer haircut than intended.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when using hair styling tools. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Cutting men's hair shorter on the sides is a simple and stylish haircut that can be achieved at home with the right tools and techniques. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve a polished and clean look.
