Best Men s Low Fade Haircuts

Written by Christine Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that is stylish and low maintenance? Look no further than the low fade haircut for men. This popular style has been around for years and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we will discuss the best men's low fade haircuts, how to achieve the look, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Achieve the Look
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintenance
  5. FAQ

Description

The low fade is a popular haircut for men that features a gradual taper from short hair on the sides and back to longer hair on top. The fade is typically a shorter length on the sides and back, gradually getting longer as it goes higher up the head. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit any preference, from a subtle fade to a more dramatic look.

How to Achieve the Look

Before getting a low fade haircut, it's important to have a clear idea of what you want. Bring pictures or examples of the style you're looking for to your barber or stylist. They will be able to recommend the best version of the low fade for your hair type and face shape.

The low fade can be achieved with clippers or scissors, depending on the desired length and texture. The sides and back are typically clipped with a shorter guard, while the top is left longer and layered for texture. The fade should be blended seamlessly for a polished finish.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and towel dry hair.
  2. Section off the top of the hair and clip it out of the way.
  3. Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down the sides and back of the head, using shorter guards to achieve the desired length.
  4. Blend the fade gradually for a polished finish.
  5. Unclip the top of the hair and cut to desired length and texture.
  6. Blend the top into the fade for a seamless finish.
  7. Clean up the neckline and sideburns for a refined look.
  8. Style as desired.

Tips for Maintenance

To maintain a low fade haircut, regular trims are recommended every 4-6 weeks. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep hair healthy and nourished. Use a styling product to add texture and hold, such as a pomade or wax. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh hair down and create a greasy appearance.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a low fade?

The low fade is a versatile haircut that can suit many face shapes. However, it is particularly flattering on those with a square or oval face shape.

Can the low fade be customized?

Yes, the low fade can be customized to suit any preference. Ask your barber or stylist for recommendations based on your hair type and face shape.

Is the low fade high maintenance?

No, the low fade is a relatively low maintenance haircut that requires regular trims and minimal styling.

Hair Care

To keep hair healthy and nourished, use a quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage hair. Use a styling product to add texture and hold, but avoid using too much product. Regular trims are recommended every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the low fade.

In conclusion, the low fade is a popular and versatile haircut for men that can be customized to suit any preference. With regular maintenance and proper hair care, this style can be a low maintenance option that looks great on any occasion.
