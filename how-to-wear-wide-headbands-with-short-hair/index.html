How To Wear Wide Headbands With Short Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that wide headbands are not for you. But, the truth is, wide headbands can look great on any hair length, including short hair. In this article, we will show you how to wear wide headbands with short hair.

Description

A wide headband is a stylish accessory that can add a pop of color and texture to any outfit. It is a versatile accessory that can be worn in a variety of ways. However, if you have short hair, you may be wondering how to wear a wide headband without looking like you have a huge forehead. The good news is that there are many ways to wear a wide headband with short hair that will look great on you.

How to Wear Wide Headbands with Short Hair

Here are some tips on how to wear wide headbands with short hair:

1. Choose the Right Headband

When choosing a wide headband, make sure to choose one that is proportionate to your head size. If you have a small head, choose a narrower headband. If you have a larger head, choose a wider headband. You should also choose a headband that complements your hair color and skin tone.

2. Wear the Headband High on Your Forehead

If you have short hair, wearing the headband high on your forehead can create the illusion of a longer face. This will make your face look more proportional and balanced. You can also wear the headband slightly tilted to one side for a more feminine look.

3. Wear the Headband Low on Your Forehead

If you prefer a more casual look, you can wear the headband low on your forehead. This will create a bohemian look that is perfect for summer. You can wear the headband with your hair down or in a messy bun.

4. Wear the Headband with a Ponytail

If you have short hair, you can wear the headband with a ponytail. This will create a cute and playful look that is perfect for a day out with friends. You can also wear the headband with a messy bun or a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

5. Wear the Headband with a Bob

If you have a bob hairstyle, you can wear the headband with your hair tucked behind your ears. This will create a sleek and polished look that is perfect for work. You can also wear the headband with your hair down for a more casual look.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to wear a wide headband with short hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Headband

Choose a headband that complements your hair color and skin tone. Make sure to choose a headband that is proportionate to your head size.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to style your hair with the headband.

Step 3: Wear the Headband High on Your Forehead

Place the headband on your forehead and slide it back towards your hairline. Make sure to wear the headband high on your forehead to create the illusion of a longer face.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair with the headband. You can wear your hair down or in a ponytail, messy bun, or half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a wide headband with short hair:

1. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that complements your headband. If you are wearing a high headband, wear your hair down or in a half-up, half-down hairstyle. If you are wearing a low headband, wear your hair in a messy bun or a ponytail.

2. Experiment with Different Colors and Textures

Experiment with different colors and textures to find the headband that best complements your hair and skin tone. You can choose a headband with a bold pattern or a solid color.

3. Play with Accessories

You can also play with accessories to add more style to your look. You can wear earrings or a necklace that complements your headband.

FAQ

Can I wear a wide headband with short hair?

Yes, you can wear a wide headband with short hair. Just make sure to choose a headband that is proportionate to your head size and complements your hair color and skin tone.

How do I wear a wide headband with short hair?

You can wear a wide headband with short hair by wearing the headband high on your forehead, low on your forehead, with a ponytail, with a bob, or with a half-up, half-down hairstyle.

Hair Care

When wearing a headband, it is important to take care of your hair. Make sure to brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know how to wear wide headbands with short hair, go ahead and try out different styles to find the one that works best for you. With the right headband and hairstyle, you can create a stylish and chic look that is perfect for any occasion.
