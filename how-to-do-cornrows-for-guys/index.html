How To Do Cornrows For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy who wants to try out a new hairstyle, cornrows might be just what you're looking for. Cornrows are a traditional African hairstyle that involves braiding the hair into a series of tight, narrow rows. In this article, we'll show you how to do cornrows for guys in a relaxed, easy-to-understand way. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced braider, you'll find everything you need to know right here.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Do Cornrows for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Getting the Perfect Cornrows

How to Do Cornrows for Guys

Before you start braiding, you'll need to gather a few supplies:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Rat-tail comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair elastics
  • Bobby pins

Once you have your supplies ready, you can start braiding:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by parting your hair into the desired number of sections. For a classic cornrow look, you'll want to create rows that run from front to back. Use the rat-tail comb to create straight lines.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to the first section of hair. This will help keep your hair smooth and make it easier to braid.
  3. Divide the first section of hair into three equal parts, and start braiding by crossing the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the middle strand.
  4. Continue braiding, but add small sections of hair to the right and left strands each time you cross them over the middle strand.
  5. Keep braiding until you reach the end of the section, then secure the braid with a hair elastic.
  6. Repeat the process for each section of hair, making sure to keep your braids neat and uniform.
  7. Once you've finished braiding all of the sections, use bobby pins to secure any loose ends and create a clean, polished look.
  8. If desired, you can also add beads or other decorative elements to your cornrows.
  9. When you're ready to take your cornrows out, simply remove the hair elastics and gently unravel the braids.
  10. Wash and condition your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup, and give your scalp a gentle massage to promote healthy hair growth.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Cornrows

Here are a few tips to help you get the best possible results:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. Dirty or oily hair can be more difficult to braid, and may not hold the style as well.
  • Use a rat-tail comb to create straight, even parts. This will help you create neat, uniform braids.
  • Don't braid too tightly. Tight braids can cause discomfort or even damage to the scalp and hair follicles.
  • Use hair gel or pomade to keep your hair smooth and make it easier to braid.
  • Take breaks if your arms or hands start to feel tired. Braiding can be time-consuming and physically demanding, so it's important to take care of yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few common questions and answers about cornrows for guys:

  • Will cornrows damage my hair? Cornrows themselves don't cause damage, but tight braids or wearing them for extended periods of time can lead to breakage or hair loss. Be sure to take breaks, and don't braid your hair too tightly.
  • How long do cornrows last? Cornrows can last anywhere from a few days to several weeks, depending on how well you take care of them and how quickly your hair grows.
  • Can I style my cornrows in different ways? Yes! Once you've created your cornrows, you can style them in a variety of ways, including updos, ponytails, and half-up, half-down styles.

Hair Care Tips for Cornrows

Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great while you have cornrows:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or greasy products on your scalp, as these can clog your hair follicles and lead to breakouts or other skin issues.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate your braids, and avoid pulling or tugging on them.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet. This will help prevent frizz and keep your hair looking neat and polished.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create perfect cornrows for guys every time. Whether you're looking for a new style or simply want to switch things up, cornrows are a great choice for any guy who wants to look his best.
