How To Stop Hair Fall Immediately At Home For Female In Malayalam

Are you tired of seeing strands of hair on your pillow, in the shower, and on your clothes? Hair fall can be a distressing problem for women of all ages. If you are looking for a natural solution to stop hair fall immediately at home, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will share tips and tricks on how to stop hair fall immediately at home for female in Malayalam.

Step by Step Guide:

1. Aloe Vera - Aloe vera is a natural ingredient that can help to prevent hair fall. Take an aloe vera leaf and extract the gel. Apply the gel on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with lukewarm water.


2. Onion Juice - Onion juice has sulphur that can help to regenerate hair follicles. Extract the juice from an onion and apply it on your scalp. Leave it for 30 minutes and rinse it off with a mild shampoo.


3. Coconut Oil - Coconut oil is an excellent natural remedy for hair fall. Massage your scalp with coconut oil and leave it overnight. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo in the morning.


4. Egg Mask - Egg contains protein that can help to strengthen hair. Beat an egg and apply it on your hair. Leave it for 30 minutes and rinse it off with a mild shampoo.


5. Fenugreek Seeds - Fenugreek seeds are an excellent source of protein and nicotinic acid that can help to prevent hair fall. Soak the seeds in water overnight and grind them into a paste. Apply the paste on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with lukewarm water.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall:

1. Eat a balanced diet rich in protein, iron, and vitamins.


2. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.


3. Do not tie your hair too tightly.


4. Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair.


5. Get enough sleep and exercise regularly.

FAQs:

1. How long will it take to see results?


Ans: It depends on the severity of your hair fall. You may see results in a few weeks or it may take a few months.


2. Can I use these remedies every day?


Ans: No, it is not recommended to use these remedies every day. Use them once or twice a week.


3. Can stress cause hair fall?


Ans: Yes, stress can cause hair fall. Try to manage your stress levels by practicing yoga, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.

Hair Care Routine:

1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo every 2-3 days.


2. Use a conditioner after shampooing.


3. Avoid using hot water to wash your hair.


4. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.


5. Oil your hair regularly with coconut or olive oil.

In conclusion, hair fall is a common problem that can be easily prevented with natural remedies and a proper hair care routine. Follow these tips and tricks to stop hair fall immediately at home for female in Malayalam.
