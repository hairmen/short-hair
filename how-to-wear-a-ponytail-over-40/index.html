How To Wear A Ponytail Over 40

Written by Aprilia Jun 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 40 and wondering if you can still rock a ponytail? The answer is yes! Ponytails are not just for young girls, they can be a chic and sophisticated hairstyle for women of all ages. In this article, we will show you how to wear a ponytail over 40 and give you some tips on how to make it look age-appropriate.

How to:

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will help ensure that your ponytail is smooth and sleek.

Step 2: Decide where you want to position your ponytail. Do you want it high on your head, or low at the nape of your neck? This will depend on the occasion and your personal preference.

Step 3: Gather your hair into a ponytail with your hands. Use a hair tie to secure it in place. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 4: Smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair with a comb or your fingers. You can also use a bit of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

Tip 1: Choose the right hair tie. Avoid using elastic bands that can damage your hair. Instead, opt for a soft scrunchie or a fabric-covered hair tie.

Tip 2: Add some volume. Use a volumizing spray or mousse at the roots of your hair before pulling it into a ponytail. This will give your hair some lift and make your ponytail look more voluminous.

Tip 3: Play with different styles. You can try a messy ponytail or a sleek and straight ponytail. Experiment with different textures and accessories to make your ponytail look unique.

FAQ:

Can I wear a ponytail if I have short hair?

Yes, you can! While it may not be a traditional ponytail, you can still gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie. This will create a mini-ponytail that is perfect for short hair.

Can I wear a ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, you can! A sleek and sophisticated ponytail can be a great hairstyle for a formal event. Just make sure to keep it neat and tidy, and consider adding some accessories like a sparkly hair clip or a headband.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you wear it in a ponytail regularly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to wear a ponytail over 40, go ahead and try it out! With these tips and tricks, you can rock a ponytail that is both stylish and age-appropriate.
