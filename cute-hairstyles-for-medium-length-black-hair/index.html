Cute Hairstyles For Medium Length Black Hair

Written by Robby Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length black hair, you're in luck! There are so many cute hairstyles that you can try. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best hairstyles for medium length black hair. We'll also provide tips on how to achieve these hairstyles and how to take care of your hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Medium Length Black Hair
  2. Step by Step Hairstyles
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Style Medium Length Black Hair

Before we jump into the hairstyles, it's important to know how to style medium length black hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Don't wash your hair too often to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

Step by Step Hairstyles

1. Twisted Updo

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Take one section and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure it with a bobby pin. Repeat on the other side. Take the remaining hair and twist it into a bun at the back of your head. Secure with bobby pins.

2. Braided Crown

Part your hair down the middle. Take a section of hair from one side and braid it. Wrap the braid around the top of your head like a crown. Secure with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side.

3. High Ponytail

Brush your hair and gather it at the crown of your head. Secure it with a hair tie. Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown for volume. Wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it.

4. Half Up Half Down

Part your hair down the middle. Take a section of hair from the front on one side and tie it into a small ponytail at the back of your head. Repeat on the other side. Leave the rest of your hair down.

5. Messy Bun

Gather your hair at the back of your head like you're going to make a ponytail. Twist your hair and wrap it into a bun. Secure with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.


Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length black hair:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz
  • Avoid using too many products on your hair
  • Wear protective styles (like braids or twists) to give your hair a break from styling
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Trim your hair every 8-12 weeks

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: Can I straighten my hair?

A: Yes, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Q: How do I prevent breakage?

A: Use a satin or silk pillowcase, avoid using too many products, and wear protective styles.


Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips for medium length black hair:

  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather

With these hairstyles and tips, you can keep your medium length black hair looking cute and healthy. Remember to take care of your hair and give it a break from styling every once in a while. Happy styling!
