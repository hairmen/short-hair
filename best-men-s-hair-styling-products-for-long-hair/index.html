Best Men s Hair Styling Products For Long Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair is a great way to show off your personal style and flair, but it can be difficult to maintain without the right products. Fortunately, there are plenty of men's hair styling products available that can help you keep your long locks looking great. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best men's hair styling products for long hair, and offer some tips on how to use them properly.

Description

Long hair for men is becoming increasingly popular, and with that popularity comes the need for products that can help keep those luscious locks in check. The best men's hair styling products for long hair are those that provide hold, control, and volume without weighing down the hair or leaving it greasy or stiff. Some of the most popular products include:

Hair Wax

Hair wax is a versatile styling product that can be used to create a variety of looks. It provides a strong hold that can last all day, and can be used to add volume and texture to long hair. Hair wax is particularly useful for taming frizz and flyaways.

Hair Gel

Hair gel is a classic styling product that has been used for decades. It provides a strong hold that can last all day, and can be used to create a variety of styles, from slicked-back to spiky. Hair gel can also add shine to the hair, making it a great choice for formal events.

Hair Spray

Hair spray is a popular styling product that can be used to create a variety of looks, from loose waves to sleek updos. It provides a flexible hold that can be brushed out easily, and can also help to add volume and texture to long hair.

Pomade

Pomade is a versatile styling product that can be used to create a variety of looks. It provides a medium hold that can be adjusted throughout the day, and can be used to add shine and definition to long hair. Pomade is particularly useful for creating classic, vintage-inspired styles.

How To Use Men's Hair Styling Products for Long Hair

The key to using men's hair styling products for long hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Here are some tips for using each type of product:

Hair Wax

  1. Warm a small amount of hair wax between your palms.
  2. Apply the wax evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  3. Use your fingers to style your hair as desired.

Hair Gel

  1. Squeeze a small amount of hair gel into your palm.
  2. Apply the gel evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  3. Use a comb to style your hair as desired.

Hair Spray

  1. Hold the hair spray can 6-8 inches away from your head.
  2. Spray the hair spray evenly over your hair, focusing on the areas that need the most hold.
  3. Style your hair as desired.

Pomade

  1. Warm a small amount of pomade between your palms.
  2. Apply the pomade evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  3. Use a comb to style your hair as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Men

If you're new to styling long hair, it can be helpful to have a step-by-step guide to follow. Here's one example:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product of your choice, such as hair wax, hair gel, hair spray, or pomade.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair as desired.
  4. If necessary, use a blow dryer to set your style.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hair spray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Men

Maintaining long hair for men requires a little extra effort, but the results are well worth it. Here are some tips to help keep your long locks looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too frequently.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or stiff.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can men with long hair use the same products as women?

A: Yes, many hair styling products are suitable for both men and women. However, men may prefer products that are specifically designed for their hair type and texture.

Q: Can I use hair wax on wet hair?

A: No, hair wax is best used on dry hair. Applying it to wet hair can dilute the product and make it less effective.

Hair Care for Long Hair for Men

Long hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for long hair for men:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With the right products and care, long hair can be a great way to express your personal style and flair. Try out some of these men's hair styling products for long hair and see which ones work best for you!
