1. Use a heat protectant spray

Before using a flat iron, always use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. This will create a barrier between your hair and the heat, reducing the risk of breakage and split ends.

2. Donât overuse a flat iron

No, it is not recommended to use a flat iron on wet hair as this can cause damage to your hair. Always make sure your hair is dry before using a flat iron.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Curly Frizzy Hair

Using a flat iron is just one part of maintaining healthy fine curly frizzy hair. Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and frizz-free:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz. Look for products that contain natural oils and avoid sulfates.

2. Avoid overwashing your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a brush on wet hair as this can cause damage.
