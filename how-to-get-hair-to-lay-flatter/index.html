How To Get Hair To Lay Flatter Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy and voluminous hair? Do you want to achieve a sleek and smooth look? If so, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing how to get hair to lay flatter, and we'll provide you with tips and tricks to make it happen.

  • How to Get Hair to Lay Flatter
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks

How to Get Hair to Lay Flatter

To get hair to lay flatter, you first need to understand what causes it to be voluminous and frizzy in the first place. Typically, frizz is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair, which causes the cuticles to lift and create an uneven surface. Voluminous hair is often caused by a combination of genetics, styling habits, and product use.

Here are some steps you can take to get hair to lay flatter:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. Look for products that contain ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and glycerin, which help to hydrate and smooth out the hair.

2. Apply a Hair Mask

Once a week, apply a deep conditioning hair mask to your hair. This will help to penetrate deeply into the hair shaft and provide long-lasting moisture. Look for masks that contain ingredients like argan oil, avocado oil, or keratin, which help to repair and strengthen the hair.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a flat iron or blow dryer, make sure to use a heat protectant spray beforehand. This will help to prevent damage to the hair and keep it looking smooth and shiny.

4. Blow Dry Hair in Sections

When blow drying your hair, divide it into sections and use a round brush to smooth it out. This will help to create a sleek and polished look. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to lay.

5. Use a Straightening Iron

For extra smoothness, you can use a straightening iron to flatten your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections to avoid damaging your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps for a sleek and smooth look:

  1. Wash hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry hair and apply a hair mask
  3. Blow dry hair in sections with a round brush
  4. Use a straightening iron to flatten hair
  5. Finish with a light hairspray or serum

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to help you get hair to lay flatter:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as opposed to a brush
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can lead to frizz and flyaways

FAQs

Q: My hair is naturally curly. Can I still achieve a smooth look?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a smooth look with curly hair. Follow the steps above, but use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.

Q: Can I use a hair oil to get my hair to lay flatter?

A: Yes, you can use a hair oil to help smooth out your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil.

Hair Care and Maintenance

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care and maintenance:

  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a sleek and smooth look that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
