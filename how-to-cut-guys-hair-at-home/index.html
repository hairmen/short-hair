How To Cut Guys Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money and give yourself or a loved one a fresh haircut, cutting hair at home is a great option. However, it can be intimidating to try cutting men's hair if you've never done it before. Follow these steps and tips to give a great haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Description

Cutting hair at home is becoming increasingly popular, especially with the pandemic limiting our ability to go out to salons and barbershops. While it may seem daunting to try cutting men's hair, with the right tools and techniques, it can be a successful and rewarding experience.

How to Cut Guys Hair at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before beginning, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, clippers, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the length and texture of the hair, and allow for a more precise cut.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Determine the desired length of the haircut. This can be done by using the clippers with the appropriate guard or by using scissors to cut hair to a specific length.

Step 4: Use the Clippers

Begin using the clippers to cut the hair to the desired length. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up, using an upward motion with the clippers. Be sure to blend as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Use Scissors for Texturizing

After using the clippers, use the scissors to texturize the hair. This will add depth and dimension to the haircut. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut small sections at a time.

Step 6: Cut the Sides and Back

Using the clippers, cut the sides and back of the head to the desired length. Be sure to blend as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 7: Cut the Top

Using the scissors, cut the top of the hair to the desired length. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut small sections at a time. Be sure to blend as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 8: Clean Up the Neckline and Around the Ears

Using the clippers, clean up the neckline and around the ears. Be sure to blend as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 9: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, style the hair as desired. Use a styling product to add texture and hold.

Tips for Cutting Guys Hair at Home

- Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.

- Use a spray bottle to keep the hair damp while cutting.

- Take breaks if needed to avoid fatigue.

- Check the length and texture of the hair frequently to ensure an even cut.

FAQs

What if I Make a Mistake?

It's okay if you make a mistake! Hair grows back and mistakes can often be fixed by going shorter or using styling products to cover up any unevenness.

What if My Clippers Aren't Cutting Well?

If your clippers aren't cutting well, try cleaning and oiling the blades. If that doesn't work, it may be time to invest in a new pair of clippers.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting hair, it's important to care for it properly. This includes washing and conditioning the hair regularly and using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week.

By following these steps and tips, cutting men's hair at home can be a successful and rewarding experience. With practice, you may even become a pro!
