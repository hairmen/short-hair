How To Make Messy Curly Hair Look Good

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that sometimes it can be difficult to tame. Messy curly hair is a common problem, but it doesn't have to be. With a few simple steps, you can make your messy curly hair look good. In this article, we will discuss how to make messy curly hair look good, from styling to hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step in making messy curly hair look good is to embrace the mess. Curly hair is naturally wild and free, so don't try to force it into a style that doesn't work for you. Instead, work with your natural texture and create a style that enhances it.

Next, invest in the right hair products. A good styling cream or gel can help tame frizz and define your curls. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will have the right ingredients to keep your hair looking its best.

Finally, experiment with different styles. Messy curly hair can look great in a variety of styles, from messy buns to loose braids. Don't be afraid to try new things and see what works best for you.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make messy curly hair look good:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  2. Apply a styling cream or gel to damp hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and minimize frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate your curls and create volume.
  5. If you want to create a messy bun or braid, use a hair tie to secure your hair in place. Don't worry if it's not perfect - messy is the goal!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help make your messy curly hair look good:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.
  • Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making messy curly hair look good:

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair if you want to. However, be aware that heat styling can cause damage and breakage, so it's important to use a heat protectant and not straighten your hair too often.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every other day, while others can go longer between washes. Experiment to see what works best for you.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your curly hair is essential to making it look good. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can snag and break your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

Making messy curly hair look good is all about embracing your natural texture and finding the right products and styling techniques. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your curly hair with confidence!
