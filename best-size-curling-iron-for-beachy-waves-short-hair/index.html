Best Size Curling Iron For Beachy Waves Short Hair

Description

Beachy waves are a classic and effortless hairstyle that look great on all hair types, including short hair. With the right size curling iron, you can easily achieve this look at home without any professional help. In this article, we’ll discuss the best size curling iron for beachy waves on short hair, step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, tips for getting the perfect beachy waves, and hair care tips to maintain your waves.

How to Achieve Beachy Waves on Short Hair

Before we get into the best size curling iron for beachy waves on short hair, it’s essential to know how to achieve this look. The first step is to wash and condition your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. After that, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent heat damage.

Tools You Need

You will need the following tools:

  • Curling iron
  • Comb or brush
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve beachy waves on short hair:

  1. Start by dividing your hair into sections. You can use clips to hold the sections in place.
  2. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the hair for a few seconds and release.
  3. Repeat step 2 until all sections are curled.
  4. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create beachy waves.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold your waves in place.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Beachy Waves

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to achieve the perfect beachy waves on short hair:

  • Use a curling iron with a barrel size of 1 inch or smaller. This size will give you tighter curls that you can loosen into waves.
  • Do not curl your hair all the way to the ends. Leaving the ends straight will create a more natural look.
  • Alternate the direction of your curls to create a more natural and effortless look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your waves.

FAQs

What size curling iron is best for beachy waves on short hair?

The best size curling iron for beachy waves on short hair is 1 inch or smaller. This size will give you tighter curls that you can loosen into waves.

How do I curl short hair without it looking too curly?

To avoid making your short hair too curly, use a curling iron with a smaller barrel size and do not curl your hair all the way to the ends. Leaving the ends straight will create a more natural and effortless look.

Hair Care Tips for Beachy Waves

To maintain your beachy waves, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your hair looking fresh and voluminous.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and make your waves look frizzy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and the right size curling iron, you can easily achieve the perfect beachy waves on short hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
