What Type Of Hairstyle Suits Me Female A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 17, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hairdo and looking for a change? Choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style can be daunting. With so many options available, it’s easy to get overwhelmed. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle for you.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hairstyle is determining your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To find out which shape your face is, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Pay attention to the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Use a measuring tape to get accurate measurements if needed.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that complement it. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to go for a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face. A square face may benefit from a soft, layered cut that adds movement and softness around the edges.

Understanding Your Hair Texture

The next factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture. Hair texture refers to the thickness, density, and curl pattern of your hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that require a lot of volume, as they can make your hair look flat and lifeless. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut that thins out your hair and makes it more manageable.

If you have curly hair, you may want to embrace your natural texture and go for a curly or wavy hairstyle. Straight hair can benefit from a sleek, smooth look or a voluminous blowout.

Matching Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Do you prefer a classic, timeless look, or are you more adventurous and experimental with your hair? Do you like to keep things simple and low-maintenance, or do you enjoy spending time styling your hair?

Your lifestyle and daily routine can also impact the type of hairstyle you choose. If you’re constantly on the go and don’t have a lot of time to spend on your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn’t require a lot of upkeep.

Hairstyles for Different Hair Lengths

Hairstyles can also be categorized by the length of your hair. Here are some ideas:

Short Hair

Short hair can be styled in many different ways. A pixie cut is a classic option that works well for oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, a layered crop can add height and elongate your face. For more texture and movement, go for a textured bob.

Medium Hair

Medium-length hair offers a lot of versatility. A layered lob works well for all face shapes and can add volume and movement to your hair. If you have wavy hair, a wavy bob can highlight your natural texture. Side-swept bangs are also a great option for adding dimension to your hair.

Long Hair

Long hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to voluminous curls. Beachy waves are a popular option for adding texture and volume to long hair. A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your length while still keeping your hair out of your face.

  • Sleek and straight

  • Side braid
  • Loose waves
  • Updo

Accessorizing Your Hair

Accessories can add a fun and playful element to your hairstyle. Here are some popular options:

  • Hair clips and barrettes
  • Headbands
  • Ribbons and bows
  • Hair scarves

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suited to your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A layered crop or a textured bob can add height and elongate a round face.

What is the best hairstyle for fine hair?

A layered cut can add volume and movement to fine hair.

Can I style my hair differently based on the occasion?

Absolutely! Your hairstyle should match the occasion and your personal style.

What should I do if I’m not happy with my new hairstyle?

Speak to your hairstylist and see if they can make any adjustments. If you’re still not happy, it’s okay to try a different style.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By taking into account your face shape, hair texture, personal style, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits you. Don’t be afraid to experiment and try new things – after all, hair grows back!
