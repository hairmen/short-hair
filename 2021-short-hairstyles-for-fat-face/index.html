2021 Short Hairstyles For Fat Face A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Daniel Jun 16, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll share some of the best 2021 short hairstyles for fat face shapes. From pixie cuts to bobs and beyond, these trendy styles will help you look and feel your best. Read on for our tips and tricks for finding the perfect short haircut for your face shape.

Description

Having a round or chubby face can make it difficult to find a flattering hairstyle. But with the right cut, you can highlight your best features and create a slimming effect. Short hairstyles are a great option for fat face shapes, as they draw attention to the upper part of the face and create a sense of height. In this article, we’ll explore some of the most popular short hairstyles for round faces, including pixie cuts, bobs, and more.

How To

Before you head to the salon, it’s important to understand your face shape and what styles will work best for you. To determine your face shape, measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, you likely have a round or chubby face. Once you know your face shape, you can start exploring short hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Research short hairstyles for your face shape
Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist
Step 4: Choose a style that flatters your features
Step 5: Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for a fat face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Opt for styles that add height to the crown of your head
  • Avoid styles that add width to the sides of your face
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and texture
  • Experiment with side-swept bangs to draw attention to your eyes
  • Choose a style that complements your personality and lifestyle

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for a fat face?

Some of the most flattering short hairstyles for round faces include pixie cuts, angled bobs, and asymmetrical styles. These cuts draw attention to the upper part of the face and create a sense of height.

Can I still have bangs with a fat face?

Absolutely! Side-swept bangs are a great option for fat face shapes, as they draw attention to the eyes and create a slimming effect. Just be sure to avoid blunt bangs, which can make your face appear wider.

How do I maintain my short haircut?

To keep your short haircut looking its best, it’s important to schedule regular trims with your hairstylist. You should also invest in quality styling products and tools, such as a good hairspray and a high-quality flat iron or curling iron.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using excessive heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness

By following these tips and choosing a short hairstyle that flatters your features, you can look and feel your best in 2021 and beyond!
