Hair Cuts Long In Front Shorter In Back A Trend That Never Goes Out Of Style

Written by Petter Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that’s trendy, chic, and never goes out of style, then you might want to consider getting a hair cut that’s long in the front and shorter in the back. Also known as an inverted bob, this haircut is perfect for women who want to add some edge to their look without going too drastic.

What is a Hair Cut Long in Front Shorter in Back?

A hair cut long in front and shorter in back is a type of bob haircut that features longer hair in the front that gradually gets shorter towards the back. This haircut creates a dramatic and edgy look that’s perfect for women who want to add some personality to their hair without sacrificing too much length.

How to Get a Hair Cut Long in Front Shorter in Back

If you’re interested in getting a hair cut long in front and shorter in back, then you should start by finding a salon or hairstylist that specializes in this type of haircut. Once you’ve found a stylist, you should bring in some pictures of the haircut to show them exactly what you want.

During the haircut, your stylist will start by cutting your hair into a bob shape. They will then begin to angle the hair towards the back, gradually getting shorter as they go. The goal is to create a seamless transition from the longer front to the shorter back.

Step by Step Guide:

  1. Find a salon or hairstylist that specializes in this type of haircut.
  2. Bring in some pictures of the haircut to show them exactly what you want.
  3. The stylist will start by cutting your hair into a bob shape.
  4. They will then begin to angle the hair towards the back, gradually getting shorter as they go.
  5. The goal is to create a seamless transition from the longer front to the shorter back.
  6. Once the haircut is complete, your stylist may add some layers or texture to give the haircut more movement.
  7. They will then style your hair to perfection, using products to add shine and hold.
  8. Finally, your stylist will show you how to maintain your new haircut and give you some styling tips.

Tips for Maintaining a Hair Cut Long in Front Shorter in Back

To keep your hair cut long in front and shorter in back looking its best, you should follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQs

1. Is a Hair Cut Long in Front Shorter in Back Suitable for All Hair Types?

Yes, a hair cut long in front and shorter in back can work for all hair types, from fine and straight to thick and curly.

2. How Long Does a Hair Cut Long in Front Shorter in Back Last?

A hair cut long in front and shorter in back can last anywhere from 6 to 12 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the haircut.

3. Can I Style My Hair Cut Long in Front Shorter in Back in Different Ways?

Yes, a hair cut long in front and shorter in back is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to messy and textured.

Hair Care for a Hair Cut Long in Front Shorter in Back

To keep your hair looking its best after a hair cut long in front and shorter in back, you should follow a regular hair care routine that includes:

  • Washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling and using a heat protectant when necessary.
  • Getting regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.

With the right care and maintenance, a hair cut long in front and shorter in back can be a stylish and edgy hairstyle that you’ll love for years to come.
