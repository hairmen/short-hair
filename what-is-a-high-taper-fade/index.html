What Is A High Taper Fade

Written by Robby Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? If so, then a high taper fade might be the perfect hairstyle for you. In this article, we will be discussing everything you need to know about a high taper fade, including what it is, how to get it, and how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a High Taper Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a High Taper Fade
  • FAQ

Description

A high taper fade is a popular haircut style that involves cutting the hair on the sides of the head very short and gradually tapering it upwards towards the top of the head. The hair on the top of the head is left longer and can be styled in a variety of different ways. This hairstyle is often paired with a short or medium-length beard, creating a stylish and modern look.

The high taper fade is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type. It is a popular choice for men of all ages and is suitable for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Get a High Taper Fade

If you are interested in getting a high taper fade, the first step is to find a skilled barber who has experience with this hairstyle. It is important to choose a barber who understands the nuances of the high taper fade and can create a look that suits your face shape and hair type.

When you visit the barber, it is important to communicate your desired look clearly. Bring in pictures of the hairstyle you want and be prepared to discuss the length and style of the hair on the top of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a high taper fade:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. This will make it easier for the barber to cut your hair.
  2. Choose the length you want for the hair on the top of your head.
  3. The barber will begin by cutting the hair on the sides of your head very short, using clippers or scissors.
  4. The barber will then gradually taper the hair upwards towards the top of your head.
  5. The hair on the top of your head will be left longer and can be styled in a variety of different ways.
  6. Finally, the barber will shape your beard (if you have one) to create a clean and polished look.

Tips for Maintaining a High Taper Fade

Once you have your high taper fade, it is important to maintain it properly to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber every 2-3 weeks to keep the hairstyle looking sharp.
  • Use a quality hair product to style the hair on the top of your head.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard looking healthy and well-groomed.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.

FAQ

Q: Is a high taper fade suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a high taper fade can be customized to suit any hair type.

Q: Is a high taper fade suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a high taper fade can be customized to suit any face shape.

Q: How long does a high taper fade last?

A: A high taper fade typically lasts 2-3 weeks before it needs to be trimmed.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking healthy and well-groomed, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Now that you know everything there is to know about a high taper fade, it's time to book an appointment with your barber and try out this stylish and trendy haircut.
