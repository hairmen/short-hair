How To Make Hair Look Messy But Good

Written by Daniel Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of perfectly coiffed hair? Do you want to give your locks a little more personality and edge? Then it's time to learn how to make your hair look messy but good. In this article, we'll show you step-by-step how to achieve that coveted "just rolled out of bed" look that is both effortless and stylish.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Look Messy but Good
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Hair Look Messy but Good

Messy but good hair is all about creating a relaxed, natural look that is full of movement and texture. To achieve this style, you'll need a few key products and some simple techniques.

Products You'll Need

  • Dry shampoo
  • Sea salt spray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Hair wax or pomade

Techniques to Try

  • Scrunching: This involves scrunching your hair with your hands to create natural waves and curls.
  • Tousling: This involves using your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look.
  • Backcombing: This involves teasing your hair at the roots to create volume and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Begin by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it on a low setting. You want your hair to be clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply Dry Shampoo

Spray dry shampoo at the roots of your hair to absorb any excess oil and add volume. Use your fingers to massage the dry shampoo into your scalp.

Step 3: Apply Sea Salt Spray

Spray sea salt spray all over your hair to create texture and waves. Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance the waves.

Step 4: Apply Texturizing Spray

Spray texturizing spray all over your hair to add even more texture and hold. Tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, undone look.

Step 5: Apply Hair Wax or Pomade

Take a small amount of hair wax or pomade and rub it between your palms. Apply it to the ends of your hair to create separation and definition.

Tips for Messy but Good Hair

  • Don't overdo it with product. A little goes a long way, so start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair after applying product. This will help distribute the product evenly and prevent tangles.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace imperfections. Messy hair is all about looking effortless and natural.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve messy but good hair with short hair?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a messy, textured look using the same techniques and products as longer hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to achieve a messy look?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. Some people can go several days without washing their hair and still achieve a messy look, while others may need to wash their hair daily. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Messy but Good Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to use the right hair care products and practices. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to achieve a messy, textured look.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect messy but good hair look that is both effortless and stylish. So go ahead, embrace your natural texture and give your hair a little edge!
