How To Maintain Digital Permed Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Oct 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to maintain your digital permed hair? Digital perms have become popular in recent years as they offer a low-maintenance way to achieve curly hair. However, it is important to take care of your permed hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to maintain digital permed hair.

How to Perm Your Hair Digitally

Digital perming is a chemical process that uses a special solution and a digital perm machine to create curls in your hair. The process involves washing your hair, applying the perm solution, and using the machine to heat your hair and set the curls. Once the curls are set, the solution is rinsed out, and your hair is styled as desired.

It is important to get your digital perm done by a professional stylist who is experienced in this technique. This will help to ensure that the results are even and that your hair remains healthy.

Step-by-Step Guide to Maintaining Digital Permed Hair

Here are some steps to follow to maintain your digital permed hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage to the curls.
  2. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair. Hot water can make your curls lose their shape and become frizzy. Use lukewarm or cool water instead.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a brush, as it can break the curls and cause frizz.
  4. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel to dry it. Instead, gently squeeze out the excess water with a towel or a microfiber cloth.
  5. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized. You can also use a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to define your curls.
  6. Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage the curls and cause frizz. Instead, air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  7. Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or a scarf.

Tips for Maintaining Digital Permed Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your digital permed hair:

  1. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes, as they can damage the curls and cause breakage.
  2. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  3. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and break the curls.
  4. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and keep your curls looking smooth.
  5. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs on Digital Permed Hair

How long does a digital perm last?

A digital perm can last up to six months if properly maintained. However, the curls may start to loosen after a few months, so you may need to get a touch-up to maintain the desired look.

Can I still straighten my hair after a digital perm?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair after a digital perm. However, it is important to use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat, as this can damage the curls.

Hair Care for Digital Permed Hair

To keep your digital permed hair looking its best, it is important to take proper care of it. Here are some additional hair care tips:

  1. Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage to the curls.
  2. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  3. Avoid using tight hair ties or clips, as they can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  4. Get regular scalp massages to improve blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.
  5. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to support hair health.

By following these tips and tricks, you can maintain your digital permed hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, and you'll be sure to have gorgeous curls that last.
