How To Cut Curly Hair Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting curly hair can be tricky, especially when it comes to pixie cuts. But with the right technique and some basic knowledge, you can achieve a beautiful and stylish curly pixie cut. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting curly hair pixie, from preparation to finishing touches.

Table of Contents

  1. Preparation
  2. Sectioning
  3. Cutting
  4. Blending
  5. Styling
  6. Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Pixie

  8. Hair Care for Curly Hair Pixie

Preparation

Before you start cutting, it's important to prepare your tools and your hair. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Towel or cape to protect your clothes
  • Mirror

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry it gently, leaving it damp but not too wet. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles, and part it where you want your pixie cut to be.

Sectioning

Sectioning your hair properly is the key to a successful curly pixie cut. Start by dividing your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use clips to hold them in place.

Next, take a small section of hair from the back and start cutting it to the desired length. It's better to start with a longer length and gradually cut more, rather than cutting too much at once.

Continue cutting the back section of your hair, moving from one side to the other. Make sure to cut each section evenly and to the same length.

Cutting

Once you've finished cutting the back section of your hair, move on to the front. Start by cutting the hair around your ears, using scissors or clippers to create a clean line.

Next, cut the top section of your hair to the desired length. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, using the technique known as point cutting. This will create texture and volume in your curls.

When cutting the front section of your hair, it's important to take your time and be precise. This section is the most visible and will frame your face, so it's essential to get it right.

Blending

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to blend the different sections together. Use your scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the back and front sections, blending the hair at the sides and around the ears.

Make sure to check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments. Don't be afraid to take a little more hair off if needed, but be careful not to overdo it.

Styling

Once you're happy with your cut, it's time to style your hair. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or curl cream, to damp hair and use your fingers to scrunch it in. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls, using a low heat and speed setting.

You can also use a curling iron or wand to define your curls and add more texture. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Pixie

  • Start with a longer length and cut gradually to avoid cutting too much off at once
  • Use point cutting to create texture and volume in your curls
  • Be patient and take your time
  • Check your work in the mirror and make adjustments as needed
  • Don't be afraid to seek help from a professional stylist if you're unsure

FAQs

Can I cut my curly hair pixie myself?

While it's possible to cut your own hair, it's always best to seek the help of a professional stylist. They have the knowledge and expertise to create the perfect curly pixie cut for your hair type and face shape.

What's the best way to maintain a curly hair pixie cut?

Regular trims are essential to keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish. Also, use products specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls moisturized and defined.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Pixie

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair pixie:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too frequently
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and stylish curly hair pixie cut that will turn heads everywhere you go. With patience, practice, and a little bit of creativity, you can create a look that's uniquely you.
