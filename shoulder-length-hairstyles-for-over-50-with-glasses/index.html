Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 50 and wear glasses? Are you looking for a new shoulder length hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and frame your glasses? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best shoulder length hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses.

Description

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more fragile. This is why shoulder length hairstyles are ideal for women over 50. They are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in many different ways. If you wear glasses, you want a hairstyle that will complement your frames and not detract from them.

How to

Before choosing a shoulder length hairstyle, you should consider your face shape and the shape of your glasses. You should also take into account your hair texture and thickness. Once you have determined these factors, you can choose a hairstyle that will suit you best.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Determine the shape of your glasses

Step 3: Consider your hair texture and thickness

Step 4: Choose a shoulder length hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and frame your glasses

Tips

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle, consider adding layers to add volume and movement to your hair. You can also add bangs to frame your face and soften your features.

If you have thinning hair, consider getting a volumizing cut that will add body to your hair. You can also use volumizing products to give your hair extra lift.

If you have thick hair, consider getting a layered cut that will thin out your hair and make it more manageable.

FAQ

Q: What are the best shoulder length hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses?

A: The best shoulder length hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses are layered cuts, bob cuts, and shag cuts.

Q: How can I make my shoulder length hairstyle look more stylish?

A: You can add highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth. You can also use styling products to create different looks, such as beachy waves or sleek and straight hair.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hairstyle looking its best, you should use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. You should also use a leave-in conditioner to prevent breakage and protect your hair from heat damage.

You should also trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape. If you color your hair, make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.

In conclusion, there are many shoulder length hairstyles that will flatter women over 50 with glasses. By considering your face shape, glasses, and hair texture, you can choose a hairstyle that will make you feel confident and stylish. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
