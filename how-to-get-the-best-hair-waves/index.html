How To Get The Best Hair Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having straight and lifeless hair? Are you looking for a way to spice up your look with some beautiful waves? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting the best hair waves. From preparation to styling, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Content

  • Washing and Conditioner
  • Drying your Hair
  • Heat Protectant

Preparation

Before you start curling your hair, it's essential to prepare it properly. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start styling. If you have any tangles, comb them out gently. If you have long hair, tie it up in sections to make it easier to style.

Washing and Conditioner

Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Choose a product that suits your hair type, whether oily, dry, or normal. Avoid using too much shampoo, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Condition your hair to keep it smooth and hydrated. Rinse your hair thoroughly, making sure there is no shampoo or conditioner residue left behind.

Drying your Hair

Blow-dry your hair with a hairdryer or let it air dry. If you're using a hairdryer, use a diffuser attachment to avoid damaging your hair. Dry your hair completely before you start curling.

Heat Protectant

Before you start curling, apply a heat protectant to your hair. A heat protectant will protect your hair from the heat of the curling iron and prevent damage. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair.

Curling Tools

Choose a curling iron that suits your hair length and the type of waves you want. If you want loose waves, choose a curling iron with a larger barrel. If you want tighter waves, choose a curling iron with a smaller barrel. Make sure your curling iron is heated to the right temperature before you start curling.

Styling your Hair

Start curling your hair from the bottom sections, working your way up. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron. Hold the hair for a few seconds, then release it. Continue curling your hair in sections until you've covered your entire head. Once you've curled your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and make them look more natural.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

1. Can I get waves without a curling iron?

Yes, you can use other methods, such as braiding or twisting your hair, to get waves. However, using a curling iron is the most effective way to get beautiful, defined waves.

2. How long will my waves last?

It depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, waves can last up to two days with proper care.

3. Can I curl my hair if it's wet?

No, you should never curl your hair when it's wet. Wet hair is more susceptible to damage and breakage when exposed to heat.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve beautiful waves that will last for days. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated. Now, go ahead and try out your new look!
