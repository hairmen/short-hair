How To Fix Wigs That Are Too Big

Written by Luffy Jun 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to keep your wig in place because it's too big for your head? Don't worry, this is a common problem that many wig wearers face. In this article, we'll show you how to fix wigs that are too big and keep them looking natural and comfortable. Follow our step-by-step guide and get ready to rock your wig with confidence!

Description

Wearing a wig can be a great way to change up your look or cover up hair loss. However, finding the right size wig can be a challenge. Many wigs come in a standard size that may not fit all head shapes and sizes. If your wig is too big, it can slide around on your head, exposing your natural hairline and making you feel self-conscious. Luckily, there are several ways to fix wigs that are too big and make them fit securely.

How to Fix Wigs That Are Too Big

Step 1: Secure the Wig Cap

The first step in fixing a wig that's too big is to make sure your wig cap is secure. If your wig cap is loose, your wig will also be loose and slide around on your head. To secure your wig cap, use bobby pins to pin it in place. Start at the front of your head and work your way back, placing bobby pins about an inch apart.

Step 2: Adjust the Wig Straps

Many wigs come with adjustable straps at the back of the wig cap. These straps can be tightened or loosened to help the wig fit more securely. To adjust the straps, locate them at the back of the wig cap and pull them tighter. Make sure the wig is snug but not too tight, as this can cause discomfort.

Step 3: Use Wig Clips

If your wig still feels loose after adjusting the straps, you can use wig clips to secure it in place. Wig clips are small clips that attach to the inside of the wig cap and clip onto your natural hair. To use wig clips, part your hair where you want the wig to sit and place the clips along the hairline, about an inch apart. Then, clip the wig onto the clips and adjust as needed.

Tips

  • Always make sure your natural hair is tucked away before putting on a wig
  • Use a wig stand or mannequin head to help style your wig
  • Don't use hot styling tools on synthetic wigs, as they can melt
  • Wash your wig regularly to keep it looking fresh and clean

FAQ

Can I cut the wig to make it fit better?

Yes, you can cut the wig to make it fit better. However, we recommend taking it to a professional stylist to avoid damaging the wig.

How do I know if my wig is too big?

If your wig slides around on your head or feels loose, it's likely too big. You may also notice gaps between the wig and your natural hairline.

Can I wear a wig if I have a small head?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have a small head. Look for wigs with adjustable straps or choose a petite size wig.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your wig looking great. Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up. Wash your wig regularly with a wig shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head. Avoid using hot styling tools on synthetic wigs, as they can melt.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily fix wigs that are too big and enjoy wearing your wig with confidence. Remember to take care of your wig and style it to suit your personal taste. With the right fit and care, your wig can look natural and beautiful for months to come.
