How To Make Your Hair Have More Volume With A Straightener

Written by Eveline Nov 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of flat and lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume to your hair without using any hair products? Look no further because we have the solution for you! In this article, we will show you how to make your hair have more volume with a straightener.

Description

Using a straightener to create volume in your hair is a quick and easy way to achieve a voluminous hairstyle without using any hair products. This method is perfect for those who want to avoid using hair products that may weigh down their hair.

How to

Follow these simple steps to achieve voluminous hair with a straightener:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a straightener.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and create volume.

Step 3: Straighten Your Hair

Take a small section of your hair and clamp the straightener at the root of your hair. Slowly glide the straightener down to the end of your hair while twisting the straightener outward. This twisting motion will create volume in your hair. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to hold the volume in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a straightener with wider plates to cover more hair at once.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Twist the straightener outwards to create more volume.
  • Use a light hairspray to hold the volume in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating volume with a straightener:

Can I use this method on curly hair?

Yes, you can use this method on curly hair. However, you may need to straighten your hair first before creating volume with a straightener.

Do I need to use any hair products?

No, you do not need to use any hair products to create volume with a straightener. However, you can use a heat protectant spray and a light hairspray to hold the volume in place.

Will this method damage my hair?

Using a straightener can cause heat damage to your hair if not done properly. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using a straightener on wet hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy and voluminous hair.

In conclusion, creating volume with a straightener is a quick and easy way to achieve voluminous hair without using any hair products. Follow these simple steps and tips to achieve the best results. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair.
