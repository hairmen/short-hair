How To Get Straight Hair Beauty Tips

Written by William Jul 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your curly or wavy hair and want a new look? Straight hair is a great option for a sleek and sophisticated look. In this article, we will provide you with beauty tips on how to achieve straight hair.

How to

There are various ways to get straight hair, such as using hair straighteners, chemical treatments, or natural remedies.

Hair Straighteners

Using a hair straightener is the easiest and quickest method to achieve straight hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small parts and start straightening from the roots to the tips.
  4. Repeat the process until all sections are straightened.
  5. Finish by applying a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Chemical Treatments

If you want long-lasting straight hair, chemical treatments such as hair relaxing, rebonding, or keratin treatment are options. However, these treatments can damage your hair if not done properly. It is recommended to go to a professional hairstylist for these treatments.

Natural Remedies

There are several natural remedies to straighten your hair without damaging it.

  • Coconut Milk: Apply coconut milk to your hair and leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off.
  • Milk and Honey: Mix milk and honey and apply it to your hair. Leave it for 2 hours before washing it off.
  • Lemon Juice and Coconut Oil: Mix lemon juice and coconut oil and apply it to your hair. Leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to get straight hair using natural remedies:

  1. Choose a natural remedy from the list above.
  2. Apply it to your hair and cover it with a shower cap.
  3. Leave it for the recommended time.
  4. Wash it off with shampoo and conditioner.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your straight hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer as it can cause frizz.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to reduce frizz and add shine.
  • Avoid tying your hair tightly as it can cause breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting straight hair:

How often should I straighten my hair?

It is recommended to straighten your hair once or twice a week to avoid damage.

Can I straighten my hair if it is wet?

No, it is not recommended to straighten your hair when it is wet as it can cause damage.

How long will the chemical treatments last?

The duration of chemical treatments depends on the treatment and your hair type. Hair relaxing can last for 6-8 weeks, rebonding can last for 6-12 months, and keratin treatment can last for 3-6 months.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, it is essential to follow a proper hair care routine.

  • Wash your hair twice a week with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after shampooing.
  • Avoid using hot water for washing your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.

By following these beauty tips and hair care routine, you can achieve and maintain straight hair.
