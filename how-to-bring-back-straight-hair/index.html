How To Bring Back Straight Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

In this era of hair experimentation, it's common for people to change their hair texture frequently. Some people love experimenting with their hair while others like to maintain their natural hair texture. However, if you have naturally straight hair, it can be a challenge to bring it back after you have experimented with other textures. In this blog post, we will discuss how to bring back straight hair after you have had a different texture for a while.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Bring Back Straight Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Straight Hair

How to Bring Back Straight Hair

If you want to bring back your naturally straight hair, you can follow these tips:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup that may be weighing down your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture and shine to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to straighten.
  • Use a straightening shampoo and conditioner to help smooth your hair and prepare it for straightening.
  • Invest in a high-quality straightening iron to achieve a sleek, straight style.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to help you bring back straight hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair and leave it on for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools for a few weeks to give your hair a break.
  4. Use a straightening shampoo and conditioner to help prepare your hair for straightening.
  5. Blow-dry your hair straight using a round brush to smooth out any bumps or kinks.
  6. Use a high-quality straightening iron to straighten your hair in small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain straight hair:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Try to avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to bring back straight hair:

Can I straighten my hair every day?

It's not recommended to straighten your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit heat styling to a few times a week.

What should I do if my hair won't stay straight?

If your hair won't stay straight, try using a stronger hold hairspray or a different straightening iron. You can also try using a hair serum to help smooth your hair and prevent frizz.

How long will it take to bring back straight hair?

The amount of time it takes to bring back straight hair can vary depending on the condition of your hair and how long you have had a different texture. It may take a few weeks to a few months to fully restore your natural hair texture.

Hair Care Tips for Straight Hair

If you have naturally straight hair, here are some tips to help you keep it looking healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for straight hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily bring back your naturally straight hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny!
