Description

If you have a long skinny face, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits your face shape. The key is to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and width to your face, creating the illusion of a more balanced and proportionate look. In this article, we will discuss the best men's haircuts for a long skinny face that will help you achieve a more flattering and confident look.

How to

Before choosing a hairstyle, it is essential to determine your face shape. To do this, measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline with a measuring tape. If your forehead and jawline are significantly longer than your cheekbones, you have a long skinny face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, the next step is to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat on top as they can elongate your face even further. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture to the sides and top of your head.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to choose the best men's haircut for a long skinny face:

  1. Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline to determine your face shape.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat on top.
  3. Opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture to the sides and top of your head.
  4. Consider getting layers or textured cuts to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  5. Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight to your head as they can accentuate your face's length.
  6. Choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features and personal style.
  7. Consult with a professional hairstylist for their expert advice on the best haircut for your face shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best men's haircut for a long skinny face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat on top.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture to the sides and top of your head.
  • Consider getting layers or textured cuts to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight to your head as they can accentuate your face's length.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features and personal style.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist for their expert advice on the best haircut for your face shape.
  • Use hair products such as volumizing mousse or hair spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: What are the best men's haircuts for a long skinny face?
A: The best men's haircuts for a long skinny face are those that add volume and width to the sides and top of your head. Examples include layered cuts, textured styles, and short hairstyles with volume on top.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long skinny face?
A: Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat on top as they can elongate your face even further. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or tight to your head as they can accentuate your face's length.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is essential to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much heat styling tools such as blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as they can damage your hair.

Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Use hair products such as volumizing mousse or hair spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Lastly, drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the best men's haircut for your long skinny face, you can achieve a more balanced and proportionate look that enhances your facial features and personal style.
