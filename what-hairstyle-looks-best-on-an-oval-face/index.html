What Hairstyle Looks Best On An Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As someone with an oval face, you have a versatile canvas to work with when it comes to hairstyles. However, with so many options available, it can be difficult to know which ones will flatter your features the most. In this comprehensive guide, we'll break down the best hairstyles for oval faces, including how to achieve them and tips for keeping your locks healthy and shiny.

Description

Oval faces are considered the most balanced facial shape because they have a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Fortunately, almost any hairstyle can work on an oval face. The key is to choose a style that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty.

How To Choose The Best Hairstyle For An Oval Face

When it comes to selecting the perfect hairstyle for an oval face, it's important to keep a few things in mind. First, consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that adds volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a style that thins out your locks and reduces bulk.

Next, think about your face shape and the features you want to highlight. Oval faces can pull off a range of styles, including long layers, blunt bobs, and asymmetrical cuts. Consider your lifestyle and personal style when selecting a hairstyle, as well. If you're someone who loves to play with color, you may want to choose a style that allows for easy highlights or balayage.

Step By Step Guide For Achieving The Perfect Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that works for your face shape and hair type, it's time to learn how to achieve the look. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and definition to your locks.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray or hair oil to keep your style in place.

Tips For Maintaining Your Locks

To keep your hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your locks. Here are a few tips to help you maintain healthy, shiny hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray or serum.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style.
  • Protect your locks from the sun and environmental damage by using products with SPF or wearing a hat.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and overall health.

FAQs

What is the most flattering hairstyle for an oval face?

Almost any hairstyle can work on an oval face, but some of the most flattering styles include long layers, blunt bobs, and side-swept bangs.

What haircuts should oval faces avoid?

Oval faces can pull off almost any haircut, but they should avoid styles that add too much volume around the crown or weight around the jawline.

Can oval faces pull off short hair?

Yes! Oval faces can pull off short haircuts, particularly those with layers or textured ends.

Hair Care Products For Oval Faces

If you're looking for hair care products that are specifically designed for oval faces, there are a few key ingredients to look for. These include:

  • Protein: Helps strengthen and repair damaged hair.
  • Vitamin E: Promotes hair growth and protects against environmental damage.
  • Argan oil: Adds shine and nourishment to dry, damaged locks.

Some of our favorite hair care products for oval faces include the Amika Soulfood Nourishing Mask, the Olaplex No. 3 Hair Perfector, and the Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face and keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful. Happy styling!
