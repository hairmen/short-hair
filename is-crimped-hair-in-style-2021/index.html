Is Crimped Hair In Style 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering if crimped hair is still in style in 2021? Look no further as we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about crimped hair, from how to achieve the look to tips on how to maintain it.

Description

Crimped hair is a style that involves creating small, zigzag-like waves or crimps in the hair. This style was popular in the 80s and 90s, but it has made a comeback in recent years. The style is often achieved with a crimping iron or by braiding damp hair and letting it dry overnight. Crimped hair adds volume and texture to the hair, making it a versatile style that can be dressed up or down.

How to Achieve Crimped Hair

There are several ways to achieve crimped hair. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Using a Crimping Iron

To achieve crimped hair using a crimping iron, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Take one section and clamp the crimping iron at the root.
  4. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds, then move it down the section of hair.
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 until you have crimped all of your hair.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Note: Be careful not to hold the crimping iron in one place for too long, as this can damage your hair.

Braiding Damp Hair

To achieve crimped hair by braiding damp hair, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Braid each section of hair tightly.
  4. Leave the braids in overnight to dry.
  5. Take out the braids in the morning and use your fingers to gently separate the waves.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Crimped Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining crimped hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can flatten the crimps.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a crimping iron.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the crimps to fall out.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about crimped hair:

Q: Can crimped hair be worn for formal events?

A: Yes, crimped hair can be dressed up or down, making it a versatile style that can be worn for any occasion.

Q: What hair types are best suited for crimped hair?

A: Crimped hair can be achieved on any hair type, but it may be more difficult to achieve on very fine or very thick hair.

Q: How long does crimped hair last?

A: Crimped hair can last for several days with proper maintenance and the use of hairspray.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair when using any heat styling tool. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using too many hair styling products, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

In conclusion, crimped hair is still in style in 2021 and is a fun and versatile style that can be achieved on any hair type. Whether you choose to use a crimping iron or braid your hair, crimped hair adds volume and texture to your hair. With proper maintenance and care, crimped hair can last for several days. So go ahead and give this fun and trendy style a try!
