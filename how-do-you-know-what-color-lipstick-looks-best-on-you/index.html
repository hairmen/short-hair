How Do You Know What Color Lipstick Looks Best On You

Written by Bardi Jul 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're someone who loves wearing lipstick, you know how important it is to choose the right shade. A great lipstick can make you feel confident, powerful, and put together. But with so many different colors and formulas to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this post, we'll explore how to figure out what color lipstick looks best on you, so you can feel your best every time you wear it.

Description

Lipstick is one of the most versatile beauty products out there. You can wear it to dress up a casual outfit, add a pop of color to a neutral look, or make a statement with a bold hue. But with so many different colors and formulas available, it can be tough to know which one is right for you. In this post, we'll explore how to choose the perfect lipstick shade for your skin tone, so you can always look and feel your best.

How to Choose the Right Lipstick Shade

Choosing the right lipstick shade is all about finding one that complements your skin tone. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right lipstick shade is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones. Neutral skin tones are a mix of both.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have a cool skin tone. If they appear greenish, you likely have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Choose a Shade Based on Your Skin Tone

Once you've determined your skin tone, it's time to choose a lipstick shade that complements it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Cool skin tones: Look for shades with blue or purple undertones, like berry or mauve.
  • Warm skin tones: Look for shades with yellow or orange undertones, like coral or peach.
  • Neutral skin tones: You can wear just about any shade, but try to avoid extreme colors that clash with your skin tone.

Step 3: Consider Your Lip Size

Another factor to consider when choosing a lipstick shade is your lip size. If you have thin lips, you may want to avoid dark or bold colors, as they can make your lips look even thinner. Instead, opt for lighter shades or neutral colors. If you have fuller lips, you can wear just about any shade, but bold colors will look especially striking.

Step 4: Think About Your Outfit

The final step in choosing the right lipstick shade is to think about your outfit. You want to choose a shade that complements your outfit, without clashing with it. If you're wearing a bold or colorful outfit, opt for a neutral or light lipstick shade. If you're wearing a neutral outfit, you can go for a bolder lipstick shade.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Lipstick Shade

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect lipstick shade:

  • Test different shades in natural light, as artificial lighting can distort colors.
  • Try on lipstick shades without any other makeup on your face, so you can see how they look on their own.
  • Consider your hair color when choosing a lipstick shade. Blonde hair looks great with pink or peachy shades, while red hair looks great with red or orange shades.
  • If you're unsure about a shade, ask a friend or sales associate for their opinion.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right lipstick shade:

What color lipstick looks best on fair skin?

For fair skin, look for shades with pink or peachy undertones, like soft pink or coral.

What color lipstick looks best on dark skin?

For dark skin, look for shades with rich, warm undertones, like deep red or burgundy.

What color lipstick looks best on olive skin?

For olive skin, look for shades with warm, golden undertones, like caramel or bronze.

Lipstick and Hair Care

When it comes to lipstick, it's important to remember that it's just one part of your overall beauty routine. To keep your lips looking and feeling their best, make sure to take care of them with a good lip balm or treatment. Additionally, make sure to take care of your hair, as it can affect the overall look of your makeup. Use products that are designed for your hair type, and avoid heat styling whenever possible.

Conclusion

Choosing the right lipstick shade can seem daunting, but it doesn't have to be. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect shade for your skin tone, lip size, and outfit. Remember to have fun with your lipstick, and don't be afraid to try new things!
