How To Extend A Dress That Is Too Small

Written by Petter Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this modern era, fashion has become an essential part of our lives. Whether it is a formal occasion or a casual outing, we all want to look our best. However, sometimes we end up buying a dress that is too small, and we feel disappointed. But don't worry, you don't have to return the dress. With a few simple steps, you can extend your dress and make it fit perfectly.

Description

Extending a dress that is too small is not as complicated as it seems. You can do it at home without any professional help. All you need is some basic tools and sewing skills. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of extending a dress step by step. We will also provide you with some tips and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to

Here are the steps to extend a dress that is too small:

Step 1: Measure the dress and your body

The first step is to measure the dress and your body. This will help you determine how much you need to extend the dress. You can use a measuring tape for this purpose. Measure the dress from the top to the bottom and from one side to the other. Then measure your body in the same way.

Step 2: Choose the right fabric

The next step is to choose the right fabric. You need a fabric that matches the dress and is stretchy. You can choose a fabric like spandex or jersey. These fabrics are easy to work with and will give your dress the required stretch.

Step 3: Cut the fabric

Now, cut the fabric according to the measurements you took in step 1. Cut the fabric in the shape of the dress so that it fits perfectly. Make sure to leave some extra fabric on the sides for sewing.

Step 4: Sew the fabric to the dress

Once you have cut the fabric, sew it to the sides of the dress. Make sure to sew it properly so that it doesn't come off. You can use a sewing machine or sew it by hand. If you are using a sewing machine, use a zigzag stitch to give the hem some stretch.

Step 5: Try on the dress

Once you have sewn the fabric to the dress, try it on. Check if it fits perfectly. If it's still too small, repeat the steps and add more fabric. Keep trying the dress until it fits perfectly.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while extending a dress:

  • Choose a fabric that matches the dress
  • Leave some extra fabric on the sides for sewing
  • Use a zigzag stitch for sewing
  • Try on the dress after every step

FAQ

Can I use any fabric to extend a dress?

No, you cannot use any fabric to extend a dress. You need a fabric that matches the dress and is stretchy. Fabrics like spandex and jersey are perfect for this purpose.

Do I need professional help to extend a dress?

No, you don't need professional help to extend a dress. You can do it at home with some basic tools and sewing skills.

What if the dress is still too small after extending it?

If the dress is still too small after extending it, you can repeat the steps and add more fabric. Keep trying the dress until it fits perfectly.

Hair Care

While extending a dress, it's essential to take care of your hair as well. Here are some quick hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful while extending your dress.
