How To Style Long Side Swept Bangs

Written by Petter Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've got long side swept bangs, you're in luck! This hairstyle is incredibly versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there's a way to style your bangs to suit your needs. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your long side swept bangs, as well as some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your hairstyle.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to style long side swept bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a small amount of styling product (such as mousse, gel, or hairspray) to your bangs to help hold them in place.

  2. Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs to one side. Make sure to direct the heat of the blow dryer downwards, towards the ends of your hair.

  3. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any flyaways or frizz. This will help give your bangs a sleek, polished look.

  4. For a casual, tousled look, use your fingers to gently tousle your bangs. This will help create texture and add volume to your hair.

  5. If you're going for a sleek, sophisticated look, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your hair.

  6. If you want to add some extra volume to your bangs, try backcombing them slightly at the roots. This will help create a fuller, more voluminous look.

  7. If you're looking for a more dramatic look, try sweeping your bangs all the way across your forehead. This will create a bold, edgy look that's perfect for a night out.

  8. Finally, finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray to help hold your bangs in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your long side swept bangs:

  • Experiment with different partings to create different looks. For example, try a deep side part for a more dramatic look.

  • If you're struggling to get your bangs to stay in place, try using a bobby pin to secure them in place.

  • Don't be afraid to play around with different textures and finishes. For example, try adding some curls or waves to your hair for a softer, more romantic look.

  • If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle, consider getting your bangs trimmed shorter. This will help reduce the amount of styling you need to do to keep your hair looking great.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long side swept bangs:

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's a good idea to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Can I style my bangs if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure to use a styling product that's designed for curly hair to help keep your curls defined and frizz-free.

How can I keep my bangs looking fresh throughout the day?

Carry a small travel-sized hairspray or styling product with you to touch up your bangs throughout the day.

Hair Care

To keep your long side swept bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

  • Use a high-quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair over time.

  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair.

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to help keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your long side swept bangs like a pro. Whether you're going for a casual, tousled look or a sleek, sophisticated style, there's a way to make your bangs work for you. So go ahead and experiment with different styles and textures, and have fun with your hair!
