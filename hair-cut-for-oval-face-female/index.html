Hair Cut For Oval Face Female The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face, consider yourself lucky! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any haircut. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide to finding the perfect hair cut for oval face female.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, high cheekbones, and a face that is longer than it is wide. This shape can pull off almost any haircut, from short to long, and from straight to curly. However, there are some styles that are more flattering than others, and we will discuss them in this article.

How to

To find the perfect hair cut for oval face female, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and determine if your face is oval, round, square, heart, or diamond-shaped. This will help you narrow down the haircuts that will be most flattering for you.

Step 2: Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture will also play a role in choosing the right haircut. If you have thin hair, you may want to go for a shorter cut with layers to add volume. If you have thick hair, you can pull off longer styles with ease.

Step 3: Look for inspiration

Look for inspiration online, in magazines, or on social media. Save photos of haircuts that you like and bring them to your hairstylist. They will be able to determine if the cut will work for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist

Finally, consult with a hairstylist. They will be able to give you personalized advice on what hair cut for oval face female will be most flattering for your face shape and hair texture.

Step by Step

Here are some haircuts that are perfect for oval face females:

1. Long layers

Long layers are perfect for oval face females because they add volume and movement to the hair. This style works best for those with straight or wavy hair.

2. Bob

Bobs are a classic haircut that works for almost any face shape, including oval. A bob that hits just below the chin is perfect for those with oval faces.

3. Pixie cut

If you're feeling daring, a pixie cut can be a great option for oval face females. This style works best for those with thick hair, and it can be styled in a variety of ways.

4. Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance out an oval face shape. They should be swept to the side and should not be too heavy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair cut for oval face female:

1. Avoid blunt cuts

Blunt cuts can make an oval face shape appear longer. Instead, opt for haircuts with layers or texture.

2. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a haircut, consider your lifestyle. If you're busy and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, opt for a low-maintenance cut.

3. Don't be afraid to experiment

Hair grows back, so don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts. You may find a style that you love but never would have tried otherwise.

FAQ

What haircuts should oval face females avoid?

Oval face females should avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face appear longer. They should also avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on top, as they can throw off the balance of the face.

Can oval face females pull off a short haircut?

Yes, oval face females can pull off a short haircut. However, they should consider their hair texture and face shape when choosing a style.

Should oval face females go for bangs?

Oval face females can go for bangs, but they should be side-swept and not too heavy.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use high-quality products and schedule regular haircuts. Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools, and avoid over-washing your hair.

In conclusion, there are many haircuts that are perfect for oval face females. By following these tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find the perfect hair cut for oval face female that will flatter your face shape and hair texture.
