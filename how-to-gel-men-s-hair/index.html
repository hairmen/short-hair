How To Gel Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Gel is a popular hair styling product that can help men achieve a variety of looks, from slick and formal to messy and casual. But if you're new to using gel, it can be a little intimidating. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of how to gel men's hair, from choosing the right product to applying it properly. Whether you're getting ready for a job interview, a night out with friends, or just want to look your best, we've got you covered.

What You'll Need

Before we get started, let's take a quick look at what you'll need to gel your hair:

  • Gel product of your choice
  • Comb or brush
  • Hair dryer (optional)
  • Water (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Gel

There are many different types of hair gel on the market, each with its own strengths and weaknesses. Some gels are designed for a strong hold, while others are more flexible and allow for movement. Consider your hair type, the style you want to achieve, and any other preferences you may have when choosing a gel product.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before applying gel, make sure your hair is clean and free of any other products. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel-dry your hair thoroughly.

Step 3: Apply Gel

Squeeze a small amount of gel into your palm and rub your hands together to distribute it evenly. Apply the gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips. Use your fingers to comb the gel through your hair, making sure it's evenly distributed.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the gel is applied, use a comb or brush to style your hair as desired. You can create a slick, formal look by combing your hair straight back, or a messy, casual look by tousling your hair with your fingers.

Step 5: Dry Your Hair (Optional)

If you want to speed up the drying process, you can use a hair dryer on low heat. Alternatively, you can simply let your hair air dry.

Tips for Using Gel

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using gel:

  • Start with a small amount of gel and add more as needed.
  • Don't apply gel to soaking wet hair, as it will dilute the product and reduce its effectiveness.
  • If you want a more natural look, mix a small amount of gel with water before applying.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can I use gel on thinning hair?

Yes, but be careful not to use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. Use a light hold gel and apply it sparingly.

How do I remove gel from my hair?

You can wash out gel with warm water and shampoo. If you have trouble getting all the product out, try using a clarifying shampoo or a hair scrub.

Can I use gel every day?

Yes, but be mindful of how much product you're using and how often you're washing your hair. Too much gel or frequent washing can dry out your hair and scalp.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling or chemical treatments.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet.
  • Stay hydrated and get plenty of rest.

Now that you know how to gel men's hair, you're ready to experiment with different styles and techniques. Remember to choose the right product, apply it properly, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Happy styling!
