Are you an Asian with a round face and medium length hair? Are you looking for ways to style your hair to flatter your facial features? Then you've come to the right place! In this blog post, we'll give you tips, how-tos, and more on how to style your medium length hair and round face Asian.

Description

Medium length hair is perfect for round-faced Asians. It's long enough to style in different ways but not too long that it becomes unmanageable. You can wear it up, down, or in between. And the best part? It flatters your round face. But how do you style it to make it look its best?

How to Style Medium Length Hair Round Face Asian

Because of your round face, you want to create angles and shapes with your hair. You want to avoid hairstyles that make your face look even rounder. Here are some hairstyles that flatter round-faced Asians with medium length hair:

  • Long layers - they create angles that complement your round face.
  • Side-swept bangs - they create an asymmetrical look, which helps to elongate your face.
  • Bob with layers - they add volume to your hair, which creates an illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Wavy or curly hair - they add texture to your hair, which also creates an illusion of a slimmer face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style medium length hair round face Asian:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, creating volume at the crown.
  5. Create waves or curls using a curling iron or wand.
  6. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the style.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair Round Face Asian

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length hair round face Asian:

  • Avoid blunt cuts.
  • Go for long layers.
  • Avoid center parts.
  • Go for side-swept bangs.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of your face.
  • Go for hairstyles that add volume at the crown of your head.
  • Use a heat protectant to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Finish with hairspray to hold the style.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight, but make sure it's not blunt. Add layers to create angles that flatter your face.

Q: Can I wear a bob with a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob, but make sure it has layers and volume at the crown of your head.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair Round Face Asian

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair round face Asian healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 8-10 weeks to keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

There you have it, tips, how-tos, and more on how to style medium length hair round face Asian. With these tips, you can rock your medium length hair and flatter your round face.
