How To Style An Undercut Pixie Cut

Written by Eveline Oct 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're reading this, congratulations on taking the plunge and getting an undercut pixie cut! Now, you might be wondering how to style it. Don't worry - we've got you covered. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about styling your new 'do.

Description

An undercut pixie cut is a short hairstyle that involves shaving the hair underneath while leaving the top layers longer. This creates a bold, edgy look that's perfect for anyone who wants to stand out.

How to Style

Styling an undercut pixie cut is surprisingly easy. Here's what you'll need:

  • Styling product (such as gel, mousse, or pomade)
  • A blow dryer
  • A round brush
  • A flat iron or curling iron (optional)

Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply your styling product of choice to your hair, focusing on the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down, using the brush to create volume and shape.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape. This step is optional - it depends on the look you're going for.
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair.

Before you can style your hair, you need to make sure it's clean and dry. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, then gently towel dry it. Don't rub your hair with the towel - this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 2: Apply your styling product of choice.

Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type and the look you're going for. Gel is great for creating a sleek, shiny look, while mousse is better for adding volume and texture. Pomade is a good choice if you want a more natural, messy look. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair using a round brush.

Using a round brush will help you create volume and shape. Start at the roots and work your way down, using the brush to lift and shape your hair. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go - this will help your style last longer.

Step 4: Use a flat iron or curling iron (optional).

If you want to add texture and shape to your hair, you can use a flat iron or curling iron. Use the flat iron to create sleek, straight strands or the curling iron to add waves or curls. Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools!

Step 5: Finish with hairspray.

To make sure your style stays in place all day, finish with a spritz of hairspray. Hold the can about 8-10 inches away from your head and spray evenly. Don't use too much - a little goes a long way.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your undercut pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with your part - you can create different looks by switching up your part.
  • If you want to add more volume, flip your head upside down while blow drying.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron - this will make a big difference in the quality of your style.
  • Don't wash your hair every day - this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Instead, try washing every other day or using dry shampoo in between washes.

FAQ

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your undercut grows out. On average, you should plan to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I dye my undercut pixie cut?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to use a dye that's safe for your hair type and follow the instructions carefully.

What if I don't like my undercut pixie cut?

If you're not happy with your new haircut, don't panic. Hair grows back! You can always grow out your undercut or try a different style.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut pixie cut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair - this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair elastics that can cause breakage - instead, try using scrunchies or hair clips.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your undercut pixie cut like a pro in no time. Have fun experimenting with different looks and don't be afraid to try something new!
