Best Hair Style For Oval Face Boy A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an oval-faced boy, choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. You need a hairstyle that not only complements your face shape but also reflects your personality. In this article, we will guide you through the best hair styles for oval face boy.

Description

An oval face is characterized by a well-balanced shape with prominent cheekbones and a narrow forehead. The jawline is slightly rounded and not as prominent as other face shapes. This shape is considered the most versatile face shape as it can pull off any hairstyle with ease.

How to Choose the Right Hair Style for an Oval Face Boy?

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face boy, the aim is to balance out the face shape. The hairstyle should create volume and width at the sides, while still maintaining the natural balance of the face. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Consider Your Hair Type

The texture and thickness of your hair play a vital role in determining the right hairstyle. For example, if you have thick hair, you can opt for a layered cut to create volume and movement. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, a short and textured cut will work best.

Tip 2: Look for Inspiration

Searching for inspiration is an excellent way to get ideas for your next hairstyle. You can look for inspiration from celebrities or even on social media platforms like Instagram and Pinterest.

Tip 3: Consult with a Professional Hairstylist

A professional hairstylist can guide you through the best hairstyles for your face shape and hair type. They will also consider your lifestyle and personal preferences when recommending a hairstyle.

Best Hair Styles for Oval Face Boy

1. Short Textured Cut

A short textured cut is an excellent option for oval face boys with fine hair. This hairstyle creates texture and volume, making the hair appear thicker. The cut should be short on the sides and back, with longer hair on top.

2. Side Part with Undercut

A side part with an undercut is a classic hairstyle that works well for oval face boys. The undercut creates volume and movement while the side part adds definition to the face. This hairstyle is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

3. Messy Quiff

A messy quiff is a trendy hairstyle that works well for oval face boys with thick hair. The hairstyle involves creating volume and height at the front of the hair, while the sides are kept short. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual and relaxed look.

4. Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a perfect hairstyle for boys with an oval face and long hair. The hairstyle involves sweeping the hair to one side, creating a natural-looking fringe. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual and relaxed look.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Boy

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and ensuring that your hairstyle looks great. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly helps to remove dirt, oil and product buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

2. Use Hair Products Sparingly

Using too much hair product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down. Use hair products sparingly and only when necessary.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts help to keep your hair looking neat and well-groomed. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for an oval face boy?

The best haircut for an oval face boy is a short textured cut, side part with undercut, messy quiff, or side-swept bangs.

How do I know if I have an oval face?

An oval face is characterized by a well-balanced shape with prominent cheekbones and a narrow forehead. The jawline is slightly rounded and not as prominent as other face shapes.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for an oval face boy is all about finding the right balance. Consider your hair type, look for inspiration, and consult with a professional hairstylist. With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can enhance your natural features and achieve a stylish and trendy look.
