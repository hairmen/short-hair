Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Simple A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium length hair can be a tricky length to style. It's not long enough for intricate updos, but it's not short enough for easy wash-and-go styles. However, there are plenty of simple and stylish hairstyles that you can try with your medium length hair. In this guide, we'll share some tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions for hairstyles for medium length hair simple in 2023.

  • How to Style Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Simple
  • Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair
  • FAQs about Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Simple
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to understand some general styling tips for medium length hair. First, invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type (e.g. curly, straight, thick, fine). This will help you achieve the best results and keep your hair healthy. Second, use heat protectant products if you regularly use heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons. Third, consider getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

Step-by-Step Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Simple

1. Messy Bun

This is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that's perfect for a casual day out or even for work. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some strands of hair around your face for a messy look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

This is a classic hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it for volume.
  2. Gather the teased section along with some hair from the sides of your head and tie it into a half-up ponytail.
  3. Optional: add a hair accessory like a clip or bow to the ponytail.

3. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for a formal event or even for everyday wear. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Optional: wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie and secure it with a bobby pin.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your medium length hair:

  • Experiment with different partings to create a new look.
  • Add volume to your hair by blow-drying it upside down.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands, clips, or bows to add some flair to your hairstyle.
  • Try out different braids like a fishtail braid or a Dutch braid for a unique look.

FAQs about Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Simple

Q: Can I still wear my hair down if it's medium length?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of ways to wear your hair down with medium length hair. Try adding some loose waves or curls for a romantic look, or straighten it for a sleek and polished style.

Q: What if I have thin or fine hair?

A: If you have thin or fine hair, avoid heavy or thick hairstyles that can weigh your hair down. Instead, opt for hairstyles with volume like a half-up half-down style or a messy bun.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Styling your hair is only one part of the equation. To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant product.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

With these tips and hairstyles for medium length hair simple, you'll be able to create a variety of stylish looks that are easy to do at home. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
