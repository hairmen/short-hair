Does Short Hair Make You Look Fat Or Skinny

Written by Bardi Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair is a popular hairstyle for women, but there are still some concerns about whether it can make you look fat or skinny. In this article, we will explore the truth behind this belief and provide tips and advice on how to style your short hair to flatter your face shape and body type.

Description

Short hair has been a popular hairstyle for women for many years. It is a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways, from a pixie cut to a bob. Some women worry that short hair will make them look fat or skinny, but this is a common misconception. The truth is that short hair can flatter any face shape and body type, as long as it is styled correctly.

How To

If you are considering getting a short haircut, there are a few things you should keep in mind to ensure that it flatters your face shape and body type:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are several different face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has its own unique features and requires a different hairstyle to flatter it.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle That Flatters Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a short hairstyle that flatters it. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds height to your hair, such as a pixie cut with long bangs. If you have an oval face, you can wear almost any short hairstyle, as this face shape is very versatile.

Step 3: Consider Your Body Type

In addition to your face shape, you should also consider your body type when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have a curvy body type, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds volume to your hair, such as a layered bob. If you have a straight body type, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds texture to your hair, such as a messy pixie cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture to your hair.
  • Use a flat iron to add sleekness to your hair.
  • Use a curling iron to add curl to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that flatters you the most.

FAQ

Does short hair make you look fat?

No, short hair does not make you look fat. The key to looking good with short hair is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and body type.

Does short hair make you look skinny?

No, short hair does not make you look skinny. Again, the key is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and body type.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Conclusion

Short hair is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that can flatter any face shape and body type. The key is to choose a hairstyle that flatters your unique features and to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can rock short hair with confidence!
