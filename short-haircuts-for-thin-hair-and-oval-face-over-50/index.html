Short Haircuts For Thin Hair And Oval Face Over 50

Written by Robby Jul 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As women age, their hair tends to thin out and lose volume, making it difficult to find a suitable hairstyle. Add an oval-shaped face to the mix, and the task becomes even more daunting. However, there are plenty of short haircuts for thin hair and oval face over 50 that can help you look your best. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to find the perfect haircut for your hair type and face shape.

Description

Thin hair can be challenging to style, but it's important to remember that a good haircut can make all the difference. When it comes to an oval face shape, the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, which makes it an ideal face shape for short haircuts. The goal is to find a haircut that adds volume and dimension to your hair, while also accentuating your facial features.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect haircut is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine which haircuts will suit your face shape and hair type. Some popular short haircuts for thin hair and oval face over 50 include:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well with thin hair and an oval face. This style features short layers that add volume to your hair, while also accentuating your facial features. The pixie cut is low maintenance and easy to style, making it an excellent choice for busy women.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short haircut that works well with an oval face shape. This style features a chin-length cut that adds volume and dimension to your hair. The bob cut can also be styled in a variety of ways, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

3. Layered Cut

The layered cut is an excellent choice for thin hair, as it adds volume and dimension to your hair. This style features short layers that create a choppy, textured look. The layered cut is perfect for women who want to add some edge to their look.

Step by Step

Once you've decided on a haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair properly. Here are some steps you can take to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is designed for thin hair and won't weigh it down. Be sure to massage your scalp gently to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.

2. Use a volumizing conditioner

Choose a conditioner that will add volume to your hair without weighing it down. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and avoid the roots, as this can cause your hair to become greasy.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle and thin. If you must use a heat styling tool, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

4. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. A stylist can also help you maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for thin hair and oval face over 50:

1. Consider your lifestyle

Choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain. If you're a busy woman, opt for a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require a lot of styling.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and haircuts. Try out a few different looks until you find one that suits you best.

3. Embrace your natural texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace it. Don't try to force your hair into a straight style, as this can damage your hair and make it look unhealthy.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my hair look thinner?

A: Not necessarily. A good haircut can add volume and dimension to your hair, making it look thicker and healthier.

Q: Can I still style my short hair?

A: Yes! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including curls, waves, and braids.

Q: Is it safe to color my thin hair?

A: Coloring your hair can be safe if you use a gentle, ammonia-free formula. Be sure to consult with a professional stylist before coloring your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when it comes to maintaining a healthy head of hair. Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

1. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Be sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

2. Avoid harsh chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and relaxers. These can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle and thin.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short haircut for thin hair and oval face over 50. Remember to consult with a professional stylist and take care of your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
