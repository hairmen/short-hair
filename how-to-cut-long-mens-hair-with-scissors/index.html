How To Cut Long Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting long men's hair with scissors can be a daunting task, especially if you've never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and professional-looking haircut right at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step-by-step, providing tips and tricks along the way for a successful cut.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparation
  • Cutting Long Men's Hair with Scissors
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Cut

Tools You'll Need

Before you begin cutting, you'll need to gather a few essential tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape to catch hair

Preparation

Before you start cutting, it's important to prepare your hair and workspace:

  • Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  • Choose a well-lit area with a large mirror.
  • Cover your shoulders with a towel or cape to catch hair.
  • Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  • Decide on the length and style you want to achieve.

Cutting Long Men's Hair with Scissors

Now that you're ready to start cutting, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair ties or clips. Start by separating the hair at the crown of your head, then continue sectioning the sides and back of your hair.

Step 2: Start Cutting

Begin cutting at the back of your head, starting with the longest sections. Use your comb to hold the hair straight, then cut across the ends with your scissors. Work from the back to the front, cutting each section to the desired length.

Step 3: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the sections to the desired length, use your scissors to blend the layers together. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle and cut into the ends to create a more natural look.

Step 4: Trim the Bangs (If Applicable)

If you have bangs, trim them separately. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut across the ends with your scissors. Use caution when cutting bangs, as they can be tricky to get right.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, use your mirror to check for any uneven spots or missed sections. Make any necessary adjustments with your scissors.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down gradually.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair straight and prevent uneven cuts.
  • Blend the layers together for a natural look.
  • Trim small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about cutting long men's hair with scissors:

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Use your scissors to make any necessary adjustments, or consider seeing a professional stylist to fix the problem.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and desired length. Generally, men with longer hair can wait longer between haircuts (every 6-8 weeks), while men with shorter hair may need to cut their hair more frequently (every 4-6 weeks).

Hair Care After the Cut

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Cutting long men's hair with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience, but it does take some practice and patience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut right at home. Remember to take your time, use caution when cutting, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
