How To Change Texture Of Hair Naturally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

Changing the texture of your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you are accustomed to using chemicals and heat. However, there are natural ways to change the texture of your hair without causing any damage. In this article, we will discuss how to change the texture of hair naturally, offering step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Texture of Hair Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Hair Texture Naturally

How to Change Texture of Hair Naturally

Changing the texture of your hair can be achieved naturally through various methods. Here are some of the most effective ways:

1. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a natural moisturizer that can help to soften hair and make it more manageable. It contains lauric acid and vitamin E, which promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair breakage. To use coconut oil, warm up a small amount in your hands and apply it to your hair from the roots to the ends. Leave it on for a few hours, or overnight, and then rinse it out with warm water.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera gel is another natural moisturizer that can help to soften hair and promote healthy hair growth. It contains enzymes that help to remove dead skin cells from the scalp, which can improve hair health. To use aloe vera gel, mix it with an equal amount of water and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes, and then rinse it out with warm water.

3. Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural conditioner that can help to soften hair and reduce frizz. It also helps to balance the pH level of the scalp, which can improve hair health. To use apple cider vinegar, mix two tablespoons with a cup of water and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for a few minutes, and then rinse it out with warm water.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to change the texture of your hair naturally:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before you start, it's important to determine your hair type. This will help you to choose the right products and methods for your hair. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type requires different care and styling techniques.

Step 2: Choose Your Method

Once you have determined your hair type, you can choose the method that will work best for you. Some methods, such as coconut oil and aloe vera, are suitable for all hair types, while others, such as henna, are better for certain types of hair.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. This will ensure that the natural products can penetrate your hair and work effectively.

Step 4: Apply Your Chosen Method

Apply your chosen method to your hair, making sure to cover your hair from the roots to the ends. Leave the product on for the recommended amount of time, and then rinse it out thoroughly with warm water.

Step 5: Repeat as Necessary

Depending on the method you choose, you may need to repeat the process several times to achieve the desired results. Be patient and consistent, and you will see a noticeable difference in your hair texture over time.

Tips for Changing Hair Texture Naturally

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when changing your hair texture naturally:

  • Be patient and consistent: Natural methods take time and effort, but the results are worth it.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Eat a balanced diet: A healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals will help to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair and undo all your hard work. Try air-drying your hair instead.

FAQs

Can I change my hair texture permanently?

No, natural methods can only change the texture of your hair temporarily. If you want to permanently change your hair texture, you will need to use chemical treatments or heat styling.

How often should I use natural methods to change my hair texture?

This depends on the method you choose and your hair type. Some methods can be used once a week, while others may need to be used more frequently. Follow the instructions for your chosen method, and adjust as necessary.

Can I use natural methods to change the color of my hair?

No, natural methods cannot change the color of your hair. If you want to change the color of your hair, you will need to use hair dye or other chemical treatments.

Hair Care for Natural Texture Change

Changing the texture of your hair naturally requires a different approach to hair care. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair after using natural methods:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo: Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, so use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Condition regularly: Conditioning your hair will help to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products that can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's rays.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Changing the texture of your hair naturally is a great way to achieve a new look without causing any damage. By following the tips and methods outlined in this article, you can achieve healthy, beautiful hair that looks and feels great.
