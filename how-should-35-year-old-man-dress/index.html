How Should 35 Year Old Man Dress A Guide To Relaxed Style

Written by Petter Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a 35-year-old man, you may be wondering how to dress for a casual occasion. Whether you're going out with friends, running errands, or attending a casual event, it's important to look stylish and put-together. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to dress in a relaxed style that's both comfortable and fashionable.

How to Dress in a Relaxed Style

Dressing in a relaxed style doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style for comfort. Here are some tips on how to achieve a relaxed look:

1. Choose Comfortable Fabrics

When dressing casually, it's important to choose fabrics that are comfortable and breathable. Avoid synthetic materials and opt for natural fibers like cotton, linen, and wool. These fabrics will keep you cool and comfortable in warmer weather.

2. Stick to Neutral Colors

Neutral colors like black, white, navy, and gray are versatile and easy to mix and match. Stick to these colors when dressing casually, and add pops of color with accessories like a watch or scarf.

3. Invest in High-Quality Basics

Investing in high-quality basics like a white T-shirt, a pair of jeans, and a leather jacket will ensure that you always have something to wear. These items are timeless and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

4. Pay Attention to Fit

When dressing casually, it's still important to pay attention to fit. Choose clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape. Avoid clothes that are too tight or too baggy.

5. Add Accessories

Accessories like a watch, sunglasses, or a hat can add style and personality to your outfit. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and reflect your personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to dress in a relaxed style:

Step 1: Choose Your Fabrics

Choose fabrics that are comfortable and breathable, like cotton, linen, and wool.

Step 2: Pick Your Colors

Stick to neutral colors like black, white, navy, and gray.

Step 3: Invest in High-Quality Basics

Invest in high-quality basics like a white T-shirt, a pair of jeans, and a leather jacket.

Step 4: Pay Attention to Fit

Choose clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape.

Step 5: Add Accessories

Add accessories like a watch, sunglasses, or a hat to add style and personality to your outfit.

Tips for Dressing in a Relaxed Style

Here are some additional tips for dressing in a relaxed style:

1. Don't Overdo It

Keep it simple and don't overdo it with accessories or patterns.

2. Dress for the Occasion

Make sure to dress appropriately for the occasion. A casual outfit may not be suitable for a formal event.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find what works for you.

4. Stay Groomed

Keep your hair and facial hair groomed and well-maintained.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a casual outfit?

A: Yes, sneakers can be worn with a casual outfit. Just make sure they're clean and in good condition.

Q: Can I wear shorts with a casual outfit?

A: Yes, shorts can be worn with a casual outfit. Just make sure they're not too short and are appropriate for the occasion.

Q: How can I add color to a neutral outfit?

A: You can add color with accessories like a watch, scarf, or shoes.

Hair Care

When dressing in a relaxed style, it's important to keep your hair and facial hair groomed and well-maintained. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and is easy to maintain. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. If you have facial hair, keep it trimmed and neat.

By following these tips, you can dress in a relaxed style that's both comfortable and stylish. Remember to choose comfortable fabrics, stick to neutral colors, invest in high-quality basics, pay attention to fit, and add accessories to complete your outfit. With a little effort, you can look great and feel confident in any casual situation.
