How To Frame Your Face With Curly Hair

Written by William Jun 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can give you a unique and beautiful look, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. One of the biggest challenges with curly hair is figuring out how to frame your face in a way that accentuates your features. In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on how to frame your face with curly hair.

  • How to Frame Your Face with Curly Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Frame Your Face with Curly Hair

The first step to framing your face with curly hair is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will complement your features.

If you have an oval face shape, you are in luck! Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, and almost any hairstyle will look good on you. If you have a round face shape, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to your crown. If you have a square face shape, you should soften your features by adding layers and texture to your hair. If you have a heart-shaped face, you should choose a hairstyle that balances out your features by adding volume to the bottom of your hair. If you have a diamond-shaped face, you should choose a hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin area. And if you have an oblong face shape, you should choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.

Step by Step

Once you have determined your face shape and chosen a hairstyle that complements your features, you can follow these steps to frame your face with curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and make them more manageable.
  2. Towel dry your hair by gently squeezing out the excess water. Do not rub your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to add definition to your curls. You can also use a straightener to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  6. To frame your face, use bobby pins or clips to pin back any strands of hair that are falling in your face. You can also use a headband or scarf to pull back your hair.
  7. Finish off your look with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you frame your face with curly hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for your face shape and hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a regular brush can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about framing your face with curly hair:

  • Can I frame my face with curly hair if I have short hair? Yes, you can still frame your face with curly hair if you have short hair. You can use bobby pins or clips to pin back any strands of hair that are falling in your face.
  • What type of curling iron should I use? You should use a curling iron or wand that is specifically designed for curly hair. Look for one that has adjustable heat settings and a ceramic or tourmaline barrel.
  • Do I need to use a curl-defining product? Yes, using a curl-defining product will help to define your curls and keep them in place. Look for one that is specifically designed for curly hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful curly hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your curls:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can frame your face with curly hair in a way that accentuates your features and gives you a beautiful and unique look.
