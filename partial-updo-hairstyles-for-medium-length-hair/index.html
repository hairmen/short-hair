Partial Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair A Guide

Written by Frank Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Partial updo hairstyles have been in trend for quite some time now, and they work wonders for medium-length hair. If you have medium-length hair and are looking for some inspiration for your next hairstyle, this guide is for you. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best partial updo hairstyles for medium-length hair, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

Description

Partial updo hairstyles are hairstyles that involve pulling back part of your hair and leaving the rest down. They are perfect for medium-length hair as they add volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. Partial updo hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in different ways to suit your mood and occasion.

How to

There are several ways to style partial updo hairstyles for medium-length hair. Here are some of the most popular ones:

1. Half-up Half-down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a classic partial updo that works well for medium-length hair. To achieve this hairstyle, divide your hair into two sections â the top and the bottom. Take the top section and tie it into a ponytail, leaving the rest of the hair down. You can leave the ponytail as it is, or you can braid it or twist it for a more intricate look.

2. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a beautiful partial updo that works well for medium-length hair. To achieve this hairstyle, part your hair down the middle and braid each section. Once the braids are done, cross them over your head and pin them in place, creating a crown-like effect.

3. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a popular partial updo that works well for medium-length hair. To achieve this hairstyle, gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it into a bun. Leave some pieces of hair loose to create a messy, effortless look. You can also pull out some strands of hair around your face to frame it.

  2. Take the top section and tie it into a ponytail.
  3. Leave the rest of the hair down.
  4. You can leave the ponytail as it is, or you can braid it or twist it for a more intricate look.
  5. Secure the hairstyle with bobby pins.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling partial updo hairstyles for medium-length hair:

  • Use hairspray or hair gel to keep the hairstyle in place.
  • Add hair accessories like flowers or hairpins to enhance the look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about partial updo hairstyles for medium-length hair:

1. Can I style partial updo hairstyles on short hair?

Partial updo hairstyles work best on medium-length hair. If you have short hair, you can try other hairstyles like a pixie cut or a bob.

2. How long does it take to style a partial updo?

The time it takes to style a partial updo depends on the complexity of the hairstyle. Simple partial updos like the half-up half-down hairstyle can take a few minutes, while more intricate styles like the braided crown can take up to an hour.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hair straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

Partial updo hairstyles for medium-length hair are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. By following the tips and tricks in this guide, you can achieve the perfect partial updo hairstyle that suits your style and personality.
