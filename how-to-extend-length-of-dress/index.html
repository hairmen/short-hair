How To Extend Length Of Dress Tips Steps And Faqs

Written by William Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have a favorite dress that no longer fits you because it's too short? Or did you accidentally shrink a dress in the wash? Instead of getting rid of the dress, why not try to extend its length? In this blog post, we will provide you with tips, steps, and FAQs on how to extend the length of a dress.

How to Extend Length of Dress?

Before we proceed with the steps on how to extend the length of a dress, let's first identify the materials that we will need:

  • Fabric that matches the dress
  • Scissors
  • Pins
  • Sewing machine or needle and thread
  • Iron

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Purchase a matching fabric

Visit a fabric store and buy a fabric that matches the color and texture of your dress. It's important that the fabric matches your dress to ensure that the extension blends in with the original dress.

Step 2: Cut the fabric

Measure the length that you want to add to your dress and cut the fabric accordingly. Make sure to add an extra inch to the length to allow for hemming.

Step 3: Hem the fabric

Using a sewing machine or needle and thread, hem the fabric by folding the edges inwards and sewing them together. This will prevent the edges from fraying.

Step 4: Pin the fabric to the dress

Pin the fabric to the bottom of the dress, making sure that it's aligned properly. Try the dress on to ensure that the length is correct and adjust if necessary.

Step 5: Sew the fabric to the dress

Sew the fabric to the dress using a sewing machine or needle and thread. Make sure to sew it securely so that it doesn't come apart after a few wears.

Step 6: Iron the dress

Iron the dress to ensure that the extension and the original dress blend in seamlessly.

Tips

  • Choose a fabric that matches the dress to ensure that the extension blends in with the original dress.
  • Try the dress on before sewing the fabric to ensure that the length is correct.
  • Sew the fabric securely to ensure that it doesn't come apart after a few wears.

FAQs

Q: Can I extend the length of a dress that has a pattern?

A: Yes, but you have to be careful in choosing the fabric. Look for a fabric that has a similar pattern to the dress to ensure that the extension blends in seamlessly.

Q: Can I extend the length of a dress that has a lining?

A: Yes, but you have to make sure that the lining is also extended. Follow the same steps above, but make sure to extend the lining as well.

Q: Can I extend the length of a dress that has a different texture?

A: Yes, but it will be more challenging to find a fabric that matches the texture of the dress. You can try to find a fabric that has a similar sheen or drape to the dress.

Hair Care

While you're at it, why not take care of your hair as well? Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you'll not only have a longer dress but also healthier hair.

Conclusion

Extending the length of a dress is a great way to give it a new lease on life. By following the tips and steps provided above, you can extend the length of your dress and make it look as good as new. Don't forget to take care of your hair while you're at it!
