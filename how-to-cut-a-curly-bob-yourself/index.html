How To Cut A Curly Bob Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the salon for a curly bob haircut? Why not try cutting it yourself? With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking curly bob without leaving your home. In this guide, we will walk you through how to cut a curly bob yourself, step by step.

How to Cut a Curly Bob Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Make sure your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your curls. Do not brush or comb your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Clip the front sections out of the way and focus on the back sections first.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger. Hold the twist at the end and cut the hair at an angle. The angle will help create layers and add texture to your curls.

Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Cut the Front Sections

Release the front sections and repeat the same process. Make sure to cut the hair slightly longer than the back sections, as the front sections will shrink more when they dry.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting, check to make sure both sides are even. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair with small snips until it is even.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

After you have finished cutting, style your hair as desired. You can use a diffuser on your blow dryer to enhance your curls or use a curling iron to create more defined curls.

Tips for Cutting a Curly Bob Yourself

  • Start with small snips and cut less than you think you need. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Make sure to cut at an angle to create layers and texture.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head to make sure both sides are even.
  • If you have very curly hair, consider cutting it when it is dry to avoid shrinkage.

FAQ

Can I cut my curly bob when it is wet?

It is best to cut curly hair when it is dry to avoid shrinkage. However, if you prefer to cut it when it is wet, make sure to use a wide-tooth comb and cut less than you think you need.

How often should I cut my curly bob?

It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your curly bob.

Hair Care for Your Curly Bob

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and shiny.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.

Cutting a curly bob yourself can save you time and money. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps and tips for a beautiful curly bob that will make heads turn.
