How To Cut Hair Equally At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you want to make sure that it's cut equally on both sides. However, with the right tools and techniques, cutting your hair at home can be a breeze. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting your hair equally at home, as well as share some tips and tricks to help you get the perfect cut every time.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • How to Cut Hair Equally at Home
  • Tips to Help You Get the Perfect Cut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips After Cutting Your Hair

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, make sure that you have all the necessary tools on hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A handheld mirror or a second mirror to see the back of your head
  • Hair clips to section your hair

Make sure that your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can lead to uneven cuts and split ends. You can find hair cutting scissors at most beauty supply stores or online.

How to Cut Hair Equally at Home

Now that you have all the necessary tools, it's time to get started. Follow these steps to cut your hair equally at home:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will give you a better idea of how your hair naturally falls. If you have curly or wavy hair, consider letting it air dry to maintain its natural texture.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Clip each section up so that it's out of the way.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin by cutting one section of your hair at a time. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut it evenly. Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut each section the same length.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use your handheld mirror or a second mirror to check the back of your head. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is cut evenly on both sides.

Tips to Help You Get the Perfect Cut

Here are some additional tips to help you get the perfect cut:

  • Start by cutting a little bit at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use the tip of your scissors to cut small sections of hair, rather than trying to cut large chunks at once.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.
  • Consider watching tutorials on YouTube or reading hair cutting guides to learn more techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair if it's really long?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if it's long. Just make sure that you have the right tools and take your time. Consider starting with small cuts and working your way up to a bigger cut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain its health and prevent split ends.

Should I cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be harder to cut evenly.

Hair Care Tips After Cutting Your Hair

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your hair equally at home and achieve the perfect cut every time. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.
