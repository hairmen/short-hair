Mens Hairstyles Medium Fade A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Robby Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium fade hairstyles have become increasingly popular among men in recent years. This style is perfect for those who want to keep their hair short but not too short, and it is versatile enough to suit any occasion. In this guide, we will take you through everything you need to know about mens hairstyles medium fade, including how to achieve the look, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Fade
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Achieve the Look

The medium fade is a popular hairstyle that involves tapering the hair on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a gradual fade that blends the shorter hair with the longer hair seamlessly. To achieve this look, you will need to visit a barber or stylist who specializes in men's haircuts. They will be able to use clippers and scissors to create the perfect fade for your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to achieve the medium fade look at home, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to ensure it is clean and free of any product buildup.
  2. Use a comb to part your hair where you want the fade to start.
  3. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to create the fade effect.
  4. Blend the shorter hair into the longer hair using scissors or clippers with no guard.
  5. Style your hair on top as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Fade

Once you have achieved the perfect medium fade, it is important to maintain it to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax for a polished look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQs

What face shape is best suited for a medium fade?

The medium fade is a versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape. However, it is particularly flattering for those with a round or oval face shape, as it helps to create a more defined jawline.

How long does it take to achieve a medium fade?

The length of time it takes to achieve a medium fade depends on your hair type and the skill of your barber or stylist. On average, it takes around 30 minutes to an hour to achieve the perfect medium fade.

Can I still style my hair on top with a medium fade?

Yes, you can still style your hair on top with a medium fade. This hairstyle is versatile enough to suit any style, from messy and textured to slick and polished.

Hair Care Advice

Keeping your hair healthy is important for maintaining a great-looking medium fade. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know everything there is to know about mens hairstyles medium fade, it's time to try it out for yourself. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a polished and professional look that is perfect for any occasion. So why not book an appointment with your barber or stylist today?
