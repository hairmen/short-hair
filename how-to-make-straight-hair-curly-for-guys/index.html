41 Popular How To Make Straight Hair Curly For Guys For Short Hair

Written by Bardi Nov 21, 2023 · 5 min read
Table of Content: - Introduction - How to Make Straight Hair Curly for Guys: The Steps - Step 1: Preparation - Step 2: Wash your Hair - Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant - Step 4: Blow Dry your Hair - Step 5: Use a Curling Iron - Step 6: Apply Hair Spray - Tips for Making Straight Hair Curly for Guys - FAQ about Making Straight Hair Curly for Guys - Hair Care Tips for Men with Curly Hair Introduction Straight hair can sometimes look lifeless and uninteresting. For guys who want to add some personality and texture to their hair, making it curly is a great option. But how can you do it without damaging your hair or looking like you just came out of a perm salon? In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps on how to make straight hair curly for guys, using easy-to-find tools and products. How to Make Straight Hair Curly for Guys: The Steps Step 1: Preparation Before you start curling your hair, you need to make sure it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with a shampoo that suits your hair type, and then towel-dry it gently. You don't want your hair to be too wet or too dry before curling. Step 2: Wash your Hair As mentioned above, it is essential to wash your hair before curling it. This step will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that can weigh your hair down and prevent it from holding curls. Use lukewarm water to wet your hair thoroughly, and then apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp. Massage your scalp gently with your fingertips and rinse your hair thoroughly. Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant Before applying heat to your hair, it is essential to use a heat protectant spray or serum. This product will create a barrier between your hair and the heat, preventing damage, and keeping your hair healthy. Spray the heat protectant evenly over your hair, focusing on the ends. Step 4: Blow Dry your Hair Using a blow dryer, start drying your hair from the roots to the ends. Use a medium heat setting and a round brush to create volume and lift. Blow dry your hair until it is 80% dry, leaving some dampness to help the curls set. Step 5: Use a Curling Iron Now it's time to start curling your hair. Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that suits your desired curl size. For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel, and for looser curls, use a larger barrel. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting from the top and wrapping it downwards. Hold the hair on the iron for 10-15 seconds and release it gently. Repeat this process until all your hair is curled. Step 6: Apply Hair Spray To keep your curls in place, apply a stronghold hairspray. Hold the hairspray can at least 8 inches away from your head and spray it evenly over your curls. Don't overdo it with the hairspray, or your hair will look stiff and unnatural. Tips for Making Straight Hair Curly for Guys - Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel to prevent damage to your hair. - Don't curl your hair too tightly, or it will look fake and overly done. - Experiment with different curl sizes and directions to create a natural-looking texture. - Use hair products that suit your hair type and curl pattern, such as mousse, gel, or wax. - Don't touch your curls too much, as this can cause frizz and flatten your hair. FAQ about Making Straight Hair Curly for Guys Q: Does curling damage my hair? A: Curling your hair can cause damage if you don't use a heat protectant or if you curl your hair too often. However, if you follow the steps and use the right products, you can minimize the damage and keep your hair healthy. Q: How long will my curls last? A: The longevity of your curls depends on your hair type, the products you use, and your daily activities. Generally, your curls can last up to 24 hours if you use a stronghold hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much. Hair Care Tips for Men with Curly Hair - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and defined. - Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. - Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently. - Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle. In conclusion, making straight hair curly for guys is a fun and easy way to switch up your look and add some personality to your hair. By following the steps and tips above, you can achieve natural-looking curls without damaging your hair. Remember to take care of your curls with the right products and hair care routine, and you'll have healthy and bouncy curls all day long.
