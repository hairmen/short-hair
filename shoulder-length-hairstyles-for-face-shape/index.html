Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Face Shape A Guide For 2023

Written by Aprilia Aug 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle, shoulder length hair is a great option. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and looks good on just about everyone. However, it's important to consider your face shape when choosing a shoulder length hairstyle. In this guide, we'll go over some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for different face shapes.

Hairstyles for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck - almost any hairstyle will look good on you! However, shoulder length hair is particularly flattering. A blunt bob that hits right at the collarbone is a great option, as is a layered cut with face-framing pieces.

Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a shoulder length hairstyle that will elongate your face. A long bob that falls just below the shoulders is a good choice, as is a layered cut with volume at the crown. Avoid blunt cuts that hit right at the chin, as they can make your face appear wider.

Hairstyles for Square Faces

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a shoulder length hairstyle that has layers or curls. A shoulder length cut with long layers that frame the face is a good choice, as is a shoulder length cut with loose waves. Avoid blunt cuts that hit right at the jawline, as they can make your face appear more angular.

Hairstyles for Heart-Shaped Faces

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose a shoulder length hairstyle that balances out your features. A shoulder length cut with long, side-swept bangs is a good choice, as is a shoulder length cut with layers that start at the chin. Avoid blunt cuts that hit right at the cheekbones, as they can make your forehead appear too prominent.

Hairstyles for Diamond-Shaped Faces

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you'll want to choose a shoulder length hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin. A shoulder length cut with layers that start at the cheekbones is a good choice, as is a shoulder length cut with a deep side part. Avoid blunt cuts that hit right at the chin, as they can make your face appear too angular.

Hairstyles for Long Faces

If you have a long face shape, you'll want to choose a shoulder length hairstyle that adds width to your face. A shoulder length cut with layers that start at the collarbone is a good choice, as is a shoulder length cut with loose waves. Avoid blunt cuts that hit right at the chin, as they can make your face appear even longer.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Not sure what your face shape is? Stand in front of a mirror and use a piece of chalk or lipstick to trace the outline of your face. Then, compare it to the following face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart-Shaped
  • Diamond-Shaped
  • Long

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a shoulder length hairstyle that will flatter your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair

To style shoulder length hair, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair, depending on your desired look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair looking great, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a shoulder length hairstyle?

A: Yes! Shoulder length hair can be styled in a variety of updos, such as a messy bun or a braided crown.

Q: Will a shoulder length hairstyle require a lot of maintenance?

A: No - shoulder length hair is easy to maintain and only requires regular trims and occasional deep conditioning treatments.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair healthy, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed based on your hair type.
  • Use products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

With these tips and tricks, you can find a shoulder length hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
