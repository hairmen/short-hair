How To Wear Scarf On Bald Head A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a baldie looking for a stylish way to protect your head from the sun or just want to add a bit of flair to your look? Scarves are the perfect accessory for you! In this guide, we will teach you how to wear a scarf on a bald head in different styles and provide hair care tips to maintain your scalp's health.

Description

Scarves are versatile and come in different colors, patterns, and fabrics, making them suitable for all seasons. They can be worn in many ways, from wrapping them around your neck, shoulders, waist, or head. Wearing a scarf on a bald head is not only fashionable but also functional. A scarf can protect your scalp from the sun, wind, or cold, preventing sunburn or dryness.

How to Wear Scarf on Bald Head

1. The Classic

The classic style involves folding the scarf into a triangle and placing the longest side at the nape of your neck, then tying the shorter ends on top of your head. You can adjust the knot's tightness to your liking and leave the scarf's tip hanging or tuck it under the knot.

2. The Turban

The turban style requires a longer scarf, preferably rectangular. Fold the scarf in half lengthwise, place it on top of your head, and bring the ends to the back. Tie the ends at the nape of your neck, then twist the remaining fabric and wrap it around the knot to create a turban.

3. The Bandana

The bandana style involves folding the scarf into a triangle and placing the longest side at the nape of your neck. Cross the shorter ends at the top of your head, then tie them at the back. You can leave the knot exposed or tuck it under the scarf.

Step by Step Guide

1. Choose your scarf

Select a scarf that matches your outfit and complements your skin tone. You can opt for a cotton or silk scarf, depending on your preference and the weather.

2. Prepare your head

Wash your scalp and apply sunscreen if you plan to be outside for an extended period. Comb your hair or shave it if necessary, to ensure a smooth surface for the scarf.

3. Fold the scarf

Fold the scarf according to the style you want to achieve. You can experiment with different folding techniques until you find the one that suits you.

4. Place the scarf

Place the scarf on your head, making sure it covers your scalp and ears. You can adjust the scarf's size and position until you feel comfortable.

5. Tie the scarf

Tie the scarf according to the style you chose, and adjust it to your liking. Make sure the knot is secure but not too tight, to avoid discomfort or headaches.

Tips

- Use a scarf clip or bobby pins to secure the scarf in place.

- Experiment with different scarf styles and colors to find your signature look.

- Choose scarves with breathable fabrics to prevent sweat and odor.

- Wash your scarves regularly to maintain their quality and hygiene.

FAQ

1. Can I wear a scarf on a bald head in winter?

Yes, you can wear a scarf on a bald head in winter to keep your head warm and protect it from the cold. Choose a scarf made of wool or cashmere for extra warmth.

2. How do I tie a scarf on a bald head without it slipping?

You can use a scarf clip or bobby pins to secure the scarf in place. You can also tie the knot tighter or use a longer scarf to wrap it around your head multiple times.

3. Can wearing a scarf on a bald head cause hair loss?

No, wearing a scarf on a bald head does not cause hair loss. However, you should choose a scarf made of breathable fabric to prevent sweat and avoid wearing it too tight to prevent discomfort.

Hair Care

Even though bald heads don't have hair, they still require proper care to maintain their health and prevent sun damage. Apply sunscreen with at least SPF 30 on your scalp before going outside, and wear a hat or scarf to protect your head from the sun. Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil, and moisturize it with a non-greasy lotion to prevent dryness.
