Best Hair Colour For Older Women

Written by Frank Aug 08, 2023 · 4 min read
Best Hair Colour for Older Women | Tips, FAQ and Hair Care

As we age, our hair naturally changes in texture, thickness and colour. Many women choose to dye their hair to maintain a youthful look or to cover grey hairs. However, with so many hair colour options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we'll discuss the best hair colour options for older women, including tips, FAQs and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hair Colour for Older Women
  • Step by Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour
  • FAQs About Hair Colour for Older Women
  • Hair Care for Coloured Hair

How to Choose the Best Hair Colour for Older Women

When choosing a hair colour as an older woman, it's important to consider factors such as skin tone, eye colour and lifestyle. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colours such as golden blonde, copper and caramel.
  • Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colours such as ash blonde, platinum and burgundy.
  • If you have grey hair, consider going a few shades lighter or adding highlights to brighten up your face.
  • If you're not sure what colour to choose, consult with a professional hairstylist who can recommend the best option for you.

Step by Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair

If you've decided to dye your hair at home, here is a step by step guide:

  1. Choose a high-quality hair dye that is specifically formulated for older women.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and do a patch test to ensure you're not allergic to the product.
  3. Wear old clothes and gloves to protect your skin from staining.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and apply the dye starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended time and rinse with cool water.
  6. Apply a conditioner to nourish your hair and prevent dryness.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've dyed your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep the colour vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for coloured hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip away the colour.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat from styling tools such as hair dryers and straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQs About Hair Colour for Older Women

1. Is it safe to dye my hair as an older woman?

Yes, it is safe as long as you use a high-quality hair dye and follow the instructions carefully. If you have any concerns, consult with a professional hairstylist or your doctor.

2. Can I dye my hair if I have grey hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have grey hair. Consider going a few shades lighter or adding highlights to brighten up your face.

3. How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how visible the roots are. As a general rule, touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.


Hair Care for Coloured Hair

Coloured hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip away the colour.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or peroxide on your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

In conclusion, choosing the best hair colour for older women requires consideration of factors such as skin tone, eye colour and lifestyle. Whether you decide to dye your hair at home or with a professional hairstylist, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. With the right hair colour and hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age.
