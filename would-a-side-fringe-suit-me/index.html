Would A Side Fringe Suit Me Your Ultimate Guide To Side Fringes

Written by Oliver Nov 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you considering getting a side fringe but unsure if it will suit you? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know before making the chop. From how to choose the right type of side fringe to how to maintain it, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s dive in!

Description

A side fringe, also known as side-swept bangs, is a hairstyle where the hair is cut diagonally across the forehead, creating a fringe that falls to one side. This style has been popular for decades and is still trendy today. It is versatile and can suit different face shapes and hair types, but it’s important to choose the right type of side fringe that will complement your features.

How to Choose the Right Side Fringe

Choosing the right side fringe can be tricky, but there are a few things to consider before making the cut:

1. Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which side fringe will suit you best. Generally, side fringes suit heart-shaped, oval, and square face shapes. If you have a round face, opt for a longer side fringe that elongates your face and creates a more balanced look.

2. Hair Type

Your hair type can also influence which side fringe will suit you. If you have thick hair, opt for a side fringe that’s longer and textured to avoid it looking bulky. If you have fine hair, go for a shorter and wispy side fringe to add volume and texture.

3. Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a side fringe. If you’re always on the go and don’t have time to style your hair frequently, go for a low maintenance side fringe that’s easy to style. If you’re willing to put in the effort, a more complex side fringe can be a great statement style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Side Fringe

If you’re confident in your hair cutting skills, you can cut your side fringe at home. However, we recommend going to a professional hairdresser to ensure the best results. If you do decide to cut your side fringe at home, follow these steps:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair straight and make sure it’s tangle-free.

2. Section Your Hair

Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should be at the top of your head, and the point should be at the center of your forehead.

3. Cut Your Hair

Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it diagonally, starting from the outer corner of your eyebrow and angling it towards the center of your forehead. Cut it slightly longer than you want it to be, as you can always go back and trim it if needed.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your side fringe by blow-drying it with a round brush or using a straightener to add texture and volume. You can also use hair products like hairspray or texturizing spray to keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Side Fringe

Once you’ve got your side fringe, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips:

1. Trim It Regularly

Trim your side fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it at the right length and prevent it from getting too long and covering your eyes.

2. Style It Daily

Style your side fringe daily to keep it looking neat and tidy. Use a straightener or blow-dry it with a round brush to add volume and texture.

3. Use Hair Products

Use hair products like hairspray or texturizing spray to keep your side fringe in place and prevent it from getting frizzy.

FAQs

1. Can anyone pull off a side fringe?

Yes, anyone can pull off a side fringe! It’s all about choosing the right type of side fringe that complements your features.

2. Can I cut my own side fringe?

Yes, you can cut your own side fringe, but we recommend going to a professional hairdresser to ensure the best results.

3. How do I know if a side fringe will suit me?

Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when deciding if a side fringe will suit you. If you’re still unsure, you can try a temporary clip-in side fringe to see how it looks before committing to a cut.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and nourished.

2. Use Heat Protection

Use heat protection products like sprays or serums when styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

4. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and eat a balanced diet to keep your hair hydrated and healthy from the inside out.

Conclusion

In conclusion, a side fringe can be a stylish and versatile hairstyle that suits different face shapes and hair types. By considering your features, lifestyle, and hair care routine, you can choose the right type of side fringe and maintain it to keep it looking its best. So, would a side fringe suit you? The answer is yes, as long as you choose the right one!
