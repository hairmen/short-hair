Looking for a short haircut that will suit your very straight hair? Look no further as we have rounded up the best short haircuts for very straight hair that will surely give you the perfect look you are looking for. Below are some of the best short haircuts that you can choose from:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a very short haircut that is perfect for very straight hair. This haircut is very low maintenance and is perfect for those who are always on the go. The pixie cut is also very versatile as it can be styled in different ways depending on the occasion. You can add some texture to your pixie cut by adding some layers or by adding some bangs.

How to Get a Pixie Cut?

To get a pixie cut, you need to go to a professional hairstylist. The hairstylist will cut your hair very short and will style it according to your preference. You can also bring a picture of the pixie cut that you want so that the hairstylist can have a reference.

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a styling product to add texture to your pixie cut
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your pixie cut

FAQ

Can I add some color to my short hair?

Yes, you can add some color to your short hair to make it look more stylish. However, make sure to use a good quality hair color to avoid damaging your hair.

Can I style my short hair in different ways?

Yes, you can style your short hair in different ways depending on the occasion. You can use a styling product to add some texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Use a heat protectant before using styling tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut
