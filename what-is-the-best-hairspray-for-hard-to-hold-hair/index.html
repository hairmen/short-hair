Description

For those with hard to hold hair, finding the right hairspray can be a challenge. With so many different brands and types of hairspray on the market, it can be difficult to determine which one will work best for your hair type and styling needs. In this article, we will discuss what is the best hairspray for hard to hold hair and provide tips for choosing and using the right hairspray for your hair.

How to Choose the Best Hairspray for Hard to Hold Hair

When choosing a hairspray for hard to hold hair, there are several factors to consider:

Hold Strength

The first thing to consider when choosing a hairspray is the hold strength. For hard to hold hair, you will want a hairspray with strong hold that can keep your style in place all day long. Look for hairsprays labeled as "strong hold" or "extra hold."

Formula

The formula of the hairspray is also important. For hard to hold hair, you will want a hairspray that is lightweight and won't weigh your hair down or make it feel stiff. Look for hairsprays that are labeled as "lightweight" or "flexible hold."

Ingredients

Finally, consider the ingredients in the hairspray. Avoid hairsprays that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make it more difficult to style. Look for hairsprays that contain nourishing ingredients like vitamins and proteins that will help to strengthen and protect your hair.

Step by Step Guide on How to Use the Best Hairspray for Hard to Hold Hair

Using the best hairspray for hard to hold hair is easy. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will ensure that the hairspray can hold your style in place without any interference from oils or moisture in your hair.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. Use a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tool to create your desired look.

Step 3: Apply Hairspray

Hold the can of hairspray about 10-12 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair. Be sure to cover all of your hair, including the roots and ends.

Step 4: Allow Hairspray to Dry

Allow the hairspray to dry completely before touching your hair. This will ensure that your style stays in place all day long.

Tips for Using the Best Hairspray for Hard to Hold Hair

Here are some additional tips for using the best hairspray for hard to hold hair:

Tip 1: Use a Light Hand

When applying hairspray, use a light hand. You don't want to apply too much hairspray, as this can weigh your hair down and make it feel stiff.

Tip 2: Don't Touch Your Hair

Once you have applied hairspray to your hair, avoid touching it. This can cause your style to come undone and make it more difficult to keep your hair in place.

Tip 3: Use a Comb

If you are having trouble getting your hair to stay in place, try using a comb to smooth down any flyaways. This can help to create a more polished look and make your style last longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can hairspray damage my hair?

A: Hairspray can dry out your hair if it contains alcohol. Look for hairsprays that are alcohol-free or contain nourishing ingredients like vitamins and proteins to protect your hair.

Q: How often should I apply hairspray to my hair?

A: You should only need to apply hairspray once a day, after styling your hair. Avoid applying hairspray multiple times throughout the day, as this can cause buildup and make your hair feel stiff.

Q: Can I use hairspray on wet hair?

A: No, you should only use hairspray on dry hair. Applying hairspray to wet hair can cause it to become sticky and difficult to style.

Hair Care Tips for Hard to Hold Hair

Finally, here are some additional hair care tips for those with hard to hold hair:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Hard to hold hair is often dry and brittle, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it more difficult to hold a style. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, or use them sparingly and with a heat protectant spray.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair more difficult to style.

By following these tips and using the best hairspray for hard to hold hair, you can achieve a lasting and stylish look that will last all day long.
