What Can I Do If My Dress Is Too Short

Written by Bardi Oct 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you've ever found yourself in a situation where you're wearing a dress that's too short, you're not alone. It's a common problem that many women face, and it can be stressful and embarrassing. But don't worry! There are several things you can do to remedy the situation and feel confident and comfortable in your dress.

Description

When you're wearing a dress that's too short, it can be uncomfortable and embarrassing. You may feel self-conscious about showing too much skin, or worried that you'll be constantly tugging at your hemline to keep it in place. But there are solutions to this problem, and with a little creativity and effort, you can make your dress work for you.

How to

The first thing you can do if your dress is too short is to add some length to it. This can be done in several ways:

1. Add a slip or petticoat

A slip or petticoat can add some length to your dress, and also create a pretty silhouette. Look for a slip or petticoat that matches the color and style of your dress, and be sure to choose one that's the right length for your dress.

2. Layer with leggings or tights

If you don't want to add length to your dress, you can try layering it with leggings or tights. This will give you some extra coverage while still allowing you to wear your dress.

3. Add a hemline extension

If you're handy with a sewing machine, you can add a hemline extension to your dress. This involves sewing a piece of fabric to the bottom of your dress to extend the length. Be sure to choose a fabric that matches your dress, and sew it on carefully to ensure a neat and tidy finish.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for adding a hemline extension to your dress:

Step 1: Choose your fabric

Choose a fabric that matches your dress in color and texture. You'll need enough fabric to add the desired length to your dress.

Step 2: Measure your dress

Measure the length of your dress, and add the desired amount of length to it. This will give you the length of fabric you'll need to add to the bottom of your dress.

Step 3: Cut your fabric

Cut your fabric to the desired length, and be sure to cut it straight and even.

Step 4: Hem your fabric

Hem the bottom of your fabric by folding it under and sewing it in place. This will create a neat and tidy finish for your hemline extension.

Step 5: Sew your fabric to your dress

Line up your fabric with the bottom of your dress, and sew it in place. Be sure to sew it on carefully and evenly, and use a matching thread color to create a seamless finish.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for dealing with a dress that's too short:

1. Choose the right shoes

The right shoes can make a big difference when it comes to the length of your dress. Choose shoes with a low heel or no heel to help elongate your legs, and avoid shoes with ankle straps or other details that can cut off your leg line.

2. Accessorize smartly

Accessorizing can help draw attention away from a short hemline. Choose statement jewelry, a bold belt, or a statement bag to add interest to your outfit and detract from the length of your dress.

3. Practice good posture

Good posture can help elongate your body and make you appear taller. Stand up straight, pull your shoulders back, and hold your head high to create a long and lean silhouette.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about dealing with a dress that's too short:

1. Can I wear a short dress to a formal event?

Yes, you can wear a short dress to a formal event, but it's important to choose a dress that's appropriate for the occasion. Look for a dress that's made from formal fabrics like silk or satin, and choose a style that's elegant and sophisticated.

2. Can I alter a dress that's too short?

Yes, you can alter a dress that's too short. You can add length to the hemline, or you can alter the dress to create a different style or silhouette that's more flattering.

Hair Care

When you're wearing a dress that's too short, your hair can be an important part of your overall look. Here are some hair care tips to help you look your best:

1. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your dress. A sleek updo or loose waves can be a great choice for a short dress.

2. Use the right products

Use the right hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type, and use styling products like hairspray or mousse to keep your style in place.

3. Take care of your hair

Take care of your hair by getting regular trims, using heat protection products when styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your dress work for you and feel confident and comfortable in any situation.
