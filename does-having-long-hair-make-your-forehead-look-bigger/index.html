Does Having Long Hair Make Your Forehead Look Bigger

Written by Robby Aug 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair has always been associated with femininity, but have you ever wondered if it can make your forehead look bigger? This is a common concern among women who have a high forehead or a receding hairline. In this article, we will discuss whether having long hair can make your forehead look bigger, and provide you with tips on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Description

While having long hair can be a great way to express your personal style, it may not be the best choice for everyone. If you have a high forehead or a receding hairline, wearing your hair in a long style may make your forehead look even bigger. This is because long hair tends to weigh down the hair near your forehead, making it appear flatter and wider.

However, this does not mean that you have to give up on having long hair altogether. By choosing the right hairstyle and styling your hair in a way that flatters your face shape, you can create the illusion of a smaller forehead and a more balanced facial structure.

How to Style Your Hair to Flatter Your Face Shape

The key to styling your hair to flatter your face shape is to create balance and proportion. This means choosing a hairstyle that complements the shape of your face, while also drawing attention away from any areas that you may want to downplay.

If you have a high forehead, one of the best hairstyles for you is a side-swept bang. This style works by creating a diagonal line across your forehead, which helps to break up the width of your forehead and draw attention to your eyes instead. You can also try a layered cut, which can add volume and movement to your hair, while also softening the look of your forehead.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you may want to consider a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head. This can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. A layered cut with volume at the crown or a side-parted pixie cut can be great options.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you may want to choose a hairstyle that softens the angles of your face. A long, layered cut with waves or curls can be a great option, as it adds movement and texture to your hair, while also creating a more feminine look.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that balances out the width of your forehead with the narrowness of your chin. A long, layered cut with face-framing layers can be a great option, as it draws attention to your cheekbones and creates a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for your face shape, here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, then apply a leave-in conditioner or styling product to help protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. If you are using a blow dryer, use a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head, then blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down.
  4. If you are using a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair.
  5. Once your hair is dry and styled, finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place without weighing it down.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle. Some styles work better with thick, curly hair, while others work better with fine, straight hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair each day. Some styles require more maintenance than others.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you. Your hair is a great way to express your personal style, so have fun with it!

FAQs

Q: Does having a fringe help to make your forehead look smaller?

A: Yes, a fringe can help to make your forehead look smaller, especially if it is a side-swept fringe that creates a diagonal line across your forehead.

Q: What is the best way to add volume to my hair?

A: The best way to add volume to your hair is to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, then blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots. You can also try using a volumizing mousse or spray.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it is also important to take care of your long hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or strong dyes.

In conclusion, having long hair does not necessarily make your forehead look bigger. By choosing the right hairstyle and styling your hair in a way that flatters your face shape, you can create a more balanced and proportionate look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
