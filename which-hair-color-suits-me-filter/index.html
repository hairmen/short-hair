Tips on Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips on choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your skin tone

  • Take into account your natural hair color

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

After coloring your hair, it's crucial to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Here are some hair care tips for colored hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Use a color-protecting hair mask once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently
  • Use a UV-protecting hair spray when going out in the sun
