What Hair Is Used For Loc Extensions A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Oct 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Loc extensions are a popular hairstyle option for those who want to achieve the look of dreadlocks without the long-term commitment. However, choosing the right type of hair for your loc extensions is crucial in achieving the desired look and ensuring that your extensions last as long as possible.

Description: What are Loc Extensions?

Loc extensions are an artificial way of creating dreadlocks by adding synthetic or human hair to your natural hair. This hairstyle is achieved by braiding or twisting the hair and then wrapping it with the extension hair. Loc extensions can be customized to suit your desired length, thickness, and color. They are a low-maintenance hairstyle option that can last up to six months with proper care.

How to: Choosing the Right Hair for Loc Extensions

Choosing the right type of hair for your loc extensions is essential in achieving the desired look and ensuring the longevity of your extensions. Here are some factors to consider when choosing hair for your loc extensions:

1. Type of Hair:

There are two types of hair that can be used for loc extensions: synthetic and human hair. Synthetic hair is cheaper and comes in various colors and textures. However, it can be prone to tangling and may not look as natural as human hair. Human hair, on the other hand, is more expensive but looks more natural and can be styled just like your natural hair.

2. Texture:

The texture of the hair you choose for your loc extensions should match the texture of your natural hair. If you have straight hair, it's best to choose straight hair extensions. If you have curly hair, it's best to choose curly hair extensions. Choosing the wrong texture can make your extensions look unnatural and stand out from your natural hair.

3. Length and Thickness:

The length and thickness of your loc extensions should be chosen based on your desired look. If you want long and thick locs, you will need to choose longer and thicker hair extensions. If you want shorter and thinner locs, you will need to choose shorter and thinner hair extensions.

Step by Step: Installing Loc Extensions

Installing loc extensions can be done at home or by a professional stylist. Here is a step-by-step guide for installing loc extensions:

1. Prepare Your Hair:

Wash and condition your hair to ensure that it's clean and free of any product buildup. Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb.

2. Create a Base:

Create a base for your loc extensions by braiding or twisting your hair. The base should be small and tight to ensure that your extensions stay in place.

3. Attach the Extensions:

Attach the extensions to your natural hair by wrapping them around the braids or twists. Use a crochet hook to pull the hair through the base and secure it in place.

4. Continue Adding Extensions:

Continue adding extensions until you achieve your desired length and thickness. Make sure that each extension is securely attached to your natural hair to prevent them from falling out.

5. Finish:

Once all the extensions are attached, use a hair tie to secure the ends of the extensions. Style your locs as desired.

Tips: How to Care for Loc Extensions

Proper hair care is essential in ensuring that your loc extensions last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your loc extensions:

1. Wash and Condition:

Wash and condition your loc extensions regularly to keep them clean and free of any product buildup. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a lightweight conditioner to prevent your locs from becoming heavy or greasy.

2. Avoid Heat:

Avoid using heat on your loc extensions as it can cause damage and weaken the hair. If you need to style your locs, use a low-heat setting on your styling tools.

3. Protect Your Hair:

Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet. This will prevent your locs from becoming tangled or frizzy while you sleep.

4. Trim Your Ends:

Trim the ends of your locs regularly to prevent split ends and keep them looking neat and tidy.

5. Visit a Professional:

Visit a professional stylist regularly to have your locs maintained and tightened. This will prevent your locs from becoming loose or falling out prematurely.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use any type of hair for loc extensions?

No, it's essential to choose the right type of hair for your loc extensions. Synthetic and human hair are the two types of hair that can be used for loc extensions.

2. How long do loc extensions last?

Loc extensions can last up to six months with proper care and maintenance.

3. Do I need to have natural hair to get loc extensions?

No, you don't need to have natural hair to get loc extensions. However, a professional stylist may recommend that you grow out your natural hair to achieve the best results.

Hair Care: Conclusion

Choosing the right type of hair for your loc extensions is crucial in achieving the desired look and ensuring that your extensions last as long as possible. Consider the type of hair, texture, length, and thickness when choosing hair for your loc extensions. Once installed, take care of your loc extensions by washing and conditioning them regularly, avoiding heat, protecting your hair at night, trimming your ends, and visiting a professional stylist regularly for maintenance.
