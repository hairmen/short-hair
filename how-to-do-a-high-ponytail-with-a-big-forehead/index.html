How To Do A High Ponytail With A Big Forehead

Written by William Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to create a high ponytail with a big forehead? Don't worry! This step-by-step guide will show you how to achieve a perfect high ponytail that flatters your face. We will also provide you with some tips and hair care advice to maintain your ponytail look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a High Ponytail with a Big Forehead
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to create a high ponytail with a big forehead:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before creating a high ponytail. This will ensure that your ponytail looks neat and tidy.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Brush your hair to remove any knots and tangles. Make sure that your hair is smooth and easy to work with.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into three parts: the top, middle, and bottom. Use a hair tie to secure the middle and bottom sections, leaving the top section loose.

Step 4: Tease the top section

Take the top section of your hair and tease it using a fine-toothed comb. This will create volume and texture, making your ponytail look fuller.

Step 5: Pull your hair back

Pull your hair back into a high ponytail, making sure that it is centered on the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure your ponytail in place.

Step 6: Smooth your hair

Smooth your hair using a brush or comb. Ensure that there are no bumps or flyaways.

Step 7: Add finishing touches

Add some finishing touches to your high ponytail, such as a hair accessory or hairspray. This will help to keep your ponytail in place and prevent it from falling out.

Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you create a perfect high ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that is strong and durable to prevent your ponytail from falling out.
  • If you have a big forehead, pull your hair back tightly to create a sleek and polished look.
  • Tease your hair to create volume and texture. This will make your ponytail look fuller.
  • Use a hairspray to keep your ponytail in place and prevent it from falling out.
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a ribbon or bow, to make your high ponytail look more stylish.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to secure a high ponytail?

The best way to secure a high ponytail is to use a hair tie that is strong and durable. Make sure that your hair tie is tight enough to hold your ponytail in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

How can I make my high ponytail look more voluminous?

You can make your high ponytail look more voluminous by teasing your hair using a fine-toothed comb. This will create texture and volume, making your ponytail look fuller.

Hair Care Advice

To maintain your high ponytail look, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair smooth and easy to work with.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to create a high ponytail with a big forehead, you can rock this hairstyle with confidence! Use our tips and hair care advice to maintain your ponytail look and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
