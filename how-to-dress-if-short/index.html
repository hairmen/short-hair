How To Dress If Short Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're on the shorter side, you might find it challenging to dress in a way that flatters your figure. However, with a few simple tips and tricks, you can create outfits that make you look taller and more confident. In this article, we'll discuss how to dress if short, including choosing the right clothes, accessorizing, and styling your hair.

How to Choose the Right Clothes

The right clothes can make all the difference when it comes to dressing for your height. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Wear Vertical Stripes

Vertical stripes create the illusion of height, so look for clothes with vertical stripes. You can wear a striped shirt or a pair of striped pants to elongate your body.

2. Opt for High-Waisted Bottoms

High-waisted bottoms, such as skirts, pants, and shorts, can make your legs look longer. They also create the illusion of a smaller waist.

3. Choose Fitted Clothes

Baggy clothes can overwhelm a petite frame, so choose fitted clothes that flatter your figure. Just be sure not to wear anything too tight, as it can create bulges and make you look shorter.

4. Avoid Oversized Accessories

Oversized accessories, such as large bags and chunky jewelry, can make you look smaller. Instead, opt for smaller accessories that complement your outfit.

How to Accessorize

Accessorizing can be a game-changer when it comes to dressing for your height. Here are some tips to help you accessorize:

1. Wear Nude Shoes

Nude shoes can make your legs look longer, especially when paired with high-waisted bottoms. Look for shoes that match your skin tone to create a seamless look.

2. Add a Belt

A belt can help define your waist and create the illusion of a longer torso. Try wearing a belt with a high-waisted skirt or pants.

3. Wear Statement Jewelry

While oversized jewelry can overwhelm a petite frame, statement jewelry can add interest to your outfit without making you look smaller. Look for pieces with interesting shapes or textures.

How to Style Your Hair

Your hair can also make a difference when it comes to dressing for your height. Here are some tips to help you style your hair:

1. Go for a Bob

A bob can create the illusion of height, especially when paired with a deep side part. Keep it sleek and straight for maximum impact.

2. Wear Your Hair Up

Wearing your hair up can elongate your neck and create the illusion of height. Try a sleek ponytail or a top knot.

3. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can also make you look taller. Try a volumizing mousse or a teasing comb to add some height to your hair.

Tips for Dressing if Short

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when dressing for your height:

1. Embrace Monochrome

Wearing all one color, or monochrome, can create a streamlined look that elongates your body. Try wearing all black or all white for an easy monochrome outfit.

2. Choose the Right Length

When it comes to skirts and dresses, be sure to choose the right length. A midi skirt that hits just below the knee can be more flattering than a mini skirt that cuts off your legs.

3. Experiment with Heels

Heels are an obvious choice for adding height, but they can be uncomfortable. Try experimenting with different heel heights and styles to find what works for you.

FAQ

Q: Can short people wear maxi dresses?

A: Yes, short people can wear maxi dresses, but it's important to choose the right length. Look for a dress that hits just above your ankle to avoid overwhelming your frame.

Q: Can short people wear wide-leg pants?

A: Yes, short people can wear wide-leg pants, but it's important to choose the right fit. Look for pants that fit at the waist and flare out at the bottom to create a flattering silhouette.

Q: Are there any colors short people should avoid?

A: No, there aren't any colors short people should avoid. However, it's important to choose colors that flatter your skin tone and complement your outfit.

Hair Care Tips for Short People

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help add volume to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

2. Try a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can add texture and volume to your hair, making it easier to style and more voluminous.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and full. Be sure to talk to your stylist about the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create outfits that make you look taller and more confident. Remember to choose clothes that flatter your figure, accessorize wisely, and style your hair in a way that complements your outfit. With a little bit of effort, you can dress to impress no matter your height!
