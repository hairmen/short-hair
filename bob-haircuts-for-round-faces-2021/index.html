Bob Haircuts For Round Faces 2021 A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 12, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be challenging. Luckily, bob haircuts are a great option for those with round faces. In this guide, we’ll go over everything you need to know about bob haircuts for round faces in 2021, including how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and more.

Description

Bob haircuts are a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. They are versatile and can be tailored to suit any face shape, including round faces. Bob haircuts for round faces typically feature a shorter length and layers that frame the face to create an illusion of length. This helps to balance out a round face and make it appear slimmer.

How to Choose the Right Bob Haircut for Your Round Face

When choosing a bob haircut for your round face, it’s important to consider your hair texture and the shape of your face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider your hair texture

If you have fine hair, a blunt bob may not be the best choice as it can make your hair look flat. Instead, opt for a layered bob with texture to add volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob can help to thin it out and create a sleek look.

2. Determine your face shape

There are a few different types of round faces, including circular, square, and heart-shaped. Determining your face shape can help you choose the right bob haircut to flatter your features.

3. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a bob haircut, it’s important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don’t have a lot of time to style your hair, a shorter bob may be a better option. If you have more time to style your hair, a longer bob with layers can be a great choice.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Bob Haircuts for Round Faces

Styling bob haircuts for round faces is relatively easy, and there are many ways to do it. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling to ensure that it’s clean and free of any product buildup.

2. Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage when using hot styling tools.

3. Use a round brush

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. This will help to create volume and bounce.

4. Add texture

Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture to your hair. You can create loose waves or curls for a more relaxed look, or use a straightener for a sleeker style.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Bob Haircuts for Round Faces

Maintaining your bob haircut is essential to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Get regular trims

To keep your bob looking its best, it’s important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

2. Use the right products

Using the right products can help to keep your bob looking healthy and shiny. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

3. Protect your hair from heat

When using hot styling tools, always use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

FAQ

1. Can a bob haircut make a round face look slimmer?

Yes, a bob haircut can create an illusion of length and help to balance out a round face, making it appear slimmer.

2. What is the best length for a bob haircut for a round face?

The best length for a bob haircut for a round face is typically chin-length or shorter. This helps to create an illusion of length and balance out the face.

3. Can I wear a middle part with a bob haircut for a round face?

Yes, you can wear a middle part with a bob haircut for a round face. However, it’s important to add some volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care Tips for Bob Haircuts for Round Faces

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is key to maintaining a stylish bob haircut. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo to wash your hair, and avoid washing it every day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.

2. Use a hydrating conditioner

Use a hydrating conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

3. Apply a hair mask once a week

Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

Bob haircuts for round faces are a great option for those looking for a stylish and versatile haircut. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering look that suits your face shape and lifestyle. Keep these tips in mind, and you’ll be sure to love your new bob haircut.
