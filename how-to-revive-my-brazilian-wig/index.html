How To Revive My Brazilian Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a Brazilian wig that has lost its luster and shine, don't worry! There are several ways to revive it and make it look as good as new. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to revive your Brazilian wig. We will also answer some frequently asked questions about wig care and maintenance. Read on to learn more!

How to Revive My Brazilian Wig: Step by Step

Reviving a Brazilian wig is easier than you might think. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Detangle your wig

Before you start, make sure your wig is detangled. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to gently comb through the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 2: Wash your wig

Wash your wig with a mild shampoo and lukewarm water. Don't use hot water, as it can damage the hair. Gently massage the shampoo into the hair and rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Condition your wig

Apply a good quality conditioner to your wig and leave it on for a few minutes. Rinse thoroughly with cool water. Don't use too much conditioner, as it can weigh down the hair.

Step 4: Dry your wig

Use a towel to gently pat your wig dry. Don't twist or wring the hair, as it can damage the strands. Let your wig air dry on a wig stand or a mannequin head.

Step 5: Style your wig

Once your wig is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a heat protectant spray if you're using heat styling tools, and don't use high heat settings. You can also use a wig spray or serum to add shine and smoothness to your wig.

Tips for Reviving Your Brazilian Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you revive your Brazilian wig:

  • Use a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, high heat settings, and harsh chemicals on your wig.
  • Don't sleep or shower with your wig on, as it can cause tangling and matting.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or a mannequin head to maintain its shape and prevent tangling.
  • Brush your wig regularly to prevent tangling and maintain its shine.

FAQ: Wig Care and Maintenance

Here are some frequently asked questions about wig care and maintenance:

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

No, you should use a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the hair.

Can I use heat styling tools on my wig?

Yes, you can use heat styling tools on your wig, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't use high heat settings.

How do I store my wig?

You should store your wig on a wig stand or a mannequin head to maintain its shape and prevent tangling.

How do I brush my wig?

You should use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to gently comb through the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Hair Care: Keep Your Wig Looking Great

Reviving your Brazilian wig is just the first step. To keep it looking great, you need to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for your wig:

  • Avoid exposing your wig to sunlight and heat, as it can damage the hair.
  • Don't use hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out the hair.
  • Store your wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture.
  • Don't wear your wig for extended periods of time, as it can cause scalp irritation and hair loss.

By following these tips and tricks, you can revive your Brazilian wig and keep it looking great for years to come!
