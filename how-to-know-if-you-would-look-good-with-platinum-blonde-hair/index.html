How To Know If You Would Look Good With Platinum Blonde Hair

Written by Oliver Nov 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of going platinum blonde but unsure if it will suit you? Platinum blonde hair is a bold and striking hair color that can completely transform your look. However, it's important to consider whether it will complement your skin tone and facial features. In this article, we'll provide you with tips on how to know if you would look good with platinum blonde hair.

  • Take Your Eye Color into Account

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is the most important factor to consider when deciding whether platinum blonde hair will suit you. If you have warm undertones, such as olive or golden skin, platinum blonde may not be the best choice for you as it can clash with your skin. On the other hand, if you have cool undertones, such as pink or blue skin, platinum blonde can complement your complexion beautifully.

Take Your Eye Color into Account

Eye color is another important factor to consider. Platinum blonde hair tends to look best on those with light-colored eyes, such as blue, green, or gray. However, if you have dark eyes, such as brown or black, you can still pull off platinum blonde hair with the right styling and makeup.

Assess Your Face Shape

Your face shape can also play a role in whether platinum blonde hair will suit you. If you have a round face, platinum blonde hair can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. If you have a square face, platinum blonde hair can soften your features and create a more feminine look. However, if you have a long face, platinum blonde hair can make your face appear even longer, so it may not be the best choice.

Think About Your Hair Type

Your hair type can also affect whether platinum blonde hair will suit you. If you have fine hair, platinum blonde hair can give your hair more dimension and make it appear thicker. However, if you have coarse or curly hair, platinum blonde hair may require more maintenance and styling to keep it looking sleek and smooth.

Try On a Wig

If you're still unsure whether platinum blonde hair will suit you, try on a wig in this color. This way, you can see how it looks on you without committing to it permanently. You can also experiment with different shades of platinum blonde, such as ash or silver, to find the one that complements your skin tone and eye color the most.

Talk to Your Stylist

Your stylist can also provide you with valuable advice on whether platinum blonde hair will suit you. They can assess your hair type, face shape, and skin tone to help you make an informed decision. They can also recommend the best shade of platinum blonde for your skin tone and eye color, as well as give you tips on how to maintain your hair's health and color.

FAQ

1. Is platinum blonde hair high maintenance?

Yes, platinum blonde hair requires more maintenance than other hair colors. It needs to be touched up every few weeks to maintain its color and prevent brassiness. It also requires regular deep conditioning treatments to keep it healthy and shiny.

2. Can I dye my hair platinum blonde at home?

We do not recommend dyeing your hair platinum blonde at home as it can be a difficult and risky process. It's best to leave it to the professionals to ensure that the color turns out evenly and that your hair remains healthy.

3. What should I do if my hair turns brassy?

If your platinum blonde hair turns brassy, use a purple shampoo or toner to neutralize the yellow tones. You can also visit your stylist for a color correction treatment.

Hair Care Tips for Platinum Blonde Hair

Platinum blonde hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Touch up your roots every few weeks to maintain the color.

By following these tips and considering your skin tone, eye color, face shape, and hair type, you can determine whether platinum blonde hair will suit you. If you're ready to take the plunge, make sure to consult with your stylist for the best results.
