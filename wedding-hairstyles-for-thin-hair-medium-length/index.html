Wedding Hairstyles For Thin Hair Medium Length Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Nov 05, 2023 · 5 min read

For brides with thin hair, finding the perfect wedding hairstyle can be a daunting task. You want a style that will flatter your face, stay in place all day, and make you feel confident and beautiful. If you have medium length hair, there are plenty of options available to you. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best wedding hairstyles for thin hair medium length, along with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Choose the Right Wedding Hairstyle for Thin Hair Medium Length

When it comes to choosing the right wedding hairstyle for thin hair medium length, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: Certain styles will be more flattering for certain face shapes. For example, an updo can elongate a round face, while a bob can balance out a heart-shaped face.
  • Think about your dress: Your hairstyle should complement your dress, not compete with it. If you have a high-necked dress, an updo can help show off the neckline. If you have a strapless dress, loose waves can soften the look.
  • Take your hair texture into account: If you have naturally curly hair, you may want to embrace your curls on your wedding day. If your hair is straight, you may want to add some waves for texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Wedding Hairstyles for Thin Hair Medium Length

Here’s how to create three different wedding hairstyles for thin hair medium length:

Loose Waves

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  2. Use a 1-inch curling iron to curl 1-inch sections of hair away from your face.
  3. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to loosen the curls into waves.
  4. Spray your hair with a flexible hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Start by curling your hair with a 1-inch curling iron.
  2. Take a 2-inch section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it to create volume.
  3. Smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Leave the rest of your hair down and curl any sections that need it.
  6. Spray your hair with a flexible hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Messy Bun

  1. Begin by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  4. Spray your hair with a flexible hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Wedding Hairstyles for Thin Hair Medium Length

Here are some tips to help you keep your wedding hairstyle looking great all day:

  • Use a hairspray with a flexible hold to keep your style in place without making it look stiff.
  • Carry extra bobby pins and a small can of hairspray with you to touch up your style throughout the day.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can make it look greasy and flat.
  • If you’re worried about your hair falling flat, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

FAQs About Wedding Hairstyles for Thin Hair Medium Length

What’s the best way to add volume to thin hair?

The best way to add volume to thin hair is to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow dry your hair upside down, and use a teasing comb to create volume at the roots.

Can I wear extensions with thin hair?

Yes, you can wear extensions with thin hair to add volume and length. Just make sure to choose extensions that are made for thin hair and apply them properly to avoid damaging your natural hair.

What’s the best way to keep my wedding hairstyle in place?

The best way to keep your wedding hairstyle in place is to use a hairspray with a flexible hold and carry extra bobby pins and hairspray with you for touch-ups throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair Medium Length

To keep your thin hair healthy and looking its best, try these tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Limit how often you wash your hair to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

With these tips and tricks, you’re sure to find the perfect wedding hairstyle for your thin hair medium length. Whether you choose loose waves, a half-up half-down style, or a messy bun, you’ll look and feel beautiful on your special day.
