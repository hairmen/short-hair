How To Know What Hairstyle Suits You Black Male

Are you a black male looking to change up your hairstyle but not sure what suits you best? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step guidance on how to determine what hairstyle suits you best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to determining the right hairstyle for you is identifying your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a picture of your face head-on.

Once you have the picture, use a ruler or a tape measure to determine the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Round face shapes have a similar width in all three areas, while oval face shapes have a slightly longer face with a narrower jawline. Square face shapes have a strong and defined jawline, while heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower jawline.

Matching Your Hairstyle with Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to match it with the right hairstyle. For round face shapes, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume on top and avoid styles that are too short on the sides. Oval face shapes can pull off a variety of hairstyles, but a classic buzz cut or a short fade is always a safe option. Square face shapes can benefit from hairstyles that soften the jawline, such as a textured fringe or a side-swept cut. Heart-shaped faces can opt for hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as a quiff or a messy side part.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Different hair types require different maintenance and styling, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type. If you have curly hair, consider a short afro or a textured crop. If you have straight hair, a sleek buzz cut or a classic side part can work well. If you have thick hair, consider a longer hairstyle that allows for more volume and movement.

Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a more classic style, a neat and tidy hairstyle such as a slicked-back undercut or a classic side part may be more suited to you. If you have a more edgy style, consider a textured crop or a messy fringe.

Face Features

Lastly, consider your face features when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a prominent nose, a textured fringe can help balance out your features. If you have high cheekbones, consider a short buzz cut to highlight your facial structure.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Bring pictures to your barber or stylist to ensure they understand the look you want.
  • Consider hair maintenance and styling when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

For round face shapes, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume on top and avoid styles that are too short on the sides.

Can I pull off a buzz cut if I have an oval face?

Yes, oval face shapes can pull off a variety of hairstyles, but a classic buzz cut or a short fade is always a safe option.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure your hairstyle looks its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type and consider incorporating a hair oil or serum to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, finding the right hairstyle for black males involves considering factors such as face shape, hair type, personal style, and face features. By following the tips and guidance outlined in this article, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that suits you best and helps you feel confident and stylish.
