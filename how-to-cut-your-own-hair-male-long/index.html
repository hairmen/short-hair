How To Cut Your Own Hair Male Long A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair can be a hassle to maintain, especially during a pandemic when salons are closed. If you're a male with long hair, you might be thinking of cutting it yourself. But the idea of cutting your own hair can be daunting, especially if you've never done it before. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair male long.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be a cost-effective and convenient way to maintain your long locks. However, it's important to note that cutting long hair can be more challenging than cutting short hair, so be prepared to take your time and have patience.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Male Long

The first step in cutting your own hair male long is to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and hair clippers (optional).

Next, find a well-lit area with a mirror where you can comfortably stand or sit. It's important to have a clear view of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by parting it down the middle and then from ear to ear. Use hair clips to hold the sections in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start cutting the back of your hair by using a mirror to see the back of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it straight across. Take small sections at a time and cut a little bit at a time to avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the sides of your hair. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at a slight angle towards the back. Again, take small sections at a time and cut a little bit at a time.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top of your hair. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it straight across. You can also use hair clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Don't cut too much off at once.
  • Use hair clippers to trim the sides and back.
  • Watch tutorials or ask a friend for help if you're unsure.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair if it's really long?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if it's really long. However, it may take more time and patience than cutting shorter hair.

Do I need to use hair clippers?

No, you don't need to use hair clippers. They're optional and can be used to trim the sides and back of your hair.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim your hair shorter or seek help from a professional stylist.

Hair Care

After cutting your own hair male long, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Trim your hair every few months to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Cutting your own hair male long can be a challenge, but with the right tools and technique, it's possible to achieve a salon-quality cut from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure.
