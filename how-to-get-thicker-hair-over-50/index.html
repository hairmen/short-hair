How To Get Thicker Hair Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 50 and struggling with thinning hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many women experience hair loss or thinning as they age. However, there are several things you can do to get thicker, healthier hair. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you get the thick, luscious hair of your dreams.

How to Get Thicker Hair Over 50: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Check Your Diet

One of the most important factors in hair health is your diet. Make sure you're getting enough protein, iron, and vitamins A and C. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth. You can find them in foods like eggs, spinach, sweet potatoes, and citrus fruits. If you're having trouble getting enough nutrients from your diet, consider taking a supplement.

Step 2: Use Thickening Shampoo and Conditioner

There are many shampoos and conditioners on the market that are designed to add volume and thickness to hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen. These ingredients can help strengthen and thicken hair over time.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage hair and make it look thinner. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Step 4: Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Talk to your stylist about getting a cut that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Step 5: Try Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a quick, temporary fix for thinning hair, consider trying hair extensions. There are many types of extensions available, from clip-ins to tape-ins to sew-ins. Talk to your stylist about which type of extension would work best for your hair.

Tips for Getting Thicker Hair Over 50

Here are some additional tips for getting thicker, healthier hair:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels
  • Consider talking to a doctor about hormonal imbalances or other health issues that could be causing hair loss

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can hair loss be reversed?

A: In some cases, yes. It depends on the cause of the hair loss. Talk to your doctor about possible treatments.

Q: Is it normal to lose hair as you age?

A: Yes, it's normal to experience some hair loss or thinning as you age. However, if you're experiencing sudden or severe hair loss, it's important to talk to a doctor.

Q: Are there any medications that can help with hair loss?

A: Yes, there are several medications that can help with hair loss. Talk to your doctor about whether any of these medications are right for you.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair Over 50

Once you've achieved the thick, luscious hair you've been dreaming of, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair
  • Avoid brushing hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes
  • Wash hair with lukewarm water, not hot water
  • Avoid over-washing hair, as this can strip it of natural oils

By following these tips and tricks, you can get thicker, healthier hair over 50. Remember, it's never too late to achieve the hair of your dreams.
