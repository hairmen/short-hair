How To Get Curly Hair With Cantu Products

Written by Eveline Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cantu is a popular hair care brand that offers a range of products specifically designed for curly and kinky hair types. If you're looking to enhance your natural curls and achieve a bouncy, defined look, Cantu products can help you achieve your hair goals. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to get curly hair with Cantu products.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you have naturally curly hair, you can use Cantu products to enhance your curls and add moisture to your hair. Here's how:

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair. Cantu offers a range of shampoos and conditioners that are formulated to add moisture and enhance curls. Use a generous amount of shampoo, lather, and rinse thoroughly. Follow up with the conditioner, making sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 2: Apply Curl Activator Cream

After washing your hair, apply Cantu's Curl Activator Cream to damp hair. This product is designed to define and moisturize curls. Use a generous amount, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Once you've applied the Curl Activator Cream, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. The diffuser will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with the diffuser. Make sure not to over-dry your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Styling Gel

To keep your curls in place, finish off with Cantu's Styling Gel. This product will help to define your curls and add shine. Apply a small amount to your hair and scrunch it gently. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your curly hair:

  • Don't over-wash your hair. Curly hair tends to be drier than other hair types, so avoid washing it every day.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best Cantu products for curly hair?

Cantu's Curl Activator Cream and Styling Gel are both great products for enhancing and defining curls.

Can I use Cantu products on relaxed hair?

Yes, Cantu products can be used on relaxed hair to add moisture and enhance curls.

Hair Care with Cantu Products

Cantu products are designed to nourish and moisturize curly and kinky hair. In addition to the Curl Activator Cream and Styling Gel, Cantu offers a range of other products, including leave-in conditioners, deep conditioning masks, and hair oils. By incorporating Cantu products into your hair care routine, you can keep your curls looking healthy and defined.

Now that you know how to get curly hair with Cantu products, it's time to start experimenting with different styles and techniques. With the right products and a little bit of practice, you can achieve gorgeous, bouncy curls that are the envy of everyone around you.
