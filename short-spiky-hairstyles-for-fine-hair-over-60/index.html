Short Spiky Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 60 A Guide

Written by Frank Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose its volume. However, this doesn't mean that we can't have fun with our hairstyles. Short spiky hairstyles for fine hair over 60 are perfect for adding some edge and personality to your look. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve this style, provide some tips, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Achieve Short Spiky Hairstyles for Fine Hair Over 60

The first step in achieving short spiky hairstyles for fine hair over 60 is to find the right cut. You'll want to work with a hairstylist who has experience with this type of style and can recommend a cut that will work well with your face shape and hair texture.

Once you have your cut, you'll need to style your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. You may also want to apply a styling product, such as a mousse or styling cream, to help add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Use a Texturizing Spray

Next, use a texturizing spray to add some texture to your hair. This will help your hair hold the spikes and give it some added volume. Apply the spray to the roots of your hair and work it through with your fingers.

Step 3: Use a Blow Dryer

Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair while shaping it into the desired style. Use your fingers to create spikes and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Use Hairspray

Finally, use a hairspray to set your hair in place. This will help your spikes hold their shape throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Short Spiky Hairstyles for Fine Hair Over 60

Here are some tips for maintaining your short spiky hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your spikes looking sharp.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo in between washes to help add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Can I Achieve Short Spiky Hairstyles for Fine Hair Over 60 If I Have Curly Hair?

Yes, you can achieve short spiky hairstyles for fine hair over 60 if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use a flat iron to straighten your hair before adding the spikes.

Can I Achieve Short Spiky Hairstyles for Fine Hair Over 60 If I Have Thinning Hair?

Yes, you can achieve short spiky hairstyles for fine hair over 60 if you have thinning hair. In fact, this style can help add the illusion of volume to your hair. Just make sure to work with a hairstylist who can recommend a cut that will work well with your hair texture.

How Often Should I Wash My Hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. However, you may want to avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Hairstyles for Fine Hair Over 60

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when you have short spiky hairstyles for fine hair over 60:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too frequently, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes or relaxers, as these can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or hair accessories, as these can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

In conclusion, short spiky hairstyles for fine hair over 60 are a great way to add some edge and personality to your look. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep your spikes looking sharp and stylish. Have fun experimenting with different styles and cuts, and don't be afraid to try something new!
