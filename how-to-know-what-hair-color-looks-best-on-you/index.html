How To Know What Hair Color Looks Best On You

Written by Bardi Aug 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be overwhelming, but with a little guidance, you can find the perfect shade that will enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we will provide you with tips on how to know what hair color looks best on you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color can make a huge difference in your appearance. The right hair color can enhance your natural features and make you look younger, while the wrong hair color can make you look older and dull. It’s important to take your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color into consideration when choosing a hair color.

How to

Here are some tips on how to know what hair color looks best on you:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining your best hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. If you have warm skin tones, you should choose hair colors that have golden or caramel undertones. If you have cool skin tones, you should choose hair colors that have ashy or cool undertones. If you have neutral skin tones, you can choose hair colors that have warm or cool undertones.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you determine your best hair color. If you have blue eyes, you should choose hair colors that have cool undertones. If you have brown eyes, you can choose hair colors that have warm or cool undertones.

3. Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Looking at your natural hair color can also help you determine your best hair color. If you have warm undertones in your hair, you should choose hair colors that have warm undertones. If you have cool undertones in your hair, you should choose hair colors that have cool undertones.

Step by Step

Here are the steps you can follow to determine your best hair color:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm skin tones. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool skin tones. If they appear blue-green, you have neutral skin tones.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Look at your eye color and determine if it’s warm or cool. If your eye color has warm undertones, you have warm eye color. If your eye color has cool undertones, you have cool eye color.

3. Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Look at your natural hair color and determine if it has warm or cool undertones. If your natural hair color has warm undertones, you have warm hair color. If your natural hair color has cool undertones, you have cool hair color.

4. Choose Your Hair Color

Based on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can choose the hair color that will look best on you. If you have warm skin tones, warm eye color, and warm hair color, you should choose hair colors that have warm undertones. If you have cool skin tones, cool eye color, and cool hair color, you should choose hair colors that have cool undertones.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how often you’re willing to maintain your hair color.
  • Always do a patch test before coloring your hair to avoid an allergic reaction.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for advice from a hair stylist.
  • Consider getting a temporary hair color before committing to a permanent one.

FAQ

What if I have multiple skin tones?

If you have multiple skin tones, choose a hair color that complements the majority of your skin tone.

Can I dye my hair a drastically different color?

You can dye your hair a drastically different color, but it’s important to consider the upkeep and whether the color will complement your skin tone and eye color.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen your hair color, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid hot tools and excessive heat.
  • Use a hair mask regularly to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid prolonged sun exposure to your hair.

Now that you know how to determine your best hair color, you can confidently choose a shade that will enhance your natural beauty. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color when choosing a hair color, and don’t be afraid to ask for advice from a hair stylist.
