How To Cut Men s Short Length Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new haircut for your curly hair? Cutting men’s short length curly hair can be quite challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut men’s short length curly hair, as well as some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Men’s Short Length Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Men’s Short Length Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Men’s Short Length Curly Hair

How to Cut Men’s Short Length Curly Hair

The first step in cutting men’s short length curly hair is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a clipper or trimmer, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Next, determine the length you want to cut your hair. It’s best to start with a longer length and gradually cut it shorter. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can’t add hair back.

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be difficult to cut, and it can also shrink when it dries, making it harder to achieve the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Begin by using the clipper or trimmer to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter one. Use the comb to guide the clipper, and make sure to blend the hair at the top of your head with the sides and back.

2. Use the scissors to cut the hair on top of your head. Take small sections of hair and cut it to the desired length. You can also use the scissors to add texture to your hair by cutting into it at an angle.

3. Use the comb to check for any uneven sections and make sure everything is blended well.

4. Use the spray bottle to dampen your hair if necessary and make any final adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Men’s Short Length Curly Hair

- Use a small pair of scissors with a pointed tip for more precision.

- Cut your hair in a well-lit area so you can see what you’re doing.

- Don’t cut your hair too short, as it can make your curls look frizzy.

- If you’re unsure about cutting your hair yourself, consider going to a professional stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. On average, men with short length curly hair should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I use clippers to cut my curly hair?

A: Yes, but make sure to use the right guard and go slowly to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Hair Care Tips for Men’s Short Length Curly Hair

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and prevent frizz.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.

- Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Cutting men’s short length curly hair can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Remember to take your time and start with a longer length, and don’t be afraid to seek professional help if you’re unsure. With proper care, your curly hair will look healthy and stylish.
