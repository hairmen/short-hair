Description

Men's hair shorter on sides longer on top is a popular hairstyle that has been around for years. This hairstyle is also known as an undercut and involves having shorter hair on the sides of the head while keeping the hair on the top of the head longer. It has become a trendy and fashion-forward look that can be seen on runways, red carpets, and in everyday life.

The men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle can be customized to suit different hair types and face shapes. It can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is low maintenance and versatile, making it a perfect choice for men who prefer a hassle-free grooming routine.

How to Achieve Men's Hair Shorter on Sides Longer on Top

The men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle can be achieved in several ways, such as using clippers, scissors, or a combination of both. The basic idea is to have the hair on the sides of the head shorter than the hair on the top of the head.

Using Clippers

To achieve the men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle using clippers, follow these steps:

  1. Choose the desired length for the hair on the sides of the head. Use a #3 or #4 clipper guard for a short, but not too short, look.
  2. Trim the hair on the sides of the head using the clipper guard of your choice. Start at the bottom and work your way up, following the natural curve of the head.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides of the head with the hair on the top of the head using a clipper-over-comb technique. This involves using the clippers to trim the hair while combing it downwards.
  4. Style the hair on the top of the head as desired.

Using Scissors

To achieve the men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle using scissors, follow these steps:

  1. Section the hair on the top of the head using clips or hair ties.
  2. Choose the desired length for the hair on the sides of the head. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides of the head with the hair on the top of the head using scissors-over-comb technique. This involves using the scissors to trim the hair while combing it downwards.
  4. Style the hair on the top of the head as desired.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle:

  1. Wash and towel dry your hair.
  2. Section the hair on the top of the head using clips or hair ties.
  3. Choose the desired length for the hair on the sides of the head.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides of the head to the desired length.
  5. Blend the hair on the sides of the head with the hair on the top of the head using clippers-over-comb or scissors-over-comb techniques.
  6. Style the hair on the top of the head as desired using products such as pomade, wax, or gel.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Hair Shorter on Sides Longer on Top

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle:

  • Visit a barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the hairstyle.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh the hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find a look that works best for you.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage the hair over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit different hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily.

Q: Can the men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle be worn with a beard?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be worn with or without a beard, depending on personal preference.

Q: Can the men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle be styled for formal occasions?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be styled in a sleek and sophisticated way for formal occasions, such as weddings or job interviews.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Shorter on Sides Longer on Top

Here are some hair care tips for men's hair shorter on sides longer on top:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent over-washing and drying out the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair, as using a brush can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on the hair, as these can damage the hair over time.

Men's hair shorter on sides longer on top is a timeless and versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different preferences and lifestyles. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or a messy and textured look, this hairstyle is a perfect choice for any occasion. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this guide, you can achieve and maintain the perfect men's hair shorter on sides longer on top hairstyle.
