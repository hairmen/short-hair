How To Get Waves In Short Pixie Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have short pixie hair, you might think that getting waves is impossible. However, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve beautiful, effortless waves in your short hair. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of getting waves in short pixie hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get Waves in Short Pixie Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Getting Waves in Short Pixie Hair
  5. FAQs

Description

Short pixie hair is a trendy and low-maintenance haircut that many women love. However, sometimes you might want to switch it up and add some texture and movement to your hair. This is where waves come in. Waves can add volume, dimension, and a playful vibe to your short hair.

There are different ways to get waves in short pixie hair, depending on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. In this article, we’ll focus on using a curling iron or wand to create natural-looking waves.

How to Get Waves in Short Pixie Hair

To get waves in short pixie hair, you’ll need a few tools and products:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Styling cream or texturizing spray
  • Hairspray
  • Comb or brush

Once you have these, you’re ready to start. Here’s an overview of the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Blow-dry your hair or let it air-dry until it’s about 80% dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb or brush to section your hair into small sections. You can create bigger or smaller sections depending on the size of the waves you want.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, starting from the roots and going towards the ends. Hold the hair for about 10-15 seconds, depending on the thickness of your hair and the heat of the iron. Release the hair and let it cool for a few seconds before moving on to the next section.

Repeat this process for all the sections of hair until your whole head is curled.

Step 4: Define the Waves

Once you’ve curled all your hair, use your fingers or a comb to gently separate the curls and create waves. You can also apply a styling cream or texturizing spray to help define the waves and add texture.

Step 5: Set the Waves

To make sure your waves stay in place, apply a hairspray to your hair. Hold the spray about 10 inches away from your head and mist lightly over your hair.

Tips for Getting Waves in Short Pixie Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best waves in your short pixie hair:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a smaller barrel size to create tighter waves.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, use a lower heat setting on your curling iron to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don’t curl your hair too tightly or too close to the roots, as this can create a dated or unnatural look.
  • Experiment with different products, such as mousse or sea salt spray, to see what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Can you get waves in short pixie hair without heat?

Yes, you can get waves in short pixie hair without heat by using braids, twists, or bantu knots. These methods involve creating sections of hair and wrapping them around each other to create waves. However, these methods may take longer and may not create as defined waves as using a curling iron or wand.

How long does it take to get waves in short pixie hair?

The time it takes to get waves in short pixie hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair and the method you’re using. Using a curling iron or wand can take anywhere from 15 to 45 minutes, depending on how many sections of hair you have and how tight you want the waves to be.

How long do waves last in short pixie hair?

The longevity of waves in short pixie hair depends on your hair type, the products you use, and your daily activities. Generally, waves can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on how well you maintain them. To make your waves last longer, you can sleep with a satin pillowcase, avoid touching your hair too much, and refresh your waves with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Hair

Getting waves in short pixie hair can be a fun way to switch up your look, but it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips for short pixie hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can get beautiful, effortless waves in your short pixie hair. Whether you’re going for a beachy vibe or a glam look, waves are a versatile and stylish option for short hair.
