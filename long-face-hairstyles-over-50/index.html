Long Face Hairstyles Over 50 Tips How To And Hair Care

Written by Christine Aug 09, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re over 50 and you have a long face, you might be wondering what hairstyles suit you best. Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this blog post, we’ll provide you with tips, how-to’s, and hair care advice for long face hairstyles over 50. Read on to find out more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a high forehead, long cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have this face shape and you’re over 50, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and complements your age. Long face hairstyles over 50 can range from short to long, and they can be curly or straight. The key is to find a hairstyle that balances your face shape and highlights your best features.

How-To

The first step in choosing a long face hairstyle over 50 is to determine your face shape. If you’re not sure, use a measuring tape to measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin. Then, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the length is longer than the width, you have a long face shape.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, consider the following factors when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your personal style and preferences
  • Your lifestyle and daily routine
  • Your hair color and skin tone

Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to choose a long face hairstyle over 50:

  1. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape and complement your age.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion and advice.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that you feel confident and comfortable in.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to find the look that suits you best.
  5. Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and hair care routine.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing long face hairstyles over 50:

  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add volume and texture.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too severe or harsh, as they can accentuate your face shape and age.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add interest and variety to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long face hairstyles over 50:

Q: Can I wear my hair long if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair long if you have a long face. However, be sure to add layers and texture to avoid a flat and lifeless look.

Q: Should I choose a curly or straight hairstyle?

A: It depends on your hair texture and personal style. Both curly and straight hairstyles can be flattering for long face shapes over 50.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining your long face hairstyle over 50:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and healthy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to moisturize and nourish your hair.

With these tips, how-to’s, and hair care advice, you’ll be able to choose a long face hairstyle over 50 that flatters your features and complements your age. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find the look that suits you best. Happy hairstyling!
