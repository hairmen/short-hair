What Men s Hairstyles Are In Right Now

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but not sure what's in right now? Look no further! In this article, we'll go over the latest trends in men's hairstyles for 2023.

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles have been popular for a while now and they're still going strong in 2023. The classic buzz cut is always a safe bet, but if you want something a little more stylish, try a textured crop or a French crop. Both of these styles have short sides and a longer, textured top.

Another popular short hairstyle is the faux hawk, which is a more modern take on the classic mohawk. It has shorter sides and a longer, spiky top, making it a great option for guys who want a more edgy look.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium length hairstyles are also in right now. The messy, textured look is popular, with longer layers on top and shorter sides. This look can be achieved with a bit of product and some tousling with your fingers.

If you want something a bit more polished, try a slicked back look. This style has been popular for a while and it's still going strong. Use a pomade or gel to slick back your hair, giving you a sleek and professional look.

Long Hairstyles

For those who like to keep their hair long, there are plenty of options as well. The man bun has been a popular style for a few years now and it's still going strong in 2023. To achieve this look, simply pull your hair back and tie it in a bun at the top of your head.

If you want something a bit more unique, try a braided hairstyle. Braids have been popular for both men and women in recent years, and they're a great way to add some interest to your long hair. Try a single braid down the back or multiple braids for a more intricate look.

Fade Hairstyles

Fade hairstyles are another popular trend in 2023. This style has short hair on the sides and back that gradually fades into longer hair on top. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on your preference.

There are many variations of the fade, including the high top fade, which has longer hair on top, and the bald fade, which has very short hair on the sides and back. The fade can also be combined with other styles, such as a slicked back top or a textured crop.

Curly Hairstyles

Curly hair is in right now, and there are many options for men with curly hair. The messy, textured look is popular, with longer layers on top and shorter sides. You can also embrace your curls and let them do their thing, with a bit of product to keep them under control.

If you want a more polished look, try a side part with your curls. This style has shorter sides and a longer, curly top that's parted to one side. Use a bit of pomade to keep your curls in place and you're good to go.

Man Bun Hairstyles

The man bun is still in style in 2023, and there are many variations to choose from. The classic man bun has longer hair on top that's pulled back into a bun at the back of the head. You can also try a half-up man bun, which has half of your hair pulled back into a bun, with the rest left down.

Another option is the braided man bun, which combines the man bun with braids for a unique look. You can also try a messy man bun, which has a more relaxed and casual feel.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

When choosing a new hairstyle, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your face shape. Certain styles look better on certain face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your features.

Next, consider your hair type. Some styles work better with thick hair, while others work better with thin or curly hair. It's also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair.

Step by Step

  1. Think about your face shape and hair type.
  2. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to put into styling your hair.
  3. Research different styles and choose one that you like.
  4. Find a good stylist who can help you achieve your desired look.
  5. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What is the most popular men's hairstyle in 2023?

The most popular men's hairstyle in 2023 is the messy, textured look with longer layers on top and shorter sides.

What is the best hairstyle for men with thinning hair?

The best hairstyle for men with thinning hair is a short, textured cut with a bit of volume on top.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on the style and how fast your hair grows. Generally, it's a good idea to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a good quality styling product to keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know what men's hairstyles are in right now, it's time to choose a style that works for you. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of options to choose from. Just remember to choose a style that flatters your features and fits your lifestyle, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
