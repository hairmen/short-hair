How To Make Blonde Hair Less Grey Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your blonde hair looking more grey than actually blonde? Don't worry, you're not alone! This is a common issue for those with naturally blonde hair or those who dye their hair blonde. We've put together some tips and tricks on how to make blonde hair less grey.

How to

There are a few things you can do to make your blonde hair less grey.

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is specifically formulated for blonde hair to counteract any unwanted brassy or yellow tones. When blonde hair starts to turn grey, it's usually because there's too much yellow in the hair. Using a purple shampoo once or twice a week can help neutralize the yellow and bring back the blonde.

2. Try a Toner

A toner is another great option for neutralizing any unwanted tones in your hair. If your hair is looking more grey than blonde, try using a toner with a cool-tone to bring back the blonde.

3. Avoid Heat

Heat can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. If you're using heat tools on your hair regularly, try to cut back and use heat protectant products when you do use them.

4. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Healthy hair is always going to look better than damaged hair. Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help restore any damage and bring back the shine in your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step by step guide on how to make blonde hair less grey.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a purple shampoo. Make sure to massage the shampoo into your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 2: Apply Toner

If your hair is still looking grey, apply a toner to your hair. Make sure to follow the instructions on the toner and leave it on for the recommended amount of time.

Step 3: Avoid Heat

Try to avoid using heat tools on your hair and use heat protectant products when you do use them.

Step 4: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to restore any damage and bring back the shine in your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make blonde hair less grey.

Tip 1: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull and lifeless. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

Tip 2: Use a Leave-in Conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from looking dry and frizzy.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will make your hair look healthier and less grey.

FAQ

Q: Can I use purple shampoo every day?

A: It's not recommended to use purple shampoo every day as it can dry out your hair. Use it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I use a toner without using purple shampoo first?

A: Yes, you can use a toner without using purple shampoo first. However, using purple shampoo can help enhance the toner's effects.

Q: Can I use a deep conditioning treatment every day?

A: It's not recommended to use a deep conditioning treatment every day as it can weigh down your hair. Use it once a week.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use these tips and tricks to make blonde hair less grey and keep it looking its best.
