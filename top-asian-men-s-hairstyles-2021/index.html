Top Asian Men s Hairstyles 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

If you're an Asian man looking to switch up your hairstyle this year, you've come to the right place. We've rounded up the top Asian men's hairstyles for 2021, from classic cuts to trendy styles. In this article, we'll cover how to achieve these looks, provide tips for maintaining them, and answer some frequently asked questions. So, without further ado, let's dive in!

  • Trendy Styles

Classic Cuts

Classic cuts never go out of style, and they're perfect for Asian men who prefer a clean, polished look. Here are some of the top classic cuts for 2021:

1. The Side Part

The side part is a timeless look that's easy to achieve. Simply comb your hair to one side and use a bit of pomade or wax to keep it in place. This look is perfect for formal occasions and work settings.

2. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another classic style that's low-maintenance and versatile. It's especially good for men with square or oval faces. Simply ask your barber to trim the sides and back short while leaving some length on top.

Trendy Styles

If you're looking for something a bit more fashion-forward, these trendy styles might be right up your alley:

1. The Undercut

The undercut has been a popular style for a few years now, and it shows no signs of going away. It involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving the top long. This style is perfect for men with thick, straight hair.

2. The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a low-maintenance style that's perfect for men who want to look stylish without putting in too much effort. It involves cutting your hair short on the sides and back while leaving some length on top. Then, use a bit of texturizing spray to create a messy, tousled look.

How to Achieve These Looks

Whether you're going for a classic cut or a trendy style, here are some general tips for achieving these looks:

  • Bring a photo of the style you want to your barber or stylist
  • Use the right products, such as pomade, wax, or texturizing spray
  • Invest in a quality comb or brush
  • Be patient and don't rush the styling process

Tips for Maintenance

Once you've achieved your desired look, here are some tips for maintaining it:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

FAQ

1. What hairstyles are best for Asian men?

Asian men can pull off a wide range of hairstyles, from classic cuts like the side part to trendy styles like the textured crop or undercut. It's all about finding the right look for your face shape and hair type.

2. How do I choose the right hairstyle for my face shape?

There are several factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle, including your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Generally, men with round faces look best with angular haircuts, while men with square faces look best with softer, more rounded styles.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, shiny hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can strip away natural oils
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, especially when wet
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

With these tips and styles in mind, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your unique look. Whether you opt for a classic cut or a trendy style, remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
