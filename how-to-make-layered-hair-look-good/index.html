How To Make Layered Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Layered hair is a popular hairstyle that can add volume and movement to your locks. However, if not styled properly, it can look messy and unflattering. In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make layered hair look good.

How to

Here are some steps you can follow to make your layered hair look good:

Step 1: Choose the Right Layers

The first step is to choose the right layers for your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist and ask for layers that will suit your features and enhance your hair's natural texture.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

To make your layered hair look good, you need to use the right products. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Apply a mousse or a styling cream to your damp hair before blow-drying to add texture and hold.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement to your layers. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

To add more texture and movement to your layers, use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair. Use a medium-sized barrel and curl small sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light-hold hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make your layered hair look good:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your layers.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • If you have fine hair, avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look flat.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making layered hair look good:

How do I know if layered hair will suit me?

Consult with your hairstylist to determine if layered hair will suit your face shape and hair texture. They can recommend the right type of layers for you.

Can I still wear my layered hair straight?

Yes, you can still wear your layered hair straight. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and use a serum to add shine and smoothness.

Hair Care

To keep your layered hair looking good, you need to take care of it. Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using hot tools every day, as it can damage your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling on your layers.

Follow these tips and tricks to make your layered hair look good. With the right styling and care, your layered hair can be a flattering and stylish hairstyle.
