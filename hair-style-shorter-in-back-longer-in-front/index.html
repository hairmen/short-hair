Hair Style Shorter In Back Longer In Front A Modern Trend

Written by Oliver Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you in search of a new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the hair style shorter in back longer in front! This modern hairstyle has taken the beauty industry by storm, and for good reason. Not only is it stylish, but it adds volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this trend, from how to achieve it to hair care tips.

Description

The hair style shorter in back longer in front is a modern twist on the classic bob. This hairstyle features shorter layers in the back and longer layers in the front. This creates an angled look that is both edgy and feminine. The shorter layers in the back add volume and texture, while the longer layers in the front frame your face and add movement. This hairstyle looks great on any hair type and can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to Achieve the Look

Achieving the hair style shorter in back longer in front is surprisingly easy. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Stylist

Before you attempt to cut your own hair, it is important to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape. They can also give you tips on how to maintain the hairstyle at home.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into three sections: top, middle, and bottom. Clip the top section out of the way, leaving the middle and bottom sections loose.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layers

Starting with the bottom section, cut the hair to the desired length. Cut in a straight line across the back of your head, creating a blunt edge.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layers

Release the middle section of your hair and comb it straight down. Using the bottom layer as a guide, cut the middle layer to the desired length.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layers

Release the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Using the middle layer as a guide, cut the top layer to the desired length.

Step 6: Add Texture

To add texture to your hair, use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk from the back layers. This will create a more natural, layered look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair shorter in back longer in front:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add even more volume
  • Use a round brush to add movement to the longer layers in the front
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and style

FAQ

What face shape does this hairstyle suit?

The hair style shorter in back longer in front looks great on all face shapes. The longer layers in front frame your face and add movement, while the shorter layers in the back add volume and texture.

Can I achieve this hairstyle if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can achieve this hairstyle if you have thin hair. In fact, the shorter layers in the back can actually make your hair appear thicker.

How long does it take to style this hair?

Styling time will vary depending on your hair type and length. On average, it should take no more than 30 minutes to style your hair shorter in back longer in front.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the health and style of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as these can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hair style shorter in back longer in front. This modern hairstyle is versatile, stylish, and easy to maintain. So why not give it a try?
