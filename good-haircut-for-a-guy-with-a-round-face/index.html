Good Haircut For A Guy With A Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of getting the wrong haircut for your round face? Do you want to know what type of haircut will suit your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for a guy with a round face. We will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect haircut and give you some tips on how to maintain it.

Description

A round face has soft features and lacks sharp angles. The width of the face is almost the same as the length, giving it a circular shape. The goal of a good haircut for a guy with a round face is to create an illusion of length and add angles to the face. The right haircut can make your face look more chiseled and defined.

How to?

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step in getting the right haircut is to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width and length of your face. If the measurements are almost the same, then you have a round face.

Step 2: Choose the right haircut

The best haircut for a guy with a round face is one that adds height and angles to the face. Here are some haircuts that will suit your face shape:

  • Short back and sides with a longer top
  • Side-parted hairstyle
  • Textured crop
  • Pompadour
  • Fringe

Step 3: Communicate with your stylist

When you go to the salon, make sure to communicate with your stylist. Show them pictures of the haircut you want and explain what you like and dislike about your current haircut. Your stylist will be able to suggest the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a styling product to create volume and texture.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much width to your face.
  • Experiment with different haircuts to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much width to your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look rounder.

Can I still have a fringe if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have a fringe if you have a round face. Opt for a side-swept fringe that adds angles to your face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a good haircut. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

In conclusion, getting the right haircut for a guy with a round face is all about creating an illusion of length and adding angles to the face. Choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair type, communicate with your stylist, and maintain your haircut with proper hair care. Follow these tips and tricks, and you will have the perfect haircut that suits your round face.
