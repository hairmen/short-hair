Hairstyles To Suit Round Face Shape Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face shape, you might find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your features. You want to create an illusion of length and angles to balance your face shape. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that can suit your round face shape. Read on for our tips and tricks on hairstyles to suit round face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles to Suit Round Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for your round face shape is to determine your face shape. Here's how to do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a tape measure to measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Write down the measurements and compare them.
  4. If your cheekbones and jawline are almost the same width as your forehead, you have a round face shape.

Now that you know your face shape, let's explore some hairstyles that can flatter your features.

Hairstyles to Suit Round Face Shape

When it comes to hairstyles for round face shape, the key is to create height and angles that elongate your face. Here are some hairstyles to try:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help create the illusion of length and volume, making your face appear slimmer. Ask your stylist to add layers starting from your cheekbones down.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create angles that balance a round face shape. Ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that reach your cheekbones.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can also suit round face shapes. Ask your stylist for a layered pixie cut that adds volume on top and keeps the sides short.

4. Bob

A bob can also flatter a round face shape if it's done correctly. Ask your stylist for a long bob that's slightly longer in the front and shorter in the back. This will create angles and elongate your face.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Aside from the hairstyles mentioned above, here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle for your round face shape:

1. Avoid Chin-Length Cuts

Chin-length cuts can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for longer cuts that create angles and elongate your face.

2. Add Volume on Top

Adding volume on top can help create the illusion of length. Consider trying a voluminous updo or a high ponytail.

3. Consider Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can help create angles and balance a round face shape. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your cheekbones and frame your face.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a middle part with a round face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear a middle part with a round face shape. Just make sure to add volume on top to create the illusion of length.

Q: Can I wear short hair with a round face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear short hair with a round face shape. Just make sure to add volume on top and keep the sides short to create angles.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Shape

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a round face shape:

1. Keep Your Hair Healthy

Keeping your hair healthy can help it look its best. Make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Using hair accessories can help add volume and create angles. Consider trying a headband or a hair clip to add some interest to your hairstyle.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and see what works best for you. Your stylist can also offer advice on what styles would suit your face shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your round face shape and helps you look your best.
