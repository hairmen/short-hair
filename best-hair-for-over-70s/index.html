Best Hair For Over 70S Tips Styles And Care

Written by Bardi Jun 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for the best hair for over 70s, you're in the right place. As we age, our hair changes, and it's essential to find a hairstyle that suits our hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for over 70s, tips, and hair care routines to keep your locks healthy and vibrant.

How to Choose the Best Hair for Over 70s

Choosing the best hair for over 70s can be daunting. However, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hair for over 70s:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining the best hair for over 70s. If you have a round face, you should opt for a hairstyle that adds height and length. For a square face, choose a style that softens your jawline. If you have an oval face, you're lucky because most hairstyles will suit you.

Think About Your Hair Texture

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and drier, making it prone to breakage. If you have thin hair, opt for a shorter hairstyle that adds volume and texture. For thick hair, choose a longer style that accentuates your hair's natural texture.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing the best hair for over 70s, it's crucial to consider your lifestyle. If you're an active person, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. However, if you're a social butterfly, opt for a more glamorous and sophisticated look.

Step by Step Guide to the Best Hair for Over 70s

Here's a step-by-step guide to the best hair for over 70s:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the best hair for over 70s is determining your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step 2: Choose Your Haircut

When choosing the best haircut for over 70s, consider your hair texture and face shape. For thin hair, opt for a layered bob or a pixie cut. If you have thick hair, choose a longer style with layers. For a round face, choose a hairstyle that adds height and length. For a square face, opt for a soft, layered style.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen your haircut, it's time to style your hair. If you have thin hair, use volumizing products to add texture and volume. For thick hair, use a smoothing serum to tame frizz and add shine. If you're going for a more glamorous look, consider adding curls or waves to your hair.

Tips for the Best Hair for Over 70s

Here are some additional tips for the best hair for over 70s:

Tip 1: Embrace Your Natural Hair Color

As we age, our hair loses its pigment, making it appear gray or white. However, embracing your natural hair color can be a beautiful and elegant look. If you're not ready to go gray, consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Tip 2: Experiment with Accessories

Accessories can add a touch of glamour and sophistication to any hairstyle. Consider adding a headband, scarf, or hairpin to your hair to elevate your look.

Tip 3: Invest in Quality Hair Products

Investing in high-quality hair products can make all the difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free, paraben-free, and contain natural ingredients.

FAQs About the Best Hair for Over 70s

What is the best haircut for over 70s with thin hair?

The best haircut for over 70s with thin hair is a layered bob or a pixie cut. These styles add volume and texture to thin hair.

What is the best haircut for over 70s with thick hair?

The best haircut for over 70s with thick hair is a longer style with layers. This style accentuates the hair's natural texture and adds movement.

How can I make my hair look fuller?

You can make your hair look fuller by using volumizing products, blow-drying your hair upside down, and adding layers to your haircut.

What hair products should I use for my over 70s hair?

You should use hair products that are sulfate-free, paraben-free, and contain natural ingredients. Look for products that add moisture, volume, and shine to your hair.

Hair Care for Over 70s

Here are some hair care tips for over 70s:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

As we age, our hair produces less oil, making it prone to dryness and breakage. Therefore, it's essential to wash your hair less frequently to prevent stripping it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

Tip 2: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

When washing your hair, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals. These ingredients can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and breakage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, causing dryness and breakage. Therefore, it's essential to protect your hair from heat by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

Overall, finding the best hair for over 70s requires considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. However, with the right haircut, styling products, and hair care routine, you can have healthy, vibrant, and beautiful hair at any age.
