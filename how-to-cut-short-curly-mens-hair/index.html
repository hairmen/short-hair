How To Cut Short Curly Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with curly hair, you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. Short hairstyles are particularly tricky, as curly hair tends to have a mind of its own. But fear not! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish, low-maintenance cut that looks great and feels great.

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have the right tools on hand:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Water spray bottle
  • Mirror

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can lead to uneven results. Comb your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.

Next, decide on the length you want your hair to be. It's best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more as needed. Keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries, so you may want to leave a little extra length to compensate.

Cutting Your Hair

Start by using your scissors to trim any longer hairs that stand out. Hold the ends of the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. This will help even out your hair and give you a starting point.

Next, use your clippers to start cutting the sides and back of your hair. You can use a comb to help guide the clippers and create a more even cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard as needed.

When cutting the top of your hair, use your scissors to create texture and shape. Use a point-cutting technique to create more movement and prevent your hair from looking too boxy.

Fading Your Hair

If you want a faded look, use your clippers to create a gradual fade from the sides to the top of your hair. Start with a longer guard at the bottom and work your way up to a shorter guard as you move higher on your head.

Blend the different lengths together using a blending comb or your clippers without a guard. Be sure to blend carefully and check your work frequently in the mirror to avoid any harsh lines.

Cleaning Up

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to clean up any stray hairs. Use your clippers or scissors to trim any hairs around your ears and neckline. You can also use a trimmer to clean up your sideburns if needed.

Styling Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to work it through. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help define your curls.

Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair. Remember, curly hair is unique and may require some trial and error to get the perfect style.

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more as needed
  • Use a comb to help guide your clippers and create a more even cut
  • Use a point-cutting technique to create more movement and prevent your hair from looking too boxy
  • Check your work frequently in the mirror to avoid any harsh lines
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair?

It's best to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my hair myself?

While it's possible to cut your hair yourself, it's often best to leave it to a professional. They have the experience and tools necessary to create a great-looking cut.

Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can lead to uneven results.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

To keep your short curly hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair, and avoid using heat tools as much as possible. Instead, opt for air-drying or diffusing your hair.

Be sure to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your style. And don't forget to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

Cutting short curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking style. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best, and don't be afraid to get creative!
