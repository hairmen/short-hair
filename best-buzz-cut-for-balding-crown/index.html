Best Buzz Cut For Balding Crown A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling with balding crown and want to try a new look? A buzz cut can be an excellent option for you! It’s a simple yet stylish hairstyle that suits almost everyone. In this post, we will guide you on the best buzz cut for balding crown, how to do it yourself, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A buzz cut is a short hairstyle where the hair is cut close to the scalp. It’s a classic haircut that has been around for decades and is still popular today. The buzz cut is an excellent option for men with balding crown as it can make your hairline less noticeable. It’s also a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn’t require much styling or upkeep.

How to

You can get a buzz cut at a salon or do it yourself at home with clippers. If you’re doing it yourself, make sure you have the right tools, including clippers, guards, scissors, and a comb. Start by washing your hair and combing it in the direction you want it to lay.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to do a buzz cut for balding crown:

  1. Choose the length of the guard you want to use.
  2. Begin at the front of your head and work your way back, using the clippers to cut the hair in even strokes.
  3. Use a shorter guard around the balding crown area to make it less noticeable.
  4. Blend the hair around the crown area with the rest of the hair to create a seamless look.
  5. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or longer hairs around the ears and neckline.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure you get the best buzz cut for balding crown:

  • Use a mirror to ensure you’re cutting your hair evenly.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to avoid cutting your hair too short.
  • Don’t forget to trim the hairs around your ears and neckline with scissors.
  • Experiment with different guard lengths to find the one that suits you best.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with hard-to-reach areas.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best buzz cut for balding crown:

Q. Can a buzz cut make balding crown less noticeable?

A. Yes, a buzz cut can make your balding crown less noticeable by blending the hair around the crown area with the rest of the hair.

Q. How often should I get a buzz cut?

A. A buzz cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can last for several weeks. You can get it trimmed every 3-4 weeks to maintain the length.

Q. What’s the best guard length for a buzz cut?

A. The best guard length for a buzz cut depends on your personal preference and the shape of your head. It’s best to experiment with different guard lengths to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care

After getting a buzz cut for balding crown, it’s essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your scalp from the sun by wearing a hat or applying sunscreen.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your buzz cut.

Overall, a buzz cut can be an excellent option for men with balding crown. It’s a simple yet stylish hairstyle that’s easy to maintain and can make your hairline less noticeable. By following the tips and instructions in this post, you can get the best buzz cut for balding crown and take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
