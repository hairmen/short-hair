How To Dry Curly Hair After Shower A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that drying it after a shower can be a challenge. The wrong technique can leave your curls frizzy, limp, or damaged. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to dry curly hair after showering, along with some tips and FAQs to help you achieve beautiful, healthy curls.

  • How to Dry Curly Hair After Shower
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Drying Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Dry Curly Hair After Shower

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first discuss the different methods for drying curly hair after a shower:

  • Air-drying
  • Plopping
  • Diffusing
  • T-shirt drying

Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages, and the best one for you will depend on your curl type, hair length, and personal preference. In general, air-drying is the gentlest method, while diffusing is the fastest.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to dry curly hair after a shower:

  1. Blot your hair gently with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously as this can cause frizz.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  3. If you prefer to air-dry, gently scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curl formation. You can also use a wide-tooth comb or a Denman brush to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  4. If you prefer to plop, lay a t-shirt flat on a surface and flip your wet hair onto it. Use the t-shirt to wrap your hair into a turban and leave it on for 15-20 minutes. This method helps to reduce frizz and enhance curl definition.
  5. If you prefer to diffuse, attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it on low heat and medium speed. Use the diffuser to lift your curls at the roots and dry them gently. Avoid touching your hair too much as this can cause frizz.
  6. If you prefer to T-shirt dry, lay a t-shirt flat on a surface and flip your wet hair onto it. Use the t-shirt to scrunch your hair gently and remove excess water. This method also helps to reduce frizz.
  7. Once your hair is dry, you can use a lightweight oil or serum to add shine and moisture.

Tips for Drying Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when drying your curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot your hair instead of a regular towel. Regular towels can be too rough and cause frizz.
  • Apply your leave-in conditioner or curl cream to soaking wet hair to help distribute the product evenly.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying to prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser on low heat and medium speed to dry your hair gently without causing heat damage.
  • Experiment with different drying methods to find the one that works best for your curl type and hair length.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about drying curly hair after a shower:

Is it better to air-dry or diffuse curly hair?

It depends on your preference and hair type. Air-drying is the gentlest method, but it can take a long time and may not define your curls as well as diffusing. Diffusing can be faster and enhance curl definition, but it can also cause heat damage if not done properly.

Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

We do not recommend using a regular towel to dry your curly hair as it can cause frizz and damage. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt instead.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair wash their hair every day, while others wash it once a week. In general, it's best to wash your hair when it feels oily or dirty.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Drying your curly hair after a shower is just one part of your hair care routine. Here are some additional tips to help you keep your curls healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls that you will love.
