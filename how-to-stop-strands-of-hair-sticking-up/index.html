How To Stop Strands Of Hair Sticking Up Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you frequently find yourself struggling with hair strands that refuse to lay flat, no matter what you do? This can be a frustrating issue to deal with, but fortunately, there are several solutions you can try to keep your hair looking smooth and polished. In this article, we'll cover how to stop strands of hair sticking up, with step-by-step instructions, helpful tips, and answers to frequently asked questions.

How to Stop Strands of Hair Sticking Up: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with a Clean Slate

The first step to preventing hair strands from sticking up is to start with a clean slate. Wash and condition your hair thoroughly, using products that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, as this can make the problem worse.

Step 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it gently. This will help prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Avoid using a brush, as this can cause static and make your hair stand up even more.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that tend to be dry. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz and static. Be sure to choose a product that is lightweight and non-greasy.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using a nozzle attachment to direct the airflow. Use a low or medium heat setting, and avoid blowing the hot air directly onto your hair. Instead, hold the dryer about 6 inches away from your hair and use a round brush to smooth your hair as you dry it.

Step 5: Use a Serum or Oil

After blow-drying your hair, apply a serum or oil to your hair to add shine and control flyaways. Be sure to use a small amount, as too much can make your hair look greasy. Focus on the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired, using a hair spray or gel to hold it in place. Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and make your hair stand up.

Tips for Preventing Strands of Hair from Sticking Up

Here are some additional tips to help prevent hair strands from sticking up:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and static
  • Avoid using hot tools on a high heat setting
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Consider getting a haircut with layers to add more movement to your hair

FAQ: How to Stop Strands of Hair Sticking Up

Q: Why does my hair stick up?

A: Hair can stick up for several reasons, including dryness, static, and breakage. Using the wrong products or over-styling your hair can also contribute to the problem.

Q: What kind of products should I use to prevent hair strands from sticking up?

A: Look for lightweight, non-greasy products, such as leave-in conditioners, serums, and oils. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Q: Can I use a brush to detangle my hair?

A: It's best to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this is gentler on your hair and less likely to cause static and frizz.

Hair Care: Preventing Strands of Hair from Sticking Up

Preventing hair strands from sticking up requires a combination of good hair care practices and the right products. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can keep your hair looking smooth and polished, no matter what your hair type or texture. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid using too many styling products, and invest in high-quality products that are tailored to your hair's needs.
