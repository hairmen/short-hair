What Size Curling Iron Is Best For Beachy Waves

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're after those tousled, effortless beachy waves, you're probably wondering what size curling iron is best to achieve those perfect, undone curls. The truth is, there's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. The size of the curling iron you use will depend on a few factors, including the length of your hair, the texture of your hair, and the type of waves you're after.

Description

Beachy waves are a timeless hairstyle that look great on everyone. They're easy to achieve, and they give your hair a natural, effortless look. But to get those perfect, undone waves, you need to choose the right curling iron size. In this article, we'll explore what size curling iron is best for beachy waves, and we'll give you some tips on how to achieve this look.

How to

The first step to achieving beachy waves is to prep your hair. You'll want to start with clean, dry hair, and apply a heat protectant spray to your locks. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy. Once your hair is prepped, it's time to choose your curling iron size.

Step by Step

If you have short hair, a curling iron with a smaller barrel size will work best. A 1-inch curling iron will give you tighter, more defined curls, while a 1.25-inch curling iron will give you looser, more relaxed waves. If you have medium-length hair, a 1.5-inch curling iron will work well, giving you loose, beachy waves. If you have long hair, you can go for a larger curling iron size. A 2-inch curling iron will give you loose, voluminous waves that are perfect for a beachy look.

When using your curling iron, it's important to use the right technique. Start by dividing your hair into sections, and then wrap the sections around the curling iron barrel, holding each section for a few seconds before releasing. To get the perfect beachy waves, alternate the direction of the curls, curling some sections towards your face, and others away from your face. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural, undone look.

Tips

When choosing your curling iron size, it's important to consider the texture of your hair. If you have fine, straight hair, you'll want to go for a smaller curling iron size to create more defined curls. If you have thick, coarse hair, you can go for a larger curling iron size to create loose, voluminous waves.

Another tip is to use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to enhance your beachy waves. These products will give your hair a natural, tousled look, and add texture and volume to your locks.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve beachy waves without a curling iron?
A: Yes, you can achieve beachy waves without a curling iron. You can use a flat iron, braids, or even a hair straightener to create waves in your hair.

Q: How long will my beachy waves last?
A: The longevity of your beachy waves will depend on your hair type and the products you use. On average, beachy waves can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Hair Care

When styling your hair with heat tools, it's important to take care of your locks. Always use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage, and avoid using high heat settings. When you're not styling your hair, give it a break from heat tools, and use nourishing hair masks and oils to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

So there you have it, everything you need to know about what size curling iron is best for beachy waves. With the right curling iron size and technique, you can achieve those perfect, effortless waves that will make you feel like you've just stepped off the beach.
