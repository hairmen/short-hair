How To See If A Hair Colour Will Suit Me Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour, and want to try something new and exciting? Changing hair colour can be a fun way to switch up your look, but it can also be intimidating, especially if you're not sure if a new colour will suit you or not. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you determine if a hair colour will look good on you.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color
  • Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining whether a hair color will suit you is to figure out your skin tone. There are three main categories of skin tone: cool, warm, and neutral. If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blue tint to it. If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow or peachy tint to it. If you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a combination of both cool and warm tones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell whether your veins are blue or green, you likely have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in which hair colors will suit you. For example, if you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colors like blonde, ash brown, and black may complement your eyes. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde, auburn, and chestnut may look best on you.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Another factor to consider is your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it may be more difficult to achieve a lighter hair color without damaging your hair. If you have light hair, you may be able to go darker without much issue.

It's also important to keep in mind that if you have gray hair, some hair colors may not cover it completely, or may fade more quickly than others.

Step 4: Try a Virtual Makeover

If you're still not sure which hair color will look best on you, try a virtual makeover. Many websites and apps offer virtual makeovers where you can upload a photo of yourself and try on different hair colors to see how they look.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time and money you're willing to invest in maintaining a new hair color.
  • Start with a semi-permanent or temporary hair color to test out a new shade before committing to a permanent color.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice on which hair color will suit you best.

FAQs About Choosing a New Hair Color

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a new hair color:

  • Q: Will a new hair color damage my hair?
  • A: It depends on the type of hair color you choose and how often you color your hair. Some hair colors can be more damaging than others, so it's important to choose a high-quality hair color and take good care of your hair.
  • Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?
  • A: It depends on the type of hair color you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair color should be touched up every 6-8 weeks, while semi-permanent hair color can last up to 4 weeks.
  • Q: Can I bleach my hair at home?
  • A: It's not recommended to bleach your hair at home, as it can be very damaging and result in uneven color. It's best to leave bleaching to a professional stylist.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause your color to fade more quickly.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can also cause your color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can determine if a hair color will suit you and make the most out of your new look. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist for advice and to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
