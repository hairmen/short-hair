How To Cut Long Shaggy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've been rocking long shaggy hair for a while and are ready for a change, cutting it yourself can be a daunting task. But fear not, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look in no time. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut long shaggy hair at home.

Description

Long shaggy hair is a popular hairstyle that's characterized by layers of varying lengths that frame the face and add movement to the hair. It works well for all hair types and is especially flattering for those with thick, wavy, or curly hair. While this style can be achieved at a salon, it can be expensive to maintain. Cutting your hair at home is a cost-effective way to keep your shaggy style looking fresh.

How to Cut Long Shaggy Hair

Before you begin, gather the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair down the middle and clip each side away. Then, create another part from ear to ear and clip the top section away from the bottom section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Working with the bottom section first, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold your scissors vertically and make small snips in an upward motion to create layers. Continue this process, working your way up to the top section.

Step 4: Cut the Top Section

Once you've finished layering the bottom section, unclip the top section and repeat the same process, but this time start from the back and work your way forward. To create more volume, you can also make small snips on the top layer of the hair.

Step 5: Trim the Ends

Once you've finished layering, you can trim the ends to create a more polished look. Hold your scissors horizontally and make small snips to get rid of any split ends or uneven lengths.

Tips

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to bigger ones to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair as you go.
  • If you're unsure about the length, it's always better to cut less than more.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're struggling.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair if it's wet?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry as wet hair can stretch and lead to uneven lengths.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Can I use regular scissors?

No, it's important to use sharp hair-cutting scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and avoid using heating tools too often. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help maintain the health of your hair.

Cutting your long shaggy hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and low-maintenance look that will have you feeling confident and refreshed.
