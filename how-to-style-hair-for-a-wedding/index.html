How To Style Hair For A Wedding Tips Tricks And Ideas

If you're attending a wedding this year, you're probably already thinking about what to wear and how to style your hair. After all, weddings are one of the most important events in people's lives, and you want to look your best. In this blog post, we'll give you some tips, tricks, and ideas on how to style your hair for a wedding.

How to Style Hair for a Wedding

When it comes to styling your hair for a wedding, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, you want to choose a style that complements your dress and overall look. Second, you want to make sure your hair stays in place throughout the day and night. Here are some popular wedding hairstyles:

Braids

Braids are a popular choice for weddings because they're easy to do and can be dressed up or down. You can do a simple braid, a French braid, or even a fishtail braid. Braids can be worn up or down, and you can add accessories like flowers or jewels to dress them up.

Updos

Updos are another popular choice for weddings because they're elegant and timeless. You can do a classic bun, a chignon, or even a messy updo. Updos can be worn with or without accessories, and you can add volume with hair extensions or teasing.

Half-Up, Half-Down

For a more casual look, you can do a half-up, half-down hairstyle. This is a great option if you want to show off your long hair but still keep it out of your face. You can add curls or waves to the bottom half of your hair for a romantic look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do a simple braided updo:

  1. Brush your hair and create a deep side part.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and braid it. Secure the end with a small elastic.
  3. Repeat step 2 on the other side of your head.
  4. Gather the rest of your hair into a low ponytail.
  5. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  6. Take one of the braids and wrap it around the bun, securing it with bobby pins.
  7. Repeat step 6 with the other braid.
  8. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Styling Hair for a Wedding

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a wedding:

  • Choose a style that complements your dress and overall look.
  • Make sure your hair stays in place throughout the day and night.
  • Add accessories like flowers or jewels to dress up your hairstyle.
  • If you're doing your hair yourself, practice beforehand to avoid any last-minute stress.
  • Consider hiring a professional stylist to help you achieve the perfect look.

FAQs

How far in advance should I book a hair stylist for my wedding?

You should book a hair stylist as soon as possible to ensure availability. Some brides book their hair stylist up to a year in advance.

Should I wash my hair the day of the wedding?

No, you should wash your hair the day before the wedding. This will give your hair enough time to dry and settle before styling.

Should I use hair extensions for my wedding?

If you want to add volume or length to your hair, hair extensions are a great option. Just make sure to choose high-quality extensions that match your hair color and texture.

Hair Care Tips for a Wedding

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind before the wedding:

  • Get a trim a few weeks before the wedding to get rid of split ends.
  • Deep condition your hair a few days before the wedding to make it soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair in the days leading up to the wedding.
  • Avoid using new hair care products in case of a reaction.
  • Don't wash your hair the day of the wedding, as it will be easier to style and hold better.

By following these tips, tricks, and ideas, you'll be able to style your hair for a wedding like a pro. Whether you choose a braided updo, a classic bun, or a half-up, half-down style, you'll look and feel your best on the big day.
