How To Pick Hair Color For Skin Tone

Written by Bardi Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to choosing a hair color that flatters your skin tone, it can be confusing and overwhelming. With so many shades and hues to choose from, it can be challenging to know where to start. But fear not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll show you how to pick hair color for skin tone in relaxed English language.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Picking the right hair color for your skin tone can make all the difference in enhancing your natural beauty. The right shade can bring out the best in your complexion, while the wrong one can make you look washed out or even clash with your skin tone.

So, whether you’re looking to go bold with a bright color or just want to switch up your current shade, it’s essential to choose a color that complements your skin tone.

How to

The first step in picking the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. Skin tones can be classified into three categories:

  • Warm
  • Cool
  • Neutral

If you’re not sure what your skin tone is, here’s a quick and easy way to find out:

Look at your veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can’t tell if they’re blue or green, you might have a neutral skin tone.

Step by step

Once you’ve determined your skin tone, it’s time to pick a hair color. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors, such as golden blonde, copper, or warm brown.
  • Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors, such as ash blonde, platinum, or cool brown.
  • Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colors, so you have more flexibility in your choices.

However, keep in mind that these are just guidelines, and there are exceptions to the rule. For example, some warm skin tones can pull off cool hair colors, and vice versa.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a hair color for your skin tone:

  • Take into account your eye color and natural hair color when choosing a shade.
  • If you’re unsure about a color, try a temporary or semi-permanent dye first before committing to a permanent color.
  • Consider the maintenance required for the color you choose. Some shades require more upkeep than others.
  • Consult with a professional colorist if you’re unsure or want a more personalized recommendation.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to pick hair color for skin tone:

Can I dye my hair a color that doesn’t complement my skin tone?

Yes, you can dye your hair any color you want. However, keep in mind that certain colors may not look as flattering on your skin tone as others.

Can I dye my hair a color that’s opposite of my skin tone?

Yes, you can, but it may not be the most flattering choice. For example, if you have a warm skin tone, a cool hair color may make you look washed out.

What if I have multiple skin tones?

If you have multiple skin tones, such as a warm forehead and cool cheeks, try to pick a hair color that complements your overall skin tone. You can also consult with a professional colorist for personalized advice.

Hair Care

After you’ve dyed your hair, it’s essential to take care of it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Picking the right hair color for your skin tone can be a daunting task, but with these tips and guidelines, you’ll be on your way to finding the perfect shade that enhances your natural beauty.
