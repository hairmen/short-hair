How To Use Claw Clip With Thick Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read
Claw clips are a great hair accessory that can help you create a variety of hairstyles. However, if you have thick hair, you may find it challenging to use claw clips. But don't worry, in this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to use claw clip with thick hair.

Description

Claw clips are a popular hair accessory that can be used to create a variety of hairstyles. They are easy to use, and they come in different shapes and sizes. However, if you have thick hair, you may find it challenging to use claw clips. Thick hair can be heavy, and it can slip out of the clip easily. But with the right technique and some practice, you can use claw clips with thick hair and create beautiful hairstyles.

How to Use Claw Clip with Thick Hair

Using claw clip with thick hair requires a bit of practice, but once you get the hang of it, it's effortless. Here's how to use claw clip with thick hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Size Claw Clip

The first thing you need to do is choose the right size claw clip. If you have thick hair, you need a bigger claw clip. A small claw clip will not be able to hold all your hair.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

The next step is to gather your hair in a ponytail or bun. Make sure it's secure and not too loose.

Step 3: Position the Claw Clip

Position the claw clip over your ponytail or bun. Make sure it's in the right position and not too tight.

Step 4: Open the Claw Clip

Open the claw clip by pressing the two sides together. Make sure it's fully open.

Step 5: Insert the Claw Clip

Insert the claw clip into your hair, making sure it's positioned correctly over your ponytail or bun.

Step 6: Close the Claw Clip

Close the claw clip by releasing the two sides. Make sure it's secure and not too tight.

Step 7: Adjust the Hairstyle

Adjust your hairstyle as desired. You can loosen or tighten the claw clip to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you use claw clip with thick hair: - Choose the right size claw clip. - Gather your hair securely before using the claw clip. - Position the claw clip correctly. - Open the claw clip fully before inserting it into your hair. - Close the claw clip securely but not too tight. - Adjust your hairstyle as desired.

FAQ

Can I use claw clip with thick curly hair?

Yes, you can use claw clip with thick curly hair. However, you may need to use a bigger claw clip to hold all your hair.

How long can I wear the claw clip?

You can wear the claw clip for as long as you like. However, if you feel any discomfort or pain, you should remove it.

Can I use claw clip with wet hair?

It's not recommended to use claw clip with wet hair as it can damage your hair. Wait until your hair is dry before using the claw clip.

Hair Care

Using claw clip with thick hair can be challenging, but it's not impossible. However, it's essential to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips for hair care: - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. - Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. - Use a deep conditioner to moisturize your hair. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. In conclusion, using claw clip with thick hair requires a bit of practice, but it's not impossible. With the right technique and some tips, you can use claw clip with thick hair and create beautiful hairstyles. Just remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage.
