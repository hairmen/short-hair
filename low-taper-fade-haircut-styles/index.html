Low Taper Fade Haircut Styles A Guide For Men

Written by Oliver Nov 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, a low taper fade might just be what you need. This haircut style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It's easy to maintain and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about low taper fade haircut styles, including how to get one, different variations, and hair care tips.

What is a Low Taper Fade Haircut?

A low taper fade haircut is a style where the hair gradually gets shorter towards the neck and sides, creating a faded effect. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on the desired look. This style is often paired with a short or medium-length top, which can be styled in various ways. The low taper fade is a popular choice for men who want a clean and professional look, as well as those who prefer a more edgy and modern style.

How to Get a Low Taper Fade Haircut

Getting a low taper fade haircut is relatively simple, but it's important to go to a barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. Here are the steps involved:

  1. First, decide on the length and style of the top. This will determine how much hair needs to be cut off the sides and back.
  2. Next, the barber will use clippers to gradually blend the hair from the longer top to the shorter sides and back. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on the desired look.
  3. Once the fade is complete, the barber will use scissors to trim the top to the desired length and style.
  4. Finally, the barber will clean up the neckline and sideburns, and style the hair as desired.

The whole process should take around 30 minutes, depending on the complexity of the style. It's important to communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about the look you want, and to ask for their input on what would suit your face shape and hair type.

Different Variations of Low Taper Fade Haircut Styles

There are many variations of the low taper fade haircut, each with its own unique look. Here are some popular options:

Low Taper Fade with Textured Top

This style features a low fade that gradually blends into a textured top, which can be styled in various ways. This is a great option for those who want a modern and edgy look.

Low Taper Fade with Side Part

This style features a low fade with a side part, creating a classic and timeless look. The top can be styled with a comb-over or slicked back, depending on the desired effect.

Low Taper Fade with Buzz Cut

This style features a low fade that blends into a buzz cut, creating a clean and minimalist look. This is a great option for those who prefer a low-maintenance haircut.

Tips for Maintaining a Low Taper Fade Haircut

To keep your low taper fade looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips to follow:

  • Get regular touch-ups every 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. This will prevent the fade from growing out and losing its shape.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and desired look. Pomade, wax, and gel are popular options for styling the top of a low taper fade.
  • Consider getting a beard trim or shaping to complement your low taper fade. This can create a cohesive and stylish look.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a low taper fade last?

A low taper fade can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. It's important to get regular touch-ups to keep the fade looking fresh and clean.

Can I get a low taper fade with curly hair?

Yes, a low taper fade can work well with curly hair. However, it's important to go to a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting and styling curly hair. They can help you choose the right length and style for your hair type.

How do I style the top of a low taper fade?

The top of a low taper fade can be styled in various ways, depending on the desired look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and face shape. Popular styling options include comb-overs, slicked back, messy textures, and side parts.

In Conclusion

A low taper fade haircut is a versatile and stylish option for men who want a clean and modern look. With different variations to choose from, it's easy to customize this style to suit your face shape and hair type. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can keep your low taper fade looking fresh and stylish for weeks to come.
