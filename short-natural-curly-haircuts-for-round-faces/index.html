Short Natural Curly Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by William Sep 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Welcome to our blog post about short natural curly haircuts for round faces! If you have a round face shape and curly hair, you might be wondering what kind of short haircut would work best for you. In this article, we'll provide you with some ideas and inspiration for the perfect short natural curly haircut!

Description

Round faces are characterized by their width, with the cheekbones being the widest part of the face. Short haircuts can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Natural curly hair has its own unique texture and can be a challenge to style. However, with the right haircut, you can enhance your natural curls and make them work for you.

How To

Whether you're going to a professional stylist or cutting your hair at home, there are some key things to keep in mind when choosing a short natural curly haircut for a round face:

  • Consider the length: A short haircut can be defined as anything from a pixie cut to a bob. The key is finding a length that complements your face shape and natural curls.
  • Think about the layers: Layers can help to add movement and volume to your curls, making them look more defined and bouncy.
  • Choose the right part: The way you part your hair can make a big difference in how your haircut looks. A side part can help to create the illusion of length, while a middle part can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short natural curly haircut for a round face:

  1. Research hairstyles: Look for inspiration online or in magazines to get an idea of what kind of haircut you want.
  2. Consult with a stylist: If you're going to a professional stylist, talk to them about your face shape and hair type to get their recommendations for a short haircut.
  3. Choose a length: Decide on the length of your haircut based on what will flatter your face shape and work with your natural curls.
  4. Get the right layers: Ask your stylist to add layers that will enhance your curls and create movement.
  5. Style your hair: After your haircut, ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home. You may need to use specific products or techniques to make your curls look their best.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short natural curly haircut:

  • Use a diffuser: A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to dry your curls while maintaining their shape.
  • Try a co-wash: Co-washing, or washing your hair with conditioner instead of shampoo, can help to keep your curls hydrated and defined.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner: A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your curls moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat can damage your curls and make them lose their shape. Try to air dry your hair as much as possible and limit your use of heat styling tools.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short natural curly haircuts for round faces:

What length should I choose for my haircut?

The length of your haircut depends on your personal preference and what will flatter your face shape. A pixie cut or bob can work well for a round face, but make sure to choose a length that complements your natural curls.

Can I still straighten my hair with a short natural curly haircut?

You can still straighten your hair with a short natural curly haircut, but keep in mind that heat styling can damage your curls over time. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray.

What products should I use to style my short natural curly haircut?

It's important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as a curl defining cream or gel. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your short natural curly haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair on a regular basis. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Depending on your hair type, you may need to wash your hair every day or every few days.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment: A deep conditioning treatment can help to nourish your curls and keep them healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly: Even with a short haircut, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Chemical treatments such as coloring or relaxing can damage your curls. If you want to change your hair color, consider using a natural dye or highlights instead.

We hope this article has provided you with some helpful tips and inspiration for your short natural curly haircut for a round face. Remember to embrace your natural curls and have fun with your new haircut!
