What Face Shape Suits Shoulder Length Hair

Written by William Jul 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle that complements your face shape is essential to look your best. Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can suit many face shapes. In this blog post, we will discuss what face shapes suit shoulder length hair and provide tips and tricks to make the most out of your hairstyle.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in various ways. It is not too short nor too long, making it perfect for every occasion. If you have a round face shape, a shoulder length bob can make your face appear slimmer. If you have a square face, a shoulder length cut with layers can soften your features. Similarly, a shoulder length cut with a side-swept fringe can complement an oval face shape.

How to

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A layered cut can add volume and texture to fine hair, while a blunt cut can add structure to thick hair. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.

Step by step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Choose a shoulder length hairstyle that complements your face shape
Step 3: Consult with your hairstylist
Step 4: Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and proper hair care

Tips

Here are some tips to make the most out of your shoulder length hairstyle:
- Add layers for volume and texture
- Consider a side-swept fringe to complement an oval face shape
- Use a curling iron or flat iron to create different hairstyles
- Use hair products, such as hairspray and mousse, to hold your hairstyle in place

FAQ

Q: What face shapes suit shoulder length hair?
A: Shoulder length hair can suit many face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces.
Q: How can I style my shoulder length hair?
A: You can style your shoulder length hair in various ways, such as straight, wavy, curly, or in an updo.
Q: How often should I trim my shoulder length hair?
A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful shoulder length hair. Here are some tips to care for your hair:
- Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
- Avoid using hot styling tools too often
- Protect your hair from the sun and heat
- Use hair masks and treatments to nourish your hair
- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

In conclusion, shoulder length hair is a versatile length that can suit many face shapes. With the right cut and style, you can enhance your features and look your best. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. Remember to maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and proper hair care.
