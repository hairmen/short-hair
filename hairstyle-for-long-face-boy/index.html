Hairstyle for Long Face Boy - Tips and Ideas

If you have a long face, it can be a bit of a challenge to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. However, with the right tips and ideas, you can easily find a hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your overall look. In this post, we will be sharing some hairstyle ideas for long face boys that you can try out.

How to Determine if You Have a Long Face?

Before we dive into the hairstyle ideas, it's important to determine if you have a long face. To do this, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin, and then divide it by three. If the result is greater than the width of your cheekbones, then you have a long face.

Hairstyle Ideas for Long Face Boys

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for long face boys as they help to break up the length of the face. You can style your hair with a side part and sweep your bangs to one side. This will create a diagonal line across your forehead, which will help to balance out the length of your face.

2. Short Crop

A short crop is another great hairstyle option for long face boys. This style is low-maintenance and easy to maintain. You can keep the sides and back of your hair short, while leaving a bit more length on top. This will create a balanced look and help to minimize the length of your face.

3. Textured Haircut

A textured haircut is a great way to add some dimension to your hair and create a more balanced look. You can opt for a choppy, layered cut that adds volume and texture to your hair. This will help to create a more rounded look and minimize the length of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Your Hair

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas, here's a step-by-step guide for styling your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, and then apply a styling product to your hair (such as a pomade or wax).
  3. Style your hair according to the hairstyle that you have chosen.
  4. Use a hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair in shape.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it difficult to maintain your hairstyle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyle for long face boys:

Q. Can long face boys have long hair?

A. Yes, long face boys can have long hair, but it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair to balance out the length of your face.

Q. What is the best hairstyle for long face boys?

A. The best hairstyle for long face boys is one that adds volume and texture to your hair and helps to balance out the length of your face. Some great options include side-swept bangs, a short crop, and a textured haircut.

Hair Care for Long Face Boys

Here are some hair care tips for long face boys:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it difficult to maintain your hairstyle.

With these hairstyle ideas and tips, you can easily find a hairstyle that flatters your long face and enhances your overall look. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair and helps to balance out the length of your face. With the right care and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking great for years to come.
