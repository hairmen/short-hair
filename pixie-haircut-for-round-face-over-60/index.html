Pixie Haircut For Round Face Over 60 A Guide For A Chic And Timeless Look

If you're over 60 and have a round face, you might be wondering what kind of haircut would suit you best. A pixie haircut is a great option that can give you a chic and timeless look. But with so many styles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to pick the right one. In this article, we'll guide you on how to choose the perfect pixie haircut for your round face and how to maintain it.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Haircut for Round Face over 60

When it comes to choosing a pixie haircut for a round face, there are a few things to consider. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

1. Consider your face shape

A pixie haircut can be very flattering for round faces, but it's important to choose the right style that complements your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a pixie cut that has volume on top and is shorter on the sides to elongate your face.

2. Think about your hair texture

Your hair texture can also influence the type of pixie haircut that would suit you best. If you have thin hair, a layered pixie cut can give you more volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a choppy pixie cut can help you manage your hair more easily.

3. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a pixie haircut, it's also important to consider your lifestyle. If you're someone who prefers low-maintenance hair, opt for a short and simple pixie cut that requires minimal styling. If you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, a longer and more textured pixie cut would be perfect for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get a Pixie Haircut

If you've decided to get a pixie haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you prepare for your salon appointment:

1. Choose a style

Before heading to the salon, choose a specific pixie cut style that you want. You can bring a photo of a celebrity or a model with a similar face shape and hair texture as yours to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

2. Wash your hair

On the day of your appointment, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly and avoid using any hair products. This will help your stylist to work with clean hair and get the best results.

3. Communicate with your stylist

When you're at the salon, make sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the style you want and any concerns you may have. Your stylist can give you suggestions and advice on what would suit you best.

4. Get the cut

Once you're ready, your stylist will start cutting your hair according to the style you've chosen. They may use a razor or scissors to create texture and volume. The length of your pixie cut will depend on your preference and face shape.

5. Style your hair

After the cut, your stylist will style your hair using hair products and tools. They may blow-dry your hair or use a flat iron to create a sleek look. If you prefer a more textured look, they may use a texturizing spray or a curling iron.

Tips for Styling Your Pixie Haircut

Now that you have your pixie haircut, here are some tips to help you style it:

1. Use the right products

Invest in good quality hair products that can help you style your pixie cut. A volumizing mousse can help create volume on top, while a texturizing spray can give you a more tousled look.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques for your pixie cut. You can try a sleek and smooth look one day and a messy and textured look the next day.

3. Accessorize

Add some accessories to your hair to make your pixie cut look more stylish. You can try a headband, a scarf, or some hair clips to add some flair to your hair.

FAQs About Pixie Haircut for Round Face over 60

1. Will a pixie haircut suit me if I have a round face?

Yes, a pixie haircut can be very flattering for round faces. Just make sure to choose a style that elongates your face and adds volume on top.

2. How often do I need to get a pixie cut?

You will need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your pixie cut.

3. Can I color my hair after getting a pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your hair after getting a pixie cut. Just make sure to use hair color products that are gentle on your hair and won't cause damage.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals and sulfates that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Protect your hair from heat

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before styling. This will help prevent heat damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Get regular trims

To maintain your pixie cut, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

A pixie haircut can be a great way to update your look and feel confident and chic. By following these tips and maintaining your hair properly, you can rock your pixie cut at any age and feel fabulous.
