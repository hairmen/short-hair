How To Wear A Graduation Cap With Long Hair Guys

Written by William Jun 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Graduation day is one of the most important days in any student's life. It is a day that marks the end of one chapter and the beginning of another. On this day, you want to look your best, but if you have long hair, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that goes well with your graduation cap. In this article, we will guide you on how to wear a graduation cap with long hair guys, step by step.

  • Tips

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step is to wash and condition your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles or knots. Use a conditioner that is designed for long hair, as it will help to keep your hair soft and manageable. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it.

Step 2: Blow-dry Your Hair

Once your hair is towel-dried, use a blow dryer to dry it completely. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume. Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall and use a styling product to keep it in place.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as you normally would, but make sure it is not too high or too low as this will affect how the graduation cap sits on your head. Keep it simple and neat.

Step 4: Put on Your Graduation Gown

Put on your graduation gown and make sure it is straight and centered on your body. The gown should be loose enough to allow for movement but not too loose that it falls off your shoulders.

Step 5: Position Your Graduation Cap

Place the graduation cap on your head with the front part facing forward. Make sure the cap is level on your head and sits comfortably on your hair. If you have long hair, tuck it behind your ears and clip it in place with bobby pins.

Tips

  • Use hair gel or wax to keep your hair in place.
  • Practice wearing your graduation cap with your hair before the actual day.
  • Get a haircut a few days before graduation to ensure that your hair looks neat and tidy.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place if needed.

FAQ

Can I wear a graduation cap with long hair?

Yes, you can wear a graduation cap with long hair. Follow the steps outlined in this article to ensure that your hair looks neat and tidy under the cap.

Do I need to cut my hair for graduation?

No, you do not need to cut your hair for graduation. However, if you want to ensure that your hair looks neat and tidy, you may want to consider getting a haircut a few days before graduation.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for long hair, and avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often. Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay, and use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

By following these simple steps, you can wear a graduation cap with long hair guys and look your best on your big day. Congratulations on your achievement, and good luck with your future endeavors!
