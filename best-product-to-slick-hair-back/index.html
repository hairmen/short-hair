Best Product To Slick Hair Back A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair getting in your face all the time? Do you want a sleek and polished look? Slicking your hair back is the perfect solution. But what's the best product to use? In this guide, we'll discuss the top products for slicking hair back, how to use them, and some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Slick Hair Back
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best product to slick hair back is a pomade. Pomades are hair styling products that have a waxy or greasy texture. They provide a medium to high hold and a shiny finish. Pomades are perfect for slicking hair back because they give the hair a sleek and polished look. Some of the top pomades for slicking hair back include:

  • Reuzel Blue Pomade
  • Suavecito Pomade
  • Layrite Superhold Pomade
  • Baxter of California Clay Pomade

How To Slick Hair Back

Slicking hair back is a simple process, but it does require some preparation. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt or oil that may be on your hair and make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it's only slightly damp. You don't want it to be too wet because the pomade won't hold as well.

Step 3: Apply Pomade

Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms until it's warm and soft. Then, apply it evenly throughout your hair, starting at the back and working your way towards the front. Use a comb to distribute the pomade evenly and make sure all of your hair is coated.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Use a comb to slick your hair back. Start at the hairline and comb backwards towards the crown of your head. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to stay.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is slicked back, you can style it however you want. You can leave it as is for a classic look, or you can add some volume by using a blow dryer and round brush.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to slicking your hair back:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp
  3. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms until it's warm and soft
  4. Apply the pomade evenly throughout your hair
  5. Use a comb to distribute the pomade evenly and slick your hair back
  6. Style your hair however you like

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect slicked back look:

  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed
  • Use a fine-tooth comb for a sleeker look or a wide-tooth comb for a more textured look
  • Blow dry your hair for added volume
  • Use a hairspray to set the style in place
  • Experiment with different pomades to find the one that works best for your hair type

FAQ

Q: Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can slick your hair back if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use a stronger hold pomade to keep your curls in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I'm slicking it back?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day if you're slicking it back. Washing it too often can dry out your hair and make it difficult to style.

Q: Can I use gel instead of pomade?

A: Yes, you can use gel instead of pomade, but it may not give you the same sleek and polished look.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when you're slicking it back. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (blow dryers, flat irons, etc.)
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy

Now that you know the best product to slick hair back, you can achieve the perfect look. Whether you're going for a classic or modern style, a good pomade will help you achieve your desired look. So go ahead, give it a try, and see how slicking your hair back can transform your look.
