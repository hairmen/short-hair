How To Make Long Hair Wavy Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair has always been a symbol of masculinity and style for men. However, sometimes it can be challenging to find ways to style long hair. One of the most popular hairstyles for men with long hair is wavy hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to make long hair wavy male. Read on to learn the step-by-step process, tips, and tricks, and hair care for perfect wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • How to Make Long Hair Wavy Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Make Long Hair Wavy Male

There are various methods to make long hair wavy, but the most popular ones are:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Braiding hair overnight
  • Using hair rollers
  • Using a straightener

Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages. However, in this article, we will focus on the most effective and quickest method- using a curling iron.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to get perfect wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly and let it dry naturally till it is 80% dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent heat damage.
  3. Plug in your curling iron and wait for it to heat up.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  5. Start curling small sections of hair at a time. Hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the section of hair around the barrel away from your face.
  6. Hold the hair for 5-10 seconds and then release it.
  7. Repeat the process for all the sections of hair.
  8. Once all the sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a wavy look.
  9. Spray some hairspray to hold the waves in place.
  10. You are now ready to rock your wavy hair!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks that will help you get the perfect wavy hair:

  • Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly as it can result in small ringlets instead of waves.
  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for loose waves.
  • Use a curling iron with a smaller barrel for tighter waves.
  • Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a wavy look.
  • Don't use too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making long hair wavy male:

  • How long does it take to make long hair wavy? It usually takes around 30-45 minutes to make long hair wavy using a curling iron.
  • Can I make my hair wavy without using heat? Yes, you can make your hair wavy without using heat by braiding your hair overnight or using hair rollers.
  • How can I make my wavy hair last longer? You can make your wavy hair last longer by spraying some hairspray or using a texturizing spray.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires proper care to maintain its texture and shine. Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizziness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause breakage and frizziness.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Follow these tips and tricks to make long hair wavy male and maintain your wavy hair's texture and shine. With the right technique and proper hair care, you can rock the perfect wavy hair look.
