Hairstyles For Long Face Older Women

Written by Aprilia Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are an older woman with a long face, you might be wondering what hairstyles would look best on you. We have put together some tips, tricks, and examples of hairstyles that would be perfect for a long face older woman. Whether you have thin or thick hair, we have got you covered. So, let’s get started!

Description

Women with long faces tend to have a narrow forehead, long chin, and high cheekbones. The goal of a hairstyle for a long face older woman is to create the illusion of a shorter face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of the face and avoiding styles that are too long or flat.

How To

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a hairstyle for a long face older woman:

  1. Identify your face shape: Before choosing a hairstyle, make sure you know your face shape. This will help you select a style that flatters your features.
  2. Choose a style that adds width: Look for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face. This will help balance out a long face shape.
  3. Avoid styles that are too long: Long hairstyles can make a long face shape appear even longer. Opt for medium-length or short hairstyles instead.
  4. Add layers: Layers can add volume and movement to hair, which can help create the illusion of a shorter face.
  5. Consider bangs: Bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long face shape. Side-swept or curtain bangs are a great option for older women.

Step by Step

Here are some examples of hairstyles that would be perfect for a long face older woman:

Short Bob

A short bob is a timeless hairstyle that suits all face shapes. For a long face shape, opt for a bob that is chin-length or shorter. This will help balance out your features and make your face appear shorter.

Layered Lob

A layered lob is a great option for older women with thin hair. The layers will add volume and movement to your hair, which can help create the illusion of a shorter face. To style, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for a long face shape. They help shorten the appearance of your face and add volume to the sides. To style, blow dry your bangs to the side using a round brush.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid center parts: Center parts can make a long face shape appear even longer. Opt for a side part instead.
  • Use volumizing products: To add volume to your hair, use volumizing shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.
  • Consider highlights: Highlights can add dimension to your hair and make it appear fuller.
  • Trim regularly: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long face older women:

What length should I choose for my hair?

A medium-length or short hairstyle is best for a long face shape. Avoid styles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even longer.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat. Center parts can also make a long face shape appear longer.

What products should I use to add volume to my hair?

Use volumizing shampoo, conditioner, and styling products to add volume to your hair. You can also try blow-drying your hair upside down to create more volume.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Shampoo and condition your hair at least once a week.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools can damage your hair. Try air-drying your hair or using heat protectant products.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your long face shape. Remember to choose a style that adds width and volume to your hair, and avoid styles that are too long or flat. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age!
