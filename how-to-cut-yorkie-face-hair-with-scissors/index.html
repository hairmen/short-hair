How To Cut Yorkie Face Hair With Scissors

Written by Aprilia Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Yorkies are adorable dogs that have long and silky hair. To keep them looking their best, it's essential to groom them regularly, especially the hair on their face. In this article, we'll discuss how to cut Yorkie face hair with scissors. We'll cover everything from how to prepare your dog for the grooming session to tips on how to make sure you don't accidentally cut too much hair.

How to Cut Yorkie Face Hair with Scissors

Cutting your Yorkie's face hair with scissors is a delicate process that requires patience and precision. Before you start, make sure you have all the supplies you need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb or brush
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Treats

It's important to make sure your dog is comfortable and relaxed before you start. Give them plenty of treats and praise, and make sure they're in a calm and quiet environment. If they get anxious or restless, take a break and try again later.

Step-by-step Guide

Here's how to cut your Yorkie's face hair with scissors:

  1. Brush and comb your dog's face hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Use your fingers to gently lift the hair on your dog's face and trim any long or straggly hairs with scissors.
  3. Hold the scissors perpendicular to the hair and cut at a slight angle to create a natural-looking edge.
  4. Take your time and cut small amounts of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much.
  5. Check your progress frequently by standing back and looking at your dog's face from different angles.
  6. Trim the hair around your dog's eyes carefully, making sure not to poke them with the scissors.
  7. If your dog has a beard, trim it to a length that's comfortable for them.
  8. Use clippers to trim any hair around your dog's nose or mouth if necessary.
  9. Finish by brushing your dog's face hair again and giving them plenty of treats and praise.

Tips for Cutting Yorkie Face Hair

Here are some tips to help you cut your Yorkie's face hair with scissors:

  • Start with clean hair. It's easier to cut and style hair that's clean and free of tangles.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can pull and damage your dog's hair.
  • Take breaks. If your dog gets restless or anxious, take a break and try again later.
  • Use treats and praise. Reward your dog frequently with treats and praise to keep them calm and motivated.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and cut small amounts of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much.
  • Be patient. Yorkies have long hair that requires regular grooming, so be patient and consistent.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting Yorkie face hair:

Q: Can I use clippers instead of scissors?
A: Yes, you can use clippers to trim your Yorkie's face hair. However, be careful not to get too close to their skin or eyes. Q: How often should I cut my Yorkie's face hair?
A: It depends on how fast their hair grows and how long you want it to be. As a general rule, it's a good idea to trim their face hair every 4-6 weeks. Q: Can I use human hair scissors on my Yorkie?
A: No, it's not recommended to use human hair scissors on your dog. They're not designed for cutting animal hair and can damage it.

Hair Care for Yorkies

Yorkies have long and silky hair that requires regular grooming to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your Yorkie's hair:

  • Brush and comb their hair daily to remove tangles and prevent matting.
  • Wash their hair every 2-3 weeks with a high-quality dog shampoo.
  • Trim their hair regularly to prevent it from getting too long and tangled.
  • Avoid using human hair products on your Yorkie, as they can be harsh and damaging.
  • Monitor their hair for signs of skin irritation or infection.

By following these tips and taking care to groom your Yorkie regularly, you can keep their hair looking healthy and beautiful.
