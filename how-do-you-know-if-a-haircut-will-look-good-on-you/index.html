How Do You Know If A Haircut Will Look Good On You

Written by Robby Oct 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Getting a new haircut can be a daunting task. You might have seen a celebrity sporting a new hairstyle and thought it would look great on you too. But, before you take the plunge and chop off your locks, it's important to know if the haircut will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine if a haircut will look good on you.

How To Determine If A Haircut Will Look Good On You?

There are several factors to consider when deciding if a haircut will suit you. Let's take a look at each one of them.

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining the hairstyle that will suit you. Different face shapes require different haircuts to balance out the features. There are six basic face shapes â round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong.

Round faces look best with haircuts that add height and volume on top. Square faces need haircuts that soften the angles of the face. Oval faces can pull off most haircuts, but look great with layers. Heart-shaped faces need haircuts that balance out the chin. Diamond faces look great with short haircuts that highlight the cheekbones. Oblong faces need haircuts that add width to the face.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays an important role in determining the right haircut for you. If you have curly hair, you might want to opt for a haircut that adds layers to enhance the curls. If you have fine hair, you might want to go for a short haircut that adds volume. If you have thick hair, you might want to go for a layered haircut that thins out the hair.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in determining the right haircut for you. If you have a busy schedule, you might want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require too much styling. If you love experimenting with different hairstyles, you might want to go for a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways.

Step By Step Guide

Now that you know the factors to consider when choosing a haircut, let's take a look at the step-by-step guide to determine if a haircut will look good on you.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a tape measure to measure your face from the hairline to the chin and from cheek to cheek. This will help you determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Once you know your face shape, research hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair texture. You can use Pinterest, Instagram, or Google for inspiration.

Step 3: Consult With Your Hairstylist

Take your research to your hairstylist and ask for their opinion. They will be able to guide you on what will suit you best based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Step 4: Consider The Maintenance

Before finalizing the haircut, consider the maintenance required. If you're someone who doesn't have the time to style your hair every day, opt for a low-maintenance haircut.

Tips To Keep In Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a new haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your natural features.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment, but always consider your face shape and hair texture.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairstylist.
  • Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist. They are professionals and know what will look best on you.
  • Consider the maintenance required before finalizing the haircut.

FAQ

What haircut suits a round face?

Round faces look best with haircuts that add height and volume on top. A layered bob or a long bob with side-swept bangs are great options.

What haircut suits a square face?

Square faces need haircuts that soften the angles of the face. A long layered haircut with soft waves or a side-swept bangs are great options.

What haircut suits an oval face?

Oval faces can pull off most haircuts, but look great with layers. A long layered haircut or a blunt bob are great options.

What haircut suits a heart-shaped face?

Heart-shaped faces need haircuts that balance out the chin. A long layered haircut with side-swept bangs or a lob with choppy ends are great options.

What haircut suits a diamond face?

Diamond faces look great with short haircuts that highlight the cheekbones. A pixie cut or a bob with choppy layers are great options.

What haircut suits an oblong face?

Oblong faces need haircuts that add width to the face. A shoulder-length haircut with layers or a side-swept bangs are great options.

Hair Care

Once you've got your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools. Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain the shape of the haircut.

In conclusion, choosing a new haircut can be overwhelming, but by considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can determine the right haircut for you. Don't be afraid to experiment, but always consult with your hairstylist and keep the maintenance required in mind.
