How To Apply Serum To Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Serums are an essential part of any hair care routine. They help keep your hair healthy, shiny, and frizz-free. However, applying serum to your hair can be tricky, especially if you’ve never done it before. In this guide, we’ll show you how to apply serum to your hair like a pro.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Apply Serum to Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Applying Serum to Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care with Serum

How to Apply Serum to Hair

Before you start applying serum to your hair, it’s essential to choose the right serum for your hair type. There are different types of serums available, such as smoothing serums, heat protectant serums, and shine serums. Once you’ve chosen the right serum for your hair type, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

The first step to applying serum to your hair is to wash and dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before applying the serum. Wet hair can dilute the serum, making it less effective.

Step 2: Take a small amount of serum

Take a small amount of serum in your palm. The amount of serum you need will depend on your hair length and thickness. Start with a small amount and add more if needed.

Step 3: Rub the serum between your palms

Warm the serum by rubbing it between your palms. This will help distribute the serum evenly in your hair.

Step 4: Apply the serum to your hair

Apply the serum evenly to your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Avoid applying too much serum to your roots, as it can make your hair oily.

Step 5: Comb your hair

Use a comb to distribute the serum evenly in your hair. This will also help detangle your hair and prevent any frizz or flyaways.

Step 6: Style your hair

You can now style your hair as desired. The serum will help keep your hair in place and add shine to it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to apply serum to your hair:

  1. Choose the right serum for your hair type.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Take a small amount of serum.
  4. Rub the serum between your palms.
  5. Apply the serum to your hair evenly, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  6. Use a comb to distribute the serum evenly in your hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Applying Serum to Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when applying serum to your hair:

  • Start with a small amount of serum and add more if needed.
  • Avoid applying too much serum to your roots, as it can make your hair oily.
  • Warm the serum by rubbing it between your palms before applying it to your hair.
  • Use a comb to distribute the serum evenly in your hair.
  • Apply serum to your hair before using any hot styling tools.
  • Choose a serum that suits your hair type and concerns.

FAQs

Q. How often should I apply serum to my hair?

A. You can apply serum to your hair once a day or as often as needed. However, it’s essential not to overdo it, as too much serum can make your hair oily and weigh it down.

Q. Can I use serum on wet hair?

A. It’s best to use serum on dry hair. Wet hair can dilute the serum, making it less effective.

Q. Can serum protect my hair from heat damage?

A. Yes, some serums contain heat protectants that can protect your hair from heat damage caused by styling tools.

Hair Care with Serum

Serums are a great way to keep your hair healthy and shiny. However, it’s essential to take care of your hair in other ways too. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes vitamins and minerals essential for healthy hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution.

By following these tips and using serum regularly, you can keep your hair healthy, shiny, and frizz-free.
