Does Blonde Hair Make You Look More Tan

Written by Eveline Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to look tanned, you may have heard that going blonde can help you achieve that sun-kissed look. But is there any truth to this? In this article, we'll explore whether blonde hair really does make you look more tan and provide you with some tips on how to achieve the perfect sun-kissed look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Blonde hair can create the illusion of a deeper tan, but it doesn't actually make your skin darker. When you have blonde hair, the contrast between your hair and skin is greater, which makes your skin appear darker by comparison. This is because blonde hair reflects light differently than darker hair colors. When light hits blonde hair, it reflects off the hair and onto the skin, creating a warmer, more golden tone.

So, while blonde hair does not actually make your skin more tan, it can create the illusion of a deeper, more golden tan.

How to

If you want to achieve the perfect sun-kissed look with blonde hair, there are a few things you can do:

Dye your hair a shade of blonde

If you're not naturally blonde, you can dye your hair a shade of blonde that complements your skin tone. Opt for a warm, golden blonde that will enhance your skin's natural warmth.

Use self-tanner

To enhance your tan and create a more golden glow, try using a self-tanner. Choose a shade that is slightly darker than your natural skin tone and apply it evenly all over your body.

Wear the right colors

Wearing colors that complement your skin tone and hair color can help enhance your sun-kissed look. Opt for warm, earthy tones like gold, bronze, and copper, and avoid colors that wash you out, like pastels.

Step by Step

To achieve the perfect sun-kissed look with blonde hair, follow these steps:

  1. Dye your hair a warm, golden shade of blonde
  2. Exfoliate your skin to ensure an even application of self-tanner
  3. Apply self-tanner all over your body, blending carefully
  4. Wear colors that complement your skin tone and hair color

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect sun-kissed look with blonde hair:

  • Avoid over-tanning, as this can damage your skin and cause premature aging
  • Use a moisturizer with SPF to protect your skin from sun damage
  • Avoid using too much self-tanner, as this can create an unnatural, orange tint
  • Experiment with different shades of blonde to find the one that complements your skin tone the best

FAQ

Will blonde hair make me look more tan?

Blonde hair can create the illusion of a deeper tan, but it does not actually make your skin more tan.

What shade of blonde should I choose to enhance my sun-kissed look?

Opt for a warm, golden shade of blonde that complements your skin tone.

Can I achieve a sun-kissed look without self-tanner?

Yes, you can achieve a sun-kissed look by simply wearing colors that complement your skin tone and hair color.

Hair Care

If you want to maintain your sun-kissed look, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your blonde hair from fading
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Visit your hairdresser regularly for trims and touch-ups

In conclusion, blonde hair can create the illusion of a deeper tan, but it does not actually make your skin more tan. To achieve the perfect sun-kissed look with blonde hair, dye your hair a warm, golden shade of blonde, use self-tanner, and wear colors that complement your skin tone and hair color. Take good care of your hair by using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
