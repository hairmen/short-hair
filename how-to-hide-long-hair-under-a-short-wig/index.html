How To Hide Long Hair Under A Short Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're planning to wear a short wig but have long hair, you may be wondering how to hide your natural hair without looking bulky or lumpy. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to hide long hair under a short wig, so you can achieve a natural and seamless look.

How to Hide Long Hair Under a Short Wig

Here are some steps you can follow to hide your long hair under a short wig:

Step 1: Tie Your Hair Back

The first step is to tie your hair back into a low ponytail or braid. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent any loose strands from sticking out. Make sure your hair is flat and smooth against your head.

Step 2: Use a Wig Cap

Next, put on a wig cap. A wig cap is a thin, stretchy cap that you wear underneath your wig. It helps keep your hair in place and provides a barrier between your hair and the wig, which can help prevent itching and irritation.

Step 3: Pin Your Hair Down

Take bobby pins and pin your hair down to your head. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up, pinning your hair as flat as possible.

Step 4: Put on Your Wig

Now it's time to put on your wig. Start by placing the front of the wig at your hairline and pulling it back over your head. Adjust the wig until it sits comfortably and securely on your head.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Lastly, style your wig to your liking. You can use a wig brush or comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps. Use hairspray or other styling products to hold your wig in place.

Tips for Hiding Long Hair Under a Short Wig

Here are some tips to help you achieve a seamless look when hiding your long hair under a short wig:

Tip 1: Choose a Close-Fitting Wig

When selecting a wig, choose one that fits snugly on your head. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent any bulges or bumps from showing through.

Tip 2: Use a Wig with a Lace Front

A wig with a lace front can help create a natural-looking hairline. The lace front allows you to blend your natural hairline with the wig, which can help hide any exposed hair.

Tip 3: Use a Wig with a Monofilament Cap

A wig with a monofilament cap has a thin, breathable material that mimics the look of a natural scalp. This can help create a more realistic look and make it easier to hide your natural hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear a short wig if I have long hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a short wig if you have long hair. You just need to follow the steps we outlined above to hide your natural hair.

Q: How do I keep my wig from slipping?

A: To keep your wig from slipping, make sure you have a snug fit. You can also use wig clips or adhesive tape to secure your wig in place.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: It depends on how often you wear your wig. If you wear it every day, you should wash it once a week. If you wear it less frequently, you can wash it every two to three weeks.

Hair Care: How to Take Care of Your Wig

Here are some tips for taking care of your wig:

Tip 1: Store Your Wig Properly

When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or in a box. Make sure it's not exposed to direct sunlight or heat, which can damage the fibers.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for wig care. Avoid using regular shampoo or conditioner, as they can damage the wig's fibers.

Tip 3: Be Gentle

When brushing or styling your wig, be gentle. Use a wig brush or comb and avoid pulling or tugging on the hair.

Now that you know how to hide long hair under a short wig, you can confidently rock any wig style you want! Follow these tips and tricks for a natural and seamless look.
