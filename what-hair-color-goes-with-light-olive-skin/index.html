What Hair Color Goes With Light Olive Skin

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have light olive skin, choosing the right hair color can be a bit tricky. You want to pick a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore what hair color goes with light olive skin and provide some tips on how to get the perfect shade.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Color
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Description

Light olive skin has a yellow-green undertone, which can make it challenging to find the right hair color. You want to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Some hair colors that work well with light olive skin are warm browns, caramel, honey blonde, and copper.

It's essential to choose a hair color that brings out your best features while also making you feel confident and beautiful. If you're uncertain about which shade to choose, consult with a professional hairstylist who can guide you through the process.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When choosing a hair color for light olive skin, consider the following factors:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone is a crucial factor when selecting a hair color. If you have light olive skin, you want to choose a shade that complements your skin tone. Stay away from hair colors that are too light or too dark, as they can wash you out or make you look too harsh.

Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have light green or hazel eyes, warm browns, honey blonde, and copper can bring out the golden flecks in your eyes. If you have blue or gray eyes, ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown shades can enhance your eye color.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also determine which hair color is right for you. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have time to maintain your hair color, choose a shade that is close to your natural hair color. If you have more time to devote to your hair, consider more high-maintenance shades like platinum blonde or bright red.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right hair color for light olive skin:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear greenish, you have a warm undertone. If they appear bluish, you have a cool undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Determine your eye color and choose a hair color that complements your eyes.

Step 3: Consult with a Professional

If you're unsure which hair color to choose, consult with a professional hairstylist who can guide you through the process.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo

Use a color-safe shampoo to protect your hair color and prevent fading.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause your hair color to fade quickly. Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when necessary.

Get Regular Touch-ups

Schedule regular touch-ups with your hairstylist to maintain your hair color and keep it looking fresh.

FAQs

What hair colors should I avoid if I have light olive skin?

You should avoid hair colors that are too light or too dark. Stay away from ash blonde or platinum blonde if you have warm undertones.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist to achieve the best results.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair color for light olive skin requires careful consideration of your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle. Warm browns, caramel, honey blonde, and copper are all great options to enhance your natural beauty. Follow our tips for maintaining your hair color and keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.
