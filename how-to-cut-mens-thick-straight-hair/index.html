How To Cut Men s Thick Straight Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, then a short and sleek style for thick straight hair might be perfect for you. However, cutting men's thick straight hair can be challenging, especially if you're doing it at home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to cut men's thick straight hair, from preparing the hair to the finishing touches. We'll also provide you with some tips and answers to frequently asked questions about men's haircuts.

How to Cut Men's Thick Straight Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare it. Shampoo and condition your hair to make it easier to manage. Then, dry your hair with a towel and a hairdryer until it's about 80% dry.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or elastics. Start by creating a center parting from the forehead to the nape of the neck. Then, divide the hair into two sections, one on each side of the head.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Start with the sides of the head. Use a clipper with a number 3 guard to trim the sides of the head. Start at the temple and work your way towards the back of the head. Repeat the process on the other side of the head.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using scissors, trim the hair on top of the head. Start at the crown and work your way towards the front of the head. Use the point-cutting technique to create texture and movement.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Using a clipper with a number 2 guard, blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top. Start at the temple and work your way towards the back of the head. Repeat the process on the other side of the head.

Step 6: Create the Fringe

Using scissors, create the fringe by cutting the hair at an angle. Start at the center of the forehead and work your way towards the temples. Use the point-cutting technique to create texture and movement.

Step 7: Refine the Cut

Check for any uneven areas and refine the cut as needed. Use scissors to trim any long strands of hair that may be sticking out.

Step 8: Finish the Cut

Use a hair clipper with a number 1 guard to clean up the neckline and sideburns. Then, use a brush to remove any loose hair from the scalp.

Tips for Cutting Men's Thick Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut for men's thick straight hair:

  • Use the right tools, such as scissors, clippers, and combs.
  • Start with clean and dry hair.
  • Work in small sections to ensure evenness and precision.
  • Use the point-cutting technique to create texture and movement.
  • Check the hair for any uneven areas and refine the cut as needed.

FAQs About Men's Haircuts

1. How often should men get a haircut?

It depends on the individual's hair type and style. Generally, men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their hairstyle.

2. Can men cut their own hair?

Yes, men can cut their own hair, but it requires the right tools and skills. If you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to go to a professional hair stylist.

3. What is the best haircut for thick straight hair?

A short and sleek style is ideal for thick straight hair. It's low-maintenance and looks great on most face shapes.

Hair Care for Men's Thick Straight Hair

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to cut men's thick straight hair, you can give yourself or someone else a stylish and low-maintenance haircut at home. Remember to use the right tools, work in small sections, and check for any uneven areas to ensure an even and precise cut. Don't forget to follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
