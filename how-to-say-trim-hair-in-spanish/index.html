How To Say Trim Hair In Spanish A Relaxed Guide

Written by Robby Sep 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning on getting a haircut in a Spanish-speaking country? Or maybe you just want to learn how to say "trim hair" in Spanish? Whatever your reason is, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to say trim hair in Spanish, as well as some related keywords to help you navigate a Spanish-speaking salon. Let's get started!

How to Say Trim Hair in Spanish

The most common way to say "trim hair" in Spanish is "cortar el pelo". This phrase is easy to remember and widely used in Spanish-speaking countries. However, there are other ways to say it depending on the region or the style you want. Here are some variations:

  • "Recortar el pelo": This is a more precise way to say "trim hair". It's often used when you want to trim just a little bit of hair, or when you want to give your hair a neater look.
  • "Podar el cabello": This phrase is commonly used in Mexico and Central America. It literally means "prune hair" and is often used when you want to get rid of split ends or damaged hair.
  • "Cortar las puntas": If you only want to trim the ends of your hair, you can use this phrase. It means "cut the tips" and is a common request in Spanish-speaking salons.

Remember to use the appropriate verb form depending on who is doing the trimming. For example, if you want to say "I want to trim my hair" you would say "Quiero cortarme el pelo". If you want to say "She is trimming my hair", you would say "Ella me está cortando el pelo".

Related Keywords

Knowing how to say "trim hair" in Spanish is just the beginning. Here are some related keywords that might come in handy when you're getting a haircut in a Spanish-speaking country:

  • "Peinar": This means "to comb" and is often used when you want a specific hairstyle.
  • "Lavar el pelo": This means "to wash hair". It's a common service offered in Spanish-speaking salons.
  • "Tinte": This means "dye" and is often used when you want to color your hair.
  • "Corte de cabello": This means "haircut". It's a more general term that can be used to request any kind of haircut.

Step by Step: Getting a Haircut in Spanish

Now that you know how to say "trim hair" in Spanish and some related keywords, let's go through the steps of getting a haircut in Spanish:

  1. Choose a salon: Look for a salon that speaks your language or has good reviews from other foreigners.
  2. Make an appointment: Call or email the salon to make an appointment. You can use the phrases "Quiero hacer una cita para cortarme el pelo" or "Me gustaría reservar una hora para cortarme el pelo".
  3. Explain what you want: Use the phrases we've discussed to explain what kind of haircut you want. You can also bring pictures or use hand gestures to make yourself clear.
  4. Ask questions: Don't be afraid to ask questions if you don't understand something. You can use phrases like "No entiendo" or "¿Puede repetirlo, por favor?"
  5. Enjoy your haircut: Sit back, relax, and enjoy your new haircut!

Tips for Getting a Haircut in Spanish

Here are some tips to make your experience getting a haircut in Spanish smoother:

  • Bring a picture: If you're not confident in your Spanish skills, bring a picture of the haircut you want.
  • Practice beforehand: Practice saying the phrases we've discussed beforehand so you feel more confident.
  • Be patient: Getting a haircut in a foreign language can be stressful, but remember to be patient and take your time.
  • Tip appropriately: Tipping etiquette varies by country, but a good rule of thumb is to tip 10-20% of the total cost of the haircut.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've got your new haircut, here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner: Look for products that are suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair, so try to air dry your hair as much as possible.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat.
  • Eat a healthy diet: A healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth.
