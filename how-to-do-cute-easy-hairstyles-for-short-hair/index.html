How To Do Cute Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair can be fun and versatile, but sometimes it can be a challenge to come up with cute and easy hairstyles. Whether you're heading to work, school, or a night out with friends, you can still rock a stylish look with your short hair. In this article, we'll show you how to do cute easy hairstyles for short hair that you can do at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to know a few tips and tricks to make styling short hair easier. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Invest in good quality hair styling tools such as a curling iron or straightener.
  • Use hair products that are designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays or pomades.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

Step by Step

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that never goes out of style. Here's how to style it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume.
  4. Finish by using a hairspray to set your style in place.

The Bob

The bob is another popular short hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. Here's a simple way to style it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair in the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.
  3. Use a straightener to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair serum to the ends of your hair to add shine.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

The Messy Bun

A messy bun is a cute and easy hairstyle that works well for short hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail, leaving a few strands loose around your face.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, tucking in any loose strands.
  4. Pull out a few strands around your face to frame it.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Accessorize with headbands, hair clips, or scarves to add some extra flair to your style.
  • Experiment with different hair colors and highlights to add dimension to your look.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl short hair?

A: Yes, you can curl short hair using a curling iron or hot rollers. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Can I still wear braids with short hair?

A: Yes, there are many different types of braids that work well with short hair, such as French braids or fishtail braids.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent split ends.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can create cute easy hairstyles for short hair that will have you looking stylish and confident every day. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
