Best Hairstyle For Short Neck And Round Face

Written by Luffy Jul 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of searching for the best hairstyle for your short neck and round face? Worry not, because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll discuss the best hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and make you look fabulous. So, let’s get started.

Description

Having a short neck and round face can be a challenging combination when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. You want to create an illusion of length in your neck and add some angles to your face. The right hairstyle can help you achieve that.

How To

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your short neck and round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in width, then you have a round face shape.

The second step is to consider your hair texture and thickness. Fine hair will require a different hairstyle than thick hair.

Once you have determined your face shape and hair texture, you can start looking for the best hairstyle.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Consider your hair texture and thickness

Step 3: Look for the best hairstyle

Step 4: Consult with a hairstylist

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

2. Opt for hairstyles that add length to your face, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.

3. Consider adding angles to your face with an asymmetrical cut or a layered bob.

4. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect one for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a short neck and round face?

A: Yes, you can, but make sure it’s a layered pixie cut that adds some height to your crown.

Q: What’s the best hair color for a short neck and round face?

A: Depending on your skin tone, you can opt for warm or cool tones. Avoid hair colors that wash you out or make your face appear rounder.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it’s essential to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and protect it from the sun. A healthy diet and lifestyle can also promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for your short neck and round face is one that adds length and angles to your face. It’s essential to determine your face shape and hair texture before choosing the right hairstyle. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths until you find the perfect one for you. And, most importantly, take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
