How To Do A High Ponytail With Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Jun 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle with your short hair? Want to switch it up and try something new? Why not try a high ponytail? This versatile hairstyle can be dressed up or down and is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will teach you how to do a high ponytail with short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A high ponytail is a simple and classic hairstyle that can be achieved with short hair. It involves gathering all of your hair at the top of your head and securing it with an elastic band. A high ponytail with short hair can give the illusion of longer hair and create a more polished look. It can also be a great hairstyle for those hot summer days when you want to keep your hair off your neck.

How to

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You will also need a brush, a hair tie or elastic band, and some hair spray.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to achieve a high ponytail with short hair:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it is smooth.
  2. Gather all of your hair at the top of your head using your hands or a brush.
  3. Secure your hair with an elastic band or hair tie. Make sure it is tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight that it hurts.
  4. If you have any flyaways or loose strands, use some hair spray to keep them in place.
  5. You can also add some accessories like a headband or hair clip to dress up your ponytail.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail with short hair:

  • If your hair is too short to gather into a ponytail, try using bobby pins to pin back any shorter pieces.
  • If you want to add some volume to your ponytail, tease the hair at the crown of your head before gathering it into a ponytail.
  • Use a hair tie or elastic band that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • If you have a round face, try pulling your ponytail back tighter to create the illusion of a longer face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a high ponytail with short hair:

Can I do a high ponytail with very short hair?

If your hair is too short to gather into a ponytail, you can try using bobby pins to pin back any shorter pieces.

Can I add extensions to my short hair to create a high ponytail?

Yes, you can add extensions to your short hair to create a high ponytail. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture.

How can I make my high ponytail look more polished?

You can make your high ponytail look more polished by using some hair spray to keep any flyaways or loose strands in place. You can also add accessories like a headband or hair clip to dress up your ponytail.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair before and after doing a high ponytail. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools.

Now that you know how to do a high ponytail with short hair, go ahead and try it out! It's a simple and versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down for any occasion.
