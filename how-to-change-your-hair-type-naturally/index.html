How To Change Your Hair Type Naturally A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same hair type for years and want to change it naturally? Do you want to know how to get curly hair or straight hair without using any harsh chemicals or styling tools? If you answered yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will guide you through the process of changing your hair type naturally. We will cover everything from the science behind hair to hair care tips. So, let's dive in!

What is Hair Type?

Before we jump into how to change your hair type, let's first understand what hair type is. Hair type is determined by the shape of your hair follicles. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has its own unique characteristics and requires different hair care methods.

How to Change Your Hair Type Naturally

Unfortunately, you cannot change your hair type permanently. However, you can change the appearance of your hair type temporarily by using natural methods. Here are some ways to change your hair type naturally:

1. Twist Out

If you have straight or wavy hair and want to get curly hair, you can do a twist-out. Twist-out is a popular natural hairstyle that involves twisting your hair into sections and letting it air dry. When you untwist your hair, you will have defined curls. To do a twist-out, follow these steps:

- Wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner

- Divide your hair into sections

- Twist each section tightly

- Let your hair air dry

- Untwist your hair

2. Roller Set

A roller set is another way to get curly hair. To do a roller set, you will need rollers of different sizes. Here are the steps to do a roller set:

- Wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner

- Divide your hair into sections

- Roll each section with a roller

- Let your hair air dry or use a hooded dryer

- Remove the rollers

3. Blowout

If you have curly or coily hair and want to get straight hair, you can do a blowout. A blowout involves using a blow dryer and a round brush to straighten your hair. Here are the steps to do a blowout:

- Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant

- Divide your hair into sections

- Use a round brush and blow dryer to straighten each section

- Apply a hair serum for shine

Tips for Changing Your Hair Type Naturally

Here are some tips for changing your hair type naturally:

- Use natural hair products

- Avoid heat styling

- Deep condition your hair regularly

- Protect your hair while sleeping

- Eat a healthy diet

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you permanently change your hair type?

A: No, you cannot permanently change your hair type.

Q: Can heat damage your hair?

A: Yes, heat can damage your hair if you use it excessively.

Q: How often should you deep condition your hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and condition. Generally, you should deep condition your hair once a week.

Hair Care

Changing your hair type naturally can be fun and exciting, but it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

- Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo

- Use a moisturizing conditioner

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

- Avoid tight hairstyles that can damage your hair

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Changing your hair type naturally is possible with the right methods and hair care. Remember to be patient and enjoy the journey. Good luck!
