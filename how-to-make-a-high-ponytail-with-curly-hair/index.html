How to Make a High Ponytail with Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, you might find it difficult to make a high ponytail. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can easily create this chic and stylish hairstyle. In this article, we will show you how to make a high ponytail with curly hair step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making a High Ponytail with Curly Hair

How to Make a High Ponytail with Curly Hair

The first step to making a high ponytail with curly hair is to prepare your hair. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, apply a curl defining cream or gel to help define your curls and prevent frizz.

Next, blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair. Once your hair is dry, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your curls.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to make a high ponytail with curly hair:

  1. Start by gathering all of your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it.
  4. Secure the wrapped hair with bobby pins.
  5. Use a curl defining cream or gel to define any loose strands of hair around your face.
  6. Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray to help keep your ponytail in place.

Tips for Making a High Ponytail with Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you make the perfect high ponytail with curly hair:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • For extra volume, backcomb the crown of your head before making the ponytail.
  • If you have fine or thin curly hair, use a hair donut to add volume to your ponytail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a high ponytail with curly hair:

Q: How do I prevent my ponytail from falling flat?

A: Use a light hold hairspray to help keep your ponytail in place. You can also tease the hair at the crown of your head for added volume.

Q: Can I make a high ponytail with short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still make a high ponytail with short curly hair. Use a hair tie to gather all of your hair at the crown of your head and secure it in place.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow drying your hair to enhance your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily make a high ponytail with curly hair. It's a versatile and chic hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
