Best Haircut For Oval Face Over 50

Written by Aprilia Nov 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you over 50 and have an oval face shape? If yes, then you might struggle to find the perfect haircut that complements your features. In this article, we will tell you about the best haircut for oval face over 50 and provide you with some tips on hair care. Let’s dive in!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile and balanced. It has a slightly rounded jawline, high cheekbones, and a forehead that is wider than the chin. Women over 50 with an oval face shape should opt for a haircut that accentuates their features and makes them look youthful.

How to

The best way to determine your face shape is by pulling your hair back and analyzing the shape of your face in the mirror. Once you have identified your face shape, it is time to choose a haircut that complements it. For an oval face shape, you should aim for a haircut that adds volume to the crown and keeps the length below the chin.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to get the perfect haircut for an oval face over 50:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of hairstyles that you like.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.
  3. Opt for a layered haircut that adds volume and movement to your hair.
  4. Choose a haircut that frames your face and accentuates your cheekbones.
  5. Consider getting bangs that sweep to the side and soften your features.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for an oval face over 50:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too harsh or severe.
  • Opt for a haircut that is easy to maintain and style.
  • Choose a haircut that complements your lifestyle and personality.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for an oval face over 50:

Q: Should I get a short or long haircut?

A: Women over 50 with an oval face shape should avoid haircuts that are too short or too long. Aim for a haircut that is below the chin and adds volume to the crown.

Q: Should I get bangs?

A: Bangs can soften your features and add a youthful touch to your haircut. Consider getting side-swept bangs that frame your face.

Q: What hair products should I use?

A: Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. For example, if you have fine hair, use volumizing products that add body and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know about the best haircut for an oval face over 50, it is time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get a new look that complements your features. Remember to take care of your hair and use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture.
