How Do I Trim My Own Short Hair At Home

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your hair trimmed every few weeks? Or, maybe you just don't have the time to make an appointment with your hairstylist? Whatever your reason may be, learning how to trim your own short hair at home can save you both time and money. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to trim your own short hair at home, some tips, FAQs and hair care advice.

How to Trim Your Own Short Hair at Home

Before we get started, it's important to understand that trimming your own hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you've never done it before. That's why we recommend that you start with small trims and work your way up to more significant cuts. Keep in mind that you can always take more hair off, but you can't put it back on!

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to trim your own short hair at home:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Comb your hair straight down.
  3. Using sharp scissors, cut your hair in a straight line across the bottom. Be sure to cut small sections at a time.
  4. Move to the sides of your head and trim the hair in front of your ears. Use your earlobe as a guide.
  5. Continue trimming around the sides of your head, taking small sections at a time.
  6. Next, move to the back of your head. Use a handheld mirror to see the back of your head, or better yet, have someone help you.
  7. Trim the hair at the nape of your neck, taking small sections at a time.
  8. If you want to add layers to your hair, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  9. After you've finished trimming, use a handheld mirror to check your work. Make any necessary touch-ups.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Trimming Your Own Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when trimming your own short hair at home:

  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it more difficult to cut.
  • Take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Don't cut too much at once. Small trims are easier to manage.
  • Invest in a handheld mirror. It will help you see the back of your head.
  • If you're unsure about something, don't hesitate to ask for help.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming your own short hair at home:

  • How often should I trim my own short hair? It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don't panic! Hair grows back, and mistakes can be fixed. Take a break and come back to it later with fresh eyes.
  • Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair? No, regular scissors are not sharp enough and can damage your hair. Use only sharp hair-cutting scissors.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or flat-ironing.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to trim your own short hair at home, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and start with small trims. With a little practice, you'll be a pro in no time!
