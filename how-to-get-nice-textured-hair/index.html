How To Get Nice Textured Hair

Written by Aprilia Oct 24, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some texture and volume to your locks? Look no further, because we’ve got the tips and tricks you need to get nice textured hair!

Description

Textured hair is all about creating movement and dimension in your hair. It can make your hair look thicker, fuller, and more interesting. There are many ways to add texture to your hair, from using the right products to styling techniques.

How To

Getting nice textured hair starts with using the right products. Look for products that are designed to add texture, such as sea salt sprays or texturizing creams. These products can help create a tousled, beachy look.

Another way to add texture to your hair is by using a curling iron or wand. Curling your hair can create waves and curls that add volume and dimension. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

You can also use braids to create texture in your hair. Braid your hair when it’s wet and leave it in overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you’ll have soft waves and texture.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair with a texturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply a texturizing spray or cream to your hair, focusing on the roots.

3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

4. Use a curling iron or wand to create waves and curls in your hair.

5. Finish with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add even more texture.

Tips

- Don’t be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair and create a more natural, textured look.

- Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Q: Can I add texture to my hair without using heat?

A: Yes! You can braid your hair when it’s wet and leave it in overnight to create texture.

Q: Will adding texture to my hair make it look frizzy?

A: Not necessarily. If you use the right products and techniques, you can create texture without making your hair look frizzy.

Hair Care

It’s important to take good care of your hair when you’re trying to add texture. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage, and avoid using heat on your hair too often. You should also get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be on your way to beautiful, textured hair in no time. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to have fun with your hair!
