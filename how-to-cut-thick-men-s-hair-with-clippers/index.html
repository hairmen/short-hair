How to Cut Thick Men's Hair with Clippers

Cutting thick men's hair with clippers may seem daunting, but it's actually pretty easy if you have the right tools and techniques. Here are the basic steps:

1. Gather Your Tools

The first thing you'll need to do is gather your tools. You'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Clipper oil
  • Brush

2. Choose the Right Clippers

When it comes to cutting thick men's hair with clippers, you'll want to choose clippers with a powerful motor and sharp blades. Look for clippers that are specifically designed for thick hair, like the Wahl Elite Pro High-Performance Haircut Kit.

3. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure your hair is clean and completely dry. Wet or damp hair can make it difficult to cut evenly.

4. Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for your hair. Use the clipper attachments to achieve the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you get the desired length.

5. Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the bottom, working your way up. Use the clippers to cut the hair in even strokes. Make sure to go over each section of hair multiple times to ensure an even cut.

6. Blend the Sides and Back

Use the scissors and comb to blend the sides and back. Start at the bottom and work your way up, cutting the hair in a diagonal direction. Make sure to blend the hair evenly.

7. Trim the Top

Use the scissors and comb to trim the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way back, cutting the hair in even strokes. Make sure to trim any uneven pieces and blend the hair evenly.

8. Clean Up

Once you're done cutting your hair, brush off any loose hairs and clean your clippers with clipper oil.

Tips for Cutting Thick Men's Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting thick men's hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.
  • Go over each section of hair multiple times to ensure an even cut.
  • Blend the sides and back with scissors to achieve a natural look.
  • Trim the top with scissors and comb for a textured look.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your thick hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Don't use too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
