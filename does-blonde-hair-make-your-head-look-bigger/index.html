Does Blonde Hair Make Your Head Look Bigger

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Blonde hair is a popular hair color choice among women worldwide. However, many women are hesitant to go blonde because they fear that it will make their head look bigger. This fear is not unfounded, as hair color can indeed affect the appearance of your head size. In this blog post, we will explore whether blonde hair makes your head look bigger and provide tips on how to make your hair work for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that blonde hair makes your head look bigger. This belief is based on the fact that blonde hair reflects light, making it appear more voluminous. Additionally, blonde hair is often styled in loose waves or curls, which can add further volume to the hair. All of this volume can create the illusion of a larger head, making some people feel self-conscious about their appearance.

How to

If you are concerned about your head appearing larger with blonde hair, there are several things you can do to mitigate the effect:

Choose the Right Shade of Blonde

The shade of blonde you choose can make a big difference in how your head appears. If you have fair skin, a light blonde shade may wash you out, making your head appear larger. Instead, opt for a darker blonde shade that complements your skin tone. If you have darker skin, a lighter blonde shade may work better for you.

Style Your Hair Wisely

The way you style your hair can also affect how your head appears. Avoid styles that add too much volume to the top of your head, such as high ponytails or bouffants. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume at the sides, such as soft waves or curls.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape can also affect how your head appears. If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for styles that elongate your face, such as a long bob or a side-swept fringe.

Step by Step

To make your head appear smaller with blonde hair, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a darker blonde shade that complements your skin tone.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head.
  3. Opt for hairstyles that add volume at the sides.
  4. Consider your face shape when choosing a hairstyle.
  5. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides if you have a round face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your head appear smaller with blonde hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to the sides of your hair.
  • Avoid overusing hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.

FAQ

Does blonde hair make your head look bigger than other hair colors?

Blonde hair reflects light, making it appear more voluminous than other hair colors. This extra volume can create the illusion of a larger head, but it is not exclusive to blonde hair. Other hair colors can also create this effect if they are styled in a way that adds volume to the hair.

Can I still go blonde if I have a small head?

Yes, you can still go blonde if you have a small head. The key is to choose the right shade of blonde and style your hair in a way that complements your face shape. By following the tips outlined in this blog post, you can make your head appear smaller with blonde hair.

Hair Care

Caring for blonde hair requires special attention, as it can be more prone to damage and breakage than other hair colors. Here are some tips for taking care of your blonde hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can keep your blonde hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Conclusion

Blonde hair can indeed make your head appear larger, but this effect can be mitigated by choosing the right shade of blonde and styling your hair in a way that complements your face shape. By following the tips outlined in this blog post, you can make your head appear smaller with blonde hair and feel confident in your appearance.
