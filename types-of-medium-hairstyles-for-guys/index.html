Types Of Medium Hairstyles For Guys

Written by Eveline Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium length hairstyles have become increasingly popular for guys in recent years. Not only do they offer versatility, but they can also be tailored to fit any type of hair texture or face shape. In this article, we'll be discussing the different types of medium hairstyles for guys, how to style them, and tips on how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium length hairstyles for guys typically fall between 2 to 6 inches in length. They're versatile and can be styled in various ways, making them perfect for any occasion. From messy and tousled to slick and polished, there are many different types of medium hairstyles to choose from.

How to Style

The key to styling medium length hair is to use the right products and tools. Some popular products for styling medium hairstyles include pomade, wax, and clay. You'll also need a comb or brush, depending on the style you're going for.

Types of Medium Hairstyles for Guys

1. The Textured Crop - This hairstyle is perfect for guys with wavy or curly hair. The hair is cut short on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and textured for a messy, tousled look.

2. The Side Part - This classic hairstyle is perfect for guys with straight or wavy hair. The hair is parted on one side and combed over to the other side for a sleek, polished look.

3. The Quiff - This hairstyle is perfect for guys with thick hair. The hair is styled upwards and back, creating volume and height. This hairstyle is perfect for a night out or a special occasion.

4. The Messy Bun - This hairstyle is perfect for guys with longer hair. Simply gather your hair into a bun at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual weekend look.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

2. Apply your preferred styling product to your hair.

3. Use a comb or brush to style your hair into the desired shape.

4. Finish with hairspray or another type of finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips

- Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

- Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

- Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques.

FAQ

Q: Can guys with thin hair still rock a medium length hairstyle?

A: Absolutely! Talk to your stylist about adding layers to your hair to create the illusion of thickness.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, strong hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length hair:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

- Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to rock any type of medium length hairstyle with ease. Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you!
