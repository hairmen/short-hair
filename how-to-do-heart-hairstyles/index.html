How To Do Heart Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Heart hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a night out with friends or a special event. These hairstyles are romantic and playful, and they're easy to do at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to create three different heart hairstyles, and give you some tips on how to care for your hair.

  • How to Create a Heart Braid

How to Create a Heart Bun

To create a heart bun, you'll need a hair tie, bobby pins, and some hairspray. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and gathering it into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  4. Take two small sections of hair from the front of your head and twist them towards the back.
  5. Cross the two sections over each other to create a heart shape.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure the heart shape to the top of the bun.
  7. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep everything in place.

Congratulations! You've now created a beautiful heart bun.

How to Create a Heart Braid

To create a heart braid, you'll need a hair tie, bobby pins, and some hairspray. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle and gathering it into two equal sections.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the top of the left section, and cross it over to the right.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the top of the right section, and cross it over to the left.
  4. Repeat this process, but as you cross each section over, add a small amount of hair from the section underneath it.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck.
  6. Tie off the braid with a hair tie.
  7. Use bobby pins to shape the braid into a heart shape.
  8. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep everything in place.

Congratulations! You've now created a beautiful heart braid.

How to Create a Half-Up Heart Hairstyle

To create a half-up heart hairstyle, you'll need a hair tie, bobby pins, and some hairspray. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and gathering the top half into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  4. Take two small sections of hair from the front of your head and twist them towards the back.
  5. Cross the two sections over each other to create a heart shape.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure the heart shape to the top of the bun.
  7. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep everything in place.

Congratulations! You've now created a beautiful half-up heart hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Heart Hairstyles

Here are some tips for maintaining your heart hairstyles:

  • Use a high-quality hairspray to keep everything in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can make it frizzy.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to gently brush your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about heart hairstyles:

  • Q: Are heart hairstyles suitable for all hair types?
  • A: Yes, heart hairstyles can be adapted for all hair types.
  • Q: Can heart hairstyles be done on short hair?
  • A: Yes, heart hairstyles can be done on short hair, but they may look slightly different.
  • Q: How long does it take to create a heart hairstyle?
  • A: The time it takes to create a heart hairstyle depends on the complexity of the style, but most can be done in under 30 minutes.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Hairstyles

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong when wearing heart hairstyles:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Don't use too many hair products, as this can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can put pressure on your hairline.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create beautiful heart hairstyles that look amazing and keep your hair healthy.
