Medium Length Hair With Bangs Oval Face A Perfect Match

Written by Petter Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval face, then you are lucky because you can rock any hairstyle. However, if you want to add some more dimension to your look, then medium length hair with bangs can be a perfect match. This hairstyle is trendy, versatile, and low-maintenance, making it ideal for many women out there. In this blog post, we will discuss how to get and maintain medium length hair with bangs for an oval face shape.

Description

Medium length hair with bangs is a hairstyle that falls between your shoulders and collarbone. Bangs are the strands of hair that fall over your forehead. This hairstyle can be customized according to your face shape, and for an oval face shape, it works wonders. Bangs can help to shorten your face length and add some angles to your face shape.

How to

If you are planning to get medium length hair with bangs, then follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Visit a professional hairstylist and discuss the type of bangs and length of hair you want. They will guide you according to your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Haircut

The hairstylist will cut your hair to the desired length and add bangs. They will use scissors or a razor to give you a textured look.

Step 3: Styling

You can style your hair with a flat iron for a straight look or use a curling iron for beachy waves. Apply some hair serum to add shine and tame any frizz.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style medium length hair with bangs:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Towel dry your hair and remove any tangles.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent heat damage. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts – top and bottom. Clip the top part away from your face.

Step 4: Straighten or Curl

Take the bottom part of your hair and straighten or curl it. Repeat the same process with the top part and your bangs.

Step 5: Apply Hair Serum

Apply some hair serum to add shine and smooth any frizz. You can also use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain medium length hair with bangs:

- Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair. - Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my bangs differently?

A: Yes, you can style your bangs differently. You can opt for side-swept bangs or blunt bangs, depending on your preference and face shape.

Q: Will medium length hair with bangs suit my hair texture?

A: Yes, medium length hair with bangs can suit any hair texture, whether it is straight, wavy, or curly.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for medium length hair with bangs:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using hot tools too often. - Protect your hair from sun damage with a hat or scarf. - Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Medium length hair with bangs can be a game-changer for your look. It is easy to manage, versatile, and can suit any face shape. Follow these tips and steps mentioned above to get and maintain medium length hair with bangs for an oval face shape.
