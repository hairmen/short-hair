How To Cut The Top Of A Man s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a man who wants to cut your own hair, or if you're a barber who wants to learn how to do it better, then you need to know how to cut the top of a man's hair with clippers. This is an important skill that can save you time and money, and it's also a great way to experiment with different hairstyles. In this article, we'll show you how to do it in a few easy steps.

How to Cut the Top of a Man's Hair with Clippers

Cutting the top of a man's hair with clippers can be a little intimidating at first, but with a little practice, you'll get the hang of it. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

The first step in cutting the top of a man's hair with clippers is to choose the right clippers. You'll want to use a pair of clippers that are specifically designed for cutting hair, as these will give you the best results. Look for clippers with adjustable blades and a variety of guards.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and will also help prevent any infections from developing. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type, and then dry it thoroughly with a towel.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard

Next, you'll need to choose the right guard for the clippers. The guard you choose will depend on how short you want the hair to be. Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a smaller one until you achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Start at the Back

Begin cutting at the back of the head, working your way forward. Use the clippers to trim the hair evenly, moving the clippers in a straight line from the back to the front of the head. Be sure to keep the clippers level and steady.

Step 5: Trim the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, move on to the sides. Use the same technique as before, moving the clippers in a straight line from the bottom of the hairline to the top. Be sure to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top of the head.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Now it's time to cut the top of the head. Use the same guard you used on the sides, or a slightly smaller one if you want the hair to be shorter. Start at the front of the head and work your way back, trimming the hair in small sections.

Step 7: Blend the Hair

Once you've finished cutting the top, it's time to blend the hair. Use a comb to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top of the head. Be sure to blend the hair evenly, so it looks natural and even.

Step 8: Check for Mistakes

Finally, check for any mistakes or uneven spots. Use the clippers or scissors to make any necessary adjustments. Be sure to take your time and go slowly, so you don't accidentally cut too much hair.

Tips for Cutting the Top of a Man's Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting the top of a man's hair with clippers:

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a smaller one. This will help you achieve a more even cut.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and go slowly to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers, but it can be a little tricky. It's best to start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one, so you don't accidentally cut too much hair.

What kind of clippers should I use?

You should use clippers that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Look for clippers with adjustable blades and a variety of guards.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you should cut your hair depends on how fast it grows and how short you want it to be. As a general rule, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips

Cutting your hair with clippers can be a great way to save time and money, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut the top of a man's hair with clippers, you can experiment with different hairstyles and save money on haircuts. Remember to take your time, go slowly, and use the right tools to achieve the best results.
