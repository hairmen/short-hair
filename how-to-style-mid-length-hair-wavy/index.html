How To Style Mid Length Hair Wavy Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have mid length hair and want to add some waves for a relaxed, beachy vibe, you're in luck! There are several ways to style mid length hair wavy, whether you want loose waves or tighter curls. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect wavy hairstyle.

Description

Mid length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many different ways, including wavy. Waves add texture and movement to your hair, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Whether you're looking for a casual, beachy look or something more polished, there's a wavy hairstyle that will work for you.

How to Style Mid Length Hair Wavy: Step by Step

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you style your hair, you'll want to prep it with some products. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks healthy and hydrated. Towel dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 2: Choose Your Styling Method

There are several ways to add waves to mid length hair, including using a curling iron, flat iron, or hair rollers. Choose the method that works best for you and your hair type. If you have naturally wavy hair, you may be able to enhance your waves with some styling products and a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Step 3: Start Styling

Once you've chosen your styling method, it's time to start creating your waves. If you're using a curling iron or flat iron, take small sections of hair and wrap them around the barrel of the iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. For hair rollers, wrap sections of hair around the rollers and secure with clips. Leave the rollers in your hair for at least 30 minutes before removing.

Step 4: Finish Your Look

Once you've created your waves, it's time to finish your look. Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add definition and hold to your waves. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness to your locks.

Tips for Styling Mid Length Hair Wavy

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect wavy hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools
  • Choose the right styling method for your hair type
  • Don't overdo it with styling products, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Experiment with different waves, from loose and beachy to tighter curls
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves

FAQ

Can I style mid length hair wavy without heat?

Absolutely! There are several ways to add waves to your hair without using heat styling tools. You can use hair rollers, braid your hair overnight, or twist sections of your hair and pin them in place while they dry.

Can I add waves to my naturally straight hair?

Yes, you can! You may need to use a curling or flat iron to create waves, but there are also styling products and techniques that can enhance your natural waves. Experiment with different methods to see what works best for you.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

When you have wavy hair, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking their best. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage, and apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do. Finally, trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Conclusion

Styling mid length hair wavy is a great way to add texture and movement to your locks. Whether you want loose, beachy waves or tighter curls, there are several methods you can use to achieve the perfect wavy hairstyle. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims and nourishing products.
