Description

A clipper without guard is a tool used for cutting hair. The length of the clipper blade without guard varies depending on the brand and model of the clipper. However, on average, the length of a clipper without guard is around 1/8 inch or 3mm.

How to

To use a clipper without guard, you need to attach the blade to the clipper. The blade should be securely fastened to the clipper to prevent it from falling off during use.

Step 1: Prepare the clipper

Clean the clipper by removing all the hair and debris from the blade and the body of the clipper. Make sure the clipper is dry before attaching the blade.

Step 2: Attach the blade

Align the blade with the clipper and slide it onto the blade holder. Make sure the blade clicks into place and is secure.

Step 3: Turn on the clipper

Plug in the clipper and turn it on. Hold the clipper at a 45-degree angle and start cutting the hair from the back of the head to the front. Use short, even strokes to ensure an even cut.

Step by step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to use a clipper without guard:

  1. Prepare the clipper by cleaning it and making sure it's dry.
  2. Attach the blade to the clipper.
  3. Turn on the clipper.
  4. Hold the clipper at a 45-degree angle.
  5. Start cutting the hair from the back of the head to the front.
  6. Use short, even strokes to ensure an even cut.
  7. Repeat the process until you have cut all the hair to the desired length.
  8. Clean the clipper and blade after use.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you use a clipper without guard:

  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head for any missed spots.
  • Trim the hair around the ears and neckline with scissors for a more polished look.

FAQ

What is the difference between a clipper with guard and without guard?

A clipper with guard has a plastic attachment that slides over the blade to control the length of the cut. A clipper without guard has a blade that cuts the hair to a specific length.

What length is a clipper without guard?

On average, the length of a clipper without guard is around 1/8 inch or 3mm.

Can I use a clipper without guard on my beard?

Yes, you can use a clipper without guard on your beard. However, it's recommended to use a guard or scissors to trim the beard to avoid cutting too much hair.

Hair Care

Using a clipper without guard can be a great way to maintain short haircuts or buzz cuts. However, it's important to take care of your hair after cutting to keep it healthy and looking its best.

Here are some tips for hair care after using a clipper without guard:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using styling tools that use heat, as this can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Visit a professional stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut.

Using a clipper without guard can be a great way to achieve a low-maintenance haircut. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can keep it looking healthy and stylish.
