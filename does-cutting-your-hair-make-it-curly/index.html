Does Cutting Your Hair Make It Curly The Truth Behind The Myth

Written by Christine Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever heard the old wives' tale that cutting your hair can make it curly? It's a common belief that's been passed down through generations, but is there any truth to it? In this article, we'll explore the science behind hair growth and whether or not cutting your hair can actually make it curly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Hair for a Curly Look
  • Step by Step Guide for Cutting Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs about Cutting Your Hair for a Curly Look

How to Cut Your Hair for a Curly Look

If you're looking to enhance your curls, there are a few ways you can cut your hair to achieve this look. One popular method is the DevaCut, which is a technique used by stylists to cut curly hair while it's dry. This helps them see the natural shape of your curls and allows them to cut accordingly.

Another option is to get layers cut into your hair. This can help create more volume and movement in your curls, giving them a bouncier look. You can also ask your stylist to use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk from your hair, which can help define your curls even more.

Step by Step Guide for Cutting Your Hair

If you're planning to cut your own hair at home, here are some steps you can follow:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would, then let it air dry or lightly blow dry it.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back.
  3. Starting with one section at a time, comb out any tangles and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  4. Using sharp scissors, cut small sections of hair at a time, cutting at an angle to create layers.
  5. Repeat this process with each section of hair until you've achieved your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've achieved your desired curly look, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as this can damage your hair and cause it to lose its curl pattern.
  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to help enhance your curls.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to damp hair before styling.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain the shape of your curls.

FAQs about Cutting Your Hair for a Curly Look

Q: Does cutting your hair make it grow faster?

A: No, cutting your hair does not actually make it grow faster. However, it can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thinner and less healthy.

Q: Can cutting your hair make it curlier?

A: No, cutting your hair cannot actually change the natural texture of your hair. However, it can help enhance your natural curl pattern and create the illusion of curlier hair.

Q: How often should I cut my hair if I want to maintain my curly look?

A: It's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of your haircuts will depend on your individual hair type and how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be a bit more challenging than caring for straight hair, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, opt for a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.

In conclusion, cutting your hair cannot actually make it curly, but it can help enhance your natural curl pattern and create the illusion of curlier hair. Whether you choose to get a professional DevaCut or cut your own hair at home, be sure to follow the proper techniques and maintain your curly look with the right hair care products and techniques.
