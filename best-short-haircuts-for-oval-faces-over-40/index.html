Best Short Haircuts For Oval Faces Over 40 A Guide For Relaxed English Speakers

Written by William Nov 01, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're an oval-faced woman over 40, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, if you're looking for the best short haircuts that flatter your features, this guide is for you. Read on to discover the most stylish and flattering short haircuts for oval faces over 40.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for oval faces over 40, let's make sure you have an oval face shape. Here's how to determine your face shape:

  1. Take a photo of your face straight on.
  2. Trace the outline of your face with a marker or pen.
  3. Compare the shape to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

If your face shape is oval, the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly equal, and your face has a slightly curved shape.

Best Short Haircuts for Oval Faces Over 40

Now that you know you have an oval face, it's time to choose a short haircut that flatters your features. Here are some of our top picks:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that looks great on oval faces over 40. It's a low-maintenance style that's both stylish and practical. The short layers add volume and movement to your hair, while the longer top layers can be styled in different ways to suit your mood and occasion.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another timeless short haircut that flatters oval faces over 40. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A chin-length bob with layers and side-swept bangs can soften your features and give you a youthful look.

3. Shaggy Cut

The shaggy cut is a trendy short haircut that's perfect for oval faces over 40. It's a layered style that adds texture and movement to your hair. The choppy layers can be styled in different ways to create a messy or polished look. A shaggy cut with side-swept bangs can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline.

4. Lob Cut

The lob cut, or long bob, is a chic short haircut that's suitable for oval faces over 40. It's a mid-length style that's longer than a bob but shorter than shoulder-length hair. The lob cut can be styled in different ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts

Now that you've chosen your short haircut, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your short hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush or a flat brush.
  5. Use a flat iron or a curling iron to style your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a hair serum to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, hair clips, and barrettes, to add interest to your style.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Oval Faces Over 40

1. Can oval-faced women over 40 wear bangs?

Yes, oval-faced women over 40 can wear bangs. Side-swept bangs can soften your features and give you a youthful look.

2. What hair color looks best on oval faces over 40?

Any hair color can look great on oval faces over 40. However, warm and natural shades, such as caramel, honey, and chestnut, can complement your skin tone and enhance your features.

3. How often should I wash my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you may want to wash it every three to four days. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Here are some hair care tips for short haircuts:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair to prevent buildup.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for oval faces over 40 and how to style and maintain them, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and rock a new look!
