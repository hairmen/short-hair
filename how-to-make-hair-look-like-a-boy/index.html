How To Make Hair Look Like A Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve a more masculine hairstyle, you may be wondering how to make hair look like a boy. Fortunately, there are several techniques and tips you can use to achieve the look you want. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to make your hair look like a boy's, as well as some helpful tips and frequently asked questions.

Description: How to Make Hair Look Like a Boy

Masculine hairstyles often involve shorter haircuts and less styling than feminine styles. If you're looking to make your hair look like a boy's, you'll want to consider getting a shorter cut and avoiding overly styled looks. Depending on your hair type and texture, there are several options to choose from, including buzz cuts, crew cuts, and short crops.

How to Make Hair Look Like a Boy: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Haircut

The first step in making your hair look like a boy's is to choose a haircut that works for you. A buzz cut is a popular option for those looking for a low-maintenance style, while a crew cut or short crop can provide a slightly longer length while still maintaining a masculine look. Consult with a stylist to determine which style will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Keep it Simple

When styling your hair to look like a boy's, it's important to keep it simple. Avoid using too much product or creating overly styled looks. Instead, opt for a more natural, effortless look. Use a small amount of product to add texture or hold, but avoid anything too shiny or greasy.

Step 3: Embrace the Messy Look

Masculine hairstyles often involve a less polished, more messy look. To achieve this style, use your fingers to create texture and volume, rather than a brush or comb. Allow your hair to air dry, or use a hair dryer on a low setting to avoid creating too much volume or frizz.

Step 4: Consider Your Hair Type

When choosing a haircut and styling your hair to look like a boy's, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. If you have thin or fine hair, a shorter cut can help create the appearance of thicker, fuller hair. Those with thick or curly hair may prefer a slightly longer cut that allows for more natural movement and texture.

Tips for Making Hair Look Like a Boy

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a masculine hairstyle:

  • Use a matte styling product to avoid a shiny or greasy look.
  • Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain the shape and style.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons.
  • Consider using a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

FAQ: Making Hair Look Like a Boy

What hairstyles are popular for boys?

Popular hairstyles for boys include buzz cuts, crew cuts, short crops, and longer styles like the shag or messy fringe.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

A shorter haircut can help create the appearance of thicker hair, as can using a texturizing product to add volume and texture.

Can I still style my hair if I want a masculine look?

Of course! There are several ways to style your hair to look more masculine, including using a matte styling product, creating a messy look with your fingers, and avoiding overly polished or styled looks.

Hair Care for a Masculine Look

To maintain a masculine hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, and avoid using hot tools that can cause damage or breakage. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style, and consider using a texturizing product or spray to add volume and texture.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily make your hair look like a boy's and achieve the masculine hairstyle you desire.
