Is It Easy To Cut Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but wondering if it's easy to cut them yourself? Curtain bangs have been a popular hairstyle for a few years now, and they can be a great way to change up your look without a drastic haircut. In this article, we'll explore how to cut curtain bangs at home, step-by-step, and answer some common questions about this hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Curtain Bangs
  • Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs
  • FAQ About Curtain Bangs
  • Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

How to Cut Curtain Bangs

Cutting your own curtain bangs can seem daunting, but it's not as difficult as it may seem. Follow these steps to achieve your desired look:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle. Use a comb to create a straight line down the middle of your hair, from your forehead to the nape of your neck.
  3. Section off your hair. Use clips to separate the rest of your hair from the section you'll be cutting.
  4. Start cutting. Begin by cutting your bangs to your desired length, making sure they're even on both sides. Use point cutting to create a textured look if desired.
  5. Blend the sides. Take small sections of hair from the sides of your bangs and cut them at a slight angle, blending them into your bangs.
  6. Trim as needed. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments to your bangs. Remember to cut less than you think you need to, as you can always cut more later.

Congratulations, you've just cut your own curtain bangs!

Tools You'll Need

To cut curtain bangs at home, you'll need a few tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • Hair clips
  • A round brush

Tips for Cutting Curtain Bangs

Here are some additional tips to make your curtain bang cutting experience easier:

  • Start with dry hair. This will help you see how your bangs will look when they're styled.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can create split ends and make your hair look frayed.
  • Don't cut too much at once. Remember, you can always cut more later, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Take breaks. If you're feeling frustrated or tired, take a break and come back to your bangs later.

FAQ About Curtain Bangs

Here are some common questions about curtain bangs:

What are curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs are a style of bangs that are parted down the middle and styled to frame the face. They're called "curtain" bangs because they open up like curtains on a window.

Will curtain bangs suit my face shape?

Curtain bangs can work for many face shapes, but it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine if they're the right look for you.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You'll need to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every few days. Over-washing can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a heat protectant before styling. This will help protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to style your bangs. This will help distribute oils from your scalp to the rest of your hair, keeping your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day. This can cause oils from your skin to transfer to your hair, making it look greasy.

In conclusion, cutting curtain bangs at home can be easy with the right tools and a little patience. Follow our step-by-step guide and hair care tips to achieve your desired look.
