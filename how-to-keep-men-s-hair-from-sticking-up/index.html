How To Keep Men s Hair From Sticking Up A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Sep 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As a man, you want to look your best every day. But sometimes, no matter how much time you spend styling your hair, it just won't stay down. Whether you have short hair or long hair, it can be frustrating when your hair refuses to cooperate. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to keep your hair from sticking up. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve a sleek, stylish look.

  • How to
  • Step by Step

How to Keep Men's Hair from Sticking Up

Before we dive into specific tips, it's important to understand why your hair might be sticking up in the first place. There are several factors that can contribute to unruly hair, including:

  • Humidity
  • Dryness
  • Friction from hats or pillows
  • Product buildup
  • Unhealthy hair habits

Now that you know some of the causes, let's look at some ways to keep your hair from sticking up:

1. Use the Right Products

The products you use can make a big difference in how your hair behaves. Look for products that are designed to provide hold and control, such as pomades, waxes, or gels. Be careful not to use too much, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

2. Brush or Comb Your Hair

Brushing or combing your hair can help distribute natural oils and prevent tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb or a boar bristle brush to gently detangle your hair from the roots to the ends. This will help keep your hair smooth and prevent it from sticking up.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help set it in place and prevent it from sticking up. Use a medium heat setting and a round brush to smooth your hair as you dry it. Be sure to aim the dryer downward to avoid creating flyaways.

4. Try a Different Haircut

Some haircuts are more prone to sticking up than others. If you're struggling to keep your hair in place, try a different style that's more suited to your hair type. A shorter haircut can be easier to manage, while longer hair may require more styling.

Step by Step: How to Keep Men's Hair from Sticking Up

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you keep your hair looking its best:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any product buildup and keep your hair hydrated.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the areas that tend to stick up. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Brush or Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or a boar bristle brush to gently detangle your hair from the roots to the ends. This will help distribute the product and prevent tangles.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair on a medium heat setting, using a round brush to smooth your hair as you dry it. Be sure to aim the dryer downward to avoid creating flyaways.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers or a comb to style it as desired. Finish with a light hairspray or finishing product to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Keeping Men's Hair from Sticking Up

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Avoid wearing hats or other accessories that can cause friction
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does my hair stick up?

A: There are several factors that can contribute to unruly hair, including humidity, dryness, friction from hats or pillows, product buildup, and unhealthy hair habits.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Most people can get away with washing their hair every other day or a few times a week. However, if you have very oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Q: What products should I use to keep my hair from sticking up?

A: Look for products that are designed to provide hold and control, such as pomades, waxes, or gels. Be careful not to use too much, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Hair Care for Men

Keeping your hair looking its best requires more than just styling. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking sleek, stylish, and perfectly groomed. Whether you have short hair or long hair, these techniques will help you achieve the look you want.
