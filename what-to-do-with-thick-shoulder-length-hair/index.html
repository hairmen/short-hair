What To Do With Thick Shoulder Length Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having thick shoulder length hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have a luxurious amount of hair to work with. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for what to do with thick shoulder length hair.

How to Style Thick Shoulder Length Hair

When it comes to styling thick shoulder length hair, there are many options. You can wear it down, pull it back into a ponytail or bun, or braid it. Here are some specific styles to try:

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular style for thick shoulder length hair. To achieve this look, apply a texturizing spray to damp hair and blow dry. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish with a light hairspray.

Sleek Straight

If you prefer a sleeker look, straighten your hair with a flat iron. Apply a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron to prevent damage. Finish with a shine serum to add some extra gloss.

Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down style is a great way to show off your hair while keeping it out of your face. Simply pull the top half of your hair back into a ponytail or bun, leaving the rest down.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Shoulder Length Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your thick shoulder length hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat tools.
  3. Choose your desired style (see above for ideas).
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen style.
  5. Finish with hairspray or other styling products as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your thick shoulder length hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using heat tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and scalp.

Frequently Asked Questions About Thick Shoulder Length Hair

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once or twice a week. Experiment to see what works best for you.

What kind of products should I use on my hair?

Use products that are specifically formulated for your hair type (e.g. thick, curly, color-treated). Look for products that are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

How can I add volume to my hair?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots. You can also try using a volumizing spray or mousse.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thick shoulder length hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it's more prone to breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to manage your thick shoulder length hair with ease. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
