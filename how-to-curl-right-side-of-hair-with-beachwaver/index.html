How to Curl Right Side of Hair with Beachwaver

The Beachwaver is a curling iron that is designed to make it easy to create beautiful, bouncy curls. Here's how you can use the Beachwaver to curl the right side of your hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start curling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. You can air dry your hair or use a blow dryer if you prefer. If you have naturally straight hair, you can add some texture by using a sea salt spray or texturizing product.

2. Section your hair

To ensure that you curl every strand of hair on the right side of your head, you need to section it. Use a clip to separate the right side of your hair from the left side. You can also divide your hair into more sections if you have thick hair.

3. Use the Beachwaver

Now it's time to use the Beachwaver. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the Beachwaver. Hold it in place for a few seconds, then release it. Repeat this process for all the sections on the right side of your head.

4. Finish with hairspray

Once you've curled all the sections on the right side of your head, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold the curls in place and prevent them from falling out throughout the day.

Tips for Using Beachwaver

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Beachwaver:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase the temperature if needed
  • Use smaller sections of hair for tighter curls and larger sections for looser waves
  • Wrap the hair around the barrel in the direction away from your face for a more natural look

FAQs

Q: Can I use the Beachwaver on wet hair?

A: No, it is not recommended to use the Beachwaver on wet hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using the Beachwaver to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How long does it take to curl the right side of my hair with the Beachwaver?

A: The time it takes to curl the right side of your hair with the Beachwaver will depend on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it takes about 20-30 minutes to curl the right side of your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
