What To Wear At A 70 S Disco Party A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Nov 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you ready to boogie down at a 70's disco party? Whether you're attending a themed birthday bash or hitting up a disco club, dressing the part is essential. Take a step back in time and relive the disco era with this complete guide on what to wear at a 70's disco party.

Description

The 70's disco era was all about glitz, glamour, and bold fashion. From bell-bottoms to platform shoes, the disco scene was a fashion lover's dream come true. To truly embrace the disco vibe, you'll want to incorporate bright colors, metallics, and plenty of sparkle into your outfit. But don't worry, with a little guidance, you'll be able to create a disco look that's all your own.

How To: What to Wear at a 70's Disco Party

Step 1: Start with the Basics

When it comes to a 70's disco party, your outfit should be all about the basics. Start with a pair of flare jeans or bell-bottoms, a colorful shirt or blouse, and a funky jacket or vest. Look for pieces with bold patterns, like stripes or polka dots, and don't be afraid to mix and match. Remember, the 70's disco era was all about breaking fashion rules.

Step 2: Add Some Sparkle

No disco outfit is complete without a little bit of sparkle. Look for pieces with sequins, glitter, or metallic accents. A sparkly top or dress is a great option, but you can also add some bling with accessories like a disco ball necklace or glittery shoes. Just be careful not to overdo it. You don't want to look like a walking disco ball.

Step 3: Embrace the Platform

Platform shoes were a staple of the disco era. Whether you opt for a pair of platform sandals or boots, they'll add some height and a touch of retro style to your outfit. Just make sure you can dance in them. You don't want to be stuck sitting on the sidelines all night.

Step 4: Finish with Accessories

The right accessories can make or break a disco outfit. Look for oversized sunglasses, hoop earrings, and funky hats to complete your look. And don't forget to add a little disco flair with a colorful scarf or headband.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you perfect your disco look:

  • Don't be afraid to mix and match patterns and colors.
  • Look for vintage pieces at thrift stores or online retailers.
  • Opt for high-waisted pants or a skirt to accentuate your curves.
  • Keep your makeup bold and bright, with lots of glitter and shimmer.
  • Don't forget to accessorize with a disco ball purse or clutch.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what to wear at a 70's disco party:

What colors should I wear to a disco party?

Disco parties are all about bright and bold colors. Think neon pinks, electric blues, and sunshine yellows. But don't be afraid to mix in some metallics, like silver or gold.

What should I do with my hair?

For a truly authentic disco look, you'll want to go for big, voluminous hair. Think Farrah Fawcett curls or a big afro. If you don't have the hair for it, consider wearing a wig or adding some hair extensions.

Can I wear a jumpsuit to a disco party?

Absolutely! Jumpsuits were a popular choice during the disco era and can be a great way to make a statement at a disco party. Look for a jumpsuit with a bold pattern or a metallic finish.

Hair Care

If you're going for a big, voluminous disco hairdo, you'll want to make sure your hair is healthy and strong. Here are some tips to keep your locks in top shape:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.
  • Limit your use of hair products like hairspray and gel, which can dry out your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to make a splash at any 70's disco party. So grab your platform shoes and get ready to boogie down!
