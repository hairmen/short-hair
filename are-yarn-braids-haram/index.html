Are Yarn Braids Haram What Muslims Need To Know

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Yarn braids have become a popular hairstyle among African American women in recent years. They are easy to install and maintain, and they can be styled in various ways. But for Muslim women who wear hijab, the question arises: are yarn braids haram? In this article, we will explore this question and provide some tips for Muslim women who want to wear yarn braids.

Description

Yarn braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves wrapping synthetic yarn around sections of hair. The yarn is then braided into the hair, creating a long-lasting style that can last for several weeks. Yarn braids come in various colors, textures, and lengths, making them a versatile hairstyle for women of all ages and hair types.

How to Install Yarn Braids

Before installing yarn braids, it is important to prepare your hair. This involves shampooing and conditioning your hair to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Then, you will need to detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or brush. Once your hair is clean and detangled, you can begin installing the yarn braids. Here are the steps:

Step 1:

Divide your hair into small sections using a rat-tail comb.

Step 2:

Take a piece of synthetic yarn and wrap it around the base of the section of hair.

Step 3:

Braid the yarn into your hair, using a three-strand braid technique.

Step 4:

Continue braiding the yarn into your hair until you reach the end of the section.

Step 5:

Repeat steps 2-4 for each section of hair until your entire head is covered in yarn braids.

Tips for Wearing Yarn Braids

Here are some tips for Muslim women who want to wear yarn braids:

Tip 1:

Choose a yarn color that matches your natural hair color or your hijab color.

Tip 2:

Avoid using yarn that is too thick or heavy, as this can put strain on your hair and scalp.

Tip 3:

Moisturize your hair and scalp regularly while wearing yarn braids, as they can dry out your hair.

FAQ

Q: Are yarn braids haram?

A: There is no clear answer to this question, as it depends on the individual's interpretation of Islamic law. Some Muslims believe that wearing any type of artificial hair is haram, while others believe that it is acceptable as long as it is not used to deceive others.

Q: Can I wear yarn braids with hijab?

A: Yes, you can wear yarn braids with hijab. Just make sure to choose a yarn color that matches your hijab color, and avoid using yarn that is too thick or heavy.

Q: How long do yarn braids last?

A: Yarn braids can last for several weeks, depending on how well they are installed and how well they are maintained. To make them last longer, avoid excessive heat styling and keep your hair and scalp moisturized.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair and scalp moisturized is essential when wearing yarn braids. You can use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated. You should also wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. If you experience any discomfort or itching while wearing yarn braids, it is important to remove them immediately and seek medical attention if necessary.

In conclusion, there is no clear answer to the question of whether yarn braids are haram. It is up to each individual Muslim to decide whether this hairstyle is acceptable according to their interpretation of Islamic law. However, if you do choose to wear yarn braids, make sure to take good care of your hair and scalp, and choose a yarn color that matches your natural hair color or hijab color.
