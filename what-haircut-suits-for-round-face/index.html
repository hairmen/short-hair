Description

Having a round face can be tricky when it comes to choosing the right haircut. A round face can appear wider and shorter than other face shapes, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that can elongate and slim down the face. In this blog post, we will discuss what haircut suits for round face in 2023, as well as provide tips, step-by-step guide, and hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best.

How to

The first step in finding the right haircut for a round face is to determine the face shape. Measure the widest part of the face, which is usually the cheeks, and the length of the face from the hairline to the chin. If the measurements are similar or the same, then it is a round face shape.

Step by step

Step 1: Determine your face shape by measuring the widest part of your face and the length from the hairline to the chin.

Step 2: Choose a haircut that will elongate and slim down the face, such as a long layered cut, angled bob, or side-swept bangs.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step 4: Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using hair care products that are suitable for your hair type.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs or short curly hair.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as layered cuts or voluminous updos.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to the hair and create a slimming effect.

FAQ

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

A: A long layered cut with side-swept bangs can complement the natural texture of curly hair and elongate the face.

Q: Can I still have short hair with a round face?

A: Yes, a short layered cut or pixie cut can work for a round face, as long as it adds height to the crown of the head and avoids adding volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and vibrant look. Here are some hair care tips for different hair types:

  • For curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define the curls and reduce frizz.
  • For straight hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to the hair. Apply a heat protectant before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • For fine hair, use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down the hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and hold.

Choosing the right haircut for a round face can be challenging, but with the right tips and guidance, you can find a hairstyle that complements your features and suits your personality. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and maintain your haircut with proper hair care to keep your hair looking its best.
