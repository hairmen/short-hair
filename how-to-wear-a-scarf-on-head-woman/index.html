How To Wear A Scarf On Head Woman A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile accessory to add to your wardrobe, a scarf worn on your head is a great option. Whether you're trying to protect your hair from the sun or simply want to add a pop of color to your outfit, a scarf can do the trick. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a scarf on your head in a few different ways, so you can find the perfect style for you.

How to Wear a Scarf on Head Woman

Step 1: Choose Your Scarf

The first step to wearing a scarf on your head is to choose the right scarf. You'll want to pick a scarf that's long and wide enough to wrap around your head a few times. Silk scarves are great for a more formal look, while cotton or linen scarves are perfect for a more casual style. Choose a scarf that matches your outfit or adds a pop of color to your look.

Step 2: Fold Your Scarf

Before you can wrap your scarf around your head, you'll need to fold it properly. Fold your scarf in half diagonally to create a triangle shape. Then, fold the scarf in half again, so it's a long rectangle shape.

Step 3: Place the Scarf on Your Head

Place the scarf on your head with the center of the scarf at the base of your skull. The two ends of the scarf should be hanging down on either side of your head.

Step 4: Tie the Scarf in the Back

Take the two ends of the scarf and tie them in the back of your head, making a knot. You can adjust the knot to make it tighter or looser, depending on your preference.

Step 5: Style Your Scarf

Now that your scarf is tied, you can style it in a few different ways. You can leave the knot at the back of your head, or you can move it to the side. You can also tuck the ends of the scarf into the knot to create a turban-like look.

Tips for Wearing a Scarf on Head Woman

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when wearing a scarf on your head:

  • Experiment with different scarf sizes and materials to find your favorite style.
  • Try tying your scarf in different ways to create different looks.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your scarf in place if it feels too loose.
  • Wear a scarf on your head with sunglasses for a chic and sophisticated look.

FAQs About Wearing a Scarf on Head Woman

Can I wear a scarf on my head if I have short hair?

Absolutely! A scarf on your head can look great with short hair. Just make sure to choose a scarf that's not too bulky, so it doesn't overwhelm your hairstyle.

What's the best way to tie a scarf on my head?

There's no one "best" way to tie a scarf on your head. Experiment with different knots and placements to find the style that works for you.

Can I wear a scarf on my head to work?

It depends on your workplace dress code. If your workplace is more formal, a silk scarf tied in a classic knot can look professional. If your workplace is more casual, a cotton or linen scarf worn in a turban-like style can add a pop of color to your outfit.

Hair Care When Wearing a Scarf on Head Woman

When wearing a scarf on your head, it's important to take care of your hair underneath. Here are a few tips:

  • Wear a silk or satin scarf underneath your main scarf to protect your hair from damage.
  • Don't tie your scarf too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Keep your hair moisturized by applying a leave-in conditioner or oil before wearing a scarf.

Now that you know how to wear a scarf on your head, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little practice, you'll soon be rocking this stylish accessory with confidence.
