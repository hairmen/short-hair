Best Hairstyles For Long Faces Over 50

Written by Oliver Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our face shape changes, and our hairstyles should change too. If you have a long face and you are over 50, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will share the best hairstyles for long faces over 50, and some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Let's get started!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long faces are characterized by a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are similar in width. The length of the face is longer than its width, which can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle. The goal is to create volume and width around the cheeks and jawline to balance out the length of the face.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face, the key is to create width and volume around the cheeks and jawline. This can be achieved with layers, curls, and waves. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make the face appear even longer.

Step by step

Here are the best hairstyles for long faces over 50:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a classic hairstyle that can work well for a long face. The layers add volume and movement, while the length is not too long. The bob can be styled with waves or curls to add even more volume.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance out the length of the face. The bangs should be swept to the side and cut at an angle to create a diagonal line across the forehead. This will create width and volume around the cheeks and jawline.

3. Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair is a great option for a long face. The length adds volume and the hair can be styled with waves or curls to add even more volume. This style can be parted in the middle or on the side.

4. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can work well for a long face. The layers add volume and movement, while the length is not too long. This style can be styled with waves or curls to add even more volume.

5. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob (long bob) is a great option for a long face. The length adds volume, while the waves add movement and width around the cheeks and jawline. This style can be parted in the middle or on the side.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a long face over 50:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make the face appear even longer
  • Choose hairstyles with volume and width around the cheeks and jawline
  • Consider adding layers, curls, or waves to create volume and movement
  • Side-swept bangs can help to balance out the length of the face
  • Consult with a stylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a long face over 50?

The best hairstyle for a long face over 50 is one that adds volume and width around the cheeks and jawline. This can be achieved with layers, curls, and waves. Side-swept bangs can also help to balance out the length of the face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face over 50?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as they can make the face appear even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short and flat, as they can make the face appear too long.

Hair Care

Regardless of your hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth

With these tips and hairstyles, you can look and feel your best with a long face over 50. Remember to consult with a stylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
