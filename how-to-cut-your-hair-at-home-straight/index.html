How To Cut Your Hair At Home Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Or maybe you're just looking for a new DIY project. Whatever the reason, cutting your own hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your hair at home straight. Follow these steps, and you'll have a fresh new look in no time!

How to Cut Your Hair at Home Straight

Before we get started, we should mention that cutting your hair at home can be a bit tricky. We recommend starting with small cuts and gradually working your way up. If you're not confident, it might be best to leave it to the professionals.

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step is to gather all the tools you'll need. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to use a mirror to help you see the back of your head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Next, wash and dry your hair. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and make it easier to cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Clip each section out of the way except for the one you'll be cutting first.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Starting with the back section, comb your hair straight down and trim the ends. Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back on.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the left and right sides. Comb your hair straight down and trim the ends. Make sure you're cutting evenly on both sides.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, it's time to cut the top. Comb your hair straight up and trim the ends. Again, start with small cuts and work your way up.

Step 7: Blend Your Hair

Once you've cut all four sections, blend your hair by combing it straight down and making small cuts to even out any uneven spots.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Take a few steps back and look in the mirror. Check your work from all angles to make sure it's even and straight. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use a blow dryer or straightener to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair at Home

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your hair at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Watch tutorial videos to get a better understanding of the process.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

While it's possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, we recommend using hair cutting scissors. They're specifically designed for cutting hair and will give you better results.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, we recommend getting a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and manageable.

Hair Care After Cutting

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut your hair at home straight like a pro. Give it a try and see how much money you can save by skipping the salon!
