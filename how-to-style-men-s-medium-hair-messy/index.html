How to Style Men's Medium Hair Messy

Messy hair is a popular trend among men, and for good reason. It gives you a carefree, effortless look that's perfect for any occasion. The key to achieving a messy hairstyle is to embrace your natural hair texture and use the right products to enhance it. Here are some tips:

1. Get the Right Cut

The first step to achieving a messy hairstyle is to get the right haircut. Your hair should be cut in layers to add volume and texture. A medium-length cut is ideal for a messy hairstyle, as it provides enough length to create different styles.

2. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural hair texture, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly. Don't try to fight it or tame it too much. Use products that enhance your natural texture and add definition to your hair.

3. Use the Right Products

Use the right products to achieve a messy look. A texturizing spray or sea salt spray can add volume and texture to your hair. A matte finish pomade or wax can help you achieve a messy, bedhead look. Apply the product to damp hair and style with your fingers.

4. Don't Overdo It

Remember, the key to a messy hairstyle is to look effortless. Don't overdo it with the products or the styling. Keep it simple and natural.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a messy hairstyle:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.

2. Apply Texturizing Spray

Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to your hair to add volume and texture. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using your fingers, not a brush. Use a low heat setting and focus on lifting your hair at the roots to add volume.

4. Apply Pomade or Wax

Apply a matte finish pomade or wax to your hair to create a messy, bedhead look. Use your fingers to style your hair and create texture.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place without making it look stiff or unnatural.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your messy hairstyle:

1. Don't Wash Your Hair Too Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it harder to style. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

2. Use Dry Shampoo

Use dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair. It can also add volume and texture.

3. Don't Brush Your Hair Too Much

Brushing your hair too much can flatten it and make it look less messy. Use your fingers to style your hair instead of a brush.

4. Visit Your Barber Regularly

Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I achieve a messy look with short hair?

Yes, you can achieve a messy look with short hair. Use a texturizing product to add volume and texture to your hair, and use your fingers to style it.

2. How do I keep my hair from looking greasy?

Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil, and don't apply too much product to your hair.

3. Can I achieve a messy look with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve a messy look with curly hair. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls, and use your fingers to create a messy look.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Medium Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your men's medium hair healthy and strong:

1. Use a Shampoo and Conditioner that's Appropriate for Your Hair Type

Using the right shampoo and conditioner for your hair type can help keep your hair healthy and strong. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo and a lightweight conditioner.

2. Avoid Using Hot Tools Too Often

Using hot tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, too often can damage your hair. Try to air dry your hair whenever possible, and use a heat protectant when using hot tools.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Now that you know how to style men's medium hair messy, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to embrace your natural texture, use the right products, and keep it simple and effortless. With these tips, you'll have a messy hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
