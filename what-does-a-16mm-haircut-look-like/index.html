What Does A 16Mm Haircut Look Like A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning to get a 16mm haircut but don't know what it would look like? We've got you covered! In this article, we'll explain everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to get it, what it looks like, and how to take care of it. Read on for our step-by-step guide.

Description

A 16mm haircut is a relatively short haircut that involves cutting the hair to a length of 16 millimeters (or 0.63 inches). It's a versatile haircut that can be worn by both men and women and can be styled in a variety of ways. It's also a popular choice for those who prefer low-maintenance hairstyles.

How to Get a 16mm Haircut

To get a 16mm haircut, you'll need to visit a hair salon or barber shop. You can bring a picture of the hairstyle you want or simply ask the stylist to cut your hair to a length of 16mm. The stylist will use clippers to cut the hair to the desired length and may use scissors to trim the hair on the top and sides.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a 16mm Haircut

1. Decide on the length: Before you go to the salon, decide on the length you want for your haircut. A 16mm length is a good starting point, but you can go shorter or longer depending on your preferences.

2. Wash your hair: Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you go to the salon. This will make it easier for the stylist to cut your hair.

3. Communicate with the stylist: Let the stylist know what you want and ask for their recommendations. They may suggest a different length or style that would suit your face shape and hair type.

4. Sit still: Once the stylist starts cutting your hair, sit still and let them do their job. Don't move your head or talk too much, as this can make it difficult for the stylist to cut your hair evenly.

5. Check the length: Once the haircut is complete, check the length to make sure it's what you wanted. If it's too short or too long, let the stylist know and they can make adjustments.

Tips for Styling a 16mm Haircut

A 16mm haircut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some tips for styling your new haircut:

- Use a small amount of hair product to add texture and hold

- Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume

- Experiment with different parting styles to find what suits you best

- Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape

FAQ

Can a 16mm haircut be styled differently?

Yes, a 16mm haircut can be styled in a variety of ways. You can add texture and volume using hair products or create a slicked-back look by using a comb and gel. Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best.

Is a 16mm haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a 16mm haircut can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Consult with your stylist to find a style that complements your features.

How do I maintain a 16mm haircut?

To maintain a 16mm haircut, trim your hair every 4-6 weeks and use hair products to add texture and hold. Wash your hair regularly and avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips

While a 16mm haircut is a low-maintenance hairstyle, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type

- Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair

- Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth

- Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf

Conclusion

A 16mm haircut is a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. Whether you're looking for a new haircut or simply want to change up your style, a 16mm haircut is a great option to consider. Follow our guide to get the perfect 16mm haircut, and use our tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
