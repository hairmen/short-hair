What Is The Best Purple Shampoo To Buy In 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're blonde, you've probably heard of purple shampoo. But what is it, and why do you need it?

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Purple shampoo is a toning shampoo that is designed to neutralize brassiness and yellow tones in blonde hair. It contains purple pigments that counteract the warm tones in blonde hair, leaving it looking brighter and cooler. If you have blonde hair, purple shampoo is an essential part of your hair care routine.

How to Use

To use purple shampoo, wet your hair and apply a generous amount of shampoo to your scalp. Massage the shampoo into your hair, focusing on areas where you have the most brassiness. Leave the shampoo on your hair for 3-5 minutes, then rinse it out. For best results, use purple shampoo once a week or as needed.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to using purple shampoo:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly
  2. Apply a generous amount of purple shampoo to your scalp
  3. Massage the shampoo into your hair, focusing on areas where you have the most brassiness
  4. Leave the shampoo on your hair for 3-5 minutes
  5. Rinse the shampoo out of your hair thoroughly

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your purple shampoo:

  • Don't leave the shampoo on your hair for too long, as it can leave a purple tint
  • Use a hydrating conditioner after using purple shampoo, as it can be drying
  • Try different brands of purple shampoo to find the one that works best for your hair
  • Use purple shampoo once a week or as needed, depending on how quickly your hair brasses

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about purple shampoo:

Q: Can I use purple shampoo on brown hair?

A: Purple shampoo is designed for blonde hair. If you have brown hair, you can use a blue shampoo to neutralize brassiness.

Q: Can I use purple shampoo every day?

A: No, you should only use purple shampoo once a week or as needed. Using it too often can leave a purple tint on your hair.

Q: Will purple shampoo make my hair lighter?

A: No, purple shampoo will not lighten your hair. It is designed to neutralize brassiness and yellow tones.

Hair Care

If you have blonde hair, using purple shampoo is an essential part of your hair care routine. It will help you maintain a bright, cool-toned blonde color, and prevent brassiness and yellow tones. Try different brands of purple shampoo to find the one that works best for your hair, and use it once a week or as needed. Don't forget to follow up with a hydrating conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny!
