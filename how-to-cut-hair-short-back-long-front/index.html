How To Cut Hair Short Back Long Front A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy, edgy haircut that's easy to maintain, the short back, long front hairstyle might be perfect for you. This style features short hair at the back of the head and gradually longer hair toward the front. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair short back long front, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Hair Short Back Long Front
  • Tips for Cutting Hair Short Back Long Front
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Back Long Front Haircut

How to Cut Hair Short Back Long Front

Before you start cutting, you'll need the right tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clippers with various guards, depending on how short you want the hair at the back. You may also want to invest in hair-cutting shears and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 1: Section off the Hair

Start by sectioning off the hair with a comb. Use hair clips to hold the sections in place. For the back, use hair clippers with a guard to trim the hair to your desired length. Start with a higher guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 2: Blend the Sides

Next, blend the sides with the back. Use the clippers without a guard to create a fade from the back to the sides. Use the comb to keep the hair even, and blend upward toward the top of the head.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Now, it's time to work on the top of the head. Use scissors or shears to cut the hair to your desired length. Start with longer sections and gradually work your way shorter toward the front of the head.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut the top, blend the layers with the sides and back. Use scissors or shears to create a natural-looking blend. Be sure to check the hair from all angles to ensure it's even.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Finally, style the hair as desired. You can use a hairdryer and a round brush to add volume, or use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look. Finish with hairspray or other styling products to keep the hair in place.

Tips for Cutting Hair Short Back Long Front

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair short back long front:

  • Start with longer hair and gradually work your way shorter toward the front.
  • Blend the layers for a natural look.
  • Use hair clippers with guards to achieve the desired length at the back.
  • Be patient and take your time; rushing can lead to mistakes.
  • Watch tutorial videos or get guidance from a professional stylist if you're not confident in cutting your hair.

FAQs

Q: Is the short back long front haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can be adapted for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for a short back long front style?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I style my short back long front hair in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in various ways, including adding volume, creating waves, or straightening it.

Hair Care Tips for Short Back Long Front Haircut

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and our step-by-step guide on how to cut hair short back long front, you can achieve a stylish and edgy haircut that's easy to maintain. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find the look that works best for you.
