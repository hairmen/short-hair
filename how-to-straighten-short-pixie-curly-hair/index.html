How to Straighten Short Pixie Curly Hair

Straightening short pixie curly hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start straightening your hair, it is important to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair. This will help to soften and detangle your hair, making it easier to straighten.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage caused by styling tools.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to straighten your hair and create a smooth finish. Be sure to use a nozzle attachment on your blow dryer to help direct the air flow.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Using a flat iron, straighten your hair in sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Be sure to use a comb to help guide your hair through the flat iron. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair have been straightened.

Step 5: Apply a Finishing Product

Apply a finishing product to your hair to help keep it straight and smooth. You can use a hair serum or a hair oil to help add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when straightening short pixie curly hair:

  • Use a flat iron with ceramic plates, as they distribute heat evenly and reduce the risk of heat damage.
  • Section your hair before straightening to ensure that all areas are covered.
  • Be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.
  • Avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a finishing product to help keep your hair straight and smooth.

FAQ

Can I straighten my hair if it is damaged?

If your hair is damaged, it is best to avoid using heat styling tools. Instead, focus on repairing your hair and avoiding further damage.

How often should I straighten my hair?

Avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage. Instead, aim to straighten your hair once or twice a week.

What should I do if my hair is still curly after straightening?

If your hair is still curly after straightening, it may be because you did not use enough heat. Be sure to use a flat iron with high heat setting to help straighten your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a straight and smooth look for your short pixie curly hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
