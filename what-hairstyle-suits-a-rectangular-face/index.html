What Hairstyle Suits A Rectangular Face

Written by Eveline Jul 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling to find a hairstyle that complements your rectangular face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. We will also provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve some of the most popular hairstyles for rectangular faces.

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a strong jawline. This face shape is also known as an oblong face shape. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a rectangular face is to add width to the face and soften the strong jawline.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for a Rectangular Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a rectangular face, it is important to consider the following factors:

  • The length of your hair
  • The texture of your hair
  • Your lifestyle
  • Your personal style

It is also important to consider the features of your face that you want to accentuate or downplay. For example, if you want to downplay your strong jawline, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide: Hairstyles for Rectangular Faces

The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for rectangular faces. Here's how to achieve the perfect bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Divide your hair into sections
  3. Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair
  4. Cut your hair to the desired length
  5. Add layers to the bottom of your hair for volume

The bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn straight, curly, or wavy.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short hairstyle that works well for rectangular faces. Here's how to achieve the perfect pixie cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Divide your hair into sections
  3. Use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length
  4. Add layers to the bottom of your hair for volume
  5. Use a texturizing spray to add texture and volume to your hair

The pixie cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for busy lifestyles.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and damage

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a rectangular face?

If you have a rectangular face, you should avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight. These hairstyles can make your face appear even longer. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can accentuate your strong jawline.

What accessories can I use to enhance my hairstyle?

You can use accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves to enhance your hairstyle. These accessories can add texture and volume to your hair and draw attention away from your strong jawline.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful hairstyle. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent damage
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy hairstyle that complements your rectangular face shape.
