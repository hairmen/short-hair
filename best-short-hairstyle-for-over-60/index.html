Best Short Hairstyle For Over 60 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle, you've come to the right place! As we age, our hair changes in texture and thickness, making it important to find a style that suits our face shape and hair type. Short hairstyles are a popular choice for over 60s as they are easy to maintain and can make you look younger. In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyle for over 60, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it.

Description

The best short hairstyle for over 60 is one that flatters your face shape, is easy to maintain, and suits your hair texture. Short hairstyles are a great option for women over 60 as they can make you look younger and enhance your natural beauty. There are many different short hairstyles to choose from, including pixies, bobs, and shags. The key is to find a style that suits your personality and lifestyle.

How to Achieve the Best Short Hairstyle for Over 60

The first step in achieving the best short hairstyle for over 60 is to find a good hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience working with mature hair and can recommend styles that will suit your face shape and hair type. Once you have found a hairstylist, discuss your options and ask for their advice. They may suggest a pixie cut, a bob, or a shag depending on your hair texture and face shape.

When choosing a style, consider your lifestyle and the amount of time you are willing to spend styling your hair. Short hairstyles can be low maintenance, but some styles require more upkeep than others. For example, a pixie cut may require more frequent trims to maintain its shape, while a bob can be left to grow out a bit before needing to be cut again.

Step by Step Guide

1. Find a good hairstylist who has experience working with mature hair.

2. Discuss your options and ask for their advice on which style would suit your face shape and hair type.

3. Consider your lifestyle and the amount of time you are willing to spend styling your hair.

4. Choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

5. Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and styling.

Tips for Maintaining the Best Short Hairstyle for Over 60

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair.

3. Use a round brush to style your hair and add volume.

4. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

5. Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for over 60?

The best short hairstyle for over 60 is one that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Some popular options include pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

How do I find a good hairstylist?

Ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or look for reviews online. Look for someone who has experience working with mature hair and can recommend styles that will suit your face shape and hair type.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

This depends on the style you choose and how fast your hair grows. A pixie cut may require more frequent trims to maintain its shape, while a bob can be left to grow out a bit before needing to be cut again. Your hairstylist can recommend a trim schedule based on your hair type and style.

Hair Care for Over 60s

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more fragile. To keep your hair healthy and hydrated, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for mature hair. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, which can damage your hair. Instead, use a round brush to add volume and style your hair. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and reduce the appearance of gray hair. Finally, use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

In conclusion, the best short hairstyle for over 60 is one that flatters your face shape, is easy to maintain, and suits your hair texture. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can look and feel your best at any age.
