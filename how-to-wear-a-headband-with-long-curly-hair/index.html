How To Wear A Headband With Long Curly Hair

Written by Frank Oct 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Headbands are a cute and easy way to add some pizzazz to your long curly hair. However, it can be tricky to find the right way to wear them without messing up your curls. In this article, we'll go over the best ways to wear a headband with long curly hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear a Headband with Long Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing Headbands with Long Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Wear a Headband with Long Curly Hair

The first thing to consider when wearing a headband with long curly hair is the type of headband you choose. There are a variety of headbands available, from thin and plastic to thick and fabric. Thin, plastic headbands can be too tight and cause headaches, while thick fabric headbands can be too bulky and ruin your curls.

When choosing a headband, opt for a soft, stretchy fabric that won't pull on your curls. A wide headband that covers your ears is a great option, as it will keep your curls out of your face and won't mess up your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a headband with long curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose a soft, stretchy headband that won't pull on your curls.
  3. Place the headband over the top of your head, just behind your hairline.
  4. Pull the headband down to cover your ears.
  5. Tuck the hair behind your ears into the headband.
  6. Fluff up your curls around the headband to create a natural, tousled look.
  7. Optional: add some hairspray or hair oil to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

Tips for Wearing Headbands with Long Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for wearing headbands with long curly hair:

  • Choose a headband that matches your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Experiment with different headband styles, such as a boho-inspired wrap or a braided headband.
  • Don't pull the headband too tight, as this can cause headaches and ruin your curls.
  • Try wearing a headband with a half-up, half-down hairstyle for a chic look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing headbands with long curly hair:

What kind of headbands are best for long curly hair?

Soft, stretchy headbands made of fabric are best for long curly hair. Avoid thin, plastic headbands that can pull on your curls.

How do I keep my curls from getting flattened by a headband?

Choose a headband that is wide enough to cover your ears and won't press down on your curls. Fluff up your curls around the headband to create a natural, tousled look.

Can I wear a headband with a high ponytail?

A wide headband can be worn with a high ponytail for a chic, sporty look. Make sure to position the headband just behind your hairline and pull it down to cover your ears.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

In order to keep your long curly hair healthy and frizz-free, it's important to follow a few hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your curls.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear a headband with confidence and show off your beautiful long curly hair.
