How To Cut The Sides Of A Man s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying for haircuts every few weeks? Learning how to cut your own hair with clippers can save you time and money. In this guide, we will focus on how to cut the sides of a man's hair with clippers.

How to

Before you begin, gather all the necessary tools. You will need clippers, guards, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 1: Attach a guard to your clippers. The guard determines the length of the hair that will be cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if necessary.

Step 2: Comb the hair on the sides of the head downwards. This will help you see where to cut.

Step 3: Starting at the bottom of the hairline, use the clippers to cut upwards. Move the clippers in an upward motion, following the natural curve of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair as you go.

Step 4: Continue cutting upwards, using the same technique. Be sure to overlap each pass slightly to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Once you have finished one side, move to the other side and repeat the process.

Tips

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head

- Take your time and go slowly

- If you're not sure about the length, start with a longer guard

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Can I cut my own hair without clippers?

Yes, you can use scissors to cut your hair. However, clippers are easier to use and give a more even cut.

How do I clean my clippers?

After each use, remove the guard and brush the hair out of the clippers. You can also use a small amount of clipper oil to keep the blades lubricated.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after cutting it. Use shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. You may also want to use a styling product to keep your hair in place.

Cutting your own hair with clippers can be intimidating at first, but with practice, you can become a pro. Follow these steps and you'll have a fresh haircut in no time!
