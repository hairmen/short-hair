What Is A Pixie Style Hair Cut

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting a new haircut that’s stylish, low maintenance, and easy to manage? Consider a pixie style hair cut! This trendy haircut has been popular for decades and has evolved into various styles over the years. In this article, we’ll discuss what is a pixie style hair cut, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

A pixie cut is a short haircut that’s typically cropped close to the scalp on the sides and back and slightly longer on top. It’s a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes, hair types, and personal styles. The classic pixie cut is short and edgy, with choppy layers and textured ends, but there are also variations such as the long pixie, undercut pixie, and curly pixie.

How to

If you’re interested in getting a pixie cut, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Research

Before you head to the salon, do some research on different pixie styles and find inspiration photos that you like. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a style.

Step 2: Consultation

Book a consultation with a hairstylist who’s experienced in pixie cuts. Discuss your desired style, maintenance, and any concerns you have. The hairstylist will also assess your hair condition and suggest any pre-cut treatments.

Step 3: Cut

Once you’ve agreed on the style, the hairstylist will start cutting your hair. Depending on the style, they may use scissors, clippers, or razors to create the desired shape and texture. The cut may take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity.

Step 4: Style

After the cut, the hairstylist will blow-dry and style your hair using a styling product. They may also show you how to style it at home and recommend products to maintain the cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you have a pixie cut:

- Keep up with regular trims to maintain the shape and prevent split ends. - Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type and texture. - Style your hair with a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. - Experiment with different styling products such as pomade, wax, or gel to achieve different looks. - Consider accessorizing your hair with headbands, clips, or scarves to add some flair to your cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

Q: Is a pixie cut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, but the style may need to be customized to suit the individual’s face shape. For example, a longer pixie may be better for a round face, while a shorter pixie may work for an oval face.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have a pixie cut?

A: Yes, there are various ways to style a pixie cut, such as slicking it back, adding volume, or creating waves. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works for you.

Q: Will a pixie cut grow out easily?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long the cut is. Generally, a pixie cut can take 3-6 months to grow out into a longer style.

Hair Care

To maintain a healthy and vibrant pixie cut, here are some hair care tips:

- Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using hot tools too frequently as they can damage your hair. - Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and pollutants by wearing a hat or scarf. - Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood circulation and healthy hair growth. - Consider getting regular hair treatments such as deep conditioning or protein treatments to nourish your hair.

Conclusion

A pixie style hair cut is a chic and low-maintenance haircut that suits various face shapes and hair types. By following the steps above, you can get a stylish and customized pixie cut that’s easy to manage and maintain. Remember to take good care of your hair and experiment with different styling products and techniques to achieve different looks. If you’re ready for a bold and transformative haircut, a pixie cut may be the perfect choice for you!
