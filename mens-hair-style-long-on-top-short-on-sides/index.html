Mens Hair Style Long on Top Short on Sides - A Complete Guide

If you're looking for a hairstyle that will give you a trendy and stylish look, then the mens hair style long on top short on sides is the perfect option for you. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and can be easily customized according to your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Description

The mens hair style long on top short on sides is a versatile hairstyle that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides. The length of the hair on the top can vary from a few inches to several inches, while the sides are usually buzzed or faded.

How to Achieve the Mens Hair Style Long on Top Short on Sides

If you're interested in getting the mens hair style long on top short on sides, then follow these simple steps:

  1. Choose the length of the hair on top according to your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist to get the perfect haircut.
  3. Use hair products like pomade, wax, or gel to style your hair on the top.
  4. Use clippers or a trimmer to keep the hair on the sides short and neat.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style the Mens Hair Style Long on Top Short on Sides

Follow these step-by-step instructions to style the mens hair style long on top short on sides:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product like pomade or wax to the hair on the top and comb it in the desired direction.
  3. Use clippers or a trimmer to trim the hair on the sides to the desired length.
  4. Blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top using scissors or clippers.
  5. Style the hair on the top using your fingers or a comb to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Hair Style Long on Top Short on Sides

To maintain the mens hair style long on top short on sides, follow these simple tips:

  • Visit a professional hairstylist regularly to maintain the length of the hair on the top and the sides.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools like flat irons or curling irons as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens hair style long on top short on sides:

  1. What face shape is best suited for the mens hair style long on top short on sides?

    2. The mens hair style long on top short on sides is suitable for most face shapes, but it works best for men with oval, square, or heart-shaped faces.

  2. What hair texture is best suited for the mens hair style long on top short on sides?

    3. The mens hair style long on top short on sides works best for men with thick, wavy, or curly hair. However, it can also be styled for men with straight hair.

  3. What styling products should I use for the mens hair style long on top short on sides?

    4. You can use hair products like pomade, wax, or gel to style the hair on the top and keep it in place. However, avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care for Mens Hair Style Long on Top Short on Sides

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the mens hair style long on top short on sides. Follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist regularly to maintain the length of your hair and keep it in good condition.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect mens hair style long on top short on sides and maintain it with ease. So, go ahead and try this trendy hairstyle for a stylish and modern look!
