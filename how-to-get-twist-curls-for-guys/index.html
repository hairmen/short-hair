How To Get Twist Curls For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 24, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle and try something new, twist curls are a great option. Not only are they stylish, but they're also fairly easy to achieve. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of getting twist curls for guys. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain your curls and answer frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Twist Curls for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Twist Curls

How to Get Twist Curls for Guys

Twist curls are a type of hairstyle where the hair is twisted into small curls. This style works best on hair that is at least 3 inches long. Here are the steps to get twist curls:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. A curl-enhancing cream or gel will work best. Be sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, about 1 inch thick. Use clips to keep the sections separated.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly from the roots to the ends. Repeat this process with all the sections of hair.

Step 5: Dry Your Hair

Once all your hair is twisted, let it air dry or use a diffuser on your hair dryer. Avoid touching or disturbing the twists while they're drying.

Step 6: Undo the Twists

Once your hair is completely dry, gently undo the twists. You can use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Now that you have twist curls, you want to make sure they stay looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before bed to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can cause frizz.
  • Refresh your curls by spritzing them with a water and conditioner mixture.

FAQ

How long do twist curls last?

Twist curls can last up to a week or more, depending on how well you maintain them.

Can I get twist curls if I have short hair?

Twist curls work best on hair that is at least 3 inches long. If your hair is shorter, you may need to wait until it grows out more.

Do twist curls work on all hair types?

Twist curls can work on most hair types, including curly, wavy, and straight hair. However, the results may vary depending on your hair's natural texture.

Hair Care for Twist Curls

When you have twist curls, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can cause damage to your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and steps, you should be able to achieve twist curls for guys. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
