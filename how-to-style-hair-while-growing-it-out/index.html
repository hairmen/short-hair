How To Style Hair While Growing It Out

Written by Luffy Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you growing out your hair? While you wait for those luscious locks to grow, you might be wondering how to style your hair in the meantime. No need to worry, we’ve got you covered! Check out our tips and tricks for styling hair while growing it out.

Description

Growing out your hair can be a long and tedious process, but it doesn’t have to be boring! There are plenty of ways to style your hair while you wait for it to grow. Whether you’re dealing with awkward lengths or just want to switch up your look, we’ve got the tools and techniques you need to keep your hair looking great.

How To

1. Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair grow out faster. Trimming off split ends and damaged hair can prevent breakage and make your hair look healthier overall. Aim for a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

2. Experiment with Accessories

Accessories are a great way to add some flair to your hair while it’s growing out. Try adding a headband, scarf, or clip to your hair to switch up your look. These accessories can also help keep your hair out of your face while you’re growing it out.

3. Embrace the Messy Look

If your hair is in that awkward “in between” phase, embrace the messy look! Messy buns, braids, and updos can all be stylish and chic. Don’t worry about getting every strand of hair in place – a messy look can be just as fashionable.

4. Use Heat Protectant

If you’re using heat tools to style your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first. This will help prevent damage to your hair and keep it healthy while you’re growing it out. Be sure to also use a lower heat setting on your tools to avoid further damage.

Step By Step

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you style your hair, be sure to start with clean, dry hair. This will help your style last longer and look its best. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Add Volume

If you’re looking to add some volume to your hair, try using a volumizing mousse or spray. Apply the product to your roots and blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

3. Experiment with Braids

Braids are a great way to add some texture and interest to your hair while it’s growing out. Try a simple braid or a more complicated style, like a fishtail or Dutch braid.

4. Use a Curling Wand

If you’re looking to add some curls to your hair, try using a curling wand. This tool can give you loose, beachy waves or tight, defined curls, depending on the size of the wand you choose.

Tips

- Be patient – growing out your hair takes time!

- Take good care of your hair by using gentle products and getting regular trims.

- Experiment with different styles and accessories to keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair while it’s growing out?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair while it’s growing out. Just be sure to use a gentle, ammonia-free dye and avoid overlapping the color on already-dyed hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair while it’s growing out?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every two to three days while it’s growing out. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

While you’re growing out your hair, it’s important to take good care of it. Use gentle products, get regular trims, and avoid using heat tools too often. With a little patience and some styling know-how, you can keep your hair looking great while it grows.
