Mens Haircuts For Thick Hair 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It can be voluminous and luscious, but it can also be stubborn and difficult to style. But, fear not! We’ve got you covered with the best mens haircuts for thick hair in 2021.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Thick Hair?

The first step in choosing the best haircut for thick hair is to consider your face shape. Certain haircuts tend to suit certain face shapes more than others. For instance, a round face usually looks good with a hairstyle that adds some height to the top of the head.

Another important consideration is your hair texture. Thick hair can be straight, wavy or curly, and each texture requires different haircuts to look its best.

Step by Step Guide to Mens Haircuts for Thick Hair 2021

1. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a low-maintenance haircut that looks great on thick-haired men. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving some length on top. The hairstylist then uses scissors to create choppy layers to add texture and volume.

2. French Crop

The French crop is another popular haircut for men with thick hair. It is a short, textured cut that features a fringe on the front. The sides and back are trimmed short, while the top is left longer to create a textured look.

3. Spiky Haircut

The spiky haircut is perfect for men who want a more edgy look. This haircut involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving some length on top. The hair on top is then styled into spikes using a hair gel or wax.

4. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic haircut that never goes out of style. It is a short haircut that involves cutting the hair to the same length all around the head. This is a great option for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that looks good on thick hair.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Haircuts for Thick Hair

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can damage it.

3. Use a comb or brush to style your hair gently.

4. Regularly trim your hair to keep it looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Q. What is the best haircut for thick hair?

A. The best haircut for thick hair depends on your face shape and hair texture. Some popular haircuts for thick hair include the textured crop, French crop, spiky haircut, and buzz cut.

Q. Can thick hair be thinned out?

A. Yes, thick hair can be thinned out using thinning shears or razor cuts. However, it is important to have this done by a professional hairstylist to ensure that your hair is not damaged.

Hair Care for Mens Haircuts for Thick Hair

To keep your thick hair looking its best, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding too much heat, and regularly trimming your hair.

Overall, there are many mens haircuts for thick hair in 2021 to choose from. By considering your face shape and hair texture, you can find the perfect haircut that suits your style and personality. Follow our tips for maintaining your haircut, and you’ll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
