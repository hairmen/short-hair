What Are Blunt Cut Layers

Written by Luffy Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that is both chic and edgy, blunt cut layers might be the perfect choice for you. These layers are all about creating a sharp, defined look that emphasizes your bone structure and face shape. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about blunt cut layers, including what they are, how to get them, and how to take care of them.

Description

Blunt cut layers are a type of haircut that involves cutting the hair in a straight line without any tapering or feathering. This creates a sharp, angular look that can be very flattering for those with strong jawlines, high cheekbones, or square faces. The layers themselves are cut at different lengths, with the shortest layers starting at the top of the head and the longest layers at the bottom.

One of the great things about blunt cut layers is that they can be customized to suit any hair type, texture, or length. Whether you have fine, straight hair or thick, curly locks, a skilled hairstylist can create a blunt cut layer look that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

How to Get Blunt Cut Layers

If you're interested in getting a blunt cut layer haircut, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with this type of cut. You can do this by asking for recommendations from friends or family members, or by doing some research online to find hairstylists in your area who specialize in this style.

Once you've found a hairstylist you feel comfortable with, the next step is to discuss your hair goals and preferences with them. They will likely ask you questions about your hair type, texture, and length, as well as your face shape and personal style. Based on this information, they will create a customized blunt cut layer look that is tailored to your needs.

During the actual haircut, your hairstylist will use sharp scissors to cut your hair in a straight line, creating the blunt cut look. They will then add layers at different lengths to create dimension and movement. The entire process typically takes around 45 minutes to an hour, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting blunt cut layers:

  1. Find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with blunt cut layers.
  2. Discuss your hair goals and preferences with them.
  3. Sit in the chair and let your hairstylist cut your hair in a straight line.
  4. Add layers at different lengths to create dimension and movement.
  5. Enjoy your new, edgy look!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your blunt cut layers:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and smoothness.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and definition.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking sharp and defined.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about blunt cut layers:

Q: Will blunt cut layers work with my hair type?

A: Yes, blunt cut layers can be customized to suit any hair type, texture, or length.

Q: Will blunt cut layers make my face look too angular?

A: Not necessarily. Blunt cut layers can actually soften your features and create a more youthful, edgy look.

Q: How long will it take for my hair to grow out if I decide I don't like my blunt cut layers?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but typically it takes around 3 to 4 months for hair to grow out to a significantly longer length.

Hair Care

To keep your blunt cut layers looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes using a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to keep your layers looking sharp.

You may also want to consider using a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair, as well as a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools. And don't forget to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside.

With the right care and maintenance, your blunt cut layers can look great for months to come!
