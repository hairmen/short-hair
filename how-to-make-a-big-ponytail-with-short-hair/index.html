How To Make A Big Ponytail With Short Hair

Written by Petter Nov 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having the same old boring hairstyle with your short hair? Do you want to learn how to make a big ponytail with short hair? Look no further! In this article, we will show you step by step how to create a big ponytail with short hair, including tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A big ponytail with short hair is a great way to add volume and style to your hair. It is a quick and easy way to change up your look and can be done in just a few simple steps. Whether you have a special event or just want to switch up your everyday hairstyle, a big ponytail with short hair is a great option.

How To

To make a big ponytail with short hair, you will need some hair elastics, bobby pins, and a brush. Follow these simple steps:

Step by Step

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair at the crown of your head and tease it with a comb or brush to add volume.
  3. Gather all of your hair, including the teased section, into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  4. Secure the ponytail with a hair elastic.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair elastic to hide it. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin.
  6. Tease the ponytail with a comb or brush to add more volume.
  7. Take a small section of hair from the underside of the ponytail and wrap it around the base to hide the hair elastic. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin.
  8. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  9. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse before blow-drying your hair to add more volume.
  • Tease your hair in small sections to create more volume and avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Experiment with different ponytail heights to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I make a big ponytail with short hair if I have layers?

A: Yes, you can still make a big ponytail with short hair even if you have layers. Just make sure to tease the top section of your hair to create more volume and use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Q: How can I make my ponytail look more polished?

A: To make your ponytail look more polished, use a comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways and finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and using the steps above, you can easily create a big ponytail with short hair that looks stylish and polished. So go ahead and try it out for yourself!
