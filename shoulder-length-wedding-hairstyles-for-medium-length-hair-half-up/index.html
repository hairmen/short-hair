Shoulder Length Wedding Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Half Up

Written by Luffy Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with shoulder length hair looking for the perfect half up hairstyle for your wedding day? Look no further! We've compiled a list of gorgeous shoulder length wedding hairstyles for medium length hair half up that will make you look and feel stunning on your special day.

Description

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length to create a half up hairstyle that is both elegant and romantic. Whether you want to add braids, curls, or accessories, there are endless options to choose from.

How to

To achieve a half up hairstyle for medium length hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair to remove any tangles and create a smooth base.
  2. Next, section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie.
  3. You can leave the bottom half of your hair straight, or add curls for added texture and volume.
  4. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure it with a bobby pin.
  5. Repeat step 4 on the other side of your head.
  6. Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and twist it towards the top of your head. Secure it with a bobby pin.
  7. Release the top half of your hair and style it as desired. You can add curls, braids, or accessories to create a unique look.
  8. Finish with hairspray to ensure your hairstyle stays in place throughout the day.

Step by step

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair to remove any tangles and create a smooth base.

Next, section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or hair tie.

You can leave the bottom half of your hair straight, or add curls for added texture and volume. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves.

Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and twist it towards the back of your head. Secure it with a bobby pin.

Step 5: Repeat Step 4 on the Other Side of Your Head

Repeat step 4 on the other side of your head.

Step 6: Twist a Small Section of Hair Towards the Top of Your Head

Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and twist it towards the top of your head. Secure it with a bobby pin.

Step 7: Release the Top Half of Your Hair

Release the top half of your hair and style it as desired. You can add curls, braids, or accessories to create a unique look.

Step 8: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with hairspray to ensure your hairstyle stays in place throughout the day.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Add accessories like flowers or a veil to make your hairstyle even more special.
  • Consider getting a professional hair stylist to create your wedding day look.

FAQ

What kind of accessories can I add to a half up hairstyle?

You can add flowers, a veil, hairpins, or a headband to a half up hairstyle to make it more unique and personalized.

How can I make my half up hairstyle last all day?

Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place and consider using bobby pins or hair ties for added security.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny leading up to your wedding day, make sure to wash and condition it regularly, use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, and consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these shoulder length wedding hairstyles for medium length hair half up, you'll be sure to turn heads on your special day. Whether you opt for loose curls or add accessories like flowers or a veil, these hairstyles are the perfect balance of elegance and romance.
