Best Hairstyle For Square Face Girl

If you have a square-shaped face, you may struggle to find hairstyles that suit you. But don't worry! In this article, we'll explore the best hairstyle for square face girl that will flatter your face shape and make you look stunning.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square chin. Your goal is to soften these angles and create a more oval shape. The best hairstyle for square face girl is one that adds volume and softness to the face. You want to draw attention away from the jawline and towards the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for square face girl is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker. Then, step back and look at the shape you've drawn. If it's a square, you have a square face shape.

Next, consider the length of your hair. Long hair can add weight to the face and create a more oval shape. If you prefer shorter hair, go for a layered cut that adds volume to the top of the head.

Best Hairstyles for Square Face Girl

Here are some of the best hairstyles for square face girl:

  • Long Layers: Long layers can help soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look. Add some waves to your hair for extra volume.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones and make your face appear longer. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look shorter.
  • Bob: A bob with layers can add volume to the crown of your head and create a more oval shape. Keep the length above the chin to avoid accentuating the jawline.
  • Updo: An updo can create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid slicked-back styles and opt for a loose, messy bun or braid.

Step by Step

Here's how to achieve the long layered look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair in the middle.
  3. Section your hair into layers, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Use a curling iron to add waves to each layer.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on the sides, as they can make your face appear wider.

FAQ

  1. Can I wear my hair straight with a square face shape? Yes, but avoid styles that are too sleek or straight. Soft waves or curls can add volume and soften the angles of your face.
  2. What is the best haircut for square face girl with curly hair? A layered haircut with long, loose curls can add volume and create a more oval shape.
  3. How can I make my face look slimmer with a square face shape? A side part can help elongate your face and draw attention away from the jawline.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the best hairstyle for square face girl, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
