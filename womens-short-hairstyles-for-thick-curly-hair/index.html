Women s Short Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair How To Style And Care For Your Locks

Written by Daniel Oct 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect short hairstyle. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right style that will flatter your face shape and complement your natural curls. In this article, we will share with you some of the best women's short hairstyles for thick curly hair, along with tips on how to care for and style your locks.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Short hairstyles are an excellent choice for women with thick curly hair. Not only do they require less maintenance, but they also add more volume and texture to your locks. However, when choosing a short hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. For example, a pixie cut may not be suitable for someone with a round face, while a bob may not work for someone with very curly hair.

How to

Before you decide on a short hairstyle for your thick curly hair, it's essential to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a style that will flatter your face shape and work with your hair type. Some popular short hairstyles for thick curly hair include:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for women with thick curly hair. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that's easy to style. The pixie cut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. You can style it by applying a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair and then diffusing it with a blow dryer.

Bob

A bob is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. The classic bob is a chin-length haircut that's perfect for women with thick curly hair. You can style it by blow-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment and then adding some texture with a curling iron or wand.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is an excellent choice for women with thick curly hair who want to add more volume and texture to their locks. This style features layers of different lengths, which can create a more natural-looking curl pattern. You can style it by applying a curl-defining product to your damp hair and then diffusing it with a blow dryer.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when styling your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing or defining product to your damp hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment, using a low heat setting.
  4. If necessary, use a curling iron or wand to define your curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to hold your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling and caring for your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a weekly deep conditioning treatment to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Invest in a quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer to achieve the perfect curl pattern.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

A pixie cut works well for women with oval, heart-shaped, or square faces. It may not be suitable for those with a round face, as it can make the face appear wider.

What type of bob is best for thick curly hair?

A chin-length bob with layers is an excellent choice for women with thick curly hair. The layers can help to create a more natural-looking curl pattern, while the length is still short enough to be low-maintenance.

Can I still straighten my hair if I have a short curly hairstyle?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have a short curly hairstyle, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and not to straighten your hair too often to prevent damage.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair healthy and beautiful, it's essential to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, applying a weekly deep conditioning treatment, and using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep. Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, and invest in a quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer to achieve the perfect curl pattern.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a gorgeous short hairstyle that will complement your thick curly hair and flatter your face shape. Remember to consult with a hairstylist before making any drastic changes to your hair, and always take good care of your locks to keep them healthy and beautiful.
