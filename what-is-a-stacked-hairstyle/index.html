What Is A Stacked Hairstyle

Written by Robby Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and modern haircut, then a stacked hairstyle might be just what you need. It's a style that's been around for a while, but it's still popular today. A stacked hairstyle is a layered haircut that's designed to create a graduated effect with the hair getting shorter towards the back. This style is also called a stacked bob, stacked haircut, or stacked layers.

Description

A stacked hairstyle is a trendy haircut that features longer hair in the front and shorter hair in the back. This hairstyle is created by cutting the hair at an angle, so it gradually gets shorter towards the back of the head. The layers are stacked on top of each other, hence the name "stacked" hairstyle. This cut is perfect for people who want to add volume and dimension to their hair while keeping it stylish and modern.

How to

If you want to get a stacked hairstyle, you should go to a professional hairstylist. They will be able to give you the perfect cut that suits your face shape and hair type. Here's what you can expect during the haircut:

Step 1: Consultation

The hairstylist will discuss with you the look you're going for and what type of stacked hairstyle will suit you best. They will also advise you on how to maintain the cut and give you some styling tips.

Step 2: Shampoo and Conditioner

The hairstylist will start by shampooing and conditioning your hair to make sure it's clean and ready for the cut.

Step 3: Sectioning

The hairstylist will section your hair and cut it at an angle, creating the stacked layers. They will use scissors to cut the hair and create the graduated effect.

Step 4: Styling

The hairstylist will blow-dry and style your hair to finish the look. They might use a straightener or curling iron to create waves or curls.

Tips

If you want to maintain your stacked hairstyle, here are some tips you should follow:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume.
  • Use a round brush to create waves or curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape.

FAQ

1. Who can get a stacked hairstyle?

Anyone can get a stacked hairstyle, but it's best suited for people with medium or thick hair. If you have thin hair, you might want to avoid this style as it can make your hair look even thinner.

2. How long does a stacked hairstyle last?

A stacked hairstyle can last up to 6-8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. You will need to get regular trims to maintain the shape.

3. Can I style my stacked hairstyle differently?

Yes, you can style your stacked hairstyle in different ways. You can straighten it, curl it, or create waves. You can also accessorize it with hair clips or headbands.

Hair Care

To keep your stacked hairstyle looking its best, you should take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Overall, a stacked hairstyle is a trendy and modern haircut that can add volume and dimension to your hair. If you're thinking of getting this style, make sure to go to a professional hairstylist and follow the tips to maintain the shape and style of your hair.
