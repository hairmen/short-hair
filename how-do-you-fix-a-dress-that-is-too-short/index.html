How To Fix A Dress That Is Too Short

Written by Robby Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having to throw out your favorite dresses because they are too short? Fear not, for there is a way to fix this problem without having to buy a new dress. In this article, we will go through step by step on how to fix a dress that is too short. We will also provide tips on how to prevent this from happening in the future.

Description

There are several reasons why a dress may be too short. It could be due to shrinkage, poor quality fabric, or even a mistake made during the manufacturing process. Whatever the reason, it can be frustrating to have a dress that you love but cannot wear because it is too short.

How to Fix a Dress That is Too Short

Fixing a dress that is too short is not as difficult as you may think. Here are the steps you can take to fix it:

Step 1: Determine the Length You Need

The first step is to determine how much length you need to add to the dress. Put the dress on and measure how much longer you want it to be.

Step 2: Choose the Fabric

Next, choose a fabric that matches the dress. This can be a challenge, especially if the dress has a unique color or pattern. You can take a swatch of the dress with you to the fabric store to help you find a matching fabric.

Step 3: Cut the Fabric

Cut the fabric to the length you need. Make sure to add an extra inch or two to allow for hemming.

Step 4: Pin the Fabric to the Dress

Pin the fabric to the bottom of the dress. Make sure to line up the seams and patterns, if applicable.

Step 5: Sew the Fabric to the Dress

Using a sewing machine or needle and thread, sew the fabric to the bottom of the dress. Make sure to use a strong, durable stitch so that it will not come apart easily.

Step 6: Hem the Bottom of the Dress

Finally, hem the bottom of the dress. Fold the fabric under and sew it using a blind hem stitch. This will create a clean, professional-looking hem.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you prevent your dresses from becoming too short:

  • Always read the care label before washing your dresses. Some fabrics may shrink in the wash.
  • Hang your dresses to dry instead of putting them in the dryer. This can help prevent shrinkage.
  • Invest in high-quality dresses. They may be more expensive, but they will last longer and are less likely to shrink or become too short.

FAQ

Can I use a different fabric to add length to my dress?

Yes, you can use a different fabric to add length to your dress. However, it is important to choose a fabric that matches the dress as closely as possible.

Do I need to use a sewing machine to fix a dress that is too short?

No, you can use a needle and thread to fix a dress that is too short. However, using a sewing machine will make the process quicker and easier.

Can I still wear my dress if it is too short?

It is ultimately up to you whether or not you want to wear a dress that is too short. However, keep in mind that it may not be appropriate for certain occasions or dress codes.

Hair Care

While fixing a dress that is too short is important, it is also important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
