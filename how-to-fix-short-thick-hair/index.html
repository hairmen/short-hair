How To Fix Short Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Nov 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short thick hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. While it can look voluminous and stylish, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Fortunately, there are many ways to fix short thick hair and make it look fabulous. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to fix short thick hair.

Description

Thick hair can be difficult to style, especially if it's short. Short thick hair tends to have a lot of volume and can be challenging to manage. However, with the right techniques and products, you can fix short thick hair and make it look amazing.

How to Fix Short Thick Hair

If you're struggling with short thick hair, here are some tips and tricks to help you fix it:

1. Get the Right Haircut

The first step in fixing short thick hair is getting the right haircut. A layered haircut can help reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable. You can also opt for a choppy bob or a pixie cut, which can look great on short thick hair.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in fixing short thick hair. Look for products that are designed for thick hair, such as volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. These products can help reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help reduce the volume and make it easier to style. Use a round brush and blow-dry your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will help smooth out the hair and reduce the volume.

4. Use a Straightener

A straightener can also help fix short thick hair. Use a high-quality straightener and straighten your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will help reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable.

5. Add Some Texture

Adding some texture to your hair can also help fix short thick hair. Use a texturizing spray or a dry shampoo to add some texture and make your hair more manageable. You can also use a curling iron to add some curls or waves to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fix short thick hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a round brush to smooth out your hair and reduce the volume.

Step 3: Straighten your hair

Straighten your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a high-quality straightener to reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable.

Step 4: Add some texture

Add some texture to your hair using a texturizing spray or a dry shampoo. You can also use a curling iron to add some curls or waves to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you fix short thick hair:

1. Use a hair serum

A hair serum can help reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable. Apply a small amount of hair serum to your hair before blow-drying or straightening.

2. Use a heat protectant

Using a heat protectant can help protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools. Apply a heat protectant to your hair before blow-drying or straightening.

3. Avoid over-washing your hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days to keep it healthy and hydrated.

4. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair mask to fix short thick hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair mask to help reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable. Look for a hair mask that is designed for thick hair.

Q: Can I use a hair oil to fix short thick hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair oil to help reduce the volume and make your hair more manageable. Look for a hair oil that is designed for thick hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for fixing short thick hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals or products that can damage your hair.

2. Protect your hair from heat

Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or straightener. This will help protect your hair from damage and keep it healthy.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein-rich foods.

4. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix short thick hair and make it look fabulous. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and hydrated.
