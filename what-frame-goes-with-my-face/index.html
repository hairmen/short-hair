What Frame Goes With My Face

Written by Petter Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new pair of glasses, you might be wondering what frame goes with your face. The right frame can enhance your facial features, while the wrong one can make you look out of proportion. In this article, we'll explore how to choose the perfect frame for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Frame
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Frame
  • FAQs
  • How to Care for Your Glasses

Description

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a frame. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. Each shape has its own unique features, and the right frame can enhance those features.

For example, if you have a round face, you'll want to choose a frame that's slightly wider than your face to create balance. If you have an oval face, you can wear almost any frame shape, but you'll want to avoid frames that are too big or too small.

How to Choose the Right Frame

There are several factors to consider when choosing the right frame:

Face Shape

As we mentioned earlier, your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider. Here's a breakdown of the different face shapes and the frames that work best:

  • Round Face: Square or rectangular frames
  • Oval Face: Almost any frame shape
  • Square Face: Round or oval frames
  • Heart-Shaped Face: Cat-eye or round frames
  • Diamond-Shaped Face: Oval or rimless frames

Skin Tone

Your skin tone can also influence the frame color you choose. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to choose frames in warm colors like gold, brown, or tortoiseshell. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose frames in cool colors like silver, black, or blue.

Hair Color

Your hair color can also influence the frame color you choose. If you have blonde hair, you'll want to choose frames in light colors like beige or pastel. If you have dark hair, you'll want to choose frames in bold colors like black or red.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right frame:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your skin tone.
  3. Consider your hair color.
  4. Try on different frames to see what looks best.
  5. Choose a frame that's comfortable and fits well.

Tips for Choosing the Right Frame

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right frame:

  • Choose a frame that's proportional to your face.
  • Don't be afraid to try on different frame shapes and colors.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a frame. If you're active, you'll want a frame that's durable and comfortable.
  • Choose a frame that complements your personal style.

FAQs

What if I have a small face?

If you have a small face, you'll want to choose a frame that's smaller in size to avoid overwhelming your features.

What if I have a wide face?

If you have a wide face, you'll want to choose a frame that's slightly wider than your face to create balance.

What if I wear makeup?

If you wear makeup, you'll want to choose a frame that doesn't interfere with your makeup application.

How to Care for Your Glasses

Once you've chosen the perfect frame, it's important to take care of your glasses to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your glasses:

  • Clean your glasses daily with a microfiber cloth.
  • Avoid using paper towels or tissues to clean your glasses, as they can scratch the lenses.
  • Store your glasses in a case when you're not wearing them to protect them from scratches and damage.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your glasses look great and last for years to come.
