How To Style Guys Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair is no longer just for women. Many men have embraced the trend and are sporting long locks. However, styling long hair can be a challenge, especially if you're new to it. In this guide, we'll show you how to style guys long hair in easy steps, from choosing the right haircut to finding the perfect products.

Description

Long hair has been making a comeback in recent years, with many men opting for longer styles. However, styling long hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're used to shorter haircuts. This guide will help you master the art of styling long hair, so you can rock your locks with confidence.

How to Style Guys Long Hair: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in styling long hair is choosing the right haircut. You want a cut that works with your hair type and face shape. Some popular long haircuts for men include the undercut, the man bun, and the layered cut. Talk to your hairstylist to find the best cut for you.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

It's important to keep your long hair clean and healthy. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair while it's still wet.

Step 3: Apply Product

Once your hair is clean and dry, it's time to apply product. Choose a product that works with your hair type and the style you're going for. Some popular products for long hair include hair wax, pomade, and sea salt spray. Apply the product to your hair evenly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to create the look you want. If you're going for a messy, textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair. If you want a more polished look, use a comb to create a sleek style.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your hair stay in place all day long. Just be sure not to overdo it, as too much hairspray can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Styling Guys Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your long hair like a pro:

  • Invest in a good haircut. A good haircut is the foundation of any great hairstyle.
  • Use the right products. Choose products that work with your hair type and the style you're going for.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle.
  • Experiment with different styles. Long hair is versatile, so don't be afraid to try new looks.
  • Take care of your hair. Long hair requires extra care, so be sure to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

FAQ

Can Guys with Thin Hair Have Long Hair?

Yes, guys with thin hair can have long hair. However, it's important to choose the right haircut and products to create the illusion of thicker hair. A layered cut can help add volume, while a volumizing spray can give your hair a fuller look.

How Often Should I Wash My Long Hair?

It's best to wash your long hair every two to three days, as washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils. However, if you have a particularly oily scalp, you may need to wash it more frequently.

How Do I Prevent Split Ends?

To prevent split ends, it's important to keep your hair moisturized. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid using hot styling tools, which can damage your hair. You can also trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Hair Care for Guys with Long Hair

Long hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to keep your long hair in top condition:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair while it's still wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, which can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your long hair looking great and feeling healthy.
