Pixie Hair For Over 60 A Perfect Hairstyle For The Modern Senior

Written by Christine Oct 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you over 60 and wondering what hairstyle would suit you best? Look no further than the pixie cut! The pixie cut is a short, stylish haircut that is perfect for seniors who want a low-maintenance but trendy look. In this article, we will guide you on how to get the perfect pixie hair for over 60, some hair care tips, and answer some frequently asked questions about the hairstyle.

How to Get Pixie Hair for Over 60

If you're considering a pixie cut, there are a few things to keep in mind before you sit down in the stylist's chair:

Find the Right Stylist

The first step to getting the perfect pixie cut is finding the right stylist. Look for a stylist who has experience cutting short hair and can help you find a style that flatters your face shape and features. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members or search for a stylist online.

Choose the Right Pixie Cut

There are many different types of pixie cuts, so it's essential to find the one that suits you best. Some popular styles include the classic pixie, textured pixie, and long pixie. Your stylist can help you choose the right style for your hair type and face shape.

Consider Your Hair Color

Your hair color can make a big difference in how your pixie cut looks. If you have gray hair, you may want to consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair. Talk to your stylist about the best hair color options for your skin tone and hair type.

Step by Step Guide to Pixie Hair for Over 60

Now that you've decided on getting a pixie cut let's go over some step-by-step instructions on how to get the perfect pixie hair for over 60:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to prepare it for the cut. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips. Start by sectioning your hair from ear to ear and then from the crown of your head to the nape of your neck.

Step 3: Cut the Back of Your Hair

Start cutting the back of your hair first. Use a comb to hold the hair in place and cut it to the desired length. Remember, you can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back, so be conservative with your cuts.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Top of Your Hair

Once you've cut the back of your hair, move on to the sides and top. Use your fingers to hold the hair in place and cut it to the desired length. Remember to cut the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it using pomade or hair wax. Use your fingers to create texture and volume, and make sure your hair looks even and well-shaped.

Tips for Maintaining Pixie Hair for Over 60

Here are some tips to keep your pixie hair looking its best:

Regular Trims

Make sure to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your pixie cut looking fresh and clean.

Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair, like pomade or hair wax, to style your hair and keep it in place.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.

Frequently Asked Questions About Pixie Hair for Over 60

Can I get a pixie cut if I have thinning hair?

Yes, you can still get a pixie cut if you have thinning hair. Talk to your stylist about the best style for your hair type, and they can help you find a cut that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Will a pixie cut make me look younger?

A pixie cut can make you look younger, as it is a trendy and modern hairstyle. However, the most important factor in looking youthful is feeling confident and comfortable in your own skin.

Can I color my hair if I have a pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have a pixie cut. Talk to your stylist about the best hair color options for your skin tone and hair type.

Conclusion

Pixie hair for over 60 is a perfect hairstyle for seniors who want a low-maintenance but trendy look. Use our step-by-step guide to get the perfect pixie cut, and follow our tips to keep your hair looking its best. Remember, the most important factor in looking youthful is feeling confident and comfortable in your own skin.
