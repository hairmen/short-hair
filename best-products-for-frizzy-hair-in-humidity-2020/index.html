How to Deal with Frizzy Hair in Humidity

Before we dive into the best products for frizzy hair in humidity, it's important to understand why your hair gets frizzy in the first place. When the humidity levels rise, moisture in the air can penetrate your hair shaft and cause it to swell. This can make your hair look frizzy, fluffy, and out of control.

So, what can you do to deal with frizzy hair in humidity? Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help hydrate your hair and prevent it from absorbing excess moisture from the air.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can further dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry after washing, rather than rubbing it vigorously with a towel.
  • Apply a frizz-fighting product to your hair before styling to help seal in moisture and prevent it from absorbing excess humidity.

Tips for Using Frizz-Fighting Products

Now that you know some of the best products for frizzy hair in humidity 2020, it's important to know how to use them effectively. Here are a few tips:

  • Start with a small amount of product, and build up as needed. Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Apply frizz-fighting products to wet hair, rather than dry hair. This will help to distribute the product evenly and ensure that it's absorbed into your hair shaft.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair, ensuring that every strand is coated.
  • Don't be afraid to touch up your hair throughout the day with a small amount of product. This can help to keep your hair looking smooth and sleek even in humid weather.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner for frizzy hair in humidity?

A: While you can certainly use a regular shampoo and conditioner, it's a good idea to choose products that are specifically formulated for frizzy hair. These products are designed to hydrate and nourish your hair, while also helping to prevent frizz in humid weather.

Q: How often should I use frizz-fighting products?

A: This will depend on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. As a general rule, you can use frizz-fighting products every time you wash your hair, or as needed throughout the day to touch up your style.

Q: Can I use multiple frizz-fighting products at once?

A: Yes, you can certainly layer multiple frizz-fighting products to achieve the desired level of control and hydration for your hair. Just be sure to start with a small amount of product and build up as needed.

Hair Care Tips for Frizzy Hair

In addition to using the best products for frizzy hair in humidity 2020, there are a few other things you can do to keep your hair looking smooth and sleek:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and make your hair more prone to frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials are gentler on your hair and can help to prevent frizz.
  • Consider getting regular trims to help prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy and damaged.

With these tips and the best products for frizzy hair in humidity 2020, you'll be able to keep your hair looking smooth and sleek all summer long. Say goodbye to frizz, and hello to beautiful, healthy hair!
