Natural Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a chic and effortless hairstyle to rock for any occasion, then natural updo hairstyles for medium length hair are perfect for you. These hairstyles not only look great but are also easy to create and maintain. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about natural updo hairstyles for medium length hair, including how to create them, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Natural updo hairstyles for medium length hair are hairstyles that embrace your natural hair texture and create a chic updo. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. They are easy to create and maintain, making them a popular choice among women with medium length hair. Natural updo hairstyles for medium length hair can also be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How to

Creating a natural updo hairstyle for medium length hair is easy. All you need is a few hairpins, a comb, and some hair styling products. The first step is to decide on the type of updo you want to create. There are many different styles to choose from, including a messy bun, a braided updo, and a twisted updo.

Step by step

Here are the basic steps to create a natural updo hairstyle for medium length hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Decide on the type of updo you want to create.
  4. Section your hair into two parts, one on top and one at the bottom.
  5. Tie the bottom section of your hair into a low ponytail.
  6. Create a bun by twisting the ponytail around the base and securing it with hairpins.
  7. Take the top section of your hair and twist it loosely.
  8. Wrap the twisted section around the bun and secure it with hairpins.
  9. Finish by spraying your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect natural updo hairstyle for medium length hair:

  • Use hair styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories, such as a headband or hair clip, to your updo.
  • Consider getting a professional to help you create your updo for a special occasion.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about natural updo hairstyles for medium length hair:

Q: Are natural updo hairstyles suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, natural updo hairstyles can be customized to suit any hair type, from straight to curly.

Q: How long does it take to create a natural updo hairstyle?

A: It depends on the style and your hair type, but most natural updo hairstyles can be created in under 30 minutes.

Q: Can I create a natural updo hairstyle without using hair styling products?

A: Yes, you can create a natural updo hairstyle without using hair styling products, but they can help to add texture and hold to your hair.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is key to creating a beautiful natural updo hairstyle for medium length hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, natural updo hairstyles for medium length hair are a great choice for any occasion. With a few hairpins and some hair styling products, you can create a chic and effortless hairstyle that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and well-maintained, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find the one that suits you best.
