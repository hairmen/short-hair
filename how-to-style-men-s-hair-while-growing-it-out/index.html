How To Style Men s Hair While Growing It Out

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're growing out your hair, it can be a challenging time to style it. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to style men's hair while growing it out. From how to choose the right products to how to deal with awkward lengths, we'll help you navigate this tricky time.

Description

Whether you're growing out a shorter cut or trying to get longer locks, it can be tough to style your hair in the in-between stages. You might be dealing with awkward lengths, unruly hair, or a lack of volume. But don't give up! With the right techniques and products, you can style your hair while it grows out.

How to

The first step in styling your hair while growing it out is to choose the right products. You'll want to use products that can help add volume, tame frizz, and define your natural texture. Here are some product recommendations:

1. Shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type. If you have curly or wavy hair, look for products that help define your curls. If you have fine hair, look for volumizing products. And if you have dry or damaged hair, look for products that can help repair and hydrate your strands.

2. Styling cream

A styling cream can help add definition and control to your hair without weighing it down. Look for a cream that's lightweight and non-greasy, so it won't make your hair look oily or limp. Apply the cream to damp hair, then style as desired.

3. Sea salt spray

If you're looking to add texture and volume to your hair, a sea salt spray is a great option. Spray it on damp hair, then scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your natural waves or curls. You can also blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to add even more volume.

Step by step

Now that you have the right products, let's go over some step-by-step instructions for styling your hair while growing it out:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet.

2. Apply your styling product

Depending on the look you're going for, apply a styling cream or sea salt spray to your hair. Use your hands to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Style your hair

Use your hands, a brush, or a comb to style your hair as desired. You can create a part, slick your hair back, or add volume to the top of your head. Experiment with different styles to find what works for you.

4. Let your hair air dry or blow-dry it

If you're using a sea salt spray, you can let your hair air dry for a natural, beachy look. If you want more control over your hair, use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair while adding volume.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair while growing it out:

- Be patient. It can take several months or even a year to grow out your hair, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't look the way you want it to right away.

- Get regular trims. Even if you're growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

- Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try out new styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's hair while growing it out:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, you may need to wash it more often to prevent oil buildup. If you have curly or coarse hair, you may be able to wash it less often. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Q: How do I deal with awkward lengths?

A: If you're dealing with awkward lengths, you can try using hair accessories like headbands or hats to hide your hair. You can also experiment with different styles, like slicking your hair back or adding volume to the top of your head.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair?

A: Use products like sea salt spray or volumizing mousse to add volume to your hair. You can also blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to create more volume.

Hair Care

While you're growing out your hair, it's important to take good care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.

- Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

- Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your hair while growing it out and achieve the look you want. Remember to be patient, experiment with different styles and products, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
