How To Find Your Perfect Hair Colour In 2023

Written by William Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking for a change? Finding your perfect hair colour can be overwhelming, but with these easy steps and tips, you'll be able to find the perfect shade that complements your skin tone and personality. Below, we've broken down the process into simple steps, including FAQs and hair care tips to keep your new hair colour looking fresh and healthy.

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Choose a Colour Palette

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a big role in determining which hair colour will look best on you. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral undertone.

For cool undertones, opt for shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, and cool black. For warm undertones, go for shades like golden blonde, chestnut brown, and warm black. Neutral undertones can choose from a variety of shades.

Step 2: Choose a Colour Palette

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start thinking about which colour palette you want to choose from. There are three main categories: blonde, brunette, and red. Blonde shades include platinum, ash, and golden. Brunette shades include chocolate, chestnut, and espresso. Red shades include copper, auburn, and burgundy.

Think about your personality and which colours you feel would complement it best. If you're a natural blonde, you may want to stick with a blonde palette. If you're feeling daring, try a red shade for a bold change.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a new hair colour, it's important to consider your lifestyle. Do you want a low-maintenance shade that won't require frequent touch-ups? Or are you willing to spend more time and money on upkeep?

If you want a low-maintenance colour, opt for shades that are close to your natural hair colour. This way, your roots won't be as noticeable as the colour grows out. If you're willing to put in the time and effort, try a bolder shade that may require more maintenance.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's always best to consult with a professional stylist. They can offer advice on which shades will work best with your skin tone and suggest styles that will complement your face shape.

A professional will also be able to properly apply the colour and ensure that it's done evenly. This will help prevent any damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour

Once you've found your perfect hair colour, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips to keep your new colour looking its best:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid hot tools and excessive heat styling
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and salt water
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

FAQs

How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on the shade you choose and how quickly your hair grows. Some shades, like blonde, may require touch-ups every 4-6 weeks. Others, like red, may be able to go longer without touch-ups. Consult with your stylist for their recommendation.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional stylist to ensure that the colour is applied evenly and doesn't cause damage to your hair.

Will dyeing my hair damage it?

Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, but using proper hair care techniques and products can minimize this. It's important to avoid excessive heat styling and use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Coloured hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and salt water
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to find your perfect hair colour and keep it looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
