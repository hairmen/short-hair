What Is A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to our blog article about what is a heart shaped face. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this face shape, including its description, how to identify it, step by step guide on how to flatter it, tips, FAQ, and hair care. So, let's dive in!

Description

A heart-shaped face is a face shape where the forehead is wider than the cheekbones, and the jawline is narrow and pointed. The chin is usually the smallest part of the face, and it looks like a heart. This face shape is often considered the most feminine and romantic among all face shapes.

How to Identify It

To identify if you have a heart-shaped face, you need to look at the following features:

  • Forehead: The forehead is usually the widest part of the face.
  • Cheekbones: The cheekbones are narrow compared to the forehead.
  • Jawline: The jawline is pointed and narrow.
  • Chin: The chin is usually small and looks like a heart.

If you have all these features, then you have a heart-shaped face.

Step by Step Guide on How to Flatter It

If you have a heart-shaped face, you can flatter it by doing the following:

  • Hairstyle: Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as a bob or a long layered cut.
  • Makeup: Use makeup to balance the face by emphasizing the eyes and downplaying the cheekbones.
  • Earrings: Wear earrings that are wider at the bottom and narrower at the top to balance the face's shape.
  • Neckline: Wear V-neck or scoop neck tops to elongate the neck and balance the face's shape.

Tips

Here are some tips for heart-shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown area.
  • Avoid wearing hair accessories near the forehead.
  • Avoid wearing earrings that are wider at the top and narrower at the bottom.
  • Avoid wearing chokers or high necklines that will shorten the neck.

FAQ

Q: Can a heart-shaped face pull off short hairstyles?

A: Yes, a heart-shaped face can pull off short hairstyles, but you need to be careful with the volume at the crown area. Opt for a tapered bob or a pixie cut that adds volume to the chin area.

Q: Can a heart-shaped face wear bangs?

A: Yes, a heart-shaped face can wear bangs, but you need to be careful with the length and the volume. Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that add volume to the chin area.

Q: Can a heart-shaped face wear statement earrings?

A: Yes, a heart-shaped face can wear statement earrings, but you need to be careful with the width. Opt for earrings that are wider at the bottom and narrower at the top to balance the face's shape.

Hair Care

If you have a heart-shaped face, you need to take care of your hair to maintain its volume and shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use volumizing shampoos and conditioners to add volume to the chin area.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the crown area.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that will weigh down your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.

In conclusion, a heart-shaped face is a beautiful and feminine face shape that can be flattered with the right hairstyle, makeup, and accessories. Follow our tips and tricks to make the most out of your heart-shaped face. We hope this article has been helpful to you!
