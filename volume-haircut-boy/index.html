Volume Haircut Boy How To Achieve The Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy haircut for your boy, the volume haircut is a great option. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. The volume haircut gives boys a stylish and modern look that is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll go over how to achieve the perfect volume haircut for your boy, step by step. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain this look and answer some frequently asked questions about this style. Let's get started!

What is a Volume Haircut Boy?

A volume haircut is a style that involves cutting the hair to create volume and texture. This style is achieved by cutting the hair in layers, which creates movement and depth. The volume haircut is a great option for boys who want a trendy and fashionable look. This style is versatile and can be adapted to suit any hair type or length.

How to Achieve the Perfect Volume Haircut Boy

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to achieving the perfect volume haircut is to choose the right style. There are many different types of volume haircuts, so it's important to choose one that suits your boy's hair type and face shape. Some popular options include the layered haircut, the shaggy haircut, and the textured crop.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting the hair, it's important to wash and dry it. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated. Once the hair is clean, dry it with a towel or a hair dryer. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 3: Start Cutting the Hair

Once the hair is dry, it's time to start cutting. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut the hair in layers. Start at the back and work your way towards the front. Use your fingers to separate the hair and create movement. Cut the hair to the desired length, keeping in mind the style you've chosen.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Once the hair is cut, it's time to style it. Use a styling product, such as a wax or a gel, to create texture and volume. Apply the product to the hair, focusing on the roots. Use your fingers to style the hair, creating movement and depth. Once you're happy with the style, let the hair dry naturally.

Tips for Maintaining the Volume Haircut Boy

Now that you've achieved the perfect volume haircut for your boy, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh down the hair
  • Brush the hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots

FAQ about Volume Haircut Boy

What is the best hair type for a volume haircut?

The volume haircut can work for any hair type, but it's best suited for thick or wavy hair. This hair type can easily be styled to create volume and texture.

Can the volume haircut be adapted for shorter hair?

Yes, the volume haircut can be adapted for shorter hair. The key is to create layers that add movement and depth to the hair.

What styling product should I use for a volume haircut?

You can use a variety of styling products for a volume haircut, including wax, gel, and mousse. Choose a product that works best for your boy's hair type and style.

Hair Care for Volume Haircut Boy

To keep your boy's hair healthy and looking great, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning the hair regularly, using a styling product that doesn't damage the hair, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. With the right care and maintenance, your boy's volume haircut will look great for months to come.

In conclusion, the volume haircut is a great option for boys who want a stylish and modern look. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can achieve the perfect volume haircut for your boy. Remember to maintain this look with regular trims and a good hair care routine. With a little effort, your boy will have a trendy and fashionable hairstyle that he'll love.
