How To Have Korean Boy Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and trendy hairstyle, consider going for the Korean boy hair look. Korean hairstyles have become increasingly popular in recent years, thanks to K-pop idols and Korean dramas. With the right styling techniques and products, you can achieve this look as well. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to have Korean boy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Have Korean Boy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Korean Boy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Have Korean Boy Hair

Korean boy hair is all about the right cut and styling techniques. The key to achieving this look is to keep your hair short on the back and sides, while leaving the top longer. This creates a neat and clean look that's easy to style. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Find a good barber or hairstylist who has experience with Korean hairstyles.
  • Bring reference pictures or show your stylist what you're looking for.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a style.
  • Invest in quality hair products that will help you achieve the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to have Korean boy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair healthy and clean.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a hair styling product, such as a pomade or wax. This will help hold the style in place.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while using a brush to style it in the desired direction. The top should be styled upwards and the sides should be brushed down and back.
  4. Once your hair is dry, apply a finishing product, such as a hairspray, to keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Korean Boy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your Korean boy hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and clean.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh between washes.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to have Korean boy hair:

What kind of hair products should I use?

It's best to use products that are specifically designed for men's hair, such as pomades, waxes, and hairsprays. Look for products that are lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

Can I achieve this look with any hair type?

Yes, you can achieve Korean boy hair with any hair type. However, some styles may work better with certain hair textures than others. Talk to your stylist about what style would work best for your hair type.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. However, this may vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and neat.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect Korean boy hair look. Remember to find a good stylist, choose the right style for your hair type, and invest in quality hair products. With a little bit of effort and care, you can have a stylish and trendy hairstyle that's sure to turn heads.
