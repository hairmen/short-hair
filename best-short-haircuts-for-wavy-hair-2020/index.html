Best Short Haircuts For Wavy Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly wavy hair every morning? Or are you simply looking for a fresh and new hairstyle for your wavy locks? Look no further as we have compiled the best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2020. From bobs to pixies, we’ve got you covered. Read on to find out more!

Description

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. While it’s beautiful to look at, it can also be challenging to manage, especially when it comes to styling. Fortunately, there are many trendy and stylish short haircuts that can help you tame your wavy locks and look your best every day. These haircuts not only accentuate your natural waves but also give you a chic and modern look.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Wavy Hair?

Choosing the right haircut for your wavy hair can be a daunting task, but it’s not impossible. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short haircut for your wavy hair.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining your face in the mirror. The four basic face shapes are round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Each shape has its own set of guidelines when it comes to choosing a haircut that flatters your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The second step is to consider your hair texture. Wavy hair can range from fine to coarse, and each texture has its own unique set of challenges. If you have fine wavy hair, you may want to opt for a layered bob that adds volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick wavy hair, a pixie cut with textured layers can help you manage your hair and make it look more polished.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

The third step is to think about your lifestyle. Are you a busy mom who doesn’t have time for daily styling? Or are you a career woman who needs a professional yet stylish haircut? Knowing your lifestyle can help you choose a short haircut that fits your needs and preferences.

Top 5 Best Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair 2020

Now that you’ve learned how to choose the right short haircut for your wavy hair, let’s take a look at the top 5 best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2020.

1. Layered Bob

The layered bob is a classic haircut that’s perfect for wavy hair. It adds volume and movement to your hair and frames your face beautifully. This haircut is easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways. You can wear it straight, tousled, or with loose waves.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring haircut that’s perfect for women who want to make a statement. It’s also a great haircut for wavy hair as it adds texture and definition to your locks. You can style it with a messy, tousled look or a sleek and polished finish.

3. Shaggy Haircut

The shaggy haircut is a layered haircut that’s perfect for wavy hair. It’s a low-maintenance haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. This haircut is perfect for women who want a carefree and effortless look.

4. Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a modern and edgy haircut that’s perfect for wavy hair. It’s a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways. You can wear it sleek and straight or with loose waves for a more relaxed look.

5. Textured Pixie Cut

The textured pixie cut is a short and sassy haircut that’s perfect for wavy hair. It’s a low-maintenance haircut that adds texture and definition to your locks. This haircut is perfect for women who want a bold and daring look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Wavy Hair

Now that you’ve chosen your short haircut for wavy hair, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut for wavy hair.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron to style your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your locks.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to tame frizz and add shine to your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I get a short haircut if my wavy hair is thin?

Yes, you can get a short haircut even if you have thin wavy hair. A layered bob or a textured pixie cut can add volume and movement to your hair.

2. Can I style my short haircut for wavy hair with a hair straightener?

Yes, you can style your short haircut for wavy hair with a hair straightener. However, it’s important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your locks.

3. How often should I get a trim for my short haircut for wavy hair?

You should get a trim for your short haircut for wavy hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair.

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.

With the right short haircut and proper hair care, you can rock your wavy locks and look your best every day. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
