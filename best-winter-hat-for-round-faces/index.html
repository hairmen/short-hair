Best Winter Hat For Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect winter hat can be a challenge. You want something that will keep you warm and stylish, but also flatter your face shape. Luckily, we've done the research for you and compiled a list of the best winter hats for round faces. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the perfect hat, provide step-by-step instructions for finding the right fit, and offer tips on how to care for your hat. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Perfect Winter Hat for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Right Fit
  • Tips for Styling and Caring for Your Winter Hat
  • Winter Hat for Round Faces: FAQs
  • Winter Hat and Hair Care Tips

How to Choose the Perfect Winter Hat for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing the perfect winter hat for round faces, there are a few things to consider:

Fabric

First, consider the fabric of the hat. You'll want something that's warm and cozy, but not too bulky. Look for hats made from wool, cashmere, or a wool-blend. These fabrics will keep you warm without adding too much volume to your head.

Shape

The shape of the hat is also important. Round faces look best with hats that have some structure and height. Avoid hats that are too flat or slouchy, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for hats with a structured crown or a slight slouch.

Details

Finally, consider the details of the hat. Look for hats with embellishments or details on the side, as this can help elongate your face. Avoid hats with details on the top, as this can make your head look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Right Fit

Now that you know what to look for in a winter hat for round faces, here's a step-by-step guide to finding the right fit:

Step 1: Measure Your Head

Use a tape measure to measure the circumference of your head. This will give you an idea of what size hat to look for.

Step 2: Try on Different Styles

Try on different styles of hats to see what looks best on you. Look for hats with a structured crown and height, and avoid hats that are too flat or slouchy.

Step 3: Check the Fit

Make sure the hat fits snugly but not too tight. It should cover your ears and sit comfortably on your head. If the hat is too loose, it can make your face look wider.

Tips for Styling and Caring for Your Winter Hat

Now that you've found the perfect winter hat for your round face, here are some tips for styling and caring for it:

Styling Tips

  • Pair your winter hat with a scarf and gloves for a complete winter look.
  • Wear your hat slightly back on your head to elongate your face.
  • Avoid wearing your hat too far down on your forehead, as this can make your face look wider.

Caring for Your Hat

  • Always check the care instructions before washing your hat.
  • Hand-wash your hat in cold water and lay flat to dry.
  • Avoid hanging your hat, as this can cause it to lose its shape.

Winter Hat for Round Faces: FAQs

What style of hat looks best on a round face?

Hats with a structured crown and height look best on round faces.

What fabric should I look for in a winter hat for round faces?

Look for hats made from wool, cashmere, or a wool-blend. These fabrics will keep you warm without adding too much volume to your head.

How should I style my winter hat?

Pair your winter hat with a scarf and gloves for a complete winter look. Wear your hat slightly back on your head to elongate your face. Avoid wearing your hat too far down on your forehead, as this can make your face look wider.

Winter Hat and Hair Care Tips

Wearing a winter hat can cause static and flatten your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great while wearing a winter hat:

  • Spray a leave-in conditioner on your hair before putting on your hat.
  • Use a volumizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair after taking off your hat.
  • Avoid wearing your hat too tight, as this can cause your hair to flatten.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect winter hat for your round face and keep your hair looking great all winter long.
