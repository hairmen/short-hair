Short Straight Hairstyles For Round Fat Faces

Written by Oliver Oct 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles but nothing seems to work for your round fat face? Well, worry no more because we have got you covered. Short straight hairstyles can work wonders for round fat faces, making them look slimmer and more defined. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve the perfect short straight hairstyle for your round fat face.

Description

Round fat faces have a softer and less defined jawline. Therefore, it is important to choose a hairstyle that can create some angles and definition to your face. Short straight hairstyles are perfect for this as they create a sleek look that elongates your face and draws attention away from your cheeks.

How to

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect short straight hairstyle for your round fat face.

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

The first step in achieving a perfect short straight hairstyle is to choose the right cut. You need to choose a style that suits your face shape and enhances your features. For round fat faces, a bob or lob that hits just below the chin is perfect. This cut creates angles and frames your face beautifully.

Step 2: Straighten Your Hair

Next, straighten your hair using a flat iron. Make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent any damage to your hair. Straightening your hair will create a sleek look that elongates your face.

Step 3: Add Some Layers

Add some layers to your hair to create movement and texture. Layers will also add some volume to your hair, making it look fuller and more defined.

Step 4: Use a Texturizing Spray

Finish off your look by using a texturizing spray. This will add some hold to your hair and create a tousled, effortless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short straight hairstyles for round fat faces:

  • Choose a cut that suits your face shape and enhances your features.
  • Straighten your hair to create a sleek look that elongates your face.
  • Add some layers to create movement and texture.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add hold and create a tousled look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have bangs with a short straight hairstyle for a round fat face?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs with a short straight hairstyle. However, make sure to choose a style that suits your face shape. Side-swept bangs are perfect for round fat faces as they create angles and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Q: Can I add highlights to my short straight hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can add highlights to your short straight hairstyle. Highlights can add dimension and depth to your hair, making it look fuller and more defined. However, make sure to choose a color that suits your skin tone and enhances your features.

Hair Care

Lastly, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant when styling. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short straight hairstyle for your round fat face. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you the best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Good luck!
