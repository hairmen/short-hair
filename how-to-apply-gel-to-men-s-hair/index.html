How To Apply Gel To Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to styling men's hair, gel is a go-to product for many. But if you're new to using gel, it can be intimidating to know where to start. In this article, we'll walk you through how to apply gel to men's hair for a sleek, polished look. From choosing the right gel to applying it correctly, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Gel
  • Preparation
  • Application
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Applying Gel to Men's Hair

Choosing the Right Gel

Before you start applying gel to your hair, it's important to choose the right product for your needs. There are several types of gel available, each with its own strengths and weaknesses.

Hold Strength

Gel comes in various hold strengths, from light to strong. Light hold gel is best for short hair or for a more natural look, while strong hold gel is ideal for longer hair or for a more structured style.

Shine

Gel can also vary in shine, from high-shine to matte. High-shine gel is perfect for a sleek, polished look, while matte gel creates a more natural, textured look.

Texture

Finally, gel can also vary in texture. Some gels are thick and sticky, while others are more lightweight and watery. The texture you choose will depend on your personal preference and hair type.

Preparation

Before applying gel to your hair, it's important to prepare your hair properly. Here's what you need to do:

Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair until it's slightly damp, but not dripping wet.

Comb Your Hair

Using a wide-tooth comb or brush, gently detangle your hair to remove any knots or tangles. This will make it easier to apply the gel evenly.

Application

Now that your hair is prepared, it's time to apply the gel. Follow these steps:

Start with a Small Amount

Start with a small amount of gel, about the size of a quarter. You can always add more later if you need it.

Distribute Evenly

Using your fingers, distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.

Style as Desired

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair as desired. If you're going for a slicked-back look, comb your hair straight back. If you're going for a more textured look, use your fingers to create volume and movement.

Finishing Touches

Once you've applied the gel and styled your hair, it's time for some finishing touches:

Avoid Touching Your Hair

Try to avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause the gel to break down and your style to fall apart.

Use a Hairspray

If you want to ensure your style stays in place all day, you can use a hairspray to set it. Simply spray a light mist over your hair from a distance of about six inches.

Tips for Applying Gel to Men's Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when applying gel to your hair:

  • Experiment with different types of gel to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Start with a small amount of gel, then add more as needed.
  • Don't apply gel to soaking wet hair, as it won't distribute evenly.
  • Use a comb or brush to distribute the gel evenly and avoid clumps.
  • Apply gel in sections, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward.

FAQs

Can I use gel on all hair types?

Yes, gel can be used on all hair types, but you may need to experiment with different types of gel to find the one that works best for your hair.

How often should I use gel?

You can use gel as often as you like, but it's a good idea to give your hair a break from styling products every few days to avoid build-up.

Can I use gel on wet hair?

No, you should apply gel to damp hair, not soaking wet hair. This will help the gel distribute evenly.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Here are a few additional hair care tips for men:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can cause build-up and make your hair look greasy.

Now that you know how to apply gel to men's hair, you're ready to experiment with different styles and products. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve a sleek, polished look that lasts all day.
