How To Make Hair Look Blonder Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Petter Dec 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your dull and lifeless hair? Do you want to make your hair look blonder without the damage caused by excessive hair dyeing? If so, you are in the right place. In this article, we will provide you with some tips, tricks and FAQs on how to make hair look blonder naturally and safely.

Description

Blond hair is always in trend. But not everyone is born with natural blond hair. However, there are ways to make your hair look blonder without damaging it. In this article, we will provide some tips and tricks on how to make hair look blonder.

How to Make Hair Look Blonder: Step by Step

Step 1: Lemon Juice

One of the easiest ways to make your hair look blonder is by using lemon juice. It is a natural bleaching agent that lightens the hair.

How to use:

  1. Mix lemon juice with water in equal parts.
  2. Apply it on your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes to an hour.
  3. Rinse it off with lukewarm water.
  4. Do this once a week for best results.

Step 2: Chamomile Tea

Chamomile tea is another natural remedy that can lighten your hair. It has a mild bleaching effect that can make your hair look blonder.

How to use:

  1. Brew a cup of chamomile tea and let it cool down.
  2. Apply it on your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes to an hour.
  3. Rinse it off with lukewarm water.
  4. Repeat this once or twice a week for best results.

Step 3: Honey

Honey is a natural hair lightener that can make your hair look blonder. It also moisturizes and nourishes the hair.

How to use:

  1. Mix honey with water in equal parts.
  2. Apply it on your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes to an hour.
  3. Rinse it off with lukewarm water.
  4. Do this once a week for best results.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to make hair look blonder:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as they can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair spray.
  • Use a purple shampoo to neutralize any brassy tones in your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look dull.

FAQs

Q1: Will lemon juice damage my hair?

A1: Lemon juice can be drying to the hair, so it is important to dilute it with water before applying it. Also, do not leave it on for too long as it can cause damage.

Q2: How long will it take to see results?

A2: It depends on your hair color and the method you use. But generally, you should see some results after a few weeks of regular use.

Q3: Can I use these methods on colored hair?

A3: It is not recommended to use these methods on colored hair as they can affect the color.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make hair look blonder, try out these methods and see what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
