Best Styles For 60 Year Olds A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Oliver Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're in your 60s and looking for a new hairstyle, you're in the right place. We've compiled a list of the best styles for 60 year olds that will have you feeling and looking fabulous. From short bobs to long layers, there's a style for everyone. Read on to discover how to achieve the perfect hairstyle for your age.

Description

As we age, our hair changes, and so do our hairstyles. What may have worked for us in our 20s or 30s may not be the best style for us now that we're in our 60s. But don't worry, there are plenty of stylish and age-appropriate hairstyles that will have you feeling confident and beautiful.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to consider your face shape. A good rule of thumb is to balance your face shape with your hairstyle. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, long layers can soften your features.

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. If you have fine hair, a shorter style with layers can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer cut with layers can help manage your hair and prevent it from looking too heavy.

Once you've determined your face shape and hair type, it's time to choose a style that you love. Here are some of the best styles for 60 year olds:

Short Bob

A short bob with layers is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works for any age. It's easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways. You can add texture with a curling iron or keep it sleek with a straightener.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a daring and stylish choice that can make you look years younger. It's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic. Plus, it's a great way to show off your facial features.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, long layers can add movement and dimension to your hair. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or wavy, and it's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can add a youthful and playful touch to any hairstyle. They're perfect for those who want to change up their look without making a drastic change. Plus, they're easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step By Step

To achieve any of these styles, it's important to work with a stylist who understands your hair type and face shape. Here are some general steps to achieve each style:

Short Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture
  3. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold
  4. Style with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold
  3. Style with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture
  3. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold
  4. Style with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture
  3. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold
  4. Sweep your bangs to the side and style as desired

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools
  • Try out different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Q: Can I still color my hair?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of hair color options that are age-appropriate and can enhance your natural beauty.

Q: What's the best way to style my hair for a special occasion?

A: It depends on the occasion and your personal style. A sleek updo or voluminous curls are always a classic choice, but don't be afraid to try something new!

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially as we age. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals

By following these tips and choosing a hairstyle that works for you, you can look and feel fabulous at any age.
