What Does A Heart Shaped Face Mean

Written by Frank Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you one of those who have a heart shaped face? Do you want to know what it means? If yes, then you are in the right place. This article will guide you through what a heart shaped face is and how to style your hair according to your face shape. So, let's dive in!

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow pointed chin. It resembles the shape of a heart, hence the name. This face shape is considered to be one of the most versatile and attractive, as it has a natural feminine look. People with heart shaped faces have a wide range of options when it comes to hairstyles and makeup.

How to Determine if You Have a Heart Shaped Face?

If you are unsure whether you have a heart shaped face or not, there are a few simple ways to find out. You can take a selfie and analyze your facial features, or you can ask a professional hairstylist or makeup artist to help you determine your face shape. Another way is to measure your face with a ruler or a measuring tape. Measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead and cheekbones are wider than your jawline, then you have a heart shaped face.

Step by Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Styling your hair according to your face shape can enhance your natural beauty and make you look more attractive. Here is a step by step guide on how to style your hair for a heart shaped face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, determine your face shape before styling your hair. This will help you choose the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

For heart shaped faces, it is recommended to go for hairstyles that balance out the wide forehead and narrow chin. Some of the most popular hairstyles for heart shaped faces are:

  • Long layers
  • Side swept bangs
  • Bob with side bangs
  • Pixie cut
  • Updo with loose curls

Step 3: Avoid Certain Hairstyles

While there are many hairstyles that suit heart shaped faces, there are a few that you should avoid. These include:

  • Slicked back hair
  • Short bangs
  • High ponytails
  • Tight buns

Step 4: Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. Try out different haircuts, colors, and styles to find the one that suits you the best. You can also seek advice from a hairstylist or a friend.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a heart shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that make your forehead look even wider
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your chin look even pointier
  • Accessorize with earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from your forehead
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too symmetrical

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a heart shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a heart shaped face. However, it is recommended to add some volume to your hair to balance out the wide forehead.

Q: Can I wear a center part with a heart shaped face?

A: A center part can make your forehead look even wider. It is recommended to go for a side part or a diagonal part instead.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Face

It is important to take care of your hair, especially if you have a heart shaped face. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too many hair products
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny

Conclusion

A heart shaped face is a beautiful and versatile face shape. With the right hairstyle and makeup, you can enhance your natural beauty and look stunning. It is important to experiment with different hairstyles and seek advice from a professional hairstylist or makeup artist. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
