Hairstyles To Suit Chubby Faces A Guide For Flattering Looks

Written by Frank Sep 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you feeling self-conscious about your round face? Worry not, because there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you look your best. In this article, we’ve compiled a list of the most flattering hairstyles for people with chubby faces. From short to long hair, we’ll cover them all. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Chubby Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Then, take a close look at the shape of your face.

If your face is widest at the cheeks and has a rounded jawline, you have a round face. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that elongate the face and draw attention away from the width of the cheeks.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Opt for haircuts that add height to the top of the head, as this will elongate the face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this will make the face look wider.
  • Long, layered haircuts are a great option for round faces, as they create movement and texture.
  • Bangs can be flattering on round faces, but they should be long and side-swept, not blunt or straight across.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

Short hairstyles can be a great option for people with round faces, as they can help to balance out the proportions of the face. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Short, choppy layers
  • Mohawk with shaved sides

Medium-Length Hairstyles for Round Faces

Medium-length haircuts are a great option for people with round faces, as they can help to create the illusion of length. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Layered bob
  • Long bob with side-swept bangs
  • Shoulder-length waves
  • Half-up, half-down hairstyle

Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

Long hair can be flattering on people with round faces, as long as it is styled correctly. Here are some of our top picks for long hairstyles:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves
  • Straight hair with a center part
  • Braided updo

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle for your round face, it’s important to know how to style it to get the most flattering look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can people with round faces wear short hair?

A: Yes, people with round faces can wear short hair, but it’s important to choose a style that adds height to the top of the head and avoids adding volume to the sides of the face.

Q: Are bangs flattering on people with round faces?

A: Yes, bangs can be flattering on people with round faces, but they should be long and side-swept, not blunt or straight across.

Q: What is the best way to style long hair for a round face?

A: The best way to style long hair for a round face is to add layers and texture, and to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care for Chubby Faces

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyle suggestions, you’re sure to find a look that flatters your round face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
