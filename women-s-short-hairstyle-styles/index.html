Women s Short Hairstyle Styles A Guide To Chic And Trendy Looks

Written by Eveline Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of long and high-maintenance hair? It's time to switch things up with a short and sassy haircut! Short hairstyles are perfect for women who want to look effortlessly chic and trendy. Whether you prefer a classic bob or an edgy pixie cut, there's a short hairstyle that will suit your face shape and personal style. In this guide, we'll show you some of the most popular women's short hairstyle styles and how to achieve them.

The Bob Cut

The bob is a classic short hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a versatile cut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. The traditional bob is a blunt cut that falls just above the shoulders, but you can also opt for a shorter or longer length. A layered bob is perfect if you want to add some movement and texture to your hair. You can also choose a side-swept or middle parting to change up your look.

To achieve a bob cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to your desired length. If you have straight hair, a blunt cut will work well. If you have curly or wavy hair, a layered cut will help to define your curls and reduce bulk. To style your bob, blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and curl the ends under.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring short hairstyle that's perfect for women who want to make a statement. It's a low-maintenance cut that's easy to style and maintain. The classic pixie cut is short on the sides and back and slightly longer on top. You can also opt for a longer or shorter version depending on your preference.

To achieve a pixie cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave some length on top. You can style your pixie cut by applying some styling wax or pomade to your hair and using your fingers to create a messy, textured look. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek and polished look.

The Lob Cut

The lob cut, or long bob, is a popular short hairstyle that's perfect for women who want a shorter cut without going too short. It's a versatile cut that can be worn straight or wavy and looks great with or without bangs. The lob cut falls just above the shoulders and is longer than a traditional bob.

To achieve a lob cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to your desired length. You can also add some layers to create movement and texture. To style your lob, you can blow-dry your hair straight or create loose waves with a curling iron. You can also add some bangs to change up your look.

The Asymmetrical Cut

The asymmetrical cut is a trendy and edgy short hairstyle that's perfect for women who want a bold and unique look. It's a cut that's longer on one side and shorter on the other, creating an asymmetrical shape. You can also add some layers and texture to create a more dynamic look.

To achieve an asymmetrical cut, ask your hairstylist to cut one side of your hair shorter than the other. You can also add some layers and texture to create movement and volume. To style your asymmetrical cut, you can blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.

The Shag Cut

The shag cut is a messy and textured short hairstyle that's perfect for women who want a laid-back and effortless look. It's a cut that's layered and choppy, creating a messy and disheveled look. The shag cut works well with all hair types and can be customized to suit your face shape.

To achieve a shag cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers and add some texture with a razor or scissors. You can style your shag cut by using a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy and tousled look. You can also add some bangs to frame your face.

The Faux Hawk Cut

The faux hawk cut is a short hairstyle that's inspired by the traditional mohawk but is less extreme. It's a cut that's shorter on the sides and longer on top, creating a spiky and textured look. The faux hawk cut is perfect for women who want an edgy and daring look.

To achieve a faux hawk cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and leave some length on top. You can also add some layers and texture to create a more dynamic look. To style your faux hawk cut, use a styling wax or gel to create spikes on the top of your hair. You can also create a messy and textured look by using your fingers to tousle your hair.

The Undercut

The undercut is a short hairstyle that's characterized by shaved or closely-cropped sides and longer hair on top. It's a bold and daring look that's perfect for women who want to make a statement. The undercut works well with all hair types and can be customized to suit your personal style.

To achieve an undercut, ask your hairstylist to shave or closely crop the sides of your hair and leave some length on top. You can also add some layers and texture to create a more dynamic look. To style your undercut, you can use a styling wax or gel to create a sleek and polished look or a messy and textured look.

The Curly Cut

The curly cut is a short hairstyle that's perfect for women with naturally curly or wavy hair. It's a cut that's layered and textured to enhance your curls and reduce bulk. The curly cut works well with all face shapes and can be customized to suit your personal style.

To achieve a curly cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers and add some texture with a razor or scissors. You can also add some bangs to frame your face. To style your curly cut, use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and reduce frizz. You can also blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to create volume and enhance your curls.

Bangs

Bangs are a great way to change up your short hairstyle and frame your face. You can choose from a variety of bang styles, including blunt, side-swept, and baby bangs. Bangs work well with all short hairstyles and can be customized to suit your face shape.

To achieve bangs, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to your desired length and add some bangs. You can also choose the length and shape of your bangs depending on your personal style. To style your bangs, you can blow-dry them with a round brush to create volume and a smooth finish. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek and polished look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs some care to look its best. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can damage it.
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your short hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

What short hairstyle is best for my face shape?

The best short hairstyle for your face shape depends on your individual features. Generally, a bob or lob cut works well for oval and heart-shaped faces, while a pixie or shag cut works well for square and round faces. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best short hairstyle for your face shape.
