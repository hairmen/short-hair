How To Cut Black Men s Hair Short Back And Sides

Written by Christine Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to give yourself or someone else a fresh new haircut? Look no further than the classic short back and sides style. This cut is universally flattering and easy to maintain. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to achieve a perfect short back and sides cut for black men.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic style that has been around for decades. It is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. This style is versatile and can be worn in many different ways depending on personal preference.

How to Cut Black Men's Hair Short Back and Sides

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a cape or towel to protect clothing.

Step 2: Start with the sides

Begin by using the clippers with a grade 1 guard to trim the sides and back of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, keeping the clippers level and making sure to blend the hair as you go. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any stray hairs with the scissors.

Step 3: Move to the top

Next, move to the top of the head and use the clippers with a grade 2 or 3 guard to trim the hair. Start at the front and work your way to the back, making sure to blend the hair as you go. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any stray hairs with the scissors.

Step 4: Clean up the neckline

Use the clippers without a guard to trim the hair around the neckline, making sure to create a clean and defined line. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any stray hairs with the scissors.

Step 5: Finishing touches

Once you are happy with the length and shape of the haircut, use the clippers to create a fade on the sides and back if desired. Blend the hair carefully to create a seamless transition from the shorter hair to the longer hair on top.

Tips

  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing through the haircut can lead to mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the sides and back of the head from different angles.
  • Experiment with different guard sizes to find the perfect length for your hair.
  • Remember to clean your tools after each use to keep them in good condition.

FAQ

How often should I get a short back and sides haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the style. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with the right tools and techniques. However, it is recommended to have a professional haircut every once in a while to ensure the best results.

Hair Care

After getting a short back and sides haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat or styling products. Regular trims will also help to maintain the style and keep it looking sharp.

Now you know how to cut black men's hair short back and sides. With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a fresh new haircut that is easy to maintain and looks great. Give it a try and see for yourself!
