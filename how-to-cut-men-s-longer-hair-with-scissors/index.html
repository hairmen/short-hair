How To Cut Men s Longer Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting men's hair with scissors can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to longer styles. However, with the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a great cut without having to visit a barber. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting men's longer hair with scissors, along with some tips and FAQs to help you along the way.

Tools Needed

The first step in cutting men's longer hair with scissors is to gather the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • A pair of sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • Hair clips or elastics
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Preparation

Before you begin cutting, it's important to prepare the hair. Start by washing and drying the hair thoroughly. Then, comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Next, decide on the length you want to cut the hair. Keep in mind that it's easier to cut hair shorter than it is to add length back, so start with a longer length and work your way down if necessary.

Sectioning the Hair

Once the hair is prepped, it's time to section it for cutting. Use your comb to create a center part, and then separate the hair into two sections. Clip one section out of the way while you work on the other.

Using your comb, create a horizontal part about an inch above the nape of the neck. Clip the hair above the part out of the way, and begin cutting the hair below the part. Work your way up the head, creating horizontal parts and cutting the hair below each part.

Cutting the Hair

When cutting the hair, it's important to use the proper technique. Hold the hair between your fingers, and use the tip of the scissors to make small, precise snips. Work your way around the head, cutting the hair to the desired length.

Blending the Hair

After the hair is cut to the desired length, it's time to blend the hair. Comb the hair straight up, and use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at an angle. This will create a soft, blended look.

Finishing Touches

Once the hair is blended, it's time for finishing touches. Check for any uneven spots or longer hairs, and trim as necessary. Style the hair as desired, and you're done!

Tips for Cutting Men's Longer Hair with Scissors

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Cut the hair in small sections to ensure precision.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven lengths.
  • Take breaks as needed to avoid hand fatigue.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut men's longer hair with scissors?

A: This depends on the length and style of the hair, as well as personal preference. Generally, a trim every 6-8 weeks is recommended to maintain the shape and health of the hair.

Q: Can I cut men's longer hair with regular scissors?

A: While it's possible to cut hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Hair cutting scissors are designed specifically for the task, and using regular scissors can lead to uneven or damaged hair.

Hair Care

After cutting men's longer hair with scissors, it's important to care for the hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many styling products. Regular trims will also help keep the hair in good condition.
