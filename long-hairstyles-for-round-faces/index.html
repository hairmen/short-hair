Long Hairstyles For Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face shape, finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of long hairstyles that can complement round faces, from layered cuts to face-framing bangs. In this article, we will explore different long hairstyles for round faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

Description

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a width that is roughly equal to the length. The goal of a long hairstyle for a round face is to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. This can be achieved through strategic layering, face-framing bangs, and volume at the crown.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for a Round Face

When choosing a long hairstyle for a round face, look for styles that create height and volume at the crown. Layers can also help to elongate the face and create movement. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as this can accentuate the roundness of the face.

Best Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

  • Long layered cut
  • Face-framing layers
  • Asymmetrical cut
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Loose waves

Worst Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

  • Blunt cut ending at the jawline
  • Center part
  • Super long hair

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Round Faces

Follow these steps to style long hair for round faces:

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown.
  3. Curl hair away from the face using a curling iron or wand.
  4. Use a texturizing spray to add movement and definition to the curls.
  5. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Long Hair

Long hair requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy and looking its best. Follow these tips for maintaining healthy long hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage to the hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair hydrated.

FAQs

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just be sure to add volume at the crown to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape.

How can I make my face look slimmer with long hair?

To make your face look slimmer with long hair, add volume at the crown and avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline. Face-framing layers and side-swept bangs can also help to create a more slimming effect.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy long hair. Follow these tips for keeping your locks in top condition:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, as this is less damaging than a brush.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair, such as tight ponytails and braids.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.

In conclusion, there are many long hairstyles that can flatter round faces. Choose a style that adds height and volume at the crown, and avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline. Follow these tips for maintaining healthy long hair, and you'll be sure to turn heads with your stunning hairstyle.
