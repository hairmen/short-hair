Men s Hair Long On Top And Back A Guide To The Trendy Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle, men's hair long on top and back is the way to go. This style has been popular for several years now, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this guide, we'll break down everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to style it to the best products to use.

Description

Men's hair long on top and back is a hairstyle where the hair is left longer on the top and back of the head while keeping the sides and the nape of the neck short. This style is also known as the "undercut" or "fade" due to the gradual transition from short to long hair. It's a versatile style that can be worn in many different ways, making it perfect for any occasion.

How To Style Men's Hair Long on Top and Back

Styling men's hair long on top and back can be done in several ways, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any product buildup and keep your hair healthy and clean.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using a brush to create volume and texture. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Once your hair is dry, apply a styling product such as wax, pomade, or gel to give your hair hold and texture. Use a small amount and work it through your hair evenly.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair in the direction you want it to go. You can create a slicked-back look, a messy textured look, or a pompadour style. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips for Men's Hair Long on Top and Back

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your hair long on top and back:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try different styles and looks.

FAQ

Is Men's Hair Long on Top and Back Suitable for All Hair Types?

Men's hair long on top and back can be worn by most hair types, but it works best on thick hair with some texture. If you have thin or fine hair, you may need to use more styling product to create volume and texture.

Can I Style Men's Hair Long on Top and Back for Formal Occasions?

Yes, you can style men's hair long on top and back for formal occasions. Simply slick your hair back with a comb and use a high-hold styling product to keep it in place.

What Products Should I Use for Men's Hair Long on Top and Back?

You can use a variety of styling products for men's hair long on top and back, including wax, pomade, gel, and hairspray. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and styling needs.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Long on Top and Back

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving the perfect look with men's hair long on top and back. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Overall, men's hair long on top and back is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. With the right products and care, you can achieve the perfect look every time.
