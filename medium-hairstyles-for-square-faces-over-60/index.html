Medium Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 60

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect medium hairstyle that will flatter your square face and make you look fabulous even in your 60s? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best medium hairstyles for square faces over 60. From how to style them to the best haircare tips, we have got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline and a wide forehead. The key to choosing the perfect medium hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the angles and create balance. Medium hairstyles, which fall between the chin and shoulders, are perfect for square faces as they accentuate the cheekbones and soften the jawline. Additionally, medium hairstyles are low maintenance and easy to style, making them perfect for the busy lifestyle of a woman over 60.

How to Choose the Perfect Medium Hairstyle for Square Faces Over 60

When choosing a medium hairstyle for a square face over 60, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the crown of the head to balance out the strong jawline.
  • Soft layers and waves will help to soften the angles of the face.
  • A side parting will help to create a diagonal line that will offset the squareness of the face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long as they can accentuate the squareness of the face.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Medium Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 60

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling medium hairstyles for square faces over 60:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are formulated for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. If you have natural waves, use a curling iron to enhance them. If not, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  5. Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Hairstyles for Square Faces Over 60

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium hairstyle:

  • Regular trims every 6-8 weeks will keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

FAQs

What are the best medium hairstyles for square faces over 60?

The best medium hairstyles for square faces over 60 are those that add volume to the crown of the head, soften the angles of the face with layers and waves, and have a side parting to create a diagonal line that offsets the squareness of the face.

Can I still wear my hair long if I have a square face?

Long hair can accentuate the squareness of the face, so it is best to stick to medium hairstyles that soften the angles of the face.

What haircare products should I use for my medium hairstyle?

Use products that are formulated for your hair type and texture. Additionally, use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools and a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you have all the information you need to choose the perfect medium hairstyle for your square face over 60, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and get ready to turn heads!
