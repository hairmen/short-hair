How To Dress With Short Hair Tips Step By Step Guide And Faq

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair is a bold statement. It is a style that is easy to maintain and versatile. You can rock a pixie cut, bob, or any short hairstyle, and still look fabulous. However, you may have questions on how to dress with short hair. Worry not! This guide will take you through the steps, tips, and frequently asked questions.

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Step-by-Step Guide

The first thing you need to consider is your face shape. Short haircuts look great on most face shapes, but some styles suit certain shapes better than others. For example, a pixie cut looks great on oval, heart, and square faces. A bob, on the other hand, suits round and square faces.

Next, consider your personal style. Do you prefer a more feminine or edgy look? Your short hair can complement your style. For a feminine look, try adding soft waves or curls to your hair. For an edgier look, consider a messy or sleek style.

When it comes to outfits, there are no strict rules. You can wear anything that makes you feel comfortable and confident. However, some styles complement short hair better than others. Here are some ideas:

1. Statement Earrings

Short hair provides the perfect canvas for showing off statement earrings. Whether you prefer hoops, chandeliers, or studs, your earrings will stand out with short hair. Just make sure they complement your outfit.

2. Bold Necklines

Short hair draws attention to your face and neck, so why not show them off? Bold necklines like off-the-shoulder, halter, and V-neck tops can enhance your overall look. Just make sure to balance the look with subtle accessories.

3. High-Waisted Bottoms

High-waisted bottoms can elongate your legs and balance out your proportions. Whether you prefer jeans, skirts, or shorts, high-waisted styles can complement your short hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you dress with short hair:

1. Experiment with Accessories

Accessories can make or break your outfit. Try experimenting with hats, scarves, and headbands to add a pop of color or texture to your look.

2. Consider Your Neckline

Your neckline can complement or clash with your short hair. Consider the shape of your neckline when choosing outfits. For example, a turtleneck can create a boxy look, while a scoop neckline can enhance your features.

3. Play with Color

Short hair provides the perfect opportunity to play with color. Whether you prefer highlights, ombre, or a bold color, your short hair can make a statement.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear long dresses with short hair?

A: Absolutely! Long dresses can complement short hair, especially if you choose a style that flatters your body shape.

Q: What about hats?

A: Hats can be tricky with short hair, but they can also be a great accessory. Try experimenting with different styles like fedoras, beanies, and baseball caps.

Q: Should I wear statement jewelry with short hair?

A: Statement jewelry can complement short hair, but it depends on the style and occasion. For a casual look, try layering delicate necklaces. For a formal event, opt for a statement necklace or bracelet.

Hair Care

Short hair is easy to maintain, but it still requires some care. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Short hair can get greasy quickly, so it's important to wash it regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

2. Use a styling product

A styling product can help you achieve your desired style and keep your hair in place. Try a mousse, gel, or pomade.

3. Get regular trims

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

With these tips and steps, you can confidently dress with short hair. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun!
