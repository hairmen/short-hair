Short Haircuts For Round Faces And Wavy Hair A Perfect Match

Written by Christine Jul 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face and wavy hair, you may be wondering what kind of haircut will best suit your features. The good news is that there are plenty of short haircuts that flatter round faces and wavy hair. In this article, we'll explore some of the most popular short haircuts for round faces and wavy hair, as well as tips for maintaining and styling your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A short haircut can help to elongate a round face and add definition to wavy hair. The key is to choose a style that complements your unique features. A layered bob, for example, can help to create the illusion of a longer face, while a pixie cut can emphasize your natural waves. Ultimately, the best short haircut for you will depend on your personal style, hair texture, and face shape.

How To

Before you head to the salon, it's important to have a clear idea of what kind of short haircut you want. Look for inspiration in magazines and online, and consider bringing photos of styles you like to your hair appointment. Be sure to communicate with your stylist about your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle, so they can recommend a cut that will be easy to maintain and style.

Step by Step

The process of getting a short haircut for round faces and wavy hair will vary depending on the style you choose. In general, however, you can expect your stylist to:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section your hair and cut it to the desired length
  3. Add layers or texture to create movement and volume
  4. Style your hair using products and tools

After your haircut, be sure to ask your stylist for tips on how to maintain and style your new look.

Tips

To keep your short haircut looking its best, try these tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your natural waves
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, which can damage your hair and make it look flat
  • Experiment with different hair products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to find what works best for your hair
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape and prevent split ends

FAQ

What short haircut is best for a round face and wavy hair?

A layered bob or a pixie cut are both great short haircuts for round faces and wavy hair. Ultimately, the best haircut for you will depend on your personal style and hair texture.

How do I style my short haircut?

The key to styling a short haircut is to use the right products and tools. Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, and consider using a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. Be sure to communicate with your stylist about your styling goals, so they can recommend the best products and tools for your hair type.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short haircut, it's important to use products that will help enhance your natural waves and prevent damage. Try using a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized. Be sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.
