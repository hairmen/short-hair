How To Blend Extensions With Short Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short, thick hair, you may have struggled with finding the right extensions to blend seamlessly with your natural hair. However, with the right technique and a few tips, you can achieve a flawless look that will make your hair appear longer and fuller. In this article, we will show you how to blend extensions with short thick hair step by step.

Description

Blending extensions with short thick hair can be a bit of a challenge, but it is not impossible. The key is to choose the right type of extensions and to blend them correctly. In this article, we will provide you with all the information you need to achieve a natural-looking blend.

How to Blend Extensions with Short Thick Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Type of Extensions

The first step in blending extensions with short thick hair is to choose the right type of extensions. Clip-in extensions are a great option for short hair because they are easy to apply and remove. They also come in different lengths, textures, and colors, so you can find the perfect match for your hair. Make sure to choose high-quality extensions that are made of real human hair for the most natural look.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying the extensions, you need to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, blow-dry your hair and straighten it using a flat iron. This will make it easier to blend the extensions and create a seamless look.

Step 3: Apply the Extensions

Start by sectioning your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Clip the top part of your hair out of the way. Then, take a weft of extensions and clip it onto the back of your head. Make sure to clip it close to your scalp so that it looks natural. Continue adding wefts until you have achieved the desired length and volume.

Step 4: Blend the Extensions

The next step is to blend the extensions with your natural hair. This is the most important step in achieving a natural-looking blend. To do this, use a fine-toothed comb to gently tease your hair at the roots where the extensions are clipped. This will help to blend the extensions with your natural hair and create a seamless look.

Tips for Blending Extensions with Short Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect blend:

  • Choose extensions that are the same length or slightly longer than your natural hair.
  • Match the texture of the extensions to your natural hair.
  • Tease your hair at the roots where the extensions are clipped to blend them with your natural hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before straightening your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim the ends of the extensions to match the length of your natural hair.

FAQs

Can you wear extensions with short thick hair?

Yes, you can wear extensions with short thick hair. The key is to choose the right type of extensions and to blend them correctly.

How do you blend clip-in extensions with short thick hair?

To blend clip-in extensions with short thick hair, start by sectioning your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Clip the top part of your hair out of the way. Then, take a weft of extensions and clip it onto the back of your head. Continue adding wefts until you have achieved the desired length and volume. Use a fine-toothed comb to gently tease your hair at the roots where the extensions are clipped to blend them with your natural hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair and extensions to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair and extensions regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat on your extensions as much as possible.
  • Use a detangling brush to brush your hair and extensions gently.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place to prevent damage.

Conclusion

Blending extensions with short thick hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right technique and a few tips, you can achieve a natural-looking blend. Remember to choose high-quality extensions, prepare your hair properly, and blend the extensions with your natural hair using a fine-toothed comb. With these tips and tricks, you can have the long, full hair you've always wanted.
