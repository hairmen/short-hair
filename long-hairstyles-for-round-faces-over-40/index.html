Long Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 40

Written by Frank Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about long hairstyles for round faces over 40. If you have a round face and are in your forties or older, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will provide you with some tips and ideas for long hairstyles that will complement your round face and make you look fabulous.

Description

As we age, our hair changes, and so do our faces. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. Women over 40 with round faces may want to opt for long hairstyles that will elongate their face and draw attention away from their roundness.

How to

The first step in finding the right long hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and take a look in the mirror. If your face is widest at the cheeks and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you likely have a round face. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start exploring different long hairstyles that will flatter your face.

Step by Step

1. Choose a long hairstyle that adds height to the crown of your head. This will help elongate your face and draw attention away from your roundness.

2. Consider adding layers to your long hairstyle. Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous and less flat.

3. Opt for a side part rather than a center part. A side part can create the illusion of length and balance out the roundness of your face.

4. Consider adding bangs to your long hairstyle. Bangs can help frame your face and draw attention to your eyes, which can be a great distraction from your roundness.

Tips

When choosing a long hairstyle for a round face, it's important to keep in mind that not all styles will work for everyone. Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.

- Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, hair clips, and hair ties, to add interest and style to your long hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail! Just be sure to add some volume to the crown of your head and avoid pulling your hair too tightly back, as this can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Q: Are there any long hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you should avoid hairstyles that create too much volume at the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as they can make your face look even rounder.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your long hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.

- Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

We hope this article has provided you with some useful tips and ideas for long hairstyles for round faces over 40. Remember, the key to finding the right hairstyle is to experiment and have fun with your hair!
