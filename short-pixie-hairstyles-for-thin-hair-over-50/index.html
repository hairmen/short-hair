Short Pixie Hairstyles For Thin Hair Over 50

Written by Oliver Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 50 and have thin hair, you might think that your hair styling options are limited. But the truth is, there are plenty of short pixie hairstyles that can work great for thin hair. In this article, we will share some tips and ideas for short pixie hairstyles for thin hair over 50.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style. It is a popular choice for women with thin hair because it can add volume and texture to the hair. The key to a great pixie haircut is finding the right style that works for your face shape and hair texture.

How to

When it comes to short pixie hairstyles for thin hair over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips:

1. Consider your face shape

When choosing a pixie haircut, it is important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to go for a longer pixie with side-swept bangs. If you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to go for a shorter pixie with a tapered back.

2. Choose the right length

The length of your pixie haircut will depend on your hair texture and personal preference. If you have thin hair, you may want to go for a shorter pixie that is easy to style and adds volume to your hair.

3. Add texture

Adding texture to your pixie haircut can help create the illusion of thicker hair. You can do this by adding layers, choppy ends, or using texturizing products.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide for creating a short pixie hairstyle for thin hair over 50:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your roots.

Step 2: Blow dry your hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, lifting at the roots to add volume. Use a texturizing spray to add texture to your hair.

Step 3: Style your hair

Use a styling wax to create definition and texture in your hair. Use your fingers to style your hair into a tousled, messy look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short pixie hairstyles for thin hair over 50:

1. Use volumizing products

Using volumizing products can help add volume and thickness to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like wheat protein or keratin.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and textures. You may find that a choppy pixie or a layered pixie works better for your hair texture and face shape.

3. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your pixie haircut looking fresh and stylish. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

What is the best pixie haircut for thin hair over 50?

The best pixie haircut for thin hair over 50 will depend on your face shape and hair texture. A shorter pixie with layers and choppy ends can help add volume and texture to thin hair.

How do I style a pixie haircut for thin hair?

To style a pixie haircut for thin hair, use volumizing products, blow dry your hair with a round brush, and use styling wax to create texture and definition.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

2. Protect your hair from heat

Using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using these tools to help protect your hair.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet.

Conclusion

Short pixie hairstyles can be a great option for women with thin hair over 50. By choosing the right style and using the right products, you can create a stylish and easy-to-maintain look that adds volume and texture to your hair.
