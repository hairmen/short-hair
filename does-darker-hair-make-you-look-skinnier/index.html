Does Darker Hair Make You Look Skinnier

Written by William Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for ways to look skinnier without going through the hassle of a diet or exercise routine? Well, you might be surprised to know that changing your hair color can drastically alter your appearance. In this article, we will explore the question, does darker hair make you look skinnier? We will also give you tips on how to achieve a slimmer look with your hair color. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve a Slimmer Look with Darker Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

When it comes to hair color, darker shades tend to create an illusion of a slimmer face. This is because dark hair color draws attention away from the face and towards the hair. Additionally, darker hair color can create shadows and depth that can make your face appear more chiseled and defined. However, it is important to note that hair color alone cannot make you look skinnier. It is just one aspect of your overall appearance.

How to Achieve a Slimmer Look with Darker Hair

If you're thinking of changing your hair color to achieve a slimmer look, here are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Skin Tone

When choosing a darker hair color, it is important to consider your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, opt for chocolate brown, auburn, or caramel highlights. If you have a cool skin tone, consider a jet black or cool brown shade.

Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can also make a big difference in your appearance. If you have a round face, consider a layered haircut that frames your face. If you have a square face, opt for a side-swept bang or soft layers around your jawline.

Accessorize Wisely

Accessories can also help you achieve a slimmer look. Consider wearing statement earrings or a bold necklace to draw attention away from your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a slimmer look with darker hair:

  1. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best shade and hairstyle for your face shape.
  3. Accessorize wisely to draw attention away from your face.
  4. Practice good hair care habits to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few additional tips and tricks to help you achieve a slimmer look with darker hair:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

FAQs

Does hair color alone make you look skinnier?

No, hair color alone cannot make you look skinnier. It is just one aspect of your overall appearance.

Can a hairstyle make you look skinnier?

Yes, the right hairstyle can create an illusion of a slimmer face.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

In conclusion, darker hair can create an illusion of a slimmer face, but it is just one aspect of your overall appearance. To achieve a slimmer look, consider choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone, choosing the right hairstyle, and accessorizing wisely. Additionally, practice good hair care habits to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Now that you know the answer to the question, does darker hair make you look skinnier, go ahead and experiment with a new hair color!
