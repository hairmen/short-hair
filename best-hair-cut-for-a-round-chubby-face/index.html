Description

Having a round chubby face can be challenging when it comes to choosing the right hair cut. You want to enhance your features and feel confident, but you also need to consider the shape of your face and the latest trends. In this 2023 guide, we will help you find the best hair cut for a round chubby face by providing you with useful tips, step-by-step instructions, and answers to frequently asked questions. You will also learn how to take care of your hair and maintain your new look.


How to Choose the Best Hair Cut for a Round Chubby Face

The first step to choosing the best hair cut for a round chubby face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in width and your face has soft curves, you have a round face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can look for hair cuts that will balance your features and elongate your face. Some of the best hair cuts for a round chubby face include:

  • Long layers: This hair cut adds volume and movement to your hair, creating an illusion of length and slimness. You can also add side-swept bangs to frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.
  • A-line bob: This hair cut is shorter in the back and longer in the front, creating an angle that elongates your face. You can also add layers and texture to add volume and dimension.
  • Side-swept pixie: This hair cut is short and edgy, but the side-swept bangs create a diagonal line that slims down your face. You can also add layers and texture to create volume and movement.
  • Shoulder-length waves: This hair cut is perfect if you want to keep your hair long but still enhance your features. The waves create texture and movement, while the length adds balance to your face.
  • Asymmetrical lob: This hair cut is longer on one side and shorter on the other, creating a diagonal line that elongates your face. You can also add layers and texture to create volume and dimension.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know which hair cuts work best for a round chubby face, it's time to choose the one that suits your style and personality. Follow these steps to get the perfect hair cut:

  1. Research the latest trends and find inspiration photos of hair cuts that you like.
  2. Consult with your hair stylist and show them the photos you have collected.
  3. Ask your hair stylist for their professional advice on which hair cut will work best for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  4. Discuss the length, layers, texture, and styling options of the hair cut.
  5. Book an appointment and get your hair cut by a professional stylist.
  6. Take care of your hair by using the right products, washing it regularly, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your hair cut.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to enhance your new hair cut:

  • Choose hair colors that complement your skin tone and enhance your hair cut.
  • Experiment with different styling options, such as curling, straightening, and braiding.
  • Use hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, to add interest and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hair styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs and curly hair.
  • Consider getting a hair treatment, such as keratin or protein, to strengthen and nourish your hair.

FAQ

What is the best hair cut for a round chubby face?

The best hair cut for a round chubby face is one that elongates your face and balances your features. Some of the best hair cuts for a round chubby face include long layers, A-line bob, side-swept pixie, shoulder-length waves, and asymmetrical lob.

How do I know if I have a round chubby face?

You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all similar in width and your face has soft curves, you have a round face shape.

Can I still have bangs if I have a round chubby face?

Yes, you can still have bangs if you have a round chubby face. However, it's best to choose side-swept bangs that frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.


Hair Care

After getting your new hair cut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and scalp.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, too often.
  • Apply hair oil or serum to add shine and nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your hair cut.
  • Protect your hair from environmental factors, such as sun exposure and pollution, by wearing a hat or scarf.
