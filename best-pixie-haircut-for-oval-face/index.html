Best Pixie Haircut For Oval Face

Written by Aprilia Oct 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Bored of the same old hairstyle? Looking for a new and bold change? A pixie haircut can be the answer to your problems! Not only is it trendy, but it is also low-maintenance and can make a bold statement about your personality. However, not all pixie haircuts are made equal. It is essential to find the right style that suits your face shape, especially if you have an oval face. In this article, we will discuss the best pixie haircut for oval face shape, along with some tips on how to care for your newly styled hair.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Haircut for Oval Face Shape

Before we dive into the best pixie haircut for oval face shape, let's first understand what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape has balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. The jawline is rounded, and the face is longer than it is wide. Now that we know our face shape let's explore some of the best pixie haircuts for oval face shape.

Short Layered Pixie Haircut

The short layered pixie haircut is an excellent option for oval face shape. It adds volume to the top of the head while keeping the sides and back short. This style accentuates the cheekbones and jawline, making it a perfect choice for those with an oval face shape.

Asymmetrical Pixie Haircut

The asymmetrical pixie haircut is a bold and edgy style. It features longer bangs and hair on one side of the head while the other side is cropped short. This hairstyle balances the proportions of the face, making it a great choice for oval face shape.

Side-Swept Pixie Haircut

The side-swept pixie haircut is a classic and timeless style. It features longer hair on the top of the head that is swept to one side. This style adds dimension and volume to the hair, making it a perfect choice for oval face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Get the Best Pixie Haircut for Oval Face Shape

Now that we have discussed the different types of pixie haircuts for oval face shape let's dive into the step-by-step process of getting the best pixie haircut for oval face shape.

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to consult with your hairstylist. They will assess your face shape and hair type and recommend the best pixie haircut for you. Communication is key, so be sure to discuss your preferences and expectations with your hairstylist.

Step 2: Preparing Your Hair

Before your hairstylist starts cutting your hair, they will wash and dry it thoroughly. This step is crucial as it helps them see the natural texture and movement of your hair.

Step 3: Cutting Your Hair

Your hairstylist will use scissors or clippers to cut your hair, depending on the style you choose. They will start at the nape of your neck and work their way up, creating layers and texture along the way.

Step 4: Styling Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, your hairstylist will style it according to the chosen pixie haircut. They will use products such as mousse, gel, or hairspray to add volume and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Haircut

Now that you have the perfect pixie haircut for oval face shape, it is essential to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking fabulous:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

Regular trims help maintain the shape and style of your pixie haircut. Aim for a trim every four to six weeks.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Tip 3: Experiment with Styling

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques. Try using a diffuser to create volume or using a flat iron to create a sleek and chic look.

FAQ

Can I get a pixie haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can! In fact, curly hair can look great in a pixie haircut. Your hairstylist will cut your hair while it is dry to see the natural curl pattern and create layers that enhance your curls.

Can I style my pixie haircut differently every day?

Yes, you can! Pixie haircuts are versatile and can be styled differently every day. Experiment with different styling techniques and products to create different looks.

Will a pixie haircut suit my oval face shape?

Yes, a pixie haircut can suit oval face shape. However, it is essential to choose the right style that complements your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best pixie haircut for your face shape.

Hair Care for Pixie Haircut

A pixie haircut requires low maintenance, but it is essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to help you take care of your pixie haircut:

Tip 1: Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons. This helps prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Use Hair Oil

Use a hair oil to add shine and moisture to your hair. Apply a few drops of hair oil to your damp hair and style as usual.

In conclusion, a pixie haircut can be a great way to change up your look and make a bold statement about your personality. The best pixie haircut for oval face shape is one that complements your face shape and enhances your features. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect pixie haircut for you and follow our tips to maintain healthy and shiny hair.
