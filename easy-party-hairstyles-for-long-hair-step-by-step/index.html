Easy Party Hairstyles For Long Hair Step By Step

Are you tired of always sporting the same hairstyle whenever you attend parties? Are you looking for easy party hairstyles for long hair step by step? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be discussing different hairstyles that you can easily recreate at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, let's discuss some basic tips on how to prepare your hair for styling:

  • Wash your hair thoroughly and let it air dry or blow dry it using low heat.
  • Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from hot tools.
  • Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing spray or mousse to add more body to your hair.

Step by Step

1. Braided Crown

To create this hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair in the middle and create two sections.
  2. Braid each section and secure the ends with an elastic band.
  3. Take one braid and wrap it around your head, securing it in place with bobby pins. Repeat with the other braid on the opposite side.
  4. Loosen the braids to create a more natural and effortless look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair down but still want to add a touch of elegance to their look. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with an elastic band.
  2. Flip the ponytail over and create a small opening above the elastic band.
  3. Take the ponytail and pull it through the opening, creating a twisted effect.
  4. Fluff the hair to create more volume and texture.

3. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Follow these steps to create this effortless yet chic look:

  1. Gather all your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it with an elastic band.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create a sleek look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect party hairstyle:

  • Use hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Add hair accessories such as hairpins, headbands, or flowers to add more glam to your look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about party hairstyles for long hair:

Q: Can I still create these hairstyles if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still recreate these hairstyles even if you have curly hair. Just make sure to straighten your hair first before styling.

Q: How long will these hairstyles last?

A: These hairstyles can last for several hours if you use enough hairspray and bobby pins to hold them in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, make sure to:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to create easy party hairstyles for long hair step by step, it's time to get creative and try out different looks for your next party. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you!
