Is Braiding Your Hair With Yarn Haram

Written by Aprilia Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Yarn braids have been around for quite some time now and are a popular choice among many people who love to experiment with their hair. However, many people have been wondering if braiding hair with yarn is haram or not. In this article, we will explore this topic in detail and answer all your questions related to it.

Description

Braiding hair with yarn is a popular hairstyle that involves wrapping yarn around small sections of hair to create a braid. This style can be done in a variety of ways and is often chosen by people who want to add some color and texture to their hair. However, the question of whether it is haram or not has been a topic of debate for some time now.

How to

If you are interested in trying out yarn braids, here is how you can do it:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Separate your hair into small sections.
  3. Take a piece of yarn and wrap it around the base of the section of hair you want to braid.
  4. Begin braiding your hair as you normally would, making sure to incorporate the yarn into the braid as you go along.
  5. Once you reach the end of the braid, tie the yarn in a knot to secure it in place.
  6. Repeat this process for each section of hair until you have completed your entire head.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to braid your hair with yarn:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Take a piece of yarn and tie it around the base of one of the sections of hair.
  4. Begin braiding your hair, incorporating the yarn into the braid as you go.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the hair section.
  6. Tie the yarn in a knot at the end of the braid to secure it.
  7. Repeat this process for each section of hair.
  8. Once you have completed your entire head, trim any excess yarn.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when braiding your hair with yarn:

  • Choose yarn that is soft and not too thick to avoid tangling and damage to your hair.
  • Make sure to tie the yarn tightly around the base of the hair section to keep it in place.
  • Be gentle when braiding your hair to avoid breakage and damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness.
  • When removing the yarn braids, be sure to do it gently to avoid tangling and breakage.

FAQ

Is braiding your hair with yarn haram?

There is no clear consensus on whether braiding your hair with yarn is haram or not. Some people believe that it is not allowed as it involves adding a foreign material to the hair, while others believe that it is permissible as it does not alter the natural state of the hair.

Can yarn braids damage your hair?

Yarn braids can cause damage to your hair if they are too tight or left in for too long. It is important to be gentle when braiding your hair and not keep the braids in for more than 8 weeks to avoid damage.

How do you care for yarn braids?

To care for your yarn braids, you should wash them every 2-3 weeks with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools and be gentle when detangling your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair when wearing yarn braids to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Be gentle when detangling your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid keeping your yarn braids in for too long to avoid damage to your hair.

In conclusion, whether braiding your hair with yarn is haram or not is a topic of debate. However, it is important to take care of your hair when wearing yarn braids to avoid damage and breakage. Follow the tips and guidelines mentioned in this article to achieve beautiful and healthy-looking hair.
