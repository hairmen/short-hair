Easy Updos For Long Hair Wedding Guest A Complete Guide

Are you attending a wedding soon and looking for a chic hairstyle to wear? Look no further than easy updos for long hair wedding guest! Updos are a classic and elegant hairstyle that never goes out of style. They are especially perfect for weddings, as they keep your hair out of your face and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to Create Easy Updos for Long Hair Wedding Guest

Creating easy updos for long hair wedding guest is simpler than you might think. Here are a few key steps to follow:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow drying it. If you have thin hair, you may want to add some texture by curling it with a curling iron or using a texturizing spray. If you have thick hair, you may want to use a straightening iron to smooth it out.

Step 2: Choose Your Updo

There are many different types of updos to choose from, including braids, buns, and twists. Consider the dress code and style of the wedding when choosing your updo. A sleek, low bun is a classic and elegant option, while a braided updo is more bohemian and relaxed.

Step 3: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need a few supplies to create your updo, including bobby pins, hair elastics, and hairspray. Make sure you have enough of each to complete your hairstyle.

Step 4: Start Styling

Begin by pulling your hair back into a ponytail or gathering it in your hands. Then, twist or braid your hair into your desired updo and secure it with bobby pins and/or hair elastics. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Easy Updos for Long Hair Wedding Guest

Follow these step-by-step instructions to create easy updos for long hair wedding guest:

Step 1: Create a Low Ponytail

Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Secure with a hair elastic.

Step 2: Twist Your Ponytail

Take your ponytail and twist it around the hair elastic until it forms a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 3: Create a Fishtail Braid

If you want to add some texture to your updo, create a fishtail braid. Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and divide it into two sections. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the right section. Then, take a small piece of hair from the outside of the right section and cross it over to the left section. Repeat this process until you have braided the entire section of hair. Secure the braid with a small hair elastic.

Step 4: Wrap the Fishtail Braid Around the Bun

Take the fishtail braid and wrap it around the bun, securing it with bobby pins as you go. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Easy Updos for Long Hair Wedding Guest

Follow these tips to create the perfect easy updos for long hair wedding guest:

  • Choose an updo that complements your face shape and dress style.
  • Practice your updo ahead of time to ensure it looks the way you want it to.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories, such as flowers or hair pins.
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your updo looking great all day.

FAQs about Easy Updos for Long Hair Wedding Guest

Q: How do I know which updo to choose?

A: Consider the dress code and style of the wedding when choosing your updo. A sleek, low bun is a classic and elegant option, while a braided updo is more bohemian and relaxed.

Q: How can I make my updo last all day?

A: Use high-quality hair products, such as hairspray and bobby pins, and practice your updo ahead of time to ensure it looks great all day.

Q: Can I add accessories to my updo?

A: Absolutely! Flowers, hair pins, and other accessories can add a touch of glamour to your updo.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Updo

Follow these hair care tips to maintain your updo:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your updo.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these easy updos for long hair wedding guest tips, you'll be able to create a chic and elegant hairstyle that complements any wedding outfit. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to make your updo truly unique. Happy styling!
