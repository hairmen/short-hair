Men s Hairstyle For Round Face Shape With Beard The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face shape and a beard, you might be wondering what kind of hairstyle would suit you. The good news is that there are plenty of options that can complement your facial features and enhance your overall look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the best men's hairstyles for round face shape with beard, plus tips on how to maintain your hair and beard for a polished appearance.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with soft curves and no prominent angles. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds some structure and definition to your face. A beard can also help to elongate your face and create a more chiseled look. When combined with the right hairstyle, your beard can be a powerful tool for enhancing your appearance.

How to

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a men's hairstyle for round face shape with beard:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hairstyle is to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking in the mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle that Adds Length

The key to choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape is to add length and definition. This can be achieved through layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles that are longer on top and shorter on the sides. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

Step 3: Choose a Beard Style that Complements Your Hair

When choosing a beard style, consider how it will complement your hairstyle. If you have a longer, layered hairstyle, a full beard can add balance and definition. If you have a shorter haircut, a trimmed goatee or soul patch can help to add some interest to your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your hair and beard:

Tip 1: Use a High-Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

A good shampoo and conditioner can help to keep your hair and beard healthy and looking their best. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and avoid using harsh chemicals that can dry out your hair and skin.

Tip 2: Trim Your Beard Regularly

Regular trimming can help to keep your beard looking neat and tidy. Use a high-quality trimmer and trim your beard to a length that complements your hairstyle.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Hairstyles and Beard Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and beard styles until you find the perfect look for you. Take inspiration from celebrities and fashion icons and try out different styles until you find one that makes you feel confident and stylish.

FAQ

Q: What kind of hairstyle should I choose for a round face shape with a beard?

A: Choose a hairstyle that adds length and definition to your face, such as a layered haircut, side-swept bangs, or a longer top with shorter sides.

Q: What kind of beard style should I choose to complement my hairstyle?

A: Choose a beard style that complements your hairstyle, such as a full beard with a longer, layered haircut, or a trimmed goatee or soul patch with a shorter haircut.

Q: How often should I trim my beard?

A: Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy. The frequency of trimming will depend on the length and style of your beard, but most men should trim their beard at least once a week.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair and beard is essential for maintaining a polished appearance. Here are some tips for keeping your hair and beard healthy and looking their best:

Tip 1: Use a High-Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type and avoid using harsh chemicals that can dry out your hair and skin.

Tip 2: Trim Your Beard Regularly

Regular trimming can help to keep your beard looking neat and tidy. Use a high-quality trimmer and trim your beard to a length that complements your hairstyle.

Tip 3: Moisturize Your Skin and Beard

Keeping your skin and beard moisturized is essential for preventing dryness and itchiness. Use a high-quality moisturizer and beard oil to keep your skin and beard hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can choose the perfect men's hairstyle for round face shape with beard and maintain a polished appearance that will turn heads wherever you go.
