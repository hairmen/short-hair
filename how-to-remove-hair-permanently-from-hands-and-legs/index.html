How To Remove Hair Permanently From Hands And Legs

Written by Luffy Sep 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly shaving or waxing your hands and legs? Do you want to get rid of unwanted hair permanently? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss various methods to remove hair permanently from hands and legs.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Remove Hair Permanently from Hands and Legs
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Permanent Hair Removal
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care after Permanent Hair Removal

How to Remove Hair Permanently from Hands and Legs

There are several methods to remove hair permanently from hands and legs. These include:

  • Laser Hair Removal
  • Electrolysis
  • Intense Pulsed Light (IPL)

Let’s discuss each method in detail.

Laser Hair Removal

Laser hair removal is a popular method to remove hair permanently. It works by directing a laser beam to the hair follicles, which damages them and prevents hair growth. This method is effective for all skin types and hair colors, except for white or gray hairs.

The procedure is relatively painless, although some people may experience a mild discomfort. It usually takes 6-8 sessions to achieve permanent hair removal.

Electrolysis

Electrolysis is another popular method to remove hair permanently. It works by inserting a tiny needle into the hair follicle and sending an electric current to the root of the hair. This damages the hair follicle and prevents hair growth.

This method is effective for all skin types and hair colors, including white or gray hairs. It can be slightly painful, but most people tolerate it well. It usually takes several sessions to achieve permanent hair removal.

Intense Pulsed Light (IPL)

Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) is a method that uses broad-spectrum light to damage the hair follicles and prevent hair growth. It is effective for most skin types and hair colors, except for white or gray hairs.

The procedure can be slightly uncomfortable, but most people tolerate it well. It usually takes several sessions to achieve permanent hair removal.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for laser hair removal:

  1. Consult with a qualified dermatologist or aesthetician to determine if laser hair removal is right for you.
  2. Avoid sun exposure and tanning beds for at least two weeks before the procedure.
  3. Shave the treatment area before the procedure.
  4. Apply a topical anesthetic to the treatment area to reduce discomfort.
  5. The aesthetician will use a laser to target the hair follicles, which will damage them and prevent hair growth.
  6. After the procedure, apply a cooling gel or cream to the treatment area.
  7. Avoid sun exposure and tanning beds for at least two weeks after the procedure.

Note: The steps may vary depending on the method used.

Tips for Permanent Hair Removal

Here are some tips to achieve permanent hair removal:

  • Choose a qualified and experienced aesthetician or dermatologist.
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning beds before and after the procedure.
  • Do not pluck or wax the treatment area before or after the procedure.
  • Follow the aftercare instructions provided by your aesthetician or dermatologist.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about permanent hair removal:

Is permanent hair removal safe?

Yes, permanent hair removal is safe when performed by a qualified and experienced aesthetician or dermatologist.

Is permanent hair removal painful?

The level of pain varies depending on the method used and the individual’s pain tolerance. However, most people tolerate the procedure well.

How long does it take to achieve permanent hair removal?

It usually takes several sessions to achieve permanent hair removal.

Hair Care after Permanent Hair Removal

After permanent hair removal, it is important to take care of your skin and hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning beds for at least two weeks after the procedure.
  • Avoid hot showers or baths for at least 24 hours after the procedure.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or exfoliants on the treatment area.
  • Moisturize the treatment area regularly.

With these tips and methods, you can achieve permanent hair removal from your hands and legs. Consult with a qualified dermatologist or aesthetician to determine which method is right for you.
