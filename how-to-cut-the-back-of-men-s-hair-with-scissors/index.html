How To Cut The Back Of Men s Hair With Scissors

Written by Robby Oct 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a simple haircut? Do you want to learn how to cut the back of men's hair with scissors? In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

Description

Cutting the back of men's hair with scissors can be tricky, especially if you are doing it for the first time. It requires a steady hand and some patience. However, with a little practice, you can master this skill and save yourself a lot of money in the long run.

How to Cut the Back of Men's Hair with Scissors: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, you need to prepare the hair. Wash and dry the hair thoroughly. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and knots. If the hair is too long, trim it with a clipper or scissors.

Step 2: Divide the Hair

Divide the hair into sections. Start by sectioning the hair at the nape of the neck. Use a comb to create a horizontal parting. Clip the top section of the hair away from the bottom section.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Starting from the center, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the tips of the scissors to cut the hair. Cut the hair at an angle, following the natural curve of the head. Repeat this process on both sides of the center section.

Work your way up to the top of the head, taking small sections of hair and cutting them at an angle. Use the previous sections as a guide to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut all the hair, it's time to blend the hair. Comb the hair to remove any loose hairs. Use the scissors to blend any uneven sections.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Men's Hair with Scissors

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a comb to create clean partings.
  • Keep your scissors sharp to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks to rest your hands and avoid cramping.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure about anything.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut hair?

A: No. Regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can damage the hair. Use hairdressing scissors for the best results.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't panic. Hair grows back. If you make a mistake, try to even it out or wait for it to grow back before trying again.

Hair Care

Cutting the back of men's hair with scissors is just one part of hair care. To keep your hair looking healthy, it's important to wash and condition it regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.

Regular trims will also help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. By following these simple steps, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.
