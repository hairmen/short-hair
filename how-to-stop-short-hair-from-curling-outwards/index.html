How To Stop Short Hair From Curling Outwards Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair, you may have experienced the frustration of your hair curling outwards instead of lying flat. This can make styling your hair a challenge and can be especially annoying if you have just gotten a fresh haircut. Fortunately, there are several tips and tricks that you can use to stop your short hair from curling outwards. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know to keep your short hair looking sleek and stylish.

  • How to Stop Short Hair from Curling Outwards
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Stop Short Hair from Curling Outwards

There are several things you can do to prevent your short hair from curling outwards:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow drying or straightening. Heat can dry out your hair and make it more prone to curling.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair smooth and prevent flyaways.
  • When blow drying your hair, use a round brush to smooth your hair down and keep it in place.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, such as gels or mousses, that can weigh your hair down and make it more prone to curling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you prevent your short hair from curling outwards:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  2. After washing your hair, use a towel to gently pat your hair dry. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a hair oil or serum to your hair to keep it smooth and prevent flyaways.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to smooth your hair down and keep it in place.
  5. Avoid using heavy styling products, such as gels or mousses.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you keep your short hair from curling outwards:

  • Avoid cutting your hair too short. Short hair that is too short can be more prone to curling outwards.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and make your hair more prone to curling.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth your hair and prevent curling.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about preventing short hair from curling outwards:

Q: Can I still use heat styling tools on my short hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Will a keratin treatment damage my hair?

A: No, but it is important to choose a reputable salon and follow their aftercare instructions.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Keeping your short hair healthy and moisturized is key to preventing it from curling outwards. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair smooth and prevent flyaways
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to smooth your hair down and keep it in place
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, such as gels or mousses

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your short hair looking sleek and stylish without having to worry about it curling outwards. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid using too much heat or heavy styling products. With a little bit of care and attention, you can keep your short hair looking beautiful and healthy.
