How To Change Direction Of Your Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Changing the direction of your hair can completely transform your look. It can add volume, create a new style, and even make you look younger. However, many people are unsure of how to change the direction of their hair without damaging it. In this article, we’ll show you how to safely and effectively change the direction of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Direction of Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Direction of Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Change Direction of Your Hair

The first step in changing the direction of your hair is to determine which direction you want it to go. This will depend on your hair type, face shape, and personal preference. Once you’ve decided on the direction, you can use a variety of styling techniques to achieve the look you want.

Here are some common techniques for changing the direction of your hair:

  • Blow-drying
  • Straightening
  • Curling
  • Braiding
  • Rolling

Each of these techniques has its own benefits and drawbacks. Blow-drying, for example, is quick and easy, but can be damaging if done too often. Straightening and curling can create dramatic changes in your hair, but can also cause damage if not done correctly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to changing the direction of your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the direction you want your hair to go.
  3. Select the appropriate styling tool (blow-dryer, straightener, etc.).
  4. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  5. Divide your hair into sections.
  6. Starting at the roots, use your styling tool to change the direction of your hair.
  7. Repeat for each section of hair.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Remember to be gentle when styling your hair, and to use heat protectant products to avoid damage. If you’re unsure about how to use a particular styling tool, consult a professional stylist.

Tips for Changing Direction of Your Hair

Here are some additional tips for changing the direction of your hair:

  • Start slowly. Gradually change the direction of your hair over several styling sessions to avoid damage.
  • Experiment with different techniques to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use high-quality styling tools and products to avoid damage.
  • Consult a professional stylist if you’re unsure about how to style your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing the direction of your hair:

Q: Will changing the direction of my hair damage it?

A: It can, especially if you use heat styling tools too often or don’t use heat protectant products. Be gentle with your hair and take breaks between styling sessions to avoid damage.

Q: How often should I change the direction of my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the styling techniques you use. As a general rule, it’s best to avoid frequent styling sessions to avoid damage.

Q: Can changing the direction of my hair make it look thinner or thicker?

A: Yes, changing the direction of your hair can make it look thinner or thicker depending on the technique you use. Straightening can make your hair look thinner, while curling can add volume.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

By following these tips and techniques, you can safely and effectively change the direction of your hair and achieve the look you want.
