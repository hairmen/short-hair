Best Short Haircuts For Diamond Face Shape

Written by William Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a diamond face shape, you know that it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that flatters your unique features. However, there are plenty of great short haircuts that can enhance your beauty and make you look stunning. In this article, we'll discuss the best short haircuts for diamond face shape, including how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones that create a diamond shape. The goal of choosing a short haircut for this face shape is to create balance and soften the angles of the face. In general, short haircuts that add volume to the crown and sides of the head, while keeping the length on top and at the nape of the neck, work best for diamond face shapes.

How to

To choose the best short haircut for your diamond face shape, start by considering your hair texture and personal style. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a pixie cut or a layered bob to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a short shag haircut may work better to remove bulk and create movement.

Step by Step

Step by step, here are the best short haircuts for diamond face shape:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. For a diamond face shape, a pixie cut with short layers on top and tapered sides can soften the angles of the face and create balance.

2. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a versatile haircut that can be worn straight or wavy, and is perfect for adding volume and texture to fine hair. For a diamond face shape, a layered bob with long layers on top and shorter layers at the sides can create a flattering frame around the face.

3. Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a classic, timeless haircut that can work for any face shape. For a diamond face shape, a blunt bob that falls just below the chin can create balance and add volume to the crown.

4. Short Shag

A short shag haircut is a layered, textured cut that works well for thick hair. For a diamond face shape, a short shag with layers throughout can remove bulk and create movement, while still maintaining volume on top.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your short haircut for diamond face shape:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown and sides of your hair.
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, lifting at the roots to create volume.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.
  • Schedule regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for diamond face shape:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a diamond face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of the face, such as wide or puffy hairstyles.

Can I wear bangs with a diamond face shape?

Yes, but choose bangs that are long and side-swept to create balance and soften the angles of the face.

How often should I wash my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but in general, washing your hair every 2-3 days is sufficient.

Hair Care

To maintain the health and beauty of your short haircut for diamond face shape, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Avoid styling products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

In conclusion, there are plenty of great short haircuts that can flatter a diamond face shape. By choosing the right haircut for your hair texture and personal style, and following these styling and maintenance tips, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and stylish.
