Does Having Shorter Hair Make You Look Fatter The Truth Revealed

Written by Luffy Jul 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a haircut but worried that it might make you look heavier? This is a common concern for many people, especially those who are conscious about their appearance. In this article, we will discuss whether having shorter hair makes you look fatter or not. We will also provide you with tips on how to manage your hair to avoid looking heavier than you are.

Table of Contents

  • Does Shorter Hair Make You Look Fatter?
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair
  • Tips to Avoid Looking Fatter with Short Hair

Does Shorter Hair Make You Look Fatter?

The answer is no. Shorter hair does not make you look fatter. Your body size and shape are determined by your genetics, diet, and lifestyle. Your hair length has nothing to do with it. However, your hairstyle can create an illusion of a fuller face or body.

When you have short hair, your face is more exposed, which can make it appear rounder or wider. But this does not mean that you are heavier. It is just an optical illusion. The same applies to long hair. If you tie your hair back tightly, your face may look slimmer.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can help you look your best. Here are some tips to consider:

  • Go for hairstyles that create height at the crown of your head. This can make your face look longer, and your body slimmer.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. This can make your face look wider.
  • Consider getting layers or bangs. This can create an illusion of a longer face and a slimmer body.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. They can make your face look wider.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who can recommend a hairstyle that suits your face shape and body type.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be easy and fun. Here is a step by step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair. This will help create texture and hold.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends. This will create volume and lift.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, or a curling iron to create waves or curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips to Avoid Looking Fatter with Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you avoid looking heavier with short hair:

  • Wear clothes that flatter your body shape.
  • Avoid wearing clothes that are too tight or too loose.
  • Avoid wearing patterns that add volume to your body.
  • Choose accessories that draw attention away from your face.
  • Wear makeup that enhances your features.

FAQ

Q: Can a haircut make me look thinner?

A: Yes, a haircut can create an illusion of a slimmer face or body. Choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference.

Q: Is short hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Short hair can be suitable for most face shapes, but it depends on the haircut. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape.

Q: Can hair color make me look thinner?

A: Yes, hair color can create an illusion of a slimmer face or body. Dark colors can make your face look slimmer, while lighter colors can make it appear wider.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy, shiny hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often. This can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, having shorter hair does not make you look fatter. It is your hairstyle that can create an illusion of a heavier face or body. By choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and look your best.
