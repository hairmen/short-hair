How To Gel Wavy Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it. You don't want to look like you just rolled out of bed, but you also don't want to spend hours in front of the mirror. This is where hair gel comes in. Hair gel can help you tame your waves and look your best. In this article, we'll show you how to gel wavy hair guys in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents:

  • Step 1: Wash Your Hair
  • Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair
  • Step 3: Apply Gel
  • Step 4: Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Gelling Wavy Hair Guys
  • FAQs about Gelling Wavy Hair Guys
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair Guys

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in gelling wavy hair guys is to wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for wavy hair. This will help to keep your hair soft and manageable. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all the shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a towel to dry it. Don't rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently pat your hair dry. You want your hair to be damp, but not soaking wet.

Step 3: Apply Gel

Next, apply a small amount of hair gel to your fingertips. Rub your hands together to distribute the gel evenly. Make sure to use a gel that is specifically designed for wavy hair. Apply the gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to apply the gel evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use your fingers or a comb to create your desired look. If you want more definition in your waves, you can scrunch your hair with your hands while it's still damp. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the process.

Tips for Gelling Wavy Hair Guys

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when gelling wavy hair guys:

  • Use a gel that is specifically designed for wavy hair.
  • Don't use too much gel, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Apply the gel evenly throughout your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the drying process.

FAQs about Gelling Wavy Hair Guys

Q: Can I use hair gel on wet hair?

A: Yes, you can apply hair gel to wet hair. In fact, applying gel to wet hair can help to distribute it evenly and prevent clumping.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day, as washing it too frequently can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to dry my hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer to dry your hair. Just be sure to use a diffuser attachment to prevent frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair Guys

In addition to gelling your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for wavy hair guys:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for wavy hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too frequently.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as this will prevent breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

By following these tips, you can keep your wavy hair looking its best and easily gel it to achieve your desired style.
