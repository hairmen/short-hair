How To Do Makeup For An Oval Face Shape

Written by Luffy Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As someone with an oval face shape, you have the ideal canvas for makeup application. Your face is well balanced and symmetrical, with a slightly wider forehead and a gently rounded chin. However, with the right makeup techniques, you can further enhance your features and create a stunning look. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do makeup for an oval face shape.

  • How to do makeup for an Oval Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to do makeup for an Oval Face Shape

When it comes to makeup application for an oval face shape, the goal is to highlight your natural features and create a balanced look. Here are some tips to follow:

1. Start with a Clean Face

Before you begin applying makeup, it's essential to start with a clean face. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser and pat it dry with a clean towel. Follow up with a moisturizer to hydrate your skin and create a smooth canvas for makeup application.

2. Apply Foundation

Apply foundation to even out your skin tone and create a flawless base. Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone and type. For an oval face shape, a light to medium coverage foundation is ideal. Apply the foundation with a brush or sponge, blending it evenly over your face and neck.

3. Conceal Under-Eye Circles

Use a concealer to cover any under-eye circles or blemishes. Choose a shade that is one or two shades lighter than your foundation. Apply the concealer in a triangle shape under your eyes and blend it well with a brush or sponge.

4. Contour Your Cheeks

Use a contouring powder or cream to accentuate your cheekbones. Apply the contouring product just below your cheekbones and blend it upwards towards your ears.

5. Highlight Your Features

Use a highlighter to add some glow to your face. Apply the highlighter to the high points of your face, such as the bridge of your nose, cheekbones, and cupid's bow.

6. Define Your Brows

Well-defined eyebrows can frame your face and enhance your features. Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to fill in sparse areas and create a natural shape that complements your face.

7. Apply Eye Makeup

Choose eye makeup that complements your eye color and skin tone. For an oval face shape, a natural eye makeup look is ideal. Apply eyeshadow to your eyelid, crease, and brow bone. Use eyeliner to define your eyes and mascara to add volume and length to your lashes.

8. Finish with Lip Color

Choose a lip color that complements your skin tone and outfit. For an oval face shape, a natural or bold lip color can work well. Apply lip color with a brush or directly from the tube, starting in the center of your lips and blending it outward.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do makeup for an oval face shape:

  1. Start with a clean face
  2. Apply foundation
  3. Conceal under-eye circles
  4. Contour your cheeks
  5. Highlight your features
  6. Define your brows
  7. Apply eye makeup
  8. Finish with lip color

Tips for Makeup Application

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when doing makeup for an oval face shape:

  • Use a primer to create a smooth base for makeup application
  • Blend your makeup well to avoid harsh lines
  • Use a setting spray to make your makeup last longer
  • Experiment with different makeup looks to find one that suits you best

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by a slightly wider forehead, gently rounded chin, and balanced features.

What makeup looks best on an oval face shape?

Natural makeup looks, bold lips, and accentuated cheekbones work well on an oval face shape.

Can an oval face shape wear any makeup look?

Yes, an oval face shape can wear any makeup look, but natural and balanced looks tend to work best.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape

In addition to makeup application, there are some hair care tips that can complement an oval face shape:

  • Choose a hair length and style that accentuates your features
  • Avoid hairstyles with too much volume on the top
  • Consider a side-swept fringe to frame your face
  • Experiment with different hair colors to find one that complements your skin tone

With these tips and techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a stunning makeup look for your oval face shape.
