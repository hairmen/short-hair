Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Long Wavy Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a 50-year-old woman with long wavy hair, you might be looking for new hairstyles that suit your age, personality, and lifestyle. In this article, we will share some ideas, tips, and tricks on how to style your long wavy hair in different ways. Whether you want to keep it simple, elegant, or trendy, we have got you covered. Let's explore some hairstyles that can make you look and feel beautiful at any age.

Description

Long wavy hair can be both a blessing and a challenge, especially as you age. On the one hand, your hair might have lost some of its natural shine, volume, and elasticity. On the other hand, you might have gained more wisdom, confidence, and freedom to express yourself. Therefore, you need hairstyles that can enhance your best features, hide your flaws, and match your personality.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's discuss some general tips on how to care for your long wavy hair. First, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that can nourish your hair and scalp. Second, avoid excessive heat styling, such as blow-drying, straightening, or curling, as it can damage your hair and make it look frizzy. Instead, let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser to enhance your natural waves. Third, trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth. Lastly, embrace your natural texture and color, as they are unique and beautiful.

Step by Step

Now, let's move on to the hairstyles. We have selected five different styles that you can try depending on the occasion, mood, and preference. For each style, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve it.

1. Loose Waves

This style is perfect for a casual day out or a relaxed weekend. To achieve it, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently and apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to prevent tangles.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and twist them loosely into buns or braids. You can also use curling rods or rollers if you prefer.
  4. Let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser on low heat.
  5. Once your hair is dry, gently untwist the sections and use your fingers to separate the waves. You can also use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to detangle your hair without losing the waves.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray or a shine serum to add volume and shine.

Voila! You have achieved a natural and effortless look that can make you feel younger and carefree.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

This style is ideal for a semi-formal event or a romantic date night. To achieve it, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. You can add some texturizing spray or mousse to enhance your waves.
  2. Section the top half of your hair and tie it into a loose ponytail or bun. You can leave some strands loose around your face to frame it.
  3. If you want to add some volume to the crown area, tease it gently with a comb or a brush.
  4. Use a curling iron or a wand to curl the bottom half of your hair. You can curl them in different directions to create a natural and messy look.
  5. Once you have curled all the sections, use your fingers to comb through them and blend them with the top half of your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a hairpin to secure the half-up half-down style.

Tada! You have created a chic and feminine look that can highlight your best features and make you stand out.

3. Side Braid

This style is perfect for a boho-inspired outfit or a beachy vibe. To achieve it, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. You can add some sea salt spray or a texture paste to create a beachy texture.
  2. Part your hair on one side and gather all the hair on the opposite side.
  3. Begin a French braid or a Dutch braid from the parting and continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck.
  4. Secure the braid with a hair tie or a bobby pin.
  5. If you want to create a messy and boho look, gently pull the strands of the braid to loosen it and create some volume.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a hairpin to secure the braid and prevent flyaways.

Wow! You have created a playful and adventurous look that can reflect your free spirit and love for nature.

4. High Ponytail

This style is perfect for a formal event or a business meeting. To achieve it, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. You can add some serum or oil to create a smooth and shiny texture.
  2. Brush your hair backwards and gather it into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a brush or a comb to smooth out the bumps and flyaways.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie or a scrunchie. You can choose a colored or patterned hair tie to add some fun and personality.
  4. Use a curling iron or a wand to curl the ends of the ponytail. You can create loose or tight curls, depending on your preference.
  5. Once you have curled the ends, use your fingers to comb through them and blend them with the rest of the ponytail.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or a hairpin to secure the ponytail and prevent frizz.

Yay! You have created a sophisticated and elegant look that can showcase your professionalism and confidence.

5. Braided Crown

This style is perfect for a romantic or bohemian wedding or a festive occasion. To achieve it, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. You can add some volumizing spray or mousse to create a fuller texture.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and braid each side into a loose and messy fishtail or Dutch braid. You can also use regular braids or twists if you prefer.
  3. Bring each braid to the opposite side of your head and secure it with bobby pins or hair ties. You can create a braided crown that wraps around your head, or a half-crown that covers only the top half of your head.
  4. If you want to add some volume or texture, gently pull the strands of the braids to loosen them and create some messiness.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or a hairpin to secure the braids and prevent them from falling apart.

Hooray! You have created a dreamy and whimsical look that can make you feel like a fairy tale princess.

Tips

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to style your long wavy hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.
  • Apply a hair mask or a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and repair your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, scarves, or flowers, to add some color and texture to your hair.
  • Consult a hairstylist or a colorist if you want to change your hair color or cut. They can suggest the best options that suit your face shape, skin tone, and hair type.
  • Embrace your gray hair or try some highlights or lowlights to add some dimension and contrast.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for 50-year-old women with long wavy hair:

1. Can I wear my hair loose and natural at this age?

Yes, you can! In fact, wearing your hair loose and natural can make you look younger and more vibrant. Just make sure to care for your hair and use the right products that enhance your waves and prevent frizz.

2. What hairstyles should I avoid at this age?

You should avoid hairstyles that are too harsh, severe, or outdated. For example, avoid tight ponytails, slicked-back hair, or overly layered cuts. Instead, choose hairstyles that are soft, feminine, and modern.
