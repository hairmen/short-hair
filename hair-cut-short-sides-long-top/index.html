Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, the hair cut short sides long top might be just what you need. This trendy haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years and it's not hard to see why. With its edgy and stylish look, it's a great option for anyone looking to make a statement. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the hair cut short sides long top, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Description

The hair cut short sides long top is a haircut where the sides of the head are cut short while leaving the top longer. The length of the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's generally left long enough to style in different ways. This hairstyle is often paired with fades or undercuts to create a more dramatic look.

How to Get the Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

If you're interested in getting the hair cut short sides long top, the first step is to find a good hairstylist who has experience with this style. Once you've found someone you trust, follow these steps:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of the top. This will depend on your personal preference, but it's important to communicate your desired length to your hairstylist.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

The next step is to cut the sides of your hair. This will usually involve using clippers to create a fade or an undercut. The length of the fade or undercut will depend on how short you want the sides to be.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Once the sides are cut, it's time to cut the top. This will involve using scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. Your hairstylist may also use a razor to add texture to the hair.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Finally, it's time to style the hair. This will depend on your personal preference, but some popular styles include slicking the hair back, creating a messy look, or adding volume to the top.

Tips for Maintaining the Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

Once you have your hair cut short sides long top, it's important to take care of it to maintain its style. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and the style you're going for.

Tip 2: Visit Your Hairstylist Regularly

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat tools to style your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

FAQ

What Face Shapes Suit the Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top?

The hair cut short sides long top can work for a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with oval or oblong faces.

Can I Wear the Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top with a Beard?

Yes! The hair cut short sides long top looks great with a beard, especially if you opt for an undercut or fade.

Hair Care for the Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the hair cut short sides long top. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will help keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

While it's important to style your hair, be careful not to over-style it. Too much heat and product can damage your hair over time.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair, so be sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when you're outside for an extended period of time.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the hair cut short sides long top with confidence. Whether you're going for a bold or subtle look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
