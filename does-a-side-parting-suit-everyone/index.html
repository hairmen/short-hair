Does A Side Parting Suit Everyone

Written by Petter Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

One of the most common questions people ask themselves when it comes to hairstyles is whether a side parting suits them. The answer to this question is not a simple yes or no because it depends on various factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we will discuss this question in more detail and provide you with some tips on how to choose the right side parting for you.

Description

A side parting is a simple hairstyle where the hair is divided into two sections. One section is combed to the left, and the other is combed to the right. This style has been around for many years and is still popular today. It's a timeless classic that can be worn by both men and women. However, the question remains, does a side parting suit everyone?

How to

Choosing the right side parting for you can be challenging, but there are some general guidelines you can follow. Firstly, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, a deep side parting can make your face look slimmer. If you have a square face, a softer, less defined parting will complement your face shape better. Secondly, consider your hair texture. If you have thin hair, a side parting can create the illusion of more volume. If you have thick hair, a middle parting may be more flattering. Lastly, consider your personal style. If you prefer a more polished look, a sleek side parting may work better for you. If you prefer a more relaxed, bohemian look, a messy side parting may be more your style.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to create a side parting:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a comb to divide your hair into two sections
  3. Decide which side you want your parting on
  4. Use the comb to create a clean, defined parting
  5. Use hair clips to keep the other section of hair out of the way
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips on how to make your side parting work for you:

  • Experiment with different parting depths to find the most flattering one for your face shape
  • Use styling products such as hairspray or pomade to keep your parting in place
  • Consider getting a trim or cut to help your parting sit more naturally
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with a headband or hair clips to add some extra flair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about side partings:

Can I wear a side parting if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! A side parting can work well with curly hair. Just make sure to define your curls with styling products to keep them looking their best.

Will a side parting make my forehead look bigger?

Not necessarily. If you have a high forehead, a side parting can actually help to balance out your features.

Is a side parting suitable for formal occasions?

Yes, a side parting can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For a formal event, consider styling your hair in a sleek, polished side parting.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's essential to take good care of your hair. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use styling products that won't damage your hair. If you're unsure which products to use, ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

In conclusion, whether a side parting suits you or not depends on various factors such as your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. However, with the right guidance and some experimentation, you can find the perfect side parting for you.
