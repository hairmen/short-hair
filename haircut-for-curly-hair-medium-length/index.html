Haircut For Curly Hair Medium Length Tips How To And Faqs

Written by Eveline Oct 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. While it can look stunning, it can also be challenging to manage, especially when it comes to haircuts. If you have medium length curly hair and are looking for a new haircut, this guide is for you. Read on for tips, how-to, and FAQs on haircut for curly hair medium length.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium length curly hair can be defined as hair that falls between the shoulders and the mid-back. This type of hair can be challenging to manage as it is prone to frizz, tangles, and dryness. However, with the right haircut, you can enhance the natural beauty of your curls and make them more manageable.

How-to

The first step in getting a haircut for curly hair medium length is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. Curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair, and not all stylists have the necessary skills. Look for a stylist with a portfolio that includes curly haircuts, and read reviews from other curly-haired clients.

Step-by-Step

The process of getting a haircut for curly hair medium length involves the following steps:

  1. Consultation with the stylist to discuss your hair type, face shape, and desired look.
  2. Washing and conditioning your hair to prepare it for the cut.
  3. Cutting the hair while dry to ensure that the curls fall naturally.
  4. Styling the hair to enhance the natural curls and create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for curly hair medium length:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair type.
  • Ask your stylist to cut your hair while dry to get a more accurate idea of how the curls will look.
  • Consider getting layers to add volume and movement to your curls.
  • Ask your stylist to use a razor or shears instead of scissors to avoid creating blunt ends that can make your curls appear frizzy.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair after getting a haircut?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair after getting a haircut. However, keep in mind that frequent heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to dryness and breakage.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for curly hair medium length?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, you should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair medium length looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangles and frizz.

Getting a haircut for curly hair medium length can be a game-changer for your hair. With the right cut and hair care routine, you can enhance the natural beauty of your curls and make them more manageable. Follow the tips and FAQs in this guide to get the best results.
