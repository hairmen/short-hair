What Is A Men s Tapered Haircut

Written by Petter Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In the world of men's haircuts, the tapered haircut is one of the most popular styles. It's a haircut that involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back, gradually getting longer as it moves up towards the top of the head. Tapered haircuts can be done with any type of hair, from straight to curly, and can be styled in a variety of ways. In this article, we'll be discussing what a men's tapered haircut is, how to get one, and how to take care of it.

What is a Tapered Haircut?

A tapered haircut is a style where the hair is cut shorter on the sides and back, while gradually getting longer towards the top of the head. The result is a gradual fade from short to long hair. This style can be done with any type of hair and can be customized to suit the individual's preferences.

How to Get a Tapered Haircut

If you're interested in getting a tapered haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist. They will be able to guide you through the process and provide recommendations based on your hair type and face shape. Before getting started, it's important to have a clear idea of the style you want. You can bring in pictures or examples to help your stylist understand your vision.

The stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair shorter, gradually getting longer as they move towards the top. They will use clippers or scissors to create the fade, depending on your preferences. The length of the hair on top can be left longer or cut to your desired length. The result will be a gradual fade from short to long hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Tapered Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a tapered haircut:

  1. Choose a skilled barber or hairstylist
  2. Have a clear idea of the style you want
  3. Communicate your preferences to the stylist
  4. The stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of the hair shorter
  5. Gradually get longer as they move towards the top
  6. Use clippers or scissors to create the fade
  7. Cut the hair on top to your desired length
  8. Style as desired

Tips for Maintaining a Tapered Haircut

Once you have your tapered haircut, it's important to take proper care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your tapered haircut:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair
  • Style your hair using a blow dryer or comb
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about tapered haircuts:

How long does a tapered haircut last?

A tapered haircut can last anywhere from two to eight weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Can I get a tapered haircut with curly hair?

Yes, a tapered haircut can be done with any type of hair, including curly hair. In fact, tapered haircuts can be a great way to show off natural curls.

What should I tell my stylist if I want a tapered haircut?

If you want a tapered haircut, it's important to communicate your preferences to your stylist. You can bring in pictures or examples to help them understand your vision. Be specific about how short you want the sides and back and how long you want the hair on top.

Hair Care for Tapered Haircuts

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a tapered haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using too much product
  • Avoid touching your hair too much
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups

A tapered haircut is a classic men's style that can be customized to suit any individual's preferences. Whether you have straight or curly hair, a tapered haircut can be a great way to show off your style. By taking proper care of your hair and visiting your stylist regularly, you can keep your tapered haircut looking great for weeks to come.
