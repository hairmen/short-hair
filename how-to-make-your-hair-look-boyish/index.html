How To Make Your Hair Look Boyish

Written by Daniel Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and want to achieve a boyish look, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing tips and tricks on how to make your hair look boyish. Whether you have long hair or short hair, these tips can work for anyone looking to achieve a more androgynous look. So, let's get started!

Description

The boyish hairstyle is all about simplicity and minimalism. It's about embracing your natural hair texture and working with it, rather than against it. This look is perfect for those who want to exude confidence and independence. It's easy to maintain and can be adapted to suit any face shape or hair type.

How to Make Your Hair Look Boyish

Step 1: Get the Right Haircut

The first step to achieving a boyish look is by getting the right haircut. A short, cropped cut is the perfect style to achieve that androgynous look. You can also consider getting an undercut or a buzz cut for an even more dramatic transformation. If you're not ready to commit to a short cut, you can always opt for a longer, layered cut that can be styled in a more boyish way.

Step 2: Embrace Your Natural Texture

One of the key elements of a boyish hairstyle is embracing your natural hair texture. Whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair, work with what you have. Avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Instead, use a lightweight styling product to add texture and volume to your hair.

Step 3: Use the Right Styling Products

The right styling product can make all the difference in achieving a boyish look. Opt for a lightweight pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair. Avoid using heavy styling products like gel or mousse that can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a boyish hairstyle:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help you style your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners or curling irons that can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What face shape is best suited for a boyish hairstyle?

A boyish hairstyle can work for any face shape. However, if you have a round face, consider getting a shorter cut that can help elongate your face.

Can I still achieve a boyish look if I have long hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a boyish look with long hair. Consider getting a layered cut that can be styled in a more androgynous way.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and vibrant look. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a boyish hairstyle that exudes confidence and independence. Remember, the key is to embrace your natural hair texture and work with what you have. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you!
