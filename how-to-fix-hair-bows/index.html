How To Fix Hair Bows A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have a favorite hair bow that has lost its shape or become untied? Don't worry, it's easier than you think to fix it! In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to fix hair bows and keep them looking their best. Plus, we'll share some tips on how to care for your hair accessories to prevent them from needing repairs in the first place.

How to Fix Hair Bows

Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread or hot glue gun
  • Hair tie or clip (optional)

Step 1: Assess the Damage

Take a close look at your hair bow and identify the problem. Is it misshapen? Are the loops uneven? Is it missing pieces? Knowing what needs to be fixed will make the repair process easier.

Step 2: Fix Misshapen Bows

If your hair bow has lost its shape, start by carefully cutting off any frayed or uneven edges. Then, reshape the bow by gently pulling and adjusting the loops until they are even and symmetrical. If necessary, you can use a hair tie or clip to hold the bow in place while you work.

Step 3: Re-tie Untied Bows

If your hair bow has come untied, start by undoing any knots or tangles. Then, retie the bow by following the original pattern. If you're not sure how to tie a bow, there are many tutorials available online.

Step 4: Replace Missing Pieces

If your hair bow is missing pieces, you can either replace them or remove them altogether. To replace missing pieces, use a needle and thread or hot glue gun to attach new pieces of ribbon or fabric. To remove missing pieces, carefully trim any frayed edges and reshape the bow as needed.

Step 5: Secure the Bow

Once you've fixed your hair bow, it's important to secure it so it stays in place. You can use a needle and thread to sew the bow onto a hair tie or clip, or use a hot glue gun to attach it directly to your hair.

Tips for Caring for Hair Bows

Now that you know how to fix hair bows, here are some tips to help you care for them and prevent the need for repairs in the future:

  • Store your hair bows in a cool, dry place to prevent them from becoming misshapen or damaged.
  • Avoid getting your hair bows wet, as this can cause the fabric to fray or the colors to bleed.
  • When traveling with your hair bows, pack them in a sturdy container or wrap them in tissue paper to prevent them from getting crushed.
  • If your hair bow has delicate embellishments, such as sequins or beads, hand wash it gently and let it air dry.

FAQ

Can I wash my hair bow?

It depends on the material. If your hair bow is made of fabric, you can hand wash it gently in cold water and lay it flat to dry. Avoid using hot water, bleach, or harsh detergents, as these can damage the fabric. If your hair bow has delicate embellishments, such as sequins or beads, it's best to spot clean it with a damp cloth.

How do I remove wrinkles from my hair bow?

If your hair bow has become wrinkled, you can use a warm iron to smooth out the fabric. Be sure to use a low heat setting and place a thin cloth over the bow to protect it from direct heat.

How can I prevent my hair bow from slipping out of my hair?

If your hair bow tends to slip out of your hair, try securing it with bobby pins or using a hair tie or clip to hold it in place. You can also try spraying your hair with hairspray or texturizing spray before putting in the bow to give it some extra grip.

Hair Care

In addition to caring for your hair bows, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair bows, you can keep them looking their best and enjoy wearing them for years to come!
