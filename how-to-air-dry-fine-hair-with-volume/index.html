How To Air Dry Fine Hair With Volume

Written by Eveline Sep 19, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have fine hair, you know how challenging it can be to create volume without weighing down your hair. Blow drying may seem like the fastest solution, but it can damage your hair in the long run. Air drying your hair is the safest and healthiest way to style your hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to air dry fine hair with volume and give you some tips on how to take care of your hair.

How to Air Dry Fine Hair with Volume

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots.

Step 2: Squeeze out as much water as possible from your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as this can cause frizz and breakage.

Step 3: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. This will give your hair extra lift and hold.

Step 4: Flip your hair upside down and scrunch it up. This will create volume and texture.

Step 5: Let your hair air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair too much as this can cause frizz.

Tips for Air Drying Fine Hair with Volume

Tip 1: Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to squeeze out excess water from your hair. These materials are gentler on your hair than regular towels.

Tip 2: Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Tip 3: If you have time, let your hair air dry for a few minutes before applying any styling products. This will help to reduce frizz and flyaways.

Tip 4: Don’t be afraid to experiment with different volumizing products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will air drying my hair make it frizzy?

A: Air drying can cause frizz, but using the right products and techniques can help to reduce it. Try using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to squeeze out excess water from your hair, and avoid touching your hair too much while it’s drying.

Q: Can I use a diffuser to air dry my hair?

A: Yes, using a diffuser can help to create volume and reduce frizz while air drying your hair. Make sure to use a low heat setting and hold the diffuser close to your roots to create lift.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Tip 1: Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.

Tip 2: Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from your hair.

Tip 3: Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Tip 4: Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip it of its natural oils. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days instead.

By following these simple tips, you can air dry your fine hair with volume and keep it healthy and beautiful.
