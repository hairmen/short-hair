How To Relax Short Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with the daily struggle of managing your short curly hair? Do you dream of having straight, sleek locks without having to spend hours straightening your hair every day? Look no further, because we have the ultimate guide on how to relax short curly hair.

Description

Relaxing your hair can be a great option for those with naturally curly hair who want to achieve a straighter look. The process involves chemically altering the hair's texture to loosen the curl pattern and make it more manageable. While relaxing your hair can be a great option, it's important to take proper care of your hair to ensure it remains healthy and strong. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to relax short curly hair safely and effectively.

How to Relax Short Curly Hair

The process of relaxing short curly hair is straightforward, but it's important to take proper precautions to avoid damaging your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin the relaxing process, it's important to prepare your hair. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Then, deep condition your hair to help protect it from damage. Finally, blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Step 2: Apply Relaxer

Apply the relaxer to your hair, being careful to only apply it to the new growth. Keep the relaxer away from your scalp to avoid burns. Follow the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully, as different products may have different application times.

Step 3: Rinse and Neutralize

After the recommended time has passed, rinse the relaxer out of your hair with warm water. Then, apply the neutralizing shampoo to your hair to stop the chemical reaction. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Step 4: Deep Condition

After rinsing out the neutralizing shampoo, apply a deep conditioner to your hair. Leave the conditioner on for at least 10 minutes to help restore moisture to your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've deep conditioned your hair, you can style it as desired. Keep in mind that your hair may be more fragile after relaxing, so avoid heat styling as much as possible to minimize damage.

Tips for Relaxing Short Curly Hair

Relaxing your hair can be a great option, but it's important to take proper care of your hair to avoid damage. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Read the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully and follow them closely.
  • Be sure to only apply the relaxer to the new growth to avoid over-processing your hair.
  • Use a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical reaction before rinsing your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair after relaxing to restore moisture and protect it from damage.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible to minimize damage.

FAQ

Is it safe to relax short curly hair?

When done properly, relaxing short curly hair can be safe. However, it's important to take proper precautions to avoid damage. Be sure to read the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully and follow them closely.

How often should I relax my hair?

You should wait at least 8-10 weeks between relaxer treatments to avoid damaging your hair. However, the frequency of relaxing your hair will depend on your hair's texture and how quickly it grows.

Can I relax my hair at home?

While it is possible to relax your hair at home, it's recommended that you visit a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair. A stylist will have the experience and knowledge to ensure the process is done safely and effectively.

Hair Care for Relaxed Short Curly Hair

After relaxing your hair, it's important to take proper care of it to ensure it remains healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your relaxed short curly hair:

  • Deep condition your hair regularly to restore moisture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap when swimming.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

With proper care, relaxed short curly hair can be a great option for those who want a straighter, more manageable look. Follow these tips and you'll be on your way to having healthy, beautiful hair.
