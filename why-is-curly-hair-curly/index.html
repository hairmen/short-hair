Why Is Curly Hair Curly

Written by Frank Oct 31, 2023 · 3 min read

Curly hair is a topic that has fascinated people for centuries. Some people believe that curly hair is a sign of good luck, while others think that it's a sign of creativity and intelligence. But what is the reason behind curly hair? In this post, we will explore the science behind why hair is curly.

What Makes Hair Curly?

The shape of your hair follicle determines whether your hair is straight or curly. Straight hair has round hair follicles, while curly hair has oval-shaped hair follicles. The shape of the hair follicle determines the shape of the hair shaft, which in turn determines whether the hair is straight or curly.

When hair follicles are straight, the hair grows out straight. However, when the hair follicles are oval, the hair grows out in a spiral or curly shape. This is because the oval shape causes the hair to grow in a corkscrew pattern, rather than straight out of the scalp.

Is Curly Hair Genetic?

Yes, curly hair is genetic. It is determined by your DNA, just like your eye color and skin tone. If your parents have curly hair, then there is a good chance that you will have curly hair as well. However, there are some cases where people have straight hair even though their parents have curly hair. This is because there are multiple genes that determine hair type, and sometimes the straight hair genes are dominant over the curly hair genes.

How to Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, then you know that it can be a challenge to care for. Here are some tips to help you keep your curls looking their best:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip the natural oils from your hair, leaving it dry and frizzy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your curls moisturized.

2. Condition your hair regularly

Conditioner helps to keep your curls hydrated and reduces frizz. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls soft and shiny.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools

Heat can damage your curls and cause them to frizz. If you must use a heat styling tool, use a heat protectant spray first.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair

Using a regular brush can cause breakage and damage to your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can you make straight hair curly?

A: Yes, you can make straight hair curly using a perm. However, perms can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to do your research and find a reputable stylist.

Q: Can you straighten curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten curly hair using a flat iron or a chemical straightening treatment. However, straightening can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to use heat protectant products and limit the amount of heat you use.

Conclusion

Curly hair is determined by your DNA and the shape of your hair follicles. If you have curly hair, it's important to care for it properly to keep it looking its best. Use sulfate-free shampoo, condition regularly, avoid heat styling tools, and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. With the right care, your curls can be the envy of everyone around you!
