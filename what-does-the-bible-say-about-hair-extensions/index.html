What Does The Bible Say About Hair Extensions

Written by Luffy Aug 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are looking for guidance on whether or not it is okay to wear hair extensions as a Christian, you may be wondering what the Bible has to say on the topic. While the Bible does not specifically mention hair extensions, there are some principles that can be applied to this topic. In this article, we will explore what the Bible has to say about hair extensions and provide some tips for caring for your hair extensions.

What is the Biblical View on Hair?

Throughout the Bible, hair is often associated with beauty, strength, and identity. In 1 Corinthians 11:15, it says, "But if a woman has long hair, it is her glory? For her hair is given to her for a covering." This verse suggests that women should have long hair, which may lead some to question whether or not it is appropriate to wear hair extensions.

However, it is important to remember that the Bible also teaches that outward appearance is not as important as inner beauty. In 1 Peter 3:3-4, it says, "Do not let your adorning be external—the braiding of hair and the putting on of gold jewelry, or the clothing you wear— but let your adorning be the hidden person of the heart with the imperishable beauty of a gentle and quiet spirit, which in God's sight is very precious." This verse suggests that while it is not necessarily wrong to braid your hair or wear jewelry, it is more important to focus on developing inner beauty.

How to Care for Your Hair Extensions

If you have decided to wear hair extensions, it is important to take proper care of them to ensure that they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your hair extensions:

1. Wash Your Hair Extensions Regularly

Just like your natural hair, your hair extensions need to be washed regularly to keep them clean and healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and be gentle when washing and detangling your hair.

2. Use Heat Protectant Spray

If you plan to use heat styling tools on your hair extensions, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Apply the spray evenly throughout your hair before using a curling iron or straightener.

3. Store Your Hair Extensions Properly

When you are not wearing your hair extensions, be sure to store them properly to prevent tangling and damage. Use a hair extension hanger or a silk bag to keep your extensions organized and tangle-free.

FAQ About Hair Extensions

1. Are hair extensions okay to wear as a Christian?

While the Bible does not specifically mention hair extensions, it is important to focus on developing inner beauty rather than outward appearance.

2. Can I wash my hair extensions?

Yes, you should wash your hair extensions regularly to keep them clean and healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and be gentle when washing and detangling your hair.

3. How long do hair extensions last?

The lifespan of hair extensions depends on the type of extensions and how well they are cared for. With proper care, hair extensions can last anywhere from a few months to a year or more.

Conclusion

While the Bible does not specifically mention hair extensions, it is important to remember that inner beauty is more important than outward appearance. If you have decided to wear hair extensions, be sure to take proper care of them to ensure that they last as long as possible. By following these tips and caring for your hair extensions, you can enjoy beautiful, healthy hair for months to come.
