Best Hairstyle For Oval Face Over 50

Written by Daniel Oct 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for the perfect hairstyle to enhance your oval face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through choosing the best hairstyle for your oval face shape and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile and balanced face shape. It is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and high cheekbones. Over 50, women tend to lose hair thickness and volume, so it's essential to choose a hairstyle that flatters their face shape and hair type while taking their age into account.

How-To

The best hairstyle for an oval face over 50 is one that accentuates your facial features while considering your hair texture and thickness. Here are some hairstyles to consider:

1. Bob

A bob haircut is a classic look that compliments an oval face shape beautifully. It is low maintenance and versatile, making it perfect for women over 50. A short bob with layers can add volume and texture to thinning hair, while a longer bob can create an elegant and sophisticated look.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that can enhance your facial features and give you a youthful look. It is perfect for women who want to embrace their gray or white hair and show off their confidence. However, it requires regular maintenance to keep it looking sharp and fresh.

3. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is an excellent choice for an oval face over 50. It adds volume and dimension to thinning hair while framing your face beautifully. You can opt for long or short layers, depending on your hair length and texture.

4. Shoulder-Length Haircut

A shoulder-length haircut is a classic and timeless look that flatters an oval face shape. It is versatile and easy to maintain, making it perfect for women over 50. You can add layers or bangs to create a personalized look that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
2. Choose a hairstyle that suits your lifestyle and personality.
3. Consider your age and hair thickness when selecting a hairstyle.
4. Take care of your hair by using nourishing hair products and avoiding heat styling tools.
5. Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or voluminous curls.
2. Embrace your natural hair texture and color.
3. Use volumizing products to add volume and texture to thinning hair.
4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth to your hair.
5. Use a wide-tooth comb or a soft-bristle brush to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I'm over 50?
A: Yes, you can dye your hair at any age. However, it's essential to choose a hair color that compliments your skin tone and enhances your natural features.

Q: Can I still wear long hair over 50?
A: Yes, you can wear long hair over 50 as long as it flatters your face shape and hair type. Consider adding layers or bangs to create volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
2. Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
3. Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
4. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
5. Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims, touch-ups, and treatments.

Choosing the best hairstyle for your oval face shape over 50 can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance and tips, you can find a look that enhances your facial features and suits your hair type and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair by using nourishing products and visiting your hairstylist regularly. Embrace your natural beauty and show off your confidence with a hairstyle that makes you feel your best!
