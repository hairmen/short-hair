Best Pixie For Oval Face How To Rock The Look In 2023

Written by William Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, then you're in luck! This face shape is said to be the most versatile and suits almost any hairstyle. However, if you're looking for a haircut that will accentuate your features and make you stand out in the crowd, then a pixie cut is the way to go. In this article, we'll discuss the best pixie for oval face shapes, how to style it, and some tips and tricks to maintain it.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is cropped close to the head, usually with short layers and wispy edges. It's a bold and daring style that can bring out your facial features, especially if you have an oval face shape. The best pixie for oval face shapes is one that adds volume and texture to your hair, while also complementing your facial structure.

How to

Step 1: Find the Right Pixie Cut

When choosing a pixie cut, consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. For oval face shapes, opt for a pixie with volume on top and shorter layers towards the back. This will create a flattering shape that accentuates your cheekbones and jawline. You can also add some side-swept bangs to frame your face and soften your features.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before styling your pixie, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add some lift and texture. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume. For a more textured look, use a flat iron or curling wand to create loose waves or curls.

Step 3: Style Your Pixie

Once your hair is prepped, it's time to style your pixie. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look. Alternatively, you can use a comb to create a more polished look with defined edges.

Tips

  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add some volume to your roots.
  • Invest in a good quality hair serum to keep your hair shiny and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add some variety to your hairstyle.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a pixie cut?

While pixie cuts can suit almost any face shape, they work particularly well on oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, opt for a longer pixie cut with shorter layers towards the back to create a more elongated look.

How often should I wash my pixie cut?

It's important to keep your pixie cut clean and fresh, but washing it too often can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Can I still style my pixie cut if I have thin hair?

Absolutely! There are many ways to add volume and texture to thin hair, such as using a volumizing mousse, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a texturizing spray. Talk to your hairstylist about the best styling products for your hair type.

Hair Care

Maintaining a pixie cut requires some effort, but it's worth it for the stunning results. To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach and hair dye, as they can weaken your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

With the right cut and styling techniques, a pixie cut can be the perfect hairstyle for oval face shapes. Follow these tips and tricks to rock the look in 2023!
