How To Cut Your Own High And Tight Haircut

Written by Oliver Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a low-maintenance, masculine haircut that is easy to maintain, then the high and tight haircut might be the perfect choice for you. The high and tight haircut is a military-inspired haircut that is characterized by short sides and back with a slightly longer top. In this article, we will show you how to cut your own high and tight haircut in easy-to-follow steps.

Description

The high and tight haircut is a popular men's haircut that has been around for decades. It is a classic, military-inspired haircut that is characterized by short sides and back with a slightly longer top. The haircut is easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways depending on the occasion. It is also a great haircut for men who have a busy lifestyle and don't have much time to style their hair.

How to Cut Your Own High and Tight Haircut

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step to cutting your own high and tight haircut is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. Make sure that the clippers have different guard sizes so that you can adjust the length of the hair according to your preference.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots. If your hair is too long, you might want to trim it with scissors before using the clippers. This will make it easier to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by using a #1 or #2 guard on the clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start from the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to guide the clippers. Make sure to use short, smooth strokes to achieve an even cut. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.

Step 4: Trim the Top

Next, use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length. You can use the clippers to blend the sides and the top together for a seamless look.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

After you have trimmed your hair, use the clippers to clean up the edges around your ears and the back of your neck. Use a smaller guard size to get a precise cut.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head to make sure that the cut is even.
  • If you are not confident in cutting your own hair, ask a friend or a professional barber for help.

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

Can I style my hair differently with a high and tight haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair differently with a high and tight haircut. You can use a hair product to create a textured look or slick it back for a more polished look.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type and avoid using too much heat styling tools. You can also use a hair oil to nourish and moisturize your hair.

Cutting your own high and tight haircut is easy and convenient. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to get the perfect high and tight haircut that suits your style and personality.
