Description

The number 1 back and sides long on top hairstyle is a classic look that has been popular for decades. This style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, textured, or messy.

This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. It is particularly popular among men who want a low-maintenance, easy-to-style look that can be dressed up or down.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the number 1 back and sides long on top hairstyle, you will need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. The barber will use clippers to trim the hair on the back and sides of the head to a number 1 length. They will then use scissors to leave the hair on top longer, usually around 2-3 inches.

The barber will then blend the hair on the sides and back into the longer hair on top, creating a seamless transition. This is typically done with scissors or clippers, depending on the desired look.

Step by Step Guide

1. Visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in the number 1 back and sides long on top hairstyle.

2. Communicate the desired length and style of the hair on top.

3. The barber will use clippers to trim the hair on the back and sides of the head to a number 1 length.

4. The barber will use scissors to leave the hair on top longer, usually around 2-3 inches.

5. The barber will blend the hair on the sides and back into the longer hair on top.

6. Style the hair on top as desired, using products such as wax or pomade to create texture and hold.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

To maintain the number 1 back and sides long on top hairstyle, it is important to visit the barber or hairstylist regularly for touch-ups. This will help to keep the hair on the back and sides of the head at the desired length, preventing it from growing out too much.

It is also important to use the right styling products for the hair on top. Products such as wax or pomade can help to create texture and hold, while also providing a natural-looking shine. However, it is important not to use too much product, as this can weigh the hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Can the number 1 back and sides long on top hairstyle be worn by men with thinning hair?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be a great option for men with thinning hair, as it can help to create the illusion of more volume and thickness.

Q: How often should I visit the barber or hairstylist for touch-ups?

A: It is recommended to visit the barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

You should also avoid using heat styling tools such as hair dryers or straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a wide-tooth comb to style it.

By following these tips and maintaining your number 1 back and sides long on top hairstyle, you can enjoy a timeless and versatile look that will never go out of style.
