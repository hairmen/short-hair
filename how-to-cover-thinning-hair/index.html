How To Cover Thinning Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, hair thinning and hair loss have become a common problem among both men and women. Thinning hair can be due to several reasons like hormonal changes, genetics, stress, and much more. But, there is no need to worry as there are several ways to cover thinning hair and get fuller, thicker-looking hair. In this blog post, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to cover thinning hair.

  • How to Cover Thinning Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cover Thinning Hair

There are several ways to cover thinning hair. Some of them are:

1. Hair Fibers:

Hair fibers are an instant solution to cover thinning hair. Hair fibers are tiny fibers that stick to your hair and create an illusion of fuller hair. They come in different colors and can be easily applied by sprinkling them on the thinning areas.

2. Hair Extensions:

Hair extensions are a great way to add volume and length to your hair. They come in different types like clip-in, tape-in, and sew-in. You can choose the one that suits you the best.

3. Hair Wigs:

Hair wigs are an excellent way to cover thinning hair. Wigs come in different styles, colors, and lengths. They are easy to wear and can give you a completely different look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cover thinning hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Product

Before covering your thinning hair, you need to choose the right product that suits you the best. You can choose from hair fibers, hair extensions, and hair wigs. Choose the product that matches your hair color and texture.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying any product, prepare your hair by washing and drying it. You can also use a volumizing spray to add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Apply the Product

Apply the product according to the instructions provided. For hair fibers, sprinkle them on the thinning areas and pat gently. For hair extensions, follow the instructions provided with the product. For hair wigs, wear the wig and adjust it according to your head size.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that you have covered your thinning hair, you can style your hair according to your preference. You can use a curling iron, straightener, or any other styling tool to create your desired look.

Tips to Cover Thinning Hair

Here are some tips to cover thinning hair:

1. Use a Volumizing Spray:

Using a volumizing spray can add volume to your hair and make it look fuller.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles:

Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids as they can pull your hair and cause further damage.

3. Get Regular Haircuts:

Getting regular haircuts can make your hair look healthier and fuller.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can hair fibers damage my hair?

No, hair fibers do not damage your hair. They are made of natural ingredients and do not contain any harmful chemicals.

Q. Can I wash my hair with hair extensions?

Yes, you can wash your hair with hair extensions, but make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Q. How long do hair wigs last?

Hair wigs can last up to 6 months to a year depending on how well you take care of them.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Thinning Hair

Here are some hair care tips to prevent thinning hair:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet:

Eating a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals can promote hair growth and prevent hair thinning.

2. Avoid Heat Styling:

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair and cause hair thinning.

3. Massage Your Scalp:

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can cover your thinning hair and get fuller, thicker-looking hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to prevent further hair thinning.
