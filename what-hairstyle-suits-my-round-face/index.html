What Hairstyle Suits My Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may have struggled to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. But fear not – there are plenty of options that can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and stylish. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for round faces and provide tips on how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Choose The Right Hairstyle For Your Round Face
  3. Step By Step Guide To Styling Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and slimness. This can be achieved by adding volume to the crown of your head, keeping the sides of your hair sleek and straight, or creating soft layers that frame your face.

How To Choose The Right Hairstyle For Your Round Face

When selecting a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle that flatters a round face:

  • Opt for a long, layered cut that falls below your chin. This will create the illusion of length and slimness.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or above. These can make your face appear wider.
  • Add volume to the crown of your head. This can be achieved with backcombing, teasing, or using volumizing products.
  • Keep the sides of your hair sleek and straight. This will help to elongate your face.
  • Avoid center parts. Instead, opt for a side part that creates asymmetry.
  • Consider getting bangs. Soft, side-swept bangs can help to balance out your features.

Step By Step Guide To Styling Your Hair

Now that you've chosen a hairstyle that flatters your round face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suited to your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the crown of your head to add volume.
  4. If you have bangs, blow dry them to the side with a small round brush.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a straightening iron to smooth the sides of your hair.
  6. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a serum or wax, to the ends of your hair to add texture.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've achieved your desired hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions with a hat or scarf.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a bob if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a long bob that falls below your chin and has layers to create movement and texture.

Q: Should I avoid curly hair if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Soft, loose curls can be flattering on a round face, but avoid tight ringlets that can make your face appear rounder.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but avoid pulling your hair too tightly back, as this can accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for a loose, messy ponytail that sits low on your neck.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Choose products that are suited to your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Now that you know what hairstyle suits your round face, it's time to embrace your natural beauty and experiment with different styles. Whether you prefer a sleek, straight look or soft, tousled waves, there's a hairstyle out there that will make you feel confident and gorgeous.
