How To Blunt Cut Long Hair At Home

Written by Eveline Aug 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts and looking for a way to trim your long locks at home? Blunt cutting long hair is a simple technique that can help you achieve a sleek, polished look without stepping out of your house. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of blunt cutting long hair at home, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Blunt Cut Long Hair at Home

Blunt cutting long hair is all about creating a straight line at the ends. Here's how you can do it at home:

What You'll Need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • A mirror

Step by Step Guide:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly.
  2. Comb your hair straight and divide it into two equal sections.
  3. Clip one section to the side and focus on the other.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight.
  5. Hold the section between your forefinger and middle finger and cut it straight across.
  6. Repeat the process with the rest of the hair, ensuring that you cut at the same length each time.
  7. Once you're done with one section, unclip the other and repeat the process.
  8. Check both sides in the mirror to make sure the length is even.
  9. Style as desired.

Blunt cutting long hair at home is a straightforward process, but it's essential to follow the steps carefully to avoid any mistakes. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Tips

  • Ensure your hair is dry before you start cutting.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid frayed ends.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's easier to cut more later than to fix a mistake.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. It's better to cut less than to regret it later.
  • Use clips to keep your hair in place while cutting.

FAQ

Q. Can I blunt cut my long hair if it's curly?

A. Yes, you can blunt cut long hair regardless of its texture. However, it's essential to cut each curl individually to ensure an even length.

Q. Can I blunt cut my hair if it's layered?

A. Yes, you can blunt cut long hair even if it's layered, but you'll need to cut each layer individually.

Q. How often should I blunt cut my long hair?

A. It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. On average, you should blunt cut long hair every 2-3 months to keep it looking sleek and polished.

Hair Care

Blunt cutting long hair at home is an excellent way to save money, but it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

Now that you know how to blunt cut long hair at home, you can save money and achieve a sleek, polished look without leaving your house. Follow our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to keep your long locks looking healthy and beautiful.
