How To Stop Hair Falling Out From Alopecia

Written by Eveline Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Alopecia is a condition that causes hair loss on the scalp and other parts of the body. It can be a difficult and distressing experience for those who suffer from it. If you are experiencing hair loss due to alopecia, there are several things you can do to help stop the hair from falling out.

Description

There are several types of alopecia, including androgenetic alopecia, alopecia areata, and telogen effluvium. Androgenetic alopecia is the most common type, and it is caused by genetics and hormones. Alopecia areata is an autoimmune disorder that causes hair loss in patches, and telogen effluvium is a condition that causes hair to fall out due to stress, a change in hormones, or medication.

How to

If you are experiencing hair loss from alopecia, there are several things you can do to help stop the hair from falling out:

1. Use Minoxidil

Minoxidil is a medication that is used to treat androgenetic alopecia. It is applied to the scalp and can help to regrow hair. It is available in over-the-counter and prescription strengths.

2. Use Steroid Injections

Steroid injections can be used to treat alopecia areata. The injections are given directly into the scalp, and they can help to stimulate hair growth.

3. Wear a Wig or Hairpiece

If you are experiencing hair loss, wearing a wig or hairpiece can be a good option. There are many different types of wigs and hairpieces available, so you can find one that looks natural and feels comfortable.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help to promote hair growth. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.

5. Reduce Stress

Stress can contribute to hair loss. Try to reduce stress by getting regular exercise, practicing relaxation techniques, and getting enough sleep.

Step by Step

If you are experiencing hair loss from alopecia, follow these steps to help stop the hair from falling out:

Step 1: Consult with a Doctor

If you are experiencing hair loss, it is important to consult with a doctor to determine the cause of the hair loss and to develop a treatment plan.

Step 2: Use Medications

If your hair loss is caused by androgenetic alopecia or alopecia areata, your doctor may prescribe medications such as minoxidil or steroid injections to help stimulate hair growth.

Step 3: Wear a Wig or Hairpiece

If you are experiencing hair loss, wearing a wig or hairpiece can be a good option. Consult with a hair specialist to find a wig or hairpiece that looks natural and feels comfortable.

Step 4: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help to promote hair growth. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.

Step 5: Reduce Stress

Stress can contribute to hair loss. Try to reduce stress by getting regular exercise, practicing relaxation techniques, and getting enough sleep.

Tips

Here are some tips to help stop hair falling out from alopecia:

1. Be Patient

It can take time for hair to regrow, so be patient with the process.

2. Protect Your Hair

Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair, as they can cause further damage.

3. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to stimulate hair growth.

FAQ

Q: Can alopecia be cured?

A: There is no cure for alopecia, but there are treatments available that can help to stimulate hair growth.

Q: Is hair loss from alopecia permanent?

A: Hair loss from alopecia can be permanent, but in some cases, hair can regrow on its own or with treatment.

Q: Can stress cause alopecia?

A: Stress can contribute to hair loss, including hair loss from alopecia.

Hair Care

If you are experiencing hair loss from alopecia, it is important to take care of your remaining hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause further damage to your hair.

3. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as it is less likely to cause damage.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

5. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and treatments, you can help to stop hair falling out from alopecia and promote hair growth. Remember to be patient and take care of your remaining hair to keep it healthy.
