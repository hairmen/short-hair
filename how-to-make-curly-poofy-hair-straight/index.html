How To Make Curly Poofy Hair Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly poofy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to tame those unruly locks. Luckily, there are several ways to make curly poofy hair straight that don't require a lot of time or effort. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to straighten your hair, including some tips and tricks that will help you achieve a sleek, straight look.

How to Make Curly Poofy Hair Straight

If you're looking to make your curly poofy hair straight, there are a few different methods you can try. Here are some of the most popular:

1. Blow Dryer and Round Brush

If you have a blow dryer and a round brush, this method is a great way to straighten your hair without using heat. Here's how:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  4. Take a section of hair and use the round brush to pull it taut.
  5. Blow dry the section of hair while using the brush to pull it straight.
  6. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way around your head.
  7. Once your hair is dry, apply a smoothing serum to help keep it straight.

This method may take some practice, but it's a great way to straighten your curly poofy hair without exposing it to excessive heat.

2. Flat Iron

If you have a flat iron, you can use it to straighten your curly poofy hair. Here's how:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  4. Take a section of hair and run the flat iron over it, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way around your head.
  6. Once your hair is straight, apply a smoothing serum to help keep it that way.

This method is quick and easy, but it does require the use of heat, so be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

3. Chemical Relaxer

If you're looking for a more permanent solution to your curly poofy hair, you can try a chemical relaxer. However, this method should only be performed by a professional hair stylist, as it can be very damaging to your hair if done incorrectly.

Tips for Straightening Your Curly Poofy Hair

Whether you're using a blow dryer and round brush or a flat iron, there are a few tips that can help you achieve a sleek, straight look:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make the process easier.
  • Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  • Apply a smoothing serum or oil to help keep your hair straight.
  • Consider using a hair straightening brush for a quick and easy solution.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making curly poofy hair straight:

Can I straighten my hair without using heat?

Yes, you can use a blow dryer and round brush to straighten your hair without using excessive heat.

Is it safe to use a chemical relaxer?

A chemical relaxer can be very damaging to your hair if done incorrectly, so it's best to have it done by a professional stylist.

How often should I straighten my hair?

You should avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage to your hair. Instead, try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Poofy Hair

In addition to straightening your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Try to air dry your hair whenever possible to minimize heat damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

By following these tips and using the methods we've outlined, you can make your curly poofy hair straight and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
