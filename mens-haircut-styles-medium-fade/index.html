Mens Haircut Styles Medium Fade A Relaxed Guide In 2023

Are you looking for a fresh new look for your hair? Do you want to try something trendy and stylish? Look no further than the medium fade haircut for men. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Medium Fade Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Styling Tips
  • FAQs

Description

The medium fade haircut is a popular style among men of all ages. It features short hair on the sides and back of the head that gradually blends into longer hair on the top. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on your preference. This style is versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways, from slick and polished to messy and textured.

How to Get a Medium Fade Haircut

If you're interested in getting a medium fade haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Be sure to bring in a photo or two of the style you're aiming for, so your stylist can get a clear idea of your vision.

Choosing the Right Fade

The next step is to decide on the right fade for your hair. A low fade will leave more hair on the sides and back, while a high fade will create a more dramatic contrast between the short and long hair. The medium fade is a happy medium that works well for most hair types and face shapes.

Deciding on the Top Length

Once you've chosen your fade, it's time to decide on the length of the hair on top. You can go for a short, buzzed look or a longer, textured style. Be sure to communicate your preferences clearly with your stylist.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a medium fade haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Sit in the barber's chair and discuss your preferences with your stylist.
  3. The stylist will begin cutting the sides and back of your hair with clippers, working from the bottom up.
  4. As they reach the top of the fade, they will switch to scissors to blend the hair seamlessly.
  5. The stylist will then move on to cutting the hair on top to your desired length and style.
  6. Once the cut is complete, your stylist will style your hair using products like pomade or wax.

Styling Tips

Here are some tips for styling your new medium fade haircut:

  • Experiment with different products to find the right one for your hair type and desired style.
  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.
  • Consider adding some texture by using a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.
  • Try different parting styles to mix things up.

FAQs

How often should I get a medium fade haircut?

It's recommended to get a medium fade haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Is the medium fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

The medium fade haircut works well for most hair types, but it may not be the best choice for very curly or thick hair.

Can I style my medium fade haircut differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! The medium fade is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit different occasions and moods.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some tips for keeping your medium fade haircut looking fresh and stylish:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair nourished and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a stylish and trendy medium fade haircut in no time. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist, experiment with styling products, and take good care of your hair for best results.
