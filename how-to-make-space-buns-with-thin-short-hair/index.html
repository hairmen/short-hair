How To Make Space Buns With Thin Short Hair

Written by William Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Space buns are a fun and stylish hairstyle that has become increasingly popular in recent years. However, for those with thin, short hair, it can be challenging to achieve the desired look. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can rock space buns with ease. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to make space buns with thin short hair and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Make Space Buns with Thin Short Hair

The key to making space buns with thin short hair is to add volume and texture to your hair. You can achieve this by using styling products such as mousse, texturizing spray, or dry shampoo. These products will help create volume and texture that will make it easier to style your hair into space buns.

Here's what you'll need to make space buns:

  • Elastic hairbands
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray
  • Styling products (mousse, texturizing spray, or dry shampoo)

Step by Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to make space buns with thin short hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying your styling products to create volume and texture.
  2. Divide your hair into two equal sections, one on each side of your head.
  3. Take one section of hair and twist it tightly, then wrap it around itself to create a bun. Secure the bun with an elastic hairband.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the other section of hair.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair and to adjust the shape of your buns.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep your buns in place.

And voila! You now have space buns with thin short hair.

Tips for Making Space Buns with Thin Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you make space buns with thin short hair:

  • Use a hair donut or hair padding to add more volume and shape to your buns.
  • Add accessories such as scrunchies, bows, or hair clips to make your space buns more stylish.
  • If you have trouble creating buns, try braiding your hair and wrapping it around itself to create a bun.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs about Space Buns with Thin Short Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about making space buns with thin short hair:

Can I make space buns with very short hair?

Yes, you can still make space buns with very short hair. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place and add volume with styling products.

Can I make space buns with thin hair?

Yes, you can still make space buns with thin hair. Use styling products to create volume and texture, and consider using a hair donut or padding to add more shape to your buns.

How do I keep my space buns in place?

Use bobby pins and hairspray to keep your space buns in place. You can also try using elastic hairbands that match your hair color to hide them.

Hair Care for Thin Short Hair

It's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for thin short hair:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients to promote hair health.

With these tips and techniques, you can make space buns with thin short hair and rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
