Is Crimped Hair In Style 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you wondering whether crimped hair is still in style in 2020? The answer is yes! In fact, crimped hair has been making a comeback in recent years, and it’s a trend that’s not going away anytime soon. If you’re looking to switch up your hairstyle and try something new, crimped hair is definitely worth considering.

What is Crimped Hair?

Crimped hair is a hairstyle that involves creating small, zigzag waves in the hair. This is typically done using a crimping iron or a special styling tool. The resulting texture is unique and eye-catching, and it can add a lot of volume and dimension to your hair.

How to Crimp Your Hair

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start crimping your hair, it’s important to make sure it’s clean and dry. You should also apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the crimping iron.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you’ll want to section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to crimp your hair evenly and ensure that you don’t miss any sections. You can use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Crimp Your Hair

Take one section of hair and clamp it between the crimping iron plates. Hold the iron in place for a few seconds, then release it and move on to the next section. Repeat this process until you’ve crimped all of your hair.

Step 4: Finish Your Look

Once you’ve crimped your hair, you can finish your look with a bit of hairspray or other styling products to help it hold its shape. You can also add accessories like hair clips or headbands to complete your look.

Tips for Crimping Your Hair

If you’re new to crimping your hair, there are a few tips that can help you get the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure even crimping.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don’t hold the crimping iron in one spot for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different crimping iron sizes to achieve different looks.

FAQ

Is crimped hair in style in 2020?

Yes, crimped hair is definitely in style in 2020. It’s a unique and eye-catching hairstyle that can add a lot of dimension and volume to your hair.

What is the best crimping iron to use?

There are many different crimping irons available, and the best one for you will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Look for a crimping iron with adjustable heat settings and ceramic plates for the best results.

Can I crimp my hair if it’s already curly?

Yes, you can definitely crimp your hair if it’s already curly. This can create a unique and interesting texture that can add a lot of dimension to your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Crimped Hair

If you’re crimping your hair regularly, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and keep it looking healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Don’t wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh styling products that can cause buildup on your hair.

Overall, crimped hair is definitely still in style in 2020, and it’s a fun and unique way to switch up your hairstyle. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy crimped look.
