Inverted Triangle Face Shape Celebrities How To Highlight Your Best Features

Are you one of the lucky few with an inverted triangle face shape? If so, you may be wondering how to highlight your best features and achieve a balanced look. We've rounded up some of the most famous inverted triangle face shape celebrities to inspire your next haircut, makeup look, or fashion choice.

Description

The inverted triangle face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. It's also sometimes called a heart-shaped face, although heart-shaped faces may have a wider chin than an inverted triangle shape. Celebrities with this face shape include:

  • Reese Witherspoon
  • Scarlett Johansson
  • Celeste Barber
  • Victoria Beckham
  • Kristen Stewart

If you have an inverted triangle face shape, you may want to emphasize your cheekbones and downplay your forehead. You can achieve this through hairstyles, makeup techniques, and fashion choices.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure whether you have an inverted triangle face shape, here's how to find out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point.
  3. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point.
  4. Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point.
  5. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

If your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you probably have an inverted triangle face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Flattering Your Face Shape

Here are some tips for highlighting your best features if you have an inverted triangle face shape:

  1. Choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layered cuts or soft waves. Avoid styles that add height to the top of your head, such as high ponytails or slicked-back buns.
  2. Contour your cheekbones with a matte bronzer or blush to make them stand out. Use a light hand and blend well to avoid harsh lines.
  3. Highlight the inner corners of your eyes and the center of your forehead with a shimmery highlighter to draw attention to your eyes and downplay your forehead.
  4. Wear tops that draw attention to your shoulders to balance out your wider forehead. Off-the-shoulder tops, boat necks, and halter tops are all great options.
  5. Choose earrings that add volume to the bottom of your face, such as statement hoops or chandelier earrings. Avoid studs or small earrings that can make your face look top-heavy.

Tips for Hairstyles, Makeup, and Fashion

Here are some more tips for flatter your inverted triangle face shape:

  • Hairstyles: Long bobs, side-swept bangs, and messy updos are all great options for inverted triangle face shapes.
  • Makeup: Use a light hand when applying foundation and concealer to avoid emphasizing your forehead. Play up your eyes with winged eyeliner and mascara.
  • Fashion: A-line skirts, wrap dresses, and wide-leg pants can help balance out your wider forehead. Avoid turtlenecks and crew necks that can make your face look more top-heavy.

FAQ

Can I wear my hair in a middle part?

Yes, but it may emphasize your wider forehead. Consider adding some volume to the sides of your hair to balance it out.

What kind of sunglasses are best for an inverted triangle face shape?

Round or oval frames can help soften your wider forehead. Avoid frames that are wider than your cheekbones.

What kind of neckline should I look for in tops?

Off-the-shoulder, boat necks, and halter tops can all help balance out your wider forehead.

Hair Care for Inverted Triangle Face Shapes

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and taking inspiration from some of the most iconic inverted triangle face shape celebrities, you can emphasize your best features and achieve a balanced and flattering look.
