How To Cut A Curly Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and low-maintenance haircut? Look no further than the curly mullet. This hairstyle has been making a comeback in recent years and is a great option for those with curly hair. In this article, we'll show you how to cut a curly mullet in easy-to-follow steps. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain your new 'do and answer some common questions about the style.

Table of Contents

  • How To Cut a Curly Mullet
  • Tips for Cutting a Curly Mullet
  • FAQs About the Curly Mullet
  • Hair Care for a Curly Mullet

How To Cut a Curly Mullet

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need a few tools, including:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties

Once you have your tools, follow these steps to cut your curly mullet:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Use a hair tie to secure the top section on the crown of your head. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to keep the hair in place, but not so tight that it creates a bump.

Step 2: Cut the Underlayer

Using your scissors, cut the bottom layer of your hair to your desired length. The length will depend on your preference, but a good rule of thumb is to keep it shorter than the top layer. Use the comb to help guide your cuts and make sure they're even.

Step 3: Cut the Top Layer

Release the top section of your hair and comb it forward. Use your scissors to cut the ends at an angle, following the shape of your head. For a more dramatic mullet, you can leave the top layer longer than the bottom layer. For a more subtle look, keep the layers closer in length.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

To blend the two layers together, use the scissors to make small, diagonal cuts at the point where the two layers meet. This will create a more gradual transition between the lengths.

Step 5: Trim the Sides

If you want a more defined mullet, you can use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down, taking care to blend the hair at the sides and back into the longer top layer.

Tips for Cutting a Curly Mullet

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect curly mullet:

  • Start with dry hair. It's easier to see the natural shape of your curls when your hair is dry.
  • Don't cut too much at once. You can always go back and trim more later.
  • Take your time. Cutting curly hair requires patience and attention to detail.
  • Ask for help if you need it. It can be difficult to cut the back of your own head, so don't be afraid to ask a friend or family member for assistance.

FAQs About the Curly Mullet

What is a curly mullet?

A curly mullet is a hairstyle that features longer hair on top and shorter hair on the bottom. The style is characterized by its dramatic length difference and its signature "party in the back" look.

What face shape is best for a curly mullet?

A curly mullet can work for a variety of face shapes, but it's especially flattering for those with round or heart-shaped faces. The longer top layer can help elongate the face, while the shorter bottom layer adds volume.

Can you style a curly mullet?

Absolutely! A curly mullet can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy and textured to sleek and polished. Use a curl defining product to enhance your natural curls, or straighten your hair for a more dramatic look.

Hair Care for a Curly Mullet

To keep your curly mullet looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to damp hair to enhance your natural curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your mullet looking fresh and tidy.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut a curly mullet with ease. Have fun experimenting with this trendy and versatile hairstyle!
