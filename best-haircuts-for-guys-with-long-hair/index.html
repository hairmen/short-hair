Best Haircuts For Guys With Long Hair

Written by William Oct 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair has been a popular trend for guys in recent years. However, it can be challenging to find the right haircut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we will guide you through some of the best haircuts for guys with long hair, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

The first step in getting the best haircut for guys with long hair is to find a professional hairstylist who specializes in long haircuts. Discuss with them your hair texture, face shape, and personal style to determine the best haircut that suits you. You can also bring some pictures of haircuts you like to give them an idea of what you want.

It is also essential to maintain your long hair by regularly washing it with shampoo and conditioner. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair and avoid using hot tools frequently to prevent damage.

Step By Step

Here are some of the best haircuts for guys with long hair:

1. The Man Bun

The man bun is a classic long haircut that has been popular for many years. To achieve this look, tie your hair into a bun at the top of your head. You can also add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or wax.

2. Long Wavy Layers

If you have wavy hair, long wavy layers can enhance your natural texture and give you a relaxed, beachy look. You can also add some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

3. The Ponytail

A ponytail is a simple and easy long haircut that suits any face shape. To achieve this look, tie your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head or on the side. You can also add some volume to your hair by teasing it at the crown.

4. The Long Side Part

The long side part is a classic haircut that suits any face shape. To achieve this look, part your hair to the side and let it fall naturally. You can also add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or wax.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your long hair:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product to prevent buildup.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or twice a week.

Q: Can I still style my long hair if I have a receding hairline?
A: Yes, you can still style your long hair if you have a receding hairline. You can try a side-swept fringe or a messy textured look to create the illusion of fuller hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain your long hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently to prevent damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat and UV rays.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from within.

Overall, the best haircut for guys with long hair is the one that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. With the right haircut and proper hair care, you can rock your long hair and look your best.
