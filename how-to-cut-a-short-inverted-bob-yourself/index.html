How To Cut A Short Inverted Bob Yourself Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and want to try something new? Why not try a short inverted bob? This chic and trendy hairstyle can be easily achieved at home with just a few tools and some patience. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut a short inverted bob yourself.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A short inverted bob is a hairstyle where the hair is shorter in the back and longer in the front. It's a great way to add some volume and texture to your hair while still keeping it short and manageable. This hairstyle is perfect for those with fine or thin hair, as it can help create the illusion of more volume.

How To

Cutting a short inverted bob yourself may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror

Step by Step

Follow these steps to achieve a short inverted bob:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into two parts, the back and the front.
  3. Start with the back section. Comb your hair straight down and cut it straight across. Use the shorter length as a guide for the rest of the back section.
  4. Move on to the front section. Comb your hair forward and cut it at an angle, starting from the back and working your way towards the front. This will create the inverted effect.
  5. Continue cutting until you have achieved your desired length.
  6. Trim any uneven pieces.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your short inverted bob cutting experience easier:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to longer ones. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will appear longer than it actually is.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and time-consuming.

FAQ

Can I cut a short inverted bob on curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a short inverted bob on curly hair. However, keep in mind that the length of your hair will appear shorter when it's dry than when it's wet. Take this into consideration when cutting your hair.

How often should I cut my short inverted bob?

You should cut your short inverted bob every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Is it easy to cut a short inverted bob yourself?

It may take some practice, but cutting a short inverted bob yourself is definitely doable. Just make sure to follow the steps carefully and take your time.

Hair Care

After cutting your short inverted bob, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your short inverted bob.

Cutting a short inverted bob yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and trendy hairstyle in the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be rocking a short inverted bob in no time!
