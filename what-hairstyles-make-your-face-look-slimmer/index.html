What Hairstyles Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Daniel Aug 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a way to slim down your face without going under the knife? Well, the answer may be as simple as changing up your hairstyle! Certain hairstyles can make your face appear slimmer and more defined. In this post, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles that can make your face look slimmer and how to achieve them.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

The first step in achieving a slimming hairstyle is to choose a style that complements your face shape. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that elongate your face, such as long layers or a side-swept bang.
  • If you have a square face, choose a hairstyle that softens your features, such as soft waves or a layered bob.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, go for a style that creates balance between your forehead and chin, such as a chin-length bob or long layers.
  • If you have an oval face, lucky you! Almost any hairstyle will flatter your face shape.

Of course, these are just general guidelines. The best way to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape is to consult with a professional stylist.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few ways to determine it:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face onto the mirror. Compare the shape to the four general face shapes mentioned above.
  • Take a selfie and use an app or online tool to analyze your face shape.
  • Ask a friend or family member with an eye for detail to help you determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles that Flatter Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, do some research to find hairstyles that flatter your features. Pinterest, Instagram, and Google are great resources for hairstyle inspiration.

Step 3: Consult with a Professional Stylist

When in doubt, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, fits your lifestyle, and complements your personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving a Slimming Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, it's time to learn how to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with a clean slate by washing and conditioning your hair. Choose products that are formulated for your hair type and texture.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage. If you have fine or thin hair, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to add body and texture.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on lifting the roots for added volume. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural texture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using a curling iron or straightener, style your hair according to your chosen hairstyle. Don't forget to apply a finishing spray or serum to lock in your style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Slimming Hairstyle

Now that you've achieved your slimming hairstyle, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Invest in quality hair care products that are formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Aim to wash it every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair and absorb excess oil.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray or serum before styling.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle's shape.

Hair Care Tips for a Slimming Hairstyle

Maintaining a slimming hairstyle starts with proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Invest in quality hair care products that are formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Don't over-wash your hair. Aim to wash it every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair and absorb excess oil.
