How To Keep Long Hair Slicked Back Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long hair and want to keep it slicked back, you're in luck. This style is timeless and can be worn in a variety of settings. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, slicked-back hair is a chic and sophisticated look. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve and maintain this look with ease.

How To

To begin, start with clean, damp hair. Apply a strong hold gel or pomade to your hair, making sure to evenly distribute it throughout. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working your way towards the back. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

If you have particularly thick or curly hair, you may want to blow dry your hair straight before applying gel or pomade. This will create a smoother base and make it easier to achieve a sleek look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect slicked-back look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a strong hold gel or pomade
  3. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front
  4. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your fingers
  5. Optional: blow dry your hair straight for a smoother base

Tips

Here are a few tips to make sure your slicked-back hair stays in place all day:

  • Use a strong hold gel or pomade
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day
  • Use a hairspray to set your style in place
  • Consider using a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping long hair slicked back:

How often should I wash my hair to maintain this style?

It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent buildup of product and oils.

What's the best way to take down my slicked-back hair at the end of the day?

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup. You may also want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is key to maintaining any style. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to keep your long hair slicked back with ease. Remember to take care of your hair and use high-quality products to achieve the best results.
