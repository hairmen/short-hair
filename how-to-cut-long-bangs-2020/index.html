How To Cut Long Bangs 2020 A Step By Step Guide With Tips And Hair Care

If you're looking to update your look with a fresh cut, why not try adding some long bangs? Long bangs can add volume, frame your face, and give you a new style without a drastic haircut. Here's our step-by-step guide on how to cut long bangs in 2020, including tips and hair care advice.

How to Cut Long Bangs 2020

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. It's also a good idea to have a picture of the style you want to achieve, so you have a visual guide.

When it comes to cutting long bangs, it's important to go slowly and trim small amounts at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's been cut. Start with dry hair, since wet hair can shrink as it dries and you may end up cutting more than you intended.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut long bangs:

  1. Section off your hair: Use hair clips to separate the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should be at the top of your head, and the point should be at the center of your forehead.
  2. Start with small cuts: Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to trim small amounts at a time. Cut in a straight line across the hair, making sure both sides are even.
  3. Check your progress: Release the hair and comb it straight. Check your progress in the mirror to make sure the length is even and the bangs are the shape you want.
  4. Repeat and adjust: Continue cutting small amounts and checking your progress until you achieve the desired length and shape.

Tips for Cutting Long Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting long bangs:

  • Start with small cuts: You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's been cut.
  • Use sharp scissors: Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve a clean cut.
  • Cut in a straight line: Use the comb as a guide to make sure you're cutting in a straight line.
  • Don't cut too short: It's better to start with longer bangs and work your way shorter, so you don't end up with bangs that are too short.
  • Trim regularly: Long bangs can grow quickly, so trim them regularly to maintain the style.

FAQ

How long should my long bangs be?

The length of your long bangs depends on your face shape and personal style. As a general rule, they should fall between your eyebrows and your eyelashes.

Can I cut my own long bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own long bangs, but it's important to go slowly and trim small amounts at a time. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting your long bangs, don't panic. You can always adjust the length or shape by cutting more or using hair clips to hide the mistake until it grows out.

Hair Care for Long Bangs

Now that you've cut your long bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Clean hair looks and feels better.
  • Blow dry with a round brush: Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.
  • Use a heat protectant: If you use heat styling tools, like a flat iron or curling iron, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim regularly: As mentioned earlier, long bangs can grow quickly, so trim them regularly to maintain the style.

Cutting long bangs is a great way to update your look without a drastic haircut. With our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect long bangs in 2020. Remember to go slowly, trim small amounts at a time, and take care of your hair to keep your bangs looking their best.
