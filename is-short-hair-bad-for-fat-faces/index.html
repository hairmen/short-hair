Description

There is a common misconception that short haircuts are not suitable for people with fat faces. However, this is not entirely true. Short haircuts can enhance your facial features and create a more flattering look, even if you have a round or chubby face.

The key to finding the right short haircut for fat faces is to choose a style that complements your facial features and balances out your proportions. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a chic and stylish look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

How to Determine the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape

Before choosing a short haircut for your fat face, it is important to determine your face shape. This will help you determine which hairstyles will complement your facial features and create a balanced look.

There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in a mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, compare these measurements to the following guidelines:

  • Round face: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width
  • Oval face: forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, and the face is longer than it is wide
  • Square face: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, and the jawline is angular
  • Heart face: forehead is wider than the jawline, and the face tapers at the chin
  • Diamond face: forehead and jawline are narrow, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face
  • Long face: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, and the face is longer than it is wide

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Short Haircut for Fat Faces

Once you have determined your face shape, you can use the following guidelines to choose the right short haircut for your fat face:

  • Round face: opt for a short haircut that adds height and volume to the crown of your head. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. A layered bob or pixie cut can be a great option.
  • Oval face: you can pull off almost any short haircut. Consider a blunt bob or a pixie cut with long, side-swept bangs.
  • Square face: choose a short haircut that softens your angular jawline. A layered bob or a side-swept pixie cut can be a great option.
  • Heart face: opt for a short haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and creates the illusion of width at the jawline. A textured pixie cut or a layered bob can be a great option.
  • Diamond face: choose a short haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face and creates the illusion of width at the temples. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option.
  • Long face: opt for a short haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face and creates the illusion of width. A layered bob or a textured pixie cut can be a great option.

Tips for Styling Short Hair on Fat Faces

Now that you have chosen the right short haircut for your fat face, it is important to know how to style it to achieve the best look. Here are some tips to help you style short hair on a fat face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a sleek, polished look.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture and definition to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for fat faces:

  • Is short hair bad for fat faces? No, short haircuts can be flattering for fat faces if you choose the right style.
  • What is the most flattering short haircut for fat faces? It depends on your face shape. Refer to the guidelines above to determine the best short haircut for your face shape.
  • What should I avoid when choosing a short haircut for my fat face? Avoid short haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Can I still have bangs with a short haircut? Yes, you can. Side-swept bangs can be a great option for short haircuts on fat faces.

Hair Care for Short Hair on Fat Faces

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for caring for short hair on fat faces:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

With these tips and guidelines, you can confidently choose the right short haircut for your fat face and style it to perfection. Remember, the key to a great haircut is confidence and self-assurance, so wear your new hairstyle with pride and rock it!
