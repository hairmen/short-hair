Hairstyles For Oblong Face Thin Hair Tips Tricks And Ideas

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face and thin hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many women with oblong faces and thin hair find it challenging to style their hair in a way that flatters their face shape and adds volume. Luckily, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll share some tips, tricks, and ideas to help you find the ideal hairstyle for your oblong face and thin hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before diving into hairstyles, it's essential to determine your face shape to find the most flattering haircut. Here's how to determine if you have an oblong face shape:

  • Your face is longer than it is wide.
  • Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Your face has a narrow appearance and is more extended than round.

Hairstyles for Oblong Face Thin Hair

Now that you know your face shape let's explore some hairstyles that are perfect for women with oblong face shapes and thin hair:

1. Bob With Blunt Bangs

A bob cut that hits just above the shoulders with blunt bangs can help shorten the appearance of an oblong face. Adding a blunt fringe can also make your hair look thicker and fuller.

2. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can add volume to thin hair and give the appearance of more fullness. The layers can also help frame your face and draw attention to your best features.

3. Long Layers

Long layers that frame your face can help soften your features and add volume to thin hair. You can also add side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a shorter face.

4. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob that hits just above the shoulders can add texture and depth to thin hair. The waves can also help create the illusion of a wider face.

5. Side-Parted Bob

A side-parted bob can help create the illusion of a shorter face while adding volume to thin hair. Adding layers to the cut can also give the appearance of fuller hair.

Tips for Styling Thin Hair

Styling thin hair can be challenging, but with these tips, you can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous:

  • Use volumizing products like mousse, root lifter, or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy hair products like oils and creams that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.
  • Use a round brush to create volume while blow-drying.
  • Try hair extensions or a hairpiece to add fullness to your hair.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is one that adds width to the face. Hairstyles with waves or curls, side-swept bangs, and layers can help create the illusion of a wider face.

What is the best hairstyle for thin hair?

The best hairstyle for thin hair is one that adds volume and texture. Layered cuts, blunt bobs, and pixie cuts are all great options for thin hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Caring for thin hair can help keep it healthy and prevent breakage. Here are some hair care tips for thin hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons that can damage your hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for thin hair to prevent breakage.

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles for oblong face thin hair, it's time to experiment and find the perfect look for you. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and looking its best.
