Best Hair For Oval Face 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval face, congratulations! You have one of the most versatile face shapes that can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the best hair for oval face 2020. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

Description

An oval face is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline being roughly the same width. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, the goal is to find a hairstyle that highlights your best features and complements the overall shape of your face.

How to Find the Best Hair for Oval Face 2020

When it comes to finding the best hair for oval face 2020, there are a few things to consider:

1. Face Shape

While an oval face can pull off almost any hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape when choosing a new hairstyle. For example, if you have a long face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add length to your face. Similarly, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in finding the best hair for oval face 2020. For example, if you have thick hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that thins out your hair to avoid adding too much volume to your face. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair.

3. Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in finding the best hair for oval face 2020. For example, if you have a busy lifestyle and don't have much time to style your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that you can easily maintain at home.

Step by Step Guide to Find the Best Hair for Oval Face 2020

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding the best hair for oval face 2020:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you have an oval face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Next, consider your hair texture. Do you have thick or fine hair? Straight or curly? Knowing your hair texture will help you choose a hairstyle that works best for your hair type.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair. If you have a busy lifestyle, opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle that you can easily maintain at home.

Step 4: Look for Inspiration

Look for inspiration online or in magazines. Save photos of hairstyles that you like and bring them to your hairstylist for reference.

Step 5: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Finally, consult with your hairstylist. They can offer advice on which hairstyles will work best for your face shape and hair type. They can also recommend products and styling techniques to help you maintain your new hairstyle.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hair for Oval Face 2020

Here are some tips for choosing the best hair for oval face 2020:

1. Emphasize Your Best Features

Choose a hairstyle that emphasizes your best features. For example, if you have high cheekbones, choose a hairstyle that pulls your hair away from your face to showcase your cheekbones.

2. Avoid Adding Length

If you have a long face, avoid hairstyles that add length to your face. Instead, choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face to balance out your features.

3. Add Volume to Fine Hair

If you have fine hair, choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair. This will make your hair appear thicker and fuller.

4. Thin Out Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, choose a hairstyle that thins out your hair to avoid adding too much volume to your face.

5. Consider Your Skin Tone

Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm hair colors like golden blondes and rich browns. If you have cool undertones, choose cool hair colors like ash blondes and cool browns.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for oval face?

The best hairstyle for an oval face is one that highlights your best features and complements the overall shape of your face. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.

Can oval face pull off short hair?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short hair. However, it's essential to consider your face shape when choosing a short hairstyle. For example, if you have a long face, you may want to avoid short hairstyles that add length to your face.

What haircuts to avoid for oval faces?

Avoid hairstyles that add length to your face if you have a long face. Similarly, if you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add width to your face.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Faces

Here are some hair care tips for oval faces:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Use the Right Products

Use products that are tailored to your hair type and texture to keep your hair healthy and manageable.

4. Protect Your Hair from Sun Damage

Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

5. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't overwash your hair as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the best hair for oval face 2020, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a new hairstyle.
