Bangs Make Me Look Younger A Guide To Youthful Haircuts

Written by William Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to freshen up your look and turn back the clock a bit, consider adding bangs to your hairstyle. Bangs have been a popular trend for years, and for good reason. Not only do they frame your face and highlight your features, but they also have a magical ability to make you look younger. In this article, we'll explore why bangs make you look younger, how to style them, and tips for caring for your new 'do.

Why Bangs Make You Look Younger

There are a few reasons why bangs can make you look younger. First, they cover up your forehead, which is often one of the first areas to show signs of aging. By hiding wrinkles or fine lines, bangs can instantly take years off your appearance. Additionally, bangs draw attention to your eyes, which are often considered the most youthful feature on your face. By highlighting your eyes, you can create a more vibrant, youthful look overall.

How to Style Your Bangs

The great thing about bangs is that they can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. Here are a few popular ways to style your bangs:

Straight Across

Straight across bangs are a classic look that works well on most face shapes. To achieve this style, simply trim your bangs so that they fall just above your eyebrows. Use a flat iron to smooth them out and create a sleek, polished look.

Side-Swept

Side-swept bangs are a more modern take on the traditional bang. To achieve this look, part your hair to one side and trim your bangs so that they fall diagonally across your forehead. This style works well on heart-shaped or oval faces.

Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs have been trending in recent years, and for good reason. This style features longer bangs that are parted down the middle, framing your face on either side. This look works well on square or round faces, as it can help to create more angles and definition.

Tips for Caring for Your Bangs

Once you've got your bangs styled just how you like them, you'll want to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips for caring for your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to keep them clean and free of oils.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Trim your bangs every few weeks to keep them at the right length.
  • Use a small amount of hair oil or serum to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

FAQ About Bangs

Here are a few common questions people have about getting bangs:

Will bangs work with my hair type?

Yes! Bangs can work with any hair type, whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair.

What if I don't like my bangs?

If you're not happy with your bangs, don't worry - they'll grow out eventually. In the meantime, you can always experiment with different styles or use bobby pins to pin them back.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

It's a good idea to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the right length. If you're comfortable doing it yourself, you can trim them at home with a pair of sharp scissors.

Conclusion

If you're looking for a way to freshen up your look and take a few years off your appearance, consider adding bangs to your hairstyle. With so many styles to choose from, you're sure to find a look that suits your face shape and personal style. Just remember to take good care of your bangs to keep them looking their best!
