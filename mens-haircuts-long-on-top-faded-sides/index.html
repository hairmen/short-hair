Mens Haircuts Long On Top Faded Sides A Style That Will Never Go Out Of Fashion

Written by Oliver Oct 04, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is both trendy and stylish, then you might want to consider mens haircuts long on top faded sides. This hairstyle has been around for years and has been loved by many men all around the world. In this blog post, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, its benefits, and how to take care of it.

Description

The mens haircuts long on top faded sides is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top while the sides are gradually faded. The hair is usually styled in a messy or slicked-back look, giving it a more relaxed and casual vibe. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to have a modern and trendy look without sacrificing their comfort and style.

How To Achieve This Look

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the mens haircuts long on top faded sides:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step in achieving this hairstyle is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. You can choose to have it long, medium, or short, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Fade Your Sides

Once you've decided on the length of your hair on top, it's time to fade the sides. You can ask your barber to do this for you or do it yourself if you have the right tools and experience. The fade should be gradual, starting from a shorter length at the bottom and gradually getting longer as it goes up.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

After you've achieved the desired length and fade, it's time to style your hair. You can use hair wax, pomade, or gel to achieve the look you want. For a more relaxed and casual look, you can style your hair in a messy or tousled look. For a more formal look, you can slick it back or part it on the side.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens haircuts long on top faded sides:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can make your hair look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircuts long on top faded sides:

Q: How long does this hairstyle last?

A: This hairstyle can last for several weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can I style my hair differently?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best.

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be achieved on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircuts long on top faded sides looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as hair dyes as they can damage your hair.

Overall, the mens haircuts long on top faded sides is a great hairstyle that is both trendy and stylish. With the right care and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking great for weeks to come.
