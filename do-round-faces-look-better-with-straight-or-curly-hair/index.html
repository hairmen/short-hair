Do Round Faces Look Better With Straight Or Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hair styling, one common question that often arises is whether round faces look better with straight or curly hair. The truth is, there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. It really depends on various factors such as the individual's facial features, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of both straight and curly hair for round faces, and provide some tips on how to style your hair to best suit your face shape.

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved features and a wide forehead and cheekbones. This face shape generally lacks sharp angles and has a circular shape, which can make it difficult to determine the best hairstyle. When deciding whether to go for straight or curly hair, it's important to consider how the hairstyle will balance out the facial features.

How to

Here are some tips on how to style your hair based on your face shape:

Straight Hair

Straight hair can help elongate the face and create the illusion of sharp angles. This can be particularly flattering for those with round faces as it adds some length to the face. However, straight hair can also emphasize the roundness of the face if it is cut too short or styled without any layers.

To style straight hair for a round face, it's best to opt for longer lengths with subtle layers that frame the face. A side part can also help create the illusion of length. To add some volume and texture, try using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.

Curly Hair

Curly hair can add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out the soft features of a round face. However, it's important to choose the right type of curls as tight, voluminous curls can make the face appear wider.

To style curly hair for a round face, it's best to go for loose, beachy waves or soft, bouncy curls. A layered haircut can also help add some dimension and shape to the hair. To maintain the curls, it's important to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance the curls.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair for a round face:

  1. Choose the right haircut based on your face shape and hair type.
  2. Opt for longer lengths with subtle layers for straight hair, or loose, beachy waves for curly hair.
  3. Add volume and texture to the hair with volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.
  4. Maintain the curls with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance the curls.
  5. Style the hair with a side part to create the illusion of length.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to style your hair for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to find what works best for you.
  • Consider consulting a hairstylist for personalized advice on the best haircut and style for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair for a round face:

  • Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a round face?
    Yes, you can still wear your hair straight if you have a round face. Just make sure to add some subtle layers to avoid emphasizing the roundness of the face.
  • What type of curls should I go for if I have a round face?
    It's best to go for loose, beachy waves or soft, bouncy curls to balance out the soft features of a round face.
  • Can I still wear bangs if I have a round face?
    Yes, but it's best to avoid blunt bangs as they can make the face appear wider. Opt for side-swept or wispy bangs instead.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you choose straight or curly hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy hair length.

In conclusion, whether you choose straight or curly hair ultimately depends on your personal style and hair type. However, by following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can find the perfect hairstyle to suit your round face.
