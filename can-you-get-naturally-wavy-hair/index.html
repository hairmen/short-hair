Can You Get Naturally Wavy Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you love the look of naturally wavy hair? Many people do, and for some, achieving that look is relatively easy. However, if you have naturally straight hair or have relaxed your hair, you may be wondering if it's possible to get naturally wavy hair. The answer is yes, but it may take some effort. In this article, we'll discuss how to get naturally wavy hair, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Naturally Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Instructions
  • Tips for Getting Naturally Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Naturally Wavy Hair

How to Get Naturally Wavy Hair

Getting naturally wavy hair can be achieved through a few different methods. These include:

Method 1: Braiding or Twisting

One way to get naturally wavy hair is to braid or twist your hair while it's wet and then let it dry. This will create a natural wave pattern in your hair. You can either braid your hair into one or multiple braids, or you can twist your hair into sections. This method works best on hair that is naturally curly or wavy, but it can also work on straight hair.

Method 2: Scrunching

Another way to get naturally wavy hair is to scrunch your hair while it's wet and then let it dry. To scrunch your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product to your wet hair and then use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. This will create a natural wave pattern in your hair.

Method 3: Heat Styling

If your hair is naturally straight or you want a more defined wave pattern, you can use heat styling tools to achieve naturally wavy hair. You can use a curling iron or wand to create waves in your hair or a straightener to create more of a beachy wave look.

Step by Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for getting naturally wavy hair:

Method 1: Braiding or Twisting

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's damp.
  2. Separate your hair into sections.
  3. Braid or twist each section of hair.
  4. Let your hair dry completely.
  5. Unbraid or untwist your hair and use your fingers to separate the waves.

Method 2: Scrunching

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's damp.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp using your hands.
  4. Let your hair dry completely without touching it.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to break up the waves.

Method 3: Heat Styling

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant product to your hair.
  3. Use a curling iron, wand, or straightener to create waves in your hair.
  4. Once you've created waves in all sections of your hair, use your fingers to separate the waves.

Tips for Getting Naturally Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for getting naturally wavy hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product to help define your waves.
  • Don't touch your hair while it's drying to avoid disrupting the wave pattern.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help enhance your natural waves.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: Can you get naturally wavy hair if you have relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can get naturally wavy hair if you have relaxed hair. However, it may take more effort to achieve the look since relaxed hair is typically straighter.

Q: How long does it take to get naturally wavy hair?

A: The amount of time it takes to get naturally wavy hair depends on the method you use and your hair type. Braiding or twisting your hair can take several hours to dry completely, while heat styling can be done in a matter of minutes.

Hair Care for Naturally Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for caring for naturally wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

With these tips and methods, you can achieve naturally wavy hair regardless of your hair type or if you have relaxed hair. Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you and enjoy your new naturally wavy locks!
