How To Style Natural Hair Without Products A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using products that weigh down your natural hair? Or maybe you just want to try something new and embrace your hair's natural texture? Whatever the reason may be, styling natural hair without products is possible and can still yield beautiful results. In this article, we will guide you through the process of styling natural hair without products.

How to Style Natural Hair Without Products

Styling natural hair without products may seem daunting at first, but it's actually quite simple. The key is to work with your hair's natural texture and use techniques that enhance its beauty. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots to avoid causing any unnecessary breakage.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help moisturize and define your natural curls or waves. You can make your own leave-in conditioner by mixing equal parts water and aloe vera gel in a spray bottle.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Here are a few techniques you can use:

  • Twist Out: Divide your hair into sections and twist each section, then let it dry overnight. In the morning, unravel the twists and fluff your hair to create volume.
  • Bantu Knots: Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a knot, then let it dry overnight. In the morning, unravel the knots and fluff your hair to create volume.
  • Pineapple: Gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a hair tie or scrunchie. This will help preserve your curls while you sleep.

Step 5: Finish with Oil

Apply a few drops of oil to your hair to add shine and seal in moisture. You can use coconut oil, olive oil, or any other oil that works well with your hair.

Tips for Styling Natural Hair Without Products

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling natural hair without products:

  • Work with your hair's natural texture instead of trying to change it.
  • Use gentle, natural products that won't weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by avoiding hot tools.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and preserve your curls.

FAQ

Can I style my natural hair without products?

Yes, you can style your natural hair without products. The key is to work with your hair's natural texture and use techniques that enhance its beauty.

What are some natural hairstyles I can try?

There are many natural hairstyles you can try, such as twist outs, bantu knots, pineapples, and more. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for your hair.

How can I keep my natural hair healthy?

To keep your natural hair healthy, make sure to wash and condition it regularly, detangle it gently, and avoid using heat tools. Also, protect your hair at night by using a silk or satin pillowcase.

Hair Care

Styling natural hair without products can be a great way to embrace your hair's natural texture and avoid using heavy products that weigh it down. However, it's still important to take care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly and protecting it from damage. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, healthy natural hair without relying on products.
