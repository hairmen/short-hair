Does Platinum Suits Everyone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you contemplating going platinum blonde but unsure if it will suit you? Platinum is a popular hair color choice that can instantly change your look. However, it's not a one-size-fits-all solution, and not everyone can pull off this shade. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about whether platinum suits everyone.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Platinum hair color is a pale, almost white shade of blonde that can make a bold statement. It's a popular choice for those seeking a dramatic change, but it's not suitable for everyone. This color requires a lot of maintenance and can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly.

Platinum hair color can be achieved through bleaching your hair to remove your natural pigment before applying the platinum shade. This process can take multiple sessions and require a lot of upkeep, as the color can fade quickly without proper care.

How to

The first step in determining if platinum suits you is to consider your skin tone. Platinum hair color best suits those with cool-toned skin, such as fair or medium skin with pink or blue undertones. Warm-toned skin, such as olive or dark skin with yellow or golden undertones, may not complement platinum hair as well.

Another factor to consider is your natural hair color. Platinum hair color is a drastic change, and those with darker natural hair may find it more challenging to achieve the desired result. This is because dark hair requires more sessions of bleaching, which can be damaging to your hair.

Step by step

If you've decided to go platinum, here are some steps to take to achieve the best results:

  • Consult with a professional stylist to determine if platinum is the right choice for you.
  • Prepare your hair by deep conditioning it to prevent damage during the bleaching process.
  • Undergo multiple sessions of bleaching to remove your natural pigment and achieve the desired shade of platinum.
  • Use a toner to remove any yellow or brassy tones from your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner regularly to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when going platinum:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner designed for blonde hair to keep your color vibrant.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

FAQ

Can anyone go platinum?

No, not everyone can pull off platinum hair color. It's best suited for those with cool-toned skin and lighter natural hair color.

Is platinum hair color damaging to your hair?

Bleaching your hair to achieve platinum hair color can be damaging if not done correctly. It's essential to deep condition your hair regularly and avoid over-processing it.

How often do I need to touch up my platinum hair?

Platinum hair color requires regular touch-ups to maintain the desired shade. This can be every four to six weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the color fades.

Hair Care

When you go platinum, it's essential to take care of your hair to prevent damage and keep your color looking vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Protect your hair from chlorine by wearing a swim cap or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, platinum hair color may not suit everyone, but it can be a dramatic and beautiful change for those who can pull it off. Consider your skin tone and natural hair color before making the decision to go platinum, and always consult with a professional stylist for the best results. With proper care and maintenance, platinum hair color can be a stunning and bold look.
