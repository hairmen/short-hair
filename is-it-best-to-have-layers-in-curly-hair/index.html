Is It Best To Have Layers In Curly Hair

Written by Robby Oct 21, 2023 · 4 min read

A common question among curly-haired individuals is whether or not layers are best for their hair. The answer? It depends on the individual’s hair type, texture, and personal preferences. In this blog post, we’ll explore the pros and cons of having layers in curly hair, as well as provide tips on how to style and care for layered curls.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Layer Curly Hair

Pros and Cons of Having Layers in Curly Hair

The benefits of having layers in curly hair include:

  • Enhanced volume and bounce
  • Reduced weight and frizz
  • More defined curls

However, layers can also have some drawbacks:

  • Shorter layers can create uneven curls
  • Over-layering can result in thinning hair
  • Difficult to style and maintain

Ultimately, the decision to have layers in curly hair should be based on personal preference and hair type. Those with thick, voluminous curls may benefit from layers, while those with fine or thinning hair may want to avoid them.

How to Layer Curly Hair

If you’re interested in layering your curly hair, it’s important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. Curly hair requires a different cutting technique than straight hair, as the curls can shrink and bounce back after being cut.

When layering curly hair, the stylist should focus on creating long, gradual layers that blend seamlessly into the rest of the hair. Short, choppy layers can create an uneven look and disrupt the natural curl pattern.

Step-by-Step Guide for Layering Curly Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide for layering curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blot your hair dry with a microfiber towel, being careful not to rub or tug at the curls.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance your curls.
  4. Part your hair where you normally would, and section it into four parts: two in the front and two in the back.
  5. Starting with one section, comb through your hair to detangle it.
  6. Using sharp, clean scissors, cut a small amount of hair at a time, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  7. Focus on creating long, gradual layers that blend into the rest of the hair.
  8. Repeat on the other three sections of your hair.
  9. Style your hair as usual.

Tips for Styling Layered Curly Hair

Styling layered curly hair can be a bit more challenging than styling straight hair, but with the right techniques and products, it can be done. Here are some tips for styling layered curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls, rather than a brush.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Scrunch your hair with a microfiber towel or t-shirt to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your curls.
  • Consider using a diffuser on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

FAQs about Layered Curly Hair

Q: Will layers make my hair look thinner?

A: If you over-layer your curly hair, it can create a thinner appearance. However, if done correctly, layers can actually enhance volume and bounce.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with layers?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with layers. Just make sure to secure all of the layers evenly.

Q: How often should I trim my layered curly hair?

A: It’s recommended to trim your curly hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain healthy ends.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Curly Hair

In addition to styling tips, there are also some important hair care tips to keep in mind when you have layered curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, or use a heat protectant spray if you do.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as it can weigh down your curls.

In conclusion, whether or not to have layers in curly hair is a personal decision that should be based on individual hair type and preferences. If you do decide to go for layers, make sure to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair and follow the proper styling and hair care techniques for the best results.
