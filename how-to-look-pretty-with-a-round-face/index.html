How To Look Pretty With A Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may feel like there are limited options when it comes to looking pretty. But fear not, there are plenty of ways to enhance your features and accentuate your natural beauty. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you look and feel your best.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • Understanding Your Face Shape
  • Makeup Tips
  • Fashion Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

One of the easiest ways to enhance your features is to make sure your hair is styled in a way that flatters your face shape. For round faces, it's best to avoid short hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for long layers or a shoulder-length cut that adds volume and lengthens your face.

Another great option for round faces is a side-swept bang, which helps to create angles and balance out the roundness of your face. And if you're looking to add some volume, try teasing the crown of your hair or using volumizing products.

Understanding Your Face Shape

Before we dive into makeup and fashion tips, it's important to understand your face shape. While every face is unique, there are generally six different face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, soft features, and a rounded chin. The goal when it comes to makeup and fashion is to create the illusion of length and angles, rather than emphasizing the roundness of your face.

Makeup Tips

When it comes to makeup, the key is to create depth and dimension. This means using darker shades to contour and lighter shades to highlight. Start by applying a foundation or BB cream that matches your skin tone, then use a bronzer or contour powder to define your cheekbones and jawline.

For eyes, you can create the illusion of length by applying eyeliner and eyeshadow in a winged shape, which helps to elongate the eyes. And for lips, try using a lip liner to create a defined shape and avoid shades that are too light or too dark, which can make your lips look smaller.

Fashion Tips

When it comes to fashion, the goal is to create a balanced silhouette. This means avoiding clothing that adds bulk to the sides of your body and opting for pieces that elongate and slim your figure.

Avoid anything too boxy or shapeless, and instead opt for pieces that nip in at the waist or create a defined shape. This could be a fitted blazer, a wrap dress, or high-waisted pants.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear short haircuts with a round face?

A: While it's generally best to avoid short haircuts that add width to the sides of your face, there are still plenty of options for short haircuts that flatter round faces. Talk to your hairstylist about finding a cut that works for you.

Q: What type of makeup should I avoid with a round face?

A: Avoid using too much blush on the apples of your cheeks, as this can emphasize the roundness of your face. It's also best to avoid using shimmery or glittery products on the center of your forehead and chin, as this can make those areas appear more prominent.

Q: Can I wear oversized clothing with a round face?

A: While oversized clothing can be comfortable and stylish, it's important to balance it out with more fitted pieces. Opt for a loose-fitting top paired with slim-fitting pants, or a flowy skirt paired with a fitted top.

Q: Should I avoid wearing hats with a round face?

A: Hats can be a great accessory for any face shape, including round faces. Just make sure to choose a hat that doesn't add too much height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear more round.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin. Remember, the key is to create balance and dimension, rather than emphasizing the roundness of your face. With the right hair, makeup, and fashion choices, you can look and feel your best every day.
