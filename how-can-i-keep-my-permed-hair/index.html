How Can I Keep My Permed Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Perming your hair can be a great way to add some volume and texture, but it also requires some upkeep. If you're wondering how to keep your permed hair looking its best, you're in the right place. Here, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you maintain your permed hair and keep it looking fabulous.

How to Keep Your Permed Hair

If you want to keep your permed hair looking great, you'll need to take some extra steps to care for it. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Moisturize Your Hair

Perming can be harsh on your hair, so it's important to keep it moisturized. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid Heat

Heat can damage your permed hair, so it's important to avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons. If you need to style your hair, use a diffuser on your blow dryer to help define your curls.

3. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your permed hair looks and feels. Look for products that are specifically designed for permed hair, like curl-enhancing shampoos and conditioners.

4. Protect Your Hair

When you're sleeping or swimming, it's important to protect your permed hair. Wear a silk or satin bonnet to bed to prevent frizz, and wear a swim cap when you're in the pool to protect your hair from chlorine.

Step by Step Guide to Keep Your Permed Hair

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. This will help define your curls and keep your hair healthy.

Step 2: Apply Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy. Leave the treatment on for at least 10 minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 3: Don't Use Heat

Avoid using heat on your permed hair, as it can damage your curls. If you need to style your hair, use a diffuser on your blow dryer to help define your curls.

Step 4: Use the Right Hair Products

Look for hair products that are specifically designed for permed hair, like curl-enhancing shampoos and conditioners. This will help keep your curls looking their best.

Step 5: Protect Your Hair

When you're sleeping or swimming, wear a silk or satin bonnet to bed to prevent frizz, and wear a swim cap when you're in the pool to protect your hair from chlorine.

Tips for Keeping Your Permed Hair

Here are some extra tips to help you keep your permed hair looking fabulous:

1. Don't Brush Your Hair

Brushing your permed hair can cause frizz and damage your curls. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wash your hair every other day, or even less often if your hair is particularly dry.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

FAQs About Permed Hair

Q: How long does a perm last?

A: A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Q: Can I color my permed hair?

A: Yes, you can color your permed hair, but it's important to wait at least two weeks after your perm before coloring your hair.

Q: Can I swim with permed hair?

A: Yes, you can swim with permed hair, but it's important to wear a swim cap to protect your hair from chlorine.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Perming your hair can be a great way to add some volume and texture, but it also requires some extra care. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your permed hair looking fabulous and healthy.

Remember to moisturize your hair, avoid heat, use the right hair products, and protect your hair when you're sleeping or swimming. With a little bit of extra care, you can keep your permed hair looking its best.
