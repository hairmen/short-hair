How To Tame Big Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Sep 01, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you have big, curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to manage. From frizz to tangles, it can be tough to keep your hair looking its best. Fortunately, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to tame your hair and keep it looking fabulous. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to manage big curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tame Big Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Big Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Big Curly Hair

How to Tame Big Curly Hair

The first step in taming big curly hair is to find the right products. Look for a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair, as these products will help to moisturize and define your curls. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help control frizz and add definition to your curls.

Another important step in managing big curly hair is to avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry. This can cause your hair to become frizzy and can lead to breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to manage your curls, consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This tool will help to dry your hair without disrupting your curls, leaving you with a defined and voluminous style.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to taming big curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair.
  2. After washing, use a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help control frizz and add definition to your curls.
  3. Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers while it's still wet.
  4. If you're in a hurry, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a styling cream or serum to define your curls and add shine.

Tips for Managing Big Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips for managing big curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause it to become frizzy.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Try sleeping on a satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and tangles.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing big curly hair:

Q: Can I brush my curly hair?

A: It's best to avoid brushing your curly hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair find that they only need to wash their hair once a week, while others prefer to wash it more frequently. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Big Curly Hair

Here are a few additional hair care tips for managing big curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Try using a silk or satin scarf to wrap your hair at night, which can help to prevent frizz and tangles.

With the right products and techniques, managing big curly hair can be a breeze. Whether you're looking to add volume, define your curls, or control frizz, there are plenty of options available. Experiment to find what works best for you, and enjoy your beautiful, curly locks!
