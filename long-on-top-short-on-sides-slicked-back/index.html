Long on Top Short on Sides Slicked Back - A Relaxed Guide

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, then the long on top short on sides slicked back look might be perfect for you. This hairstyle has been popular for years and is still going strong in 2023. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Description

The long on top short on sides slicked back hairstyle is a classic look that has been popular for decades. It features longer hair on the top of the head that is slicked back, while the sides and back are cut short. This gives the hairstyle a clean and polished look that is perfect for any occasion.

How to Get the Long on Top Short on Sides Slicked Back Look

The first step to achieving the long on top short on sides slicked back look is to find a good stylist who is familiar with the style. You'll want someone who has experience with this type of haircut and who can advise you on the best way to achieve the look based on your hair type and face shape.

Choosing the Right Length

The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Generally, the hair on the top of your head should be at least 4-6 inches long, while the sides and back should be much shorter, around 1-2 inches. The longer the hair on top, the more dramatic the slicked back look will be.

Choosing the Right Products

Once you have the right haircut, the next step is to choose the right products to style your hair. You'll need a good quality pomade or hair wax to help you achieve the slicked back look. Look for a product that provides a strong hold and a natural shine, and avoid anything that is too greasy or heavy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the long on top short on sides slicked back look:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair product to your fingertips and work it through your hair, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward.
  4. Use a comb to slick back the hair on the top of your head, starting at the crown and working your way forward. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  5. Use the comb to create a part on one side of your head, and then slick back the hair on either side of the part.
  6. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways, and then use the comb to create a clean line between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  7. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  8. Use a small amount of hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long on top short on sides slicked back hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back of your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides slicked back hairstyle:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the long on top short on sides slicked back hairstyle can work for all hair types, but it may require different products and styling techniques depending on your hair type.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides and back of your hair looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and skin looking great.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect long on top short on sides slicked back hairstyle and keep it looking great for years to come!
