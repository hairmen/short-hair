How To Blow Dry Curly Hair Guys Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Robby Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to blow dry curly hair for guys. We will cover the basics of blow drying, some tips and tricks, and some frequently asked questions. We hope that after reading this article, you will be able to achieve the desired look for your curly hair.

Description

Blow drying curly hair can be a daunting task for many guys. Curly hair is naturally dry and prone to frizzing, which can make it difficult to style. But with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a smooth, defined look for your curls. Blow drying can help to add volume, shape, and definition to your curls, and can also help to reduce frizz and enhance shine.

How to Blow Dry Curly Hair Guys

The following steps will guide you on how to blow dry curly hair guys:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Wet Hair

Before you start blow drying your hair, make sure it is clean and wet. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated and nourished. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start blow drying your hair, apply a heat protectant product to help protect your curls from heat damage. This will also help to reduce frizz and enhance shine.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up to the top sections.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer. A diffuser is a great tool for blow drying curly hair as it helps to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. It also helps to enhance the natural curl pattern of your hair.

Step 5: Start Blow Drying

Start blow drying your hair on a low to medium heat setting. Use the diffuser to gently scrunch your curls as you blow dry. This will help to enhance the natural curl pattern of your hair and add volume. Avoid using a high heat setting as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Step 6: Finish with a Cool Shot

Once you have finished blow drying your hair, use the cool shot button on your blow dryer to set your curls. This will help to reduce frizz and enhance shine.

Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair Guys

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when blow drying your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls before blow drying.
  • Don't use a towel to dry your hair as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out any excess water.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while blow drying as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream after blow drying to help keep your curls hydrated and defined.

FAQs

Q: Can I blow dry my hair every day?

A: Blow drying your hair every day can cause damage and dryness. It is best to limit blow drying to once or twice a week and use a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting.

Q: What is the best blow dryer for curly hair?

A: Look for a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment and multiple heat settings. The Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer and the DevaCurl DevaDryer are both great options for curly hair.

Q: How can I reduce frizz when blow drying my curly hair?

A: Use a heat protectant product, a diffuser attachment, and avoid using a high heat setting. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream after blow drying to help reduce frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Guys

Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as this can cause damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define and hydrate your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Protect your curls from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swimming cap when swimming.

Blow drying curly hair guys can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a smooth, defined look for your curls. Remember to use a diffuser attachment, a heat protectant product, and avoid using a high heat setting to help reduce frizz and enhance shine. Follow these tips and tricks and take care of your curly hair to keep it looking healthy and defined.
