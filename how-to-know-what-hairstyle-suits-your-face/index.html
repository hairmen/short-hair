How To Know What Hairstyle Suits Your Face

Written by Petter Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle but aren't sure what will suit your face shape, you're not alone. It can be overwhelming to choose from the countless hairstyles out there, but with a bit of guidance, you can find the perfect one for you. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to know what hairstyle suits your face.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hairstyle
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is determining your face shape. There are several face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Here's how to determine yours:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face is as wide as it is long, and your cheeks are the widest part of your face.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your face is as wide as it is long, and your jawline is strong and square.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is pointed.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, here's how to choose the right hairstyle:

  1. Consider your hair texture. Do you have thick, curly hair, or fine, straight hair?
  2. Think about your lifestyle. Do you want a low-maintenance hairstyle, or are you willing to spend time styling your hair?
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines and online galleries for hairstyles that appeal to you and that would work with your face shape.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can give you personalized advice on what hairstyle would suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing your hairstyle:

  • Consider your age. Some hairstyles may be more flattering at certain ages than others.
  • Think about your hair color. Some hairstyles may look better with certain hair colors.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. If you're not sure if a certain hairstyle will work for you, try it out and see how you feel.
  • Consider your face features. For example, if you have a prominent nose, you may want to avoid hairstyles that draw attention to it.

FAQs

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's possible to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, you can use the guidelines for each face shape and choose a hairstyle that flatters all of your features.

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle even if it's not recommended for my face shape?

Of course! Ultimately, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know how to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, it's time to book that appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look!
