How to Get a Short Fade Haircut

A short fade haircut is a trendy haircut that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top longer. The hair on the sides and back gradually fades into the skin, creating a clean and sharp look. To get a short fade haircut, you will need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in cutting men's hair. You can bring a picture of the style you want to show your barber or stylist, or you can ask them for recommendations based on your face shape and hair type. It's important to communicate clearly with your barber or stylist to ensure that you get the desired result.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Fade Haircut

1. Choose the type of fade you want: There are different types of fades, including low fade, mid fade, and high fade. Choose the type of fade that suits your face shape and hair type.

2. Decide on the length of the hair on top: The length of the hair on top can vary based on your personal preference. You can go for a longer or shorter style, depending on the look you want to achieve.

3. Consult with your barber or stylist: Talk to your barber or stylist about the style you want to achieve and ask for their recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

4. Sit back and relax: Let the barber or stylist work their magic and create the perfect short fade haircut for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Fade Haircut

1. Schedule regular haircuts: Short fade haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. You should visit your barber or stylist every 2-3 weeks to get a touch-up.

2. Use the right hair products: Use hair products that are designed for your hair type and style. Consult with your barber or stylist to find out which products are best for your hair.

3. Wash and condition your hair regularly: Keep your hair clean and healthy by washing and conditioning it regularly.

4. Protect your hair from damage: Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.

FAQs About Men's Short Fade Haircuts 2020

1. Who can get a short fade haircut?

Short fade haircuts can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. However, the style may not be suitable for men with very curly or thick hair.

2. How long does a short fade haircut last?

Short fade haircuts require regular maintenance, so they typically last 2-3 weeks before needing a touch-up.

3. Can I style my short fade haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your short fade haircut in different ways, depending on the length of the hair on top and your personal preference. You can use hair products to create different looks, such as a messy or slicked-back style.

4. How do I choose the right type of fade?

The type of fade you choose should be based on your face shape and hair type. Low fades are ideal for men with round faces, while high fades are better suited for men with long faces. Mid fades are a good option for most face shapes.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Short Fade Haircuts

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.

2. Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness.

3. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness.

4. Use a comb or brush to style your hair and remove tangles.

5. Consider getting a hair trimmer to maintain your short fade haircut between visits to the barber or stylist.

Men's short fade haircuts 2020 are a stylish and trendy choice for those who want to look sharp and fashionable. By following the tips and advice in this guide, you can get a short fade haircut that suits your face shape and hair type, and maintain it to keep it looking sharp and stylish.
