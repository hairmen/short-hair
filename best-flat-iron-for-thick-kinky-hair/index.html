Best Flat Iron For Thick Kinky Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours trying to straighten your thick kinky hair? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best flat iron for thick kinky hair, how to use it, and tips for maintaining healthy hair. Let's get started!

  • How to Use

Description

When it comes to finding the best flat iron for thick kinky hair, there are a few key factors to consider. First and foremost, the flat iron should have high heat settings to effectively straighten thick hair. Additionally, it should have plates that are wide enough to accommodate large sections of hair.


After researching and testing various flat irons, we have determined that the best flat iron for thick kinky hair is the BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron.


Key Features:

  • Titanium plates for even heat distribution
  • Ultra-thin design for easy handling
  • Five-inch plates for fast styling
  • 50 heat settings up to 450℉

With its titanium plates and high heat settings, the BaBylissPRO flat iron is able to straighten even the thickest and kinkiest hair with ease. Additionally, its ultra-thin design makes it comfortable to hold and maneuver, while its five-inch plates allow for efficient styling.

How to Use

Before using any flat iron, it is important to properly prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Next, towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. This will help to prevent damage and breakage caused by high heat.


Once your hair is prepped, plug in your flat iron and select the appropriate heat setting for your hair type. For thick kinky hair, we recommend using a higher heat setting, around 400-450℉.


Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Using hair clips, section your hair into manageable parts. This will make it easier to straighten each section and ensure that you do not miss any areas.


Step 2: Start at the Roots

Starting at the roots, clamp the flat iron onto a small section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process on each section of hair until all of your hair is straightened.


Step 3: Finish with a Serum

Once your hair is straightened, apply a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz. This will help to keep your hair looking sleek and smooth throughout the day.


Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Using a flat iron regularly can cause damage to your hair if not used properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while using a flat iron:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Do not use the flat iron on wet hair
  • Do not leave the flat iron in one area of your hair for too long
  • Use a serum or oil to add moisture and protect your hair
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent excessive damage

FAQ

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

A: No, using a flat iron on wet hair can cause damage and breakage. Always make sure your hair is completely dry before using any heat styling tools.


Q: How often should I use a flat iron on my hair?

A: It is best to limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent excessive damage. Try using a flat iron once or twice a week, and use other styling methods, such as braids or twists, in between.


Q: How do I clean my flat iron?

A: To clean your flat iron, wait until it has cooled down completely. Then, use a damp cloth or cotton swab to wipe away any product buildup or residue. Do not use any harsh chemicals or abrasive materials on your flat iron.


Hair Care Products

In addition to using a high-quality flat iron, using the right hair care products can also help to maintain healthy hair. Here are some products that we recommend:

  • Shampoo and conditioner for thick, kinky hair
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Serum or oil for added moisture and shine

With the right flat iron and hair care routine, you can achieve sleek, straight hair without sacrificing the health of your hair. Try the BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron and follow our tips for maintaining healthy hair for the best results!
