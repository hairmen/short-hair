What Was The Clothing Style In The 60S

Written by William Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about the clothing style in the 60s. This decade was a time of change, and it was reflected in the clothing people wore. In this article, we will explore what the clothing style was like in the 60s and give you some tips on how to recreate the look today.

Introduction to the 60s Clothing Style

The 60s were a time of rebellion and change. This was reflected in the clothing people wore, which was characterized by bright colors, bold prints, and unconventional designs. Women's clothing was often tight-fitting and short, while men's clothing was more relaxed and comfortable.

Women's Clothing in the 60s

Women's clothing in the 60s was characterized by mini skirts, shift dresses, and bold patterns. The "mod" look was popular, which featured bright colors, geometric patterns, and bold accessories. Women also wore tight-fitting pants and low-cut blouses, which were seen as a form of rebellion against traditional gender roles.

Men's Clothing in the 60s

Men's clothing in the 60s was more conservative than women's clothing, but it still reflected the changing times. Men wore slim-fitting suits and trousers, often in bold colors and patterns. Casual clothing like polo shirts and khakis also became popular, especially among younger men.

How to Recreate the 60s Look

Recreating the 60s look is easy with the right clothing and accessories. Look for bold, bright colors and patterns, and don't be afraid to mix and match different styles. Accessorize with oversized sunglasses, chunky jewelry, and colorful scarves to complete the look.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the 60s Look

To achieve the perfect 60s look, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a bold, bright-colored outfit.
  2. Add accessories like oversized sunglasses and chunky jewelry.
  3. Style your hair in a bouffant or beehive style.
  4. Apply bold makeup, like winged eyeliner and bright lipstick.

Tips for Recreating the 60s Look

Here are some tips for recreating the 60s look:

  • Shop at vintage stores for authentic 60s clothing.
  • Use accessories like scarves and hats to add interest to your outfit.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match patterns and colors.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, like a French twist or a bob.

FAQ About the 60s Clothing Style

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 60s clothing style:

  • Q: What were popular fabrics in the 60s?
  • A: Popular fabrics in the 60s included polyester, cotton, and rayon.
  • Q: What kind of shoes were popular in the 60s?
  • A: Platform shoes, go-go boots, and Mary Janes were popular in the 60s.
  • Q: What kind of jewelry was popular in the 60s?
  • A: Chunky, oversized jewelry was popular in the 60s.

The Importance of Hair in the 60s Look

Hair was an important part of the 60s look, especially for women. Popular hairstyles included the bouffant, beehive, and pixie cut. Men often wore their hair longer and styled it in a more relaxed, natural way.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect 60s Hairstyle

To achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle, follow these tips:

  • Use a lot of hairspray to hold your style in place.
  • Invest in a good teasing comb to add volume to your hair.
  • Consider getting a wig if you don't want to cut or style your hair.

Conclusion

The clothing style in the 60s was a reflection of the changing times. With bold colors, patterns, and unconventional designs, it was a time of rebellion and self-expression. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can recreate the 60s look and bring a bit of that rebellious spirit into your wardrobe today.
