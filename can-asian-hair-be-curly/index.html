Can Asian Hair Be Curly

Written by Robby Nov 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Asian hair is known for its straight and silky texture, but many people wonder if it can be curly. The answer is yes, Asian hair can be curly, but it may take some effort and proper hair care to achieve the desired look. In this blog post, we will discuss how to get curly hair as an Asian, step-by-step, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Description

Asian hair is typically straight and thick, due to the shape of the hair follicle. However, there are some Asians who have naturally curly hair. The type of hair you have depends on your genetics, so if your parents have curly hair, there’s a chance you may have it too. But if you were not born with curly hair, there are ways to get it.

How to get curly hair as an Asian

If you want to get curly hair as an Asian, there are several methods you can try:

1. Use a perm

A perm is a chemical process that changes the shape of your hair. It can be used to make straight hair curly, and it’s a popular method for Asians who want curly hair. However, perms can be damaging to your hair, so it’s important to find a stylist who knows how to do it properly.

2. Use a curling wand or curling iron

You can also use a curling wand or curling iron to create curls in your hair. This method is temporary and does not involve any chemicals. You can use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Braid your hair

Another way to get curly hair is to braid your hair when it’s wet and leave it overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you’ll have natural-looking curls. You can use a mousse or hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Step by Step

If you’re interested in getting curly hair, here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Determine your hair type

The first step is to determine your hair type. Asian hair can be thick, fine, or coarse, and the type of hair you have will affect the method you use to get curly hair.

Step 2: Choose a method

Once you know your hair type, you can choose a method for getting curly hair. You can use a perm, curling wand, or braid your hair.

Step 3: Find a stylist or do it yourself

If you’re using a perm, it’s important to find a stylist who knows how to do it properly. If you’re using a curling wand or braiding your hair, you can do it yourself.

Step 4: Care for your curly hair

After you’ve achieved curly hair, it’s important to take care of it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting and maintaining curly hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause damage and frizz. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Avoid heat styling tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause frizz. If you must use a heat styling tool, use a heat protectant spray first.

3. Use a diffuser

A diffuser can help distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer when you’re drying your curly hair.

FAQ

1. Is it safe to use a perm on Asian hair?

Yes, it’s safe to use a perm on Asian hair, but it’s important to find a stylist who knows how to do it properly. Perms can be damaging to your hair if they’re not done correctly.

2. How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows.

3. Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it’s important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet. This will prevent breakage and damage.

2. Don’t wash your hair too often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause damage and frizz. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

In conclusion, Asian hair can be curly, and there are several methods you can try to achieve the look. Whether you use a perm, curling wand, or braid your hair, it’s important to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and frizz-free.
