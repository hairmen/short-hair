Mens Low Fade Short Haircut The Classic And Timeless Hairstyle

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that's both classic and contemporary at the same time? Look no further than the mens low fade short haircut. This timeless hairstyle has been around for decades, and it's still as popular as ever today. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect mens low fade short haircut, as well as some tips on how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Mens Low Fade Short Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Low Fade Short Haircut
  • FAQs

Description

The mens low fade short haircut is characterized by a gradual tapering of hair length from the top of the head down to the neck. This gradual taper is achieved by using clippers to gradually fade the hair from a longer length at the top to a shorter length at the bottom. The result is a clean, sharp look that's perfect for any occasion.

How to Get the Mens Low Fade Short Haircut

If you're thinking about getting the mens low fade short haircut, the first step is to find a good barber or hair stylist who is experienced in this style. Once you've found the right person, it's important to communicate clearly about the type of haircut you want.

Choose Your Fade Level

One of the key decisions you'll need to make when getting a mens low fade short haircut is how low you want the fade to go. A low fade typically starts just above the ear, while a high fade starts closer to the temple. Choose the level of fade that works best for your face shape and personal style.

Choose Your Hair Length

Another important decision is how short you want your hair to be. The mens low fade short haircut typically has longer hair on top, which can be styled in a variety of ways, while the sides and back are much shorter. You can choose to keep your hair relatively long on top, or go for a more conservative length.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the mens low fade short haircut:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

Before your haircut, make sure you shampoo and condition your hair. This will help your stylist work with your hair more easily.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Your stylist will start by cutting the top of your hair to the desired length. This is usually longer than the sides and back.

Step 3: Create a Guide Line

Your stylist will use clippers to create a guide line around the sides and back of your head. This guide line will be used to create the fade.

Step 4: Gradually Fade the Hair

Using clippers, your stylist will gradually fade the hair from the top down to the guide line. This will create a smooth transition from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Your stylist will use scissors and clippers to blend the hair on the sides and back, creating a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 6: Trim and Style the Top

Finally, your stylist will trim and style the hair on top, creating the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Low Fade Short Haircut

To keep your mens low fade short haircut looking sharp, here are a few tips to follow:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy

FAQs

How long does a mens low fade short haircut last?

A mens low fade short haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

What type of hair works best for a mens low fade short haircut?

A mens low fade short haircut works best for people with straight or wavy hair.

Can I style my mens low fade short haircut in different ways?

Absolutely! The beauty of the mens low fade short haircut is that it's versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Hair Care for Mens Low Fade Short Haircut

To keep your mens low fade short haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, which can damage your hair
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place

With these tips, you can keep your mens low fade short haircut looking sharp and stylish for weeks to come.

So, what are you waiting for? Book an appointment with your stylist today and get the classic and timeless mens low fade short haircut.
