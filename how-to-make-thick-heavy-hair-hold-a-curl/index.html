How To Make Thick Heavy Hair Hold A Curl Tips Tricks And Hair Care

If you have thick, heavy hair, you know that it can be difficult to get curls that will last all day. But fear not! With the right tips and tricks, you can make your curls stay put. In this article, we will discuss how to make thick heavy hair hold a curl, step by step instructions, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

How to Make Thick Heavy Hair Hold a Curl

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help give your hair some lift and make it easier to curl.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using any heat styling tools. This will help prevent damage to your hair and keep it healthy.

Step 3: Use a curling iron with a barrel size of at least 1 inch. This size will give you loose, natural-looking curls that will last longer than tighter curls.

Step 4: Take small sections of hair and curl them away from your face. Hold the curling iron in place for about 10 seconds, then release the curl.

Step 5: After curling each section, use a clip to hold the curl in place while it cools. This will help the curl set and last longer.

Step 6: Once you have curled all of your hair, wait for it to cool completely before brushing it out. This will help the curls hold their shape and last longer.

Tips

Tip 1: Use a hairspray specifically designed for holding curls. Look for one that is labeled "firm hold" or "extra hold."

Tip 2: Don't be afraid to use a lot of hairspray. This will help keep your curls in place and prevent them from falling out throughout the day.

Tip 3: If you have particularly stubborn hair that won't hold a curl, try using a smaller barrel size on your curling iron.

FAQ

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: With the right products and techniques, your curls should last all day or even into the next day.

Q: Can I curl my hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can use foam rollers or flexi rods to curl your hair without heat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Tip 2: Don't use heat styling tools on your hair every day. Give your hair a break by air-drying it or using heat-free styling techniques.

Tip 3: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This will help prevent breakage and damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your thick heavy hair hold a curl all day long. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
