Is Silver Hair In Style 2021 The Ultimate Guide To Rocking The Trend

Written by Robby Nov 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hair game this year? Silver hair may just be the way to go! In this guide, we’ll be exploring the trend of silver hair in 2021, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it. Let’s dive in!

Description

Silver hair has been a popular hair trend for a few years now, and it’s only growing in popularity. It’s a bold and edgy look that can be achieved in a variety of ways, from a full head of silver to silver highlights or ombre. Silver hair is a versatile trend that can be tailored to fit any style, from edgy to elegant.

How to Achieve Silver Hair

The process of achieving silver hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you’re starting with dark hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Bleach Your Hair

In order to achieve silver hair, your hair needs to be lightened to a very pale blonde. This means you’ll need to bleach your hair. It’s important to note that bleaching can be damaging to your hair, so it’s best to leave this step to a professional stylist.

Step 2: Tone Your Hair

After bleaching, your hair may have yellow or brassy tones. To achieve the silver look, you’ll need to tone your hair with a purple or blue toner. This will neutralize any unwanted tones and give your hair a cool, silver hue.

Step 3: Maintain Your Silver Hair

Once you’ve achieved your desired silver shade, it’s important to maintain it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for silver hair to prevent fading and yellowing. You may also want to consider using a purple or blue toning shampoo to maintain the cool silver tone.

Tips for Rocking Silver Hair

Now that you’ve achieved your silver hair, it’s time to rock it! Here are some tips for styling and caring for your silver locks:

Tip 1: Experiment with Different Styles

Silver hair is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Play around with different styles, from edgy to elegant, to find what works best for you.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Heat styling can be damaging to your hair, especially if it’s already been bleached. Protect your hair by using a heat protectant spray or by air-drying whenever possible.

Tip 3: Embrace the Fade

Silver hair may fade over time, but that doesn’t mean you have to touch it up constantly. Embrace the fade and let your hair evolve naturally.

FAQ

Q: Will silver hair work for all hair types?

A: Yes! Silver hair can work for all hair types, but it may be more difficult to achieve on dark or thick hair.

Q: Is silver hair high maintenance?

A: Yes, silver hair can be high maintenance. It requires regular toning and maintenance to prevent fading and yellowing.

Q: Can I achieve silver hair at home?

A: It’s possible to achieve silver hair at home, but it’s recommended to leave the bleaching to a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Silver Hair

Now that you’ve achieved your silver hair, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy silver hair:

TIP 1: Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo and Conditioner

Sulfates can be harsh on your hair and can cause fading and yellowing. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for silver hair to maintain its color.

TIP 2: Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Heat styling can be damaging to your hair, especially if it’s already been bleached. Protect your hair by using a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.

TIP 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

TIP 4: Deep Condition Once a Week

Deep conditioning once a week can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use a hydrating hair mask or leave-in conditioner to add moisture to your hair.

TIP 5: Avoid Chlorine

Chlorine can cause your hair to turn green or yellow, especially if it’s been bleached. Avoid swimming in chlorinated pools or wear a swim cap to protect your hair.

Overall, silver hair is a bold and edgy trend that’s here to stay. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can rock the silver hair trend with confidence!
