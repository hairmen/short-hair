How To Lengthen A Too Short Dress

Written by Daniel Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having to throw away your favorite dresses because they are just too short? Well, worry no more! In this article, we will show you how to lengthen a too short dress in a few easy steps. Whether you need to add just a few inches or several, these tips will help you transform your dress into a new and improved version.

Description

Short dresses can be cute and flirty, but they can also be impractical for certain occasions. Maybe you bought a dress online and it turned out to be shorter than you expected or you've had a dress for a while and it's become too short due to wear and tear. Whatever the reason, you don't have to give up on your favorite dress just yet. With a few simple tricks, you can give your dress a new lease on life.

How to Lengthen a Too Short Dress: Step by Step

Step 1: Find the right fabric

The first step in lengthening a too short dress is to find a fabric that matches the dress. This can be a challenging task, but it's important to find a fabric that matches the color, texture, and weight of the original dress as closely as possible. You can either buy a matching fabric from a craft store or use fabric from an old dress or piece of clothing that you no longer wear.

Step 2: Measure and cut the fabric

Next, measure the length you want to add to the dress and cut the fabric accordingly. Make sure to leave a little extra fabric on each end for hemming.

Step 3: Sew the fabric to the dress

Pin the fabric to the bottom of the dress and sew it in place, making sure to match the seams as closely as possible. Use a sewing machine or hand-sew the fabric to the dress, depending on your skill level and preference.

Step 4: Hem the dress

Once the fabric is sewn in place, hem the dress to give it a finished look. You can either hand-sew the hem or use a sewing machine if you have one.

Tips for Lengthening a Too Short Dress

  • Choose a fabric that matches the color and texture of the dress as closely as possible.
  • Be careful not to stretch the fabric while sewing it to the dress, as this can cause puckering.
  • Practice hemming on a scrap piece of fabric before hemming the dress to ensure a neat, professional finish.

FAQs

Can I lengthen any dress?

It's possible to lengthen most dresses, but it depends on the style and construction of the dress. Dresses with a simple, straight design are usually the easiest to lengthen, while dresses with a lot of detail or a complicated shape may be more difficult.

Do I need to be an expert seamstress to lengthen a dress?

No, you don't need to be an expert seamstress to lengthen a dress. Basic sewing skills are all you need, and there are plenty of tutorials and videos online to help guide you through the process.

Can I take a lengthened dress back to its original length?

Yes, it's possible to remove the added fabric and take a lengthened dress back to its original length. However, this will require some additional sewing and may not be as simple as the lengthening process.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing a Lengthened Dress

When wearing a lengthened dress, it's important to consider how your hair will look with the new length. If you've added several inches to the dress, you may want to wear your hair up or in a sleek ponytail to balance out the new length. If you've only added a few inches, you can wear your hair down or in a loose braid for a more relaxed look.

By following these tips, you can easily lengthen a too short dress and give it a new lease on life. With a little bit of sewing and some creativity, you can transform your favorite dress into a stylish and practical piece that you can wear for years to come.
