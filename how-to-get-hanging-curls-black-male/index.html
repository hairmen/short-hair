How To Get Hanging Curls Black Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a black male with curly hair looking to achieve hanging curls? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get hanging curls black male. With proper care and maintenance, you can achieve the curly look you desire.

Description

Hanging curls are a popular hairstyle for black males. They are characterized by tight, defined curls that hang down from the head. Achieving this hairstyle requires proper care and maintenance of curly hair. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve the look you desire.

How to Get Hanging Curls Black Male

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

The first step to achieving hanging curls is to shampoo and condition your hair. Look for products specifically designed for curly hair. Shampoo your hair twice to ensure that all dirt and product buildup is removed. Apply conditioner and leave it in for several minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After shampooing and conditioning your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and prevent frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Next, apply a curl enhancing product to your hair. This will help to define your curls and give them the bounce and volume you desire. Use a dime-sized amount of product and distribute it evenly through your hair.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to prevent frizz and maintain the definition of your curls. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a pick or wide-tooth comb to fluff out your curls and create volume. You can also apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your hair from drying out and breaking.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve hanging curls if I have relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve hanging curls with relaxed hair. However, you may need to use additional products and techniques to maintain the curl definition.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every 7-10 days, depending on your hair type and level of activity.

Q: How can I prevent my curls from becoming frizzy?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner and curl enhancing product, and avoid using heat styling tools. You can also use a hair oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Hair Care

To maintain your hanging curls, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a deep conditioner once a week to hydrate your curls and prevent breakage. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.

With proper care and maintenance, you can achieve hanging curls black male. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the curly hairstyle you desire.
