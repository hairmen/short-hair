Description

Slicked back hair is a timeless hairstyle that can be worn by both men and women. It's a versatile look that can be achieved by using a variety of products, but what is the best thing to use to slick back hair? In this article, we will explore the different options and give you tips on how to achieve a perfect slicked back look.

How to Slick Back Hair

Before we dive into the best products to use for slicking back hair, let's talk about the basics of how to achieve the look. Slicking back hair requires clean, dry hair that is combed or brushed back and away from the face. The hair should be slicked back tightly to ensure it stays in place throughout the day.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to slick back hair:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.
  2. Apply your preferred product to your hair.
  3. Using a comb or brush, comb your hair back and away from your face.
  4. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  5. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a strong hold product to keep your hair in place.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Invest in a good quality comb or brush to ensure your hair is slicked back smoothly.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ about Slicking Back Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back hair:

What is the best product to use for slicking back hair?

The best product to use for slicking back hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Pomade, gel, and wax are popular choices, but you can also use hairspray or even hair oil for a more natural look.

Can slicking back hair cause hair loss?

Slicking back hair can cause tension on the hair follicles, which can lead to hair loss over time. It's important to avoid pulling your hair too tightly and to take breaks from slicked back hairstyles to give your hair a rest.

Can women slick back their hair?

Absolutely! Slicked back hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by both men and women. It's a great way to keep your hair out of your face and create a sleek, polished look.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

When wearing your hair slicked back, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some hair care tips for slicked back hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when slicking it back to prevent tension on the hair follicles.

By following these tips and using the best product for your hair type, you can achieve a perfect slicked back look that will last all day.
