How To Dry Wavy Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it’s easy to style and looks great with minimal effort. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to drying it. In this post, we’ll show you how to dry wavy hair guys, so you can look your best every day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Dry Wavy Hair Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Drying Wavy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Dry Wavy Hair Guys

Drying wavy hair can be tricky, but with the right technique, you can achieve a great look. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Use a Microfiber Towel

Start by using a microfiber towel to gently squeeze excess water from your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat tools, it’s important to protect your hair from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer is essential for drying wavy hair. It distributes the heat evenly and reduces frizz. Start by using the diffuser on a low heat setting, and work your way up to higher heat if necessary.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Drying your hair upside down will help create volume and enhance your natural waves. Use the diffuser to dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed guide on how to dry wavy hair guys:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause frizz and dryness.

Step 2: Blot Your Hair

Use a microfiber towel to gently blot your hair, removing excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and turn it on a low heat setting. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it in circular motions, working your way down to the ends of your hair.

Step 5: Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and continue to use the diffuser to dry your hair. Use your hands to scrunch your hair as you dry it, which will help enhance your natural waves.

Step 6: Finish with a Hair Spray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place throughout the day.

Tips for Drying Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when drying wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Blot your hair with a microfiber towel to remove excess water without causing damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.
  • Dry your hair upside down to enhance your natural waves and create volume.
  • Scrunch your hair as you dry it to enhance your waves.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place throughout the day.

FAQs

Can I dry my wavy hair with a regular towel?

Absolutely not. Rubbing your hair with a regular towel can cause damage and frizz. Use a microfiber towel to gently blot your hair instead.

Can I use a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment?

You can, but it’s not recommended. Using a hair dryer without a diffuser can cause frizz and damage to your hair. A diffuser attachment helps distribute the heat evenly and reduces frizz.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools every day, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily dry wavy hair guys and achieve a great look every day. Remember to take care of your hair with proper hair care and products, and you’ll have healthy, beautiful waves that everyone will envy.
