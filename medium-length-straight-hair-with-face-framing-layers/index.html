Medium Length Straight Hair With Face Framing Layers

Written by Aprilia Jun 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that combines the simplicity of medium length straight hair with the added dimension of face framing layers? Look no further than this versatile and easy-to-maintain style. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about rocking medium length straight hair with face framing layers, including how to achieve the look, tips for maintaining your style, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

Medium length straight hair with face framing layers is a classic and timeless style that flatters many face shapes and hair types. The haircut typically features layers that start at the chin or cheekbone and taper down to the ends, adding depth and movement to the hair while highlighting the cheekbones and jawline. This style works particularly well for those with fine or thin hair, as the layers can add volume and texture to the hair.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve this style, start by booking an appointment with a trusted hairstylist who has experience cutting face framing layers. During your appointment, communicate your desired length and layering techniques to ensure that you get the exact look you want. After your haircut, it's up to you to maintain your style by blow drying your hair straight and using a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways.

Step by Step

1. Book an appointment with a hairstylist who has experience cutting face framing layers.

2. Communicate your desired length and layering techniques to your hairstylist.

3. After your haircut, wash and condition your hair using products designed for straight hair.

4. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush.

5. Use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways.

6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length straight hair with face framing layers:

- Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or blow dryer to prevent damage to your hair.

- Schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your layers looking fresh.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.

FAQ

Q: Will face framing layers work for my hair type?

A: Face framing layers work well for many hair types, including fine, thin, and thick hair.

Q: How long does this style typically take to achieve?

A: The styling process typically takes around 30 minutes to an hour, depending on your hair length and thickness.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying and damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length straight hair with face framing layers looking its best, it's important to follow a healthy hair care routine. This includes using a shampoo and conditioner designed for straight hair, using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools, and avoiding over-processing your hair with color or chemical treatments. Additionally, be sure to schedule regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your layers.

Overall, medium length straight hair with face framing layers is a stylish and versatile style that's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet chic look. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy head of hair that's sure to turn heads.
