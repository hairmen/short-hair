How To Cut Spiky Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle? Look no further than the spiky haircut. This style has been popular for decades and is still a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut spiky haircut step-by-step, including tips and tricks for perfecting the look. Let’s get started!

How to Cut Spiky Haircut

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Brush
  • Hair gel or pomade

Make sure your tools are clean and sharp. Dull scissors or clippers can result in an uneven haircut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and comb it out. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Using your clippers, start cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use a shorter guard for a more dramatic look or a longer guard for a more subtle look. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to keep the hair in place.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Now it’s time to cut the top of your hair. Use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to create texture. Cut the hair shorter towards the back and longer towards the front.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Using a brush, style your hair upwards to create the spiky look. Apply hair gel or pomade to help hold the hair in place. Experiment with different styles until you find the perfect look.

Tips for Cutting a Spiky Haircut

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors and clippers
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time
  • Create texture by cutting at an angle
  • Experiment with different styles

FAQs

How often should I cut my spiky haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the style. Generally, a spiky haircut should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Can I cut a spiky haircut at home?

Yes, you can cut a spiky haircut at home with the right tools and techniques. However, if you’re unsure, it’s best to visit a professional barber or hairstylist.

What hair products should I use for a spiky haircut?

Use a hair gel or pomade to help hold the hair in place. Look for products that are specifically designed for spiky hairstyles.

Hair Care for Spiky Haircut

To keep your spiky haircut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to avoid damaging your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the style

Now that you know how to cut a spiky haircut, it’s time to give it a try. With a little practice and patience, you’ll be rocking a trendy and edgy hairstyle in no time.
