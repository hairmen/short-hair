How To Put Extensions In Thin Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin hair and want to add some volume and length, hair extensions can be a great solution. However, the process of putting extensions in thin hair can be a bit more challenging than with thicker hair. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to put extensions in thin hair.

How to Put Extensions in Thin Hair

Putting extensions in thin hair requires some extra care and attention. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Type of Extensions

There are different types of extensions available in the market, such as clip-in, tape-in, sew-in, and fusion. For thin hair, clip-in extensions are the best option. They are easy to apply and remove, and they do not put too much pressure on your hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before putting in the extensions, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Then, blow-dry your hair to create a smooth base for the extensions.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start from the bottom and work your way up. This will help you to apply the extensions evenly and avoid any tangles.

Step 4: Apply the Extensions

Take one weft of the extensions and clip it onto the root of the sectioned hair. Make sure the clip is secure and close to your scalp. Then, gently brush your hair to blend the extensions with your natural hair. Repeat this process with the rest of the wefts, working your way up.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have applied all the extensions, style your hair as desired. You can curl, straighten, or braid your hair to create different looks.

Tips for Putting Extensions in Thin Hair

Here are some tips that can help you to put extensions in thin hair:

  • Choose high-quality extensions that match your hair color and texture.
  • Avoid applying too many extensions as they can put a strain on your hair.
  • Use a gentle brush to avoid pulling or damaging your hair.
  • Do not sleep with your extensions on as this can cause tangling and breakage.
  • Take care of your extensions by washing them regularly and storing them properly.

FAQ

Q: Can I put extensions in thin hair without damaging it?

A: Yes, you can put extensions in thin hair without damaging it if you choose the right type of extensions and apply them carefully. Clip-in extensions are the best option for thin hair as they do not put too much pressure on your hair.

Q: How long do clip-in extensions last in thin hair?

A: Clip-in extensions can last up to 6 months in thin hair if you take good care of them. However, you should remove them every night before sleeping and avoid using heat styling tools excessively.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Thin hair requires special care to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips that can help you to take care of your thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can put a strain on your hair.

By following these tips and using the right hair extensions, you can add volume and length to your thin hair without damaging it.
