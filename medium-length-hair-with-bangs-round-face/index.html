Medium Length Hair With Bangs Round Face The Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Aprilia Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and want to change things up? If you have a round face, medium length hair with bangs could be the perfect hairstyle for you. This hairstyle is versatile, easy to maintain, and suits all ages. In this post, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect medium length hair with bangs for a round face. We will also provide you with tips and answers to some FAQs. So, let's get started!

Description

Medium length hair is hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. It is a popular length because it is easy to style and maintain. Bangs are hair that falls over the forehead. They are also known as fringes. Bangs can be cut in various styles, such as blunt, side-swept, or wispy. A round face is one that is widest at the cheeks and has a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that make your face look rounder. Instead, you should go for hairstyles that add length and angles to your face. Medium length hair with bangs is perfect for a round face because it creates angles and makes the face look slimmer.

How to Get Medium Length Hair with Bangs for a Round Face

Getting medium length hair with bangs for a round face is easy. Here's how:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Hairstylist

Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best bangs style for your face shape. They will also help you choose the right length for your medium length hair.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair before going to the salon. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair.

Step 3: Get Your Hair Cut

Get your hair cut to the desired length. Your hairstylist will also cut your bangs to the desired length and style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

You can style your hair in various ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly. You can also style your bangs in different ways, such as side-swept or blunt. Experiment to find the best style for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length hair with bangs:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair shiny and frizz-free.
  • Avoid using too much hair products as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

FAQs

1. Can I get medium length hair with bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can get medium length hair with bangs if you have curly hair. However, you should choose a bangs style that suits your curls.

2. Can I style my bangs differently every day?

Yes, you can style your bangs differently every day. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. However, if you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair with bangs healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly as it can cause breakage and damage.

So, there you have it, a complete guide to medium length hair with bangs for a round face. Follow these tips and steps to get the perfect hairstyle for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Flaunt your new hairstyle with confidence!
