Men s Haircut Long On Top Short In Back A Relaxed Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and trendy haircut, then the men's haircut long on top short in back might be the perfect hairstyle for you. This hairstyle is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head while the back and sides are trimmed short. It's a versatile and low-maintenance haircut that can suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short in Back

The men's haircut long on top short in back is relatively easy to achieve, especially if you have a skilled barber or hairstylist. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get this hairstyle:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of the top hair. You can go for a short or medium length, depending on your preference and hair type. If you have thick hair, you can go for a longer length to balance the volume.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Back

The next step is to trim the sides and back of your hair with clippers. You can choose to have a fade or undercut, depending on the style you want to achieve. Make sure to blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back for a seamless transition.

Step 3: Cut the Top Hair

Once the sides and back are trimmed, the hairstylist or barber can then cut the top hair to the desired length. You can choose to have layers or texture to add volume and movement to the hair. Don't forget to communicate your preferences and ask for recommendations from your hairstylist or barber.

Step 4: Style the Hair

The last step is to style the hair using a hair product that suits your hair type and desired look. You can go for a natural or messy look by using a texturizing paste or wax. Alternatively, you can slick back the hair for a more formal look using a pomade or gel.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short in Back

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's haircut long on top short in back:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting a touch-up every 4-6 weeks to keep the haircut looking fresh and stylish.

FAQs

Is the men's haircut long on top short in back suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the men's haircut long on top short in back can suit different face shapes. However, the hairstylist or barber can adjust the length and style to complement your face shape and features.

Can I style the men's haircut long on top short in back differently?

Yes, you can style the men's haircut long on top short in back differently depending on the occasion and your preference. You can slick back the hair for a formal look or leave it messy for a casual look.

Do I need to use a specific hair product for the men's haircut long on top short in back?

No, you can use different hair products depending on your hair type and desired look. However, it's recommended to use a product that provides hold and texture without making the hair look greasy or stiff.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Long on Top Short in Back

To maintain the health and appearance of the men's haircut long on top short in back, it's essential to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp condition.
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals that can damage the hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle the hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently or use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that provides essential nutrients for hair growth and health.

The men's haircut long on top short in back is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that can suit different preferences and lifestyles. By following the tips and maintaining a proper hair care routine, you can enjoy a fresh and trendy haircut that enhances your appearance and confidence.
