Simple Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 · 5 min read
If you have medium length hair, you might be wondering what kind of updo hairstyles you can create without too much fuss. Fortunately, there are plenty of simple and chic updos that you can do at home. In this article, we'll walk you through some of the best updo hairstyles for medium length hair, along with tips on how to achieve them and how to care for your hair.

Description

Medium length hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you're looking for an updo that's both easy and elegant. But with a few simple techniques, you can create a variety of updo styles that will work for any occasion. Whether you're headed to a wedding or just want to look polished for work, these updo hairstyles for medium length hair will help you achieve the perfect look.

How To

Before we dive into the step-by-step instructions for creating these updo hairstyles, it's important to prepare your hair properly. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry it until it's damp. Apply a bit of styling product to help hold your hair in place, such as a mousse or a styling cream. Now that your hair is prepped, you're ready to create your updo. Here are some of the best updo hairstyles for medium length hair:

1. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic updo that's perfect for medium length hair. To create this style, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun, then secure it with bobby pins. Loosen a few strands of hair around your face for a relaxed, messy look.

2. The Classic Chignon

For a more polished look, try the classic chignon. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun at the nape of your neck, then secure it with bobby pins. Use a comb to smooth any flyaways, and finish with a spritz of hairspray.

3. The Half-Up Top Knot

The half-up top knot is a trendy updo that's easy to create. Start by pulling the top half of your hair back into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun at the crown of your head, then secure it with bobby pins. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose for a more casual look.

Step-by-Step

Now that you know which updo hairstyles to try, let's go through the step-by-step instructions for creating each one.

The Messy Bun

1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. 2. Twist the ponytail into a bun. 3. Secure the bun with bobby pins. 4. Loosen a few strands of hair around your face for a relaxed, messy look.

The Classic Chignon

1. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail. 2. Twist the ponytail into a bun at the nape of your neck. 3. Secure the bun with bobby pins. 4. Use a comb to smooth any flyaways. 5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray.

The Half-Up Top Knot

1. Pull the top half of your hair back into a ponytail. 2. Twist the ponytail into a bun at the crown of your head. 3. Secure the bun with bobby pins. 4. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect updo: - Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look. - Don't be afraid to use hairspray to keep your updo in place. - If you have trouble with flyaways, try using a bit of hair gel or pomade. - Experiment with different updo styles to find the one that works best for your hair type and face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I create an updo with short hair? A: While it's harder to create an updo with short hair, it's not impossible. Try using bobby pins and hairspray to secure your hair in place, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Q: How do I care for my hair after creating an updo? A: To care for your hair after creating an updo, gently comb out any tangles and use a bit of conditioner to restore moisture. Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to follow a few basic hair care tips: - Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using heat styling tools too often. - Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals. - Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best. With these tips and techniques, you can create beautiful updo hairstyles for medium length hair that will turn heads wherever you go. So go ahead and experiment with different styles, and have fun creating the perfect updo for any occasion.
