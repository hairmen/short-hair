What Face Shape Do Curtain Bangs Look Good On

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking about getting curtain bangs but not sure if they will suit your face shape? Curtain bangs have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. They can add a soft and romantic touch to any hairstyle, and can be styled in multiple ways. In this article, we will discuss what face shape curtain bangs look good on, how to style them, and some tips and FAQs to help you decide if they are right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted down the middle, framing the face on either side. They are longer than traditional bangs and can be styled in a variety of ways. Curtain bangs can add a soft and romantic touch to any hairstyle and are versatile enough to suit different face shapes.

How to

Before deciding to get curtain bangs, it's important to know what face shape they look good on. Generally, curtain bangs look good on oval, round, heart-shaped, and square-shaped faces. They can also help balance out a long face shape.

Step by step

If you've decided to go for curtain bangs, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose the right length for your face shape.
  2. Decide on the width of the bangs.
  3. Part your hair down the middle.
  4. Hold the bangs straight down and cut them at an angle.
  5. Style them according to your preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting curtain bangs:

  • Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on what length and width would suit your face shape.
  • Consider your hair texture and whether curtain bangs will require more maintenance.
  • Experiment with different styling options to find what suits you best.

FAQ

What face shape do curtain bangs look good on?

Curtain bangs look good on oval, round, heart-shaped, and square-shaped faces. They can also help balance out a long face shape.

Can I style curtain bangs in different ways?

Yes, curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in multiple ways. You can wear them straight, wavy, or curled.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

It depends on your hair texture. If you have naturally straight hair, they may require less maintenance. However, if you have curly or wavy hair, you may need to style them more often.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your bangs with heat tools.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends.

Overall, curtain bangs can add a soft and romantic touch to any hairstyle. Before getting them, consider your face shape, hair texture, and maintenance requirements. With the right length and width, curtain bangs can suit different face shapes and can be styled in multiple ways.
