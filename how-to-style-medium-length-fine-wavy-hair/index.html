How to Style Medium Length Fine Wavy Hair

When it comes to styling medium length fine wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, it's important to use the right products and tools to achieve your desired look. Additionally, understanding your hair type and texture can help you choose the right style for your locks.

Some popular styles for medium length fine wavy hair include beachy waves, sleek and straight styles, and braids. To achieve these looks, you'll need to use the right products and tools, including a curling iron, flat iron, or hair straightener, and a variety of styling products such as mousse, hairspray, and texturizing spray.

Beachy Waves

To achieve beachy waves, start by washing your hair and applying a texturizing spray. Next, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. If you don't have a curling iron, you can also achieve this look by braiding your hair while it's damp and then letting it air dry. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate the waves and apply a bit of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Sleek and Straight

For a sleek and straight style, start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Next, use a flat iron or hair straightener to straighten your hair, working in small sections. Once your hair is straightened, apply a bit of hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

Braids

Braids are a great option for medium length fine wavy hair, as they can help to control frizz and add texture to your locks. To achieve this look, start by parting your hair down the middle and creating two equal sections. Braid each section, securing the ends with a hair tie. You can also create a variety of braids, including fishtail braids and Dutch braids, for a more intricate style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your medium length fine wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray.
  2. Choose your desired style, whether it's beachy waves, sleek and straight, or braids.
  3. Use the appropriate tools and products to achieve your desired look, such as a curling iron, flat iron, or texturizing spray.
  4. Once your hair is styled, apply a bit of hairspray or hair serum to hold the style in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Fine Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your medium length fine wavy hair:

  • Choose the right products for your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to use heat styling tools, but always use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

What are the best products for styling medium length fine wavy hair?

The best products for styling medium length fine wavy hair include texturizing spray, mousse, hairspray, and hair serum. It's important to choose products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

Is it okay to use heat styling tools on medium length fine wavy hair?

Yes, it's okay to use heat styling tools on medium length fine wavy hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Additionally, try to limit your use of heat styling tools to once or twice a week.

How often should I wash my medium length fine wavy hair?

It's recommended to wash your medium length fine wavy hair every two to three days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care for Medium Length Fine Wavy Hair

To keep your medium length fine wavy hair looking its best, it's important to follow a hair care routine that includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as deep conditioning treatments once a week. Additionally, try to limit your use of heat styling tools and always use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

By following these tips, tricks, and hair care advice, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your medium length fine wavy hair. Whether you prefer beachy waves, sleek and straight styles, or braids, there's a look for everyone.
