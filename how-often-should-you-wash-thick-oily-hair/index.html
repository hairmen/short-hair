How Often Should You Wash Thick Oily Hair

Written by Bardi Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick, oily hair, you may be wondering how often you should be washing it. With so much conflicting advice out there, it can be hard to know what to do. Some people say you should wash your hair every day, while others recommend washing it just once a week. So, what's the right answer?

Description

Thick, oily hair can be a challenge to manage. It can quickly become greasy and weighed down, making it difficult to style. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, while not washing it enough can leave it looking and feeling dirty. So, how do you find the right balance?

How To

The first step in figuring out how often to wash your hair is to understand your hair type. Thick, oily hair tends to produce more oil than other hair types, which means it can get greasy more quickly. However, washing it too often can actually make the problem worse. Here are some tips to help you find the right washing schedule:

Step 1: Start with once a week

If you're used to washing your hair every day or every other day, it may be hard to imagine going a whole week without washing it. However, starting with once a week can help your hair adjust to a new routine. Use a gentle shampoo that's designed for oily hair, and be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any buildup.

Step 2: Pay attention to your hair

After a week of washing your hair once, pay attention to how it looks and feels. Does it feel greasy? Does it look dirty? If so, you may need to wash it more often. If it still looks and feels clean, you can stick with once a week.

Step 3: Adjust as needed

Everyone's hair is different, so there's no one-size-fits-all answer to how often you should wash your hair. If you find that your hair is getting greasy too quickly, you may need to wash it more often. On the other hand, if you find that it's getting too dry or damaged, you may need to wash it less often.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your thick, oily hair looking its best:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any buildup
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Try using dry shampoo between washes to absorb excess oil
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to help protect and nourish your hair

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about washing thick, oily hair:

How often should I wash my hair if it's really oily?

If your hair is very oily, you may need to wash it every 2-3 days. However, be careful not to over-wash it, as this can lead to dryness and damage.

Can I use conditioner on oily hair?

Yes, you can use conditioner on oily hair, but be sure to choose a lightweight formula that won't weigh your hair down. Apply it only to the ends of your hair, and rinse thoroughly.

Should I use hot or cold water to wash my hair?

Cold water is best for washing oily hair, as it helps to close the hair cuticle and prevent excess oil production. Hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it even more oily.

Hair Care

Taking care of thick, oily hair can be a challenge, but with the right washing schedule and products, you can keep your hair looking and feeling its best. Be sure to pay attention to your hair's needs and adjust your routine as needed. With a little patience and experimentation, you can find the perfect routine for your hair type.
