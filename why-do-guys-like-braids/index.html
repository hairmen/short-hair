Why Do Guys Like Braids

Written by Daniel Nov 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Braids have been a popular hairstyle for centuries, and they continue to be a favorite among men and women alike. But why do guys like braids? Some guys find braids to be an attractive and unique hairstyle that sets them apart from the crowd. Braids can also be a low-maintenance hairstyle that allows guys to get up and go without spending a lot of time on their hair. Additionally, braids can be a practical hairstyle for guys who are active and need to keep their hair out of their face.

How to

If you're interested in trying out braids for yourself, there are a few different ways to wear them. Some popular braid styles for guys include:

Box Braids

Box braids are a classic braid style that involves sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped sections and braiding each section. This style can be time-consuming to install, but it can last for several weeks with proper care.

Cornrows

Cornrows are another popular braid style that involves braiding the hair close to the scalp in straight lines. This style can be done in a variety of patterns and can last for several weeks with proper care.

Fishtail Braids

Fishtail braids are a more intricate braid style that involves weaving the hair into a pattern that resembles a fishtail. This style can be a bit more challenging to do, but it can be a great option for guys who want a more unique and eye-catching braid style.

Step by Step

If you're interested in trying out braids for yourself, here's a step-by-step guide to installing box braids:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small, square-shaped sections.
  3. Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.
  4. Begin braiding the hair by crossing the right section over the middle section, then crossing the left section over the new middle section.
  5. Continue braiding in this pattern until you reach the end of the section.
  6. Tie off the end of the braid with a hair elastic.
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 for each section of hair.

Tips

If you're new to braiding, here are a few tips to help you get started:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a comb to create even sections of hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to each section of hair to help the braid hold its shape.
  • Take your time and be patient. Braiding can be a bit challenging at first, but with practice, you'll get the hang of it.

FAQ

Q: Can guys with short hair wear braids?

A: Yes, guys with short hair can wear braids. However, the style options may be a bit more limited, and the braids may need to be smaller to accommodate the shorter hair length.

Q: How long do braids typically last?

A: The length of time that braids last can vary depending on the style, hair type, and how well they are cared for. Generally, box braids and cornrows can last for several weeks if properly cared for.

Q: Do braids damage hair?

A: Braids themselves do not typically damage hair, but improper installation or lack of proper hair care can lead to damage or breakage.

Hair Care

If you're wearing braids, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep the style looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but be gentle when shampooing and avoid rubbing the braids too vigorously.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the braids and your natural hair.
  • Cover your hair with a satin or silk scarf at night to protect the braids and prevent frizz.

In conclusion, guys like braids for a variety of reasons, including their unique and attractive look, low-maintenance nature, and practicality for active lifestyles. If you're interested in trying out braids for yourself, there are a variety of styles to choose from, and with proper care, your braids can last for several weeks.
