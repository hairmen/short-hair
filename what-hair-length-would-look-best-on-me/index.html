What Hair Length Would Look Best On Me

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but still unsure of what looks best on you? Well, worry no more! In this article, we will discuss what hair length would look best on you based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. So, let's dive in!

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining what hair length would look best on you is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has a different set of characteristics that can be enhanced or softened by different hairstyles.

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile and can look great with any hair length. A round face shape can benefit from longer hairstyles that create the illusion of a longer face. A square face shape can be softened with hairstyles that have layers and waves. A heart-shaped face can be balanced with hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of the face. A diamond face shape can be complemented with hairstyles that frame the face.

Considering Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a hair length. If you have fine hair, shorter hairstyles can add volume and body. If you have thick hair, longer hairstyles can help to weigh down your hair and make it more manageable. Curly hair can benefit from longer hairstyles that allow for more defined curls, while straight hair can look great with shorter hairstyles that add texture.

Factoring in Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor to consider when choosing a hair length. If you prefer a more classic and polished look, a shorter hairstyle may be best for you. If you prefer a more bohemian and carefree look, longer hairstyles with loose waves may be more your style.

Short Hair

Short hairstyles are perfect for those who prefer a low-maintenance look. A pixie cut can be a great option for those with an oval face shape and fine hair texture. A bob cut can look great on any face shape and hair texture, and can be styled in different ways to suit your personal style.

Medium Hair

Medium hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Shoulder-length hairstyles can be great for those with a round or square face shape, as they can create the illusion of a longer face. Layered hairstyles can also add volume and texture to any hair texture.

Long Hair

Long hairstyles can be stunning and dramatic, but they require more maintenance than shorter hairstyles. Long hair can look great on those with a heart or diamond face shape, as it can help to balance out the face. Loose waves and braids can add texture and volume to long hair.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of what hair length you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles can look great on anyone, regardless of face shape or hair texture. It's all about finding the right cut for you and your personal style.

2. Will a long hairstyle require more maintenance?

Yes, long hairstyles require more maintenance than shorter hairstyles. They require more frequent washing, conditioning, and styling to keep them looking healthy and shiny.

3. Should I consider my skin tone when choosing a hair length?

While your skin tone is not the most important factor to consider when choosing a hair length, it can be a helpful factor to consider when choosing a hair color. Certain hair colors can complement or clash with certain skin tones, so it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.


Choosing the right hair length can be a daunting task, but with these tips and factors to consider, you can find the perfect hairstyle for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny, and don't be afraid to try new styles and experiment with different lengths. Happy hairstyling!
