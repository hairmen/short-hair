Do Guys Like Curly Hair Or Straight Hair Better

Written by Daniel Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hair, there are a lot of opinions out there about what looks good and what doesn't. One of the most common questions that women ask themselves is whether guys prefer curly hair or straight hair. In this article, we'll explore this question in depth and give you some tips on how to make the most of your hair, no matter what type it is.

Description

Curly hair and straight hair are both beautiful in their own way, but there's no denying that they have different looks and textures. Curly hair tends to be more voluminous, while straight hair is sleek and shiny. Both styles can be flattering, depending on the person and the occasion.

How to

If you're trying to decide whether to wear your hair curly or straight, consider the following tips:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape can have a big impact on how your hair looks. For example, if you have a round face, curly hair can help to add some dimension and make your face look slimmer. On the other hand, if you have a long face, straight hair can help to balance out your features.

Think About the Occasion

The occasion can also play a role in how you choose to wear your hair. If you're going to a formal event, straight hair may be more appropriate, while curly hair can be great for a more casual outing.

Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to see what looks best on you. You may find that you like your hair curly one day and straight the next.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving the perfect curly or straight look:

Curly Hair

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to your damp hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, or let it air dry.
  4. Scrunch your hair gently to enhance the curls.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to keep your curls in place.

Straight Hair

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair in small sections.
  4. Finish with a serum or shine spray to add some extra shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making the most of your curly or straight hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer, flat iron, and curling iron to make styling easier.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on what styles would look best on you.

FAQ

Do guys like curly hair or straight hair better?

There's no one answer to this question, as different guys have different preferences. Some may prefer curly hair because it looks more natural, while others may prefer straight hair because it looks sleek and polished.

Is it better to wear your hair curly or straight?

It really depends on your personal style and the occasion. Both curly and straight hair can be beautiful, so experiment with different styles to see what looks best on you.

Hair Care

No matter what type of hair you have, taking good care of it is essential to keeping it healthy and looking its best. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and use styling products that are appropriate for your hair type. Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools too often and be sure to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

So, do guys like curly hair or straight hair better? The truth is, it doesn't really matter. What's most important is that you feel confident and beautiful in your own skin, no matter what type of hair you have.
