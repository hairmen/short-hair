Does Long Hair Suit Round Faces

Written by Aprilia Oct 02, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a change? Do you have a round face and wondering if long hair would suit you? You’re not alone! Many people with round faces have this question in mind. In this article, we’ll answer this question and provide you with some tips and suggestions on how to style your long hair to flatter your round face.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved features, and the width and length of the face are almost the same. People with round faces typically have fuller cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. When it comes to hairstyles, the goal is to create an illusion of a longer and slimmer face.

Long hair can look great on people with round faces if styled correctly. It can create a vertical illusion and make your face appear longer. However, it’s essential to keep in mind that not all long hairstyles suit round faces.

How to

The first step in determining whether long hair suits your round face is to identify your face shape. Look in the mirror and observe the shape of your face. If the length and width of your face are almost the same, and you have a rounded chin and full cheeks, you have a round face.

Once you’ve identified your face shape, the next step is to choose a long hairstyle that flatters your features. There are various long hairstyles that suit round faces, such as:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves
  • Loose curls

Step by Step

If you’re unsure how to style your long hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools
  3. Section your hair into manageable parts
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to create the desired hairstyle
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long hair:

  • Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face appear rounder
  • Opt for layers to add dimension and movement to your hair
  • Side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face
  • Use volumizing products to add volume to the crown of your head
  • Keep your hair away from your face to create a vertical illusion
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly as they can emphasize your round face

FAQ

1. Can people with round faces wear long hair?

Yes, people with round faces can wear long hair if styled correctly. Long hair can create an illusion of a longer and slimmer face.

2. What are the best long hairstyles for round faces?

The best long hairstyles for round faces are long layers, side-swept bangs, beachy waves, and loose curls.

Hair Care

Long hair requires proper care and maintenance to stay healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

Now that you know that long hair can suit round faces, it’s time to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
