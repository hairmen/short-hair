Most Popular Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As women age, it's important to find hairstyles that work with their changing hair texture and face shape. At 60 years old, there are many options for hairstyles that are both stylish and age-appropriate. In this article, we'll cover the most popular hairstyles for 60 year old women, how to achieve them, and tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Short Hairstyles
  • Medium Hairstyles
  • Long Hairstyles

Short Hairstyles

Short haircuts are low-maintenance and can look very stylish on women over 60. The classic pixie cut is a great option for those who want a short haircut that is easy to maintain. Another popular short hairstyle is the bob, which can be worn straight or with waves for a more casual look.

To achieve a pixie cut, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving longer pieces on top. You can style it with a bit of product for added texture and volume. For a bob, ask your stylist for a chin-length cut that is angled slightly shorter in the back.

Tips for styling short hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to your roots
  • Use a texturizing spray for added texture
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your haircut

Medium Hairstyles

Medium length hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. A shoulder-length cut with layers can add volume and movement to your hair. Another option is a layered bob that falls just above the shoulders.

To achieve a layered cut, ask your stylist to add layers throughout your hair. You can style it with a curling iron for added volume and waves.

Tips for styling medium length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Try a side part for a more sophisticated look
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated

Long Hairstyles

Long hair can be very flattering on women over 60, but it's important to keep it well-maintained. A layered cut with face-framing layers can add volume and movement to your hair. Another option is to wear your hair in loose waves for a more casual look.

To achieve a layered cut, ask your stylist to add long layers throughout your hair. For loose waves, use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create soft, natural waves.

Tips for styling long hair:

  • Use a hair serum to tame frizz
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy

Hair Care

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more fragile. It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Consider taking a hair supplement to support hair health

FAQ

Q: Can I still dye my hair at 60?

A: Absolutely! Many women continue to dye their hair well into their 60s and beyond. Just make sure to use a dye that is gentle on your hair and scalp.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: What if I have thinning hair?

A: There are many products available that can help to add volume to thinning hair, such as volumizing mousses and thickening sprays. Talk to your stylist about the best products for your hair type.
