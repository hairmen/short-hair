Haircuts for Curly Hair Medium Length - Tips, How to, and FAQ

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it can look beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you have curly hair that falls into the medium length category, you may be wondering what the best haircuts are for your hair type. In this guide, we'll provide you with tips, how to, and FAQ on getting haircuts for curly hair medium length that will make your curls look their best.

How to Get the Best Haircuts for Curly Hair Medium Length

When it comes to getting a haircut for curly hair, it's important to find a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair. Curly hair has a tendency to shrink up when it dries, so it's important to leave enough length so that your hair still falls into the medium length category once it has dried.

When discussing your haircut with your stylist, be sure to mention the texture of your hair. Curly hair can range from loose waves to tight coils, and each texture requires a different approach to cutting. For example, if you have tight coils, your stylist may recommend a layered haircut to help prevent your curls from becoming too heavy and weighed down.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. Follow these steps to ensure you get the best haircut for your curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Section your hair into four equal parts, using clips to hold them in place.
  3. Starting with one section, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  4. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, rather than straight across. This will help prevent your curls from looking too blunt and heavy.
  5. Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut in small increments to avoid cutting off too much length.
  6. Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Curly hair requires some extra TLC to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Invest in a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQ About Haircuts for Curly Hair Medium Length

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for curly hair medium length:

  1. What is the best haircut for curly hair medium length? The best haircut for curly hair medium length is one that takes into account the texture and density of your hair. A layered haircut is often a good choice for curly hair, as it helps prevent your curls from becoming too heavy.
  2. How often should I get a haircut? It's recommended that you get a haircut every 8-12 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  3. Can I straighten my curly hair? Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use heat protectant products and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your curls.
  4. What should I do if I don't like my haircut? If you're unhappy with your haircut, talk to your stylist and see if they can make any adjustments. Most stylists are happy to work with you to ensure you're happy with your haircut.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a slightly different approach than caring for straight hair. Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to help define your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.

By following these tips and getting regular haircuts, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
