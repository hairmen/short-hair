How Do I Style My Short Layered Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 08, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have short layered hair, you may be wondering how to style it to make the most of your cut. There are many options available to you, whether you want a casual, everyday look or a more formal style for a special occasion. In this post, we’ll explore some tips and tricks for styling short layered hair, as well as some frequently asked questions about hair care and maintenance.

Description

Short layered hair is a trendy and versatile style that can be customized to fit your unique look. This cut features different lengths of hair on top and bottom, creating a layered effect that adds volume and texture. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, short layers can give you a fresh and modern look.

How To

So, how do you style your short layered hair? Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Blow Dry With A Round Brush

For a sleek and polished look, blow dry your hair with a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to create volume and shape. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

2. Use A Curling Iron

If you have naturally straight hair, add some curls or waves with a curling iron. Choose a barrel size that suits your desired look, and wrap sections of hair around the iron for a few seconds. Use your fingers to loosen the curls and add texture.

3. Try A Texturizing Spray

For a more relaxed and tousled look, use a texturizing spray on your hair. Spray the product onto dry hair and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle the strands. This will create a messy and effortless style.

Step By Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your short layered hair:

1. Start With Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type. Towel dry your hair before styling.

2. Apply A Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse, gel, or cream. This will help to hold your style in place.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use the brush to create volume and shape.

4. Use A Curling Iron (Optional)

If you want to add curls or waves to your hair, use a curling iron to create the desired effect.

5. Finish With A Texturizing Spray

Finish your look with a texturizing spray, which will add texture and hold to your hair. Spray the product onto your hair and tousle with your fingers.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short layered hair:

1. Use Heat Protectant

Before using a blow dryer or curling iron, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.

2. Play With Parting

Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for your face shape and hair texture.

3. Don’t Overdo It

Less is often more when it comes to styling short hair. Avoid using too much product or over-styling your hair, which can make it look stiff and unnatural.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short layered hair:

1. How Often Should I Get My Hair Trimmed?

It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your short layered cut.

2. Can I Dye My Short Layered Hair?

Yes, you can dye your short layered hair. However, it’s important to choose a dye that is safe for your hair and to follow the instructions carefully.

3. How Can I Make My Short Layered Hair Look Thicker?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider adding layers to create the illusion of thickness. You can also use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hair type, but especially for short layered hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Use A Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and free of harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

2. Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and apply it after shampooing to help moisturize and protect your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair From Heat

Use a heat protectant before blow drying or using a curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to style your short layered hair with confidence and ease. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more relaxed and tousled style, there are many options available to you. Remember to care for your hair and maintain regular trims to keep your short layered cut looking its best.
