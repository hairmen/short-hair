Mens Haircuts Long On Top Short Sides

Written by Christine Nov 17, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then the mens haircuts long on top short sides is perfect for you. This style has been popular for years, and it’s not going away anytime soon. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Description

The mens haircuts long on top short sides is a style where the top of the hair is left long while the sides are trimmed short. This creates a contrast between the two lengths and adds dimension to the hair. The style can be customized to suit different hair types and face shapes.

How to Get It

The mens haircuts long on top short sides can be achieved in several ways. The most common method is to use clippers to cut the sides short and scissors to trim the top. The stylist will leave the top longer and blend it with the sides to create a seamless look.

It’s important to communicate with your stylist and show them a picture of the style you want. This will ensure that you get the haircut you desire. You can also ask for advice on how to style your hair at home.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair and towel dry it. 2. Use a comb to part your hair where you want it. 3. Use clippers to cut the sides of your hair short. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length. 4. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Start with small sections and work your way around your head. 5. Blend the top with the sides using scissors or clippers. 6. Style your hair using a hair product of your choice.

Tips

- Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type. - Use a hairdryer to style your hair for a more polished look. - Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens haircuts long on top short sides can be customized to suit different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Can I style my hair differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles using a hair product of your choice. Some popular styles include slicked-back, messy, and textured.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type. - Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Conclusion

The mens haircuts long on top short sides is a timeless and versatile style that’s perfect for any occasion. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve this look at home or visit your stylist for a professional cut. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
