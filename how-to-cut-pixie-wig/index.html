How To Cut Pixie Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Pixie wigs are incredibly trendy and stylish. They are perfect for those who want to change their look without making permanent changes to their hair. While purchasing a pixie wig is easy, cutting it can be a daunting task. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your pixie wig to perfection. In this guide, we will show you how to cut a pixie wig step-by-step.

How to Cut Pixie Wig?

Before you start cutting your pixie wig, you will need a few tools. These include:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hairspray
  • Mannequin head or stand

Once you have gathered your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Wig

Wash the wig with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or residue. Dry the wig using a towel or a hairdryer on a low heat setting.

Step 2: Secure the Wig on a Mannequin Head or Stand

Place the wig on a mannequin head or stand to keep it steady while you cut it.

Step 3: Comb the Wig

Comb the wig to remove any tangles or knots. Make sure the hair is evenly distributed before you start cutting.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting the wig from the back, using small scissors. Cut the hair in small sections to ensure a clean and even cut. Gradually work your way towards the front of the wig.

Step 5: Check the Length

Once you have cut the hair, check the length to ensure it is even. If you notice any uneven spots, trim them until the hair is the desired length.

Step 6: Style the Wig

Once you are satisfied with the length, style the wig using hairspray or other styling products. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to add volume and texture to the hair.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Wig

Here are some tips that will help you cut your pixie wig like a pro:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and even cut.
  • Cut the hair in small sections to avoid making any mistakes.
  • Take breaks in between to avoid straining your eyes and hands.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Can I Cut a Synthetic Pixie Wig?

Yes, you can cut a synthetic pixie wig. However, you will need to be extra careful as synthetic wigs are more delicate than human hair wigs. Make sure to use a sharp pair of scissors and cut the hair in small sections.

Do I Need to Cut the Wig Every Time I Wear It?

No, you do not need to cut the wig every time you wear it. However, if you notice any uneven spots or want to change the style, you can trim the hair accordingly.

How Do I Care for My Pixie Wig?

To care for your pixie wig, brush it regularly with a wide-tooth comb or brush. Wash it with a shampoo and conditioner designed for wigs. Avoid using hot styling tools as they can damage the hair. Store the wig in a cool and dry place to prevent it from getting damaged.

Conclusion

Cutting a pixie wig can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Follow the steps outlined in this guide, and you will be able to cut your pixie wig to perfection. Remember to take your time, be patient, and experiment with different styles and lengths to find the one that suits you best.
