How To Cover Hair With Makeup A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair getting in the way while you apply your makeup? Or maybe you're struggling with hair loss or thinning hair and want to learn how to cover it up with makeup? Whatever your reason, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll show you how to cover your hair with makeup step-by-step, along with some helpful tips and FAQs. Let's get started!

How to Cover Hair with Makeup

First things first, you'll need to gather the right tools and products for the job. Here's what you'll need:

  • Foundation or concealer that matches your skin tone
  • A clean, dry makeup brush or sponge
  • Setting powder
  • Hairspray (optional)

Once you have everything you need, it's time to get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cover your hair with makeup:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet or oily, the makeup won't stick as well.
  2. Apply foundation or concealer to the roots of your hair using a makeup brush or sponge. Blend it in well, making sure there are no harsh lines.
  3. If you're using foundation, apply setting powder on top to help it stay in place.
  4. If you have any bald spots or thinning hair, use a small brush or sponge to apply more makeup to those areas.
  5. If you want extra hold, spray your hair with hairspray. This will help the makeup stay in place all day.

And that's it! You're now ready to style your hair and apply the rest of your makeup as usual.

Tips for Covering Hair with Makeup

Here are some additional tips to help you cover your hair with makeup:

  • Use a foundation or concealer that matches your skin tone as closely as possible. This will help the makeup blend in seamlessly.
  • Be gentle when applying the makeup to your hair. Roughly rubbing or pulling on your hair can cause breakage and damage.
  • If you're using hairspray, make sure to hold the can at least 8 inches away from your head to avoid getting too much product on your hair.
  • To avoid getting makeup on your clothes, use a towel or cape to protect your clothing while you apply the makeup.

FAQs about Covering Hair with Makeup

Can I use regular makeup on my hair?

Yes! As long as you're using a foundation or concealer that matches your skin tone, you can use regular makeup on your hair.

Will covering my hair with makeup damage it?

No, as long as you're gentle when applying the makeup and avoid pulling or rubbing your hair too roughly, it shouldn't cause any damage.

How long will the makeup stay in my hair?

This will depend on a few factors, such as how much hairspray you use and how active you are throughout the day. In general, the makeup should stay in place for several hours.

Hair Care Tips

While covering your hair with makeup can be a great way to hide thinning hair or bald spots, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.

By following these tips and using makeup to cover any problem areas, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
