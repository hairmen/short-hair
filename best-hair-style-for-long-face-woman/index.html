Best Hair Style For Long Face Woman

Written by Petter Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a woman with a long face? Do you find it challenging to choose the best hair style that complements your facial features? A long face can be challenging to style, but with the right hair cut, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a look that flatters your face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best hair style for long face woman and provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow width. The best hair style for long face woman is one that adds width to the face and balances out the length. A good hair cut will help to shorten the face and create the illusion of a wider face. The right hair style will also draw attention away from the forehead and chin and highlight your best features.

How To

The first step in choosing the best hair style for long face woman is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin and the width of your face at the widest point. If your face is longer than it is wide, then you have a long face shape. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start to look for hair styles that will complement your features.

Step By Step

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in long face shapes. They can recommend the best hair style for your face shape and provide you with tips on how to maintain your hair.

Step 2: Consider hair styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layers or waves. These styles will help to widen your face and balance out the length.

Step 3: Avoid hair styles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate your face even more. Instead, opt for hair styles that are shoulder length or shorter.

Step 4: Bangs can be a great way to shorten the face and draw attention away from a high forehead. Consider side-swept or wispy bangs that blend into the rest of your hair.

Tips

Tip 1: Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

Tip 2: Use styling products such as mousse or volumizing spray to add volume to your hair.

Tip 3: Avoid wearing your hair in a tight ponytail or bun, as this can elongate your face even more.

Tip 4: Experiment with different hair styles and accessories to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight, but it is best to avoid hair styles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate your face even more.

Q: What is the best hair color for a long face?

A: Adding highlights or lowlights to your hair can create depth and dimension, which can help to balance out a long face.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, it is essential to use products that are designed for your hair type. If you have long hair, consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your locks moisturized and healthy. Regular trims are also important to maintain the health of your hair and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best hair style for long face woman is one that adds width to the face and balances out the length. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in long face shapes to find the perfect hair cut for you. Experiment with different hair styles and accessories to find the one that works best for your features. With the right hair cut and care, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a look that flatters your face shape.
