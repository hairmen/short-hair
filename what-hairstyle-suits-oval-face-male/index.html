Description

While there is no one-size-fits-all solution when it comes to hairstyles, there are certain haircuts and styles that work better for different face shapes. For oval face male, there are several hairstyles that can flatter your features and bring out your best self. This article will guide you through the process of determining your face shape and finding the best hairstyle for oval face male.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval face male, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, you'll need to look in the mirror and take note of the following measurements:

  • Forehead: Measure from the peak of one eyebrow arch to the peak of the opposite arch.
  • Chin: Measure from the tip of your chin to the bottom of your lip.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward. Multiply that number by two to get your jawline measurement.

Once you have these measurements, compare them to the following face shape descriptions:

  • Oval: Your forehead is slightly wider than your chin, and your face gently tapers toward your jaw.
  • Round: Your face is nearly as wide as it is long, with full cheeks and a rounded chin.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal in width, and your chin is squared off.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheeks, and your face tapers to a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower.
  • Triangle: Your jawline is wider than your forehead, and your face tapers to a narrow chin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Find the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Males

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the best hairstyle for oval face male. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to consider your hair type. Is it straight, wavy, or curly? Is it thick or thin? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that will work best for you.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also play a role in choosing a hairstyle. Do you have a corporate job that requires a more conservative look? Do you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle that you can quickly style and go? Consider your lifestyle before choosing a hairstyle.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Now that you know your hair type and lifestyle, it's time to find inspiration. Look for hairstyles that you like and that will complement your face shape. You can find inspiration from magazines, social media, or by checking out celebrity hairstyles.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is an expert and can help you choose a hairstyle that will work best for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Be sure to bring pictures of the hairstyles you like and ask for their professional opinion.

Tips for Maintaining Your Oval Face Male Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for oval face male, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair instead of your fingers.
  • Consider using hair products like gel, pomade, or hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

FAQs

What hairstyles suit oval face male?

There are several hairstyles that work well for oval face male, including:

  • The classic side part
  • The quiff
  • The pompadour
  • The textured crop
  • The messy fringe

What hairstyles should oval face male avoid?

Oval face male should avoid hairstyles that add too much height to the top of the head or that are too long and cover the forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Male

Finally, here are some hair care tips for oval face male:

  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers, straighteners, and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair with hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for oval face male and maintain it for years to come. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before making a decision. Good luck!
