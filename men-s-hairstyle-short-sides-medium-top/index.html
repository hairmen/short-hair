Description

The men's hairstyle short sides medium top has been in trend for a while now, and it's still going strong. This hairstyle features short sides and a medium-length top that can be styled in various ways. It's a versatile haircut that suits men of all ages and face shapes. The short sides create a clean and sharp look, while the medium top adds volume and texture to the hair. Whether you want a neat and professional look or a messy and casual one, this hairstyle can be easily tailored to your preferences.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the men's hairstyle short sides medium top, you need to visit a professional hairstylist who can cut your hair according to your face shape and hair type. The hairstylist will use clippers to trim the sides and scissors to cut the top. You can ask for a fade or undercut on the sides, depending on how short you want them to be. The top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, textured, or combed over. You can also add a side part or a fringe to the top for a more personalized touch.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to achieve the men's hairstyle short sides medium top:

  1. Visit a professional hairstylist who can cut your hair according to your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose the length of the sides and the style of the top that you prefer.
  3. Ask the hairstylist to use clippers to trim the sides and scissors to cut the top.
  4. Style the top according to your preference using a hair product of your choice.
  5. Add a side part or a fringe to the top for a more personalized touch.
  6. Use a hair dryer to set the style in place.
  7. Apply a finishing product to add shine and hold to the hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's hairstyle short sides medium top:

  • Visit a hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim or touch-up.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and desired style.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction of the style to set it in place.
  • Avoid overusing hair products, as they can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Try different styling techniques to create different looks with the same haircut.

FAQ

Can I achieve the men's hairstyle short sides medium top at home?

It's not recommended to cut your own hair, especially if you're going for a specific style like the men's hairstyle short sides medium top. It's best to visit a professional hairstylist who can give you the perfect cut according to your face shape and hair type.

Can I style the men's hairstyle short sides medium top in different ways?

Yes, the men's hairstyle short sides medium top is a versatile haircut that can be styled in various ways. You can try slicked back, messy, textured, or combed over styles, and add a side part or a fringe to the top for a more personalized touch.

Is the men's hairstyle short sides medium top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the men's hairstyle short sides medium top suits men of all face shapes. The short sides create a clean and sharp look, while the medium top adds volume and texture to the hair, making it a versatile and flattering haircut for all.

Hair Care for Men's Short Sides Medium Top Hairstyle

To maintain the men's hairstyle short sides medium top, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and desired style.
  • Avoid overusing hair products, as they can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction of the style to set it in place.
  • Visit a hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim or touch-up.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve and maintain the men's hairstyle short sides medium top. It's a versatile and stylish haircut that can be tailored to your preferences and personality. So, go ahead and try out this trendy hairstyle, and rock it with confidence!
