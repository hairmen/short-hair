How To Get Messy Wavy Hair

Are you tired of your straight and boring hair? Do you want to add some texture and volume to your locks? Look no further than messy wavy hair! This hairstyle is effortless, chic, and perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will show you how to achieve this look in a few easy steps.

Step by Step:

Follow these simple steps to achieve messy wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start with clean hair that has been washed and conditioned. This will ensure that your hair is free from any dirt and oils that can weigh it down.

Step 2: Apply a texturizing spray

Apply a texturizing spray to your hair when itâs still damp. This will help create the messy texture and add volume to your hair.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help enhance your natural wave pattern and create more volume.

Step 4: Use a curling iron

Take small sections of hair and wrap them around a curling iron. Hold the iron for a few seconds before releasing the hair. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Break up the curls

Use your fingers to break up the curls and create a messy texture. This will help your hair look more natural and effortless.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish the look with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your messy wavy hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays to find one that works best for your hair type.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as this can cause frizz and disrupt your waves.

Hair Care:

To keep your messy wavy hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
