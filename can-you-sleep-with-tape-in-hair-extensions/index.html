Can You Sleep With Tape In Hair Extensions

Written by Frank Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering getting tape-in hair extensions or have recently had them installed, you may be wondering if it's safe to sleep with them. After all, you don't want to damage your extensions or wake up with a tangled mess in the morning. In this blog post, we'll explore whether or not you can sleep with tape-in hair extensions and provide tips for doing so safely.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Sleep with Tape-In Hair Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Sleeping with Tape-In Hair Extensions

Description

Tape-in hair extensions are a popular way to add length and volume to your hair without the use of clips or other accessories. These extensions are attached to your natural hair using a special type of tape that is safe and secure. However, because the tape is adhesive, you may be wondering if it's safe to sleep with them.

How to Sleep with Tape-In Hair Extensions

The good news is that it is safe to sleep with tape-in hair extensions. However, there are some things you should keep in mind to ensure your extensions stay safe and secure while you sleep.

Use a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are a great investment for anyone with hair extensions. They help to reduce friction between your hair and the pillow, which can cause tangling and damage. Silk also helps to maintain the moisture balance in your hair, which is essential for healthy-looking extensions.

Tie Your Hair Up

Before you go to bed, tie your hair up in a loose bun or ponytail. This will help to keep your extensions in place and prevent them from getting tangled or damaged while you sleep.

Avoid Sleeping on Wet Hair

Wet hair is more prone to damage and tangling, so it's best to avoid sleeping on wet hair if possible. If you must sleep on wet hair, make sure to tie it up in a loose bun or ponytail to prevent tangling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to sleeping with tape-in hair extensions:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles
  2. Tie your hair up in a loose bun or ponytail
  3. Use a silk pillowcase
  4. Avoid sleeping on wet hair

Tips for Sleeping with Tape-In Hair Extensions

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when sleeping with tape-in hair extensions:

  • Avoid sleeping on your back if possible, as this can cause tangling
  • Use a detangling spray before brushing your hair in the morning
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions
  • Be gentle when brushing your hair to prevent damage
  • Don't pull or tug on your extensions

FAQs

Can I Sleep with Wet Hair Extensions?

It's best to avoid sleeping with wet hair extensions, as wet hair is more prone to tangling and damage. If you must sleep with wet hair, tie it up in a loose bun or ponytail to prevent tangling.

Can I Sleep on My Stomach with Tape-In Hair Extensions?

Sleeping on your stomach can cause tangling and damage to your extensions. It's best to avoid sleeping on your stomach if possible.

Can I Sleep with Tape-In Hair Extensions Every Night?

While it is safe to sleep with tape-in hair extensions, it's best to give your hair and extensions a break every once in a while. Aim to give your hair a break at least once a week to prevent damage and keep your extensions looking their best.

Hair Care for Tape-In Hair Extensions

In addition to sleeping with your extensions, it's important to take good care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for taking care of your tape-in hair extensions:

  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangling
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions
  • Be gentle when brushing your hair to prevent damage
  • Don't pull or tug on your extensions
  • Use a detangling spray before brushing your hair

By following these tips, you can sleep with your tape-in hair extensions safely and keep them looking their best for as long as possible.
