How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Long On Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish new haircut for men with long hair on top? Look no further than the classic scissor cut. This timeless style is perfect for those who want to maintain length on top while keeping the sides and back trimmed. Keep reading for a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair with scissors long on top.

Description

The scissor cut is a classic men's hairstyle that features longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. It's a versatile look that can be tailored to suit any face shape, hair type, or personal style.

How to

Before starting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here's how to cut men's hair with scissors long on top:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Comb and Section Hair

Using a comb, part the hair on top where you want to make the cut. Then, section off the hair on the sides and back using hair clips or hair ties.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting at the front of the head, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, moving upwards towards the top of the head.

Step 4: Work Your Way Back

Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way towards the back of the head. Be sure to maintain the length on top while gradually shortening the hair on the sides and back.

Step 5: Blend the Sides and Back

Once you've cut all the hair on top, it's time to blend the sides and back. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Before finishing, make sure to check for evenness. Use the scissors to trim any sections that may be longer than others, and make sure both sides are symmetrical.

Step 7: Style as Desired

After cutting, style the hair as desired. You can use a pomade or wax to create texture or slick the hair back.

Tips

When cutting men's hair with scissors long on top, keep these tips in mind:

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure evenness
  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid mistakes
  • Blend the sides and back gradually to create a natural look
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair as needed

FAQ

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut men's hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and avoid split ends.

Can I cut my hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your hair at home with the right tools and technique. However, it's always recommended to seek the help of a professional stylist if you're unsure.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best after a scissor cut, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling with tools like hair dryers or straighteners
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep hair healthy and hydrated

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut men's hair with scissors long on top like a pro. Give it a try and see how this classic style can elevate your look!
