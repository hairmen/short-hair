How To Cut Hairline With Clippers

Written by William Aug 08, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the hair salon just to get your hairline trimmed? With the right tools and technique, you can easily cut your own hairline at home using clippers. In this article, we’ll go over how to cut hairline with clippers step by step, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice.

How To:

Before we dive into the steps, let's talk about the tools you’ll need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • A mirror
  • A comb
  • Scissors (optional)
  • Hair clip (optional)

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to see your hairline and ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair Forward

Using a comb, pull your hair forward so that your hairline is exposed. Make sure your hair is even and straight.

Step 3: Choose Your Guard Size

Choose a guard size that matches the length you want for your hairline. It’s always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting at one temple, use the clippers to cut along your natural hairline. Move the clippers in a smooth motion, following the curve of your head.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After each pass, use a mirror to check your progress. Make sure the cut is even and straight.

Step 6: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you’ve finished one side, repeat the same steps on the other side.

Step 7: Clean Up Stray Hairs

Using scissors or the clippers without a guard, clean up any stray hairs around your hairline.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Once you’re satisfied with your hairline, style your hair as usual.

Tips:

  • Always start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Take breaks if you start to feel tired or shaky.
  • Use a hair clip to hold your hair back if it’s getting in the way.
  • Don’t cut too far into your hairline, as this can make it look unnatural.

FAQ:

Can I cut my hairline with scissors?

Yes, but it’s much easier to use clippers with guards to get an even cut.

What guard size should I use?

Start with a longer guard and work your way down. A size 3 or 4 is a good starting point for most people.

Hair Care:

After you’ve cut your hairline, make sure to keep it clean and moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Now that you know how to cut hairline with clippers, you can save money and time by doing it yourself at home. Just remember to take your time and use the right tools, and you’ll have a perfect hairline in no time!
