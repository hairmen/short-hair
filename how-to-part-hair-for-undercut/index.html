How To Part Hair For Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering an undercut hairstyle? Parting your hair for an undercut can be tricky, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a clean and stylish look. In this article, we'll teach you how to part hair for undercut and provide some tips for maintaining your new style.

How to Part Hair for Undercut: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start parting your hair, gather the necessary tools. You will need a comb, hair clips, and scissors. It's also a good idea to have a mirror in front of you and a second mirror behind you to check your progress.

Step 2: Decide on Your Part

Decide where you want to part your hair. Undercuts usually have a shaved or short section on one side or the back of the head, so choose a part that will allow for this. You can part your hair down the middle, on one side, or in a zigzag pattern.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by clipping the top layer out of the way. You will be working with the bottom layer first. Use your comb to create a straight line where you want to part your hair.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, cut the hair below the line you created. It's important to cut in small sections to ensure accuracy. Take your time and make sure the hair is even.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Use the mirrors to check your progress. Make sure the line is straight and even. If you notice any unevenness, go back and make small corrections.

Step 6: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you are satisfied with one side, repeat the process on the other side. Make sure the part is symmetrical.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is parted for the undercut, you can style it however you like. You can create a sleek look with gel or pomade, or leave it natural for a more casual style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut

Now that you have an undercut, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Regularly trim the undercut to keep it looking fresh.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I trim my undercut?

We recommend trimming your undercut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Can I part my hair differently after I've undercut it?

Yes, you can part your hair differently after you've undercut it. Just keep in mind that the shaved or short section will still be visible, so choose a part that complements the style.

Do I need to use a specific type of scissors to cut my hair?

You don't need to use a specific type of scissors, but it's important to use sharp ones. Dull scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts.

Hair Care for Undercut

Undercut hairstyles are popular because they're low-maintenance, but that doesn't mean you can neglect your hair. Keep your hair healthy by washing it regularly, using quality products, and getting regular trims. If you have any concerns about the health of your hair or scalp, consult a professional stylist or dermatologist.

Parting your hair for an undercut can be a fun and edgy way to switch up your look. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish and unique hairstyle. Remember to maintain your undercut properly to keep it looking fresh and healthy.
