How To Straighten Frizzy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have frizzy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to tame those flyaways and get a sleek, straight look. Fortunately, with the right tools and techniques, you can straighten frizzy hair at home. In this post, we’ll walk you through how to straighten hair frizzy step by step, including some tips and tricks to help you get the best results.

  1. Description
  2. How to Straighten Hair Frizzy
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Straightening Frizzy Hair

Description

Frizzy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, genetics, and damage from heat styling tools. When hair is frizzy, it means that the cuticles on the hair strands are raised, which makes the hair look rough and unmanageable.

Straightening frizzy hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a sleek, straight look. In the next section, we’ll go over how to straighten hair frizzy using a few different methods.

How to Straighten Hair Frizzy

There are several methods you can use to straighten frizzy hair, including:

  • Using a flat iron
  • Using a straightening brush
  • Using a blow dryer and brush

Regardless of the method you choose, there are a few things you should do before you start straightening your hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for frizzy hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Blow dry your hair until it is about 80% dry before you start straightening.

Step-by-Step Guide

Method 1: Using a Flat Iron

Here’s how to straighten hair frizzy using a flat iron:

  1. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  2. Take one section of hair and clamp the flat iron near the roots.
  3. Slowly glide the flat iron down the length of the hair, pulling it straight as you go.
  4. Repeat this process on each section of hair until your entire head is straightened.
  5. Finish with a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Method 2: Using a Straightening Brush

Here’s how to straighten hair frizzy using a straightening brush:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  3. Take one section of hair and run the straightening brush through it from roots to ends.
  4. Repeat this process on each section of hair until your entire head is straightened.
  5. Finish with a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Method 3: Using a Blow Dryer and Brush

Here’s how to straighten hair frizzy using a blow dryer and brush:

  1. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  2. Take one section of hair and use a round brush to pull it straight as you blow dry it.
  3. Repeat this process on each section of hair until your entire head is straightened.
  4. Finish with a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

Tips for Straightening Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when straightening frizzy hair:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron, straightening brush, or blow dryer to get the best results.
  • Invest in a good heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to straighten.
  • Use a hair serum or oil after straightening to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten frizzy hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can straighten frizzy hair without heat by using a straightening cream or serum. These products are designed to smooth out your hair and make it less prone to frizz.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It’s best to limit heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair. Try to straighten your hair no more than once a week, and use a heat protectant spray or serum every time you straighten.

Q: What should I do if my hair is still frizzy after straightening?

A: If your hair is still frizzy after straightening, try using a hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness. You can also try using a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz spray to tame flyaways.

Hair Care Tips for Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for frizzy hair:

  • Use products that are designed for frizzy hair, such as shampoos and conditioners that contain smoothing ingredients like keratin or argan oil.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage and make your hair more prone to frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and always use a heat protectant spray or serum when you do use them.

With the right tools and techniques, you can straighten frizzy hair and get a sleek, smooth look. Just remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and hydrated.
