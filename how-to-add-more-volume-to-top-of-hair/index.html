How To Add More Volume To Top Of Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're someone who struggles with flat, lifeless hair, you're not alone. Many people find that their hair lacks volume, particularly at the crown. Fortunately, there are several ways to add more volume to the top of your hair, from simple styling tricks to more involved treatments. In this article, we'll take you through everything you need to know to achieve voluminous, bouncy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Add More Volume to Top of Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Adding Volume to Your Hair
  • FAQ

How to Add More Volume to Top of Hair

There are several ways to add volume to your hair, but the key to achieving volume at the top of your head is to focus on creating lift and height at the roots. Here are some popular methods for adding volume to the top of your hair:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

One of the easiest ways to add volume to the top of your hair is to blow dry your hair upside down. This technique helps to lift the roots and create volume at the crown. Simply flip your head upside down and use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots. Once your hair is dry, flip your head back up and style as desired.

2. Tease Your Hair

Teasing, or backcombing, is another popular technique for adding volume to the top of your hair. To tease your hair, take a small section of hair at the crown and backcomb it by gently combing it backwards towards the roots. Repeat this process with small sections of hair until you've achieved your desired level of volume. Be sure to smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased sections to hide any knots or tangles.

3. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help to add volume to your hair from the very beginning of your hair care routine. Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume and lift to your hair, and be sure to apply the conditioner only to the ends of your hair to avoid weighing down the roots.

4. Try a Root Lifting Spray or Mousse

Root lifting sprays and mousses are specially formulated to add volume to the roots of your hair. Apply the product to damp hair, focusing on the roots, and then blow dry your hair using a round brush to create lift and height.

Step by Step Guide

If you're looking for a more detailed guide to adding volume to the top of your hair, here's a step by step process:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, focusing on the roots. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a Root Lifting Spray or Mousse

Apply a root lifting spray or mousse to damp hair, focusing on the roots. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots. Once your hair is dry, flip your head back up and style as desired.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair (Optional)

If you want even more volume, you can tease your hair at the crown. Take a small section of hair and backcomb it gently towards the roots, repeating the process with small sections until you've achieved your desired level of volume.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place and add extra volume.

Tips for Adding Volume to Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you add volume to the top of your hair:

  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add extra lift to your roots
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat
  • Try a layered haircut to add more movement and volume to your hair
  • Consider getting a professional blowout, which can add volume and bounce to your hair that lasts for several days

FAQ

What causes flat, lifeless hair?

Flat, lifeless hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, and environmental factors such as humidity or pollution. It can also be caused by using heavy styling products or over-brushing your hair.

Can I add volume to my hair without using heat?

Yes, you can add volume to your hair without using heat by using a volumizing dry shampoo or mousse, or by teasing your hair at the crown. You can also try a layered haircut to add more movement and volume to your hair.

How can I make my voluminous hairstyle last longer?

To make your voluminous hairstyle last longer, try using a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain volume overnight.

Hair Care Tips for Volume

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain volume in your hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look flat
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as brushing can cause breakage and make your hair look flat
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look flat
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase, which can help to prevent hair breakage and maintain volume

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve voluminous, bouncy hair that looks and feels great. Whether you're getting ready for a special event or just want to add some extra lift to your everyday style, these tips are sure to help you achieve the volume you desire.
