Description

If you have thick hair, you know the struggle of finding the right haircut. Short haircuts with bangs can be a great option for thick hair, as they help to lighten the weight and add some dimension to the hair. Bangs can also be a fun way to change up your look and experiment with different styles. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short haircuts with bangs for thick hair, how to style them, and how to maintain them.

How to Style

Styling short haircuts with bangs for thick hair can be easy and low-maintenance. Here are some tips:

  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and shape to your bangs
  • Use a texturizing spray to add some texture and definition to your hair
  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look
  • Try using a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleeker look

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style short haircuts with bangs for thick hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a texturizing spray to add some texture and grip to your hair
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on your bangs to add volume and shape
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if desired
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling short haircuts with bangs for thick hair:

  • Experiment with different bang lengths to find the right style for your face shape
  • Try adding some highlights or lowlights to add some dimension to your hair
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add even more volume and body to your hair
  • Consider consulting with a hairstylist to find the best short haircut with bangs for your hair type and face shape

FAQ

What short haircuts with bangs are best for thick hair?

Some of the best short haircuts with bangs for thick hair include the bob with bangs, the pixie cut with bangs, and the lob with side-swept bangs.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It is recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

How do I maintain my short haircut with bangs?

To maintain your short haircut with bangs, it is important to trim your bangs regularly and use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thick hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat tools too often and always use a heat protectant
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Try using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
