What Is The Reason For Extreme Hair Fall

What is the Reason for Extreme Hair Fall? | Hair Care Tips 2023

If you are experiencing extreme hair fall, you might be wondering what the reasons behind it are. Hair fall can be caused by a variety of factors, and in this article, we will explore some of the most common reasons for extreme hair fall. We will also provide some tips on how to prevent hair fall and keep your hair healthy and strong.

Hormonal Changes

Hormonal changes can cause extreme hair fall in both men and women. For example, women may experience hair fall during pregnancy or after giving birth due to hormonal changes. Hormonal imbalances caused by thyroid problems or menopause can also lead to hair fall. In men, hormonal changes associated with aging can cause hair fall and balding.

How to Prevent Hair Fall due to Hormonal Changes

If you are experiencing hair fall due to hormonal changes, it is important to seek medical advice. Your doctor may prescribe medications or hormone therapy to regulate your hormone levels and prevent hair fall. You can also try natural remedies such as eating a balanced diet, exercising regularly, and reducing stress levels.

Nutritional Deficiencies

Nutritional deficiencies can also cause extreme hair fall. A lack of essential vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, and biotin can weaken hair follicles and cause hair fall. Eating a diet that is low in protein can also lead to hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall due to Nutritional Deficiencies

Eating a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein can help prevent hair fall due to nutritional deficiencies. You can also take supplements to ensure that you are getting enough essential vitamins and minerals.

Stress

Stress can also cause extreme hair fall. When you are under a lot of stress, your body produces hormones that can cause hair to fall out. Stress can also lead to poor sleep quality, which can further aggravate hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall due to Stress

To prevent hair fall due to stress, it is important to manage your stress levels. You can try relaxation techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises. Getting regular exercise and getting enough sleep can also help reduce stress levels.

Medications

Some medications can cause hair fall as a side effect. For example, chemotherapy drugs used to treat cancer can cause hair fall. Other medications such as antidepressants, blood thinners, and acne medications can also cause hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall due to Medications

If you are experiencing hair fall due to medications, it is important to talk to your doctor. Your doctor may be able to prescribe alternative medications or adjust your dosage to prevent hair fall.

Medical Conditions

Some medical conditions can also cause hair fall. For example, alopecia areata is an autoimmune disorder that can cause hair to fall out in patches. Scalp infections, such as ringworm, can also cause hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall due to Medical Conditions

If you are experiencing hair fall due to a medical condition, it is important to seek medical advice. Your doctor can diagnose the underlying condition and prescribe appropriate treatment to prevent hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some tips on how to prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein.
  • Get regular exercise.
  • Reduce stress levels.
  • Avoid harsh hair treatments such as coloring, perming, or straightening.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, or buns.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as hair dryers, curling irons, or straighteners.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle wet hair.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to sunlight for long periods of time.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.

FAQ

Q: How much hair fall is normal?

A: It is normal to lose up to 100 hairs per day. However, if you are experiencing more than that, it could be a sign of hair fall.

Q: Can hair fall be reversed?

A: Depending on the underlying cause of hair fall, it may be reversible. However, it is important to seek medical advice to determine the underlying cause of hair fall.

Q: Are there any home remedies for hair fall?

A: There are several home remedies that can help prevent hair fall, such as using onion juice, aloe vera, or coconut oil. However, it is important to note that these remedies may not work for everyone and it is important to seek medical advice for persistent hair fall.
