How To Style Short Hair Everyday Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair can be a lot of fun to style, but it can also be challenging to come up with new looks every day. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, there are plenty of ways to switch up your style and keep things interesting. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling short hair every day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair Everyday
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Style Short Hair Everyday

There are many different ways to style short hair, but some of the most popular include:

  • Messy waves
  • Straight and sleek
  • Curls
  • Braids and twists
  • Pompadour

Experiment with different styles to see what works best for your hair type and face shape. You may also want to consider using styling products like mousse, hairspray, and wax to help hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply styling product to your hair
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair (if desired)
  5. Create waves or curls using a curling iron or wand
  6. Add volume to your roots using a teasing comb
  7. Create braids or twists (if desired)
  8. Finish with hairspray to set your style

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short hair:

  • Invest in good quality styling tools, like a curling iron, flat iron, and round brush
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Try out different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, and barrettes
  • Don't be afraid to use hair extensions to add length and volume

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair:

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes! You can create a small ponytail or a messy bun with short hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Some people can go a few days between washes, while others need to wash their hair every day.

Q: What should I do if my short hair won't hold a curl?

A: Try using a stronger hold hairspray or using a smaller curling iron.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair still needs some TLC! Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools every day
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short hair every day and keep things interesting. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you!
