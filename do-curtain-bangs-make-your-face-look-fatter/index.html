Do Curtain Bangs Make Your Face Look Fatter

Written by Oliver Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but worried they might make your face look fatter? This is a common concern for many people, but the truth is, it depends on a few factors. In this blog post, we will discuss whether or not curtain bangs make your face look fatter and provide some tips on how to style them to flatter your face shape.

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. They are called "curtain" bangs because they frame the face like a set of curtains. Curtain bangs can be cut to different lengths, but typically they are longer at the sides and shorter in the middle. They are versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making them a popular choice for people of all ages.

How to

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, curtain bangs can actually help to slim it down by drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. If you have a square or angular face, longer curtain bangs can help to soften your features. It's also important to consider your hair type and texture. Curtain bangs work best on hair that is straight or has a slight wave. If you have curly hair, curtain bangs may be harder to style and maintain.

Step by Step

If you've decided to get curtain bangs, here are some tips on how to style them:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs, pulling them to the side as you go.
  3. Once your bangs are dry, use a straightener to smooth out any kinks or flyaways.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz.
  5. Part your bangs down the middle and style them to the side.
  6. Use a light-hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling curtain bangs:

  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and add volume to your bangs.
  • If you have fine hair, consider adding some layers to your bangs to add texture and volume.
  • Don't be afraid to trim your bangs yourself between salon visits to keep them looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Do curtain bangs make your face look fatter?

It depends on your face shape and how you style them. If you have a round face, curtain bangs can actually help to slim it down. If you have a square or angular face, longer curtain bangs can help to soften your features.

What face shape is best for curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can work on any face shape, but they are particularly flattering on round or heart-shaped faces.

Are curtain bangs high maintenance?

Curtain bangs require regular trims to keep them looking neat and tidy, but they are relatively low maintenance compared to some other hairstyles.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep your curtain bangs looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Overall, curtain bangs can be a flattering and stylish hairstyle for many people. Whether or not they make your face look fatter depends on your face shape and how you style them. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can rock curtain bangs with confidence!
