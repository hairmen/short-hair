Guys Haircut Longer On Top A Trending Style That Will Never Go Out Of Fashion

Written by Petter Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? If so, then a guys haircut longer on top might be just what you need. This style has been trending for years and is still popular in 2023. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The guys haircut longer on top is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, including messy, slicked back, or with a side part. It is a versatile haircut that can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

If you want to get a guys haircut longer on top, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style. You can bring a picture of the style you want to show them, or you can ask for their advice on what would look best on you.

Step by Step

Here are the basic steps involved in getting a guys haircut longer on top:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Sit in the barber or hairstylist's chair and explain the style you want.
  3. The barber or hairstylist will trim the sides and back of your hair with clippers.
  4. They will then cut the hair on top of your head with scissors to the desired length.
  5. If you want a more textured look, they may use thinning shears or a razor to create layers.
  6. After the haircut, they will style your hair using pomade, gel, or another styling product.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your guys haircut longer on top:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that suits your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or hairstylist for advice on how to style your hair.

FAQ

Q: Is the guys haircut longer on top suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this style can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Talk to your barber or hairstylist about what will look best on you.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my guys haircut longer on top?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how neat you want it to look. Generally, getting a trim every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Hair Care

To keep your guys haircut longer on top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do need to use hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, which is essential for healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the guys haircut longer on top is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that is still trending in 2023. Whether you want a messy, textured look or a slicked-back style, this haircut can be adapted to suit your preferences. Talk to your barber or hairstylist about how to get the look you want, and don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
