How to Cut Thick Wavy Hair Short: A Step-by-Step Guide

Cutting thick wavy hair short can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. This guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your hair short and maintain its shape.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be voluminous and difficult to manage, which is why many people choose to cut it short. Short hair is easier to style and maintain, and it can also give you a more youthful appearance. However, cutting thick wavy hair requires special techniques to ensure that the hair looks healthy and bouncy.

How to Cut Thick Wavy Hair Short

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Then, part your hair in the middle to create two equal sections.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back, using a pair of sharp scissors. Make sure that you cut the hair in a straight line, following the natural shape of your head. Avoid cutting too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven results.

Once you have finished cutting the back section of your hair, move on to the sides. Use the same technique to cut the hair on the sides, making sure that the length is even.

Finally, cut the hair on the top of your head. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, following the natural shape of your head. Make sure that the length is even, and that there are no jagged edges.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a hairdryer and a round brush to create volume, or you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as this can lead to uneven results.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair requires precision and attention to detail.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my thick wavy hair short myself?

A: Yes, you can cut your hair short yourself. However, it is recommended that you seek the help of a professional hairstylist if you are not confident in your cutting skills.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It is recommended that you cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage. Use a heat protectant spray when using styling tools, and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Conclusion

Cutting thick wavy hair short can be challenging, but with the right techniques and tools, it can be done easily. Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve a stylish and healthy looking haircut. Remember to take care of your hair properly after cutting it, to ensure that it stays healthy and vibrant.
