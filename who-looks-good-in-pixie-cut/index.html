Who Looks Good In Pixie Cut A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In recent years, the pixie cut has become a popular hairstyle for women of all ages. It's a short, low-maintenance style that can be both edgy and feminine. But not everyone can pull off this look. In this article, we'll be discussing who looks good in a pixie cut and how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cut

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that usually features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. Women with oval faces and delicate features tend to look good in pixie cuts.

How to Get a Pixie Cut

If you're thinking of getting a pixie cut, it's important to find a good stylist who has experience cutting short hair. Before you commit to the cut, you can try on different pixie styles using virtual makeover tools online.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a pixie cut:

  1. Choose the right pixie style for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Show your stylist pictures of the style you want.
  3. Get a consultation with your stylist to discuss the cut.
  4. Wash your hair and let it air dry.
  5. Sit down in the stylist's chair and let them section your hair.
  6. The stylist will start cutting your hair, starting from the back.
  7. They will continue cutting the sides and top of your hair.
  8. Once the cut is done, the stylist will blow dry and style your hair.
  9. Ask for advice on how to maintain your pixie cut.
  10. Enjoy your new look!

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut

To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use a texturizing product to add volume and definition.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as spiking or slicking back.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to create different looks.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

Who looks good in a pixie cut?

Women with oval faces and delicate features tend to look good in pixie cuts. However, with the right styling and cut, anyone can pull off this look.

How do I know if a pixie cut will suit me?

You can try on different pixie styles using virtual makeover tools online. It's also a good idea to consult with a stylist who has experience cutting short hair.

Is a pixie cut easy to maintain?

Yes, a pixie cut is easy to maintain. However, you will need to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Hair Care for Pixie Cut

To keep your pixie cut looking healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Now that you know who looks good in a pixie cut and how to care for it, you can confidently rock this stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle!
