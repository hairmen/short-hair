Does Dark Hair Make You Look Thinner

Written by Bardi Oct 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to slim down your face, you might be wondering if changing your hair color can do the trick. Specifically, you might be wondering: does dark hair make you look thinner? In this blog post, we'll explore the answer to that question, including how to achieve the look, step by step tips, and hair care.

Description

Many people believe that dark hair can make your face look slimmer. The idea is that darker shades create a shadow effect, which can help to contour your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and a slimmer jawline. However, it's worth noting that this effect isn't always guaranteed, and there are other factors to consider, such as your skin tone and face shape.

How To Achieve The Look

If you want to try out dark hair to see if it makes you look thinner, there are a few steps you can take to achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose the right shade

The key to achieving a slimming effect with dark hair is to choose the right shade. Generally, shades that are a few shades darker than your natural color tend to work best. You'll also want to consider your skin tone and eye color when choosing a shade.

Step 2: Get the right cut

The right haircut can also help to slim down your face. Consider a style that features layers or angles around the face, as these can help to create a more defined jawline and cheekbones.

Step 3: Style your hair strategically

The way you style your hair can also make a difference. Consider using volumizing products or teasing your hair at the crown to create more height at the top of your head. This can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Step By Step Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to achieve a slimming effect with dark hair:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as these can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider using a contouring product to further define your cheekbones and jawline.
  • Don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether dark hair can make you look thinner:

Does dark hair always make you look thinner?

No, the effect of dark hair on your face shape will depend on various factors, including your skin tone and face shape.

What other factors can affect how thin your face looks?

Other factors that can affect how thin your face looks include your hairstyle, makeup, and clothing choices.

Can hair color make you look younger?

Yes, hair color can make you look younger by brightening up your complexion and creating a more youthful vibe.

Hair Care

If you decide to dye your hair a darker shade, it's important to take good care of your locks. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny dark hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Conclusion

So, does dark hair make you look thinner? The answer is: it depends. While dark hair can create a slimming effect for some people, it's not a guarantee. However, if you're curious about the effect it might have on you, there's no harm in giving it a try! Just remember to choose the right shade, cut, and styling techniques, and take good care of your hair.
