How To Style Thick Wavy Hair Female In Relaxed English Language

Written by Christine Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It's voluminous, full of texture and body, but it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you're a female with thick wavy hair, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that's both flattering and manageable. In this article, we'll share some tips and techniques for styling thick wavy hair in relaxed, easy-to-understand English.

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair Female

When it comes to styling thick wavy hair, the key is to work with your natural texture rather than against it. Here are some style options:

1. Long Layers

If you're looking to add some shape and movement to your thick wavy hair, consider adding long layers. Long layers will help to remove some of the bulk from your hair while still maintaining its natural volume and texture.

2. Bob

A bob is a great option if you're looking to go shorter with your thick wavy hair. It's a classic style that's both chic and easy to maintain. Opt for a shoulder-length bob to keep your hair looking full and voluminous.

3. Beachy Waves

If you're looking for a low-maintenance style that still looks put-together, beachy waves are the way to go. This style is perfect for thick wavy hair because it allows you to embrace your natural texture while adding some definition and movement.

4. Half-Up Half-Down

If you're looking for a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, try a half-up half-down hairstyle. This style is perfect for thick wavy hair because it allows you to show off your natural texture while still keeping your hair out of your face.

Step by Step

Now that you have some style options, here's how to achieve them:

1. Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Divide your hair into sections
  3. Starting from the bottom, take a 1-inch section of hair and twist it away from your face
  4. Use a pair of scissors to cut the ends of the twist
  5. Repeat this process throughout your hair, working your way up to the top

2. Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section your hair into 4 parts (top, sides, and back)
  3. Starting at the back, use a straightener to create a slight curl in each section of hair
  4. Once you've curled all of your hair, use a comb to create a side part
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray

3. Beachy Waves

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a texturizing spray throughout your hair
  3. Divide your hair into sections
  4. Starting from the bottom, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair
  5. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the waves

4. Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section the top half of your hair and clip it away
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout the bottom half of your hair
  4. Once you've curled all of your hair, release the top section and use your fingers to tousle your hair
  5. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and tie them together at the back of your head with a hair tie

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling thick wavy hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer to help tame your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works for you

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people with thick wavy hair can go up to a week without washing, while others prefer to wash their hair every other day. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for you.

Q: What type of products should I use on my thick wavy hair?

A: Look for products that are designed specifically for thick or wavy hair, such as a texturizing spray or a curl-enhancing cream. Avoid products that are heavy or contain harsh chemicals, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy, beautiful hair starts with proper hair care. Here are some tips for taking care of your thick wavy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently style your thick wavy hair in a way that's both flattering and manageable. Embrace your natural texture and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
