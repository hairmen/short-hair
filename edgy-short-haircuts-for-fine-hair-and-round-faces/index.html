Edgy Short Haircuts For Fine Hair And Round Faces

Written by Bardi Dec 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have fine hair and a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. Many styles can leave your hair looking limp and lifeless, while others can make your face appear even rounder. But don't worry, edgy short haircuts are here to rescue you! Here are some of the best styles for fine hair and round faces.

Description

Edgy short haircuts are perfect for those who want to add a bit of edge and personality to their look. They are typically characterized by asymmetrical cuts, choppy layers, and bold colors. These styles can be adapted to suit any face shape, but they work particularly well for those with fine hair and round faces.

How to

The key to achieving the perfect edgy haircut is to find a stylist who understands your hair type and face shape. When you book your appointment, be sure to bring in pictures of the styles you like, and be prepared to discuss your hair care routine and styling habits. Your stylist can then recommend the best cut and color for you.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair, and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.

3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray or wax to add definition and shape.

5. If desired, finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

1. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

2. Use a dry shampoo between washes to add volume and texture.

3. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

4. Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ

1. Will an edgy haircut work for me?

Edgy haircuts can work for anyone, regardless of hair type or face shape. The key is to find a style that complements your features and personality.

2. How often do I need to get my hair cut?

This will depend on the style you choose, as well as how quickly your hair grows. On average, it is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

3. Can I still style my hair in different ways?

Absolutely! Edgy haircuts are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your edgy haircut looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and avoid over-styling or using heat tools on a daily basis. Regular trims will also help to keep your haircut looking fresh and edgy.

Now, armed with these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock an edgy short haircut that flatters your fine hair and round face. Go ahead, take the plunge, and transform your look!
