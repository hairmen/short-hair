Short Hair Suit Me Better Why You Should Consider Chopping Your Locks

Written by Oliver Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a change in your hairstyle, you might want to consider going short. Short hair not only looks stylish and trendy, but it can also be a practical and low-maintenance option. In this article, we'll explore why short hair can suit you better, how to achieve the perfect short haircut, and how to care for your new look.

Why Short Hair Can Suit You Better

Short hair can suit you better for a number of reasons:

Low Maintenance

Short hair requires minimal styling and upkeep, which is great if you're short on time or prefer a fuss-free look. You can easily wash and go or add a little product to create texture and volume.

Face Shape

Short hair can flatter a range of face shapes, including oval, heart, and square. A short haircut can also draw attention to your facial features and highlight your best assets.

Age

Short hair can make you look younger and more youthful, especially if you opt for a modern or edgy style. A short haircut can also help to conceal fine lines and wrinkles around the face and neck.

How to Achieve the Perfect Short Haircut

Before you take the plunge and chop off your locks, it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are some tips for achieving the perfect short haircut:

Consult a Professional

A hairdresser or stylist can help you choose a short haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. They can also advise you on the best products and styling techniques to maintain your new look.

Research Styles

Take inspiration from magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles to find a short haircut that you love. Consider your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle when choosing a style.

Be Bold

A short haircut can be a bold and daring choice, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths. Remember that hair grows back, so you can always change your mind and try something new.

Tips for Caring for Your Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep your new look in tip-top condition:

Wash and Condition Regularly

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished. Short hair can become greasy and oily quickly, so it's important to wash it regularly.

Invest in Good Products

Invest in high-quality hair products, such as styling gel, mousse, and hairspray, to keep your short hair looking sleek and polished. Look for products that are specifically designed for short hair.

Get Regular Trims

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and style. Visit your hairdresser every 6-8 weeks for a trim or touch-up.

FAQ about Short Hair

Is short hair suitable for all face shapes?

Short hair can flatter a range of face shapes, but it's important to choose a style that suits your individual features. Consult a professional stylist for advice on the best short haircut for your face shape.

Does short hair require a lot of maintenance?

Short hair is generally low-maintenance, but it does require regular trims and styling to keep it looking its best. Invest in good products and visit your hairdresser every 6-8 weeks for a trim.

Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

Yes, short hair can be styled for special occasions using products and styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners. Ask your hairdresser for advice on how to style your short hair for different events.

Final Thoughts

Short hair can be a stylish and practical choice for women of all ages and face shapes. By choosing the right style, consulting a professional, and caring for your new look, you can rock a short haircut with confidence and ease.
