How To Wear Bandana In Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Bandanas have been a fashion staple for decades, and it’s not just for women. Men can also rock this accessory, especially when it comes to hair. Whether you’re going for a casual or edgy look, a bandana can add a touch of style to your outfit. In this guide, we’ll show you how to wear bandana in hair guys.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Bandana in Hair Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing a Bandana in Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Wear Bandana in Hair Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s first understand the different ways to wear a bandana in hair:

  • Headband style
  • Pirate style
  • Top knot style
  • Ponytail style
  • Bun style

Each style gives a different vibe, so choose the one that suits your personality and outfit the most.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different ways to wear a bandana in hair, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve each style:

Headband Style

This is the most common way to wear a bandana in hair, and it’s pretty simple:

  1. Fold the bandana in half diagonally to form a triangle.
  2. Place the bandana on top of your head with the point of the triangle facing forward.
  3. Tie the ends of the bandana at the back of your head.
  4. Adjust the bandana to your desired position.

Pirate Style

This style is perfect for a casual or beach look:

  1. Follow the same steps as the headband style.
  2. Bring the ends of the bandana to the front of your head and tie a knot on your forehead.

Top Knot Style

This style is perfect for guys with longer hair:

  1. Gather your hair into a top knot.
  2. Place the bandana on top of your head with the point of the triangle facing forward.
  3. Tie the ends of the bandana around the base of the knot.

Ponytail Style

This style is perfect for guys with shorter hair:

  1. Tie your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Place the bandana on top of your head with the point of the triangle facing forward.
  3. Tie the ends of the bandana at the back of your head, just above the ponytail.

Bun Style

This style is perfect for a sleek and polished look:

  1. Gather your hair into a low bun.
  2. Place the bandana on top of your head with the point of the triangle facing forward.
  3. Tie the ends of the bandana around the bun.

Tips for Wearing a Bandana in Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a bandana in hair:

  • Choose a bandana that complements your outfit.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the bandana in place.
  • Don’t tie the bandana too tight as it can cause headaches.

FAQs

Can I wear a bandana in hair to work?

It depends on your workplace dress code. If you work in a creative or casual environment, a bandana can add a touch of personality to your outfit. However, if your workplace has a strict dress code, it’s best to avoid wearing a bandana in hair.

Can I wear a bandana in hair with glasses?

Absolutely! In fact, wearing a bandana in hair can add a stylish touch to your glasses.

Can I wear a bandana in hair if I have short hair?

Yes, you can! The ponytail style is perfect for guys with short hair.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a bandana in hair can cause hair breakage and damage if not done properly. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid wearing a bandana in hair every day to prevent hair breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid tying the bandana too tight to prevent headaches and hair breakage.
  • Don’t wear a bandana in hair while sleeping as it can cause hair breakage.

With these tips and step-by-step guide, you can now rock a bandana in hair like a pro. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with it!
