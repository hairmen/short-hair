How To Sleep With Bellami Hair Extensions

Written by Aprilia Sep 22, 2023 ¬∑ 3 min read

Are you struggling to figure out how to sleep with your Bellami hair extensions? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you sleep comfortably with your Bellami hair extensions.

  • How to Sleep with Bellami Hair Extensions

How to Sleep with Bellami Hair Extensions

Sleeping with Bellami hair extensions can be a bit tricky, but it is not impossible. Follow these simple steps to help you sleep comfortably with your extensions:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before going to bed, brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will prevent your hair extensions from getting tangled while you sleep.

Step 2: Secure Your Hair

Secure your Bellami hair extensions in a loose braid or ponytail. This will prevent the extensions from getting tangled or damaged while you sleep.

Step 3: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent friction between your hair and the pillow. This will also help to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you sleep comfortably with your Bellami hair extensions:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Secure your hair in a loose braid or ponytail.
  3. Put on a silk bonnet or use a silk pillowcase.
  4. Go to sleep and enjoy a good night's rest!

Tips for Sleeping with Bellami Hair Extensions

Here are some tips that will help you sleep comfortably with your Bellami hair extensions:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent friction between your hair and the pillow.
  • Secure your hair in a loose braid or ponytail to prevent tangles.
  • Use a silk bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as it can cause tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools before bed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sleep with wet hair extensions?

No, it is not recommended to sleep with wet hair extensions. Wet hair is more prone to tangling and breakage, which can damage your extensions over time.

Should I brush my hair extensions before bed?

Yes, it is important to brush your hair extensions before bed to remove any tangles or knots. This will prevent your extensions from getting tangled while you sleep.

Can I use a regular pillowcase?

While you can use a regular pillowcase, it is recommended to use a silk pillowcase to prevent friction between your hair and the pillow. This will also help to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.

Hair Care Tips for Bellami Hair Extensions

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your Bellami hair extensions:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your extensions.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions as much as possible.
  • Brush your extensions gently to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair or extensions.

Now that you know how to sleep with Bellami hair extensions, you can enjoy a good night's rest without worrying about damaging your extensions. Follow these simple steps, tips, and hair care advice to keep your extensions looking beautiful and healthy for as long as possible.
