Hairstyle For Oval Face Male Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 12, 2023 · 4 min read

As a man with an oval face and curly hair, finding the right hairstyle can be tricky. You want to showcase your curls, but also ensure that your face shape is complemented. In this guide, we’ll cover the best hairstyle options for oval face male curly hair, as well as provide tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Hairstyle for Oval Face Male Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Oval Face Male Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Oval Face Male Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Hairstyles for Oval Face Male Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Male Curly Hair

How to Choose a Hairstyle for Oval Face Male Curly Hair

When choosing a hairstyle for oval face male curly hair, it’s important to keep your face shape in mind. Oval faces are versatile and can work with a variety of hairstyles, but you want to choose one that complements your features.

Some popular options for oval face male curly hair include:

  • Short curly haircuts, such as a buzz cut or crew cut
  • Medium-length curly hairstyles, such as a messy fringe or textured curls
  • Long curly hair, such as shoulder-length curls or a curly man bun
  • Curly undercut hairstyles, which feature shorter sides and longer curls on top

Consider your personal style and the amount of time you’re willing to spend styling your hair when choosing a hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Oval Face Male Curly Hair

Styling oval face male curly hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it’s easy to achieve a great look. Follow these steps to style your curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a curl-defining product, such as a curl cream or gel
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls while maintaining their shape
  4. Apply a finishing product, such as a pomade or styling wax, to help hold your curls in place
  5. Use your fingers to shape your curls and create your desired style

Remember that curly hair is unique and may require some trial and error to find the right styling routine.

Tips for Maintaining Oval Face Male Curly Hair

Maintaining oval face male curly hair requires a bit of extra effort, but it’s worth it for the perfect curls. Here are some tips for keeping your curls healthy and defined:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can damage your curls
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep split ends at bay
  • Consider deep conditioning treatments to keep your curls moisturized and healthy

FAQs About Hairstyles for Oval Face Male Curly Hair

What hairstyles should I avoid with an oval face and curly hair?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can elongate or widen your face. Also avoid styles that are too slicked back or flat, as these can make your face appear wider.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

While it’s possible to straighten curly hair, it can be damaging and may require a lot of heat and product. Instead, embrace your curls and find a style that works for you.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it’s recommended to wash it every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of moisture.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Male Curly Hair

In addition to styling and maintaining your curly hair, it’s important to take care of it with a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can dry it out
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls, rather than a brush
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized

By following these tips and finding the right hairstyle for your oval face male curly hair, you can rock your curls with confidence.
