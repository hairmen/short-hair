How to Style Messy Curly Hair Guys

The key to styling messy curly hair is to embrace the natural texture of your hair. You don't want to try to make it look too perfect, as that can actually make it look frizzy or greasy. Instead, you want to create a relaxed, messy look that shows off your natural curls.

There are a few different ways to style messy curly hair, but one of the easiest and most popular ways is to use a styling cream or pomade. These products can help to define your curls and give them some weight and hold, without making your hair look greasy or crunchy.

Another option is to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This can help to dry your hair without disrupting your curls, which can create a more natural, messy look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to style messy curly hair guys in just a few easy steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. If your hair tends to be dry or frizzy, you may want to apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair before styling.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. Avoid applying too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  3. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create some texture. This will help to define your curls and create a messy, relaxed look.
  4. If you want more volume, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Hold the diffuser close to your roots and use a low heat setting to dry your hair. Avoid touching your hair too much as it dries, as this can disrupt your curls.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair again and create more texture. You can also use a bit more styling cream or pomade at this point if you feel like you need it.
  6. Finish by using a light hold hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips for Styling Messy Curly Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you style messy curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can disrupt your curls and create frizz.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • If your hair tends to be dry or frizzy, consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • If you have a special occasion coming up and want to create a more polished look, consider getting a professional haircut or styling.

FAQs

Q: Can I use gel instead of styling cream?

A: While gel can work for some people, it can also make your hair look crunchy or greasy if you use too much. Styling cream or pomade tends to be a better option for creating a natural, messy look.

Q: Can I style messy curly hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use air drying or diffusing to style your hair without using heat. However, keep in mind that it may take longer for your hair to dry, and you may not get as much volume or definition as you would with heat styling.

Hair Care for Messy Curly Hair

To keep your messy curly hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style messy curly hair guys in a way that looks effortless and cool. Embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
