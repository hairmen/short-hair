How To Look Androgynous With A Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face, you might think that achieving an androgynous look is impossible. However, with the right styling and makeup, anyone can look androgynous. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to look androgynous with a round face.

Description

Androgynous fashion is all about blurring the lines between masculine and feminine styles. It's a great way to express yourself and stand out from the crowd. However, if you have a round face, you might feel like you can't pull off an androgynous look. But don't worry, with the right techniques, you can look just as androgynous as anyone else.

How to Look Androgynous with a Round Face

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make all the difference when it comes to achieving an androgynous look. For those with round faces, a short, layered haircut can help to create angles and definition. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face look rounder.

Step 2: Play with Makeup

Makeup is a great way to create an androgynous look. For those with round faces, contouring can be your best friend. Use a darker shade of foundation or concealer to create shadows on the sides of your face, making it look more angular. Avoid using blush on the apples of your cheeks, as this will make your face look rounder.

Step 3: Dress for the Occasion

Choosing the right clothing is also important when it comes to achieving an androgynous look. For a more masculine look, opt for tailored suits or blazers. For a more feminine look, try wearing skirts or dresses with a more structured silhouette. Avoid clothing that adds volume to your midsection, as this will make your face look rounder in comparison.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve an androgynous look:

  • Accessorize with hats or statement jewelry to add some edge to your look.
  • Try experimenting with different textures, such as leather or denim.
  • Keep your eyebrows groomed and shaped, as this can help to create a more angular look.
  • Invest in a good pair of shoes, such as combat boots or loafers, to complete your look.

FAQ

Can I still wear makeup if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Makeup can be a great way to create an androgynous look, regardless of your face shape. Just make sure to use contouring techniques to create shadows and angles.

What kind of clothing should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid clothing that adds volume to your midsection, as this will make your face look rounder in comparison. Instead, opt for clothing with a more structured silhouette.

Can I still wear dresses if I want to achieve an androgynous look?

Yes! Dresses can be a great way to achieve an androgynous look. Just make sure to choose styles with a more structured silhouette, rather than those that add volume to your midsection.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining a short, layered haircut, regular trims are key. Make sure to visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh. Use a lightweight styling product, such as a texturizing spray, to add volume and definition to your hair without weighing it down.
