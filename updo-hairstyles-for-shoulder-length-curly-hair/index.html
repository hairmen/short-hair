Updo Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing your shoulder length curly hair down all the time? Do you want to switch things up with a chic and elegant updo hairstyle? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be exploring different updo hairstyles that are perfect for shoulder length curly hair. Whether you have a special event or just want to switch up your everyday look, these updo hairstyles are sure to impress.

Description

Updo hairstyles are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish. However, finding an updo hairstyle that works for shoulder length curly hair can be a challenge. Shoulder length curly hair is versatile, but it can also be tricky to work with. That's why we've compiled a list of updo hairstyles that are perfect for this hair type. From braids to buns, we've got you covered.

How To

Creating an updo hairstyle for shoulder length curly hair may seem daunting, but it's actually quite simple. All you need are a few styling tools and some patience. Here's how to create the perfect updo:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to tame frizz and make your curls more manageable. Once you've washed your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls.

Step 2: Choose your updo

There are many updo hairstyles to choose from, so pick one that works for your hair type and the occasion. Some popular updo hairstyles for shoulder length curly hair include braids, buns, and twists.

Step 3: Create your updo

Follow the instructions for your chosen updo hairstyle. Generally, you'll need to gather your hair into a ponytail or bun and secure it with bobby pins. Use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls or waves.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

To keep your updo in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to tame any flyaways and keep your curls looking defined.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating an updo hairstyle for shoulder length curly hair:

  • Use hair accessories like clips and headbands to add some extra flair to your updo.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different updo hairstyles to find what works for you.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to help define your curls before creating your updo.
  • If you're creating an updo for a special event, practice beforehand to avoid any last-minute disasters.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about updo hairstyles for shoulder length curly hair:

What updo hairstyles work best for shoulder length curly hair?

Braids, buns, and twists are all great updo hairstyles for shoulder length curly hair. They help to keep your hair out of your face while still showcasing your natural curls.

How do I keep my updo in place all day?

Use bobby pins and hairspray to keep your updo in place. You can also use a hair tie or elastic band to secure your hair before creating your updo.

Can I create an updo hairstyle for shoulder length curly hair without using heat?

Absolutely! You can create an updo hairstyle for shoulder length curly hair without using heat by using braids, twists, and buns. These styles are perfect for those who want to avoid heat damage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips to keep your shoulder length curly hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help tame frizz and add shine.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls before styling.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture and vitality to your curls.

With these tips and updo hairstyles, you can rock your shoulder length curly hair with confidence. Experiment with different updo styles to find what works for you, and don't forget to take care of your curls with proper hair care.
