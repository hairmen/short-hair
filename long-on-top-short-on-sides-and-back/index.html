Long On Top Short On Sides And Back A Trendy Hairstyle For Men

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and versatile, then long on top short on sides and back might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been in vogue for a while now and is still going strong in 2023. It's a popular choice among men of all ages and hair types, and for good reason. The long on top short on sides and back hairstyle is easy to maintain, looks great, and can be styled in different ways to suit your personality and preferences.

What is Long on Top Short on Sides and Back?

The long on top short on sides and back hairstyle, as the name suggests, is characterized by longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on the top can be anywhere from 2 to 6 inches long, while the sides and back are usually trimmed to a shorter length. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back. The hairstyle can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

How to Get Long on Top Short on Sides and Back Hairstyle?

If you're interested in getting the long on top short on sides and back hairstyle, here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step is to choose the right haircut. You can either go to a professional hairstylist or do it yourself at home. The key is to have longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. You can ask your hairstylist to give you a fade or undercut on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it's important to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair clean, healthy, and easy to style.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is clean and dry, it's time to style it. You can use a hair gel, wax, or pomade to style your hair in different ways. You can slick it back, make it messy or tousled, or create a side part. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining Long on Top Short on Sides and Back Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your long on top short on sides and back hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to keep your look fresh and interesting.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much heat, as it can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

FAQs About Long on Top Short on Sides and Back Hairstyle

Q. Is the long on top short on sides and back hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A. Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit different hair types, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

Q. How often should I get a trim?

A. It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Q. Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A. Yes, you can experiment with different styles to suit different occasions, whether it's a formal event or a casual outing with friends.

Hair Care Tips for Long on Top Short on Sides and Back Hairstyle

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long on top short on sides and back hairstyle looking great:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean, healthy, and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Overall, the long on top short on sides and back hairstyle is a trendy and versatile option for men who want to look stylish and chic. With a little bit of care and maintenance, you can rock this hairstyle and make it your own.
