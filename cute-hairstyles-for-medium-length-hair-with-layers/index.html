Cute Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair With Layers

Written by Oliver Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length hair with layers, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that is cute and flattering. Luckily, there are many hairstyles that work well with this hair type. Whether you have natural waves or straight locks, there is a look that will suit you. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve cute hairstyles for medium length hair with layers.

Description

Medium length hair with layers is a versatile hair type that can be styled in many different ways. This hair type is not too short or too long, making it perfect for a range of hairstyles. Layers add texture and movement to the hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. Cute hairstyles for medium length hair with layers can range from simple updos to beachy waves.

How to

To achieve cute hairstyles for medium length hair with layers, you will need some styling tools and products. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl your hair in sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use a smaller curling iron for tighter curls and a larger curling iron for looser waves. Once you have curled all of your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

Step 3: Add Texture

To add texture to your hair, use a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray. Spray it onto your hair and scrunch it with your fingers. This will give your hair a beachy, tousled look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Tips

When styling your medium length hair with layers, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Choose a curling iron that is the right size for your hair.
  • Use a round brush to add volume when blow drying your hair.
  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

What are some cute hairstyles for medium length hair with layers?

Some cute hairstyles for medium length hair with layers include beachy waves, a messy bun, a half-up, half-down style, and a braided crown.

How do I curl my medium length hair with layers?

To curl your medium length hair with layers, use a curling iron and start at the bottom. Curl each section of hair and work your way up. Use your fingers to loosen the curls for a more natural look.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair with layers looking its best, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Cute hairstyles for medium length hair with layers are easy to achieve with the right tools and techniques. Whether you prefer a tousled, beachy look or a sleek and polished style, there is a hairstyle that will work for you. Experiment with different styles and products to find the ones that suit your hair type and personal style.
