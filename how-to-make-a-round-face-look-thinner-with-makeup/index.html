How To Make A Round Face Look Thinner With Makeup Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find a way to make your round face look thinner? Don't worry! With the right makeup techniques, you can easily create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we'll guide you through step-by-step on how to make a round face look thinner with makeup.

How to Make a Round Face Look Thinner with Makeup

Step 1: Create a Flawless Base

Start by creating a flawless base for your makeup. Apply a primer to smooth out your skin and reduce the appearance of pores. Then, use a foundation that matches your skin tone and blend it well. Make sure to blend it down to your neck to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 2: Contour Your Cheeks

Contouring is the key to making a round face look thinner. Use a matte bronzer to contour your cheeks. Apply it in a diagonal line from the top of your ear to the corner of your mouth. Blend it well using a fluffy brush to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Highlight Your Cheekbones

Highlighting your cheekbones will draw attention to the highest point of your face, making it appear slimmer. Apply a highlighter on top of your cheekbones, just above the contour line. Blend it well using a fluffy brush.

Step 4: Define Your Jawline

Defining your jawline will make your face appear more structured and less round. Use a contour powder or a matte bronzer to define your jawline. Apply it along your jawline and blend it well.

Step 5: Define Your Brows

Well-defined brows will help to balance out your face and make it appear more symmetrical. Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in your brows and create a defined arch.

Step 6: Apply Eye Makeup

Draw attention to your eyes by applying eye makeup. Use a neutral eyeshadow on your eyelids and a darker shade on the crease to create depth. Apply mascara to your upper and lower lashes to define your eyes.

Step 7: Use Lipstick

Choose a lipstick that complements your skin tone and apply it to your lips. Avoid using dark or bold colors as they will make your face appear fuller. Instead, opt for nude or light shades.

Tips for Making a Round Face Look Thinner

Here are some additional tips to help you make your round face look thinner:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face
  • Wear earrings that are long and thin
  • Use a matte finish powder to set your makeup
  • Use a light hand when applying blush

FAQ

Q: Can makeup really make my face look thinner?

Yes, with the right makeup techniques, you can easily create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: How do I choose the right contour shade for my skin tone?

Choose a shade that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone.

Q: Can I still wear bold lipstick if I have a round face?

Yes, but make sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and avoid dark or bold colors.

Hair Care

Aside from makeup, your hairstyle can also make a big difference in making your face appear thinner. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your face, such as big curls or a lot of layers. Instead, opt for long, straight hair or a sleek bob.

Now that you know how to make a round face look thinner with makeup, it's time to put these tips into practice. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to create a slimmer, more defined face in no time!
