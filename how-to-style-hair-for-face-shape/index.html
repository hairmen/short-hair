How To Style Hair For Face Shape A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Nov 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle is essential to enhance your facial features and complement your overall look. However, with so many options available, it can be challenging to determine which hairstyle will suit your face shape the best. In this article, we will guide you through the process of styling your hair for your face shape, from determining your face shape to choosing the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to styling your hair for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Here's how you can determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead is wider than your jawline.
  • Round: Your face length and width are approximately the same, and your jawline is soft.
  • Square: Your face length, width, and jawline are all approximately the same.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, and your chin is pointed.
  • Long: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all approximately the same width.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start choosing the right hairstyle that flatters your features.

Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Oval faces are considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, if you want to accentuate your features, you can try the following hairstyles:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob cut
  • Wavy or curly hair
  • Updo hairstyles

These hairstyles will help highlight your cheekbones and jawline and create a balanced look.

Hairstyles for Round Face Shape

Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear wider, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs. Instead, try the following hairstyles to elongate your face:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • High ponytail
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Messy bun

These hairstyles will create the illusion of a longer face and add definition to your cheekbones and jawline.

Hairstyles for Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your angular features. Try the following hairstyles:

  • Soft layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long bob
  • Loose waves
  • Low ponytail

These hairstyles will help create a more rounded look and add softness to your features.

Hairstyles for Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance your forehead and chin. Try the following hairstyles:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bob
  • Wavy hair
  • Half-up half-down hairstyle
  • Pixie cut with volume on top

These hairstyles will help create a more balanced look and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Hairstyles for Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you want to balance your cheekbones and forehead. Try the following hairstyles:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bob
  • Wavy hair
  • Pixie cut with volume on top
  • Low ponytail

These hairstyles will help create a more balanced look and highlight your cheekbones and jawline.

Tips for Styling Your Hair for Your Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for your face shape:

  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine which hairstyles will flatter your features the most.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect one that suits your personality and lifestyle.
  • Use hair products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add volume or hold your hairstyle in place.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Accessorize your hair with hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add a touch of style to your look.

FAQs About Styling Hair for Face Shape

Q: Can I still try a hairstyle that doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Of course! These guidelines are meant to be a starting point, but you should always feel free to experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.

Q: Can I change my face shape with a hairstyle?

A: While a hairstyle can accentuate or soften certain features, it cannot change the underlying bone structure of your face.

Q: How often should I change my hairstyle?

A: There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. It ultimately depends on your preferences and lifestyle. Some people prefer to stick with one hairstyle for years, while others like to change it up every few months.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

After you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hairstyle looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use heat protectant when using heat styling tools, such as hair straighteners or curling irons.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle's shape.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these hair care tips, you can maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking its best.

Conclusion

Styling your hair for your face shape can make a world of difference in enhancing your features and complementing your overall look. By following these guidelines and tips, you can choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and personality. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
