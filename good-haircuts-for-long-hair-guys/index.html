Good Haircuts For Long Hair Guys In 2023

Written by Christine Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a guy with long hair, you know the importance of finding the right haircut that suits your style and personality. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best haircuts for long hair guys in 2023, including how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care.

  2. How to Achieve Good Haircuts for Long Hair Guys
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Long Hair

Description

Long hair is a popular trend for men in 2023, and there are plenty of haircuts to choose from that can help you stand out from the crowd. Good haircuts for long hair guys include the classic flow hairstyle, the messy bun, the undercut, the man bun, and the side swept bangs. Each haircut offers a unique style that can be customized to suit your personality and preferences.

How to Achieve Good Haircuts for Long Hair Guys

The key to achieving good haircuts for long hair guys is to find a style that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Consult with a hairstylist to help you determine which style works best for you. Consider the length, texture, and thickness of your hair to help you choose the right haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve some of the most popular haircuts for long hair guys:

Classic Flow Hairstyle

1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it.
2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair.
3. Comb your hair back and to the side, creating a flowy look.
4. Blow dry your hair on low heat to set the style.
5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Messy Bun

1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with a hair tie.
3. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.
4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Undercut

1. Shave the sides and back of your head.
2. Leave the top of your hair long.
3. Style the top of your hair into a slicked back look or a messy style.
4. Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to hold the style in place.
5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Man Bun

1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with a hair tie.
3. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Side Swept Bangs

1. Part your hair to one side.
2. Sweep the front part of your hair to the side, creating a side swept bang.
3. Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to hold the style in place.
4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair

Here are some tips for styling long hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Don’t wash your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply hair products to damp hair for better hold.
  • Blow dry your hair on low heat to avoid damaging it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about good haircuts for long hair guys:

What is the best haircut for long hair guys?

The best haircut for long hair guys depends on their face shape, hair type, and personal style. Some popular haircuts include the classic flow hairstyle, the messy bun, the undercut, the man bun, and the side swept bangs.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Long Hair Guys

Here are some tips for taking care of long hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and haircuts, you can confidently rock your long hair in 2023. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to find the best haircut for you and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
