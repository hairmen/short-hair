How To Make Thin Hair On Top Look Thicker Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Oct 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thin hair on top, you may feel self-conscious about your appearance. Fortunately, there are many ways to make your hair look thicker and fuller. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for achieving a voluminous hairdo that will make you feel confident and beautiful.

How to Make Thin Hair on Top Look Thicker:

1. Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make a huge difference when it comes to making thin hair look thicker. Ask your stylist for a layered cut that will create volume and movement. Avoid blunt cuts or bangs, as they can make your hair look flat and thin.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

Look for a volumizing shampoo that is designed to add body and fullness to your hair. These shampoos contain ingredients that help to lift your hair at the roots, making it look thicker and fuller.

3. Condition Carefully

While it's important to condition your hair to keep it healthy, using too much conditioner can weigh down thin hair and make it look flat. Use a lightweight conditioner and apply it only to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.

4. Blow Dry with Care

Blow drying your hair can help to add volume and lift, but you need to do it carefully. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while you blow dry, and finish with a blast of cool air to lock in the volume.

5. Try a Volumizing Mousse

A volumizing mousse can work wonders for thin hair. Apply a small amount to your roots and work it through your hair, then blow dry as usual. The mousse will help to create volume and hold your style in place.

6. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair may seem like an old-fashioned trick, but it can be a great way to add volume and fullness to thin hair. Use a fine-tooth comb to gently backcomb your hair at the roots, then smooth the top layer for a polished finish.

7. Use Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a more permanent solution to thin hair, consider using hair extensions. There are many different types of extensions available, from clip-ins to tape-ins to sew-ins. Talk to your stylist about which type would be best for your hair type and lifestyle.

8. Experiment with Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can be a great way to add volume and interest to thin hair. Try using a headband, scarf, or clip to create a focal point and draw attention away from thinning areas.

9. Change Your Part

If you always part your hair in the same place, try switching it up to create the illusion of volume. A deep side part can add instant lift and fullness to thin hair.

10. Consider a Hair Transplant

If you've tried everything and still can't achieve the full, voluminous hair you desire, you may want to consider a hair transplant. This procedure involves transferring hair follicles from one part of your scalp to another to create a fuller, thicker head of hair. Talk to your doctor about whether this option is right for you.

Tips for Maintaining Thicker-Looking Hair

Once you've achieved your desired level of volume and fullness, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking thick and healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look dry and flat.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as brushes can pull and damage thin hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can thin hair be made thicker?

Yes, there are many ways to make thin hair look thicker, from using volumizing shampoos to getting a layered haircut.

2. What is the best haircut for thin hair?

A layered haircut is best for thin hair, as it creates movement and volume.

3. Can hair extensions damage thin hair?

Hair extensions can damage thin hair if they are not applied correctly or if they are too heavy. Talk to your stylist about which type of extensions would be best for your hair type.

4. Can hair transplants really make hair look thicker?

Yes, hair transplants can be a great way to create a fuller, thicker head of hair. Talk to your doctor about whether this option is right for you.

In Conclusion

If you have thin hair on top, don't despair. There are many ways to make your hair look thicker and fuller, from choosing the right haircut to using volumizing shampoos and mousse. Experiment with different techniques and products until you find the ones that work best for you, and enjoy your new, voluminous hairdo!
