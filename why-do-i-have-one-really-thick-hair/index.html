Why Do I Have One Really Thick Hair Understanding The Science Behind It

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs

Description

Have you ever noticed one strand of hair that seems to be thicker than the rest? You’re not alone! Many people have one hair that stands out from the rest. But why does this happen? In this article, we’ll explore the science behind why you might have one really thick hair.

How To

Before we dive into the science, let’s cover some basics. Hair thickness is determined by the size of the hair follicle. The size of your hair follicles is determined by genetics. So, if you have one really thick hair, it’s likely because that hair follicle is larger than the others.

Step By Step

So, why does one hair follicle end up larger than the others? It’s not entirely clear, but it could be due to a variety of factors:

  • Genetics: As we mentioned earlier, hair thickness is determined by genetics. It’s possible that you inherited a larger hair follicle from one of your parents.
  • Hormones: Hormones play a big role in hair growth. It’s possible that a hormonal imbalance could cause one hair follicle to grow larger than the others.
  • Injury: If you’ve ever had an injury to your scalp, it’s possible that the hair follicle was damaged and then grew back larger than the others.
  • Age: As we age, our hair follicles can become smaller, which can lead to thinner hair. It’s possible that one follicle just didn’t get the memo and continued to grow.

Tips

So, what can you do about that one really thick hair? The answer is…not much. Since hair thickness is determined by genetics, there’s really no way to change it. However, you can embrace it! That one really thick hair can actually be a cool feature that sets you apart.

FAQs

Q: Is it normal to have one really thick hair?

A: Yes, it’s completely normal!

Q: Can I make my hair thicker?

A: Unfortunately, no. Hair thickness is determined by genetics.

Q: Should I cut my really thick hair?

A: That’s up to you! If it bothers you, you can definitely cut it. But if you like it, embrace it!

Hair Care

While you can’t change the thickness of your hair, you can take care of it to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Get enough sleep
  • Protect your hair from the sun

Remember, that one really thick hair is just a small part of what makes you unique. Embrace it and take care of your locks!
