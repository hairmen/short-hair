Best Hair For Oblong Face

Written by Robby Aug 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oblong face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. An oblong face is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a high forehead and a pointed chin. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hair for oblong face, how to style it, tips, and hair care tips.

Description

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oblong faces, let's take a closer look at what an oblong face shape is. As mentioned earlier, an oblong face is long and narrow, with a high forehead and a pointed chin. The cheekbones are typically not prominent, and the face has a straight, almost rectangular shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, here's a quick and easy way to find out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror
  2. Pull your hair back off your face
  3. Trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner
  4. Step back and look at the shape you've drawn

If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are about the same width, you likely have an oblong face.

Best Hair for Oblong Face

Now that you know your face shape let's talk about the best hair for oblong face. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle is to add width and volume to your face. Here are some hairstyles that will flatter an oblong face:

1. Shoulder-Length Hair with Layers

Shoulder-length hair with layers is a great option for oblong faces. The layers will add volume and movement to your hair, while the length will balance out your face shape. You can also add side-swept bangs to soften the look.

2. Long Bob

A long bob, also known as a lob, is another great option for oblong faces. The length will add width to your face, while the blunt cut will give you a chic and sophisticated look. You can also add some waves or curls for added volume.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling daring, a pixie cut can also work well for oblong faces. The short cut will balance out your face shape and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. You can also add some texture or layers to add volume.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you've chosen the best hair for oblong face, here's a step-by-step guide on how to style it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume
  3. If you have long hair, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair
  4. Apply a volumizing mousse or hairspray to hold the style in place
  5. If you have bangs, blow-dry them with a round brush and sweep them to the side

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face look even longer
  • Avoid hairstyles with too much volume at the crown, as they can make your face look longer
  • Avoid center parts, as they can elongate your face even more
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or styling products, as they can weigh down your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils

FAQs

1. Can I wear bangs with an oblong face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with an oblong face. However, it's best to avoid blunt bangs that cut straight across your forehead. Instead, go for side-swept bangs that will soften your features.

2. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail with an oblong face?

Absolutely! A ponytail can be a great option for oblong faces. However, make sure to add some volume to your hair at the crown to balance out your face shape.

3. What kind of hairstyles should I avoid with an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid center parts and hairstyles with too much volume at the crown, as they can elongate your face even more.

Now that you know the best hair for oblong face, it's time to choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Remember to add volume and width to your hair to balance out your face shape, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
