How To Add Volume To Thin Frizzy Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of struggling with your thin, frizzy hair? Do you wish you could add more volume to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will go over the best tips and tricks to add volume to your thin frizzy hair.

Description

Thin, frizzy hair can be frustrating to deal with. It can be difficult to style and often lacks volume. However, there are several ways you can add volume to your hair to create a fuller, more voluminous look.

How to

There are several ways to add volume to your thin frizzy hair. Here are some of the best methods:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a great way to add volume. This technique allows the hair to stand away from the scalp, creating a fuller look. To do this, flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair using a round brush.

2. Use Volumizing Products

Using volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, can help give your hair a boost. Apply these products to the roots of your hair, then blow dry using a round brush to add even more volume.

3. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can also help create more volume. Use a teasing comb to backcomb the hair at the crown of your head, then smooth the top layer to hide the teased hair.

Step-by-Step

Step 1:

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2:

Blow dry your hair upside down using a round brush.

Step 3:

Apply a volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray to the roots of your hair.

Step 4:

Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to add volume.

Step 5:

Tease the hair at the crown of your head using a teasing comb.

Step 6:

Smooth the top layer of hair to hide the teased hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help add volume to your thin frizzy hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking flat. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

3. Use a Diffuser

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help add volume and reduce frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I add volume to my hair without blow drying it?

A: Yes, you can add volume to your hair by using volumizing products and teasing it.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. However, washing your hair every other day or every few days is a good starting point.

Q: Can I use a straightener to add volume to my hair?

A: No, using a straightener can actually flatten your hair and make it look even thinner.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to maintaining its health and adding volume. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Use Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant spray before blow drying your hair can help prevent damage and breakage.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

3. Use a Deep Conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week can help nourish and moisturize your hair, making it look shinier and healthier.

By following these tips and tricks, you can add volume to your thin frizzy hair and achieve the voluminous, full look you desire.
