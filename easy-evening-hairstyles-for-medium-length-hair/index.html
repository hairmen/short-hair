Description

Are you tired of always wearing your hair down or in a basic ponytail for evening events? If you have medium length hair, there are plenty of easy and elegant hairstyles to try out for your next outing. Whether you're attending a formal event or just going out for a night with friends, these hairstyles will have you looking and feeling your best.

How to

Before jumping into the hairstyles, it's important to know how to prepare your hair for the evening. Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that work well with your hair type. Blow dry your hair or let it air dry, depending on your preference. If you have naturally curly hair, you can enhance your curls with a curling mousse or cream. Next, gather your hair styling tools, such as a curling wand or straightener, hair ties, bobby pins, and hairspray.

Step by step

1. Messy Bun

Start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy look. Finish with hairspray to hold the bun in place.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

Section off the top half of your hair and pull it back into a ponytail. Secure with a hair tie. Use a curling wand or straightener to add some waves or curls to the bottom half of your hair. Finish with hairspray.

3. Side Braid

Part your hair to one side and braid the hair on the opposite side. You can choose to do a regular braid or a fishtail braid. Secure the end with a hair tie and pull out a few strands of hair around your face. Finish with hairspray.

4. Sleek Ponytail

Brush your hair back into a sleek ponytail. Use a straightener to straighten any flyaways or frizz. Finish with hairspray.

5. Twisted Updo

Start by twisting two sections of hair from the front of your head. Bring the two twists to the back of your head and secure with bobby pins. Gather the rest of your hair into a low bun and secure with bobby pins. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

  • Use heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • If you have thin hair, try teasing your roots to add volume to your hairstyles.
  • Accessorize your hairstyles with hair clips, headbands, or flowers.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for medium length hair?

The best hairstyle for medium length hair depends on the occasion and personal preference. Some popular options include messy buns, half-up half-down styles, and sleek ponytails.

How can I keep my evening hairstyle in place all night?

Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hair healthy and strong, make sure to regularly wash and condition your hair using products that work well with your hair type. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair. Limit the use of hot tools and give your hair a break every now and then. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
