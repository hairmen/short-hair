How To Cut Older Men s Hair Short Back And Sides A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read
If you're looking to give an older man a fresh new look, one of the best ways to do it is by cutting his hair short back and sides. This classic hairstyle is simple, low-maintenance, and looks great on almost anyone. In this guide, we'll take you through the process of cutting an older man's hair short back and sides, step by step. We'll also give you some tips on how to make the process as easy and stress-free as possible. Let's get started!

Description

Short back and sides is a classic men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides of the head, while leaving the hair on top slightly longer. This style is versatile and can be modified to suit any face shape or hair type. It's also a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling, making it a great choice for older men who want to look their best without spending a lot of time on their hair.

How to Cut Older Men's Hair Short Back and Sides

To cut an older man's hair short back and sides, you'll need the following tools: - Clippers - Scissors - Comb - Haircutting cape or towel - Spray bottle filled with water - Mirror Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps: 1. Start by washing the hair and towel-drying it until it's damp. 2. Use the clippers to cut the hair on the back and sides of the head to your desired length. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown, using a clipper guard to ensure a consistent length. 3. Use the scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the back and sides. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle to create a natural-looking blend. 4. Use the comb to lift the hair on top and cut it to your desired length. You can cut it straight across or at an angle, depending on the look you're going for. 5. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven sections. 6. Use the clippers to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck, using a smaller clipper guard to get a closer cut. 7. Use the comb and scissors to blend the hair around the ears and the back of the neck with the rest of the hair. 8. Check the hair in the mirror from all angles to make sure it's even and well-blended. 9. Clean up any loose hairs around the ears and the back of the neck with the clippers or scissors. 10. Style the hair as desired using a small amount of pomade or styling product.

Tips

- Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to a shorter one to avoid cutting the hair too short. - Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair as needed. - Take your time and go slowly to avoid making mistakes. - Use a mirror to check the hair from all angles. - Have the person sit still and avoid making sudden movements to prevent accidental cuts.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut an older man's hair short back and sides at home? A: Yes, you can cut an older man's hair short back and sides at home with the right tools and some practice. However, if you're not confident in your hairstyling skills, it's best to leave it to a professional. Q: What kind of face shape is best for short back and sides? A: Short back and sides is a versatile hairstyle that can suit any face shape. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep an older man's hair looking great after a short back and sides haircut, follow these tips: - Shampoo and condition the hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable. - Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons, as it can damage the hair. - Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and prevent split ends.
