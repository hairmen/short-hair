Men s Haircut Longer Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Sep 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle that is easy to maintain? Look no further than the men's haircut longer top short sides. This popular hairstyle has been embraced by men of all ages and hair types, and it's not hard to see why. In this ultimate guide, we will discuss everything you need to know about this versatile haircut, including how to achieve it, tips for maintenance and care, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The men's haircut longer top short sides is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast between the longer and shorter sections of hair, giving the hairstyle a modern and edgy look. This style can be achieved with any hair type, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

How to Achieve the Men's Haircut Longer Top Short Sides

If you are interested in getting the men's haircut longer top short sides, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this style. Once you have found a professional, you can follow these steps to achieve the look:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step in achieving the men's haircut longer top short sides is to decide on the length of the hair on the top of the head. This can vary depending on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Generally, the hair on the top should be around 2-4 inches long.

Step 2: Determine the Fade

The next step is to determine the length of the hair on the sides and back. This is where the fade comes in. The fade is a gradual transition from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back. You can choose from a high fade, mid fade, or low fade, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once you have determined the length of the hair on the top and sides, your barber will begin cutting and styling your hair. They will use scissors or clippers to cut the hair to the desired length and shape it to create the desired style. You can choose to style the hair on top in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

Tips for Maintenance and Care

Once you have achieved the men's haircut longer top short sides, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintenance and care:

Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential for maintaining the men's haircut longer top short sides. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, if you want a sleek, polished look, use a high-quality pomade. If you prefer a more natural, messy style, use a salt spray or texturizing spray.

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking sharp, it's important to get regular trims. This will help maintain the length and shape of your hair and prevent split ends and other damage. Most men will need a trim every 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly their hair grows.

Protect Your Hair from Damage

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to protect it from damage. This means avoiding harsh chemicals, heat styling tools, and excessive exposure to the sun. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut longer top short sides:

Is the men's haircut longer top short sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the men's haircut longer top short sides can be achieved with any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often do I need to get a trim?

Most men will need a trim every 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly their hair grows.

Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, you can style the hair on top in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo, conditioning your hair after every wash, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. You should also avoid harsh chemicals, heat styling tools, and excessive exposure to the sun.

In conclusion, the men's haircut longer top short sides is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that can be achieved with any hair type. By following a good hair care routine and getting regular trims, you can keep your haircut looking great and maintain its shape and length. Whether you prefer a sleek, polished look or a more natural, messy style, the men's haircut longer top short sides is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
