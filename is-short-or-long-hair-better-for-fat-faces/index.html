Is Short Or Long Hair Better For Fat Faces

Written by Robby Sep 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, many people with a round or fat face often wonder whether short or long hair is better for their face shape. While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, there are some general guidelines that can help you decide which length will look best on you.

Description

If you have a round or fat face, you may worry that certain hairstyles will make your face look even rounder or fuller. While this can be a concern, the good news is that there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and bring out your best features.

When it comes to deciding between short and long hair, there are a few things to consider. Short hair can be a good choice for a round face because it can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the width. However, long hair can also be a good choice if it is styled in the right way.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Fat Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a fat face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  1. Consider your face shape: While a round face is often referred to as a "fat" face, it's important to remember that there are different face shapes within this category. For example, you may have a round face with prominent cheekbones or a round face with a more angular jawline. Understanding your specific face shape can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.
  2. Think about length: As mentioned earlier, both short and long hair can work for a round face, but it's important to choose the right length for your specific face shape. A general rule of thumb is to avoid hairstyles that end at the widest part of your face, as this can make your face look even rounder.
  3. Consider texture: The texture of your hair can also play a role in how flattering a hairstyle is for your face shape. For example, if you have very fine hair, a blunt bob may not be the best choice as it can make your face look even rounder. Instead, a layered cut can add volume and movement to your hair.
  4. Look for hairstyles that create height: Adding height to your hair can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. This can be achieved through styles such as a top knot, a high ponytail, or a voluminous updo.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Follow these steps to choose the right hairstyle for your fat face:

  1. Determine your face shape: Take a close look at your face in the mirror and try to identify its shape. Is it round? Oval? Heart-shaped?
  2. Consider your hair texture: Think about the natural texture of your hair. Is it fine? Thick? Curly?
  3. Choose a length: Decide whether you want short or long hair, and choose a length that flatters your face shape.
  4. Look for styles that add height: As mentioned earlier, adding height to your hair can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Look for styles that create volume at the crown of your head.
  5. Consult with a stylist: If you're still not sure which hairstyle is best for you, consider consulting with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and works well with your hair texture.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle for a Fat Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a fat face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make a round face look even rounder. Instead, opt for layered cuts that add movement and volume to your hair.
  • Consider bangs: Bangs can be a good choice for a round face, as they can help to create a more angular look. However, be sure to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.
  • Play with different styles: Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles until you find one that works for you. Experiment with different lengths, textures, and styles to see what looks best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is short hair or long hair better for a fat face?

Both short and long hair can work for a fat face, but it's important to choose the right length for your specific face shape. Short hair can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the width, while long hair can be flattering if it is styled in the right way.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a fat face?

Blunt cuts and hairstyles that end at the widest part of your face should generally be avoided if you have a fat face. Instead, opt for layered cuts, styles that add height, and hairstyles that create movement and volume.

Can bangs be flattering for a fat face?

Yes, bangs can be a good choice for a round face, as they can help to create a more angular look. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape.

Hair Care for a Fat Face

Regardless of which hairstyle you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Remember, the most important thing when choosing a hairstyle is to choose one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you prefer short or long hair, there is a hairstyle out there that will flatter your face shape and bring out your best features.
