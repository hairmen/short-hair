How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts for yourself or your family? Learning how to cut men’s hair with clippers can save you time and money. With the help of a YouTube video, you can easily learn the basics of cutting hair with clippers. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting men’s hair with clippers in a relaxed English language.

Description

Before we dive into the “how-to” of cutting hair with clippers, it’s important to understand the tools you’ll need. You’ll need a set of clippers, guards, scissors, a comb, and a cape or towel to cover the person’s clothes. It’s also important to have a clean and well-lit area to work in.

How to

Now, let’s get started! First, you’ll want to start with clean and dry hair. If the hair is wet, it will be harder to cut and may not give you the desired result. Begin by using the longest guard on your clippers and start at the base of the neck. Move the clippers upward in a straight line, following the natural curve of the head. Continue this process, moving from the back of the head to the sides, and finally, the top.

After you’ve finished using the longest guard, switch to a shorter one and repeat the process. Remember to always use the guards in order from longest to shortest, to avoid making any mistakes. You can always go back and cut more hair, but it’s much harder to fix a mistake.

Step-by-Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use the longest guard on your clippers.

3. Start at the base of the neck and move upward in a straight line.

4. Move from the back of the head to the sides, and finally, the top.

5. Switch to a shorter guard and repeat the process.

6. Use guards in order from longest to shortest.

7. Always go back and cut more hair if needed.

8. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.

9. Clean up the hairline around the ears and neck with a smaller guard or no guard at all.

10. Style the hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting men’s hair with clippers:

- Start with the longest guard and work your way down to avoid making mistakes.

- Take your time and go slowly, especially if you’re new to cutting hair.

- Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.

- Clean up the hairline around the ears and neck with a smaller guard or no guard at all.

- Watch a YouTube video to familiarize yourself with the process before you begin.

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have when learning how to cut men’s hair with clippers:

- How do I choose the right guard size?

You’ll want to choose a guard size based on the length of hair you want to leave. The longer the guard, the longer the hair will be.

- How often should I replace my clippers?

You should replace your clippers when they become dull or stop working properly.

- Can I use clippers to cut my own hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut your own hair, but it may be more difficult to get the back of your head.

Hair Care

After you’ve finished cutting the hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for the person’s hair type. If the person wants to style their hair, use a product that’s suitable for their hair type and style.

Cutting men’s hair with clippers may seem daunting at first, but with the help of a YouTube video and these tips, you’ll be able to give great haircuts in no time. Remember to take your time and go slowly, and always use the guards in order from longest to shortest. Happy cutting!
