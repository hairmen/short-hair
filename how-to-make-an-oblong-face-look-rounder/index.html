How To Make An Oblong Face Look Rounder

Written by William Sep 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may feel like your face is too long and narrow. While there's nothing wrong with an oblong face shape, you may want to make your face look rounder for a different look. In this blog post, we'll show you how to make an oblong face look rounder with some easy tips and tricks.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead and a defined jawline. This face shape is often seen as elegant and sophisticated, but some people may want to make their face look rounder for a softer, more youthful appearance.

How to Make an Oblong Face Look Rounder

Step 1: Use Contouring

Contouring is an excellent way to create the illusion of a rounder face. Use a contouring powder or cream that's a few shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows on the sides of your forehead and jawline. Blend well to create a natural-looking shadow.

Step 2: Add Blush

Apply a blush to the apples of your cheeks to create a rounder look. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and apply it in a circular motion to the apples of your cheeks. Blend well to create a natural-looking flush.

Step 3: Try a New Hairstyle

Changing your hairstyle can also create the illusion of a rounder face. Consider getting a haircut with layers or adding bangs to your current style. These styles can help to soften your face and create a rounder look.

Step 4: Wear Round Glasses

If you wear glasses, consider choosing a round frame to create a rounder look. Round glasses can help to balance out an oblong face shape and make your face look rounder.

Step 5: Use Highlighter

Apply a highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. This will help to create a more three-dimensional look and make your face appear rounder.

Tips

  • Choose hairstyles and makeup that complement your face shape.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your skin from the sun with sunscreen to prevent premature aging and damage.

FAQ

What is an oblong face shape?

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead and a defined jawline.

Can I change my face shape?

While you can't change your face shape entirely, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and accessories to create the illusion of a different shape.

Are there any hairstyles that can make my face look rounder?

Hairstyles with layers, bangs, or waves can help to soften an oblong face shape and create a rounder look.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-cared for can help to enhance your overall appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create the illusion of a rounder face and enhance your natural beauty. Experiment with different hairstyles, makeup techniques, and accessories to find the look that works best for you.
