Low Bald Fade Haircut Curly Hair A Guide For Men

Written by Robby Jul 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a stylish haircut that is perfect for your curly hair? A low bald fade haircut might just be what you need. This type of haircut involves a gradual tapering of hair from the top down to the sides and back, which creates a clean and neat appearance. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve a low bald fade haircut for your curly hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to help you maintain your new look.

Description of a Low Bald Fade Haircut

A low bald fade haircut is a type of haircut that involves a gradual tapering of hair from the top down to the sides and back of the head. This haircut is perfect for men with curly hair who want to achieve a clean and neat appearance. The low bald fade haircut involves cutting the hair very close to the scalp, creating a bald or skin-like appearance. This type of haircut is perfect for men who want to maintain a clean and professional look while still showcasing their curly hair.

How to Achieve a Low Bald Fade Haircut for Curly Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber or Hair Stylist

Choosing the right barber or hair stylist is essential when it comes to achieving the perfect low bald fade haircut. Look for a barber or hair stylist who specializes in cutting curly hair to ensure that they can achieve the look you want. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or family members who have similar hair types.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Hair

Deciding on the length of your hair is crucial when it comes to achieving a low bald fade haircut. You can choose to keep your hair longer on top or go for a shorter length. Make sure to communicate your preferences with your barber or hair stylist to ensure that they can achieve the look you want.

Step 3: Start with the Top

The first step in achieving a low bald fade haircut is to start with the top of your head. Your barber or hair stylist will use scissors or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. They will also shape and style your hair to ensure that it looks neat and clean.

Step 4: Work on the Sides and Back

After cutting the top of your hair, your barber or hair stylist will work on the sides and back of your head. They will use clippers to create a gradual taper from the top down to the sides and back. This will create a clean and neat appearance and give you the low bald fade haircut look that you desire.

Step 5: Finish with the Neckline and Sideburns

The final step in achieving a low bald fade haircut is to finish with the neckline and sideburns. Your barber or hair stylist will use clippers to create a clean and defined neckline and sideburns. They will also use a razor to create a sharp and precise line.

Tips for Maintaining Your Low Bald Fade Haircut

Now that you have achieved your low bald fade haircut, it is essential to maintain it to keep it looking neat and clean. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new look:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to help keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Visit your barber or hair stylist regularly to maintain your low bald fade haircut.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingertips to style your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat tools such as blow dryers and flat irons to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

1. How often do I need to get a low bald fade haircut?

You should visit your barber or hair stylist every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain your low bald fade haircut.

2. Can I achieve a low bald fade haircut at home?

We recommend that you visit a professional barber or hair stylist to achieve the perfect low bald fade haircut. However, if you have experience cutting hair, you can try to achieve this look at home.

3. Can I still showcase my curly hair with a low bald fade haircut?

Yes, a low bald fade haircut is perfect for showcasing your curly hair while still maintaining a clean and neat appearance.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special attention and care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip away natural oils and cause damage.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to help keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools such as blow dryers and flat irons to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingertips to style your hair gently.

In conclusion, a low bald fade haircut is a perfect option for men with curly hair who want to maintain a clean and neat appearance while still showcasing their curly hair. Follow these steps, tips and hair care tips to achieve and maintain your new look.
