Medium Short Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thick curly hair, finding the right haircut can be challenging. You want a style that will showcase your beautiful curls but also keep them under control. That's where medium short haircuts come in. These styles are perfect for thick curly hair because they provide enough length to showcase your curls but still keep them manageable. In this guide, we'll explore the best medium short haircuts for thick curly hair, how to style them, and how to take care of your curls.

Medium Short Haircuts for Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing a medium short haircut for thick curly hair, there are several options to choose from. Here are some of our favorites:

Bob

The bob is a classic haircut that works well for almost any hair type, including thick curly hair. For this style, the hair is cut to chin length or just below and is typically styled with a center or side part. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and stylish.

Layered Lob

The layered lob is a longer version of the bob and is perfect for those who want a little more length in their hair. This style is cut to just below the shoulders and features layers throughout the hair to add movement and texture. The layered lob is a great option for those with thick curly hair because it helps to control the volume and keep the curls from becoming too overwhelming.

Pixie

For those who want a shorter haircut, the pixie is a great option. This style is cut short on the sides and back and is longer on top. The hair is typically styled with a side swept bang and can be worn curly or straight. The pixie is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that is easy to style and looks great with minimal effort.

Cropped Curls

If you want a truly short haircut, the cropped curls style is a great option. This style is cut short all over and is perfect for those who want a bold and edgy look. The hair is typically styled with a messy, textured look and can be worn curly or straight.

How to Style Your Haircut

Once you've chosen your medium short haircut, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling your new haircut:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.
  • Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair to help keep your curls under control.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as braids or updos, to keep your look fresh and interesting.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your medium short haircut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any pieces that need extra attention.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your curls.

FAQs About Medium Short Haircuts for Thick Curly Hair

Q: Will a medium short haircut work for my thick curly hair?

A: Yes, a medium short haircut is a great option for those with thick curly hair. These haircuts provide enough length to showcase your curls but still keep them manageable.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I wear my hair curly or straight with a medium short haircut?

A: Yes, a medium short haircut can be worn curly or straight. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for thick curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as this can dry out your curls.

With these tips and haircut options, you can confidently rock your thick curly hair with a stylish medium short haircut. Remember to take care of your curls and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
