Short Pixie Cut With Undercut A Bold And Edgy Style

Written by Oliver Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is bold, edgy, and easy to maintain? Look no further than the short pixie cut with undercut. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years and is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish look. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about the short pixie cut with undercut, including how to achieve the look, tips for maintaining it, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The short pixie cut with undercut is a hairstyle that features short, closely cropped hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. The undercut refers to the shaved area on the sides and back of the head, which creates a dramatic contrast with the longer hair on top. This style is popular among women and men and can be customized to suit different hair textures and face shapes.

How To

If you are considering getting a short pixie cut with undercut, there are a few things you should know before you take the plunge. First, find a hairstylist who is experienced in creating this style. They will be able to advise you on the best cut for your face shape and hair texture. Once you have found a stylist you trust, follow these steps to achieve the look:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your stylist starts cutting, they will consult with you to determine the length and shape of your hair. This is a good time to ask any questions you may have and discuss any concerns you may have about the style.

Step 2: Shave the Undercut

The stylist will begin by shaving the sides and back of your head to create the undercut. This is typically done with clippers, and the length of the hair can be adjusted to your preference.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Next, the stylist will cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length. This can be anywhere from a few inches to a short, cropped style. The length and shape of the top will depend on your face shape and personal style.

Step 4: Style

Finally, the stylist will style your hair using a combination of products and tools. This can include a blow dryer, flat iron, and styling wax or gel. They will show you how to style your hair at home, so you can maintain the look between appointments.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short pixie cut with undercut:

  • Get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and extend the life of your style.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Consider adding a pop of color to your hair to make the style even more dramatic.

FAQ

Is the short pixie cut with undercut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can be customized to suit different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Is the short pixie cut with undercut low maintenance?

Yes, this style is low maintenance and easy to style. However, you will need to get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your short pixie cut with undercut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Conclusion

The short pixie cut with undercut is a bold and edgy style that is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish look. With the right hairstylist and a little bit of maintenance, you can rock this style with confidence. We hope this blog post has provided you with all the information you need to know about the short pixie cut with undercut. Now, go out there and show off your new look!
