How To Cut A Temp Fade Step By Step

Written by William Aug 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this blog post, we will be discussing how to cut a temp fade step by step. A temp fade is a popular hairstyle that involves a gradual fade from short hair at the temples to longer hair at the top of the head. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways and is suitable for all hair types. This article will provide you with all the information you need to know about how to cut a temp fade.

Description

A temp fade is a short hairstyle that involves a gradual fade from short hair at the temples to longer hair at the top of the head. It is a popular hairstyle that is suitable for all hair types and can be styled in various ways. The main advantage of a temp fade is that it is easy to maintain and can be done at home with the right tools.

How to Cut a Temp Fade Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

The first step in cutting a temp fade is to prepare the hair. Wash the hair and towel dry it. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and knots. If necessary, trim any split ends with a pair of scissors.

Step 2: Create the Fade

The next step is to create the fade. Use a pair of clippers with a guard size of 1 to create a line around the head, about 2 inches above the ears. This will be the starting point for the fade. Use the same clippers with a guard size of 2 to create a fade from the line to the temple. Repeat this process around the head, gradually increasing the guard size until you reach the top of the head.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

The next step is to blend the hair. Use a pair of scissors to blend the hair between the fade and the longer hair at the top of the head. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim the ends with the scissors until the hair is blended evenly.

Step 4: Style the Hair

The final step is to style the hair. Use a styling product to create the desired hairstyle. You can style the hair in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or spiked.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut a temp fade:

  • Use a mirror to see the back of the head
  • Start with a higher guard size and gradually decrease it to create a more gradual fade
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it with scissors for a more natural look
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best

FAQ

Q: Can I cut a temp fade myself?

A: Yes, you can cut a temp fade yourself with the right tools and technique. However, it is recommended to have a professional cut your hair if you are not confident in your skills.

Q: How often do I need to cut my hair for a temp fade?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the fade grows out. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the fade.

Hair Care

To maintain a temp fade, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the fade

By following these tips, you can maintain a healthy and stylish temp fade.
