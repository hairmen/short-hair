How To Find Your Own Hairstyle

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to switch things up and find a new look that suits you? Finding your own hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be a fun and exciting journey. In this article, we’ll guide you on how to find your own hairstyle in a relaxed English language, with step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Find Your Own Hairstyle
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Find Your Own Hairstyle

Before you start looking for a new hairstyle, it’s important to know what suits you best. Your face shape, hair texture, and personal style all play a role in finding the perfect hairstyle. Here are some steps to help you find your own hairstyle:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Knowing your face shape is crucial in finding the right hairstyle for you. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. Take a good look at your face in the mirror and determine which shape it resembles the most.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in finding the perfect hairstyle. If you have straight hair, you can pull off a sleek, sophisticated look. If you have curly hair, you can opt for a more relaxed, bohemian vibe.

3. Determine Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when finding your own hairstyle. Are you more of a laid-back, casual person, or do you prefer a more polished, sophisticated look? Your hairstyle should reflect your personal style and make you feel confident and comfortable.

Step by Step

Now that you know what to consider when finding your own hairstyle, here are some step-by-step instructions to help you along the way:

1. Research

Start by researching different hairstyles that you like. You can browse online or in magazines to get inspiration. Save pictures of hairstyles that catch your eye and keep them in a folder or on your phone for reference.

2. Consult with a Professional

If you’re unsure about what hairstyle would suit you best, it’s always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and recommend a hairstyle that would work for you.

3. Experiment

Once you’ve found a hairstyle that you like, it’s time to experiment. Try different variations of the hairstyle and see what works best for you. You can also play around with hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add some extra flair to your look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when finding your own hairstyle:

  • Don’t be afraid to try something new
  • Consider your lifestyle before choosing a hairstyle
  • Take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best
  • Ask for second opinions from friends or family members

FAQs

What if I don’t know my face shape?

If you’re unsure about your face shape, you can use a ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Can I change my hairstyle if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle even if you have curly hair. Just make sure to choose a hairstyle that works with your natural texture and doesn’t require excessive heat styling or chemical treatments.

Hair Care

Once you’ve found your own hairstyle, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair

Now that you know how to find your own hairstyle, it’s time to start experimenting and finding the perfect look for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and don’t be afraid to try something new. With the right hairstyle, you’ll feel confident and ready to take on the world.
