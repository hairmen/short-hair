How To Make Round Face Look Smaller Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jun 12, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have a round face, you may feel that your features appear disproportionately large. Fortunately, there are plenty of ways to create the illusion of a slimmer, more angular face shape. In this article, we'll explore how to make round face look smaller through a variety of styling and makeup techniques.

How to Make Round Face Look Smaller: Step by Step

Step 1: Contour

Contouring is an excellent way to create the illusion of a slimmer face shape. Use a cool-toned contouring powder or cream to add shadow to your cheeks, temples, and jawline. Blend well for a natural-looking finish.

Step 2: Highlight

Highlighting is the perfect complement to contouring. Use a shimmery powder or cream to add light to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. This will create contrast and enhance the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make a world of difference in how your face appears. Opt for longer, face-framing layers that draw attention away from your cheeks, or try a sleek, high ponytail to elongate your face shape. Avoid blunt bangs or styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Step 4: Experiment with Makeup

Makeup can be a powerful tool for creating the illusion of a slimmer face. Try a darker shade of foundation or bronzer around the perimeter of your face, blending well for a seamless finish. You can also experiment with bold lip colors or eye makeup to draw attention to other parts of your face.

Tips for Making Round Face Look Smaller

Here are a few additional tips to help you make your round face look smaller:

Tip 1: Wear Statement Earrings

Statement earrings draw attention to your neck and shoulders, creating a vertical line that elongates your face shape. Opt for long, dangly earrings or geometric shapes that add interest to your look.

Tip 2: Choose the Right Glasses

If you wear glasses, choose frames that are angular and slightly wider than your face. This will create balance and add definition to your features.

Tip 3: Embrace Your Cheekbones

Round faces often have beautiful, prominent cheekbones. Emphasize this feature with a pop of blush or contouring powder.

FAQ: How to Make Round Face Look Smaller

Q: Do I have to contour every day?

A: No, contouring can be time-consuming and is not necessary for every day. Instead, focus on choosing hairstyles and clothing that flatter your face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part with a round face?

A: Yes, a middle part can work well with a round face shape. However, consider adding volume at the crown of your head to create balance.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for creating a flattering look with a round face shape. Keep your hair hydrated with regular conditioning treatments and use heat protectant spray when styling. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make your face appear larger.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your round face look smaller and more defined. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your look!
