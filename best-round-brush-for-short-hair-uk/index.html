Best Round Brush For Short Hair Uk The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 01, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short hair, you know that finding the right styling tools can be a challenge. But fear not, because we've compiled a list of the best round brushes for short hair in the UK. From ceramic to boar bristle, we've got you covered. So, let's dive into the world of round brushes!

Description

A round brush is a must-have tool for anyone who wants to achieve a salon-worthy blowout. It's perfect for smoothing out frizz and creating volume, making it ideal for those with short hair. But not all round brushes are created equal. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the best one for your hair type and styling needs.

How to Choose the Right Round Brush for Short Hair

Before we get into our top picks, let's talk about how to choose the right round brush for your short hair. Here are some factors to consider:

Bristles

There are two main types of bristles: synthetic and natural. Synthetic bristles are usually made from nylon or plastic and are best for detangling and adding shine. Natural bristles, on the other hand, are made from animal hair (usually boar) and are great for smoothing and creating volume. If you have fine hair, a brush with synthetic bristles may be best. If you have thick, coarse hair, a brush with natural bristles may be more effective.

Size

The size of the brush will depend on the length of your hair. For short hair, a small to medium-sized brush (around 1-2 inches in diameter) is best. If your hair is chin-length or shorter, a brush with a smaller diameter will give you more control and precision.

Shape

Round brushes come in different shapes, including cylindrical, barrel, and concave. For short hair, a cylindrical or barrel-shaped brush is best. These shapes allow you to create volume and movement without adding too much curl.

Material

Round brushes can be made from a variety of materials, including ceramic, tourmaline, and titanium. Ceramic brushes are great for reducing frizz and speeding up drying time. Tourmaline brushes emit negative ions, which help to smooth and add shine. Titanium brushes are lightweight and durable, making them a good choice for travel.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Round Brush on Short Hair

Now that you've chosen your round brush, it's time to put it to use. Here's a step-by-step guide to using a round brush on short hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair until it's about 80% dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Use Your Round Brush

Take a section of hair and place your round brush underneath it, close to the roots. Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to dry the hair while rolling the brush towards the ends. Repeat this process until all sections of hair are dry and styled.

Step 4: Add Finish Touches

Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and smooth down any flyaways.

Tips for Using Round Brushes on Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when using a round brush on short hair:

- Start with a smaller section of hair to ensure that the brush can grip the hair properly. - Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair with the heat of the hair dryer. - Don't forget to roll the brush towards the ends of your hair for a smooth finish. - Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

No, it's best to use a round brush on hair that's about 80% dry. Wet hair is more prone to damage, and using a round brush on wet hair can cause breakage and frizz.

How often should I clean my round brush?

You should clean your round brush at least once a month to remove any buildup of product or hair. To clean your brush, remove any hair and then soak it in warm, soapy water for about 15 minutes. Use a toothbrush to gently scrub away any buildup, then rinse the brush and let it air dry.

Can I use a round brush on all types of short hair?

Yes, you can use a round brush on all types of short hair, but you may need to adjust the size and type of brush depending on your hair texture and length.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Using the right styling tools is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some additional tips for keeping your short hair looking its best:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. - Use a conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your hair. - Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. - Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

There you have it, our ultimate guide to the best round brushes for short hair in the UK. Whether you're looking for a brush to add volume or create a smooth finish, we've got you covered. Remember to choose a brush that's the right size, shape, and material for your hair type, and always use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage. Happy styling!
