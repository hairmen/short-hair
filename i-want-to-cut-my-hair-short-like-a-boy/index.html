I Want To Cut My Hair Short Like A Boy A Guide On How To Get The Look

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to switch things up? Do you find yourself admiring the short hairstyles of boys? If you're thinking, "I want to cut my hair short like a boy," then this guide is for you. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step process on how to achieve a short, boyish haircut, as well as tips on how to care for your new 'do.

How to Cut Your Hair Short Like a Boy

The first step in getting the short, boyish haircut you desire is to find a reputable hair salon. Look for one that has experience in cutting short hair and ask for recommendations from friends or family who have had a similar haircut. Once you've found a salon, it's time to decide on the specific haircut you want.

Step 1: Choose Your Haircut

There are many different types of short haircuts you can choose from, depending on your personal style and face shape. Some popular options include the classic undercut, the buzz cut, and the side-swept pixie cut. Do some research and find a style that speaks to you.

Step 2: Talk to Your Stylist

Once you've chosen your desired haircut, it's time to talk to your stylist. Bring in pictures of the style you want, and discuss any concerns or questions you may have. Your stylist will be able to give you advice on whether the style will suit your face shape and hair type, and will work with you to create the perfect look.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Now it's time for the fun part – getting your hair cut! Sit back, relax, and let your stylist work their magic. Depending on the style you've chosen, the cut may take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour. Once it's done, you'll have a fresh, new look that's sure to turn heads.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Now that you've got your new 'do, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Short hair can get greasy quickly, so it's important to wash it regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner and avoid over-washing, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Use the Right Styling Products

Styling products can help you achieve the look you want, so it's important to use the right ones. For a sleek, polished look, use a pomade or wax. For a more textured, messy look, opt for a salt spray or matte paste.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

To keep your short haircut looking sharp, it's important to get regular trims. Aim to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking tidy and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a short haircut suit my face shape?

A: Short haircuts can suit any face shape, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features. Your stylist will be able to give you advice on which cuts will work best for you.

Q: Will a short haircut be easy to maintain?

A: Short haircuts can be low-maintenance, but it's important to keep up with regular trims and use the right styling products to keep your hair looking its best.

Q: Can I still style my short hair in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Final Thoughts

If you're thinking, "I want to cut my hair short like a boy," then go for it! A short, boyish haircut can be a fun and stylish way to switch up your look. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect cut, and remember to have fun with your new hair.
