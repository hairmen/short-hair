What Is The Best Hair Dryer Brush For Thick Hair

Written by Frank Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours blow-drying and styling your thick hair? A hair dryer brush can save you time and effort, giving you a salon-worthy blowout at home. But with so many options on the market, it can be challenging to know which brush is best for your hair type. In this article, we'll explore the best hair dryer brush for thick hair and provide tips for achieving the perfect blowout.

Why Use a Hair Dryer Brush?

A hair dryer brush combines the power of a hair dryer with the styling capabilities of a brush. It allows you to dry and style your hair at the same time, cutting down on styling time and reducing heat damage. Plus, the bristles on the brush can help smooth frizz and add shine to your hair.

How to Choose the Best Hair Dryer Brush for Thick Hair

When choosing a hair dryer brush for thick hair, you'll want to look for a few key features:

Powerful Motor

Thick hair requires a powerful motor to dry and style effectively. Look for a brush with at least 1000 watts of power to ensure it can handle your hair type.

Large Barrel

A larger barrel will make it easier to style your hair and create volume. Look for a brush with a barrel diameter of at least 1.5 inches.

Ceramic or Tourmaline Technology

Ceramic or tourmaline technology helps distribute heat evenly and reduces the risk of heat damage. Look for a brush with these features to protect your hair while styling.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Hair Dryer Brush

Now that you've chosen the best hair dryer brush for your hair type, it's time to learn how to use it. Follow these steps for a flawless blowout:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for your hair type. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 4: Begin Styling

Start with the bottom section of your hair. Use the brush to pull your hair taut and begin drying from the roots to the ends. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 5: Add Volume

To add volume, use the brush to lift your hair at the roots while drying. You can also use the cool shot button on your hair dryer to set the style in place.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your blowout with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Using a Hair Dryer Brush on Thick Hair

Here are a few additional tips to achieve the perfect blowout:

Use a Detangling Brush

Before using your hair dryer brush, use a detangling brush to remove any knots and tangles in your hair. This will make it easier to style and reduce breakage.

Start with Low Heat

Start with low heat and increase the temperature as needed. High heat can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Use a Concentrator Nozzle

A concentrator nozzle can help direct the airflow and make it easier to style your hair.

FAQ

What is the best hair dryer brush for thick curly hair?

The best hair dryer brush for thick curly hair is one with a large barrel, ceramic or tourmaline technology, and multiple heat settings. The Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer & Volumizer is a popular choice.

Can you use a hair dryer brush on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a hair dryer brush on wet hair. Towel-dry your hair first to remove excess water before using the brush.

How often should I clean my hair dryer brush?

You should clean your hair dryer brush at least once a month to remove any buildup of product or hair. Use a small brush or toothbrush to remove any debris and wipe clean with a damp cloth.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Using a hair dryer brush is just one way to care for your thick hair. Here are a few additional tips:

Use a Deep Conditioner

Thick hair can be prone to dryness, so use a deep conditioner once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

Avoid Heat Damage

Use heat styling tools sparingly and always use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Trim Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best hair dryer brush for thick hair is one with a powerful motor, large barrel, and ceramic or tourmaline technology. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve the perfect blowout and care for your thick hair.
