Do Guys Like Curly Or Wavy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering whether guys prefer curly or wavy hair? Look no further because we’ve got you covered in this ultimate guide. From the pros and cons of each hairstyle to tips on how to care for your curls or waves, we’ve got everything you need to know.

Table of Contents

  • Pros and Cons of Curly and Wavy Hair
  • How to Get Curls or Waves
  • Tips for Caring for Curly or Wavy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Pros and Cons of Curly and Wavy Hair

Before we dive into whether guys like curly or wavy hair, let’s take a look at the pros and cons of each hairstyle.

Curly Hair

Pros:

  • Curly hair is unique and eye-catching.
  • It can add volume and bounce to your hair.
  • There are many different types of curls, so you can choose the style that suits you best.

Cons:

  • Curly hair can be harder to manage and style.
  • It can become frizzy and tangled easily.
  • Some people find it difficult to find the right products and styling techniques for their curls.

Wavy Hair

Pros:

  • Wavy hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.
  • It can add texture and movement to your hair.
  • Wavy hair is often easier to manage than curly hair.

Cons:

  • Some people may find that their waves fall flat or lose their shape throughout the day.
  • It can be difficult to find the right products and styling techniques for your waves.
  • Wavy hair can become frizzy and tangled if not properly cared for.

Overall, both curly and wavy hair have their pros and cons. It really comes down to personal preference and what works best for your hair type and lifestyle.

How to Get Curls or Waves

If you have naturally straight hair and want to add some curls or waves, there are a few different methods you can try.

Curling Iron or Wand: Using a curling iron or wand is probably the most popular method for creating curls or waves. Simply section off your hair and wrap small sections around the wand or iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. You can vary the size of the sections and the direction you wrap the hair to create different types of curls or waves.

Braids: Another method for creating waves is to braid your hair when it’s damp and leave it overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you should have some natural-looking waves.

Salt Spray: If you have natural waves but want to enhance them, try using a salt spray. Simply spritz it on damp hair and scrunch with your hands to create textured waves.

Tips for Caring for Curly or Wavy Hair

Whether you have naturally curly or wavy hair or you’ve created the look with styling tools, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your curls or waves, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance your curls or waves and add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do guys like curly or wavy hair?

A: It really depends on the guy. Some may prefer curly hair because it’s unique and eye-catching, while others may prefer wavy hair because it’s more versatile and easier to manage.

Q: Can I get curls or waves if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, there are a few different methods you can try, such as using a curling iron or wand, braiding your hair, or using a salt spray.

Q: How can I keep my curls or waves looking great?

A: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream, and avoid using heat tools too often.

Now that you have all the information you need, you can decide whether curly or wavy hair is right for you. Remember, the most important thing is to take good care of your hair and choose the style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
