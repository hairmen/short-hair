How To Determine If You Have A Long Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read
If you're wondering whether you have a long face, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about determining whether you have a long face shape. From tips and tricks to frequently asked questions, we've got you covered. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

Determining your face shape is an important step in figuring out what hairstyles and makeup looks work best for you. If you have a long face, for example, you might want to avoid hairstyles that elongate your face even more. But how do you know if you have a long face to begin with? That's what we're here to help you figure out.

How to Determine if You Have a Long Face

There are a few key features that can help you determine whether you have a long face shape. Here's what to look for:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

The first step in determining your face shape is to measure it. Use a tape measure to measure the distance from your hairline to your chin, and then measure the distance from your cheekbone to your other cheekbone. Write down these measurements.

Step 2: Compare Your Measurements

Next, compare the two measurements you just took. If the measurement from your hairline to your chin is significantly longer than the measurement from your cheekbone to your other cheekbone, you may have a long face shape.

Step 3: Look for Other Features

In addition to measuring your face, there are other features you can look for that may indicate a long face shape. For example, if you have a high forehead or a prominent chin, these features may make your face appear longer.

Tips for Long-Faced Individuals

If you've determined that you have a long face shape, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to help balance out your features. Here are some ideas: - Try a hairstyle with layers or waves to add volume and fullness to your hair - Experiment with bangs to help shorten the appearance of your forehead - Use contouring makeup techniques to help create the illusion of a shorter face - Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as these can make your face appear even longer

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long face shapes:

Q: Are there any celebrities with long face shapes?

A: Yes! Celebrities like Sarah Jessica Parker, Liv Tyler, and Gisele Bundchen all have long face shapes.

Q: Can I still wear a middle part with a long face shape?

A: Absolutely! Just be sure to add some volume to your hair to help balance out the length of your face.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid with a long face shape?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as these can make your face appear even longer. You may also want to avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back, as this can elongate your face even more.

Hair Care for Long-Faced Individuals

If you have a long face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to help balance out your features. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind: - Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add fullness to your hair - Avoid heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair - Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends - Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look for you

Conclusion

Determining whether you have a long face shape is an important step in figuring out what hairstyles and makeup looks work best for you. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this post, you can help balance out your features and create a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
