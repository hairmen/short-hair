Short Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Frank Oct 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair can be tricky to manage at any age, but it can become even more challenging as you get older. And if you have thick curly hair, it can be even more challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type and age. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50.

Description

Thick curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be difficult to manage. As you age, your hair may become less voluminous, and your curls may become less defined. This can make it even more challenging to find the right hairstyle. But with the right haircut and styling products, you can embrace your curls and create a chic and trendy look that complements your age and style.

How to

The first step in finding the right short hairstyle for your thick curly hair is to consult with your hairstylist. They can help you choose a style that suits your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Here are some popular short hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well with thick curly hair. This style is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance look that is easy to style and maintain. The pixie cut is also versatile and can be styled in many ways, from messy and textured to sleek and polished.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short hairstyle for thick curly hair. It is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. The bob cut is perfect for women who want a chic and trendy look that is easy to style and maintain.

3. Layered Cut

The layered cut is a great option for women with thick curly hair. It adds volume and texture to your hair and creates a more defined curl pattern. The layered cut is also versatile and can be styled in many ways, from messy and tousled to sleek and polished.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to help you style your short curly hair:

1. Shampoo and Condition

Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. This will help to smooth and define your curls and prepare your hair for styling.

2. Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that is designed for curly hair, such as a curl cream or mousse. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

3. Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your curls and create volume.

4. Style

Style your hair using a curling iron or wand. This will help to define your curls and create a more polished look. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short curly hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for curly hair, such as curl creams, mousses, and gels. These products will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

FAQ

Q. Can I dye my curly hair?

A. Yes, you can dye your curly hair. Just make sure to use a dye that is designed for curly hair and follow the instructions carefully.

Q. Will cutting my hair make it less curly?

A. No, cutting your hair will not make it less curly. However, cutting your hair can help to enhance your curls and create a more defined curl pattern.

Q. How often should I wash my curly hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Deep Condition

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and nourished.

3. Protect Your Hair at Night

Protect your hair at night by using a silk or satin pillowcase. This will help to prevent frizz and breakage.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50 can be chic and trendy with the right haircut and styling products. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect style for you and follow these tips to maintain healthy and beautiful curly hair.
