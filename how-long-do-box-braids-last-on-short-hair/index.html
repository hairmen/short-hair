How Long Do Box Braids Last On Short Hair

Written by Petter Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you've recently cut your hair short and are considering getting box braids, you may be wondering how long they will last. Box braids are a popular protective style for all hair lengths, but they can be especially practical for those with short hair. In this blog post, we'll dive into the details of how long box braids last on short hair and provide tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Box Braids on Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Installing Box Braids on Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Box Braids on Short Hair
  • FAQs About Box Braids on Short Hair
  • Hair Care for Box Braids on Short Hair

How to Get Box Braids on Short Hair

Before we dive into how long box braids last on short hair, let's first discuss how to get them. If you have short hair, you'll need to find a skilled stylist who can install small and tight braids. It's important to note that the length of your hair will impact the size of the braids. The shorter your hair, the smaller the braids will need to be.

When getting box braids on short hair, it's essential to have a consultation with your stylist. During this consultation, you'll discuss the length and thickness of the braids, the type of hair extensions to use, and the overall look you want to achieve.

Step-by-Step Guide to Installing Box Braids on Short Hair

Now that you know how to get box braids on short hair let's dive into the step-by-step process of installing them:

  1. Wash your hair and blow-dry it thoroughly.
  2. Section your hair into small parts.
  3. Using a rat-tail comb, create a small braid at the root of each section using your natural hair.
  4. Add extensions to each braid and continue braiding until you reach the end.
  5. Seal the ends of the braids with hot water to prevent them from unraveling.

It's important to note that the installation process can take several hours, depending on the size and number of braids you're getting. But the end result is worth it.

Tips for Maintaining Box Braids on Short Hair

Now that you have your box braids installed, it's essential to take good care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your braids every two weeks with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Don't scratch your scalp or pull on your braids.
  • Keep your scalp moisturized with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.

FAQs About Box Braids on Short Hair

How long do box braids last on short hair?

Box braids can last anywhere from four to eight weeks on short hair, depending on how well you take care of them.

Can you swim with box braids on short hair?

Yes, you can swim with box braids on short hair, but it's important to protect your braids by wearing a swim cap and rinsing your hair with fresh water after swimming.

Can you style box braids on short hair?

Yes, you can style box braids on short hair. You can wear them down, in a ponytail, or in a bun. You can also add accessories like beads and clips.

Hair Care for Box Braids on Short Hair

When it comes to hair care for box braids on short hair, it's essential to keep your scalp and braids clean and moisturized. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your braids every two weeks with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Apply a light oil or leave-in conditioner to your scalp and braids to keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage.

In conclusion, box braids can last anywhere from four to eight weeks on short hair, depending on how well you take care of them. If you're considering getting box braids on short hair, be sure to find a skilled stylist and follow our tips for maintaining them. With proper care, your box braids will look fabulous for weeks to come!
