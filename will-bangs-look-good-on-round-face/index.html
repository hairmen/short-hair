Will Bangs Look Good On Round Face

Written by Bardi Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning to get bangs but have a round face? You might be wondering if bangs will look good on your face shape. Well, the answer is yes! Bangs can look great on a round face, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs to flatter your features. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting bangs for a round face, including how to choose the right style, how to care for your bangs, and more.

Description

A round face is typically characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal with bangs is to create angles and balance out the roundness of the face. The right type of bangs can help elongate the face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

How to Choose the Right Style of Bangs

When it comes to choosing the right style of bangs for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the length of your forehead. If you have a short forehead, avoid blunt bangs that hit right at the brow line. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and gradually get shorter towards the middle.

If you have a longer forehead, blunt bangs can work well, but make sure they're not too heavy or thick. Wispy, textured bangs that are slightly longer on the sides can also look great on a round face.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have curly or wavy hair, avoid straight-across bangs, as they can be difficult to style and may end up looking too heavy. Instead, opt for soft, textured bangs that blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Bangs for a Round Face

Once you've chosen the right type of bangs for your round face, it's important to know how to style them properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  • Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it straight.
  • Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.
  • If you have straight-across bangs, use a flat iron to smooth them out and create a polished look.
  • For side-swept bangs, use a curling iron to curl the ends slightly and create a soft, romantic look.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you have bangs, it's important to keep them looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.
  • Blow-dry your bangs every day to keep them looking polished and prevent them from becoming greasy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can cause them to become oily and lose their shape.
  • Use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh and prevent them from becoming greasy.

FAQs

Q: Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A: No, not necessarily. The right type of bangs can actually help balance out the roundness of your face and create angles.

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes! You can still wear a ponytail with bangs. Just make sure to leave your bangs out or pin them back with bobby pins.

Q: How do I know if bangs are right for me?

A: The best way to know if bangs are right for you is to consult with a hairstylist. They can recommend the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking great, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent your bangs from becoming greasy.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and scalp healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your bangs look dry and brittle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, bangs can look great on a round face as long as you choose the right type of bangs to flatter your features. Keep these tips in mind when choosing and styling your bangs, and remember to take care of them properly to keep them looking great.
