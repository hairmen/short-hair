How To Make Long Hair Look Short For Halloween

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to switch up your look this Halloween? Maybe you want to dress up as a character with short hair, but you have long locks? Well, don't worry! In this article, we will show you how to make long hair look short for Halloween. From wigs to hairstyles, we've got you covered.

Description

Long hair is beautiful, but sometimes we want to switch it up and try something new. Halloween is the perfect time to experiment with different hairstyles and looks. If you're looking to make your long hair look short, there are a few options available. You can try wearing a wig, or you can style your hair in a way that gives the illusion of shorter locks.

How to

Option 1: Wear a Wig

Wearing a wig is the easiest way to make your long hair look short. You can purchase a wig online or at your local costume shop. When selecting a wig, make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and skin tone.

Once you have your wig, follow these steps:

  1. Put your hair in a low ponytail or braid.
  2. Put on a wig cap to keep your hair in place.
  3. Put the wig on, making sure to adjust it to fit properly.
  4. Style the wig as desired.

Option 2: Style Your Hair

If you don't want to wear a wig, you can style your hair in a way that gives the illusion of shorter locks. Here's how:

  1. Start by putting your hair in a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil on itself.
  3. Start tucking in the ends of the coiled ponytail under the base of the ponytail.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure the coiled hair in place.
  5. Use a hair tie to secure any loose strands of hair.
  6. Use hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips

  • When choosing a wig, make sure to select a style that complements your face shape and skin tone.
  • Use hairspray to keep your style in place.
  • Practice your hairstyle before Halloween to ensure it looks the way you want it to.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, or hats to complete your Halloween look.

FAQ

Can I make my long hair look short without cutting it?

Yes, you can make your long hair look short by wearing a wig or styling your hair in a way that gives the illusion of shorter locks.

How do I choose the right wig for my face shape?

When choosing a wig, consider your face shape and skin tone. Consult with a hairstylist or costume shop employee for recommendations.

How do I make my hairstyle last all night?

Use hairspray to keep your style in place, and carry extra bobby pins and hair ties with you just in case.

Hair Care

Remember to take good care of your hair, whether you're wearing a wig or styling it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heated styling tools too often. If you're wearing a wig, follow the care instructions provided by the manufacturer.

Now that you know how to make long hair look short for Halloween, it's time to start planning your costume! Whether you opt for a wig or a styled hairstyle, have fun experimenting with different looks.
