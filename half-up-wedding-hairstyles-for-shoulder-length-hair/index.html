Half Up Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by William Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In need of wedding hairstyle inspiration for your shoulder length hair? Look no further than the half up hairstyle! This versatile style can be dressed up or down, and is perfect for any wedding venue or season. Here, we'll go through some beautiful half up wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair and provide tips and tricks for achieving the looks.

Description

The half up hairstyle is a classic bridal look that is both elegant and romantic. It involves pulling back the top half of your hair while leaving the rest of it down. This style is perfect for showcasing your beautiful locks while keeping them out of your face on your big day.

How to

To create a half up wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair and blow drying it until it's completely dry.
  2. Part your hair down the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.
  3. Gather the top half of your hair, starting at your temples, and pull it back towards the crown of your head.
  4. Secure the hair with bobby pins or a hair tie, leaving a few strands loose to frame your face.
  5. Take a curling iron or wand and curl the bottom half of your hair, creating loose waves or tight curls.
  6. Finish the look with hairspray to hold everything in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for creating a half up wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry

Start by washing your hair and blow drying it until it's completely dry. Use a round brush to add volume to your hair as you dry it.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Part your hair down the middle or on the side, depending on your preference. Use a comb to create a clean part.

Step 3: Gather Top Half

Gather the top half of your hair, starting at your temples, and pull it back towards the crown of your head. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step 4: Secure with Pins or Tie

Secure the hair with bobby pins or a hair tie, leaving a few strands loose to frame your face. Make sure the hair is secure so it doesn't fall out during the wedding ceremony or reception.

Step 5: Curl Bottom Half

Take a curling iron or wand and curl the bottom half of your hair, creating loose waves or tight curls. Take your time with this step to ensure all of your hair is curled evenly.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with hairspray to hold everything in place. Choose a hairspray that provides a strong hold without making your hair feel stiff or crunchy.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect half up wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Add volume to your hair by teasing the top section before pulling it back.
  • Try adding a hair accessory, such as a flower crown or sparkly hairpin, to dress up the look.
  • Experiment with different curl sizes to find the perfect wave or curl for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about half up wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

What is the best hair length for a half up wedding hairstyle?

A half up wedding hairstyle can work for any hair length, but it's especially flattering for those with shoulder length hair.

Should I use hairspray to hold my half up hairstyle in place?

Yes, hairspray is a must for keeping your half up hairstyle in place throughout the wedding ceremony and reception.

Can I add hair accessories to my half up wedding hairstyle?

Absolutely! Hair accessories, such as flowers, pins, or tiaras, can add a beautiful touch to your half up hairstyle.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day to ensure it looks healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.

With these half up wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair and tips for achieving the look, you'll be sure to turn heads on your big day.
