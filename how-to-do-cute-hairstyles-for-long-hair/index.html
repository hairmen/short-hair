How To Do Cute Hairstyles For Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long hair is a beautiful asset that can be styled in many ways. Whether you want to keep it simple or add some flair, there are plenty of cute hairstyles for long hair that you can try. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve some of the most popular styles. From braids to buns, we've got you covered!

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: How to Do a Messy Bun
  • Section 2: How to Do a Side Braid
  • Section 3: How to Do Beach Waves
  • Section 4: How to Do a Half-Up Half-Down Style
  • Section 5: Tips for Maintaining Long Hair
  • Section 6: Frequently Asked Questions

Section 1: How to Do a Messy Bun

A messy bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for long hair. Here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you start styling. It's best to work with hair that's not too greasy or oily.

Step 2: Gather your hair into a high ponytail

Pull your hair back into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. You can make the ponytail as tight or loose as you want.

Step 3: Twist and wrap your hair

Take your ponytail and twist it around the base, creating a bun shape. Secure the bun with another elastic band or bobby pins. Don't worry about making it perfect - the messier, the better!

Step 4: Pull out some strands

To give your messy bun a more relaxed look, gently pull out some strands of hair around your face and at the nape of your neck. This will create a soft, undone effect.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Spray your finished bun with some hairspray to hold it in place. And there you have it - a cute and effortless messy bun!

Section 2: How to Do a Side Braid

A side braid is a versatile style that can be worn for any occasion. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Part your hair to one side

Start by parting your hair to one side. This will create a natural flow for your braid.

Step 2: Gather your hair to one side

Brush your hair to one side and gather it into a low ponytail. Secure it with an elastic band.

Step 3: Start braiding

Take the ponytail and divide it into three sections. Begin braiding the sections together, starting at the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Add in hair as you go

As you braid, add in sections of hair from the side opposite your part. This will create a diagonal braid that wraps around your head.

Step 5: Secure the end

Once you've braided all the way down, secure the end with an elastic band. Gently pull out some strands to create a softer, more relaxed look.

Section 3: How to Do Beach Waves

Beach waves are a popular style that can be achieved with long hair. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting at the nape of your neck. Clip each section up as you go.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron, curl each section of hair away from your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing it.

Step 4: Shake it out

Once you've curled all your hair, shake it out to create loose, beachy waves. You can use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls and create more volume.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Spray your finished waves with some hairspray to hold them in place. And there you have it - beachy, effortless waves!

Section 4: How to Do a Half-Up Half-Down Style

A half-up half-down style is a cute and easy way to keep your hair out of your face. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Divide your hair

Start by dividing your hair into two sections - top and bottom.

Step 2: Secure the top section

Pull the top section of your hair back and secure it with an elastic band or hair clip. You can make the ponytail as high or low as you want.

Step 3: Style the bottom section

Leave the bottom section of your hair down, and style it however you like - straight, curled, or wavy.

Step 4: Add some flair

To add some flair to your half-up half-down style, you can braid the top section or twist it into a bun. You can also add accessories like clips or headbands.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Spray your finished style with some hairspray to hold it in place. And there you have it - a cute and simple half-up half-down style!

Section 5: Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

Long hair requires some extra care to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too dry
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it's more prone to damage

Section 6: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I keep my long hair from getting tangled?

A: Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help prevent tangles.

Q: How can I add volume to my long hair?

A: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and blow dry your hair upside down to create more lift. You can also tease your hair at the roots or use a texturizing spray.

Q: How often should I trim my long hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep it healthy.

With these cute hairstyles for long hair and tips for maintaining it, you can keep your locks looking beautiful and stylish. Try out these styles and find the ones that work best for you!
