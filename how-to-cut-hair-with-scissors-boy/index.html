How To Cut Hair With Scissors Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting a boy's hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done at home. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair with scissors boy. We will also include tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice to help you achieve a great haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • Preparation
  • Cutting Process
  • Tips for a Great Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care after the Haircut

Tools Needed

Before you start cutting hair, you need to make sure you have the necessary tools. Here are the tools you will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clippers (optional)
  • Hair spray or water spritzer
  • Cape or towel to drape over the boy's shoulders

Preparation

Before you start cutting hair, you need to make sure the boy's hair is clean and dry. Wet hair tends to cling together, making it harder to cut. Here are the steps to prepare the boy's hair:

  1. Wash the boy's hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Dry the hair with a towel until it is damp.
  3. Use a comb to remove any tangles or knots.
  4. Section the hair into manageable parts using hair clips.

Cutting Process

Now that the hair is prepped, it's time to start cutting. Follow these steps:

  1. Start by cutting the bangs. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Be sure to cut it to the desired length, keeping in mind that the hair will shrink as it dries.
  2. Move on to the sides and back of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.
  3. Blend the top of the head into the sides and back. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length.
  4. Use the hair clippers to trim the hair around the ears and neck.
  5. Check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting hair:

  • Always cut less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Take breaks if needed to avoid getting tired or frustrated.
  • Have a picture of the desired haircut for reference.
  • Use hair spray or water spritzer to keep the hair damp while cutting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair:

How often should I cut my boy's hair?

It depends on how fast the boy's hair grows. On average, hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks.

What if I make a mistake while cutting hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting hair, don't panic. It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always go to a professional hair stylist to fix any mistakes.

How do I clean my hair cutting scissors?

After using hair cutting scissors, wipe them clean with a damp cloth. Do not use water or soap, as it can damage the scissors. Store the scissors in a safe place.

Hair Care after the Haircut

After cutting hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner regularly.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, as it can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the haircut.

Now that you know how to cut hair with scissors boy, you can confidently give your boy a great haircut. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
