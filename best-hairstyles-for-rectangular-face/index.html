Best Hairstyles For Rectangular Face

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone with a rectangular face shape? Do you find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that complements your face shape? Well, worry not! We’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll discuss the best hairstyles for rectangular face shape that will enhance your features and give you a stunning look.

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long and straight face with a strong jawline. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost of the same width. The aim of choosing the right hairstyle for a rectangular face is to soften the angles and create an illusion of a wider face. It’s all about balancing the proportions and enhancing the best features.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

Choosing the right hairstyle depends on various factors like hair type, length, and texture. However, when it comes to rectangular face shape, there are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Avoid styles that add height to the top of the head
  • Opt for hairstyles that add width to the sides of the face
  • Choose styles that add volume to the crown
  • Avoid center parting, as it elongates the face even more

Best Hairstyles for Rectangular Face

1. Long Layers

Long layers are the perfect hairstyle for rectangular face shape. The layers add volume to the crown and soften the angles of the face. You can part your hair in the middle or side, depending on your preference. Waves or curls also add width to the sides of the face.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is another great option for rectangular face shape. The bob adds volume to the crown, while the bangs add width to the forehead. The side-swept bangs soften the angles of the face and create an illusion of a wider face.

3. Shoulder-Length Hair with Layers

Shoulder-length hair with layers is another great option for rectangular face shape. The layers add volume to the crown, while the shoulder-length hair adds width to the sides of the face. You can style it with loose waves or curls to add more volume and texture.

4. Pixie Cut with Side-Swept Bangs

If you’re looking for a short hairstyle, then a pixie cut with side-swept bangs is perfect for rectangular face shape. The pixie cut adds volume to the crown, while the side-swept bangs add width to the forehead. The short hair length also adds width to the sides of the face.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to style your hair in a long layered hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the crown
  4. Part your hair in the middle or side
  5. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls
  6. Apply a texturizing spray to add texture and hold the curls

Tips

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head
  • Choose styles that add width to the sides of the face
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture and hold the curls

FAQs

1. Can I have a center parting with a rectangular face shape?

No, it’s best to avoid center parting with a rectangular face shape, as it elongates the face even more. Instead, opt for a side parting.

2. Can I have bangs with a rectangular face shape?

Yes, you can have bangs with a rectangular face shape. Side-swept bangs are the best option, as they add width to the forehead and soften the angles of the face.

3. Can I have short hair with a rectangular face shape?

Yes, you can have short hair with a rectangular face shape. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs is a great option, as it adds volume to the crown and width to the forehead.

Hair Care

It’s essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Condition your hair after shampooing
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

Now that you know the best hairstyles for rectangular face shape, you can choose the one that suits you the best. Remember to follow the guidelines and tips to get the perfect look. Happy styling!
