How To Use Hair Clippers To Cut Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts every few weeks? If so, investing in a pair of hair clippers can be a great way to save money and maintain your hairstyle from the comfort of your own home. However, if you've never used hair clippers before, the task can seem daunting. That's why we've created this step-by-step guide to help you get started.

How to Use Hair Clippers to Cut Men's Hair

Description

Hair clippers are electric devices that use blades to trim and cut hair. They come in different sizes and are designed for different hair types and lengths. When using hair clippers, it's important to choose the right clipper size and blade guard to achieve the desired length.

How to

Before beginning, make sure your hair is clean and dry. It's also important to have a mirror, comb, and towel on hand. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the right blade guard

Before you start, choose the right blade guard for the length of hair you want. The shorter the guard, the shorter the cut. It's best to start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if you want a shorter cut.

Step 2: Comb your hair

Use a comb to remove any tangles and evenly distribute your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure an even result.

Step 3: Turn on the clippers

Plug in the clippers and turn them on. Hold them perpendicular to your head and start from the bottom, working your way up against the direction of hair growth.

Step 4: Cut your hair

Start cutting your hair, moving the clippers upwards in a smooth motion. Use your free hand to hold down any hair that may be sticking up.

Step 5: Cut the sides and back

Next, move to the back and sides of your head. Use a mirror to ensure an even cut.

Step 6: Trim the edges

Use the clippers without a guard to trim the edges around your ears and neck. Be careful not to cut too much or too high.

Step 7: Clean up

After you're done, clean up any loose hair and take a shower to remove any hair that may have fallen on your skin.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if you want a shorter cut
  • Use a mirror to ensure an even cut
  • Be careful not to cut too much or too high when trimming the edges
  • Take your time and don't rush the process

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using hair clippers:

Q: Can I use hair clippers on wet hair?

A: No, it's best to use hair clippers on dry hair. Wet hair can cause the blades to rust and damage the clippers.

Q: How often should I replace the blades on my clippers?

A: It depends on how often you use them. If you use them frequently, it's best to replace the blades every 6-12 months.

Q: How do I clean my clippers?

A: After each use, remove the blade and brush away any loose hair. Use a small amount of clipper oil to lubricate the blades and prevent rust.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after cutting it with clippers. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type and style. You can also use a styling product to help keep your hair in place.

Cutting your own hair with clippers can be a great way to save money and maintain your hairstyle from the comfort of your own home. With these tips and steps, you'll be on your way to a successful haircut in no time.
