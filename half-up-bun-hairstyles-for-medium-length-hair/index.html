Half Up Bun Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles for your medium length hair? Why not try a half up bun hairstyle? This trendy hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of creating a half up bun hairstyle for medium length hair, along with tips and tricks to make it look perfect every time.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A half up bun hairstyle is a combination of a half-up hairstyle and a bun. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your personal style. The hairstyle involves taking the top half of your hair and tying it up into a bun while leaving the rest of your hair down. The bun can be positioned at the crown of your head or at the back of your head. This hairstyle is perfect for medium length hair, as it adds volume and texture to your hair without making it look too bulky.

How To

To create a half up bun hairstyle for medium length hair, you will need the following:

  • Elastic hairbands
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairbrush
  • Hair spray (optional)

Step By Step

Follow these steps to create a half up bun hairstyle:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take the top half of your hair and tie it up into a ponytail. Make sure to leave some hair at the front to frame your face.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Use your fingers to loosen the bun and create a messy, textured look.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic hairband to hide it.
  6. Secure the wrapped section of hair with a bobby pin.
  7. Use hair spray to set the hairstyle in place (optional).

Tips

Here are some tips to make your half up bun hairstyle look perfect:

  • For a more voluminous look, tease the hair at the crown of your head before tying it up into a ponytail.
  • Use a hairband that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Add hair accessories such as hair clips or flowers to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different bun placements to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about half up bun hairstyles:

Can I create a half up bun hairstyle with short hair?

While it may be more difficult to create a half up bun hairstyle with short hair, it is possible. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place and create a small bun at the top of your head.

How can I make my half up bun hairstyle look more polished?

Use a hair straightener to straighten the hair at the front and create a sleek look. You can also use hair serum to tame any flyaways.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, make sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals.

With this guide, you can now create a stylish and trendy half up bun hairstyle for your medium length hair. Experiment with different bun placements and accessories to make the hairstyle your own. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
