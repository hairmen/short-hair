Best Bob For Round Fat Face How To Choose The Perfect Hairstyle

Written by William Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round and chubby face, finding the right hairstyle can be quite challenging. You want a haircut that will flatter your features and make you look slimmer and more youthful. One of the best options is a bob haircut. But with so many variations of bobs, how do you know which one is best for you? In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the best bob for round fat face.

Understanding Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different types of bobs, let's first talk about your face shape. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of any hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and slimness.

How to Measure Your Face

To determine if you have a round face, measure your face's width and length with a tape measure. If your face is about as wide as it is long, you likely have a round face shape.

Choosing the Best Bob

When it comes to choosing the best bob for round fat face, there are a few things to consider.

Length

The length of your bob is crucial when it comes to slimming down your face. The best length for a round face is a long bob that falls just below the chin. This length will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Layers

Layers are another essential factor to consider when choosing a bob for a round face. Layers can add movement and dimension to your hair, making it appear less heavy and bulky. However, too many layers can make your face look wider. Opt for long, subtle layers that will add volume without adding width.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to a bob haircut, but they can also be the downfall of your hairstyle if not done correctly. For a round face, avoid blunt bangs that will emphasize the width of your face. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that will create an asymmetrical look and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Texture

Texture is an essential element of any hairstyle, but it's crucial when choosing a bob for a round face. Adding texture to your hair can create the illusion of length and slimness. Consider adding soft waves or curls to your bob to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob

Once you've found the perfect bob for your round face, it's essential to maintain it properly. Here are a few tips to keep your bob looking its best:

- Visit your stylist regularly to keep your bob looking fresh and healthy. - Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. - Avoid heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat. - Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and bounce. - Consider investing in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to style your hair at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a bob a good hairstyle for a round face?

Yes, a bob can be a great hairstyle for a round face if done correctly. A long bob with subtle layers and side-swept bangs can create the illusion of length and slimness.

Can I wear my bob straight or should I add texture?

Adding texture to your bob can create the illusion of length and slimness. Soft waves or curls can add volume and dimension to your hair, making it appear less heavy and bulky. However, if you prefer straight hair, make sure to use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and bounce.

Conclusion

Choosing the best bob for round fat face can be a daunting task, but with the right guidance, it can be easy and fun. Remember to consider the length, layers, bangs, and texture of your bob when choosing the perfect style for your face shape. And don't forget to maintain your bob properly to keep it looking fresh and healthy. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel confident and beautiful every day.
