Best Hairstyle For Large Round Face

Written by Luffy Aug 07, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your large round face? Don’t worry, you’re not alone! Many people with this face shape often struggle to find a hairstyle that compliments their features. But fear not, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best hairstyles for large round faces, how to achieve them, and some tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Large round faces are characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to elongate the face and add definition to the jawline. The best hairstyles for large round faces are those that create the illusion of length and angles.

How to

The first step in achieving the best hairstyle for your large round face is to determine your face shape. Once you’ve determined that you have a round face, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. The best hairstyles for large round faces are those that add height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides of the hair close to the face.

Step by Step

Here’s how to achieve the best hairstyle for your large round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create volume at the crown of your head by backcombing or using a volumizing spray.
  3. Keep the sides of your hair close to your face by tucking them behind your ears or using bobby pins to secure them.
  4. Add curls or waves to the ends of your hair to create movement and texture.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hairstyle for your large round face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs or a straight bob, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Try a side-swept bang or layered haircut to add angles to your face.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on the sides, as they can add width to your face.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a large round face?

Avoid blunt bangs or a straight bob, as they can make your face appear rounder. Also avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on the sides, as they can add width to your face.

What hairstyles are best for large round faces?

The best hairstyles for large round faces are those that add height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides of the hair close to the face. Try a side-swept bang or layered haircut to add angles to your face.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for your large round face, it’s time to try it out! Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you’ll be sure to turn heads wherever you go!
