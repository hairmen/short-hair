Short Pixie Bob Style Haircuts A Perfect Choice For A Bold And Confident Look

Written by Petter Oct 08, 2023 · 4 min read
If you are looking for a fresh and edgy haircut, the short pixie bob style is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is a combination of a classic bob and a pixie cut, resulting in a bold and confident look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short pixie bob style haircuts, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Styling Tips
  • FAQs

Description

Short pixie bob style haircuts are characterized by short layers at the back and sides of the head, with longer layers on top. This style can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type, making it a versatile choice for any woman. The short length of the haircut makes it easy to maintain, and the edgy look is perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hairstyle.

How to Get the Look

Getting a short pixie bob style haircut is easy, but it is important to find a stylist who has experience with this style. You can bring a photo of the style you want to your stylist to give them a better idea of what you are looking for. It is also important to consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a short pixie bob style.

Face Shape

The short pixie bob style works best on women with oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, you may want to consider a longer bob or a different haircut.

Hair Type

This style works best on women with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten your hair before getting the haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a short pixie bob style haircut: 1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. 2. Section your hair into four parts – two at the front and two at the back. 3. Starting at the back, cut the hair to the desired length using scissors or clippers. 4. Work your way up to the crown of your head, cutting the hair in small sections. 5. Cut the hair on the sides of your head, starting at the bottom and working your way up. 6. Blend the back and sides of your hair with the top layers. 7. Style your hair using a texturizing product for an edgy look.

Styling Tips

Here are some tips for styling your short pixie bob: - Use a texturizing product to add volume and texture to your hair. - Use a flat iron to straighten your hair for a sleek look. - Use a curling iron or wand to add curls or waves to your hair. - Experiment with different parting styles to change up your look. - Add highlights or lowlights to give your hair dimension.

FAQs

Q: Will a short pixie bob style haircut work for me?

A: Short pixie bob style haircuts work best on women with oval or heart-shaped faces and straight or wavy hair.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut?

A: Short pixie bob style haircuts need to be maintained every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your short pixie bob style looking its best, you will need to follow a few hair care tips: - Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily. - Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. - Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools. - Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style. - Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair. In conclusion, the short pixie bob style haircut is a bold and confident look that is perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hairstyle. With the right stylist and the right styling products, you can achieve this edgy look and maintain it with ease. So, go ahead and give this style a try – you won't regret it!
