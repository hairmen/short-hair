What Do You Call A Ponytail On Top Of Your Head

Written by William Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever wondered what that popular hairstyle is called where you tie your hair on top of your head? You're not alone! This hairstyle has been around for ages, and it's known by several names, including the top knot, high bun, and the most common, the ponytail on top of your head.

What is a Ponytail on Top of Your Head?

A ponytail on top of your head is a hairstyle where you tie your hair into a small bun or knot on the crown of your head, leaving the rest of your hair hanging down. It's a simple yet versatile style that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. This hairstyle is not only practical, but it's also trendy and easy to achieve.

How to Create a Ponytail on Top of Your Head

If you're wondering how to achieve this popular hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow-dry it until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Tie your hair into a high ponytail

Gather your hair on the crown of your head and tie it into a high ponytail using an elastic band.

Step 4: Create a bun or knot

Twist your ponytail until it starts to coil around the base. Then, wrap it around the base of the ponytail to create a bun or knot. Secure it with bobby pins or another elastic band.

Step 5: Finish off the look

Spray your hair with hairspray to keep it in place and smooth any flyaways with a brush or comb.

Tips for a Perfect Ponytail on Top of Your Head

Here are some tips to make sure you get the perfect ponytail on top of your head:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to avoid it being visible.
  • Don't tie your hair too tight to prevent headaches.
  • Add volume to your hair by teasing the crown before tying it into a ponytail.
  • Spray hairspray on an old toothbrush and use it to smooth any flyaways around the bun.

FAQ About Ponytail on Top of Your Head

Can I wear a ponytail on top of my head every day?

Yes, a ponytail on top of your head is a versatile hairstyle that you can wear every day. Just make sure you don't tie it too tight to avoid headaches.

Can I wear a ponytail on top of my head to a formal event?

Yes, you can dress up your ponytail on top of your head by adding some accessories, such as a headband or hair clip. You can also create a sleeker look by using a smoothing serum or gel.

Is a ponytail on top of my head damaging to my hair?

A ponytail on top of your head is not damaging to your hair if you don't tie it too tight. However, wearing your hair in the same style every day can cause breakage or damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Ponytail on Top of Your Head

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy while wearing a ponytail on top of your head:

  • Avoid tying your hair too tight to prevent breakage or damage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Don't wear a ponytail on top of your head every day to avoid breakage or damage.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup in your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.

In conclusion, a ponytail on top of your head is a simple yet trendy hairstyle that you can wear every day or dress up for a formal event. Just make sure you don't tie your hair too tight and take care of your hair to avoid breakage or damage.
