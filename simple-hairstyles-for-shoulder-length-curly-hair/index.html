Simple Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Do you have shoulder-length curly hair and struggle to find simple hairstyles that suit your hair length and texture? Look no further! In this article, we'll share some easy-to-do hairstyles that you can try at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder Length Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Hairstyle Tutorials
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

How to Style Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right styling techniques, you can make your hair look gorgeous. Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder-length curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to your hair to define your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.

Step-by-Step Hairstyle Tutorials

1. Half-Up Half-Down

Take the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the bottom half of your hair down. You can add some curls to the bottom half of your hair for added volume.

2. Messy Bun

Gather all of your hair at the top of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie. Leave some strands of hair loose to create a messy look.

3. Side Braid

Take a section of hair from the front of your head and start braiding it towards the back of your head. Secure the braid with a hair tie. You can add some curls to the rest of your hair for added volume.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
