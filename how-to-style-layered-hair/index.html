How To Style Layered Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Oct 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Layered hair is one of the most versatile hairstyles out there. It can add volume, movement and dimension to any hair type, making it a popular choice among women. If you have layered hair, you may be wondering how to style it in different ways. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style layered hair like a pro.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Layered Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. FAQs

Description

Layered hair refers to a hairstyle where different lengths of hair are cut to create a layered effect. This is done to add volume, texture, and movement to the hair. Layered hair can be styled in many different ways, including updos, ponytails, braids, and more. In this article, we will focus on some of the most popular ways to style layered hair.

How to Style Layered Hair

Styling layered hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a variety of looks. Here are some of the most popular ways to style layered hair:

1. Straight and Sleek

If you have straight hair, you can achieve a sleek and polished look by using a flat iron. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage. Then, section your hair and run the flat iron over each section from the roots to the ends. Finish with a shine serum to add some extra gloss.

2. Beachy Waves

If you want to add some texture to your hair, beachy waves are a great option. Start by spraying a texturizing spray to your hair to add some grip. Then, use a curling wand to curl sections of your hair, alternating the direction of the curls. Finish with a sea salt spray to add some extra volume and texture.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your layers while keeping your hair out of your face. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair, and securing it with a hair tie. Then, curl the bottom half of your hair with a curling iron. Release the top half of your hair, and backcomb the crown of your head to add some volume. Finally, pull back the top section of your hair and secure it with bobby pins.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style layered hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can damage your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. Focus on the roots to add some lift.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using the techniques mentioned above, or experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your layered hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make all the difference when it comes to styling your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

2. Experiment with Different Techniques

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. You may be surprised at how different your hair can look with just a few changes.

3. Use Accessories

Accessories like hair clips, headbands, and scrunchies can add some extra flair to your layered hair. Experiment with different accessories to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I style my layered hair if it is short?

A: Yes, you can still style your layered hair even if it is short. Look for styles that are suitable for your hair length.

Q: Can I style my layered hair if it is curly?

A: Yes, curly hair can be styled in many different ways, including updos, braids, and more. Look for styles that work with your natural texture.

Q: How often should I trim my layered hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your layers.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your layered hair looking healthy and beautiful:

1. Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a good shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type can help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair, so try to limit your use of heat styling tools. When you do use them, be sure to apply a heat protectant first.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, so be sure to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, styling layered hair can be a fun and creative process. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new. With the right tools and care, your layered hair can look beautiful and healthy.
