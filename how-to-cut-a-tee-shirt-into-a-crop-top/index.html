How To Cut A Tee Shirt Into A Crop Top

Written by William Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Want to turn your old tee shirts into trendy crop tops? Cutting a tee shirt into a crop top is a great way to revamp your wardrobe without spending a lot of money. In this post, we'll show you how to cut a tee shirt into a crop top in a few easy steps.

How to Cut a Tee Shirt into a Crop Top

Cutting a tee shirt into a crop top is a simple process that only requires a few tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • A tee shirt
  • Sharp scissors
  • A ruler or measuring tape
  • Chalk or a washable marker

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to turn your tee shirt into a crop top:

  1. Wash and dry your tee shirt to ensure that it doesn't shrink after you cut it.
  2. Put on the tee shirt and use a ruler or measuring tape to mark the desired length of the crop top.
  3. Use chalk or a washable marker to draw a straight line across the tee shirt at the marked length.
  4. Cut along the marked line with a pair of sharp scissors.
  5. If you want to add a hem to your crop top, fold the bottom edge of the tee shirt up by 1/2 inch and iron it in place. Then, fold it up again by 1/2 inch and iron it again. Finally, sew the hem in place with a needle and thread or a sewing machine.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting a tee shirt into a crop top:

  • Measure twice, cut once. Double-check your measurements before you start cutting to ensure that your crop top is the right length.
  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts. Dull scissors can make jagged edges and ruin your crop top.
  • If you want a more fitted crop top, cut the tee shirt shorter than you think you need to. You can always trim it down more later if it's too long.
  • Experiment with different styles of crop tops. You can cut a tee shirt into a classic crop top, a tie-front crop top, or a halter-neck crop top, among other styles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a tee shirt into a crop top:

  • Can I cut any tee shirt into a crop top? Yes, you can cut any tee shirt into a crop top, but it's best to choose a tee shirt that's a little bit loose-fitting so that you have room to work with.
  • Do I need to sew the edges of the crop top? No, you don't need to sew the edges of the crop top if you don't want to. Raw edges can give your crop top a more edgy, casual look.
  • What should I wear with a crop top? You can wear a crop top with high-waisted jeans or shorts, a maxi skirt, or a pair of leggings. Just make sure to balance the proportions of your outfit so that you don't show too much skin.

Hair Care

Now that you know how to cut a tee shirt into a crop top, why not try a new hairstyle to complete your look? Here are some easy hairstyles that you can try:

  • A messy bun
  • A fishtail braid
  • A half-up, half-down style
  • A sleek ponytail

Remember to use a heat protectant spray if you're using hot tools on your hair, and to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.

That's all there is to it! Cutting a tee shirt into a crop top is a fun and easy DIY project that can give your wardrobe a fresh new look. Happy cutting!
