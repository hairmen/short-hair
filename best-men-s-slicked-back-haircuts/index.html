Best Men s Slicked Back Haircuts

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In 2023, men's hairstyles are still a hot topic. One of the most popular and timeless hairstyles for men is the slicked back look. This style is versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways, from a classic side part to a modern undercut. In this article, we will explore the best men's slicked back haircuts, how to achieve them, and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking sleek and stylish.

Description

The slicked back hairstyle is a classic look that has been popular for decades. It is characterized by hair that is combed back and held in place with pomade or gel. The style can be worn in a variety of ways, from a classic side part to a modern undercut. Whether you have short or long hair, there is a slicked back haircut that will suit you.

How to Achieve the Slicked Back Look

Before you can achieve the perfect slicked back look, you need to have the right haircut. The most important thing is to have enough length on top to be able to slick it back. Generally, the sides and back should be shorter than the top, but the exact length will depend on the specific style you are going for.

Once you have the right haircut, you will need to invest in some good quality hair products. Pomade or gel is essential for holding your hair in place and giving it a sleek finish. You may also want to use a hair dryer to help set the style and give it more volume.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to achieving the perfect slicked back haircut:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your hands and rub them together to warm up the product.
  3. Work the product through your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward.
  4. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting at the crown of your head and working towards the front.
  5. Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to help set the style and give your hair more volume.
  6. Finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Slicked Back Haircut

Once you have achieved the perfect slicked back look, you will want to maintain it for as long as possible. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from product buildup.
  • Use a high quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a comb to touch up your style throughout the day.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about slicked back haircuts:

Q: What is the best product to use for a slicked back look?

A: This will depend on your hair type and the specific style you are going for. Pomade and gel are both good options, but you may need to experiment to find the right product for you.

Q: Can I achieve a slicked back look with short hair?

A: Yes, but you will need to have enough length on top to be able to slick it back. Talk to your barber about the best haircut for your hair length and texture.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and how much product you use. In general, it is a good idea to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

If you want to keep your slicked back haircut looking its best, you will need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, shiny hair:

  • Use a high quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, as this will help to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.
  • Avoid wearing hats or other head coverings for long periods of time, as this can cause your hair to become flattened and greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle and keep it looking fabulous for weeks to come.
