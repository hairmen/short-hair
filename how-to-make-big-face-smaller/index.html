How To Make A Big Face Smaller Tips And Tricks

Written by William Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you feel self-conscious about having a big face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with this issue, but thankfully there are ways to make your face appear smaller. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to achieve a more proportionate look.

How to Make a Big Face Smaller

There are several ways to make a big face appear smaller, including makeup techniques, facial exercises, and hairstyles. Let's take a closer look at each of these methods.

Makeup Techniques

Makeup can be a powerful tool when it comes to creating the illusion of a smaller face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Contouring: Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows on the sides of your face, which will help to make it appear slimmer.
  • Highlighting: Apply a highlighter to the center of your face, such as your forehead, nose, and cheeks. This will draw attention to the center of your face and away from the outer edges.
  • Blush: Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending it upwards towards your temples. This will add some color to your face while also creating a more angular look.

Facial Exercises

Another way to make your face appear smaller is by doing facial exercises. These exercises can help to tone and tighten the muscles in your face, creating a more defined look. Here are some exercises to try:

  • Jawline exercise: Place your thumb under your chin and press gently while pushing your chin upward. This will help to tone your jawline.
  • Cheek exercise: Smile as wide as you can, then place your fingers on the apples of your cheeks and gently lift them upwards. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  • Neck exercise: Tilt your head back and look up at the ceiling. Pucker your lips and hold for a few seconds, then release. This will help to tone the muscles in your neck and chin.

Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can also make a big difference when it comes to making your face appear smaller. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Layers: Adding layers to your hair can help to create movement and dimension, which can make your face appear more proportionate.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to create a more angular look, while blunt bangs can make your face appear shorter.
  • Updos: Pulling your hair up into a bun or ponytail can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the ways to make your face appear smaller, let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look:

  1. Start by applying your makeup, using the contouring, highlighting, and blush techniques mentioned above.
  2. Next, try out some of the facial exercises to help tone and tighten your muscles.
  3. Finally, experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to make your face appear smaller:

  • Drink plenty of water: Staying hydrated can help to reduce puffiness in your face.
  • Avoid salty foods: Eating too much salt can cause your body to retain water, which can lead to a bloated face.
  • Get enough sleep: Lack of sleep can cause your face to appear swollen and puffy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions when it comes to making a big face appear smaller:

How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

It can take several weeks or even months to see results from facial exercises, so be patient and consistent.

Can certain hairstyles make a big face appear even bigger?

Yes, hairstyles with too much volume or width can make your face appear even bigger. Stick to styles that create movement and dimension, such as layers or a side part.

Is it possible to permanently change the shape of my face?

No, it's not possible to permanently change the shape of your face without surgery. However, the tips and tricks mentioned in this article can help to create the illusion of a more proportionate face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair in order to maintain a healthy, shiny, and voluminous look. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top shape:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your big face appear smaller and more proportionate. Remember, everyone is unique and beautiful in their own way, so embrace your features and be confident in yourself!
