Short Shaved Sides Haircut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for an edgy and stylish haircut, then the short shaved sides haircut should definitely be on your list. This trendy haircut has been popular for a few years now and shows no signs of slowing down. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the short shaved sides haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The short shaved sides haircut is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by both men and women. As the name suggests, this haircut features short hair on the sides and back of the head, while the top is left longer. The length of the top can vary, but it is usually long enough to style in different ways.

One of the reasons why the short shaved sides haircut is so popular is because it's easy to maintain. The short sides and back require little to no styling, while the longer top can be styled in a variety of ways. Plus, this haircut looks great on all face shapes and hair types.

How to Get the Short Shaved Sides Haircut

If you're interested in getting the short shaved sides haircut, the first step is to find a good hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience with this type of haircut and can give you the look you want.

Before your appointment, it's a good idea to gather some pictures of the short shaved sides haircut that you like. This will give your hairstylist a clear idea of what you're looking for and help them determine the best way to achieve the look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Short Shaved Sides Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the short shaved sides haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Using clippers, your hairstylist will trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  3. The hairstylist will then use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head to the desired length.
  4. If you want a more dramatic look, your hairstylist may shave a line into the hair where the short sides meet the longer top.
  5. Your hairstylist will then style your hair according to your preferences.

Tips for Styling the Short Shaved Sides Haircut

The short shaved sides haircut is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a styling product to add texture to the longer hair on top.
  • You can slick back the longer hair on top for a more polished look.
  • Add some volume to the top by blow-drying your hair upside down.
  • You can also style your hair into a messy, textured look for a more casual vibe.

FAQ

Is the short shaved sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the short shaved sides haircut looks great on all face shapes.

How often do I need to get the short shaved sides haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows, but most people need to get this haircut every four to six weeks to maintain the look.

Hair Care Tips for the Short Shaved Sides Haircut

To keep your short shaved sides haircut looking great, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the short sides and keep the longer hair on top looking great.

In conclusion, the short shaved sides haircut is a stylish and versatile haircut that looks great on both men and women. Whether you want a polished or casual look, this haircut can be styled in many different ways. Use these tips and tricks to get the most out of your short shaved sides haircut and keep it looking great.
