What To Do With Messy Curly Hair In The Morning: Tips And Tricks

If you have curly hair, you know the struggle of waking up with a messy mane in the morning. It can be frustrating to deal with unruly curls, especially when you're short on time. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on what to do with messy curly hair in the morning.

Table of Contents

  1. How To
  2. Step By Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How To

The first thing you need to do is avoid brushing or combing your curly hair when it's dry. This can cause frizz and breakage, making your curls even more unruly. Instead, use your fingers to gently detangle your curls while they're wet.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair. This will help define your curls and keep them hydrated throughout the day. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair.

Once you've applied your product, you can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser to speed up the process. A diffuser is a tool that attaches to your hair dryer and helps distribute the heat evenly, which can help reduce frizz and define your curls.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on what to do with messy curly hair in the morning:

  1. Wet your hair in the shower or with a spray bottle.
  2. Gently detangle your curls with your fingers.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair.
  4. Distribute the product evenly through your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  5. Either air dry your hair or use a diffuser to speed up the process.
  6. Once your hair is dry, you can use your fingers to scrunch your curls and add more definition.
  7. Use a hairspray or hair oil to tame any frizz or flyaways.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your messy curly hair:

  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • Try not to touch your hair throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing curly hair:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most curly hair experts recommend washing your hair once or twice a week.

What's the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is by air drying or using a diffuser. Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz.

How can I prevent frizz?

You can prevent frizz by using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream, sleeping on a satin pillowcase, and avoiding hot tools.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can manage your messy curly hair in the morning and rock your natural curls with confidence.
