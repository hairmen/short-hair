How To Do Jerry Curls For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 04, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to add some texture and bounce to your hair, jerry curls might be the perfect look for you. This classic hairstyle was popularized in the 1980s and is still a favorite among men today. In this article, we'll show you how to do jerry curls for guys in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language. Keep reading to learn more!

How to Do Jerry Curls for Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about what jerry curls are and what you'll need to achieve this look. Jerry curls are a type of perm that creates tight, bouncy curls. To get jerry curls, you'll need a styling gel or cream, a perm kit, a plastic cap, and a comb. You can get perm kits and styling products at your local beauty supply store or online.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve jerry curls:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling gel or cream to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Comb your hair to ensure that the product is evenly distributed.
  4. Put on a plastic cap and let the product sit for the recommended amount of time, which is usually 15-20 minutes.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  6. Apply the perm solution to your hair, following the instructions on the kit.
  7. Put on another plastic cap and let the solution sit for the recommended amount of time.
  8. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  9. Apply the neutralizing solution to your hair, following the instructions on the kit.
  10. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  11. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair and let it air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair with a blow dryer.

Congratulations, you now have jerry curls!

Tips for Maintaining Jerry Curls

Here are some tips to keep your jerry curls looking fresh:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Apply a moisturizing oil to your hair daily.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible.
  • Get touch-ups every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your curls.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about jerry curls:

Q: Can I get jerry curls if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can get jerry curls on straight hair, but it might not be as tight as it would be on curly or kinky hair.

Q: How long do jerry curls last?

A: Jerry curls can last up to 6-8 weeks with proper maintenance.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have jerry curls?

A: It's best to wait at least 2 weeks after getting jerry curls before dyeing your hair to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Jerry Curls

Here are some tips for caring for your hair with jerry curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush on your curls, as it can cause frizz.
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Apply a deep conditioner to your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

Now that you know how to do jerry curls for guys, you can try this classic hairstyle for yourself. With proper maintenance and care, your jerry curls will look great for weeks to come. Have fun experimenting with different styling products to get the perfect look for you!
