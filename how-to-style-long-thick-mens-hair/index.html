How To Style Long Thick Men s Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Long thick hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. It is a blessing because it gives you more styling options, but it can also be a curse because it can be difficult to manage. In this guide, we will show you how to style long thick men’s hair to help you achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Long Thick Men’s Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Long Thick Men’s Hair
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Men’s Hair

Description

Long thick hair can be styled in various ways, from a slick back to a messy bun. However, it is essential to know how to style it properly to achieve the desired look. Styling long thick hair requires the right products, tools, and techniques. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know to style long thick men’s hair.

How to Style Long Thick Men’s Hair

Before you start styling your long thick hair, it is essential to choose the right haircut. A layered haircut can help reduce the volume of your hair and make it more manageable. Here are some popular hairstyles for long thick men’s hair:

  • Slick back
  • Man bun
  • Half-up half-down
  • Top knot
  • Side part

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style long thick men’s hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair while it is still damp.
  3. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while using a brush to style it in the desired direction.
  4. Finish off the look by applying a hairspray or finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Thick Men’s Hair

Here are some tips for styling long thick men’s hair:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Invest in quality styling products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling it.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to avoid damaging it.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long thick men’s hair:

  • Q: Can I style long thick hair without using any products?
  • A: It is possible, but using a styling product can help hold the style in place and make it last longer.
  • Q: Can I style long thick hair if it is curly?
  • A: Yes, you can. Use a curl-enhancing product and a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Q: How often should I wash my long thick hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair type, but washing it every 2-3 days is usually sufficient.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Men’s Hair

Here are some hair care tips for long thick men’s hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and manageable.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair ties that are too tight, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Styling long thick men’s hair can be challenging, but with the right products, tools, and techniques, you can achieve the look you want. Follow the tips and guidelines in this guide to help you style your long thick hair with ease.
