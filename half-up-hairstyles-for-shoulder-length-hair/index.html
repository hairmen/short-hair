Half Up Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have shoulder length hair, you know that it can be a challenge to find hairstyles that work for you. But half up hairstyles are the perfect solution! They’re easy to do, versatile, and look great on anyone. In this post, we’ll share some of our favorite half up hairstyles for shoulder length hair, along with tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Description

Half up hairstyles combine the best of both worlds: the freedom of having your hair down, and the convenience of having it out of your face. They’re perfect for any occasion, whether you’re going to work or a night out with friends.

For shoulder length hair, half up hairstyles can be especially flattering. They help to add volume and dimension to your hair, while also showcasing your beautiful locks.

How to Style Half Up Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair

The great thing about half up hairstyles is that they’re so easy to style! Here’s how:

  1. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair. You can use a comb or your fingers to do this.
  2. Gather the section of hair that you want to pull back, and secure it with a hair tie or clip.
  3. You can leave the rest of your hair down, or style it in any way you like. Some popular options include loose waves, curls, or a sleek straight look.

That’s it! You’re ready to go.

Step-by-Step Guide to Half Up Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair

If you want to get a little more creative with your half up hairstyles, here are some step-by-step instructions for a few of our favorites:

Twisted Half Up

  1. Section off the top half of your hair, and divide it into two equal sections.
  2. Twist each section away from your face, and then twist them together in the back.
  3. Secure the twisted section with a hair tie or clip.
  4. Leave the rest of your hair down, or style it in any way you like.

Bow Half Up

  1. Section off the top half of your hair, and gather it into a ponytail at the back of your head.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the underside of the ponytail, and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it.
  3. Divide the ponytail into two equal sections, and create a bow by looping each section over the middle and securing it with bobby pins.
  4. Leave the rest of your hair down, or style it in any way you like.

Tips for Styling Half Up Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your half up hairstyles:

  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair before styling.
  • Add accessories like headbands or hair clips to dress up your half up hairstyles.
  • Experiment with different types of twists or braids to create unique looks.
  • Try using a curling iron or straightener to add even more variation to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about half up hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

Can I wear a half up hairstyle if I have thin hair?

Absolutely! Half up hairstyles can actually help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Do I need any special tools to create a half up hairstyle?

Nope! All you need is a hair tie or clip to secure the top section of your hair.

Can I wear a half up hairstyle to a formal event?

Definitely. Half up hairstyles can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair healthy and looking its best, here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too frequently.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and half up hairstyles in your arsenal, you’re sure to look amazing every day.
