How To Style Your Hair While Blow Drying

Written by William Sep 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

In today's world, blow drying your hair is one of the most popular ways to style it. Not only does it save you time, but it also gives you the flexibility to try different hairstyles. However, it can be challenging to achieve the desired look if you do not know how to style your hair while blow drying. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to style your hair while blow drying.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Blow drying your hair can be a quick and easy way to style your locks. However, it is essential to use the right techniques and tools to achieve the best results. Whether you want to create a voluminous blowout or a sleek, straight look, styling your hair while blow drying requires some practice and patience. In this article, we will provide you with the tips and tricks to achieve the perfect hairstyle while blow drying your hair.

How to

Before you start blow drying your hair, you need to prepare it properly. Make sure your hair is clean and detangled. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Then, follow these steps to style your hair while blow drying:

Step 1: Divide your hair into sections

Use clips to divide your hair into sections. Start from the bottom layer and work your way up. This will help you to dry each section thoroughly and avoid missing any spots.

Step 2: Use a round brush

Choose a round brush that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, a large round brush can give you volume, while a small round brush can create curls. Start at the roots and use the brush to pull your hair upward and away from your scalp. Then, use the blow dryer to follow the brush downwards.

Step 3: Use the right temperature and speed

Use a medium heat setting and a high speed to dry your hair. Avoid using high heat as it can damage your hair. If you have fine or thin hair, use a lower heat setting. If you have thick or curly hair, you may need to use a higher heat setting, but be sure to keep the blow dryer moving to prevent heat damage.

Step 4: Finish with cold air

Once you have dried your hair, use the cool shot button on your blow dryer to finish it off. This will help to set your hair and give it a smooth, shiny finish.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair while blow drying:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  3. Start at the roots and use a round brush to pull your hair upward and away from your scalp.
  4. Use the blow dryer to follow the brush downwards.
  5. Repeat this process for each section.
  6. Once you have dried your hair, use the cool shot button on your blow dryer to finish it off.

Tips

To achieve the best results while blow drying your hair, follow these tips:

  • Choose a round brush that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Use a medium heat setting and a high speed to dry your hair.
  • Avoid using high heat as it can damage your hair.
  • Use the cool shot button on your blow dryer to finish off your hair.
  • Keep the blow dryer moving to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a nozzle attachment on your blow dryer to focus the airflow.
  • Do not blow dry your hair every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Can I blow dry my hair every day?

No, it is not recommended to blow dry your hair every day as it can cause heat damage and dryness. Try to limit blow drying to once or twice a week and use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

Which brush is best for blow drying?

The best brush for blow drying depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, a large round brush can give you volume, while a small round brush can create curls. Choose a brush that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle while blow drying your hair. Remember to use the right techniques and tools to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy and shiny.
