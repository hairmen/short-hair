Men s Haircut Fade Sides Short Top A Guide For Relaxed Style

If you're looking for a trendy, low-maintenance hairstyle, then the men's haircut fade sides short top is the perfect choice for you. This style features a short top with faded sides, creating a clean and sharp look that's easy to maintain. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Fade Sides Short Top

The men's haircut fade sides short top is a popular style that can be achieved at most barbershops. To get this style, you'll need to ask for a short top with faded sides. The barber will use clippers to gradually blend the hair on the sides and back of your head, creating a fade that transitions into the longer hair on top. Depending on your preference, you can choose a high, mid, or low fade.

It's important to bring a photo of the style you want to your barber to ensure that they understand exactly what you're looking for. This will also help them to make any necessary adjustments to customize the style to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Men's Haircut Fade Sides Short Top

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the men's haircut fade sides short top:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Brush your hair and remove any tangles.
  3. Decide on the length of the hair on top and communicate this to your barber.
  4. Choose the desired fade level (high, mid, or low) and communicate this to your barber.
  5. The barber will use clippers to gradually fade the hair on the sides and back of your head.
  6. Once the fade is complete, the barber will trim and style the hair on top to your desired length.
  7. The barber will use scissors to shape the hair on top and add texture.
  8. The haircut is complete and ready to style.

Styling Tips for the Men's Haircut Fade Sides Short Top

The men's haircut fade sides short top is a versatile style that can be styled in many different ways. Here are some tips for styling this haircut:

  • Use a quality pomade or wax to style the hair on top.
  • Apply the product to damp hair for a natural look or dry hair for a more textured look.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on top.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back, messy, or spiky.
  • Don't be afraid to use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to the hair on top.

FAQs About the Men's Haircut Fade Sides Short Top

Q: How often do I need to get this haircut?

A: It's recommended to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Q: Can this style be worn by all hair types?

A: Yes, the men's haircut fade sides short top can be worn by all hair types, but the length on top may need to be adjusted to suit thicker or thinner hair.

Q: Is this style easy to maintain?

A: Yes, the men's haircut fade sides short top is a low-maintenance style that can be easily maintained with regular trims and simple styling techniques.

Hair Care Tips for the Men's Haircut Fade Sides Short Top

Here are some hair care tips to keep your men's haircut fade sides short top looking sharp:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, on the hair on top.
  • Use a quality hair oil or serum to keep the hair on top looking healthy and shiny.
  • Don't forget to use a sunscreen or hat to protect your scalp from the sun.
  • Regularly trim the hair on the sides and back to maintain the fade.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve and maintain the men's haircut fade sides short top for a relaxed and stylish look.
