How To Make Face Look Fatter Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of having a slim face and want to make it look fuller? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to make face look fatter. We will also answer some frequently asked questions related to this topic. So, let's get started.

How to Make Face Look Fatter

If you want to make your face look fatter, there are several things you can do. Some of them are:

Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that includes healthy fats, carbohydrates, and protein can help you gain weight and make your face look fuller. Foods that are rich in healthy fats include avocados, nuts, and fatty fish. You can also add carbohydrates to your diet by eating fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Protein-rich foods like eggs, chicken, and tofu can also help you gain weight.

Drink Plenty of Water

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your body hydrated and prevent your face from looking too thin. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water a day.

Exercise

Exercising can help you gain weight and make your face look fuller. Focus on weightlifting and strength training exercises that target your facial muscles.

Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for your overall health and can also help you maintain a healthy weight. Aim to get at least 7-8 hours of sleep each night.

Step by Step: How to Make Face Look Fatter

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to make your face look fatter:

Step 1: Apply a Contouring Powder

Apply a contouring powder that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to the hollows of your cheeks. Blend the powder well to create the illusion of fuller cheeks.

Step 2: Use a Highlighter

Using a highlighter, apply it to the high points of your face, like your cheekbones, brow bone, and bridge of your nose. This will help to create a contrast between the highlighted areas and the contoured areas, making your face look fuller.

Step 3: Apply Blush

Apply a rosy blush to the apples of your cheeks. This will help to create a natural flush and make your face look fuller.

Step 4: Use a Lip Plumper

Using a lip plumper can help to make your lips look fuller, which can also help to make your face look fuller overall.

Tips to Make Face Look Fatter

Here are some additional tips to make your face look fatter:

Eat More Frequently

Eating more frequently can help you gain weight and make your face look fuller. Try to eat 5-6 small meals a day instead of 3 large meals.

Avoid Smoking and Drinking

Smoking and drinking can cause your face to look thin and gaunt. Avoid smoking and limit your alcohol intake to maintain a fuller face.

Get a Haircut

Getting a haircut that frames your face can help to make it look fuller. A short bob or layered haircut can help to create the illusion of fuller cheeks.

FAQ

Q: Can facial exercises help to make my face look fatter?

A: Yes, facial exercises can help to strengthen your facial muscles and make your face look fuller.

Q: Is it safe to use a lip plumper?

A: Yes, most lip plumpers are safe to use. However, if you have sensitive skin, it's best to test the product on a small area first to make sure you don't have an adverse reaction.

Hair Care: How to Make Face Look Fatter

Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can help to make your face look fuller. A hairstyle that is layered or has volume at the crown can help to create the illusion of fuller cheeks.

Use Hair Products

Using hair products like mousse or volumizing spray can also help to add volume to your hair, which can make your face look fuller.


In conclusion, making your face look fatter is not difficult. By following the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can achieve a fuller and plumper face. Remember to eat a balanced diet, exercise, and get enough sleep to maintain a healthy weight. Also, use makeup and haircare products to your advantage to create the illusion of a fuller face.
