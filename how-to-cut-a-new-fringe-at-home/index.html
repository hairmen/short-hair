How To Cut A New Fringe At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Do you want to change your look without spending a lot of money? Cutting a new fringe at home is an easy and affordable way to do so. In this guide, we will provide you with a step-by-step process to help you achieve a new fringe look in the comfort of your own home.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 1:

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it to remove any tangles.

Step 2:

Section off the hair that you want to cut for your new fringe. Use hair clips to separate it from the rest of your hair.

Step 3:

Using a comb, gently comb your hair downward to ensure that it is straight.

Step 4:

Next, take the scissors and hold them vertically. Cut your hair in small increments to avoid cutting off too much at once.

Step 5:

After each cut, use the comb to check the length of your fringe. Continue cutting until you achieve your desired length.

Step 6:

If you want to add texture to your fringe, use the scissors to make small snips. This will give your fringe a more natural look.

Step 7:

Once you have finished cutting your fringe, brush it to remove any loose hair.

Tips

When cutting your fringe, make sure to cut it slightly longer than you want it to be. You can always trim it more later.

If you are nervous about cutting your own hair, start by cutting a small amount at a time.

Use hair clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way while you cut your fringe.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my fringe when it is wet?

A: It is best to cut your hair when it is dry, as wet hair can be longer and harder to cut accurately.

Q: What if I make a mistake while cutting my fringe?

A: Don't panic! If you make a mistake, you can always visit a hairdresser to have it fixed.

Hair Care

After cutting your fringe, make sure to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and soft.

Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools on your fringe, as this can cause damage.

With these simple steps, you can easily cut a new fringe at home and change up your look. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
