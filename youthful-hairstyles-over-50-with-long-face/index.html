Youthful Hairstyles Over 50 With Long Face How To Achieve The Best Look

Written by Frank Jul 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its luster, and we may feel like it's time to chop it all off. However, there are plenty of youthful hairstyles for women over 50 with long faces that can make us look and feel fabulous. In this article, we will explore how to achieve the best look for your long face shape and provide tips and tricks to maintain your hair's health.

Description

A long face shape is typically longer than it is wide and can sometimes appear more angular. The key to finding the perfect youthful hairstyle is to create balance and proportion. By adding volume and texture to the right areas, you can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. In this article, we will explore different hairstyles that work well for long face shapes and help you achieve the best look.

How to Achieve the Best Look

The first step to achieving the best look for your long face shape is to find a hairstyle that complements your features. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best look:

Tip #1: Add Layers

Adding layers to your hairstyle can create the illusion of volume and movement. By layering your hair, you can create a more textured and youthful look. Layers also help to soften any harsh angles in your face shape and create a more balanced appearance.

Tip #2: Choose the Right Length

When it comes to choosing the right length for your hair, it's important to find a length that complements your face shape. For long face shapes, mid-length to long hairstyles work well. Longer hair can help to elongate your face shape, while mid-length hair can add volume and create a more balanced appearance.

Tip #3: Experiment with Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to add a youthful touch to your hairstyle. For long face shapes, side-swept bangs work well as they can help to soften any harsh angles in your face shape. Wispy bangs can also create a more textured look and add volume to your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a youthful hairstyle for women over 50 with a long face shape:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hair Stylist

The first step is to consult with your hair stylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape. Your stylist can help you choose the right length and style that complements your features and hair texture.

Step 2: Add Layers

If you decide to add layers to your hair, your stylist will start by sectioning your hair and cutting layers into each section. The layers should be cut at different lengths to create texture and movement.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, you can style it at home using a blow dryer and round brush. Start by blow-drying your hair while using the brush to add volume and texture. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain a youthful hairstyle:

Tip #1: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help to maintain the health and texture of your hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals.

Tip #2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling can damage your hair over time, so it's important to protect it from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or flat iron, and avoid using high heat settings on your styling tools.

Tip #3: Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to maintain the shape and texture of your hair. Aim to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about youthful hairstyles for women over 50 with long face shapes:

What hairstyles work well for long face shapes?

Mid-length to long hairstyles with layers and side-swept bangs work well for long face shapes.

How do I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and using a volumizing spray or mousse.

How can I protect my hair from heat damage?

You can protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray, avoiding high heat settings on your styling tools, and limiting the use of heat styling tools.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for maintaining the health of your hair:

Tip #1: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help to maintain the health and texture of your hair. Aim to eat a diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.

Tip #2: Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates and parabens. These chemicals can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage over time.

Tip #3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair over time, so it's important to protect it with a hat or scarf when you're outside for extended periods of time.

In conclusion, there are plenty of youthful hairstyles for women over 50 with long face shapes. By adding layers, choosing the right length, and experimenting with bangs, you can achieve a more youthful and balanced appearance. Remember to use the right products, protect your hair from heat damage, and get regular haircuts to maintain the health and texture of your hair. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the best look for your long face shape.
