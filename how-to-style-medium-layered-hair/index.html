How To Style Medium Layered Hair A Complete Guide

If you have medium length hair with layers, you have a lot of styling options available to you. From beachy waves to sleek straight locks, there are many different ways to style your hair depending on the occasion. In this article, we will take you through a step-by-step guide on how to style medium layered hair. Let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Medium Layered Hair

Before we dive into the different styling options, it's important to note that the key to styling medium layered hair is to embrace the layers. Layers add dimension and texture to your hair, so don't be afraid to show them off.

When it comes to styling medium layered hair, you have a few different options:

  • Straight and Sleek
  • Beachy Waves
  • Messy Updo
  • Braids
  • Half-Up Half-Down

Each of these styles can be achieved with different tools and techniques, so let's take a closer look at each one.

Straight and Sleek

If you prefer a more polished look, straight and sleek hair is the way to go. To achieve this look, start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your hair in sections. Finish with a shine spray to add some extra gloss.

Beachy Waves

For a more relaxed, beachy look, try creating some loose waves in your hair. You can achieve this with a curling iron or a straightener. Simply wrap sections of your hair around the iron and hold for a few seconds. Finish with some texturizing spray to define the waves.

Messy Updo

If you're looking for an easy and stylish updo, try a messy bun or top knot. Start by pulling your hair into a high ponytail, then twist and wrap the hair around the base of the ponytail. Secure with bobby pins and pull out a few pieces to create a messy, effortless look.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some texture and interest to your hair. Whether you opt for a simple braid or a more intricate fishtail braid, this style is perfect for medium layered hair. Try a side braid or a braided crown for an extra special touch.

Half-Up Half-Down

If you want to show off your layers but still keep your hair out of your face, try a half-up half-down style. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure with a clip or elastic. You can leave the bottom half straight or add some curls or waves for added texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different styling options, let's go through a step-by-step guide for each one.

Straight and Sleek

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair and use a flat iron to straighten each section.
  3. Finish with a shine spray.

Beachy Waves

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair and use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves.
  3. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Messy Updo

  1. Pull your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist and wrap the hair around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure with bobby pins and pull out a few pieces for a messy look.

Braids

  1. Choose your braid style and section your hair accordingly.
  2. Create your braid, starting at the top and working your way down.
  3. Secure the end of the braid with an elastic.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Section the top half of your hair and pull it back.
  2. Secure with a clip or elastic.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair straight or add curls or waves.

Tips for Styling Medium Layered Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your medium layered hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to add some volume to your hair with a volumizing spray or mousse.

FAQs

How often should I wash my medium layered hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can go longer between washes. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Can I still wear a ponytail with medium layered hair?

Yes! A ponytail is a classic and easy hairstyle that works well with medium layered hair. Try a high ponytail or a low ponytail with some face-framing layers.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Layered Hair

Here are a few tips to keep your medium layered hair looking its best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep the layers looking fresh.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and styling options, you can rock your medium layered hair with confidence. Remember to embrace the layers and have fun experimenting with different styles!
