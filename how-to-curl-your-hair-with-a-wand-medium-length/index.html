How To Curl Your Hair With A Wand Medium Length A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 15, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re looking for a quick and easy way to add some volume and texture to your medium-length hair, curling it with a wand is a great option. Not only is it simple to do, but it also creates a natural-looking wave that’s perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to achieve beautiful, bouncy curls with a wand.

How to

Before we get started, here’s what you’ll need:

  • A medium-barrel wand (1-1.5 inches in diameter)
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray

Now, let’s get started!

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the wand. Hold for 5-10 seconds, then release. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Set Your Curls

Let your curls cool for a few minutes before running your fingers through them to loosen them up. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don’t hold the wand on your hair for too long, as this can cause damage.
  • Use hair clips to section your hair, making it easier to curl.
  • Don’t forget to let your curls cool before touching them.
  • Finish with hairspray to hold your curls in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling your hair with a wand:

Q: What type of wand should I use for medium-length hair?

A: A medium-barrel wand (1-1.5 inches in diameter) is perfect for medium-length hair.

Q: How long should I hold the wand on my hair?

A: Hold the wand on your hair for 5-10 seconds, depending on the thickness of your hair.

Q: How can I prevent damage to my hair when curling it with a wand?

A: Use a heat protectant spray and don’t hold the wand on your hair for too long.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, make sure to use a deep conditioning treatment regularly and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls with a wand in no time. Whether you’re getting ready for a special occasion or just want to add some volume to your everyday look, curling your hair with a wand is a great option for medium-length hair.
