How To Do A Women s Short Layered Haircut

Written by Robby Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short layered haircuts are a popular choice for women who want a low-maintenance yet stylish hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily achieve a layered haircut at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of doing a women's short layered haircut.

Description

A women's short layered haircut is a hairstyle where the hair is cut into layers of different lengths. The shortest layers are usually near the crown of the head, while the longest layers are at the ends. This creates a textured and voluminous look that is easy to style and maintain.

How to

To do a women's short layered haircut, you will need the following tools:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle

Follow these steps to achieve a short layered haircut:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles. Let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer to dry it completely.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Create a bottom section that starts at the nape of your neck and a top section that starts at the crown of your head. Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section.

Step 3: Cut the bottom section

Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut it to the desired length. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have cut the entire bottom section.

Step 4: Cut the top section

Release the top section of your hair and comb it straight up. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length, making sure to blend the top layers with the bottom layers. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you have cut the entire top section.

Step 5: Add texture

To add texture to your haircut, take small sections of hair and point cut them with your scissors. This will create choppy layers that add volume and movement to your hair.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls, or simply leave it natural.

Tips

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more later, but you can't undo a bad haircut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean and even cuts.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or unsure of what you are doing.

FAQ

Can I do a short layered haircut on curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, it is important to cut curly hair when it is dry to ensure that you can see the natural curl pattern and avoid cutting too much.

How often should I cut my short layered haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the shape of your haircut. On average, short layered haircuts should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Can I color my hair after doing a short layered haircut?

Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to wait at least 48 hours after cutting your hair before coloring it to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining a short layered haircut is easy with the right hair care routine. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use hair oils or serums to add shine and nourishment to your hair.

Conclusion

Doing a women's short layered haircut is easy and fun with the right tools and techniques. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that you will love.
