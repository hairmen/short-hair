How Can I Prevent Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment

Written by Oliver Jul 30, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after getting a keratin treatment? You're not alone. Many people suffer from hair loss after undergoing this treatment. However, there are several steps you can take to prevent hair loss after keratin treatment. In this article, we will discuss how you can prevent hair loss after keratin treatment through various methods and treatments.

Table of Content

  • How to Prevent Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Preventing Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment
  • FAQs About Preventing Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment
  • Hair Care Products for Preventing Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment

How to Prevent Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment

The first step in preventing hair loss after keratin treatment is to make sure that you choose a reputable salon or stylist. A professional and experienced stylist will ensure that your hair is not over-processed, which can lead to hair loss. Additionally, they will use high-quality keratin products that are less damaging to your hair.

Another effective way to prevent hair loss after keratin treatment is to limit the frequency of the treatment. While keratin treatments can make your hair look silky and smooth, overuse can lead to hair loss. We recommend getting the treatment every 3-4 months to avoid any damage.

Lastly, make sure you take proper care of your hair after the treatment. Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for keratin-treated hair. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners or curling irons to minimize hair damage.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to help you prevent hair loss after keratin treatment:

  1. Choose a reputable salon or stylist
  2. Limit the frequency of the treatment
  3. Take proper care of your hair after the treatment

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment

Here are some tips to help you prevent hair loss after keratin treatment:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Avoid tying your hair in a tight ponytail or bun
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction while sleeping
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners or curling irons

FAQs About Preventing Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment

Q: How long does hair loss last after keratin treatment?

A: Hair loss after keratin treatment should stop within a month or two. If the hair loss persists, consult your stylist or a dermatologist.

Q: Can keratin treatment cause permanent hair loss?

A: Keratin treatment can cause permanent hair loss if overused or if the treatment is not done correctly. That's why it's important to choose a reputable salon or stylist and limit the frequency of the treatment.

Q: Can I dye my hair after keratin treatment?

A: It's best to wait at least two weeks before dyeing your hair after a keratin treatment. The chemicals in hair dye can strip away the keratin treatment and cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care Products for Preventing Hair Loss After Keratin Treatment

Here are some hair care products that can help prevent hair loss after keratin treatment:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Keratin-infused hair masks
  • Hair oils containing argan, coconut or jojoba oil
  • Hair supplements containing biotin, vitamin A, C and E

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can prevent hair loss after keratin treatment and enjoy silky, smooth and healthy hair.
