Best Hairstyles For Round Fat Faces

Written by Petter Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round, fat face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that can work wonders for a round, fat face. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for round, fat faces, and give you tips on how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have a round, fat face, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, focus on hairstyles that elongate your face and draw attention to your best features. Styles with layers, angles, and height at the crown can all work wonders for a round, fat face.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for a round, fat face, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, a short, layered bob might be a great choice. If you have fine, straight hair, a long, sleek style with layers can be very flattering.

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles can be a great choice for a round, fat face. A short, layered bob can add height at the crown and elongate your face. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs can also be very flattering. If you prefer a longer look, try a long bob with layers around the face.

Medium-Length Hairstyles

Medium-length hairstyles can also work well for a round, fat face. A shoulder-length cut with layers can be very flattering, especially if you add some volume at the crown. A long, layered bob with side-swept bangs can also be a great choice.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles can be tricky for a round, fat face, but with the right cut, they can be very flattering. Try a long, layered style with face-framing layers. A side-swept bang can also help elongate your face. If you have thick hair, consider adding some texture with layers or a choppy cut.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best hairstyles for round, fat faces:

Short Layered Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the top layer of hair and clip it out of the way.
  3. Cut the bottom layer of hair to your desired length.
  4. Release the top layer of hair and cut it to a slightly shorter length.
  5. Add layers around the face, focusing on the bottom layer of hair.
  6. Style with a volumizing mousse or spray for added height.

Long Layered Style

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Cut the hair to your desired length, adding layers around the face.
  3. Add texture with a razor or scissors, focusing on the ends of the hair.
  4. Create a side part and add side-swept bangs.
  5. Style with a curling iron or flat iron for added texture.

Tips

  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Use layers to add height and elongate your face.
  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Add texture to your hair with layers or a choppy cut.
  • Use styling products to add volume and hold to your hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round, fat face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or a wide, blunt bob.

What haircuts can make my face look slimmer?

Styles with layers, angles, and height at the crown can all help elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

What styling products should I use to add volume to my hair?

Use a volumizing mousse or spray, or try a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is key to keeping your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best, no matter what your face shape or hair type may be.
