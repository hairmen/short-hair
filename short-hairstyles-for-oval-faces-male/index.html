Short Hairstyles For Oval Faces Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 03, 2023 · 5 min read

Oval faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape, allowing men to experiment with different hairstyles. However, choosing the right hairstyle can make all the difference in enhancing your features and overall appearance. In this guide, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for oval faces male, along with tips and tricks to maintain them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Short hairstyles are a popular choice for many men, especially those with oval faces. This face shape is characterized by a balanced proportion of features, including a slightly curved jawline, high cheekbones, and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin. The key to finding the right short hairstyle is to choose one that complements your facial features and highlights your best qualities.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a short hairstyle for oval faces male, it's important to consider the following factors:

Face Shape

While oval faces are considered to be the most versatile, not all short hairstyles will suit this face shape. It's essential to choose a style that complements your facial features and enhances your overall appearance.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also play a role in choosing the right hairstyle. For instance, if you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a short, layered cut that adds volume and texture. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a shorter, more structured style.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also impact the type of short hairstyle you choose. If you're someone who has a busy schedule and doesn't have much time to style your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style that requires minimal upkeep.

Step by Step Guide to Styling

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling some of the best short hairstyles for oval faces male:

Short Pompadour

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product
3. Using a comb, create a side part
4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume
5. Use a comb to smooth the sides and back
6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place

Crew Cut

1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo
2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product
3. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair, leaving the top longer
4. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to the desired length
5. Use a comb to style your hair forward
6. Finish with a medium hold hairspray to keep the style in place

Short Caesar Cut

1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo
2. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product
3. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair, leaving the top slightly longer
4. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to the desired length
5. Use a comb to style your hair forward and slightly to the side
6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the style in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling short hairstyles for oval faces male:

  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Use a comb to create a structured look, or your fingers to create a more relaxed, messy look
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best short hairstyles for oval faces male?

The best short hairstyles for oval faces male include the short pompadour, crew cut, and short Caesar cut.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles to suit different occasions, such as a more structured look for a formal event or a messy, relaxed look for a casual outing.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your short hairstyle looking its best:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from environmental damage
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can clog your pores and cause irritation

With the right short hairstyle, you can enhance your features and create a stylish, modern look. By following these tips and tricks and taking proper care of your hair, you can maintain your short hairstyle and keep it looking its best.
