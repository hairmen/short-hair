Hairstyles For Oval Shaped Head Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Oct 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you an oval shaped head male struggling to find the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! This guide will provide you with a variety of hairstyles that will suit your face shape and make you stand out from the crowd. Read on for tips, step by step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval shaped head is considered to be the ideal face shape for men, as it is well-proportioned and versatile. However, finding the right hairstyle to complement this face shape can be challenging. The key is to choose a hairstyle that balances out the features of your face and highlights your best qualities. In this guide, we will provide you with a variety of hairstyles that are perfect for oval shaped head males.

How to

Before choosing a hairstyle, it is important to determine your hair type and texture. This will help you choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and suits your hair type. Once you have determined your hair type, consider your personal style and preferences. Do you prefer a classic or modern look? Short or long hair?

Short Hairstyles:

Short hairstyles are perfect for oval shaped head males who want a low-maintenance look. The buzz cut, crew cut, and high and tight are popular short hairstyles that are easy to maintain and look great on oval shaped heads. These hairstyles are perfect for men who prefer a classic, clean-cut look.

Medium Hairstyles:

Medium hairstyles are perfect for oval shaped head males who want a more versatile look. The side part, slick back, and textured crop are popular medium hairstyles that are easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways. These hairstyles are perfect for men who want a modern, stylish look.

Long Hairstyles:

Long hairstyles are perfect for oval shaped head males who want a more relaxed, casual look. The man bun, ponytail, and shoulder-length hair are popular long hairstyles that are easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways. These hairstyles are perfect for men who want a laid-back, bohemian look.

Step by step

Here are step by step instructions for some of the most popular hairstyles for oval shaped head males:

Buzz Cut:

  1. Start by trimming your hair to a short length with clippers.
  2. Use a guard to adjust the length of the hair, if necessary.
  3. Trim the hair evenly all around the head.
  4. Clean up the edges with scissors.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to add shine and hold.

Side Part:

  1. Start by combing your hair to one side.
  2. Use a comb to create a clean part on the opposite side.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or gel, to hold the hair in place.
  4. Use a comb to smooth down any flyaways and create a polished look.

Man Bun:

  1. Gather your hair at the crown of your head.
  2. Tie your hair into a bun using a hair tie.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hairs.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as hairspray or wax, to hold the bun in place.

Tips

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and hair products.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist for advice and guidance.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oval shaped head males:

What is the best hairstyle for oval shaped head males?

The best hairstyle for oval shaped head males is one that balances out the features of the face and highlights the best qualities. Short, medium, and long hairstyles can all look great on oval shaped head males.

What hair products should I use for my hairstyle?

The hair products you use will depend on the hairstyle you choose and your personal preferences. Pomade, wax, gel, and hairspray are all popular hair products that can be used to hold and style hair.

How often should I get a haircut?

It is recommended that you get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and well-groomed.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, well-groomed hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.

With these tips and hairstyles, you are sure to find the perfect look for your oval shaped head. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and hair products. With proper care and maintenance, your hair will look healthy and well-groomed, and you will feel confident and stylish.
