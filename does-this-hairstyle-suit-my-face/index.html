Does This Hairstyle Suit My Face

Description

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're unsure if it will suit your face. We've all had that one haircut that we thought would look amazing, but instead, it left us feeling disappointed and self-conscious. This is why it's important to determine if a hairstyle suits your face before making any drastic changes to your hair.

How to Determine If a Hairstyle Suits Your Face

The first step in determining whether a hairstyle suits your face is to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Once you've identified your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features.

Hairstyles for Oval Faces

Oval faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape, as most hairstyles will suit this shape. However, if you have an oval face, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles that frame your face, such as bobs or long layers.

Hairstyles for Round Faces

Round faces are characterized by their full cheeks and rounded chin. If you have a round face, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as high ponytails or updos.

Hairstyles for Square Faces

Square faces are characterized by their strong jawline and forehead. If you have a square face, it's best to avoid hairstyles that accentuate your jawline, such as bobs that end at the jawline. Instead, opt for hairstyles that soften your features, such as long layers or soft waves.

Hairstyles for Heart Faces

Heart faces are characterized by their wide forehead and narrow chin. If you have a heart-shaped face, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead appear even wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the bottom of your head, such as chin-length bobs or layered hairstyles.

Hairstyles for Diamond Faces

Diamond faces are characterized by their narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. If you have a diamond face, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheekbones, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head or the bottom of your head, such as pixie cuts or long layers.

Hairstyles for Triangle Faces

Triangle faces are characterized by their narrow forehead and wide jawline. If you have a triangle face, it's best to avoid hairstyles that accentuate your jawline, such as bobs that end at the jawline. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as high ponytails or updos.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape and the hairstyles that will suit your features, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Look for inspiration: Browse through magazines or online to find hairstyles that you like.
  2. Consider your lifestyle: Choose a hairstyle that will suit your lifestyle and the amount of time you're willing to spend on your hair each day.
  3. Consult with a stylist: Your hairstylist can help you determine if a hairstyle will suit your face and give you advice on how to maintain your new look.
  4. Try it out: If you're unsure about a hairstyle, try it out with a wig or by using a virtual hairstyle app.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture: Some hairstyles will work better with certain hair textures than others.
  • Avoid drastic changes: If you're unsure about a new hairstyle, it's best to make small changes gradually.
  • Ask for recommendations: Your hairstylist can recommend hairstyles that will suit your face and features.
  • Consider your hair color: Certain hairstyles will work better with certain hair colors.

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can pull off a short hairstyle if you have a round face. However, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a square face. However, it's best to avoid straight-across bangs, as this can accentuate your strong jawline.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oval face?

A: If you have an oval face, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge, but by following these tips and guidelines, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that suits your face and enhances your features.
