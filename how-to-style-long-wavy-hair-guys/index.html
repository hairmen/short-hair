How To Style Long Wavy Hair Guys Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long wavy hair guys have been in trend for quite some time now. And if you're one of them, then you're in luck because we have some amazing tips and tricks for you to style your hair. From beachy waves to sleek and straight hair, we've got you covered. Read on to find out more.

How to

Before we dive into different hairstyles, let's first talk about some general tips and tricks to help you style your long wavy hair:

1. Use the right products

Using the right hair products is crucial to achieving the perfect hairstyle. For long wavy hair, we recommend using a good quality shampoo and conditioner that will help keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

2. Invest in a good quality hairbrush

Long wavy hair can easily get tangled, so investing in a good quality hairbrush is essential. Use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a small comb or brush, as it can lead to breakage and damage your hair.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it dry and frizzy. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like flat iron or hair dryer. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

4. Get regular trims

Long wavy hair can get split ends and become dry and damaged. Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. We recommend getting a trim every six to eight weeks.

Step by Step

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular hairstyle for long wavy hair guys. Here's how you can achieve this look:

Step 1:

Start with clean and dry hair. Apply a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunch it with your hands.

Step 2:

Divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around a curling wand. Hold it for a few seconds and release. Repeat this step until you've curled all your hair.

Step 3:

Run your fingers through your hair to break the curls and create a tousled look. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

Sleek and Straight

If you're looking for a sleek and straight look, here's how you can achieve it:

Step 1:

Start with clean and dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

Step 2:

Divide your hair into sections and run a flat iron over each section. Make sure to move the flat iron slowly and smoothly to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3:

Apply a small amount of hair serum to your hair to add shine and smoothness. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long wavy hair:

1. Experiment with different hairstyles

Don't be afraid to try different hairstyles and see what works best for you. You can also mix and match different hairstyles to create a unique look.

2. Use hair accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scrunchies can add a pop of color and style to your hair. Experiment with different accessories and see what works best for you.

3. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair every two to three days.

FAQs

1. Can I style my long wavy hair without heat?

Yes, you can. You can achieve beachy waves by applying a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunching it with your hands. You can also braid your hair overnight to create natural waves.

2. How often should I trim my long wavy hair?

We recommend getting a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Can I use hair gel to style my long wavy hair?

Yes, you can. Hair gel can help keep your hair in place and add some shine. However, make sure to use a small amount, as too much can make your hair look greasy.

Hair Care

Long wavy hair requires some extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Deep condition your hair

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep it soft and healthy. Apply a hair mask to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

Styling long wavy hair guys can be fun and exciting. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve different hairstyles and keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
