Guys Short Hair Fade A Trendy Hairstyle For Men

Written by Aprilia Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, the guys short hair fade is an excellent choice. This hairstyle has been a popular trend for men for many years, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about the guys short hair fade, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get a Guys Short Hair Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Guys Short Hair Fade
  • Tips for Styling a Guys Short Hair Fade
  • Frequently Asked Questions About Guys Short Hair Fades
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining a Guys Short Hair Fade

How to Get a Guys Short Hair Fade

The guys short hair fade is achieved by gradually cutting the hair shorter on the sides and back, while leaving more length on the top. This creates a tapered, blended look that is both modern and classic. To get a guys short hair fade, you'll need to visit a professional barber or hair stylist who has experience with this type of haircut.

When you go to your appointment, be sure to bring a photo of the style you want, as well as any other preferences you may have. Your barber or stylist will work with you to determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a Guys Short Hair Fade

1. Start by cutting the sides and back of the hair with a clipper guard that is one size longer than the desired length.

2. Use a smaller clipper guard to trim the hair around the edges and create a clean line.

3. Blend the hair on the sides and back with scissors, gradually cutting shorter towards the bottom.

4. Cut the hair on top to the desired length, leaving more length towards the front.

5. Use scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Tips for Styling a Guys Short Hair Fade

The guys short hair fade is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. Here are a few tips for achieving different looks:

  • To create a classic, polished look, use a comb and pomade to slick the hair back.
  • For a messy, textured look, use a matte styling product and work the hair with your fingers.
  • If you want a more dramatic look, consider adding a hard part or a shaved design to the side of your head.

Frequently Asked Questions About Guys Short Hair Fades

Q: How often do I need to get a guys short hair fade?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, a guys short hair fade should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking sharp.

Q: Can I get a guys short hair fade if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! The guys short hair fade works well with all hair types, including curly hair. Your barber or stylist can help you determine the best length and style for your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining a Guys Short Hair Fade

To keep your guys short hair fade looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a styling product that is appropriate for your hair type and desired look.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim and touch-up.

Overall, the guys short hair fade is a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that looks great on men of all ages and hair types. Whether you prefer a classic or modern look, this haircut is sure to impress.
