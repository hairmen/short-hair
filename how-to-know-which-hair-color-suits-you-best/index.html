How To Know Which Hair Color Suits You Best

Written by Eveline Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color? Do you want to change it up but don't know which color will suit you best? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but with a few tips and tricks, you can find the perfect shade for your skin tone, eye color, and personality. In this article, we'll show you how to know which hair color suits you best.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are two main types of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones have a yellow or golden undertone, while cool skin tones have a pink or blue undertone. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to hold a white piece of paper up to your face. If your skin looks yellowish, you have a warm skin tone. If your skin looks pinkish, you have a cool skin tone.

Choosing the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone

Once you've determined your skin tone, it's time to choose a hair color that complements it. If you have a warm skin tone, opt for hair colors with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, honey brown, or copper. If you have a cool skin tone, opt for hair colors with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy.

If you're unsure which hair color to choose, consider consulting a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a color that will complement your skin tone and look natural.

Considering Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, consider hair colors with warm undertones, such as golden blonde or honey brown. If you have brown eyes, consider hair colors with cool undertones, such as ash brown or black.

Matching Your Personality

Your personality can also influence the hair color you choose. If you're outgoing and adventurous, consider bold hair colors, such as purple or pink. If you're more reserved, consider natural-looking hair colors, such as blonde or brown.

Tips for Trying a New Hair Color

Trying a new hair color can be nerve-wracking, but there are a few tips you can follow to make the process smoother. First, consider trying a semi-permanent hair color before committing to a permanent one. This will give you a chance to see how the color looks on you without making a long-term commitment.

Second, consider using a color-depositing shampoo or conditioner to maintain your new hair color. This will help keep your color vibrant and prevent it from fading.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for sensitive skin. Look for products that are free of harsh chemicals and fragrances.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This will depend on the type of hair dye you're using and how quickly your hair grows. As a general rule, it's a good idea to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Color

Once you've chosen a new hair color, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can maintain your new hair color and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
