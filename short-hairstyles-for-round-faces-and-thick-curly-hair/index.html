Short Hairstyles For Round Faces And Thick Curly Hair

Written by Frank Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face and thick curly hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss some of the best short hairstyles for round faces and thick curly hair. We'll provide you with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Short hairstyles for round faces and thick curly hair are designed to work with the natural texture of your hair. They are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and trendy. These hairstyles are great for showcasing your facial features and can help to slim down the appearance of a round face.

How To

When it comes to short hairstyles for round faces and thick curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to choose a style that works with your natural hair texture. Second, you'll want to consider your face shape and choose a style that complements your features. Third, you'll want to choose a style that is easy to maintain and style on a daily basis.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the best short hairstyles for round faces and thick curly hair:

1. Pixie Cut

To achieve a pixie cut, start by cutting your hair short all over. Use a razor to create texture and movement. Style your hair with a pomade or wax to add definition and hold.

2. Bob Cut

To achieve a bob cut, cut your hair to just below your jawline. Use a razor to create texture and movement. Style your hair with a curling iron or wand to add volume and bounce.

3. Curly Shag

To achieve a curly shag, cut your hair to shoulder length. Use a razor to create layers and texture. Style your hair with a curling iron or wand to add volume and definition.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyles for round faces and thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and slimness. Pixie cuts, bobs, and curly shags are all great options.

What is the best way to style thick curly hair?

The best way to style thick curly hair is to use a curling iron or wand to define your curls. Use a styling product to add definition and hold.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hairstyles for round faces and thick curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than twice a week to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and thick curly hair. Remember to choose a style that works with your natural hair texture and complements your features. And most importantly, have fun with your hair and experiment with different styles!
