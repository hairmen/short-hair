How Long Should I Keep My Hair In Braids

Written by Oliver Oct 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Braids are one of the most popular protective hairstyles for natural hair. They not only look stylish but also keep your hair protected from environmental factors and breakage. However, one of the most common questions that people have when it comes to braids is, how long should I keep my hair in braids? In this article, we will explore the answer to this question and provide you with tips on how to care for your hair while it’s in braids.

Table of Contents

How Long Should I Keep My Hair in Braids?

The answer to this question varies depending on the person and the type of braids that they have. In general, it is recommended that you keep your hair in braids for no longer than 8 weeks. This is because leaving your hair in braids for too long can cause damage, including breakage and hair loss. Additionally, leaving your hair in braids for too long can cause your scalp to become dry and itchy, which can lead to dandruff and other scalp conditions.

It is important to note that if you have smaller braids, you may need to take them out sooner than if you have larger braids. This is because smaller braids can put more tension on your hair and scalp, which can lead to damage if they are left in for too long.

How to Braid Your Hair

If you are new to braiding your hair, don’t worry! It’s easier than you think. Here are the basic steps to braiding your hair:

Step-by-Step Guide to Braiding Your Hair

  1. Start by dividing your hair into three sections.
  2. Take the right section and cross it over the middle section.
  3. Take the left section and cross it over the middle section.
  4. Continue crossing the right and left sections over the middle section until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Secure the end of your braid with a hair tie or elastic.

Once you have mastered the basic braid, you can experiment with different styles, such as fishtail braids, Dutch braids, and French braids.

Tips for Braiding Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when braiding your hair:

  • Make sure your hair is detangled before you start braiding.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized while it’s in braids.
  • Don’t braid your hair too tightly. This can cause tension on your scalp and lead to breakage.
  • Take breaks between braiding sessions to allow your scalp to rest.

FAQs about Braiding Your Hair

Q: Will braiding my hair make it grow faster?

A: Braiding your hair will not make it grow faster. However, it can help protect your hair from breakage, which can make it appear longer over time.

Q: Can I wash my hair while it’s in braids?

A: Yes, you can wash your hair while it’s in braids. However, you should be gentle when washing your hair to avoid causing damage.

Q: How long should I wait before braiding my hair again?

A: It’s recommended that you wait at least two weeks before braiding your hair again to give your scalp time to rest.

Hair Care Tips for Braids

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair while it’s in braids:

  • Keep your scalp moisturized by using a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can cause buildup on your scalp.
  • Take breaks between braiding sessions to allow your scalp to rest.
  • Don’t leave your braids in for too long.

In conclusion, braids are a great protective hairstyle for natural hair, but it’s important to take care of your hair while it’s in braids and not leave them in for too long. By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great!
