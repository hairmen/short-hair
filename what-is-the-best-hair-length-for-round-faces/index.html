What Is The Best Hair Length For Round Faces

Written by Aprilia Jun 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. Many people believe that short hair is the only option for round faces, but that isn't necessarily true. In this article, we'll explore what is the best hair length for round faces and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect style.

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved features and a lack of strong angles. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and adds angles to your face. The right hair length can help balance your face shape and draw attention to your eyes, cheekbones, and jawline.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best hair length for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are a few different methods you can use to do this:

  • Look in a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner pencil. Compare the shape to the following descriptions.
  • Measure your face with a tape measure. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all roughly the same width, you have a round face.
  • Take a selfie and use an app or website that can analyze your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hair Length for Round Faces

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the best hair length for round faces:

  1. Consider your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a longer length to avoid adding extra volume to your face. If you have fine hair, a shorter length can help create the illusion of fullness.
  2. Focus on length. The best hair length for round faces is typically shoulder-length or longer. This length can help elongate your face and create the illusion of angles.
  3. Avoid blunt cuts. Blunt cuts can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for layers or choppy ends to add texture and movement to your hair.
  4. Consider bangs. Bangs can help balance your face shape and draw attention to your eyes. Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs can be particularly flattering for round faces.
  5. Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try different hairstyles until you find the one that works best for you. You may find that a layered bob or a long, loose wave is the perfect fit.

Tips for Styling Round Face Shapes

Once you've found the perfect hair length for your face shape, there are a few tips you can use to style your hair for maximum impact:

  • Avoid center parts. Center parts can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a side part to create the illusion of length.
  • Add volume at the crown. Adding volume at the crown can help create the illusion of height and length.
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add waves or curls. These styles can help create the illusion of angles and length.
  • Avoid tight, slicked-back styles. These styles can emphasize your round face shape.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights. Adding color to your hair can help create the illusion of depth and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair length for round faces:

  • Q: Can I have short hair if I have a round face?
    A: Yes, you can have short hair if you have a round face. However, you may want to opt for a longer pixie cut or a bob with choppy layers to create the illusion of length.
  • Q: What if I want a bob?
    A: If you want a bob, opt for a longer length that falls just below your chin. This length can help elongate your face and create the illusion of angles.
  • Q: Can I have bangs?
    A: Yes, you can have bangs if you have a round face. Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs can be particularly flattering for round faces.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Once you've found the perfect hair length for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care for round faces:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your style looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you can choose the best hair length for round faces and create a style that flatters your features and highlights your best assets.
