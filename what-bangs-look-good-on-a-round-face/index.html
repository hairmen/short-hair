What Bangs Look Good On A Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but worry that they might not look good on your round face? Fear not, as we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll discuss what bangs look good on a round face and provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

Description

Bangs are a great way to change up your hairstyle and add a fresh new look to your overall appearance. However, not all bangs are created equal, and what looks good on one face shape might not necessarily look good on another. If you have a round face, you need to be extra careful about the type of bangs you choose, as they can either enhance or detract from your features.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for a Round Face

Before you jump right in and get bangs, you need to consider a few factors:

1. Face Shape

As we've mentioned, round faces require a specific type of bangs. Generally speaking, you want to go for bangs that elongate your face and draw attention away from the width. Avoid blunt bangs and opt for layered or side-swept bangs instead.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in determining what type of bangs will look best on you. If you have fine hair, you might want to go for wispy, feathered bangs that add volume and texture. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you can pull off heavier bangs that make a statement.

3. Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing your bangs. If you're always on the go and don't have time to style your hair every day, you might want to opt for low-maintenance bangs that don't require a lot of upkeep.

Step by Step Guide: How to Determine the Best Bangs for Your Round Face

Follow these steps to determine the best bangs for your round face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar, you have a round face.

Step 2: Research Different Types of Bangs

Research different types of bangs that are suitable for round faces, such as layered, side-swept, or curtain bangs.

Step 3: Consult with Your Stylist

Consult with your stylist to determine which type of bangs will work best for your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Take the Plunge

Finally, take the plunge and get your new bangs! Make sure to follow your stylist's advice on how to style and maintain your new 'do.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you've got your new bangs, you'll want to make sure they stay looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your bangs:

1. Invest in a Good Quality Hair Dryer

A good quality hair dryer will make styling your bangs a breeze. Look for a hair dryer with a concentrator nozzle to help you achieve a sleek, polished look.

2. Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can help absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking fresh in between washes. Just spray a little bit at your roots and comb through.

3. Trim Regularly

To keep your bangs looking neat and tidy, make sure to trim them regularly. You can either do it yourself or visit your stylist for a touch-up.

FAQs

Q: Can I still get bangs if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! You just need to choose the right type of bangs that flatter your face shape.

Q: What type of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid blunt or straight-across bangs, as they can make your face look even rounder.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair and bangs looking healthy:

1. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. This will help keep your hair and scalp healthy.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners to prevent heat damage.

3. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

In conclusion, there are many different types of bangs that look good on a round face. By following our tips and tricks, you can find the perfect bangs for your face shape and hair texture. Remember to take good care of your hair and bangs to keep them looking healthy and fresh!
