How To Check Which Hair Colour Suits My Face Online

Written by Oliver Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your same old hair colour? Do you want to experiment with a new shade but not sure which one would suit your face? Worry not, you can now check which hair colour suits your face online! Read on to find out how.

Description

Choosing the right hair colour is not just about following the latest trends, but it also depends on your skin tone, eye colour, and facial features. With the help of online tools, you can now determine which hair colour would look best on you without any hassle.

How to Check Which Hair Colour Suits My Face Online

Follow these simple steps to check which hair colour suits your face:

Step 1: Find a Reliable Online Hair Colour Tool

The first step is to find a reliable online hair colour tool that can help you visualize different hair colours on your face. You can search for such tools on Google or check out popular beauty websites.

Step 2: Upload Your Photo

Once you have found a suitable tool, upload your photo on the website. Make sure to choose a clear photo with good lighting and without any filters.

Step 3: Choose Different Hair Colours

Now comes the fun part! Choose different hair colours from the tool and see how they would look on your face. You can experiment with different shades of brown, blonde, red, or even funky colours like purple or pink.

Step 4: Save Your Favourite Looks

Save your favourite looks and compare them side by side to see which one suits you best. You can also share your photos with your friends and family to get their opinion.

Tips

While choosing a hair colour, keep these tips in mind:

  • Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone.
  • Consider your natural hair colour and eye colour.
  • Go for a shade that matches your personality and lifestyle.
  • Consult a professional hairstylist before making any drastic changes.

FAQ

1. Can I use these tools for free?

Yes, most online hair colour tools are free to use.

2. Are these tools accurate?

While these tools can give you a general idea of how a hair colour would look on you, they may not be 100% accurate. The best way to determine which hair colour suits you is to consult a professional hairstylist.

3. Can I use these tools on my phone?

Yes, most online hair colour tools are compatible with smartphones.

Hair Care

After choosing a new hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shine and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for coloured hair.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to check which hair colour suits your face online, it's time to experiment with different shades and find your perfect match. Happy colouring!
