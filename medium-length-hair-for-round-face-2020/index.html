Medium Length Hair For Round Face 2020 A Complete Guide

Description

Medium length hair is perfect for round faces because it adds length and definition to the face. It also provides a lot of styling options, including waves, curls, layers, and bangs. In 2020, the trend is all about natural and effortless hairstyles that require minimal maintenance.

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it is essential to find one that elongates the face, adds height, and creates angles. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face and accentuate the roundness.

How to Style Medium Length Hair for Round Face 2020

The first step in styling medium length hair for a round face is to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. Some of the best haircuts for round faces include long bobs, layered cuts, and asymmetrical cuts. These hairstyles create angles, add height, and elongate the face.

Once you have the right haircut, you can start experimenting with different styles. For a natural and effortless look, you can add loose waves or curls to your hair using a curling wand or iron. You can also create a messy bun or a half-up, half-down hairstyle using bobby pins.

If you prefer a sleeker look, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron or blow dryer. You can also add a side part to create the illusion of an elongated face. Adding bangs is also an excellent way to frame the face and create angles.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style medium length hair for round face:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and detangle it using a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  4. Choose your preferred styling tool, such as a curling wand, flat iron, or blow dryer.
  5. Section your hair and start styling from the bottom layers, working your way up.
  6. Once you have styled all the sections, apply a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle for Round Face

Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle for round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt cuts and bobs that end at the jawline.
  • Choose hairstyles that create angles and add height, such as long bobs, layered cuts, and asymmetrical cuts.
  • Adding bangs is an excellent way to create angles and frame the face.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as loose waves, curls, and messy buns.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hair for round face:

1. Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face?

A pixie cut may not be the best hairstyle for a round face because it accentuates the roundness of the face. However, if you want to try a pixie cut, you can opt for a longer version with side-swept bangs.

2. Can I wear a middle part with medium length hair?

A middle part may not be the best option for a round face because it creates symmetry and accentuates the roundness of the face. Instead, opt for a side part to create the illusion of an elongated face.

3. How often should I trim my medium length hair?

It is recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair with a suitable shampoo and conditioner that matches your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your medium length hair to flatter your round face and enhance your features. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds angles, creates height, and elongates the face. Experiment with different styles and consult with a hairstylist to find the best hairstyle for you. Happy styling!
