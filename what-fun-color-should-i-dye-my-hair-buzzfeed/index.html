What Fun Color Should I Dye My Hair Buzzfeed

Written by Robby Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hair game, you're not alone. Many people are just as curious about what fun color they should dye their hair as you are. Luckily, Buzzfeed has some great ideas to help you decide. From bold and bright to subtle and pastel, there's a color out there that's perfect for you. So let's dive in and find out what fun color you should dye your hair Buzzfeed style!

Description

Are you tired of the same old hair color? Do you want something that will make you stand out from the crowd? Then look no further than Buzzfeed's guide to fun hair colors. In this article, we'll explore some of the most popular and trendy colors, as well as some tips for dyeing your hair at home. Whether you're looking for a bold and daring look or something more subtle and sweet, we've got you covered.

How To

Before you jump into dyeing your hair, it's important to do some research and preparation. Here are some steps you should follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Color

The first step is to decide what color you want. Buzzfeed has a great quiz that can help you determine what color would look best on you. Some popular options include:

  • Pink
  • Purple
  • Blue
  • Green
  • Red
  • Yellow

Once you've decided on a color, it's time to choose a shade. Do you want a bright and bold color, or something more pastel and subdued? Consider your skin tone and natural hair color when making your decision.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start dyeing your hair, you'll need to gather some supplies. This includes:

  • Hair dye
  • Bleach (if you're going from dark to light)
  • Gloves
  • Old t-shirt
  • Towels
  • Vaseline (to protect your skin from the dye)

Step 3: Prep Your Hair

Before you start dyeing your hair, you'll need to prep it. This includes:

  • Washing your hair
  • Drying it completely
  • Applying Vaseline around your hairline and ears to protect your skin

Step 4: Dye Your Hair

Now it's time to dye your hair! Follow the instructions on the box carefully, and make sure to wear your gloves. Apply the dye evenly, making sure to saturate your hair completely. Leave it on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out thoroughly.

Step 5: Care for Your Hair

After you've dyed your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes:

  • Using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding hot showers and styling tools
  • Protecting your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider going to a professional if you're not comfortable dyeing your hair at home
  • Do a patch test before dyeing your hair to make sure you're not allergic to the dye
  • Use a toner to neutralize any brassy tones in your hair
  • Be prepared for some upkeep – fun colors may fade faster than traditional colors

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about dyeing your hair a fun color:

  • Is it safe to dye your hair at home?
  • What's the best way to maintain a fun hair color?
  • Will dyeing my hair damage it?
  • Can I dye my hair a fun color if I have dark hair?
  • How long will my fun color last?

Hair Care

When it comes to dyeing your hair a fun color, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoiding hot styling tools, and protecting your hair from the sun and chlorine. You should also get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Dyeing your hair a fun color can be a great way to express your personality and stand out from the crowd. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock your new look with confidence!
