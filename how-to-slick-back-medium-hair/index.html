How To Slick Back Medium Hair

Are you looking for a sleek and sophisticated hairstyle that will make you look sharp and put together? Slicking back your medium length hair might be the perfect solution for you. In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to slick back medium hair, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Slick Back Medium Hair

Slicking back medium hair is a classic hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of settings, from the office to a night out. Here's how to achieve the look:

What You'll Need:

  • Comb or brush
  • Hair gel, pomade, or wax

Step by Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to slick back your medium length hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair will not hold the style, so make sure it's completely dry before you begin.
  2. Use a comb or brush to part your hair on one side, or slick it straight back if you prefer.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair gel, pomade, or wax to your fingertips and rub them together to warm up the product.
  4. Starting at the front of your hairline, use your fingertips to smooth the product back through your hair. Make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Use a comb to slick your hair back even further, starting from the front and working your way back. Be sure to comb the sides of your hair as well.
  6. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your fingertips, using a small amount of product if necessary.
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Medium Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel, pomade, or wax that is designed for your hair type. For example, if you have thin or fine hair, choose a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down.
  • Don't apply too much product. A little goes a long way, and too much can make your hair look greasy or clumpy.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that works best for your face shape and hair type.
  • If you have stubborn hair that won't stay in place, try blow-drying it in the direction you want it to go before applying product.
  • Keep a comb or brush handy throughout the day to touch up your hair if it starts to lose its shape.

FAQs

Is slicking back medium hair suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, slicking back medium hair can work for all face shapes. However, you may want to experiment with different partings to find the one that flatters your features the most.

Can I slick back my medium hair without using products?

It's possible to slick back your hair without using products, but it may not hold the style as well. If you have naturally oily hair, you may be able to get away with just using a comb or brush to smooth your hair back.

How often should I wash my hair if I'm slicking it back?

It's important to keep your hair clean and free from product buildup if you're slicking it back regularly. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed to keep it looking and feeling fresh.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hairstyles

When you're slicking back your medium length hair, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain a healthy scalp. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for gentle, sulfate-free formulas instead.

Now that you know how to slick back medium hair, you can rock this classic hairstyle with confidence. With a little practice and the right products, you'll be able to achieve a sleek and sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion.
