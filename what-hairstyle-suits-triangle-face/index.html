What Hairstyle Suits Triangle Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a triangle face, you're in luck as there are a variety of hairstyles that can complement your features. In this post, we'll be discussing the different types of hairstyles that would suit your face shape and provide tips on how to maintain your hairstyle.

Description: What is a Triangle Face?

A triangle face is characterized by a wide forehead and narrow jawline. It's the opposite of a heart-shaped face, which has a narrow forehead and a wider jawline. If you have a triangle face, you might also have a pointed chin, which can make your face look sharper.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

Determining your face shape is important when choosing a hairstyle that suits you. To determine your face shape, you can follow these simple steps:

  1. Take a photo of your face facing forward.
  2. Use a ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Tips:

Make sure you take the photo in good lighting and keep your hair away from your face to get an accurate measurement.

Hairstyles for Triangle Faces

Here are some hairstyles that would suit a triangle face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can soften your jawline and balance out your face shape. This hairstyle works best with straight or wavy hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can draw attention away from your forehead and add volume to your hair. This hairstyle works best with medium to long hair.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut can help balance your face shape and make your jawline appear narrower. This hairstyle works best with straight or wavy hair.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can help highlight your cheekbones and draw attention away from your forehead. This hairstyle works best with straight or slightly wavy hair.

5. Waves

Waves can add volume to your hair and soften your jawline. This hairstyle works best with medium to long hair.

Step by Step: How to Maintain Your Hairstyle?

Here are some tips on how to maintain your hairstyle:

  1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  2. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  3. Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  4. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  5. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Tips:

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best products and maintenance routine for your hairstyle.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have a triangle face?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair regardless of your face shape. However, make sure to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a triangle face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail regardless of your face shape. However, try to avoid pulling your hair too tight, as it can accentuate your jawline.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  2. Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  3. Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  4. Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  5. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Tips:

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hair care routine for your hair type.

In conclusion, if you have a triangle face, there are plenty of hairstyles that can complement your features. From long layers to a pixie cut, choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and style. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful look.
