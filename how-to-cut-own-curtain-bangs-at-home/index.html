How To Cut Own Curtain Bangs At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that can add a touch of glam to your look? Curtain bangs are the perfect choice! They are trendy, chic, and can frame your face beautifully. But, going to the salon can be expensive, and it can be hard to find a stylist who can give you the perfect cut. The good news is that you can cut your own curtain bangs at home with the right tools and techniques! In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of cutting your own curtain bangs at home.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that is parted in the middle and swept to the sides, framing your face. They are a popular choice for those who want to try a new look without committing to a full fringe. Curtain bangs can work well for any hair length and texture, and they are easy to style.

How to Cut Own Curtain Bangs at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your own curtain bangs, you need to gather the right tools. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip
  • Hair ties

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Now that you have your tools, the first thing you need to do is to section your hair. Use a comb to part your hair down the middle, from your forehead to the back of your head. Tie up the rest of your hair with a hair tie or clip, so it doesn't get in the way when you start cutting.

Step 3: Determine the Length of Your Bangs

Before you start cutting, you need to determine the length of your bangs. Take a small section of hair from the front of your hairline and hold it straight down. Use your fingers to measure the desired length of your bangs. Keep in mind that the hair will look shorter when it dries, so it's better to start with a longer length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can be difficult to fix if you make a mistake. Cut slowly and carefully, checking the length as you go.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Once you have cut the middle section of your bangs, it's time to cut the sides. Take a small section of hair from one side and comb it straight down. Use the middle section as a guide and cut the side section at an angle. Repeat the same process on the other side.

Step 6: Check the Length

Once you have finished cutting your bangs, check the length. If they are too long, you can trim them a little bit more. If they are too short, you can't add hair back, so be very careful.

Step 7: Style Your Curtain Bangs

Now that you have cut your curtain bangs, it's time to style them. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your bangs. Blow-dry them while using the brush to create a slight curve. You can also use a straightener or curling iron to add more texture or curl to your bangs.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and cut slowly to avoid any mistakes
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting it
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can be difficult to fix if you make a mistake
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Check the length frequently as you cut to avoid any unevenness

FAQs

Can I cut my own curtain bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs if you have curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to bounce up when it dries, so it's better to start with a longer length than you want.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and neat.

How do I maintain my curtain bangs?

To maintain your curtain bangs, you should wash them regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style them, and avoid using too much heat or styling products, which can damage your hair.

Hair Care

Cutting your own curtain bangs at home can be easy and fun, as long as you have the right tools and techniques. Remember to start with a longer length than you want and cut slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes. Once you have cut your bangs, use a blow dryer and a round brush to style them. Don't forget to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and neat.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a beautiful and trendy hairstyle that will enhance your look and add a touch of glam to your everyday life!
