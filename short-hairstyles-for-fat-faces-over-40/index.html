Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces Over 40 A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are over 40 and have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task. Fortunately, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and make you look younger and more stylish. In this article, we will share some of the best short hairstyles for fat faces over 40, as well as tips on how to maintain and care for your hair.

Description

Short hairstyles are ideal for fat faces as they help to create an illusion of a slimmer face. The key to finding the right short hairstyle for your face shape is to choose a style that adds height and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short. This will help to balance out your face shape and make it look more oval.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Fat Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for your fat face, there are several factors to consider, including:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair type and texture
  • Your lifestyle and personality

If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this will make your face look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of your head, such as pixie cuts, bobs, and layered styles.

If you have curly or wavy hair, you can embrace your natural texture by opting for a layered bob or a shaggy pixie cut. On the other hand, if you have straight hair, you can add texture and movement to your hair by getting a chin-length bob or a layered pixie cut.

When choosing a short hairstyle, it is important to consider your lifestyle and personality. If you are a busy mom or professional, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be styled quickly and easily. On the other hand, if you love experimenting with different hairstyles and have more time to spend on your hair, you can opt for a more complex style that requires more maintenance.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Fat Faces over 40

Styling short hair can be intimidating, especially if you are used to longer hairstyles. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish looks that flatter your face shape. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type and texture.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to add volume and height to the top of your head.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair.
  5. Finish by applying a styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add definition and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim and to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools, such as a flat iron, curling iron, and blow dryer, to create a variety of looks.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for fat faces over 40:

  • What is the best short hairstyle for a fat face over 40? The best short hairstyle for a fat face over 40 is one that adds height and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short. Pixie cuts, bobs, and layered styles are all great options.
  • Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle? Yes, you can wear bangs with a short hairstyle. However, it is important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or wispy bangs are great options for a round face.
  • How can I add volume to my short hairstyle? You can add volume to your short hairstyle by blow-drying your hair with a round brush, using a volumizing mousse or spray, or backcombing your hair at the roots.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than longer hair. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming too brittle.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With the right short hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best at any age. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob, or layered style, remember to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. Happy styling!
