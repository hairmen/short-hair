How To Keep Little Hairs From Sticking Up

Written by William Sep 29, 2023 · 3 min read

  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

If you have relaxed hair, you know that those little hairs sticking up can be annoying. These little hairs, also known as flyaways, can make your hair look messy and unpolished. But don't worry, there are ways to keep those little hairs from sticking up and ruining your hairstyle.

How To

There are a few different methods you can use to keep those little hairs from sticking up:

  • Using hairspray
  • Using a hair pomade or wax
  • Using a leave-in conditioner
  • Using a toothbrush or mascara wand

Step by Step

Using Hairspray

Step 1: Spray a small amount of hairspray onto a toothbrush or a clean mascara wand.

Step 2: Gently brush the hairspray onto your little hairs, smoothing them down.

Step 3: Repeat as necessary until all of your little hairs are smoothed down.

Using a Hair Pomade or Wax

Step 1: Take a small amount of hair pomade or wax and rub it between your palms to warm it up.

Step 2: Gently apply the pomade or wax to your little hairs, smoothing them down.

Step 3: Repeat as necessary until all of your little hairs are smoothed down.

Using a Leave-In Conditioner

Step 1: Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your fingertips.

Step 2: Gently apply the leave-in conditioner to your little hairs, smoothing them down.

Step 3: Repeat as necessary until all of your little hairs are smoothed down.

Using a Toothbrush or Mascara Wand

Step 1: Spray a small amount of hairspray onto a toothbrush or clean mascara wand.

Step 2: Gently brush the toothbrush or mascara wand over your little hairs, smoothing them down.

Step 3: Repeat as necessary until all of your little hairs are smoothed down.

Tips

  • Use a light hand when applying products to your little hairs to avoid weighing them down.
  • Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or oily.
  • Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause your little hairs to stick up again.

FAQ

Q: What causes little hairs to stick up?

A: Little hairs can stick up for a variety of reasons, including humidity, static, and damaged hair.

Q: Can I use hairspray on my relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray on your relaxed hair, but be sure to use a light hand and avoid using too much product.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained can help prevent little hairs from sticking up. Be sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use products that are designed for your hair type.

By following these tips and techniques, you can keep those little hairs from sticking up and maintain a polished, put-together look.
