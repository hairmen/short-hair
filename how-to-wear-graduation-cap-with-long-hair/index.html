How To Wear Graduation Cap With Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Graduation day is a special moment for every student. After years of hard work, you're finally receiving your diploma and moving on to the next stage of your life. However, one of the biggest challenges for graduates with long hair is how to wear their graduation cap without ruining their hairstyle. In this blog post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to wear a graduation cap with long hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Graduation Cap with Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Wear a Graduation Cap with Long Hair

First things first, it's important to understand the structure of a graduation cap. The cap consists of a square board with a tassel attached to the center. The board is placed on top of the head, while the tassel hangs down the back.

When it comes to wearing a graduation cap with long hair, there are two options:

  1. Wear your hair down and tuck it behind your ears
  2. Wear your hair in a low bun or ponytail

Step-by-Step Guide

Option 1: Wear Your Hair Down

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle or on the side.
  2. Brush your hair and apply a hair serum to smooth down any flyaways.
  3. Place the graduation cap on your head, making sure it's centered.
  4. Tuck your hair behind your ears and adjust the cap as needed.
  5. Secure the cap with bobby pins if necessary.
  6. Attach the tassel to the button on the top of the cap.

Option 2: Wear Your Hair in a Low Bun or Ponytail

  1. Start by brushing your hair and pulling it back into a low bun or ponytail.
  2. Secure the bun or ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Place the graduation cap on your head, making sure it's centered.
  4. Tilt the cap slightly forward to accommodate the bun or ponytail.
  5. Secure the cap with bobby pins if necessary.
  6. Attach the tassel to the button on the top of the cap.

Tips for Wearing a Graduation Cap with Long Hair

  • Use bobby pins to secure the cap in place, especially if you're wearing your hair down.
  • Apply hairspray to your hair before putting on the cap to help it stay in place.
  • If you're wearing your hair in a bun or ponytail, make sure it's not too tight as this can cause headaches during the ceremony.
  • Try on your graduation cap before the ceremony to ensure it fits properly and to practice wearing it with your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear a graduation cap with braids?

A: Yes, you can wear a graduation cap with braids. Follow the same steps as you would for wearing your hair in a low bun or ponytail.

Q: Can I wear a graduation cap with a high bun?

A: While it's possible to wear a graduation cap with a high bun, it's not recommended as it can cause the cap to sit unevenly on your head. Stick to a low bun or ponytail instead.

Hair Care Tips for Graduation Day

Graduation day is a long day filled with photo opportunities and celebrations. Here are some hair care tips to keep your locks looking great throughout the day:

  • Wash your hair the day before the ceremony to ensure it's clean and fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Bring a travel-sized hairspray and hair serum with you to touch up your hair throughout the day.
  • Consider bringing a hairbrush or comb to fix any flyaways or tangles.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear your graduation cap with long hair confidently and proudly. Congratulations, graduate!
