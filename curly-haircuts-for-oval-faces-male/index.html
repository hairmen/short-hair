Curly Haircuts For Oval Faces Male

Written by Daniel Sep 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut and want to try something new? If you have an oval face and curly hair, you're in luck! There are plenty of curly haircuts that will compliment your features and enhance your natural curls. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect curly haircut for oval faces male.

Description

An oval face is considered to be the ideal face shape for men as it has well-balanced proportions. It's characterized by a forehead that's slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones. Curly hair, on the other hand, adds volume and texture to the hair and makes it look fuller. However, when it comes to choosing the right haircut, it's essential to consider the face shape and hair texture.

How to Choose the Right Curly Haircut?

The first step in choosing the right curly haircut for oval faces male is to identify the type of curls you have. Are they loose curls, ringlets, or kinky curls? Once you know the type of curls you have, you can choose a haircut that will enhance your natural curls and compliment your face shape.

Another factor to consider is the length of your hair. Shorter haircuts work well for oval faces as they highlight the cheekbones and jawline. However, if you prefer longer hair, you can opt for layered haircuts that add volume and texture to your curls.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Curly Haircut

Step 1: Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling your hair daily.

Step 2: Find a hairstylist who specializes in curly haircuts. It's essential to choose someone who understands the unique needs of curly hair and can guide you through the process of maintaining your curls.

Step 3: Schedule a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss your hair goals and preferences. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to help your hairstylist understand your vision.

Step 4: During the haircut, your hairstylist will cut your hair while it's dry to ensure that the length and shape are perfect for your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Haircut

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your curls while they're damp to enhance their definition and reduce frizz.

Tip 3: Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your curls. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

FAQ

What is the best curly haircut for oval faces?

The best curly haircut for oval faces is one that adds volume and texture to your hair. Shorter haircuts like the buzz cut or the curly fringe work well for oval faces as they highlight the cheekbones and jawline. Longer hairstyles like the curly quiff or the curly slick back can also work well for oval faces when paired with the right styling products.

Can I get a curly haircut if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can get a curly haircut even if you have straight hair. However, it's essential to consider the amount of maintenance required to style curly hair daily. If you're willing to spend time styling your hair, you can use curling products or a perm to achieve curly hair.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires extra care and attention to maintain its shape and texture. It's essential to use products that are designed for curly hair and to avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your curls. Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair and avoid using heat styling tools. With the right care, you can enjoy a healthy and vibrant head of curls!
