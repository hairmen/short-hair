How Should I Wear My Hair Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture, thickness, and color. This can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters our face shape, enhances our features, and fits our lifestyle. If you're wondering how should I wear my hair over 50, you're not alone.

Description

In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to wear your hair over 50. We'll cover different hairstyles that suit different face shapes, hair types, and personal preferences. We'll also discuss how to care for your hair to keep it healthy, shiny, and manageable. Whether you want to embrace your natural gray, try a new color, or experiment with a new cut or style, we've got you covered.

How to

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle over 50, there are a few factors to consider:

Face shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyles will look best on you. Some of the most common face shapes include:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart-shaped

Each of these face shapes has different features and proportions that can be accentuated or softened with the right hairstyle. For example:

  • Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height and volume on top, such as layered bobs, pixie cuts, or messy updos.
  • Square faces look best with hairstyles that soften the jawline and cheekbones, such as layered shags, wavy bobs, or side-swept bangs.
  • Oval faces are versatile and can pull off many different hairstyles, from short crops to long layers.
  • Heart-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that balance the forehead and chin, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, or chin-length bobs.

Hair type

Your hair type also plays a role in determining which hairstyles will suit you best. Some common hair types include:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Thick
  • Thin

Each of these hair types has different textures, densities, and styling needs. For example:

  • Straight hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to beachy waves or messy buns.
  • Wavy hair looks best with hairstyles that enhance its natural texture, such as layered cuts or loose curls.
  • Curly hair can be styled in many different ways, from tight coils to loose waves or braids.
  • Thick hair can handle many different hairstyles, from long layers to angled bobs or blunt cuts.
  • Thin hair can benefit from layered cuts, textured styles, or volumizing products.

Personal preference

Finally, your personal preference plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle you should wear over 50. Some women prefer low-maintenance hairstyles that require little styling or upkeep, while others prefer more elaborate styles that show off their creativity and individuality.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to create some popular hairstyles for women over 50:

Pixie cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the top and crown of your hair with clips.
  3. Using a #3 or #4 guard, trim the sides and back of your hair to the desired length.
  4. Blend the top and sides with scissors or clippers.
  5. Use a texturizing product to add volume and texture.

Bob cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the top and crown of your hair with clips.
  3. Using a razor or scissors, cut the hair to the desired length.
  4. Add layers and texture to the hair to create movement and volume.
  5. Style the hair with a round brush and blow dryer, or use a straightening iron for a sleek look.

Updo

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun or chignon and secure with bobby pins.
  4. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to the hair.
  5. Accessorize with a headband, scarf, or decorative pins.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle over 50:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Experiment with color to enhance your natural features and complement your skin tone.
  • Invest in quality hair products that nourish, protect, and style your hair.
  • Visit a professional stylist regularly to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing your hair over 50:

Q: Should I cut my hair short or keep it long?

A: It depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal preference. Short hair can be low-maintenance and flattering, while long hair can be versatile and feminine. Consider trying different lengths and styles to see what works best for you.

Q: Can I still dye my hair over 50?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair over 50. However, you may want to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural features. Consider consulting with a professional stylist to find the right shade and formula for your hair type and texture.

Q: How can I make thin hair look thicker?

A: You can make thin hair look thicker by using volumizing products, such as mousse, root lifter, or thickening spray. You can also try adding layers to your hair to create volume and movement. Consider a short, layered cut or a bob with angled layers to add depth and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful over 50:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes that can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support hair health, such as iron, zinc, biotin, and vitamins A and C.

We hope these tips and tricks have helped answer your question of how should I wear my hair over 50. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident, beautiful, and comfortable. Whether you prefer short, long, curly, or straight hair, there's a style out there that's perfect for you. Happy styling!
