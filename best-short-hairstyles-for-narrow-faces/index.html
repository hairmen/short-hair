Best Short Hairstyles For Narrow Faces

Written by Frank Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles and still not being able to find the perfect one to suit your narrow face? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best short hairstyles for narrow faces that are sure to complement your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

Narrow faces are characterized by a long and slender shape, with a pointed chin and a high forehead. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a narrow face is to add width and volume to the sides of the face, while keeping the hair off the forehead. Short hairstyles are a great way to achieve this look, as they create the illusion of fuller and wider cheeks, while also highlighting the cheekbones and jawline.

How To

The first step in choosing the right short hairstyle for a narrow face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline, and comparing them to the length of your face. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Step by Step

1. Start by parting your hair on the side, as this will create the illusion of wider cheeks and a shorter forehead.

2. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

3. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as these can make a narrow face appear even longer and thinner.

4. Use styling products such as mousse or hairspray to add texture and volume to your hair, and to keep it in place throughout the day.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for a narrow face, it is important to consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have thin or fine hair, opt for a layered cut that adds volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, choose a style that is easy to maintain and doesn't require too much styling.

Another tip is to experiment with different hair colors and highlights, as these can also add depth and dimension to a narrow face. Warm tones such as caramel or honey blonde can help to soften the features and create a more natural look.

FAQ

Q: What are some of the best short hairstyles for narrow faces?

A: Some of the best short hairstyles for narrow faces include layered bobs, pixie cuts with side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

Q: Can I still wear a short hairstyle if I have a narrow face?

A: Yes, absolutely! Short hairstyles can be incredibly flattering for narrow faces, as long as they are styled in a way that adds volume and dimension to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining a short hairstyle for a narrow face, it is important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner that will help to strengthen and protect your hair, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.

Regular trims are also essential for keeping your hair looking its best, as they can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. And don't forget to protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your narrow face, and feel confident and beautiful every day!
