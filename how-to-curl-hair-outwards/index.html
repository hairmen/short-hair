How To Curl Hair Outwards A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read
One of the best ways to add volume and bounce to your hair is by curling it outwards. This technique creates an effortless and natural look that's perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to curl hair outwards in a few simple steps.

Description

Curling hair outwards is a technique that involves curling the hair away from your face, creating a natural-looking curl that adds volume and bounce. This technique is perfect for those who want to create a fun and flirty look without the hassle of complicated hairstyles.

How to Curl Hair Outwards

To get started, you'll need a few tools, including a curling iron, heat protectant spray, and a comb. Here's a step-by-step guide to curling hair outwards:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin curling your hair, it's essential to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then apply a heat protectant spray to your damp hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy and shiny.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a comb to section it into small sections. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Clip each section of hair with a hair clip to keep it out of the way while you curl the rest of your hair.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Curling Iron

Plug in your curling iron and let it heat up. The temperature will depend on your hair type, so be sure to check the instructions that came with your curling iron.

Step 4: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold the curling iron vertically and start curling away from your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds, then release it. Continue curling each section of hair until all your hair is curled.

Step 5: Style Your Curls

Once you've curled all your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a natural-looking style. You can also use a brush or comb to create a more polished look.

Tips for Curling Hair Outwards

- Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. - Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to curl. - Hold the curling iron vertically and curl away from your face for the best results. - Use your fingers to separate the curls and create a natural look. - Apply a hair spray to hold the curls in place.

FAQ

What type of curling iron should I use?

You can use any type of curling iron, but a 1-inch or 1.5-inch barrel is ideal for creating loose curls.

How long should I hold the curling iron on my hair?

Hold the curling iron on your hair for 5-10 seconds, depending on your hair type and the heat of your curling iron.

Can I curl my hair outwards without a curling iron?

Yes, you can use a straightening iron or hot rollers to curl your hair outwards.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking great, it's essential to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Also, be sure to trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Curling your hair outwards is an easy way to add volume and bounce to your hair. With a few simple tools and techniques, you can create a natural and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion. Try this technique today and see how it can transform your hair!
