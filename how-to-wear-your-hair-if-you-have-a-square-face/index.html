How To Wear Your Hair If You Have A Square Face

Written by Christine Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square face, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your facial features. A square face is characterized by a prominent jawline, sharp angles, and a broad forehead. To soften your features and create balance, you need a hairstyle that adds volume and roundness to your face. In this article, we'll show you how to wear your hair if you have a square face, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Wear Your Hair if You Have a Square Face

The key to wearing your hair if you have a square face is to create softness and volume around your jawline and forehead. This means opting for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head while keeping the sides and ends soft and loose. Here are some hairstyle ideas that work well for square faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for square faces as they soften your features and create movement around your face. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that start from your cheekbones and taper down to the ends. This will create a flattering frame around your face and draw attention away from your jawline.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften your forehead and create balance in your face. Opt for long, wispy bangs that graze your eyebrows and blend seamlessly into your hair. Avoid blunt bangs as they can accentuate your square jawline.

3. Soft Waves

Soft waves add volume and texture to your hair, creating a flattering frame around your face. Use a curling iron to create loose waves that start from the mid-lengths of your hair and gently curve away from your face. Avoid tight curls as they can make your face look wider.

4. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a quick and easy way to add height to your crown and elongate your face. Pull your hair up into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Use a comb to tease the crown of your head and create volume. Leave a few strands of hair loose around your face to soften your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing your hair if you have a square face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, determine your face shape to ensure that you're picking a style that flatters your features. Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you have a square face.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that adds softness and volume to your face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, soft waves, and high ponytails are all great options for square faces.

Step 3: Prep Your Hair

Prep your hair by washing it with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to the hairstyle you've chosen. Use a curling iron to create soft waves or a high ponytail to add height to your crown. Finish with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a square face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or straight, shoulder-length hair.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to your crown, such as high ponytails or half-up hairstyles.
  • Soft, loose waves or curls can help soften your features and create a flattering frame around your face.
  • Side-swept bangs can help balance your forehead and create a more oval-shaped face.
  • Avoid center parts as they can accentuate your square jawline.

FAQs

What is a square face?

A square face is characterized by a prominent jawline, sharp angles, and a broad forehead. The width of the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline is roughly the same.

What hairstyles are best for square faces?

Hairstyles that add softness and volume to your face are best for square faces. Long layers, side-swept bangs, soft waves, and high ponytails are all great options.

Can I wear a bob if I have a square face?

Avoid blunt bobs that end at your jawline as they can accentuate your square jaw. Opt for a longer bob that ends just below your chin or a layered bob that adds volume around your face.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a square face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and bounce to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy, oily hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.
  • Use a curling iron to create soft waves or a high ponytail to add height to your crown.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy, bouncy look.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can create a flattering look that softens your features and highlights your best assets. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
