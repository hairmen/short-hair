How To Change Texture Of My Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hair game, changing the texture of your hair can be a great option. Whether you have naturally curly hair and want to go straight or have straight hair and want to add some waves, there are a variety of ways to change your hair texture. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step, as well as offer some tips and tricks for maintaining your new texture.

How to Change Texture of My Hair

There are several ways to change the texture of your hair, including heat styling, chemical treatments, and protective styling. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Heat Styling

One of the easiest ways to change your hair texture is through heat styling. Whether you're using a flat iron to straighten your curls or a curling wand to add waves, heat styling can transform your hair in just a few minutes. However, it's important to use heat protectant products to prevent damage to your hair.

Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments, such as relaxers, perms, and keratin treatments, can permanently alter the texture of your hair. These treatments can be done at a salon or at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair or scalp.

Protective Styling

Protective styling involves wearing your hair in a style that protects it from damage, such as braids, twists, or weaves. This can help to preserve your hair's natural texture while giving you a new look. However, it's important to take breaks between protective styles to give your hair a chance to breathe.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to changing the texture of your hair:

Step 1: Determine your hair type

Before you can change your hair texture, it's important to know your hair type. This will help you to choose the right products and methods for your hair.

Step 2: Choose your method

Decide which method you want to use to change your hair texture. Consider factors such as cost, time, and potential damage to your hair.

Step 3: Prepare your hair

Prep your hair by washing and conditioning it. If you're using heat styling, apply a heat protectant product. If you're doing a chemical treatment, follow the instructions carefully.

Step 4: Style your hair

Style your hair using your chosen method. If you're using heat styling, section your hair and work in small sections to avoid heat damage. If you're doing a chemical treatment, follow the instructions carefully and be sure to use gloves to protect your hands.

Step 5: Maintain your new texture

Once you've changed the texture of your hair, it's important to maintain it. Use products that are designed for your new texture, and avoid heat styling or chemical treatments that could damage your hair.

Tips for Changing Your Hair Texture

Here are some additional tips for changing your hair texture:

  • Consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure about which method to use
  • Use high-quality products that are designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage
  • Take breaks between chemical treatments to give your hair a chance to recover
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions about changing the texture of your hair:

How long does it take to change the texture of my hair?

The length of time it takes to change your hair texture depends on the method you use. Heat styling can be done in a matter of minutes, while chemical treatments can take several hours.

Will changing my hair texture damage my hair?

It's possible that changing your hair texture could cause damage, especially if you're using heat styling or chemical treatments. However, using high-quality products and taking proper care of your hair can minimize the risk of damage.

Can I change my hair texture if I have natural hair?

Yes, you can change the texture of your natural hair using heat styling or protective styling. However, chemical treatments may not be suitable for all hair types.

Hair Care for Different Textures

Once you've changed the texture of your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair based on your new texture:

Straight hair

Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.

Curly hair

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated, and avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.

Wavy hair

Use a curl-enhancing product to define your waves, and avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

Conclusion

Changing the texture of your hair can be a fun way to switch up your look. Whether you're going straight, curly, or somewhere in between, there are a variety of methods to choose from. Just be sure to take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great!
