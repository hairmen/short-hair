Mid Length Hairstyles For Oblong Face

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your oblong face shape? Mid length hairstyles are the perfect choice for you! This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect mid length hairstyle for your oblong face.

Description

People with oblong face shapes have a long and narrow face with defined cheekbones and a pointed chin. Mid length hairstyles are perfect for oblong faces because they add volume to the sides of the face and create a balanced look. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your features and bring out your natural beauty.

How to

The first step in creating a mid length hairstyle for an oblong face is to find the right haircut. A layered cut with soft, side-swept bangs is an excellent choice. Layers will add volume and texture to your hair, while the bangs will frame your face and create balance.

Once you have the right cut, it's time to style your hair. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish. Then, use a curling iron to create loose, soft curls. To add more volume, tease the roots of your hair with a comb or brush.

Step by step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to create a mid length hairstyle for an oblong face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and a smooth finish.
  4. Part your hair on one side and create soft, side-swept bangs.
  5. Use a curling iron to create loose, soft curls.
  6. Tease the roots of your hair to add volume.
  7. Use hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating a mid length hairstyle for an oblong face:

  • Choose a haircut with layers and soft, side-swept bangs to add volume and balance to your face.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and a smooth finish.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose, soft curls.
  • Tease the roots of your hair to add volume.
  • Use hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid length hairstyles for oblong faces:

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

A layered cut with soft, side-swept bangs is the best choice for an oblong face. Layers will add volume and texture to your hair, while the bangs will frame your face and create balance.

How can I add volume to my mid length hair?

You can add volume to your mid length hair by blow-drying it with a round brush, using a volumizing mousse or spray, and teasing the roots of your hair.

How can I keep my mid length hairstyle in place?

You can keep your mid length hairstyle in place by using hairspray or texturizing spray. These products will hold your hair in place and add texture and definition to your style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining a healthy and beautiful mid length hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care, you can create a beautiful mid length hairstyle that flatters your oblong face shape. Follow these tips and instructions to achieve the perfect look for you!
