How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Only

Written by Eveline Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the barbershop? Do you want to save some money and learn how to cut men's hair with scissors only? If yes, then you are in the right place. This step-by-step guide will teach you how to cut men's hair with scissors only.

Description

Cutting men's hair with scissors only is a simple process that can be done at home. It requires some basic tools such as scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You don't need any fancy equipment or expensive tools to achieve a professional-looking haircut. With some patience, practice, and these tips, you can become a pro at cutting men's hair with scissors only.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Only

Follow these steps to cut men's hair with scissors only:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you begin cutting the hair, make sure that it is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for the hair type. Rinse the hair thoroughly and dry it with a towel or a hairdryer.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use a comb to section the hair into manageable parts. Start by dividing the hair into two sections, one at the front and one at the back. Use hair clips to secure the hair in place.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Start cutting the hair from the back. Use a comb to pull the hair straight and then cut it with scissors. Work your way from the back to the front. Cut the hair in small sections to ensure that it is even. Repeat the process on the other side.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair at the top and the sides. This will give the hair a natural look. Use your fingers to feel for any uneven parts and then cut them with scissors.

Step 5: Trim the Sideburns

Use scissors to trim the sideburns. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Make sure that both sides are even.

Step 6: Check for Uneven Parts

Use a mirror to check for any uneven parts. If you find any, use scissors to trim them. Make sure that both sides are even.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Use a comb to style the hair. You can use hair gel or wax to keep the hair in place. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut men's hair with scissors only:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Cut the hair in small sections to ensure that it is even.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Use a mirror to check for any uneven parts.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. Follow the steps mentioned above and be patient.

Do I need any special tools to cut men's hair with scissors only?

No, you don't need any special tools. You just need scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Is it difficult to cut men's hair with scissors only?

No, it's not difficult. It just requires some practice and patience.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important to maintain its health and appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with scissors only, you can save money and achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Follow these steps, be patient, and you'll be a pro in no time.
