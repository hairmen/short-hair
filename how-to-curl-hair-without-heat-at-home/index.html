How To Curl Hair Without Heat At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you want to achieve beautiful curls without damaging your hair with heat styling tools? Curling your hair without heat is a great alternative to achieve bouncy and defined curls. In this article, we will provide you with a step by step guide on how to curl hair without heat at home.

How to Curl Hair Without Heat at Home

Here are the steps on how to curl hair without heat at home:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to achieving beautiful curls without heat is to wash and condition your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Product

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a leave-in product that can help define your curls. You can use a curl-enhancing cream or mousse.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips. This will make it easier for you to curl your hair without heat.

Step 4: Twist Each Section

Take a section of your hair and twist it tightly. You can twist it in one direction or alternate directions to achieve a more natural-looking curl.

Step 5: Secure the Twisted Section

Once you have twisted the section, secure it with a clip or bobby pin.

Step 6: Repeat the Process

Repeat the twisting process on the rest of your hair until all sections are twisted and secured.

Step 7: Let Your Hair Dry

Leave your hair twisted and secured for several hours or overnight until it is completely dry.

Step 8: Undo the Twisted Sections

After your hair is completely dry, undo the twisted sections. You can use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to separate the curls.

Step 9: Apply a Hair Spray

If you want your curls to last longer, apply a hair spray that can hold your curls in place.

Tips for Curling Hair Without Heat at Home

Here are some tips for curling hair without heat at home:

  • Use a leave-in product to define your curls.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to curl without heat.
  • Twist each section tightly for more defined curls.
  • Let your hair dry completely before undoing the twisted sections.
  • Apply a hair spray to hold your curls in place.

FAQ

Can I curl hair without heat?

Yes, you can curl hair without heat. You can use various techniques such as twisting, braiding, or using rollers to achieve beautiful curls.

How long does it take to curl hair without heat?

It can take several hours or overnight to curl your hair without heat. It depends on the thickness and length of your hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair even when you are not using heat styling tools. Use gentle hair products that suit your hair type and avoid using too much hair spray or other styling products that can damage your hair.

Curling your hair without heat is a great alternative to achieve beautiful curls without damaging your hair. Follow the steps and tips we have provided in this article to achieve bouncy and defined curls.
