Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular cheekbones. Women with square faces often worry about whether short hair will look good on them or not. However, the good news is that square faces can look great with short hair as long as the right style is chosen.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you decide on a short hairstyle, it is important to determine your face shape. To determine whether you have a square face or not, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the outline. If your jawline is strong and your forehead is wide with angular cheekbones, you have a square face.

  2. Choose a style that softens your strong jawline. A side-swept fringe or a layered bob can help to soften your jawline.
  3. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head. This can make your face appear wider than it actually is. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the top of your head.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on Square Faces

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
