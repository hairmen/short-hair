How To Describe Guys Hair In Relaxed English Language

Written by Eveline Aug 18, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to describe a guy's hair to someone else, whether it's to a friend or a stylist, you might find yourself struggling to find the right words. In this article, we'll break down some tips and tricks for describing guys' hair in relaxed English language. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Describe Guys Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Describing Guys Hair
  5. FAQs

Description

When describing a guy's hair, there are a few key elements to consider:

  • Length
  • Texture
  • Color
  • Style

By considering these elements, you can start to build a picture of what the guy's hair looks like. Let's break down each element further.

Length

The length of a guy's hair can vary from very short to very long. Here are some terms you can use to describe different hair lengths:

  • Buzz cut - very short hair, usually less than 1/4 inch long
  • Short - hair that is longer than a buzz cut but still relatively short, usually less than 2 inches long
  • Medium - hair that falls between short and long, usually between 2 and 6 inches long
  • Long - hair that is longer than 6 inches, usually shoulder-length or longer

Texture

The texture of a guy's hair can range from straight to curly to wavy. Here are some terms you can use to describe different hair textures:

  • Straight - hair that does not have any curl or wave
  • Curly - hair that has tight, defined curls
  • Wavy - hair that has loose, flowing waves
  • Coarse - hair that is thick and rough to the touch
  • Fine - hair that is thin and delicate to the touch

Color

The color of a guy's hair can vary from blonde to black and everything in between. Here are some terms you can use to describe different hair colors:

  • Blonde
  • Brown
  • Black
  • Red
  • Grey

Style

The style of a guy's hair can also vary widely. Here are some terms you can use to describe different hair styles:

  • Crew cut - a short, tapered cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides and back
  • Quiff - a style where the hair is combed upward and back from the forehead
  • Pompadour - a style where the hair is swept upward and back from the forehead and styled with product
  • Comb over - a style where the hair is combed to one side and styled with product
  • Undercut - a style where the hair on the sides and back is cut very short and the hair on top is left longer

How to Describe Guys Hair

Now that we've covered the key elements to consider when describing a guy's hair, let's break down how to put it all together:

  1. Start with the length: Is the guy's hair short, medium, or long?
  2. Next, consider the texture: Is the hair straight, curly, or wavy? Is it thick or thin?
  3. Then, think about the color: Is the hair blonde, brown, black, or a different color?
  4. Finally, describe the style: Is the hair cut in a crew cut, quiff, or some other style? Is it styled with product?

By following these steps, you can create a clear and concise description of a guy's hair that anyone can understand.

Step by Step Guide

Let's break down the process of describing a guy's hair step by step:

  1. Start with the length: Is the guy's hair short, medium, or long?
    • If the hair is very short, describe it as a buzz cut.
    • If the hair is longer than a buzz cut but still relatively short, describe it as short.
    • If the hair falls between short and long, describe it as medium.
    • If the hair is longer than 6 inches, describe it as long.
  2. Next, consider the texture: Is the hair straight, curly, or wavy? Is it thick or thin?
    • If the hair is straight, describe it as straight.
    • If the hair has tight, defined curls, describe it as curly.
    • If the hair has loose, flowing waves, describe it as wavy.
    • If the hair is thick and rough to the touch, describe it as coarse.
    • If the hair is thin and delicate to the touch, describe it as fine.
  3. Then, think about the color: Is the hair blonde, brown, black, or a different color?
    • If the hair is blonde, describe it as blonde.
    • If the hair is brown, describe it as brown.
    • If the hair is black, describe it as black.
    • If the hair is red or another color, describe it as such.
  4. Finally, describe the style: Is the hair cut in a crew cut, quiff, or some other style? Is it styled with product?
    • If the hair is cut short and tapered, describe it as a crew cut.
    • If the hair is combed upward and back from the forehead, describe it as a quiff.
    • If the hair is swept upward and back from the forehead and styled with product, describe it as a pompadour.
    • If the hair is combed to one side and styled with product, describe it as a comb over.
    • If the hair on the sides and back is cut very short and the hair on top is left longer, describe it as an undercut.

Tips for Describing Guys Hair

Here are some additional tips for describing guys' hair:

  • Be specific: The more specific you can be, the easier it will be for someone else to understand what you're describing.
  • Use comparisons: Comparing a guy's hair to something else can be a helpful way to describe it. For example, you could say someone's hair is like "a lion's mane" or "a surfer's waves."
  • Consider the audience: If you're describing a guy's hair to a friend, you might be able to use more casual language than if you're describing it to a stylist.

FAQs

What if I don't know the name of a specific hairstyle?

Don't worry too much about the name of a specific hairstyle. Instead, focus on describing the style itself. For example, you could say someone's hair is "combed to one side and styled with product" instead of using the term "comb over."

What if the guy's hair doesn't fit neatly into one category?

It's okay if a guy's hair doesn't fit neatly into one category. You can use multiple terms to describe different elements of their hair. For example, you could say someone has "long, wavy hair that's styled with product."

What if the guy's hair is balding or thinning?

If a guy's hair is balding or thinning, you could describe it as "short and thinning" or "balding on top."
