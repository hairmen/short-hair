How to Tame Frizzy Hair in Humidity?

Humidity can cause hair to become frizzy and unmanageable. Here are some tips to help you tame frizzy hair in humid weather:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo that is formulated for frizzy hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help moisturize it and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as they can further damage it and make it more prone to frizzing.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to help smooth down frizz and add shine.
  • Avoid touching your hair as much as possible as this can cause frizz.

Step by Step Guide to Using Hair Care Products
