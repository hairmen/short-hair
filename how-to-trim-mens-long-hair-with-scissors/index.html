How To Trim Men s Long Hair With Scissors

Written by Bardi Jun 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a man with long hair, you know how important it is to keep your locks looking good. One of the most important aspects of maintaining long hair is regular trimming. In this article, we'll show you how to trim men's long hair with scissors in a relaxed and simple way.

Description

Trimming men's long hair with scissors is a great way to maintain the length and shape of your hair. This technique is simple and can be done at home with the right tools and techniques. With a little practice, you'll be able to trim your own hair and save money on salon visits.

How to

Here's how to trim men's long hair with scissors:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step in trimming your long hair is to wash it thoroughly and then dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start trimming.

Step 2: Gather Your Tools

You'll need a few tools to trim your hair, including a pair of good-quality hair cutting scissors, a comb, and some hair clips. Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Take a comb and divide your hair into sections. Clip each section of hair to keep it out of the way while you work on the other sections. This will make it easier to see what you're doing and help you avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 4: Start Trimming

Start trimming your hair by cutting small sections at a time. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Make sure you don't cut too much hair at once. You can always cut more later if needed.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

As you trim your hair, check your progress in the mirror. Make sure that both sides of your hair are even and that you're achieving the look you want.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you trim your men's long hair with scissors:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Cheap scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to achieve the look you want.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to trim small sections at a time to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Use a comb to keep your hair in place while you trim it.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Trimming your hair can be tiring, so take breaks as needed to avoid making mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about trimming men's long hair with scissors:

Can I trim my hair myself?

Yes, you can trim your hair yourself. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking trim at home.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended that you trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and length.

Can I trim my hair when it's wet?

It's generally best to trim your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be harder to work with and can make it harder to achieve the look you want.

Hair Care

Trimming your men's long hair with scissors is just one part of a good hair care routine. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that's suited to your hair type.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

Now that you know how to trim men's long hair with scissors, you can achieve a professional-looking trim at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can maintain the length and shape of your hair and keep it looking its best.
