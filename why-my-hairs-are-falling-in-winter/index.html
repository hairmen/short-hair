Why My Hairs Are Falling In Winter

Written by Petter Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Winter is a beautiful season, but it can be harsh on your hair. Many people experience hair fall during this season, and it can be frustrating. In this article, we will explore the reasons why your hairs are falling in winter and what you can do to prevent it. Let’s get started.

Description

Hair fall is a common problem that affects people of all ages and genders. It is normal to lose around 50-100 hairs per day, but if you notice excessive hair fall, it can be a sign of an underlying issue. Winter hair fall is a common problem that affects many people due to the cold and dry weather. The low humidity and lack of moisture can make your hair dry and brittle, leading to hair fall.

How to Prevent Hair Fall in Winter?

Here are some tips that can help you prevent hair fall in winter:

1. Keep Your Scalp Clean

Keeping your scalp clean is essential to prevent hair fall. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair twice a week. Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

2. Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturizing your hair is essential to prevent hair fall in winter. Use a deep conditioning hair mask once a week to moisturize your hair. You can also use natural oils like coconut oil, olive oil, or almond oil to moisturize your hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like hairdryers, flat irons, and curling irons can damage your hair, making it dry and brittle. Avoid using these tools as much as possible, or use them on low heat settings.

4. Wear a Hat

Wearing a hat or a scarf can protect your hair from the cold and dry weather. It can also prevent your hair from breaking due to friction with your clothes.

5. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet is essential for healthy hair. Include foods that are rich in vitamins A, B, C, D, and E, as well as iron, zinc, and protein. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Q. Why Does Hair Fall Increase in Winter?

A. Hair fall increases in winter due to the cold and dry weather. The lack of moisture and low humidity can make your hair dry and brittle, leading to hair fall.

Q. Can Stress Cause Hair Fall in Winter?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall in winter. Stress can disrupt the hair growth cycle, leading to hair fall.

Q. Can I Prevent Hair Fall in Winter?

A. Yes, you can prevent hair fall in winter by following a healthy hair care routine, eating a balanced diet, and protecting your hair from the cold and dry weather.

Hair Care Tips for Winter

Here are some additional hair care tips that can help you prevent hair fall in winter:

1. Use a Humidifier

Using a humidifier can help to add moisture to the air, preventing your hair from becoming dry and brittle.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can prevent split ends, which can lead to hair fall.

3. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation, promoting healthy hair growth.

4. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can prevent your hair from breaking due to friction with your pillow.

These are some tips that can help you prevent hair fall in winter. Follow a healthy hair care routine and protect your hair from the cold and dry weather to maintain healthy and strong hair.
