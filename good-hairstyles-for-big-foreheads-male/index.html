Best Hairstyles For Men With Big Foreheads

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Big foreheads can sometimes make men feel self-conscious about their appearance. However, having a big forehead should not limit your hairstyle options. There are many hairstyles that can complement your facial features and make you look more confident. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for men with big foreheads.

Description

Having a big forehead is not a disadvantage. It can make you look more intelligent, and it is a feature that many people find attractive. The key is to find a hairstyle that balances your facial features and enhances your best features. Whether you have a receding hairline or a high hairline, there are many hairstyles that can make you look great.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your big forehead is to determine the shape of your face. Men with big foreheads usually have one of three face shapes: oval, square, or heart-shaped. Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you can wear almost any hairstyle. However, a hairstyle with bangs or layers can help to balance your facial features. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your forehead appear even bigger.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you can choose a hairstyle that softens your features. A hairstyle with textured layers or a side-swept fringe can help to balance your facial features. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat, as they can make your forehead appear even bigger.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you can choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head. A hairstyle with a side-swept fringe or a messy quiff can help to balance your facial features. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat, as they can make your forehead appear even bigger.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the best hairstyles for men with big foreheads:

Messy Quiff

  1. Apply a small amount of pomade to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair while using your fingers to create volume at the top of your head.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to create a messy quiff.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Side-Swept Fringe

  1. Apply a small amount of styling cream to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair while using a brush to create a side-swept fringe.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of pomade to add texture to your hair.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Textured Layers

  1. Apply a small amount of styling cream to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair while using your fingers to create volume at the top of your head.
  3. Use a small amount of pomade to add texture to your hair.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hairstyle for your big forehead:

  • Consult with a hairstylist to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat, as they can make your forehead appear even bigger.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that you feel confident with.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or hairspray, to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for men with big foreheads:

What is the best haircut for a receding hairline?

The best haircut for a receding hairline is a short haircut with a side-swept fringe or textured layers. These hairstyles can help to conceal your receding hairline and balance your facial features.

Can I wear a buzz cut if I have a big forehead?

A buzz cut can make your forehead appear even bigger. It is best to choose a hairstyle with some volume, such as a messy quiff or textured layers.

How do I know if a hairstyle suits my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo every other day.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can make your big forehead work to your advantage. Embrace your unique features and experiment with different hairstyles to find one that makes you feel confident and stylish.
