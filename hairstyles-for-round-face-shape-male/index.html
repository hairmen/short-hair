Hairstyles for Round Face Shape Male - Tips, How-to, and FAQ

Are you having trouble finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape? Look no further than this guide, which includes tips, step-by-step instructions, and frequently asked questions.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape, you need to determine what shape your face actually is. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back off your face.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to draw an outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape of the outline. Is it round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped?
  4. If you have a round face shape, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your chin is rounded.

Hairstyles for Round Face Shape Male

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that complements it. Here are some styles that work well for men with round faces:

Short Buzz Cut

A short buzz cut can make your face look more angular and less round. Keep the sides and back short and the top a little longer.

Side Part

A side part can add height to your hair and make your face look longer. Keep the sides short and the top longer.

Textured Crop

A textured crop with choppy layers can add volume and texture to your hair, making your face look slimmer.

Undercut

An undercut with a disconnected top can create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Quiff

A quiff with volume on top can elongate your face and create a more angular look.

Tips for Styling Round Face Shape Male

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look even rounder.
  • Instead, focus on adding height and volume to the top of your head.
  • Use a matte hair product to avoid adding shine, which can make your face look rounder.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for round face shape male?

The best haircut for round face shape male is one that adds height and volume to the top of the head and keeps the sides and back short.

What hairstyle should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a full fringe or a wide mohawk.

Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to add volume and height to the top of your head to balance out your face shape.

Hair Care for Round Face Shape Male

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and styles in mind, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape. Experiment with different looks and don't be afraid to try something new!
