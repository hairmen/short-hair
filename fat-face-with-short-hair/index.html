Fat Face With Short Hair A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Bardi Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round, full face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, there are plenty of options for those with a fat face and short hair. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for finding a flattering hairstyle that suits your face shape and highlights your best features.

Description

A fat face with short hair can be a tricky combination. Short hair can accentuate the roundness of the face, while longer hair can weigh down your features. However, with the right cut and styling, you can create a flattering look that complements your face shape.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your fat face is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the widest part of your face. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you likely have a round face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Step by step

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your crown. This will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. A pixie cut with volume on top or a short bob with layers can be flattering.
  2. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or jawline. These can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a shorter cut that ends above your jawline or longer layers that frame your face.
  3. Add texture to your hair with waves or curls. This will create movement and dimension, drawing attention away from the roundness of your face.
  4. Consider adding side-swept bangs to your hairstyle. This can help to balance out the width of your face and draw attention to your eyes.
  5. Avoid center parts, which can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a side part that creates asymmetry and adds dimension to your hair.

Tips

When styling your hair, keep these tips in mind:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add lift to your roots.
  • Avoid sleek, straight styles that can emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Consider using a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair while blow-drying.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about hairstyles for a fat face with short hair:

What is the best hairstyle for a fat face with short hair?

The best hairstyle for a fat face with short hair is one that adds height to your crown and avoids blunt cuts that end at your chin or jawline. Consider a pixie cut with volume on top or a short bob with layers.

What hairstyles should I avoid with a fat face and short hair?

Avoid sleek, straight styles and blunt cuts that end at your chin or jawline. Center parts can also make your face appear wider.

Can I add color to my hair if I have a fat face with short hair?

Absolutely! Adding highlights or lowlights can add dimension and texture to your hair, which can be flattering for a round face shape.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to choose products that are gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are free of harsh chemicals and sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair, and avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage. Consider using a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to style your hair, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
