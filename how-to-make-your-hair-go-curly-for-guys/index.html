How To Make Your Hair Go Curly For Guys

Written by Luffy Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair has always been popular, and it's not just for women. Men can also rock the curly hair look. But, what if you have straight hair? Don't worry; you can still have curly hair without going to the salon. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair go curly for guys.

Description

Curly hair can be achieved through different methods, such as using styling products, heat tools, and natural methods. The key is to find the method that works best for you and your hair type. Whether you have short or long hair, you can achieve curly hair with these tips and techniques.

How to Make Your Hair Go Curly for Guys

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz. Don't use too much conditioner as it can weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. Choose a product that suits your hair type and the level of hold you want. For example, if you have fine hair, use a lightweight mousse. If you have thick hair, use a gel or cream. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer. A diffuser helps distribute heat evenly and enhances your curls. Set your hair dryer to a low heat setting and dry your hair in sections. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you dry it. This will help create more defined curls.

Step 4: Use Heat Tools

If you want more defined curls, you can use a curling iron or wand. Choose a barrel size that matches the size of curls you want. Wrap small sections of your hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds. Release the curl and move on to the next section. Repeat until your entire head is curled.

Tips

  • Don't touch your hair too much as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and tame frizz.
  • Consider getting a haircut that suits curly hair, such as layers or a textured cut.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair as it can break your curls.

FAQ

Can I make my hair go curly if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can make your hair go curly even if you have straight hair. Follow the steps above or consider getting a perm.

How long will my curls last?

It depends on your hair type, the products you use, and the weather. Generally, your curls can last up to two days or longer with the right care.

Hair Care

To maintain your curly hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools too often as it can damage your hair. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair go curly for guys, it's time to try it out. Experiment with different products and techniques until you find the perfect look for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
