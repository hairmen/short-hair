10 Trendy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs

Written by Daniel Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have shoulder length hair with bangs, you may be wondering what hairstyles will suit you best. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will share with you 10 trendy hairstyles that are perfect for shoulder length hair with bangs. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these hairstyles will help you stand out. So, let's get started!

Description

Shoulder length hair with bangs is a versatile hair length. You can style it in many different ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous. Bangs add an extra element of style to your hair, framing your face and giving you a youthful appearance. In this article, we will explore different hairstyles that work well with shoulder length hair and bangs, including updos, braids, and layered cuts.

How To

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about how to achieve the perfect look. First, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. Use a heat protectant if you plan on using heat styling tools. Use a brush or comb to detangle your hair, and section it off if necessary. Finally, use hairspray or hair gel to set your style in place.

Step by Step

1. Sleek and Straight
- Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
- Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
- Part your hair in the middle or to one side.
- Tuck your hair behind your ears, or leave it loose.
- Finish with hairspray or hair oil for shine.

2. Curly and Voluminous
- Apply a curling mousse to your hair.
- Use a curling iron to create loose curls.
- Part your hair in the middle or to one side.
- Use your fingers to loosen the curls for volume.
- Finish with hairspray or hair oil for shine.

3. Messy Bun
- Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
- Twist your hair into a bun and secure with bobby pins.
- Leave your bangs loose or pin them back.
- Use your fingers to loosen the bun for a messy look.
- Finish with hairspray or hair oil for shine.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant to avoid damage from heat styling tools.
- Experiment with different parting styles to find what suits you best.
- Don't be afraid to use hair accessories like bobby pins, headbands, or hair clips.
- Use hairspray or hair gel to set your style in place.
- Consult with a hairstylist for personalized advice on what style works best with your face shape and hair texture.

FAQ

Q. Can I still wear a ponytail with shoulder length hair and bangs?
A. Yes, you can. A low ponytail with bangs framing your face can look chic and sophisticated.

Q. Are there any hairstyles I should avoid with shoulder length hair and bangs?
A. Avoid heavy bangs that cover your forehead completely, as they can make your face appear smaller. Also, avoid overly voluminous hairstyles that can overwhelm your face.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid using hot tools too frequently. Get regular trims to prevent split ends, and use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated. With proper care, your shoulder length hair with bangs will look stunning and stylish.

There you have it! 10 trendy hairstyles for shoulder length hair with bangs. Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best, and don't be afraid to try something new. With the right hairstyle, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
