How To Make Your Face Look Less Chubby With Makeup

Written by Daniel Aug 02, 2023 · 3 min read

If you want to make your face look less chubby, there are a few makeup tricks you can use. By contouring and highlighting your face in the right places, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face. In this article, we’ll show you how to make your face look less chubby with makeup, step by step.

Description

Having a chubby face is not necessarily a bad thing, but if you want to create a more defined look, makeup can help. Contouring and highlighting are the key techniques to create a slimmer face. By using darker shades to contour and lighter shades to highlight, you can create shadows and highlights that will make your face look more sculpted.

How to Make Your Face Look Less Chubby with Makeup: Step by Step

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Before you start applying makeup, make sure your skin is clean and moisturized. Apply a primer to help your makeup last longer.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply foundation all over your face, using a sponge or a brush. Choose a shade that matches your skin tone perfectly to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Contour Your Face

Using a contour brush, apply a darker shade of foundation or a contour powder to the areas you want to slim down. This includes the sides of your forehead, the hollows of your cheeks, and your jawline. Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Highlight Your Face

Using a highlighter, apply a lighter shade of foundation or a highlight powder to the areas you want to bring forward. This includes the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, the tops of your cheekbones, and your cupid’s bow. Blend well.

Step 5: Set Your Makeup

Using a setting powder or spray, set your makeup to make it last longer.

Tips

  • Use a light hand when applying makeup to avoid a cakey look.
  • Choose a contour shade that is only one or two shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a matte highlighter to avoid a shiny look.

FAQ

Q: Can makeup really make my face look less chubby?

A: Yes, by contouring and highlighting, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Q: What kind of brush should I use for contouring?

A: Use a contour brush, which is angled to fit the contours of your face.

Q: Can I use cream products instead of powder?

A: Yes, cream products can work well for contouring and highlighting, but make sure to blend well.

Hair Care

When it comes to making your face look less chubby, your hairstyle can also make a difference. A long, layered haircut can help to elongate your face, while a shorter, blunt cut can make your face appear wider. Bangs can also help to balance out a round face, but make sure to choose a style that suits your face shape.

By using these makeup and hair tips, you can create a more defined look and make your face look less chubby. Remember to practice and experiment to find the techniques that work best for you.
