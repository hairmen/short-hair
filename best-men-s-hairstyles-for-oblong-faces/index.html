Best Men s Hairstyles For Oblong Faces

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't seem to flatter your oblong face shape? Don't worry, you're not alone. A lot of men struggle to find the perfect haircut that suits their face shape. In this article, we'll be discussing the best men's hairstyles for oblong faces, along with some tips on how to maintain and style your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

Oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with prominent cheekbones and forehead. The key to finding the best hairstyle for this face shape is to choose a haircut that adds width to your face, making it appear more balanced. The hairstyle should also complement your features and enhance your overall look.

How to

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oblong faces, it's important to understand the basics of hair styling. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle based on your hair type, texture, and density.
  • Consider your lifestyle and the amount of time you're willing to spend on styling your hair.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Step by Step

Here are the best men's hairstyles for oblong faces:

1. Short Crop

The short crop is a classic hairstyle that suits most face shapes, including oblong faces. This style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top. The short crop adds volume and texture to your hair, making it appear thicker and fuller.

2. Textured Quiff

The textured quiff is a trendy hairstyle that works well for oblong faces. This style features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top styled into a messy quiff. The textured quiff adds volume and width to your face, making it appear more balanced.

3. Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. This style features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top styled to one side. The side part adds width to your face, making it appear more balanced.

4. Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a popular hairstyle that suits oblong faces. This style features longer hair on top styled into a messy fringe, with shorter hair on the sides and back. The messy fringe adds volume and texture to your hair, making it appear thicker and fuller.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Take care of your hair by avoiding excessive heat styling and chemical treatments.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles with too much height on top, as they can make your face appear even longer. Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or flat, as they can make your face appear narrower.

Should I use hair products to style my hair?

Yes, using hair products can help you achieve the look you want and keep your hair in place throughout the day. Choose products that suit your hair type and texture, such as pomade, wax, or gel.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and nourished, it's important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using excessive heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can now find the best men's hairstyles for oblong faces. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type, and to invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
