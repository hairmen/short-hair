Medium Length Hair For Oval Faces Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky! This face shape is versatile and can pull off many different hairstyles. In this guide, we’ll focus on medium length hair for oval faces. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Description: What is an Oval Face Shape?

Before we dive into hairstyle ideas, let’s define what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, a narrow forehead, and a slightly rounded jawline. Many people consider this to be the “ideal” face shape because it can pull off many different hairstyles.

How to: Choosing the Right Medium Length Hairstyle for Your Oval Face

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for your oval face, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. Here are some tips:

Tip #1: Choose a hairstyle that complements your hair type

If you have straight hair, consider a blunt bob or a lob. If you have wavy hair, try a layered medium length cut. If you have curly hair, go for a shoulder-length cut with layers to add volume and texture.

Tip #2: Consider your lifestyle

If you’re always on the go, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that you can easily style in the morning. If you have more time to spend on your hair, consider a more complex hairstyle with braids or twists.

Tip #3: Think about your personal style

Do you prefer a classic or trendy look? Are you more of a minimalist or a maximalist? Choose a hairstyle that reflects your personal style.

Step by Step: How to Style Medium Length Hair for Oval Faces

Now that you’ve chosen the perfect medium length hairstyle for your oval face, it’s time to style it! Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step #1: Wash and condition your hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Wash and condition your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type.

Step #2: Apply styling products

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or styling cream, to your hair. This will help to add volume and texture.

Step #3: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to add volume and create a smooth, polished look.

Step #4: Style your hair

Using a curling iron or flat iron, style your hair as desired. You can add loose waves, curls, or straighten your hair.

Tips: How to Maintain Medium Length Hair for Oval Faces

Now that you’ve styled your hair, here are some tips to help you maintain your medium length hair for oval faces:

Tip #1: Use the right hair products

Use products that are appropriate for your hair type. For example, if you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Tip #2: Protect your hair from heat damage

Before using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent heat damage.

Tip #3: Get regular haircuts

To keep your medium length hair looking its best, get regular haircuts. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Medium Length Hair for Oval Faces

Q: What are some medium length hairstyles for oval faces?

A: Some medium length hairstyles for oval faces include a lob, a layered haircut, a blunt bob, and a shoulder-length cut with layers.

Q: Can I style my medium length hair for oval faces without heat?

A: Yes! You can style your hair using techniques such as braiding or twisting. You can also use products, such as mousse or styling cream, to add texture and hold.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair or a more relaxed lifestyle, you may be able to wash your hair less frequently.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

Medium length hair for oval faces is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. By choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape, considering your lifestyle and personal style, and following our styling and maintenance tips, you can achieve a look that is both flattering and easy to maintain. Happy styling!
