How To Know If You Suit Long Or Short Hair

Written by Bardi Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. One of the biggest decisions you'll have to make is whether to go for long or short hair. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to know if you suit long or short hair, with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care tips to help you make the right decision.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle
  • Tips for Choosing Long or Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding whether you suit long or short hair is to determine your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, and knowing your face shape can help you choose a style that flatters your features.

There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, oblong, heart, and diamond. If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are plenty of online guides that can help you figure it out.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step is to consider your hair type. Is your hair straight, wavy, or curly? Thin or thick? Knowing your hair type can help you choose a style that works with your natural texture and volume.

For example, if you have thin, fine hair, a short bob may be a better option than long, heavy layers. On the other hand, if you have thick, curly hair, a long, layered style may be more flattering than a short, blunt cut.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Finally, think about your lifestyle. Do you have time to style long hair every day, or do you need a low-maintenance style that you can wash and go? Do you work in a conservative office where short hair is more appropriate, or do you have a job that allows for more creative expression?

Considering your lifestyle can help you choose a style that works for your daily routine and professional environment.

Tips for Choosing Long or Short Hair

  • Long hair can be more versatile, allowing for more styling options, but it requires more maintenance.
  • Short hair can be easier to manage and style, but it may not be as versatile.
  • Consider trying a wig or hair extensions to experiment with different lengths before committing to a cut.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who can help you determine which length and style will work best for your face shape and hair type.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the cut. A short bob with layers can actually elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. Consult with a hairstylist to find a cut that flatters your features.

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes! You can still wear a ponytail with shorter hair. Simply gather your hair into a small ponytail at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you choose long or short hair, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-styling with heat tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools to minimize damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health, such as biotin, vitamin C, and omega-3 fatty acids.

With these tips and steps, you can determine whether long or short hair is the right choice for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and consult with a hairstylist before making a final decision.
