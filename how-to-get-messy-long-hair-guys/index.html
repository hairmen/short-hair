How To Get Messy Long Hair Guys A Guide For Relaxed Hairstyles

Written by Christine Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair has been a trend for men in the past years, and the messy look is no exception. It gives a laid-back, effortless vibe that can enhance any guy's look. However, getting that perfect messy hairstyle can be tricky, especially if you're dealing with long hair. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve that messy long hair look that will make you stand out.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Messy Long Hair Guys

The first step to achieving that messy long hair look is to know your hair type. Depending on your hair's texture and thickness, you may need to adjust your styling techniques. Here are some tips:

  • For fine hair: Use lightweight styling products to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • For thick hair: Use a stronger hold product to keep your hair in place.
  • For curly hair: Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • For straight hair: Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Once you know your hair type, it's time to choose your styling product. Here are some options:

  • Pomade: Provides a medium to strong hold and a natural finish.
  • Clay: Provides a matte finish and a strong hold.
  • Hair wax: Provides a flexible hold and a natural finish.
  • Hairspray: Provides a strong hold and can be used to set your hairstyle in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your hair type and styling product, here's how to get that messy long hair look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply your styling product. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  3. If you have long hair, tie it up in a loose bun or braid to let it dry naturally. This will give your hair a natural wave and texture.
  4. If you have shorter hair, use your fingers to scrunch your hair while blow-drying it on a low heat setting. This will create volume and texture.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.
  6. If needed, use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Messy Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep your messy long hair looking great:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy look.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, try washing it every other day or every two days.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

What do I do if my hair is too thick for the messy look?

If you have thick hair, try using a stronger hold styling product, such as clay or hair wax. You can also ask your hairstylist to thin out your hair to make it more manageable.

What if my hair is too long for the messy look?

If you have very long hair, try tying it up in a loose bun or braid to create natural waves and texture. You can also ask your hairstylist for a layered cut to add volume and movement to your hair.

How do I prevent my hair from getting too greasy?

Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy. You can also try using dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair.

Hair Care for Messy Long Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your messy long hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, gently pat your hair dry.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Now that you know how to get messy long hair guys, it's time to try it out for yourself. With the right styling product and techniques, you can achieve that laid-back, effortless look that will make you stand out. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
