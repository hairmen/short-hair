How To Style Short Straight Hair Guys

Written by Eveline Jul 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair is a classic look that never goes out of style. However, it can be challenging to style short straight hair, especially for guys. In this article, we have put together a step-by-step guide on how to style short straight hair guys. Whether you want a casual or a formal look, these tips will help you achieve your desired hairstyle.

Description

Short straight hair is a popular hairstyle among guys. However, it can be challenging to style it as it lacks volume and texture. This can make it difficult to create different hairstyles. In this article, we will cover different ways you can style your short straight hair, including the products you need and the steps to follow.

How to Style Short Straight Hair Guys

Styling short straight hair can be achieved in several ways. You can try a slicked-back look, a messy look, or a side-swept hairstyle. To achieve any of these looks, you will need the right products, including a good quality hair gel or pomade.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling it.

Step 2: Apply a hair styling product. Depending on the style you want to achieve, apply a hair gel or pomade to your hair. Use a small amount to avoid weighing down your hair.

Step 3: Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair. Use a comb to create a slicked-back look or a side-swept hairstyle. If you want a messy look, use your fingers to create texture.

Step 4: Finish with a hairspray. To keep your hairstyle in place, use a hairspray. This will ensure your hair stays in place all day.

Tips for Maintaining Short Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short straight hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Use a hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place all day.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

Q. How often should I wash my short straight hair?
A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. However, if you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day.

Q. Can I use a hairdryer to style my short straight hair?
A. Yes, you can use a hairdryer to style your hair. However, make sure you use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Straight Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short straight hair healthy:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

In conclusion, styling short straight hair guys can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve any hairstyle you want. Follow these tips and tricks to create a stylish and modern look that suits your personality and lifestyle.
