Shoulder Length Hair Face Framing Layers A Trending Style In 2023

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, shoulder length hair with face framing layers is the perfect option. This hairstyle is versatile and suits all face shapes and hair types, making it a popular choice among women in 2023. In this blog post, we will discuss how to achieve this style, tips for maintaining it, and answer frequently asked questions.

Description

Shoulder length hair face framing layers is a hairstyle that involves cutting layers around the face to frame it. The layers are cut in a way that accentuates the cheekbones and jawline. This style is perfect for those who want to add dimension to their hair without sacrificing length. It is also an excellent option for those with thin hair as the layers create the illusion of volume.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair Face Framing Layers

The best way to achieve this hairstyle is to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your face shape and recommend the best layering technique to frame your face. However, if you prefer to do it at home, here's how:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back.
  3. Starting with the back section, create a guide by taking a small section of hair and cutting it to your desired length.
  4. Using the guide, cut the rest of the back section in a straight line. Be careful not to cut too much hair as the layers will be added later.
  5. Repeat the same process for the left and right sides.
  6. For the top section, create a guide by taking a small section of hair from the crown and cutting it to your desired length.
  7. Using the guide, cut the rest of the top section in a straight line.
  8. To add layers, take small sections of hair from the front and cut them in a downward motion. This will create face framing layers.
  9. Once you have finished adding layers, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair Face Framing Layers

To maintain this hairstyle, here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and remove split ends.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip the natural oils and make it dry.

FAQ

Is shoulder length hair face framing layers suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, it is suitable for all face shapes. However, the layering technique may vary depending on your face shape.

Can I add highlights to shoulder length hair face framing layers?

Yes, highlights can add dimension to the layers and enhance the face-framing effect.

Can I style my hair in different ways with shoulder length hair face framing layers?

Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage it.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too many chemical treatments on your hair as they can weaken it.

Shoulder length hair face framing layers is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to add dimension to their hair without sacrificing length. It is suitable for all face shapes and hair types, making it a popular choice among women in 2023. By following the tips mentioned above, you can maintain this hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
