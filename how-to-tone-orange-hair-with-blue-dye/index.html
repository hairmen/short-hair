How To Tone Orange Hair With Blue Dye

Written by Bardi Nov 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling with orange hair after bleaching or coloring? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience orange tones in their hair, especially when trying to go lighter. But there's a solution - toning with blue dye. In this blog post, we'll walk you through how to tone orange hair with blue dye, step by step, along with some tips and FAQs to help you achieve your desired hair color.

How to Tone Orange Hair with Blue Dye

When it comes to toning orange hair, blue is the opposite color on the color wheel, making it the perfect complementary color to neutralize the orange tones. Here's how you can tone your orange hair with blue dye:

What You'll Need:

  • Blue hair dye
  • Developer
  • Bowl and brush
  • Gloves
  • Towel

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to tone your orange hair with blue dye:

  1. Start by mixing the blue hair dye and developer in a bowl according to the instructions on the packaging.
  2. Put on gloves to protect your hands from the dye.
  3. Apply the mixture to your hair using a brush, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Leave the dye on your hair for the recommended time on the packaging. Be sure to keep an eye on your hair to avoid over-toning or damaging it.
  5. Rinse the dye out of your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  6. Use a towel to gently pat your hair dry.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when toning your orange hair with blue dye:

  • Choose a blue hair dye with a level 9 or 10, as these shades are the most effective in neutralizing orange tones.
  • Avoid leaving the dye on your hair for too long, as this can lead to over-toning and damage.
  • Be sure to use a developer that's appropriate for your hair type and the dye you're using.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo before toning to remove any buildup on your hair, which can affect the toning process.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about toning orange hair with blue dye:

Can I tone orange hair with purple shampoo?

No, purple shampoo is specifically designed to neutralize yellow tones in blonde hair, not orange tones.

How long will the toning effect last?

The toning effect can last anywhere from a few washes to a couple of weeks, depending on your hair type and how often you wash your hair.

Can I tone my hair with blue dye if it's not orange?

No, toning with blue dye is specifically for neutralizing orange tones in hair.

Hair Care after Toning

After toning your orange hair with blue dye, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the color from your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water, as this can cause the color to fade and damage your hair.

By following these tips and using blue dye to tone your orange hair, you can achieve your desired hair color and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
