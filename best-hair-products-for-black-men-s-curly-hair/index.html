Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your black men's curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than once a week to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply products to wet hair for best results.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage curly hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or using a satin or silk pillowcase.
