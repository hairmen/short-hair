How To Cut Long Men s Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're tired of your long locks and want to change things up, cutting your hair short can be a great option. But before you grab the scissors and start hacking away, it's important to know exactly how to do it to avoid any mishaps. In this article, we'll go through a step-by-step guide on how to cut long men's hair short, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Cut Long Men's Hair Short

Cutting long hair short can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great look. Here's how:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. These include:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Mirror

Make sure your scissors are sharp and your clippers are fully charged before you start.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want. It's a good idea to start longer and gradually cut more if needed. Use your fingers to measure the length you want, and mark it with a clip or hair tie.

Step 3: Wet Your Hair

Wet hair is easier to cut, so spray your hair with water until it's damp. This will also help you see the natural fall of your hair.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Start with the sides and back of your head. Use your clippers with a guard to cut your hair to the desired length. If you're not sure which guard to use, start with a longer one and work your way down. Use your comb to guide the clippers, and make sure you're cutting evenly.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Once you've cut the sides and back, it's time to tackle the top. Use your scissors to cut small sections at a time, starting at the front and working your way back. Use your fingers as a guide to cut to the desired length, and make sure you're cutting evenly.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

To create a more natural look, you'll want to blend the layers. Use your scissors to cut into the hair, creating texture and blending the layers together. Make sure you're cutting at an angle to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 7: Clean Up the Edges

Use your clippers without a guard to clean up the edges around your ears and neck. Be careful not to cut too high, as this can create an uneven look.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, check your work in the mirror. Make sure everything is even and there are no stray hairs. If you notice any uneven spots, use your scissors or clippers to fix them.

Step 9: Style as Desired

Now that you've cut your hair short, it's time to style it. Use a styling product like pomade or wax to create your desired look. Experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.

Step 10: Maintain Your Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking sharp. Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim, and use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

Tips for Cutting Long Men's Hair Short

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting long men's hair short:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way down if needed
  • Cut small sections at a time
  • Use your fingers to measure the length you want
  • Blend the layers for a natural look
  • Check your work in the mirror regularly

FAQs

1. Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair, but it's important to have the right tools and technique. If you're not confident, it's best to visit a barber or stylist.

2. How often should I get my hair cut?

Short hair requires regular maintenance, so it's best to visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

3. How do I know which guard to use on my clippers?

If you're not sure which guard to use, start with a longer one and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims
  • Experiment with different styling products
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often

By following these tips, you can keep your short hair looking sharp and stylish.

Conclusion

Cutting long men's hair short can be a great way to change up your look, but it's important to know how to do it properly. By following this step-by-step guide and keeping our tips and FAQs in mind, you can achieve a great short hairstyle that looks sharp and stylish. Just remember to maintain your hair regularly and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
