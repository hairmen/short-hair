Shoulder Length Hairstyles With Long Fringe A Relaxed Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle, shoulder length hair with a long fringe may be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is suitable for various hair types and face shapes, and it can be customized to fit your personal style. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve and maintain a shoulder length hairstyle with a long fringe.

Description

Shoulder length hairstyles with long fringe feature hair that falls just above or below the shoulders, with a longer section of hair in the front that frames the face. This hairstyle can be customized with layers, curls, or straight hair to create different looks. The fringe can be side-swept, blunt, or textured, depending on your preference.

How to Achieve a Shoulder Length Hairstyle with Long Fringe

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before you decide to get a shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe, it's essential to consult with your hairstylist to determine whether this style is suitable for your face shape and hair type. Your hairstylist can also recommend the best fringe style for you and help you choose the right length for your hair.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair to Shoulder Length

Once you've consulted with your hairstylist, it's time to cut your hair to shoulder length. Your hairstylist will use a combination of scissors and a razor to create the desired length and texture. If you prefer layers, your hairstylist will also add them at this stage.

Step 3: Add a Long Fringe

After cutting your hair to shoulder length, your hairstylist will add a long fringe that frames your face. The fringe can be side-swept, blunt, or textured, depending on your preference. Your hairstylist may also use a razor to add texture and movement to your fringe.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hairstyles with Long Fringe

Tip 1: Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and texture. If you have curly hair, use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair to prevent frizz and dryness. If you have straight hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tip 2: Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a straightener or curling iron, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Apply the spray to your hair before using the heat styling tool.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the length and shape of your shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe. Regular trims also prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQ

1. Is a shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe suitable for all face shapes?

A shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe is suitable for various face shapes. However, it's essential to consult with your hairstylist to determine whether this style is suitable for your face shape.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair twice a week.

3. Can I style my shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe in different ways?

Yes, you can style your shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe in different ways. You can try different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle. You can also curl or straighten your hair for a different look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. To keep your shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.

With proper care and maintenance, your shoulder length hairstyle with long fringe can look beautiful and stylish for months to come.
