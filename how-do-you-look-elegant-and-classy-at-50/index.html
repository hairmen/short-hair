How To Look Elegant And Classy At 50

Written by Frank Sep 20, 2023 · 4 min read

When women reach the age of 50, they may start to feel that they need to update their style to look age-appropriate yet elegant and classy. Fortunately, it's easy to achieve that look with a few simple tips and tricks. In this blog post, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to look elegant and classy at 50.

Table of Contents:

  • Part 1: How to Dress Elegantly at 50
  • Part 2: Hair Care for Women Over 50
  • Part 3: Frequently Asked Questions

Part 1: How to Dress Elegantly at 50

When it comes to dressing elegantly at 50, the key is to choose clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape. Here are some tips:

1. Invest in Classic Pieces

Classic pieces such as a well-tailored blazer, a white button-down shirt, and a little black dress are timeless and versatile. They can be dressed up or down and never go out of style. Invest in high-quality versions of these pieces that will last for years.

2. Opt for Neutral Colors

Neutral colors such as black, white, navy, and beige are sophisticated and elegant. They also make it easy to mix and match different pieces in your wardrobe. Add pops of color with accessories such as scarves, jewelry, and handbags.

3. Choose Flattering Silhouettes

Choose clothes that flatter your body shape. If you have a pear-shaped body, opt for A-line skirts and dresses that cinch at the waist. If you have an hourglass figure, choose clothes that highlight your curves. Avoid baggy clothes that hide your figure.

4. Pay Attention to Fabric and Texture

Choose fabrics and textures that are luxurious and high-quality. Silk, cashmere, and wool are great choices for dresses, blouses, and sweaters. Avoid cheap fabrics that look and feel flimsy.

Part 2: Hair Care for Women Over 50

As women age, their hair may become thinner and more brittle. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and elegant:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle and nourishing. Look for products that contain ingredients such as keratin, biotin, and argan oil that help to strengthen and protect your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as hairdryers, flat irons, and curling irons too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on low.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

4. Embrace Your Natural Hair Texture

Embrace your natural hair texture and work with it instead of against it. If you have curly hair, use products that define and enhance your curls. If you have straight hair, use products that add volume and texture.

Part 3: Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I still wear jeans at 50?

Yes, you can still wear jeans at 50. Opt for dark-wash jeans that are tailored and fit well. Pair them with a blouse or sweater and accessorize with a scarf or statement jewelry.

2. What kind of shoes should I wear to look elegant and classy?

Choose shoes that are comfortable yet stylish. Classic options such as ballet flats, loafers, and pumps are great choices. Avoid shoes with too high of a heel or too much embellishment.

3. Should I wear makeup at 50?

Makeup can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel more confident. Opt for a natural look with neutral eyeshadows, a touch of blush, and a tinted lip balm. Use a moisturizer with SPF to protect your skin from the sun.

By following these tips for dressing elegantly, taking care of your hair, and accessorizing with simple yet stylish pieces, you can look elegant and classy at 50. Remember to choose clothes that fit well, flatter your body shape, and make you feel confident and comfortable.
