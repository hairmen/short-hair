Cute Quick Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle every day? Do you wish you could switch it up without spending hours in front of the mirror? Look no further! We have compiled a list of cute and quick hairstyles for medium length hair that will have you looking fabulous in no time.

Description

Medium length hair can be tricky to style. It’s not long enough for some updos, but it’s not short enough for a simple ponytail. That’s why we’ve put together a list of cute and quick hairstyles that are perfect for medium length hair. Whether you’re going to work or a night out with friends, these hairstyles will have you looking your best without taking up too much of your time.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it’s important to know how to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Once your hair is clean, apply a heat protectant to avoid any damage from styling tools. Blow dry your hair or let it air dry before styling.

1. Half Up Half Down

This is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Simply take the top half of your hair and tie it in a ponytail, leaving the bottom half down. You can leave it as is, or add some curls or waves for an extra touch.

2. Messy Bun

The messy bun is perfect for those days when you don’t have time to wash your hair. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun and pull out a few pieces of hair to create a messy look.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to add some texture to your hair. You can do a simple braid down the back, or try a fishtail braid for something more intricate. You can also do a half-up braid or a braided crown for a boho look.

4. Low Bun

If you’re looking for a more polished look, try a low bun. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure it and pull out a few pieces of hair to frame your face.

Step by Step

Here are step by step instructions for each hairstyle:

1. Half Up Half Down

  1. Take the top half of your hair and tie it in a ponytail.
  2. Leave the bottom half down.
  3. Add curls or waves if desired.

2. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair to create a messy look.

3. Braids

  1. Choose your desired braid style.
  2. Divide your hair into sections if necessary.
  3. Braid your hair.
  4. Secure the end with a hair tie.

4. Low Bun

  1. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair to frame your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to make these hairstyles even easier:

  • Invest in good quality hair ties and bobby pins.
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different textures and accessories.
  • Don’t be afraid to add a pop of color with hair chalk or temporary hair dye.

FAQ

1. How do I choose the right hairstyle for my face shape?

There are many resources online that can help you determine your face shape and which hairstyles are the most flattering. Some general guidelines include:

  • Oval face: most hairstyles will work
  • Round face: avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides
  • Square face: soft, layered styles work best
  • Heart-shaped face: styles that frame the face work well

2. How do I keep my hair healthy?

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use products that are appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a heat protectant when using styling tools
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water

With these cute and quick hairstyles for medium length hair, you can switch up your look without spending hours in front of the mirror. Try them out and see which ones work best for you!
